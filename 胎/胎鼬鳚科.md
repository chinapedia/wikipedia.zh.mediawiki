'''胎鼬鳚科'''（Bythitidae），又稱'''须鳚'''、'''深海鼬魚科''' ，為[[鼬魚目|鼬魚目]]的其中一科。是一種[[胎生|胎生]]魚，分佈於世界各地的熱帶及亞熱帶海域。其體型很小，約只有{{convert|5|-|10|cm|in}}，生活在珊瑚礁及水面，有些深海及洞穴种类，视觉功能几乎或完全退化。卵生或卵胎生。深海种类可见于约7300米深处，是该深度水层中数量最大的类群。<ref name=EoF>{{cite book |editor=Paxton, J.R. & Eschmeyer, W.N.|author= Neilsen, Jørgen G.|year=1998|title=Encyclopedia of Fishes|publisher= Academic Press|location=San Diego|page= 134|isbn= 0-12-547665-5}}</ref>

到2002年為止，該科已發現有110多個新五物種，2006年丹麥和德國科學家又一次性新增26個，<ref name=SMN2005>{{cite journal | journal = The Beagle, Records of the Museum and Art Galleries of the Northern Territory | volume = 21 | year = 2005 | pages = 73–163 | title = Review of the Dinematichthyini (Teleostei: Bythitidae) of the Indo-West Pacific. Part I. ''Diancistrus'' and Two New Genera with 26 New Species | url=http://search.informit.com.au/documentSummary;dn=012814779649528;res=IELHSS | author = Schwarzhans, Werner; Møller, Peter Rask; Nielsen, Jørgen G.}}</ref> 2007年由同樣的科學家新增8屬20種。<ref name=20new>{{cite journal | journal = The Beagle, Records of the Museum and Art Galleries of the Northern Territory | volume = 23 | year = 2007 | pages = 29–110 | title = Review of the Dinematichthyini (Teleostei, Bythitidae) of the Indo-West pacific. Part III. Beaglichthys, Brosmolus, Monothrix and 8 new genera with description of 20 new species. | author = Schwarzhans & Møller}}</ref>
