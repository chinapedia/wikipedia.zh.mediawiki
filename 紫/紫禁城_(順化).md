'''紫禁城'''（{{vie|v=Tử Cấm thành|hn=紫禁城}}）位于[[顺化皇城|顺化皇城]]后部，[[阮朝|阮朝]]皇帝日常活动和后妃居住的场所，1822年用围墙包围，禁止男子进入，故名紫禁城。<ref>Có tài liệu {{cite web |url=http://www.hueworldheritage.org.vn/quanthe/Tucamthanh/index.asp |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2013-11-30 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110827095359/http://www.hueworldheritage.org.vn/quanthe/Tucamthanh/index.asp |archivedate=2011-08-27 }} ghi năm Minh Mạng thứ 3 (1822)</ref><ref>[http://www.thegioidulich.com.vn/trung-quoc/co-cung-tu-cam-thanh-bac-kinh-2.html Cố Cung - Tử Cấm Thành Bắc Kinh] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080820121632/http://www.thegioidulich.com.vn/trung-quoc/co-cung-tu-cam-thanh-bac-kinh-2.html |date=2008-08-20 }}</ref>

紫禁城内部拥有众多殿宇。目前大部分毁于战火。
