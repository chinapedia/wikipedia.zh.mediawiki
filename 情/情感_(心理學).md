'''情感'''（英語：Affect）是心理學中用來描述[[感覺|感覺]]或[[情绪|情緒]]經驗的概念，情感一詞在其他領域有不同的含義<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://www.worldcat.org/oclc/463856021|title=Psychology|last=Neil.|first=Martin, G.|last2=William.|first2=Buskist,|date=2010|publisher=Allyn and Bacon|year=|isbn=9780273720119|edition=4th ed|location=Harlow, England|pages=|chapter=|oclc=463856021}}</ref>。在心理學中，情感是生物體與刺激相互作用的導體。這個詞有時也指情感表達，像是以面部、聲音或手勢等行為作為情感的指標<ref>{{Cite book|url=http://www.worldcat.org/title/apa-dictionary-of-psychology/oclc/173190772|title=APA dictionary of psychology|last=VandenBos|first=Gary R|date=2006|publisher=American Psychological Association|year=|isbn=1591473802|location=Washington, DC.|pages=|language=en|chapter=}}</ref>。

情感領域是現代心理學描述的三個分支之一，這三個分支為[[認知|認知]]、[[意動|意動]]和情感。情感也在「心理學ABC（ABC of psychology）」的行列中，即 「情感（Affect）」、「[[行為|行為]]（Behavior）」和「[[認知|認知]]（Cognition）」<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Affect is a form of cognition: A neurobiological analysis|author=|url=https://doi.org/10.1080/02699930701437931|last=Duncan|first=Seth|last2=Barrett|first2=Lisa Feldman|date=2007-09|journal=Cognition & Emotion|issue=6|doi=10.1080/02699930701437931|others=|year=|volume=21|page=|pages=1184–1211|language=en|issn=0269-9931|pmc=2396787|pmid=18509504}}</ref>。 

情感狀態是一種心理－生理學結構（psycho-physiological constructs）。目前大多數的觀點認為，情感狀態主要沿著三個主要方面變化：效價（valence）、喚起（arousal）和動機強度（Motivational salience）<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Does Negative Affect Always Narrow and Positive Affect Always Broaden the Mind? Considering the Influence of Motivational Intensity on Cognitive Scope|url=http://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.1177/0963721413481353|last=Harmon-Jones|first=Eddie|last2=Gable|first2=Philip A.|date=2013-8|journal=Current Directions in Psychological Science|issue=4|doi=10.1177/0963721413481353|volume=22|pages=301–307|language=en|issn=0963-7214|last3=Price|first3=Tom F.}}</ref>。效價是對經驗狀態主觀的正面評價。情緒效價則是指情緒的後果、情緒引發的情境或主觀感受的態度<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Attitudes toward emotions.|url=http://doi.apa.org/getdoi.cfm?doi=10.1037/a0024951|last=Harmon-Jones|first=Eddie|last2=Harmon-Jones|first2=Cindy|date=2011|journal=Journal of Personality and Social Psychology|issue=6|doi=10.1037/a0024951|volume=101|pages=1332–1350|language=en|issn=1939-1315|last3=Amodio|first3=David M.|last4=Gable|first4=Philip A.}}</ref>。喚起能夠透過交感神經系統的啟動來做客觀的測量，也能夠通過自我報告進行主觀的評估。喚起是一種與動機強度密切相關的結構，但它們的不同之處在於動機必然會引起動作，而喚醒則不然<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Does arousal per se account for the influence of appetitive stimuli on attentional scope and the late positive potential?: Approach, arousal, and attentional scope|url=http://doi.wiley.com/10.1111/psyp.12023|last=Gable|first=Philip A.|last2=Harmon-Jones|first2=Eddie|date=2013-4|journal=Psychophysiology|issue=4|doi=10.1111/psyp.12023|volume=50|pages=344–350|language=en}}</ref>。動機強度是指對於行動的衝動<ref>{{Cite web|title=emotion {{!}} The Penguin Dictionary of Psychology - Credo Reference|url=https://search.credoreference.com/content/entry/penguinpsyc/emotion/0|work=search.credoreference.com|accessdate=2019-02-26}}</ref>、渴望走向或遠離刺激的力量。簡單動作也可以沒有動機衝動<ref>{{Cite journal|title=What is Approach Motivation?|url=http://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.1177/1754073913477509|last=Harmon-Jones|first=Eddie|last2=Harmon-Jones|first2=Cindy|date=2013-7|journal=Emotion Review|issue=3|doi=10.1177/1754073913477509|volume=5|pages=291–295|language=en|issn=1754-0739|last3=Price|first3=Tom F.}}</ref>。
