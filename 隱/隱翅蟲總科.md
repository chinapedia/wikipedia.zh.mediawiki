'''隱翅蟲總科'''（學名：{{lang|la|'''Staphylinoidea'''}}）是[[多食亞目|多食亞目]][[甲蟲|甲蟲]]之下的一個總科，屬於[[隐翅虫下目|隐翅虫下目]]的一員。本總科物種分佈全世界，而且多樣性很大，單單是已經命名的物種就有五萬八千種以上<ref name=forest>{{cite news |url=http://recreation.forest.gov.tw/forestpaper/WebShow_01.aspx?SID=617&Page=1 |author=雙流-糞金龜 |title=含冤待雪的隱翅蟲 |publisher=森林報 |date=2014-07-04 |accessdate=2016-08-18 |language=zh-hant }}</ref>。

==特徵==
大多數隱翅蟲類物種皆為中型到小型的甲蟲，有一對變短了的[[鞘翅|鞘翅]]（[[翅鞘|elytra]]）。The hind wings have no accessory posterior ridge (locking device), no wedge cell and apical cell. Abdominal 8th segment not entirely invaginated in the 7th. Head mostly without coronal suture.<ref>{{cite journal |first1=M. |last1=Hansen |title=Phylogeny and classification of the staphyliniform beetle families (Coleoptera) |journal=Biologiske Skrifter |volume=48 |location=[[哥本哈根|Copenhagen]] |year=1997 |language=en }}</ref>

* {{tsl|en|Hydraenidae||Hydraenidae}} Mulsant 1844
* [[球蕈甲科|球蕈甲科]]（[[球蕈甲科|Leiodidae]] <small>Fleming, 1821</small> = [[Anisotomidae|Anisotomidae]]）
* {{tsl|en|Ptiliidae||Ptiliidae}} Erichson 1845

* {{tsl|en|Scydmaenidae||Scydmaenidae}} Leach 1815
* [[埋葬虫科|埋葬虫科]]（[[葬甲科|Silphidae]] <small>Latreille, 1807</small>）
