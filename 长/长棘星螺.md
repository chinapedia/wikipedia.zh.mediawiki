'''长棘星螺'''（学名：''{{lang|la|Guildfordia yoka}}''），又名'''長刺螺'''{{r|zgdwz}}，是一種深海[[海螺|海螺]]的[[物種|物種]]，屬於[[海洋生物|海洋]][[腹足綱|腹足綱]][[軟體動物|軟體動物]]的[[蝾螺科|蝾螺科]][[刺螺屬|刺螺屬]]的一個物種{{r|shell_174|WoRMS_413406}}。

==分佈與棲息地==
這種熱帶的海洋生物物種棲息於西太平洋的日本和菲律賓對開海域，見於水深{{convert|200|and|500|m|abbr=on}}的海洋{{r|Sealife}}；
也見於[[中国大陆|中国大陆]]、[[台湾|台湾]]{{r|shell_174}}。常栖息在潮下带{{r|shell_174}}。

==型態描述==
The size of the shell varies between {{convert|70|and|105|mm|abbr=on}}. The color pattern of the shell varies from very light brownish to purple-brown. Some specimens contain only 6 [[Whorl_(mollusc)|whorl]]s instead of 7 as in the holotype. The upper whorls are smooth, then follows the [[body_whorl|body whorl]] with scarcely 2 rows of granules, instead of 7 or 8. Towards the keel it has very irregular radiating ribs, which leave however a nearly smooth zone above the keel, with only a few spiral striae.

The base of the shell is less convex than the upper part. Some specimens lack the rose-coloured line round the umbilical callosity. The [[Aperture_(mollusc)|aperture]] is oval, thick, with the nucleus in the external lower corner. The outer surface is slightly rugose, by irregular wrinkles, almost parallel with the basal margin of the [[Operculum_(gastropod)|operculum]]. The nucleus is marked by an olive spot.<ref name="Schepman, 1908"/>

==Biology==
Embryos of ''Guildfordia yoka'' develop into free-swimming planktonic larvae with several bands of cilia ([[trocophore|trocophore]]). Later they develop into juvenile [[veliger|veliger]]s, finally into fully grown adults.<ref name=Sealife/>
