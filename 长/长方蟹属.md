'''长方蟹属'''（[[学名|学名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Metaplax'''''}}），舊屬[[方蟹科|方蟹科]]<ref name='id12037'>{{cite web| url =http://www.bioinfo.cn/db05/BjdwSpecies.php?action=view&id=12037| title =秀丽长方蟹| work =《中国动物物种编目数据库》| publisher =中国科学院微生物研究所| author =中国科学院动物研究所| accessdate =2009-04-28| language =zh-hans| deadurl =yes| archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20160305004523/http://www.bioinfo.cn/db05/BjdwSpecies.php?action=view&id=12037| archivedate =2016-03-05}}</ref>，今屬[[方蟹總科|方蟹總科]][[弓蟹科|弓蟹科]][[圓方蟹亞科|圓方蟹亞科]]<ref name=WoRMS_439870>{{cite WoRMS |last=Davie |first=P. |year=2011|title=''Metaplax'' <small>H. Milne Edwards, 1852 </small> |id=439870 }}</ref>。本屬的[[模式種|模式種]]是''Metaplax distincta'' <small>H. Milne Edwards, 1852</small> (type by subsequent designation)。

本屬物種有寄生物種''Megacepon disparatum'' <small>An, Boyko & Li, 2012</small><ref name=WoRMS_439870 />。

==物種==
本屬連同在2003年確認的「新」物種，合計有12個物種<ref name=rmbr>{{cite journal |url=http://rmbr.nus.edu.sg/rbz/biblio/51/51rbz379-386.pdf |title=HE 
|journal=[[The_Raffles_Bulletin_of_Zoology|The Raffles Bulletin of Zoology]] |year=2003 |volume=51 |issue=2 |pages=379-386 |title=A New Species of Metaplax (Crustacea: Brachyura: Varunidae: Cyclograpsinae) from Vietnam|language=en }}</ref>，分別如下<ref name=WoRMS_439870 />：
* ''Metaplax crenulata'' <small>(Gerstaecker, 1856)</small>
* ''Metaplax dentipes'' <small>(Heller, 1865)</small>
* ''Metaplax distincta'' <small>H. Milne Edwards, 1852</small>
*[[秀丽长方蟹|秀丽长方蟹]] ''Metaplax elegans'' <small>de Man, 1888</small>
*[[鵝貢長方蟹|鵝貢長方蟹]] ''Metaplax gocongensis'' <small>Davie & Nguyen, 2003</small><ref name=rmbr />
* ''Metaplax indica'' <small>H. Milne Edwards, 1852</small>
* ''Metaplax intermedia'' <small>de Man, 1888</small>
*[[长足长方蟹|长足长方蟹]] ''Metaplax longipes'' <small>Stimpson, 1858</small>
*[[西方長方蟹|西方長方蟹]] ''Metaplax occidentalis'' <small>Pretzmann, 1971</small>
*[[沈氏长方蟹|沈氏长方蟹]] ''Metaplax sheni'' <small>Gordon, 1930</small>
*[[少疣长方蟹|少疣长方蟹]] ''Metaplax takahashii'' <small>Sakai, 1939</small>
* ''Metaplax tredecim'' <small>Tweedie, 1950</small>
