'''美国罕见疾病组织'''（[[英文|英文]]：'''The National Organization for Rare Disorders '''，[[縮寫|縮寫]]：'''NORD'''）是[[美国|美国]]一家以为[[罕见病|罕见病]]患者服务为宗旨的[[非营利组织|非营利组织]]，主要方式是推动和资助相关研究、教育和合作<ref name=about>{{Cite web | accessdate=14 February 2009 | title=About NORD | url=http://www.rarediseases.org/info/about.html | publisher=National Organization of Rare Diseases | date=30 July 2008 | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090217225011/http://rarediseases.org/info/about.html | archivedate=2009年2月17日 }}</ref>。该组织成立于1983年，创办者是阿贝·迈耶斯，多个罕见病支持团体参与创办<ref name=about/><ref name=reuters>{{Cite web | url=http://www.reuters.com/article/pressRelease/idUS81376+05-May-2008+BW20080505 | title=The National Organization for Rare Disorders (NORD) Names Peter Saltonstall New President | work=[[Reuters|Reuters]] | date=5 May 2008 | accessdate=14 February 2009 }}{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref> 。该组织是一家 [[501(c)条款|501(c)3]]免税组织<ref name=about/>。

==历史==
1982年，罕见病家庭和支持团体成立了一个“非正式联盟”，呼吁立法支持[[孤儿药|孤儿药]]和治疗罕见病的药物研发<ref name=retirement>{{Cite web | url=http://www.eurordis.org/article.php3?id_article=1498 | title=Abbey Meyers, President of NORD, announces her retirement | publisher=European Organization for Rare Disorders | accessdate=14 February 2009 | date=July 2007 | first=Jerome | last=Parisse-Brassens | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071009003136/http://www.eurordis.org/article.php3?id_article=1498 | archivedate=2007年10月9日 }} </ref>。这个组织成功地使[[美国国会|美国国会]]在1983年初通过了{{link-en|孤儿药法案|Orphan Drug Act of 1983}}（ODA）<ref name=about/><ref name=retirement/><ref name=millions>{{Cite web | url=http://sev.prnewswire.com/health-care-hospitals/20090213/DC7071513022009-1.html | title=Millions Around World to Observe Rare Disease Day | accessdate=14 February 2009 | date=13 February 2009 | publisher=[[PR_Newswire|PR Newswire]] }}{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref name=henkel>{{Cite web| url=http://www.fda.gov/Fdac/features/1999/399_orph.html | last=Henkel | first=John | title=Orphan Drug Law Matures into Medical Mainstay | year=1999 | work=FDA Consumer | publisher=U.S. Food and Drug Administration | accessdate=14 February 2009}}</ref>。

联盟最初由阿贝·迈耶斯领导，一位[[妥瑞症|妥瑞症]]患者的母亲<ref name=pp>{{Cite web| url=http://powerfulpatient.org/archive/2008/08b_diagnosis.php | work=Powerful Patient | year=2008 | title=Abbey Meyers and the National Organization for Rare Disorders | publisher=VHL Family Alliance | first=Joyce | last=Graff | accessdate=14 February 2009}}</ref>。妥瑞症不是罕见病，但迈耶斯的儿子得到一种孤儿药的救治。孤儿药法案通过后，这一联盟成立了美国罕见疾病组织，由迈耶斯担任主席。2007年，她宣布卸任主席职务<ref name=retirement/>。现任主席为皮特·索顿斯托尔<ref name=reuters/>。

美国罕见疾病组织自成立以来，一直收到美国联邦政府拨款<ref name=retirement/>和社会捐赠资助。

==活动==
美国罕见疾病组织主要活动包括资助罕见病的研究和治疗、游说立法（除了推动孤儿药法案通过，还推动立法使病例公开在互联网上和研发相关[[医疗器械|医疗器械]]<ref name=retirement/>）、普及罕见病知识、资助罕见病患者就医等<ref name=about/><ref name=rare>{{Cite web| url=http://www.southcoasttoday.com/apps/pbcs.dll/article?AID=/20090202/LIFE/902020307/-1/ENTERTAIN | title='Rare' diseases aren't rare to those who suffer them | accessdate=14 February 2009 | date=2 February 2009 | first=Paul G. | last=Donohue | work=[[The_Standard-Times_(New_Bedford)|The Standard-Times]]}}</ref>。2009年2月，美国罕见疾病组织在美国发起开展[[国际罕见病日|国际罕见病日]]活动，这是美国首次开展国际罕见病日活动（欧洲在2008年2月第一次举办国际罕见病日活动）<ref name=millions/><ref name=mystery>{{Cite web | url=http://sev.prnewswire.com/television/20090209/PH6815409022009-1.html | publisher=[[PR_Newswire|PR Newswire]] | accessdate=14 February 2009 | title=National Organization for Rare Disorders To Partner With Discovery Health and 'MYSTERY DIAGNOSIS' for Rare Disease Day 2009 | date=9 February 2009 | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090617184654/http://sev.prnewswire.com/television/20090209/PH6815409022009-1.html | archivedate=2009年6月17日 }}</ref><ref name=pharmi>{{Cite web | url=http://www.pharmiweb.com/pressreleases/pressrel.asp?ROW_ID=5655 | title=February 28th is Rare Disease Day | date=12 February 2009 | accessdate=14 February 2009 | work=PharmiWeb.com | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140222230834/http://www.pharmiweb.com/pressreleases/pressrel.asp?ROW_ID=5655 | archivedate=2014年2月22日 }}</ref>。
