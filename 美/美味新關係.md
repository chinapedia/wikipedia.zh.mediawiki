《'''美味新關係'''》（{{lang-en|''Young & Hungry''}}）是一部由{{link-en|大衛·霍爾登|David Holden (screenwriter)}}開創的[[美國|美國]][[情境喜劇|情境喜劇]]，並由演出[[迪士尼頻道|迪士尼]]《[[小查與寇弟的頂級生活|小查與寇弟的頂級生活]]》、《[[歌舞青春|歌舞青春]]》系列成名的[[艾希莉·提斯代爾|艾希莉·緹絲黛爾]]擔任執行製作。本劇由迪士尼熱門影集《[[孟漢娜|孟漢娜]]》的[[艾蜜莉·奧斯蒙|艾蜜莉·奧斯蒙]]與{{link-en|喬納森·史當思齊|Jonathan Sadowski}}擔綱出演，並於2014年6月25日在[[ABC家庭|ABC Family]]黃金時段播出。<ref name="ABC Family Summer">{{cite web|url=http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2014/03/10/abc-family-announces-summer-premiere-dates-for-pretty-little-liars-switched-at-birth-chasing-life-mystery-girls-chasing-life/243459/ |title=ABC Family Announces Summer Premiere Dates for 'Pretty Little Liars', 'Switched at Birth' 'Chasing Life', 'Mystery Girls' & 'Young & Hungry' |last=Kondolojy |first=Amanda |publisher=[[TV_by_the_Numbers|TV by the Numbers]]|date=2014-03-10 |accessdate=2014-03-10}}</ref><ref name="pickup">{{cite web|url=http://www.deadline.com/2014/01/abc-family-picks-up-comedy-pilots-starring-tori-spelling-jennie-garth-emily-osment-to-series/|title=ABC Family Picks Up Comedy Pilots Starring Tori Spelling, Jennie Garth & Emily Osment To Series|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|publisher=[[Deadline.com|Deadline.com]]|date=2014-01-06|accessdate=2014-01-07}}</ref>

2018年3月15日，Freeform證實第5季為本劇最終季，但預計開發2小時電影版來為劇集作結。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tvline.com/2018/03/15/young-and-hungry-cancelled-season-5-premiere-date-movie/|title=Young & Hungry to End With Season 5 — But (as Usual) There's a Twist!|author=Andy Swift|publisher={{le|TVLine|TVLine}}|date=2018-03-15|accessdate=2018-03-15}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://thefutoncritic.com/news/2018/03/15/beloved-freeform-comedy-young-and-hungry-returns-for-its-farewell-season-on-wednesday-june-20-615113/20180315freeform01/|title=Beloved Freeform Comedy "Young & Hungry" Returns for its Farewell Season on Wednesday, June 20|publisher={{le|富頓評論家|The Futon Critic}}|date=2018-03-15|accessdate=2018-03-15}}</ref>

== 故事大綱 ==
本劇講述一名科技新貴喬許（{{link-en|喬納森·史當思齊|Jonathan Sadowski}} 飾演）雇用了一名好勝心強的年輕美食部落客佳碧（[[艾蜜莉·奧斯蒙|艾蜜莉·奧斯蒙]] 飾演）為其新任私人主廚。同時，佳碧必須向喬許及其個性刻薄的個人助理艾利特（{{link-en|瑞斯·李|Rex Lee (actor)}} 飾演）證明自己的廚藝。本故事是改編自住於[[舊金山|舊金山]]的一位美食部落客佳碧·莫斯科沃的故事。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.pressdemocrat.com/article/20140108/articles/140109658 |title=Smith: Our Gabi inspires a TV show |publisher=The Press Democrat |date=2014-01-08 |accessdate=2014-10-03}}</ref>

== 開發與製作 ==
2013年8月23日，[[ABC家庭|ABC Family]]預訂了試播集。試播集由{{link-en|大衛·霍爾登|David Holden (screenwriter)}}撰寫並由{{link-en|安迪·卡德福|Andy Cadiff}}擔任導演。[[艾希莉·提斯代爾|艾希莉·緹絲黛爾]]、艾瑞克·泰南貝姆、金姆·泰南貝姆及潔西卡·洛德斯擔任執行製作。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2013/08/23/abc-family-greenlights-production-of-three-new-comedy-pilots-32012/20130823abcfamily02/|title=ABC Family Greenlights Production of Three New Comedy Pilots|publisher=The Futon Critic|date=2013-08-23|accessdate=2014-01-07}}</ref>

2014年4月21日試播集正式開拍。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/EmilyOsment/status/458413489271222272 |title=Twitter / EmilyOsment: And a very nice first day on |publisher=Twitter.com |date=2014-04-21 |accessdate=2014-06-01}}</ref>2014年1月6日，ABC Family安排本劇在2014年6月25日於黃金時段播出，隨後播出《{{link-en|神祕女郎|Mystery Girls}}》。<ref name="ABC Family Summer"/>

2014年9月29日，ABC Family宣布續訂第2季。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tvline.com/2014/09/29/young-hungry-renewed-season-2-abc-family/ |title=ABC Family Renews Young & Hungry, Picks Up Procedural Drama Stitchers |last=Iannucci|first=Rebecca|date=2014-09-29|accessdate=2014-09-30}}</ref>2015年8月19日，ABC Family宣布續訂第3季。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2015/08/young-hungry-renewed-season-3-abc-family-1201501619/|title=‘Young & Hungry’ Renewed For Season  3 On ABC Family - Deadline|author=Denise Petski|work=Deadline|accessdate=2015-08-19}}</ref>2016年2月20日，艾蜜莉·奧斯蒙於[[Twitter|Twitter]]上表示將會有第4季。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/EmilyOsment/status/700840535737520128|title=Emily Osment on Twitter|work=[[Twitter|Twitter]]|accessdate=2016-02-21}}</ref>2016年3月7日，Freeform正式宣布續訂第4季。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2016/03/young-hungry-renewed-fourth-season-freeform-ashley-tisdale-1201715530/|title=‘Young & Hungry’ Renewed For Fourth Season By Freeform|author=Denise Petski|work=Deadline|date=2016-03-07|accessdate=2016-03-07}}</ref>2016年10月24日，艾蜜莉·奧斯蒙搶先於個人Twitter上宣布第5季將正式開拍，<ref>{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/EmilyOsment/status/790560470923042816|title=Going back to work for season 5 of Young & Hungry in one week. No one has announced it yet so I am! You're welcome!|publisher=Twitter|accessdate=2016-10-24|date=2016-10-24}}</ref>Freeform隨後於10月27日正式宣布續訂第5季。<ref name="Season 5">{{cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/young-hungry-renewed-season-5-941770|title=Freeform Renews 'Young & Hungry' for Fifth Season|author=Kate Stanhope|work=[[荷里活報道|好萊塢報導]]|date=2016-10-27|accessdate=2016-10-28}}</ref>2017年1月25日，Freeform宣布追加第5季10集集數至20集。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2017/01/young-hungry-comedy-gets-season-5-back-order-freeform-1201894462/|title=‘Young & Hungry’ Comedy Series Gets Season 5 Back Order At Freeform|last=Petski|first=Denise|publisher=Deadline|date=2017-01-25|accessdate=2017-01-27}}</ref>

== 衍生劇 ==
2016年4月7日，[[Freeform|Freeform]]宣布預計開發由[[艾希莉·提斯代爾|艾希莉·緹絲黛爾]]主演並製作的衍生劇。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2016/04/young-hungry-spinoff-ashley-tisdale-later-bitches-late-night-talk-show-pretty-little-liars-premiere-date-season-7-freeform-1201733759/|title=Freeform Developing ‘Young & Hungry’ Spinoff Starring Ashley Tisdale, Late-Night Talk Show, Sets ‘PLL’ Premiere Date|author=Nellie Andreeva|work=Deadline|date=2016-04-07|accessdate=2016-04-07}}</ref>2016年5月23日，公布衍生劇定名為《閨蜜新關係》（{{lang|en|''Young & Sofia''}}），並將以[[艾梅·卡莉羅|艾梅·卡莉羅]]於本劇飾演的蘇菲雅·羅德里格茲為故事中心，講述其成為了艾希莉·緹絲黛爾所飾演的蘿瑾·洛林斯之下屬，《[[美少女的謊言|美少女的謊言]]》[[斯蒂夫·泰利|斯蒂夫·泰利]]將飾演性感陽剛的記者肯德瑞克，而曾與艾希莉·緹絲黛爾在《[[友情理髮店|友情理髮店]]》合作過的[[瑞恩·平克斯頓|瑞恩·平克斯頓]]則將飾演里歐，另外，該劇將會在本劇的第4季第8集做後門試映。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2016/05/young-and-hungry-spinoff-steve-talley-star-male-lead-ryan-pinkston-cast-title-details-freeform-1201761573/|title=‘Young & Hungry’ Spinoff: Steve Talley Set As Male Lead, Ryan Pinkston Cast, Title & Other Details Revealed|author=Nellie Andreeva|work=Deadline|date=2016-05-23|accessdate=2016-06-03}}</ref>2017年1月11日，Freeform正式宣布將不會預訂衍生系列。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2017/01/young-hungry-spinoff-dead-freeform-1201883871/|title=‘Young & Hungry’ Spinoff Not Going Forward At Freeform|author=Nellie Andreeva|work=Deadline|date=2017-01-11|accessdate=2017-01-11}}</ref>

== 評價 ==
《美味新關係》獲得好壞參半的評價。於[[爛番茄|爛番茄]]整合的評論中，第一季獲得43%新鮮度、4.86/10分（7位評論家）。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.rottentomatoes.com/tv/young-and-hungry/s01/|title=Young & Hungry: Season 1|publisher=[[爛番茄|爛番茄]]|accessdate=2015-09-19}}</ref>在[[Metacritic|Metacritic]]整合的評論中，第一季獲得48分（滿分100分；5位評論家），屬好壞參半的評價。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.metacritic.com/tv/young-hungry|title=Young & Hungry – Season 1 Reviews|publisher=[[Metacritic|Metacritic]]|accessdate=2015-09-19}}</ref>
