'''梅根·妮可'''（{{lang-en|'''Megan Nicole'''}}，{{bd|1993年|9月1日}}），美國網絡歌手、[[YouTube|YouTube]]翻唱歌手<ref name=abc13>{{cite web|title=RADIO DISNEY STAR MEGAN NICOLE TO PERFORM AT MAGNIFICENT MILE LIGHTS FESTIVAL|url=http://abc13.com/archive/9337338/|publisher={{link-en|KTRK-TV|KTRK-TV|ABC 13}}|accessdate=2014-05-18}}</ref><ref name="tribune">{{cite web|title=YouTube sensation Megan Nicole to appear at NW Family Expo in Tacoma|url=http://www.thenewstribune.com/2014/04/09/3141065/youtube-sensation-megan-nicole.html|publisher={{link-en|The News Tribune|The News Tribune}}|accessdate=2014-05-18|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6R4ym5o2Z?url=http://www.thenewstribune.com/2014/04/09/3141065/youtube-sensation-megan-nicole.html|archivedate=2014-07-15}}</ref>。出生於德克薩斯州休斯頓市，後移居加利福尼亞州洛杉磯市。她于2009年12月首次在YouTube上传歌曲，曲目为翻唱自[[Kings_of_Leon|Kings of Leon]]的''Use Somebody''。至2017年，其YouTube视频总观看次数已经超过8亿2856万次<ref name="tribune" /><ref name=bio>{{cite web|title=Megan Nicole - Biography|url=http://www.megannicolemusic.com/biography|accessdate=2014-05-18|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140527215620/https://www.megannicolemusic.com/biography|archivedate=2014-05-27}}</ref>。
