'''美洲擬獅'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|''Panthera atrox''}}）又名'''擬獅'''或'''擬獅兽'''，是一種從[[化石|化石]]得知已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的[[貓科|貓科]][[動物|動物]]，生存於[[更新世|更新世]]時期的[[北美洲|北美洲]]（340,000 - 11,000 年前）<ref name=dinosaurs.about>{{cite web | last = Strauss | first = Bob | authorlink = | title = American Lion (''Panthera leo atrox'') | work = About.Com Dinosaurs | publisher = [[The_New_York_Times_Company|The New York Times Company]] | url =http://dinosaurs.about.com/od/mesozoicmammals/p/American-Lion-Panthera-Leo-Atrox.htm | accessdate = 2012-03-10}}</ref>。基因研究顯示牠是[[穴獅|穴獅]]的[[旁系群|旁系群]]<ref name=Barnett>{{Cite journal | last1 = Barnett | first1 = R. | last2 = Shapiro | first2 = B. | authorlink2=Beth Shapiro| last3 = Barnes | first3 = I. A. N. | last4 = Ho | first4 = S. Y. W. | last5 = Burger | first5 = J. | authorlink5=Joachim Burger| last6 = Yamaguchi | first6 = N. | last7 = Higham | first7 = T. F. G. | last8 = Wheeler | first8 = H. T. | last9 = Rosendahl | first9 = W. | last10 = Sher | first10 = A. V. | last11 = Sotnikova | first11 = M. | last12 = Kuznetsova | first12 = T. | last13 = Baryshnikov | first13 = G. F. | last14 = Martin | first14 = L. D. | last15 = Harington | first15 = C. R. | last16 = Burns | first16 = J. A. | last17 = Cooper | first17 = A. | title = Phylogeography of lions (''Panthera leo'' ssp.) reveals three distinct taxa and a late Pleistocene reduction in genetic diversity | doi = 10.1111/j.1365-294X.2009.04134.x | journal = Molecular Ecology | volume = 18 | issue = 8 | pages = 1668–1677 | year = 2009 |url = https://www.researchgate.net/publication/24216045 | pmid =  19302360| pmc = }}</ref>，為[[更新世巨型動物群|更新世巨型動物群]]，化石主要發現於[[美國|美國]][[洛杉磯|洛杉磯]]的[[拉布雷亞瀝青坑|拉布雷亞瀝青坑]]。美洲擬獅體型與[[更新世|更新世]]中期的[[原始獅|原始獅]]大小相近，且比現今[[非洲|非洲]]的獅子大25%，為目前已知體型最大的貓科<ref name=SDNHM>{{Cite web|author=Dr. Tom Deméré|title=SDNHM Fossil Mysteries Field Guide:American Lion|publisher=San Diego Natural History Museum|url=http://www.sdnhm.org/exhibits/mystery/fg_lion.html|accessdate=2007-07-31|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090625205421/http://www.sdnhm.org/exhibits/mystery/fg_lion.html|archivedate=2009-06-25}}</ref>。

==分佈==
美洲擬獅在[[更新世|更新世]]最先出現於[[阿拉斯加|阿拉斯加]]南部（[[桑加蒙間冰期|桑加蒙間冰期]]，為最後一次的[[間冰期|間冰期]]），並散佈至[[亞伯達省|亞伯達省]]與[[馬里蘭州|馬里蘭州]]，最南可至[[墨西哥|墨西哥]][[恰帕斯州|恰帕斯州]]<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Marisol Montellano | first1 = Ballesteros | last2 = Carbot-Chanona | first2 = Gerardo | title = ''Panthera leo atrox'' (Mammalia: Carnivora: Felidae) in Chiapas, Mexico | url = https://chiapas.academia.edu/GerardoCarbotChanona/Papers/1033452/Panthera_leo_atrox_Mammalia_Carnivora_Felidae_in_Chiapas_Mexico | journal = The Southwestern Naturalist | volume = 54 | issue = 2| pages = 217–222 | doi=10.1894/clg-20.1| year = 2009 }}</ref>，北美洲中只有[[加拿大東部|加拿大東部]]及[[美國東北部|美國東北部]]不見其蹤影，可能因為當時當地環境為[[北方針葉林|北方針葉林]]的關係<ref name="Quaternary extinctions" /><ref name="Database">{{cite web|url=http://fossilworks.org/bridge.pl?a=taxonInfo&taxon_no=162281|title=''Panthera leo atrox''|last=|first=|date=|work=[[Paleobiology_Database|Paleobiology Database]]|publisher=|format=|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|accessdate=2012-03-11}}</ref><ref name="Harrington1996">{{Cite web|url=http://www.tc.gov.yk.ca/publications/Lion_1996.pdf|title=American Lion|last=Harrington|first=C. R.|date=March 1996|website=[[Yukon_Beringia_Interpretive_Centre|Yukon Beringia Interpretive Centre]]|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171030054134/http://www.tc.gov.yk.ca/publications/Lion_1996.pdf|archive-date=2017-10-30|dead-url=|access-date=2017-10-30}}</ref>。原先美洲擬獅被認為在[[南北美洲生物大遷徙|南北美洲生物大遷徙]]時擴展至[[南美洲|南美洲]]東北部<ref name="Kurtén1980">{{cite book|last1= Kurtén|first1= B.|last2= Anderson|first2= E.|title= Pleistocene Mammals of North America|url= https://books.google.com/books?id=nBWVngEACAAJ|year= 1980|publisher= Columbia University Press|isbn= 978-0231037334|oclc= 759120597}}</ref>，然而[[祕魯|祕魯]][[塔拉拉|塔拉拉]]瀝青坑發現的化石其實屬於一種大型的[[美洲豹|美洲豹]]<ref name="Seymour1983">{{Cite thesis |last=Seymour |first=Kevin L. |title=The Felinae (Mammalia: Felidae) from the Late Pleistocene tar seeps at Talara, Peru, with a critical examination of the fossil and recent felines of North and South America |date=1983 |degree=MSc |publisher=University of Toronto}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal |last=Seymour |first=Kevin L. |date=2015 |title=Perusing Talara: Overview of the Late Pleistocene fossils from the tar seeps of Peru |url=http://www.tarpits.org/sites/default/files/pdfs/Scott%20et%20al%202015.%20LACM%20SS%2042.pdf#page=107 |journal=Science Series |volume=42 |pages=97–109}}</ref><ref name="Yamaguchi2004">{{Cite journal | last1 = Yamaguchi | first1 = N. | last2 = Cooper | first2 = A. | last3 = Werdelin | first3 = L. | last4 = MacDonald | first4 = D. ==生活環境==
美洲擬獅和現代[[獅子|獅子]]一樣生活在[[疏林草原|疏林草原]]和[[草原|草原]]<ref name="SDNHM" />，一些美洲擬獅是生活在冰冷的[[氣候|氣候]]環境。牠們有可能生活於[[洞穴|洞穴]]中或岩縫間，以躲避冰冷的天氣<ref name="Harrington1996" />。牠們可能以草或樹葉鋪巢，就像[[東北虎|東北虎]]般<ref name="Harrington1996" />。

美洲擬獅在[[拉布雷亞瀝青坑|拉布雷亞瀝青坑]]中的數量較[[斯劍虎|斯劍虎]]或[[恐狼|恐狼]]為少，相信牠們比劍齒虎更有智慧避開陷阱<ref name=SDNHM/>，或不是獵食坑中獵物。美洲擬獅似乎是獵食[[鹿|鹿]]、{{link-en|史氏馬|Equus scotti}}、[[駱駝|駱駝]]、{{link-en|加州貘|California tapirs}}、[[美洲野牛|美洲野牛]]、[[猛獁象|猛獁象]]及其他巨型的[[草食性|草食性]][[有蹄類|有蹄類]]<ref name="Christiansen & Harris 2009" /><ref name="Harrington1996" />。

美洲擬獅的[[滅絕|滅絕]]與[[全新世滅絕事件|全新世滅絕事件]]有關。牠們的骨頭可以在[[舊石器時代|舊石器時代]][[美洲原住民|美洲原住民]]的垃圾中發現，所以被[[人類|人類]]獵殺有可能是成因之一<ref name="Quaternary extinctions"/><ref name="Harrington1996" />。

美洲擬獅的第一個標本是一個[[顎骨|顎骨]]，其複製品現正擺放在{{link-en|費城自然科學學院|Academy of Natural Sciences of Drexel University}}的[[約瑟夫·萊迪|約瑟夫·萊迪]]（Joseph Leidy）雕像之下。

==分類==
美洲擬獅被認為是[[獅|獅]]的[[亞種|亞種]]，但有的認為本身是一個[[種|種]]。在比較[[頭顱骨|頭顱骨]]後，有指[[穴獅|穴獅]]較美洲擬獅更為接近[[老虎|老虎]]<ref name=Groiss1996>{{Cite journal en|author=Groiss, J.Th.|year=1996|title=Der Hohlentiger ''Panthera tigris spelaea'' (Goldfuss)|journal=Neves Jahrb. Geol. Palaont. Abh.|volume=7|pages=399-414}}</ref>，但是[[基因|基因]]研究結果指美洲擬獅與現今[[獅子|獅子]]有更接近的基因關係。<ref>{{Cite journal en|author=Burger, Joachim ''et al.''|year=2004|title=Molecular phylogeny of the extinct cave lion ''Panthera leo spelaea''|journal=Mol. Phylogenet. Evol.|volume=30|pages=841-849|url=http://www.uni-mainz.de/FB/Biologie/Anthropologie/MolA/Download/Burger%202004.pdf|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070925204424/http://www.uni-mainz.de/FB/Biologie/Anthropologie/MolA/Download/Burger%202004.pdf|archivedate=2007-09-25}}</ref>
