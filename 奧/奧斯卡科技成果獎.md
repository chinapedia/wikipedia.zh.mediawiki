'''技術成就獎'''（Technical Achievement Award）是由[[美國電影藝術與科學學會|美國電影藝術與科學學會]]頒發的三項[[科學技術獎|科學技術獎]]其中的一項，獎項是頒發給在技術成就上對[[電影|電影]]產業進步有傑出貢獻的個人或公司。獎項本身係頒發證書，其中描述得獎者的成就，及所有參與人員名單。<ref name=TechAch>{{cite web|author=[[Academy_of_Motion_Picture_Arts_and_Sciences|Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences]] |title=Technical Achievement Award |url=http://www.oscars.org/aboutacademyawards/awards/technical.html |accessdate=2008-08-01 |quote=Technical Achievement Awards may be made for those accomplishments that contribute to the progress of the industry. A certificate is printed describing the achievement and listing the names of all of the individuals who contributed to its development. |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080622044728/http://www.oscars.org/aboutacademyawards/awards/technical.html |archivedate=2008-06-22 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>獎項通常在每年奧斯卡金像獎典禮前數週舉行晚宴頒發，頒獎實況只會在金像獎典禮上簡短插播。
