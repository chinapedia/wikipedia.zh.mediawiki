'''奧利佛·諾伍德'''（'''{{lang-en|Oliver James Norwood}}'''<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.premierleague.com/content/dam/premierleague/site-content/News/publications/squad-lists/squad-lists-february-2012.pdf |title=Premier League clubs submit squad lists |work=PremierLeague.com |publisher=Premier League |page=24 |format=PDF |date=2 February 2012 |accessdate=2 February 2012 }}</ref>，{{bd|1991年|4月12日}}）是[[英國|英國]]的一位足球運動員，在場上司職中場。他出身自曼聯的青訓營，現時效力[[英冠|英冠]][[謝菲爾德聯足球俱樂部|錫菲聯]]。在這之前他曾效力於[[哈德斯菲爾德足球俱樂部|哈德斯菲爾德足球俱樂部]]<ref>{{cite news |first=Neil |last=Goulding |title=BARNSLEY: Waiting game for Reds as deal for Oliver Norwood is agreed |url=http://www.thestar.co.uk/sport/football/barnsley/barnsley-waiting-game-for-reds-as-deal-for-oliver-norwood-is-agreed-1-4686555 |work=Sheffield Star |publisher=Johnston Publishing |date=28 June 2012 |accessdate=28 June 2012 }}</ref>。雖然他出生在英格蘭並且曾代表英格蘭參加青年隊賽事<ref>{{cite news |title=Peacock names squad |url=http://www.thefa.com/England/mens-u17s/News/2009/England17Estoniasquad |work=TheFA.com |publisher=The Football Association |date=24 September 2007 |accessdate=13 August 2010 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Lions hit Malta for six |url=http://www.thefa.com/England/mens-u17s/News/2009/England17Malta |work=TheFA.com |publisher=The Football Association |date=21 October 2007 |accessdate=13 August 2010 }}</ref>，但他選擇代表北愛爾蘭參加國際足球賽事<ref>{{cite news |title=Italy 3–0 Northern Ireland |url=http://www.uefa.com/uefaeuro/season=2012/matches/round=15171/match=2002167/postmatch/lineups/index.html |work=UEFA |publisher=Union of European Football Associations  |date=11 October 2011 |accessdate=12 February 2012 }}</ref>。
