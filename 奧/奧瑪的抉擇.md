《'''奥马尔'''》({{lang-ar|عمر}}) 是一部2013年[[巴勒斯坦|巴勒斯坦]]剧情片，导演为[[Hany_Abu-Assad|Hany Abu-Assad]]。本片入选[[第66屆坎城影展|第66屆坎城影展]][[一種注目|一種注目]]單元<ref name="Cannes2013">{{cite web|url=http://www.festival-cannes.fr/en/article/59652.html |title=2013 Official Selection|date=30 April 2013|accessdate=30 April 2013|work=Cannes}}</ref>，并赢得评审团大奖<ref name="HWR-UCR">{{cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/cannes-missing-picture-wins-regard-558551 |title=Cannes: 'The Missing Picture' Wins Un Certain Regard Prize |date=26 May 2013|accessdate=26 May 2013|work=Hollywood Reporter}}</ref>。并在同年的[[多伦多电影节|多伦多电影节]]上亮相<ref name="TIFF2013">{{cite web|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/film/2013/jul/23/toronto-film-festival-lineup |title=Toronto film festival 2013: the full line-up |accessdate=2013-07-24|work=The Guardian}}</ref>。赢得2013年[[亚太影展|亚太影展]]最佳电影<ref>{{cite web|title=Asia Pacific Screen Awards Announced in Australia|work=Australia Network News|url=http://www.abc.net.au/news/2013-12-12/an-aspa-winners/5153858|date=13 December 2013}}</ref>。影片更获得[[第86届奥斯卡金像奖|第86届奥斯卡金像奖]]最佳外语片提名<ref name="Palestine">{{cite web |url=http://www.deadline.com/2013/09/foreign-language-oscar-israel-submits-bethlehem-palestine-goes-with-omar/ |title= Foreign Language Oscar: Israel Submits ‘Bethlehem’; Palestine Goes With ‘Omar’ |accessdate=2013-09-30 |work=Deadline.com}}</ref><ref name="Oscar Nom">{{cite web |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/entertainment-arts-25757345 |title=Oscars: Main nominations 2014 |accessdate=2014-01-16 |work=BBC News}}</ref>。

== 剧情 ==
在巴勒斯坦被占领期间的动荡时代，三位从小玩到大的朋友没有携手并进，反而逐渐走上互相伤害、互相诋毁之路。

==制作==
导演 Hany Abu-Assad 表示一个晚上他有了拍摄本片的想法，写下故事大纲花费了四个小时，编写剧本耗时仅四天<ref>{{cite web|last=Tartaglione|first=Nancy|url=http://www.deadline.com/2013/12/foreign-language-oscar-preview-a-long-list-of-strong-contenders-for-such-a-shortlist-of-possibles/|title=Foreign Language Oscar Preview A Long List Of Strong Contenders For Such a Shortlist Of Possibles Nominees|publisher=Deadline London|date=15 December 2013}}</ref>。之后用了一年时间筹集资金，并于2012年底开拍，取景地包括巴勒斯坦的[[拿撒勒|拿撒勒]]、[[納布盧斯|納布盧斯]]和遥远的[[难民营|难民营]]<ref>{{cite web|last=Ritman|first=Alex|url=http://www.thenational.ae/arts-culture/film/hany-abu-assad-presents-a-direct-hit-from-the-west-bank-at-diff|title=Hany Abu-Assad presents a direct hit from the West Bank at DIFF|publisher=[[The_National|The National]]|date=3 December 2013|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140210121653/http://www.thenational.ae/arts-culture/film/hany-abu-assad-presents-a-direct-hit-from-the-west-bank-at-diff|archivedate=2014年2月10日}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://vimeo.com/79587305|title=AFI FEST: Hany Abu-Assad talks about making of Omar|date=12 November 2013}}</ref>。
