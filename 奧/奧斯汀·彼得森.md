'''奧斯汀·彼得森'''（{{lang-en|Austin Petersen}}；{{bd|1981年|2月19日|}}），是美國的政治活動家、[[不可知论|不可知论]]者<ref>{{cite web |last1=Petersen |first1=Austin |title=Agnostic ma'am. Are you familiar with the difference? |url=https://twitter.com/AP4LP/status/731645493038727168?lang=en |website=Twitter |accessdate=August 17, 2016}}</ref>和作家，擁有[[密蘇里州立大學|密蘇里州立大學]][[音樂劇|音樂劇]]學位<ref name="kc">{{cite news|last1=Montgomery|first1=Rick|title=Kansas Citian lost Libertarian Party race, but likely ran campaign of the future|url=http://www.kansascity.com/news/politics-government/article83153432.html|accessdate=7 August 2016|work=[[Kansas_City_Star|Kansas City Star]]|date=20 June 2016}}</ref><ref name="official">{{cite web|title=Petersen at a glance:|url=http://austinpetersen2016.com/about/|website=austinpetersen2016.com|publisher=Petersen for President|accessdate=7 August 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160808204923/http://austinpetersen2016.com/about/|archive-date=2016-08-08|dead-url=yes}}</ref>，他因在[[2016年美國總統選舉|2016年美國總統選舉]]{{le|2016年自由意志黨總統提名初選|Libertarian Party presidential primaries, 2016|自由意志黨的總統提名}}位列第三名而知名。

彼得森在2015年宣布參與自由意志黨的總統選舉提名<ref>{{cite news|last1=Zeoli|first1=Rich|title=The Libertarian Candidate For President Wants Voters To Join A ‘Generational Movement’|url=http://philadelphia.cbslocal.com/2016/04/28/the-libertarian-candidate-for-president-wants-voters-to-join-a-generational-movement/|accessdate=7 August 2016|work=[[KYW-TV|KYW-TV]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Rogers|first1=Brooke|title=Can Austin Petersen Unite Disenfranchsied Republicans?|url=http://www.nationalreview.com/article/435610/austin-petersen-pro-life-libertarian|accessdate=7 August 2016|work=[[National_Review|National Review]]|date=19 May 2016}}</ref>，《[[洛杉磯時報|洛杉磯時報]]》報導{{le|馬特·韋爾奇|Matt Welch}}形容彼得森是「渴望自由意志主義的傢伙」（"an eager libertarian dudebro on the make"）<ref>{{cite news|last1=Welch|first1=Matt|title=Op-Ed Meet the libertarians — the #NeverTrump movement's last hope|url=http://www.latimes.com/opinion/op-ed/la-oe-welch-libertarians-never-trump-20160519-snap-story.html|work=[[Los_Angeles_Times|Los Angeles Times]]}}</ref>。

2016年5月29日，他在{{le|2016年自由意志黨全國代表大會|2016 Libertarian National Convention}}第二輪投票輸給[[加里·約翰遜|加里·約翰遜]]，失去提名資格<ref name="C-SPAN Live">{{cite AV media |date=May 29, 2016 |title=Libertarian Party National Convention |language=en |url=http://www.c-span.org/video/?409917-1/libertarian-party-national-convention-selects-nominee&live |access-date=May 29, 2016 |format=Live Video |location=[[Orlando,_Florida|Orlando, Florida]] |publisher=[[C-SPAN|C-SPAN]]}}</ref>，然後彼得森恭賀約翰遜獲得提名，送給他一支喬治·華盛頓手槍的複製品；其後彼得森因為約翰遜接過手槍後「毫不客氣地打發它在垃圾桶」而拒絕承認約翰遜挑選的副總統人選[[威廉·韋爾德|威廉·韋爾德]]；不過到最後還是支持二人出選<ref>{{cite web |last=Chakraborty |first=Barnini |url=http://www.foxnews.com/politics/2016/06/01/libertarian-nominee-accepts-rival-s-gift-washington-replica-pistol-then-trashes-it.html |title=Libertarian nominee accepts rival's gift of Washington replica pistol – then trashes it |work=Fox News |accessdate=June 3, 2016}}</ref><ref name="wt">{{cite news|last1=Chasmar|first1=Jessica|title=Gary Johnson tossed rival Austin Petersen’s gift of George Washington’s replica pistol in trash|url=http://www.washingtontimes.com/news/2016/jun/2/gary-johnson-tossed-rival-austin-petersons-gift-of/|accessdate=7 August 2016|work=[[Washington_Times|Washington Times]]|date=2 June 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Weigel|first1=David|title=Is Libertarian Gary Johnson a factor in Clinton-Trump matchup?|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/politics/is-libertarian-gary-johnson-a-factor-in-clinton-trump-matchup/2016/06/10/05025d84-2dc5-11e6-9b37-42985f6a265c_story.html|accessdate=10 June 2016|work=[[Washington_Post|Washington Post]]|date=7 August 2016}}</ref>。
