[[file:The_sketch_(drawing)_of_roller_chain,_Leonardo_da_Vinci.jpg|thumb]]
'''滚子链'''是一种用于传送机械动力链条，广泛应用於家庭、工业和农业机械，其中包括[[输送机|输送机]]、绘图机、印刷机、汽车、摩托车、以及[[自行车|自行车]]。它由一系列短圆柱滚子链接在一起，由一个称为[[链轮|链轮]]的齿轮驱动。这是一个简单，可靠，高效的动力传递装置。 <ref>As much as 98% efficient under ideal conditions, according to {{cite web|last=Kidd |first=Matt D. |coauthors=N. E. Loch, R. L. Reuben |year=1998 |url=http://www.hw.ac.uk/mecWWW/research/mdk/res.htm |title=Bicycle Chain Efficiency |work=The Engineering of Sport conference |publisher=Heriot Watt University |accessdate=2006-05-16 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20060206210227/http://www.hw.ac.uk/mecWWW/research/mdk/res.htm |archivedate=2006-02-06 |deadurl=yes }}</ref> 

虽然[[汉斯·瑞诺德|汉斯·瑞诺德]]被认为于1880年发明了滚子链，16世纪[[列奥纳多·达·芬奇|达芬奇]]的草图显示一个链条以及滚子轴承。<ref>In the 16th century, Leonardo da Vinci made sketches of what appears to be the first steel chain. These chains were probably designed to transmit pulling, not wrapping, power because they consist only of plates and pins and have metal fittings. However, da Vinci's sketch does show a roller bearing.{{cite book | title=The Complete Guide to Chain | editor=Tsubakimoto Chain Co. | publisher=Kogyo Chosaki Publishing Co., Ltd. | isbn=0-9658932-0-0 | year=1997 | pages=240 | id=p. 211 | url=http://chain-guide.com/breaks/brief-history-of-chain.html | accessdate = 2006-05-17}}</ref>
