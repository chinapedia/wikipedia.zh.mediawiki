'''争取社会公正运动'''（{{lang-en|Movement for Social Justice}}，缩写为MSJ）是[[特立尼达和多巴哥|特立尼达和多巴哥]]的一个政党。该党的领袖是David Abdulah。该党的智库是[[争取社会公正基金会|争取社会公正基金会]]。该党是[[圣保罗论坛|圣保罗论坛]]的成员。

该党成员曾作为以[[联合民族大会|联合民族大会]]为首的“[[人民伙伴|人民伙伴]]”联盟的候选人参加[[2010年特立尼达和多巴哥大选|2010年特立尼达和多巴哥大选]]。<ref>[http://news.co.tt/public_html/article.php?story=2012052106000734 "Then there were four"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304041526/http://news.co.tt/public_html/article.php?story=2012052106000734 |date=2016-03-04 }}, Breaking News Trinidad and Tobago, 21 May 2012.</ref><ref>[http://www.newsday.co.tt/news/0,161928.html Cecily Asson, "MSJ Out Partnership",] ''Newsday'', 18 June 2012.</ref><ref>[http://www.caribjournal.com/2012/06/18/trinidads-movement-for-social-justice-leaves-coalition-government/ "Trinidad’s Movement for Social Justice Leaves Coalition Government",] ''Caribbean Journal'', 18 June 2012.</ref><ref>[http://www.trinidadexpress.com/commentaries/A_movement_for_social_justice-163807326.html John Spence, "A movement for social justice",] {{Wayback|url=http://www.trinidadexpress.com/commentaries/A_movement_for_social_justice-163807326.html |date=20170202010032 }} ''Trinidad Express newspapers'', 26 July 2012.</ref>
