{{Vgname|争分夺秒|na=Split/Second|eu=Split/Second: Velocity|cn=争分夺秒|tw=瞬间爆发}}是一款由{{link-en|黑岩工作室|Black Rock Studio}}开发，{{link-en|迪斯尼互动工作室|Disney Interactive Studios}}发行的[[竞速游戏|竞速游戏]]。该游戏于2010年5月登陆[[Microsoft_Windows|Microsoft Windows]]、[[PlayStation_3|PlayStation 3]]和[[Xbox_360|Xbox 360]]平台之上<ref name=Release>{{cite web|url=http://www.joystiq.com/2010/02/24/split-second-may-18-release-date|title=Split/Second: Velocity Release Date Announced|publisher=Joystiq}}</ref>。2011年6月，游戏登陆{{link-en|OnLive|OnLive}}平台<ref name="Release for OnLive">{{cite web|url=http://onlivefans.com/news/onlive-adds-5-new-games-to-their-service-today/981/|title=Split/Second Released on OnLive|publisher=OnLiveFans}}</ref>。游戏中，玩家参加了一个虚构的[[真人實境秀|电视真人秀]]节目，故事情节随之展开<ref>{{cite web|url=http://uk.ps3.ign.com/articles/961/961359p1.html|title=Disney Announces Split/Second|publisher=[[IGN|IGN]]|author=Ryan Geddes|date=2009-03-11}}</ref>。
