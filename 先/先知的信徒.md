'''先知的信徒'''（{{lang-so|Ahlu Suna Waljamaaca}}）是[[索马里|索马里]]的一个[[准军事团体|准军事团体]]，它由温和的[[苏菲派|苏菲派]]组成，反对索马里的极端[[伊斯兰主义|伊斯兰主义]]团体（例如[[青年党_(索马里)|青年党]]）。他们声称为阻止严厉的[[伊斯兰教法|伊斯兰教法]]和[[瓦哈比主义|瓦哈比主义]]在索马里被强制推行，保护自身的[[逊尼|逊尼]]-[[苏菲主义|苏菲]]传统和普遍温和的宗教观点而战。<ref name="bbc2009-06-08">{{cite news |title=Somali rage at grave desecration |author=Mohamed Mohamed |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/africa/8077725.stm |agency=BBC Somali Service |publisher=[[BBC_News|BBC News]] |date=2009-06-08 |accessdate=2010-04-01 |quote=Most Somalis are Sufi Muslims, who do not share the strict Saudi Arabian-inspired Wahhabi interpretation of Islam with the hardline al-Shabab group. They embrace music, dancing and meditation and are appalled at the desecration of the graves.... The umbrella group Ahlu Sunnah Wal Jama (Sufi Sects in Somalia) has condemned the actions of what they call the ideology of modern Wahhabism and the desecrations of graves. They see Wahhabism as foreign and ultimately un-Islamic. }}</ref> 

[[索马里内战|索马里内战]]期间，“先知的信徒”组织曾和[[军阀|军阀]][[穆罕默德·法拉赫·艾迪德|穆罕默德·法拉赫·艾迪德]]结盟。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://webarchive.ssrc.org/Somalia_Hoehne_v10.pdf|title=Counter-terrorism in Somalia: How external interference helped to produce militant Islamism|publisher=Webarchive.ssrc.org|format=PDF|accessdate=15 October 2014}}</ref>
