'''先锋日'''（{{lang-en|Pioneer Day}}）是一个犹它州官方节日，日期是每年的7月24日,<ref name="Legal holidays">{{citation |title= Utah Code 63G-1-301 (Legal holidays) |url= http://www.le.utah.gov/code/TITLE63G/htm/63G01_030100.htm |publisher= [[Utah_State_Legislature|Utah State Legislature]] |accessdate= 2013-08-05 |deadurl= yes |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131021175137/http://www.le.utah.gov/code/TITLE63G/htm/63G01_030100.htm |archivedate= 2013-10-21 }}</ref>它的意义是纪念[[杨百翰|杨百翰]]及摩门教先行者于1847年7月24日进入[[盐湖城|盐湖城]]<ref>{{citation |last= Schindler |first= Harold |title= Ailing Young Rolls Out of Canyon, Likes Look of the Salt Lake Valley |newspaper= [[Salt_Lake_Tribune|Salt Lake Tribune]] |series= Mormon Trail Series |date= 24 July 1997 |id= Article ID: 100F84BB82E32C16 }}</ref>.先锋日除了是[[犹他州|犹他州]]的法定节日，而且还是被耶稣基督后期圣徒教会的摩门教徒认为的特殊日子，<ref>{{citation |last= Hinckley |first= Gordon B. |authorlink= Gordon B. Hinckley|title= Remarks at Pioneer Day Commemoration Concert |url= http://www.lds.org/ensign/2001/10/remarks-at-pioneer-day-commemoration-concert?lang=eng |date=October 2001 |journal= Ensign |publisher= LDS Church }}</ref><ref>{{citation |last= Reynolds |first= Sydney Smith |title= They Belong to Us All |url= http://www.lds.org/liahona/1989/06/they-belong-to-us-all?lang=eng |date=June 1989 |journal= [[Liahona_(magazine)|Liahona]] |publisher= LDS Church }}</ref>,一些[[耶稣基督后期圣徒教会|耶稣基督后期圣徒教会]]教徒会加入[[摩门教|摩门教]]徒的游行<ref>{{citation |title= Mormons Celebrate Their Pioneer Heritage |url= http://www.mormonnewsroom.org/ldsnewsroom/eng/news-releases-stories/mormons-celebrate-their-pioneer-heritage |work= Newsroom |publisher= LDS Church |accessdate= 2013-08-05 }}</ref>，全美国乃至全世界的耶稣基督末世圣徒教会都会用聚餐，唱歌，跳舞等形式庆祝先锋日.<ref>{{citation |last= Gaunt |first= LaRene Porter |title= Celebrate! |url= http://www.lds.org/ensign/2005/04/celebrate |date=April 2005 |journal= Ensign |publisher= LDS Church }}</ref><ref>{{Citation |last= Bailey |first= Tamara Leatham |contribution= Pioneer Parties |contribution-url= http://www.lds.org/new-era/1997/07/fyi-for-your-info?lang=eng |title= FYI: For Your Info |date=July 1997 |journal= [[New_Era_(magazine)|New Era]] |publisher= LDS Church }}</ref>
