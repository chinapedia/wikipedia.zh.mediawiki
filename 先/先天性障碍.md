'''先天性障礙'''，又稱'''先天性疾病'''、'''先天畸形'''、'''先天缺陷'''<ref>{{cite book|author1=Ruth A. Hannon|title=Porth pathophysiology : concepts of altered health states|date=2010|publisher=Wolters Kluwer Health/Lippincott Williams & Wilkins|location=Philadelphia, PA|isbn=9781605477817|page=128|edition=1st Canadian ed.|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=2-MFXOEG0lcC&pg=PA128}}</ref>，是指[[發育|發育]]中的[[胎兒|胎兒]]因為[[遺傳性疾病|遺傳性疾病]]或發育環境等因素導致某個部位特徵[[细胞结构|結構]]畸形，導致在嬰兒[[出生|出生]]時即有的病症<ref name=NIH2017Def/>，包括了{{le|身體障礙|Physical disability|身體}}、[[智能障礙|智能]]以及{{le|發展遲緩|Developmental disability|發展}}上的[[身心障礙|障礙]]<ref name=NIH2017Def>{{cite web|title=Birth Defects: Condition Information|url=https://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/birthdefects/conditioninfo/Pages/default.aspx|website=www.nichd.nih.gov|accessdate=8 December 2017}}</ref>。障礙程度可能輕微，也可能嚴重<ref name=CDC2017Fact>{{cite web|title=Facts Birth Defects|url=https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/birthdefects/facts.html|website=Centers for Disease Control and Prevention|accessdate=8 December 2017|language=en-us|date=7 September 2017}}</ref>。先天性障碍可以分為二類：結構性缺陷是指身體器官或部位在外形或是結構上的缺陷，而{{le|功能性缺陷|Functional disorder}}是指身體部位在功能上的缺陷.<ref name=NIH2017What/>。功能性缺陷包括[[代謝疾病|代謝疾病]]及[[退行性疾病|退行性疾病]]<ref name=NIH2017What/>。有些先天性障碍則同時包括了結構性缺陷以及功能性缺陷二者<ref name=NIH2017What>{{cite web|title=What are the types of birth defects?|url=https://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/birthdefects/conditioninfo/pages/types.aspx|website=www.nichd.nih.gov|accessdate=8 December 2017}}</ref>。

先天性障碍可能是因為{{le|遺傳疾病|Genetic disorder}}、[[染色体畸变|染色体畸变]]，或是[[懷孕|懷孕]]時暴露在特定化學物質、服用特定藥物或是{{le|母嬰垂直傳染|Vertically transmitted infection|懷孕時感染}}<ref name=NIH2017Ca>{{cite web|title=What causes birth defects?|url=https://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/birthdefects/conditioninfo/pages/causes.aspx|website=www.nichd.nih.gov|accessdate=8 December 2017}}</ref>。風險因子包括[[葉酸缺乏症|葉酸缺乏症]]、懷孕時飲酒或是吸煙、[[妊娠糖尿病|妊娠糖尿病]]控制不當、或是[[高齡產婦|孕婦年齡超過35歲]]<ref name=NIH2017Risk>{{cite web|title=How many people are affected by/at risk for birth defects?|url=https://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/birthdefects/conditioninfo/pages/risk.aspx|website=www.nichd.nih.gov|accessdate=8 December 2017}}</ref><ref name=CDC2017Fact/>。許多先天性障碍可能是因為多種原因造成<ref name=CDC2017Fact/>。先天性障碍可能是嬰兒出生時即可看出的，也有些是透過{{le|新生兒篩檢|Newborn screening}}得知.<ref name=NIH2017Diag/>。許多的先天性障碍可以在懷孕時在[[產前診斷|產前診斷]]檢查出來
<ref name=NIH2017Diag>{{cite web|title=How do health care providers diagnose birth defects?|url=https://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/birthdefects/conditioninfo/pages/diagnosed.aspx|website=www.nichd.nih.gov|accessdate=8 December 2017}}</ref>。

根據動物實驗，發育中的胎兒所得到的[[營養|營養]]取決於父母的進食，也有可能對胎兒出生以後長期影響到他的生活型態<ref name="ConsultantLive.com">{{cite web |url=http://www.consultantlive.com/display/article/10162/1532211 |title=Pre-Prenatal Care: A Primary Care Primer on the Future |author=Gregory W. Rutecki |year=2010 |publisher=ConsultantLive.com |accessdate=25 September 2010 }}</ref>。也動物實驗也指出，父母的[[思維|思維]]、[[生活|生活]]作息也會影響到胎兒的發育過程，由實驗可知，父親如果整天酗[[酒|酒]]、少吃蔬果類的[[食物|食物]]、[[吸菸|吸菸]]、吸[[毒品|毒品]]等不良行為，更容易讓胎兒造成基因突變而導致先天性障礙<ref name= "Abel">{{cite journal|last1=Abel|first1=E. L.|title=Paternal contribution to fetal alcohol syndrome|journal=Addiction Biology|date=2004|volume=9|issue=2|pages=127–133|doi=10.1080/13556210410001716980|pmid=15223537}}</ref><ref name= "DeSantis">{{cite journal|last1=De Santis|first1=Marco|last2=Cesari|first2=Elena|last3=Cavaliere|first3=Annafranca|last4=Ligato|first4=Maria Serena|last5=Nobili|first5=Elena|last6=Visconti|first6=Daniela|last7=Caruso|first7=Alessandro|title=Paternal exposure and counselling: Experience of a Teratology Information Service|journal=Reproductive Toxicology|date=September 2008|volume=26|issue=1|pages=42–46|doi=10.1016/j.reprotox.2008.06.003|pmid=18598753}}</ref><ref name= "Sartorius">{{cite journal|last1=Sartorius|first1=G. A.|last2=Nieschlag|first2=E.|title=Paternal age and reproduction|journal=Human Reproduction Update|date=20 August 2009|volume=16|issue=1|pages=65–79|doi=10.1093/humupd/dmp027|pmid=19696093}}</ref><ref name= "anderson">{{cite journal|last1=Anderson|first1=Diana|last2=Schmid|first2=ThomasE|last3=Baumgartner|first3=Adolf|title=Male-mediated developmental toxicity|journal=Asian Journal of Andrology|date=2014|volume=16|issue=1|pages=81|doi=10.4103/1008-682X.122342|pmid=24369136}}</ref>。

先天性障碍的處理方式因種類而有所不同<ref name=NIH2017Tx/>，包括有治療、藥物、手術以及[[輔助技術|輔助技術]]<ref name=NIH2017Tx>{{cite web|title=What are the treatments for birth defects?|url=https://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/birthdefects/conditioninfo/pages/treatment.aspx|website=www.nichd.nih.gov|accessdate=8 December 2017}}</ref>。在2015年有9600萬人有先天性障碍<ref name=GBD2015Pre>{{cite journal|last1=GBD 2015 Disease and Injury Incidence and Prevalence|first1=Collaborators.|title=Global, regional, and national incidence, prevalence, and years lived with disability for 310 diseases and injuries, 1990-2015: a systematic analysis for the Global Burden of Disease Study 2015.|journal=Lancet|date=8 October 2016|volume=388|issue=10053|pages=1545–1602|pmid=27733282|doi=10.1016/S0140-6736(16)31678-6|pmc=5055577}}</ref> 。在美國有3%的新生兒有先天性障碍<ref name=CDC2015Epi>{{cite web|title=Birth Defects|url=https://www.cdc.gov/ncbddd/birthdefects/data.html|accessdate=17 Jan 2016|date=Dec 15, 2015}}</ref> ，在1990年時全世界因先天性障碍死亡的人數有75.1萬人，到2015年降到62.8萬人<ref name=GBD204>{{cite journal|last1=GBD 2013 Mortality and Causes of Death|first1=Collaborators|title=Global, regional, and national age-sex specific all-cause and cause-specific mortality for 240 causes of death, 1990–2013: a systematic analysis for the Global Burden of Disease Study 2013.|journal=Lancet|date=17 December 2014|pmid=25530442|doi=10.1016/S0140-6736(14)61682-2|volume=385|issue=9963|pages=117–71|pmc=4340604}}</ref><ref name=GBD2015De/>。主要的死因有[[先天性心臟病|先天性心臟病]]（30.3萬人）及{{le|神经管缺陷|Neural tube defect}}（6.5萬人）<ref name=GBD2015De>{{cite journal|last1=GBD 2015 Mortality and Causes of Death|first1=Collaborators.|title=Global, regional, and national life expectancy, all-cause mortality, and cause-specific mortality for 249 causes of death, 1980-2015: a systematic analysis for the Global Burden of Disease Study 2015.|journal=Lancet|date=8 October 2016|volume=388|issue=10053|pages=1459–1544|pmid=27733281|doi=10.1016/s0140-6736(16)31012-1|pmc=5388903}}</ref>。

==部分先天性障碍的舉例==
*[[先天性水痘症候群|先天性水痘症候群]]：患者的[[皮膚|皮膚]]常因色素沈著而黝黑。
*[[先天性遗传多毛症|先天性遗传多毛症]]：出生不久之後全身上下長滿了烏黑而堅硬的毛。
*[[先天性橫膈疝氣|先天性橫膈疝氣]]：限制[[肺|肺]]部生長。
*[[地中海貧血|地中海貧血]]：不能有效地製造[[紅血球|紅血球]]，其帶氧能力亦不足。
*[[根達綜合症|根達綜合症]]：[[酵素|酵素]]或尿卟啉原Ⅲ共合成酶不足。
*[[腦水腫|先天性腦積水]]：包圍著[[腦部|腦部]]與[[脊髓|脊髓]]的[[腦脊液|腦脊液]]異常增加或累積，導致顱內壓異常增加的情形。
*[[先天性碘缺乏症候群|先天性碘缺乏症候群]]：嚴重阻礙身心發展。
*[[胎兒酒精綜合症|胎兒酒精綜合症]]：母親在[[懷孕|懷孕]]期間的[[酒精|酒精]]消耗量可能會引起各種先天性障礙的長期連續性：[[頭顱|顱面]]畸形<ref>Jones K, Smith D (1975). "The fetal alcohol syndrome". Teratology 12 (1): 1–10. doi:10.1002/tera.1420120102. PMID 1162620.</ref>、[[腦|腦]]的損傷<ref>Clarren S, Alvord E, Sumi S, Streissguth A, Smith D (1978). "Brain malformations related to prenatal exposure to ethanol". J Pediatr 92 (1): 64–7. doi:10.1016/S0022-3476(78)80072-9. PMID 619080.</ref>、[[智能障礙|智能障礙]]<ref>Lancet. 1986 Nov 22;2(8517):1222. PMID 2877359</ref>、[[心臟病|心臟病]]、[[腎|腎]]功能異常、[[骨骼|骨骼]]異常、[[雙眼|雙眼]]異常<ref>Strömland K, Pinazo-Durán M (2002). "Ophthalmic involvement in the fetal alcohol syndrome: clinical and animal model studies". Alcohol Alcohol 37 (1): 2–8. doi:10.1093/alcalc/37.1.2. PMID 11825849.</ref>等。在[[美國|美國]]，受影響兒童的患病率估計至少有1%<ref>May, PA.; Gossage, JP. (2001). "Estimating the prevalence of fetal alcohol syndrome. A summary.". Alcohol Res Health 25 (3): 159–67. PMID 11810953.</ref>，在[[加拿大|加拿大]]也是如此。
