'''保羅·斯庫利'''（Paul Scully，{{bd|1968年|4月29日}}）是一位[[英格蘭|英格蘭]]政治人物，他的黨籍是[[保守黨_(英國)|保守黨]]。自2015年開始，他擔任[[薩頓和奇姆_(英國國會選區)|薩頓和奇姆]]選區選出的英國下議院議員<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.bbc.com/news/politics/constituencies/E14000984 | title=Sutton & Cheam Parliamentary constituency | publisher=BBC | accessdate=8 May 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.suttonguardian.co.uk/news/12939030.Conservative_Paul_Scully_elected_to_Sutton_and_Cheam_after_defeating_Lib_Dem_Paul_Burstow/ | title=Conservative Paul Scully elected to Sutton and Cheam after defeating Lib Dem Paul Burstow | publisher=Sutton Guardian | date=7 May 2015 | accessdate=11 May 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.theguardian.com/politics/2015/may/08/election-results-in-london-rolling-coverage | title=London results: rolling coverage | publisher=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]] | date=8 May 2015 | accessdate=11 May 2015}}</ref>。他畢業於[[雷丁大學|雷丁大學]]<ref>"SCULLY, Paul Stuart", ''[[Who's_Who_(UK)|Who's Who 2016]]'', [[A_&_C_Black|A & C Black]].</ref>。他是一位緬甸人後裔<ref>[http://www.irrawaddy.org/burma/british-mp-elect-proud-of-burma-heritage.html "-{British MP-Elect ‘Proud’ of Burma Heritage}-"] (''[[The_Irrawaddy|The Irrawaddy]]'', 13 May 2015). [https://web.archive.org/web/20151209232903/http://www.irrawaddy.com/burma/british-mp-elect-proud-of-burma-heritage.html Archived copy].</ref>。
