'''國家警察紀念碑'''（{{lang-en|National Police Memorial}}）是一座位於[[英國|英國]][[倫敦|倫敦]]鬧區的紀念碑，用以紀念英國境內值勤時身亡的[[警員|警員]]，由英國建築師[[諾曼·福斯特|諾曼·福斯特勳爵]]和[[丹麥|丹麥]]建築師{{Link-en|佩爾·阿諾迪|Per Arnoldi}}設計、於2005年揭幕，而福斯特事務所的專案建築師為彼得·里德利（{{lang|en|Peter Ridley}}）。

== 歷史背景 ==
1984年時，英國電影導演{{Link-en|米高·溫納|Michael Winner}}在{{Link-en|伊馮妮·弗萊徹命案|Murder of Yvonne Fletcher|伊馮妮·弗萊徹警員命案}}後組建了{{Link-en|警察紀念信託|Police Memorial Trust}}组织，其創會之初主要專注於警察殉職地點的小型紀念物樹立工作<ref>{{cite web|title=Police Memorial Trust |publisher=Police Memorial Trust |date=2006-08-21 |url=http://www.policememorial.org.uk/Police_Memorial_Trust/Police_Memorial_Trust.htm |archivedate=2007-09-28 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070928031004/http://www.policememorial.org.uk/Police_Memorial_Trust/Police_Memorial_Trust.htm |accessdate=2008-01-01 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>，1990年代中期之後亦著手遊說、籌資興建更大的單座紀念設施，以茲紀念所有在勤務過程中喪生的警員<ref>{{Cite news  | last =Cavendish  | first = Lucy  | title = Calm Down Dear, It's Michael Winner  | newspaper = Evening Standard |date =2005-04-24 |url = http://www.standard.co.uk/showbiz/calm-down-dear-its-michael-winner-7262056.html |archivedate=2015-10-03 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151003171348/http://www.standard.co.uk/showbiz/calm-down-dear-its-michael-winner-7262056.html }}</ref>。溫納表示「陸、海、空三軍士兵的紀念物已是司空見慣，但警察打的卻是一場無始無終的戰爭」<ref name="NPMhomepage">{{cite web |title =The National Police Memorial  | publisher =Police Roll of Honour Trust |date = 2005-04-28 |url = http://www.policememorial.org.uk/Police_Memorial_Trust/NPM.htm |archivedate=2013-03-29 |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20130329050915/http://www.policememorial.org.uk/index.php?page=police-memorial-trust |accessdate =2008-01-01  }}</ref>。

溫納自費捐獻了50萬英鎊投入立碑活動<ref name="recognising">{{cite news |title = Recognising the ultimate sacrifice |publisher = BBC News |date = 2005-04-26 |url = http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/uk/4482619.stm  |accessdate = 2008-01-01 |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20081002020122/http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/uk/4482619.stm |archivedate = 2008-10-02}}</ref>，而經費當中剩餘的230萬則透過公眾募款籌得<ref name="recognising" />。歷經10年活動後，{{Link-en|西敏市議會|Westminster City Council}}在2002年10月批准了規劃<ref>{{cite web |title = National Police Memorial granted planning permission after 10-year campaign  | publisher = Foster & Partners |date = 2002-10-04  | url = http://www.fosterandpartners.com/News/085/Default.aspx  | accessdate =2008-01-01  |archivedate=2012-02-08 |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120208014426/http://www.fosterandpartners.com/News/085/Default.aspx}}</ref>。

== 設計及興建 ==
[[File:Maintenance_work_on_Police_Memorial.jpg|left]]望向警察紀念碑]]

國家警察紀念碑依據[[諾曼·福斯特|諾曼·福斯特勳爵]]和{{Link-en|佩爾·阿諾迪|Per Arnoldi}}的設計而建，坐落於[[林蔭路_(倫敦)|林蔭路]]和{{Link-en|騎兵衛隊路|Horse Guards Road}}交會口、直接位在[[海軍部_(英國)|舊海軍部大樓]]外。當時該址由[[倫敦地鐵|倫敦地鐵]][[貝克盧線|貝克盧線]]的一座通風井佔據<ref name="recognising"/>。

2004年7月22日，本址進行了象徵性的動工儀式，與會者包括[[英國首相|英國首相]][[東尼·布萊爾|東尼·布萊爾]]、溫納導演。[[倫敦警察廳|倫敦警察廳]]和{{Link-en|大曼徹斯特警察|Greater Manchester Police}}也派員出席，代表英國殉職人數最高的兩支警隊<ref name="NPMhomepage" />。

2005年4月26日，[[英國女王|英國女王]][[伊莉莎白二世|伊莉莎白二世]]和布萊爾首相替警察紀念碑揭幕<ref name="NPMhomepage"/>，當時英國另外兩大黨黨魁[[迈克尔·霍华德|迈克尔·霍华德]]和[[查爾斯·甘迺迪|查爾斯·甘迺迪]]也到場觀禮<ref>{{Cite news |title = Inspector Named in Police Memorial |newspaper = Oxford Times |date = 2005-04-28 |url = http://www.oxfordtimes.co.uk/news/6557421.Inspector_named_in_police_memorial/ |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160213062840/http://www.oxfordtimes.co.uk/news/6557421.Inspector_named_in_police_memorial/ |archivedate = 2016-02-13}}</ref>，並由56名身穿英國各地警隊制服的警察擔當[[儀仗隊|儀仗]]勤務<ref name="NPMhomepage" />。女王致詞時表示「（紀念碑）理當坐落在[[林蔭路_(倫敦)|林蔭路]]，這顯然相當合適，倫敦此區與我國的生活方式息息相關。當人們途經本碑時，我希望他們能佇足深思其所呈現的榮耀傳統。我確信這裡所記載的英勇和個人犧牲，將會繼續鼓舞我們」<ref name="Unveiled">{{cite news  |title = National Police Memorial Unveiled |publisher = BBC News |date = 2005-04-26 |url = http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/uk/4484269.stm |accessdate = 2008-01-01  |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20071230103937/http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/uk/4484269.stm |archivedate = 2007-12-30}}</ref>。

[[File:National_Police_Mem_column.jpg|right]]
2006年，國家警察紀念碑獲頒[[英國皇家建築師協會|英國皇家建築師協會獎]]<ref>{{Cite journal |title = Winner, but no prizes |journal = Building |year = 2006 |issue = 26  |url = http://www.building.co.uk/plaque-removal/3069739.article |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160213052232/http://www.building.co.uk/plaque-removal/3069739.article |archivedate = 2016-02-13}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title = RIBA Awards 2006  publisher = Royal Institute of British Architects |url = http://www.e-architect.co.uk/awards/riba_awards_2006.htm |accessdate =2008-01-01  |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131113124655/http://www.e-architect.co.uk/awards/riba_awards_2006.htm |archivedate = 2013-11-13}}</ref>，並歸類為「藝術休閒類獎」。溫納對此事表達了驚訝之意，認為這種分類相當奇怪，表示自己無法想像「1,600名殉職警員怎麼能算成『藝術與休閒』」<ref name="FatPig">{{Cite news |author = {{Link-en|米高·溫納|Michael Winner|Winner, Michael}} |title=The Fat Pig |newspaper = Daily Mail |date = 2007-10-16 |url = http://www.dailymail.co.uk/pages/live/articles/health/thehealthnews.html?in_article_id=487816&in_page_id=1797  |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20081021012939/http://www.dailymail.co.uk/health/article-487816/The-fat-pig-How-I-turned-thinner-Michael-Winner.html |archivedate = 2008-10-21}}</ref>。

==建築構成==
[[File:National_Police_Memorial.JPG|left]]

國家警察紀念碑包含兩座分離的建築體，由{{Link-en|珀貝克石|Purbeck stone}}砌成的平台相連<ref name="NPMhomepage" />。其中一座建築是具有長方形黑色外殼、表面攀附著[[地錦屬|爬藤]]的通風井，北面的外牆沒有藤蔓覆蓋，上部雕刻著警察職務章和「國家警察紀念碑：表彰服勤者」（{{lang|en|The National Police Memorial: Honouring Those who Serve}}）的字樣。下方的牆上還內嵌著一面{{Link-en|展示橱窗|Display case}}，裡面放著一本載錄殉職員警名冊的榮譽榜<ref name="Foster">{{cite web |title =National Police Memorial |publisher =Foster & Partners |url = http://www.fosterandpartners.com/Projects/0890/Default.aspx |accessdate =2008-01-01  |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120902031603/http://www.fosterandpartners.com/Projects/0890/Default.aspx |archivedate=2012-09-02}}</ref>。

黑色方體以北不遠處，為一座矗立在{{Link-ko|倒影池|reflecting pool}}中的玻璃柱，內部由[[光導纖維|纖維光纜]]照明出藍色光暈<ref name="Glazine">{{cite web|title=Haran Glass Builds Tribute to Fallen Heroes |publisher=The Glazine |url=http://www.theglazine.com/ProjectOfWeek/HaranGlass175.html |accessdate=2008-01-01 |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20081010063813/http://www.theglazine.com/ProjectOfWeek/HaranGlass175.html |archivedate=2008-10-10 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>，象徵英國各警察局外部依慣懸掛的藍燈<ref name="Foster"/>。玻璃柱的設置目的，旨在替展示櫥窗將林蔭路上的人車遮蔽開來，柱體高度約有7.4-{zh-cn:米; zh-tw:公尺; zh-hk:米;}-，由622片玻璃堆疊起來（其表面積總計954平方-{zh-cn:米; zh-tw:公尺; zh-hk:米;}-），總重量則達28.6[[公噸|公噸]]<ref name="Glazine" />。

== 榮譽榜 ==
[[File:National_Pol_Memorial_book.jpg|right]]

國家警察紀念碑包括一本放在玻璃展示窗後面的榮譽榜，記載了大約1,600名在從事逮捕或危險勤務時喪命的警員<ref name="Unveiled" />，第一位入冊者則是一位在1680年殉職、姓名未知的[[捕快|捕役]]。本榜的編冊依據是警察榮譽榜信託（{{lang|en|Police Roll of Honour Trust}}）记录的4,000人名单<ref>{{cite news |last = Allen |first = Liam |title = Remembering the Ultimate Sacrifice |publisher = BBC News |date = 2004-10-03 |url = http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/uk/3711476.stm |accessdate = 2008-01-01 |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20061020181403/http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/uk/3711476.stm |archivedate = 2006-10-20 }}</ref>，其中列出全體殉職警務人員<ref name="NPMhomepage" />。

== 批評 ==
國家警察紀念碑落成後，曾遭致一些批評，指出榮譽榜記載者僅限於執行逮捕期間殉職，或死於犯人行罪的警察，而非所有在執勤中喪命的警務人員<ref>{{Cite news |last=Basnett |first=Guy |title=PC Missed off Memorial |newspaper=The Journal |date=2005-05-13 |url = http://icnewcastle.icnetwork.co.uk/0100news/thejournal/2005/05/13/pc-missed-off-memorial-50081-15512209/ |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20110814094349/http://www.journallive.co.uk/north-east-news/todays-news/2005/05/13/pc-missed-off-memorial-50081-15512209/ |archivedate=2011-08-14}}</ref>。立碑工程開始之前，倫敦歷史公園集團（{{lang|en|London Historical Parks Group}}）和當地居民協會也曾對紀念碑的選址發起反對<ref>{{Cite news |last = Harris |first = Ed |title = Honour at Last for Police Dead |newspaper = Evening Standard |date = 2005-04-22 |url = http://www.thisislondon.co.uk/news/article-18097508-details/Honour+at+last+for+police+dead/article.do  |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160213051851/https://www.questia.com/newspaper/1G1-131824275/honour-at-last-for-police-dead-queen-to-open-national |archivedate = 2016-02-13}}</ref>。此外，由於紀念碑的警員名單內包括[[北愛爾蘭問題|北愛爾蘭衝突]]期間喪命的{{Link-en|皇家阿爾斯特警隊|Royal Ulster Constabulary}}隊員，而使[[愛爾蘭共和主義|愛爾蘭共和派]]激起爭論。此類人士和其支持者中，有一部分認為阿爾斯特警隊和{{Link-en|北愛爾蘭警務處|Police Service of Northern Ireland}}[[de_facto|事實上]]都是到外邦佔領的[[準軍事部隊|準軍事部隊]]，故其傷亡者既不屬「國家」，亦非「警察」<ref>{{Cite news |last = Wallace |first = Ashleigh |title = RUC Names to be Engraved on Tribute to Police |newspaper = Belfast Telegraph |date = 2004-07-31 |url = http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1P2-10707969.html |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160111030407/https://www.highbeam.com/doc/1P2-10707969.html |archivedate = 2016-01-11 }}</ref>。
