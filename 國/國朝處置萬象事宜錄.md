《'''國朝處置萬象事宜錄'''》（[[越南語|越南語]]：'''''Quốc Triều Xử Trí Vạn Tượng Sự Nghi Lục'''''），又稱《'''國朝處置萬象事宜'''》，[[越南|越南]][[阮朝|阮朝]]歷史文獻，由[[乂安省|乂安]]官員[[吳高朗|吳高朗]]於1827年以漢文編撰。該書提及1827年[[萬象王國|萬象]]國王[[阿弩|阿弩]]（即昭阿努）反對[[暹羅國|暹羅國]]操縱而起兵，因失利而向阮朝求援，阮朝出兵護送阿弩返國的史實。因[[老撾|老撾]]、泰國（暹羅）有關該次戰事的記錄稀少或經過改寫，而本書提供的史料可作補充，並對研究當時[[印支半島|印支半島]]（即中南半島）國際關係有參考價值。

== 成書的背景 ==
《'''國朝處置萬象事宜錄'''》的編撰，與[[印支半島|印支半島]]（又稱中南半島）的地緣政治有關。越南鄰近的[[萬象王國|萬象王國]]（在今[[老撾|老撾]]），在18世紀、19世紀初時被[[暹羅國|暹羅國]]（今泰國）所控制。到1820年代，有「最後的戰士君王」之稱<ref>Grant Evans: ''A Short History of Laos: The Land in Between''., p.25.  Allen & Unwin, Crows Nest NSW, Australia.</ref>的萬象國王[[阿弩|阿弩]]（即阿努。書中又稱作「佋阿弩」）不甘於暹羅操縱，乃於1826年（[[明命|明命]]七年）起兵襲擊暹人，一度抵達暹羅中部的[[呵叻|呵叻]]、[[沙拉武里|沙拉武里]]等地。其後暹王[[拉瑪三世|拉瑪三世]]（書中稱作「佛王」）策動反攻，於1827年（明命八年）擊敗萬象軍，阿弩逃到越南[[乂安|乂安]]（即義安）。<ref>姆·耳·馬尼奇·瓊賽《老撾史》，福建人民出版社，172─182。</ref>阿弩向[[阮朝|阮朝]]求助，[[明命帝|明命帝]]得悉後，派軍護送阿弩回萬象。阿弩返國後再與暹人作戰，但又遭失敗，再向阮廷要求增援。明命見阿弩再時常出戰但無所獲，便不再出兵協助，最後阿弩失敗，被俘送暹羅，其反抗暹人的行動亦以失敗告終。<ref>陳重金（即陳仲金）《越南通史》（即《越南史略》），北京商務印書館，337─338。</ref>

本書作者[[吳高朗|吳高朗]]（又作高朗、黎高朗），字令甫，[[古藤|古藤]]人，號圓齋。<ref>馮承鈞《西域南海史地考證論著彙輯·安南書錄》，231頁；[http://140.109.24.175/pasweb/Opac_book/book_detail.asp?systemno=0000000199 越南漢喃文獻目錄資料庫系統檢索─書目詳細資料：《國朝處置萬象事誼錄》]。</ref>在阮廷派軍到萬象時，吳高朗為乂安鎮書記，故此亦參與其事，做過「繕草傳文」<ref>Ngo Cao Lang: ''The Journal of Our Imperial Court's Actions with regard to the Incident Involving the Kingdom of Ten Thousand Elephants'', in ''Vietnamese Source Materials concerning The 1827 Conflict between the Court of Siam and the Lao Principalities'', p.223.  The Centre for East Asian Cultural Studies for Unesco, The Toyo Bunko.</ref>、將哀牢文件「演成華字（漢字）」<ref>Ngo Cao Lang: ''The Journal of Our Imperial Court's Actions with regard to the Incident Involving the Kingdom of Ten Thousand Elephants'', in ''Vietnamese Source Materials concerning The 1827 Conflict between the Court of Siam and the Lao Principalities'', p.190.  The Centre for East Asian Cultural Studies for Unesco, The Toyo Bunko.</ref>等工作。1827年10月18日（明命八年農曆八月二十八日），吳高朗受[[禮部|禮部]]之命，將阮朝處理萬象事宜的資料，編成報告，亦即本書，並由阮廷定命為《'''國朝處置萬象事宜錄'''》。<ref>>Mayoury and Pheuiphanh Ngaosrivathana: ''Vietnamese Source Materials concerning The 1827 Conflict between the Court of Siam and the Lao Principalities'', p.24-25.  The Centre for East Asian Cultural Studies for Unesco, The Toyo Bunko.</ref>

== 內容概述 ==
[[File:Chao_Anouvong.jpg|thumb]]國王[[阿弩|阿弩]]（昭阿努）]]
關於本書的內容，「越南漢喃文獻目錄資料庫系統檢索」裡有簡明的描述，說它是[[阮朝|阮朝]]「對[[暹羅|暹羅]]討伐[[萬象王國|萬象]]一事的處置，即讓萬象向越南求救」。<ref name="越南漢喃文獻目錄資料庫系統檢索">[http://140.109.24.175/pasweb/Opac_book/book_detail.asp?systemno=0000000199 越南漢喃文獻目錄資料庫系統檢索─書目詳細資料：《國朝處置萬象事誼錄》]</ref>

從現存的內容可知，它的卷一起自1827年（明命八年）農曆五月，[[乂安省|乂安鎮]]向阮廷通報「暹羅兵追擊萬象國王（[[阿弩|阿弩]]）」<ref>Ngo Cao Lang: ''The Journal of Our Imperial Court's Actions with regard to the Incident Involving the Kingdom of Ten Thousand Elephants'', in ''Vietnamese Source Materials concerning The 1827 Conflict between the Court of Siam and the Lao Principalities'', p.266.  The Centre for East Asian Cultural Studies for Unesco, The Toyo Bunko.</ref>，萬象人「懇請我朝廷容許居駐」。<ref>Ngo Cao Lang: ''The Journal of Our Imperial Court's Actions with regard to the Incident Involving the Kingdom of Ten Thousand Elephants'', in ''Vietnamese Source Materials concerning The 1827 Conflict between the Court of Siam and the Lao Principalities'', p.265.  The Centre for East Asian Cultural Studies for Unesco, The Toyo Bunko.</ref>阮朝君臣經過商議後，決定派兵「將萬象國王及所屬人民，安插于貢蠻[[三峝|三峝]]地分」，又「擇地駐扎，嚴加巡防」，以備暹軍來犯。<ref>Ngo Cao Lang: ''The Journal of Our Imperial Court's Actions with regard to the Incident Involving the Kingdom of Ten Thousand Elephants'', in ''Vietnamese Source Materials concerning The 1827 Conflict between the Court of Siam and the Lao Principalities'', p.264.  The Centre for East Asian Cultural Studies for Unesco, The Toyo Bunko.</ref>在出兵之初，阮朝[[明命帝|明命帝]]持觀望態度，曾說過暹羅對萬象的征伐「尚屬傳聞，未為確鑿」<ref>Ngo Cao Lang: ''The Journal of Our Imperial Court's Actions with regard to the Incident Involving the Kingdom of Ten Thousand Elephants'', in ''Vietnamese Source Materials concerning The 1827 Conflict between the Court of Siam and the Lao Principalities'', p.242.  The Centre for East Asian Cultural Studies for Unesco, The Toyo Bunko.</ref>，並由於越軍在該地「間有患病，或物故者」，明命覺得「虛費機籌」而憂惱。<ref>Ngo Cao Lang: ''The Journal of Our Imperial Court's Actions with regard to the Incident Involving the Kingdom of Ten Thousand Elephants'', in ''Vietnamese Source Materials concerning The 1827 Conflict between the Court of Siam and the Lao Principalities'', p.230.  The Centre for East Asian Cultural Studies for Unesco, The Toyo Bunko.</ref>其後經過調查，證實萬象國土確已被攻佔，「暹兵扎在萬象、[[乐凡芒|樂凡]]地面」，越方乃在乂安為萬象國王眷屬修築公館安置。<ref>Ngo Cao Lang: ''The Journal of Our Imperial Court's Actions with regard to the Incident Involving the Kingdom of Ten Thousand Elephants'', in ''Vietnamese Source Materials concerning The 1827 Conflict between the Court of Siam and the Lao Principalities'', p.225.  The Centre for East Asian Cultural Studies for Unesco, The Toyo Bunko.</ref>

卷二提到，由於萬象形勢嚴峻，阮廷遂進行外交調停，在該年底「投書暹將，並護送蠻人（萬象人）歸國」。<ref>Ngo Cao Lang: ''The Journal of Our Imperial Court's Actions with regard to the Incident Involving the Kingdom of Ten Thousand Elephants'', in ''Vietnamese Source Materials concerning The 1827 Conflict between the Court of Siam and the Lao Principalities'', p.190.  The Centre for East Asian Cultural Studies for Unesco, The Toyo Bunko.</ref>另外還有暹羅將領[[博丁德差|臘蘇帕伐迪]]（Phraya Ratchasuphawadi，書中稱作「醜頗禍𠫾」）給越方的書信，信中提出「阿弩為臣不忠，萌心背叛，致暹王遣兵追拿」<ref>Ngo Cao Lang: ''The Journal of Our Imperial Court's Actions with regard to the Incident Involving the Kingdom of Ten Thousand Elephants'', in ''Vietnamese Source Materials concerning The 1827 Conflict between the Court of Siam and the Lao Principalities'', p.187.  The Centre for East Asian Cultural Studies for Unesco, The Toyo Bunko.</ref>、[[芒族|芒族]]部落「為阿弩之所逼勒，阿弩若此過動，致各芒困苦弗獲寧居」等指控。後面部份的內容，現存抄本已缺。<ref>Ngo Cao Lang: ''The Journal of Our Imperial Court's Actions with regard to the Incident Involving the Kingdom of Ten Thousand Elephants'', in ''Vietnamese Source Materials concerning The 1827 Conflict between the Court of Siam and the Lao Principalities'', p.183.  The Centre for East Asian Cultural Studies for Unesco, The Toyo Bunko.</ref>

== 史料價值 ==
據東南亞史學者Mayoury、Pheuiphanh Ngaosrivathana所提及，[[老撾|老撾]]留下的[[阿弩|阿弩]]王起兵資料甚少，而[[暹羅|暹羅]]軍隊取得的老撾相關文獻也少，而暹羅的文獻記錄又曾被改寫。而《國朝處置萬象事宜錄》把阿弩事件按日記事，資料充足可信，可補史料不足，並讓後世讀者從中了解[[阮朝|阮氏朝廷]]、[[曼谷王朝|曼谷王朝]]及老撾人等勢力如何作出決策的情況。<ref>Mayoury and Pheuiphanh Ngaosrivathana: ''Vietnamese Source Materials concerning The 1827 Conflict between the Court of Siam and the Lao Principalities'', p.18-19.  The Centre for East Asian Cultural Studies for Unesco, The Toyo Bunko.</ref>此外，本書還可反駁後世政客的老撾「受越南控制」的觀點，相反老撾大部分地區被曼谷王朝直接控制，阮廷對此也無可奈何。<ref>Mayoury and Pheuiphanh Ngaosrivathana: ''Vietnamese Source Materials concerning The 1827 Conflict between the Court of Siam and the Lao Principalities'', p.20-21.  The Centre for East Asian Cultural Studies for Unesco, The Toyo Bunko.</ref>

在本書裡還可一瞥當時[[印支|印支]]地區的生活習俗，如書中記載阮廷向阿弩王贈予的米、豬隻、魚露，暹羅將領招待越南使節的乾果、糯稻等資料，可反映當時生產情況。<ref>Mayoury and Pheuiphanh Ngaosrivathana: ''Vietnamese Source Materials concerning The 1827 Conflict between the Court of Siam and the Lao Principalities'', p.23.  The Centre for East Asian Cultural Studies for Unesco, The Toyo Bunko.</ref>

== 流傳情況 ==
本書的流傳情況，從1884年的《內閣目錄》裡可知它原本著錄有五冊，為1868年[[集賢院_(阮朝)|集賢院]]購進。<ref>馮承鈞《西域南海史地考證論著彙輯·安南書錄》，231頁。</ref>在後世有抄本流傳，頁數165頁。<ref name="越南漢喃文獻目錄資料庫系統檢索"/>在[[法屬印度支那|法屬時期]]，[[法國遠東學院|法國遠東學院]]取得抄本一份，內有165頁（兩卷），編號為「A. 949」，並製作[[微縮膠卷|微縮膠卷]]副本。後來北越（[[越南民主共和國|越南民主共和國]]）取得該抄本，收藏在[[越南社會科學委員會|越南社會科學委員會]]東南亞研究部。1977年，越南社會科學委員會委任學者Do Thi Hao譯成[[越南文字母|越南語]]版本。而微縮膠卷副本則被[[法國遠東學院|法國遠東學院]]收藏。此外，南越時期（[[越南共和國|越南共和國]]）亦有學者收藏本書的抄本及越譯手抄本。<ref>Mayoury and Pheuiphanh Ngaosrivathana: ''Vietnamese Source Materials concerning The 1827 Conflict between the Court of Siam and the Lao Principalities'', p.24-25.  The Centre for East Asian Cultural Studies for Unesco, The Toyo Bunko.</ref>

東南亞史學者Mayoury、Pheuiphanh Ngaosrivathana指出，此書在後世的推廣和引用不多，原因之一是它的文字艱深，不易閱讀，並在2001年將本書譯成[[英語|英語]]，並收錄兩卷本的漢文手抄本的（卷一完整，卷二有缺），成書出版，書名為《''Vietnamese Source Materials concerning The 1827 Conflict between the Court of Siam and the Lao Principalities''》，由[[日本|日本]][[東洋文庫|東洋文庫]]東亞文化研究中心出版。<ref>Mayoury and Pheuiphanh Ngaosrivathana: ''Vietnamese Source Materials concerning The 1827 Conflict between the Court of Siam and the Lao Principalities'', The Centre for East Asian Cultural Studies for Unesco, The Toyo Bunko.</ref>
