'''伊本·泰米葉'''（Taqī ad-Dīn Aḥmad ibn Taymiyyah，[[阿拉伯语|Arabic]]：تقي الدين أحمد ابن تيمية<!--，{{bd|1263年|1月22日|1328年|9月26日}}-->）是一個爭議性的<ref>Ibn Taymiyya and his Times, Oxford University Press, Pakistan. {{cite web |url=http://ukcatalogue.oup.com/product/9780195478341.do |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2016-12-11 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151011185024/http://ukcatalogue.oup.com/product/9780195478341.do |archivedate=2015-10-11 }}</ref><ref>Ibn Taymiyyah, Taqi al-Din Ahmad,  The Oxford Dictionary of Islam. http://www.oxfordreference.com/view/10.1093/acref/9780195125580.001.0001/acref-9780195125580-e-959?rskey=2XYR29&result=959</ref>[[伊斯蘭黃金時代|伊斯蘭黃金時代]][[遜尼派|遜尼派]][[伊斯蘭教神學|神學家]]、[[法学家|法学家]]、{{le|伊斯蘭哲學邏輯|Logic in Islamic philosophy|邏輯學家}}及改革家。伊本·泰米葉是[[艾哈迈德·伊本·罕百里|艾哈迈德·伊本·罕百里]]創立的[[伊斯蘭教法學|伊斯蘭教法學]][[罕百里教法|罕百里]]中的一員，也是{{le|Abdul-Qadir Gilani|Abdul-Qadir Gilani}}（12世紀[[蘇非主義|蘇非主義]]者及[[瓦利_(稱號)|瓦利]]）創立的蘇非主義[[塔里卡|塔里卡]]{{le|Qadiriyya|Qadiriyya}}中的一員<ref name="Makdisi, 1973 pp 118-28"/><ref name="Arch91"/><ref name="Rapoport 334"/><ref name="Halverson 2010 48–49"/>。伊本·泰米葉對於當時遜尼派廣為接受的教義，例如{{le|tawassul|tawassul}}（聖人的代禱）及對{{le|聖者祠|ziyarat|ziyarat}}（伊斯蘭教的聖者墓）的敬拜等，採取了有爭議的，反傳統的觀點，这也讓他在當時[[乌力马|宗教學者]]的正統中很不受歡迎，他一生中也因為這些原因數次下監<ref name="dx.doi.org">Laoust, H., “Ibn Taymiyya”, in: Encyclopaedia of Islam, Second Edition, Edited by: P. Bearman, Th. Bianquis, C.E. Bosworth, E. van Donzel, W.P. Heinrichs. Consulted online on 13 December 2016 <http://dx.doi.org/10.1163/1573-3912_islam_SIM_3388></ref>。著名旅行家[[伊本·白图泰|伊本·白图泰]]评价泰米叶时说他儘管能進行學者式的討論但“脑子有问题”。<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=UuBECQAAQBAJ&pg=PA133&dq=ibn+Abd+al-Wahhab&hl=zh-TW&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwifrb6zypLfAhWLCXwKHRTDB0o4FBDoAQgoMAA#v=onepage&q=ibn%20Abd%20al-Wahhab&f=false|title=The Emergence of Modern Shi'ism: Islamic Reform in Iraq and Iran|last=Heern|first=Zackery M|publisher=Oneworld Publications|year=2015|isbn=9781780744971|location=|pages=133}}</ref>

尽管在其生活的时代以及后世都被看做一个小角色<ref>Yossef Rapoport and Shahab Ahmed, Introduction in Ibn Taymiyya and His Times, eds. Yossef Rapoport and Shahab Ahmed (Karachi: Oxford University Press, 2010), 6</ref>，泰米叶在当代已经成为最重要的中世纪伊斯兰学者之一。<ref name="dx.doi.org" />他对[[古兰经|古兰经]]、[[聖行|圣行]]的独特解读、对伊斯兰教一些传统的反对影响了[[瓦哈比派|瓦哈比派]]、[[萨拉菲运动|萨拉菲运动]]以及[[聖戰主義|圣战者们]]。<ref name="Kepel, Gilles 2003 p.194">Kepel, Gilles, ''The Prophet and the Pharaoh'', (2003), p.194</ref><ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=OLvTNk75hUoC&pg=PA318&dq=kepel+ibn+taymiyya&hl=en&sa=X&ei=1d_iU6CCA4qXyATawYHgBg&ved=0CEgQ6AEwBQ#v=onepage&q=kepel%20ibn%20taymiyya&f=false|title=Jihad: The Trail of Political Islam|first1=Gilles|date=2003|last1=Kepel}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|title=A Genealogy of Radical Islam|url=http://afil.tamu.edu/Readings%202015/Sena--Studies%20in%20Conflict%20and%20Terrorism.pdf|last=Wiktorowicz|first=Quintan|journal=Studies in Conflict & Terrorism|year=2005|volume=28|pages=83|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170214205841/http://afil.tamu.edu/Readings%202015/Sena--Studies%20in%20Conflict%20and%20Terrorism.pdf|archivedate=2017-02-14|deadurl=yes|via=Taylor & Francis Inc.|df=}}</ref>
