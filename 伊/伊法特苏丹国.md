'''伊法特苏丹国'''是中世纪在[[非洲之角|非洲之角]]的一个[[穆斯林|穆斯林]]王朝。<ref>[http://books.google.ae/books?id=kSgmwugWCGMC&pg=PA41&lpg=PA41&dq=Ifat+sultanate+ethiopia+the+land+its+people+history+and+culture&source=bl&ots=piHMu23spI&sig=FsEkmDYp6DFwPvBIRryCxmcfl6Q&hl=en&sa=X&ei=85TgU7-gCfSW0QWGoYDgBQ&redir_esc=y#v=onepage&q=Ifat%20sultanate%20ethiopia%20the%20land%20its%20people%20history%20and%20culture&f=false  -page 41 of Ethiopia: The Land, Its people, History and Culture]</ref><ref>J. Gordon Melton and Martin Baumann, Religions of the World, Second Edition: A Comprehensive Encyclopedia of Beliefs and Practices, page 2663</ref><ref>Asafa Jalata, State Crises, Globalisation, And National Movements In North-east Africa page 3-4</ref><ref>Encyclopedia of Africa south of the Sahara, page 62</ref>伊法特国由瓦拉什马王朝建立，国家的中心是在[[塞拉|塞拉]]和谢瓦古城。王国统治了今天[[埃塞俄比亚|埃塞俄比亚]]的东部、[[吉布提|吉布提]]和[[索马里|索马里]]北部的部分地区。
