'''伊恩·布萊克福德'''（{{lang-en|Ian Blackford}}，{{bd|1961年|5月14日}}）是一位[[蘇格蘭民族黨|蘇格蘭民族黨]]的政治人物。

==生平==
布萊克福德出生在愛丁堡，從政之前他曾經是一位商人<ref name="Meanish Pier">{{cite web |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-scotland-highlands-islands-18059237 |title=Glendale Trust may take over Meanish Pier on Skye |work=[[BBC_News|BBC News]] |publisher=[[BBC|BBC]] |date=15 May 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.scotsman.com/news/trust-aims-to-tell-tale-of-crofters-victory-in-new-heritage-centre-1-785651 |title=Trust aims to tell tale of crofters' victory in new heritage centre |work=The Scotsman |date=10 January 2010 |accessdate=17 April 2015}}</ref><ref name="FlySkye">{{cite news |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-scotland-highlands-islands-22796956 |title=Flights to and from Skye could start in 2014 group says |work=BBC News |publisher=BBC |date=6 June 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.whfp.com/2014/01/10/skye-air-service-campaigners-to-meet-caa/ |title=Skye air service campaigners to meet CAA |first=Keith |last=MacKenzie |work=West Highland Free Press |date=17 April 2015 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150417210159/http://www.whfp.com/2014/01/10/skye-air-service-campaigners-to-meet-caa/ |archivedate=2015年4月17日 }}</ref>。

自2015年5月開始，他是[[羅斯、斯凱和洛哈伯_(英國國會選區)|羅斯、斯凱和洛哈伯]]選區選出的[[英國下議院|英國下議院]]議員<ref>http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/politics/constituencies/S14000055</ref>。
