'''伊拉堤芝士'''（{{lang-fr|Ossau-iraty}}）產自[[法國|法國]]的西南部。伊拉堤芝士外形為圓筒狀，外殼平滑，呈灰黃色，芝士肉是淡黃色，質志柔軟，上面有些小洞眼，帶點類似[[葡萄酒|葡萄酒]]的甜味及酸味。

== 源起 ==
伊拉堤起司產於法國西南部，[[北巴斯克|北巴斯克]]地區和[[贝阿恩|贝阿恩]]<ref>{{cite news | newspaper = [[Daily_Telegraph|Daily Telegraph]] | title = France: Hard cheese for losers in the war of the noses | at = Ossau-Iraty | author = Anthony Peregrine | date = July 20, 2002 | url = https://www.telegraph.co.uk/travel/destinations/europe/france/724714/France-Hard-cheese-for-losers-in-the-war-of-the-noses.html | accessdate = 2014-02-27}}</ref>。
