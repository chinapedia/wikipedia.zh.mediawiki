== 一般公民权利 ==
=== 新聞自由 ===
伊斯蘭國控制地區的[[新聞工作者|新聞工作者]]必須先效忠該組織才能從事新聞工作，他們在攝影和公開圖片前須經伊斯蘭國同意，經常遭受不同形式的惡劣對待<ref name=news>{{cite news |author1=Rana Sabbagh |title=Syria: Inside the World’s Deadliest Place for Journalists |url=http://gijn.org/2014/12/03/syria-inside-the-worlds-deadliest-place-for-journalists/ |accessdate=2015-10-10 |agency=Global Investigative Journalism Network |date=2014-12-03 |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20151010155627/http://gijn.org/2014/12/03/syria-inside-the-worlds-deadliest-place-for-journalists/ |archivedate=2015-10-10 |deadurl=no }}</ref>。如果伊斯蘭國控制地區有新聞工作者受雇於反對伊斯蘭國的組織，他們的攝影器材會遭到沒收；如果新聞工作者不遵從伊斯蘭國的指示的話，他們會被拘禁入獄、遭到[[鞭刑|鞭刑]]或被殺<ref name=news/>。一些身處伊斯蘭國控制地區的專業新聞工作者會在網上發佈新聞報導，「[[拉卡正無聲地被屠宰|拉卡正無聲地被屠宰]]」便是其中一例<ref name=news/>。

伊斯蘭國曾多次襲擊新聞工作者，並企圖奪取其控制地區內的傳媒設施<ref>{{cite news |title=Report: Press freedom experienced 'drastic decline' in 2014 |url=http://america.aljazeera.com/articles/2015/2/12/report-press-freedom-experienced-drastic-decline-in-2014.html |accessdate=2015-10-10 |agency=Al Jazeera America |date=2015-02-12 |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20150214163024/http://america.aljazeera.com/articles/2015/2/12/report-press-freedom-experienced-drastic-decline-in-2014.html |archivedate=2015-02-14 |deadurl=no }}</ref>。[[無國界記者|無國界記者]]指出，伊斯蘭國意圖掌控[[新聞|新聞]]和[[資訊|資訊]]，針對不宣傳{{link-en|伊斯蘭國意識形態|Ideology of the Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant}}的新聞從業員；伊斯蘭國被無國界記者形容為「新聞自由在[[伊拉克|伊拉克]]和[[敘利亞|敘利亞]]遭受的最大威脅」<ref>{{cite news |title=ISIS – MAJOR THREAT TO MEDIA FREEDOM IN BOTH IRAQ AND SYRIA |url=http://en.rsf.org/iraq-isis-major-threat-to-media-freedom-30-12-2013,45680.html |accessdate=2015-10-10 |agency=Reporters Without Borders |date=2013-12-30 |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20151010161746/http://en.rsf.org/iraq-isis-major-threat-to-media-freedom-30-12-2013,45680.html |archivedate=2015-10-10 |deadurl=no }}</ref>。

=== 宗教自由 ===
伊斯蘭國被指在其控制地區內殺害和[[歧視|歧視]][[基督教徒|基督教徒]]，這些措施包括：禁止基督教徒受雇於{{link-en|公營部門|Public sector}}、破壞[[基督教|基督教]]的建築物和其他文物、要求基督教徒繳納其他民眾不用繳交的特殊[[稅項|稅項]]、強迫患病或殘疾的基督教徒改信[[伊斯蘭教|伊斯蘭教]]，以至驅逐或[[屠殺|屠殺]]控制地區內的基督教徒<ref name=religion>{{cite web |author1=Tom Malinowski |title=ISIL's Persecution of Religious Minorities in Iraq and Syria |url=http://www.state.gov/j/drl/rls/rm/2014/231483.htm |website=U.S. Department of State |accessdate=2015-10-10 |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20151010173648/http://www.state.gov/j/drl/rls/rm/2014/231483.htm# |archivedate=2015-10-10 |deadurl=yes |date=2014-09-10 }}</ref>。大量居於伊斯蘭國控制地區的基督徒逃離家鄉，但他們逃難時可能會被武裝份子截停，財物亦遭到沒收<ref>{{cite news |author1=Victor Beattie |title=US, UN Denounce ISIL Persecution of Iraqi Minorities |url=http://www.voanews.com/content/us-un-denounce-isil-persecuriton-of-iraqi-minorities/1962569.html |accessdate=2015-10-11 |agency=VOANews.com |date=2014-07-22 |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20151011013105/http://www.voanews.com/content/us-un-denounce-isil-persecuriton-of-iraqi-minorities/1962569.html |archivedate=2015-10-11 |deadurl=no }}</ref>。伊斯蘭國武裝份子曾在[[利比亞|利比亞]][[綁架|綁架]]21名信奉基督教的[[埃及|埃及]]勞工，先迫令其[[下跪|下跪]]，再把他們[[斬首|斬首]]<ref>{{cite news |title=Islamic State: Egyptian Christians held in Libya 'killed' |url=http://www.bbc.com/news/world-31481797 |accessdate=2015-10-11 |agency=BBC |date=2015-02-15 |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20150216000227/http://www.bbc.com/news/world-31481797 |archivedate=2015-02-16 |deadurl=no }}</ref>。

除了基督徒外，[[雅茲迪|雅茲迪]]教徒也{{link-en|伊斯蘭國對雅茲迪教徒的迫害|Persecution of Yazidis by ISIL|受伊斯蘭國迫害}}，迫害手法包括綁架、[[人口販運|人口販運]]、驅逐、[[酷刑|酷刑]]及殺害等<ref name=religion/>。伊斯蘭國亦迫害其控制地區內的[[什葉派|什葉派]][[穆斯林|穆斯林]]；該組織認為，什葉派的穆斯林是[[叛教|叛教]]者，要使伊斯蘭教純潔，就必須讓他們全部死去<ref>{{cite news |author1=ALISSA J. RUBIN |title=Questions Rebels Use to Tell Sunni From Shiite |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2014/06/25/world/middleeast/questioning-helps-rebels-tell-sunnis-from-shiites.html?_r=0 |accessdate=2015-10-11 |agency=The New York Times |date=2014-06-24 |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20140903190411/http://www.nytimes.com/2014/06/25/world/middleeast/questioning-helps-rebels-tell-sunnis-from-shiites.html?_r=0 |archivedate=2014-09-03 |deadurl=no }}</ref>。伊斯蘭國有神職人員在[[佈道|佈道]]時表示，什葉派穆斯林是伊斯蘭教的敵人，又聲言要「在[[真主|真主]]的旨意下」，像宰殺[[羊隻|羊隻]]般殺害他們<ref>{{cite news |author1=ROBERT SPENCER |title=ISIS Muslim cleric tells Shia: "Allah willing, we shall slaughter you like sheep" |url=http://www.jihadwatch.org/2015/07/isis-muslim-cleric-tells-shia-allah-willing-we-shall-slaughter-you-like-sheep |accessdate=2015-10-11 |agency=Jihad Watch |date=2015-07-23 |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20151011014436/http://www.jihadwatch.org/2015/07/isis-muslim-cleric-tells-shia-allah-willing-we-shall-slaughter-you-like-sheep |archivedate=2015-10-11 |deadurl=no }}</ref>。

=== 食物權 ===
伊斯蘭國控制下的敘利亞在2015年上旬出現[[小麥|小麥]]短缺問題，當地的[[農民|農民]]受武裝衝突、[[燃料|燃料]]不足、氣候欠佳等問題困擾；農民投訴指，伊斯蘭國沒有支援農民耕作，又沒有像敘利亞政府這樣向他們採購莊稼，令農民要尋找新買家，期間經常遭到中間人欺騙<ref>{{cite news |author1=Maggie Fick |title=ISIS-Controlled Iraq Is Looking At A Wheat Shortage |url=http://www.businessinsider.com/r-special-report-for-islamic-state-wheat-season-sows-seeds-of-discontent-2015-1 |accessdate=2015-10-11 |agency=Business Insider |date=2015-01-20 |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20151011113318/http://uk.businessinsider.com/r-special-report-for-islamic-state-wheat-season-sows-seeds-of-discontent-2015-1?r=US&IR=T |archivedate=2015-10-11 |deadurl=no }}</ref>。
