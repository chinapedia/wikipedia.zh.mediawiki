'''伊瑪爾地產'''（{{lang-en|'''Emaar Properties'''}}）是[[阿拉伯聯合酋長國|阿拉伯聯合酋長國]]的一家公司，也是[[迪拜證券交易所|迪拜證券交易所]]認可的公眾股份有限公司（Public Joint Stock Company）。該公司創立於1997年，實收股本10億[[阿聯酋迪爾汗|阿聯酋迪爾汗]]，<ref name="realestate.theemiratesnetwork.com">{{cite web|url=http://realestate.theemiratesnetwork.com/developers/emaar_properties.php |title=Emaar Properties PJSC - Dubai Property Developer - TEN Real Estate [UAE] |publisher=Realestate.theemiratesnetwork.com |date= |accessdate=2011-11-14}}</ref><ref name="ameinfo.com">[http://www.ameinfo.com/financial_markets/UAE/Company_AE0022 "Emaar"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130617180117/http://www.ameinfo.com/financial_markets/UAE/Company_AE0022/ |date=2013-06-17 }}. ameinfo.com.</ref> 是現在[[波斯灣|波斯灣]]地區最大的土地和地產開發商。<ref name="realestate.theemiratesnetwork.com"/><ref name="ameinfo.com"/><ref name="arabianbusiness.com">{{cite web |url=http://www.arabianbusiness.com/financial-markets/companies/41/profile |title=Emaar Properties - UAE - Company Directory |publisher=ArabianBusiness.com |date= |accessdate=2011-11-14 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100430033425/http://www.arabianbusiness.com/financial-markets/companies/41/profile |archivedate=2010-04-30 }}</ref> 伊瑪爾地產涉足房地產投資和開發、物業服務、教育、醫療保健、零售業、酒店服務業等諸多領域，同時也是金融服務供應商。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bloomberg.com/quote/EMAAR:UH|title=EMAAR:UH|publisher=Bloomberg|accessdate=17 April 2013}}</ref> 2007年，迪拜政府通過[[迪拜投資公司|迪拜投資公司]]交換價值280億阿聯酋迪爾汗的土地，<ref>{{cite web |last=Monday |first=Reuters |url=http://www.arabianbusiness.com/property/article/9602-emaar-to-hold-55-more-land-in-dubai |title=Emaar to hold 55% more land in Dubai |publisher=Arabianbusiness.com |date=2007-03-19 |accessdate=2011-11-14 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120930005017/http://www.arabianbusiness.com/property/article/9602-emaar-to-hold-55-more-land-in-dubai |archivedate=2012-09-30 }}</ref> 掌握了伊瑪爾地產32%的股權。<ref name="realestate.theemiratesnetwork.com"/><ref name="ameinfo.com"/><ref name="arabianbusiness.com"/>

位於杜拜的世界第一高樓[[哈里發塔|哈里發塔]]，即由伊瑪爾地產籌建與擁有。
