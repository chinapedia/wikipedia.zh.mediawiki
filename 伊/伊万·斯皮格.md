'''伊万·汤玛斯·斯皮格''' （{{lang-en|'''Evan Thomas Spiegel'''}}，{{bd|1990年|6月4日|catIdx=Spiegel, Evan}}）是[[美国|美国]][[猶太人|猶太人]][[互联网|互联网]][[创业家|创业家]]。他与罗伯特·墨菲联合创办了[[流動軟件應用程式|移动应用]][[Snapchat|Snapchat]]，他目前是[[Snapchat|Snapchat]] [[Chief_Executive_Officer|CEO]]。<ref>{{cite news |last=MacMillan |first=Douglas|url=http://blogs.wsj.com/digits/2013/11/20/snapchat-ceo-says-70-of-users-are-women/ |title=Snapchat CEO: 70% of Users Are Women |newspaper=[[Wall_Street_Journal|Wall Street Journal]] |date=November 20, 2013}}</ref>

== 早年 ==
他在[[斯坦福大学|斯坦福大学]]学习产品设计，<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.crunchbase.com/person/evan-spiegel |title=Evan Spiegel |work=[[TechCrunch|CrunchBase]]|accessdate=December 10, 2013}}</ref> 他提交的[[Snapchat|Snapchat]]是作为一个课堂项目。<ref>{{cite news |last=Colao |first=J. J.|title=Snapchat: The Biggest No-Revenue Mobile App Since Instagram |url=http://www.forbes.com/sites/jjcolao/2012/11/27/snapchat-the-biggest-no-revenue-mobile-app-since-instagram/ |newspaper=[[Forbes|Forbes]]|date=November 27, 2012 }}</ref> 2012年就在毕业前，他从[[史丹佛大学|史丹佛大学]]休學从而专注于[[Snapchat|Snapchat]]<ref name=CNET/><ref>{{cite news |agency=[[Associated_Press|Associated Press]]|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2013/11/02/evan-spiegel_n_4201128.html |title=Snapchat CEO Evan Spiegel Lives With His Dad, Doesn't Plan To Leave|date=November 2, 2013 |work=[[Huffington_Post|Huffington Post]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last=Matthews |first=Natalie |url=http://www.elle.com/news/culture/evan-spiegel-snapchat |title=Finally, a Tech CEO With Style: A Closer Look at Snapchat's Evan Spiegel |work=[[Elle_(magazine)|Elle]] |date=November 14, 2013}}</ref>。他强调[[谷歌|谷歌]]是他所敬仰的公司<ref>{{cite news |last=Warman |first=Matt |url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/technology/social-media/10452668/Snapchats-Evan-Spiegel-Deleting-should-be-the-default.html |title=Snapchat's Evan Spiegel: 'Deleting should be the default'|newspaper=[[Daily_Telegraph|The Telegraph]] |date=November 16, 2013}}</ref>,因此[[Snapchat|Snapchat]]2017年初与[[google云平台|google云平台]]达成5年每年4亿美元的合同,而[[Snapchat|Snapchat]]2016年的收入不过才4.045亿美元<ref>[http://www.businessinsider.com/google-cloud-platform-wins-snapchat-2017-2 Snap will pay Google at least $400 million a year for the next 5 years].Business Insider.[2017-02-02].</ref>。
