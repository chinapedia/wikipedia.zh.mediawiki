'''皇冠噴泉'''（{{lang-en|''Crown Fountain''}}）是坐落於[[芝加哥|芝加哥]][[千禧公園|千禧公園]]的[[公共藝術|公共藝術]]與互動作品，由[[加泰羅尼亞|加泰羅尼亞]]藝術家[[約姆·普朗薩|約姆·普朗薩]]設計，2004年7月啟用<ref name=Artropolis/><ref name=AT>{{cite web|url=http://www.architechmag.com/articles/detailarchitech.asp?articleid=2622 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070928010624/http://www.architechmag.com/articles/detailarchitech.asp?articleid=2622 |archivedate=2007-09-28 |accessdate=2007-06-13 |publisher=Stamats Business Media |year=2005 |month=July/August |work=Archi·Tech |title=Crown Fountain |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。皇冠噴泉造價1700萬[[美元|美元]]<ref name=CsCFuLlad>{{cite web|url=http://www.ledsmagazine.com/features/2/5/3|title=Chicago's stunning Crown Fountain uses LED lights and displays|accessdate=2008-03-18|publisher=PennWell Corporation|work=LEDs Magazine|date=May 2005}}</ref>，由黑[[花崗岩|花崗岩]]製的[[倒影池|倒影池]]構成，兩側是以[[玻璃磚|玻璃磚]]建的塔樓，高15.2公尺（50英尺）<ref name=Artropolis>{{cite web|url=http://mmart.com/artropolis/citywide_events/public_art/index.html |accessdate=2007-06-13 |date=2007 |publisher=Merchandise Mart Properties, Inc |title=Artropolis |deadurl=bot: unknown |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071105232924/http://mmart.com/artropolis/citywide_events/public_art/index.html |archivedate=2007-11-05 }}</ref>，並裝有[[發光二極體|發光二極體]]（LED）可展示內部臉部的數位影像。受限於天氣，噴泉只於五月至十月間供水<ref>{{cite web |url= http://egov.cityofchicago.org/city/webportal/portalCategoryTreeAction.do?deptMainCategoryOID=-536887892&categoryPath=%2fCity+Agencies%2fCity+Departments%2fCity+of+Chicago%2fSub+Agencies%2fMillennium+Park%2fFAQ+Categories&success=FAQ&entityName=Millennium+Park&topChannelName=SubAgency&contentType=COC_FAQ&com.broadvision.session.new=Yes |title= Frequently Asked Questions |accessdate= 2008-06-08 |publisher= City of Chicago |deadurl= yes |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20090307000417/http://egov.cityofchicago.org/city/webportal/portalCategoryTreeAction.do?deptMainCategoryOID=-536887892&categoryPath=%2FCity%2BAgencies%2FCity%2BDepartments%2FCity%2Bof%2BChicago%2FSub%2BAgencies%2FMillennium%2BPark%2FFAQ%2BCategories&success=FAQ&entityName=Millennium%2BPark&topChannelName=SubAgency&contentType=COC_FAQ&com.broadvision.session.new=Yes |archivedate= 2009-03-07 }}</ref>，不定時自二塔流注，並通過塔樓正面的管口噴出。
