'''謝里夫·伊斯梅爾·穆罕默德'''（{{lang-ar|شريف إسماعيل}}；{{bd|1955年|7月6日|}}），是一名[[埃及|埃及]]工程師與政治人物。

伊斯梅爾在在艾因夏姆斯大學修讀機械工程，1978年畢業<ref name=mofp>{{cite web|title=Meet Egypt's new Prime Minister: Sherif Ismail|url=http://www.madamasr.com/sections/politics/meet-egypts-new-prime-minister-sherif-ismail|publisher=[[Mada_Masr|Mada Masr]]|date=13 September 2015|accessdate=5 February 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160315122012/http://www.madamasr.com/sections/politics/meet-egypts-new-prime-minister-sherif-ismail#|archive-date=2016-03-15|dead-url=yes}}</ref>，後在國營石油化工和天然氣公司出任管理層，亦當過2002年成立的埃及石化控股執行副主席<ref name=Belda2005>{{cite book|author=Pascal Belda|title=Ebizguide Egypt|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=EL4xJptWHLoC&pg=PA94|accessdate=20 July 2013|year=2005|publisher=MTH Multimedia S.L.|isbn=978-84-933978-0-7|page=94}}</ref><ref name=pav2004>{{cite journal|last=Bogomolov|first=Pavel|title=Thirty years later|journal=Oil of Russia|year=2004|issue=3|url=http://www.oilru.com/or/17/219/|accessdate=20 July 2013}}</ref>，接著又被任命為{{le|埃及天然氣控股公司|Egyptian Natural Gas Holding Company}}的主席<ref>{{cite web|title=On working meeting of Alexander Medvedev, Hany Soliman Ali, Sherif Soussa and Ibrahim Ahmed|url=http://www.gazprom.com/press/news/2006/december/article63676/|work=Gazprom|accessdate=20 July 2013|date=26 December 2006|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160801004231/http://www.gazprom.com/press/news/2006/december/article63676/|archivedate=1 八月 2016}}</ref>。

2013年至2015年間他出任埃及政府的{{le|石油部 (埃及)|Ministry of Petroleum (Egypt)|石油部長}}，到2015年9月19日受任命為[[埃及总理|埃及总理]]<ref>{{cite news|url=http://english.ahram.org.eg/NewsContent/1/64/141862/Egypt/Politics-/Egypts-Sherif-Ismail-cabinet-with--new-faces-sworn.aspx|title=Egypt's Sherif Ismail cabinet with 16 new faces sworn in by President Sisi|date=19 September 2015|accessdate=19 September 2015|work=Ahram Online}}</ref>。
