'''灾难日'''（阿拉伯語：يوم النكبة ''Yawm an-Nakba''），又稱'''浩劫日'''，為[[巴勒斯坦|巴勒斯坦]]紀念日，時間為每年的5月15日，因几十万[[巴勒斯坦人|巴勒斯坦人]]于1948年5月15日[[以色列|以色列]]独立后，被[[犹太人|犹太人]]赶出家园之故。5月15日同時也是{{le|以色列國慶日|Yom Ha'atzmaut}}，或称作独立日（[[希伯来语|希伯来语]]：יום העצמאות）<ref name=Lesch>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.ca/books?id=CDYsAQAAIAAJ&q=%22nakba+day%22&dq=%22nakba+day%22&hl=en&ei=9zzMTaPaC9HJswbnsMy3AQ&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=10&ved=0CE8Q6AEwCTgo|page=102|title=History in Dispute: The Middle East since 1945|author=David W. Lesch, Benjamin Frankel|edition=Illustrated|publisher=St. James Press|year=2004|ISBN=<!--1-55862-472-4, -->9781558624726|quote=The Palestinian recalled their "Nakba Day", "catastrophe" — the displacement that accompanied the creation of the State of Israel — in 1948.}}</ref>。

== 制定意義 ==
{{main|{{link-en|1948年巴勒斯坦人大流散|1948 Palestinian exodus}}|{{link-en|巴勒斯坦難民|Palestinian refugees}}}}
在{{le|1948年巴勒斯坦戰爭|1948 Palestine War}}期間，約有700,000名巴勒斯坦人{{le|1948年巴勒斯坦人大流散|1948 Palestinian exodus|流離失所}}。數以百計的巴勒斯坦村莊被摧毀<ref name= "r3">Morris, Benny (2003).'' The Birth of the Palestinian Refugee Problem Revisited''. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press. ISBN 0-521-00967-7, p. 604.</ref><ref name = "r4">Khalidi, Walid (Ed.) (1992). ''All That Remains: The Palestinian Villages Occupied and Depopulated by Israel in 1948''. Washington: Institute for Palestine Studies. ISBN 0-88728-224-5.</ref>。

[[File:Palestinian_refugees.jpg|thumb]]
至今，這些難民的後裔已達數以百萬計<ref name=reuters/><ref name=Kamrava>{{cite book|title=The modern Middle East: a political history since the First World War|author=Mehran Kamrava|page=125|edition=Illustrated|publisher=University of California Press|year=2005|ISBN=<!--0-520-24150-9, -->9780520241503|url=http://books.google.ca/books?id=SKSwpfLUYDEC&pg=PA125&dq=nakba+%22loss+of+palestine%22&hl=en&ei=x-DQTZjVEIX3sgbE5OjFCw&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CDQQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q=nakba%20%22loss%20of%20palestine%22&f=false}}</ref>，而這些難民後裔一直將1948年的流離事件稱作「災難日」<ref name=Farsoun>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.ca/books?id=3ntlybo9BEQC&pg=PA14&dq=nakba+refugees+return&hl=en&ei=7EzNTYKhOYvasgal_9WxCw&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=3&ved=0CDkQ6AEwAjgK#v=onepage&q=nakba%20refugees%20return&f=false|page=14|title=Culture and customs of the Palestinians|author=Samih K. Farsoun|edition=Illustrated|publisher=Greenwood Publishing Group|year=2004|ISBN=9780313320514}}</ref><ref name=Gregory>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.ca/books?id=yo6VyhJKKb8C&pg=PA86&dq=nakba+past+present&hl=en&ei=OHbNTZ_dFcWKswaE9JS4Cw&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=5&ved=0CEAQ6AEwBDgU#v=onepage&q&f=false|page=86|title=The colonial present: Afghanistan, Palestine, Iraq|author=Derek Gregory|edition=Illustrated, reprint|publisher=Wiley-Blackwell|year=2004|ISBN=9781577180906}}</ref>。

災難日的原文為「Nakba」，意為「災難」。在{{link-en|巴勒斯坦民族主義|Palestinian nationalism|巴勒斯坦民族主義者}}定義此節日之前，阿拉伯人所稱的「災難年」是指1920年，即列強[[鄂圖曼帝國的分裂|瓜分鄂圖曼帝國]]的那年<ref name="Antonius 312">{{citation|first=George|last=Antonius|author-link=George Antonius|title=The Arab awakening: the story of the Arab national movement|page=312|origyear=1946|url=http://books.google.com/books?ei=WiTQTbiYBJLpgQe8l-29DA&ct=result&hl=fr&id=GTsVAAAAMAAJ&dq=antonius+arab+awakening+nakba&q=nakba#search_anchor|year=1979|publisher=Putnam|quote=The year 1920 has an evil name in Arab annals: it is referred to as the Year of the Catastrophe (<sup>c</sup>Ām al-Nakba). It saw the first armed risings that occurred in protest against the post-War settlement imposed by the Allies on the Arab countries. In that year, serious outbreaks took place in Syria, Palestine, and Iraq}}</ref>。最早利用「Nakba」這個語詞的是[[敘利亞|敘利亞]]作家{{link-en|康斯坦丁·茲瑞克|Constantine Zureiq}}，他在1956年的英文著作《「''Nakba''」的意義》（''Ma<sup>c</sup>nā an-Nakba''）中提到<ref name=Davis>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.ca/books?id=wlKjZwMwz0wC&pg=PA236&dq=nakba+%22constantine+zurayk%22&hl=en&ei=TIrNTaqCCZHOswbGy4W5Cw&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=4&ved=0CDgQ6AEwAw#v=onepage&q=nakba%20%22constantine%20zurayk%22&f=false|page=237|title=Palestinian Village Histories: Geographies of the Displaced|author=Rochelle Davis|edition=Illustrated|publisher=Stanford University Press|year=2010|ISBN=9780804773133}}</ref>。

一開始，「Nakba」這個字在巴勒斯坦並不普遍。即是1948年後許多年，[[黎巴嫩|黎巴嫩]]的巴勒斯坦難民仍然拒絕使用這個字，因為他們堅信他們會回去巴勒斯坦，並以「回歸者」自稱<ref name=Sadi>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.ca/books?id=A4U9iw4R1TYC&pg=PA253&dq=nakba+term&hl=en&ei=qYXNTZybAczDswbosNm6Cw&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=2&ved=0CC8Q6AEwAQ#v=onepage&q=nakba%20term&f=false|pages=253–254|title=Nakba: Palestine, 1948, and the claims of memory|author=Ahmad H. Sa'di, Lila Abu-Lughod|edition=Illustrated|publisher=Columbia University Press|year=2007|ISBN=9780231135795}}</ref>。到了1950年代和1960年代，描述那次事件的語詞包括「''al-'ightiṣāb''」（強姦），或是更暗喻性的語詞「''al-'aḥdāth''」（那次事件）、「''al-hijra''」（那次流離），以及「''lammā sharnā wa-tla'nā''」（我們黔面而去那天）<ref name=Sadi/>

1970年代，黎巴嫩的[[巴勒斯坦解放組織|巴勒斯坦解放組織]]並不傾向宣傳大離散等待有負面色彩故事，比較喜歡革命等積極主題<ref name=Sadi/>。
