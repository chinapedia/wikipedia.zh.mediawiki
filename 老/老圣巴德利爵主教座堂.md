'''老圣巴德利爵主教座堂'''（The Basilica of Saint Patrick's Old Cathedral）位于[[纽约市|纽约市]][[曼哈顿|曼哈顿]]桑树街260-264号，正门目前设于莫特街。它兴建于1809年到1815年，哥特复兴风格。<ref name=fromatoz>{{cite fromatoz}}, p.236</ref>此处曾为[[天主教纽约总教区|天主教纽约总教区]]的主教座堂，直到现在的[[圣巴德利爵主教座堂_(纽约)|圣巴德利爵主教座堂]]于1879年揭幕。<ref name="nrhpinv_ny">{{cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=5376|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: Old St. Patrick's Cathedral Complex|date=March 1977|accessdate=2009-10-31|author=Betty J. Ezequelle|publisher=New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121009101756/http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=5376|archivedate=2012-10-09}} ''See also:'' {{cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=5375|title=Accompanying 11 photos|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121009101807/http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=5375|archivedate=2012-10-09}}</ref><ref name=Lafort>Remigius Lafort, S.T.D., Censor, ''[http://books.google.com/books?id=KL4YAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA303 The Catholic Church in the United States of America: Undertaken to Celebrate the Golden Jubilee of His Holiness, Pope Pius X. Volume 3: The Province of Baltimore and the Province of New York, Section 1: Comprising the Archdiocese of New York and the Diocese of Brooklyn, Buffalo and Ogdensburg Together with some Supplementary Articles on Religious Communities of Women.]'' New York City: The Catholic Editing Company, 1914, pp. 303-307.</ref>语言使用英语和西班牙语。

1966年该堂被列为纽约市地标，1977年被列入[[國家史蹟名錄|國家史蹟名錄]]。<ref name="nris"/>2010年3月17日被教宗封为[[宗座圣殿|宗座圣殿]]。
