'''內魯塞特·怀克里夫·金'''（Neluset Wyclef Jean，1972年10月17日－），生於[[海地|海地]]，9岁随全家移居[[美国|美国]][[纽约市|纽约市]][[布鲁克林区|布鲁克林区]]，是一名音乐家、创作歌手、饶舌歌手、唱片制作人和社会活动家，唱片曾多次获铂金认证。2010年8月6日搭乘私人飛機前往[[海地|海地]]，並正式登記參選[[海地總統|海地總統]]<ref>[http://www.reuters.com/article/idUSTRE6745FD20100806 ''Wyclef Jean registers as Haiti presidential contender'' by Joseph Guyler Delva, Aug 6, 2010 (Reuters)]</ref>，但最後被選舉委員會以未符合在海地居住5年的資格被拒絕<ref name="time1" /><ref name="bbca">[http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-latin-america-11053604 Wyclef Jean to appeal against Haiti election ruling] – BBC News, Aug 22, 2010. <br>''Haiti's electoral commission said that Mr Jean was ineligible to stand as the Haitian constitution requires candidates to have lived in the country for five years prior to an election. The 40-year-old singer lives in the US.''</ref>。
