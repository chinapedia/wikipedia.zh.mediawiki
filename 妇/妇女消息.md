《'''妇女消息'''》是由[[阿富汗妇女革命协会|阿富汗妇女革命协会]]发行的女性杂志。它起初是波斯语/普什图语和乌尔都语季刊，重点是妇女权利，具有强烈社会主义倾向，反对原教旨主义。<ref>Chahryar Adle, Madhavan K.. Palat, Anara Tabyshalieva, [https://books.google.com/books?id=XPfcfF8LRWQC&pg=PA547&lpg=PA547&dq=Payam-e-Zan&source=bl&ots=yM8y7f_CtA&sig=-HLPkB8nH9PkNWMTJFpZ_pN2vGg&hl=en&sa=X&ei=wpwfUbfiPOPmygHKhIH4Ag&ved=0CC4Q6AEwADgU#v=onepage&q=Payam-e-Zan&f=false Towards the Contemporary Period: From the Mid-nineteenth to the End of the Twentieth Century], p. 547, UNESCO, 2005.</ref> 随着时间的推移，它成为了更普遍的政治和社会新闻的主要来源。<ref>Anne E. Brodsky, [https://books.google.com/books?id=Ft-DzrWF0qwC&pg=PA22&lpg=PA22&dq=Payam-e-Zan&source=bl&ots=_piTLDFf-1&sig=EbbTKYVA-rmkQDvkEbP62R3iAbI&hl=en&sa=X&ei=UZsfUZ_eB6afyAHiz4CICw&ved=0CGwQ6AEwDQ#v=onepage&q=Payam-e-Zan&f=false With All Our Strength: The Revolutionary Association of the Women of Afghanistan], p. 22, Routledge, 2003</ref> 它现在是一个线上杂志，是www.rawa.org的一部分。<ref>[http://www.rawa.org/payam49e.htm RAWA]</ref>

该杂志于1981年由[[米娜·克什瓦·卡马尔|米娜·克什瓦·卡马尔]]创办，主要由[[阿富汗妇女革命协会|阿富汗妇女革命协会]]志愿者发行，她们经常面临巨大的个人风险。一般来说，志愿者会带着杂志副本漫步在巴基斯坦和阿富汗的市场，随着文字的传播，女性会找到经销商。女性工作人员通常不会在其家乡工作，以避免被认出后受到迫害。<ref>Anne E. Brodsky, [https://books.google.com/books?id=Ft-DzrWF0qwC&pg=PA164&lpg=PA164&dq=Payam-e-Zan&source=bl&ots=_ph-OKFc-7&sig=D00G6m9-QVnCX1FrIIxZ_KLLLZo&hl=en&sa=X&ei=-7oGUaG3AcyLqQHf4ICQDg&ved=0CEcQ6AEwBTgK#v=onepage&q=Payam-e-Zan&f=false With All Our Strength: The Revolutionary Association of the Women of Afghanistan], p. 164, Routledge, 2003</ref>
