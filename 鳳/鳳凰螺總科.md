'''鳳凰螺總科'''（學名：{{lang|la|Stromboidea}}，原名{{lang|la|Strombacea}}）是[[腹足綱|腹足綱]][[新進腹足類|新進腹足類]][[玉黍螺類|玉黍螺類]]支序之下的一個總科，包括了中型至非常大型的[[海螺|海螺]]。

==分類==
* [[Aporrhaidae|Aporrhaidae]] Gray, 1850 - the pelican's foot shells, synonyms: Aporrhaididae, Aporrhaiidae,
** Subfamily [[Aporrhainae|Aporrhainae]] Gray, 1850
** Subfamily [[Arrhoginae|Arrhoginae]] Popenoe, 1983
** † Subfamily [[Harpagodinae|Harpagodinae]] Pchelintsev, 1963
** † Subfamily [[Perissopterinae|Perissopterinae]] Korotkov, 1992
** † Subfamily [[Spinigerinae|Spinigerinae]] Korotkov, 1992 (inv.)
* † [[Colombellinidae|Colombellinidae]] P. Fischer, 1884
* † [[Pugnellidae|Pugnellidae]] Kiel & Bandel, 1999
* [[Seraphsidae|Seraphsidae]] Gray, 1853 - synonym: Terebellidae
* [[鳳凰螺科|鳳凰螺科]] Strombidae [[Constantine_Samuel_Rafinesque|Rafinesque]], 1815 - the "true [[conch|conch]]s"
* [[Struthiolariidae|Struthiolariidae]] Gabb, 1868
* † [[Thersiteidae|Thersiteidae]] Savornin, 1915
* [[Tylostomatidae|Tylostomatidae]] Stoliczka, 1868
