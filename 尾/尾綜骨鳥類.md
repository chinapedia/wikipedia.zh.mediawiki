'''尾綜骨鳥類'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|Pygostylia}}）是一個包含[[孔子鳥|孔子鳥]]及[[鳥胸骨類|鳥胸骨類]]的[[演化支|演化支]]。它們是[[孔子鳥科|孔子鳥科]]及[[今鳥亞綱|今鳥亞綱]]的共同祖先。<ref name="Chiappe1997">{{cite journal|last=Chiappe|first=L.|year=1997|title=The Chinese early bird ''Confuciusornis'' and the paraphyletic status of Sauriurae|journal=Journal of Vertebrate Paleontology|volume=17|series=3|issue=37A}}</ref>
==形态==
尾綜骨鳥目有四個[[共有衍徵|共有衍徵]]：
# 擁有[[尾綜骨|尾綜骨]]；
# 沒有下椎弓突（Hyposphene）-下椎弓凹（hypantrum）；
# [[恥骨|恥骨]]後傾，與[[骶骨|骶骨]]主軸形成45-65°；
# [[脛跗骨|脛跗骨]]的內髁呈球根狀。<ref name="Chiappe2001">{{cite book|last1=Chiappe|first1=Luis|editor1-first=Luis|editor1-last=Chiappe|editor2-last=Witmer,|title=Basal bird phylogeny in ''Mesozoic Birds: above the heads of dinosaurs|year=2001|publisher=University of California Press}}</ref>
