'''恙蟲病東方體'''（{{lang|la|''Orientia tsutsugamushi''}}）是[[恙蟲病|恙蟲病]]的病原體。其種小名「''tsutsugamushi''」源自於[[日语|日语]]「{{lang|ja|つつがむし}}」，為日本漢字「{{lang|ja|恙虫}}」和「病」兩字的發音<ref name="pmid14501995">{{cite journal |author=Watt G, Parola P|title=Scrub typhus and tropical rickettsioses
|journal=Curr Opin Infect Dis|volume=16|issue=5|pages=429–36|date=October 2003|pmid=14501995|doi=10.1097/00001432-200310000-00009
|url=http://meta.wkhealth.com/pt/pt-core/template-journal/lwwgateway/media/landingpage.htm?issn=0951-7375&volume=16&issue=5&spage=429}}
</ref>。恙蟲東方體的自然病媒及傳染窩為[[鉤恙蟎屬|鉤恙蟎屬]]類的恙蟎<ref>{{cite journal|author=Traub R, Wisseman CL Jr|title=The ecology of chigger-borne rickettsiosis (scrub typhus)|journal=J Med Entomol|year=1974|volume=11|issue=3 |pages=237–303 |url=http://www.ingentaconnect.com/content/esa/jme/1974/00000011/00000003/art00001|pmid=4212400 |doi=10.1093/jmedent/11.3.237}}</ref><ref name="Kelly2009">{{cite journal|author=Kelly DJ, Fuerst PA, Ching W-M, Richards AL|title=Scrub typhus: The geographic distribution of phenotypic and genotypic variants of ''Orientia tsutsugamushi''|journal=Clin Infect Dis|year=2009|volume=48|issue=s3|doi=10.1086/596576|pmid=19220144|pages=S203–30
}}</ref>。

恙蟲病東方體為胞內病原體，需寄生於真核細胞內才能繁殖。其外膜結構類似[[革兰氏阴性菌|革兰氏阴性菌]]，但並不容易以[[革蘭氏染色|革蘭氏染色]]染色，必須採用{{tsl|en|Gimenez stain}}。恙蟲病東方體具有多種血清型，主要血清型包含 Karp（占總感染病例的半數）<ref name="Kelly2009"/>，另外還有Gilliam（25%）<ref name="Kelly2009"/>、Kato（<10%）<ref name="Kelly2009"/>，以及Kawasaki等<ref>{{cite journal|vauthors=Yamamoto S, Kawabata N, Tamura A, etal |title=Immunological properties of ''Rickettsia tsutsugamushi,'' Kawasaki strain, isolated from a patient in Kyushu|journal=Microbiol Immunol|year=1986|volume=30|issue=7 |doi=10.1111/j.1348-0421.1986.tb02988.x|pmid=3095612 |url=http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/j.1348-0421.1986.tb02988.x/abstract|pages=611–20}}</ref>。恙蟲東方體的血清型變化相當多樣<ref>{{cite journal

}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|author=Bakshi D, Singhal P, Mahajan SK, Subramaniam P, Tuteja U, Batra HV|title=Development of a real-time PCR assay for the diagnosis of scrub typhus cases in India and evidence of the prevalence of new genotype of ''O. tsutsugamushi''|journal=Acta Trop|year=2007|volume=104|issue=1|pages=63–71|pmid=17870041|doi=10.1016/j.actatropica.2007.07.013}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Parola P, Blacksell SD, Phetsouvanh R, etal |journal=Emerg Infect Dis|year=2008|volume=14|pmid=18760027|issue=9|pmc=2603112|pages=1483–1485|doi=10.3201/eid1409.071259|title=Genotyping of ''Orientia tsutsugamushi'' from Humans with Scrub Typhus, Laos}}</ref>，如[[馬來西亞|馬來西亞]]同時就有八種血清型通報<ref>{{cite journal|author=Shirai A, Tanskul PL, Andre, RG|title=''Rickettsia tsutsugamushi'' strains found in chiggers collected in Thailand|journal=Southeast Asian J Trop Med Public Health|volume=12|issue=1|pages=1–6|year=1981|pmid=6789455|display-authors=etal}}</ref>。恙蟲病棘手的部分在於感染某一血清型之後，不代表對於其他血清型會具有免疫力。另外恙蟲東方體也可能在同一個體重複感染。

==微生物學==
恙蟲東方體一開始被歸類於[[立克次體|立克次體]]<ref name="pmid14501995"/>，但後來被重新分類於{{Internal link helper/en|東方體屬|Orienta}}，恙蟲東方體為該屬的唯一物種<ref>{{cite journal
|author=Tamura A, Ohashi N, Urakami H, Miyamura S
|title=Classification of ''Rickettsia tsutsugamushi'' in a new genus, Orientia gen. nov., as ''Orientia tsutsugamushi'' comb. nov
|journal=Int J Syst Bacteriol
|volume=45
|issue=3
|year=1995
|pages=589–591
|pmid=8590688
|doi=10.1099/00207713-45-3-589}}</ref>。

本種長約 1.2 至 3.0 µm，寬約 0.5 µm ，屬於絕對胞內寄生細菌。由於該物種毒力高，需於[[生物安全防护水平|第三級以上防護實驗室]]才能操作<ref>{{cite book|author=Centers for Disease Control and Prevention|title=Biosafety in Microbiological and Biomedical Laboratories  (BMBL)|edition=4th|chapter=Section VII-E: Rickettsial Agents|publisher=U.S. Government Printing Office|pages=149–53|url=http://www.cdc.gov/od/ohs/biosfty/bmbl4/bmbl4s7e.htm|year=1999|location=Washington DC|accessdate=13 Mar 2009}}</ref>。

==抗生素療法==
{{details|恙蟲病}}
恙蟲病東方體在體外對於[[多西环素|多西环素]]、[[利福平|利福平]]，及[[阿奇霉素|阿奇霉素]]等抗生素敏感。但對[[β-内酰胺|β-内酰胺]]類藥物（如[[青黴素|青黴素]]）則有抗藥性，因為本物種缺乏[[肽聚糖|肽聚糖]]<ref>{{cite journal|author=Amano K, Tamura A, Ohashi N, Urakami H, Kaya S, Fukushi K|title=Deficiency of peptidoglycan and lipopolysaccharide components in Rickettsia tsutsugamushi|journal=Infect Immun|volume=55|year=1987|pages=2290–2 |pmid=3114150|issue=9|pmc=260693}}</ref>。另外[[氨基糖苷类抗生素|氨基糖苷类抗生素]]（如[[庆大霉素|庆大霉素]]）在人體中也無效，因為該物種在活體內會寄生於細胞內，但此類抗生素無法穿越細胞膜。

==疫苗==

目前恙蟲東方體尚無有效的疫苗，因為該物種具有許多血清型，且即使感染了一種血清型，也無法確保人體能對於另一種血清型產生免疫力。
