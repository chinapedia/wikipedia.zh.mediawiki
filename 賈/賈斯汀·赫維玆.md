'''贾斯汀·赫尔维茨'''（Justin Hurwitz，{{bd|1985年|1月22日||}}）是一名美国作曲家和编剧，最主要的作品是2016年的歌舞片《[[樂來樂愛你|樂來樂愛你]]》<ref name="deadline.com">{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2016/12/emma-stone-la-la-land-damien-chazelle-justin-hurwitz-oscars-interview-1201866006/|title=How 'La La Land' Director Damien Chazelle, His Team & Lionsgate Faced The Music & Resurrected The Original Hollywood Musical|last=D'Alessandro|first=Anthony|date=December 10, 2016|publisher=[[Deadline.com|Deadline.com]]|accessdate=December 12, 2016}}</ref><ref name="yahoo_news">{{cite web|url=https://ca.news.yahoo.com/moonlight-named-best-picture-by-l-a-film-critics-032417581.html|title='Moonlight' Named Best Picture by L.A. Film Critics|work=[[Yahoo!|Yahoo!]]|accessdate=December 12, 2016}}</ref><ref name="atlantic">{{cite web|url=https://www.theatlantic.com/entertainment/archive/2016/12/la-la-land-review-damien-chazelle-ryan-gosling-emma-stone/510092/|title=The Novelty and Nostalgia of 'La La Land'|last=Orr|first=Christopher|date=December 9, 2016|work=[[The_Atlantic|The Atlantic]]|accessdate=December 12, 2016}}</ref><ref name="wheretowatch">{{cite news|url=https://www.wheretowatch.com/2014/09/whiplash-composer-justin-hurwitz-settles-the-score|title=Whiplash Composer Justin Hurwitz Settles the Score|date=September 23, 2014|author=Bryan Abrams|work=wheretowatch.com}}</ref>。另外，他也跟[[达米恩·查泽雷|达米恩·查泽雷]]合作了 ''Guy and Madeline on a Park Bench'' 和《[[爆裂鼓手|爆裂鼓手]]》。

《爱乐之城》赢得了[[第74届金球奖|第74届金球奖]]最佳原创配乐和最佳原创歌曲奖（''[[星光之城|星光之城]]''）、[[英国电影学院奖|英国电影学院奖]]最佳配乐，以及[[第89届奥斯卡金像奖|第89届奥斯卡金像奖]]最佳原创配乐、最佳原创歌曲（''[[星光之城|星光之城]]''）两个奖。<ref>{{cite web|title=Justin Hurwitz wins best original score in a motion picture at Golden Globes|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/justin-hurwitz-wins-best-original-score-a-motion-picture-golden-globes-2017-960450|work=[[The_Hollywood_Reporter|The Hollywood Reporter]]}}</ref>
