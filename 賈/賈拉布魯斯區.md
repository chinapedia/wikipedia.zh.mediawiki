'''賈拉布魯斯區'''（{{lang-ar|منطقة جرابلس}}；{{lang-en|Jarabulus District}}）或譯'''傑拉布盧斯區'''，為[[敘利亞|敘利亞]][[阿勒颇省|阿勒颇省]]的[[区_(叙利亚)|區]]，位在敘利亞北部。[[賈拉布魯斯|賈拉布魯斯]]為該區的首府。

2004年的人口普查中該區人口為58,889人<ref name=census2004 />，有[[敘利亞土庫曼人|土庫曼人]]、[[敘利亞人|敘利亞人]]及{{le|敘利亞庫德人|Kurds in Syria|庫德人}}等民族<ref>{{cite web|last=van Wilgenburg|first=Wladimir|title=Kurdish Strategy Towards Ethnically-Mixed Areas in the Syrian Conflict|url=http://www.jamestown.org/programs/tm/single/?tx_ttnews%5Btt_news%5D=41754&cHash=bbedc896f6cefadf8d7284fe2c7fe764|work=Terrorism Monitor Volume: 11 Issue: 23|publisher=The Jamestown Foundation|accessdate=27 December 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Khalifa|first=Mustafa|title=The impossible partition of Syria|url=http://www.arab-reform.net/sites/default/files/ThematicStudies_Syria_M.Khalifa_October13_Final_Layout_En_0.pdf|work=Thematic Studies|publisher=Arab Reform Initiative|accessdate=27 December 2013|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131227230837/http://www.arab-reform.net/sites/default/files/ThematicStudies_Syria_M.Khalifa_October13_Final_Layout_En_0.pdf|archivedate=十二月 27, 2013|deadurl=yes}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|title=SURİYE’DE GÜVENLİ BÖLGE TARTIŞMALARI: TÜRKİYE AÇISINDAN RİSKLER, FIRSATLAR VE SENARYOLAR|year=2012|publisher=ORTADOĞU STRATEJİK ARAŞTIRMALAR MERKEZİ|isbn=978-605-4615-10-0|url=http://www.orsam.org.tr/en/enUploads/Article/Files/2012410_2012410_115raport%C3%BCrkin.pdf|accessdate=27 December 2013|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131227233206/http://www.orsam.org.tr/en/enUploads/Article/Files/2012410_2012410_115raport%C3%BCrkin.pdf|archivedate=十二月 27, 2013|language=Turkish, English, Arabic|deadurl=yes}}</ref> 。該區為[[幼發拉底河|幼發拉底河]]進入敘利亞的首要地區。
