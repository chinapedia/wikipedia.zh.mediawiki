'''诺拉·罗伯茨'''（{{lang-en|'''Nora Roberts'''}}，本名'''埃莉诺·玛丽·罗伯逊'''，{{lang-en|'''Eleanor Marie Robertson'''}}，{{bd|1950年|10月10日|catIdx=R}}）是一位美国畅销书作家，出版过超过209部言情小说。<ref name="Clark">{{citation|last=Clark|first=Blanche|title='The $60 million woman'|newspaper=Herald Sun|url=http://www.heraldsun.com.au/lifestyle/books/the-60-million-woman/story-fn5bodiu-1225963179743|date=2010-11-30|accessdate=2010-12-06}}</ref>她的笔名有'''J. D. Robb'''，'''Jill March'''和'''Sarah Hardesty'''。

诺拉·罗伯茨是第一位入选美国浪漫作家名人堂的作家。截至2011年，她的小说曾进入[[紐約時報暢銷書榜|紐約時報暢銷書榜]]861周，其中包括176周的第一名。

==个人生活==
===早期===
1950年10月10日埃莉诺·玛丽·罗伯逊出生于[[马里兰州|马里兰州]][[银泉_(马里兰州)|银泉]]，是五个孩子最小的。<ref name="vernon">{{citation |last=Vernon |first=Cheril |title='Queen of Romance' still going strong |newspaper=Palestine Herald-Press |url=http://www.palestineherald.com/features/local_story_203002337.html?keyword=topstory |date=2007-07-22 |accessdate=2007-08-08 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20130111072340/http://www.palestineherald.com/features/local_story_203002337.html?keyword=topstory |archivedate=2013-01-11 }}</ref>她是爱尔兰后裔。<ref>Irish Times May 12, 2007</ref>她的家人都酷爱读书，所以书在她的生活中总是很重要。<ref name="bookreporter"/>她在一所天主教学校学习。<ref name="bookreporter2">{{cite web|title=Author Nora Roberts October 1998 |publisher=BookReporter|url=http://www.bookreporter.com/authors/au-roberts-nora.asp|accessdate=2007-08-09|date=1998-10|last=House|first=Jeanny}}</ref>高中二年级时，罗伯茨转学到当地的公立学校，蒙哥马利布莱尔高中。<ref>{{cite web|title=Senior picture from Blair High School 1968 Silverlogue Yearbook|url=http://www.itsallaboutfamily.com/gallery/main.php?g2_itemId=51400&g2_navId=x6c68125d|publisher=Itsallaboutfamily.com|accessdate=2013-03-17|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120215230105/http://www.itsallaboutfamily.com/gallery/main.php?g2_itemId=51400&g2_navId=x6c68125d|archivedate=2012-02-15}}</ref>在那里她遇到了她的第一任丈夫Ronald Aufdem-Brinke。1968年高中毕业后他们结婚。<ref name="kloberdanz">{{cite web|last=Kloberdanz|first=Kristin|title=Meet the Authors – Nora Roberts|Book Magazine|date=March–April 2002|accessdate=2007-08-10|url=http://www.barnesandnoble.com/writers/writerdetails.asp?z=y&cid=881767#interview|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070711182242/http://www.barnesandnoble.com/writers/writerdetails.asp?z=y&cid=881767#interview|archivedate=2007-07-11}}</ref><ref name="Plag"/>

罗伯茨的丈夫在他父亲的公司工作。她生了两个儿子，Dan和Jason。罗伯茨成为了一个家庭主妇。罗伯茨花了很多时间做工艺品，包括陶瓷和缝制孩子的衣服。<ref name="kloberdanz"/>与丈夫1983年离婚。

===现在===
罗伯茨与第二任丈夫Bruce Wilder相遇，他是一个木匠，因为罗伯茨聘请他为她修书架而认识。1985年7月他们结婚。她的丈夫拥有并经营一家书店。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ttpbooks.com/ourstory.html |title=Turn the Page Bookstore |publisher=Ttpbooks.com |accessdate=2013-10-31}}</ref>还拥有附近历史悠久的一家酒店。<ref name=nyt>{{cite news|last=La Gorce|first=Tammy|title=Maryland's Civil War Country Seeks a Softer Side|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2010/04/30/travel/30boonsboro.html?scp=1&sq=Nora%20Roberts&st=cse&_r=0|accessdate=2013-04-06|newspaper=The New York Times|date=2010-04-29}}</ref>

当罗伯茨专注于写一部小说时<ref name="nuckols">{{cite web|last=Nuckols|first=Ben|title=For Romance Titan Roberts, Writing Novels is a 9-to-5 Job|publisher=WTOP News|date=2006-08-07|url=http://www.wtopnews.com/index.php?nid=25&pid=0&sid=872864&page=1|accessdate=2007-08-09|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071010162819/http://www.wtopnews.com/index.php?nid=25&pid=0&sid=872864&page=1|archivedate=2007-10-10|df=}}</ref>，每天要工作8个小时，即使度假也一样。<ref name="Plag"/>罗伯茨不是开始一个大纲或剧情梗概，而是设想一个重要事件，人物，背景等。<ref name="nuckols2">{{citation|last=Nuckols|first=Ben|title=Nora Roberts, 9-to-5 storyteller: Her writing output and sales are huge, her work is routine|newspaper=The Record (Bergen County, New Jersey)|date=2006-08-22|page=F07}}</ref>然后她开始写有一个故事基本元素的很短的初稿。完成初稿后，罗伯茨又回到了小说的开头。第二稿通常开始增加细节，增加“纹理和色彩”的工作，以及对人物进行更深入的研究。在修正最后定稿后发送给经纪人。<ref name="aar1997">{{cite web|last=Gold|first=Laurie|author2=Linda Mowery|title=Nora Roberts on her MacGregor Series|url=http://www.likesbooks.com/roberts.html|publisher=All About Romance|date=1997-09-22|accessdate=2007-08-09|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070823003128/http://www.likesbooks.com/roberts.html|archivedate=2007-08-23}}</ref>

她经常写三部曲，这样她就可以继续保留相同角色。<ref name="schendel">{{cite web|last=Schendel|first=Jennifer|title=The Appeal of the Romance Series|publisher=All About Romance|url=http://www.likesbooks.com/128.html#robb|date=2001-11-15|accessdate=2007-08-09|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070807072307/http://www.likesbooks.com/128.html#robb|archivedate=2007-08-07}}</ref>她的三部曲都是平装版本出版，因为罗伯茨认为，等待精装版本对读者来说时间太长。<ref name="bookreporter">{{cite web|title=Author Nora Roberts|publisher=BookReporter|url=http://www.bookreporter.com/authors/au-roberts-nora.asp|accessdate=2007-08-09|date=2000-03-10|last=Weiner|first=Debbie}}</ref>

罗伯茨在互联网上做了很多研究，因为她讨厌飞行。<ref name="Plag">{{citation|last=Quinn |first=Judy |date=1998-02-23 |url=http://www.publishersweekly.com/article/CA165397.html?pubdate=2%2F23%2F1998&display=archive |title=Nora Roberts: A Celebration of Emotions |newspaper=Publishers Weekly |accessdate=2006-12-25 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080208100926/http://www.publishersweekly.com/article/CA165397.html?pubdate=2%2F23%2F1998&display=archive |archivedate=2008-02-08 }} </ref>

她是一个狂热的棒球迷。<ref>{{cite web|title=Suns release 2007 promotional schedule|url=http://www.milb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20070402&content_id=201534&vkey=pr_milb&fext=.jsp|accessdate=2014-05-17|newspaper=milb.com|date=2007-04-02}}</ref>
