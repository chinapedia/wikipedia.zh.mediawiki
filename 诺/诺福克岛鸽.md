'''诺福克岛鸽'''（[[学名|学名]]：''Hemiphaga novaeseelandiae spadicea''）有时亦被称作'''伍德鸠'''（Wood quest）。是{{tsl|en|Hemiphaga novaeseelandiae|新西兰鸠}}的一个[[亚种|亚种]]，为[[诺福克岛|诺福克岛]]的特有亚种。于20世纪[[灭绝|灭绝]]。早期的记录证实了这种鸟的存在，但其的习性不明。根据其他亚种的习性推断，它们可能以植物的[[果实|果实]]为食。<ref name="Garnett">{{cite web | url = http://www.environment.gov.au/biodiversity/threatened/publications/action/birds2000/pubs/nz-pigeon.pdf | work = The Action Plan for Australian Birds 2000 | author = Stephen T. Garnett and Gabriel M. Crowley | publisher = Environment Australia | date = 2000 | accessdate = 2014-08-24 | title = New Zealand Pigeon (Norfolk Island) | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20110521012227/http://www.environment.gov.au/biodiversity/threatened/publications/action/birds2000/pubs/nz-pigeon.pdf | archivedate = 2011-05-21 }}</ref>

诺福克岛鸽[[灭绝|灭绝]]的原因是由于[[猫|猫]]、[[黄鼠狼|黄鼠狼]]的入侵造成的，欧洲殖民者对其[[栖息地|栖息地]]的破坏和对其过度的猎杀亦是原因之一。<ref name = "Garnett" />[[波利尼西亚人|波利尼西亚人]]也会狩猎诺福克岛鸽<ref>{{cite journal | title = Avifauna from the Emily Bay Settlement Site, Norfolk Island:A Preliminary Account | journal = Records of the Australian Museum | volume = 27 | url = http://www.amonline.net.au/pdf/publications/1343_complete.pdf | date = 2001 | accessdate = 2014-08-24 | pages = 85–100 | author = Richard N. Holdaway & Atholl Anderson | doi=10.3853/j.0812-7387.27.2001.1343}}</ref> 。当欧洲殖民者登陆[[诺福克岛|诺福克岛]]后，将诺福克岛鸽和其他鸟类作为他们的食物来源。于殖民军官道登·威廉·贝斯特·阿贝尔（Abel Dottin William Best）少尉的记录中，诺福克岛鸽于1838年仍然很普遍<ref>{{cite journal | title = Ensign Best's bird observations on Norfolk Island | pages = 319–322 | journal = Notornis | publisher = Ornithological Society of New Zealand | volume = 32 | date = December 1985 | url = http://www.notornis.org.nz/free_issues/Notornis_32-1985/Notornis_32_4.pdf | accessdate = 2014-08-24 | author = James L. Moore | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20081017211931/http://www.notornis.org.nz/free_issues/Notornis_32-1985/Notornis_32_4.pdf | archivedate = 2008-10-17 }}</ref>，随后数量开始下降。最后一次目击发生于1901年<ref>{{cite book | title = The Encyclopedia of Vanished Species | last = Day | first = David | date = 1989 | publisher = Mclaren Publishing Limited | place = Hong Kong | isbn = 0-947889-30-2}}</ref> 。

于1971年2月24日，诺福克岛政府发行了诺福克岛鸽的[[邮票|邮票]]以对其进行纪念。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.captaincooksociety.com/ccsu33part3.pdf | title = Bird Stamps Related To ''Captain James Cook'' | date = 2005 | publisher= Captain Cook Society | accessdate = 2014-08-24}}</ref>
