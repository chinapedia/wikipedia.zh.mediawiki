《'''阿凡达：电影原声带'''》（{{lang-en|''Avatar: Music from the Motion Picture''}}）[[詹姆斯·卡梅隆|詹姆斯·卡梅隆]]执导的[[三维电影|3D]][[科幻电影|科幻]][[电影|电影]][[阿凡达|阿凡达]]的配乐专辑。配乐、歌曲由[[詹姆斯·霍纳|詹姆斯·霍纳]]创作。[http://www.avatarscore.com 官方网站]上可以查询到专辑曲目表。

==专辑信息==
作曲家[[詹姆斯·霍纳|詹姆斯·霍纳]]为电影[[阿凡达|阿凡达]]配乐，这是他继''[[异形_(电影)|异型]]''和''[[泰坦尼克号_(1997年电影)|泰坦尼克号]]''之后第三次与卡梅隆合作。 <ref>{{cite news | author = Mikael Carlsson | title = Fox confirms Horner on Cameron's 'Avatar' | work = Film Music Weekly | date = 2007-06-19 | url =  | accessdate=2007-06-19}}</ref> 2008年3月，霍纳与一支小型合唱团录制了用[[纳威语|纳威语]]演唱的部分电影配乐。<ref>{{cite news | author = Jim Dorey | title = Na'vi Alien Language Incorporated In 'Avatar' Music Soundtrack | work = MarketSaw Blog | date = 2008-04-02 | url = http://marketsaw.blogspot.com/2008/04/navi-alien-language-incorporated-in.html | accessdate=2008-04-21}}</ref> 他也与[[民族音乐学|民族音乐学家]]王达·布莱恩一起为电影中的外星种族[[纳威人|纳威人]]创造音乐文化。<ref>{{cite news | author = Ann Lucas | title = Alumni News | publisher = UCLA Department of Ethnomusicology | date = Fall 2007 | url = http://www.ethnomusic.ucla.edu/publications/newsletters/newsletterfall07.pdf | accessdate = 2008-07-24 | format = PDF | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://www.webcitation.org/5mpzpE6Od?url=http://www.ethnomusic.ucla.edu/publications/newsletters/newsletterfall07.pdf | archivedate = 2010-01-17 }}</ref> 第一次配乐演奏计划在2009年春展开。<ref>{{cite news | author = Mikael Carlsson | title = Top-10 Most Anticipated Scores of 2009 | publisher = Upcoming Film Scores | date = 2009-01-03 | url = http://upcomingfilmscores.blogspot.com/2009/01/top-10-most-anticipated-scores-of-2009.html | accessdate = 2009-01-07 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20090117190500/http://upcomingfilmscores.blogspot.com/2009/01/top-10-most-anticipated-scores-of-2009.html | archivedate = 2009-01-17 | deadurl = yes }}</ref>  英国歌手[[利昂娜·刘易斯|利昂娜·刘易斯]]演唱了主题曲"[[我看见你_(阿凡达主题曲)|I See You]]"。2009年12月15日，片方在[[MySpace|MySpace]]上公布了由杰克·纳瓦导演的音乐录影带。<ref>{{cite news | author = Fukushima, Glenn
 | title = Atlantic Unveils "AVATAR: MUSIC FROM THE MOTION PICTURE"; Official Companion Album to James Cameron's Upcoming Epic Adventure Features Music Composed & Conducted by Oscar®-Winner James Horner, Plus "I See You (Theme From Avatar)," Performed by Leona Lewis | publisher = marketwire | date = 2009-11-16 | url = http://www.marketwire.com/press-release/Atlantic-Records-1076978.html | accessdate=2009-11-22}}</ref>

"Into the Na'vi World" (进入纳威人的世界)作为一首专辑附赠歌曲仅由官网独家提供。<ref>[http://www.avatarscore.com]</ref> 实体和数字版本专辑皆不包含此首曲目。

==提名==
配乐获得了[[第82届奥斯卡金像奖|第82届奥斯卡金像奖]]最佳配乐的提名。 主题曲"[[我看见你_(阿凡达主题曲)|I See You]]"获得了[[第67届金球奖|第67届金球奖]]最佳原创歌曲的[[金球奖_(影视奖项)|金球奖]]提名。
