'''阿扎瓦德民族解放运动'''（{{lang-ar|الحركة الوطنية لتحرير أزواد}}，{{lang-fr|Mouvement National pour la Libération de l'Azawad}}，簡稱'''MNLA'''），以前的名称是'''阿扎瓦德民族运动'''<Ref name="Africa"/>（{{lang-fr|Mouvement national de l'Azawad}}，簡稱'''MNA'''）是活动于[[马里|马里]][[阿扎瓦德|阿扎瓦德地区]]一个政治军事组织。该组织据说由[[图阿雷格人|图阿雷格人]]组成，曾经协助利比亚政府军作战<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.google.com/hostednews/ap/article/ALeqM5hMSOBkx7DTl29wj-3y5u-439CxXw?docId=80cfb271010b4a499761b797dc31eb62|title=Tuareg rebels attack 6th town in Mali|date=31 January 2012|agency=[[Associated_Press|Associated Press]]|work=Google News|last=Vogl|first=Martin|accessdate=2012-02-04}}</ref>，但是一些图阿雷格人站在利比亚[[全国过渡委员会|全国过渡委员会]]一边。利比亚内战于2011年10月结束后，阿扎瓦德民族解放运动随即宣布成立，并包含其他撒哈拉部落人。马里政府指控该组织与[[伊斯兰马格里布基地组织|伊斯兰马格里布基地组织]]有联系<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/world/africa/mali-government-official-says-al-qaida-fighters-among-those-attacking-northern-towns/2012/01/27/gIQANfYCVQ_story.html|title=Mali government official says al-Qaida fighters among those attacking northern towns|date=27 january 2012|publisher=[[The_Washington_Post|The Washington Post]]|work=washingtonpost.com|accessdate=2012-02-04}}{{dead link|date=十一月 2017 |bot=InternetArchiveBot }}</ref>，但是MNLA否认这一指控。

==历史==
[[Image:Azawad_in_context.JPG|thumb]]
从1916年（那时候是法属殖民地时期）以来，至少有5次图阿雷格叛乱。2011年卡扎菲政府被推翻后，在利比亚政府军中的一些图阿雷格人转移到马里北部。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://allafrica.com/stories/201201200939.html|title=Mali: 47 Die in Clashes Between Troops, Rebels - Ministry|date=19 January 2012|work=allafrica.com|accessdate=2012-02-05}}</ref>这些军事人员与马里北方图阿雷格运动（Northern Mali Tuareg Movement）融合，组建了阿扎瓦德民族解放运动。由于利比亚内战及战后利比亚国内混乱局势，大量重武器、弹药流散到撒哈拉部落地区。<ref name="magharebia">{{cite news|url=http://www.magharebia.com/cocoon/awi/xhtml1/en_GB/features/awi/features/2012/01/30/feature-01|title=Loose Libyan missiles threaten air traffic |date=30 January 2012|last1=Oumar|first1=Jemal|last2=Ramzi|first2=Walid|work=Magharebia|accessdate=2012-02-05}}</ref><ref name="1source"/>这大大加强了图阿雷格人的军事实力。<ref name="Africa">{{cite news|url=http://thinkafricapress.com/mali/causes-uprising-northern-mali-tuareg|title=The Causes of the Uprising in Northern Mali|date=6 February 2012|last=Morgan|first=Andy|publisher=Think Africa Press|accessdate=2012-02-10|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120310211808/http://thinkafricapress.com/mali/causes-uprising-northern-mali-tuareg|archivedate=10 三月 2012}}</ref>

[[File:MNLA_flag.svg|thumb]]
马里政府指控阿扎瓦德民族解放运动与伊斯兰马格里布基地组织有联系<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/world/africa/mali-government-official-says-al-qaida-fighters-among-those-attacking-northern-towns/2012/01/27/gIQANfYCVQ_story.html|title=Mali government official says al-Qaida fighters among those attacking northern towns|date=27 January 2012|work=[[The_Washington_Post|The Washington Post]]|accessdate=2012-02-04}}{{dead link|date=十一月 2017 |bot=InternetArchiveBot }}</ref>，但阿扎瓦德民族解放运动否认这一指控<ref>{{cite news|url=http://af.reuters.com/article/topNews/idAFJOE80Q0AW20120127|title=Mali says rebels fight with Qaeda, rebels deny|date=27 January 2012|agency=[[Reuters|Reuters]]|work=Reuters Africa|accessdate=2012-02-05}}</ref>。

阿扎瓦德民族解放运动组建于2011年10月。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.google.com/hostednews/ap/article/ALeqM5hJpQPmk10FgL-18kQKNxds59_VUg?docId=8ed20fbed5254a03b250f00f52b0682d|title=In Mali, a Tuareg rebellion _ without Gadhafi|date=26 January 2012|last=Vogl|first=Martin|agency=[[Associated_Press|Associated Press]]|work=Google News|accessdate=2012-02-05}}</ref>但其前身据说建立时间要早近一年。<ref name="eur">{{cite news|url=http://www.eurasiareview.com/20012012-mali-fighting-in-north-the-new-touareg-war/|title=Mali: Fighting In North; The New Touareg War|date=20 January 2012|publisher=Eurasia Review|accessdate=2012-02-10}}</ref>相較於其他有關組織，MNLA把自己定位解放所有阿扎瓦德人民（[[桑海人|桑海人]]、[[阿拉伯人|阿拉伯人]]、[[富拉尼人|富拉尼人]]和[[圖阿雷格人|圖阿雷格人]]）的運動。<Ref name="Africa"/><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mnlamov.net/english/101-they-are-not-mercenaries.html|title=They are not mercenaries|date=22 January 2012|work=mnlamov.net|accessdate=2012-02-10}}</ref>

==独立战争==
阿扎瓦德民族解放运动在2012年1月开始了武装军事斗争<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.janes.com/products/janes/defence-security-report.aspx?ID=1065932249&channel=security|title=JTIC Brief: MNLA re-awakens Tuareg separatism in Mali|date=2 February 2012|publisher=[[Jane's_Information_Group|Jane's Information Group]]|accessdate=2012-02-04|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20121208220008/http://www.janes.com/products/janes/defence-security-report.aspx?ID=1065932249&channel=security|archivedate=2012年12月8日|df=}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-africa-16643507|title=Dozens of Tuareg rebels dead in Mali clash, says army|date=20 January 2012|work=[[BBC|BBC]]|accessdate=2012-02-04}}</ref>，目的是要解放在政府控制下的三个半[[大区|大区]]<ref name="1source"/>以赢得阿扎瓦德的独立。<ref name="reuters"/>
[[File:Northern_Mali_conflict.svg|thumb]]

Besieged by Fighters Fleeing Libya|date=2 February 2012|last=Stewart|first=Scott|publisher=[[Stratfor|Stratfor]]|accessdate=2012-02-05}}</ref>，控制了马里北方部分地区。<ref>{{cite February 2012|agency=Agence France-Presse|work=Google News|accessdate=2012-02-05}}</ref>还攻占了马里第5大城镇莱雷（Léré）。<ref name="AJ120126">{{cite by anti-rebellion protests|date=2 February 2012|agency=[[Reuters|Reuters]]|work=Reuters Africa|accessdate=2012-02-05}}</ref>[[国际红十字委员会|国际红十字委员会]]说3,500人逃入[[毛里塔尼亚|毛里塔尼亚]]，10,000人逃入[[尼日尔|尼日尔]]以躲避战乱。<ref 2012年4月6日，阿扎瓦德民族解放运动宣布独立战争胜利结束，阿扎瓦德独立<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.mnlamov.net/component/content/article/169-declaration-dindependance-de-lazawad.html|title=DÉCLARATION D’INDÉPENDANCE DE L'AZAWAD|date=2012-04-06|accessdate=2012-04-07}}</ref>。

2012年5月26日，阿扎瓦德民族解放運動與伊斯蘭武裝組織[[伊斯蘭衛士|伊斯蘭衛士]]宣佈達成合併協議，建立一個伊斯蘭國家。<ref name=BBC265>{{cite web |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-africa-18224004 |title=Mali Tuareg and Islamist rebels agree on Sharia state |date=26 May 2012 |publisher=BBC News |accessdate=27 May 2012}}</ref>稍後有報道指阿扎瓦德民族解放運動決定退出協議，與伊斯蘭衛士保持距離。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.fasozine.com/index.php/le-blog-de-barkbiiga/le-blog-de-barkbiiga/8518-nord-mali-le-mnla-refuse-de-se-mettre-len-sardiner|agency=FasoZine|title=Nord Mali: le MNLA refuse de se mettre "en sardine"!|first=Bark|last=Biiga|date=3 June 2012|accessdate=3 June 2012|language=fr|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120608054030/http://www.fasozine.com/index.php/le-blog-de-barkbiiga/le-blog-de-barkbiiga/8518-nord-mali-le-mnla-refuse-de-se-mettre-len-sardiner|archivedate=8 六月 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.voanews.com/content/mali-islamists-reopen-talks-with-tuareg-rebels/1146352.html|agency=Voice of America|title=Mali Islamists Reopen Talks With Tuareg Rebels|date=2 June 2012|accessdate=2 June 2012}}</ref>雙方持續衝突，<ref>{{cite web|url = http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-africa-18377168|title = Mali rebel groups 'clash in Kidal'|date = 8 June 2012|publisher = BBC News}}</ref>在6月的[[加奧|加奧]]之戰，伊斯蘭衛士與[[西非聖戰統一運動|西非聖戰統一運動]]合力擊敗阿扎瓦德民族解放運動，伊斯蘭衛士隨後宣佈控制了馬里北方所有城市。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.reuters.com/article/2012/06/28/us-mali-crisis-idUSBRE85R15720120628 |title=Islamists declare full control of Mali's north |author=Tiemoko Diallo and Adama Diarra |date=28 June 2012 |publisher=Reuters |accessdate=29 June 2012}}</ref>

==领导层==
创立时的一位领导人是Moussa Ag Acharatoumane<ref name="AJ120126" />。该组织另一位有影响力的领导人是Ibrahim Ag Bahanga（其岳父Hama Ag Sid-{’}-Ahmed是该地区以前叛乱组织的发言人），但于2011年8月26日死亡于利比亚。该组织军事领导人是Ag Mohamed Najem上校<ref name="Africa"/><ref name="eur"/>，是马里本地图阿雷格人。<ref name="1source">{{cite news|url=http://www.thecuttingedgenews.com/index.php?article=71898&pageid=20&pagename=Security|title=Returning Malian Mercenaries Present a Challenge for Mali and the West|date=2 February 2012|last=Stewart|first=Scott|work=[[The_Cutting_Edge_News|The Cutting Edge News]]|accessdate=2012-02-05}}</ref>该组织大约有40位军官<ref name="Africa"/>，阿扎瓦德民族解放运动总书记是Bilal Ag Cherif<ref name="eur"/>，发言人是Hama Ag Sid'Ahmed，居住在欧洲<ref name="reuters"/>。
