<!-- Definition and medical uses -->
'''阿司匹林'''<ref group="注">或译作'''-{zh-hans:阿司匹灵;zh-hant:阿士匹靈}-'''、'''-{zh-hans:阿斯匹灵;zh-tw:阿司匹林;zh-hk:阿斯匹靈}-'''、'''-{zh-hans:阿士匹灵;zh-hk:阿司匹林;zh-tw:阿士匹靈;}-'''</ref>（{{lang-en|Aspirin}}），也称'''乙酰水杨酸'''（{{lang-en|acetylsalicylic acid}}），是[[水杨酸|水杨酸]]类[[药物|药物]]，通常用作止痛剂、{{link-en|解热药|antipyretic}}和[[消炎药|消炎药]]<ref name=AHSF2016>{{cite web|title=Aspirin|url=https://www.drugs.com/monograph/aspirin.html|website=Drugs.com|publisher=American Society of Health-System Pharmacists|accessdate=2016-08-30|date=2016-06-06|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170425142242/https://www.drugs.com/monograph/aspirin.html|archivedate=2017-04-25}}</ref>，亦能用於治療某些特定的[[發炎性疾病|發炎性疾病]]，例如[[川崎氏病|川崎氏病]]、[[心包炎|心包炎]]，以及[[風溼熱|風溼熱]]等等<ref name=AHSF2016/>。[[心肌梗塞|心肌梗塞]]後馬上給藥能降低死亡的風險<ref name=AHSF2016/>。本品也能防止[[血小板|血小板]]在[[血管|血管]]破损处凝集，有[[抗凝劑|抗凝]]作用。高心血管風險患者长期低剂量服用可预防[[心脏病|心脏病]]、[[中风|中风]]与[[血栓|血栓]]<ref name=AHSF2016/>。该药还可有效预防特定幾种[[癌症|癌症]]，特别是[[直肠癌|直肠癌]]。<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Patrignani|first1=P|last2=Patrono|first2=C|title=Aspirin and Cancer.|journal=Journal of the American College of Cardiology|date=2016-08-30|volume=68|issue=9|pages=967–76|pmid=27561771|doi=10.1016/j.jacc.2016.05.083}}</ref>。對於[[止痛|止痛]]及[[發燒|發燒]]而言，藥效一般會於30分鐘內發揮<ref name=AHSF2016/>。阿司匹林是一种[[非甾体抗炎药|非甾体抗炎药]]（NSAID），在[[抗炎性|抗發炎]]的角色上與其他NSAID類似，但阿斯匹靈還具有抗血小板凝集的效果<ref name=AHSF2016/>。

<!-- Adverse effects，此段更新至2018/1/23-->
阿司匹林的其中一個常見的[[不良反应_(医学)|副作用]]是會引起[[消化不良|胃部不適]]<ref name=AHSF2016/>。更嚴重的副作用則包含[[胃及十二指肠潰瘍|胃潰瘍]]、{{link-en|胃出血|stomach bleeding}}等等，也可能會使[[哮喘|氣喘]]惡化<ref name=AHSF2016/>。其中年長者、[[酗酒|酗酒]]者，以及還有服用其他[[非甾体抗炎药|非甾体抗炎药]]或[[抗凝剂|抗凝剂]]者，出血風險更高<ref name=AHSF2016/>，[[妊娠|妊娠]]後期也不建議用藥<ref name=AHSF2016/>。有[[感染|感染]]的孩童不建議用藥，因为这会增加患[[瑞氏综合征|瑞氏综合征]]的风险。<ref name=AHSF2016/>。高劑量者可能會引起[[耳鸣|耳鸣]]<ref name=AHSF2016/>。

<!-- Mechanism of action -->
虽然它们都有名为水杨酸的类似[[分子结构|结构]]，作用相似（解热、消炎、镇痛），抑制的[[环氧化酶|环氧化酶]]（COX）也相同，但阿司匹林的不同之处在于其抑制作用[[酶抑制剂|不可逆]]，而且对环氧化酶-1（COX-1）的抑制作用比对环氧化酶-2的（COX-2）更强<ref name="Goodman and Gilman's">{{Cite book |last=Burke |first=Anne |last2=Smyth |first2=Emer |last3=FitzGerald |first3=Garret A. |chapter=26: Analgesic Antipyretic and Antiinflammatory Agents |title={{le|Goodman & Gilman's The Pharmacological Basis of Therapeutics}} |editor1-first=Laurence L. |editor1-last=Brunton |editor2-first=John S. |editor2-last=Lazo |editor3-first=Keith |editor3-last=Parker |publisher=McGraw-Hill |year=2006 |edition=11 |isbn=978-0-07-142280-2 |location=New York |pages=671–716}}</ref>。

<!-- History -->
阿司匹林衍生自[[柳树|柳树]]皮中发现的化学物质。早在2400年前柳树皮就用来治病，[[希波克拉底|希波克拉底]]就用它来治头痛<ref name=Jon2015>{{cite book|last1=Jones|first1=Alan|title=Chemistry: An Introduction for Medical and Health Sciences|date=2015|publisher=John Wiley & Sons|isbn=9780470092903|pages=5–6|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=ubE0ILq_aDQC&pg=PA6|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|last1=Ravina|first1=Enrique|title=The Evolution of Drug Discovery: From Traditional Medicines to Modern Drugs|date=2011|publisher=John Wiley & Sons|isbn=9783527326693|page=24|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=iDNy0XxGqT8C&pg=PA24|language=en|access-date=2018-01-22|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171201033149/https://books.google.ca/books?id=iDNy0XxGqT8C&pg=PA24|archive-date=2017-12-01|dead-url=no}}</ref>。1763年，在[[牛津大学|牛津大学]]的[[沃德姆学院|沃德姆学院]]，{{link-en|爱德华·斯通|Edward Stone (clergyman)}}首次从柳树皮中发现了阿司匹林的有效成分水杨酸<ref>{{cite journal |author=Stone Edmund |title=An Account of the Success of the Bark of the Willow in the Cure of Agues. In a Letter to the Right Honourable George Earl of Macclesfield, President of R. S. from the Rev. Mr. Edmund Stone, of Chipping-Norton in Oxfordshire |journal=Philosophical Transactions of the Royal Society of London |volume=53 |pages=195–200 |year=1763 |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=j0JFAAAAcAAJ&pg=PA195#v=onepage&q&f=false |jstor=105721 |doi=10.1098/rstl.1763.0033}}</ref>。1853年，化學家{{tsl|en|Charles Frédéric Gerhardt|查爾斯·弗雷德里克·格哈特}}將[[水杨酸钠|水杨酸钠]]以[[乙酰氯|乙酰氯]]處理，首次合成出乙醯水楊酸<ref name=Jeffreys2008>{{cite book|last1=Jeffreys|first1=Diarmuid|title=Aspirin the remarkable story of a wonder drug.|date=2008|publisher=Bloomsbury Publishing USA|isbn=9781596918160|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=a9gkBwAAQBAJ&dq|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170908213430/https://books.google.com/books?id=a9gkBwAAQBAJ&dq|archivedate=2017-09-08}}{{rp|46–48}}</ref>。此後五十年，化學家們逐步提升生產的效率<ref name=Jeffreys2008/>{{rp|69–75}}。1897年，[[德国|德国]][[拜耳|拜耳]]開始研究乙醯水楊酸的醫療用途，以代替高刺激性的水楊酸類藥物<ref name=Jeffreys2008/>{{rp|69–75}}。到1899年，拜耳以阿司匹林（Aspirin）為[[商標|商標]]，將本品銷售至全球<ref name=MannPlummer1991>{{cite book|last1=Mann|first1=Charles C.|last2=Plummer|first2=Mark L.|title=The aspirin wars : money, medicine, and 100 years of rampant competition|date=1991|publisher=Knopf|location=New York|isbn=0-394-57894-5|page=27|edition=1st}}</ref>。此後五十年，阿斯匹靈躍升成為使用最廣泛的藥物之一<ref name="ACS">{{cite web |url=http://pubs.acs.org/cen/coverstory/83/8325/8325aspirin.html |title=Aspirin |accessdate=2007-08-13 |work=Chemical & Engineering News}}</ref>。目前，拜耳公司在很多國家對於「阿司匹靈」一名的專利權已經過期，或是已經賣給其他公司<ref name=ACS/>。

<!-- Society and cost -->
本品是当今世界上应用最广泛的药物之一，每年的消费量约40,000[[公噸|公噸]]（約500至1200億[[片剂|錠]]）<ref name=Jon2015/><ref name="COX2002">{{Cite journal |title=Cyclooxygenase-3 (COX-3): filling in the gaps toward a COX continuum? |journal=Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences of the United States of America|year=2002|author2=Warner TD, Mitchell JA|volume=99|issue=21|pages=13371–3|pmid=12374850|author=Warner, T D|pmc=129677|doi=10.1073/pnas.222543099|bibcode=2002PNAS...9913371W}}</ref>。本品列名於[[世界卫生组织基本药物标准清单|世界卫生组织基本药物标准清单]]之中，為基礎公衛體系必備藥物之一<ref name=WHO19th>{{cite web|title=WHO Model List of Essential Medicines (19th List)|url=http://www.who.int/medicines/publications/essentialmedicines/EML_2015_FINAL_amended_NOV2015.pdf?ua=1|work=World Health Organization|accessdate=2016-12-08|date=April 2015|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161213052708/http://www.who.int/medicines/publications/essentialmedicines/EML_2015_FINAL_amended_NOV2015.pdf?ua=1|archivedate=2016-12-13}}</ref>。{{As of|2014}}，每劑在[[发展中国家|发展中国家]]的[[批發|批發價]]約介於0.002至0.025[[美元|美元]]之間<ref>{{cite web|title=Acetylsalicylic Acid|url=http://mshpriceguide.org/en/single-drug-information/?DMFId=3&searchYear=2014|website=International Drug Price Indicator Guide|accessdate=2016-08-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180122072358/http://mshpriceguide.org/en/single-drug-information/?DMFId=3&searchYear=2014|archive-date=2018-01-22|dead-url=no}}</ref>。{{As of|2015}}，每月劑量在[[美國|美國]]的價格低於25.00美金<ref name=Ric2015>{{cite book|last1=Hamilton|first1=Richart|title=Tarascon pocket pharmacopoeia |edition=2015 deluxe lab-coat|date=2015|publisher=Jones & Bartlett Learning|isbn=9781284057560|page=5}}</ref>。本品目前屬於[[通用名药物|通用名药物]]<ref name=AHSF2016/>。

10.1097/WCO.0b013e3282fee843 }}</ref>。可以有效地治疗某些形式的{{link-en|神经性疼痛|neuropathic pain}}<ref>{{cite journal|last=Kingery|first=WS|title=A critical review of controlled clinical trials for peripheral neuropathic pain and complex regional pain syndromes|journal={{tsl|en|Pain (journal)||Pain}} |date=November 1997|volume=73|issue=2|pages=123–39|pmid=9415498|doi=10.1016/S0304-3959(97)00049-3}}</ref>。

====头痛====
阿司匹林及其复方制剂都能有效治疗某几种[[头痛|头痛]]，但对另外几种则效果不明。因其他疾病或创伤导致的继发性头痛需要及时在医疗机构接受治疗。

{{link-en|国际头痛分类标准|International Classification of Headache Disorders}}（ICHD）把原发性头痛分为{{link-en|紧张性头痛|tension headache}}、[[偏头痛|偏头痛]]和[[丛集性头痛|丛集性头痛]]等类别。普遍{{Who}}认为包括阿司匹林在内的非处方止痛药可以有效治疗紧张性头痛<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Loder|first1=E|author2=Rizzoli, P|title=Tension-type headache|journal=[[The_BMJ|BMJ (Clinical research ed.)]]|date=2008-01-12 | volume=336 | issue=7635 | pages=88–92 | pmid=18187725 | doi=10.1136/bmj.39412.705868.AD|pmc=2190284}}</ref>。

阿司匹林，特别是和[[对乙酰氨基酚|对乙酰氨基酚]]、[[咖啡因|咖啡因]]组成复方药物（如阿咖酚散），被认为是治疗偏头痛的首选，在疼痛刚发作时最有效，药效相当于服用低剂量的[[舒马曲坦|舒马曲坦]]<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Gilmore|first1=B|author2=Michael, M|title=Treatment of acute migraine headache|journal=American family physician|date=2011-02-01|volume=83|issue=3|pages=271–80|pmid=21302868}}</ref>。

===炎症与发热===
阿司匹林可以不可逆地抑制[[环氧化酶|环氧化酶]]（COX）来调节[[前列腺素|前列腺素]]系统，進而達到疼痛控制及退燒的效果<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Bartfai|first1=T|author2=Conti, B|title=Fever|journal={{tsl|en|The Scientific World Journal||The Scientific World Journal}}|date=2010-03-16|volume=10|pages=490–503|doi=10.1100/tsw.2010.50|pmid=20305990|pmc=2850202}}</ref>。也可以治疗某些急性或慢性的发炎性疾病<ref>{{cite journal |authors=Thea Morris, Melanie Stables, Adrian Hobbs, Patricia de Souza, Paul Colville-Nash, Tim Warner, Justine Newson, Geoffrey Bellingan, and Derek W. Gilroy  |title=Effects of low-dose aspirin on acute inflammatory responses in humans |journal={{tsl|en|Journal of Immunology||Journal of Immunology}} |volume=183 |issue=3 |pages=2089–2096 |date=2009-08-01 |pmid=19597002 |doi=10.4049/jimmunol.0900477 }}</ref>，如[[类风湿性关节炎|类风湿性关节炎]]。<ref name=AHFS />。阿司匹林是一种公认的成人用{{link-en|退烧药|antipyretic}}，但许多医学协会（包含{{tsl|en|American Academy of Family Physicians|美國家庭醫學會}}、[[美國兒科學會|美國兒科學會]]，以及[[美国食品药品监督管理局|美国食品药品监督管理局]]）及监管机构强烈反对用它治疗儿童发热，因为儿童在有病毒或细菌感染时使用水杨酸类药物可能会患上[[瑞氏综合征|瑞氏综合征]]，患病几率虽小，但致死率很高<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Pugliese|first1=A|author2=Beltramo, T |author3=Torre, D|title=Reye's and Reye's-like syndromes|journal=Cell biochemistry and function|date=October 2008|volume=26|issue=7|pages=741–6|pmid=18711704|doi=10.1002/cbf.1465}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last1=Beutler|first1=AI|author2=Chesnut, GT |author3=Mattingly, JC |author4=Jamieson, B|title=FPIN's Clinical Inquiries. Aspirin use in children for fever or viral syndromes|journal=American Family Physician|date=2009-12-15|volume=80|issue=12|page=1472|pmid=20000310}}</ref><ref name=AAPweb>{{cite web|title=Medications Used to Treat Fever|url=http://www.healthychildren.org/English/health-issues/conditions/fever/Pages/Medications-Used-to-Treat-Fever.aspx|publisher=American Academy of Pediatrics|accessdate=2012-11-25|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130218084054/http://www.healthychildren.org/English/health-issues/conditions/fever/Pages/Medications-Used-to-Treat-Fever.aspx|archivedate=2013-02-18}}</ref>。鉴于这一风险，[[美国食品药品监督管理局|美国食品药品监督管理局]]（FDA）从1986年开始要求所有含阿司匹林的药物都需注明儿童和青少年不宜服用<ref name="FDA 1986 FR">{{cite journal|title=51 FR 8180|url=http://www.fda.gov/downloads/Drugs/DevelopmentApprovalProcess/DevelopmentResources/Over-the-CounterOTCDrugs/StatusofOTCRulemakings/UCM078593.pdf|journal=United States Federal Register|volume=51|issue=45|date=1986-03-07|accessdate=2012-11-25|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110819130409/http://www.fda.gov/downloads/Drugs/DevelopmentApprovalProcess/DevelopmentResources/Over-the-CounterOTCDrugs/StatusofOTCRulemakings/UCM078593.pdf|archivedate=2011-08-19}}</ref>。

===心脏病和中风===
1970年初，牛津大学心血管内科的名誉教授{{link-en|彼得·斯莱特|Peter Sleight}}研究了阿司匹林对心脏功能的影响和预防中风的效果<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/research-confirms-and-points-to-future-aspirin-uses-for-disease-prevention-75958547.html |title=Research Confirms and Points to Future Aspirin Uses For Disease Prevention – re> BERLIN, Dec. 1 /PRNewswire/ |publisher=Prnewswire.com |date= |accessdate=2014-05-05 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140327144401/http://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/research-confirms-and-points-to-future-aspirin-uses-for-disease-prevention-75958547.html |archive-date=2014-03-27 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。斯莱特和他的团队为研究该药用于防治其他疾病打下基础。一份2015的报告指出，50岁的心脏病高危人群每日服用低剂量阿司匹林获益最大<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.drugs.com/news/their-50s-benefit-most-low-aspirin-report-says-58263.html | title=People in Their 50s Benefit Most From Low-Dose Aspirin, Report Says | accessdate=2015-09-24 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150925112058/http://www.drugs.com/news/their-50s-benefit-most-low-aspirin-report-says-58263.html | archive-date=2015-09-25 | dead-url=no }}</ref>。

阿斯匹靈在[[心肌梗死|心肌梗死]]的治療上面扮演重要的角色<ref>{{cite journal |title=Myocardial infarction with ST-segment elevation: the acute management of myocardial infarction with ST-segment elevation [Internet] |journal=NICE Clinical Guidelines |issue=167 |date=July 2013 |at=17.2 Asprin |url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmedhealth/PMH0071000/# |pmid=25340241 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151231192814/http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmedhealth/PMH0071000/ |archivedate=2015-12-31}}</ref>。一項臨床研究發現在懷疑有[[心肌梗死|ST時段上升心肌梗塞]]（STEMI）的患者，阿斯匹靈能夠將30日死亡率從11.8%降低至9.4%<ref name=Qu2009/>。在這些患者中，大出血的風險不會因為服藥增加，但小出血的風險會上升<ref name=Qu2009>{{cite web |last1=Quaas |first1=Joshua |title=Aspirin given immediately for a major heart attack (STEMI) |publisher=The NNT |url=http://www.thennt.com/nnt/aspirin-for-major-heart-attack/ |accessdate=2016-08-10 |date=2009-11-28 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160809181554/http://www.thennt.com/nnt/aspirin-for-major-heart-attack/ |archivedate=2016-08-09}}</ref>。

阿司匹林能够[[预防|预防]]高心血管風險的人群發生心脏病和中风的機會，低剂量服用时能延缓心血管疾病的进程，降低有病史的人群的复发率（即“次級预防”）<ref name=pmid20112887>{{cite journal|last1=Hall|first1=SL|author2=Lorenc, T|title=Secondary prevention of coronary artery ===术后===
美国{{link-en|卫生保健研究和质量监督局|Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality}}（AHRQ）在一份指南中建议，完成[[冠状动脉再成形术|冠状动脉再成形术]]（PCI），例如安装[[冠状动脉|冠状动脉]][[支架|支架]]后，应终身服用阿司匹林。<ref>{{cite web|last=National Guideline Clearinghouse (NGC)|title=2011 ACCF/AHA/SCAI guideline for percutaneous coronary artery intervention. ===预防癌症===

===胃肠道反应===
阿司匹林会增加消化道出血的风险。<ref name="H Toft">{{Cite journal|author=Sørensen HT |title=Risk of upper gastrointestinal bleeding associated with use of low-dose aspirin |journal=Am. J. Gastroenterol. |volume=95 |issue=9 |pages=2218–24 |year=2000 |pmid=11007221 |doi=10.1111/j.1572-0241.2000.02248.x |author2=Mellemkjaer L|author3=Blot WJ|last4=Nielsen|first4=Gunnar Lauge|last5=Steffensen|first5=Flemming Hald|last6=McLaughlin|first6=Joseph K.|last7=Olsen|first7=Jorgen H.}}</ref> 尽管有些{{link-en|肠溶片|enteric coating}}在广告中宣称“不伤胃”，但研究表明肠溶片并未降低出血风险。<ref name="H Toft" /> 若该药和其他非甾体抗炎药联用，出血风险还会增加。<ref name="H Toft" /> 阿司匹林和氯吡格雷或华法林联用也会增加上消化道出血的风险。<ref>{{Cite journal|author=Delaney JA, Opatrny L, Brophy JM & Suissa S |year=2007 |title=Drug–drug interactions between antithrombotic medications and the risk of gastrointestinal bleeding |journal=CMAJ |volume=177 |issue=4 |pages=347–51 |pmid=17698822 |doi=10.1503/cmaj.070186 |pmc=1942107}}</ref>

阿司匹林对COX-1的抑制似乎启动了胃的防御机制，使COX-2活性增强，<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Wallace | first1 = J. L. | last4 = Sardina | first4 = M. | last5 = Romano | first5 = M. | last6 = Del Soldato | first6 = P. | last7 = Morelli | first7 = A. | title = Interaction of a selective cyclooxygenase-2 inhibitor with aspirin and NO-releasing aspirin in the human gastric mucosa | journal = Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences | volume = 100 | issue = 19 | pages = 10937–10941 | year = 2003 | pmid = 12960371 | pmc = 196906 | doi = 10.1073/pnas.1933204100 }}</ref> 因此，当阿司匹林与任何“天然”的会抑制COX-2的补充剂（如大蒜提取物，姜黄素，越桔，松树皮，银杏，鱼油，白藜芦醇，染料木黄酮，槲皮素，间苯二酚等）联用时，必须特别小心。

除了肠溶片外，制药公司还会利用“缓冲剂”来缓解消化道出血的问题。缓冲剂旨在防止阿司匹林集結在胃壁上，不过它的效果存在争议。几乎所有抗酸药里的缓冲剂都能使用，如Bufferin使用[[氧化镁|氧化镁]]，还有制剂使用[[碳酸钙|碳酸钙]]的。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://antoine.frostburg.edu/chem/senese/101/acidbase/faq/buffered-aspirin.shtml |title=General Chemistry Online: FAQ: Acids and bases: What is the buffer system in buffered aspirin? |publisher=Antoine.frostburg.edu |accessdate=2011-05-11 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110414145143/http://antoine.frostburg.edu/chem/senese/101/acidbase/faq/buffered-aspirin.shtml |archive-date=2011-04-14 |dead-url=no }}</ref>

最近有对阿司匹林与[[维生素C|维生素C]]联用以保护胃黏膜的研究。服用相同剂量的维生素C和阿司匹林与单独服用阿司匹林相比，能减少对胃的伤害。<ref name="Dammann">{{cite journal | last1 = Dammann | first1 = H. ===瑞氏综合征===
{{main|瑞氏综合征}}

瑞氏综合征是一种罕见的严重疾病，特征是急性{{link-en|脑病|Encephalopathy}}和[[脂肪肝|脂肪肝]]，发生在少年儿童服用阿司匹林治疗发热或其他感染时。从1981年到1997年，[[美国疾病控制与预防中心|美国疾病控制与预防中心]]接到1207宗未满18岁的瑞氏综合征病患报告。其中93%在综合征出现三周之前就已患病，主要是[[呼吸道感染|呼吸道感染]]、[[水痘|水痘]]和[[腹泻|腹泻]]。81.9%的受检儿童都检出了水杨酸。<ref name=Belay>{{Cite journal|author=Belay ED, Bresee JS, Holman RC, Khan AS, Shahriari A, Schonberger LB |title=Reye's syndrome in the United States from 1981 through 1997 |journal=N. Engl. J. Med. |volume=340 |issue=18 |pages=1377–82 |year=1999 |pmid=10228187 |doi= 10.1056/NEJM199905063401801}}</ref> 出现阿司匹林引起瑞氏综合征的报告后，美国就采取了预防性的安全措施（如{{link-en|美国卫生局局长|Surgeon General of the United States|卫生局局长}}发出警告，更改含阿司匹林药品的标识），美国儿童的阿司匹林用量明显下降，瑞氏综合征的病例报告也明显减少。同样，英国发出儿童不宜服用阿司匹林的警告后，药物用量和病例报告也有减少。<ref name=Belay />[[美国食品药品监督管理局|美国食品药品监督管理局]]现在建议12岁以下儿童发热都不能服用阿司匹林或含有阿司匹林的药物。<ref name="BMJ2002-Macdonald"/> 英国{{link-en|药品和医疗产品监管署|Medicines and Healthcare Products Regulatory Agency}}也建议16岁以下儿童不应服用阿司匹林，除非另有医嘱。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.nhs.uk/conditions/Reyes-syndrome/Pages/Introduction.aspx |title=Reye's syndrome |publisher=[[NHS|NHS]] |accessdate=2015-08-14 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160809194915/http://www.nhs.uk/Conditions/Reyes-syndrome/Pages/Introduction.aspx |archive-date=2016-08-09 |dead-url=no }}</ref>

===其他不良反应===
小部分人服用阿司匹林后会产生类似于过敏的反应，如[[荨麻疹|荨麻疹]]、水肿和头痛。这种反应是由于{{link-en|水杨酸不耐受|salicylate intolerance}}，并不是真正的[[过敏|过敏]]，而是连一点点水杨酸都无法代谢所导致的[[药物过量|药物过量]]。

有些人服用阿司匹林会产生[[血管性水肿|皮肤组织水肿]]，有研究发现有些病患服药1到6小时后就会发生。不过，阿司匹林单独服用并不会导致水肿，和非甾体抗炎药联用时才会发生。<ref>{{Cite journal|author=Berges-Gimeno MP & Stevenson DD |year=2004 |title=Nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug-induced reactions and desensitization |journal=J Asthma |volume=41 |issue=4 |pages=375–84 |pmid=15281324 |doi=10.1081/JAS-120037650}}</ref>

阿司匹林会增加脑部微出血的风险，[[磁共振成像|磁共振成像]]（MRI）可见5至10毫米的斑块，或者是更小的低信号斑块。<ref>{{cite journal | author = Vernooij MW, Haag MD, der Lugt A, Hofman A, Krestin GP, Stricker BH, Breteler MM | year = 2009 | title = Use of antithrombotic drugs and the presence of cerebral microbleeds: the Rotterdam Scan Study| journal = Arch Neurol | volume = 66 | issue = 6| pages = 714–20 | pmid = 19364926 | doi=10.1001/archneurol.2009.42}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | author = Gorelick PB | year = 2009 | title = Cerebral microbleeds: evidence of heightened risk associated with aspirin use | journal = Arch Neurol | volume = 66 | issue = 6| pages = 691–3 | pmid = 19506128 | doi=10.1001/archneurol.2009.85}}</ref>

一项研究估计每天平均服用270毫克的阿司匹林后，[[脑出血|脑出血]]（ICH）的概率绝对值增加了万分之12，<ref name=He1998/> 与此相比，[[心肌梗死|心肌梗死]]的概率绝对值下降了万分之137，缺血性中风的概率绝对值则下降了万分之39。<ref name=He1998>{{cite journal | last1 = He | first1 = J. | last2 = Whelton | first2 = P. K. | last3 = Vu | first3 = B. | last4 = Klag | first4 = M. R.|last6=Hillbom|first6=M.|last1=Saloheimo}} <!--cite doi not quite connecting to right place{{cite journal|title=Regular Aspirin-Use Preceding the Onset of Primary Intracerebral Hemorrhage is an Independent Predictor for Death|journal=Stroke|date=2006-01-01|url=http://stroke.ahajournals.org/content/37/1/129|language=en|volume=37|issue=1|doi=10.1161/01.str.0000196991.03618.31|pages=129–133|issn=0039-2499|accessdate=2018-04-02|author=Pertti Saloheimo, Mikko Ahonen, Seppo Juvela, Juhani Pyhtinen, Eeva-Riitta Savolainen, Matti Hillbom|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180604175607/http://stroke.ahajournals.org/content/37/1/129|archive-date=2018-06-04|dead-url=no}}--></ref>。

阿司匹林和其他非甾体抗炎药会抑制[[前列腺素|前列腺素]]合成，引起低肾素性{{link-en|低醛固酮症|hyporeninemic hypoaldosteronism}}，可能引发[[高血钾症|高血钾症]]。不过，当肾功能和血容量都正常时，这些药物并不会导致高血钾症。<ref>{{cite book|title=Medical knowledge self-assessment program for students 4|publisher=American College of Physicians|author=Clerkship Directors in Internal Medicine|volume=Nephrology 227, Item 29}}</ref>

阿司匹林在术后十天内都能引起长时间出血。一项研究选择了6499名手术病人进行观察，发现其中有30人需要再次进行手术以控制出血。这30人中有20人是弥漫性出血，另外10人只有一个部位出血。弥漫性出血是由术前单独使用阿司匹林或和其他非甾体抗炎药联用引起的，而离散的出血则不是。<ref>{{Cite journal|author=Scher, K.S. |year=1996 |title=Unplanned reoperation for bleeding |journal=Am Surg |volume=62 |issue=1 |pages=52–55 |pmid=8540646}}</ref>

2015年7月9日，美国食品药品监督管理局提升了对非甾体抗炎药增加心脏病和中风风险的警告。阿司匹林虽然也是非甾体抗炎药，但并不在警告的范围内。<ref name="FDA-20150709">{{cite web |author=Staff |title=FDA Strengthens Warning of Heart Attack and Stroke Risk for Non-Steroidal Anti-Inflammatory Drugs |url=http://www.fda.gov/ForConsumers/ConsumerUpdates/ucm453610.htm |date=2015-07-09 |work=[[FDA|FDA]] |accessdate=2015-07-09 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150711004922/http://www.fda.gov/ForConsumers/ConsumerUpdates/ucm453610.htm |archive-date=2015-07-11 |dead-url=no }}</ref>

===过量服用===
阿司匹林过量分为急性和慢性。急性过量是指一次性服用大剂量的药物，而慢性过量则是指一段时间内服用超过正常剂量的药物。急性过量的[[死亡率|死亡率]]是2%。慢性过量的死亡率更高達25%，<ref>{{cite web|author=Kreplick, LW.|year=2001|title=Salicylate Toxicity in Emergency Medicine|publisher={{le|Medscape|Medscape}}|url=http://misc.medscape.com/pi/android/medscapeapp/html/A818242-business.html|access-date=2015-08-15|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120831184805/http://misc.medscape.com/pi/android/medscapeapp/html/A818242-business.html|archive-date=2012-08-31|dead-url=no}}</ref>且对儿童影响尤为严重。<ref name="Pediatrics1982-gaudreault">{{Cite journal|author=Gaudreault P, Temple AR, Lovejoy FH Jr |title=The relative severity of acute versus chronic salicylate poisoning in children: a clinical comparison |journal=Pediatrics |year=1982 |pages=566–9 |volume=70 |issue=4 |pmid= 7122154}} (primary source)</ref> 中毒的治疗方法有使用[[活性炭|活性炭]]、[[静脉注射|静脉注射]][[葡萄糖|葡萄糖]]和[[生理盐水|生理盐水]]，使用[[碳酸氢钠|碳酸氢钠]]，还有[[透析|透析]]。<ref>{{Cite book |title=Rosen's emergency medicine: concepts and clinical practice |last=Marx |first=John |year=2006 |publisher=Mosby/Elsevier |isbn=978-0-323-02845-5 |page=2242}}</ref> 通常用自动分光光度法测量血浆中阿司匹林的活性代谢产物，即水杨酸来诊断中毒。一般来说，正常服药治疗后血浆中水杨酸含量为30-100毫克每升，高剂量服用的患者血浆中的含量为50-300毫克每升，急性中毒患者血浆中的含量为700-1400毫克每升。服用[[次水杨酸铋|次水杨酸铋]]、[[水杨酸甲酯|水杨酸甲酯]]和[[水杨酸钠|水杨酸钠]]后也会产生水杨酸。<ref>{{cite journal |author=Morra P, Bartle WR, Walker SE, Lee SN, Bowles SK, Reeves RA |title=Serum concentrations of salicylic acid following topically applied salicylate derivatives |journal=Ann. Pharmacother |volume=30 |pages=935–40 |year=1996 |pmid=8876850 |issue=9}}</ref><ref>{{cite book |author=R. Baselt |title=Disposition of Toxic Drugs and Chemicals in Man |edition=9th |publisher=Biomedical Publications |location=Seal Beach, California |year=2011 |pages=20–23}}</ref>

==性质==
阿司匹林是一种白色晶体，[[熔点|熔点]]{{convert|136|C|F}}，在{{convert|140|C|F}}时分解<ref name="Myers2007">{{cite book|author=Richard Leroy Myers|title=The 100 Most Important Chemical Compounds: A Reference Guide|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=MwpQWcIKMzAC&pg=PA10|accessdate=2012-11-18|date=2007-08-30|publisher=ABC-CLIO|isbn=978-0-313-33758-1|page=10}}</ref>。

阿司匹林是水杨酸的[[乙酰|乙酰]]衍生物，呈弱酸性，在{{convert|25|C|F}}下[[酸度系数|酸度系数]]为3.5。<ref name="asaaciddissconst">{{cite web|title = Acetylsalicylic acid|publisher = Jinno Laboratory, School of Materials Science, Toyohashi University of Technology|date = 1996-03-04|url =http://chrom.tutms.tut.ac.jp/JINNO/DRUGDATA/07acetylsalicylic_acid.html|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120120224558/http://chrom.tutms.tut.ac.jp/JINNO/DRUGDATA/07acetylsalicylic_acid.html|archivedate=2012-01-20|accessdate=2014-04-12}}</ref> 阿司匹林可以在[[醋酸铵|醋酸铵]]或[[碱金属|碱金属]]的[[醋酸盐|醋酸盐]]、[[碳酸盐|碳酸盐]]、[[柠檬酸盐|柠檬酸盐]]和[[氢氧化物|氢氧化物]]溶液中迅速分解。阿司匹林在干燥空气中性质稳定，但在潮湿的环境中会逐渐[[水解|水解]]成[[乙酸|乙酸]]和[[水杨酸|水杨酸]]。在碱性溶液中，阿司匹林迅速水解，生成只含有水杨酸盐与乙酸盐的澄清溶液。<ref>{{cite encyclopedia|editor=EF Reynolds|encyclopedia=Martindale: The Extra Pharmacopoeia|edition=28th|year=1982|pages=234–82|title=Aspirin and similar analgesic and anti-inflammatory agents}}</ref>

如同[[面粉厂|面粉厂]]一样，生产阿司匹林的工厂也需要留意空气中阿司匹林的含量，因为过量的粉末会导致[[粉尘爆炸|粉尘爆炸]]。在美国，[[美国国家职业安全卫生研究所|美国国家职业安全卫生研究所]]（NIOSH）将{{link-en|建议暴露限值|recommended exposure limit}}定为5毫克每立方米（时间加权平均）。<ref>{{Cite web|url = http://www.cdc.gov/niosh/npg/npgd0010.html|title = Acetylsalicylic acid|date = 2015-02-13|accessdate = |website = Pocket Guide to Chemical Hazards|publisher = NIOSH|last = |first = |archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20150530222806/http://www.cdc.gov/niosh/npg/npgd0010.html|archive-date = 2015-05-30|dead-url = no}}</ref>1989年，美国{{link-en|职业安全与健康管理局|Occupational Safety and Health Administration}}（OSHA）将{{link-en|允许最大暴露限值|permissible exposure limit}}定为5毫克每立方米，但这项规定在1993年OSHA和[[美国劳工联合会-产业工会联合会|美国劳工联合会-产业工会联合会]]（AFL–CIO）的诉讼中被废除。 <ref>{{Cite web|url = http://www.cdc.gov/niosh/npg/nengapdxg.html|title = Appendix G: 1989 Air Contaminants Update Project - Exposure Limits NOT in Effect|date = 2015-02-13|accessdate = |website = NIOSH Pocket Guide to Chemical Hazards|publisher = NIOSH|last = |first = |archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20150630023354/http://www.cdc.gov/niosh/npg/nengapdxg.html|archive-date = 2015-06-30|dead-url = no}}</ref>

===多晶型性===
[[多晶型性|多晶型性]]是同一种物质形成多种[[晶体结构|晶体结构]]的能力，它对药物成分的开发至关重要。很多药物只有一种晶体结构经过监管部门批准。有很长一段时间，人们只知道阿司匹林的一种晶体结构，从1960年起开始怀疑它还有一种晶体结构，2005年才发现了这种神秘的结构。<ref name=Vishweshwar>{{cite journal | last1 = Vishweshwar | first1 = P. | last2 = McMahon | first2 = J. A. | last3 = Oliveira | first3 = M. | last4 = Peterson | first4 = M. L. | last5 = Zaworotko | first5 = M. J. | title = The Predictably Elusive Form II of Aspirin | journal = Journal of the American Chemical Society | volume = 127 | issue = 48 | pages = 16802–16803 | year = 2005 | pmid = 16316223 | pmc =  | doi = 10.1021/ja056455b }}</ref> 邦德等人测定了这种结构的细节。<ref>{{Cite journal|author= Bond, Andrew D.; Boese, Roland; and Desiraju, Gautam R. |title = On the Polymorphism of Aspirin: Crystalline Aspirin as Intergrowths of Two "Polymorphic" Domains|journal = [[Angewandte_Chemie_International_Edition|Angewandte Chemie International Edition]]|year = 2007|volume = 46|issue = 4|pages = 618–622|doi = 10.1002/anie.200603373|pmid= 17139692}}</ref> 这种新的晶体结构是在热[[乙腈|乙腈]]中使阿司匹林和{{link-en|左乙拉西坦|Levetiracetam}}共同结晶时发现的。<ref name=Vishweshwar/> <!--{cn|第二种结构只有在100 [[开尔文|K]]时才稳定，在室温下会转化成第一种结构。在第一种结构中，两个水杨酸分子形成中心对称的[[二聚体|二聚体]]，其中呈酸性的[[甲基|甲基]]提供质子与[[羰基|羰基]]形成[[氢键|氢键]]，而在新合成的第二种结构中，每个水杨酸分子不是和一个分子，而是和相邻的两个分子形成这种氢键。两种结构中[[羧基|羧基]]所形成的氢键相同，都是形成二聚体。-->

==制法==
制取阿司匹林的反应通常归为[[酯化反应|酯化反应]]。[[水杨酸|水杨酸]]和[[乙酸酐|乙酸酐]]（一种乙酸的衍生物）发生反应，水杨酸中的[[羟基|羟基]]替换为[[酯基|酯基]]（R-OH → R-OCOCH<sub>3</sub>），生成阿司匹林和副产物[[乙酸|乙酸]]。通常用少量[[硫酸|硫酸]]作催化剂（有时用[[磷酸|磷酸]]）。<ref>{{Cite book|title=Experimental Organic Chemistry |last=Palleros |first=Daniel R. |year=2000 |publisher=John Wiley & Sons|location=New York|isbn=0-471-28250-2 |page=494}}</ref>

==作用机理==

===对前列腺素和血栓素的抑制===
阿司匹林能抑制前列腺素和血栓素是因为该药能不可逆地使合成前列腺素和血栓素所需的[[环氧合酶|环氧合酶]]（COX，学名叫前列腺素氧化环化酶，PTGS）失活。阿司匹林能使PTGS[[活性位点|活性位点]]中的一个[[丝氨酸|丝氨酸]]残基[[乙酰化|乙酰化]]，这是它和其他非甾体抗炎药（如[[双氯芬酸钠|双氯芬酸钠]]和[[布洛芬|布洛芬]]）的不同之处，因为其他的药抑制作用都是可逆的<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Diclofenac and NS-398, a selective cyclooxygenase-2 inhibitor, decrease agonist-induced contractions of the pig isolated ureter|url=http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/11221916|last=Mastrangelo|first=D.|last2=Wisard|first2=M.|date=2000-12-01|journal=Urological Research|issue=6|volume=28|pages=376–382|issn=0300-5623|pmid=11221916|last3=Rohner|first3=S.|last4=Leisinger|first4=H.|last5=Iselin|first5=C. E.|author=|access-date=2016-02-25|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160919063855/http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/11221916|archive-date=2016-09-19|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|title=Differential Sensitivity and Mechanism of Inhibition of COX-2 Oxygenation of Arachidonic Acid and 2-Arachidonoylglycerol by Ibuprofen and Mefenamic Acid|url=http://pubs.acs.org/doi/abs/10.1021/bi900999z|last=Prusakiewicz|first=Jeffery J.|last2=Duggan|first2=Kelsey C.|date=2009-07-20|journal=Biochemistry|issue=31|doi=10.1021/bi900999z|volume=48|pages=7353–7355|language=EN|pmc=2720641|pmid=19603831|last3=Rouzer|first3=Carol A.|last4=Marnett|first4=Lawrence J.}}</ref>。

阿司匹林低剂量服用时能阻止血小板中[[血栓素A2|血栓素A2]]的合成，这会在受影响的血小板的生命周期（约8–9天）内抑制血小板聚集。阿司匹林的这种抗血栓作用可用于降低心脏病发生率。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.americanheart.org/presenter.jhtml?identifier=4456 |title=Aspirin in Heart Attack and Stroke Prevention |accessdate=2008-05-08 |publisher=American Heart Association |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080331031146/http://www.americanheart.org/presenter.jhtml?identifier=4456 |archivedate=2008-03-31}}</ref> 每天服用40 mg的阿司匹林能显著抑制血栓素A2的最大急性合成量，但不影响前列腺素I2的合成，不过服用剂量更高时就会抑制前列腺素I2的合成。<ref>{{Cite journal |last1=Tohgi |first1=H |author2=S Konno, K Tamura, B Kimura and K Kawano|year = 1992|title = Effects of low-to-high doses of aspirin on platelet aggregability and metabolites of thromboxane A2 and prostacyclin|journal = Stroke| volume = 23|pages = 1400–1403 |pmid=1412574|issue = 10| doi=10.1161/01.STR.23.10.1400}}</ref>

前列腺素是身体局部产生的一种[[激素|激素]]，它有多种作用，包括在[[下丘脑|下丘脑]]中调节体温<ref name="VealeCooper1977">{{cite journal|last1=Veale|first1=W. L.|last2=Cooper|first2=K. E.|last3=Pittman|first3=Q. J.|title=Role of Prostaglandins in Fever and Temperature Regulation|year=1977|pages=145–167|doi=10.1007/978-1-4615-8055-3_6|PMC=3081099}}</ref>
，将痛觉传递到大脑，还会引起炎症<ref name="RicciottiFitzGerald2011">{{cite journal|last1=Ricciotti|first1=E.|last2=FitzGerald|first2=G. A.|title=Prostaglandins and Inflammation|journal=Arteriosclerosis, Thrombosis, and Vascular Biology|volume=31|issue=5|year=2011|pages=986–1000|issn=1079-5642|doi=10.1161/ATVBAHA.110.207449}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|title = Rubin's Pathology 6th edition|last = Rumin|first = Raphel|publisher = Wolters Kluwer|year = 2011|isbn = 978-1-4511-0912-2|location = USA|pages = 59}}</ref>。血栓素会使血小板聚集形成[[血栓|血栓]]，而心肌梗死主要是由血栓导致的，因此低剂量服用阿司匹林能有效防止心肌梗死<ref name="BNF" />。

===对COX-1和COX-2的抑制===
阿司匹林可以抑制环氧化酶-1（COX-1）和环氧化酶-2（COX-2）。它能不可逆地抑制COX-1并且改变COX-2的酶活性。COX-2通常产生的大多是会促进发炎的{{link-en|前列腺素类激素|Prostanoid}}，但受阿司匹林作用后则产生能抗炎的{{link-en|脂氧素|lipoxin}}。新一代非甾体抗炎药——昔布类{{link-en|COX-2抑制剂|COX-2 inhibitor}}——可以单独抑制COX-2，以减少对胃肠道的副作用。<ref>{{cite journal|
volume=2|issue=3|title=Pharmacokinetic Solubility And Dissolution Profile of Non-Steroidal Anti-Inflammatory Drugs|author=Achhrish goel, Ruchi gupta Anubhav goswami Madhu soodan sharma Yogesh sharma|year=2011}}</ref><ref name="COX2002" />

然而许多新一代的COX-2抑制剂如[[罗非昔布|罗非昔布]]在过去十年内都遭到了撤回，因为有证据表明它们会增加患心脏病和中风的风险。人体的血管内皮细胞原本会合成COX-2。选择性抑制COX-2后，因为血小板里的COX-1未受影响，前列腺素（尤其是[[前列环素|前列环素]]PGI2）的合成相比血栓素会有所降低。这样PGI2抗凝血的保护作用就消失了，这会增加血栓、心肌梗死和其他相关的循环系统疾病的风险。因为血小板没有DNA，它的COX-1若被阿司匹林不可逆地抑制，就无法再生，这是和昔布类可逆抑制剂的不同之处。<ref>{{cite journal |journal=Curr Pharm Des |year=2007 |volume=13 |issue=22 |pages=2215–27 |title=Mechanisms underlying the cardiovascular effects of COX-inhibition: benefits and risks |author=Martínez-González J, Badimon L |pmid=17691994 |doi=10.2174/138161207781368774|last2=Badimon }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |journal=J Cardiovasc Pharmacol |title=COX-2 inhibitors and cardiovascular risk |author=Funk CD, FitzGerald GA |date=November 2007 |volume=50 |issue=5 |pages=470–9 |pmid=18030055 |doi=10.1097/FJC.0b013e318157f72d|last2=Fitzgerald }}</ref>

此外，阿斯匹靈除了有抑制COX-2的環氧化能力之外，還能將其轉化為類似[[脂加氧酶|脂加氧酶]]的酵素。被阿斯匹靈處理過後的COX-2可以將多種[[多元不飽和脂肪|多元不飽和脂肪酸]]轉為過氧化物，這些過氧化物又會被代謝為具有抗發炎活性的{{Le|特異性促修復介質|specialized proresolving mediators}}，如{{Le|脂氧素|lipoxin}}、{{Le|消散素|resolvins}}、{{Le|巨噬細胞消炎介質|maresin}}等等。<ref name="pmid25895638">{{cite journal|title=Lipoxins and aspirin-triggered lipoxins in resolution of inflammation|url=|journal=[[European_Journal_of_Pharmacology|European Journal of Pharmacology]]|issue=|doi=10.1016/j.ejphar.2015.03.083|year=2015|volume=760|pages=49–63|pmid=25895638|vauthors=Romano M, Cianci E, Simiele F, Recchiuti A}}</ref><ref name="pmid23747022">{{cite journal|title=Resolution phase lipid mediators of inflammation: agonists of resolution|url=|journal=[[Current_Opinion_in_Pharmacology|Current Opinion in Pharmacology]]|issue=4|doi=10.1016/j.coph.2013.05.012|year=2013|volume=13|pages=632–40|pmc=3732499|pmid=23747022|vauthors=Serhan CN, Chiang N}}</ref><ref name="pmid26546723">{{cite journal|title=Docosapentaenoic acid derived metabolites and mediators - The new world of lipid mediator medicine in a nutshell|journal=[[European_Journal_of_Pharmacology|European Journal of Pharmacology]]|issue=|doi=10.1016/j.ejphar.2015.11.002|year=2015|volume=785|pages=108–115|pmid=26546723|vauthors=Weylandt KH}}</ref>

===其他机理===

==药剂学==

[[Image:5_grain_aspirin.jpg|thumb]]

一般来说，成人用于治疗发烧或关节炎时每天服用四次，<ref name=BNF>{{Cite book|title={{le|英國國家處方集|British National Formulary}}|edition=45|year=2003|publisher= [[British_Medical_Journal|British Medical Journal]] and {{le|英國皇家藥劑師協會|Royal Pharmaceutical Society of Great Britain}}}}</ref> 这和以前治疗风湿热时所用的剂量接近。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.medscape.com/druginfo/monograph?cid=med&drugid=3881&drugname=Aspirin+EC+Oral&monotype=monograph |title=Aspirin monograph: dosages, etc |publisher=Medscape.com |accessdate=2011-05-11}}</ref> 有或怀疑有冠状动脉病史的人要预防[[心肌梗塞|心肌梗塞]]（MI），每天低剂量服用一次即可。<ref name=BNF />

USPSTF在2009年3月向45-79岁的男性和55-79岁的女性建议，如果阿司匹林降低男性心肌梗死和女性中风的风险所带来的潜在效益要大于引起消化道出血的潜在危害，那么就提倡服用该药以预防冠状动脉心脏疾病。<ref name=medscape>{{cite web|url=http://cme.medscape.com/viewarticle/589895?src=cmemp |title=Aspirin: More Evidence That Low Dose Is All That Is Needed (from Medscape) |publisher=Cme.medscape.com |accessdate=2011-05-11}}</ref> WHI的研究表明女性如果坚持低剂量（75毫克或81毫克）服用，死于心血管疾病的风险就会降低25%，总死亡率降低14%。<ref name=medscape /> 低剂量服用阿司匹林（每天75毫克或81毫克）也和心血管疾病发病率降低有关，长期服用以预防疾病的患者利用这种方式可以兼顾药物的有效性和安全性。<ref name=medscape />

儿童服用阿司匹林治疗[[川崎病|川崎病]]时，服用剂量和体重相关，头两周每天服用四次，接下来六至八周降低剂量，每天服用一次。<ref>{{Cite book|title={{le|英國國家處方集兒童版|British National Formulary for Children|British National Formulary for Children}}|year=2006|publisher= [[British_Medical_Journal|British Medical Journal]] and {{le|英國皇家藥劑師協會|Royal Pharmaceutical Society|Royal Pharmaceutical Society}}}}</ref>

==药代动力学==
乙酰水杨酸是一种[[弱酸|弱酸]]，口服后在[[胃|胃]]的[[胃酸|酸性环境]]中几乎不[[电离|电离]]，而是迅速经[[细胞膜|细胞膜]]吸收。[[小肠|小肠]]中较高的[[pH|pH]]促进了药物的电离，从而减缓了药物在小肠中的吸收。过量服用时药物会凝结，所以吸收更慢，血浆浓度在服用后24小时内都会上升。<ref name="'RK Ferguson'">{{Cite journal|title=Death following self-poisoning with aspirin|journal=Journal of the American Medical Association|date=1970-08-17|first1=RK|last1=Ferguson|author2=Boutros, AR|volume=213|issue=7|pages=1186–8|pmid=5468267|doi=10.1001/jama.213.7.1186}}</ref><ref name="'FL Kaufman'">{{Cite journal|title=Darvon poisoning with delayed salicylism: a case report|journal=Pediatrics|date=April 1970|first1=FL|last1=Kaufman|author2=Dubansky, AS|volume=49|issue=4|pages=610–1|pmid=5013423}}</ref><ref name="G Levy">{{Cite journal |doi=10.1056/NEJM197208312870903|title=Salicylate accumulation kinetics in man |journal=New England Journal of Medicine |date=1972-08-31 |first1=G |last1=Levy |author2=Tsuchiya, T |volume=287 |issue=9 |pages=430–2 |pmid=5044917}}</ref>

血液中的水杨酸有50–80%与[[白蛋白|白蛋白]]结合，其余是具有活性的电离态。药物和蛋白质的结合和浓度有关。结合位点饱和以后游离态的水杨酸就会增加，其毒性也会增强。药物的[[分布体积|分布体积]]是0.1–0.2升每千克。酸中毒会增强水杨酸向组织中的渗透，从而增加药物的分布体积。<ref name="G Levy"/>

若按治疗剂量服用，则有多达80%的水杨酸在[[肝脏|肝脏]]中[[代谢|代谢]]。它和[[甘氨酸|甘氨酸]]反应生成{{link-en|水杨酰胺乙酸|salicyluric acid}}，但这种[[代谢途径|代谢途径]]容量有限。少量水杨酸也会羟基化形成[[龙胆酸|龙胆酸]]。大剂量服用时，药物代谢从一级反应变为零级反应，因为代谢途径已饱和，[[肾脏|肾脏]]的排出变得更加重要。<ref name="G Levy"/>

水杨酸主要通过肾脏作为水杨酰胺乙酸（75%）、游离水杨酸（10%）、水杨酸苯酚（10%）、酰基葡萄糖醛酸苷（5%）、[[龙胆酸|龙胆酸]]（< 1%）、{{link-en|2,3-二羟基苯甲酸|2,3-Dihydroxybenzoic acid}}排泄。<ref>2,3-Dihydroxybenzoic acid is a product of human aspirin metabolism. Martin Grootveld and Barry Halliwell, Biochemical Pharmacology, Volume 37, Issue 2, 15 January 1988, pages 271–280, {{doi|10.1016/0006-2952(88)90729-0}}</ref>当摄入低剂量时（小于250mg，成人），所有途径都通过一级动力学，[[消除半衰期|消除半衰期]]约为2.0至4.5小时。<ref name="'O Hartwig'">{{Cite journal|title=Pharmacokinetic considerations of common analgesics and antipyretics|journal=American Journal of Medicine|date=1983-11-14|first=Otto H|last=Hartwig|volume=75|issue=5A|pages=30–7|pmid=6606362|doi=10.1016/0002-9343(83)90230-9}}</ref><ref name="'AK Done'">{{Cite journal|title=Salicylate intoxication. Significance of measurements of salicylate in blood in cases of acute ingestion|journal=Pediatrics|date=November 1960|first=AK|last=Done|volume=26|pages=800–7|pmid=13723722}}</ref> 当摄入高剂量水杨酸时（大于4000mg），半衰期会延长至15-30小时，<ref name="Chyka2007">{{Cite journal|author=Chyka PA, Erdman AR, Christianson G, Wax PM, Booze LL, Manoguerra AS, Caravati EM, Nelson LS, Olson KR, Cobaugh DJ, Scharman EJ, Woolf AD, Troutman WG; Americal Association of Poison Control Centers; Healthcare Systems Bureau, Health Resources and Services Administration, Department of Health and Human Services. |title=Salicylate poisoning: an evidence-based consensus guideline for out-of-hospital management |journal=Clin Toxicol (Phila) |volume=45 |issue=2 |pages=95–131 |year=2007 |pmid=17364628 |doi=10.1080/15563650600907140}}</ref>因为水杨酰胺乙酸和水杨酚醛葡糖苷酸的生物转化途径已饱和。<ref>{{Cite journal|author=Prescott LF, Balali-Mood M, Critchley JA, Johnstone AF, Proudfoot AT |title=Diuresis or urinary alkalinisation for salicylate poisoning? |journal=Br Med J (Clin Res Ed) |volume=285 |issue=6352 |pages=1383–6 |year=1982 |pmid=6291695 |pmc=1500395 |doi=10.1136/bmj.285.6352.1383|last2=Balali-Mood |last3=Critchley |last4=Johnstone |last5=Proudfoot }}</ref> 代谢途径的饱和使得肾脏对水杨酸的排泄更加重要，而[[尿液|尿液]][[酸碱度|酸碱度]]对其影响也更为敏感。当尿液的pH值从5升至8时，肾脏对水杨酸的清除能力会提升10-20倍。通过碱化尿液来增加水杨酸的清除率便是利用了这一点。<ref name="EmergMed2002-Dargan">{{Cite journal|author=Dargan PI, Wallace CI, Jones AL.|title=An evidenced based flowchart to guide the management of acute salicylate (aspirin) overdose|journal=Emerg Med J|year=2002|pages=206–9|volume=19|issue=3|pmid= 11971828 |doi=10.1136/emj.19.3.206|pmc=1725844|last2=Wallace|last3=Jones}}</ref>

==历史==
[[File:Aspirine-1923.jpg|thumb]]
自古以来，人们就知道含有活性成分水杨酸的植物提取物（如[[柳树|柳树]][[树皮|皮]]和[[绣线菊属|绣线菊属]]植物）能够镇痛、退烧。[[希波克拉底|希波克拉底]]（约前460年–前377年）留下的历史记录就描述了柳树的树皮和树叶磨成的粉能够缓解以上症状。<ref>{{cite web|author=Mary Bellis |url=http://inventors.about.com/library/inventors/blaspirin.htm |title=History of aspirin |publisher=Inventors.about.com |date=2010-06-16 |accessdate=2011-05-11}}</ref>

1763年英国牧师{{link-en|爱德华·斯通|Edward Stone (cleric)}}在牛津发现阿司匹林的活性成分水杨酸。法国化学家{{link-en|查尔斯·弗雷德里克·格哈特|Charles Frédéric Gerhardt}}首先于1853年合成了乙酰水杨酸。他在制取和研究各种[[酸酐|酸酐]]的性质时，把[[乙酰氯|乙酰氯]]和[[水杨酸钠|水杨酸钠]]混合，二者发生剧烈反应，熔化后又很快凝固了。因为当时还没有{{link-en|分子结构理论|Structural theory}}，格哈特把所得的化合物称为“水杨酸乙酸酐”（''wasserfreie Salicylsäure-Essigsäure''）。他为撰写关于酸酐的论文进行了很多反应，这个制备阿司匹林的反应只是其中之一，后来他也没有进一步研究。<ref name=gerhardt>{{Cite journal|author=Gerhardt, Ch.|title=Untersuchungen über die wasserfreien organischen Säuren |trans_title=Investigations into anhydrous organic acids|journal=Annalen der Chemie und Pharmacie|volume=87|pages=149–179|year=1853 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Wus2AAAAYAAJ&pg=PA149#v=onepage&q&f=false|doi=10.1002/jlac.18530870107|language=de}}  特别是162–163页.</ref>

[[File:BayerHeroin.png|thumb]]、赖塞托和{{link-en|醋氨沙洛|Salophen}}的广告]]
六年之后的1859年，冯·基尔姆让水杨酸和乙酰氯反应，制得了分析纯的乙酰水杨酸，他称之为“乙酰化水杨酸”（'' acetylierte Salicylsäure''）<ref name=gilm>{{Cite journal|author=von Gilm H|title=Acetylderivate der Phloretin- und Salicylsäure|trans_title=Acetyl derivatives of phloretic and salicylic acids|journal=Annalen der Chemie und Pharmacie|volume=112|issue=2|pages=180–182|year=1859|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=NYs8AAAAIAAJ&pg=RA1-PA180#v=onepage&q&f=false|doi=10.1002/jlac.18591120207|language=de}}</ref> 1869年，施罗德、普林兹霍恩和克劳特重复了格哈特（利用水杨酸钠）的和基尔姆（利用水杨酸）的合成方式，结果证实两个反应产物相同——乙酰水杨酸。他们第一次确定了产物的正确结构——乙酰基和酚基上的氧相连。<ref>{{Cite journal|author= Schröder, Prinzhorn, Kraut K|title=Ueber Salicylverbindungen|trans_title=On compounds of salicylic acid|journal=Annalen der Chemie und Pharmacie|volume=150 |issue=1 |pages=1–20 |year=1869|url=http://babel.hathitrust.org/cgi/pt?id=uva.x002457978;view=1up;seq=399|doi=10.1002/jlac.18691500102|last2=Prinzhorn|last3=Kraut|language=de}}特别是9–13页，乙酰水杨酸的结构在12页上。</ref>

1897年拜耳公司的化学家把[[旋果蚊子草|旋果蚊子草]]（''Filipendula ulmaria''）合成的[[水杨苷|水杨苷]]经过修饰后合成了一种药物，它比纯净的水杨酸对消化道刺激更小。这个项目由哪个化学家领衔存在争议。拜耳说合成是由[[费利克斯·霍夫曼|费利克斯·霍夫曼]]完成的，但后来犹太化学家{{link-en|阿瑟·艾兴格林|Arthur Eichengrün}}声称他才是首席研究员，而他的贡献记录被[[納粹德國|纳粹]]政权抹去了。<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Mahdi|first1=JG|author2=Mahdi, AJ, Mahdi, AJ, Bowen, ID|title=The historical analysis of aspirin discovery, its relation to the willow tree and antiproliferative and anticancer potential|journal=Cell proliferation|date=April 2006|volume=39|issue=2|pages=147–55|pmid=16542349|doi=10.1111/j.1365-2184.2006.00377.x}}</ref><ref name="Distillations">{{cite journal |last1=Dick |first1=Brian |title=Hard Work and Happenstance |url=https://www.sciencehistory.org/distillations/magazine/hard-work-and-happenstance |journal=Distillations |publisher=[[Science_History_Institute|Science History Institute]] |date=2018 |volume=4 |issue=1 |pages=44-45 |accessdate=2018-07-11 |author= |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180711234027/https://www.sciencehistory.org/distillations/magazine/hard-work-and-happenstance |archive-date=2018-07-11 |dead-url=no }}</ref>这种药学名叫乙酰水杨酸，拜耳公司把它称为阿司匹林（Aspirin），这来自于旋果蚊子草的植物名（''{{lang-la|Filipendula ulmaria}}''）。<ref>{{cite journal |last=Singer |first=H. |title=Ueber Aspirin |journal=Pflügers Archiv: European Journal of Physiology |volume=84 |issue=11–12 |year=1901 |pages=527–546 |doi=10.1007/BF01769129}}</ref> 到了1899年，拜耳已在全球市场销售此药。<ref name=Jeffreys_73>{{Harvnb|Jeffreys|2005|pp=73}}</ref> 二十世纪上半叶，阿司匹林越来越受欢迎，这是因为人们认为它在[[1918年流感大流行|1918年流感大流行]]中发挥了作用。然而最近的研究却显示，它也是流感致死率高的部分原因，不过这种说法颇受争议，未被广泛认可。<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Starko|first1=Karen M.|title = Salicylates and Pandemic Influenza Mortality, 1918–1919 Pharmacology, Pathology, and Historic Evidence |journal=Clinical Infectious Diseases|volume=49|year=2009|doi=10.1086/606060|issue=9|pmid=19788357|pages=1405–1410}}</ref> 阿司匹林带来的丰厚利润使药厂激烈竞争，该药的各种品牌和产品像雨后春笋般冒了出来，在1917年拜耳公司的美国专利过期了以后更是如此。<ref name=Jeffreys_136>{{Harvnb|Jeffreys|2005|pp=136–142, 151–152}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.history.com/this-day-in-history.do?action=VideoArticle&id=52415 |title=Bayer patents aspirin – This Day in History – 3/6/1899 |publisher=History.com |accessdate=2011-05-11 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20091008193942/http://www.history.com/this-day-in-history.do?action=VideoArticle&id=52415 |archivedate=2009-10-08 }}</ref>

[[对乙酰氨基酚|对乙酰氨基酚]]和[[布洛芬|布洛芬]]于1956年和1959年相继问世以后，阿司匹林的使用率开始下降。<ref name=Jeffreys_212>{{Harvnb|Jeffreys|2005|pp=212–217}}</ref>60和70年代，[[约翰·范恩|约翰·范恩]]等人发现了阿司匹林的作用机理，60至80年代的其他研究和临床试验证明该药有抗凝血的药效，可降低血栓疾病的发病率。<ref name=Jeffreys_226>{{Harvnb|Jeffreys|2005|pp=226–231}}</ref> 由于广泛用于预防心脏病和中风，阿司匹林的销量从20世纪末开始复苏，21世纪以来持续向好。<ref name=Jeffreys_267>{{Harvnb|Jeffreys|2005|pp=267–269}}</ref>

==兽用==
兽医有时用阿司匹林来镇痛或抗血栓，主要给狗用，有时给马用，不过现在一般会用副作用较少的新疗法<ref name="aavpt">{{cite web|author1=American Academy of Veterinary Pharmacology and Therapeutics |title=ASPIRIN Veterinary—Systemic |url=http://c.ymcdn.com/sites/www.aavpt.org/resource/resmgr/imported/aspirin.pdf |accessdate=2016-02-25 |archivedate=2016-02-25 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160225085340/http://c.ymcdn.com/sites/www.aavpt.org/resource/resmgr/imported/aspirin.pdf }}</ref>。

狗和马都会出现水杨酸产生的胃肠道副作用，不过阿司匹林可以用来治疗老年狗的[[关节炎|关节炎]]，也有治疗马的{{link-en|蹄叶炎|laminitis}}的可能。<ref>{{cite web|last = Crosby|first = Janet Tobiassen|title = Veterinary Questions and Answers|publisher = About.com|year = 2006|url = http://vetmedicine.about.com/cs/altvetmedgeneral/a/dogcataspirin.htm|accessdate =2007-09-05| archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20070908151020/http://vetmedicine.about.com/cs/altvetmedgeneral/a/dogcataspirin.htm| archivedate= 2007-09-08 | deadurl= no}}</ref><ref name="CambridgeH">{{Cite journal|author=Cambridge H, Lees P, Hooke RE, Russell CS|title=Antithrombotic actions of aspirin in the horse |journal=Equine Vet J |volume=23 |issue=2 |pages=123–7 |year=1991 |pmid=1904347|doi=10.1111/j.2042-3306.1991.tb02736.x|last2=Lees |last3=Hooke |last4=Russell }}</ref> 不过现在该药已很少用于治疗蹄叶炎，因为可能适得其反。阿司匹林应该只在[[兽医|兽医]]的直接指导下使用，特别是猫，因其缺乏有助于药物排出的[[葡萄糖醛酸|葡萄糖醛酸]]，用药比较危险。<ref>{{cite book|editor=Lappin, Michael R.|title=Feline internal medicine secrets|year=2001|publisher=Hanley & Belfus|location=Philadelphia|isbn=1-56053-461-3|page=160}}</ref> 连续4周每48小时给猫服用25毫克/千克体重的阿司匹林并不产生临床中毒症状。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.merckmanuals.com/vet/toxicology/toxicities_from_human_drugs/analgesics_toxicity.html|title=Analgesics (Toxicity): Toxicities from Human Drugs: Merck Veterinary Manual|accessdate=2015-08-24|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150411095033/http://www.merckmanuals.com/vet/toxicology/toxicities_from_human_drugs/analgesics_toxicity.html|archive-date=2015-04-11|dead-url=no}}</ref> 推荐用于猫的解热镇痛剂量是每48小时10毫克/千克体重。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ansci.cornell.edu/plants/toxcat/toxcat.html|title=Plants Poisonous to Livestock - Cornell University Department of Animal Science|accessdate=2015-08-24|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150816192109/http://www.ansci.cornell.edu/plants/toxcat/toxcat.html|archive-date=2015-08-16|dead-url=no}}</ref>
