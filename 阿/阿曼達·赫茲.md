'''阿曼達·藍道夫·赫茲'''（{{lang-en|Amanda Randolph Hearst}}，{{bd|1984年|1月5日|catIdx=Hearst, Amanda}}）是一位[[美國|美國]][[社交名媛|社交名媛]]、[[行動主義|行動主義者]]、<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.prabalgurung.com/the-journal-detail.php?blogid=325 |title=Stylish Professional Series : Amanda Hearst |publisher=Prabal Gurung |date=2011-06-09 |accessdate=2013-10-20 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141214103557/http://www.prabalgurung.com/the-journal-detail.php?blogid=325 |archivedate=2014-12-14 }}</ref>[[時尚模特兒|時尚模特兒]]及[[威廉·藍道夫·赫茲|威廉·藍道夫·赫茲]]的媒體企業集團[[赫茲國際集團|赫茲國際集團]]的繼承人。她是《[[美麗佳人|美麗佳人]]》的副市場編輯，<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2012/04/05/fashion/amanda-hearst-works-at-marie-claire-and-also-fights-unhealthy-puppy-mills.html|title=Amanda Hearst Works at Marie Claire and Also Fights Unhealthy Puppy Mills|newspaper=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|accessdate=2013-10-20}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=|url=http://www.humanesociety.org/about/events/friendsoffinn/amanda_hearst_friends_of_finn.html|title=Meet Amanda Hearst, Founder and Chair of Friends of Finn : The Humane Society of the United States|publisher=Humanesociety.org|date=|accessdate=2013-10-20}}{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>及致力於停止對小狗不人道待遇的組織Friends of Finn的創辦人。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.humanesociety.org/about/events/friendsoffinn/about_friends_of_finn.html |title=Friends of Finn Members : The Humane Society of the United States |publisher=Humanesociety.org |date= |accessdate=2013-10-20 }}{{dead link|date=十一月 2017 |bot=InternetArchiveBot }}</ref>她同時也是河流保護組織{{le|Riverkeeper|Riverkeeper}}的少年理事會的聯席主席。<ref name="riverkeeper1">{{cite web |url=http://www.riverkeeper.org/about-us/our-board/amanda-hearst/ |title=Amanda Hearst |publisher=Riverkeeper |date= |accessdate=2013-10-20 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131021055342/http://www.riverkeeper.org/about-us/our-board/amanda-hearst/ |archivedate=2013-10-21 }}</ref>

==家族與早年生活==
她是[[安妮·赫茲|安妮·赫茲]]的女兒、[[帕蒂·赫茲|帕蒂·赫茲]]的外甥女及媒體大亨[[威廉·藍道夫·赫茲|威廉·藍道夫·赫茲]]的曾孫女。她的父親理查德·麥克切斯尼（Richard McChesney）在赫茲出生前與她的母親分居。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.style.com/w/feat_story/051706/full_page.html|title=A League of Their Own|accessdate=2007-06-22|last=Lau|first=Venessa|date=2006|work=Style.com|publisher=CondéNet, Inc.|quote=Patty's sister, Anne Hearst, separated from Amanda's father, Richard McChesney, before Amanda was born.|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20060619115230/http://www.style.com/w/feat_story/051706/full_page.html|archivedate=2006-06-19|deadurl=yes}}</ref>她亦是小說家[[傑·麥克倫尼|傑·麥克倫尼]]的繼女，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.vanityfair.com/society/features/2010/08/summer-hamptons-style-portfolio-201008 |title=Photos: Behind the Hamptons Hedges |publisher=Vanity Fair |date= |accessdate=2013-10-20}}</ref>及[[莉蒂亞·赫斯特-蕭|莉蒂亞·赫斯特-蕭]]的表姊。

赫茲進入紐約的{{le|沙潘學校|Chapin School}}就讀，之後從[[康乃狄克州|康乃狄克州]][[沃靈福德_(康乃狄克州)|沃靈福德]]的寄宿學校{{le|喬特羅斯瑪麗中學|Choate Rosemary Hall}}畢業。<ref name="VanityFair">{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.vanityfair.com/style/features/2009/06/next-gen-portfolio200906#slide=18 |title=Photos: Portraits of 'Fortune's Children' |publisher=Vanity Fair |date= |accessdate=2013-10-20}}</ref>她在短暫就讀於[[波士頓學院|波士頓學院]]後，中途輟學以追尋模特兒生涯。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://fashion.telegraph.co.uk/news-features/TMG3361847/Amanda-Hearst-heiress-not-in-scandal-shock.html |title=Amanda Hearst: heiress not in scandal shock - Telegraph |publisher=Fashion.telegraph.co.uk |date= |accessdate=2013-10-20}}</ref>她最終被說服重返校園，進入[[福坦莫大學|福坦莫大學]]就讀，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://fordhamnotes.blogspot.in/2011/02/aileyfordham-bfa-in-dance-performance.html |title=Fordham Notes: Ailey/Fordham B.F.A. in Dance Performance Benefit |publisher=Fordhamnotes.blogspot.in |date=2011-02-14 |accessdate=2013-10-20}}</ref>在2008年以[[藝術歷史|藝術歷史]][[文學士|文學士]]學位畢業。<ref name="VanityFair"/><ref name="riverkeeper1"/>

==模特兒生涯==
赫茲身為前{{le|IMG Models|IMG Models|IMG模特兒}}，曾登上《{{le|Town & Country|Town & Country}}》、《[[哈潑時尚|哈潑時尚]]》、《[[柯夢波丹|柯夢波丹]]》等主流雜誌封面，且她也曾登上《[[浮華世界|浮華世界]]》。<ref name="riverkeeper1"/>

赫茲在2012年為[[莉雅·琦比德|莉雅·琦比德]]所設計的道德時尚新系列擔任模特兒。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/femail/article-2205254/Heiress-Amanda-Hearst-steps-camera-pose-supermodel-Liya-Kebedes-ethical-fashion-line.html#ixzz27oBZxUsB |title=Heiress Amanda Hearst steps back in front of the camera to pose for supermodel Liya Kebede's ethical fashion line | Mail Online |publisher=Dailymail.co.uk |date=2012-09-19 |accessdate=2013-10-20}}</ref>
