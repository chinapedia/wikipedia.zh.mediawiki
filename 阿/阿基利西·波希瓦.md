'''撒美拉·阿基利西·波希瓦'''（{{lang-en|Samiuela 'Akilisi Pōhiva}}；{{bd|1941年|4月7日|}}），是一名[[湯加|湯加]]民主活動家和政治人物、{{le|友好群島民主黨|Democratic Party of the Friendly Islands}}黨魁。2014年起擔任[[汤加首相|汤加首相]]，是當地第一位[[平民|平民]]首相<ref name=tdnews>{{cite news |first=Iliesa |last=Tora |title=Dawn of a New Era: Pohiva is the first elected commoner to be PM |url=http://www.tongadailynews.to/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/TDN92-PG1-eng.pdf |work=[[Tonga_Daily_News|Tonga Daily News]] |date=2014-12-31 |accessdate=2015-02-09 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150210045203/http://www.tongadailynews.to/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/TDN92-PG1-eng.pdf |archivedate=2015-02-10 }}</ref><ref name=abcnews>{{cite news|first=|last=|title=Tongan democracy activist becomes first commoner elected as PM |url=http://www.abc.net.au/news/2014-12-29/tongan-democracy-activist-becomes-first-commoner-elected-pm/5991824 |work=[[ABC_News_(Australia)|ABC News (Australia)]] |date=2014-12-29 |accessdate=2015-02-09}}</ref>。

波希瓦也是[[汤加立法会|汤加立法会]]年資最高的議員，自1987年出任<ref>According to his [http://parliament.gov.to/people's_reps.htm profile] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080821115924/http://parliament.gov.to/people%27s_reps.htm |date=2008-08-21 }} at the Tongan Parliament he had served 18 consecutive years when re-elected in 2005.</ref>。
