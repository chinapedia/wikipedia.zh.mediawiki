'''阿博布羅西''' ( Agbogbloshie ) 是[[加纳|加纳]][[阿克拉|阿克拉市]]的边郊城镇，作为工业国家合法或非法出口并倾倒[[电子垃圾|电子垃圾]]的目标地区而出名。每年有数百万吨的电子垃圾在这儿被处理，故阿博布羅西也被称为“数字填埋场”。<ref name="r4">{{cite web | url=http://www.pbs.org/frontlineworld/stories/ghana804/video/video_index.html | title=World Ghana: Digital Dumping Ground | publisher=PBS | date=2011-02-04 | accessdate=2014-02-13}}</ref><ref name="r3" />

整个城镇大约占地四亩，坐落于库尔勒澙湖湖岸，位于阿克拉中央商务区的西北部。<ref name=r1>{{cite web|last=Safo |first=Amos |title=End of the Road for 'Sodom and Gomorrah' Squatters. |url=http://www.newsfromafrica.org/newsfromafrica/articles/art_827.html |publisher=News From Africa |accessdate=15 March 2011|deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110718110241/http://www.newsfromafrica.org/newsfromafrica/articles/art_827.html |archivedate=18 July 2011 }}</ref><ref name=r2>{{cite web|last=Afenah|first=Afia|title=(Re)claiming Citizenship Rights in Accra, Ghana.|url=http://base.d-p-h.info/es/fiches/dph/fiche-dph-8430.html|publisher=DPH. HIC (Habitat International Coalition)|accessdate=15 March 2011}}</ref>这儿居住有约40,000名加纳人，其中大部分是从农村地区移民而来。<ref name="r3">{{cite web | url=http://news.peacefmonline.com/pages/news/200909/25988.php | title=Time up for Sodom and Gomorrah | date=2009-09-04 | accessdate=2014-02-13 | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140221235825/http://news.peacefmonline.com/pages/news/200909/25988.php | archivedate=2014-02-21 }}</ref><ref name="r1"/> 由于这儿恶劣的生活条件和极高的犯罪率，该地区被戏称为[[所多玛与蛾摩拉|所多玛与蛾摩拉]]<ref name="r0">{{cite news|last=Hugo|first=Pieter|title=Ghana's e-Waste Dump Seeps Poison|url=http://www.newsweek.com/2011/07/24/ghana-s-e-waste-dump-seeps-poison.html|newspaper=Newsweek|date=July 24, 2011|accessdate=2014-02-13}}</ref>

[[巴塞尔公约|巴塞尔公约]]禁止从发达国家向欠发达国家运输有害废弃物。目前只有3个国家尚未签署该公约，包括[[美国|美国]]、[[阿富汗|阿富汗]]、[[海地|海地]]<ref>{{cite news|last=Claiborne|first=Ron|date=2 August 2009|url=http://abcnews.go.com/GMA/Weekend/story?id=8215714&page=1|title=U.S. Electronic Waste Gets Sent to Africa|work=ABC News}}</ref>。每个月都会有从世界各国，包括[[美国|美国]]<ref name="r5">{{cite web | url=http://www.ghanaweb.com/GhanaHomePage/NewsArchive/artikel.php?ID=188950 | title=Electronic Waste Dumping on Ghana Still Continues | publisher=GhanaWeb | date=25 Aug 2010 | accessdate=15 Mar 2011}}</ref> 、英国<ref>{{cite news|date=16 May 2011|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/panorama/hi/front_page/newsid_9483000/9483148.stm|title=Britain's e-waste illegally leaking into West Africa |work=BBC News}}</ref>及[[日本|日本]]，非法运输来的货柜到达阿博布羅西。那些不受保护的工人们，其中很多是年轻的孩子，花费一整天的时间在其中寻找金属用于出售，通常是通过燃烧电器元件并用他们的双手拆除。在这个过程中，他们每天最多可以挣得八到十加纳赛地（4到6[[美金|美金]]左右）。<ref>{{cite news|date=20 August 2008|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/earth/earthnews/3349945/Health-risk-for-Ghana-children-from-electronic-goods-recycling.html|title=Health risk for Ghana children from electronic goods recycling|work=The Telegraph}}</ref>

这些废弃物的处理将大量有毒化学物质排放到[[空气|空气]]、[[土壤|土壤]]及水源之中。这些有毒物质会影响[[生殖系统|生殖系统]]、[[神经系统|神经系统]]及[[大脑|大脑]]的发育，尤其是对儿童伤害更为严重。随着该地区持续不断的被污染，对人类身体健康及阿博布羅西地区环境的担忧逐渐增加<ref name="r1"/><ref name="r6">[http://www.greenpeace.org/international/en/news/features/poisoning-the-poor-electroni/ "Poisoning the Poor – Electronic Waste in Ghana | Greenpeace International."] Inspiring Action for a Green and Peaceful Future | Greenpeace USA. 5 Aug. 2008. Web. 15 Mar. 2011.</ref><ref name="r7">Dogbevi, Emmanuel K. [http://www.ghanabusinessnews.com/2010/06/07/e-waste-in-ghana-–-how-many-children-are-dying-from-lead-poisoning/ "E-waste in Ghana – How Many Children Are Dying from Lead Poisoning?"] Ghana Business News. 7 June 2010. Web. 15 Mar. 2011.</ref>。在20世纪初，加纳政府投入资助及贷款，开始实行库尔勒泻湖生态恢复工程（KLERP），一项环境整治及恢复项目。该项目将会通过疏浚湖泊和奥道运河来尽力解决排水入海的问题。
