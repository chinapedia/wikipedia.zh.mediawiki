18世纪前，现代[[阿富汗|阿富汗]][[国家|国家]]所控制的区域是分裂的，多数地区是被的[[印度|印度]]的[[德里苏丹国|德里苏丹国]]统治。西部的[[赫拉特|赫拉特]]属于[[波斯|波斯]]的[[萨非王朝|萨非王朝]]，北部的[[马扎里沙里夫|马扎里沙里夫]]属于[[布哈拉汗国|布哈拉汗国]]。南部的[[坎大哈|坎大哈]]是[[莫卧儿帝国|莫卧儿帝国]]和萨非王朝的争夺区。<ref>{{Cite book|title=A Historical Atlas of Afghanistan|last1=Romano|first1=Amy|authorlink=|coauthors=|volume=|year=2003|publisher=The Rosen Publishing Group|location=|isbn=0823938638|page=28|pages=64|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=TKUxyVCrYn0C&lpg=PP1&pg=PA28#v=onepage&q&f=false|accessdate=2010-10-17}}</ref>被压迫的现代阿富汗人（[[普什图族|普什图族]]）多次寻求独立，都没能成功。<ref name="Otfinoski"/> 

1709年，Mirwais Hotak在坎大哈带领阿富汗人脱离了波斯的统治。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://persian.packhum.org/persian/pf?file=90001014&ct=29|title=An Outline Of The History Of Persia During The Last Two Centuries (A.D. 1722-1922)|page=29|work=Edward Granville Browne|publisher=Packard Humanities Institute|location=London|accessdate=2010-09-24}}</ref> Mirwais建立城堡将坎大哈作为国家的首都。<ref name="Otfinoski">{{Cite book|title=Afghanistan|last1=Otfinoski|first1=Steven|authorlink=|coauthors=|volume=|year=2004|publisher=Infobase Publishing|location=|isbn=0816050562|page=7|pages=130|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=fbXmk-EauHIC&lpg=PP1&pg=PA7#v=onepage&q&f=false|accessdate=2010-09-27}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|title=History of Afghanistan, from the Earliest Period to the Outbreak of the War of 1878|last1=Malleson|first1=George Bruce|authorlink=|coauthors=|volume=|year=1878|publisher=Elibron.com|location=London|isbn=1402172788|page=227|pages=|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=pqNGBEmHUd4C&lpg=PP1&pg=PA227#v=onepage&q&f=false|accessdate=2010-09-27}}</ref>一系列战争后，阿富汗在1747年由[[艾哈迈德沙·杜兰尼|艾哈迈德沙·杜兰尼]]建立[[杜兰尼王朝|杜兰尼王朝]]。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cia.gov/library/publications/the-world-factbook/fields/2028.html?countryName=Afghanistan&countryCode=af&regionCode=sas&#af|title=Afghanistan|quote=''Ahmad Shah DURRANI unified the Pashtun tribes and founded Afghanistan in 1747.''|work=[[The_World_Factbook|The World Factbook]]|publisher=[[中央情报局|Central Intelligence Agency]]|accessdate=2010-10-23}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/7798/Afghanistan/21392/Last-Afghan-empire |title=Last Afghan empire |accessdate=2010-08-22 |work=Louis Dupree, Nancy Hatch Dupree and others |publisher=[[Encyclopædia_Britannica|Encyclopædia Britannica]] Online Version|accessdate=2010-09-24}}</ref> 艾哈迈德沙·杜兰尼被视为阿富汗的[[国父|国父]]，<ref>{{Cite book|title=Ahmad Shah Durrani: Father of Modern Afghanistan |last1=Ganḍā |first1=Singh |authorlink=|coauthors=|volume=|year=1959|publisher=Asia Pub.
