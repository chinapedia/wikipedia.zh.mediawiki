'''阿諾德·帕爾默邀請賽'''（Arnold Palmer Invitational）是美國高爾夫球[[PGA巡迴賽|PGA巡迴賽]]的賽事之一，創建於1979年。萬事達卡是該賽事的贊助商之一<ref>{{cite pressrelease |url=http://newsroom.mastercard.com/press-releases/mastercard-extends-presenting-sponsorship-of-arnold-palmer-invitational/ |title=MasterCard Extends Presenting Sponsorship of Arnold Palmer Invitational |work=MasterCard.com |date=March 21, 2012 |accessdate=March 26, 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.arnoldpalmerinvitational.com/content/news/2012/03/mastercard_extends_sponsorship.aspx |title=MasterCard Extends Sponsorship |work=ArnoldPalmerInvitational.com |date=March 21, 2012 |accessdate=March 26, 2012 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120407211623/http://www.arnoldpalmerinvitational.com/content/news/2012/03/mastercard_extends_sponsorship.aspx |archivedate=四月 7, 2012 }}</ref>。只有在前一年PGA巡迴賽獎金榜排名前70位的球員才有資格參加這項賽事<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.pgatour.com/news/2007/11/01/at_stake.html |title=What's at stake for 2008 |publisher=PGA Tour |date=November 1, 2007 |accessdate=March 18, 2013}}</ref>。
