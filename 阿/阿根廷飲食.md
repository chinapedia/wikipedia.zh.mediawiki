'''阿根廷飲食'''融合了印第安人和地中海的飲食文化。阿根廷畜牧產業發達，人均肉食消費量也非常高。阿根廷人均年牛肉消費量達100公斤<ref>''National Geographic Magazine''. March 1958.</ref>。由於阿根廷是一個移民國家，使得阿根廷飲食非常多樣化<ref name=gastronomy>{{cite web |url=http://www.argentina.ar/_en/tourism/gastronomy/index.php |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2016-02-26 |0=2012-03-23 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120323163849/http://www.argentina.ar/_en/tourism/gastronomy/index.php |archivedate=2012年3月23日 |df= }}, 'Argentine Gastronomy', June 6, 2008</ref><ref>[http://gosouthamerica.about.com/od/cuisine/a/ArgChile.htm "Cuisine of Argentina and Chile"] - [http://www.about.com About.com]</ref><ref name="ref1">[https://web.archive.org/web/20070610215422/http://www.cels.org.ar/Site_cels/publicaciones/informes_pdf/1998.Capitulo7.pdf]</ref><ref name="ref2">[https://web.archive.org/web/20110814202421/http://docentes.fe.unl.pt/~satpeg/PapersInova/Labor%20and%20Immigration%20in%20LA-2005.pdf]</ref>，不同地區亦有當地的地域特色。
