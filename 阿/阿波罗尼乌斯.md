''Handbook to Life in Ancient Greece''.Oxford University Press,USA.ISBN 9780195124910.</ref>其司书为[[泽农|泽农]]（[[:en:Zenon|Zenon]]）。他在[[法尤姆|法尤姆]]的[[腓拉德尔菲亚|腓拉德尔菲亚]]（[[:en:Philade_lphia|Philade lphia]]）拥有大片地产，其商船在埃及与[[累范特|累范特]]（[[:en:Levant|Levant]]）从事商业活动。<ref>Erskine (ed.) 2003: ''chapters on various Hellenistic rules, with detailed further reading''.See also reading for chapter 2.</ref>
