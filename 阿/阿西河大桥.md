'''阿西河大桥'''（{{lang-en|Athi River Super Bridge}}）是[[蒙巴萨-内罗毕标轨铁路|蒙巴萨-内罗毕标轨铁路]]（SGR）最长的桥梁，位于[[肯尼亚|肯尼亚]]首都[[奈洛比|奈洛比]]附近的阿西河镇，跨过[[阿西-加拉纳河|阿西-加拉纳河]]，同时也是非洲第二长的铁路桥，仅次于[[唐娜安娜大桥|唐娜安娜大桥]]（3.67公里）。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.africainfrastructuredigest.com/rail/athi-river-super-bridge-progress/|title=Athi River Super Bridge Progress|date=28 April 2016|work=Africa Infrastructure Digest|quote=When complete, the bridge will be among the longest bridges in Africa dislodging Armando Emilio Guebuza Bridge in Mozambique from number 6 (six) at 2.37 km.}}</ref>
