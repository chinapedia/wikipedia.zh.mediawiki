'''阿薩博特'''（'''Asabot'''）是[[埃塞俄比亞|埃塞俄比亞]]東部[[奧羅米亞州|奧羅米亞州]][[西哈勒爾蓋地區|西哈勒爾蓋地區]]的小鎮，座標{{Coord|9|15|N|40|34|E}}。鎮中有電話服務<ref name=NAI-web>[http://130.238.24.99/library/resources/dossiers/local_history_of_ethiopia/A/ORTARG.pdf "Local History in Ethiopia"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070927023043/http://130.238.24.99/library/resources/dossiers/local_history_of_ethiopia/A/ORTARG.pdf |date=2007-09-27 }} The Nordic Africa Institute website (accessed 26 July 2009)</ref>，2009年5月起提供流動電話服務<ref>[http://www.ena.gov.et/EnglishNews/2009/May/23May09/88181.htm "Six towns in W. Hararge get access to mobile telephone service"]{{dead link|date=十一月 2017 |bot=InternetArchiveBot }}, Ethiopian News Agency 23 May 2009 (accessed 30 May 2009)</ref>。根據[[埃塞俄比亞中央統計局|埃塞俄比亞中央統計局]]2005年的資料，阿薩博特人口約13,130，其中男性6,767人，女性6,363人。<ref>[http://www.csa.gov.et/text_files/2005_national_statistics.htm CSA 2005 National Statistics], Table B.4</ref>
