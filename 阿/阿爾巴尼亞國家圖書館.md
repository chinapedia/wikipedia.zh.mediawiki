'''阿爾巴尼亞國家圖書館'''（{{lang-sq|Biblioteka Kombëtare e Shqipërisë}}）是[[阿爾巴尼亞|阿爾巴尼亞]]的[[國家圖書館|國家圖書館]]，是該國最古老的文化機構之一。<ref>DOMI, E. (2011). The National Library of Albania: Tradition and Modern Vision. Alexandria, 21(3), 15-24.</ref> 阿爾巴尼亞國家圖書館位於該國首都[[地拉那|地拉那]]，<ref name="libdex">{{cite web | title = National Library of the Republic of Albania | url = http://www.libdex.com/data/11/5699.html | publisher = LibDex | accessdate = 2008-08-01 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20080706172900/http://www.libdex.com/data/11/5699.html | archivedate = 2008-07-06 }}</ref> 創立於1920年，<ref name="NLA">{{cite book | author = Etleva Domi | title = National Library of Albania | url = http://www.informaworld.com/smpp/content~content=a713531790~db=all~order=title | publisher = Encyclopedia of Library and Information Science | doi = 10.1081/E-ELIS-120008530 | date = 2003-06-23 | accessdate = 2008-08-01}}</ref> 1922年12月10日正式開館。<ref name="NLA" /><ref name="NatBib">{{cite web | title = Albanian National Bibliography | url = http://www.library.uiuc.edu/spx/class/nationalbib/natbibalbania.htm | publisher = University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign | accessdate = 2008-08-01 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20070518223800/http://www.library.uiuc.edu/spx/class/nationalbib/natbibalbania.htm | archivedate = 2007-05-18 }} </ref>
