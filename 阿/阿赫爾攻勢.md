'''阿赫爾攻勢'''是[[敘利亞民主力量|敘利亞民主力量]]在[[叙利亚内战|叙利亚内战]]中發動的一次攻勢，目標為奪取[[伊斯蘭國|伊斯蘭國]]佔據的[[伊拉克|伊拉克]]-[[敘利亞|敘利亞]]邊境戰略城鎮[[阿赫爾|阿赫爾]]。

== 攻勢 ==
攻勢於2015年10月31日發起。<ref>[http://www.reuters.com/article/2015/10/31/us-mideast-crisis-syria-idUSKCN0SP0EA20151031 "New U.S.-backed Syrian rebel alliance launches offensive against Islamic State"].</ref>

進攻發起的第一週，SDF攻下了阿赫爾附近和[[哈塞克|哈塞克]]南方一些村莊和地點，伊斯蘭國則以[[汽车炸弹|汽车炸弹]]反制。其中一次汽車炸彈擊中了SDF的巡邏隊，造成數十人死亡。<ref name="mcclatchydc">{{cite web|url=http://www.mcclatchydc.com/news/nation-world/world/middle-east/article42319425.html|title=Kurds, Arab allies hit hard by Islamic State car bomb|work=mcclatchydc|accessdate=3 November 2015|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151103171610/http://www.mcclatchydc.com/news/nation-world/world/middle-east/article42319425.html|archivedate=3 十一月 2015}}</ref>還有一名加拿大籍的SDF戰鬥人員在爭奪一座重要的農場時遭到自殺炸彈炸死。一名達吉斯坦的伊斯蘭國首領也在這週被殺死。<ref name="village">Master. </ref><ref name="4villages">[http://www.syriahr.com/en/2015/11/syrian-democratic-forces-advance-again-and-us-led-coalition-warplanes-hit-abo-al-hol-area/ -{Syrian Democratic Forces advance again, and US-led coalition’ warplanes hit Abo al- Hol area}-]</ref><ref name="newadvances">[http://www.syriahr.com/en/2015/11/violent-continuous-clashes-in-the-countryside-of-al-hasakah-and-al-hool-and-more-casualties-in-al-bokamal/ Violent continuous clashes in the countryside of Al-Hasakah and al-Hool and more casualties in al-Bokamal]</ref><ref name="border">[http://www.syriahr.com/en/2015/11/the-democratic-syrian-forces-advances-over-is-in-hasakah-countryside/ The democratic Syrian forces advances over IS in Hasakah countryside]</ref><ref>Master. [http://www.syriahr.com/en/2015/11/10-is-militants-killed-in-clashes-in-abo-al-hol-while-explosion-targets-the-joint-head-of-the-office-of-legislative-council-in-al-jazira-district/ "10 IS militants killed in clashes in Abo al- Hol, while explosion targets the joint head of the Office of Legislative Council in al- Jazira District"]. </ref><ref name="8ISIS">[http://www.syriahr.com/en/2015/11/dagestani-commander-and-7-isis-members-died-and-sdf-advance-in-al-hool-al-hasakah/ Dagestani commander and 7 ISIS members died and SDF advance in al-Hool at Al-Hasakah province]</ref><ref>[http://www.syriahr.com/en/2015/11/a-canadian-fighter-in-sdf-killed-in-an-explosion-in-abo-al-hol-area-in-the-countryside-of-al-hasakah/ A Canadian fighter in SDF killed in an explosion in Abo al- Hol area in the countryside of al- Hasakah]</ref><ref>[http://www.syriahr.com/en/2015/11/clashes-in-aleppo-and-al-hasakah-countryside/ Clashes in Aleppo and al-Hasakah countryside]</ref>SDF宣稱他們在進攻發起後控制了12座新的村莊。<ref name="12claim">[http://www.alaan.tv/news/world-news/141942/syria-democratic-forces-controls-four-new-villages-in-hasaka "قوات سوريا الديمقراطية" تسيطر على أربع قرى جديدة في الحسكة]</ref>

進攻發起的第二週，SDF從伊斯蘭國手中攻下了一部份的哈塞克–夏達迪公路和兩座村莊。<ref name="8novembergains">[http://www.syriahr.com/en/2015/11/sdf-advance-again-in-the-countryside-of-al-hasakah-and-warplanes-target-the-southeastern-countryside-of-homs/ SDF advance again in the countryside of Al-Hasakah and warplanes target the southeastern countryside of Homs]</ref>此時，SDF已經宣稱佔領了36個村莊、350平方公里領土，殺死伊斯蘭國戰鬥人員178人。兩天後，SDF又攻下了一座山丘，獲取兩部伊斯蘭國車輛。<ref name="hill4deads">[http://www.syriahr.com/en/2015/11/is-militants-killed-during-the-advancement-of-sdf-fighters-in-al-hol-area/ IS militants killed during the advancement of SDF fighters in al- Hol area]</ref>

11月11日攻勢持續，SDF攻下[[阿赫爾|阿赫爾]]東北方的城鎮Al-Khatuniyah，也推進至[[哈塞克|哈塞克]]的南郊。13日，SDF攻下[[阿赫爾|阿赫爾]]，繳獲大批武器彈藥。進攻發起的第三週，SDF已經宣稱攻下200個村莊，奪取領土1362平方公里，殺死493名伊斯蘭國戰鬥人員。<ref name="200villages">{{cite web|url=http://www.france24.com/en/20151116-syrian-kurdish-arab-alliance-captures-nearly-200-villages?ns_campaign=reseaux_sociaux&ns_source=FB&ns_mchannel=social&ns_linkname=depeche&aef_campaign_ref=partage_user&aef_campaign_date=2015-11-16|title=Syrian Kurdish-Arab alliance captures nearly 200 villages from IS|work=France 24|date=27 November 2015|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151117103931/http://www.france24.com/en/20151116-syrian-kurdish-arab-alliance-captures-nearly-200-villages?ns_campaign=reseaux_sociaux&ns_source=FB&ns_mchannel=social&ns_linkname=depeche&aef_campaign_ref=partage_user&aef_campaign_date=2015-11-16|archivedate=17 十一月 2015}}</ref>
