'''阿居兰苏丹国'''（{{lang-so|''Dawladdii Ajuuraan''}}, {{lang-ar|الدولة الأجورانيون}}）<ref name="His 40">{{cite book|ref=harv|last=Njoku|first=Raphael Chijioke|title=The History of Somalia|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=FlL2vE_qRQ8C&pg=PA40|year=2013|publisher=Greenwood|isbn=978-0-313-37857-7|page=40}}</ref>，简称'''阿居兰'''，<ref>[http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/11299/Ajuran Encyclopedia Britannica: Ajuran]</ref>是[[索马里人|索马里人]]建立的 [[穆斯林|穆斯林]][[苏丹国|苏丹国]]<ref>{{cite book|ref=harv|last=Luling|first=Virginia|title=Somali Sultanate: the Geledi city-state over 150 years|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=s0Y_AQAAIAAJ&pg=PA17|year=2002|publisher=Transaction Publishers|isbn=978-1-874209-98-0|page=17}}</ref><ref>Luc Cambrézy, Populations réfugiées: de l'exil au retour, p.316</ref><ref>{{cite journal |first=Mohamed Haji |last=Mukhtar |title=The Emergence and Role of Political Parties in the Inter-River Region of Somalia from 1947–1960 |work=Ufahamu |volume=17 |issue=2 |p=98 |url=http://escholarship.org/uc/item/7h11k656}}</ref>。中世纪统治[[非洲之角|非洲之角]]的大部分。通过强大的中央集权和对侵略者的进攻性军事姿态，阿居兰苏丹国成功抵制了奥罗莫人从西面的入侵和[[葡萄牙帝国|葡萄牙帝国]]从东面的入侵。重新建立从古代和中世纪早期时期索马里海域的贸易路线，对外贸易兴盛，来自[[东亚|东亚]]、[[南亚|南亚]]、[[欧洲|欧洲]]、[[中东|中东]]、[[北非|北非]]和非洲东部的船舶在沿海省份的海域航行。<ref name="Shapes">{{cite book|last=Shelley|first=Fred M.|title=Nation Shapes: The Story behind the World's Borders|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=5qlXatHRJtMC&pg=PA358|year=2013|publisher=ABC-CLIO|isbn=978-1-61069-106-2|page=358}}</ref>
