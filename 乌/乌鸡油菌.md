'''烏雞油菌'''（[[學名|學名]]''Polyozellus multiplex''），又名'''烏茸菌'''或'''烏舞茸'''，是一種[[革菌科|革菌科]][[真菌|真菌]]。它們是單型的，即[[屬|屬]]內只有它們一個[[物種|物種]]。它的[[孢子臺|孢子臺]]形狀獨特，藍紫色的[[傘狀帽|傘狀帽]]像是一束束的，底部皺褶至[[蕈柄|蕈柄]]。

烏雞油菌的分類曾有多次改動，不論是在[[科|科]]或[[屬|屬]]的層級。它們的生長地包括[[北美洲|北美洲]]及[[東亞|東亞]]的[[溫帶|溫帶]][[針葉林|針葉林]]。它們是可食用菌，現今也有栽種作商業用途。烏雞油菌內含生物活性的polyozellin，可以用來抑制[[胃癌|胃癌]]。

==歷史及分類==
烏雞油菌最初是於1899年由Lucien M. Underwood所描述。他將之分類為[[雞油菌屬|雞油菌屬]]中的新[[物種|物種]]，但指它有可能是屬於另一屬。<ref name=Underwood1899>{{cite journal en|last=Underwood LM.|year=1899|title=A new ''Cantharellus'' from Maine|journal=Bulletin of the Torrey Botanical Club|volume=26|issue=5|pages=254–5|url=http://www.biodiversitylibrary.org/page/710161|doi=10.2307/2477751}}</ref>於1910年，William Murrill認為烏雞油菌的[[蕈柄|蕈柄]]結構獨特，並將之分類在全新的''Polyozellus''屬中。<ref>{{cite journal en|last= Murrill WA.|year=1910|title=Chanterel|journal=North American Flora|volume=9|page=167–71}}</ref>於1920年，Curtis Gates Lloyd從[[日本|日本]]獲得的烏雞油菌標本看為另一新物種，並命名為''Phyllocarbon yasudai''。<ref>{{cite journal en|last=Lloyd CG.|year=1921|title=Botanical notes|journal=Mycological Writings|volume=6|page=1066}}</ref>

[[image:Gomphus_clavatus1.jpg|thumb]]的極端生長形態。]]
但是後來要到1937年在[[加拿大|加拿大]][[魁北克|魁北克]]才再發現烏雞油菌的標本。<ref name=Mounce1937/>翌年，Paul Shope認為''Polyozellus''一屬是多餘的，並指烏雞油菌的[[孢子臺|孢子臺]]與[[喇叭菌屬|喇叭菌屬]]的不謀而合。<ref name=Shope1938>{{cite journal en|last=Shope PF.|year=1938|title=Further notes on ''Cantharellus multiplex''|journal=Mycologia|volume=30|issue=4|pages=372–4|doi=10.2307/3754462}}</ref>於1939年，Lee Overholts指前兩名學者都忽略了Calvin Kauffman於1925年就從[[洛磯山脈|洛磯山脈]]及[[喀斯喀特山脈|喀斯喀特山脈]]採集回來的標本的研究。<ref>{{cite journal en|last=Kauffman C.|year=1925|title=The fungus flora of Mt Hood, with some new species|journal=Papers of the Michigan Academy of Science, Arts and Letters|volume=5|page=115–48}}</ref>Calvin Kauffman相信烏雞油菌其實是生長在極端環境的[[陀螺菌|陀螺菌]]，所以並不贊同將之分類在喇叭菌屬中。<ref>{{cite journal en|last=Overholts LM.|year=1939|title=''Cantharellus multiplex'' again|journal=Mycologia|volume=31|issue=2|pages=231, 233|doi=10.2307/3754572}}</ref>Alexander H. Smith及Elizabeth Morse於1947年將烏雞油菌分類在雞油菌屬下新的''Polyozellus''組中，但仍指出它們有獨特於此屬的[[孢子|孢子]]。<ref name=Smith1947>{{cite journal en|author=Smith AH, Morse EE.|year=1947|title=The genus ''Cantharellus'' in the Western United States|journal=Mycologia|volume=39|issue=5|pages=497–534|doi=10.2307/3755192|issn=00275514}}</ref>

1953年，[[今関六也|今関六也]]發現烏雞油菌的孢子呈亞球形及有結節，是其他雞油菌屬所沒有的。而這種特徵卻在[[革菌科|革菌科]]中非常普遍，加上烏雞油菌本身也像其他革菌科般呈深色、帶有強烈的氣味及含有[[革菌酸|革菌酸]]，所以他建議將烏雞油菌分類在新的Phylacteriaceae科中。<ref>{{cite journal en|last=Sawada M.|year=1952|title=Studies on pigment in fungi (I). On the distribution of thelephoric acid in fungi|journal=Journal of the Japanese Forestry Society|volume=34|pages=110–3}}</ref>但這建議卻未得到學界的接受，例如[[川村清一|川村清一]]於1954年就將之分類在[[革菌屬|革菌屬]]中。<ref>{{cite book en|last=Kawamura S.|year=1954|title=Icones of Japanese Fungi. Vol. 6|publisher=Kazama-Shobo|location=Tokyo|page=638}}</ref>

於2009年，[[真菌索引|真菌索引]]（Index Fungorum）<ref>{{cite web|title=Index Fungorum - genus record|url=http://www.indexfungorum.org/Names/genusrecord.asp?RecordID=18327|publisher=CABI|accessdate=2009-09-02}}</ref>及MycoBank<ref>{{cite web|author=Stalpers J. (Curator)|title=MycoBank: genus ''Polyozellus'' Murrill 1910|url=http://www.mycobank.org/MycoTaxo.aspx?Link=T&Rec=18327|date=03-09-2009|publisher=International Mycological Association|accessdate=2009-09-02}}</ref>都將烏雞油菌列在革菌科下。<ref name="Pilz2003">{{cite book en|title=Ecology and management of commercially harvested chanterelle mushrooms. General Technical Report PNW-GTR-576|last=Pilz D, Norvell L, Danell E, Molina R.|year=2003|publisher=Department of Agriculture, Forest Service, Pacific Northwest Research Station|location=Portland, OR|url=http://www.fs.fed.us/pnw/pubs/pnw_gtr576.pdf|accessdate=2009-09-23}}</ref>屬名的[[希臘文|希臘文]]意思是「很多分枝」<ref name=Richard1993/>；[[種小名|種小名]]則指「很多塊」，是參照其複雜的子實體結構而取的。<ref name="isbn0-472-85610-3">{{cite book en|author=Weber NS, Smith AH|title=The Mushroom Hunter's Field Guide|publisher=University of Michigan Press|location=Ann Arbor, Mich|year=1980|page=60|id=ISBN 0-472-85610-3|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=TYI4f6fqrfkC&pg=RA1-PA60&dq=polyozellus+multiplex#v=onepage&q=polyozellus%20multiplex&f=false}}</ref>

==特徵==
[[image:Polyozellus_multiplex_23792.jpg|thumb]]。]]
烏雞油菌是一種[[雞油菌|雞油菌]]。<ref name=Richard1993>{{cite journal en|last=Homola RL.|year=1993|title=Cantharelloid fungi of Maine|journal=Maine Naturalist|volume=1|issue=2|pages=5–12|doi=10.2307/3858219}}</ref>[[子實體|子實體]]呈扇或漏斗狀，在地上一束的生長，一束的[[直徑|直徑]]可以達1米<ref name=Shope1938>{{cite journal en|last=Shope PF.|year=1938|title=Further notes on ''Cantharellus multiplex''|journal=Mycologia|volume=30|issue=4|pages=372–4|doi=10.2307/3754462}}</ref><ref name=Smith1947/>，平均則有30厘米。<ref name=McKnight1987>{{cite book en|author=McKnight VB, McKnight KH.|title=A Field Guide to Mushrooms, North America|publisher=Houghton Mifflin|location=Boston|year=1987|page=77|id=ISBN 0-395-91090-0|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=kSdA3V7Z9WcC&pg=PA77&dq=Polyozellus+multiplex}}</ref>

個別的[[傘狀帽|傘狀帽]]長闊各有3-5厘米，呈紫黑色，邊緣白色及藍綠色。<ref name=Underwood1899/>表面有多個同心的環狀絨毛，邊緣有一層纖細絨毛，帽散開及成波浪狀。底面有[[子實層|子實層]]，子實層呈薄而密的皺褶，一般呈與表面相同或較淡的顏色。<ref name=Arora1986/>在不同的位點也有發現不同的顏色，例如在[[阿拉斯加|阿拉斯加]]的烏雞油菌都是呈烏黑色的，而底部呈深灰色。<ref name=Arora1991>{{cite book en|author=Arora D.|title=All that the Rain Promises and more: a Hip Pocket Guide to Western Mushrooms|publisher=Ten Speed Press|location=Berkeley, Calif|year=1991|page=12|id=ISBN 0-89815-388-3|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=87ct90d4B9gC&pg=PA12&dq=Polyozellus+multiplex&lr=}}</ref>

子實體可以高15厘米及闊10厘米。[[蕈柄|蕈柄]]呈深紫黑色，表面光滑無毛，且與基部融合。蕈柄闊1.5-2厘米及長達5厘米。[[菌髓|菌髓]]呈深紫色，質感柔軟及易脆。[[孢子印|孢子印]]是白色的。<ref name=Kuo2003>{{cite web|title=''Polyozellus multiplex'': The blue chanterelle|url=http://www.mushroomexpert.com/polyozellus_multiplex.html|date=2003|publisher=MushroomExpert.com|author=Kuo M.|accessdate=2009-08-23}}</ref>

[[image:Craterellus_cornucopiodes_12457.jpg|thumb]]。]]

[[孢子|孢子]]接近球狀至[[橢圓形|橢圓形]]，表面有細小的結節覆蓋，大小約為6–8.5×5.5–8微米。<ref name=Kuo2003/>微觀下孢子是透明的，在[[氫氧化鉀|氫氧化鉀]]下孢子會變為綠色。孢子並非[[澱粉質|澱粉質]]，所以在[[梅瑞氏染劑|梅瑞氏染劑]]下不會轉變為藍色。[[囊狀體|囊狀體]]呈絲狀，闊3-4微米，長28-40微米。[[菌蓋皮層|菌蓋皮層]]是由[菌絲]]組床，在氫氧化鉀下會轉變為[[橄欖|橄欖]]綠色。它們有[[扣子體|扣子體]]，但並非所有[[隔膜|隔膜]]也有。<ref name=Bigelow1978/>[[孢子臺|孢子臺]]的大小為32–38×5–6微米，有四個孢子。<ref name=Smith1947/>

===相似物種===
[[灰喇叭菌|灰喇叭菌]]也像烏雞油菌般有黑色的[[子實體|子實體]]及光滑的[[子實層|子實層]]，但其肉較薄，子實體呈柱狀及灰至黑色。[[芳香喇叭菌|芳香喇叭菌]]傾向一束生長，但卻是呈橙色而不是藍色的。<ref name=Bessette1987>{{cite book en|author=Sundberg W, Bessette A.|title=Mushrooms: A Quick Reference Guide to Mushrooms of North America (Macmillan Field Guides)|publisher=Collier Books|location=New York|year=1987|page=52|id=ISBN 0-02-063690-3}}</ref>[[釘菇|釘菇]]的形狀及形態很像烏雞油菌，但肉質較厚，且是呈淺紫色至粉紅色。<ref name="Pilz2003"/>

烏雞油菌是外生根菌的，即菌絲與[[植物|植物]]的根部[[共生|共生]]，而菌絲一般不會穿透根部[[細胞|細胞]]。<ref name=Stamets2005>{{cite book en|author=Stamets P.|title=Mycelium Running: How Mushrooms Can Help Save the World|publisher=Ten Speed Press|location=Berkeley, Calif|year=2005|page=34|id=ISBN 1-58008-579-2|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=NPI8_-omzvsC&pg=PA34&dq=Polyozellus+multiplex&lr=}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal en|last=Lee S-S, Kim D-H, Chung H-C.|year=2000|title=Ectomycorrhizal roots collected from the bases of the four edible basidiocarps around Mt. Wol-Ak|journal=Mycobiology|volume=28|issue=1|pages=27–32}}</ref>烏雞油菌生長在[[雲杉屬|雲杉屬]]及[[冷杉屬|冷杉屬]]的[[針葉林|針葉林]]<ref name=Bessette1992/>，尤其是在較高的[[海拔|海拔]]。<ref name=Ammirati1987>{{cite book en|author=Ammirati JF, McKenny M, Stuntz DE|title=The New Savory Wild Mushroom|publisher=University of Washington Press|location=Seattle|year=1987|page=31|id=ISBN 0-295-96480-4}}</ref>它們很多時都於[[夏天|夏天]]及[[冬天|冬天]]生長。<ref name=Bigelow1978>{{cite journal en|last=Bigelo HE|year=1978|title=The cantharelloid fungi of New England and adjacent areas|journal=Mycologia|volume=70|issue=4|pages=707–56|doi=10.2307/3759354|issn=00275514}}</ref>

烏雞油菌分佈在北部及山區，較少會見到。在[[美國|美國]][[緬因州|緬因州]]、[[俄勒岡州|俄勒岡州]]、[[科羅拉多州|科羅拉多州]]及[[阿拉斯加|阿拉斯加]]、[[加拿大|加拿大]][[魁北克省|魁北克省]]及[[英屬哥倫比亞|英屬哥倫比亞]]<ref name=Mounce1937>{{cite journal en|last=Mounce I, Jackson HAC|year=1937|title=Two Canadian collections of ''Cantharellus multiplex''|journal=Mycologia|volume=29|issue=3|pages=286–8|doi=10.2307/3754283}}</ref><ref name=Smith1947/><ref>{{cite journal en|last=Pomerleau R, Cooke WB|year=1978|title=The cantharelloid fungi of New England and adjacent areas|journal=Mycologia|volume=70|issue=4|pages=707–56|doi=10.2307/3759354}}</ref>、[[中國|中國]]<ref name=Lee2003/>、[[日本|日本]]及[[韓國|韓國]]。<ref name=Imazeki1953>{{cite journal en|last=Imazeki R|year=1953|title=''Polyozellus multiplex'' and the family Phylacteriaceae|journal=Mycologia|volume=45|issue=4|pages=555–61}}</ref>其他[[真菌|真菌]]也有類似零散的分佈。<ref>{{cite book en|title=Evolutionary Generics of Fungi|last=Yang ZL|editor-last=Xu J|chapter=Diversity and Biogeography of Higher Fungi in China|year=2005|publisher=Horizon Bioscience|location=Norfolk, U.K.|id=ISBN 978-1-904933-15-1|pages=35–61}}</ref>烏雞油菌也有在[[夏洛特皇后群島|夏洛特皇后群島]]培植作商業用途。<ref name="urlFungi of Haida Gwaii">{{cite web|url=http://www.geog.ubc.ca/biodiversity/eflora/FungiofHaidaGwaii.html|author=Kroeger P, Ceska O, Roberts C, Kendrick B.|title=Fungi of Haida Gwaii|publisher=E-Flora BC|year=2007|accessdate=2009-07-07}}</ref>

==用途==

Fungi|author=Castellano MA, O'Dell T|url=http://www.blm.gov/or/plans/surveyandmanage/MR/Fungi/GRP06.htm|date=1997|publisher=U.S. Dept. of Management. ===生物活性物質===
[[File:Polyozellin.svg|thumb]]。<ref name="pmid9360624"/>]]
烏雞油菌可以分解出polyozellin，能抑制[[阿爾茨海默氏病|阿爾茨海默氏病]]中處理[[蛋白質|蛋白質]]的[[脯氨酰內肽酶|脯氨酰內肽酶]]。所以[[科學家|科學家]]都希望透過研究這些物質來發展治療方法。<ref name="pmid9360624">{{cite journal en|author=Hwang JS, Song KS, Kim WG, Lee TH, Koshino H, Yoo ID|title=Polyozellin, a new inhibitor of prolyl endopeptidase from ''Polyozellus multiplex''|journal=The Journal of Antibiotics|volume=50|issue=9|pages=773–7|year=1997|pmid=9360624|accessdate=2009-07-05}}</ref>從烏雞油菌的抽取物中發現有類似polyozellin[[二苯并呋喃|二苯并呋喃]]的衍生物，包括kynapcin-12<ref name="pmid10994814">{{cite journal en|author=Lee HJ, Rhee IK, Lee KB, Yoo ID, Song KS|title=Kynapcin-12, a new ''p''-terphenyl derivative from ''Polyozellus multiplex'', inhibits prolyl endopeptidase|journal=The Journal of Antibiotics|volume=53|issue=7|pages=714–9|year=2000|pmid=10994814}}</ref>、-13、-28<ref name="pmid12243451">{{cite journal en|author=Kim SI, Park IH, Song KS|title=kynapcin-13 and -28, new benzofuran prolyl endopeptidase inhibitors from ''Polyozellus multiplex''|journal=The Journal of Antibiotics|volume=55|issue=7|pages=623–8|year=2002|pmid=12243451}}</ref>、及-24。<ref>{{cite journal en|last=Song KS, Raskin I|year=2002|title=A prolyl endopeptidase-inhibiting benzofuran dimer from ''Polyozellus multiflex''|journal=Journal of Natural Products|volume=65|issue=1|pages=76–8|doi=10.1021/np010194b}}</ref>

===抗腫瘤===
烏雞油菌的描取物具有壓抑[[胃癌|胃癌]]的效用。<ref name=Lee2003/><ref name="pmid16030389">{{cite journal en|author=Lull C, Wichers HJ, Savelkoul HF|title=Antiinflammatory and immunomodulating properties of fungal metabolites|journal=Mediators of Inflammation|volume=2005|issue=2|pages=63–80|year=2005|pmid=16030389|pmc=1160565|doi=10.1155/MI.2005.63|accessdate=2009-07-05}}</ref>只要吃低濃度（約0.5-1%）的烏雞油菌抽取物，就可以促進[[谷胱甘肽轉硫酶|谷胱甘肽轉硫酶]]及[[超氧化物歧化酶|超氧化物歧化酶]]的活性，且可以增加[[穀胱甘肽|穀胱甘肽]]的份量。這些抽取物也可以擴大[[腫瘤蛋白53|腫瘤蛋白53]]的[[基因表現|基因表現]]。腫瘤蛋白53是一種重要的[[抑癌基因|抑癌基因]]，可以保護[[基因組|基因組]]免受破壞。<ref name=Lee2003>{{cite journal en|author=Lee IS, Nishikawa A|title=''Polyozellus multiplex'', a Korean wild mushroom, as a potent chemopreventive agent against stomach cancer|journal=Life Sciences|volume=73|issue=25|pages=3225–34|year=2003|pmid=14561527|doi=10.1016/j.lfs.2003.06.006|url=http://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0024320503007707|accessdate=2009-07-05}}</ref>polyozellin也具有抗癌的特性。<ref name="pmid14759131">{{cite journal en|author=Kim JH, Lee JS, Song KS, Kwon CS, Kim YK, Kim JS|title=Polyozellin isolated from ''Polyozellus multiplex'' induces phase 2 enzymes in mouse hepatoma cells and differentiation in human myeloid leukaemic cell lines|journal=Journal of Agricultural and Food Chemistry|volume=52|issue=3|pages=451–5|year=2004|pmid=14759131|doi=10.1021/jf034748n|accessdate=2009-07-05}}</ref><ref name="pmid16783701">{{cite journal en|author=Jin XY, Lee SH, Kim JY, Zhao YZ, Park EJ, Lee BS, Nan JX, Song KS, Ko G, Sohn DH|title=Polyozellin inhibits nitric oxide production by down-regulating LPS-induced activity of NF-κB and SAPK/JNK in RAW 264.7 cells|journal=Planta Medica|volume=72|issue=9|pages=857–9|year=2006|pmid=16783701|doi=10.1055/s-2006-946640|url=http://www.thieme-connect.com/DOI/DOI?10.1055/s-2006-946640}}</ref>
