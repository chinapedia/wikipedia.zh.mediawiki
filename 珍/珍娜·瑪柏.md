'''珍娜·妮可·穆雷'''（{{lang|en|Jenna Nicole Mourey}}，{{bd|1986年|9月15日<ref>{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/Jenna_Marbles/status/643859770882002945|title=Jenna Mourey/Marbles on Twitter|work=Twitter}}</ref><ref>{{cite av media|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nIsgoafssIo|title=Things I Get Excited About As An Adult|date=August 27, 2015|publisher=JennaMarbles|via=YouTube}}</ref>}}），較廣為人知的是化名'''珍娜·瑪柏'''（Jenna Marbles），美國YouTuber、喜劇演員與演員。截至2017年4月，珍娜·瑪柏已經有超過1716萬名訂閱者與22億觀看次數<ref>{{cite av media|url=https://www.youtube.com/user/JennaMarbles|title=JennaMarbles|work=YouTube}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title= JennaMarbles - YouTube Channel Statistics - WatchinToday|url=https://watchin.today/channel/UC9gFih9rw0zNCK3ZtoKQQyA|work=watchin.today|accessdate=2019-04-17|}}</ref>，是YouTube第20多訂閱者與曾經為最大的女性頻道<ref>{{Cite web |title=PewDiePie, Jenna Marbles, CaptainSparklez: The Web's Biggest Stars |work=[[Reason_(magazine)|Reason]] |date=October 1, 2014 |accessdate=October 10, 2014 |url=http://reason.com/archives/2014/10/01/pewdiepie-captainsparklez-skydoesminecra }}</ref>。

== 早年生活 ==
穆雷於1986年9月15日出生於[[羅徹斯特_(紐約州)|羅徹斯特]]，並在該處長大，她父親是一位化學家，母親狄波拉（Deborah）則是一位市場行銷，他們在穆雷還小的時候離婚，由她的爸爸和後來結婚的繼母照顧<ref name="DrewML">{{cite av media|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sCxzaHC30Ec|title=Draw My Life- Jenna Marbles|date=March 28, 2013|publisher=JennaMarbles|via=YouTube}}</ref>。她中學就讀於{{link-en|布萊頓高中|Brighton High School (Rochester, New York)}}，2004年畢業後，穆雷搬到[[波士頓|波士頓]]就讀[[薩福克大學|薩福克大學]]的[[心理學|心理學]]系<ref name="biography">{{cite video|url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6Sl-1X58ObY|title=Draw My Life- Jenna Marbles|publisher={{youtube|u=JennaMarbles|Jenna Marbles}}|date=2013-03-28}}</ref>。在獲得[[理學士|理學士]]學位後，她轉讀[[波士頓大學|波士頓大學]]的體育心理學系並獲得碩士學位<ref name="variety">{{Cite web |title=YouTube Star Jenna Marbles: 5 Facts About Her Rise to Internet Fame |first=Todd |last=Spangler |work=[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]] |date=July 18, 2013 |accessdate=January 16, 2015 |url=http://variety.com/2013/digital/news/youtube-star-jenna-marbles-5-facts-about-her-rise-to-internet-fame-1200564607/}}</ref>。之後穆雷在{{link-en|Barstool Sports|Barstool Sports}}找到一份版主助理的工作，在此之間她創建了一個名叫「StoolLaLa」的網站<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.nbcnews.com/id/53821689/ns/business-small_business/t/man-behind-bible-bro-culture/#.U5DQ8fldWBI |title=The Man Behind the 'Bible of Bro Culture' |publisher=NBC News |date=December 13, 2013}}</ref>。2010年的夏天，她搬到[[麻薩諸塞州|麻薩諸塞州]]的[[劍橋_(麻薩諸塞州)|劍橋]]，並以800美元租金與其他人同居一間三房公寓，期間穆雷當過酒保跟夜店舞者等工作<ref name="nytimes.com">{{cite web|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2013/04/14/fashion/jenna-marbles.html|title=The Woman With 1 Billion Clicks: Jenna Marbles|date=April 14, 2013|work=The New York Times}}</ref>。

== YouTube ==
[[File:Jenna_Marbles_by_Gage_Skidmore.jpg|thumb]]
穆雷於2010年2月16日以「珍娜·瑪柏{{註|其化名Marbles來自她的狗「瑪柏先生」（Mr. Marbles）}}」（JennaMarbles）為用戶名創建YouTube頻道，其第一部影片於隔天上傳<ref>{{cite av media|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w6ieL3Cr06E|title=Charles Franklin Marbles is a Sad Sad Man|date=February 17, 2010|publisher=JennaMarbles|via=YouTube}}</ref>，逢星期三或四上傳新影片。「珍娜·瑪柏」這頻道成名於《How to trick people into thinking you're good looking》和《How To Avoid Talking To People You Don't Want To Talk To》這2部影片，其中《How to trick people into thinking you're good looking》在上傳後的第一週便錄得超級530萬次的觀看數<ref>{{Cite web |title=The Woman With 1 Billion Clicks: Jenna Marbles |last=O'Leary |first=Amy |work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] |date=April 12, 2013 |accessdate=October 10, 2014 |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2013/04/14/fashion/jenna-marbles.html?pagewanted=all}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Jenna Marbles on 1 Billion Views, Set to Overtake Ray William Johnson? |first=Jeff |last=Klima |work=[[New_Media_Rockstars|New Media Rockstars]] |date=April 22, 2013 |accessdate=October 10, 2014 |url=http://newmediarockstars.com/2013/04/jenna-marbles-on-1-billion-views-set-to-overtake-ray-william-johnson-video/}}</ref>，而《How To Avoid Talking To People You Don't Want To Talk To》則於2011年8月分別被《[[紐約時報|紐約時報]]》<ref name="nytimes1">{{cite news|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2011/08/14/fashion/with-grinding-an-unwanted-advance-at-the-dance.html |title=Rendering Grinders Toothless |newspaper=''The New York Times'' |date=August 12, 2011 |accessdate=October 4, 2011}}</ref>和《[[ABC新聞|ABC新聞]]》<ref name=abcnews>{{cite web|url=http://abcnews.go.com/US/jenna-marbles-anti-dirty-dance-teens-grinding/story?id=14314828 |title="Jenna Marbles'" Anti-Dirty Dance: Teens Say No to Grinding - ABC News |publisher=''ABC News'' |date=August 16, 2011 |accessdate=October 4, 2011}}</ref>報導。在該影片中，穆雷說：「我厭倦那些用自己的生殖器接觸著我背部的傢伙，而原因只是因為我在夜店出現或在酒吧跳舞<ref name="nytimes1"/><ref name=abcnews/>。」

穆雷亦曾在其他頻道的影片中出現或為影片中出現的角色配音。例如在《[[經典饒舌爭霸戰|經典饒舌爭霸戰]]》第2季第13集《[[亞當|亞當]]V.S[[夏娃|夏娃]]》中飾演夏娃<ref>{{cite web|author=Sam Gutelle|url=http://www.tubefilter.com/2013/02/11/epic-rap-battles-of-history-jenna-marbles/|title=Epic Rap Battles of History Pits Man Against Woman With Jenna Marbles|publisher=''Tubefilter''|date=February 11, 2013|accessdate=February 25, 2013}}</ref>、為《[[柳丁擱來亂|柳丁擱來亂]]》其中一集《Fake n' Bacon》的香蕉配音<ref>{{cite av media|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oZzyi-1JZ7s|title=Annoying Orange - Fake N Bacon|date=September 30, 2011|publisher=Annoying Orange|via=YouTube}}</ref>。而和她合作過的YouTuber包括{{link-en|漢娜·哈特|Hannah Hart}}<ref>{{cite av media|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BdbsAubqKvU|title=Charades Against Humanity (ft. Jenna Marbles!)|date=August 13, 2014|publisher=MyHarto|via=YouTube}}</ref>、[[NigaHiga|NigaHiga]]<ref>{{cite av media|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B2LG3DmVBgM|title=I Dare You (ft. Jenna Marbles & Julien)|date=October 4, 2014|publisher=nigahiga|via=YouTube}}</ref>、[[謝恩·多森|謝恩·多森]]<ref>{{cite av media|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3y6ZuiEAV4M|title=Speech Jammer Challenge With Shane Dawson|date=May 20, 2015|publisher=JennaMarbles|via=YouTube}}</ref>等。

== 其他 ==
穆雷在由[[Smosh|Smosh]]領銜主演的電影《{{link-en|Smosh：大電影|Smosh: The Movie}}》中飾演她自己<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://variety.com/2014/digital/news/youtubes-smosh-guys-on-making-their-first-movie-we-had-to-learn-how-to-act-1201311820/|title=YouTube’s Smosh Guys on Making Their First Movie: ‘We Had to Learn How to Act’|last=Spangler|first=Todd|website=Variety|language=en-US|access-date=2016-03-07}}</ref>。她同時也是{{link-en|SiriusXM Hits 1|SiriusXM Hits 1}}的節目《YouTube 15》的主持人<ref>{{cite web|url=http://blog.siriusxm.com/tag/jenna-marbles/|title=jenna marbles - SiriusXM Blog|work=siriusxm.com}}</ref>。2014年10月，穆雷成為《[[逞能倒霉蛋|逞能倒霉蛋]]》第四季第9集的來賓<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.mtv.ca/shows/ridiculousness/video/season-4/ridiculousness-s4-ep9/1721575/0/1|title=RIDICULOUSNESS (S.4 EP.9|last=Spangler|website=MTV|accessdate=2014-01-30|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161225235646/http://www.mtv.ca/shows/ridiculousness/video/season-4/ridiculousness-s4-ep9/1721575/0/1|archivedate=2016-12-25|deadurl=yes}}</ref>。

== 個人生活 ==
穆雷有一位哥哥，名叫德文（Devin）。穆雷自2013年6月起與同為YouTuber的朱利安·索洛米塔（Julien Solomita）交往<ref>{{cite av media|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TGAn1o6eyiU|title=Jenna's Rachet Salon With Julien Solomita|date=December 14, 2012|publisher=JennaMarbles|via=YouTube}}</ref>，截止2016年8月，他的頻道有88萬訂閱數<ref>{{cite av media|url=https://www.youtube.com/user/TheFightingSolo/about|title=Julien Solomita|work=YouTube}}</ref>。在此之前他曾跟另外2個名叫威利（Willy，2000年–2004年）和馬克斯·懷茲（Max Weisz，2011年–2012年）男性交往過<ref name="DrewML" />。

穆雷很愛小動物，也樂於觀看粉絲給她的寵物照片<ref>{{cite av media|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IQHtAsLsq8E|title=How To Talk To Animals|date=November 2, 2011|publisher=JennaMarbles|via=YouTube}}</ref>。她養了2隻狗，分別是[[芝娃娃|芝娃娃]]「瑪柏先生」（Mr. Marbles{{註|全名為查爾斯·富蘭克林·瑪柏·穆雷先生（Mr. Charles Franklin Marbles Mourey）}}）和{{link-en|義大利靈𤟥|Italian greyhound}}「克米特·渥姆」（Kermith Worm）<ref>{{cite av media|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4McGrXmSStc|title=My Dogs Try Fruits And Vegetables|date=February 25, 2016|publisher=JennaMarbles|via=YouTube}}</ref>，2隻寵物狗由於經常在影片中出現的關係所以很受觀眾喜愛，而穆雷的博客網站也有售賣基於牠們設計的狗玩具<ref name="variety"/>。
