'''珍珠项链'''來自於英語的[[性俚語|性俚語]]「pearl necklace」，是指男性在另一人的[[頸|頸部]]、[[胸膛|胸膛]]或[[乳房|乳房]]上[[射精|射精]]，因為精液在沾停於胸頸部類似於[[透明|透明]]的[[珍珠|珍珠]][[项链|项链]]，故此得名<ref>''[http://www.amazon.com/gp/reader/0060392320 Sex Tips for Straight Women from a Gay Man]'': Dan Anderson. Regan Books, 1997. p 106-7.</ref><ref>''[http://www.amazon.com/gp/reader/0446692816?v=search-inside&keywords=%22Pearl%20necklace%22%20sex The Vice Guide to Sex and Drugs and Rock and Roll]'': Suroosh Alvi, Gavin McInnes, Shane Smith. Warner Books, 2003. p 35</ref>。

珍珠項鍊的成因可能是[[乳交|乳交]]射精、[[自慰|自慰]]射精或是[[陰莖口交|口交]]、[[性交|性交]]過程中將陰莖抽出體外射精造成的的<ref name="davis">Davis, 1996, pages 125-127</ref>。這種體外射精的方式也是[[性工作者|性工作者]]在面對不願意戴[[保險套|保險套]]的客人時，避免[[體內射精|體內射精]]的替代方式之一<ref name="davis" /><ref>{{cite book |title= Live sex acts: women performing erotic labor |author= Wendy Chapkis |edition= illustrated |publisher= Taylor & Francis |year= 1997 |isbn= 0-415-91288-1 |page= 170 (and footnote 57 on page 232) |url= http://books.google.com/books?id=ipmRWrCqdFcC}}</ref>。
