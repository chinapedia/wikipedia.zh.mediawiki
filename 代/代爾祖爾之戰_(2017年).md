2017年1月14日開始的'''代爾祖爾之戰'''是[[伊斯兰国|伊斯兰国]]向[[代爾祖爾之圍|圍困多時]]的敘利亞[[代尔祖尔省|代尔祖尔省]]首府[[代尔祖尔|代尔祖尔]]發動的新攻勢。

2017年1月16日，伊斯蘭國攻佔聯繫代爾祖爾機場與敘政府軍主要控制區的道路，把敘軍在代爾祖爾的控制區分割成東西兩部分。<ref name=telegraph>{{cite web|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/2017/01/16/syrian-rebels-agree-attend-russia-brokered-peace-talks/ |title=Islamic State launches major offensive to take Syrian city |publisher=The Telegraph|author=Josie Ensor|date=2017-01-16 |accessdate=2017-01-16}}</ref><ref name=gains>{{cite web|url=http://www.wsj.com/articles/islamic-state-gains-in-syria-province-1484676322 |title=Islamic State Gains in Remote Syria Outpost |first1=Noam|last1=Raydan|first2=Nour|last2=Alakraa|date=2017-01-17 |publisher=|via=Wall Street Journal}}</ref>[[世界糧食計劃署|世界糧食計劃署]]同日宣佈暫停在代爾祖爾空投糧食的行動。<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/2017/jan/17/islamic-state-deir-ez-zor-syria-world-food-programme |title=Islamic State surrounds military airport in Deir ez-Zor, eastern Syria |publisher=The Guardian |date=2017-01-17}}</ref>

在俄敘空軍支援下，地面上的敘軍於1月17日發起反攻。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.theaustralian.com.au/news/world/the-times/isis-fights-to-capture-new-base-in-syria/news-story/9d51a55c44d4d2118b58dc2b4ca333bc|title=ISIS fights to capture new base in Syria|publisher=The Australian}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.gulf-times.com/story/528362/Embattled-Syria-troops-clash-with-IS-in-Deir-Ezzor|title=Embattled Syria troops clash with IS in Deir Ezzor|publisher=Gulf Times}}</ref>

1月23日至24日之間的夜晚，約500名敘軍士兵經空運抵達代爾祖爾增援。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://southfront.org/more-details-on-the-effort-to-insert-syrian-airborne-troops-into-deir-ez-zor/|title=More Details On The Effort To Insert Syrian Airborne Troops Into Deir ez-Zor|date=2017-01-30 |publisher=|accessdate=2017-02-11}}</ref>

1月29日，世界糧食計劃署恢復在代爾祖爾空投糧食的行動。<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://sputniknews.com/middleeast/201701301050155294-un-syria-aid/ |title=UN Resumes Aid Deliveries to Syria’s Deir ez-Zor After Two-Week Pause |date=2017-01-30 |publisher=Sputnik}}</ref>
