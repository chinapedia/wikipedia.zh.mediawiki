'''代谢型谷氨酸受体'''（{{lang-en|'''metabotropic glutamate receptors'''}}，簡稱'''mGluR'''），屬於[[谷氨酸受体|谷氨酸受体]]的一種類型（另一类为'''离子型谷氨酸受体'''），可藉由間接代謝過程進行活化。該受體是[[GPCR家族C組|GPCR家族C組]]的成員<ref name="Bonsi">{{cite journal | author = Bonsi P, Cuomo D, De Persis C, Centonze D, Bernardi G, Calabresi P, Pisani A | title = Modulatory action of metabotropic glutamate receptor (mGluR) 5 on mGluR1 function in striatal cholinergic interneurons | series = 49 | journal = Neuropharmacology | volume = Suppl 1 | issue = | pages = 104–13 | year = 2005 | pmid = 16005029 | doi = 10.1016/j.neuropharm.2005.05.012 }}</ref>。就像所有麩胺酸鹽[[受体_(生物化学)|受體]]，該受體會與[[谷氨酸|谷氨酸]]結合，是一種具有興奮性[[神經傳遞物質|神經傳遞物質]]的[[胺基酸|胺基酸]]。

==功能与结构==
在中枢和周围神经系统中，mGluR执行许多种功能：比如涉及[[学习|学习]]、[[記憶|記憶]]、[[焦慮|焦慮]]以及[[疼痛|疼痛]]的感知<ref name="Ohashi">{{cite journal | author = Ohashi H, Maruyama T, Higashi-Matsumoto H, Nomoto T, Nishimura S, Takeuchi Y | title = A novel binding assay for metabotropic glutamate receptors using [3H] L-quisqualic acid and recombinant receptors | journal = Z. Naturforsch., C, J. Biosci. | volume = 57 | issue = 3-4 | pages = 348–55 | year = 2002 | pmid = 12064739 | doi = | issn = | url = http://www.znaturforsch.com/sc/57c/s57c0348.pdf | format = subscription required }}</ref>。它们在[[脑|脑]]的[[海马体|海马体]]、[[小脑|小脑]]<ref name="Hinoi">{{cite journal | author = Hinoi E, Ogita K, Takeuchi Y, Ohashi H, Maruyama T, Yoneda Y | title = Characterization with [3H]quisqualate of group I metabotropic glutamate receptor subtype in rat central and peripheral excitable tissues | journal = Neurochem. Int. | volume = 38 | issue = 3 | pages = 277–85 | year = 2001 | pmid = 11099787 | doi = 10.1016/S0197-0186(00)00075-9 }}</ref>[[大脑皮质|大脑皮质]]等其他组织及周围组织<ref name="Chu and Hablitz">{{cite journal | author = Chu Z, Hablitz JJ | title = Quisqualate induces an inward current via mGluR activation in neocortical pyramidal neurons | journal = Brain Res. | volume = 879 | issue = 1-2 | pages = 88–92 | year = 2000 | pmid = 11011009 | doi = 10.1016/S0006-8993(00)02752-9 }}</ref>神经[[突触|突触]]的突触前和突触后[[神經元|神經元]]都有发现。

如同其它的[[代谢型受体|代谢型受体]]，mGluRs也有一个[[G蛋白偶联受体|七跨膜结构域]]跨过细胞膜<ref name="Platt">{{cite journal | author = Platt SR | title = The role of glutamate in central nervous system health and disease--a review | journal = Vet. J. | volume = 173 | issue = 2 | pages = 278–86 | year = 2007 | pmid = 16376594 | doi = 10.1016/j.tvjl.2005.11.007 }}</ref>，但不像[[配体门控离子通道|离子型受体]]，mGluRs并没有[[离子通道|离子通道]]的作用，而是通过{{le|生化级联反应|biochemical cascade}}来使其它蛋白（如[[离子通道|离子通道]]）变构<ref name="Gabriel">{{cite journal | author = Gabriel L, Lvov A, Orthodoxou D, Rittenhouse A, Kobertz W, Melikian H | title = The Acid-sensitive, Anesthetic-activated Potassium Leak Channel, KCNK3, Is Regulated by 14-3-3β-dependent, Protein Kinase C (PKC)-mediated Endocytic Trafficking | journal = JBC | volume = 287 | issue = 39 | pages = 32354–32366 | year = 2012 | pmid = 22846993 | doi = 10.1074/jbc.M112.391458 }}</ref>，从而改变[[突触|突触]]的兴奋性。比如{{le|神经传递|neurotransmission}}的突触前抑制<ref>Sladeczek F., Momiyama A.,Takahashi T. (1992). "Presynaptic inhibitory action of metabotropic glutamate receptor agonist on excitatory transmission in visual cortical neurons". Proc. Roy. Soc. Lond. B 1993 253, 297-303.</ref>和突触后反应的调节甚至诱导<ref name="Bonsi"/><ref name="Chu and Hablitz"/><ref name="Platt"/><ref name="Endoh">{{cite journal | author = Endoh T | title = Characterization of modulatory effects of postsynaptic metabotropic glutamate receptors on calcium currents in rat nucleus tractus solitarius | journal = Brain Res. | volume = 1024 | issue = 1-2 | pages = 212–24 | year = 2004 | pmid = 15451384 | doi = 10.1016/j.brainres.2004.07.074 }}</ref>。

mGluRs的{{le|G蛋白偶联受体寡聚物|GPCR oligomer|二聚化}}需要[[激动剂|激动剂]]的信号介导。<ref name="pmid22988116">{{cite journal |author=El Moustaine D, Granier S, Doumazane E, ''et al.'' |title=Distinct roles of metabotropic glutamate receptor dimerization in agonist activation and G-protein coupling |journal=Proc. Natl. Acad. Sci. U.S.A. |volume=109 |issue=40 |pages=16342–7 |year=2012 |pmid=22988116 |pmc=3479612 |doi=10.1073/pnas.1205838109 |url=}}</ref>
==分类==
八种不同的mGluR（mGluR<sub>1</sub>到mGluR<sub>8</sub>，基因为{{Gene|GRM1}}-{{Gene|GRM8}}）由其结构和生理活性<ref name="Ohashi"/>可分为I-III三个类型<ref name="Bonsi"/><ref name="Hinoi"/><ref name="Chu and Hablitz"/><ref name="Endoh"/>。mGluR还可以进一步分为亚型，如mGluR<sub>7a</sub>和mGluR<sub>7b</sub>。
