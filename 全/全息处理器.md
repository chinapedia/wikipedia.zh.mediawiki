'''全息处理器'''（Holographic Processing Unit, HPU）是可以与[[CPU|CPU]]与[[GPU|GPU]]共同运作的一块[[协处理器|协处理器]]芯片，由[[微软|微软]]在2015年1月21日公布。它集成于微软[[HoloLens|HoloLens]]之中，使得HoloLens能够在现实中叠加显示虚拟的全息物品（[[增强现实|增强现实]]）。<ref>{{cite web|title=Microsoft Reveals Windows Holographic, An Augmented Reality User Interface For The World|url=http://techcrunch.com/2015/01/21/microsoft-reveals-windows-holographic-an-augmented-reality-user-interface-for-the-world/|website=TechCrunch.com|accessdate=22 January 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Project HoloLens: Our Exclusive Hands-On With Microsoft’s Holographic Goggles|url=http://www.wired.com/2015/01/microsoft-hands-on/|website=Wired.com|accessdate=22 January 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Microsoft leaps into 3D computing with Windows Holographic and HoloLens|url=http://www.pcworld.com/article/2872707/microsoft-leaps-into-3d-computing-with-windows-holographic-and-hololens.html|website=PC World|accessdate=22 January 2015}}</ref>该处理器提供给诸如HoloLens一类的设备来确定当前用户的视觉范围，绘制周围环境以及处理身体姿势和语音命令。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Goldman|first1=David|title=Microsoft's insane holographic vision of the future|url=http://money.cnn.com/2015/01/21/technology/innovationnation/microsoft-windows-holographic/|website=CNN Money|accessdate=23 January 2015}}</ref>

==用途==
HPU是为微软HoloLens开发的，也是首次用于HoloLens这一需要为之开发新型协处理器芯片的增强现实的设备。目前还有待观察HPU在其他设备上的成功实现。不过，根据微软的Alex Kipman称，HPU能够“将能够处理实时其所有传感器产生的TB级数据。”<ref>{{cite web|last1=Adhikari|first1=Richard|title=Microsoft Just Handed Us Our Holographic Future|url=http://www.technewsworld.com/story/81614.html?rss=1|website=Tech News World|accessdate=23 January 2015}}</ref>
