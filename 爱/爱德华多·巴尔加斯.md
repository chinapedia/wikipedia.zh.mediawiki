'''伊度阿度·捷西斯·華加斯·羅哈斯'''（{{lang-es|'''Eduardo Jesús Vargas Rojas'''}}，{{IPA-es|eˈðwarðo ˈβaɾɣas}}；{{bd|1989年|11月20日|catIdx=Vargas Rojas}}）是一名[[智利|智利]][[足球員|足球員]]，司職[[前鋒|前鋒]]，亦可擔任中場邊路工作，現時效力[[墨西哥足球超級聯賽|墨超]]球隊[[新莱昂自治大学老虎足球俱乐部|新莱昂自治大学]]。

==俱樂部生涯==
<!--
{{TransH}}
巴尔加斯最早在[[倫卡|倫卡]]的業餘劇樂部踢球,因為家住得離[[圣地亚哥_(智利|圣地亚哥)]]的大俱樂部遠，他沒有加入任何的青年隊,直到2006年，他在第三級別的業餘聯賽踢球時候，他參加了一項在[[蒙特港|蒙特港]]舉行的錦標賽，當時他是隊內的頭號得分手。

不久之後,{{Tsl|en|Osvaldo Hurtado (footballer)|奧斯瓦爾多·烏爾塔多(智利足球運動員)|奧斯瓦爾多·烏爾塔多}} [[Osvaldo_Hurtado_(footballer)|Osvaldo Hurtado]] contacted Vargas with [[Jorge_Aravena|Jorge Aravena]] (In this moment coach of [[Cobreloa|Cobreloa]]), where he was submitted to several evaluations. ===智利大學城Universidad de Chile===
{{TransH}}
In January 2010, [[Club_Universidad_de_Chile|Club Universidad de Chile]] signed the winger from [[Cobreloa|Cobreloa]] for around $700.000. His high caliber performances in the [[2011_Chilean_Primera_División_season|Chilean Championship]] and the [[2011_Copa_Sudamericana|2011 Copa Sudamericana]] have piqued the interest of several prominent European clubs, including [[F.C._Internazionale_Milano|Internazionale]] with manager [[Claudio_Ranieri|Claudio Ranieri]] recently alluding to a January move for the forward.

In November 2011, he was chosen as a candidate for [[South_American_Footballer_of_the_Year|South American Footballer of the Year]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.latercera.com/noticia/deportes/2011/11/656-407381-9-eduardo-vargas-pelea-con-neymar-para-ser-el-mejor-de-america.shtml|title=Eduardo Vargas pelea con Neymar para ser el mejor de América |work=latercera.com |accessdate=25 November 2011|language=es}}</ref>(finally, was only defeated by [[Neymar|Neymar]], and got the second position), and in December he was chosen [[Chilean_Footballer_of_the_Year|Chilean Footballer of the Year 2011]].

Eduardo Vargas scored twice in the Final of the [[2011_Copa_Sudamericana|2011 Copa Sudamericana]]. He scored in the third minute and in the 87th. His two goals helped "La U" achieve its first international title, and he was elected the best player of the tournament. Also, Vargas is the top scorer of the [[Copa_Sudamericana|Copa Sudamericana]]'s history with 11 goals.

{{transH}}

We signed the contract and we also won the competition by strong Club who wanted the player. This is the testimony that our observatory is always open and we are continuously looking for young talents in the world that can make the case of Napoli and for the growth of our team".<ref>{{cite news|url=http://espnfc.com/news/story/_/id/1000667/napoli-president-announces-eduardo-vargas-signing?cc=5901|publisher=ESPN FC|title=Napoli Announce Vargas Signing|date=23 December 2011|accessdate=2011-12-23}}</ref>Vargas arrived at Napoli on 6 January 2012 and signed on 9 January.<ref>[http://www.emol.com/noticias/deportes/2012/01/09/520760/eduardo-vargas-en-su-presentacion-en-napoli-me-da-gusto-que-me-comparen-con-sanchez.html Eduardo Vargas en su presentación en Napoli: "Me da gusto que me comparen con Sánchez" (in Spanish)]</ref>
On 20 September 2012, the first match of Napoli's [[Europa_League|Europa League]] campaign, Vargas scored three goals in a 4-0 victory against [[AIK_Fotboll|AIK Fotboll]]. This was the first time that a Chilean player scored a [[hat-trick|hat-trick]] in a continental competition in Europe.
