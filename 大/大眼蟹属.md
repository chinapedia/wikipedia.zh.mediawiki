'''大眼蟹屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Macrophthalmus'''''}}）是[[短尾下目|短尾下目]][[沙蟹總科|沙蟹總科]]之下的一個[[屬_(生物)|屬]]<ref name="Grave">{{cite journal|journal=[[Raffles_Bulletin_of_Zoology|Raffles Bulletin of Zoology]] |year=2009 |volume=Suppl. 21 |pages=1–109 |title=A classification of living and fossil genera of decapod crustaceans |author=Sammy De Grave, N. Dean Pentcheff, Shane T. Ahyong ''et al.'' |url=http://rmbr.nus.edu.sg/rbz/biblio/s21/s21rbz1-109.pdf |format=[[PDF|PDF]] |language=en |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110606064728/http://rmbr.nus.edu.sg/rbz/biblio/s21/s21rbz1-109.pdf |archivedate=2011-06-06 }}</ref>，廣泛分布於[[印度太平洋區|印度太平洋區]]<ref>{{cite journal |author=M. A. Pancucci-Papadopoulou, M. Corsini-Foka & M. Naletaki |title=''Macrophthalmus graeffei'' A. Milne Edwards, 1873 (Crustacea: Brachyura: Macrophthalmidae): a new Indo-Pacific guest off Rhodes Island (SE Aegean Sea, Greece) |year=2010 |journal=[[Mediterranean_Marine_Science|Mediterranean Marine Science]] |url=http://www.medit-mar-sc.net/files/201005/06-114535Medit.Mar.Sci.v11n1_2010_xx-xxPANCUCCI_PAPADOPOULOU_411.pdf |format=[[PDF|PDF]] |volume=11 |issue=1 |pages=''in press'' |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110723204229/http://www.medit-mar-sc.net/files/201005/06-114535Medit.Mar.Sci.v11n1_2010_xx-xxPANCUCCI_PAPADOPOULOU_411.pdf |archivedate=2011-07-23 }}</ref>。本屬原來是[[沙蟹科|沙蟹科]][[大眼蟹亞科|大眼蟹亞科]]之下的一個屬，後來分類重組，成為了[[大眼蟹科|大眼蟹科]][[大眼蟹亞科|大眼蟹亞科]]之下的一個屬<ref name="e-info" />。
