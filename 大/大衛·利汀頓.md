'''-{zh-cn:戴维·罗伊·利丁顿; zh-tw:大衛·羅伊·利汀頓; zh-hk:李達德}-'''，[[英帝國司令勳章|CBE]]（{{lang-en|'''David Roy Lidington'''}}，{{bd|1956年|6月30日|||Lidington, David}}）是一位[[英国|英国]]政治人物、[[保守黨_(英國)|保守黨黨員]]。自1992年開始，他擔任[[艾爾斯伯里_(英國國會選區)|艾爾斯伯里]]選區選出的英國下議院議員，现任内阁办公厅大臣兼[[蘭開斯特公爵領地事務大臣|蘭開斯特公爵領地事務大臣]]。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://uk.reuters.com/article/uk-britain-politics-lidington/uk-pm-may-appoints-david-lidington-as-cabinet-office-minister-mays-office-idUKKBN1EX1D5?il=0|title=UK PM May appoints David Lidington as cabinet office minister - May's|last=Editorial|first=Reuters|work=U.K.|access-date=2018-01-08|language=en-GB}}</ref>

2010年到2018年，他任卡梅伦政府外交部歐洲副大臣，[[文翠珊|文翠珊]]內閣[[樞密院議長|樞密院議長]]兼[[下議院領袖|下議院領袖]]，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.number10.gov.uk/news/topstorynews/2010/05/her-majestys-government-49840 |title=Her Majesty’s Government |date=13 May 2010|accessdate=13 November 2010 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100515034600/http://www.number10.gov.uk/news/topstorynews/2010/05/her-majestys-government-49840 |archivedate=15 May 2010 }}</ref>[[司法大臣_(英国)|司法大臣]]兼[[大法官_(英國)|大法官]]等职，也曾任{{le|影子北愛爾蘭事務大臣|Shadow Secretary of State for Northern Ireland}}，{{le|影子環境食物及郊野事務大臣|Shadow Secretary of State for Environment, Food and Rural Affairs}}，[[英国外交大臣|外交大臣]][[特别顾问|特别顾问]]，[[英国内政大臣|内政大臣]]特别顾问等职。

2010年獲委任為[[英國樞密院|樞密院顧問官]]，2011年宣誓就職。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://webarchive.nationalarchives.gov.uk/20110221120034/http%3A//www.privy-council.org.uk/files/pdf/document2010-08-17-090328.pdf|title=ORDERS APPROVED AT THE PRIVY COUNCIL HELD BY THE QUEEN AT BUCKINGHAM PALACE ON 15TH DECEMBER 2010|language=en|website=The National Archive}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110405220459/http://www.privy-council.org.uk/files/pdf/website%20list%209%20feb%202011.pdf|title=ORDERS APPROVED AT THE PRIVY COUNCIL HELD BY THE QUEEN AT BUCKINGHAM PALACE ON 5TH APRIL 2011|language=en|website=The National Archive}}</ref>

== 早年经历 ==
利丁顿受教育于[[赫特福德郡|赫特福德郡]]与大伦敦接壤的{{le|埃尔斯特里|Elstree}}{{le|阿斯克男校|Haberdashers' Aske's Boys' School}}，后入[[剑桥大学悉尼·萨塞克斯学院|剑桥大学悉尼·萨塞克斯学院]]研习[[历史学|历史学]]，<ref name="auto">{{Cite web|url=https://www.gov.uk/government/people/david-lidington|title=The Rt Hon David Lidington MP – GOV.UK|website=www.gov.uk|accessdate=22 June 2017}}</ref>1988年以题为《伊丽莎白一世1558-1576年间{{le|财务法院|Exchequer of Pleas}}刑事成文法的强制执行》<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.co.uk/books/about/The_Enforcement_of_the_Penal_Statutes_at.html?id=SpkHGwAACAAJ&redir_esc=y|title=The Enforcement of the Penal Statutes at the Court of the Exchequer C.1558-c.1576|last=Lidington|first=David Roy|date=1988|publisher=University of Cambridge|language=en}}</ref>获历史学博士学位。在剑桥时，他还是{{le|剑桥大学保守协会|Cambridge University Conservative Association}}主席和{{le|剑桥大学学生会|Cambridge University Students' Union}}副主席。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.cam.ac.uk/news/a-new-home-for-cambridge-university-students-union|title=A new home for Cambridge University Students' Union|date=1 October 2007|work=University of Cambridge|access-date=22 June 2017}}</ref>

利丁顿曾在两家能源企业[[英国石油公司|英国石油公司]]和[[力拓集团|力拓集团]]工作过，1987年成为内政大臣[[道格拉斯·赫德|道格拉斯·赫德]]的特别顾问。1989年赫德转任外交大臣，利丁顿随之赴任。<ref name="auto" />

1987年利丁顿曾出马参选大伦敦泰晤士河南岸的[[沃克斯霍尔_(英国国会选区)|沃克斯霍尔选区]]未克。

== 私人生活 ==
利丁顿与其妻海伦育有四子。<ref name="auto" />他曾为[[公理会|公理会]]信徒，但现在为[[英国国教|英国国教]]徒。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.independent.co.uk/news/uk/politics/david-lidington-lgbt-rights-gay-marriage-theresa-may-justice-secretary-a7786751.html|title=Theresa May appoints Justice Secretary opposed to LGBT rights|date=12 June 2017|work=The Independent|accessdate=3 January 2018}}</ref>1979年他作为队长带领[[剑桥大学悉尼·萨塞克斯学院|剑桥大学悉尼·萨塞克斯学院]]队获《{{le|大学挑战赛|University Challenge}}》系列比赛的冠军。2002年该队又赢得该节目举办开播40周年纪念“总冠军”系列比赛。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/pressoffice/pressreleases/stories/2002/08_august/13/university_challenge_reunited.shtml|title=BBC – Press Office – University Challenge Reunited final|publisher=BBC|access-date=22 June 2017}}</ref>
