'''大卫·叶茨'''（{{lang-en|'''David Yates'''}}，{{bd|1963年|10月8日|catIdx=Y}}）是一位英国导演和制片人，曾执导一些故事片，短片和制作电视节目。

他最为主流所知的导演作品是哈利·波特电影系列的后四部：《[[哈利波特：鳳凰會的密令_(電影)|哈利·波特与凤凰社]]》，《[[哈利波特：混血王子的背叛_(電影)|哈利·波特与“混血王子”]]》，《[[哈利·波特与死亡圣器（上）|哈利·波特与死亡圣器（上）]]》和《[[哈利·波特与死亡圣器（下）|哈利·波特与死亡圣器（下）]]》 （2007{{ndash}}2011）。<ref name="DGA-DavidYatesDirectorProfile">{{Cite news|url=http://www.dga.org/Craft/DGAQ/All-Articles/1001-Spring-2010/Profile-David-Yates.aspx |title=The Wizard Behind the Camera |author=Andrew Pulver |publisher=DGA Quarterly, Directors Guild of America |accessdate=2011-03-04}}</ref><ref name="BAFTABritannia">{{cite news|url=http://losangeles.broadwayworld.com/article/BAFTA-Honors-John-Lasseter-and-David-Yates-1130-20110628 |title=BAFTA Honors John Lasseter and David Yates 11/30 |publisher=Broadway World (Los Angeles) |date=2011-06-28 |accessdate=2011-06-28 |quote=The worldwide success of Mr. Lasseter for Walt Disney and Pixar Animation Studios and Mr. Yates' contribution to the final four parts of the ‘Harry Potter' franchise makes them global wizards in their own right, and are delighted to honor these remarkable filmmakers with this year's Britannia Award.}}</ref>他这一系列的工作使他获得在商业和导演荣誉上的成功。

叶茨职业生涯早期曾执导各种短片，并成为一个多产的电视导演。曾经获得大不列颠导演协会杰出导演成就奖和[[黃金時段艾美獎|黃金時段艾美獎]]。<ref name="DGA-DavidYatesDirectorProfile"/><ref name="VirginMedia-DavidYates">{{cite news|url=http://www.virginmedia.com/movies/movieextras/top10s/potter-hbp-rumours.php |title=Ten Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince facts |publisher=Virgin Media |accessdate=2011-04-19|quote=Director David Yates, one of British TV's most respected director (thanks to the drama series State Of Play)...}}</ref>

叶茨是“Directors UK”的创始成员。<ref>{{cite news| url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/media/inside-story-in-the-right-direction--the-cream-of-britains-television-directing-talent-848048.html |title=Inside Story: In the right direction – the cream of Britain's television directing talent |work=[[The_Independent|The Independent]] |date=2008-06-16 |accessdate=2011-08-03 |location=London}}</ref>他是与[[华纳兄弟|华纳兄弟]]合作的导演和制片人。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.variety.com/article/VR1118053263.html |title=David Yates inks first-look deal with Warner Bros. |work=Variety.com |date=2012-04-27 |accessdate=2012-04-28}}</ref>

==早期生活==
1963年大卫·叶茨出生于英格兰兰开夏郡。小时候他的父母就去世了。<ref name="DavidYatesCollider2011">{{cite news| url=http://collider.com/david-yates-interview-harry-potter-deathly-hallows-part-2/101507/?replytocom=155909#respond |title=Exclusive: Director David Yates Talks HARRY POTTER AND THE DEATHLY HALLOWS – PART 2, Deleted Scenes, Future Projects, and More |work=Collider.com |date=2011-07-10 |accessdate=2011-07-11}}</ref>他在[[雨山_(英國)|雨山]]长大，在看了[[斯蒂芬·斯皮尔伯格|斯蒂芬·斯皮尔伯格]]1975年的电影《[[大白鯊_(電影)|大白鯊]]》后他决定投身电影创作。<ref name="DavidYatesEarlyLifeBAFTA">{{Cite news|url=http://www.sthelensstar.co.uk/news/8850608.Yates_enjoys_Harry_Potter_BAFTA_honour/ |title=St Helens-born David Yates enjoys Harry Potter BAFTA honour |work=St Helens Star |date= 2011-02-13|accessdate=2011-03-11}}</ref>在圣海伦斯学院完成社会学，政治学和文学的课程后他来到[[艾塞克斯大學|艾塞克斯大學]]。<ref name="Yahoo!Movies-DavidYatesBio">{{Cite news|url=http://movies.yahoo.com/movie/contributor/1807432789/bio |title=David Yates Biography|publisher=Yahoo! Movies |accessdate=2011-03-04}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.sthelensreporter.co.uk/news/local/harry_potter_director_celebrates_box_office_smash_1_734230 |title=Harry Potter director celebrates box office smash |work=St Helens Reporter |date=2009-08-03 |accessdate=2011-03-11}}</ref><ref name="David Yates Casarotto">{{cite news|url=http://www.casarotto.co.uk/assets/x/51023.pdf |title=David Yates |accessdate=2011-08-16 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110807145401/http://www.casarotto.co.uk/assets/x/51023.pdf |archivedate=7 八月 2011 }}</ref>在那里，他成立了影视制作协会。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.echo-news.co.uk/echofeatures/9835286.Directing_Harry_Potter_was_magic/ |title=Directing Harry Potter was Magic |work=Echo News |date=2012-07-26 |accessdate=2012-09-23 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120727123019/http://www.echo-news.co.uk/echofeatures/9835286.Directing_Harry_Potter_was_magic/ |archivedate=27 七月 2012 }}</ref>1987年他毕业获得[[文学士|学士]]学位。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.essex.ac.uk/news/event.aspx?e_id=4262 |title=Harry Potter director and Arctic explorer honoured at Graduation 2012 |work=University of Essex |date=2012-07-17 |accessdate=2012-09-23 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130125213452/http://www.essex.ac.uk/news/event.aspx?e_id=4262 |archivedate=2013年1月25日 }}</ref>

== 个人生活 ==
