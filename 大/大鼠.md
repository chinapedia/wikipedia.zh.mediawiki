'''大鼠'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Rattus norvegicus'' f. ''domestica'' <ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.ru/books?id=dxDS18Z-wLMC&pg=PA147&dq=was+aber+nicht+richtig+ist.+Korrekt+ist+die+Bezeichnung+Bezeichnung+rattus+norvegicus,+forma+domestica.+Also+Wanderratte,+domestizierte&hl=ru&sa=X&ei=x_j4Ur3UKoKq4AT9zYDYDA&ved=0CDMQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q&f=false|title=Das andere Rattenbuch|last=Weiss-Geissler|first=Erika|date=2004|publisher=BoD – Books on Demand|year=|isbn=9783833415258|location=|pages=|language=de|chapter=}}</ref>{{Rp|147}}）是[[褐家鼠|褐家鼠]]（''Rattus norvegicus''）的驯化型<ref name="langton-history">{{cite book | title = Rat: How the World's Most Notorious Rodent Clawed Its Way to the Top | first = Jerry | last = Langton | chapter = Entertainer, Test Subject, and Family Friend | chapterurl = https://books.google.com/books?id=n488n52-wYUC&pg=PA87&source=gbs_toc_r&cad=0_0 | publisher = Macmillan | accessdate=2009-01-08 | year = 2007 | isbn = 0-312-36384-2}}</ref>，生理和心理上都与野生的褐家鼠存在区别<ref name=Merck2009>{{cite book|title=Merck Veterinary Manual – Zoonoses:Introduction|url=http://www.merckvetmanual.com/mvm/index.jsp?cfile=htm/bc/220100.htm|chapter=Table on Global Zoonoses|year=2008|publisher=Merck and Co., Inc|accessdate=2009-01-11|chapter-url=http://www.merckvetmanual.com/mvm/htm/bc/tzns01.htm|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304200931/http://www.merckvetmanual.com/mvm/index.jsp?cfile=htm%2Fbc%2F220100.htm|archive-date=2016-03-04|dead-url=no}}</ref>。以褐家鼠为基础培育出的[[实验大鼠|动物实验用大鼠]]是[[医学|医学]]及生物学实验常用的[[实验动物|实验动物]]之一，存在多种品系<ref name="Vandenbergh">{{cite journal|last1=Vandenbergh|first1=J. G.|title=Use of House Mice in Biomedical Research|journal=ILAR Journal|date=2000-01-01|volume=41|issue=3|pages=133–135|doi=10.1093/ilar.41.3.133}}</ref>。

大鼠也是能在市面上买到的家养[[宠物|宠物]]之一<ref name=Merck2009/>。宠物大鼠有一定传播疾病的风险<ref name=IDPH-rat>{{cite web |title=Reducing the Risk of Human Infection from Pet Rodents |url=http://www.dph.illinois.gov/sites/default/files/publications/Rat-Borne-Diseases-2017.pdf |website=IDPH |accessdate=2019-02-01}}</ref>。比如，在与野生的褐家鼠接触后，仍然有传播{{le|链状芽孢杆菌|Streptobacillus moniliformis}}（''Streptobacillus moniliformis''）等病原体的风险<ref name="merck-general">{{cite web|url=https://www.merckvetmanual.com/exotic-and-laboratory-animals/rodents/mice-and-rats-as-pets|title=Mice and Rats as Pets|accessdate=2009-01-09|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170402230941/http://www.merckvetmanual.com/exotic-and-laboratory-animals/rodents/mice-and-rats-as-pets|archive-date=2017-04-02|dead-url=no}}</ref>。通过清洗笼舍、触摸后洗手等方法可以降低宠物大鼠传播疾病的风险<ref name=IDPH-rat/>。

==特征==
当作宠物饲养的大鼠与野生的褐家鼠毛色有很大区别，牠們的毛色大都是野生大鼠中很少有的突变型，包括肉桂色、浅黄褐色、米色、蓝色，以及巧克力色等，部分宠物大鼠的毛还会出现颜色变化，呈现深浅环纹等图案<ref name="ratgen">{{cite web|url=http://afrma.org/ratgenpart3.htm|title=Rat Genetics, part 3|first=Nichole|last=Royer|publisher=AFRMA|accessdate=2009-01-09|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110724225442/http://afrma.org/ratgenpart3.htm|archive-date=2011-07-24|dead-url=no}}</ref>。宠物大鼠的眼色包括黑色、粉色，以及红色，部分宠物大鼠两边眼睛的颜色不同<ref name=afrma-std>{{cite web|url=http://afrma.org/fancyrm.htm|title=American Fancy Rat and Mouse Association standards|publisher=AFRMA|accessdate=2006-11-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110708002619/http://www.afrma.org/fancyrm.htm|archive-date=2011-07-08|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name=carol>{{cite book|title=Rats|last=Daly|first=Carol H.|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=7aNn84hsNs8C&pg=PA15&dq=international+rat+standard#PPA15,M1|publisher=Barron's Educational Series|year=2002|isbn=0-7641-2012-3|edition=2|page=15}}</ref>。一部分实验用大鼠是[[白化症|白化]]品系，毛色为白色、眼色为红色<ref name="LaudetKuramoto2012">{{cite journal|last1=Laudet|first1=Vincent|last2=Kuramoto|first2=Takashi|last3=Nakanishi|first3=Satoshi|last4=Ochiai|first4=Masako|last5=Nakagama|first5=Hitoshi|last6=Voigt|first6=Birger|last7=Serikawa|first7=Tadao|title=Origins of Albino and Hooded Rats: Implications from Molecular Genetic Analysis across Modern Laboratory Rat Strains|journal=PLoS ONE|volume=7|issue=8|year=2012|pages=e43059|issn=1932-6203|doi=10.1371/journal.pone.0043059}}</ref>。

培育出的无尾和无毛大鼠面临[[伦理学|伦理]]争议，因为尾巴和毛对大鼠的正常生存具有重要意义，无毛大鼠和无尾大鼠的生存质量相比有尾巴和有毛的大鼠低<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nfrs.org/banned.html|title=Banned varieties|publisher=The National Fancy Rat Society|accessdate=2008-11-11|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081018171106/http://www.nfrs.org/banned.html|archivedate=2008-10-18}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.afrma.org/taillessrats.htm|title=Tailless Rats|last=Royer|first=Nichole|year=1998|work=AFRMA Rat & Mouse Tales|publisher=American Fancy Rat and Mouse Association|accessdate=2008-11-14|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170609151614/http://www.afrma.org/taillessrats.htm|archive-date=2017-06-09|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

===社会性===
大鼠是相对具有较强[[社会性|社会性]]的动物，同一个笼子中最好要有多隻大鼠，单独饲养一隻大鼠是不明智的<ref>{{Cite web|url= http://www.nfrs.org/company.html|title= National Fancy Rat Society|website = www.nfrs.org|access-date = 2016-02-25|deadurl = yes|archiveurl  = https://web.archive.org/web/20161124222202/http://www.nfrs.org/company.html|archivedate = 2016-11-24}}</ref>。雄性大鼠之间共同生活之初会发生打斗，但在分出优劣后，攻击行为就会停止<ref>{{cite web|title=Frequently Asked Questions Concerning Domestic Fancy Rats|url=http://www.altpet.net/rodents/rats/rf13.html|work=Can 2 male rats co-exist in one cage?|accessdate=2011-09-02|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180729141543/http://www.altpet.net/rodents/rats/rf13.html|archive-date=2018-07-29|dead-url=no}}</ref>。如果需要大鼠进行[[交配|交配]]，雌性和雄性大鼠的鼠龄都应该在六个月以下，而且同一笼中最好不应出现两隻或以上的雄性大鼠，一雌一雄或二雌一雄是较好的搭配。临交配才将雌性和雄性大鼠放在同一笼中，交配行为也不一定会发生<ref>{{cite web |title=RAT BREEDING COLONY MANAGEMENT |url=https://www.mcgill.ca/research/files/research/609_-_rat_breeding_colony_management_-_march_2016.pdf |website=McGill |accessdate=2019-02-01}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Ducommun|first1=Debbie|title=Introducing new Rats|url=http://www.ratfanclub.org/newrat.html|access-date=2019-02-01|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180706151747/http://ratfanclub.org/newrat.html|archive-date=2018-07-06|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.rattenforum.de/phpbb/faq/|title=Antworten auf oft gestellte Fragen zu Ratten|first=|last=Rattenforum|work=rattenforum.de|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150823105451/http://www.rattenforum.de/phpbb/faq/|archivedate=2015-08-23}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url = http://www.ratfanclub.org/newrat.html|title = the Rat Report|website = www.ratfanclub.org|access-date = 2016-02-25|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20160229064636/http://www.ratfanclub.org/newrat.html|archive-date = 2016-02-29|dead-url = no}}</ref>。

==公共卫生==
宠物大鼠有一定传播疾病的风险，牠們带上病原体的最直接途径是与野生褐家鼠接触时，野生褐家鼠将病原体传播给宠物大鼠<ref name=IDPH-rat/>。比如，宠物大鼠与野生褐家鼠接触之后，会有传播{{le|链状芽孢杆菌|Streptobacillus moniliformis}}（''Streptobacillus moniliformis''）等病原体的风险<ref name=IDPH-rat/><ref name="merck-general"/>。

2004年，美国的一场[[沙門氏菌|沙門氏菌]]爆发源头就是当地居民饲养的宠物大鼠<ref name="cdc">{{cite web|url=https://www.cdc.gov/mmwr/preview/mmwrhtml/mm5417a3.htm|title=Outbreak of Multidrug-Resistant Salmonella Typhimurium Associated with Rodents Purchased at Retail Pet Stores|date=2005-05-06|publisher=[[Center_for_Disease_Control|Center for Disease Control]]|accessdate=2008-11-14|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190128152728/https://www.cdc.gov/mmwr/preview/mmwrhtml/mm5417a3.htm|archive-date=2019-01-28|dead-url=no}}</ref>。2017年，也有一场由宠物大鼠引发的{{link-en|汉城病毒|Seoul orthohantavirus}}爆发<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cdc.gov/hantavirus/outbreaks/seoul-virus/index.html|title=Multi-state Outbreak of Seoul Virus|last=|first=|date=|website=www.cdc.gov|language=en-us|access-date=2017-05-27|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170518175533/https://www.cdc.gov/hantavirus/outbreaks/seoul-virus/index.html|archive-date=2017-05-18|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

能预防宠物大鼠传染人的方法有：清洗笼舍（但不能使用厨房水槽，最好戴上面罩和手套）、触摸后洗手、不亲吻宠物大鼠、不把牠們拿到离脸部太近的位置、不让宠物大鼠进入卧室和储存食物和烹饪食物的区域等<ref name=IDPH-rat/>。

==历史==
[[File:Rat-baiting6.jpg|thumb]]
对褐家鼠的驯化历史可追溯到18到19世纪。当时，欧洲开始出现以捕捉褐家鼠为生的{{link-en|捕鼠人|Rat-catcher}}职业。捕鼠人在捕捉到褐家鼠后，一般会将其杀死或出售给一种名为“{{link-en|鼠下料|Rat-baiting}}”的赌博活动<ref name="krinke">{{cite book | last = Krinke | first = George J. | others = Gillian R. Bullock (series ed.), Tracie Bunton (series ed.) | title = The Laboratory Rat (Handbook of Experimental Animals) | publisher = Academic Press | date = 2000-06-15 | pages = 3–16 | chapter = History, Strains and Models | isbn = 0-12-426400-X}}</ref>。所谓鼠下料是指，将[[狗|狗]]和一些褐家鼠放在同一个坑里，参与者就狗能在多久时间内杀死所有的鼠进行[[下注|下注]]。鼠下料直到20世纪初都是一种很受欢迎的活动，在这一游戏风靡的时期，一些捕鼠人和赌博游戏的参与者将一些毛色奇异的褐家鼠留下进行饲养<ref name="langton-history"/><ref name="rmca-history">{{cite web | url = http://www.rmca.org/Articles/domestication.htm | title = The Domestication of the Rat | last = Hilscher-Conklin | first = Caryl | publisher = Rat & Mouse Club of America | accessdate = 2008-11-10 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20180427055148/http://www.rmca.org/Articles/domestication.htm | archive-date = 2018-04-27 | dead-url = no }}</ref>。一般认为，自诩为[[维多利亚女王|维多利亚女王]]御用捕鼠人的{{le|杰克·布莱克 (捕鼠人)|Jack Black (rat catcher)|杰克·布莱克}}和当时伦敦最大的娱乐场所经营者{{le|傑米·肖|Jemmy Shaw}}两人开创了将褐家鼠当作宠物饲养的先河，现在许多市场上能买到的宠物大鼠都是由他们最先豢养的<ref name="langton-history" /><ref name="afrma-history">{{cite web | url = http://afrma.org/rminfo4a.htm | title = The History of Fancy Rats | publisher = American Fancy Rat and Mouse Association | accessdate = 2008-11-10 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20110724225441/http://www.afrma.org/rminfo4a.htm | archive-date = 2011-07-24 | dead-url = no }}</ref>。

人类使用大鼠进行动物实验的历史可以追溯到19世纪中叶<ref name=Sharp2012>{{cite book |author1=Patrick Sharp |author2=Jason Villano |title=The Laboratory Rat |date=2012 |publisher=CRC Press |isbn=978-1-4398-2987-5 |edition=The Second Edition}}</ref>{{rp|3}}<ref name=JHU-rat>{{cite web |title=The Rat |url=http://web.jhu.edu/animalcare/procedures/rat.html |website=Johns Hopkins University}}</ref>，最早使用大鼠进行动物实验的研究是1828年一项关于[[饥饿|饥饿]]的研究。可以认为大鼠是第一种专门驯化用于科学实验的动物<ref>{{cite book |editor=Georg J Krinke |title=Handbook of Experimental Animals: The Laboratory Rat |date=2000 |publisher=Academic Press |isbn=978-0-12-426400-7|doi=10.1016/B978-0-12-426400-7.X5037-7}}</ref>。第一种驯化[[实验大鼠|用于动物实验的大鼠]]是Wistar大鼠，由威斯塔研究所在1906年首先完成驯化<ref>{{cite web|url=https://rgd.mcw.edu/rgdweb/report/strain/main.html?id=13508588|title=Strain: WI|publisher=RGD|accessdate=2019-02-03}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://wistar.org/about-wistar/our-history/timeline|title=Evolution of The Wistar Institute|publisher=The Wistar Institution|accessdate=2019-02-03}}</ref>。
