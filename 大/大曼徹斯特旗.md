'''大曼徹斯特郡旗'''（'''Flag of Greater Manchester'''）是一面代表[[英國|英國]][[英格蘭|英格蘭]][[大曼徹斯特|大曼徹斯特]]都會區的非正式旗幟<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ons.gov.uk/ons/guide-method/geography/beginner-s-guide/maps/uk-counties-and-unitary-administrations-as-at-2009.pdf|title=UK counties and unitary administrations as at 2009|accessdate=2013-10-21|format=pdf|publisher=Office for National Statistics }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.flyingcolours.org/product-detail.php?ID=3267|title=Greater Manchester|accessdate=2013-08-12|work=County Flags|publisher=Flying Colours Flagmakers|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131023015517/http://www.flyingcolours.org/product-detail.php?ID=3267|archivedate=2013-10-23}}</ref>。大曼徹斯特旗雖然沒有正式註冊，但擁有特殊法律地位<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.gov.uk/government/uploads/system/uploads/attachment_data/file/15438/flying_flags_guide.pdf |title=Plain English guide to flying flags |accessdate=2013-08-12 |date=November 2012 |format=PDF |publisher=Department for Communities and Local Government |page=1 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131021091206/https://www.gov.uk/government/uploads/system/uploads/attachment_data/file/15438/flying_flags_guide.pdf |archivedate=2013-10-21 }}</ref>。大曼徹斯特旗為紅底，上有十個黃色的塔樓圖案，象徵大曼徹斯特都市區的十個自治市，包括博爾頓、伯里、曼徹斯特、奧爾德姆、羅奇代爾、塔姆塞德、特拉福德、索爾福德、斯托克波特、維根。大曼徹斯特旗現在在英國國家鐵路曼徹斯特皮卡迪利站前飄揚。
