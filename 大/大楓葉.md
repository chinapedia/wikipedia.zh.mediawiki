'''大楓葉'''（{{lang-en|'''Big Maple Leaf'''}}）是重100公斤（220磅、3,215[[金衡制|金衡制盎司]]）、面額1,000,000[[加元|加元]]的金幣，共鑄造6枚<ref name = "CAN-mint">{{cite web | title = The Million Dollar Coin – a true milestone in minting | publisher = [[加拿大皇家鑄幣廠|加拿大皇家鑄幣廠]] | url = http://www.mint.ca/store/mint/about-the-mint/million-dollar-coin-1600006 | accessdate = 2017-04-01 | language = en}}</ref><ref name = "1MG">{{cite news | title = Massive $1M gold coin from Canadian Mint stolen in Berlin | publisher = [[美聯社|美聯社]] | work = [[加拿大廣播公司|加拿大廣播公司]] | url = http://www.cbc.ca/news/entertainment/cdn-coin-berlin-stolen-1.4042325 | date = 2017-03-27 | accessdate = 2017-04-01}}</ref>，2017年3月時每枚價值約4,000,000[[美元|美元]]<ref name = "cnn">{{cite news | title = 200-lb gold coin worth estimated $4 million is stolen from German museum | first = Donie | last = O'Sullivan | publisher = [[CNN|CNN]] | url = http://edition.cnn.com/2017/03/27/europe/german-coin-stolen-trnd/index.html | date = 2017-03-27 | accessdate = 2017-04-01 | language = en}}</ref>。2017年3月27日凌晨其中一枚在[[柏林|柏林]][[博德博物館|博德博物館]]被竊。

== 外觀 ==
每枚大楓葉金幣直徑{{convert|53|cm|in}}，厚度{{convert|3|cm|in}}，純度999.99/1000，2007年時由[[加拿大皇家鑄幣廠|加拿大皇家鑄幣廠]]鑄造，該年10月被[[金氏世界紀錄大全|金氏世界紀錄大全]]認證為世界最大金幣。金幣正面為[[英國女王|英國女王]][[伊麗莎白二世|伊麗莎白二世]]，反面為[[楓葉|楓葉]]<ref name = "CAN-mint" /><ref name = "usa">{{cite news | title = Thieves nab 221-pound gold coin from German museum | publisher = [[今日美國|今日美國]] | url = http://www.usatoday.com/videos/news/world/2017/03/28/thieves-nab-221-pound-gold-coin-german-museum/99722222/ | date = 2017-03-28 | accessdate = 2017-04-01 | language = en}}</ref>。

== 失竊 ==
2017年3月27日凌晨其中一枚金幣在德國柏林[[博德博物館|博德博物館]]的錢幣櫃（{{lang|de|''Münzkabinett''}}）被竊<ref name = "1MG" />。該展示櫃以超過500,000枚錢幣收藏著名，包含100,000枚希臘時代和50,000羅馬時代錢幣，但只有極少部份展出。

[[加拿大皇家鑄幣廠|加拿大皇家鑄幣廠]]一位發言人表示，被竊金幣非該鑄幣廠所有。在鑄造第一枚金幣（存放在[[渥太華|渥太華]]）後，鑄幣廠另鑄造5枚金幣供私人收藏<ref name="1MG"/>。這枚金幣自2010年12月起在該博物館展出直到被竊<ref name = "smb">{{cite web | title = 1 million-dollar gold coin of Queen Elizabeth II on view in the Bode-Museum | author = Bode-Museum | website = [[博德博物館|博德博物館]] | url = http://www.smb.museum/en/whats-new/detail/queen-elizabeth-ii-auf-1-million-dollar-goldmuenze-im-bode-museum-zu-sehen.html | date = 2015-06-22 | accessdate = 2017-04-01 | language = en}}</ref><ref name = "smithsonian">{{cite news | title = World’s Largest Gold Coin Stolen From Berlin Museum | first = Brigit | last = Katz | publisher = [[史密森尼_(雜誌)|史密森尼雜誌]] | url=http://www.smithsonianmag.com/smart-news/worlds-largest-gold-coin-stolen-berlin-museum-180962690/ |date = 2017-03-28 | accessdate = 2017-04-01 | language = en}}</ref>。
