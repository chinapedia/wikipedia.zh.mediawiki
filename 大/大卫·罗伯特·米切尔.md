'''大卫·罗伯特·米切尔'''（David Robert Mitchell，{{bd|1974年||||catIdx=M}}）是一位[[美国电影|美国电影]]导演和编剧。主要作品有2010年的 ''The Myth of the American Sleepover''，以及2014年得到广泛好评的[[恐怖片|恐怖片]]《[[神秘追随|神秘追随]]》。<ref name="nyti_Indi">{{Cite web| title = Indie Horror Movie 'It Follows' Gets Wide Theater Release| last = Press | first = The Associated| work = New York Times| date = 27 March 2015| accessdate = 2015-03-28| url = http://www.nytimes.com/aponline/2015/03/27/arts/ap-us-film-it-follows.html}}</ref><ref name="past_TheF">{{Cite web| title = The Feeling of Following: An Interview with David Robert Mitchell| author = Andy Crump| work = Paste Magazine| date = 2015-03-17| accessdate = 2015-03-28| url = http://www.pastemagazine.com/articles/2015/03/the-feeling-of-following-an-interview-with-david-r.html}}</ref>

==生平==
他生于[[密歇根州|密歇根州]]的Clawson，毕业于[[佛罗里达州立大学|佛罗里达州立大学]]<ref name="detr_Davi">{{Cite web| title = David Robert Mitchell brings the spooky to Detroit| author = Adam Graham| work = Detroit News| date = 20 March 2015| accessdate = 2015-03-28| url = http://www.detroitnews.com/story/entertainment/2015/03/20/david-robert-mitchell-brings-spooky-detroit/25045183/}}</ref>。
