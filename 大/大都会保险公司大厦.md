'''大都会保险公司大楼'''（MetLife Building）是位于[[纽约市|纽约市]][[曼哈顿中城|曼哈顿中城]][[中央车站|中央车站]]上方的[[摩天大楼|摩天大楼]]，具体地址为[[公园大道|公园大道]]200号，东45街交口。于1958–63年修建，最初名为[[泛美航空|泛美航空]]大厦（Pan Am Building），它是纽约第17高、美国第50高的建筑。

==历史==
1960年9月，[[泛美航空|泛美航空]]创始人[[胡安·特里普|胡安·特里普]]以1.15亿美元的价格，和大楼的开发商签下25年的使用合同，租下大楼56,900平方米的使用面积，共15个楼层，以及位于45街的售票处。<ref name=Clausenp137>Clausen, p. [https://books.google.com/books?id=E6qRuyzOogIC&pg=PA137 137].</ref>

1963年泛美大厦（当时的名称）投入使用，是当时面积最大的办公大楼<ref>Horsley, Carter C. [http://www.thecityreview.com/panam.html The MetLife Building], The Midtown Book. Accessed September 30, 2007. "When it was completed, the {{convert|2400000|sqft|m2|abbr=on}} building became the world's largest office building in bulk, a title it would lose a few years later to 55 Water Street downtown."</ref>，由于建筑的比例和巨大的身形，一开始并不被看好。泛美大厦的落成，让位于其北侧的[[汉姆斯利大厦|汉姆斯利大厦]]和南侧的[[大中央總站|大中央總站]]瞬间显得无比矮小。<ref>{{cite news |last=Gray |first=Christopher |date=October 7, 2001 |title=Streetscapes/The MetLife Building, Originally the Pan Am Building; Critics Once Called It Ugly; Now They're Not Sure |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2001/10/07/realestate/streetscapes-metlife-building-originally-pan-am-building-critics-once-called-it.html?pagewanted=all |work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] |accessdate=September 30, 2015}}</ref>泛美大厦保持世界最大办公面积的记录，直到1970年被[[世界贸易中心|世界贸易中心]]（2001年毁于[[911袭击|911袭击]]）超越，后来1972年落成的[[沃特街55号|沃特街55号]]也拥有更大的办公面积。

作为大楼最大的租户，泛美在纽约市立法禁止公司在大楼顶部安装商标前，把巨大的“PAN AM”标志挂在大楼的顶端。<ref name=FinalPanAmDeparture>{{Cite news |last=Dunlap |first=David W. |date=September 4, 1992 |title=Final Pan Am Departure |url=https://www.nytimes.com/1992/09/04/nyregion/final-pan-am-departure.html |work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] |access-date=July 27, 2017}}</ref>南北侧各有两个4.6米高的“PAN AM”标志，东西两侧为7.6米高的泛美地球商标。<ref>Schneider, Daniel B. [https://query.nytimes.com/gst/fullpage.html?res=9401E6DA1739F936A35752C0A961958260 "F.Y.I."], ''[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]'', January 5, 1997. Accessed September 30, 2007. "Q. I recall that it was 1963 when the huge Pan Am letters were put atop what is now the Met Life building and that it was 1992 when they were taken down.... A. Most of the letters and the accompanying logos did not survive removal; exceptions are in warehouses.... The letters, each about {{convert|15|ft|m|1}} tall, and the logos—{{convert|25|ft|m|sing=on}}-wide globes—had to be cut into sections and pulled up onto the roof by technicians from Universal Unlimited, who built and installed their replacements, the Met Life signs."</ref>

泛美一直使用15个楼层，直至1981年，[[大都會人壽保險|大都會人壽保險]]买下大楼。到1991年，泛美只剩下租用4个楼层，同年泛美把公司总部迁移到[[迈阿密|迈阿密]]，不久后泛美倒闭。泛美倒闭后，大都会人寿保险总裁罗伯特·施瓦茨表示，将把大楼顶上“PAN AM”的标志取下。当时大都会人寿保险的总部位于[[大都会人寿保险大楼|大都会人寿保险大楼]]。<ref name=FinalPanAmDeparture>{{Cite news |last=Dunlap |first=David W. |date=September 4, 1992 |title=Final Pan Am Departure |url=https://www.nytimes.com/1992/09/04/nyregion/final-pan-am-departure.html |work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] |access-date=July 27, 2017}}</ref>

2005年，大都会人寿保险以17.2亿美元把大楼出售，刷新了当时大楼成交记录。买方是给地产商{{link-en|铁狮门地产公司|Tishman Speyer}}，纽约市府退休员工基金和纽约市府退休教师基金。<ref>Ramirez, Anthony. "[https://query.nytimes.com/gst/fullpage.html?res=9901E5DA113FF931A35757C0A9639C8B63 MetLife Sells 2nd Building, A Landmark On Park Ave.]" ''[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]''. April 2, 2005. Retrieved on August 25, 2009.</ref>2015年，有消息披露，全美最富有的房地产开发商[[唐纳德·布伦|唐纳德·布伦]]名下的[[欧文公司|欧文公司]]拥有该大楼97.3%的股份。铁狮门地产公司继续担任大楼的管理方，但只拥有不到3%的股权。<ref>{{cite news|author=Mulholland, Sarah and de Jong, David|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2015-03-11/billionaire-bren-is-secret-owner-of-nyc-s-metlife-tower|work=[[Bloomberg_News|Bloomberg News]]|date= March 11, 2015|title=Billionaire Bren Is Secret Owner of NYC’s MetLife Tower}}</ref>

2017年，楼顶的“MetLife”标志由[[霓虹|霓虹]]灯管改为LED，以节省能源。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://nypost.com/2017/06/07/metlife-building-will-get-a-pricey-new-sign-to-save-energy/|title=MetLife building is changing its iconic neon sign|last=Weiss|first=Lois|date=June 7, 2017|website=New York Post|access-date=September 10, 2017}}</ref>

==直升机服务==
1965年12月21日，纽约航空开始使用[[CH-46海骑士直升机|107-II直升机]]从当时泛美大厦楼顶的[[直升机停机坪|停机坪]]，运送旅客到[[约翰·肯尼迪国际机场|肯尼迪国际机场]]。1968年2月18日，用旅客不足暂停营业。<ref name="Schneider">Schneider, Daniel B. [https://www.nytimes.com/1999/07/25/nyregion/fyi-729590.html "F.Y.I."] ''[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]'', July 25, 1999. Accessed September 30, 2007. "Q. Back in the 1960s and 1970s, helicopters bound for Kennedy International Airport used to take off from a deck atop the old Pan Am Building. Why was the service halted? A. As many as 360 helicopter flights a day were planned by New York Airways after the 59-story Pan Am building was completed in 1963, but a bitter public outcry delayed the first few flights until Dec. 21, 1965.... The operation proved unprofitable, however, since the helicopters carried an average of only eight passengers, and the heliport, which had cost $1 million to build, closed in 1968.... After another round of hearings—and renewed protests—flights resumed in February 1977. Three months later, the landing gear on one of the Sikorsky S-61 helicopters collapsed while passengers were boarding, flipping it on its side and sending a 20-foot rotor blade skidding across the roof and over the west parapet wall. Within hours, the heliport was closed indefinitely."</ref><ref>Hudson, Edward. [https://www.nytimes.com/1968/02/16/archives/helicopter-service-from-roof-of-pan-am-building-suspended-pan-am.html "Helicopter Service From Roof Of Pan Am Building Suspended; PAN AM SUSPENDS COPTER SERVICES"], ''[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]'', February 19, 1968. Accessed September 30, 2007. "Helicopter operations from the 59-story roof of the Pan Am Building were suspended last night as a result of a dispute over the future financial support of the operation by Pan American World Airways."</ref>

1977年纽约航空恢复了这趟短途接送服务，并使用{{link-en|西科斯基S-61|Sikorsky S-61}}直升机。1977年5月16日，一架S-61L直升机载着20名旅客刚降落不到一分钟，右侧的[[起落架|起落架]]突然断裂，导致[[旋翼|旋翼]]还在高速运转的直升机侧翻。S-61L共有5根长达6.1米（20英尺）的旋翼，其中的一根从根部断裂，飞向刚刚下飞机的乘客，导致其中的三人当场死亡，另有一人送医不治。<ref name="19770517+FortScottTribune+Helicopter">[[Associated_Press|Associated Press]]. "Five Dead in Helicopter Crash". ''[[The_Fort_Scott_Tribune|The Fort Scott Tribune]]'': Tuesday, May 17, 1977, page 1.</ref>这根旋翼飞出大楼顶楼后，砸向地面，在[[麦迪逊大道|麦迪逊大道]]和43街的拐角处，砸死一名女性行人。另有两人受到重伤。<ref>NTSB. [http://ntlsearch.bts.gov/tris/record/tris/01077007.html Aircraft Accident Report - New York Airways, Inc., Sikorsky S-61L, N619PA Pan Am Building Heliport, New York, New York, May 16, 1977]. ([http://www.airdisaster.com/reports/ntsb/AAR77-09.pdf PDF] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070930023757/http://www.airdisaster.com/reports/ntsb/AAR77-09.pdf |date=September 30, 2007 }})</ref><ref name="19770517+Beaver+Helicopter">UPI. Helicopter Crash Kills Five. ''[[The_Beaver_County_Times|The Beaver County Times]]'' (Penn.): Tuesday, May 17, 1977, A-13.</ref>从此这项短途的直升机服务被迫停止，并再也没有启用。<ref name="Schneider" />
