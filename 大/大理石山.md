'''大理石山'''（{{lang-en|Marble Hill}}）是[[紐約市|紐約市]]的一個地區，位於[[曼哈頓|曼哈頓]]北部，也是曼哈頓區唯一一個位於北美大陸本土的飛地。因哈林河的建設，大理石山在1895年至1914年期間曾是一個島嶼。1914年以後，大理石山和北美大陸本土的[[布朗克斯|布朗克斯]]相連<ref>[http://www.nyc.gov/html/dcp/html/bytes/cdguide.shtml Tax Block & Tax Lot Base Map Files on CD-ROM] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070805015203/http://www.nyc.gov/html/dcp/html/bytes/cdguide.shtml |date=2007-08-05 }}, [[New_York_City_Department_of_City_Planning|New York City Department of City Planning]]. Accessed July 26, 2007. "Marble Hill is a neighborhood that is part of the borough of Manhattan but is administratively often included with neighboring areas of the Bronx. Parts of Marble Hill are within Bronx Community District 7; the rest is within Bronx Community District 8."</ref>，但在行政區劃上屬於曼哈頓區。
