在[[幾何學|幾何學]]中，'''大截半二十面體'''是一種非凸均勻多面體，屬於星形多面體，其在非凸均勻多面體被編號為U<sub>54</sub><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://america.pink/great-icosidodecahedron_1794145.html|title=Great icosidodecahedron: Related polyhedra|publisher=america.pink||archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20160831084023/http://america.pink/great-icosidodecahedron_1794145.html| archivedate =2016-08-31}}</ref>、在溫尼爾多面體模型被編號為[[溫尼爾多面體模型列表|W]]<sub>94</sub><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://colinspics.org/life/maths/polyhedra/W94%20Great%20Icosidodecahedron.htm|title=W<sub>94</sub> Great Icosidodecahedron|publisher=colinspics|archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160831083848/http://colinspics.org/life/maths/polyhedra/W94%20Great%20Icosidodecahedron.htm | archivedate =2016-08-31}}</ref>。其在施萊夫利符號中可以用r{3,5/2}表示，其為[[大星形十二面體|大星形十二面體]]和[[大二十面體|大二十面體]]的[[截半_(幾何)|截半多面體]]。

== 性質 ==
大截半二十面體共有32個面、60條邊和30個頂點<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mathconsult.ch/static/unipoly/54.html|title=Uniform Polyhedra 54: Great Icosidodecahedron|publisher=mathconsult|archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160831084119/https://www.mathconsult.ch/static/unipoly/54.html | archivedate =2016-08-31}}</ref>，其30個面分別由20個正三角形和12個五角星組成<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://websites.networksolutions.com/share/scrapbook/61/610304/GreatIcosiDodecahedron.pdf|title=Construction of Great IcosiDodecahedron|publisher=network solutions||archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160831084223/http://websites.networksolutions.com/share/scrapbook/61/610304/GreatIcosiDodecahedron.pdf | archivedate =2016-08-31}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.polyedergarten.de/polyhedrix/e_gid1.htm|title = Gid (Great Icosidodecahedron)-Facetings|publisher=polyedergarten|archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20160323105458/http://polyedergarten.de/polyhedrix/e_gid1.htm| archivedate =2016-03-23}}</ref>，每個頂點都是2個三角形和2個五角星的公共頂點，在[[施萊夫利符號|施萊夫利符號]]中可以用<math>\left\{ \begin{matrix}
3 \\
\frac{5}{2}
\end{matrix} \right\}</math>來表示<ref>{{mathworld | urlname = GreatIcosidodecahedron| title = Great Icosidodecahedron}}</ref>。

== 對偶多面體 ==
{{main|大菱形三十面體}}
大截半二十面體的對偶多面體是大菱形三十面體，是一種等面多面體，所有面都由[[菱形|菱形]]組成，每個菱形被其他面相交的部分皆相同。但由於其不具有點可遞的性質，也就是說，其並非所有頂角等角，因此不是均勻多面體。

== 作為凹多面體 ==
作為凹多面體，其可以視為由60個左右對稱的五邊形、12個正五邊形和60個鷂形組成，此種結構非常適合用於製作出大截半二十面體的紙模型<ref name = "book_smith1997cut">{{cite book|title=Cut and Assemble 3-D Star Shapes|author=Smith, A.G.|isbn=9780486296517|series=Dover Children's Activity Bks|url=https://books.google.com.tw/books?id=ypypFjGwFPEC|year=1997|publisher=Dover Publications, Incorporated|pages=p. 23}}</ref>。

=== 對偶複合體 ===
大截半二十面體與其對偶的複合體為'''複合大截半二十面體大菱形三十面體'''。其共有62個面、120條邊和62個頂點，其尤拉示性數為4，虧格為-1，具有12個非凸面，在威佐夫記號中以(2 {{!}} <sup>5</sup>/<sub>2</sub> 3)表示<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://bulatov.org/polyhedra/uniform_compounds/uc59.html|title=compound of great icosidodecahedron and great rhombic triacontahedron|publisher=bulatov.org|archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20150906151137/http://bulatov.org/polyhedra/uniform_compounds/uc59.html | archivedate =2015-09-06}}</ref>。
