'''大军团广场'''（Grand Army Plaza）位于[[曼哈顿|曼哈顿]][[59街|59街]]与[[第五大道|第五大道]]交叉路口，[[中央公园|中央公园]]的东南角，[[广场饭店|广场饭店]]前。兴建广场的构思最早是由雕塑家卡尔·比特在1898年建议。它得名于[[波多马克军团|波多马克军团]]。它是由Carrère and Hastings建筑事务所设计，于1916年完成。

广场为椭圆形，由59街平分为南北两部分。北部有[[威廉·特库姆塞·舍曼|谢尔曼]]的金色骑马雕像，作者是奥古斯塔斯·圣·高登斯。广场南部有[[约瑟夫·普立策|约瑟夫·普立策]]出资的普利策纪念喷泉。喷泉顶部的罗马丰腴女神波蒙那铜像由卡尔·比特设计。1990年，广场进行了整修，耗资370万美元。<ref name="Citycyclopedia">Kenneth T. Jackson: ''The Encyclopedia of New York City'': The New York Historical Society; Yale University Press; 1995. P. 497-498.</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nytimes.com/1990/03/01/garden/currents-grand-army-plaza-s-rather-grand-history.html|title=Currents; Grand Army Plaza's Rather Grand History|last= LOUIE|first=ELAINE |date=1 March 1990|work=[[New_York_Times|New York Times]]|accessdate=14 April 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nytimes.com/1990/06/28/arts/review-architecture-a-restored-grand-army-plaza-with-a-new-coat-for-the-general.html?pagewanted=1|title=Review/Architecture; A Restored Grand Army Plaza, With a New Coat for the General|last= GOLDBERGER|first=PAUL |date=28 June 1990|work=New York Times|accessdate=14 April 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nytimes.com/1990/07/02/opinion/grand-army-plaza-redressed.html?pagewanted=1|title=Grand Army Plaza, Redressed|date=2 July 1990|work=New York Times|accessdate=14 April 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|last=Morgan|first=Ann Lee|title=The Oxford dictionary of American art and artists|publisher=Oxford University Press|date=2007|series=Oxford reference online|page=45|isbn=9780195128789|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=pyvjWAcwnHEC&pg=PA45}}</ref> 著名的[[广场饭店|广场饭店]]即得名于此广场，并坐落在广场西南角。
