==大洋洲的定義==
大洋洲，世界面積最小的洲，佔全球總陸地面積的6%<ref name="Scholastic Atlas of the World">{{cite book|title=Scholastic Atlas of the World|year=2003}} "Oceania is the smallest of all the continents"</ref>，是位於南半球的一個大洲。主要分為兩部分：澳大利亞大陸與太平洋各島嶼。<ref name="Lewis 1997 32">{{cite book|last=Lewis |first=Martin W. |authorlink= |coauthors=Kären E. Wigen |title=The Myth of Continents: a Critique of Metageography |year=1997 |publisher=University of California Press |location=Berkeley |id=ISBN 0-520-20742-4, ISBN 0-520-20743-2 |page=32 |chapter= |quote= Interestingly enough, the answer [from a scholar who sought to calculate the number of continents] conformed almost precisely to the conventional list: North America, South America, Europe, Asia, Oceania (Australia plus New Zealand), Africa, and Antarctica.}}</ref>大洋洲面積約為8,525,989平方公里（3,291,903平方英里）<ref name="millenniumindicators.un.org">[http://millenniumindicators.un.org/unsd/methods/m49/m49regin.htm Composition of macro geographical (continental) regions, geographical sub-regions, and selected economic and other groupings], United Nations Statistics Division. Revised August 28, 2007. Accessed on line October 11, 2007.</ref>，人口約為35,670,000人，是世界人口第二少的洲，僅次於[[南极洲|南极洲]]。<ref name="Philip's E.A.E.P Atlas">{{cite book| url=http://books.google.ch/books?id=14kIKhpAcUYC&printsec=frontcover&source=gbs_ge_summary_r&cad=0#v=onepage&q&f=false|title= Philip's E.A.E.P Atlas|page=79|year=2003}}</ref>包括[[美國|美國]]在內的一些國家並不承認大洋洲為一個洲，認為在[[地質學|地質學]]上，澳大利亞大陸是一個獨立的洲──[[澳洲|澳洲]] （Australia）。<ref>{{cite book |last=Theroux |first=Paul |title=The happy isles of Oceania: Paddling the Pacific |year=1992 |publisher=Penguin |location=London |isbn=0-14-015976-2 |pages= |chapter= |quote = }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |quotes= |last=Wareham |first=Evelyn |authorlink= |title=From Explorers to Evangelists: Archivists, Recordkeeping, and Remembering in the Pacific Islands |journal=Archival Science |volume=2 |issue=3–4 |pages=187–207 |doi=10.1007/BF02435621 |date=September 2002}}</ref>

大洋洲包括了印度尼西亞的巴布亞地區（[[巴布亞省|巴布亞省]]與[[西巴布亞省|西巴布亞省]]）、[[巴布亞新畿內亞|巴布亞新畿內亞]]、[[澳大利亞|澳大利亞]]、[[新西蘭|新西蘭]]及[[南太平洋|南太平洋]]的[[島國|島國]]。<ref>{{Cite journal

大洋洲和亞洲之間的邊界至今還沒有統一的定義。在地理角度上，整個[[新畿內亞島|新畿內亞島]]皆位於[[印度-澳洲板塊|印度-澳洲板塊]]內<ref>[http://www.columbia.edu/cu/pr/95/18688.html (1995) Geologists Find: An Earth Plate Is Breaking in Two]</ref>，因此新畿內亞島和印尼其他島嶼之間的海域是大洋洲和亞洲之間的邊界。<ref>MacKay (1864, 1885) ''Elements of Modern Geography'', p 283</ref>在政治角度上，印度尼西亞是一個亞洲國家，而巴布亞新畿內亞則是一個大洋洲國家，故大洋洲和亞洲之間的邊界為印度尼西亞與巴布亞新畿內亞之間的國界。<ref>Douglas & Ballard (2008) ''Foreign bodies: Oceania and the science of race 1750–1940''</ref>在[[生物地理學|生物地理學]]角度上，[[馬來群島|馬來群島]]與大洋洲的生態是以[[華萊士線|華萊士線]]劃分，故故大洋洲和亞洲之間的邊界為華萊士線本身。<ref name="Mayr44">{{Citation  | last = Mayr  | first = E.  | title = Wallace's Line in the Light of Recent Zoogeographic Studies  | journal = The Quarterly Review of Biology  | volume = 19 | issue = 1  | pages = 1–14  | year = 1944  | jstor = 2808563}}</ref><ref name="Udvardy 1975">Udvardy. 1975. ''A classification of the biogeographical provinces of the world''</ref>在文化角度上，大洋洲文化圈主要覆蓋[[美拉尼西亞|美拉尼西亞]]、[[密克罗尼西亚群岛|密克罗尼西亚群岛]]、[[玻里尼西亞|玻里尼西亞]]、澳大利亞與紐西蘭，因此大洋洲和亞洲之間的邊界是以美拉尼西亞西北方與馬來群島接壤之地劃分。<ref>[http://hpgl.stanford.edu/publications/PNAS_2000_v97_p8225.pdf Polynesian origins: Insights from the Y chromosome] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20030811071633/http://hpgl.stanford.edu/publications/PNAS_2000_v97_p8225.pdf |date=2003-08-11 }}</ref>一小部份人甚至會將馬來群島併入大洋洲中。<ref name=Wallace>{{cite web

有不少大洋洲地區雖然位於大洋洲內，但在文化、歷史及政治方面均與其他洲有較大的聯繫。例如屬於[[南美洲|南美洲]]國家[[智利|智利]]的[[复活节岛|复活节岛]]<ref>{{citation|url=http://www.portalrapanui.cl/easterisland/index.htm "Welcome to Rapa Nui - Isla de Pascua - Easter Island"|title=Portal RapaNui, the island's official website|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111101221510/http://www.portalrapanui.cl/easterisland/index.htm|archivedate=2011-11-01}}</ref>；屬於[[北美洲|北美洲]]國家[[美國|美國]]的[[夏威夷|夏威夷]]<ref>{{citation|last=Cooper|first=George|last2=Daws|first2=Gavan|title=Land and power in Hawaii|publisher=University of Hawaii Press|year=1990|pages= 4|isbn=978-0-8248-1303-1}}</ref>；屬於[[亞洲|亞洲]]國家[[日本|日本]]的[[小笠原群島|小笠原群島]]、[[南鳥島|南鳥島]]、[[沖鳥礁|沖鳥礁]]、[[火山列島|火山列島]]<ref>Tanaka, Hiroyuki (1993).
