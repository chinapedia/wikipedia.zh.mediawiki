'''大衛·F·桑德柏格'''（{{lang-en|'''David F. Sandberg'''}}，{{bd|1981年|12月23日}}）是一位[[瑞典|瑞典]]男導演、監製和編劇，他時常製作多部低預算的恐怖短片。2016年，桑德柏格所執導的電影導演處女作《[[鬼關燈|鬼關燈]]》是改編自他自編自導的2013年短片《Lights Out》，電影獲得了正面的評價和成功的票房<ref name="toro_'Lig">{{Cite web| title = 'Lights Out': David F. Sandberg goes from YouTube to Hollywood| author = Slotek, Jim| work = Toronto Sun| date = 2016-07-21| accessdate = 2016-07-25| url = http://www.torontosun.com/2016/07/21/lights-out-david-f-sandberg-goes-from-youtube-to-hollywood}}</ref>。

== 職業生涯 ==
債務累累的桑德柏格於2013年拍了恐怖短片《Lights Out》而在網上獲得了關注。後來，他改編自其短片而執導了《[[鬼關燈|鬼關燈]]》，電影於2016年上映並獲得了極高的讚譽<ref name="lati_HowD">{{Cite web| title = How David Sandberg turned a viral-short sensation into a horror feature with 'Lights Out'| last = Zeitchik | first = Steven| work = Los Angeles Times| date = 8 June 2016| accessdate = 2016-07-25| url = http://www.latimes.com/entertainment/movies/la-et-mn-laff-lights-out-david-sandberg-20160602-snap-story.html}}</ref><ref name="forb_'Lig">{{Cite web| title = 'Lights Out' Director David F. Sandberg Talks Going From Zero To $5 Million Budgets And His Hollywood Horror Story| last = Thompson | first = Simon| work = Forbes| date = 21 July 2016| accessdate = 2016-07-25| url = http://www.forbes.com/sites/simonthompson/2016/07/21/lights-out-director-talks-going-from-zero-to-5-million-budgets-and-his-hollywood-horror-story/#3e1054a71e6f}}</ref>。2016年3月，他被聘請擔任《[[安娜貝爾：造孽|安娜貝爾：造孽]]》的導演，該片為2014年恐怖片《[[安娜貝爾_(電影)|安娜貝爾]]》的續集《[[安娜貝爾：造孽|安娜貝爾：造孽]]》，定於2017年上映<ref name="holl_HowN">{{Cite web| title = How New Line Cinema Is Making a Killing in Horror| author = Borys Kit| work = The Hollywood Reporter| date = 2016-07-21| accessdate = 2016-07-25| url = http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/how-new-line-cinema-is-912458}}</ref><ref name="pare_‘Ann">{{Cite web| title = ‘Annabelle 2’ Release Date, Latest News & Updates: ‘Annabelle’ Sequel Bringing More Evil Dolls To Haunt Orphans? Instagram Photos Hint Start Of Filming| author = Kristine Walker| work = Parent Herald| date = 2016-07-02| accessdate = 2016-07-25| url = http://www.parentherald.com/articles/52395/20160702/annabelle-2-release-date-latest-news-updates-annabelle-sequel-bringing-more-evil-dolls-haunt-orphans-instagram-photos-hint-start-filming.htm}}</ref>。2017年7月，桑德柏格成為了[[DC擴展宇宙|DC擴展宇宙]]電影《[[沙贊_(電影)|沙贊]]》的導演，該片改編自[[DC漫畫|DC漫畫]]旗下的[[驚奇隊長_(DC漫畫)|同名角色]]<ref name="Shazam">{{Cite web| title ='Shazam!' Is Next DC Movie to Shoot (Exclusive)| author = Borys Kit| work = Hollywoodreporter| url = http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/heat-vision/shazam-is-next-dc-movie-shoot-1022821| date = 2016-07-02| accessdate = 2016-07-25}}</ref>。
