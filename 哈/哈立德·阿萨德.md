'''哈立德·阿萨德'''（{{lang-ar|خالد الأسعد }}；{{bd|1934年||2015年|8月18日|catIdx=K}}<ref name="mr palm">{{cite web |url=http://www.bbc.com/news/world-middle-east-33991863|title=Profile: Khaled al-Asaad, Syria's 'Mr Palmyra'|date=19 August 2015 |accessdate=21 August 2015 |work=BBC}}</ref>）是叙利亚的一位考古学家。

==生平==
哈立德出生在叙利亚的[[塔德莫|塔德莫]]（又称巴尔米拉新城），在那里度过了他大部分的人生<ref name="independent.co.uk">{{cite web|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/people/khaled-alasaad-authority-on-the-antiquities-of-the-syrian-city-of-palmyra-who-was-devoted-to-studying-and-protecting-its-treasures-10464467.html|title=Khaled al-Asaad: Authority on the antiquities of the Syrian city of Palmyra who was devoted to studying and protecting its treasures|work=The Independent|accessdate=22 August 2015}}</ref>。曾在[[大马士革大学|大马士革大学]]学习，并获得[[历史学|历史学]]文凭<ref name=Davies2015/>。1954年加入叙利亚的[[阿拉伯复兴社会党－叙利亚地区|阿拉伯复兴社会党]]<ref name=Davies2015/>，并且是[[阿萨德|阿萨德]]政权的坚定支持者。<ref>{{cite news|title=Islamic State and antiquities: Nothing is sacred|url=http://www.economist.com/news/middle-east-and-africa/21661824-jihadists-murder-man-who-tended-palmyras-art-decades-nothing-sacred|accessdate=23 August 2015|work=[[The_Economist|The Economist]]|date=22 August 2015}}</ref>

哈立德在1963年被任命为塔德莫文物研究主任以及[[塔德莫博物馆|塔德莫博物馆]]馆长<ref>{{cite news|title=叙利亚著名考古学家遭ISIS斩首|url=http://cn.nytimes.com/readers-translation/20151120/c20isis-reader/|accessdate=2015-11-30|publisher=纽约时报中文网|date=2015年11月20日}}</ref>，自此他花费了40年时间来发掘和重建[[巴尔米拉|巴尔米拉]]古城遗址<ref>http://www.theatlantic.com/international/archive/2015/08/palmyra-isis-khalid-al-asaad-beheading-isis/402148/</ref><ref name="BBC">{{cite web |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-middle-east-33984006 |title=Syrian archaeologist 'killed in Palmyra' by IS militants |accessdate=19 August 2015 |work=BBC News}}</ref>，并以发掘该遗址而在世界上知名。此遗址后来被[[联合国教科文组织|联合国教科文组织]]认定为世界遗产<ref name=Davies2015>{{Cite news|title = Khaled al-Asaad profile: the Howard Carter of Palmyra|url = http://www.theguardian.com/world/2015/aug/19/khaled-al-asaad-profile-syria-isis-howard-carter-palmyra-archaeology|newspaper = [[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]|date = 19 August 2015|access-date = 19 August 2015|issn =|first = Caroline|last = Davies}}</ref>。他曾带领过[[美国|美国]]、[[波兰|波兰]]、[[德国|德国]]、[[法国|法国]]和[[瑞士|瑞士]]的考古队，并能够熟练地使用[[亚拉姆语|亚拉姆语]]，直到2011年为止他还经常翻译亚拉姆语的文献。

2015年5月，塔德莫被极端组织“[[伊斯兰国|伊斯兰国]]”攻陷，哈立德协助转移了城中博物馆里的文物<ref name=Davies2015/>，以避免落入“伊斯兰国”手中。“伊斯兰国”企图从哈立德口中知道这些文物的下落，但被拒绝了。8月12日，哈立德被“伊斯兰国”当作“叛教者”斩首处死<ref name="BBC"/>，理由是他保护巴尔米拉遗址的偶像崇拜文物。根据“伊斯兰国”支持者发布的哈立德尸体照片显示，哈立德的尸体被绑在塔德莫街上的一个红绿灯上示众，他的头被放在尸体的脚下。随后，又被转移到巴尔米拉遗址上示众。<ref name=usnews>{{Cite web|url = http://www.usnews.com/news/world/articles/2015/08/19/is-beheads-leading-syrian-antiquities-scholar-in-palmyra|title = Islamic State beheads Syrian antiquities scholar in ancient town of Palmyra|date =19 August 2015 |accessdate = 19 August 2015|website = |work =US News & World Report |first1=Albert|last1=Aji|first2=Bassem|last2=Mroue |agency=Associated Press}}</ref><ref>[http://uk.reuters.com/article/2015/08/18/uk-mideast-crisis-archaeology-idUKKCN0QN24I20150818 Islamic State militants behead archaeologist in Palmyra – Syrian official], Reuters, 18 August 2015.</ref><ref>[http://www.tagesschau.de/ausland/palmyra-133.html IS tötet früheren Chef-Archäologen von Palmyra], ''tagesschau.de'', 19 August 2015.</ref><ref name="Guardian">{{Cite web|url = http://www.theguardian.com/world/2015/aug/18/isis-beheads-archaeologist-syria|title = Beheaded Syrian scholar refused to lead Isis to hidden Palmyra antiquities|date = 19 August 2015|accessdate = |website = |newspaper = The Guardian|last = |first = }}</ref><ref>http://www.theatlantic.com/international/archive/2015/08/palmyra-isis-khalid-al-asaad-beheading-isis/402148/</ref>

叙利亚文物部门、联合国教科文组织以及其他一些学者纷纷对“伊斯兰国”的行为表示强烈谴责。<ref name="Guardian"/><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.theartnewspaper.com/news/news/158701/|title=Italy’s museums honour archaeologist murdered by Isil|work=theartnewspaper.com|accessdate=22 August 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.ilsecoloxix.it/p/mondo/2015/08/20/ARmW9haF-franceschini_bandiere_italiani.shtml| title=Franceschini: «Bandiere a mezz’asta nei musei italiani in memoria di Khaled Asaad»| publisher=''Il Secolo XIX''| date=20 August 2015 | accessdate=20 August 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.syriaonline.sy/?f=Details&catid=22&pageid=18020&g=1|title=Syrian TV  - Update 1-UNESCO condemns ISIS beheading of Palmyra archeologist, Italian museums fly flags at half-mast|work=Syrian TV -Update 1-UNESCO condemns ISIS beheading of Palmyra archeologist, Italian museums fly flags at half-mast|accessdate=20 August 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/agra/All-civilised-people-must-condemn-murder-of-al-Asaad-by-IS-Aligarh-Historians-Society/articleshow/48581821.cms|title=All civilised people must condemn murder of al-Asaad by IS: Aligarh Historians Society|work=The Times of India|accessdate=22 August 2015}}</ref>
