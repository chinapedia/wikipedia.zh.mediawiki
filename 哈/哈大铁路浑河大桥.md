==特点==
这座桥采用“筑岛围堰”的方法<ref>{{cite news|title=浑河又搭桥 先填个半岛|author=谭皓|url=http://news.liao1.com/epaper/hscb/html/2007-11/02/content_27371.htm|work=华商晨报|date=2007-11-02|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141228113010/http://news.liao1.com/epaper/hscb/html/2007-11/02/content_27371.htm|archivedate=2014-12-28}}</ref>，并利用了移动移动模架造桥机进行预制箱梁施工<ref>{{cite news|title=哈大铁路马总屯特大桥进入架梁施工阶段|url=http://www.ln.xinhuanet.com/2008-10/02/content_14544983.htm|publisher=新华网|date=2008-10-02|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141228113009/http://www.ln.xinhuanet.com/2008-10/02/content_14544983.htm|archivedate=2014-12-28}}</ref>。
==作用==
使哈大铁路的线路比之前的线路缩短20公里。
