'''哈肯萨克河'''（{{lang-en|Hackensack River}}）是流经美国[[纽约州|纽约州]]和[[新泽西州|新泽西州]]的一条河流，长约72km，注入[[紐約港|紐約港]]的[[纽瓦克湾|纽瓦克湾]]。哈肯萨克河[[流域|流域]]涵盖了[[纽约|纽约]]下[[哈德遜河|哈德遜河]]以西的郊区，与哈德遜河近似平行。19和20世紀遭工業污染，到21世紀初才隨著沿河製造業的衰退而有所好轉，但仍然受城市廢水排放的困擾。<ref>Wright, Jim. [http://www.hackensackriverkeeper.org/Articles.php?ID=123 "Hackensack River is getting cleaner."] {{Wayback|url=http://www.hackensackriverkeeper.org/Articles.php?ID=123 |date=20160303182649 }} ''The Record.'' August 26, 2007.</ref><ref>New Jersey Department of Environmental Protection. Trenton, NJ. [http://www.nj.gov/dep/watershedmgt/DOCS/WMAFactsheets/WMA05.pdf "Watershed Management Area 5 Factsheet."] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090327015134/http://www.nj.gov/dep/watershedmgt/DOCS/WMAFactsheets/WMA05.pdf |date=2009-03-27 }} Bureau of Watershed Planning. July 17, 2007.</ref>
