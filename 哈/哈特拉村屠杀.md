'''哈特拉村屠杀'''是[[叙利亚内战|叙利亚内战]]期间[[叙利亚反对派|叙利亚反对派]]武装于2013年6月1日在叙利亚东部靠近[[代尔祖尔|代尔祖尔]]的哈特拉村犯下的一起[[屠杀|屠杀]]事件，在屠杀中有60名[[什叶派|什叶派]]村民遇害。遇害者中有一些是有武装，但至少30人是平民。<ref name=raid>[http://mobile.nytimes.com/2013/06/13/world/middleeast/syria.html?from=global.home Dozens of Shiites Reported Killed in Raid by Syria Rebels]</ref><ref>[http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/worldnews/middleeast/syria/10114887/60-Shiite-villagers-killed-as-France-warns-of-turning-point-in-Syria.html 60 Shiite villagers killed as France warns of 'turning point' in Syria]</ref><ref name=Hatla>[http://www.washingtonpost.com/world/middle_east/activists-syrian-rebels-attack-village-in-countrys-east-killing-dozens-of-local-shiites/2013/06/12/a050c7e8-d32e-11e2-b3a2-3bf5eb37b9d0_story.html Syrian rebels attack village in country's east, killing dozens of local Shiites, activists say]</ref>而根据联合国的报告，在屠杀中有30人遇害。<ref>[http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/worldnews/middleeast/syria/10303007/Assad-regime-accused-of-string-of-Syria-massacres-in-UN-report.html Assad regime accused of string of Syria massacres in UN report ]</ref>

反对派武装进攻和占领哈特拉村据报道是对来自该村的亲政府什叶派民兵之前一天发动进攻的报复。当时什叶派民兵进攻了村子附近的一处反对派武装的据点，打死4名反对派武装分子。<ref name=Hatla/>

6月11日，一段视频被上传到网上，标题为“攻击和清洗哈特拉”。视频中显示武装分子在举行庆祝活动并挥舞着绘有萨拉菲主义字样的黑色旗帜。武装分子的话中带有浓重的教派主义色彩。拍摄这段视频的摄影师说道：“这就是什叶派，这是什叶派的尸体，这就是他们的结局。”这段视频显示犯下屠杀罪行的不是叙利亚人，可能是来自科威特。<ref>[http://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/middle-east/syria-60-shia-muslims-massacred-in-rebel-cleansing-of-hatla-8656301.html -{Syria: 60 Shia Muslims massacred in rebel ‘cleansing’ of Hatla}-]</ref>根据一名在当地活动的伊斯兰主义活动人士的[[Facebook|Facebook]]主页，[[叙利亚自由军|叙利亚自由军]]和[[努斯拉陣線|胜利阵线]]的成员卷入了这起屠杀。<ref name=extremist/>

根据反对派活动人士的说法，大部分的死者是亲政府的什叶派民兵，但也有平民被杀，其中包括妇女和儿童。<ref name=eastern/><ref name=extremist/>死者中还包括3名什叶派神职人员。<ref>[http://english.al-akhbar.com/content/un-says-93000-killed-syria-amid-reports-new-massacre UN says 93,000 killed in Syria amid reports of new massacre]</ref>阿拉伯叙利亚通讯社（SANA）声称屠杀中有30名平民遇害。<ref name=raid/>在反对派占领该村期间，反对派武装分子还焚烧了民宅以及一座什叶派清真寺。<ref>{{cite news|title=Reports of 'massacre' in eastern Syria|url=http://www.aljazeera.com/news/middleeast/2013/06/201361294148169694.html|newspaper=Al Jazeera|date=12 June 2013}}</ref><ref name=extremist>[http://www.npr.org/templates/story/story.php?storyId=190884583 Syrian Extremist Rebels Raid Shiite Village]</ref>在袭击中有10名反对派武装丧生。大约150名什叶派村民逃往了附近政府军控制的Jafra村。<ref name=Hatla/>

这起屠杀事件凸显了“叙利亚国内冲突中浓厚的教派斗争属性”。<ref name=eastern>[http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-middle-east-22870776 Syria rebels 'kill Shia residents of eastern village']</ref>来自[[科威特|科威特]]的武装分子头目Shafi al-Ajmi威胁称将对[[阿勒颇|阿勒颇]]附近的Nubl和al-Zahraa两座什叶派村庄发动袭击并杀死那些叙利亚政府的支持者。<ref name=raid>[http://mobile.nytimes.com/2013/06/13/world/middleeast/syria.html?from=global.home Dozens of Shiites Reported Killed in Raid by Syria Rebels]</ref>
