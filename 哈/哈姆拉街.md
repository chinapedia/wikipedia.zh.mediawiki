'''哈姆拉街'''（{{lang-ar|شارع حمراء}}，Rue Hamra，意即第31街）是位於[[黎巴嫩|黎巴嫩]][[貝魯特|貝魯特]]的一條街道，是貝魯特的經濟文化中心。[[黎巴嫩內戰|黎巴嫩內戰]]之前也是時尚中心。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.ikamalebanon.com/accommodations/region/beirut_acc/district/hamra.htm |title=Hamra |publisher=Ikamalebanon.com |date= |accessdate=2012-02-11 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120211075334/http://www.ikamalebanon.com/accommodations/region/beirut_acc/district/hamra.htm |archivedate=2012-02-11 }}</ref>

[[普利策獎|普利策獎]]得主Borzou Daragahi將這條街道描述為“宗教紛爭中的多宗教和政治自由堡壘”。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://articles.latimes.com/2008/may/19/world/fg-lebanon19 |title=Sarjoun Kantar | There's One Street in Beirut Where All Are Welcome|publisher=Los Angeles Times|date=19 May 2008|accessdate=11 February 2012}}</ref> 這條街道上同時並存著[[遜尼派|遜尼派]]、[[默基特希腊礼天主教会|默基特希腊礼天主教会]]和[[希臘東正教|希臘東正教]]的清真寺和教堂。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://latimesblogs.latimes.com/babylonbeyond/2008/05/lebanon-if-hamr.html |title=LEBANON: If Hamra Street Could Talk|publisher=Los Angeles Times|date=20 May 2008|accessdate=2012-02-11}}</ref>
