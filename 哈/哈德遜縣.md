'''哈德遜县'''（'''Hudson County'''）是[[美國|美國]][[新澤西州|新澤西州]]東北部的一個縣，東隔[[哈德遜河|哈德遜河]]與[[紐約市|紐約市]][[曼哈頓島|曼哈頓島]]相望，南與[[史泰登島|史泰登島]]相望，是[[紐約都會區|紐約都會區]]的一部份，[[澤西市|澤西市]]為其中最大的城市與郡治<ref name=NACo/>。面積162平方公里，為紐澤西州最小的郡，卻是美國內人口密度最高的郡之一。根據[[2010年美國人口普查|2010年美國人口普查]]，共有人口634,266人<ref name=Census2010/>，比[[2000年美國人口普查|2000年美國人口普查]]的608,975人<ref name=Census2000>[http://factfinder2.census.gov/bkmk/table/1.0/en/DEC/00_SF1/DP1/0500000US34017 DP-1 - Profile of General Demographic Characteristics: 2000; Census 2000 Summary File 1 (SF 1) 100-Percent Data for Hudson County, New Jersey], [[美國人口普查局|美國人口普查局]]. Accessed January 21, 2013.</ref>增加了25,291人(+4.2%)，取代[[孟莫斯郡_(紐澤西州)|孟莫斯郡]]成為紐澤西州人口第四多的郡<ref name=Census2010LWD>[http://lwd.dol.state.nj.us/labor/lpa/pub/lmv/LMV_1.pdf NJ Labor Market Views] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130920152414/http://lwd.dol.state.nj.us/labor/lpa/pub/lmv/LMV_1.pdf |date=2013-09-20 }}, [[New_Jersey_Department_of_Labor_and_Workforce_Development|New Jersey Department of Labor and Workforce Development]], March 15, 2011. Accessed October 4, 2013.</ref><ref name=CPH232/>。根據2010年的人口普查，哈德遜郡人口密度為每平方英哩有13,731.4人，是美國第六高的郡<ref>Staff. [http://usatoday30.usatoday.com/news/nation/census/profile/ "Census 2010 data show population and diversity trends"], ''[[USA_Today|USA Today]]''. Accessed October 4, 2013. Click on "Population per Square Mile" to sort counties nationwide by descending population density."</ref>。成立於1840年2月22日，以[[英國|英國]]探險家[[亨利·哈德遜|亨利·哈德遜]]命名的哈德遜河取名<ref>[[Joseph_Nathan_Kane|Kane, Joseph Nathan]]; and Aiken, Charles Curry. [http://books.google.com/books?id=yC9vFvCuW84C&pg=PA140 ''The American Counties: Origins of County Names, Dates of Creation, and Population Data, 1950-2000''], p. 140. [[Scarecrow_Press|Scarecrow Press]], 2005. ISBN 978-0-8108-5036-1. Accessed January 21, 2013.</ref>。

===地形===
[[File:Aster_newyorkcity_lrg.jpg|thumb]]核心的衛星照片。一千萬多人居住在照片範圍內，哈德遜郡大致位於照片左側的半島上。]]
根據2010年人口普查，哈德遜郡總面積為{{convert|62.31|sqmi}}，其中{{convert|46.19|sqmi}}(74.1%)為陸地面積，而{{convert|16.12|sqmi}}(25.9%)為水體面積<ref name=CensusArea>[https://web.archive.org/web/20120611040243/http://www.census.gov/geo/www/gazetteer/files/Gaz_counties_34.txt Census 2010 U.S. Gazetteer Files: New Jersey Counties], [[United_States_Census_Bureau|United States Census Bureau]], Backed up by the [[Internet_Archive|Internet Archive]] as of June 11, 2012. Accessed October 4, 2013.</ref>，為紐澤西州下21個郡中面積最小的郡，比次小的[[友聯郡_(紐澤西州)|友聯郡]]面積一半都不到<ref name=CensusArea/>，亦為美國國內陸地面積第八小的郡<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20130930014430/http://quickfacts.census.gov/qfd/download/DataSet.txt 2010 Census: State and County Quick Facts], [[United_States_Census_Bureau|United States Census Bureau]], backed up by the [[Internet_Archive|Internet Archive]] as of September 30, 2013. Accessed October 4, 2013.</ref>

哈德遜郡位於[[紐約都會區|紐約都會區]]的核心處，紐澤西州的東北部，東臨[[哈德遜河|哈德遜河]]與[[上紐約灣|上紐約灣]]，南扼凡庫爾水道([[:en:Kill_van_Kull|Kill van Kull]])，西向[[紐華克灣|紐華克灣]]([[:en:Newark_Bay|Newark Bay]])、哈肯薩克河([[:en:Hackensack_River|Hackensack River]])、及百賽克河([[:en:Passaic_River|Passaic River]])，北接[[博根郡|博根郡]]為其為一的陸地交接處<ref>{{Cite book

哈德遜郡最顯著的地形是紐澤西懸崖([[:en:The_Palisades_(Hudson_River)|New Jersey Palisades]])。北部的地形非常陡峭，因為懸崖東面與河岸的距離非常近，不過西面則較為平緩為[[單面山|單面山]]地形。地勢到了南部的半島變逐漸低緩下來，造成平緩的海岸。哈德遜郡西部地區是屬於紐澤西濕地([[:en:New_Jersey_Meadowlands|New Jersey Meadowlands]])一部份。郡內最高點位於西紐約鎮，為[[海拔|海拔]]79公尺<ref>[http://www.peakbagger.com/list.aspx?lid=13320 New Jersey County High Points], Peakbagger.com. Accessed October 4, 2013.</ref><ref>{{cite peakbagger |pid=7139 |name=Hudson County High Point, New Jersey}}</ref>；最低點為[[海平面|海平面]]。哈德遜郡瀕臨[[紐約港|紐約港]]，其中著名的[[埃利斯島|埃利斯島]]與[[自由島|自由島]]全部位於哈德遜郡境內的水域，並不是位於[[紐約市|紐約市]]的水域，不過自由島為紐約州管理，而埃利斯島則是由紐約州與紐澤西州共同管理<ref>[http://www.cnn.com/US/9805/26/scotus.ellis.island/ New Jersey wins claim to Ellis Island] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081011053117/http://cnn.com/US/9805/26/scotus.ellis.island/ |date=2008-10-11 }}, [[CNN.com|CNN.com]], May 26, 1998</ref>。

[[Image:HudsonRiverJavitsCenter.agr.JPG|thumb]]
哈德遜郡鄰近的行政區各為，東面紐約州[[曼哈頓|紐約郡]]與[[布魯克林|國王郡]]，西向紐澤西州[[艾塞克斯郡_(紐澤西州)|艾塞克斯郡]]與[[友聯郡_(紐澤西州)|友聯郡]]，南臨紐約州[[史泰登島|里奇蒙郡]]，北接紐澤西州[[博根郡|博根郡]]是其為一的陸地交界。因為哈德遜郡緊鄰曼哈頓，所以時常被稱為[[紐約市|紐約市]]的[[行政區_(紐約市)|第六區]]<ref>Holusha, John. [http://www.nytimes.com/1998/10/11/realestate/commercial-property-jersey-riverfront-hudson-s-west-bank-optimistic-developers.html "Commercial Property / The Jersey Riverfront; On the Hudson's West Bank, Optimistic Developers"], ''[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]'', October 11, 1998. Accessed September 30, 2013. "'That simply is out of the question in midtown,' he said, adding that some formerly fringe areas in Midtown South that had previously been available were filled up as well. Given that the buildings on the New Jersey waterfront are new and equipped with the latest technology and just a few stops on the PATH trains from Manhattan, they become an attractive alternative. Retail Center"], ''[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]'', April 19, 1998. Accessed September 30, 2013.</ref>

哈德遜郡主要位於哈肯賽克河與哈德遜河之間的細長形[[半島|半島]]上，當地稱之為博根岬([[:en:Bergen_Neck|Bergen Neck]])。其城市發展非常密集，沒有顯著的間隔，城鎮之間的街道與市區常常連綿成一氣，難以分辨。郡道501(甘迺迪大道，[[:en:County_Route_501_(New_Jersey)|Kennedy Boulevard]])自南向北穿越整個博根岬半島<ref>[http://www.state.nj.us/transportation/refdata/sldiag/00000501__-.pdf New Jersey County Route 501] [[straight-line_diagram|straight-line diagram]] from the [[New_Jersey_Department_of_Transportation|New Jersey Department of Transportation]]</ref>。

* 郡內唯一列入美國百大人口城鎮的城市是[[澤西市|澤西市]]。根據[[美國人口普查局|美國人口普查局]]2012年的估計，排名為全美第75名<ref>[http://files.hawaii.gov/dbedt/census/popestimate/2012-subcounty-population-hawaii/PEP_2012_PEPANNRSIP.US12A.pdf PEPANNRSIP-Geography-United States: Annual Estimates of the Resident Population for Incorporated Places Over 50,000, Ranked by July 1, 2012 Population: April 1, 2010 to July 1, 2012 - United States -- Places Over 50,000 Population 2012 Population Estimates] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131004232920/http://files.hawaii.gov/dbedt/census/popestimate/2012-subcounty-population-hawaii/PEP_2012_PEPANNRSIP.US12A.pdf |date=2013年10月4日 }}, [[;en:United_States_Census_Bureau|;en:United States Census Bureau]] data archived by the Hawaii Department of Business, Economic Development & Tourism. Accessed October 4, 2013.</ref>。
* 在五萬人以上的城鎮中，友聯市([[:en:Union_City,_New_Jersey|Union City]])是美國人口密度最高的城鎮<ref>[http://www.demographia.com/db-2000city50kdens.htm 2000 Census: US Municipalities Over 50,000: Ranked by 2000 Density], accessed March 22, 2007</ref><ref name=StandUp>Al Sullivan. * 北博根([[:en:North_Bergen,_New_Jersey|North Bergen]]))為全美僅次於[[舊金山|舊金山]]的第二多丘密集的城市<ref name=Most>[http://www.hudsonreporter.com/site/news.cfm?BRD=1291&dept_id=523585&newsid=17118972&PAG=461&rfi=9 Most liquor licenses? * 北哈德遜([[:en:North_Hudson,_New_Jersey|North Hudson]])是維霍肯到北博根之間城鎮的集合體稱呼，有美國內僅次於[[邁阿密|邁阿密]]的第二大古巴裔美國人人口<ref name=StandUp/>。
