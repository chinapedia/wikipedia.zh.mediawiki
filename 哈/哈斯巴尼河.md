'''哈斯巴尼河'''（{{lang-ar|الحاصباني}} / [[ALA-LC|ALA-LC]]: ''al-Ḥāṣbānī''），又稱'''斯納溪'''（{{lang-he|נחל שניר}} / ''Nahal Snir''），是[[約旦河|約旦河]]的主要[[支流|支流]]。
 
哈斯巴尼河水源主要來自[[黎巴嫩|黎巴嫩]]的兩個[[泉|泉]]：<ref>[[FAO|FAO]] (Water Resources section) {{cite web |url=http://www.fao.org/nr/water/aquastat/countries/lebanon/index.stm |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2011-04-26 |0=2008-04-09 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080409054730/http://www.fao.org/nr/water/aquastat/countries/lebanon/index.stm |archivedate=2008-04-09 }}<blockquote>Overall, there are about 40 major streams in Lebanon and, based on the hydrographic system, the country can be divided into five regions: …[including] the Hasbani river basin in the south-east. <blockquote></ref><ref>[[United_Nations_University|UNU]] The Jordan River [http://www.unu.edu/unupress/unupbooks/80858e/80858E06.htm] <blockquote>The Dan spring, the largest of the sources of the upper Jordan, lies wholly within Israel close to the border with Syria. The spring sources of the Hasbani River lie entirely within Lebanon. The spring source of the Banias River is in Syria. These three small streams unite 6 km inside Israel at about 70 m above sea level to form the upper Jordan River. <blockquote></ref>{{link-en|瓦扎尼|Wazzani}}與哈克茲比耶，其中後者由哈斯巴尼河最源頭的一群湧泉所構成。<ref>[[United_Nations_University|UNU]] The Jordan River [http://www.unu.edu/unupress/unupbooks/80858e/80858E06.htm]</ref>哈斯巴尼河先黎巴嫩境內流了40公里，然後穿越國界與{{link-en|班尼亞斯河|Banias River}}與{{link-en|丹河 (中東)|Dan River (Middle East)|丹河}}在以色列北部匯集，形成約旦河。<ref>[[MERIP|MERIP]] Heightened Israeli-Lebanese Tensions Over Jordan's Headwaters [http://www.merip.org/mero/mero093002.html]</ref>由{{link-en|蓋傑爾|Ghajar}}往南大約4公里的河道，成為黎巴嫩與[[戈蘭高地|戈蘭高地]]（現由以色列佔領）邊界。
