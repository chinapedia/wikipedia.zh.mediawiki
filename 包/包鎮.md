'''包鎮'''（{{lang-my|ပေါက်မြို့နယ်}}，{{lang-en|Pauk Township}}）為[[緬甸|緬甸]][[馬圭省|馬圭省]][[木各具縣|木各具縣]]的鎮區<ref name="MIMU001">[http://www.burmalibrary.org/docs6/MIMU001_A3_SD%20&%20Township%20Overview.pdf "Myanmar States/Divisions & Townships Overview Map"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101203160828/http://www.burmalibrary.org/docs6/MIMU001_A3_SD%20%26%20Township%20Overview.pdf |date=2010-12-03 }} Myanmar Information Management Unit (MIMU)</ref>。2014年人口171,514人<ref name=citypopulation/>，區域面積2,452.8平方公里<ref name=citypopulation/>。該鎮下分129個村莊。[[包_(緬甸)|包]]為該鎮之首府。

包鎮座標區域範圍於北緯21度10分至21度49分、東經94度18分至94度44分之間<ref name="IGI">''Imperial gazetteer of India: provincial series, Volume 11'' [http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=4f-1AAAAIAAJ&pg=PA31 page 31]</ref>。{{link-en|鳩河|Kyaw River}}為該鎮的主要河流<ref name="IGI"/>。

* [[甘高鎮|甘高鎮]]：位在包鎮北邊
