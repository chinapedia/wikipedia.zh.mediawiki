'''异苯并呋喃'''（'''Isobenzofuran'''）是一种[[杂环化合物|杂环化合物]]，结构上由苯环和呋喃环稠合而成，化学式C<sub>8</sub>H<sub>6</sub>O，是[[苯并呋喃|苯并呋喃]]的异构体。  

异苯并呋喃有高反应活性，迅速聚合。<ref>{{ cite journal | authorlink1 = Louis Fieser | author1 = Fieser, L. F. | author2 = Haddadin, M. J. | title = Isobenzofuran, a Transient Intermediate | journal = [[Journal_of_the_American_Chemical_Society|Journal of the American Chemical Society]] | year = 1964 | volume = 86 | issue = 10 | pages = 2081–2082 | doi = 10.1021/ja01064a044 }}</ref>仅能在低温下能制备得到。<ref>{{ cite journal | author = Wege, D. | title = Isolation of Isobenzofuran | journal = [[Tetrahedron_Letters|Tetrahedron Letters]] | year = 1971 | volume = 12 | issue = 25 | pages = 2337–2338 | doi = 10.1016/S0040-4039(01)96856-X }}</ref>虽然其本身不稳定，但异苯并呋喃环结构存在于很多稳定分子，<ref>{{ cite book | title = Heterocyclic Chemistry | author = Joule, J. A.; Mills, K.; Smith, G. F. | edition = 3rd | year = 1995 | pages = 364–365 | publisher = CRC Press | isbn = 978-0748740697 }}</ref>例如[[邻苯二甲酸酐|邻苯二甲酸酐]]。
