'''异端尖叉'''（'''Heretic's fork'''）是一种[[酷刑|酷刑]]工具，通常由一段金属、金属两端的双叉以及皮带组成。<ref name=torturemuseum>{{cite web |url=http://www.torturamuseum.com/instruments.html |title=Instruments Gallery |accessdate=2008-07-15 |publisher=Historical Torture Museum}}</ref>

使用时，将此工具放在[[胸骨|胸骨]]与[[咽喉|咽喉]]之间，同时通过绕在脖子上的皮带固定。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.artic.edu/aic/collections/artwork/72280 |title=About This Artwork - The Heretic's Fork, c. 1985 |author=Golub, L. |date=1985 |work=The Art Institute of Chicago - The Collection |publisher=The Art Institute of Chicago |accessdate=13 January 2010}}</ref>带有此工具的人将会无法睡觉，当他的头因打瞌睡而低下时，两端的尖叉就会刺入其咽喉和胸部从而造成剧痛。<ref name=torturemuseum/><ref>{{cite web |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=APwDAAAAMBAJ&pg=PA41&lpg=PA41&dq=heretic's+fork+magazine&source=bl&ots=-1Pm4yi8Bn&sig=Bs3nfJh_WllrfLuw_yqYegOEFLU&hl=en&ei=o9VNS7r2OJCTkAWHg4SpDQ&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=2&ved=0CAoQ6AEwAQ#v=onepage&q=&f=false |title=Hurts so good |author=Di Perna, A. |date=September 2008 |work=Tune-Ups - Profiles |publisher= |accessdate=13 January 2010}}</ref>这种简易工具能够长时间剥夺人的睡眠时间。而当人长时间无法入睡时，招供就会变得更为容易。<ref name=torturemuseum/>

传统上，这种叉上会刻有拉丁文''abiuro''（意为“我放弃”），曾被许多[[异端裁判所|异端裁判所]]所使用。<ref>{{cite book |title=The Paleolithic Paradigm  |last=Stocker |first=T. |year=2009 |publisher=AuthorHouse |isbn=1449022928 |page=64 |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=jKT1N1tGZRYC&pg=PA64&dq=heretic's+fork&cd=4#v=onepage&q=heretic's%20fork&f=false |accessdate=13 January 2010}}</ref>
