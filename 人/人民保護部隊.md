'''人民保護部隊'''（{{lang-ku|Yekîneyên Parastina Gel}}，缩写为YPG）是[[敘利亞|敘利亞]][[庫爾德人|庫爾德人]]政党[[民主联盟党_(叙利亚)|民主聯盟黨]]（PYD）的下属军事组织。<ref name=Vice>{{cite news|last=Gold|first=Danny|url=http://www.vice.com/read/meet-the-ypg|title=Meet the YPG, the Kurdish militia that doesn't want help from anyone|magazine=[[Vice_(magazine)|VICE]]|date=31 October 2012|accessdate=9 October 2014| quote=A member of YPG’s central command … said that the YPG formed in 2004 shortly after the Qamishlo riots, when a number of Kurdish youth realized that they needed to be able to defend themselves more efficiently. They did not officially declare themselves until the revolution started in 2011.}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.al-monitor.com/pulse/originals/2013/04/conflict-intensifies-syrian-kurdish-region.html|title=Conflict intensifies in Kurdish area of Syria|last=van Wilgenburg|first=Wladimir|date=5 April 2013|work=[[Al-Monitor|Al-Monitor]]|accessdate=9 October 2014|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141015165404/http://www.al-monitor.com/pulse/originals/2013/04/conflict-intensifies-syrian-kurdish-region.html|archivedate=2014年10月15日}}</ref> 這支部隊成立初期，在[[敘利亞內戰|敘利亞內戰]]中採取守勢，<ref name=Vice/><ref>{{cite web|url=http://rudaw.net/english/interview/22072013|title=YPG Commander: Kurds Are Bulwark Against Islamic Extremism in Syria |publisher=Rudaw|accessdate=9 October 2014|date=22 July 2013}}</ref>對抗其它試圖佔據敘利亞庫爾德族地區的非庫爾德族武裝。其後，人民保護部隊開始進攻被[[伊斯蘭國|伊斯蘭國]]佔領及主要由阿拉伯人居住的敘利亞其它地區。<ref name="pitarakis">{{cite news |last1=Pitarakis |first1=Lefteris |last2=Mroue |first2=Bassem |date=14 June 2015 |title=Thousands of Syrians flee into Turkey amid intense fighting |url=http://bigstory.ap.org/article/7f62663318e94589bf6097825914a088/kurds-nearing-key-islamic-state-held-syrian-border-town |agency=AP |quote=Thousands of Syrians cut through a border fence and crossed over into Turkey … fleeing intense fighting … between Kurdish fighters and jihadis.}}</ref>

2012年7月，人民保護部隊逐走駐守[[科巴尼|科巴尼]]的敘利亞政府軍，並奪取[[阿穆達|阿穆達]]和[[阿夫林_(敘利亞)|阿夫林]]。<ref name=lib>{{cite news|title=Liberated Kurdish cities in Syrian Kurdistan move into next phase|url=http://ekurd.net/mismas/articles/misc2012/7/syriakurd554.htm|accessdate=28 July 2012|work=Rudaw|date=25 July 2012|location=Erbil, Iraq|last=Ahmed|first=Hevidar}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Kurds Give Ultimatum to Syrian Security Forces|url=http://www.ekurd.net/mismas/articles/misc2012/7/syriakurd541.htm|accessdate=9 October 2014|publisher=Rudaw|date=21 July 2012}}</ref>至2012年12月，人民保護部隊已擴充至8個旅。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://scientiahumana.org/2012/12/04/the-kurdish-protection-units-have-formed-a-new-brigade-in-the-al-bab-region|title=The Kurdish Protection Units have formed a new brigade in the Al–Bab region|work=Scientia Humana|date=4 December 2012|accessdate=18 December 2013|quote=Kurdish Information Center|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130108025429/http://scientiahumana.org/2012/12/04/the-kurdish-protection-units-have-formed-a-new-brigade-in-the-al-bab-region|archivedate=2013年1月8日}}</ref>

人民保護部隊自從逐走[[艾因角|艾因角]]的聖戰分子後，與[[伊斯蘭主義|伊斯蘭主義]]武裝分子開始了衝突。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://english.ahram.org.eg/NewsContent/2/8/76750/World/Region/Kurds-expel-jihadists-from-flashpoint-Syrian-town-.aspx|title=Kurds expel jihadists from flashpoint Syrian town: NGO|work=AFP|date=17 July 2013|accessdate=13 February 2015}}</ref>

2014年，人民保護部隊與[[自由敘利亞軍|自由敘利亞軍]]合作，對抗[[拉卡省|拉卡省]]的伊斯蘭國武裝。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://aranews.net/2014/05/fsa-and-ypg-cooperate-against-isil-militants-in-syrias-tel-abyad/|title=FSA and YPG cooperate against ISIL militants in Syria's Tel Abyad|date=12 May 2014|publisher=ARA News|accessdate=9 October 2014|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141008154840/http://aranews.net/2014/05/fsa-and-ypg-cooperate-against-isil-militants-in-syrias-tel-abyad/|archivedate=2014年10月8日}}</ref>到2015年，人民保衛軍的兵力約有25,000人；自由敘利亞軍和一些[[伊斯蘭主義|伊斯蘭主義]]武裝團體反對人民保衛軍進佔阿拉伯人居住地區，他們指控人民保護部隊在佔領的村莊驅逐阿拉伯人。<ref>{{Cite news |url=http://www.voachinese.com/content/syria-us-20151006/2994741.html |title=对立派系及分析家:将受美国军援的叙反抗武装不靠谱 |publisher=美國之音 |date=2015-10-07}}</ref>

人民保护部队有一支对应的专由女性组成的部队：[[妇女保护部队|妇女保护部队]]。

人民保護部隊是2015年10月成立的「[[敘利亞民主力量|敘利亞民主力量]]」的成員之一。

2016年8月24日，土耳其展開名為「[[幼發拉底之盾行動|幼發拉底之盾行動]]」的軍事攻勢，土耳其軍隊協助親土耳其武裝分子奪取敘利亞北部邊境城鎮[[賈拉布魯斯|賈拉布魯斯]]後，開始攻擊附近的人民保护部队控制區。<ref name=40dead>{{cite web|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/wires/afp/article-3762099/Turkish-bombardment-kills-20-civilians-Syria-monitor.html|title=Turkey ramps up Syria offensive with deadly bombings |work=Daily Mail Online |date=2016-08-28 |accessdate=2016-08-28}}</ref>
