'''人體增强'''（{{lang-en|Human enhancement}}）是指那些希望通过自然或人工的手段暂时或永久的克服现在[[人体|人体]]局限的尝试。这个术语有时适用于使用技术手段选择或改变人类的素质和能力，而不管这些改变是否超越现在人类的极限。现在的考验是，这种技术是否用于非[[治疗|治疗]]的目的。一些[[伦理学家|伦理学家]]将该术语限定为为了非治疗目的在[[人类生物学|人类生物学]]上使用特定技术——包括[[神经学|神经学]]、[[计算机|计算机]]、[[基因治疗|基因治疗]]以及[[纳米技术|纳米技术]]。<ref name="Hughes 2004 (paper)">{{cite journal| author = Hughes, James|    title = Human Enhancement on the Agenda|    year = 2004 |   url = http://ieet.org/index.php/IEET/more/human_enhancement_on_the_agenda/| accessdate=2007-02-02}}</ref><ref>Moore, P., "Enhancing Me: The Hope and the Hype of Human Enhancement", John Wiley, 2008</ref>

==技术== 
人类增强技术不仅是为了治疗[[疾病|疾病]]和[[残疾|残疾]]，而且也为了增强人类素质。<ref name="Enhancement Technologies Group 1998">{{cite journal|    author = Enhancement Technologies Group|    title = Writings by group participants|  year = 1998 |   url = http://www.ucl.ac.uk/~ucbtdag/bioethics/writings/index.html| accessdate=2007-02-02}}</ref>某些学术圈子中，人类增强技术意味着[[新兴技术|新兴技术]]和[[会聚技术|会聚技术]]。<ref name="Roco and Bainbridge 2004"/>另一些时候它与[[人类遗传工程|人类遗传工程]]大致同义，<ref name="Agar 2004">{{cite book|author = Agar, Nicholas|title = Liberal Eugenics: In Defence of Human Enhancement|year = 2004|isbn = 1-4051-2390-7}}</ref><ref name="Parens 2000">{{cite book| author = Parens, Erik| title = Enhancing Human Traits: Ethical and Social Implications| publisher = Georgetown University Press| year = 2000| isbn = 0-87840-780-4}}</ref>更通常的，它是指综合运用纳米技术、[[生物技术|生物技术]]、[[信息技术|信息技术]]和[[认知科学|认知科学]]来改善人类的功效。<ref name="Roco and Bainbridge 2004">{{cite book| author = Roco, Mihail C. and Bainbridge, William Sims, eds.| title = Converging Technologies for Improving Human Performance| publisher = Springer| year = 2004| isbn = 1402012543}}</ref>

==伦理==
{{See also|超人类主义}}
自1990年代开始，一些学者（比如来自[[伦理学和新兴技术研究所|伦理学和新兴技术研究所]]的会员）<ref name="Bailey 2006">{{cite journal|    author = Bailey, Ronald|    title = The Right to Human Enhancement: And also uplifting animals and the rapture of the nerds|    year = 2006 |   url = http://www.reason.com/news/show/116489.html| accessdate=2007-03-03}}</ref>成为了人类增强的有力支持者，而另外一些学者（如[[总统生物伦理委员会|总统生物伦理委员会]]成员）<ref name="Members of the President's Council on Bioethics 2003">{{cite book| author = Members of the President's Council on Bioethics| title = Beyond Therapy: Biotechnology and the Pursuit of Happiness| url = http://www.bioethics.gov/reports/beyondtherapy/| publisher = President's Council on Bioethics| year = 2003| deadurl = yes| archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20070202061234/http://www.bioethics.gov/reports/beyondtherapy/| archivedate = 2007-02-02}}</ref>成为它的坦率批评者。<ref name="Hughes 2004 (book)">{{cite book| author = Hughes, James| title = Citizen Cyborg: Why Democratic Societies Must Respond to the Redesigned Human of the Future| publisher = Westview Press| year = 2004| isbn = 0-8133-4198-1}}</ref>

拥护者们越来越多的被认为是[[超人类主义|超人类主义]]者，这种有争议的理念认同公民对保持或修饰他们自己的意识以及身体所具有的自由，为了保证他们的[[选择自由|选择自由]]，并在对他们自己及后代使用增强技术时[[知情同意|知情同意]]。<ref Name="Ford 2005">{{cite web|last=Ford |first=Alyssa |authorlink= |title=Humanity: The Remix |work=Utne Magazine |publisher= |date=May / June 2005 |url=http://www.twliterary.com/jhughes_utne.html |accessdate=2007-03-03 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20060313212747/http://www.twliterary.com/jhughes_utne.html |archivedate=2006-03-13 }}</ref>

[[神经营销学|神经营销学]]顾问扎克·林奇认为[[神经科技|神经科技]]对社会的影响比[[基因疗法|基因疗法]]更迅速，作为激进人类增强的一条途径将遭遇更小阻力。他还认为“使能”的概念应该被增加到“治疗”对“增强”的辩论之中。<ref name="R. U. Sirius 2005">{{cite news| author = R. U. Sirius| title = The NeuroAge: Zack Lynch In Conversation With R.U. Sirius| publisher = Life Enhancement Products| year = 2005}}</ref>

虽然很多人类增强的提议需要依靠[[边缘科学|边缘科学]]，它的理念和前景已然引起了公众的争论。<ref name="The Royal Society & The Royal Academy of Engineering 2004">{{cite journal|   author = The Royal Society & The Royal Academy of Engineering|   title = Nanoscience and nanotechnologies (Ch. 6)|   year = 2004 |   url = http://www.nanotec.org.uk/report/chapter6.pdf|   accessdate=2006-12-05}}</ref><ref name="European Parliament 2006">{{cite journal| author = European Parliament|  title = Technology Assessment on Converging Technologies|   year = 2006|   url = http://www.europarl.europa.eu/stoa/publications/studies/stoa183_en.pdf| accessdate=2006-12-06}}</ref><ref name="European Parliament 2009">{{cite journal| author = European Parliament|  title = Human Enhancement|   year = 2009|   url = http://www.europarl.europa.eu/stoa/publications/studies/stoa2007-13_en.pdf| accessdate=2010-01-10}}</ref>

许多批评家认为“人类增强”是一种贴标签式的话语，带有[[优生学|优生学]]的含义，因为它可能包含人类遗传特性的增进，以达到一种生物[[适应度|适应度]]的规范（以损失[[生物多样性|生物多样性]]和[[神经多样性|神经多样性]]为代价），这会引起超出人们期望的负效应。此外，他们断言那些无疑是良性的增强，像“更少疾病”，超出规范之外，就算是这些改良也需要伦理上的权衡，像[[注意力不足过动症争议|注意力不足过动症争议]]所显示的那样。<ref name="Carrico 2007">{{cite journal|    author = Carrico, Dale|    title =  Modification, Consent, and Prosthetic Self-Determination|    year = 2007 |   url = http://ieet.org/index.php/IEET/more/carrico20070226/| accessdate=2007-04-03}}</ref>

不过，对人类增强最普遍的批评是它通常是轻率、自私、短视的，忽视在个人以及社会上产生的长期后果，比如恐惧有些增强会为那样能够使用这些技术的人创造不公正的生理或心理优势，或者由于得到技术的机会不同而拉大富人同穷人的差距。<ref name="Ian 2002">{{cite journal| author = Mooney, Pat Roy| title = Beyond Cloning: Making Well People "Better"| year = 2002 | url = http://www.worldwatch.org/node/521| accessdate=2007-02-02}}</ref><ref name="Fukuyama 2002">{{cite book| author = Fukuyama, Francis| title = Our Posthuman Future: Consequences of the Biotechnology Revolution| publisher = Farrar Straus & Giroux| year = 2002| isbn = 0-374-23643-7| authorlink = }}</ref><ref name="IBHF">{{cite journal|author=Institute on Biotechnology and the Human Future |title=Human "Enhancement" |url=http://www.thehumanfuture.org/themes/human_enhancement/background.html |accessdate=2007-02-02 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070209224106/http://www.thehumanfuture.org/themes/human_enhancement/background.html |archivedate=2007-02-09 }}</ref>

相应的，一些支持者希望使用更中立的词汇，用“使能”代替“增强”，以吸引公众的兴趣；<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.aaas.org/spp/sfrl/projects/human_enhancement/pdfs/HESummaryReport.pdf |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2010-11-29 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20101129174400/http://www.aaas.org/spp/sfrl/projects/human_enhancement/pdfs/HESummaryReport.pdf |archivedate=2010-11-29 }}</ref>以保卫和支持对使能技术的独立安全试验、以及这些技术的廉价和普及性。<ref name="Hughes 2004 (book)"/>
