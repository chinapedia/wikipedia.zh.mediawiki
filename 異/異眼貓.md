'''異眼貓'''是指[[貓|貓]]的其中一隻[[眼睛|眼睛]]是[[藍色|藍色]]，而另一隻眼睛是[[綠色|綠色]]、[[黃色|黃色]]或[[褐色|褐色]]的現象。此為[[虹膜異色症|虹膜異色症]]<ref name=purina>Foster, Race and Smith, Marty, ([[DVM|DVM]]s), [http://purinaone.com/catcare_cond_atoz_article.asp?Seed=791&ArticleNumber=77 Heterochromia] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070217183413/http://www.purinaone.com/catcare_cond_atoz_article.asp?Seed=791&ArticleNumber=77 |date=2007-02-17 }}, A-Z Health Library, Purina-One. Retrieved February 2007.</ref><ref name=peted>Foster, Race and Smith, Marty ([[DVM|DVM]]s), [http://www.peteducation.com/article.cfm?cls=1&cat=1344&articleid=294 Heterochromia: Eye Color], Peteducation.com. Retrieved February 2007.</ref>發生在貓身上的情形，但也可能對其他動物造成影響。此種疾病最常影響白色的貓，但其他顏色的貓也可能患病；只要當個體具有白色斑點基因時就有可能發生。<ref name=boots>[http://www.angelfire.com/fl/furryboots/white.html "White" Genetics Page], Norwegian Forest Cats, Furry Boots. Retrieved February 2007.</ref>

==起因==
異眼貓的起因是由於[[上位性（顯性）基因白|上位性（顯性）基因白]]（可能改變任何其他顏色的基因，使其變成一隻完全白色的貓）<ref name=messy1>Hartwell, Sarah. [http://www.messybeast.com/whitecat.htm White Cats, Eye Colours and Deafness], Messybeast.com. Retrieved February 2007.</ref>或[[白色斑點基因|白色斑點基因]]（這是在雙色貓和[[晚禮服貓|晚禮服貓]]（Tuxedo Cat）身上存在的基因）<ref name=messy2>Hartwell, Sarah. [http://www.messybeast.com/bicolours.htm Beautiful Bi-colours: Tuxedo and Magpie cats], Messybeast.com. Retrieved February 2007.</ref>在貓的生長期阻止了[[黑色素|黑色素]]顆粒到達眼睛，<ref name=boots/>造成一邊[[虹膜|虹膜]]呈現藍色，而另一邊呈現綠色、黃色或褐色的情形。<ref name=messy1>Hartwell, Sarah. [http://www.messybeast.com/whitecat.htm White Cats, Eye Colours and Deafness], Messybeast.com. Retrieved February 2007.</ref>
