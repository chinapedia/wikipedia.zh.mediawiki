'''小q勒让德多项式'''(Little q-Legender polynomials)是一个以[[基本超几何函数|基本超几何函数]]定义的[[正交多项式|正交多项式]]]]<ref>Roelof Koekoek, Hypergeometric Orthogonal Polynomials and its q-Analogues, p486,Springer,2010</ref>




<math>P_n(x|q)=\;_{2}\phi_1\left(\begin{matrix} 
q^{-n} & q^{n+1}   \\ 
q   \end{matrix} 
; q,qx \right)</math>
==极限关系==
小q勒让德多项式→[[勒让德多项式|勒让德多项式]]

<math>\lim_{q \to 1}p_{n}(x|q)=P_{n}(1-2x)</math>
