'''小行星225'''是[[主小行星帶|主小行星帶]]上非常大的[[小行星|小行星]]，是第225顆被人類發現的[[小行星|小行星]]，位于[[火星|火星]]和[[木星|木星]]之間的[[小行星帶|小行星帶]]。<ref name="BMSALPO39_4_213"/>

它是由奧地利天文學家[[約翰·帕利扎|約翰·帕利扎]]於1882年4月19日發現，將其命名為Henrietta，命名來源是天文學家[[皮埃尔·让森|皮埃尔·让森]]的妻子。<!--

This is classified as a [[C-型小行星|C-型小行星]] and is probably composed of privitive [[碳|碳]]aceous material. It has very dark surface, with an albedo of 0.040. [[光度测定_(天文学)|光度测定 (天文学)]] measurements made from the Oakley Southern Sky Observatory during 2012 gave a [[光變曲線|光變曲線]] with a period of 7.352 ± 0.003 hours and a variation in brightness of 0.18 ± 0.02 in magnitude. This is consistent with a synodic rotation period of 7.356 ± 0.001 hours determined in 2000.<ref name="BMSALPO39_4_213"/>

In 2001, the asteroid was detected by radar from the [[阿雷西博天文台|阿雷西博天文台]] at a distance of 1.58 AU. The resulting data yielded an effective diameter of {{nowrap|128 ± 16 km}}.<ref name="icarus186_1_126"/>

225 Henrietta belongs to [[原神星族|原神星族]] {{tsl|en|asteroid group|}} of asteroids and is probably in a 4:7 [[轨道共振|轨道共振]] with the [[行星|行星]] [[木星|木星]].-->
