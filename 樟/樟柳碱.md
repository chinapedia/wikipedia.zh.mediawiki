'''樟柳碱'''('''Anisodine'''，或'''daturamine''')是一种[[生物碱|生物碱]]和[[抗膽鹼劑|抗膽鹼劑]]药物，在中国大陆用作[[解痙藥|解痙藥]]。<ref name="pmid2879586">{{cite journal |vauthors=Varma DR, Yue TL | title = Adrenoceptor blocking properties of atropine-like agents anisodamine and anisodine on brain and cardiovascular tissues of rats | journal = British Journal of Pharmacology | volume = 87 | issue = 3 | pages = 587–94 |date=March 1986 | pmid = 2879586 | pmc = 1916562 | doi = 10.1111/j.1476-5381.1986.tb10201.x| url = }}</ref><ref name="urlDictionary of pharmacological agents - Google Books">{{cite book | url = https://books.google.com/books?id=DeX7jgInYFMC&lpg=RA1-PA152&dq=anisodine&lr=&as_brr=3&pg=RA1-PA152#v=onepage&q=anisodine&f=false | title = Dictionary of pharmacological agents - Google Books | format = | work = | accessdate = }}</ref>它属于[[莨菪烷|莨菪烷]]型[[生物鹼|生物鹼]]，天然存在于[[茄科|茄科]]植物。<ref name="urlDictionary of pharmacological agents - Google Books"/>樟柳碱的生物活性机理是作为抗膽鹼劑与[[蕈毒鹼型乙醯膽鹼受體|蕈毒鹼型乙醯膽鹼受體]]以及作为{{en-link|α肾上腺素能受体激动剂|alpha-adrenergic agonist|激动剂}}与{{en-link|α1-肾上腺素能受体|alpha-adrenergic agonist|α<sub>1</sub>-肾上腺素能受体}}结合。<ref name="pmid2879586" />
