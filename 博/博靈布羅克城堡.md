'''博靈布羅克城堡'''（Bolingbroke Castle）是位於[[英格蘭|英格蘭]][[林肯郡|林肯郡]]博靈布羅克的一個城堡遺跡。博靈布羅克城堡始建於6世紀至7世紀時期<ref name="history">{{cite web|url=http://community.lincolnshire.gov.uk/BolingbrokeCastle/section.asp?catId=21791|title=Bolingbroke Castle - a brief history|accessdate=29 May 2009|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090622105725/http://community.lincolnshire.gov.uk/BolingbrokeCastle/section.asp?catId=21791|archivedate=2009年6月22日}}</ref>。至15至16世紀，城堡已經年久失修。現在博靈布羅克城堡被列入英格蘭遺產。夏季這裡會公演莎士比亞的劇目<ref name=SS>{{cite news|title=Bolingbroke Castle to host open air Shakespeare show|url=http://www.skegnessstandard.co.uk/news/business/bolingbroke-castle-to-host-open-air-shakespeare-show-1-3983360|accessdate=6 May 2013|newspaper=Skegness Standard|date=22 June 2012}}</ref>。
