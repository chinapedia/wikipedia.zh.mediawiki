'''房租过高党'''（{{lang-en|Rent Is Too Damn High Party}}），又译作'''房租太他妈高党'''，是[[纽约|纽约]]的一个在2005年和2009年[[纽约|纽约]]市长选举以及在2010年的州长选举提名过候选人的[[政党|政党]]。[[吉米·麦克米倫|吉米·麦克米倫]]是该党两次市长选举和一次州长选举的候选人。<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.mcmillan09.org/ |title=存档副本 |access-date=2010-11-03 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090130075536/http://www.mcmillan09.org/ |archive-date=2009-01-30 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>在2005年选举中他收到了4000多张选票。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://vote.nyc.ny.us/pdf/results/2005/general/Manhattan/Croossover%20Mayor%20Re-Cap%20NYC.pdf |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2009-11-03 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20091122151212/http://vote.nyc.ny.us/pdf/results/2005/general/Manhattan/Croossover%20Mayor%20Re-Cap%20NYC.pdf |archivedate=2009-11-22 }}</ref>该党在州内中有三个注册的会员。为参与民主党的初选，麦克米兰自己注册为一个[[美国民主党|民主党人]]。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.observer.com/2010/politics/jimmy-mcmillan-not-registered-member-rent-too-damn-high-party |title=Jimmy McMillan Is Not A Registered Member of The Rent Is Too Damn High Party | The New York Observer |publisher=Observer.com |date= |accessdate=2010-10-21 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20101023061537/http://www.observer.com/2010/politics/jimmy-mcmillan-not-registered-member-rent-too-damn-high-party |archivedate=2010-10-23 }}</ref><ref>Freedlander, David (2010-10-19). [http://www.observer.com/2010/politics/rent-too-damn-high-statewide-registration-three Rent Is Too Damn High Statewide Registration: Three]. ''New York Observer''. Retrieved 2010-10-19.</ref>

== 选举纲领 ==
房租过高党的主要纲领是纽约城市居民[[房租|房租]]过高，应降低以致纾缓财政压力、消除贫困和增加纽约居民的就业机会。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/blogs/dailypolitics/2010/10/jimmy-mcmillan-has-but-one-mes.html |title=Jimmy McMillan Has But One Message, People... Updated x 2 |publisher=Nydailynews.com |date= |accessdate=2010-10-21}}</ref>党口号之一就是「早餐、午餐和晚餐」，意味着该党旨在消除在纽约市的饥饿和贫穷。在2010年州长选举中，麦克米倫让他的政党容忍[[同性恋婚姻|同性恋婚姻]]<ref>{{cite news|last=|first=|url=http://news.sina.com.cn/w/2010-10-27/115921362283.shtml|title=美国老汉组建"房租太他妈高了"政党 获两千张选票|publisher=中国青年报|date=2010-10-28 |accessdate=2010-11-02}}</ref>。 他也是这次州长选举电视辩论的话题人物<ref>{{cite news|last=|first=|url=http://star.news.sohu.com/s2010/face34/|title=非新闻·小人物第34期-搜狐评论|publisher=搜狐|date=2010-11-03 |accessdate=2010-11-03}}</ref>。

== 过去的选举 ==
[[File:RITDHballotSM.png|thumb]]
在2009市长选举的竞选中，党的名称中“damn”这个单词被从官方选票中去除，因为党名有17个字母长，超出了州选举委员会规定的长度限制15个字母。<ref>{{cite news|last=Ong |first=Bao |url=http://cityroom.blogs.nytimes.com/2009/10/06/a-candidates-one-word-battle-with-the-elections-board/ |title=A Candidate's One-Word Battle With the Elections Board - NYTimes.com |publisher=Cityroom.blogs.nytimes.com |date=2009-10-06 |accessdate=2010-10-21}}</ref>2009年，在不被麦克米倫认可的情况下<ref>{{Cite journal|last=Amira |first=Dan |url=http://nymag.com/daily/intel/2009/10/rent_is_too_damn_high_party_ha.html |title=Rent Is Too (Damn) High Party Has Falling Out With Its Own Candidate - Daily Intel |publisher=Nymag.com |date= |accessdate=2010-10-21}}</ref>，萨利姆·埃贾兹竞选市财政官。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/news/election_2009/2009/10/28/2009-10-28_salim_ejaz.html |title=Salim Ejaz, Rent is Too Damn High Party for City Controller |publisher=Nydailynews.com |date=2009-10-29 |accessdate=2010-10-21}}</ref>

该党名「damn」一词，在2010年的选票上取其諧音將「too」縮寫成「2」而得以恢复。麦克米倫这次参选州长，而约瑟夫·赫夫挑战[[基尔斯滕·吉利布兰德|基尔斯滕·吉利布兰德]]持有的[[美国参议院|美国参议院]]席位。
