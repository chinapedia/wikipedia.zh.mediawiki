'''吓死爹妹子'''（{{lang en|Overly Attached Girlfriend}}，縮寫：{{lang|en|OAG}}）是一個在[[YouTube|YouTube]]影片「JB Fanvideo」首次出現的[[網絡爆紅|網絡爆紅]]虛構人物。該段影片原本是一段戲仿比賽的參影片，由YouTube用戶莉納（{{lang|en|Laina}}）於2012年6月6日上傳。<ref name="Fanvideo"/>

==成為網絡迷因==
吓死爹妹子於Youtube影片「JB Fanvideo」首次亮相，該段影片是於2012年6月6日由YouTube用戶莉納上傳的。<ref name="Fanvideo"/>起初，該段影片是一部用來參加[[賈斯汀·比伯|賈斯汀·比伯]]《{{link-en|男朋友 (賈斯汀·比伯)|Boyfriend (Justin Bieber song)|男朋友}}》戲仿比賽「女朋友」的影片。可是，該段影片卻被視為是為了諷刺賈斯汀·比伯而作的。在影片開始時，一名女性正在展露一個固定的笑容。這個「固定的笑容」便成為了影片的象徵。而該歌曲的歌詞則與在[[Facebook|Facebook]]跟踪男友有關。<ref>Wenjia Zhao. [http://www.forbes.com/sites/wenjiazhao/2012/06/14/rise-of-the-overly-attached-girlfriend/ "Rise of the Overly Attached Girlfriend".] ''Forbes'', 6/14/2012.</ref>該影片在上傳後不久即為社交新聞網站[[reddit|reddit]]所注意，並於上傳的第一日迅速累積了逾170,000次觀看次數。<ref>Ileana Llorens. [http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2012/06/07/overly-attached-girlfriend_n_1579133.html "Overly Attached Girlfriend: Justin Bieber Fan Creates 'Boyfriend' Parody About Attached Girlfriend".] ''[[Huffington_Post|Huffington Post]]'', 6/8/2012.</ref>此影片令到上傳者莉納成為一名[[網絡紅人|網絡紅人]]。截至2013年10月19日，莉納已擁有逾878,000名訂閱者，並且累積影片觀看次數已超越10,100,000次。儘管她的用戶名仍然是「wzr0173」，但顯示出來的用戶名已變成了「莉納」。<ref name="Youtube channel">[http://www.youtube.com/user/wzr0713/about about Laina - Youtube]</ref>

隨著影片爆紅，吓死爹妹子便成為了一個[[網絡迷因|網絡迷因]]，尤以該「固定的笑容」為最。而這些網絡迷因大多以圖片的形式出現，並將影片中的女性描述為一位跟蹤狂、嫉妒的傢伙和為愛人而不顧一切的女性。<ref>George Jones. [http://www.wistv.com/story/18790908/viral-do-not-date-this-girl-ever "Viral: Do not date this girl ... ever".] {{Wayback|url=http://www.wistv.com/story/18790908/viral-do-not-date-this-girl-ever |date=20131102022540 }} ''[[WIS|WIS]]'', 6/14/2012.</ref><ref>[http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2161236/Justin-Bieber-competition-Overly-Attached-Girlfriends-cover-version-spawns-hundreds-internet-memes.html "Meet 'Overly Attached Girlfriend!' Hilarious Justin Bieber cover version spawns hundreds of Internet memes".] ''[[Daily_Mail|Daily Mail]]'', 6/18/2012.</ref>這些圖片也在社交網站上迅速傳播。<ref>Fruzsina Eördögh. [http://www.dailydot.com/entertainment/bieber-girlfriend-contest-overly-attached-meme/ "Bieber girlfriend contest spawns Overly Attached Girlfriend meme".] ''The Daily Dot'', 6/7/2012.</ref>並被重製成各種東西。<ref>Lorraine Murphy. [http://www.dailydot.com/entertainment/morning-gif-bieber-girlfriend-meme/ "The Morning GIF: Overly Attached Girlfriend".] ''[[The_Daily_Dot|The Daily Dot]]'', 6/8/2012.</ref><ref>Alexander Abad-Santos, Jen Doll. [http://www.theatlanticwire.com/entertainment/2012/06/guide-attachment-dating-what-type-attachment-dater-are-you/53444/ "What Type of Attachment Dater Are You?".] ''[[The_Atlantic_Wire|The Atlantic Wire]]'', 6/12/2012.</ref>

因為影片獲得極大的回響，莉納便決定繼續製作同類影片，並且每個星期會更新一次。除了吓死爹妹子系列影片外，莉納還會製作其他類型的影片，如慈善籌款和採訪。影片發佈一年後，莉納已有595,000名訂閱者和76,500,000次累積觀看次數。<ref name="Youtube channel"/>而該影片本身也累積逾16,000,000次觀看次數。<ref name="Fanvideo">[http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Yh0AhrY9GjA JB Fanvideo - Youtube]</ref>

== 吓死爹妹子系列 ==
於2012年6月18日，莉納上傳發另一段影片。這次，影片中戲仿的對象並非賈斯汀·比伯的歌，而是[[卡莉·蕾·杰普森|卡莉·蕾·杰普森]]的單曲《[[有空叩我|有空叩我]]》。這段影片也變得非常流行。<ref>James Plafke. [http://www.geekosystem.com/overly-attached-girlfriend-carly-rae-jepsen-call-me-maybe/#0 "Overly Attached Girlfriend is Back With Parody of Carly Rae Jepsen’s 'Call Me Maybe'".] ''[[Geekosystem|Geekosystem]]'', 6/18/2012.</ref>除了《有空叩我》，莉納還製作了多個戲仿影片，其中包括[[後街男孩|後街男孩]]的《[[As_Long_as_You_Love_Me|As Long as You Love Me]]》、[[艾薇兒·拉維尼|艾薇兒·拉維尼]]的《[[女朋友_(艾薇兒·拉維尼歌曲)|女朋友]]》、[[凱莎|凱莎]]的《[[Your_Love_Is_My_Drug|Your Love Is My Drug]]》、[[泰勒·史薇芙特|泰勒·史薇芙特]]的《[[与我同在_(歌曲)|与我同在]]》等等。<ref>Brian Anthony Hernandez. [http://news.yahoo.com/overly-attached-girlfriend-returns-medley-stalker-songs-131019961.html "Overly Attached Girlfriend Returns With Medley of Stalker Songs".] Yahoo! News.</ref>而莉納於2012年12月17日和2013年1月14日上傳的了泰勒·斯威夫特《[[我们再也回不去了|我们再也回不去了]]》和[[一世代|一世代]]《[[What_Makes_You_Beautiful|What Makes You Beautiful]]》的戲仿影片則並非吓死爹妹子系列影片。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YBtWfMjyTtg |title=1D Parody - "Maybe You're Delusional" |publisher=YouTube |date= |accessdate=2013-01-16}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=Laina242,812 likes · 1,947 talking about this |url=http://www.facebook.com/laina622 |title=Laina |publisher=Facebook |date= |accessdate=2013-01-16}}</ref>於2013年6月6日，為了慶祝吓死爹妹子一周年紀念，莉納上傳了一部關於影片概覽、觀眾反應和電視露面的影片。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y99f8xcdGl0|title=Our One Year Anniversary|date=6 June 2013}}</ref>於2013年6月13日，她上傳了她的首個政治影片。該影片的戲仿對象為美国国歌《[[星条旗_(美国国歌)|星条旗]]》。在影片中，莉納指出她正在透過[[Skype|Skype]]、[[Facebook|Facebook]]和[[Google|Google]]監視觀眾們。這個影片實係諷刺美國用來監視全球的[[稜鏡計畫|稜鏡計畫]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=o3cw4185gvM |title=Overly Attached Uncle Sam |publisher=YouTube |date=2013-06-13 |accessdate=2013-06-17}}</ref>

==吓死爹妹子的身份==
上傳吓死爹妹子影片的用戶莉納是其真實名稱，本名為莉納·莫里斯（Laina Morris）。莫里斯生於1991年6月22日，出生於[[登頓_(德克薩斯州)|登頓]]。<ref>{{cite web|author=The Daily Dot |url=http://www.dailydot.com/culture/overly-attached-girlfriend-identified/ |title=Overly attached girlfriend identified |publisher=Daily Dot |date=2012-06-21 |accessdate=2013-03-12}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://centraltrack.com/Culture/1295/A-New-Sensation/We-Found-The-Overly-Attached-Girlfriend-Shes-A-Sophomore-At-UNT |title=Central Track // A New Sensation |publisher=Centraltrack.com |date=2012-06-25 |accessdate=2013-03-12 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130224002147/http://centraltrack.com/Culture/1295/A-New-Sensation/We-Found-The-Overly-Attached-Girlfriend-Shes-A-Sophomore-At-UNT |archivedate=2013-02-24 }}</ref><ref name="Gorman 2012">{{cite news|last=Gorman|first=Sean|title=Video: Denton girl responsible for Overly Attached Girlfriend meme|url=http://www.pegasusnews.com/news/2012/jul/19/denton-girl-overly-attached-girlfriend-meme/|accessdate=14 January 2013|newspaper=North Texas Daily|date=July 19, 2012|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131001183415/http://www.pegasusnews.com/news/2012/jul/19/denton-girl-overly-attached-girlfriend-meme/|archivedate=2013年10月1日}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://issuu.com/ntdaily/docs/magazine2 |title=Behind The Meme: Dentonite An Internet Sensation |last = Gorman |first = Sean |work=On The Record Magazine |publisher=NTDaily |date=2012-07-13 |accessdate=2013-01-15}}</ref>於2012年，莉納被[[Mashable|Mashable]]列為「15位因互聯網而聞名的人」之一。<ref>{{cite web|author=Christine Erickson|url=http://mashable.com/2012/11/15/internet-famous-people-2012/|title=15 People Made Famous by the Internet in 2012|work=Mashable|date=November 15, 2012|accessdate=December 4, 2012}}</ref><ref name="Whiteley 2012" />

莉納曾經嘗試展露自己一個更溫和的，更好玩的一面，但仍無法擺脫其「吓死爹妹子」的形象。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g9TfdBIfXoY |title=Two Years Later - Kia Soul |publisher=YouTube |date=2012-11-08 |accessdate=2013-01-10}}</ref>莉納亦曾製作多種影片，如慈善籌款影片和喜劇影片<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lQKPqDWBA8w |title=American Music Awards |publisher=YouTube |date=2012-11-26 |accessdate=2013-01-15}}</ref>，但仍然無助於改善形象。<ref name="Whiteley 2012">{{cite web |author=Jason Whitely |url=http://www.wfaa.com/home/Viral-video-star-shares-her-success-with-charity-181119251.html |title=Viral video star shares her success with charity |publisher=Wfaa.com |date=2012-11-27 |accessdate=2013-01-10 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121201031705/http://www.wfaa.com/home/Viral-video-star-shares-her-success-with-charity-181119251.html |archivedate=2012-12-01 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://rally.org/surfcamp |title=Surf Camp |publisher=Rally.org |date= |accessdate=2013-01-10}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mXt-PH6TW7w |title=Dare to Share: Episode 1 - Surf Camp |publisher=YouTube |date= |accessdate=2013-01-10}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=k4_DPboeEx8 |title=Laina (Overly Attached Girlfriend) on the Dare to Share Project | WHAT'S TRENDING |publisher=YouTube |date= |accessdate=2013-01-10}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.crowdsourcing.org/editorial/dare-to-share-most-unique-crowdfunding-campaigns-of-2012/22769 |title=Dare to Share: Most Unique Crowdfunding Campaigns of 2012 |publisher=Crowdsourcing.org |date=2012-12-21 |accessdate=2013-01-18 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130126111655/http://www.crowdsourcing.org/editorial/dare-to-share-most-unique-crowdfunding-campaigns-of-2012/22769 |archivedate=2013-01-26 }}</ref>
