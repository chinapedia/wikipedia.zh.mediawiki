'''龙担虎'''（学名：''Panthera zdanskyi''）是一种生存于约255到216万年前[[更新世|更新世]]初期的[[猫科|猫科]][[豹属|豹属]]动物。头骨发掘于[[中国|中国]][[甘肃|甘肃]]。<ref name=gjdl>{{cite web| title =甘肃龙担虎化石表明老虎可能起源于中国| url =http://news.dili360.com/kgtm/kgxc/2011/1205/31549.shtml| publisher =中国国家地理网| language =zh-hans| date =2011-12-05| deadurl =yes| archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20140304054001/http://news.dili360.com/kgtm/kgxc/2011/1205/31549.shtml| archivedate =2014-03-04}}</ref><ref name=hsw>{{cite web| title =甘肃惊现最古老老虎化石| url =http://bbs.uua.cn/thread-147128-1-1.html| publisher =化石网| language =zh-hans| date =2011-10-18| deadurl =yes| archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20140221175714/http://bbs.uua.cn/thread-147128-1-1.html| archivedate =2014-02-21}}</ref>

== 特征 ==
从[[形态学|形态学]]分析上来看和现存的[[虎|虎]]有很高的相似度，和虎具有很近的亲缘关系。但是龙担虎并不是从虎一支中演化出现的，而是一个和虎拥有共同的祖先的单独分支进化的古老原始物种，所以龙担虎的名字并不一定代表它是真正意义上的“老虎”。<ref name=Pzdan>{{cite journal | authors=Ji H. Mazák, Per Christiansen and Andrew C. Kitchener | year=2011 | title=Oldest Known Pantherine Skull and Evolution of the Tiger | url=http://www.plosone.org/article/info%3Adoi%2F10.1371%2Fjournal.pone.0025483 | language=en | journal=PLoS ONE | volume=6 | issue=10 | pages=e25483 | doi=10.1371/journal.pone.0025483 }}</ref>

体型小于虎更接近于[[美洲豹|美洲豹]]。<ref name=prehf>{{cite web | title = Panthera zdanskyi | url = http://prehistoric-fauna.com/panthera%20zdanskyi | publisher = prehistoric-fauna | language = en | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140222014816/http://prehistoric-fauna.com/panthera%20zdanskyi | archivedate = 2014-02-22 }}</ref>按照和头骨的比例来算[[犬齿|犬齿]]的长度大于现存所有的豹属成员。口鼻部凸出，双颊巨大，据推测具有很强的咬合力。<ref name=Pzdan/>
