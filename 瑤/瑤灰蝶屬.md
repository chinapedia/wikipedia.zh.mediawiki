Version 11 April 2008 (under construction). http://tolweb.org/Iolaus_s._s./120719/2008.04.11 in The Tree of Life Web Project, http://tolweb.org/ {{en}}</ref>。狹義上此屬不包括''[[Etesiolaus|Etesiolaus]]''和''[[Stugeta|Stugeta]]''分類的物種<ref>Brower, Andrew V. Z. 2008. Iolaus s. l. Hübner 1819. Version 11 April 2008 (under construction). http://tolweb.org/Iolaus_s._l./112357/2008.04.11 in The Tree of Life Web Project, http://tolweb.org/ {{en}}</ref>。
