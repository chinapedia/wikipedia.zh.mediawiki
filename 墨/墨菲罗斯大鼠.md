'''墨菲罗斯大鼠'''（{{lang|en|Murphy Roths Large}}）是在1999年发现的具有显著的[[组织|组织]][[再生|再生]]能力的[[小家鼠|小家鼠]]。<ref>{{cite journal |last1=Heber-Katz |first1=E |last2=Leferovich |first2=JM |last3=Bedelbaeva |first3=K |last4=Gourevitch |first4=D |title=Spallanzani's mouse: a model of restoration and regeneration |journal=Current topics in microbiology and immunology |volume=280 |pages=165–89 |year=2004 |pmid=14594211}}</ref><ref>[http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/science/nature/4888080.stm BBC News: Mouse sheds light on regeneration], 11 April 2006, By Rebecca Morelle, BBC News</ref><ref>[http://www.sciencentral.com/articles/view.php3?article_id=218392774&cat=1_2 ScienCentral: Self-Healing Mice] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081020095559/http://www.sciencentral.com/articles/view.php3?article_id=218392774&cat=1_2 |date=2008-10-20 }}, 2006/04/18, by Lindsay Carswell, ScienCentral News</ref>

== 研究 ==

墨菲罗斯大鼠是[[小鼠属|小鼠属]]中表现再生能力最强的品种。正在研究这些动物并试图把这种能力应用到人类身上。

通过比较愈合良好的MRL鼠和愈合不良的{{link-en|C57BL/6|C57BL/6}}鼠的[[基因表现|基因表现]]差异，已经确定36个[[基因|基因]]用于决定这种愈合能力<ref>{{cite journal | pmid = 15781240 | doi=10.1016/j.bbrc.2005.02.143 | volume=330 | issue=1 | title=Isolation of wound healing/regeneration genes using restrictive fragment differential display-PCR in MRL/MPJ and C57BL/6 mice | pages=117–22 | author=Masinde G, Li X, Baylink DJ, Nguyen B, Mohan S | journal = Biochemical and Biophysical Research Communications|date=April 2005}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|journal=Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences|date=2007 July|volume=104|issue=27 |title=Inhibition of p21-activated kinase rescues symptoms of fragile X syndrome in mice|author=Mansuo L. Hayashi, B. S. Shankaranarayana Rao, Jin-Soo Seo, Han-Saem Choi, Bridget M. Dolan, Se-Young Choi, Sumantra Chattarji, and Susumu Tonegawa|doi=10.1073/pnas.0705003104|pages=11489–94|pmid=17592139|pmc=1899186}}</ref>。

MRL鼠的再生能力保护他们免于[[心肌梗死|心肌梗死]]，成年哺乳动物心肌细胞的再生是十分局限的，因为心脏的心肌细胞几乎都处于{{link-en|G0期|G0 phase}}，即终末分化期。MRL鼠在心脏病发作后同一般的鼠类的心肌损伤和瘢痕组织数几乎一样<ref name=BiochemBRC_Abdullah_2005>{{cite journal|journal=Wound Repair Regen|date=2005 Mar-April|volume=13|issue=2 |pages=205–208|title=MRL mice fail to heal the heart in response to ischemia-reperfusion injury|author=Abdullah I, Lepore JJ, Epstein JA, Parmacek MS, Gruber PJ|pmid=15828946|doi=10.1111/j.1067-1927.2005.130212.x }}</ref>，然而，根据最新的研究证明，这种情况并非永远如此，心脏损伤后的MRL小鼠能够再生损伤部位的组织<ref>[http://www.eurekalert.org/pub_releases/2001-08/wi-rit080201.php Regeneration in the mammalian heart demonstrated by Wistar researchers]</ref>。
