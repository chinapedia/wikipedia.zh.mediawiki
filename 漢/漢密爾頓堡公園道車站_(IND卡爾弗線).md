'''漢密爾頓堡公園道車站'''（{{lang-en|Fort Hamilton Parkway}}）是[[紐約地鐵|紐約地鐵]][[IND卡爾弗線|IND卡爾弗線]]的一個慢車[[地鐵站|地鐵站]]，設有[[紐約地鐵F線|F線]]與[[紐約地鐵G線|G線]]（任何時候停站）列車服務。

此站設有兩個[[側式月台|側式月台]]和兩條慢車軌道。未使用的快車軌道在車站下方通過，無法在月台上看見。

車站以南，下層快車軌道設有鐘口預留作為沿{{link-en|漢密爾頓堡公園道|Fort Hamilton Parkway}}或與之平行的第十大道的地鐵路線。65街附近分成兩線後，主線以{{link-en|灣脊區 (布魯克林)|Bay Ridge, Brooklyn|灣脊區}}86街為總站，另一條路線則由主線向西前往{{link-en|史泰登島隧道|Staten Island Tunnel|已部分建成的隧道}}前往[[史泰登島|史泰登島]]。另外還有連接[[BMT西城線|BMT西城線]]到[[漢密爾頓堡公園道車站_(BMT西城線)|新烏德勒支大道]]的計劃。這些路線作為{{link-en|紐約地鐵擴展計劃 (1929－1940年)|Proposed New York City Subway expansion (1929–1940)|IND第二系統}}一部分規劃<ref name=Raskin-RoutesNotTaken-2013>{{Cite Routes Not Taken}}</ref><ref name="WheelsDroveNY-2012">{{cite book|author1=Roger P. Roess|author2=Gene Sansone|title=The Wheels That Drove New York: A History of the New York City Transit System|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=qfZ0VxuLoc0C&pg=PA416|date=August 23, 2012|publisher=Springer Science & Business Media|isbn=978-3-642-30484-2|pages=416–417}}</ref><ref name=Richmondtown-INDRedHookTunnel-1930>{{cite web|title=Suggested Rapid Transit Lines in Richmond Borough|url=http://www.historicrichmondtown.org/treasures/online-collections-database|website=[[Historic_Richmond_Town|historicrichmondtown.org]]|publisher=[[Historic_Richmond_Town|Historic Richmond Town]]|accessdate=July 31, 2015|date=1930|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150717072021/http://www.historicrichmondtown.org/treasures/online-collections-database|archivedate=2015-07-17}}</ref><ref name=Panix-SITunnel>{{cite web|url=http://www.panix.com/~danielc/nyc/sibktunl.htm |title=DC: A Tunnel from SI to Brooklyn? |publisher=Daniel Convissor |date= |accessdate=December 16, 2010}}</ref><ref name=NYTimes-SITunnel-April1931>{{cite web|title=Transit Progress on Staten Island|url=https://query.nytimes.com/mem/archive/pdf?res=9907E7D9113CE133A2575AC1A9629C946094D6CF|website=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|accessdate=June 27, 2015|date=April 19, 1931}}</ref><ref name=NYTImes-SITunnel-April1932>{{cite web|title=City Rapid Transit Urged in Richmond|url=https://query.nytimes.com/mem/archive/pdf?res=9906E3D6173EE633A2575AC1A9629C946394D6CF|website=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|accessdate=June 27, 2015|date=April 19, 1932}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=New Yorkers Urge Loan For Tunnel|url=https://query.nytimes.com/mem/archive/pdf?res=980CEEDD1331E633A25751C2A96F9C946394D6CF|website=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|accessdate=June 27, 2015|location=[[Washington,_D.C.|Washington, D.C.]]|date=September 22, 1932}}</ref><ref name=BklynEagle-Vogel-Culver-WestEnd-Aug1940>{{cite web|title=Vogel to Press for West End L, Culver Links|url=https://www.newspapers.com/image/52647398/?terms=culver%2Bramp%2Bsubway|via=[[Newspapers.com|Newspapers.com]]|newspaper=[[Brooklyn_Daily_Eagle|Brooklyn Daily Eagle]]|pages=1–2|date=August 14, 1940}}</ref>。

IND卡爾弗線的教堂大道和漢密爾頓堡公園道車站是最後在月台層由[[白熾燈|白熾燈]]更換成[[螢光燈|螢光燈]]的車站，在1987年進行。
