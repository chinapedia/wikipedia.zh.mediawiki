'''漢娜·基特'''（{{lang-en|Hannah Jeter}} <ref name="theplayerstribune.com">{{cite web|url=http://www.theplayerstribune.com/hannah-jeter-the-derek-jeter-i-know/|title=The Derek I Know - The Players' Tribune|publisher=|accessdate=2017-02-27}}</ref>，{{bd|1990年|5月5日}}），原名'''漢娜·戴維斯'''（{{lang-en|Hannah Davis}}），<ref>{{cite news|title=Happy birthday to 2015 SI Swimsuit cover model Hannah Davis!|url=http://www.si.com/swim-daily/2015/05/05/hannah-davis-25th-birthday|publisher=Sports Illustrated|accessdate=2016-02-27|first=Kelsey|last=Hendrix|date=2015-05-05}}</ref><ref name=us-engaged>{{cite news| url = http://www.usmagazine.com/celebrity-news/news/derek-jeter-confirms-hannah-davis-engagement-details-2015311 | title=Derek Jeter Confirms Hannah Davis Engagement: Details!| work=Us Weekly | date= 2015-11-03 | first = Stephanie | last = Webber |accessdate= 2015-11-03| quote= ...Jeter, 25, ...}}</ref>是一位[[美國人|美國]][[時尚|時尚]][[模特兒|模特兒]]，她曾亮相於2015年[[運動畫刊泳裝特輯|運動畫刊泳裝特輯]]封面。

==職業生涯==
戴維斯為[[拉夫·勞倫馬球|拉夫·勞倫馬球]]女裝Black Label出席代言活動，並且登上他們2006年的春夏時裝秀。拉夫·勞倫的香水花漾年華橘子搖滾（Ralph Rocks）的名人。<ref name="fmd">{{en}}{{cite web |url=http://www.fashionmodeldirectory.com/models/Hannah_Davis/ |title=HANNAH DAVIS |work=fashionmodeldirectory.com |publisher=The Fashion Model Directory |accessdate=2014-05-12 }}</ref>

她曾亮相於義大利2006年的《{{link-en|D雜誌|D Magazine|D}}》雜誌、墨西哥2009年7月的《[[ELLE|ELLE]]》、法國2012年9月的《[[FHM|FHM]]》還有南非2013年4月的《FHM》的雜誌封面。<ref name="fmd"/>

戴維斯曾是[[維多利亞的秘密|維多利亞的秘密]]、{{link-en|美國之鷹服飾|American Eagle Outfitters}}、[[湯米·希爾費格|湯米·希爾費格]]及[[Levi's|Levi's]]的模特兒。<ref name="fmd"/>她曾作為[[DirecTV|DirecTV]]精靈出現於電視廣告。<ref>{{en}}{{cite news |last=Wyatt |first=Swain |url=http://coed.com/2013/01/07/whos-that-girl-hannah-davis-directv-genie-photos/ |title=WHO'S THAT GIRL: HANNAH DAVIS, THE DIRECTV GENIE [PHOTOS] |work=coed.com |date=2013-01-07 |accessdate=2014-05-12 }}</ref>

2013年2月9日，戴維斯被宣布成為[[運動畫刊泳裝特輯|2013年運動畫刊泳裝特輯]]的一員。<ref name="b4in"/>

==個人生活==
戴維斯成長於[[美國|美國]][[美屬維京群島|美屬維京群島]][[聖托馬斯島_(美屬維爾京群島)|聖托馬斯島]]，黛比（Debi）與老康恩·傑·戴維斯（Conn Jay Davis, Sr.）之女。<ref>{{en}}{{cite journal en|url = http://issuu.com/davisandelkinscollege/docs/fall_2013/42|journal = Forward|title = Alumni Notes|pages = 42|volume = 58|issue = 2|year = 2013|publisher = Davis & Elkins College|accessdate = 2015-02-12}}</ref><ref>{{en}}{{cite web|url=http://athletics.colgate.edu/Football/archives/2004season/2004%20Player%20Profiles.PDF|title=Meet the Raiders|pages=24|format=PDF|work=goColgateRaiders.com|accessdate=2015-02-12}}</ref>她表示那裏成長最棒的一部份是「有友善的人民、美好的天氣還有能在海灘上的海浪聲中醒來」。<ref name="motw">{{en}}{{cite web |url=http://models.com/model_culture/model_of_week/Hannah/index.html |title=Model of the Week – Hannah |work=models.com |publisher=MODELS.com, Inc. |accessdate=2014-05-12 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070919114022/http://models.com/model_culture/model_of_week/Hannah/index.html |archivedate=2007-09-19 }}</ref>

戴維斯曾是一位出色的網球明星，在八歲時便開始打網球，她曾參加較低等級的加勒比網球巡迴賽，在14歲時被攝影師金尼·愛德華茲（Ginny Edwards）發掘前，她幾乎要進入職業網球生涯，而為了模特兒事業在18歲左右時便退出網球圈。<ref>{{en}}{{cite news |last=Gray |first=Andy |url=http://swimdaily.si.com/2013/03/22/hannah-davis-meet-the-model/ |title=Meet the Model: Hannah Davis |work=swimdaily.si.com |publisher=Time Warner Company |date=2013-03-22 |accessdate=2014-05-12 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140219073843/http://swimdaily.si.com/2013/03/22/hannah-davis-meet-the-model/ |archivedate=2014-02-19 }}</ref><ref>{{en}}{{cite news |last=Gray |first=Andy |url=http://swimdaily.si.com/2013/09/03/hannah-davis-discusses-tennis-modeling-sinow/ |title=Hannah Davis discusses the U.S. Open, why she pursued modeling instead of tennis |work=swimdaily.si.com |publisher=Time Warner Company |date=2013-09-03 |accessdate=2014-05-12 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140511015454/http://swimdaily.si.com/2013/09/03/hannah-davis-discusses-tennis-modeling-sinow/ |archivedate=2014-05-11 }}</ref>她也曾是維爾京群島國家排球隊的一員。<ref>{{en}}{{cite news |last=Lyn Pesce |first=Nicole |last2=Friedman |first2=Molly |url=http://www.nydailynews.com/entertainment/gossip/meet-derek-jeter-squeeze-hannah-davis-article-1.1428402 |title=Bronx bombshell: Meet Hannah Davis, the main squeeze of Derek Jeter |work=nydailynews.com |publisher=[[:en:Daily_News_(New_York)|New York Daily News]] |date=2013-08-16 |accessdate=2014-05-12 }}</ref>

據2015年10月的報導指出，戴維斯已和前[[紐約洋基|紐約洋基]]球員[[德瑞克·基特|德瑞克·基特]]訂婚。<ref>{{cite web |title= Derek Jeter set to marry Hannah Davis |url= http://www.nydailynews.com/entertainment/gossip/confidential/derek-jeter-set-marry-hannah-davis-article-1.2413797 |website= New York Daily News |publisher= [[:en:Daily_News_(New_York)|New York Daily News]] |date= 2015-10-27 |accessdate= 2015-10-27}}</ref>

2015年11月初，前[[紐約洋基|紐約洋基]]球員[[德瑞克·基特|德瑞克·基特]]證實此前有關他與漢娜訂婚的報導。<ref name=us-engaged /><ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.nydailynews.com/entertainment/gossip/confidential/derek-jeter-set-marry-hannah-davis-article-1.2413797|title= Derek Jeter set to marry Hannah Jeter |work= Daily News |location = New York|date= 2015-10-27|accessdate=2015-11-03}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.theplayerstribune.com/derek-jeter-meet-my-dog-kane/ | title=Kane|first=Derek|last=Jeter| date=2015-11-03| publisher=The Players' Tribune (Jeter official blog) | accessdate= 2015-11-06| archivedate= 2015-11-06|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151106035937/http://www.theplayerstribune.com/derek-jeter-meet-my-dog-kane/ | deadurl=no}}</ref>2016年7月9日，兩人在[[納帕谷|納帕谷]]結婚。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/tvshowbiz/article-3682817/Home-run-Yankees-icon-Derek-Jeter-Sports-Illustrated-model-Hannah-Davis-marry-California.html|title=Yankees legend Derek Jeter and Sports Illustrated model Hannah Davis marry in California |first1=Anneta |last1=Konstantinides |first2=Caitlin |last2=O'Toole |website=dailymail.co.uk|publisher=DailyMail|date=2016-07-10|accessdate=2017-04-20}}</ref>2017年2月13日，基特宣布她已懷上一名女兒。<ref name="theplayerstribune.com"/>
