《'''-{zh-cn:碧昂丝; zh-tw:絕對碧昂絲}-'''》（{{lang-en|''Beyoncé''}}）是[[美國|美國]]歌手[[碧昂絲·諾利斯|碧昂絲·諾利斯]]的第5張[[錄音室專輯|錄音室專輯]]，2013年12月13日由{{link-en|Parkwood娛樂|Parkwood Entertainment}}與[[哥倫比亞唱片|哥倫比亞唱片]]發行。這張專輯為「[[視覺專輯|視覺專輯]]」，每首歌曲都有音樂錄影帶來說明歌曲產生的過程，共收錄14首歌曲和17支音樂錄影帶<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/5839719/how-beyonce-stayed-a-secret-and-which-songs-will-be-the-first-singles|title=How 'Beyonce' Stayed a Secret, and Which Songs Will Be the First Singles|publisher=billboard|date=2013-12-13|language=en}}</ref>。

==反響==
專輯發行前沒有任何宣傳，碧昂絲只透過[[Instagram|Instagram]]和[[Facebook|Facebook]]宣布<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.cw.com.tw/article/article.action?id=5054593#|title=零宣傳費用！碧昂絲新專輯３小時賣８萬張|publisher=天下雜誌|date=2013-12-16|language=zh-tw}}</ref>，此舉意外掀起大眾的討論，根據[[推特|推特]]的統計，12個小時就有超過120萬則推文<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/5839698/beyonces-surprise-album-20-tweets-from-mind-blown-musicians|title=Beyonce's Surprise Album: 20 Tweets From Mind-Blown Musicians|publisher=billboard|date=2013-12-13|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://m.cn.nytimes.com/culture/20131219/t19beyonce/zh-hant/|title=碧昂斯發新專輯，拋棄傳統宣傳依賴社交媒體|publisher=紐約時報中文網|date=2013-12-19|language=zh-tw}}</ref>。

於[[iTunes_Store|iTunes Store]]推出僅三天全球就售出828,773張，[[美国|美國]]首周617,213張，刷新[[iTunes_Store|iTunes Store]]的專輯銷售紀錄，並同時在108個國家的iTunes Store都獲得第一名。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/5839825/beyonce-fastest-selling-album-ever-in-itunes-store-828773-in-three-days|title='Beyonce' Fastest-Selling Album Ever In iTunes Store: 828,773 In Three Days|publisher=billboard|date=2013-12-16|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.apple.com/pr/library/2013/12/16BEYONC-Shatters-iTunes-Store-Records-With-Over-828-773-Albums-Sold-in-Just-Three-Days.html |title=BEYONCÉ Shatters iTunes Store Records With 828,773 Albums Sold in Just Three Days |publisher=billboard |date=2013-12-16 |language=en |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131216210459/http://www.apple.com/pr/library/2013/12/16BEYONC-Shatters-iTunes-Store-Records-With-Over-828-773-Albums-Sold-in-Just-Three-Days.html |archivedate=2013-12-16 }}</ref>至今發行的5張個人專輯都連續空降告示牌專輯榜冠軍，是第一位女歌手達成此項紀錄。這張專輯評價及反應相當良好，市場銷售細水長流，至今已賣出超過800萬張。 <ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.chinatimes.com/newspapers/20131222000496-260112|title=新專輯6天賣破百萬 碧昂絲告示牌5連霸|publisher=紐約時報中文網|date=2013-12-22|language=zh-tw}}</ref>

==榮譽==
《-{zh-cn:碧昂丝; zh-tw:絕對碧昂絲}-》在[[第57屆葛萊美獎|第57屆葛萊美獎]]共入圍六項，成為[[葛萊美獎|葛萊美獎]]有史以來入圍次數最多的女歌手。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.ettoday.net/news/20141207/435764.htm#ixzz3euEzKnV9|title=碧昂絲入圍6項葛萊美　台灣《聽見彩虹謠》也驚豔國際|publisher=ETtoday|date=2014-12-07|language=zh-tw}}</ref>最後總共獲得三項大獎，包含最佳節奏藍調表演、最佳節奏藍調歌曲、最佳立體聲專輯。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.ettoday.net/news/20150209/465138.htm|title=葛萊美／第57屆葛萊美得獎名單！山姆史密斯成最大贏家|publisher=ETtoday|date=2015-02-09|language=zh-tw}}</ref>

* "Flawless" contains two parts – "Bow Down" and "Flawless", which are presented as one song, and one video. "Bow Down" was initially part of a previously recorded song entitled "Bow Down/I Been On".
* "Grown Woman" is presented after the credits on DVD while it is presented before the credits in digital format.
* "Grown Woman" is written by Mosley, [[Kelly_Sheehan|Kelly Sheehan]], Knowles, Nash, Chris Godbey, Harmon, Darryl Pearson, and Garland Mosley. It is produced by Timbaland, with co-production by Harmon.

'''Sample credits'''
* "Partition" contains an interpolation of the French-dubbed version of the 1998 film ''[[The_Big_Lebowski|The Big Lebowski]]'', performed by Hajiba Fahmy.
* "Flawless" contains portions of the speech "We should all be feminists", written and delivered by [[Chimamanda_Ngozi_Adichie|Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie]].
* "Heaven" contains portions of "[[The_Lord's_Prayer|The Lord's Prayer]]" in Spanish, performed by Melissa Vargas.
