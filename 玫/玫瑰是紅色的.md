'''玫瑰是紅色的'''（'''Roses are red'''）是一首英格蘭詩歌，也指由這首詩引發出的一系列[[打油詩|打油詩]]。它常被認為是一首愛情詩，也被編成民歌傳唱。

==歌詞==
這首歌最常見的版本是
{{MultiCol}}
:Roses are red,
:Violets are blue,
:Sugar is sweet,
:And so are you.
{{ColBreak}} 
:玫瑰是紅色的，
:紫羅蘭是藍色的，
:糖是甜的，
:你也是一樣。
{{EndMultiCol}}

==起源==

這首歌的歷史至少可以追溯至1590年[[埃德蒙·斯賓塞|埃德蒙·斯賓塞]]的《[[仙后|仙后]]》（Book Three, Canto 6, Stanza 6）：<ref>[http://www.sacred-texts.com/neu/eng/fq/fq32.htm ''The Faerie Queene'', Cant. VI.]</ref>

:It was upon a Sommers shynie day,
:When [[赫利俄斯|Titan]] faire his beames did display,
:In a fresh fountaine, farre from all mens vew,
:She bath'd her brest, the boyling heat t'allay;
:She bath'd with roses red, and violets blew,
:And all the sweetest flowers, that in the forrest grew.  

現代[[情人節|情人節]]常用的歌詞則來自1784年的英格蘭童謠集《Gammer Gurton's Garland》：

:The rose is red, the violet's blue,
:The honey's sweet, and so are you.
:Thou are my love and I am thine;
:I drew thee to my Valentine:
:The [[抽籤|lot]] was cast and then I drew,
:And Fortune said it shou'd be you.<ref>I. Opie and P. Opie, ''The Oxford Dictionary of Nursery Rhymes'' (Oxford University Press, 1951, 2nd edn., 1997), p. 375.</ref>

[[維克多·雨果|維克多·雨果]]1862年出版的《[[悲慘世界|悲慘世界]]》中，芳汀也唱過這首歌，法語為：<ref>[http://www.gutenberg.org/files/17489/17489-h/17489-h.htm#Chapitre_VIg ''Les misérables'', Tome I by Victor Hugo] at [[Project_Gutenberg|Project Gutenberg]]</ref>

:Les bleuets sont bleus, les roses sont roses,
:Les bleuets sont bleus, j'aime mes amours.

==改編==

這首詩還被改編成了諷刺版，例如：<ref>S. J. Bronner, ''American Children’s Folklore'' (August House: 1988), p. 84.</ref>
{{MultiCol}}
:Roses are red.
:Violets are blue.
:Onions stink.
:And so do you.<ref name=Hopscotch>"[http://www.hopscotch.com.au/hopscotch-articles/2005/5/18/jill-still-playing-jacks-and-hopscotch-endures/ Jill Still Playing Jacks And Hopscotch Endures]", retrieved 17 September 2009.</ref>
{{ColBreak}}
:玫瑰是紅色的，
:紫羅蘭是藍色的，
:洋蔥發臭，
:你也是一樣。
{{EndMultiCol}}
[[馬克思兄弟|馬克思兄弟]]的電影《[[趾高氣揚|趾高氣揚]]》中則將之改編為：
{{MultiCol}}
:Cirrhosis are red,
:so violets are blue,
:so sugar is sweet,
:so so are you.<ref>[http://www.marx-brothers.org/whyaduck/info/movies/scenes/wagstaff.htm Horse feathers]</ref>
{{ColBreak}}
:肝硬化是紅色的，
:所以紫羅蘭是藍色的，
:所以糖是甜的，
:所以你也是一樣。
{{EndMultiCol}}
