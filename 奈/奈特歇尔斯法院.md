'''奈特歇尔斯法院'''{{lang-en|Knightshayes Court}}是一座[[维多利亚时代|维多利亚时代]]的[[英國鄉村別墅|英國鄉村別墅]]，位于[[英格兰|英格兰]][[德文郡|德文郡]][[蒂弗頓|蒂弗頓]]，由[[威廉·伯吉斯|威廉·伯吉斯]]设计。{{sfn|Cherry & Pevsner|p.526}}  The house is [[登錄建築|登錄建築]].<ref>{{cite web|author=Good Stuff |url=http://britishlistedbuildings.co.uk/en-485137-knightshayes-court-tiverton |title=Knightshayes Court - Tiverton - Devon - England |publisher=British Listed Buildings |date=1975-05-12 |accessdate=2016-09-04}}</ref> The gardens are Grade II* listed in the {{tsl|en|National Register of Historic Parks and Gardens||National Register of Historic Parks and Gardens}}.<ref>{{NHLE |num=1000487 |desc=Knightshayes Court |accessdate=12 February 2016 }}</ref>
