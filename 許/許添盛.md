'''許添盛'''（{{birth date and age|1968|1|12}}<ref>[https://www.facebook.com/profile.php?id=100005123131857&sk=about]</ref>）。雲林人，畢業於[[台北医学院|台北医学院]]。已婚。其著作《許醫師諮商現場：安頓情緒的45個絕妙處方》曾獲中國時報開卷版100種抗煞好書推薦。<ref>[http://www.laloln.com/index.php?Itemid=104&flypage=flypage.tpl&option=com_virtuemart&page=shop.product_details&pop=1&product_id=39&vmcchk=1&Itemid=104 許醫師諮商現場：安頓情緒的45個絕妙處方] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140407070757/http://www.laloln.com/index.php?Itemid=104&flypage=flypage.tpl&option=com_virtuemart&page=shop.product_details&pop=1&product_id=39&vmcchk=1&Itemid=104 |date=2014-04-07 }}</ref> 《绝处逢生》一書获得行政院[[卫生署|卫生署]]国民健康局「2004健康好书(癌症防治类)推介奖」。<ref>[http://c.360webcache.com/c?m=4720db8d2ecaabf24c6d56c5bef8eca9&q=%E8%A8%B1%E6%B7%BB%E7%9B%9B+%E6%A6%AE%E7%8D%B2&u=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.sethtaiwan.com%2Farticle.php%3Fid%3D24 赛斯身心灵整体健康观]</ref>該書亦有英文版翻譯。<ref>[http://www.amazon.com/The-Secret-Healing-Cancer-Psychological/dp/0984928502 The Secret To Healing Cancer]</ref><ref>[https://sites.google.com/a/seth.org.tw/education/products/books/thesecrettohealingcancer THE SECRET TO HEALING CANCER(絕處逢生外版)]</ref>
