'''噻蒽'''（化学式：C<sub>12</sub>H<sub>8</sub>S<sub>2</sub>）是一种含硫[[杂环化合物|杂环化合物]]，易被[[氧化|氧化]]。

==结构==
类似于其它1,4-二噻英，但不同于其氧代类似物[[二苯并对二噁英|二苯并二噁英]]，噻蒽的形状不是平面的，而是弯曲的，两个苯并基团间的折叠角为128°。<ref>{{Cite journal | last1 = Hosoya | first1 = S. | title = Molecular shapes of thianthrene and related heterocyclic compounds | journal = Acta Crystallographica | volume = 16 | pages = 310–312 | year = 1963 | doi = 10.1107/S0365110X63000797 | issue = 4}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Gallaher | first1 = K. L. | last2 = Bauer | first2 = S. H. | title = Structure and inversion potential of thianthren | journal = Journal of the Chemical Society, Faraday Transactions 2 | volume = 71 | pages = 1173–1182 | year = 1975 | doi = 10.1039/F29757101173}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Aroney | first1 = M. J. | last2 = Le Fèvre | first2 = R. J. W. | last3 = Saxby | first3 = J. D. | title = 92. Molecular polarisability. The apparent conformations of thianthren and of three of its oxides as solutes in benzene | journal = Journal of the Chemical Society (Resumed) | pages = 571–575 | year = 1965 | doi = 10.1039/JR9650000571}}</ref>

==制备==
噻蒽可以通过在氯化铝存在下用[[二氯化二硫|二氯化二硫]]和[[苯|苯]]的反应制备。<ref>{{cite patent | title = Process for the manufacture of thianthrene | country = US | number = 3997560 | status = patent | gdate = 1976-12-14}}.</ref>

== 历史 ==
John Stenhouse第一次通过[[苯磺酸钠|苯磺酸钠]]的干馏制备出噻蒽。<ref>{{cite journal| last1 = Stenhouse| first1 = J.| title = Ueber die Producte der trockenen Destillation der sulfobenzolsauren Salze| journal = Annalen der Chemie und Pharmacie| volume = 149| pages = 247–255| year = 1869| doi = 10.1002/jlac.18691490216| issue = 2}}</ref> 它可以被[[硫酸|硫酸]]氧化，形成红色的[[自由基|自由基]][[阳离子|阳离子]]<ref>W. Dilthey: Versammlungsberichte Bonner Chemische Gesellschaft, ''[[Angewandte_Chemie|Angewandte Chemie]]'', Volume 42, Issue 24, pp. 668–670, 15. June 1929; {{DOI|10.1002/ange.19290422405}}.</ref>，该离子C<sub>12</sub>H<sub>8</sub>S<sub>2</sub><sup>•+</sup>通过电子顺磁共振表征。四种不同的文献描述了C<sub>12</sub>H<sub>8</sub>S<sub>2</sub><sup>•+</sup>盐的晶体结构。<ref>{{cite book | url = https://books.google.com/books?id=1LdKSYIQtPYC&pg=PA202 | chapter = EPR and the History of the Thianthrene Cation Radical | first = Henry J. | last = Shine | title = Foundations of modern EPR | isbn = 978-981-02-3295-5 | date = July 1998}}</ref>
