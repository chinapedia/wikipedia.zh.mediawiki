'''维鲁萨'''（Wilusa，[[赫梯语|赫梯语]]：<sup>URU</sup>''Wi-lu-ša''）是[[青铜时代|青铜时代]]晚期位于[[小亚细亚|小亚细亚]]西部[[亚苏瓦盟|亚苏瓦盟]]的一座[[城邦|城邦]]。<ref>J. Latacz, ''[http://www.uni-tuebingen.de/troia/eng/wilusaeng.pdf Wilusa (Wilios/Troia)] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110606173702/http://www.uni-tuebingen.de/troia/eng/wilusaeng.pdf |date=2011-06-06 }}'' (2001)</ref>

在现存的公元前13世纪的[[赫梯|赫梯]]文献中，维鲁萨之名多次出现。<ref>[http://www.hittites.info/translations.aspx?text=translations/historical%2fManapa-Tarhunta+Letter.html Translation of the Manapa-Tarhunta Letter] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131104112702/http://www.hittites.info/translations.aspx?text=translations%2Fhistorical%2FManapa-Tarhunta+Letter.html |date=2013-11-04 }}</ref><ref>Hoffner, Beckman. ''[http://books.google.ca/books?id=7B2y1lSFMFAC&pg=PA297 Letters from the Hittite Kingdom]'', 2009. p. 297.</ref>一些学者认为维鲁萨可以对应[[特洛伊|特洛伊]]VIIa考古遗址，也即是[[特洛伊战争|特洛伊战争]]史诗集群中所描述的特洛伊。赫梯语中的Wilusa被认为可能与[[希腊语|希腊语]]中表示特洛伊的''Ilios''/''Ilion'' (Ἴλιος, Ἴλιον)同源。<ref>R. S. P. Beekes, ''Etymological Dictionary of Greek'', Brill, 2009, p. 588.</ref>
