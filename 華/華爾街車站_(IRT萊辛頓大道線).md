'''華爾街車站'''（{{lang-en|Wall Street}}）是[[紐約地鐵|紐約地鐵]][[IRT萊辛頓大道線|IRT萊辛頓大道線]]一個[[地鐵站|地鐵站]]，位於[[曼哈頓|曼哈頓]][[百老匯|百老匯]]和[[華爾街|華爾街]]交界，設有[[紐約地鐵4號線|4號線]]（任何時候停站）與[[紐約地鐵5號線|5號線]]（任何時候停站（深夜除外））列車服務。

此站於1905年6月12日作為原初地鐵由福爾頓街的延長線啟用<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://query.nytimes.com/mem/archive-free/pdf?res=9503E5D71E3DE633A25750C1A9609C946497D6CF|title=SUBWAY TRAINS RUN AGAIN THIS MORNING; Through Service Promised for the rush-Hour Crowds. TUNNEL PUMPED OUT AT LAST Big Water Main That Burst Was an Old One, Pressed Into Service Again After a Five-Hour Watch.|access-date=2016-09-18}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://query.nytimes.com/mem/archive-free/pdf?res=9800EFDE103AE733A25754C0A9609C946497D6CF|title=SUBWAY TO WALL ST. OPEN IN TEN DAYS; And All the Way to the Bronx by July 1. WHOLE ROAD READY IN AUGUST As to the Air Therein, William Barclay Parsons Says It Is Pure and Can't Be Bettered.|access-date=2016-09-18}}</ref>。1994年1月6日，此站與[[南碼頭-白廳街車站|白廳街車站]]引入了自動三棍閘機<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.mta.info/nyct/facts/ffhist.htm|title=About NYC Transit - History|date=2002-10-19|access-date=2016-09-18|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20021019203759/http://www.mta.info/nyct/facts/ffhist.htm|archivedate=2002-10-19}}</ref>。

華爾街車站是一個地下車站，設有兩個[[側式月台|側式月台]]和兩條軌道，兩個月台之間有少許偏差。
