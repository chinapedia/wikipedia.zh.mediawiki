'''钙通道'''（{{lang-en|Calcium channel}}，台湾极少数时亦称为'''鈣徑'''）是选择性通透[[钙|Ca<sup>2+</sup>]]的[[离子通道|离子通道]]，有时也是[[电压依赖性钙通道|电压依赖性钙通道]]（{{tsl|en|Voltage-dependent calcium channel|VDCC}}）的同义词<ref>[http://www.mercksource.com/pp/us/cns/cns_hl_dorlands_split.jsp?pg=/ppdocs/us/common/dorlands/dorland/two/000019812.htm ''calcium channel''] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090510082358/http://www.mercksource.com/pp/us/cns/cns_hl_dorlands_split.jsp?pg=%2Fppdocs%2Fus%2Fcommon%2Fdorlands%2Fdorland%2Ftwo%2F000019812.htm |date=2009-05-10 }} at Dorland's Medical Dictionary</ref>，而另外一种钙通道是[[配体门控性钙通道|配体门控性钙通道]]（{{tsl|en|Ligand-gated ion channel|LGCC}}）。<ref name="pmid8791458">{{cite journal |author=F Striggow，BE Ehrlich |title=《Ligand-gated calcium channels inside and out》 |journal=Curr. Opin. Cell Biol. |volume=总第8期 |issue=第4期 |pages=第490–495页 |pmid=8791458 |doi= |url=http://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0955-0674(96)80025-1|date=August 1996}}</ref>
