'''貝西薩哈'''（[[英語|英語]]：Besishahar；{{lang-ne|[[:ne:बेसीशहर_नगरपालिका|बेसीशहर नगरपालिका]]}}），是[[尼泊爾|尼泊爾]]的一個[[基層政權|城鎮]]，也是[[甘達基專區|甘達基專區]][[拉姆瓊縣|拉姆瓊縣]]的縣治所在地。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ddclamjung.gov.np|title= Lamjung District|publisher= District Development Committee Lamjung|accessdate=2014-09-15}}</ref>貝西薩哈城鎮係於2014年5月15日由鄰近幾個聚落共同組成。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ddclamjung.gov.np/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=259&Itemid=240|title= Population & Ward Division Of Besishahar Municipality|publisher= District Development Committee Lamjung|accessdate=2014-09-15}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.myrepublica.com/portal/index.php?action=news_details&news_id=74336|title=72 new municipalities announced|publisher=My Republica.com|accessdate=2014-05-15}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ekantipur.com/the-kathmandu-post/2014/05/08/news/govt-announces-72-new-municipalities/262559.html|title=Government announces 72 new municipalities|publisher= The Kathmandu Post|accessdate=2014-05-15}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ddclamjung.gov.np/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=80&Itemid=71|title= Map Of Besishahar Municipality & Lamjung District|publisher= District Development Committee Lamjung|accessdate=2014-12-15}}</ref>這裡有不同的[[種姓|種姓]]與[[宗教|宗教]]，因而擁有多元[[文化|文化]]。

貝西薩哈基礎政區具有溫暖的亞熱帶氣候型態，與落葉闊葉林的植被環境。在貝西薩哈城鎮北端，可以眺望[[安納布爾納峰|安納布爾納峰]]、[[魚尾峰|魚尾峰]]、拉姆瓊喜馬爾峰等美景。該城鎮下轄14個聚落。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://myrepublica.com/portal/index.php?action=news_details&news_id=79671|title=New Municipalities Come In To Effect (With Names Of Included VDCs And New Ward Areas)|publisher=Republica|accessdate=2014-06-15|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140806004150/http://myrepublica.com/portal/index.php?action=news_details&news_id=79671|archivedate=2014-08-06}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ddclamjung.gov.np/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=259&Itemid=240|title= Population & Ward Division Of Besishahar Municipality|publisher= District Development Committee Lamjung|accessdate=2014-09-15}}</ref>
