'''巨猿'''（[[学名|学名]]：''{{lang|la|Gigantopithecus}}''）是已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的一[[屬|屬]][[猿|猿]]，生存於約100-30萬年前的[[中國|中國]]、[[印度|印度]]及[[越南|越南]]<ref name=Christmas>{{cite web | last=Christmas | first=Jane | url=http://dailynews.mcmaster.ca/story.cfm?id=3637 | title=Giant Ape lived alongside humans | publisher=McMaster University | date=2005-11-07 | accessdate=2007-12-06 | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120206215219/http://dailynews.mcmaster.ca/story.cfm?id=3637 | archivedate=2012-02-06 }}</ref>，與幾種[[人科|人科]]在時間框及地理位置上相同。<ref name=Ciochon>{{cite journal en | author=Ciochon, R. ''et al'' | year=1996 | title=Dated Co-Occurrence of ''Homo erectus'' and ''Gigantopithecus'' from Tham Khuyen Cave, Vietnam | journal=Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences of the United States of America | volume=93 | issue=7 | pages=3016-3020 | url=http://www.uiowa.edu/~bioanth/PNAS%20Giganto-Vietnam.pdf | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080731123959/http://www.uiowa.edu/~bioanth/PNAS%20Giganto-Vietnam.pdf | archivedate=2008-07-31 }}</ref>[[化石紀錄|化石紀錄]]指[[步氏巨猿|步氏巨猿]]是最巨大的猿，站立時高3米及重545公斤。<ref name=Christmas/><ref name=ApeThatWas>{{cite web|last=Ciochon |first=Russell L |title=The Ape that Was - Asian fossils reveal humanity's giant cousin |url=http://www.uiowa.edu/%7Ebioanth/giganto.html |publisher=University of Iowa |accessdate=2007-12-06 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150525202625/http://www.uiowa.edu/~bioanth/giganto.html |archivedate=2015-05-25 }}</ref><ref name=Pettifor>{{cite book en| last=Pettifor | first=Eric | title=Selected Readings in Physical Anthropology | chapter=From the Teeth of the Dragon: ''Gigantopithecus Blacki''| pages=pp 143-149 | url=http://www.wynja.com/arch/gigantopithecus.html | year=2000 | origyear=1995 | publisher=Kendall/Hunt Publishing Company | isbn=0787271551}}</ref>

==化石遺骸==
[[File:F._Schrenk_mit_Gigantopithecus-Molar_2005.jpg|thumb]]的[[臼齒|臼齒]]。]]
首個由[[人類學家|人類學家]][[孔尼華|孔尼華]]（Ralph von Koenigswald）描述的巨猿[[化石|化石]]是於1935年在香港的一家中藥店發現的，包括了一些化石化的[[牙齒|牙齒]]及骨頭，預備磨碎作為[[中藥|中藥]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.uiowa.edu/~nathist/Site/giganto.html |title=How Gigantopithecus was discovered |publisher=The University of Iowa Museum of Natural History |accessdate=2007-12-06 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071012201552/http://www.uiowa.edu/~nathist/Site/giganto.html |archivedate=2007-10-12 }}</ref>孔尼華將這個牠命名巨猿。<ref name=Relethford>{{cite book en| author=Relethford, J. | title=The Human Species: An Introduction to Biological Anthropology | publisher=McGraw-Hill | year=2003 | isbn=978-0767430227}}</ref>

很少巨猿的化石被發現。除了在那中藥店發現的[[臼齒|臼齒]]外，在[[中國|中國]][[柳州|柳州]]亦有發現[[步氏巨猿|步氏巨猿]]的牙齒及幾塊[[顎骨|顎骨]]。<ref name=ApeThatWas/>其他發現的地方包括[[越南|越南]]及[[印度|印度]]。<ref name=Ciochon/><ref name=Pettifor/>從化石的發現地可見巨猿是分佈在[[亞洲|亞洲]]東南部。

中國體質人類學家及古靈長類學家[[董悌忱|董悌忱]]認為由於巨猿自身俱有一系列鮮明的差異性,例如巨猿下頜聯合正中矢狀斷面及第三下頜與其他猿類及人類下頜齒槽弓輪廓的區別。巨猿代表了靈長類譜系樹上一個完全獨立的分支。因此，他以這種理由認為Gigantopithecinae是一個以Gigantopithecus為其分類屬的新亞科，在邏輯上屬於Pongidae，而不是Hominidae。
