'''富士施樂大廈'''（'''Fuji Xerox Towers'''，原稱：'''IBM Towers'''<ref name=DIRECTORY>{{citeweb | title = Asia Travel : Map of Fuji Xerox Towers (S) 079907 | publisher = StreetDirectory.com | url = http://www.streetdirectory.com/asia_travel/travel/travel_id_12740/travel_site_3915/ | accessdate = 2008-02-11 }}</ref>）是[[新加坡|新加坡]]中心區的一座38層[[摩天大樓|摩天大樓]]，高達{{convert|165|m}}<ref>{{citeweb | title = Anson Road | publisher = [[National_Library_Board|National Library Board]] | url = http://infopedia.nlb.gov.sg/articles/SIP_348_2004-12-17.html | accessdate = 2008-02-11 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20080129052042/http://infopedia.nlb.gov.sg/articles/SIP_348_2004-12-17.html | archivedate = 2008-01-29 }}</ref><ref name=PAGENATION>{{citeweb | title = Fuji Xerox Tower Building is a place in Singapore on the Map of Singapore | publisher = SGPageNation | url = http://sg.pagenation.com/sin/Fuji%20Xerox%20Building_103.8438_1.2729.map | accessdate = 2008-02-11 }}</ref>。

原先因為[[IBM|IBM]]在2004年前一直是其關鍵承租人而被稱為IBM Towers，2004年8月中旬，[[富士施樂|富士施樂]]租下了{{convert|93000|sqft|m2}}，轉而成為新的關鍵承租人，因而也獲得了大廈的命名權<ref name="PAGENATION"/>。IBM後來搬到了[[樟宜商业园|樟宜商业园]]。<ref name="PAGENATION"/>
