'''四硼化铁'''（FeB<sub>4</sub>）是新近发现的一种[[超硬材料|超硬材料]]和[[超导材料|超导材料]]，已由[[拜罗伊特大学|拜罗伊特大学]]的[[缑慧阳|缑慧阳]]等人在实验室中成功合成。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://arxiv.org/abs/1304.5106|title=Discovery of a superhard iron tetraboride superconductor|last=Huiyang Gou|coauthors=et al|date=18 April 2013|work=arXiv:1304.5106 [cond-mat.mtrl-sci]|accessdate=17 October 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|url=http://physics.aps.org/featured-article-pdf/10.1103/PhysRevLett.111.157002 |last=Huiyang Gou|coauthors=et al|date=11 October 2013|title=Discovery of a Superhard Iron Tetraboride Superconductor|journal=Physical Review Letters|volume=111|issue=157002|doi=10.1103/PhysRevLett.111.157002|accessdate=17 October 2013}}</ref>它是第一种先由计算机设计，再根据计算结果制备出的[[超导体|超导体]]。<ref>{{cite news|author=Rachel Coker|title=First Computer-Designed Superconductor Created|url=http://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2013/10/131008132851.htm|accessdate=25 November 2013|newspaper=ScienceDaily|date=8 October 2013}}</ref>
