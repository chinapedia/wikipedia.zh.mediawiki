'''四氢噻吩溴化亚金'''（{{lang-en|Bromo(tetrahydrothiophene)gold(I)|}}）是[[金|金]]的配合物，和更常用的[[四氢噻吩氯化亚金|四氢噻吩氯化亚金]]類似，[[四氢噻吩|四氢噻吩]]基容易被其他較強的配基取代，形成線性的金溴錯合物。

此化合物可以用[[溴金酸|溴金酸]]（由[[氯金酸|氯金酸]]和[[氫溴酸|氫溴酸]]制備）和[[四氢噻吩|四氢噻吩]]制備<ref>{{cite journal | last1 =Eiter | first1 =Lauren C. | last2 =Hall | first2 =Nathan W. | last3 =Day | first3 =Cynthia S. | last4 =Saluta | first4 =Gilda | last5 =Kucera | first5 =Gregory L. | last6 =Bierbach | first6 =Ulrich | title =Gold(I) Analogues of a Platinum–Acridine Antitumor Agent are only Moderately Cytotoxic but Show Potent Activity Against Mycobacterium Tuberculosis | journal =Journal of Medicinal Chemistry | volume =52 | pages =6519–22 | year =2009 | doi =10.1021/jm9012856 | issue =21 | pmid =19803526 | pmc =3176588}}</ref>。
