'''四氟化錳'''，是锰的氟化物中，锰元素价态最高的化合物，同时也是一种强[[氧化剂|氧化剂]]，可以分解为[[三氟化錳|三氟化錳]]和[[氟气|氟气]]，通过合成四氟化锰随后将其分解产生氟气的方法可以纯化单质氟<ref name="AstorPrepn"/><ref name="SolvayPurif">{{citation | assignee = Solvay Fluor | title = Process for the purification of elemental fluorine | country-code = WO | publication-date = 2009-06-18 | editor1-last = Seseke-Koyro | editor1-first = Ulrich | editor2-last = Garcia-Juan | editor2-first = Placido | editor3-last = Palsherm | editor3-first = Stefan | editor4-last = Schulz | editor4-first = Alf | contribution = PCT Appl. | publication-place = 2009074562}}.</ref>。

==合成方法==
四氟化锰的制备方法在1961年被明确地报道出来{{NoteTag|在此之前虽有关于四氟化锰合成方法的文献报道<ref>{{citation | first = W. H. | last = Melville | title = Contribution towards the History of the Fluorides of Manganese | journal = Proc. Am. Acad. Arts Sci. | volume = 12 | year = 1876 | pages = 228–34 | jstor = 25138452 | doi = 10.2307/25138452}}.</ref>，但是合成出的化合物却与已知的四氟化锰的性质不符。}}。通过在550°C下将氟气通入二氟化锰或其他二价锰化合物并与之反应即可得到四氟化锰，产生的四氟化锰升华成气态后在一个指形冷冻器内凝华得到四氟化锰的固体产物<ref name="Hoppe"/><ref>{{citation | first1 = Rudolf | last1 = Hoppe | first2 = Wolfgang | last2 = Dähne | first3 = Wilhelm | last3 = Klemm | title = Mangantetrafluorid mit einem Anhang über LiMnF<sub>5</sub> und LiMnF<sub>4</sub> | journal = Justus Liebigs Ann. Chem. | volume = 658 | issue = 1 | pages = 1–5 | year = 1962 | doi = 10.1002/jlac.19626580102}}.</ref>。尽管可以通过增加氟气压力（4.5－6 bar，180－320 °C）来阻止产物的升华并用机械搅拌的方法避免产物晶粒的烧结<ref name="AstorPrepn"/><ref name="SolvayPrepn">{{citation | assignee = Solvay Fluor | title = Method for preparing manganese tetrafluoride | country-code = WO | publication-date = 2009-06-18 | editor1-last = Seseke-Koyro | editor1-first = Ulrich | editor2-last = Garcia-Juan | editor2-first = Placido | editor3-last = Palsherm | editor3-first = Stefan | editor4-last = Schulz | editor4-first = Alf | contribution = PCT Appl. | publication-place = 2009074560}}.</ref>，但这仍是合成四氟化锰最常见的方法。该反应也可以锰粉为原料在[[流化床|流化床]]上进行<ref>{{citation | first1 = H. | last1 = Roesky | first2 = O. | last2 = Glemser | title = A New Preparation of Manganese Tetrafluoride | journal = Angew. Chem., Int. Ed. Engl. | volume = 2 | issue = 10 | page = 626 | year = 1963 | doi = 10.1002/anie.196306262}}.</ref><ref>{{citation | first1 = Herbert W. | last1 = Roesky | first2 = Oskar | last2 = Glemser | first3 = Karl-Heinz | last3 = Hellberg | title = Darstellung von Metallfluoriden in der Wirbelschicht | journal = Chem. Ber. | volume = 98 | issue = 6 | pages = 2046–48 | year = 1965 | doi = 10.1002/cber.19650980642}}.</ref>。

其他制备四氟化锰的方法包括用[[二氟化氪|二氟化氪]]氟化[[二氟化锰|二氟化锰]]<ref>{{citation | first1 = Karel | last1 = Lutar | first2 = Adolf | last2 = Jesih | first3 = Boris | last3 = Žemva | title = KrF<sub>2</sub>/MnF<sub>4</sub> adducts from KrF<sub>2</sub>/MnF<sub>2</sub> interaction in HF as a route to high purity MnF<sub>4</sub> | journal = Polyhedron | volume = 7 | issue = 13 | year = 1988 | pages = 1217–19 | doi = 10.1016/S0277-5387(00)81212-7}}.</ref>，二氟化锰与溶解在液态氟化氢中的氟气在紫外光照射下发生氟化反应<ref name="Mazej">{{citation | first = Z. | last = Mazej | title = Room temperature syntheses of MnF<sub>3</sub>, MnF<sub>4</sub> and hexafluoromanganete(IV) salts of alkali cations | journal = J. Chem. | volume = 25 | issue = 21 | pages = 3721–24 | doi = 10.1021/ic00241a001}}.</ref>：
