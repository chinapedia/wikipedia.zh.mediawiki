'''森諾曼期－土侖期灭绝事件'''（英语：Cenomanian-Turonian boundary event，簡稱OAE2）是发生于白堊紀的二個[[海洋含氧量降低|海洋含氧量降低]]物种[[灭绝事件|灭绝事件]]之一。該次事件大約發生於9150 ± 860 萬年前<ref>{{cite web|url=http://nora.nerc.ac.uk/7741/ |title=U-Pb and Re-Os geochronology of the Aptian/Albian and Cenomanian/Turonian stage boundaries : implications for timescale calibration, osmium seawater composition and Re-Os systematics in organic-rich sediments - NERC Open Research Archive |publisher=Nora.nerc.ac.uk |date= |accessdate=2010-09-13}}</ref>，不過亦有研究指出其約發生於9300-9400萬年前<ref>{{cite journal
 | author = Leckie, R|author2=Bralower, T. |author3=Cashman, R.

 | issue = 3

}}</ref>。
