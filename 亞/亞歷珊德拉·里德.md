'''亞歷珊德拉·里德·瓦利'''（{{kor|k='''알렉산드라 레이드 바리'''|rm='''Al Rek San Deu Ra Ri Deu Ba Ri'''}}，{{lang-en|'''Alexandra Reid Varley'''}}，{{bd|1990年|3月5日|}}），藝名'''Alexandra'''（{{kor|k='''알렉산드라'''|rm='''Al Rek San Deu Ra'''}}），多數寫作'''Alex'''（{{kor|k='''알렉스'''|rm='''Al Rek Seu'''}}），在[[韓國|韓國]]發展的[[美國|美國]]女[[歌手|歌手]]、[[模特兒|模特兒]]，曾為[[DR_Music|DR Music]]旗下女子組合[[RANIA|RANIA]]成員，在隊中擔任第四任隊長、主Rapper，在2015年11月5日以新成員身份隨[[RANIA|RANIA]]發行第二張迷你專輯《Demonstrate》正式出道，2017年8月19日退出。

== 簡歷 ==
===1990年至2003年：出生、早年生活===
'''Alex'''於1990年3月5日在[[美國|美國]][[堪薩斯州|堪薩斯州]][[勞倫斯_(堪薩斯州)|勞倫斯]]出生。'''Alex'''的父親是美國白人，而母親是美國黑人。在青少年時期，'''Alex'''曾就讀[[普萊諾高中|普萊諾高中]]。

===2004年至2014年：參加《Endurance 3: Hawaii》、Solo出道===
2004年，'''Alex'''參加青少年生存節目《{{link-en|Endurance 3: Hawaii|Endurance (TV series)#Season 3 - Endurance 3: Hawaii (2004–05)}}》，在第3集"Pick Your Partner"與Bjorn Leum組成綠隊的第三組可惜在第6集"Squaring Off"被淘汰。

被淘汰後，'''Alex'''與[[Def_Jam唱片公司|Def Jam]]簽唱片約<ref>{{cite web | url = http://omonatheydidnt.livejournal.com/16953215.html | title = Hay Omona! Come and get to know Alexandra Reid, RaNia's new member | author = kwikimart | date = November 4, 2015 | website = [[LiveJournal|LiveJournal]] | publisher = [[LiveJournal|LiveJournal]] | accessdate = October 22, 2016}}</ref><ref name="OogeeWoogee">{{cite web | url = http://oogeewoogee.com/alexandra-reid-talks-african-americans-in-k-pop-hip-hop-and-the-work-ethic-of-rania/ | title = ALEXANDRA REID TALKS AFRICAN-AMERICANS IN K-POP, HIP-HOP AND THE WORK ETHIC OF RANIA | last = Brutus | first = Wilkine | date = November 27, 2015 | website = [[OogeeWoogee|OogeeWoogee]] | publisher = [[OogeeWoogee|OogeeWoogee]] | accessdate = October 22, 2016 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20161022223735/http://oogeewoogee.com/alexandra-reid-talks-african-americans-in-k-pop-hip-hop-and-the-work-ethic-of-rania/ | archive-date = 2016-10-22 | dead-url = yes }}</ref>。

在正式出道前，'''Alex'''用了大概6年的時間練習。終於在2011年11月7日，'''Alex'''發行出道單曲《Body To Body》，這首單曲'''Alex'''有份參與創作<ref name="Body To Body">{{cite web | url = https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pc-QeGgffUo | title = Body To Body (Lyrics) | last = Reid | first = Alex | date = August 3, 2012 | website = [[YouTube|YouTube]] | publisher = [[YouTube|YouTube]] | accessdate = October 22, 2016}}</ref><ref name="Body To Body" /><ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7sWvvSbrCfU | title = Alex Reid- "Body To Body" (In The Studio) | last = Reid | first = Alex | date = August 3, 2012 | website = [[YouTube|YouTube]] | publisher = [[YouTube|YouTube]] | accessdate = October 22, 2016}}</ref>。

2012年8月3日，'''Alex'''發行首張迷你專輯《Letters To My Ex》中的主打歌《Soldier (ft. [[Traphik|Traphik]])》<ref name="Soldier">{{cite web | url = https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MMq_dJeI9do | title = "Soldier" Alex Reid ft. Traphik (prod. by J.R Rotem) | last = Reid | first = Alex | date = August 3, 2012 | website = [[YouTube|YouTube]] | publisher = [[YouTube|YouTube]] | accessdate = October 22, 2016}}</ref>。

2012年8月10日，'''Alex'''發行同樣收錄在首張迷你專輯《Letters To My Ex》中的《My Ex》<ref name="My Ex">{{cite web | url = https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B67QoKRuIPs | title = My Ex -Alex Reid (Lyrics VIdeo).mov | last = Reid | first = Alex | date = August 10, 2012 | website = [[YouTube|YouTube]] | publisher = [[YouTube|YouTube]] | accessdate = October 22, 2016}}</ref>。

2012年8月31日，'''Alex'''正式發行首張迷你專輯《Letter To My Ex》<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.datpiff.com/Alex-Reid-Letters-To-My-Ex-mixtape.395643.html | title = LETTERS TO MY EX | last = Reid | first = Alex | date = September 12, 2012 | website = [[DatPiff|DatPiff]] | publisher = [[DatPiff|DatPiff]] | accessdate = October 22, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.bam1987tv.com/2012/09/new-music-thealexreid-letters-to-my-ex.html | title = NEW MUSIC: @THEALEXREID - LETTERS TO MY EX EP | last = Reid | first = Alex | date = September 14, 2012 | website = [[Bam_VS_Media|Bam VS Media]] | publisher = [[Bam_VS_Media|Bam VS Media]] | accessdate = October 22, 2016}}</ref>。

2014年，'''Alex'''在SoundCloud公開《Want Me Some U》和《Good Girl》<ref>{{cite web | url = https://soundcloud.com/soalexreid/want-me-some-u | title = ALEX REID - Want Me Some U | last = Reid | first = Alex | date = 2014 | website = [[SoundCloud|SoundCloud]] | publisher = [[SoundCloud|SoundCloud]] | accessdate = October 22, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = https://soundcloud.com/soalexreid/good-girl | title = ALEX REID - Good Girl | last = Reid | first = Alex | date = 2014 | website = [[SoundCloud|SoundCloud]] | publisher = [[SoundCloud|SoundCloud]] | accessdate = October 22, 2016}}</ref>。

===2015年至2017年：BP RANIA===
{{main|RANIA}}
2015年11月4日（即[[RANIA|RANIA]]公開《Demonstrate》原版MV的日子），DR Music宣布'''Alex'''加入[[BP_RANIA|BP RANIA]]<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.xportsnews.com/jenter/?ac=article_view&entry_id=654544&_REFERER=http%253A%252F%252Fsearch.naver.com%252Fsearch.naver%253Fwhere%253Dnexearch%2526query%253D%2525EB%25259D%2525BC%2525EB%25258B%252588%2525EC%252595%252584%2526sm%253Dtop_hty%2526fbm%253D1%2526ie%253Dutf8 | title = 라니아, 美 흑인 래퍼 알렉산드라 합류 '화려한 이력' | date = November 4, 2015 | website = [[Xsports_News|Xsports News]] | publisher = [[Xsports_News|Xsports News]] | accessdate = October 22, 2016}}</ref>，這也是韓國團體中，第一次有女黑人Rapper的加入。但是，由於'''Alex'''簽證問題，未能完全參與《Demonstrate》的舞蹈部分，只能參與開首部分和結尾部分<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/k-town/6753071/rania-new-african-american-member-alexandra-demonstrate | title = K-Pop Girl Group Rania Adds African-American Member | last = Benjamin | first = Jeff | date = November 5, 2015 | website = [[Billboard|Billboard]] | publisher = [[Billboard|Billboard]] | accessdate = October 22, 2016}}</ref>。2015年11月11日，DR Music正式公開加入'''Alex'''片段的《Demonstrate》修改版MV<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kQ3xLBwWiCI | title = RANIA 라니아- Demonstrate Music Video (Modified version with Alex) |  author = DRMUSIC RAINA | date = November 11, 2016 | website = [[YouTube|YouTube]] | publisher = [[YouTube|YouTube]] | accessdate = October 22, 2016}}</ref>。

2016年初，DR Music宣布'''Alex'''、慧美將以[[RANIA|RANIA]]的首個子團在6月活動<ref name="Makestar">{{cite web | url = https://www.makestar.co/projects/singer_rania | title = Rania’s mini-album making | author = [[Makestar|Makestar]] | date = November 5, 2015 | website = [[Makestar|Makestar]] | publisher = [[Makestar|Makestar]] | accessdate = October 22, 2016}}</ref>。可惜，此計劃隨著Di、T-ae、Xia退團和新成員加入問題而擱置<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.soompi.com/2016/05/26/ranias-xia-di-and-t-ae-leave-and-join-new-group/ | title = RANIA’s Xia, Di, And T-ae Leave And Join New Group | last = Do | first = K. | date = May 26, 2016 | website = [[Soompi|Soompi]] | publisher = [[Soompi|Soompi]] | accessdate = October 22, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.inquisitr.com/3160565/di-t-ae-and-xia-no-longer-with-rania-k-pop-idols-departs-dr-music-to-join-new-girl-group-for-enter-hama/ | title = Di, T-ae, And Xia No Longer With RaNia: K-Pop Idols Departs DR Music To Join New Girl Group For ENTER HAMA | last = Omega | first = Jan | date = June 2, 2016 | website = [[Inquisitr|Inquisitr]] | publisher = [[Inquisitr|Inquisitr]] | accessdate = October 22, 2016}}</ref>。 8月15日，Alex在個人[[Twitter|Twitter]]上宣布她擔任了RANIA的新隊長，這也是韓國團體中，第一次由女黑人擔任隊長<ref>[http://twitter.com/thealexreid/status/764879171663241217 Alex在8月15日發布的Twitter貼文]</ref>。

2017年8月19日，DR Music在Facebook上宣布Alexandra不再是RANIA的一員<ref>[https://www.facebook.com/drmusicrania/posts/796124980548724 DR Music於2017年8月19日在官方Facebook專頁發布的貼文]</ref>。
