'''亞發基唐諾''' ([[英語|英語]]：'''Arphugitonos''') 是於《{{tsl|en|Greek Apocalypse of Ezra|希臘語以斯拉啟示錄}}》中被提及的一位[[天使|天使]]，在此書中祂作為將在末世之日統治世界的九位天使之一被啟示給[[以斯拉|以斯拉]]。<ref>{{cite book |last1=Walker |first1=Alexander |editor1-first=Alexander |editor1-last=Roberts |others=James Donaldson and A. Cleveland Coxe |title=Ante-Nicene Fathers: The Writings of the Fathers Down to A.D. 325 |url=http://www.newadvent.org/fathers/0829.htm |accessdate=October 20, 2011 |volume=8 |year=1886 |publisher=Christian Literature Publishing Co. |location=Buffalo, NY |language= |quote=Do you know, Esdras, the names of the angels at the end of the world? Michael, Gabriel, Uriel, Raphael, Gabuthelon, Aker, Arphugitonos, Beburos, Zebuleon.}}</ref>這九位被提及的天使是：[[米迦勒|米迦勒]]、[[加百列|加百列]]、[[烏列爾|烏列爾]]、[[拉斐爾_(天使)|拉斐爾]]、[[加布澤隆|加布澤隆]]、[[阿克爾_(天使)|阿克爾]]、'''亞發基唐諾'''、[[俾布羅斯|俾布羅斯]]和[[澤布倫|澤布倫]]。亞發基唐諾並不被認為是一位[[天使長|天使長]]，且祂只被記載於[[偽典|非正典]]經書裡。<ref>''The Old Testament Pseudepigrapha: Apocalyptic Literature & Testaments''.
