'''亞洲田徑總會'''是[[亞洲|亞洲]]的洲際[[田徑|田徑]]體育運動管理機構。總部位於[[新加坡|新加坡]]。它主辦[[亞洲田徑錦標賽|亞洲田徑錦標賽]]和其他洲際比賽。現任主席為卡達籍的[[Dahlan_Jumaan_al-Hamad|Dahlan Jumaan al-Hamad]]，他在2013年當選。<ref>[http://www.gulf-times.com/sport/192/details/358067/dahlan-ousts-kalmadi-as-asian-athletics-chief Dahlan ousts Kalmadi as Asian athletics chief]. ''Gulf Times'' (2013-07-01). Retrieved on 2013-07-07.</ref>印度籍的[[Suresh_Kalmadi|Suresh Kalmadi]]在2000年至2013年間擔任主席。<ref>[http://www.iaaf.org/mm/Document/News/GeneralNews/05/50/99/20091211124641_httppostedfile_NEWS108_17554.pdf IAAF Newsletter Edition 108] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121023234954/http://www.iaaf.org/mm/Document/News/GeneralNews/05/50/99/20091211124641_httppostedfile_NEWS108_17554.pdf |date=2012-10-23 }}. [[IAAF|IAAF]] (2009-12-11). Retrieved on 2009-12-12.</ref>
