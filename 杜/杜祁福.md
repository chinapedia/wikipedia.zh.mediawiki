'''杜祁福'''（{{lang-en|'''Creflo Augustus Dollar, Jr.'''}}，'''小祁福·奧古斯都·杜拉爾'''；{{bd|1962年|1月28日}}）是一位[[美國|美國]]的[[電視佈道家|電視佈道家]]、[[牧師|牧師]]以及一家以美國[[喬治亞州|喬治亞州]][[富爾頓縣_(喬治亞州)|富爾頓縣]]為基地的非宗派[[世界改變者教會|世界改變者教會]]<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.worldchangers.org/Contact-Us.aspx?p=1 |title=Contact Us |publisher=[[世界改變者教會|World Changers Church International]] |accessdate=2009-05-25 |language=en |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090206052357/http://worldchangers.org/Contact-Us.aspx?p=1 |archivedate=2009-02-06 }}</ref><ref name="christianpost">{{cite news |url=http://m.chinese.christianpost.com/article/%E6%9D%9C%E7%A5%81%E7%A6%8F%E7%9A%84%E5%A5%A2%E8%8F%AF%E9%A3%9B%E6%A9%9F-19463/ |title=杜祁福的奢華飛機 |author=Rod Anderson |publisher=[[基督郵報|基督郵報]]中文版 |accessdate=2015-04-18 |language=zh |date=2015-03-19 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150419122123/http://m.chinese.christianpost.com/article/%E6%9D%9C%E7%A5%81%E7%A6%8F%E7%9A%84%E5%A5%A2%E8%8F%AF%E9%A3%9B%E6%A9%9F-19463/ |archivedate=2015年4月19日 |df= }}</ref>、[[杜祁福同工聯會|杜祁福同工聯會]]（Creflo Dollar Ministerial Association；舊稱[[国际公约同工|国际公约同工]]，International Covenant Ministries）、[[杜祁福同工會|杜祁福同工會]]（Dollar Ministries）與[[Arrow唱片公司|Arrow唱片公司]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.creflodollarministries.org/About/Ministries.aspx |title=Creflo Dollar Ministries |accessdate=2010-06-27 |language=en |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100612230414/https://creflodollarministries.org/About/Ministries.aspx |archivedate=2010-06-12 }}</ref>的創辦人。他是一位公認的基督教界的领袖<ref>{{cite journal |url=https://www.churchchina.org/files/201309/cc1309.doc |date=2013-09 |title=健康教会机构事工九标志 |author=文/J. Mack Stiles Print  译/杖恩  校/煦 |journal=《教会》雙月刊 |volume=第43期 |language=zh-hans |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150419210450/https://www.churchchina.org/files/201309/cc1309.doc |archivedate=2015-04-19 }}</ref>。

有消息來源報告說，杜祁福的真實姓名是'''邁克爾·史密斯'''<ref name="NY"/>，但杜祁福已否認，指那只不過是「[[都市傳說|都市傳說]]」<ref name=mysearch>{{cite news|last1=Raboteau|first1=Emily| authorlink1=Emily Raboteau|title=My search for Creflo Dollar|url=http://www.salon.com/2013/01/06/my_search_for_creflo_dollar/|accessdate=2015-01-18|work=Salon|date=2013-01-06 |language=en }}</ref>。

==生平==
===出生及成長===
杜祁福於1962年1月28日出生，是老祁福·奧古斯都·杜拉爾(1936-1993)及太太的兒子。杜祁福出生後入讀當地學校，在浸信會成長。

1984年，杜祁福從[[卡羅爾頓|卡羅爾頓]]的[[西喬治亞大學|西喬治亞大學]]畢業，取得教育[[理學士|理學士]]學位<ref>"2/10 Creflo Dollar – Winning the War in your Mind ." Download Christian. N.p., n.d. Web. June 23, 2010. {{cite web |url=http://www.download-christian.com/?p=147 |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2015-04-19 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150117215617/http://www.download-christian.com/?p=147 |archivedate=2015-01-17 }} </ref>。

杜祁福和他的妻子杜塔菲養有五個孩子，住在[[亞特蘭大|亞特蘭大]]。1998年，他被[[奧勒.羅拔士大學|奧勒.羅拔士大學]]授予了神學[[榮譽博士學位|榮譽博士學位]] <ref name=about>{{cite web|url=http://www.creflodollarministries.org/About/CrefloDollar.aspx |title=About Creflo Dollar |accessdate=2015-01-17 |language=en |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150122031323/http://creflodollarministries.org/About/CrefloDollar.aspx |archivedate=2015-01-22 }}</ref>。

===建立教會===
他在他的官方網站指出，他在1986年看見[[異象|異象]]，指神要他開始發展「世界改變者同工基督徒中心」。教會的第一次崇拜聚會在州首府近郊的[[:en:College_Park,_Georgia|College Park]]一家小學的咖啡廳舉行，當時只有八人出席。 

杜祁福後來把教會名稱改為現時的[[世界改變者教會|世界改變者教會]]（World Changers Church International，WCCI），而教會亦從學校的咖啡廳搬遷到一個專用的小教堂。小教堂每個星期日舉辦四場崇拜，後來更加入每週一次的電台廣播。1995年12月24日，WCCI遷入現址，提供8500個座位的設施，被稱為「世界巨蛋」。教會曾表示，世界巨蛋的建築成本近18000000美元未經任何銀行融資建設。 <ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.worldchangerschurchnewyork.org/bio_t.aspx |title="Dr.Creflo A. Dollar Biography." |publisher=World Changers Church |location=New York |date=2010-06-12 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20101204061941/http://worldchangerschurchnewyork.org/bio_t.aspx |archivedate=2010-12-04 }}</ref>。截至2007年，報告指教會在2006年度各地有30,000名成員，共69000000美元收入（總現金集合）<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.ajc.com/metro/content/metro/stories/2007/11/11/preachers11111.html |title=Figures released by megachurch |publisher=''[[The_Atlanta_Journal-Constitution|The Atlanta Journal-Constitution]]'' |date=2007-11-11 |first= |last= |accessdate=2007-11-17 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071114115406/http://www.ajc.com/metro/content/metro/stories/2007/11/11/preachers11111.html |archivedate=2007-11-14 |deadurl=yes }}</ref> 。 

在2012年10月，杜祁福的佈道所租用[[洛伊斯天堂影院|洛伊斯天堂影院]]在[[紐約|紐約]][[布朗克斯區|布朗克斯區]]的舊址，作為新教堂的位置。 該建築是紐約市的地標，在1920年代作為電影院及表演舞台而建造的<ref>{{cite news |author= DANIEL BEEKMAN|title= Iconic Paradise Theater on Grand Concourse set to begin next act as megachurch of Creflo Dollar|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/bronx/world-changers-church-new-york-occupy-paradise-article-1.1211185|publisher=The New York Times|date=December 2, 2012 |accessdate=July 7, 2013}}</ref>。

2013年3月15日，杜祁福Creflo美元踏上了巡演宣教活動<ref>{{cite news |author= Elev8|title= Dr. Creflo Dollar Announces 2013 "Change Conventions"|url=http://elev8.com/707631/dr-creflo-dollar-announces-2013-change-conventions/|publisher=Elev8|date=February 6, 2013|accessdate=July 22, 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|author=Travel and Tour World|title=Creflo Dollar’s ‘Change Experience’ to Impact Thousands in Orlando Oct. 11|url=http://www.travelandtourworld.com/news/article/creflo-dollars-change-experience-to-impact-thousands-in-orlando-oct-11/|publisher=Travel and Tour World|date=October 9, 2013|accessdate=November 11, 2013|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131203003232/http://www.travelandtourworld.com/news/article/creflo-dollars-change-experience-to-impact-thousands-in-orlando-oct-11/|archivedate=2013年12月3日|df=}}</ref>。

杜祁福亦會為會議演講，他也是一位作家，撰寫關於他的「[[成功福音|成功福音]]」<ref name="NY2">{{cite news |author=Michael Luo|title= Preaching a Gospel of Wealth in a Glittery Market, New York|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2006/01/15/nyregion/15prosperity.html |publisher=The New York Times |date=January 15, 2006 |accessdate=June 27, 2010}}</ref><ref name="NY">{{cite web|url=http://nypress.com/the-best-thing-about-evangelist-creflo-dollar-is-his-audience/|last=Lavallee|first=Andrew|publisher=New York Press|title=The Best Thing About Evangelist Creflo Dollar Is His Audience|date=August 14, 2001|accessdate=December 10, 2014}}</ref>。

==財務及其他爭議事件==
雖然杜祁福是一位成功的教會人物，但他自己的言行不時引起爭議<ref name="post852" />，而其教會亦曾發生爭議事件。宗教学者[[莱哲·阿斯兰|莱哲·阿斯兰]]（Reza Aslan）曾在公開演說裡谴责他的「成功福音」这种福音的解释，認為他就好像他的新書裡提及在《[[新約聖經|新約聖經]]》裡的狂熱份子[[奮銳黨|奮銳黨]]<ref name="christianpost" />。以下列出幾項幾年比較知名的爭議事件。

Dollar is known for his controversial teachings regarding [[prosperity_theology|prosperity theology]], or the gospel of wealth.<ref name="NY2"/> He has long been criticized for living a lavish lifestyle; he owns two [[Rolls-Royce|Rolls-Royce]]s, a private jet, and real estate such as a million-dollar home in Atlanta, a $2.5 million home in [[Demarest,_New_Jersey|Demarest, New Jersey]], and a $2.5 million home in Manhattan,<ref name="NY2">{{cite news |author=Michael Luo|title= When asked to explain his lifestyle, he has been quoted as responding "that's just how the Dollar rolls, baby." Preaching a Gospel of Wealth in a Glittery Market, New York|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2006/01/15/nyregion/15prosperity.html?_r=1&pagewanted=all|publisher=The New York Times |date=January 15, 2006 |accessdate=September 7, 2010}}</ref> which he sold for $3.75 million in 2012.<ref>{{cite news | url=http://observer.com/2012/04/televangelist-creflo-dollar-sells-manhattan-condo/ | date=April 25, 2012 | title=Televangelist Creflo Dollar Sells Manhattan Condo (Pictures)|author=Kim Velsey|newspaper=The New York Observer}}</ref> Dollar has refused to disclose his salary. Creflo Dollar Ministries received a grade of "F" for financial transparency by the organization [[Ministry_Watch|Ministry Watch]].<ref name="NY2"/><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.ministrywatch.com/profile/creflo-dollar-ministries.aspx |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2009-11-11 |0=2009-09-30 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090930073114/http://www.ministrywatch.com/profile/creflo-dollar-ministries.aspx |archivedate=2009-09-30 }}</ref>

On November 6, 2007, United States Senator [[Chuck_Grassley|Chuck Grassley]] of [[Iowa|Iowa]], as Chairman of the [[United_States_Senate_Committee_on_Finance|Senate Finance Committee]], began an [[United_States_Senate_inquiry_into_the_tax-exempt_status_of_religious_organizations|investigation of several ministries]], including Dollar's. Grassley asked for financial information to determine whether Dollar made any personal profit from financial donations and requested that Dollar's ministry make the information available by December 6, 2007. The investigation also asked for information from five other televangelists: [[Benny_Hinn|Benny Hinn]], [[Kenneth_Copeland|Kenneth Copeland]], [[Eddie_L._Long|Eddie L. Long]], [[Joyce_Meyer|Joyce Meyer]], and [[Paula_White|Paula White]].<ref>{{cite news| url=http://www.usatoday.com/news/washington/2007-11-07-televangelist-probe_N.htm | work=USA Today | title=Sen. Grassley probes televangelists' finances | date=November 7, 2007 | accessdate=April 30, 2010}}</ref> Dollar contested the probe, arguing that the proper governmental entity to examine religious groups is the IRS, not the Committee on Finance.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.creflodollarministries.org/About/documents/LegalResponse03032008.pdf |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2015-02-03 |0=2015-09-23 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150923211054/http://www.creflodollarministries.org/About/documents/LegalResponse03032008.pdf |archivedate=2015-09-23 }}</ref>

===渉嫌虐兒事件===
On June 8, 2012, the Fayette County Sheriff's Office filed a report claiming Dollar committed acts of [[simple_assault|simple assault/battery]] and cruelty toward a child.  The report stated that his then 15-year-old daughter reported she was choked and hit by her father, Dollar. Dollar informed deputies that he was only trying to restrain his daughter, who, he claimed, was acting disrespectfully after he forbade her from attending a party. He stated that while he was holding his daughter, she began to hit him. He stated he wrestled her to the floor, spanked her and sent her to her room, according to the report.<ref>http://www.11alive.com/assetpool/documents/120608125907_creflo_arrest.pdf{{Dead link|date=2018年6月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=no }} Fayette County Sheriff's Office Deputy Report</ref>  Dollar released a statement after the charges were filed, denying that he assaulted his daughter.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.washingtontimes.com/news/2012/jun/10/megachurch-pastor-creflo-dollar-denies-punching-ch/|title=Megachurch pastor Creflo Dollar denies punching, choking daughter|work=The Washingtion Times|accessdate=March 13, 2015}}</ref> In January 2013, prosecutors dropped charges after Dollar completed an anger-management program. Fayette County Solicitor General James Inagawa said Dollar paid about $1,000 in court fees as part of a pretrial intervention program and had complied with all the conditions placed on him to resolve the case. Inagawa noted that the wishes of Dollar's daughter were taken into account before prosecutors allowed the pastor to participate in the pretrial intervention program.<ref>http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2013/01/25/creflo-dollar-abuse-charge-dropped_n_2552369.html Reuters: Creflo Dollar Abuse Charge Dropped After Pastor Seeks Anger Management</ref>
