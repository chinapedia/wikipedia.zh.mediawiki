{{Vgname|行屍：第二季|en=The Walking Dead: Season Two}}是電子遊戲《[[行屍_(遊戲)|行屍]]》第一季的續集，以Robert Kirkman原創漫畫《[[行屍_(漫畫)|行屍]]》為本的冒險遊戲。遊戲由Telltale Games開發。第二季第一集數碼版在2013年12月起發售，零售DVD版則會在整季完結後發售。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.escapistmagazine.com/news/view/118317-TellTale-Games-Planning-The-Walking-Dead-Season-Two |title=Telltale Games Planning The Walking Dead Season Two |publisher=[[The_Escapist_(magazine)|The Escapist]] |accessdate=2012-07-16 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.joystiq.com/2012/07/07/the-walking-dead-season-2-insinuated-by-telltale/ |title=The Walking Dead Season Two Insinuated by Telltale |publisher=Joystiq |accessdate=2011-03-08 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.gamasutra.com/view/news/173786/More_The_Walking_Dead_games_on_the_way_from_Telltale_Activision.php |title=More The Walking Dead Games in the Way From Telltale, Activision |publisher=[[Gamasutra|Gamasutra]] |accessdate=2011-03-08 }}</ref>第二季繼續由第一季相同演員旁白聲演。玩家在第一季、額外集數《行屍400天》（The Walking Dead 400 Days）和第二季內的決定都會被記錄，永久影響接下來的劇情。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://multiplayerblog.mtv.com/2013/01/07/save-game-imports-for-the-walking-dead-season-2/ |publisher=[[MTV|MTV.com]] |title=Telltale Looking Into Save Game Imports for The Walking Dead Season 2 |date=2013-01-07 |accessdate=2013-01-08}}</ref>本來第一季出現的克萊門汀（Clementine）會在第二季成為遊戲主角<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.ign.com/articles/2013/07/20/comic-con-clementine-will-be-in-walking-dead-game-season-2 | title = Comic-Con: Clementine Will Be in Walking Dead Game Season 2 | first = Andrew | last = Goldfarb | date = 2013-07-20 | accessdate = 2013-07-20 | publisher = IGN }}</ref>
