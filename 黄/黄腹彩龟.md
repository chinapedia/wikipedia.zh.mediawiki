'''黄腹彩龟'''（[[学名|学名]]：''{{lang|la|Trachemys scripta scripta}}''）是一种水陆两栖龟类，属[[泽龟科|泽龟科]][[彩龟属|彩龟属]]，也是[[彩龟|彩龟]]的[[指名亚种|指名亚种]]，分布于[[美国|美国]]东南部，尤其集中于[[佛罗里达州|佛罗里达州]]及[[弗吉尼亚州|弗吉尼亚州]]东南部<ref>Conant, R., J. Collins. 1991. ''Peterson Field Guides: Reptiles and Amphibians of Eastern/Central North America.'' Boston: Houghton Mifflin Co.</ref>，是当地最常见的水龟<ref>{{cite journal | title=Genetic Divergence among Populations of the Yellow-Bellied Slider Turtle (Pseudemys scripta) Separated by Aquatic and Terrestrial Habitats | first=K. T. | last=Scriber | coauthors=et al. | journal=Copeia | volume=1986 | issue=3 | date=August 4, 1986 | pages=691–700 | doi=10.2307/1444951 | publisher=American Society of Ichthyologists and Herpetologists | jstor=1444951}}</ref>。在水流缓慢的河流、[[泛滥平原|泛滥平原]][[沼泽|沼泽]]、[[池塘|池塘]]及季节性湿地都有其踪迹。

[[红耳彩龟|红耳彩龟]]及[[坎伯兰彩龟|坎伯兰彩龟]]是其同种近亲。
