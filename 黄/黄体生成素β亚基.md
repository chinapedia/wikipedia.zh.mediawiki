== 基因 ==

该基因位于19号染色体q13.3，编码[[黄体生成素|黄体生成素]]的β亚基<ref name="entrez" />。而在[[马科|马科]]动物中，黄体生成素的β亚基和绒毛膜促性腺激素的β亚基是由一个基因编码的<ref name="pmid1379674">{{cite journal | author = Sherman GB, Wolfe MW, Farmerie TA, Clay CM, Threadgill DS, Sharp DC, Nilson JH | title = A single gene encodes the beta-subunits of equine luteinizing hormone and chorionic gonadotropin | journal = Mol. Endocrinol. | volume = 6 | issue = 6 | pages = 951–9 |date=June 1992 | pmid = 1379674 | doi = 10.1210/me.6.6.951 }}</ref>。
