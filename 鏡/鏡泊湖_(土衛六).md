[[土衛六|土衛六]]的'''鏡泊湖'''（'''Jingpo Lacus'''）是位於土衛六北極區的一個[[土衛六湖泊|湖泊]]，面積與土衛六的[[安大略湖_(土衛六)|安大略湖]]相近<ref name = "USGS">{{cite web
 | title = Titan lacūs
 | work = [http://planetarynames.wr.usgs.gov/ Gazetteer of Planetary Nomenclature]
 | publisher = [[太空地质学研究计划|USGS Astrogeology Science Center]]
 | date = | url = http://planetarynames.wr.usgs.gov/SearchResults?target=TITAN&featureType=Lacus,%20lac%C5%ABs
 | format = | doi = | accessdate = 2012-03-17}}</ref>，是土衛六表面最大的幾個湖泊，面積排名在[[克拉肯海|克拉肯海]]、[[丽姬亚海|丽姬亚海]]和[[蓬加海|蓬加海]]之後<ref name = "USGS_maria">{{cite web
 | title = Titan maria
 | work = [http://planetarynames.wr.usgs.gov/ Gazetteer of Planetary Nomenclature]
 | publisher = [[太空地质学研究计划|USGS Astrogeology Science Center]]
 | date = | url = http://planetarynames.wr.usgs.gov/SearchResults?target=TITAN&featureType=Mare%2C%20maria
 | format = | doi = | accessdate = 2012-03-16}}</ref>。該湖泊內的流體是液態[[碳氫化合物|碳氫化合物]]（主要是[[甲烷|甲烷]]和[[乙烷|乙烷]]）。它的位置在克拉肯海以西，中心座標73° N, 336° W，長度約240公里<ref name = "USGS"/>，相當於地球上的[[奥涅加湖|奥涅加湖]]。它的名稱來自中國[[黑龙江省|黑龙江省]]的[[鏡泊湖|鏡泊湖]]。

==近年的探測==
2009年7月8日，[[卡西尼－惠更斯号|卡西尼－惠更斯号]]的可見光與紅外線測繪光譜儀（Visual and Infrared Mapping Spectrometer，VIMS）在鏡泊湖上方座標71° N, 337° W處以5微米的紅外線波段觀測到了{{link-en|镜面反射 (物理)|Specular reflection|鏡面反射}}現象（有時會被不太準確地描述發生在克拉肯海南方湖岸線上空<ref name = "press">{{Cite web
  | last = Cook
  | first = J.-R. C.
  | authorlink =
  | title = Glint of Sunlight Confirms Liquid in Northern Lake District of Titan
  | work = [http://www.nasa.gov/mission_pages/cassini/main/index.html NASA web site Cassini mission page]
  | publisher = [[NASA|NASA]]
  | date = 2009-12-17
  | url = http://www.nasa.gov/mission_pages/cassini/whycassini/cassini20091217.html
  | accessdate = 2009-12-18}}</ref>）。這個現象暗示了該區域是一個像鏡面一樣平坦的區域，因此該次觀測證實了從雷達成像中推斷該區域是大面積液態區域的結論。該次觀測在土衛六北極區進入15年的黑暗冬季之後不久進行的<ref name="VIMS_specular">{{cite web | last = Lakdawalla | first = E. | title = Cassini VIMS sees the long-awaited glint off a Titan lake | work = [http://planetary.org/blog/ The Planetary Society Blog] | publisher = [[行星學會|Planetary Society]] | date = 17 December 2009 | url = http://planetary.org/blog/article/00002267/ | accessdate = 2009-12-17}}</ref>。
