'''桑葚胚'''（[[拉丁文|拉丁文]]中的''morus''指的是[[桑葚|桑葚]]的意思）是屬於[[胚胎|胚胎]]的早期[[胚胎發育|發育]]，包含[[囊胚腔|囊胚腔]]外圍的[[細胞|細胞]]，包覆細胞外層的環帶稱為[[透明帶|透明帶]]<ref>{{Cite book|author=Boklage, Charles E.|title=How New Humans Are Made: Cells and Embryos, Twins and Chimeras, Left and Right, Mind/Self/Soul, Sex, and Schizophrenia|publisher=World Scientific|year=2009|isbn=9789812835130|page=217|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=j7GfgRIgP9YC&pg=PT233}}</ref>。

桑葚胚是合子[[卵裂|胚胎分裂]]一直到32細胞時期，整個形狀像一顆[[桑葚|桑葚]]，所以被稱為桑葚胚<ref>{{cite book|editors=Sherman, Lawrence S. et al.|title=Human embryology|edition=3rd|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences|year=2001|isbn=9780443065835|page=20|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=H5qw-jiMc44C&pg=PA20}}</ref>。 在受精數天之後，位於桑葚胚表面的細胞開始緊密接觸，讓細胞與細胞之間進行[[胞橋小體|胞橋小體連結]]與[[間隙連接|間隙連接]]，這個變化是非常細微的，並不易查覺，這個過程稱為[[緻密化|緻密化]]。<ref>{{cite book|authors=Chard, Tim & Lilford, Richard|title=Basic sciences for obstetrics and gynaecology|publisher=Springer|year=1995|isbn=9783540199038|page=18|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=MsPsw4ejMxwC&pg=PA18}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|authors=Mercader, Amparo et al.|chapter=Human embryo culture|editors=Lanza, Robert & Klimanskaya, Irina|title=Essential stem cell methods|publisher=Academic Press|year=2008|isbn=9780123747419|page=343|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=aPcvphPV6AgC&pg=PA343}}</ref>。在此之後桑葚胚的細胞释放黏性[[液體|液體]]，造成桑葚胚中央形成孔洞，成為囊胚<ref>{{Cite book|author=Patestas, Maria Antoniou & Gartner, Leslie P.|title=A textbook of neuroanatomy|publisher=Wiley-Blackwell|year=2006|isbn=9781405103404|page=11|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=JEEnkNuuYkoC&pg=PA11}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|authors=Geisert, R.D. & Malayer, J.R.|chapter=Implantation: Blastocyst formation|editors=Hafez, B. & Hafez, Elsayed S.E.|title=Reproduction in farm animals|publisher=Wiley-Blackwell|year=2000|isbn=9780683305777|page=118|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=baz2TC1y2esC&pg=PA118}}</ref>。囊胚的外層細胞會形成最初的胚胎[[上皮細胞|上皮細胞]]（[[滋養外胚層|滋養外胚層]]）。然而有些細胞會存留在內層，形成內細胞群或是[[多潛能幹細胞|多潛能幹細胞]]。其中內細胞群細胞會形成[[胚體|胚體]]，而滋養外胚層則形成[[胎盤|胎盤]]以及其他胚胎組織<ref>{{cite book|authors=Morali, Olivier G. et al.|chapter=Epithelium-Mesenchyme Transitions are Crucial Morphogenetic Events Occurring During Early Development|editor=Savagner, Pierre|title=Rise and fall of epithelial phenotype: concepts of epithelial-mesenchymal transition|publisher=Springer|year=2005|isbn=9780306482397|page=16|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=3a54uGVK03cC&pg=PA16}}</ref><ref>{{cite manual|publisher=CSHL Press|year=2003|isbn=9780879695910|pages=60–61|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=4juoa5xMs8oC&pg=PA60}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|authors=Connell, R.J. & Cutner, A.|chapter=Basic
