'''通變系統'''（'''Diasystem'''）是一個[[語言學|語言學]]的名詞，指同一種語言因為地理、政治、文字或其他非語言學的原因，而被認為是兩種不同的語言系統。它不同於[[方言|方言]]：方言可能是因為長久的分隔，而使語言間產生了語音上的變化，繼而發展成為方言。但對於通變系統內的語言，卻沒有這種語音上的變化；不過亦有可能因為政治或地理環境的變化，而使用詞有所不同。

is a term used in structural [[dialect|dialect]]ology, to refer to a single genetic [[language|language]] which has two or more standard forms. The dialects are often divided into separate languages due to political reasons, most obviously with [[Serbo-Croatian|Serbo-Croatian]], which is split into [[Bosnian_language|Bosnian]], [[Croatian_language|Croatian]] and [[Serbian_language|Serbian]] due to their use by different Slavic groups in the [[Balkan|Balkan]]s and their status in the nations of [[Bosnia_and_Herzegovina|Bosnia and Herzegovina]], [[Croatia|Croatia]] and [[Serbia|Serbia]] respectively. Other possible differences between languages include vocabulary, such as [[Occitan_language|Occitan]] being affected by [[French_language|French]] and [[Catalan_language|Catalan]] by [[Spanish_language|Spanish]] words, and writing systems, such as [[Hindi_language|Hindi]] in [[Devanagari|Devanagari]] and [[Urdu|Urdu]] in the [[Arabic_script|Arabic script]], despite being [[mutually_intelligible|mutually intelligible]] to some degree.-->
