'''路什維爾站'''（{{lang-en|Lutherville station}}）為[[巴爾的摩輕軌|巴爾的摩輕軌]]的車站之一，其站址位於里奇利路西端路底，停車場有286個車位，供通勤旅客使用。可轉乘馬里蘭交通管理局8、9號線。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://mtamaryland.com/services/lightrail/parking/ |title=Light Rail Parking |publisher=Maryland Transit Administration |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090418042456/http://www.mtamaryland.com/services/lightrail/parking |archivedate=2009-04-18 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>輕軌於1992年開通以前，本站的停車場稱為路什維爾轉乘停車場。

此段路線以前是[[北方中央鐵路|北方中央鐵路]]的鐵路路線，該鐵路在路什維爾也有設站。MTA的11路公車由此發車開往巴爾的摩市，不過在輕軌通車後一個月該路公車便改以陶森為營運中心，而8路公車則延駛至路什維爾。

==2009年7月的事故==
2009年7月5日，查票員在路什維爾輕軌站附近的軌道上發現兩具青少年遺體。<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.abc2news.com/mostpopular/story/2-People-Struck-By-Light-Rail-Train/Ou2QIZXPQEGCWV_-zMya1w.cspx |title=2 People Struck by Light Rail Train |date=July 2009 |publisher=[[WMAR-TV|WMAR-TV]]}}{{dead link |date=October 2013}}</ref> 馬里蘭運輸局認為他們的死因是意外。<ref>{{cite news |last=Dresser |first=Michael |url=http://articles.baltimoresun.com/2009-07-08/news/0907070074_1_northbound-train-tracks-mta |title=Teens' Deaths On Light Rail Called Accident By Mta |work=[[Baltimore_Sun|Baltimore Sun]] |date=2009-07-08 |accessdate=2012-02-22}}</ref>並沒有輕軌列車駕駛員通報該意外，但監視錄影器拍下了列車撞擊的影像。<ref>{{cite news |first=Mary |last=Bubala |url=http://wjz.com/local/Lutherville.light.rail.2.1075177.html |title=MTA Confirm Timeline Of Light Rail Accident |publisher=[[WJZ-TV|WJZ-TV]] |date=2009-07-09 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090918183009/http://wjz.com/local/Lutherville.light.rail.2.1075177.html |archivedate=2009-09-18 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>據信兩人當時逆向沿軌道行走，認為自己可以看到駛來的列車，卻不知道當時雙向列車暫時都由同一條軌道行駛。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.baltimoresun.com/news/maryland/baltimore-county/bal-rail-deaths0707,0,4210758.story |title=Losses in Lutherville - Our View: A Light Rail Accident in Lutherville Takes Two Teenagers' Lives, Raising Questions about Unusual Circumstances and Employee (In)Actions |newspaper=[[The_Baltimore_Sun|The Baltimore Sun]] |date=2009-07-09 |accessdate=2012-02-22 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20120731032328/http://www.baltimoresun.com/news/maryland/baltimore-county/bal-rail-deaths0707,0,4210758.story |archivedate=2012-07-31 }}</ref>該班車駕駛員表示並不知道撞到人，於是接下來便由司法調查司機當時是否使用行動電話或傳簡訊。<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.wbaltv.com/news/19996498/detail.html |title=Light Rail Death Investigation Centers On Driver |publisher=[[WBAL-TV|WBAL-TV]] |date=2009-07-08 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120218133328/http://www.wbaltv.com/news/19996498/detail.html |archivedate=2012-02-18 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>死者的家屬事後在車站舉行了追悼會。

基於該事件，2009年12月馬里蘭交通管理局宣布將開除兩名駕駛員並懲處六名職員。<ref name="baltimoresun1">{{cite news |first=Nick |last=Madigan |url=http://www.baltimoresun.com/features/commuting/bal-lightrail17,0,123319.story |title=MTA to Fire Two in Deadly Accident - Train Operators to Lose Jobs, Others Face Discipline Teens on Light Rail Track Hit in July |newspaper=[[The_Baltimore_Sun|The Baltimore Sun]] |date=2009-12-19 |accessdate=2012-02-22}}{{dead link|date=October 2013}}</ref><ref name="autogenerated1">{{cite web |url=http://wjz.com/local/Lutherville.light.rail.2.1378789.html |title=Lutherville light rail |publisher=[[WJZ-TV|WJZ-TV]]}}{{dead link|date=February 2012}}</ref>

駕駛員並未遭到起訴。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://wbal.com/apps/news/templates/story.aspx?articleid=33043&zoneid=3 |title=Teens' Families 'Disappointed' No Charges Filed In Light Rail Deaths |publisher=[[WBAL_(AM)|WBAL (AM)]] |date=2009-08-08 |accessdate=2012-02-22}}{{dead link|date=October 2013}}</ref>死者的父母則獲得賠償。<ref name="autogenerated1"/>

由於穿越軌道導致撞擊，馬里蘭交通管理局建立了新的規定。包括加強現有關於穿越軌道的法規，如果發現有人在軌道上或接近軌道要立刻通知警察，以及加強關於穿越軌道者尚未離開之前的行車速度的規定。<ref name="baltimoresun1"/><ref name="autogenerated1"/>
