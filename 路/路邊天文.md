== 歷史 ==
路邊天文的歷史，可以回顧到19世紀甚至更早<ref>[http://books.google.com/books?id=L29AAAAAIAAJ&pg=PA262&dq=telescope+on+a+street&lr=%20 The Story of the Heavens - By Robert Stawell Ball – Herschel, after 1774]</ref><ref>[http://books.google.com/books?id=b3ZEAAAAIAAJ&q=%22sidewalk+astronomer%22&dq=%22sidewalk+astronomer%22&lr=%20&pgis=1 The Historical Society of Southern California quarterly by the Historical Society of Southern California, Pioneers of Los Angeles County v. 38 – 1956 “The "sidewalk astronomer" was conspicuous in Los Angeles. One such gentleman in the late 1870’s and early ‘80’s was Mr Grosser by name, who, for a small fee would allow the passerby to gaze not only through his sizeable telescope but also through the illumined microscope he carried”]</ref><ref>[http://books.google.com/books?id=tY4VAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA35&dq=%22telescope+on+a+sidewalk%22 Theatrical management in the West and South for thirty years By Solomon Smith, Sinclair Hamilton Collection of American Illustrated Books  before 1868 – mention for pay]</ref><ref>[http://books.google.com/books?id=m80GAAAAYAAJ&pg=RA4-PA15&dq=six+sidewalk+astronomers&lr=%20 Journal of the Franklin Institute – 1880 – page 418 - Sidewalk Astronomers in Paris]</ref>。根據《劍橋插圖天文史》，18世紀的一些[[歐洲|歐洲]][[天文學家|天文學家]]為幫補生計，會以收費方式，用望遠鏡向路人展示各種奇妙的天象。到了19世紀，歐美各地都有些用天文望遠鏡來賺錢的人。他們在[[公園|公園]]上架設望遠鏡，等待那些充滿好奇心的人經過。他們手執[[銀|銀]][[懷表|懷表]]，收費按分鐘計，客人會坐在木盒上觀看[[月球|月球]]和[[土星|土星]]等天體，是19世紀的特殊景觀。在那些日子，天文望遠鏡是相當昂貴的玩意，而且產量異常稀少，尋常百姓根本買不起，所以這是一門尚算有利可圖的生意。特別是出現[[日蝕|日蝕]]和[[月蝕|月蝕]]的時候，這種服務特別受歡迎。然而，有些[[天文愛好者|天文愛好者]]是免費讓大眾觀賞的<ref>[http://books.google.com/books?id=CJ4sAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA298&dq=%22new+york%22+street+corner+telescope&lr=%20 Friends' intelligencer Page 298  1862]</ref><ref>[http://books.google.com/books?id=tY4VAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA35&dq=%22telescope+on+a+sidewalk%22 Theatrical management in the West and South for thirty years By Solomon Smith, Sinclair Hamilton Collection of American Illustrated Books  before 1868 – mention for pay]</ref><ref>[http://books.google.com/books?id=b3ZEAAAAIAAJ&q=%22sidewalk+astronomer%22&dq=%22sidewalk+astronomer%22&lr=%20&pgis=1 Jerome Sydney Meyer, "Great inventions" - Page 51]</ref>。像是早在1930年於[[路易斯安那州|路易斯安那州]][[紐奧良|紐奧良]]的法蘭克·馬林<ref name = "Brown1">{{Citation| last = Brown| first = John| title = John Brown's New Orleans Sidewalk Astronomy web page| date = 2009-01-16| url = http://www.sidewalkastronomy.com/| accessdate = 2009-06-23}}</ref>和1870年代在[[洛杉磯|洛杉磯]]的葛羅斯先生( Mr Grosser)，他不只架設望遠鏡，還有附著照明的顯微鏡。儘管現在還有會收費來讓大眾透過他們架設的望遠鏡觀看天體，但是近年來，路邊天文經常和[[利他主義|利他的]]個人或團體結合，提供免費的公共教育服務，讓民眾觀賞夜空的天體<ref name = "Moser1">"{{Citation| last = Moser| first = Don| title = 35 Who Made a Difference: John Dobson| publisher = Smithsonian.com| date = 2005-11-01| url = http://www.smithsonianmag.com/people-places/dobson.html| accessdate = 2009-06-23}}</ref>。

因為路邊天文是在[[光汙染|光汙染]]的區域內活動，路邊天文學家往往選擇可以看見明亮天體，像是行星、月球和明亮恆星的時間來活動。在白天，路邊天文學也經常會使用太陽濾鏡讓民眾透過望遠鏡觀賞太陽。通常，使用的望遠鏡會比''百貨公司販賣的望遠鏡''還大，有些還是非常的巨大<ref name="books.google.com"/><ref name="Moser1"/>。

== 業餘天文的活動 ==
[[Image:The_moon_and_saturn.jpg|200px]]
隨著[[業餘天文學#天文社團|業餘天文團體]]的出現和成長，路邊天文與大眾天文教育結合成為一般人士想透過望遠鏡觀賞天體的免費管道，這通常包括業餘的天文學家或天文團體，與小至中等尺寸(口徑)的望遠鏡。像是由[[约翰·道布森|約翰·杜布森]]創立的[[舊金山|舊金山]]路邊天文學家組織，就是秉持著教育大眾，讓他們透過望遠鏡來認識我們所居住宇宙的理想，創立的組織
<ref>[http://www.sidewalkastronomers.us/id1.html Sidewalk Astronomers]</ref><ref>[https://www.webcitation.org/query?url=http://www.geocities.com/capecanaveral/6389/Page15.htm&date=2009-10-25+16:53:08 Los Angeles Sidewalk Astronomers - The Sidewalk Astronomers, who we are and what we do!]</ref><ref>[http://www.angelfire.com/co/DATSCOPE/whoarewe.html Liverpool Astronomical Society Sidewalk Astronomers]</ref><ref name="space1">{{Citation| last = | first = | title = John Dobson: Amateur Astronomy's Revolutionary (Page 2)| work = | publisher = space.com| date = 2000-05-05| url = http://www.space.com/scienceastronomy/astronomy/dobson_astronomer_two_000507.html| doi = | accessdate = 2009-06-23| deadurl = yes| archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20090524231108/http://www.space.com/scienceastronomy/astronomy/dobson_astronomer_two_000507.html| archivedate = 2009-05-24}}</ref>。杜布森為了推展路邊天文還設計出了大且便宜，稱為'''路邊望遠鏡'''的[[反射望遠鏡|反射望遠鏡]]，但一般都稱之為[[杜布森望遠鏡|杜布森望遠鏡]]<ref>[http://books.google.com/books?id=qnN_KRFgITQC&pg=PA17&dq=dobsonian+dobson&lr=%20 Fred Watson "'''Why is Uranus Upside Down?'''", Inc NetLibrary - Page 17]</ref>。
