[[路易斯安那州|路易斯安那州]]是[[美国南部|美国南部]]州份。[[2010年美国人口普查|2010年美国人口普查]]结果表明，该州共有453万3479人，在[[美国州份|全美各州]]名列[[美国各州人口列表|第25位]]，陆地面积则以11万1897.6平方公里排名第33<ref name="Census 2010">{{cite web | url = http://factfinder.census.gov/bkmk/table/1.0/en/DEC/10_SF1/GCTPH1.ST10/0400000US01 | title = GCT-PH1 – Population, Housing Units, Area, and Density: 2010 – State — Place and (in selected states) County Subdivision | publisher = United States Census Bureau | work = 2010 United States Census | accessdate = 2017-02-07}}</ref>。全州分为[[路易斯安那州行政区划|64个堂区]]，堂区的行政地位同其他州的[[县_(美国)|县]]相同，所有堂区内共注册成立有308个[[基层政权|基层政权]]，其中绝大多数是市、镇、村，占304个，另外四个是市-堂区合一的市法团<ref name="Census Louisiana 2010">{{cite web | url = https://www.census.gov/prod/cen2010/cph-2-20.pdf | format = [[便携式文档格式|PDF]] | title = Louisiana: 2010 Population and Housing Unit Counts 2010 Census of Population and Housing | date = 2012-09 | publisher = [[美国人口调查局|United States Census Bureau]]| work = [[2010年美国人口普查|2010 United States Census]]| accessdate = 2015-06-22 | page = III-2 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150622104129/http://www.census.gov/prod/cen2010/cph-2-20.pdf | deadurl = yes | archivedate = 2015-06-22}}</ref>。所有基层政权的陆地面积总和只占全州的7.9%，但居住总人口占全州45.3%<ref name="Census 2010" />。

根据2015年修订的路易斯安那州法律，任何未经注册的地区只要人口达到最低标准，就可由当地居民自行申请组建基层政权<ref>{{Cite web | title = 2015 Louisiana Laws Revised Statutes TITLE 33 - Municipalities and Parishes RS 33:1 - Petition for incorporation; contents; circulation; required signatures | author = Louisiana State Legislature | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20170111081556/http://law.justia.com/codes/louisiana/2015/code-revisedstatutes/title-33/rs-33-1/ | url = http://law.justia.com/codes/louisiana/2015/code-revisedstatutes/title-33/rs-33-1/ | publisher = Louisiana State Legislature | accessdate = 2017-02-07 | archivedate = 2017-01-11 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。所有基层政权按人口分成市、镇、村三类。人口达到5000为市，1001至4999为镇，1000或以下为村<ref>{{Cite web | title = 2015 Louisiana Laws Revised Statutes TITLE 33 - Municipalities and Parishes RS 33:341 - Division into cities, towns, and villages | publisher = Louisiana State Legislature | accessdate = 2017-02-07 | url = http://law.justia.com/codes/louisiana/2015/code-revisedstatutes/title-33/rs-33-341/ | author = Louisiana State Legislature | archivedate = 2017-01-11 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20170111142718/http://law.justia.com/codes/louisiana/2015/code-revisedstatutes/title-33/rs-33-341}}</ref>。如果人口普查结果表明基层政权因人口增减而需变更类别、且有政权议会提出申请，那么州长就有权重新分类<ref>{{Cite web | title = 2015 Louisiana Laws Revised Statutes TITLE 33 - Municipalities and Parishes RS 33:342 - Change in classification of municipality; governor's proclamation; census not conclusive; judicial notice | accessdate = 2017-02-07 | url = http://law.justia.com/codes/louisiana/2015/code-revisedstatutes/title-33/rs-33-342/ | author = Louisiana State Legislature | publisher = Louisiana State Legislature | archivedate = 2017-01-11 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20170111204129/http://law.justia.com/codes/louisiana/2015/code-revisedstatutes/title-33/rs-33-342}}</ref>。基层政权拥有包括征税、偿债在内的各项地方政府职能<ref>{{Cite web | title = 2015 Louisiana Laws Revised Statutes TITLE 33 - Municipalities and Parishes RS 33:361 - Municipal powers | author = Louisiana State Legislature | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20170111081552/http://law.justia.com/codes/louisiana/2015/code-revisedstatutes/title-33/rs-33-361/ | archivedate = 2017-01-11 | deadurl = no | publisher = Louisiana State Legislature | accessdate = 2017-02-07 | url = http://law.justia.com/codes/louisiana/2015/code-revisedstatutes/title-33/rs-33-361/}}</ref>。

2010年人口普查结果显示，路易斯安那州以[[巴吞鲁日_(路易斯安那州)|巴吞鲁日]]居民最多，为38万4452人；相比之下，最少的[[芒德_(路易斯安那州)|芒德]]只有19人<ref name="Census 2010" />。全州各基层政权以[[泰勒博恩堂区_(路易斯安那州)|候马]]面积最大，达3190.4平方公里；[[拿破仑维尔_(路易斯安那州)|拿破仑维尔]]最小，只有0.44平方公里<ref name="Census 2010" />。[[纳契托什_(路易斯安那州)|纳契托什]]于1712年成立，是全州老资格的基层政权，2005年成立的[[中央_(路易斯安那州)|中央]]则是最年轻的基层政权<ref name="Muniprofile" />。
