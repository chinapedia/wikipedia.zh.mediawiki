'''弗格森'''（'''{{lang-en|Ferguson}}'''）是[[美國|美國]][[密蘇里州|密蘇里州]][[聖路易斯縣_(密蘇里州)|聖路易斯縣]]的一個城市，<ref name="GNIS"/> [[2010年美國人口普查|2010年美國人口普查]]顯示當地有21,203人。<ref name="Census 2010">{{cite web| url=http://factfinder2.census.gov| title=Race, Hispanic or Latino, Age, and Housing Occupancy: 2010 Census Redistricting Data (Public Law 94-171) Summary File (QT-PL), Ferguson city, Missouri| publisher=U.S. Census Bureau, American FactFinder 2| accessdate=October 21, 2011}}</ref> 這裡是[[艾默生電氣公司|艾默生電氣公司]]總部所在地。

威廉·B·弗格森（William B. Ferguson）於1855年將這裡的{{convert|10|acre}}土地立契轉讓給[[沃什巴鐵路|沃什巴鐵路]]用來建立新的站台。<ref>{{cite book|last=Fox|first=Tim|title=Where We Live: A Guide to St. Louis Communities|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=xRnVeManRJ4C&pg=PA186|year=1995|publisher=Missouri History Museum|isbn=978-1-883982-12-6|page=186}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|last1=Bryan|first1=William Smith|last2=Rose|first2=Robert|last3=Elwang|first3=William Wilson|title=A History of the Pioneer Families of Missouri: With Numerous Sketches, Anecdotes, Adventures, Etc., Relating to Early Days in Missouri. Also the Lives of Daniel Boone and the Celebrated Indian Chief Black Hawk, with Numerous Biographies and Histories of Primitive Institutions|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=JeEXAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA167|year=1876|publisher=Lucas brothers|page=167}}</ref> 在這個叫做弗格森站的站台的周邊逐漸發展出了聚居地。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.fergusoncity.com/141/City-History | title=City History | publisher=City of Ferguson, MO | accessdate=13 August 2014}}</ref> 1894年形成了一個有法人地位的市。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://aboutstlouis.com/local/communities/ferguson-missouri | title=Ferguson Missouri | publisher=Aboutstlouis.com | accessdate=13 August 2014}}</ref> 2014年8月9日，在此發生[[邁克爾·布朗命案|邁克爾·布朗命案]]，引起美國社會震動。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.nbcnews.com/storyline/michael-brown-shooting/ferguson-chief-names-darren-wilson-cop-who-shot-michael-brown-n181326 | title=Ferguson Chief Names Darren Wilson as Cop Who Shot Michael Brown | publisher=NBCNews.com | date=15 August 2014 | accessdate=15 August 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/news/post-nation/wp/2014/08/15/ferguson-police-releasing-name-of-officer-who-shot-michael-brown/ | title=Ferguson police call Michael Brown a robbery suspect, identify Darren Wilson as officer who shot him | date=15 August 2014 | accessdate=15 August 2014 | author=Berman, Mark; Lowery, Wesley}}</ref>
