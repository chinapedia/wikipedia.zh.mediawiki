'''弗柯望博物館'''（{{lang-de|'''Museum Folkwang'''}}）是一座位於德國[[埃森|埃森]]的美術館，主要收藏19世紀到20世紀的藝術作品。1922年時由'''Essener Kunstmuseum'''（1906年成立）和'''Folkwang Museum'''（1902年成立）合併而成。 <ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.saatchi-gallery.co.uk/museums/museum-profile/Museum+Folkwang,+Essen/396.html |title=Museum Folkwang, Essen |publisher=The Saatchi Gallery |date= |accessdate=2013-01-17}}</ref> 此外隸屬於該博物館的德國海報博物館（Deutsche Plakat Museum）還收藏有大約三十四萬張海報。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dasschoenstemuseumderwelt.de/en/exhibition/introduction.html |title=Introduction – Das schönste Museum der Welt – Museum Foklkwang bis 1933 |publisher=Dasschoenstemuseumderwelt.de |date=20 August 2010|accessdate=24 August 2010}}</ref>

“Folkwang”來自北歐神話中女神[[弗蕾亞|弗蕾亞]]的宮殿[[弗爾克范格|弗爾克范格]]（Fólkvangr）。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.folkwang-uni.de/en/home/hochschule/about-folkwang/history/ |title=History |publisher=Folkwang-uni.de |date= |accessdate=24 August 2010}}</ref> 2007年，[[大衛·奇普菲爾德|大衛·奇普菲爾德]]為弗柯望博物館設計了新的延伸部分，以滿足日益擴大的藏品收藏需要。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dezeen.com/2010/02/03/museum-folkwang-by-david-chipperfield/ |title=Blog Archive » Museum Folkwang by David Chipperfield|publisher=Dezeen|date=3 February 2010|accessdate=24 August 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Woodman |first=Ellis |url=http://www.bdonline.co.uk/buildings/david-chipperfield%E2%80%99s-museum-folkwang-in-essen-germany/3158321.article |title=David Chipperfield’s Museum Folkwang in Essen, Germany | Building Studies | Building Design |publisher=Bdonline.co.uk |date=19 February 2010|accessdate=24 August 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.essen-fuer-das-ruhrgebiet.ruhr2010.de/en/programme/re-designing-the-metropolis/structural-culture/the-new-museum-folkwang.html|title=The new Museum Folkwang: RUHR.2010|publisher=Essen-fuer-das-ruhrgebiet.ruhr2010.de|date=|accessdate=24 August 2010|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110106133345/http://www.essen-fuer-das-ruhrgebiet.ruhr2010.de/en/programme/re-designing-the-metropolis/structural-culture/the-new-museum-folkwang.html|archivedate=2011年1月6日}}</ref>
