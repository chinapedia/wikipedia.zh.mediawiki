'''弗雷迪·格雷'''（{{lang-en|Freddie Gray}}，1989年8月16日－2015年4月12日），[[非裔美国人|非裔美国人]]，因私藏[[弹簧刀|弹簧刀]]被[[巴尔的摩警察局|巴尔的摩警察局]]拘留，在押送期间，格雷因[[脊髓|脊髓]]和[[喉头|喉头]]受伤陷入昏迷，被送往创伤中心接受救治<ref name=":1"/><ref name="theguardian20150420"/><ref name="atlantic-mysterious death"/>，最终于2015年4月19日不治身亡<ref name="atlantic-mysterious death"/>。

受伤的情况目前尚未明确，目击者认为，警方在逮捕格雷时动用了不必要的武力，但涉事警员否认该说法<ref name="theguardian20150420"/><ref name="atlantic-mysterious death"/><ref name=bsun-45minmystery/>。2015年4月21日，为了调查该事件，六名巴尔的摩警察被带薪停职<ref name="theguardian20150420"/>。局长安东尼·巴茨（{{lang|en|Anthony Batts}}）表示警方未给格雷提供及时的医疗救助，也没有在前往警察局时将格雷扣紧<ref name=":3"/>——医学调查报告表明格雷在运输过程中曾遭到伤害<ref name=":3"/><ref name="cnn-injuryvan"/>。

2015年5月1日，巴尔的摩州检察官收到法医报告，裁定格雷的死系[[他杀|他杀]]<ref name="Blinder.NYT"/>。检察官表示，逮捕格雷是非法的，因为所谓的弹簧刀是合乎法定大小的小刀<ref name="Blinder.NYT"/>。检察官称他们有可能向六名涉事警察提出刑事指控。一名警察被控[[极端轻率谋杀罪|极端轻率]][[谋杀|二级谋杀罪]]，其他警察被控以误杀或非法逮捕等罪行<ref name=Blinder.NYT>{{cite news|last1=Blinder|first1=Alan|title=Prosecutors Charge 6 Baltimore Officers in Freddie Gray Death|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2015/05/02/us/freddie-gray-autopsy-report-given-to-baltimore-prosecutors.html?_r=0|accessdate=2015-05-01|newspaper=New York Times|date=2015-05-01}}</ref>。

受到2014年[[迈克尔·布朗枪击案|迈克尔·布朗枪击案]]的影响，格雷的死引发了一系列持续中的抗议和骚乱。2015年4月25日，巴尔的摩市中心的一场大型抗议演变成[[2015年巴尔的摩暴动|暴动]]，造成34人被捕，15名警察受伤<ref name=":8" />。在格雷葬礼举行的4月27日，骚乱升级，当地企业和一家[[CVS|CVS]]药店遭到掠夺并被烧毁，州长[[拉里·霍根|拉里·霍根]]（{{lang|en|Larry Hogan}}）宣布进入[[紧急状态|紧急状态]]，马里兰州[[国民警卫队|国民警卫队]]前往戒备。

== 背景 ==
弗雷迪·格雷是葛洛莉亚·达登（{{lang|en|Gloria Darden}}）25岁的儿子，有两个孪生姐妹弗雷德瑞卡（{{lang|en|Fredericka}}）和卡萝莱纳（{{lang|en|Carolina}}）<ref name=":4">{{Cite web|title = Beginning of Freddie Gray's life as sad as its end, court case shows|url = http://www.baltimoresun.com/news/maryland/baltimore-city/bs-md-freddie-gray-lead-paint-20150423-story.html#page=1|accessdate = 2015-04-23|first = Jean|last = Marbella|date = 2015-04-23}}</ref>。去世时，格雷住在她姐姐在吉利摩尔·霍莫斯（{{lang|en|Gilmor Homes}}）社区的家中<ref name=":4" />。格雷身高{{convert|5|ft|8|in}}，体重{{convert|145|lbs}}，有轻罪和毒品罪行前科<ref name=":7">{{Cite news|title = The death of Freddie Gray: What we know - and don't know|url = http://www.latimes.com/nation/la-na-baltimore-freddie-gray-20150422-story.html|newspaper = Los Angeles Times|access-date = 2015-04-26|issn = 0458-3035|language = en-US|date = 2015-04-22|last = Muskal|first = Michael}}</ref>。他在马里兰州一共被捕22次，原因主要是持有和分销分发毒品<ref name=":10"> {{Cite web|title = <nowiki>FREDDIE GRAY’S ARREST RECORD: Here’s The Rap Sheet Of The Dude They’re Destroying Baltimore Over</nowiki> |url = http://clashdaily.com/2015/04/freddy-grays-arrest-record-heres-the-rap-sheet-of-the-dude-theyre-destroying-baltimore-over/#|accessdate = 2015-04-29|first = Jean|last = Marbella|date = 2015-04-29}}</ref>。格雷曾涉及20件刑事法庭案件，其中有5件在他死亡时仍然有效<ref name=cnn-whatweknow>{{cite web|title=What we know, don't know about Freddie Gray's death|url=http://www.cnn.com/2015/04/22/us/baltimore-freddie-gray-what-we-know/|website=CNN.com|accessdate=2015-04-29|date=2015-04-22}}</ref><ref name=":7">{{Cite news|title = The death of Freddie Gray: What we know - and don't know|url = http://www.latimes.com/nation/la-na-baltimore-freddie-gray-20150422-story.html|newspaper = Los Angeles Times|access-date = 2015-04-26|issn = 0458-3035|language = en-US|date = 2015-04-22|last = Muskal|first = Michael}}</ref>。

== 被捕和遇害 ==
[[File:Timeline_of_Freddie_Gray's_arrest.svg|thumb]]
2015年4月12日，警方在巴尔的摩高度贫穷、高利贷、毒品交易和暴力犯罪盛行的吉摩尔之家住宅项目（{{lang|en|Gilmor Homes}}）附近的街道上碰到格雷<ref name=TNY.murderCase>{{cite web|title=Freddie Gray’s Death Becomes a Murder Case|url=http://www.newyorker.com/news/amy-davidson/freddie-grays-death-becomes-a-murder-case|publisher=The New Yorker|accessdate=2015-05-01}}</ref>。根据巴尔的摩警察局提供的报告<ref name=":1" /><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.baltimoresun.com/news/maryland/crime/blog/bal-charging-documents-for-freddie-gray-20150420-htmlstory.html |title= Charging documents for Freddie Gray |work= Baltimore Sun |publisher= Tribune Publishing |date= 2015-04-28 |accessdate=2015-04-30}}</ref>，上午8点39分，警方“眼神接触”了格雷<ref name=TNY.murderCase/><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.foxnews.com/us/2015/05/01/timeline-freddie-gray-arrest-on-april-12/|title=A timeline of Freddie Gray's arrest on April 12|work=foxnews.com|accessdate=2015-05-02}}</ref>，“意外注意到警察”的格雷慌忙逃跑，被追上去的警察不用武力抓获。逮捕期间，警察表示从格雷“前方的右侧口袋搜到一把藏匿的弹簧刀。收缴了小刀的警察发现弹簧使得小刀能单手使用<ref name=bsun-45minmystery /><ref name=":0">{{cite web|last=McLaughlin|first=Eliott|title=Freddie Gray death: Protesters rally in Baltimore|work=CNN.com|date=2015-04-21|url=http://www.cnn.com/2015/04/21/us/baltimore-freddie-gray-death/index.html|accessdate=2015-04-22}}</ref>。”警察加勒特·米勒（{{lang|en|Garrett Miller}}）以格雷违法19 59 22法令为由正式指控被告“在巴尔的摩市範圍內非法携带、持有和出售一把带有自动弹簧或其他刀片开关装置、通常被稱為弹簧刀的小刀”。调查发现這個指控不正確，因为那把刀只是可以折合，而可折合的刀是合法的<ref name=bbc-homicide/>。

两名路人录下了格雷被拖进警方面包车的视频，片段中的格雷表示出疼痛状，被警官拖进警车。认识的格雷的一名旁观者表示，警察将格雷“摁住”——一名警察将格雷的腿弯向背后，另一名则把格雷的脖子摁向膝盖处。，后来大部分证人同时认为格雷“不能走路”<ref>{{cite web |url= http://edition.cnn.com/videos/tv/2015/04/23/ac-freddie-gray-witness-speaks-to-anderson-cooper.cnn
|title= Freddie Gray witness speaks to Anderson Cooper|work= CNN|publisher= CNN|accessdate=2015-05-01}}</ref>，“不能挪步子”<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7YV0EtkWyno&feature=youtu.be
|title= New video shows arrest of Freddie Gray in Baltimore|work= CNN|publisher= CNN|accessdate=2015-05-01}}</ref>，“他的腿受伤了，大家都看到了他被拖拽的情景<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/video/news/video-1176745/Baltimore-Police-drag-Freddie-Gray-van-arrest.html |title= Baltimore police drag Freddie Gray |work= Daily Mail|publisher= Daily Mail accessdate=2015-05-01 }}</ref>。”另一名目击者告诉《[[巴尔的摩太阳报|巴尔的摩太阳报]]》，声称自己看到格雷被棍棒殴打<ref name=bsun-45minmystery/><ref name=":2"/>。

根据警方时间表，格雷遭拘捕后有11分钟呆在运输车里，30分钟后被传唤的医护人员把他送往医院<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nytimes.com/interactive/2015/04/30/us/what-happened-freddie-gray-arrested-by-baltimore-police-department-map-timeline.html|title=The Timeline of Freddie Gray’s Arrest and the Charges Filed|work=NYTimes.com|accessdate=2015-05-01}}</ref>。面包车曾停下来四次。上午8点46分，警方认为格雷“怒火中烧”，就把他卸下，给他戴脚镣。后来的停车被一名私人保安的相机记录下来，显示出面包车在一家杂货店处。上午8点59分，第二名犯人被带上车子，此事警方正检查格雷的身体状况。在警方报告中，米勒表示“车子行至西区期间，曾对被告人实施急救，并立即前往创伤中心<ref name=bsun-45minmystery>{{cite news|last1=Rector|first1=Kevin|title=The 45-minute mystery of Freddie Gray's death|url=http://www.baltimoresun.com/news/maryland/baltimore-city/bs-md-gray-ticker-20150425-story.html#page=1|accessdate=2015-04-28|work=The Baltimore Sun}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://m.nydailynews.com/news/national/events-leading-freddie-gray-death-police-custody-article-1.2205414|title=Events Leading to Freddie Gray's Death|work=nydailynews.com|accessdate=2015-04-30}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Baltimore Police Promise Full Investigation Into Man's Death After Arrest|url=http://www.npr.org/blogs/thetwo-way/2015/04/20/401110943/baltimore-police-promise-full-investigation-into-mans-death-after-arrest|accessdate=2015-04-22|publisher= NPR|last=Peralta|first=Eyder|date=2015-04-20}}</ref>。昏迷中的格雷最终被带到马里兰州大学R·亚当斯·考利创伤休克中心（{{lang|en|University of Maryland R Adams Cowley Shock Trauma Center}}）<ref>{{cite news|title=Baltimore police: Freddie Gray died from a 'tragic injury to his spinal cord'|url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/news/morning-mix/wp/2015/04/20/baltimore-police-freddie-gray-arrested-without-force-or-incident-before-fatal-injury/|newspaper=The Washington Post|date=2015-04-20|accessdate=2015-04-26|issn=0190-8286|language=en-US|first=Lindsey|last=Bever|first2=Abby|last2=Ohlheiser}}</ref>。

媒体表示警方的粗鲁驾驶、戴着手铐的囚徒在驾驶得不稳定的车子里没有戴安全带，可能是格雷受伤的因素<ref name=donovan>{{cite news|last1=Donovan|first1=Doug|last2=Puente|first2=Mark|title=Freddie Gray not the first to come out of Baltimore police van with serious injuries|url=http://www.baltimoresun.com/news/maryland/baltimore-city/bs-md-gray-rough-rides-20150423-story.html#page=1|accessdate=2015-04-28|work=The Baltimore Sun|date=2015-04-23}}</ref><ref name=wusa>{{cite news|title=Baltimore police prisoner rough ride history|last1=Broom|first1=Scott|url=http://www.wusa9.com/videos/news/local/2015/04/24/26329441/|accessdate=2015-04-29|publisher=WUSA 9|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150501101007/http://www.wusa9.com/videos/news/local/2015/04/24/26329441/|archivedate=2015年5月1日|df=}}</ref>。

接下来的一周中，格雷家的律师表示格雷抢救过来但仍处于昏迷状态，至少[[心停止|心跳呼吸骤停]]一次，需要进行漫长的手术治疗以竭力挽救生命<ref name=":1">{{cite web|title=Baltimore looks into Freddie Gray police custody death|url=http://www.cnn.com/2015/04/20/us/baltimore-freddie-gray-death/index.html|publisher=CNN|accessdate=2015-04-22|date=2015-04-20|last=McLaughlin|first=Eliott C.|last2=Brumfield|first2=Ben|last3=Ford}}</ref>。家人们表示，格雷脖颈处的[[脊椎|脊椎]]被切断80%，三个[[椎体|椎体]]和[[喉头|喉头]]受伤<ref name="theguardian20150420">{{Cite web|title = Six Baltimore officers suspended over police-van death of Freddie Gray|url = http://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2015/apr/20/baltimore-officers-suspended-death-freddie-gray|website = The Guardian|accessdate =2015-04-24|first = Oliver|last = Laughland|first2 = Jon|last2 = Swaine|date = 2015-04-20}}</ref><ref name="atlantic-mysterious death">{{Cite web|title = The Mysterious Death of Freddie Gray|url = http://www.theatlantic.com/politics/archive/2015/04/the-mysterious-death-of-freddie-gray/391119/|accessdate =2015-04-26|last = Graham|first = David A.|publication-date = 2015-04-22}}</ref>。警方证实，脊髓损伤导致了格雷的死亡<ref name="theguardian20150420"/><ref name="atlantic-mysterious death"/>。律师同时质疑对格雷曾藏有弹簧刀的证言，表示刀子实际上是“法定大小的随身小折刀<ref name=cnn-attorneystatement>{{cite web|title=Arresting officers provide statements in Freddie Gray death|url=http://www.cnn.com/2015/04/22/us/baltimore-freddie-gray-death/|website=CNN.com|accessdate=2015-05-01|date=2015-04-22}}</ref>。”2015年4月19日，被捕一个星期后，格雷不治身亡<ref name=":0"/>。

=== 调查 ===
[[巴尔的摩警察局|巴尔的摩警察局]]不停薪暂停了六名涉事警察的职务以调查格雷的死。这六名警察分别为中尉布莱恩·赖斯（{{lang|en|Brian Rice}}，从警18年），41岁、中士艾莉西亚·怀特（{{lang|en|Alicia White}}，从警5年），30岁、警官威廉·波特（{{lang|en|William Porter}}，从警5年），25岁、警官加勒特·米勒（{{lang|en|Garrett Miller}}，从警3年），26岁、警官爱德华·尼禄（{{lang|en|Edward Nero}}，从警3年），29岁和警官凯撒·古德森（{{lang|en|Caesar Goodson}}，从警16年），45岁<ref>Miller, Jayne. [http://m.wbaltv.com/news/charging-documents-freddie-gray-ran-from-police/32468120 6 officers suspended in Freddie Gray case], WBAL, April 20, 2015. Retrieved on April 30, 2015.</ref>。警察局长安东尼·巴茨2015年4月24日表示，“我们知道我们的警察雇员多次没有将他及时送医<ref name=":3">{{Cite web|title = Police: Freddie Gray didn't get timely medical care after arrest|url = http://www.cnn.com/2015/04/24/us/baltimore-freddie-gray-death/index.html|website = CNN|accessdate = 2015-04-24|last = Payne|first = Ed|last2 = Almasy|first2 = Steve|last3 = Pearson|first3 = Michael|date = 2015-04-24}}</ref>。”他也承认，警方将格雷带上面包车前往警察局时没能将他锁紧<ref name=":3"/>。[[美国司法部|美国司法部]]亦开始案件的调查<ref>{{Cite web|title = Feds investigating Baltimore man's death in police custody|url = http://www.cbsnews.com/videos/feds-investigating-baltimore-mans-death-in-police-custody/|website = www.cbsnews.com|accessdate = April 22, 2015|last = Boswell|first = Craig|date = April 21, 2015}}</ref>。

2015年4月30日，曾录下格雷被捕过程的证人凯文·摩尔（{{lang|en|Kevin Moore}}）在枪口胁迫下被警方以“骚扰和恐吓罪”逮捕。摩尔表示，他已跟警方合作，把格雷被捕的视频交出以作调查。他声称虽说是在协助调查，他的照片却被警方公开以作进一步调查<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/americas/the-man-who-filmed-freddie-gray-video-has-been-arrested-at-gunpoint-10217973.html|title=The Man Who Filmed Freddie Gray Video has been Arrested at Gunpoint|work=independent.co.uk|accessdate=2015-05-01}}</ref>。他后来获释，但和摩尔一同被捕的两个证人仍然在押<ref>{{cite web|url=http://heavy.com/news/2015/04/kevin-moore-filmed-freddie-gray-arrest-arrested-charges-baltimore-ferguson-cop-watch-curfew-video-photo/|title=Kevin Moore: Five Facts You Need to Know|work=heavy.com|accessdate=2015-05-01}}</ref>。当天，法医表示格雷撞进面包车引起持续的伤害。“显然他的脖子被弄断，头部的持续伤痕与面包车后部的螺栓相吻合<ref name="cnn-injuryvan">{{cite web|url=http://www.cnn.com/2015/04/30/us/baltimore-freddie-gray-death-investigation/|title=Report: Freddie Gray sustained injury in back of police van|work=CNN.com|accessdate=2015-04-30}}</ref>。

=== 控告 ===
2015年5月1日，巴尔的摩检察官办公室裁定弗雷迪·格雷系[[他杀|他杀]]致死，有可能向六名涉事警察提出刑事指控。巴尔的摩州律师[[玛丽莲·莫斯|玛丽莲·莫斯]]（{{lang|en|Marilyn Mosby}}）表示，巴尔的摩警方采用非法行动，而“（弗雷迪·格雷）犯罪”<ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2015/may/01/baltimore-freddie-gray-death |title= Freddie Gray death: cries of 'justice' in Baltimore after six officers charged |date= 2015-05-01 |accessdate= 2015-05-01 |publisher= The Guardian |author1= Jon Swaine |author2= Oliver Laughland |author3= Raya Jalabi}}</ref>。莫斯说，格雷“被戴上手铐和脚镣时颈部遭受关键受伤，在警方车辆内行为奔放<ref name=NTY.Charged>{{cite web|title=6 Baltimore Police Officers Charged in Freddie Gray Death|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2015/05/02/us/freddie-gray-autopsy-report-given-to-baltimore-prosecutors.html|accessdate=2015-05-01}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.reuters.com/article/2015/05/01/us-usa-police-baltimore-idUSKBN0NL1GO20150501|title=Six Baltimore police officers face murder, other charges in death of black man|work=Reuters.com|accessdate=2015-05-01}}</ref>。”莫斯表示，警方“未能给出逮捕格雷先生的原因，因为他没有犯罪<ref name="NYTimesCharges" />。”莫斯指控警方非法禁锢罪，因为隔离携带的小刀属合法尺寸，并不是警方所声称的格雷被捕时持有的弹簧刀<ref name=bbc-homicide>{{cite web|title=Freddie Gray: Baltimore police to face criminal charges|url=http://www.bbc.com/news/world-us-canada-32554371|website=BBC News|accessdate=2015-05-01|date=2015-05-01}}</ref>。六名警察被拘留在巴尔的摩中央惩处与人事中心（{{lang|en|Baltimore Central Booking and Intake Center}}）<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.baltimoresun.com/news/maryland/baltimore-city/bs-md-ci-freddie-gray-mosby-presser-0502-20150501-story.html#page=1|title=Six officers charged in death of Freddie Gray|work=baltimoresun.com|accessdate=2015-05-01}}</ref><ref name=BSun.Charged>{{cite web|title=Six officers charged in death of Freddie Gray|url=http://www.baltimoresun.com/news/maryland/baltimore-city/bs-md-ci-freddie-gray-mosby-presser-0502-20150501-story.html#page=1|publisher=Baltimore Sun|accessdate=2015-05-01}}</ref>。

三名警察面临着[[过失杀人|过失杀人]]指控，一人被控[[极端轻率谋杀罪|极端轻率]][[谋杀|二级谋杀罪]]。谋杀指控至少可能被判处30年监禁，误杀和殴打罪行的最高刑罚可达10年监禁<ref name = "npr">{{cite web|url=http://www.npr.org/blogs/thetwo-way/2015/05/01/403496063/freddie-gray-update-new-speculation-on-his-death-and-peaceful-protests|title=Charges Against Officers In Freddie Gray's Death Range From Murder To Assault|work=npr.org|accessdate=2015-05-01}}</ref>。所有六名警察遭惩处的同一日被保释。两名警察以25万美元保释，其他四名的保释金为35万美元<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.baltimoresun.com/news/maryland/crime/blog/bs-md-ci-what-is-next-for-officers-20150501-story.html|title=All six Baltimore officers charged in Freddie Gray's death|work=Baltimore Sun|accessdate=2015-05-01}}</ref>。

市长斯蒂芬妮·罗林斯-布莱克（{{lang|en|Stephanie Rawlings-Blake}}）表示，巴尔的摩警察局不会给有暴力倾向和种族主义的警方有立身之地<ref name=NYTimesCharges>{{cite web|authors=Alan Blinder and Richard Perez-Pena|title=6 Baltimore Police Officers Charged in Freddie Gray Death|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2015/05/02/us/freddie-gray-autopsy-report-given-to-baltimore-prosecutors.html|website=NY Times|accessdate=2015-05-01|date=2015-05-01}}</ref>。警方工会分会会长吉恩·瑞安（{{lang|en|Gene Ryan}}）表示，尽管局面悲惨，但“没有涉事警方能为格雷先生的死负责<ref name=NYTimesCharges/>。”
