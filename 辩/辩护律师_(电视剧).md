《'''辯護律師'''》（{{lang|en|The Defenders}}）是[[哥倫比亞廣播公司|哥倫比亞廣播公司]]于2010年9月22日開播的秋季檔律政類[[喜劇|喜劇]]劇集。本劇講述了[[美國|美國]][[內華達州|內華達州]][[拉斯維加斯|拉斯維加斯]]兩名[[辯護律師|辯護律師]]搭檔Michael Cristalli和Marc Saggese為他們的客戶辯護以及他們各自的個人生活。<ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2010/05/17/cbs-welcomes-four-to-freshman-class/8685/|title=CBS Welcomes Four to Freshman Class|date= May 17, 2010 |work=The Futon Critic|accessdate=May 20, 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2010/05/19/cbs-announces-2010-2011-primetime-schedule/20100519cbs01/|title=CBS Announces 2010-2011 Primetime Schedule |date= May 19, 2010 |work=The Futon Critic|accessdate=May 20, 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nypost.com/p/entertainment/tv/double_play_v8zKUpz6pufmPIrvGCzoOJ|title=Vegas attorneys inspire 'The Defenders' on CBS|date=June 26, 2010|work=New York Post|accessdate=September 25, 2010}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2010/07/22/cbs-announces-2010-2011-premiere-dates/20100722cbs02/|title=CBS Announces 2010-2011 Premiere Dates|date=July 22, 2010|work=The Futon Critic|accessdate=July 31, 2010}}</ref>
