'''葉形幼體'''（'''phyllosoma'''）指[[無螯下目|無螯下目]]下的現存的[[蝦|蝦]]的[[幼蟲|幼體]]階段。<ref name="Scholtz&Richter">{{cite journal |author=Gerhard Scholtz & Stefan Richter |year=1995 |title=Phylogenetic systematics of the reptantian Decapoda (Crustacea, Malacostraca) |journal=[[Zoological_Journal_of_the_Linnean_Society|Zoological Journal of the Linnean Society]] |volume=113 |pages=289–328 |doi=10.1111/j.1096-3642.1995.tb00936.x |url=http://decapoda.nhm.org/pdfs/826/826.pdf |format=[[Portable_Document_Format|PDF]] |issue=3}}</ref><ref name="Palero2009a">{{cite journal |author=Ferran Palero, Keith A. Crandall, Pere Abelló, Enrique Macpherson & Marta Pascual |year=2009 |title=Phylogenetic relationships between spiny, slipper and coral lobsters (Crustacea, Decapoda, Achelata) |journal=[[Molecular_Phylogenetics_and_Evolution|Molecular Phylogenetics and Evolution]] |volume=50 |issue=1 |pages=152–162 |doi=10.1016/j.ympev.2008.10.003 |pmid=18957325 |url=http://crandalllab.byu.edu/Portals/20/docs/publications/PaleroMPE2009Achelata.pdf |format=[[Portable_Document_Format|PDF]] |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111008214426/http://crandalllab.byu.edu/Portals/20/docs/publications/PaleroMPE2009Achelata.pdf |archivedate=2011-10-08 }}</ref> 這種透明的幼體瘦而扁平，有長足。這一階段可能持續數年之久，最終葉形幼體可以長到很大。<ref>[http://www.teara.govt.nz/en/crabs-crayfish-and-other-crustaceans/3/4 www.teara.govt.nz], contains a picture of phyllosome on the palm</ref> [[龍蝦|龍蝦]]的葉形幼體在變成底棲的成體之前還需經過另外一個過渡階段。<ref name="Diaz">{{cite journal |author=David Diaz, Marc Mari, Pere Abello & Montserrat Demestre |year=2001 |title=Settlement and juvenile habitat of the European spiny lobster ''Palinurus elephas'' (Crustacea : Decapoda : Palinuridae) in the western Mediterranean Sea |journal=[[Scientia_Marina|Scientia Marina]] |volume=65 |pages=347–356 |url=http://digital.csic.es/bitstream/10261/5438/1/juvenile.pdf |format=[[Portable_Document_Format|PDF]] |accessdate=2010-09-28 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110720143555/http://digital.csic.es/bitstream/10261/5438/1/juvenile.pdf |archivedate=2011-07-20 |deadurl=yes }}</ref><ref name="Palero2008a">{{cite journal |author=Ferran Palero, Guillermo Guerao & Paul F. Clark |year=2008 |title= ''Palinustus mossambicus'' Barnard 1926 (Crustacea: Decapoda: Achelata: Palinuridae); morphology of the puerulus stage |journal=[[Zootaxa|Zootaxa]] |volume=1857 |pages=44–54 |url=http://www.mapress.com/zootaxa/2008/f/z01857p054f.pdf |format=[[Portable_Document_Format|PDF]] excerpt}}</ref>

雖然葉形幼體是一個十分值得關注的階段，但是現在對此的研究卻並不深。<ref name="Goñi">{{cite journal |author=Raquel Goñi & Daniel Latrouite |year=2005 |title=Review of the biology, ecology and fisheries of ''Palinurus'' spp. species of European waters:''Palinurus elephas'' (Fabricius, 1787) and ''Palinurus mauritanicus'' (Gruvel, 1911) |journal=[[Cahiers_de_Biologie_Marine|Cahiers de Biologie Marine]] |volume=46 |pages=127–142 |url=http://archimer.ifremer.fr/doc/2005/publication-3625.pdf |format=[[Portable_Document_Format|PDF]]}}</ref><ref name="Kittaka">{{cite journal |author=Jiro Kittaka & Fernando A.
