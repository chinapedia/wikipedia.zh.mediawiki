'''佐丹·魯克·羅迪斯'''（{{lang-en|'''Jordan Luke Rhodes'''}}，{{bd|1990年|2月5日|catIdx=Rhodes, Jordan}}）是一名在[[英格蘭|英格蘭]]出生的[[蘇格蘭|蘇格蘭]]職業足球員，司職[[前鋒_(足球)|前鋒]]，現時被[[英冠|英冠]]球會[[谢周三足球俱乐部|谢周三]]外借至[[诺里奇城足球俱乐部|諾域治]]。

==生平==

===早年===
Born in [[Oldham|Oldham]], [[Greater_Manchester|Greater Manchester]], while his father [[Andy_Rhodes|Andy Rhodes]] was playing for [[Oldham_Athletic_A.F.C.|Oldham Athletic]]. Rhodes senior moved to Scotland later that year when he joined [[Dunfermline_Athletic_F.C.|Dunfermline Athletic]] and stayed in Scotland until 1998 also playing for [[St._Johnstone_F.C.|St Johnstone]] and [[Airdrieonians_F.C.|Airdrieonians]]. There were breaks in the time Rhodes  spent playing in Scotland when he had a loan spells at [[Bolton_Wanderers_F.C.|Bolton Wanderers]] and [[Scarborough_F.C.|Scarborough]]. Thus Jordan Rhodes spent much of his childhood living and being schooled in Scotland. Initially he followed in his fathers footsteps and played as a goalkeeper as a youngster. It was in [[Cairneyhill|Cairneyhill]], near [[Dunfermline|Dunfermline]] where Jordan was coached by the influential Michael Boyle. Rhodes lists Boyle as one of the people that have shaped his style of play.

Andy Rhodes joined [[Ipswich_Town_F.C.|Ipswich Town]] as a coach in 2004. Jordan attended [[Kesgrave_High_School|Kesgrave High School]] in [[Ipswich|Ipswich]] through his secondary school years where he was scouted and recruited by Ipswich Town in 2005.

===球會===
<!--
;葉士域治
Rhodes was bought by Ipswich from [[Barnsley_F.C.|Barnsley]] for £5,000 in March 2005 when his father joined [[Ipswich_Town_F.C.|Ipswich Town]] as a goalkeeping coach. He scored over 40 goals for Ipswich Town under-16s, under-18s and [[reserve_team|reserve team]]s in his first full season at the club. He has also been called up to the England under-17s team, but pulled out through injury.

At the start of the [[2006–07_in_English_football|2006–07]] season Rhodes suffered numerous injuries including a dislocated shoulder and being knocked unconscious. He made his debut for Ipswich in the [[2007–08_in_English_football|2007–08]] season, coming on as a late [[substitute_(association_football)|substitute]] against [[Burnley_F.C.|Burnley]].<ref>{{Cite web | url = http://www.soccerbase.com/results3.sd?gameid=538698|publisher = Soccerbase | accessdate = 12 October 2009| title = Ipswich 0 (0) – 0 (0) Burnley  }}</ref>

On 9 April 2008, Rhodes scored his first league goal in the 73rd minute against [[Cardiff_City_F.C.|Cardiff City]] and was the equalising goal in a 1–1 draw, after coming into the game as a half time substitute.<ref>{{Cite news | url = http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/eng_div_1/7332058.stm| title = Ipswich 1–1 Cardiff  |publisher = BBC Sport | accessdate = 12 October 2009| date = 9 April 2008}}</ref>

He joined [[Oxford_United_F.C.|Oxford United]] in the [[Conference_National|Conference National]] on a one month [[loan_(association_football)|loan]] deal on 10 October 2007. He did not score in his four league appearances but under the watchful eye of Ned Kelly, his mentor in the City of Dreaming Spires, scored in both [[FA_Cup|FA Cup]] appearances. He was recalled from his loan spell on 6 November by Ipswich so that he would be able to play in the club's [[FA_Youth_Cup|FA Youth Cup]] games.<ref>[http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/i/ipswich_town/7080875.stm "Ipswich recall Rhodes from Oxford"] BBC Sport Retrieved on 7 November 2007</ref>
