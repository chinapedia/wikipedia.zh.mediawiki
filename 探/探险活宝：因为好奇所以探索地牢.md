{{vgname|探险活宝：因为好奇所以探索地牢|en=Adventure Time: Explore the Dungeon Because I Don't Know!}}是[[WayForward_Technologies|WayForward Technologies]]开发，[[D3_Publisher|D3 Publisher]]发行的[[动作冒险游戏|动作冒险游戏]]。游戏于2013年在[[PlayStation_3|PlayStation 3]]和[[Xbox_360|Xbox 360]]发行，后来移植到[[Windows|Windows]]、[[Wii_U|Wii U]]和[[任天堂3DS|任天堂3DS]]。

游戏获媒体普遍批评<ref>{{cite web|title=AT:ETDBIDK (PS3)|url=http://www.metacritic.com/game/playstation-3/adventure-time-explore-the-dungeon-because-i-dont-know!|publisher=MetaCritic|accessdate=26 November 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=AT:ETDBIDK! X360|url=http://www.metacritic.com/game/xbox-360/adventure-time-explore-the-dungeon-because-i-dont-know!|publisher=Metacritic|accessdate=5 December 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=AT:ETDBIDK!|url=http://www.metacritic.com/game/wii-u/adventure-time-explore-the-dungeon-because-i-dont-know!|publisher=MetaCritic|accessdate=11 December 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=AT:ETDBIDK!|url=http://www.metacritic.com/game/3ds/adventure-time-explore-the-dungeon-because-i-dont-know!|publisher=MetaCritic|accessdate=24 December 2013}}</ref>，称游戏玩法单调没有深度<ref>{{cite web|title=Adventure Time: Explore the Dungeon Because I DON'T KNOW! for Xbox 360 Reviews |url=http://www.metacritic.com/game/xbox-360/adventure-time-explore-the-dungeon-because-i-dont-know!|publisher=Metacritic|accessdate=26 November 2013}}</ref>。
