'''赛门铁克端点防护'''（{{lang-en|Symantec Endpoint Protection}}）是由[[赛门铁克|赛门铁克]]开发的安全软件套件，包括[[杀毒软件|杀毒软件]]、[[入侵检测系统|入侵检测系统]]和[[防火墙|防火墙]]，适用于[[服务器|服务器]]和[[桌上型電腦|台式电脑]]<ref name="two" />，在[[端点安全|端点安全]]产品中拥有最大的市场份额<ref name="gartner">{{Cite web |url=https://www.gartner.com/doc/reprints?id=1-3N82LG5&ct=161205&st=sb |title=Magic Quadrant for Endpoint Protection Platforms |last=Ouellet |first=Eric |last2=McShane |first2=Ian |date=2017-01-30 |website=gartner.com |publisher=[[高德纳咨询公司|Gartner]] |last3=Litan |first3=Avivah |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170824060129/https://www.gartner.com/doc/reprints?id=1-3N82LG5&ct=161205&st=sb |archivedate=2017-08-24 }}</ref>。

==版本历史==
赛门铁克端点防护首次发行于2007年9月，版本号为11.0<ref name="Messmer 2007">{{cite web | last=Messmer | first=Ellen | title=Symantec revamps endpoint security product | website=Network World | date=2007-09-24 | url=http://www.networkworld.com/article/2285937/lan-wan/symantec-revamps-endpoint-security-product.html | accessdate=2017-04-16 | deadurl=no | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170816195918/http://www.networkworld.com/article/2285937/lan-wan/symantec-revamps-endpoint-security-product.html | archivedate=2017-08-16 }}</ref>。端点防护整合了多个安全软件产品，包括Symantec Antivirus Corporate Edition 10.0、客户端安全、网络访问控制和Sygate Enterprise Edition<ref name="Messmer 2007"/>。端点防护也有新的特性<ref name="Messmer 2007"/>。例如，它可以阻止数据传输至未授权的设备，如[[U盘|U盘]]或[[蓝牙|蓝牙]]设备<ref name="Messmer 2007"/>。

当时，Symantec Antivirus Corporate Edition因其臃肿笨拙受到广泛批评<ref name="two" />。端点防护11.0旨在解决这些批评问题<ref name="two">{{Cite news |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=LmAEAAAAMBAJ&pg=PA36 |title=Troubled Waters |last=Walsh |first=Lawrence |date=November 2007 |work=CSO Magazine |publisher=CXO Media |issue=10 |volume=6}}</ref>。Symantec Corporate Edition 10.0的磁盘占用空间接近100 MB，而端点防护仅占用21 MB<ref name="two" />。

==特色==
赛门铁克端点防护是一个安全软件套件，它包括了[[入侵检测系统|入侵检测系统]]、[[防火墙|防火墙]]和[[杀毒软件|杀毒软件]]的功能<ref name="SearchSecurity20182">{{cite web | title=Symantec Endpoint Protection and the details for buyers to know | website=SearchSecurity | date=2018-02-08 | url=http://searchsecurity.techtarget.com/feature/Antimalware-protection-products-Symantec-Endpoint-Protection | access-date=2018-02-08 | deadurl=no | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180209002822/http://searchsecurity.techtarget.com/feature/Antimalware-protection-products-Symantec-Endpoint-Protection | archivedate=2018-02-09 }}</ref>。根据《SC Magazine》的说法，端点防护还具有[[数据丢失预防软件|数据丢失预防软件]]一些典型功能<ref name="scmag" />。它通常安装在运行[[Microsoft_Windows|Windows]]、[[Linux|Linux]]或[[macOS|macOS]]的服务器上<ref name="one">{{Cite news |url=https://www.scmagazine.com/symantec-endpoint-protection-12-v121/review/6652/ |title=Symantec Endpoint Protection 12 v12.1 |last=Stephenson |first=Peter |date=2012-08-01 |work=SC Magazine |access-date=2017-04-16 |publisher=Haymarket Media Group |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170816192138/https://www.scmagazine.com/symantec-endpoint-protection-12-v121/review/6652/ |archivedate=2017-08-16 }}</ref>。截至2018年，14版是目前官方唯一支持的版本<ref name="Symantec Enterprise Technical Support 2017">{{Cite web |url=https://support.symantec.com/en_US/article.TECH154475.html |title=Released versions of Symantec Endpoint Protection |date=2017-03-16 |website=Enterprise Technical Support |publisher=[[Symantec|Symantec]] |access-date=2017-04-18 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170816193413/https://support.symantec.com/en_US/article.TECH154475.html |archivedate=2017-08-16 }}</ref>。

端点防护会扫描计算机以查找安全威胁<ref name="SearchSecurity20182"/>。它可以阻止未经批准的程序运行<ref name="SearchSecurity20182"/>，并应用防火墙策略控制网络流量<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.symantec.com/docs/HOWTO80961 |title=About the Symantec Endpoint Protection firewall |date=2016-10-28 |website=Enterprise Technical Support |publisher=[[Symantec|Symantec]]}}</ref>；尝试识别并阻止企业网络或来自[[网页浏览器|网页浏览器]]中的恶意流量<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.symantec.com/docs/HOWTO80870 |title=How intrusion prevention works |date=2016-10-28 |website=Enterprise Technical Support |publisher=[[Symantec|Symantec]]}}</ref>；使用来自用户的汇总信息识别恶意软件<ref name="scmag" />。截至2016年，赛门铁克称使用了来自175个国家的1.75亿台安装了Endpoint Security的设备的数据<ref name="scmag" />。

端点防护有一个管理控制台，IT部门可以使用它修改每个部件的安全策略<ref name="SearchSecurity20182"/>，例如从防病毒扫描中排除某些程序或文件<ref name="scmag" />。套件不直接管理移动设备，而是在其连接到计算机时将其视为外围设备，并保护计算机免受移动设备上恶意软件的侵害<ref name="scmag" />。

==漏洞==
2012年初，赛门铁克端点防护的[[源代码|源代码]]被盗取并在网上发布<ref name="Vijayan 2012">{{cite web | last=Vijayan | first=Jaikumar | title=Symantec confirms source code leak in two enterprise security products | website=Computerworld | date=2012-01-06 | url=http://www.computerworld.com/article/2501007/cybercrime-hacking/symantec-confirms-source-code-leak-in-two-enterprise-security-products.html | accessdate=2017-04-18 | deadurl=no | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170816195611/http://www.computerworld.com/article/2501007/cybercrime-hacking/symantec-confirms-source-code-leak-in-two-enterprise-security-products.html | archivedate=2017-08-16 }}</ref>。一个自称“The Lords of Dharmaraja”（宗教之王）的黑客组织宣布为此事负责，称源代码是从印度[[军事情报|军事情报]]部门中盗取的<ref name="Akhtar 2012">{{Cite web |url=https://www.cnet.com/news/that-stolen-symantec-source-code-its-for-older-enterprise-products/ |title=That stolen Symantec source code? It's for older enterprise products |last=Akhtar |first=Iyaz |date=2012-01-06 |website=[[CNET|CNET]] |publisher=[[CBS互動|CBS Interactive]] |access-date=2017-04-18 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170816193024/https://www.cnet.com/news/that-stolen-symantec-source-code-its-for-older-enterprise-products/ |archivedate=2017-08-16 }}</ref>。印度政府要求供应商提交出售给政府的计算机程序的源代码，确保它们不被用于[[间谍|间谍]]活动<ref name="Vijayan 2012"/>。2012年7月，端点防护的更新引发了兼容性问题，在运行某些第三方[[文件系统|文件系统]][[驱动程序|驱动程序]]的[[Windows_XP|Windows XP]]计算机上触发[[蓝屏死机|蓝屏死机]]<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.scmagazineuk.com/news/symantec-fixes-blue-screen-of-death-bug/article/546098/ |title=Symantec fixes 'blue screen of death' bug |last=Raywood |first=Dan |date=2012-07-16 |website=SC Magazine UK |publisher=Haymarket Media Group |access-date=2017-04-16|archivedate=2018-04-28|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180428070253/https://www.scmagazineuk.com/symantec-fixes-blue-screen-of-death-bug/article/546098/ }}</ref>。2014年，Offensive Security在对一金融服务机构进行[[渗透测试|渗透测试]]期间发现了赛门铁克端点防护的一个漏洞<ref name="Kirk 2014" />。应用程序和设备控制驱动程序中的漏洞允许已登录用户访问系统<ref name="Kirk 2014">{{Cite web |url=http://www.networkworld.com/article/2461981/security/symantec-patches-privilege-escalation-flaws-in-endpoint-protection.html |title=Symantec patches privilege escalation flaws in Endpoint Protection |last=Kirk |first=Jeremy |date=2014-08-05 |website=Network World |publisher=[[国际数据集团|IDG]] |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170816233305/http://www.networkworld.com/article/2461981/security/symantec-patches-privilege-escalation-flaws-in-endpoint-protection.html |archivedate=2017-08-16 }}</ref>。此漏洞在当年8月份得到修补<ref name="Kirk 2014" />。

==评价==
[[高德纳咨询公司|高德纳公司]]表示，赛门铁克端点防护14是当下最全面的端点安全产品之一，经常在独立测试中取得优秀成绩<ref name="gartner" />。但是，客户会对产品开发和公司发展中“几乎不停的变化”感到“疲劳”<ref name="gartner" />。《SC Magazine》称端点防护14是“此类工具中最全面的……具有极佳的程序安装和文档说明”<ref name="scmag">{{Cite news |url=https://www.scmagazine.com/symantec-endpoint-protection-14/review/7116/ |title=Symantec Endpoint Protection 14 |last=Stephenson |first=Peter |date=2016-08-22 |work=SC Magazine |access-date=2017-04-20 |publisher=Haymarket Media Group |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170602125811/https://www.scmagazine.com/symantec-endpoint-protection-14/review/7116/ |archivedate=2017-06-02 }}</ref>。端点防护“安装和管理过程简单易懂”，但支持费用“有点高”<ref name="scmag" />。

Forrester报道称，12.1版是市场上最完整的终端安全软件产品，但软件不同的IT安全功能未得到很好地整合<ref name="forrester">{{Cite web |url=https://www.forrester.com/report/The+Forrester+Wave+Endpoint+Security+Suites+Q4+2016/-/E-RES113145 |title=The Forrester Wave: Endpoint Security Suites, Q4 2016 |last=Sherman |first=Chris |last2=McClean |first2=Christopher |date=2016-10-19 |registration=yes |last3=Schiano |first3=Salvatore |last4=Dostie |first4=Peggy |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161022024840/https://www.forrester.com/report/The+Forrester+Wave+Endpoint+Security+Suites+Q4+2016/-/E-RES113145 |archivedate=2016-10-22 }}</ref>。报告推测整合问题将在14版中解决<ref name="forrester" />。《Network World》根据NSS Labs测试的数据将赛门铁克端点防护列为端点安全产品的第六名<ref name="Smith 2017">{{Cite web |url=http://www.networkworld.com/article/3170114/security/nss-labs-rated-13-advanced-endpoint-security-products-flagged-2-with-caution-rating.html |title=NSS Labs rated 13 advanced endpoint security products, flagged 2 with caution rating |date=2017-02-15 |website=Network World |publisher=[[国际数据集团|IDG]] |access-date=2017-04-18 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170517100153/http://www.networkworld.com/article/3170114/security/nss-labs-rated-13-advanced-endpoint-security-products-flagged-2-with-caution-rating.html |archivedate=2017-05-17 }}</ref>。
