'''自动酿酒综合征'''（[[英文|英文]]：'''Auto-brewery syndrome'''），也被称为'''肠道发酵综合征'''（英文：'''gut fermentation syndrome'''），是一种在[[消化系统|消化系统]]内通过[[内源|内源性]][[发酵|发酵]]产得[[酒精|酒精]]使人[[酒精中毒|致醉]]的一种罕见的疾病。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.npr.org/blogs/thesalt/2013/09/17/223345977/auto-brewery-syndrome-apparently-you-can-make-beer-in-your-gut|title=Auto-Brewery Syndrome: Apparently, You Can Make Beer In Your Gut|author=Michaeleen Doucleff|publisher=NPR|date=September 17, 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|title=A Case Study of Gut Fermentation Syndrome (Auto-Brewery) with Saccharomyces cerevisiae as the Causative Organism|journal=International Journal of Clinical Medicine|volume=2013|date=2013-07-04|doi=10.4236/ijcm.2013.47054|issn=2158-2882|language=en|url=http://www.scirp.org/journal/PaperInformation.aspx?PaperID=33912|accessdate=2018-04-02|author=Barbara Cordell, Justin McCarthy}}</ref>在许多的案例中记载有这种疾病。<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0015736884723255|pages=461–471|title=Intragastrointestinal Alcohol Fermentation Syndrome: Report of Two Cases and Review of the Literature|journal=Journal of the Forensic Science Society|volume=24|issue=5|accessdate=2018-04-02|doi=10.1016/s0015-7368(84)72325-5|author=H. Kaji, Y. Asanuma, O. Yahara, H. Shibue, M. Hisamura, N. Saito, Y. Kawakami, M. Murao}}</ref>但大多数医生公然否认这种疾病，认为这种疾病是[[伪科学|伪科学]]，因为[[微生物|微生物]]并不适合在肠道中发酵分解食物产生酒精。但据由美国传染病学会在2009年发布的《临床实践指南》中指出，念球菌是最常见的感染人类的[[真菌|真菌]]，可以涉及到任何[[器官|器官]]。当人类免疫系统低下时，很多原本无害的细菌便会打破这种免疫稳态，甚至于产生全身各处[[感染|感染]]。<ref name=natural />
 	
这种内源性发酵的疾病已被司机用来作为[[酒驾|酒驾]]的抗辩的理由。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/10976182 |title=Endogenous ethanol 'auto-brewery syndrome' as a drunk-driving defence challenge |author=Logan BK, Jones AW |date=2000 Jul |work=Medicine, science, and the law |publisher= |accessdate=2013-02-27}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.straightdope.com/columns/read/2677/designated-drunk |title=Designated drunk: Can you get intoxicated without actually drinking alcohol? |author=Cecil Adams |date=October 20, 2006 |work=The Straight Dope |publisher= |accessdate=2013-02-27}}</ref>

据调查它不可能是导致[[嬰兒猝死症|嬰兒猝死症]]的元凶。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://link.springer.com/article/10.1007%2FBF01873798?LI=true |title=Endogenous alcohol production by intestinal fermentation in sudden infant death |author=P. Geertinger MD, J. Bodenhoff, K. Helweg-Larsen, A. Lund |date=1982-09-01 |work=Zeitschrift für Rechtsmedizin |publisher=Springer-Verlag |accessdate=2013-02-27}}</ref>

== 病因 ==
该疾病可能是由于胃肠道中驻留了过多的[[酵母菌|酵母菌]]或者[[念珠菌|白色念珠菌]]所导致产生并在血液中吸收了过高浓度的酒精（大于80毫克/分升）。<ref name=nlm>{{cite web |url=http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/10976182|title=Endogenous ethanol 'auto-brewery syndrome' as a drunk-driving defence challenge. |author= |date=2000-07 |work=pubmed |publisher= |accessdate=2013-09-22}}</ref>但由于疾病缺乏普遍性，故无法完全下结论。

== 症状 ==
该疾病由于胃肠道中的酵母菌等发酵所产生的酒精浓度过高会使人产生[[酒精中毒|醉酒]]状态。但病人在此前并未曾有饮酒史。且[[肝脏|肝脏]]要对生成的酒精进行[[代谢|代谢]]，若这种情况长期维持下去，患者很容易肝功能衰竭。<ref name=nlm />

== 治疗 ==
目前并无很好的治疗方案，饮食中尽量少食用单糖类物质（如[[淀粉|淀粉]]及[[碳水化合物|碳水化合物]]）可以减少此病的体征。<ref name=natural>{{cite web |url=http://www.naturalnews.com/026864_candida_infection_symptoms.html|title=Not an Intestinal Candida Infection: Call it Gut Fermentation Syndrome （不是肠道念珠菌感染：致肠道发酵综合症）|author= |date=2009-08-17 |work=NaturalNews|publisher= |accessdate=2013-09-22}}</ref>但由于疾病缺乏普遍性，故无法完全下结论。
