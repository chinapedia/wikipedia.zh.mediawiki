'''锰(V)酸钾'''（K<sub>3</sub>MnO<sub>4</sub>）是一种淡蓝色的盐，它是很少见的Mn(V)的含氧酸盐。

== 制备 ==
最早制得的锰(V)酸盐是Na<sub>3</sub>MnO<sub>4</sub>·10H<sub>2</sub>O，但后来发现它实际上是Na<sub>3</sub>MnO<sub>4</sub>·0.25NaOH·10H<sub>2</sub>O。<ref name=wc>{{cite book|title=《无机化学丛书》第九卷：锰分族、铁系、铂系|pages=P46-47|isbn=7-03-002238-6|location=北京|publisher=科学出版社}}</ref>

*用过量的[[亚硫酸钾|亚硫酸钾]]还原[[高锰酸钾|高锰酸钾]]：<ref name="C&W">{{Cotton&Wilkinson4th|page=746}}.</ref><ref name="G&E">{{Greenwood&Earnshaw1st|pages=1221–22}}.</ref>
::MnO{{su|b=4|p=−}} + SO{{su|b=3|p=2−}} + H<sub>2</sub>O → MnO{{su|b=4|p=3−}} + SO{{su|b=4|p=2−}} + {{nowrap|2 H<sup>+</sup>}}
*[[锰酸钾|锰酸钾]]与[[过氧化氢|过氧化氢]]在10mol/L的[[氢氧化钾|氢氧化钾]]溶液中反应：<ref>{{citation | title = Oxidation of hydrocarbons. 18. Mechanism of the reaction between permanganate and carbon-carbon double bonds | first1 = Donald G. | last1 = Lee | first2 = Tao | last2 = Chen | journal = J. Am. Chem. ::{{nowrap|2 MnO{{su|b=4|p=2−}}}} + H<sub>2</sub>O<sub>2</sub> + {{nowrap|2 OH<sup>−</sup>}} → {{nowrap|2 MnO{{su|b=4|p=3−}}}} + O<sub>2</sub> + {{nowrap|2 H<sub>2</sub>O}}
*在3-10mol/L的氢氧化钾溶液中用[[扁桃酸|扁桃酸]]还原锰酸钾：<ref name="Mandelate"/>
::{{nowrap|2 MnO{{su|b=4|p=2−}}}} + C{{su|b=8}}H{{su|b=7}}O{{su|b=3|p=−}} + {{nowrap|2 OH<sup>−</sup>}} → {{nowrap|2 MnO{{su|b=4|p=3−}}}} + C{{su|b=8}}H{{su|b=5}}O{{su|b=3|p=−}} + {{nowrap|2 H<sub>2</sub>O}}

== 性质 ==
锰(V)酸盐在无水和无二氧化碳的条件下能稳定存在。在水溶液中因歧化而不稳定，尤其是在碱性溶液中。<ref name="C&W"/><ref name="G&E"/>估计pH=14的水溶液中它的[[标准电极电势|标准电极电势]]如下所示：<ref>{{RubberBible62nd|page=D-134}}.</ref><ref>{{citation | title = Manganese – compounds – standard reduction potentials | url = http://www.webelements.com/manganese/compounds.html | publisher = WebElements | accessdate = 2010-06-26}}.</ref><ref name="Epot">{{citation | title = Rate of the MnO<sub>4</sub><sup>−</sup>/MnO<sub>4</sub><sup>2−</sup> and MnO<sub>4</sub><sup>2−</sup>/MnO<sub>4</sub><sup>3−</sup> electrode reactions in alkaline solutions at solid electrodes | first1 = K. | last1 = Sekula-Brzezińska | first2 = P.
