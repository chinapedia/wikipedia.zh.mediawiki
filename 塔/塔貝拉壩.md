'''塔貝拉壩'''（[[英語|英語]]：Tarbela Dam；{{lang-ur|'''تربیلا بند'''}}），是[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]]境內[[印度河|印度河]]上的一座[[水壩|水壩]]，也是世界最大的土石壩及第二大的結構體。<ref name="wcd-study">{{cite report|title=Tarbela Dam and related aspects of the Indus River Basin, Pakistan |url=http://www.dams.org/docs/kbase/studies/cspkmain.pdf |format=PDF |author=Asianics Agro-Dev. International (Pvt) Ltd. |year=2000 |publisher=World Commission on Dams |location=Cape Town |accessdate=2010-10-28 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100613114429/http://dams.org/docs/kbase/studies/cspkmain.pdf |archivedate=2010-06-13 }}</ref><ref name="eo-dam">{{cite web|url=http://earth.jsc.nasa.gov/EarthObservatory/Tarbela_Dam,_Pakistan.htm |title=Tarbela Dam, Pakistan |work=Earth Observatory |publisher=National Aeronautics and Space Administration |author=Earth Sciences Web Team |accessdate=2010-10-28 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100715214717/http://earth.jsc.nasa.gov/EarthObservatory/Tarbela_Dam%2C_Pakistan.htm |archivedate=2010-07-15 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.pakistanpaedia.com/mega/tarbela_dam.html|title=Tarbela Dam|publisher=PakistanPaedia|date=2006-08-07|accessdate=2012-01-26}}</ref> 

它座落於[[開伯爾－普赫圖赫瓦省|開伯爾－普赫圖赫瓦省]][[哈利普爾縣|哈利普爾縣]]，約在[[伊斯蘭瑪巴德|伊斯蘭瑪巴德]]西北邊{{convert|50|km|mi}}處。壩體高於河床約{{convert|485|ft|m}}，形成塔貝拉水庫，面積約{{convert|250|km2|sqmi|adj=on}}。

該壩於1974年興建完成，是設計用來儲存印度河水，以便進行[[灌溉|灌溉]]、{{link-en|洪水調節|flood control}}，以及[[水力發電|發電]]。<ref name="Rodney White">{{cite book|title=Evacuation of Sediments from Reservoirs|publisher=Thomas Telford Publishing|isbn=978-0727729538|pages=163–169|url=http://books.google.com.pk/books?id=xmPjUnwJYQYC&pg=PA163|author=Rodney White|accessdate=4 August 2013|date=1 January 2001}}</ref>
