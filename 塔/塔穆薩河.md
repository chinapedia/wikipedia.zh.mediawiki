'''塔穆薩河'''（Tamsa River），是位於[[印度|印度]][[中央邦|中央邦]]及[[北方邦|北方邦]]的一條河流，為[[恆河|恆河]]右岸支流。幹流河長{{convert|264|km}}，流域面積{{convert|16860|km2}}。<ref>{{Cite book|url =http://books.google.co.in/books?id=Zj9Pbd0-eesC&pg=PA71&lpg=PA71&dq=Purwa+Falls+Rewa+Tourism&source=bl&ots=QEntYsLbzK&sig=qoDu--d-LeGSxCXKmBnqJTl1GSk&hl=en&ei=8b02TLfoDoWyrAfEjfmyAg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=5&ved=0CCQQ6AEwBA#v=onepage&q&f=false | title = India’s Water Wealth | work= p. 71, The Tons | first=  | last= K.L.Rao  |publisher = Google books | accessdate = 2010-07-10 }}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|url =http://books.google.co.in/books?id=_ce8FpJzR-4C&pg=PA24&lpg=PA24&dq=Tamsa+River+Tons&source=bl&ots=yhdamjpn3Z&sig=wM3KmeT6kICDFmsjyXInXO9ragE&hl=en&ei=eEU4TOHSDtCFrQf30-m2CA&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=7&ved=0CCsQ6AEwBg#v=onepage&q=Tamsa%20River%20Tons&f=false | title =Uttar Pradesh General Knowledge | work= p. 24 | first=  | last= Upkar Prakashan Editorial Board  |publisher = Google books | accessdate = 2010-07-10 }}</ref>

塔穆薩河發源於{{link-en|凱穆爾山脈|Kaimur Range}}，幹流流經肥沃的中央邦[[薩地納縣|薩地納縣]]及[[雷瓦縣|雷瓦縣]]。在普瓦高原的邊緣，塔穆薩河及其支流形成許多瀑布。在塔穆薩河於中央邦與北方邦交界處匯集右岸支流貝倫河後，流入北方邦的[[安拉阿巴德縣|安拉阿巴德縣]]，最終於錫爾薩（Sirsa）附近注入恆河。
