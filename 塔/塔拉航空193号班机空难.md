'''塔拉航空193号班机'''是一班由[[尼泊尔|尼泊尔]]塔拉航空运营，从尼泊尔[[博克拉|博克拉]]{{Link-en|博克拉機場|Pokhara Airport}}飞往佐莫索姆[[佐莫索姆機場|佐莫索姆機場]]的定期国内航班。2016年2月24日，此次航班在起飞后不久失踪，之后被证实坠毁于[[道拉吉里专区|道拉吉里专区]]莫雅格迪区达纳村附近的山林中。<ref name="TKP">{{cite news|title=Missing Tara Air plane with 23 onboard could have crashed in Myagdi's Rupse|url=http://kathmandupost.ekantipur.com/news/2016-02-24/a-tara-air-plane-en-route-to-jomsom-from-pokhara-missing.html|accessdate=2016-02-24|work=The Kathmandu Post|date=2016-02-24}}</ref><ref name="Hradecky">{{cite news|last1=Hradecky|first1=Simon|title=Accident: Tara DHC6 near Pokhara on Feb 24th 2016, aircraft missing enroute|url=http://avherald.com/h?article=49468c0e&opt=0|accessdate=2016-02-24|work=Aviation Herald|date=2016-02-24}}</ref><ref name="Guardian">{{cite news|title=Plane crash feared in Nepal as flight carrying 21 goes missing in mountains|url=http://www.theguardian.com/world/2016/feb/24/plane-crash-nepal-missing-mountains-pokhara-jomsom|accessdate=2016-02-24|work=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]|date=2016-02-24}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.independent.ie/world-news/asia-pacific/missing-nepal-tara-air-passenger-plane-found-crashed-in-jungle-amid-fears-no-one-survived-34481080.html|title=Missing Nepal Tara Air passenger plane 'found crashed in jungle' amid fears no one survived|date=2016-02-24}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|title=尼泊尔失联客机证实坠毁：残骸被发现 遗体四散|author=中国新闻网|url=http://news.qq.com/a/20160224/047608.htm|work=腾讯网|date=2016-02-24}}</ref>

==涉事飞机==
涉事班机是一架2012年由加拿大Viking Air获得许可证制造的[[德哈维兰|德哈维兰加拿大]][[DHC-6_400雙水獺飛機|DHC-6 400雙水獺飛機]]，原注册号为C-GUVT<ref name="Airframes">{{cite web|title=Aircraft Registration Database Lookup|url=http://www.airframes.org/reg/cguvt|publisher=Airframes.org|date=2016-02-24}}</ref>
。2015年9月交付给尼泊尔塔拉航空，注册号变更为9N-AHH<ref>{{cite web |title=ASN Aircraft accident Viking Air DHC-6 Twin Otter 400 9N-AHH Dana, Myagdi district |url=https://aviation-safety.net/database/record.php?id=20160224-0 |publisher=Aviation Safety Network |access-date=2016-02-24 |first=Harro |last=Ranter}}</ref>。

此次航班于当地时间24日7:50（[[UTC+05:45|UTC+05:45]]）从博克拉機場出发，原计划飞行18分钟抵达佐莫索姆，但在起飞后10分钟与地面失去联絡。尼泊尔民航部长于当天下午向媒体确认这架飞机坠毁。<ref name="BBC20160224">{{cite news|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-asia-35647929 |title=Nepal small passenger plane wreckage found |date=2016-02-24|accessdate=2016-02-24|publisher=BBC News}}</ref>
