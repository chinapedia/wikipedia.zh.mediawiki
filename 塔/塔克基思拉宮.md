'''塔克基思拉宮'''（طاق كسرى / تاق کسرا / ایوان مدائن）是一座[[波斯|波斯]][[薩珊王朝|薩珊王朝]]建築，位於[[泰西封|泰西封]][[薩爾曼公園|薩爾曼公園]]。這是古城泰西封的唯一殘存的建築，其拱狀結構是世界最大磚造拱形建築<ref>{{cite book |title=Taq-I-Kasra (Arch of Ctesiphon) |last= |first= |editor=, Chris |year=2008 |publisher=worldheritagesite |isbn= |pages=}}</ref>。

==歷史==
塔克基思拉宮建造的確切時間仍未確定，可能開始在公元540年[[霍斯勞一世|霍斯勞一世]]侵略[[拜占庭|拜占庭]]後<ref>Because of the vicissitudes of historical transmission, ''eventually no less than four Sasanian rulers were quoted as its builders: Shapur I (241–273), Shapur II (310–379), Khosrau I Anushirvan (531–579) and Khosrau II Parvez (590–628).'' {{Cite journal|author=Kurz, Otto|year=1941|title=The Date of the Ṭāq i Kisrā|journal=[[The_Journal_of_the_Royal_Asiatic_Society_of_Great_Britain_and_Ireland|The Journal of the Royal Asiatic Society of Great Britain and Ireland]] | series =  (New Series)|volume=73|number=1|pages=37–41|url= http://www.jstor.org/stable/25221709}}</ref><ref name=Julian185>{{cite book |title=The Seventy Wonders of the Ancient world The Great Monuments and How they were Built |last=Reade |first=Dr Julian |editor=Scarre, Chris |year=1999 |publisher=Thames & Hudson |isbn=0-500-05096-1 |pages=185–186}}</ref>。拱形大廳高約37米，寬26米，長50米，曾經是世界最大單拱建築。

塔克基思拉宮在公元637年遭到阿拉伯人佔領，被當作[[清真寺|清真寺]]使用<ref name=Julian185 />。

在1909年，嚴重水災破壞大部分的塔克基思拉宮。
