'''塔什干－薩馬爾罕高速鐵路'''（{{lang-uz|''AFROSIYOB''}}）是一段長344公里的快速鐵路，其連接烏茲別克兩大城：首都[[塔什干|塔什干]]與古都[[薩馬爾罕|薩馬爾罕]]。這條鐵路線沿線經過四個州，分別是[[塔什干州|塔什干州]]、[[錫爾河州|錫爾河州]]、[[吉扎克州|吉扎克州]]以及[[撒馬爾罕州|撒馬爾罕州]]。此鐵路線以AFROSIYOB命名，並且終年無休。

== 營運現狀 ==
[[烏茲別克鐵路|烏茲別克鐵路管理部]]以3800萬歐元向[[西班牙|西班牙]][[塔爾高_(公司)|塔爾高車輛公司]]訂購高速鐵路車輛。<ref name="can"/><ref name="rg">{{cite web | url=http://www.railwaygazette.com/index.php?id=44&no_cache=1&tx_ttnews%5Btt_news%5D=13680&cHash=dca8f029fa | title=Talgo 250 arrives in Toshkent | publisher=Railway Gazette International | date=July 26, 2011 | accessdate=August 1, 2011 | archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/60cmUfRH8?url=http://www.railwaygazette.com/index.php?id=44 | archivedate=2011-08-02 | dead-url=no }}</ref>首列訂購列車於2011年7月22日運抵烏國首都[[塔什干|塔什干]]。<ref name="rg"/><ref>[http://www.uzdaily.com/articles-id-15111.htm First high-speed Talgo electricity train arrives in Tashkent, 24 July 2011 ]</ref>每列列車包含：兩節動力機電車輛以及八節車廂，每節車廂可以載運257名乘客。<ref name="rg"/>全線於2011年8月26日開始上線試運轉。<ref>[http://www.uzdaily.com/articles-id-15511.htm Uzdaily: First high-speed electricity train carries out first trip from Samarkand and Tashkent, 27 August 2011 ]</ref><ref>[http://www.travelpulse.com/uzbekistan-launches-high-speed-tashkentsmarkand-rail-line.html Uzbekistan Launches High Speed Tashkent-Smarkand Rail Line, September 08 2011]</ref>

2011年10月8日，全線正式上線服務。初期營運每周兩個來回於[[塔什干|塔什干]]與[[薩馬爾罕|薩馬爾罕]]。<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.uzrailway.uz/rus.html?id=376 |title=ГАЖК «Ўзбекистон темир йўллари», ОАО «Узжелдоропасс» оъявляет, что началась продажа проездных документов на высокоскоростной поезд «Afrosiyob» |access-date=2016-01-21 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111008232743/http://uzrailway.uz/rus.html?id=376 |archive-date=2011-10-08 |dead-url=yes }}</ref><ref>[http://www.uzdaily.com/articles-id-15984.htm High-speed Afrosiyob train starts to run between Tashkent and Samarkand, 6 October 2011]</ref>事實上，在初期運行的期間，路程時間是多於預期的，總共花費兩個半小時。<ref name=ab>{{Cite web |url=http://www.ut.uz/eng/business/25_hours_to_samarqand.mgr |title=Uzbekistan Today: 2.5 Hours to Samarqand |access-date=2016-01-21 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120421131607/http://www.ut.uz/eng/business/25_hours_to_samarqand.mgr |archive-date=2012-04-21 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>2012年2月，再有更多列車上線服務後，更改為每日一個來回班次，<ref>[http://uzdaily.com/articles-id-17417.htm High-speed train Afrosiyob to run seven times a week]. UzDaily.com. 13 February 2012</ref>總路程時間降低為兩小時又八分鐘。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.gazeta.uz/2013/02/12/afrosiyob/|title= Поездка в Самарканд на поезде Afrosiyob теперь занимает два часа |publisher=Gazeta.uz |language=ru |accessdate= |date=2013-02-12}}</ref>
