'''《街報》'''({{lang-en|Street News}})，是在美國[[紐約市|紐約市]]，由[[露宿者|露宿者]]販售的報紙。它創始於1989年，為最早並至今持續發行的現代街頭報紙，同時也是美國街頭報紙運動的起源<ref name="review">{{Template:Cite web|url=http://www.rrj.ca/issue/2002/summer/373/|title=Small Papers, Big Issues|accessdate=12 February 2009|last=Brown|first=Ann M.|year=2002|work=[[Ryerson_Review_of_Journalism|Ryerson Review of Journalism]]|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/20070911030448/http://www.rrj.ca:80/issue/2002/summer/373/|archivedate=September 11, 2007|deadurl=yes}}</ref><ref name="Levinson">{{Template:Cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=q-PgHH8TJi8C&pg=RA1-PA539|title=Encyclopedia of Homelessness|last=Heinz|first=Teresa L.|last2=Levinson|first2=David|publisher=SAGE|accessdate=2009-02-12|year=2004|isbn=0-7619-2751-4|edition=illustrated|pages=539}}</ref><ref>Although [[波特蘭_(俄勒岡州)|Portland]], [[俄勒冈州|Oregon]]'s ''Homeless Times'' was founded before it, ''Street News'' is the earliest-published paper that is still active (North American Street Newspaper Association 2008).  </ref>。 其目標為提供紐約許多[[露宿者|露宿者]]及失業者一個自給自足的方式。<ref name="Howley">{{Template:Cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=MslR_FE5SI4C&pg=PA65|title=Community Media: People, Places, and Communication Technologies|last=Howley|first=Kevin|publisher=Cambridge University Press|accessdate=2009-03-11|year=2005|isbn=0-521-79228-2|pages=65–66}}</ref> 《街報》的誕生迅速啟發了更多其他街頭報紙的創立，包括[[芝加哥|芝加哥]]的{{link-en|StreetWise|StreetWise}}<ref name="green">{{Template:Cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=Vqtgo0Zi0jEC&pg=PA34|title=Print Culture in a Diverse America|last=Green|first=Norma Fay|publisher=University of Illinois Press|accessdate=2009-03-11|others=eds. James Philip Danky, Wayne A. Wiegand|year=1998|isbn=0-252-06699-5|pages=34–49|chapter=Chicago's ''StreetWise'' at the Crossroads: A Case Study of a Newspaper to Empower the Homeless in the 1990s}}</ref> 及[[英國|英國]]的''[[大誌雜誌|The Big Issue]]'';<ref>{{Template:Cite web|url=http://www.bigissue.com/History_34.php|title=The Big Issue History|accessdate=13 January 2009|publisher=[[The_Big_Issue|The Big Issue]]}}</ref>  也因此《街報》被稱為街頭報紙運動的"先驅"。<ref name="Sthlm">{{Template:Cite web|url=http://www.nyaorganisationer.soch.lu.se/SituationSthlm.html|title=The transnational street paper movement|accessdate=12 February 2009|last=Magnusson|first=Jan A|work=[[Situation_Sthlm|Situation Sthlm]]}}</ref> 《街報》及[[大誌雜誌|大誌雜誌]]也成為了全球街頭報紙的原型。<ref name="Levinson">{{Template:Cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=q-PgHH8TJi8C&pg=RA1-PA539|title=Encyclopedia of Homelessness|last=Heinz|first=Teresa L.|last2=Levinson|first2=David|publisher=SAGE|accessdate=2009-02-12|year=2004|isbn=0-7619-2751-4|edition=illustrated|pages=539}}</ref>

自2002年之後，《街報》由John Levi "Indio" Washington Jr.擔任編輯，每年印行3,000份，共有6期，由15人進行販售，每位販售者可從每本1.25美元的售價中取得75分美元的收入<ref name="Fried">{{Template:Cite news|url=http://query.nytimes.com/gst/fullpage.html?res=9A02E0D81E3BF93BA35751C1A9649C8B63|title=Following Up|last=Fried|first=Joseph P.|date=2002-12-08|work=The New York Times|accessdate=2009-03-13|pages=section 1 page 57 of the New York edition}}</ref><ref name="Rosenbaum">{{Template:Cite news|url=http://www.time.com/time/europe/magazine/2003/0210/homeless/paper.html|title=The News from the Street|last=Rosenbaum|first=Andrew|date=2003-02-10|work=Time Magazine|accessdate=2009-03-13}}</ref>。
