'''民主党'''是一个[[利比亚|利比亚]]政党，2011年7月14日由[[利比亚自由与民主运动|利比亚自由与民主运动]]组建成立，以追求后[[卡扎菲|卡扎菲]]时代的民主道路<ref>{{cite web
| url         = http://online.wsj.com/article/SB10001424053111903341404576479730765330232.html?mod=WSJEUROPE_hpp_sections_opinion
| title       = Libya's Unraveling Opposition
| publisher   = The Wall Street Journal
| author      = Ranj Alaaldin
| date        = 2011年7月31日
}}</ref>。

==政治理念==

该党追求[[世俗主义|世俗主义]]与[[自由主义|自由主义]]价值观。该组织认为[[利比亚全国委员会|全国委员会]]是利比亚过渡时期的必要组织，而非正统的权力机构<ref>{{cite web
| url         = http://www.defenceviewpoints.co.uk/articles-and-analysis/gaddafi-is-not-the-only-potential-dictator-in-libya
| title       = Gaddafi is not the only potential dictator in Libya.
| author      = Ahmed Shebani
| date        = 18 March 2011
}}</ref>。认为民主过渡需要[[联合国|联合国]]的监督，并维护国家的独立性<ref>{{cite web
| url         = http://www.defenceviewpoints.co.uk/articles-and-analysis/calls-for-return-of-adrian-pelt-commission-for-libya
| title       = Calls for return of Adrian Pelt commission for Libya.
| author      = Ahmed Shebani
| date        = 15 March 2011
}}</ref>。

该党支持[[犹太复国运动|犹太复国运动]]，承认[[以色列|以色列]]的合法地位<ref>"The Democratic Party adheres to the policy of recognizing the state of Israel according to the Arab Peace Initiative that was presented to The Beirut Arab Summit by king Abdullah of Saudi Arabia and adopted by it. This policy of recognizing Israel follows the Turkish and Egyptian examples. The DP also adopts the two states solution which upholds the legitimate rights of the Palestinians" see[http://thedemocraticpartylibya.org] {{Wayback|url=http://thedemocraticpartylibya.org/ |date=20111130013250 }}</ref>。也支持利比亚[[犹太人|犹太人]]的合法权利。支持[[政教分离|政教分离]]、信仰自由，并尊重所有宗教<ref>{{cite web
 |url         = http://thedemocraticpartylibya.org/#
 |title       = Press release on the establishment of the Democratic Party

 |archiveurl  = https://web.archive.org/web/20111130013250/http://thedemocraticpartylibya.org/
 |archivedate = 2011年11月30日
 |df          = 
}}</ref>，以此来反对极端宗教主义和[[基地组织|基地组织]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://libyanfreedomdemocracycampaign.com/#/defeat-al-qaida/4551090578 |title=How to defeat Al-Qaida in Libya |work=Libyan Freedom and Democracy Campaign Web Site |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110616124903/http://libyanfreedomdemocracycampaign.com/ |archivedate=2011-06-16 }}</ref>。

该党目前与一些[[智库|智库]]密切合作，比如[[club_of_Madrid|club of Madrid]], [[Gorbachev_Foundation|Gorbachev Foundation]] 和 [[The_Westminster_Foundation_for_Democracy|The Westminster Foundation for Democracy]]，以实现自己的目标。
