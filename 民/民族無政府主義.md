'''民族无政府主义'''（{{lang-en|'''National-Anarchism'''}}）是一种激进的[[反资本主义|反资本主义]]、反[[国家主义|国家主义]]的[[右翼|右翼]][[意识形态|意识形态]]，强调民族[[新部落主义|新部落主义]]。这种意识形态预言资本主义制度的崩溃，并试图建立[[自治|自治]][[社区|社区]]。<ref name="Macklin 2005">{{Cite journal | last = Macklin | first = Graham D. | authorlink =  | title = Co-opting the counter culture: Troy Southgate and the National Revolutionary Faction | journal = Patterns of Prejudice | volume = 39 | issue = 3 | pages = 301–326 | date = September 2005 | url = http://www.new-right.org/?p=53 | doi = 10.1080/00313220500198292 | accessdate = 2009-02-17 | ref = CITEREFMacklin2005 | author =  | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20090821160926/http://www.new-right.org/?p=53 | archive-date = 2009-08-21 | dead-url = yes }}</ref>

[[File:N-A_star.gif|thumb]]

== 词语释意 ==
民族无政府主义这个词可以追溯到1920年代。<ref name="Macklin 2005"/> 但在1990年代，该词被重新定义并得到流行，用于描绘已經超越了「左」和「右」的[[第三位置主义|第三位置主义]]与[[无政府主义|无政府主义]]的复合思维<ref name="nur21">Troy Southgate; interviewed in [[#CITEREFFyodorov2006|''Kinovar'' magazine, February 2006]].</ref>。
