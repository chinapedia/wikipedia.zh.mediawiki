'''雅各布·阿佩爾'''（{{lang-en|Jacob M. Appel}}，{{bd|1973年|2月21日}}）是一位[[美國|美國]][[作者|作家]]、[[生物倫理學|生物倫理學]]家、[[醫生|醫生]]、[[律師|律師]]和社會評論家。<ref>Nagamatsu, Sequoia "A Few Words with the Ubiquitous Jacob M. Appel"  ''Prince Mincer'' Journal http://primemincer.com/ confirmed 26 April 2013</ref> 生於[[布朗克斯|布朗克斯]]，並且在[[斯卡斯代爾|斯卡斯代爾]]成長。<ref>Rosenblum, Constance.  ''Boulevard of Dreams'', New York University Press, 2009, P. 174</ref>阿佩爾最為人所知的是他的[[短篇小說|短篇小說]]，他的工作是[[劇作家|劇作家]]，寫作領域包括複製的道德、[[器官捐贈|器官捐贈]]、神經科學道德和[[安樂死|安樂死]]。<ref>Nagamatsu, Sequoia "A Few Words with the Ubiquitous Jacob M. Appel"  ''Prince Mincer'' Journal http://primemincer.com/ confirmed 26 April 2013</ref>阿佩爾的小說，''The Man Who Wouldn't Stand Up''，贏得2012年丹地國際書獎。<ref>[http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-scotland-tayside-central-20088112 Dundee International Book Prize won by Jacob M Appel], BBC, 25 October 2012</ref><ref>[http://www.scotsman.com/lifestyle/books/features/book-review-the-man-who-wouldn-t-stand-up-jacob-appel-1-2643009 Book review: The Man Who Wouldn’t Stand Up, Jacob Appel], ''The Scotsman'', Lifestyle, 17 Nov 2012</ref><ref>Jacob M Appel named as Dundee International Book Prize winner, The Courier, 9 January 2013</ref>
