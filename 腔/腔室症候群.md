'''腔室症候群'''是對肢體、生命產生威脅的一種狀況，是由於身體某部位神經、血管及肌肉在一個封閉的的空間（[[腔室|腔室]]）中受到壓迫<ref>http://emedicine.medscape.com/article/828456-overview</ref>。起因於腔室中升高的壓力，造成血管灌流不足，導致組織缺氧而壞死。腔室症候群最常發生在前臂及小腿，<ref>Medline Plus. URL: http://www.nlm.nih.gov/medlineplus/ency/article/001224.htm Accessed 23 December 2009</ref> 並可分成急性、亞急性及慢性腔室症候群。根據蘭金（Rankin, 1981）的定義，腔室症候群的起因是一個[[密閉空間|密閉空間]]中的壓力，使得該空間的循環及組織功能的受到阻礙。

==原因==
因為組成腔室的[[結締組織|結締組織]]延展性降低使得流經該腔室的血流量減少，或是由於腔室中肌肉的腫脹造成腔室中壓力的劇烈升高。常造成腔室症候群的原因包括[[脛骨|脛骨]]或前臂骨折，由於組織的傷害、出血、血管穿刺、靜脈藥物注射，長時間固定、肢體的壓迫，粉碎性的傷害及燒燙傷造成的[[再灌注性損傷|再灌注性損傷]]。<ref name="pmid18186372">{{cite journal |author=Konstantakos EK, Dalstrom DJ, Nelles ME, Laughlin RT, Prayson MJ |title=Diagnosis and management of extremity compartment syndromes: an orthopaedic perspective |journal=Am Surg |volume=73 |issue=12 |pages=1199–209 |pmid= 18186372|doi= |url= |accessdate= |date=December 2007}}</ref><ref name="pmid18382196">{{cite journal |author=Maerz L, Kaplan LJ |title=Abdominal compartment syndrome |journal=Crit. Care Med. |volume=36 |issue=4 Suppl |pages=S212–5 |pmid= 18382196|doi=10.1097/CCM.0b013e318168e333 |url=http://meta.wkhealth.com/pt/pt-core/template-journal/lwwgateway/media/landingpage.htm?doi=10.1097/CCM.0b013e318168e333 |accessdate=|date=April 2008}}</ref> 另一個可能的原因是由於服用[[肌氨酸|肌氨酸]]，有研究指出服用肌氨酸的病史與腔室症候群有一定關係。<ref name="pmid12937429">{{cite journal |author=Potteiger JA, Carper MJ, Randall JC, Magee LJ, Jacobsen DJ, Hulver MW |title=Changes in Lower Leg Anterior Compartment Pressure Before, During, and After Creatine Supplementation |journal=J Athl Train |volume=37 |issue=2 |pages=157–163 |pmid=12937429 |pmc=164339 |doi= |url=http://www.nata.org/jat/readers/archives/36.1/i1062-6050-036-01-0085.pdf |accessdate= |format=PDF |date=June 2002 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081217045533/http://www.nata.org/jat/readers/archives/36.1/i1062-6050-036-01-0085.pdf |archivedate=2008-12-17 }}</ref><ref name="pmid16619092">{{cite journal |author=Hile AM, Anderson JM, Fiala KA, Stevenson JH, Casa DJ, Maresh CM |title=Creatine supplementation and anterior compartment pressure during exercise in the heat in dehydrated men |journal=J Athl Train |volume=41 |issue=1 |pages=30–5 |year=2006 |pmid= 16619092|pmc= 1421498|doi= |url= |accessdate= }}</ref>
