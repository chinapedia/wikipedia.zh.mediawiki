'''茶樹油'''（{{lang-en|'''Tea tree oil'''}}或'''melaleuca oil'''）是一種含[[樟腦|樟腦]]味道的[[精油|精油]]，顏色淺黃至透明<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.holisticonline.com/aromatherapy/aroma_ess-oil-tea-tree-oil.htm | title = Directory of Essential Oils for Aromatherapy: Tea-Tree Oil (Melaleuca alternifolia) | publisher = Holistics Online | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20101123170406/http://holisticonline.com/Aromatherapy/aroma_ess-oil-tea-tree-oil.htm | archivedate = 2010-11-23 }}</ref>，由原生於[[澳大利亞|澳大利亞]][[昆士蘭|昆士蘭]]南部至[[新南威爾斯|新南威爾斯]]北部沿岸的{{link-en|互葉白千層|Melaleuca alternifolia}}提煉而成。茶樹油不能口服<ref name=poison>{{cite web|url=http://www.poison.org/poisonpost/winter2010/teatreeoil.htm|title=Tea Tree Oil|publisher=National Capital Poison Center|accessdate=4 December 2013}}</ref>，多數用作美容或[[護膚品|護膚品]]的原材料<ref name=euopinion>[http://ec.europa.eu/health/ph_risk/committees/04_sccp/docs/sccp_o_160.pdf] SCCP/1155/08 Scientific Committee on Consumer Products
SCCP OPINION ON Tea tree oil- European Union Commission Health and Consumer Union protection director general- adopted 18th plenary of 16 December 2008</ref>。

==歷史==
在澳大利亞及[[紐西蘭|紐西蘭]]，[[桃金孃科|桃金孃科]][[香桃木屬|香桃木屬]]的植物時被喚作茶樹。隨着亞瑟·彭福爾德（Arthur Penfold）發現茶樹油的強大殺菌特性之後，其商業提煉正式在1920年代開始。
