《[[她_(电影)|她]]》（{{lang-en|''Her''}}）是一部[[2013年电影|2013年]]的[[美国电影|美国]][[喜剧片|喜剧]][[剧情片|剧情片]]，由[[斯派克·琼斯|斯派克·琼斯]]自编自导并担任[[制片人|制片人]]。[[电影配乐|电影配乐]]由[[拱廊之火|拱廊之火]]作曲，霍伊特·范·霍特玛担任[[摄影师|摄影师]]（{{lang|en|Hoyte van Hoytema}}）。这也是琼斯独立完成的[[编剧|编剧]]处女作<ref>{{cite web | archivedate = 2014-08-12 | url = http://www.ibtimes.com/her-trailer-new-spike-jonze-film-featuring-joaquin-phoenix-siri-scarlett-johansson-video-1378109 | title = 'Her' Trailer: New Spike Jonze Film Featuring Joaquin Phoenix And A Siri-Like Scarlett Johansson [VIDEO] | publisher = The International Business Times | date = 2013-08-08 | accessdate = 2014-08-21 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140812193148/http://www.ibtimes.com/her-trailer-new-spike-jonze-film-featuring-joaquin-phoenix-siri-scarlett-johansson-video-1378109}}</ref>。影片讲述一位名叫西奥多·托姆布雷（{{lang|en|Theodore Twombly}}，[[杰昆·菲尼克斯|杰昆·菲尼克斯]]饰）的男子经历[[离婚|离婚]]后，与智能计算机[[操作系统|操作系统]]萨曼莎（{{lang|en|Samantha}}，[[斯嘉丽·约翰逊|斯嘉丽·约翰逊]]配音）发展出浪漫感情的故事。电影中的其他几位主要演员还包括[[艾米·亚当斯|艾米·亚当斯]]、[[鲁妮·玛拉|鲁妮·玛拉]]和[[奥利维亚·王尔德|奥利维亚·王尔德]]<ref>{{cite news | work = [[纽约时报|The New York Times]] | date = 2013-12-17 | accessdate = 2014-08-21 | url = http://www.nytimes.com/2013/12/18/movies/her-directed-by-spike-jonze.html?pagewanted=all&_r=0 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131217230421/http://www.nytimes.com/2013/12/18/movies/her-directed-by-spike-jonze.html?pagewanted=all&_r=0 | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2013-12-17 | title = Disembodied, but, Oh, What a Voice}}</ref>。

影片于2013年10月12日在[[纽约电影节|纽约电影节]][[首演|首映]]<ref>{{cite news | archivedate = 2014-08-08 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140808051023/http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/race/new-york-film-fest-spike-648097 | url = http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/race/new-york-film-fest-spike-648097 | title = New York Film Fest: Spike Jonze's 'Her' Closes 51st Edition on an Eccentric Note | work = [[好莱坞报道|The Hollywood Reporter]] | publisher = [[普罗米修斯环球媒体|Prometheus Global Media]] | date = 2013-10-13 | accessdate = 2014-08-21 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。[[华纳兄弟|华纳兄弟]]先从12月18日起安排《她》在6家[[电影院|电影院]]限量发行，之后再于2014年1月10日起在[[美国|美国]]和[[加拿大|加拿大]]的超过1700家电影院全面上映。电影的拍摄预算约为2300万[[美元|美元]]，收获的全球票房超过4700万美元<ref>{{cite web | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140812020857/http://boxofficemojo.com/movies/?id=her2013.htm | archivedate = 2014-08-12 | url = http://www.boxofficemojo.com/movies/?id=her2013.htm | title = Her (2013) | publisher = IMDb.com | work = [[Box_Office_Mojo|Box Office Mojo]] | accessdate = 2014-08-21 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。根据[[烂番茄|烂番茄]]上收集的228篇评论文章，其中有215篇给出了“新鲜”的正面评价，“新鲜度”达到94%，[[加权平均数|平均评分]]8.5（最高10分）<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/her/ | title = Her (2013) | publisher = Flixster | work = Rotten Tomatoes | accessdate = 2014-08-21}}</ref>；而{{lang|en|[[metacritic|metacritic]]}}上收集的46篇专业评论中则有42篇好评，无差评，4篇褒贬不一，综合评分90<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.metacritic.com/movie/her | publisher = CBS | work = metacritic | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140819015510/http://www.metacritic.com/movie/her | accessdate = 2014-08-21 | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2014-08-19 | title = Her}}</ref>。
