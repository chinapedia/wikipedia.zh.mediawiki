'''傑西·李·索弗'''（'''Jesse Lee Soffer'''，{{bd|1984年|4月23日}}）是美國的一位演員。他最著名的作品是在[[肥皂劇|肥皂劇]][[地球照轉|地球照轉]]中出演Will Munson角色，這一角色幫助他獲得三個日間艾美獎提名<ref>{{cite news|title=As the World Turns – Awards|url=http://www.nytimes.com/tv/show/153507/As-the-World-Turns/awards|work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=THE NATIONAL TELEVISION ACADEMY ANNOUNCES 33rd ANNUAL DAYTIME EMMY AWARD NOMINATIONS|url=http://emmyonline.com/day_33rd_nominations|publisher=[[Emmy_Award|emmyonline.com]]|date=February 8, 2006}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=34th Annual Daytime Emmy Winners|url=http://www.cbsnews.com/news/34th-annual-daytime-emmy-winners/|publisher=[[CBS|CBS]]|date=March 14, 2007}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=THE NATIONAL ACADEMY OF TELEVISION ARTS & SCIENCES ANNOUNCES 35th ANNUAL DAYTIME ENTERTAINMENT EMMY® AWARD NOMINATIONS|url=http://emmyonline.com/day_35th_nominations|publisher=[[Emmy_Award|emmyonline.com]]|date=April 30, 2008}}</ref>。他也出演了芝加哥火焰等電視劇。
