'''傑森·艾塞克'''（{{lang-en|'''Jason Isaacs'''}}，1963年6月6日－），或譯'''-{zh-hans:杰森·艾塞克; zh-hant:傑森·艾薩克; zh-hk:積遜·艾錫斯;}-'''，是出生於[[利物浦|利物浦]]的[[英國|英國]][[演員|演員]]<ref name=Rees3>{{cite news|author=Jasper Rees|title='There is a streak of cruelty in me': Actor Jason Isaacs Says Life Prepared Him to Become a Specialist in Unattractive Characters|work=[[The_Daily_Telegraph|The Daily Telegraph]], Review|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/arts/main.jhtml?xml=/arts/2007/01/27/baisaacs127.xml|date=27 January 2007|accessdate=27 June 2008|quote=The way he tells it, Isaacs's career is a big mistake. He grew up in [[Liverpool|Liverpool]], then London, went to [[Bristol_University|Bristol University]] to read law and felt uneasy among peers 'who all sounded like Hugh Grant'. He signed up for the drama society when he saw an audition for a part with 'Northern accent required'. ... He applied to drama schools with no intention of going. But the rather stern admissions tutor who offered him a place at [[Central_School_of_Speech_and_Drama|Central School of Speech and Drama]] in London took umbrage at his vacillation. 'She said, "This is a vocation. If you're not interested, tell me now." I was so ''English'' and too embarrassed to tell her the truth.' }}</ref>，在[[美國|美國]]影視作品中的登場演出也很活躍。

艾塞克因為在電影演員生涯中，演過的人物以[[反派|反派]]居多而著稱。他最有名的飾演角色為《[[哈利波特|哈利波特]]》系列電影中的[[食死人|食死人]][[魯休思·馬份|魯休思·馬份]]（{{lang|en|Lucius Malfoy}}）、《[[孤軍雄心|-{zh-cn:爱国者;zh-hk:孤軍雄心;zh-tw:決戰時刻;}-]]》裡面殘暴的[[英國陸軍|英國陸軍]]上校威廉·塔文頓（{{lang|en|Colonel William Tavington}}）、美國電視影集《{{Link-en|龍爭虎鬥 (2006年電視劇)|Brotherhood (2006 TV series)|龍爭虎鬥}}》裡的罪犯麥可·卡菲（{{lang|en|Michael Caffee}}） <ref name=Marx>{{cite news|author=Rebecca Flint Marx|title=Jason Isaacs: Biography|url=http://www.moviefone.com/celebrity/jason-isaacs/1802996/biography|work=[[Allmovie|Allmovie]]|publisher=[[Moviefone|Moviefone]]|accessdate=29 June 2008|quote=Although he first became interested in acting in part because 'it was a great way to meet girls,' Isaacs soon found deeper meaning in the theatre (in one interview he was quoted as saying 'I could release myself into acting in a way that I was not released socially') and duly dropped out of Bristol to hone his skills at London's [[Central_School_of_Speech_and_Drama|Central School of Speech and Drama]].|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081218232651/http://www.moviefone.com/celebrity/jason-isaacs/1802996/biography|archivedate=2008年12月18日}}</ref><ref name=IMDb>{{cite web|title=Biography for Jason Isaacs|url=http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0005042/bio|work=[[Internet_Movie_Database|Internet Movie Database]]|accessdate=25 June 2008}} [Includes unsourced "Personal Quotes".]</ref>。

儘管大部分的參與作品是以電影和電視劇為主，但身為一名舞台演員，艾塞克同樣也演出過數部舞台劇，著名作包括在{{Link-en|迪克蘭‧唐納倫|Declan Donnellan}}執導的[[戲劇|戲劇]]《[[天使在美國|天使在美國]]》第一部「千禧年降臨」（{{lang|en|Millennium Approaches}}，1992年）和第二部「重建」（{{lang|en|Perestroika}}，1993年）裡演出的路易·愛恩森（{{lang|en|Louis Ironson}}）<ref name=IMDbOther>{{cite web|title=Jason Isaacs: Other Works|url=http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0005042/otherworks |work=[[Internet_Movie_Database|Internet Movie Database]]|accessdate=25 June 2008}}</ref><ref name=NTArchive>{{Cite web|url=http://www.nationaltheatre.org.uk/South20Bank2019921995+9789.twl|title=NT: Archive: Stage by Stage: 1992–1995|work=[[Royal_National_Theatre|Royal National Theatre]]|publisher=nationaltheatre.org.uk|accessdate=25 June 2008|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081207183621/http://www.nationaltheatre.org.uk/South20Bank2019921995+9789.twl|archivedate=2008年12月7日}}</ref>、及2007年在[[特拉法加工作室|特拉法加工作室]]演出的《{{Link-en|啞巴侍應|The Dumb Waiter|-{zh-tw:啞巴侍應;zh-cn:送菜升降机;}-}}》50週年改編版<ref name=Friedman>{{cite news|author=Sonia Friedman  Productions|title=Dumb Waiter Limited Run|url=http://www.soniafriedman.com/news_press_releases/dumb_waiter_limited_run|work=Sonia Friedman Productions press release|date=3 January 2007|accessdate=23 June 2008|quote=Strictly limited run: [[Lee_Evans_(comedian)|Lee Evans]] and Jason Isaacs to star in major revival of Harold Pinter's ''[[The_Dumb_Waiter|The Dumb Waiter]]'' directed by Harry Burton ... To coincide with the play's 50th anniversary....}}</ref><ref name=Rees>{{cite news|author=Jasper Rees|title='There is a streak of cruelty in me': Actor Jason Isaacs Says Life Prepared Him to Become a Specialist in Unattractive Characters|work=[[The_Daily_Telegraph|The Daily Telegraph]], Review|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/arts/main.jhtml?xml=%2Farts%2F2007%2F01%2F27%2Fbaisaacs127.xml|date=27 January 2007|accessdate=18 March 2012|quote=The more cheery and pliant of the two [Gus] is played by [[Lee_Evans_(comedian)|Lee Evans]], the more menacing [Ben] by Isaacs. Characteristically. 'Far from what you would think,' [Isaacs] says, 'Lee is the one who went to art school and is familiar with [[Samuel_Beckett|Beckett]] and [[Harold_Pinter|Pinter]]. I wasn't going to do this until I read it. It is crackingly funny. I realised how much of a debt [[Quentin_Tarantino|Tarantino]] owes. The Pinter scholars can go off and discuss whatever they like in dusty rooms, but Lee and I and Harry Burton, the director, are trying to come up with something really engaging and exciting. It's never been more relevant. The whole play exists on this undercurrent of fear and paranoia. It's a very scary time to live in the world, and these two guys are in a room scared and working out what to do about it.' |deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081207142331/http://www.telegraph.co.uk/arts/main.jhtml?xml=%2Farts%2F2007%2F01%2F27%2Fbaisaacs127.xml|archivedate=2008年12月7日}}</ref><ref name=Ansdell>{{cite news|author=Caroline Ansdell|title=Review Round-up: Critics Find Waiter Not So Dumb.}}</ref>。他也從2012年的3月到5月間，在[[國家廣播公司|美國國家廣播公司]]（{{lang|en|NBC}}）的電視劇《[[異度覺醒|異度覺醒]]》裡演出主角布里頓警探（{{lang|en|Michael Britten}}）。
