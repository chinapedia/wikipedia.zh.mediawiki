'''冰島－印度關係'''，是指[[冰島共和國|冰島共和國]]和[[印度共和國|印度共和國]]之間的雙邊關係。歷史上，冰島和印度兩國的關係一直友好，但缺乏實質性的合作<ref name="My background helps me: Kalam ">{{cite news|url=http://www.hindu.com/2005/05/30/stories/2005053014431200.htm|title=My background helps me: Kalam |first=K.V. Prasad |date=2005-05-30|publisher=The Hindu|accessdate=2008-09-28}}</ref>。

== 背景 ==
冰島和印度於1975年建交。印度在冰島於[[英國|英國]][[倫敦|倫敦]]的[[大使館|大使館]]設有代表，而印度亦在[[挪威|挪威]][[奧斯陸|奧斯陸]]設大使館。自2005年起，兩國開始加強外交和經濟關係<ref name="Inauguration of the Embassy of Iceland in New Delhi" />；該年，[[冰島總統|冰島總統]][[格里姆松|格里姆松]]首次到印度的外訪，又承諾支持印度成為[[聯合國安理會常任理事國|聯合國安理會常任理事國]]之一，是第一個有如此表態的[[北歐|北歐]]國家；時任[[印度總統|印度總統]][[阿卜杜勒·卡拉姆|阿卜杜勒·卡拉姆]]於2005年5月又到冰島進行正式訪問<ref name="My background helps me: Kalam " />。其後，冰島於2006年在印度[[新德里|新德里]]設置新的大使館<ref name="Inauguration of the Embassy of Iceland in New Delhi">{{cite web|url=http://www.iceland.org/in/the-embassy/news-and-events/nr/1748 |title=Inauguration of the Embassy of Iceland in New Delhi |date=2006-02-26 |publisher=Icelandic Foreign Service |accessdate=2008-09-28 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20061003172058/http://www.iceland.org/in/the-embassy/news-and-events/nr/1748 |archivedate=2006-10-03 }}</ref>。[[印度海軍|印度海軍]]亦曾派員參觀冰島<ref name="Indian Navy Team in Iceland">{{cite web|url=http://www.iceland.org/in/the-embassy/news-and-events/nr/2929 |title=Indian Navy Team in Iceland |date=2006-08-25 |publisher=Icelandic Foreign Service |accessdate=2008-09-28 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081203003017/http://www.iceland.org./in/the-embassy/news-and-events/nr/2929 |archivedate=2008-12-03 }}</ref>。[[貢納爾·保爾|貢納爾·保爾]]松是現任[[冰島駐印度大使|冰島駐印度大使]]<ref name="About the Embassy">{{cite web|url=http://www.iceland.org/in/the-embassy/about-the-embassy/ |title=About the Embassy |publisher=Icelandic Foreign Service |accessdate=2008-09-28 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080928072152/http://www.iceland.org/in/the-embassy/about-the-embassy/ |archivedate=2008-09-28 }}</ref>，[[S·納坦|S·納坦]]則是[[印度駐冰島大使|印度駐冰島大使]]<ref name="Shri S. Swaminathan to be India’s first resident Ambassador to Iceland">{{cite web|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090410162813/http://meaindia.nic.in/pressrelease/2008/09/03pr01.htm|url=http://meaindia.nic.in/pressrelease/2008/09/03pr01.htm|title=Shri S. Swaminathan to be India’s first resident Ambassador to Iceland|date=September 3, 2008|publisher=NIC India Ministry of External Affairs, New Delhi|accessdate=2008-09-28|archivedate=2009-04-10}} {{Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot}}</ref>。

2007年，兩國雙邊貿易總額為26,700,000美元，其中冰島到印度的出口總額為1,140,000美元，主要出口產品包括[[魚製品|魚製品]]（主要是[[鱈魚|鱈魚]]）、工業產品和醫療產品；同年，印度到冰島的出口總額為18,950,000美元，主要出口產品包括日常消費品和工業原材料<ref name="Iceland India Business Guide 2007 " />。兩國之間的經貿關係至今仍然是有限的，但被外界認為具有增長潛力<ref name="Iceland India Business Guide 2007 ">{{cite web|url=http://www.iceland.org/media/india/BG07_FINAL.pdf |title=Iceland India Business Guide 2007 |date=2007-04-20 |publisher=Icelandic Foreign Service |accessdate=2008-09-28 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080517212704/http://www.iceland.org/media/india/BG07_FINAL.pdf |archivedate=2008-05-17 }}</ref>。

印度與冰島簽署了[[租稅協定|租稅協定]]，以加強經濟合作；兩國也簽訂了[[諒解備忘錄|諒解備忘錄]]，於可再生能源領域合作，開發氫能[[燃料電池|燃料電池]]<ref name="India and Iceland conclude tax agreement">{{cite news|url=http://www.icenews.is/index.php/2007/11/30/india-and-iceland-conclude-tax-agreement/|title=India and Iceland conclude tax agreement|first=A. Rienstra|date=Nov 30, 2007|publisher=International Nordic News|accessdate=2008-09-28}}</ref>。兩國亦簽署了另一份諒解備忘錄，合作研究[[地震|地震]]<ref name="Iceland and India enter into cooperation on earthquake prediction research">{{cite web|url=http://www.iceland.org/in/the-embassy/news-and-events/nr/3807 |title=Iceland and India enter into cooperation on earthquake prediction research |date=10.4.2007 |publisher=Icelandic Foreign Service |accessdate=2008-09-28 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081203001826/http://www.iceland.org./in/the-embassy/news-and-events/nr/3807 |archivedate=2008-12-03 }}</ref>；2007年兩國再簽署了一份諒解備忘錄，合作研究[[可持續漁業發展|可持續漁業發展]]<ref name="Important Bilateral Treaties and Agreements">{{cite web|url=http://meaindia.nic.in/foreignrelation/iceland.pdf |title=Important Bilateral Treaties and Agreements |publisher=Ministry of External Affairs of India |accessdate=2000-12-31 |archivedate=2009-02-26 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090226073737/http://meaindia.nic.in/foreignrelation/iceland.pdf |deadurl=yes }} </ref>。
