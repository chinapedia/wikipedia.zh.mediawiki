'''鬚緣蜆蝶屬'''（學名：'''''Helicopis'''''）是[[蜆蝶科|蜆蝶科]][[蜆蝶亞科|蜆蝶亞科]][[鬚緣蜆蝶族|鬚緣蜆蝶族]]裡的一個[[屬|屬]]。共有3個物種，分佈於[[新熱帶界|新熱帶界]]<ref>Brower, Andrew V. Z. 2008. Helicopis Fabricius 1807. Helicopis [Illiger] 1807 suppressed, ICZN Op. 232, Rusticus Hübner 1807 suppressed, ICZN Op. 137, Hexuropteris Hübner 1819 currently viewed as a subjective junior synonym, Erotion Billberg 1820 junior objective synonym. Version 04 June 2008 (under construction). http://tolweb.org/Helicopis/105560/2008.06.04 in The Tree of Life Web Project, http://tolweb.org/ {{en}}</ref>。後翅尾突跟[[線灰蝶亞科|線灰蝶亞科]]的物種相似。幼蟲[[寄主|寄主]]為[[天南星科|天南星科]]植物<ref>Mota, L. L., Kaminski, L. A., & Freitas, A. V. (2014). Last instar larvae and pupae of Ourocnemis archytas and Anteros formosus (Lepidoptera: Riodinidae), with a summary of known host plants for the tribe Helicopini. Zootaxa, 3838(4), 435-444. {{en}}</ref>。

== 物種 ==
* [[鬚緣蜆蝶|鬚緣蜆蝶]] ''Helicopis cupido''
** 指名亞種 ''H. c. cupido'' = 異斑鬚緣蜆蝶 ''H. divergens''
* [[白鬚緣蜆蝶|白鬚緣蜆蝶]] ''Helicopis endymiaena''
* [[尖鬚緣蜆蝶|尖鬚緣蜆蝶]] ''Helicopis gnidus'' = ''H. acis''
** 中型鬚緣蜆蝶 ''H. g. medialis''
** 黑基鬚緣蜆蝶 ''H. g. nigrobasalis''
