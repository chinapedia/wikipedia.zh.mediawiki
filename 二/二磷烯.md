'''二磷烯'''又称'''乙磷烯'''，是一种[[磷|磷]]的[[氢化物|氢化物]]，[[化学式|化学式]]为(PH)<sub>2</sub>。它存在''E''型和''Z''型的[[顺反异构|顺反异构]]体<ref>{{cite journal|last=Lu|first=Tongxiang|coauthors=Simmonett, Andrew C., Evangelista, Francesco A., Yamaguchi, Yukio, Schaefer, Henry F.|title=Diphosphene and Diphosphinylidene|journal=The Journal of Physical Chemistry A|date=26 November 2009|volume=113|issue=47|pages=13227–13236|doi=10.1021/jp904028a}}</ref>。二磷烯也是{{link-en|二膦烯|Diphosphenes}}类化合物的母体，它们的通式为(PR)<sub>2</sub>，其中R代表一个[[有机物|有机]][[基团|基团]]<ref>{{cite journal|last=Yoshifuji|first=Masaaki|coauthors=Shibayama, Katsuhiro, Inamoto, Naoki, Hirotsu, Ken, Higuchi, Taiichi|title=Reaction of the diphosphene ArP?PAr (Ar = 2,4,6-But 3C6H2) with sulphur: isolation and X-ray structure of the diphosphene monosulphide|journal=Journal of the Chemical Society, Chemical Communications|date=1 January 1983|issue=16|pages=862|doi=10.1039/C39830000862}}</ref>。
