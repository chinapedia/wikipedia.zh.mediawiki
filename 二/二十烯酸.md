'''二十烯酸'''（{{lang-en|Paullinic acid}}）是一种在植物内可以找到的[[ω−7脂肪酸|ω−7]][[不饱和脂肪酸|不饱和]][[脂肪酸|脂肪酸]]。二十烯酸在[[瓜拿纳|瓜拿纳]]中可以大量提取，二十烯酸的英文名（'''Paullinic''' acid）就来自于瓜拿纳的学名（'''''Paullinia''' cupana''）。<ref>{{Cite journal | pmid = 14506841 | year = 2003 | last1 = Avato | first1 = P | last2 = Pesante | first2 = MA | last3 = Fanizzi | first3 = FP | last4 = Santos | first4 = CA | title = Seed oil composition of Paullinia cupana var. Sorbilis (Mart.) Ducke | volume = 38 | issue = 7 | pages = 773–80 | journal = Lipids | doi=10.1007/s11745-003-1126-5}}</ref>
