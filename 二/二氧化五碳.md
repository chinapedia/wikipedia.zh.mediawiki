'''二氧化五碳'''是一種由[[碳|碳]]和[[氧|氧]]組成的[[化合物|化合物]]，二氧化五碳分子在室溫下在溶液中能穩定存在<ref name="maier">{{cite journal
  | title = C5O2 (1,2,3,4-Pentatetraene-1,5-dione), a New Oxide of Carbon
  | author = Günther Maier, Hans Peter Reisenauer, Ulrich Schäfer, and Heinz Balli
  | journal = Angewandte Chemie International Edition in English
  | year = 1988
  | volume = 27
  | issue = 4
  | pages = 566–568
  | doi = 10.1002/anie.198805661
}}</ref>。它由G. Maier於1988年成功合成。它可由[[均苯三酚|均苯三酚]]（{{chem|C|6|H|6|O|3}}）（有[[酚|酚]]與[[酮|酮]]兩種形式）熱分解或[[2,4,6三（重氮）环己烷-1,3,5-三酮|2,4,6三（重氮）环己烷-1,3,5-三酮]]（{{chem|C|6|N|3|O|3}}）蒸汽闪光分解得到<ref name="feastw">
  Frank W. Eastwood (1997), ''Gas Phase Pyrolytic Methods for the Preparation of Carbon-Hydrogen and Carbon-Hydrogen-Oxygen Compounds.''. In Yannick Vallée''Gas Phase Reactions in Organic Synthesis''.CRC Press; ISBN 9056990810, 9789056990817
</ref>。純質二氧化五碳在低溫下可穩定存在，於-96℃開始[[聚合|聚合]]。
