'''二硒化氫'''（{{Lang-en|Hydrogen diselenide}}），又稱'''乙硒烷'''或'''二硒烷'''<ref>{{cite web | title=化學命名原則 | url=http://210.71.71.8/ischool/public/resource_view/open.php?file=77a6fd84d360a74163f9cb706cdf9618.pdf | publisher=[[國立編譯館|國立編譯館]] | date=2011-04-01 | accessdate=2016-02-27 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160322000648/http://210.71.71.8/ischool/public/resource_view/open.php?file=77a6fd84d360a74163f9cb706cdf9618.pdf | archive-date=2016-03-22 | dead-url=no }}</ref>，是一種[[無機化合物|無機]]硒化合物，其化學式為H<sub>2</sub>Se<sub>2</sub><ref name="macintyre1992dictionary">{{cite book |author= Macintyre, J.E. |title= Dictionary of Inorganic Compounds | publisher= Taylor & Francis |year=1992 |pages=293 |isbn= 9780412301209 }}</ref>，亦可計為(SeH)<sub>2</sub>，其結構類似於[[過氧化氫|過氧化氫]]（H<sub>2</sub>O<sub>2</sub>）、[[硫氧化氫|硫氧化氫]]（H<sub>2</sub>SO）與[[二硫化氫|二硫化氫]]（H<sub>2</sub>S<sub>2</sub>），但比前三者更接近90度。二硒化氫[[常溫|常溫]]下容易分解為[[硒化氫|硒化氫]]（H<sub>2</sub>Se）和硒單質而無法穩定存在，但可以在一些溶液中存在<ref name="shaw2013inorganic">{{cite book |author= Shaw, B.L. and Stavely, L.A.K.|series=Commonwealth and international library: Chemistry division |title= Inorganic Hydrides: The Commonwealth and International Library: Chemistry Division| publisher= Elsevier Science |year=2013 |pages=76 |isbn= 9781483160320 }}</ref>。二硒化氫可以用在一些有機合成的中間過程<ref name = "CraigBayse">{{Cite journal | title =Craig A. == 結構 ==
二硒化氫分子的[[兩面角|兩面角]]為89.9度，[[氫|H]]-Se-Se[[鍵角|鍵角]]為90.5度，H-Se[[鍵長|鍵長]]為1.536[[埃|埃]]，Se-Se鍵長為2.380埃。二硒化氫扭轉運動電勢能壘高度與[[硫氧化氫|硫氧化氫]]類似，而扭轉穿隧的分解則與[[二硫化氫|二硫化氫]]有相同的觀測值<ref name="Gottselig Quack Willeke 2003 pp. 353–362">{{cite journal | last=Gottselig | first=Michael | last2=Quack | first2=Martin | last3=Willeke | first3=Martin | title=Mode-selective stereomutation tunneling as compared to parity violation in hydrogen diselenide isotopomers1,2,3H280Se2 | journal=Israel Journal of Chemistry | publisher=Wiley-Blackwell | volume=43 | issue=3-4 | year=2003 | issn=0021-2148 | doi=10.1560/1khd-g0yt-3yeu-n16p | pages=353–362}}</ref>。此外，二硒化氫和二碲化氫（hydrogen ditelluride）<ref name="Cornelis E. C. A. Hop, Marco A. Medina">{{Cite journal | author = Cornelis E. C. A. Hop, Marco A. Medina | title = H<sub>2</sub>Te<sub>2</sub> Is Stable in the Gas Phase | publisher = J. Am. Chem. Soc. | year = 1994 | volume = 116 | issue = 7 | pages = pp 3163–3164 | doi = 10.1021/ja00086a072 | date = April 1994 | accessdate = 2010-11-14}}</ref>的[[分子結構|分子結構]]都有轉動屏障的現象。<ref name="Ewig Mei Van Wazer 1980 pp. 241–245">{{cite journal | last=Ewig | first=Carl S. | last2=Mei | first2=Elizabeth H. | last3=Van Wazer | first3=John R. | title=Rotational barriers in H2Se2and H2Te2 | journal=Molecular Physics | publisher=Informa UK Limited | volume=40 | issue=1 | year=1980 | issn=0026-8976 | doi=10.1080/00268978000101431 | pages=241–245}}</ref>

== 結構類似的硒化合物 ==
還有一些硒化合物的結構類似[[過氧化氫|過氧化氫]]，如硒氧化氫、硒硫化氫，但他們都不穩定。
