}}'''二焦磷酸三铁'''（[[化学式|化学式]]：Fe<sub>3</sub>(P<sub>2</sub>O<sub>7</sub>)<sub>2</sub>）是铁的[[焦磷酸盐|焦磷酸盐]]中的一种，其中同时含有Fe<sup>2+</sup>和Fe<sup>3+</sup>两种价态。此外，铁的焦磷酸盐还有[[焦磷酸亚铁|Fe<sub>2</sub>P<sub>2</sub>O<sub>7</sub>]]、Fe<sub>7</sub>(P<sub>2</sub>O<sub>7</sub>)<sub>4</sub>、[[焦磷酸铁|Fe<sub>4</sub>(P<sub>2</sub>O<sub>7</sub>)<sub>3</sub>]]等。<ref>LK Elbouaanani, B Malaman, R Gerardin, ''et al''. Crystal Structure Refinement and Magnetic Properties of Fe<sub>4</sub>(P<sub>2</sub>O<sub>7</sub>)<sub>3</sub> Studied by Neutron Diffraction and Mössbauer Techniques. Journal of Solid State Chemistry, 2002. 163 (2): 412-420. doi:10.1006/jssc.2001.9415</ref>
==性质==
二焦磷酸三铁有α-和β-两种晶型。其中α型的焦磷酸盐可以选择性地促进[[异丁酸|异丁酸]]脱氢反应。<ref>Jacques C. Védrine. [https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Jacques_Vedrine/publication/226298449_Partial_Oxidation_Reactions_on_Phosphate-Based_Catalysts/links/5448d3820cf2d62c3052ca1c/Partial-Oxidation-Reactions-on-Phosphate-Based-Catalysts.pdf Partial oxidation reactions on phosphate-based catalysts]. Topics in Catalysis 11/12 (2000) 147–152</ref>
