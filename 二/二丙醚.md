二丙醚（'''Dipropyl ether'''）是一种[[醚类|醚类]]，[[结构简式|结构简式]](CH<sub>3</sub>CH<sub>2</sub>CH<sub>2</sub>)<sub>2</sub>O，是一种无色易燃有香气的液体。
==制备==
===酸催化醇分子间脱水===
[[正丙醇|正丙醇]]在[[对甲苯磺酸|对甲苯磺酸]]（一种强酸）催化下加热，脱水生成二丙醚。该法可用于从[[伯醇|伯醇]]制对称的醚类。 and heat, in the same way other symmetrical ethers may be formed.<ref name="merck">{{Cite book| title = Merck Index of Chemicals and Drugs | edition = 14th | year = 2006| isbn=978-0-911910-00-1| editor = O'Neil, Maryadele; Heckelman, Patricia; Koch, Cherie; Roman, Kristin| publisher = Merck Research Laboratories}}</ref>

===威廉姆逊合成===
碱性条件下正丙基醇钠与正丙基卤代烷发生[[亲核取代反应|亲核取代反应]]，得二丙醚。<ref>{{Cite book| title = Organic Chemistry, 3rd ed.| year = 2004| isbn=978-0763735869| author = Fox, Marye; Whitesell, James| publisher = Jones & Bartlett Publishers}}</ref>

==安全性==

作为一种醚，二丙醚在空气中缓慢地[[自然氧化|自然氧化]]生成加热爆炸的[[过氧化物|过氧化物]]。<ref name="merck" /> [[2,6-二叔丁基對甲酚|2,6-二叔丁基對甲酚]]常为抗氧化剂添加到醚类试剂中。 <ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.sigmaaldrich.com/catalog/product/sial/296082?lang=en&region=US | title = Diethyl ether product listing | publisher = Sigma-Aldrich | accessdate = 2012-07-03}}</ref>蒸馏前需加亚铁盐除过氧化物，并且不允许蒸干。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.ccohs.ca/oshanswers/chemicals/organic/organic_peroxide.html#_1_2 | title = Organic peroxide hazards | publisher = Canadian Centre for Occupational Health and Safety | accessdate = 2012-07-03}}</ref>
