'''威斯敏斯特圣玛格丽特教堂'''（St. Margaret's Church, Westminster Abbey）是一座[[英国圣公会|英国圣公会]]教堂，位于[[:en:Parliament_Square|国会广场]][[西敏寺|西敏寺]]的场地，是[[英国下议院|英国下议院]]的[[牧区|牧区]]教堂<ref>{{cite web
 | url = http://www.westminster-abbey.org/st-margarets/history/
 | title = St. Margaret's, Westminster Parish details
 | author = Westminster Abbey
 | accessdate = 2008-05-03
 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20080305183607/http://www.westminster-abbey.org/st-margarets/history/
 | archivedate = 2008-03-05
 | deadurl = yes
 }}</ref>。紀念[[童貞瑪加利大|安提阿的聖玛格丽特]]<ref>{{cite book
 | last = Pevsner
 | first = N.
 | authorlink = Nikolaus Pevsner
 | coauthors = Bradley, Simon
 | title = The Buildings of England: London 6 - Westminster
 | year = 2003
 | publisher = Penguin
 | location = Uxbridge
 | isbn = 0300095953
}}</ref>。

该教堂由[[本笃会|本笃会]]修士创建于12世纪，住在修院附近的居民<ref>{{cite web
 | url = http://www.ourpasthistory.com/England/st-margaret-westminster
 | title = St. Margaret's, Westminster
 | author = McManus, Mark
 | accessdate = 2008-05-03
 | deadurl = yes
 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20080611105551/http://www.ourpasthistory.com/England/st-margaret-westminster
 | archivedate = 2008-06-11
 }}</ref>可以在此较简陋的牧区教堂单独礼拜。该教堂重建于1486年到1523年，1614年成为西敏宫的牧区教堂，17世纪的[[清教徒|清教徒]]，不满[[高派|高度仪式化]]的西敏寺，选择在较“合适的”圣玛格丽特教堂礼拜。<ref>{{cite book
 | last = Wright
 | first = A.
 | coauthors = Smith, P.
 | title = Parliament Past and Present
 | year = 1868
 | publisher = Hutchinson & Co
 | location = London
}}</ref> 这一作法一直持续至今。

西北塔楼由约翰·詹姆斯重建于1734年至1738年，同时，整座建筑用波特兰石包裹。约翰·拉夫堡·皮尔逊为东部和西部都增加了门廊。1877年，乔治·吉尔伯特·斯科特爵士将教堂的内部被改建为目前的模样，<ref>{{cite book
 | last = Scott
 | first = George Gilbert
 | authorlink = George Gilbert Scott
 | editor = Stamp, Gavin
 | title = Personal and Professional Recollections
 | origyear = 1879
 | year = 1995
 | publisher = Sampson Low, Marston, Searle & Rivington] Stamford: Paul Watkins Publishing
 | location = London
 | isbn = 1871615267
}}</ref> 但是都铎风格的许多特征依然存在。

东侧窗户为1509年的[[弗蘭德|弗蘭德]][[花窗玻璃|花窗玻璃]]，庆祝[[阿拉贡的凯瑟琳|阿拉贡的凯瑟琳]]与[[亨利八世|亨利八世]]订婚<ref>{{cite web
 | url = http://www.westminster-abbey.org/st-margarets/visit-us/the-east-end3
 | title = St Margaret's Church - The east window
 | author = Dean and Chapter, Westminster Abbey
 | accessdate = 2010-10-21
 | work = St Margaret's Church
}}</ref>。其他窗户纪念[[沃爾特·雷利|沃爾特·雷利]]<ref>{{cite web
 | url = http://www.britannia.com/bios/raleigh/executio.html
 | title = Sir Walter Raleigh - Execution
 | author = Smith, Christopher
 | accessdate = 2008-05-03
 | work = Britannia Biographies
}}</ref>以及诗人[[约翰·弥尔顿|约翰·弥尔顿]]（本堂教友）。

[[温斯顿·丘吉尔|温斯顿·丘吉尔]]在这座教堂举行婚礼<ref>{{cite book
 | last = Gilbert
 | first = Martin
 | title = Churchill: a life
 | year = 1991
 | publisher = Heinemann
 | location = London
 | isbn = 0434291838
}}</ref>。西敏宫、西敏寺和圣玛格丽特教堂一同被列为[[世界遗产|世界遗产]]。国会议员和上下议院工作人员可以在这座教堂结婚。
