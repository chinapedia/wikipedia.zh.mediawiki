'''威爾士三一聖大衛大學'''（{{lang-cy|'''Prifysgol Cymru, Y Drindod Dewi Sant'''}}，{{lang-en|'''University of Wales, Trinity Saint David'''}}）是位於[[英國|英國]][[西南威爾士|西南威爾士]]的一所[[書院聯邦制|書院聯邦制]]大學，校區在[[蘭彼得|蘭彼得]]、[[卡馬森|卡馬森]]和[[斯溫西|斯溫西]]，此外在[[倫敦|倫敦]]也有分校區。<ref>University of Wales, Trinity Saint David (2010); ''Delivering a vision: The Creation of a New University''; Trinity Saint David Trust, p. 3</ref>

該大學由威爾士的[[威爾士蘭彼得大學|威爾士蘭彼得大學]]和[[三一大學學院|三一大學學院]]兩所高等教育機構在2010年合併而成。<ref name="royal_seal_approval"/><ref name="trinitysaintdavid.ac.uk"/> 2011年宣佈[[威爾士大學|威爾士大學]]也將併入此大學。<ref name=autogenerated2>[http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-wales-15410424 University of Wales effectively abolished in merger] - BBC News, 21 October 2011</ref><ref name=autogenerated3>http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-wales-15157119 http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-wales-15171830  http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-wales-15191954</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Warning not to strip University of Wales assets|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-wales-15689167|accessdate=17 November 2011|newspaper=BBC News}}</ref> 2013年8月1日[[斯旺西都會大學|斯旺西都會大學]]併入該大學。<ref>[http://www.thisissouthwales.co.uk/Uni-merger-goes-ahead-Met-dissolved/story-19511435-detail/story.html#axzz2ZnmhLKAE This is South Wales - Uni merger goes ahead after Met is dissolved]</ref>
