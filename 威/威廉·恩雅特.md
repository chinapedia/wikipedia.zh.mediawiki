'''威廉·恩雅特'''（William Enyart；{{bd|1949年|9月22日}}）是[[美國|美國]]的一位政治人物。他在2012年至2013年期間曾經擔任伊利諾州第12選舉區選出的美國眾議院議員。他的黨籍是[[民主黨_(美國)|民主黨]]<ref name=times>{{cite news|title=William 'Bill' L. Enyart, Jr.|url=http://www.washingtontimes.com/campaign-2012/candidates/william-bill-l-enyart-jr-62250/|accessdate=May 23, 2014|newspaper=Washington Times|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140523011304/http://www.washingtontimes.com/campaign-2012/candidates/william-bill-l-enyart-jr-62250/|archivedate=2014年5月23日}}</ref><ref name=results>{{cite news|title=Bill Enyart officially becomes 12th District's congressman|url=http://thesouthern.com/news/local/govt-and-politics/bill-enyart-officially-becomes-th-district-s-congressman/article_16f5670c-55e2-11e2-941c-0019bb2963f4.html|accessdate=May 23, 2014|newspaper=The Southern|date=1-3-2013}}</ref>。在進入政壇之前，恩雅特曾經是一位律師。恩雅特在2014年的選舉中不敵[[麥克·博斯特|麥克·博斯特]]，未能連任<ref>{{cite news|last=Sweet|first=Lynn|title=Three Illinois freshmen Democrats--Schneider, Bustos, Enyart--seen as vulnerable in 2014|url=http://blogs.suntimes.com/sweet/2013/03/three_illinois_freshmen_democr.html|accessdate=23 May 2014|newspaper=Chicago Sun-Times|date=3-5-2013|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140523230407/http://blogs.suntimes.com/sweet/2013/03/three_illinois_freshmen_democr.html|archivedate=2014年5月23日}}</ref><ref name=ivn>{{cite news|last=Wicklander|first=Carl|title=Large Percentage of Undecided Voters in IL-12 Leaves Election a Toss-Up|url=http://ivn.us/2014/02/03/large-percentage-undecided-voters-il-12-leaves-election-toss|accessdate=May 22, 2014|newspaper=Independent Voter Network|date=February 3, 2014}}</ref>。
