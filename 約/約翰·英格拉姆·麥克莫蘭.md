'''約翰·英格拉姆·麥克莫蘭'''（{{lang|en|'''John Ingram McMorran'''}}，{{bd|1889年|6月19日|2003年|2月24日|}}）為[[美国|美国]][[超級人瑞|超級人瑞]]，也是[[最長壽者|獲驗證的世上最長壽者]]之一，在世113歲又250天<ref>{{cite news |first= Myrna|last= Oliver|authorlink= |coauthors= |title=J. McMorran, 113; Oldest Man in U.S., 5th-Oldest Ever. |url=http://pqasb.pqarchiver.com/latimes/access/295731161.html?dids=295731161:295731161&FMT=ABS&FMTS=ABS:FT&date=Feb+27%2C+2003&author=Myrna+Oliver&pub=Los+Angeles+Times&desc=Obituaries%3B+J.+McMorran%2C+113%3B+Oldest+Man+in+U.S.%2C+5th-Oldest+Ever&pqatl=google |quote= McMorran was history's fifth-oldest man, said Dr. [[L._Stephen_Coles|L. Stephen Coles]] of the [[加州大学洛杉矶分校|加州大学洛杉矶分校]]-based [[Gerontology_Research_Group|Gerontology Research Group]], a nonprofit international research organization that verifies birth dates and tracks the 35 to 45 people around the globe who are 110 and older. Coles said that 41 people remain alive in that category, and that the oldest living American is [[Mary_Christian|Mary Christian]] – one week McMorran's senior – of San Pablo, Calif. The verifiably oldest living man in the United States, he said, is now [[Fred_H._Hale,_Sr.|Fred Hale]] of New York state, who is 112. |publisher=[[洛杉磯時報|洛杉磯時報]] |date=February 27, 2003 |accessdate=2007-12-18 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |first= |last= |authorlink= |coauthors= |title=America's Oldest Man Parties On. |url=http://nl.newsbank.com/nl-search/we/Archives?p_product=MH&s_site=miami&p_multi=MH&p_theme=realcities&p_action=search&p_maxdocs=200&p_topdoc=1&p_text_direct-0=0F4618A8CC8639CD&p_field_direct-0=document_id&p_perpage=10&p_sort=YMD_date:D&s_trackval=GooglePM |quote=So what does a man believed to be the oldest American do on his birthday? 約翰·英格拉姆·麥克莫蘭出生於美國[[密西根州|密西根州]]{{le|古德蘭鎮區 (密西根州)|Goodland Township, Michigan|古德蘭鎮區}}，為喬治·麥克莫蘭（George McMorran，1859年10月15日－？）和利迪亞·英格拉姆（Lydia Ingram）所生育當中最年長的子女。前[[美国众议院|美國眾議員]]{{le|亨利·戈登·麥克莫蘭|Henry Gordon McMorran}}（1844年－1929年）也是英格拉姆·麥克莫蘭的表親。他的親妹妹莉莉亞·伊麗莎白·韋布（Lillia Elizabeth Webb，婚前名為莉莉亞·伊麗莎白·麥克莫蘭）在世103歲<ref>Social Security Death Index at ancestry.com</ref>。麥克莫蘭有[[吸煙|吸煙]]、飲[[酒|酒]]以及吃油膩食物等習慣<ref name=spt/><ref>{{cite news |first= |last= |authorlink= |coauthors= |title=The immortals Farewell to some of the world's more off-beat legends. |url= |quote= John McMorran America's oldest man, who departed this world at the age of 113, despite spending more than a century smoking, drinking and eating greasy food ...  |publisher=[[獨立報|獨立報]] |date=December 28, 2003 |accessdate=2007-12-18 }}</ref>。

麥克莫蘭在2001年成為美國[[獲驗證的最長壽男性列表|獲驗證的最長壽男性]]<ref name=spt>{{cite news |first= |last= |authorlink= |coauthors= |title= Briefly nation's oldest, Mary L. Parr dies at 113. |url=http://www.sptimes.com/2002/10/30/TampaBay/Briefly_nation_s_olde.shtml |quote=McMorran, a retired farmer and truck driver who smoked a pack of cigarettes a day at one point, became the heir apparent until [[老人學|老人學]], a [[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]] investigator for the Gerontology Research Group, found Miss Parr. |publisher=[[St._Petersburg_Times|St. Petersburg Times]] |date= |accessdate=2007-12-18 }}</ref>。他住進位於[[佛罗里达州|佛罗里达州]][[莱克兰_(佛罗里达州)|莱克兰]]的「Tandem Healthcare」療養院直到他過世為止。
