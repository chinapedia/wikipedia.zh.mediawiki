'''約翰·法索'''（John Faso，{{bd|1952年|8月25日}}）是[[美國|美國]][[紐約州|紐約州]]的一位[[共和黨_(美國)|共和黨]]政治家。2016年，他當選為紐約州第19國會選區的美國眾議院議員<ref>{{cite web|url=http://nypost.com/2015/09/20/new-york-gets-another-chance-to-listen-to-john-faso/|title=New York gets another chance to listen to John Faso|first=Post Editorial|last=Board|date=21 September 2015|publisher=}}</ref>。他是一位愛爾蘭人後裔<ref name="nyt1">{{cite news|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2006/10/18/nyregion/18faso.html?_r=3|date=October 18, 2006|newspaper=The New York Times|first=Patrick|last=Healy|title=An Ill-Timed Candidate Believes His Time Is Now|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140703141203/http://www.nytimes.com/2006/10/18/nyregion/18faso.html?_r=3|archivedate=2014年7月3日|deadurl=yes}}</ref>，畢業於[[喬治城大學|喬治城大學]]<ref>Herszenhorn, David M. [http://www.nytimes.com/2006/11/02/nyregion/02schools.html "In the Race for Governor, a Big Divide on School Aid"], ''[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]'', November 2, 2006. Accessed December 6, 2007. "Mr. Faso, whose father worked as a janitor in the Catholic grammar school that he attended on Long Island, went on to Archbishop Molloy High School in Queens and the State University of New York at Brockport."</ref>。
