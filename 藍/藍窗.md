'''藍窗'''（{{lang-mt|it-Tieqa Żerqa}}）是[[马耳他|马耳他]]第二大岛[[戈佐岛|戈佐岛]]<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=http://www.infzm.com/content/123468|title=蓝窗终究还是坍塌了|work=南方周末|accessdate=2017-03-10}}</ref>西面海岸的一個28米-{}-高[[石灰岩|石灰岩]][[天然拱|天然拱]]，是马耳他的主要旅游地标之一，在许多照片中都被用作背景<ref name="times2013-07-19" />。美國電視劇《[[权力的游戏_(电视剧)|權力遊戲]]》第一季曾在藍窗取景。人们可以透过蓝窗看到蓝色的海洋和天空，因此将其命名为蓝窗<ref name=":0" />。

蓝窗位于特殊保护区中，由于海浪長期沖刷石灰岩壁而形成<ref name="europeancommission" /><ref name="report">{{cite report |author=Geoscience Consulting |date=July 2013 |title=Geological and geotechnical report on the Azure Window, Gozo: Rock assessment and recommendations for preservation and conservation. |publisher=Ministry for Sustainable Development, the Environment and Climate Change  |url=https://msdec.gov.mt/en/Documents/Downloads/Azure%20Window%20Report%20July%202013.pdf |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170308143754/https://msdec.gov.mt/en/Documents/Downloads/Azure%20Window%20Report%20July%202013.pdf |archivedate=2017-03-08 |format=PDF 
}}</ref>。蓝窗长期受冲刷，因此持续发生坍塌事件。2017年3月8日，蓝窗坍塌。马耳他的总统对此非常难过：“这一消息令人‘心碎’”<ref name=":1">{{Cite news|url=http://news.xinhuanet.com/world/2017-03/10/c_1120604385.htm|title=马耳他考虑用3D全息图再现“蓝窗”|work=新华网|accessdate=2017-03-10}}</ref>。马耳他政府于次日宣布会考虑用3D结构数码全息图使它再现<ref name=":1" />。

==历史沿革==
{{Location map |Malta |width=240 |relief=yes |lat_deg=36 |lat_min=03 |lat_sec=12.8 |lat_dir=N |lon_deg=14 |lon_min=11 |lon_sec=18.1 |lon_dir=E |caption=在馬耳他的位置}}由于海水和雨水对崖面的侵蚀，藍窗在经过时长约五百年的演变后最终形成<ref name="times2013-07-19">{{cite news |last1=Martin |first1=Ivan |title=The Azure Window is still safe... for now |url=http://www.timesofmalta.com/articles/view/20130719/local/The-Azure-Window-is-still-safe-for-now.478580|work=Times of Malta（马耳他时报） |date=2013-07-19 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160610053139/http://www.timesofmalta.com/articles/view/20130719/local/The-Azure-Window-is-still-safe-for-now.478580 |archivedate=2016-06-10 }}</ref><ref name="europeancommission">{{cite report |title=Dwejra — A Coastal Nature Park |url=http://ec.europa.eu/environment/life/project/Projects/files/laymanReport/LIFE03_TCY_MT_0000047_LAYMAN.pdf |date= |publisher=[[European_Commission|European Commission]] |work=Life Project |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080925122804/http://ec.europa.eu/environment/life/project/Projects/files/laymanReport/LIFE03_TCY_MT_0000047_LAYMAN.pdf |archive-date=2008-09-25 |pages=1–8 |format=PDF
 }}</ref>。1998年，蓝窗和戈佐岛的其他部分被列入联合国教科文组织世界遗产名录的预备名单中<ref name="europeancommission" /><ref>{{cite web |title=Qawra/Dwejra |url=http://whc.unesco.org/en/tentativelists/980/ |publisher=[[UNESCO|UNESCO]] |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160401185143/http://whc.unesco.org/en/tentativelists/980/ |archivedate=2016-04-01 }}</ref>。20世纪80年代至21世纪期间，蓝窗顶板的部分区域倒塌，拱门显著加宽<ref name="report" />。2012年4月，蓝窗外边缘的一块大岩石塌陷，拱门的尺寸进一步增加<ref>{{cite news |title=Part of Dwejra 'Azure window' collapses |url=http://www.timesofmalta.com/articles/view/20120417/local/part-of-dwejra-window-collapses.415805 |work=Times of Malta（马耳他时报） |date=2012-04-17 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160610055600/http://www.timesofmalta.com/articles/view/20120417/local/part-of-dwejra-window-collapses.415805 |archivedate=2016-06-10
 }}</ref>。2013年3月，蓝窗再度发生岩崩。四个月后，一份关于当地地质和岩土的报告发布<ref name="europeancommission" /><ref name="report" />。根据该报告的说法，虽然岩石的坍塌会继续发生，靠近拱门可能会比较危险，蓝窗仍然“相对稳定，并将继续保持多年”<ref name="europeancommission" /><ref name="report" />。[[File:Fels0013.jpg|thumb]]

随后的几年内，岩石的进一步坠落和开裂不断被媒体报道出来<ref>{{cite news |title=A huge part of the Dwejra Window rock collapsed |url=http://www.tvm.com.mt/en/news/a-huge-part-of-the-dwejra-window-rock-collapsed/ |work=[[TVM_(Malta)|TVM]] |date=2016-06-26 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160629060527/http://www.tvm.com.mt/en/news/a-huge-part-of-the-dwejra-window-rock-collapsed |archivedate=2016-06-29
 }}</ref>。当地的渔民不再将船只开到蓝窗附近，他们还在蓝窗附近设立了警告标志以阻止人们走上蓝窗<ref name="europeancommission" /><ref>{{cite news |title=Dwejra Azure Window fissure grows, collapse may be close |url=http://www.independent.com.mt/articles/2015-12-06/local-news/Dwejra-Azure-Window-fissure-grows-collapse-may-be-close-6736150001 |work=[[The_Malta_Independent|The Malta Independent]]|date=2015-12-06 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160623035556/http://www.independent.com.mt/articles/2015-12-06/local-news/Dwejra-Azure-Window-fissure-grows-collapse-may-be-close-6736150001 |archivedate=2016-06-23 }}</ref>。

美國電視劇《[[权力的游戏_(电视剧)|權力遊戲]]》第一季曾在藍窗取景<ref>{{cite news |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20120401123724/http://travel.usatoday.com/destinations/story/2012-04-01/Where-the-HBO-hit-Game-of-Thrones-was-filmed/53876876/1 |archivedate=2012-04-01 |url=http://travel.usatoday.com/destinations/story/2012-04-01/Where-the-HBO-hit-Game-of-Thrones-was-filmed/53876876/1 |title=Where HBO's hit 'Game of Thrones' was filmed |work=[[USA_Today|USA Today]] |first=Josh |last=Roberts |date=2012-04-01
 }}</ref>。過去藍窗在長期海浪侵蝕下，即不時發生塌石，兩邊石墩之間的「窗口」不斷擴大。自2016年12月起，馬耳他政府禁止遊人在藍窗石拱上行走，違者罚款1500歐元<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.timesofmalta.com/articles/view/20161203/local/1500-if-you-step-on-azure-window-from-now-on.632770|title=€1,500 fine if you step on Azure Window from now on|date=2016-12-03|work=Times of Malta（马耳他时报）|accessdate=|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161204125526/http://www.timesofmalta.com/articles/view/20161203/local/1500-if-you-step-on-azure-window-from-now-on.632770|archivedate=2016-12-04
}}</ref>。由於執法不力，仍舊有遊人在拱门顶部行走<ref>{{cite news |title=Dozens trespass across Azure Window without a care in the world |url=http://www.timesofmalta.com/articles/view/20170304/local/watch-dozens-trespass-across-azure-window-without-a-care-in-the-world.641403 |work=Times of Malta（马耳他时报） |date=2017-03-04 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170304155727/http://www.timesofmalta.com/articles/view/20170304/local/watch-dozens-trespass-across-azure-window-without-a-care-in-the-world.641403 |archivedate=2017-03-04 }}</ref>。

2017年3月8日上午，蓝窗因風暴吹襲完全塌入海中<ref name=":0" /><ref name="BBC"/><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.reuters.com/article/us-malta-rock-idUSKBN16F1CY|title=Malta's 'Azure Window' rock formation collapses into the sea|date=2017-03-08|publisher=Reuters|last2=Balmer|first2=Crispian|last1=Schicluna|first1=Chris|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170308164625/http://www.reuters.com/article/us-malta-rock-idUSKBN16F1CY|archive-date=2017-03-08}}</ref><ref name="independent2017-03-082">{{cite news|url=http://www.independent.com.mt/articles/2017-03-08/local-news/Dwejra-Window-collapses-6736171375|title=Dwejra Window collapses; geologist says pillar gave way|date=2017-03-08|work=[[The_Malta_Independent|The Malta Independent]]|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170308161340/http://www.independent.com.mt/articles/2017-03-08/local-news/Dwejra-Window-collapses-6736171375|archivedate=2017-03-08}}</ref>。蓝窗的坍塌引发了全球各大媒体的关注<ref name="BBC">{{cite news |title=Malta's Azure Window collapses into the sea |url=http://www.bbc.com/news/world-europe-39207196 |work=[[BBC_News_Online|BBC News Online]] |date=2017-03-08 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170308143504/http://www.bbc.com/news/world-europe-39207196 |archivedate=2017-03-08 }}</ref><ref name="telegraph">{{cite news |title=Malta's famous Azure Window rock formation has collapsed |url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/travel/destinations/europe/malta/gozo/articles/malta-gozo-azure-window-collapses-erosion/ |work=[[The_Daily_Telegraph|The Daily Telegraph]] |date=2017-03-08 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170308155056/http://www.telegraph.co.uk/travel/destinations/europe/malta/gozo/articles/malta-gozo-azure-window-collapses-erosion/ |archivedate=2017-03-08
 }}</ref>。马耳他的环境和资源局表示这次坍塌是马耳他自然遗产的重大损失<ref name="independent2017-03-08">{{cite news |title=Dwejra Window collapses; geologist says pillar gave way |url=http://www.independent.com.mt/articles/2017-03-08/local-news/Dwejra-Window-collapses-6736171375 |work=[[The_Malta_Independent|The Malta Independent]] |date=2017-03-08 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170308161340/http://www.independent.com.mt/articles/2017-03-08/local-news/Dwejra-Window-collapses-6736171375 |archivedate=2017-03-08 }}</ref>。有关部门还要求游客禁止在蓝窗的所在海域内潜水以保护遗址，残留物被准确的定位、研究后才可能取消此规定<ref name=":1" />。

== 地质情况 ==
{{Wide image|Azure Window 360.jpg|1200px|{{center|蓝窗的全景及其周边环境，摄于2012年}}}}
蓝窗是一个自然拱洞，高度约为28米，跨度约为25米，由两种不同的珊瑚状石灰岩组成<ref name="report2">{{cite report|author=Geoscience Consulting|date=July 2013|title=Geological and geotechnical report on the Azure Window, Gozo: Rock assessment and recommendations for preservation and conservation.|publisher=Ministry for Sustainable Development, the Environment and Climate Change <!-- (Ref: MG 22/2005), Malta -->|url=https://msdec.gov.mt/en/Documents/Downloads/Azure%20Window%20Report%20July%202013.pdf|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170308143754/https://msdec.gov.mt/en/Documents/Downloads/Azure%20Window%20Report%20July%202013.pdf|archivedate=2017-03-08|format=PDF}}</ref>。它位于戈佐內海附近，附近还有一座海岛。此外，戈佐岛东北部约3.7公里处还有一座名为{{lang|mt|Wied il-Mielaħ}} Window的自然拱门。<ref name="europeancommission"/><ref name="report"/><ref>{{cite book |title=Malta – Guide Verdi Europa |url=https://books.google.it/books?id=nMRhDlyU0p4C&pg=PA143 |date=2007 |publisher=Touring Editore |page=143 |isbn=9788836533176 }}</ref>
