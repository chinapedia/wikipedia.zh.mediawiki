'''蓝山'''（'''Blue Mountains'''），[[牙买加|牙买加]]东部山脉。从[[金斯敦|金斯敦]]以北13公里处的[[斯托尼希尔|斯托尼希尔]]向东延伸约50公里到[[加勒比海|加勒比海]]，最高点[[蓝山峰|蓝山峰]]海拔2,256 米  (7402 ft)。<ref>Hamilton, Janice (2005), [http://books.google.com/books?id=vbNjuQbAzt0C&pg=PA10&dq=blue+mountains+jamaica&hl=cs&ei=Tm6DTYX3AcPbgQedu6XJCA&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=6&ved=0CEwQ6AEwBTgK#v=onepage&q=blue%20mountains%20jamaica&f=false ''Jamaica in Pictures''], Lerner Publications, p. 10, ISBN 0-8225-2394-9</ref>山上长着茂密的蕨类植物，面向信风的山坡年均降雨量5000毫米。冬季温度可降至7°C。在当地海拔2,000至5,000英尺出产的[[蓝山咖啡|蓝山咖啡]]世界闻名<ref>{{Cite web| url=http://jamaica-gleaner.com/pages/history/story0029.html| title=Jamaican Coffee - "A Beverage of Distinction"| author=Rebecca Tortello| publisher=A Jamaica Gleaner Feature| accessdate=19 March 2011| deadurl=yes| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110128170512/http://jamaica-gleaner.com/pages/history/story0029.html| archivedate=28 一月 2011}}</ref>。从前在大种植园中种植[[咖啡|咖啡]]，现由小农在谷地种植。
