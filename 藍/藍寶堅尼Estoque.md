'''藍寶堅尼Estoque'''（{{lang-es|Lamborghini Estoque}}）是[[藍寶堅尼|藍寶堅尼]]生產的一輛[[概念車|概念車]]，與當前藍寶堅尼生產的中置引擎雙人座跑車有所不同，為一輛四門[[轎車|轎車]]。

Estoque於2008年[[巴黎車展|巴黎車展]]首次亮相。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://jalopnik.com/5055103/lamborghini-estoque-is-the-lamborghini-urus |title=Lamborghini Estoque Is The Lamborghini Urus?|first=Ben |last=Wojdyla |publisher=Jalopnik.com |date=2008-09-26 |accessdate=2010-12-14}}</ref><ref name=TopGear3289>{{cite web |url=http://www.topgear.com/content/news/stories/3289/ |title=Secret new Lambo revealed |publisher=Top Gear |date=2008-10-02 |accessdate=2010-12-14 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081005033930/http://www.topgear.com/content/news/stories/3289/ |archivedate=2008-10-05 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dieselstation.com/cars/lamborghini-estoque-sedan-sports-car-a959.html |title=Estoque Concept |first=Ketan |last=Patel|publisher=Dieselstation.com |date= 2008-10-03 |accessdate=2010-12-14}}</ref>Estoque是繼[[藍寶堅尼LM002|LM002]]多用途車後的首輛藍寶堅尼前置引擎車。經介紹，它是「一個價值23萬美元四門轎車的概念」。<ref name=EdmundsFirstLook>{{cite web|last=Stahl|first=Andreas|title=Lamborghini Estoque Concept First Look|url=http://www.insideline.com/lamborghini/estoque/lamborghini-estoque-concept-first-look.html|work=Edmunds.com|accessdate=2011-08-03|date=2008-09-30|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100302083113/http://www.insideline.com/lamborghini/estoque/lamborghini-estoque-concept-first-look.html|archivedate=2010-03-02}}</ref>儘管一直有著輿論猜測Estoque的引擎可能會改由V12、V8，甚或是藍寶堅尼品牌總監曼弗雷德·菲茨杰拉德（Manfred Fitzgerald）所提議的混合動力或渦輪增壓柴油引擎，但它當前的配置仍是5.2升V10引擎。<ref name=TopGear3289 />

與其他藍寶堅尼跑車命名法則相同，Estoque得名於[[鬥牛|鬥牛]]運動及其傳統：Estoque是一種傳統上由鬥牛士使用的劍。<ref name=EdmundsFirstLook />

==生產方面的推測==
據2009年3月22日的報導指出，藍寶堅尼Estoque的車產計畫將被取消。藍寶堅尼高層對此作出回應，表示Estoque還未達到生產規劃階段，且是否生產Estoque的決定也一直受到銷售與營銷方面的考慮而延遲。<ref>{{cite web| url= http://www.insideline.com/lamborghini/estoque/il-exclusive-no-green-light-yet-for-lamborghini-estoque.html| title= IL Exclusive: No Green Light - Yet - for Lamborghini Estoque Concept| first= Matt| last= Davis| date= 2009-03-23| work= Edmunds Inside Line| publisher= Edmunds| accessdate= 2011-05-14| deadurl= yes| archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20100122154212/http://www.insideline.com/lamborghini/estoque/il-exclusive-no-green-light-yet-for-lamborghini-estoque.html| archivedate= 2010-01-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite web| url = http://www.topgear.com/uk/car-news/Lamborghini-Estoque-2009-03-25| title = Estoque: dead or alive?| date = 2009-03-25| work= Top Gear.com| publisher = BBC Worldwide| accessdate= 2011-05-14}}</ref>藍寶堅尼董事長[[斯蒂芬·溫克爾曼|斯蒂芬·溫克爾曼]]（Stephan Winkelmann）表明生產四門的藍寶堅尼是有可能的，並指出Estoque是藍寶堅尼在跑車市場外的一個機會。<ref>{{cite web| url = http://www.autocar.co.uk/News/NewsArticle/AllCars/254414/| title= Lambo 4dr saloon is back on| first= John| last = McIlroy| date = 2010-12-03| work       = Autocar.co.uk| publisher= Haymarket Consumer Media| location= Hammersmith, London, UK| accessdate = 2011-05-14}}</ref>然而，溫克爾曼還表示藍寶堅尼沒有任何生產Estoque的計畫，它僅是一輛概念車。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://magazine.windingroad.com/issue/63/|title=Winding Road Magazine | Issue 63|work=WindingRoad.com|date=2010-12|accessdate=2010-12-14|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131203034023/http://magazine.windingroad.com/issue/63/|archivedate=2013-12-03|deadurl=yes}}</ref>
