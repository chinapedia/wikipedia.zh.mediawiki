'''潮汐尾'''是從一個星系延伸至太空中，是薄且延長的[[恆星|恆星]]和[[星際氣體|星際氣體]]區域。潮汐尾的發生是[[交互作用星系|交互作用星系]]之間的[[星系潮汐|星系潮汐]]力作用的結果。星系有潮汐尾的例子力包括[[蝌蚪星系|蝌蚪星系]]和[[雙鼠星系|雙鼠星系]]。潮汐力可以讓星系中為數可觀的氣體彈射進入潮汐尾中，例如[[觸鬚星系|觸鬚星系]]，可見的氣態物質有將近一半是在尾的結構內被發現<ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1086/173373 | title = Modeling the Spatial Distribution of Star Formation in Interacting Disk Galaxies | journal = Astrophysical Journal | volume = 418 | pages = 82–99 | date = 1993 | author = Mihos, Christopher J. | display-authors = etal | bibcode = 1993ApJ...418...82M }}</ref>。在這些星系的潮汐尾內，大約10%有[[恆星形成|恆星形成]]在尾內發生<ref>{{cite journal | title = Remarkable Disk and Off-Nuclear Starburst Activity in the Tadpole Galaxy as revealed by the Spitzer Space Telescope | author = Jarrett, T. H. | display-authors = etal | journal = Astronomical Journal | volume = 131 | issue = 1 | pages = 261–281 | date = 2006 | doi = 10.1086/498414 | bibcode=2006AJ....131..261J|arxiv = astro-ph/0510788 }}</ref>。總體來看，宇宙中所有的恆星形成大約有1%是在潮汐尾內發生<ref>{{cite web | title = 'Shot in the Dark' Star Explosion Stuns Astronomers | url = http://www.nasa.gov/centers/goddard/news/topstory/2007/intergalatic_shot.html | publisher = NASA Goddard Flight Center | date = 18 December 2007 | author = Naeye, Robert | accessdate = 18 June 2010}}</ref>。

一些對交互作用星系有如觸鬚星系，有兩條明顯的尾巴，而其它的只有一條尾巴。大多數的潮汐尾都會隨著所在的星系自轉而略微彎曲。那些似乎是直的潮汐尾實際上可能也是彎曲的，只是從側面看呈現直的狀態。

== 歷史 ==
這種現在被稱為潮汐尾的現象，是[[弗裡茨·茲威基|弗裡茨·茲威基]]在1953年率先進行廣泛的研究<ref>{{cite journal | title = Luminous and dark formations of intergalactic matter | author = Zwicky, Fritz | journal = [[Physics_Today|Physics Today]] | date = April 1953 | volume = 6 | issue = 4 | pages = 7–11 | doi = 10.1063/1.3061224|bibcode = 1953PhT.....6....7Z }}</ref>。有些天文物理學家表示，他們還疑這種擴展不僅僅是潮汐力的結果<ref>{{cite journal | title = The Possibility of a Long Lifetime for Intergalactic Arms | author = Zasov, A. V. | journal = Soviet Astronomy – AJ | volume = 11 | issue = 5 | date = 1968 | bibcode = 1968SvA....11..785Z}}</ref><ref>{{cite conference | title = Cosmic rays and radio waves as manifestations of a hot universe | author = Gold, T. | author2 = Hoyle, F. | last-author-amp = yes | booktitle = Paris Symposium on Radio Astronomy | publisher = Stanford University Press | pages = 583–588 | url = http://books.google.com/books?id=g56aAAAAIAAJ&pg=PA583&lpg=PA583&dq=paris+symposium+on+radio+astronomy+gold+hoyle&source=bl&ots=1tbiV1-YOy&sig=LwVdsRXnLZq_PfT3Bq9TUBvyICQ&hl=en&ei=Z3kbTIKpJoKBlAeP-eXfCQ&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CBIQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q&f=false | date = 1959}}</ref>，包括茲威基自己，也敘述了這種"非正統"的看法<ref>{{cite journal | author = Zwicky, Fritz | title = Intergalactic Bridges | journal = Astronomical Society of the Pacific Leaflets | volume = 9 | pages = 17–24 | date = 1963 | bibcode = 1963ASPL....9...17Z}}</ref>。 [[Boris_Vorontsov-Velyaminov|Boris Vorontsov-Velyaminov]]認為這些尾巴僅由潮汐力造成，是太細也太長（有些大小達到100,000[[秒差距|秒差距]]） <ref>{{cite conference | title = Interaction of Multiple Systems | author = Vorontsov-Velyaminov, B. | booktitle = Problems of Extra-Galactic Research | date = 1962 | publisher = Macmillan Press | pages = 194–200 | url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/full/1962IAUS...15..194V | bibcode = 1962IAUS...15..194V }}</ref>，作為重力應該產生廣泛的扭曲。然而，在1972年，著名的天文學家[[Alar_Toomre|Alar Toomre]]證明了潮汐尾確實是潮汐力造成的<ref>{{cite journal | title = Galactic Bridges and Tails | journal = Astrophysical Journal | volume = 178 | pages = 623–666 | date = 15 December 1972 | author = Toomre, Alan | author2 = Toomre, Juri | last-author-amp = yes | doi = 10.1086/151823 | bibcode=1972ApJ...178..623T}}</ref>。
