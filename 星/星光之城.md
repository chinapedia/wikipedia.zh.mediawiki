《'''星光之城'''》（{{lang-en|''City Of Stars''}}）是[[瑞恩·高斯林|瑞恩·高斯林]]和[[艾玛·斯通|艾玛·斯通]]在电影《[[樂來越愛你|樂來越愛你]]》（{{lang|en|''La La Land''}}）中演唱的歌曲，该曲同时为电影的主题曲，由[[贾斯汀·赫维玆|贾斯汀·赫维玆]]作曲、{{link-en|巴塞克与保罗|Pasek and Paul}}作词<ref>{{cite web|url=http://variety.com/2016/film/awards/listen-to-city-of-stars-from-the-la-la-land-soundtrack-arriving-dec-9-1201920139/|title=Listen to ‘City of Stars’ From the ‘La La Land’ Soundtrack, Arriving Dec. 9|accessdate=2017-01-09|date=2016-11-17|work=[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]]|first=Kristopher|last=Tapley}}</ref>。獲得[[第74屆金球獎|第74屆金球獎]]最佳原創歌曲。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://time.com/4622027/golden-globes-2017-winners/|title=Golden Globes 2017: See All the Winners|accessdate=2017-01-09|date=2017-01-08|work=[[Time_(magazine)|Time]]}}</ref>

== 背景 ==
該單曲由[[達米恩·查澤雷|達米恩·查澤雷]]在[[哈佛大學|哈佛大學]]的同班同學[[贾斯汀·赫维玆|贾斯汀·赫维玆]]編排；他先前也曾為查澤雷的兩部電影創作曲目<ref name="LATimes">{{cite web|url=http://www.latimes.com/entertainment/movies/la-et-mn-la-la-land-emma-stone-damien-chazelle-20160907-snap-story.html|title=With 'La La Land,' Emma Stone and director Damien Chazelle aim to show that original musicals aren't all tapped out|accessdate=2016-10-10|author=Rebecca Reegan|date=2016-09-12|work=[[Los_Angeles_Times|Los Angeles Times]]}}</ref>。該配樂歌詞是由{{link-en|巴塞克與保羅|Pasek and Paul}}寫作<ref name="VOGUE">{{cite web|url=http://www.vogue.com/13490922/emma-stone-november-cover-la-la-land-movie-ryan-gosling/|title=Emma Stone Takes the Biggest Leap of Her Career With La La Land|accessdate=2016-10-16|author=Jason Gay|date=2016-10-14|work=[[Vogue_(magazine)|Vogue]]}}</ref>。
