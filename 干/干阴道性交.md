'''干阴道性交'''（{{lang-en|dry sex}}）是一种在女性[[陰道乾燥|陰道乾燥]]的情况下进行的[[性交|性交]]。可以通过使用催欲草药、家用洗涤剂、消毒剂<ref name="cirp.org">{{cite web|url=http://www.cirp.org/library/disease/HIV/baleta1/ |title=Concern voiced over "dry sex" practices in South Africa |publisher=Cirp.org |date= |accessdate=2011-07-05}}</ref>，擦干[[阴道|阴道]]<ref name="popline.org">{{cite web |url=http://www.popline.org/docs/1123/108468.html |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2012-01-04 |0=2009-05-09 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090509214542/http://www.popline.org/docs/1123/108468.html |archivedate=2009-05-09 }}</ref>，在阴道中放置树叶<ref name="popline.org"/>等方式清除阴道分泌液。<ref name=IFPP>International Family Planning Perspectives, Volume 24, Number 2, June 1998, [http://www.alanguttmacher.org/pubs/journals/2409398.html Vaginal Drying Agents and HIV Transmission] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110224032801/http://www.alanguttmacher.org/pubs/journals/2409398.html |date=2011-02-24 }} by Karen E. Kun.</ref>

干燥使得阴道更紧，从而能使男性增加性快感。<ref>http://www.jstor.org/pss/3775349</ref>一些坚持干阴道性交的男性认为“湿润”的女性是不守贞操的。<ref>{{cite journal |doi=10.1016/S0968-8080(96)90004-X |author=Sunanda Ray, Nyasha Gumbo, Michael Mbizvo |title=Local Voices: What Some Harare Men Say about Preparation for Sex |journal=Reproductive Health Matters |volume=4 |issue=7 |pages=34–45 |jstor=3775349 |publisher=Reproductive Health Matters|date=May 1996}}</ref>然而，干阴道性交对女性来说十分痛苦。<ref name="cirp.org"/>这种性行为常见于[[撒哈拉以南非洲|撒哈拉以南非洲]]<ref name="cirp.org" /><ref name="popline.org" />，亦在[[南美洲|南美洲]][[苏里南|苏里南]]女性中存在<ref>{{cite journal |author=van Andel T, de Korte S, Koopmans D, Behari-Ramdas J, Ruysschaert S |title=Dry sex in Suriname |journal=J Ethnopharmacol |volume=116 |issue=1 |pages=84–8 |pmid=18083316 |doi=10.1016/j.jep.2007.11.003 |date=February 2008}}</ref>。

== 健康风险 ==
干阴道性交被认为与[[艾滋病|艾滋病]]在撒哈拉以南非洲的高发有关。<ref name="cirp.org" />这种行为在很多方面都增加了性交双方感染艾滋病<ref name="cirp.org"/><ref name=IFPP />等[[性传播疾病|性传播疾病]]的可能性。例如缺乏阴道分泌液会导致阴道组织的撕伤，进而增加感染艾滋病的风险。<ref>{{cite news |author=Hyena, Hank |title="Dry sex" worsens AIDS numbers in southern Africa |url=http://www.salon.com/health/sex/urge/world/1999/12/10/drysex/ |publisher=Salon.com |date=1999-12-10 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.salon.com/health/sex/urge/world/1999/12/10/drysex/ |title="Dry sex" worsens AIDS numbers in southern Africa - Africa |publisher=Salon.com |date=1999-12-10 |accessdate=2011-07-05}}</ref>擦干阴道还可能会擦除能够抵抗性传播疾病的[[乳酸杆菌|乳酸杆菌]]。此外，干阴道性交时因摩擦力的增加会加大[[避孕套|避孕套]]破裂的风险。而干阴道性交导致的阴道炎症与外伤性损害也都会增加性传播疾病的传播可能性。
