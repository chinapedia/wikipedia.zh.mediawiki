'''坎開河'''（[[英語|英語]]：Kankai River；{{lang-ne|कन्काई नदी}}；{{lang-hi|कंकई नदी}}），是流經[[尼泊爾|尼泊爾]]與[[印度|印度]]的跨界河流，為[[默哈嫩達河|默哈嫩達河]]的支流。河名「坎開」來自[[梵語|梵語]]詞彙「KANAKA」，意為黃金。

梅吉河發源於尼泊爾的{{link-en|瑪哈巴拉特山脈|Lower Himalayan Range}}。它流經尼泊爾東南部，隨後流入印度[[比哈爾邦|比哈爾邦]]，最終在[[基商甘傑縣|基商甘傑縣]]注入默哈嫩達河。<ref>{{cite web| url=http://emily7787.wikispaces.com/file/view/Five+Themes+of+Geography+Master.pdf| title=Five Themes of Geography| work=Rivers| first=| last=| publisher=| accessdate=2010-05-22| deadurl=yes| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110718051221/http://emily7787.wikispaces.com/file/view/Five+Themes+of+Geography+Master.pdf| archivedate=2011-07-18}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url = http://www.newworldencyclopedia.org/entry/Gurkha_War |  title= Gurkha War| work = | first=  | last=  | publisher = New World Encyclopaedia |accessdate = 2010-05-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://kishanganj.bih.nic.in/river.htm |title=Kishanganj district |work= |first= |last= |publisher=Kishanganj district administration |accessdate=2010-05-22 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100408074040/http://kishanganj.bih.nic.in/river.htm |archivedate=2010-04-08 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url =http://www.india9.com/i9show/Kankai-River-42058.htm    | title = Kankai River  | work =     | first=  | last=  | publisher =india9  |accessdate = 2010-05-23}}</ref>
