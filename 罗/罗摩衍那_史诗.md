'''罗摩衍那: 史诗'''（Ramayana: The Epic）是一部2010年电脑动画电影<ref name=standard>{{Cite news|url=http://www.business-standard.com/india/news/warner-bros-to-distribute-ramayana/408038/|last=Chakraborty|first=Swarup|title=Warner Bros to distribute Ramayana|date=14 September 2010|accessdate=18 September 2010|work=[[Business_Standard|Business Standard]]|publisher=Business Standard Limited}}</ref>，由印度的[[Maya_Digital_Media|Maya Digital Media]]制片。<ref name=hr>{{Cite journal|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/hr/content_display/asia/news/e3ia02b5fdf7bb7dfc160982c1f9a1886f0|last=Bhushan|first=Nyay|title=Warner India to release 'Ramayana'|date=14 September 2010|accessdate=18 September 2010|work=[[The_Hollywood_Reporter|The Hollywood Reporter]]|publisher=[[e5_Global_Media|e5 Global Media]]|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20130125110308/http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/hr/content_display/asia/news/e3ia02b5fdf7bb7dfc160982c1f9a1886f0|archivedate=2013年1月25日|df=}} </ref> 导演为[[Chetan_Desai|Chetan Desai]]<ref name=hr/><ref name=standard/>，制片人为[[Ketan_Mehta|Ketan Mehta]]，<ref name=msn>{{Cite web|url=http://entertainment.in.msn.com/gallery.aspx?cp-documentid=4393520|title=''Ramayana: The Epic'' slideshow|date=17 September 2010|accessdate=18 September 2010|work=[[MSN|MSN]] Entertainment India|publisher=[[Microsoft|Microsoft]]}}</ref> 2010年10月15日上映。<ref name=hr/>

== 剧情 ==
电影改编自印度史诗《[[罗摩衍那|罗摩衍那]]》，讲述[[罗摩|罗摩]]从出生至战胜十首魔王[[罗波那|罗波那]]的故事。<ref name=express>{{Cite news|url=http://www.expressindia.com/latest-news/Warner-Bros-to-distribute-RamayanaThe-Epic/681690/|author=Agencies|title=Warner Bros to distribute Ramayana-The Epic|date=15 September 2010|accessdate=18 September 2010|work=''ExpressIndia.com''|publisher=The Indian Express Limited|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121010101156/http://www.expressindia.com/latest-news/Warner-Bros-to-distribute-RamayanaThe-Epic/681690/|archivedate=2012年10月10日}}</ref>
