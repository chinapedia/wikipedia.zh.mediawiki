'''罗伯聃''' ({{lang-en|Robert Thom}}; {{bd|1807年||1846年}}) 是19世纪英格兰的[[翻译家|翻译家]]，为广州的[[怡和洋行|怡和洋行]]工作。在第一次鸦片战争时期被借调到英国武装部队。

==生平==
1807年出生在英国，罗伯聃在[[怡和洋行|怡和洋行]]工作时学会的中文。<ref name="FAIRBANK">{{Cite book|title=Trade and Diplomacy on the China Coast: The Opening of the Treaty Ports , 1842-1854, Volumes 1-2|first=John King|last=Fairbank|ISBN=0-674-89835-4|year=1953|publisher=Harvard University Press|page=126}}</ref>

==文学作品==
1840年罗伯聃将[[伊索寓言|伊索寓言]] 翻译为中文，据说仅有的能说流利北京话的西方学者。<ref name="KASKE">{{Cite book|url= https://books.google.com/books?id=okhrBBmnHVQC&pg=PA68|last=Kaske|first=Elisabeth|title=The Politics of Language in Chinese Education, 1895-1919|publisher=Brill|year=2007|page=68|isbn= 978-9004163676}}</ref> 他还翻译了明朝的''The Lasting resentment of Miss Keaou Lwan Wang, a Chinese Tale'' 以及为中文学生出过几本教科书。<ref name="PICHON">{{Cite book|title=China Trade and Empire: Jardine, Matheson & Co. and the Origins of British Rule in Hong Kong 1827-1843|first=Alain|last=Le Pichon|year=2006|publisher=Oxford University Press|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=0ql7CqEV6d4C&pg=PA184|page=184|ISBN=978-0197263372}}</ref>
