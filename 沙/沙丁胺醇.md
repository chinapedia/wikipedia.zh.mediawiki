<!-- Definition and medical uses -->
'''沙丁胺醇'''（Salbutamol），常見商品名'''泛得林'''（Ventolin）<ref name=brands/>，為一種[[支气管扩张药|用於擴張肺部大型及中型氣管]]的藥物<ref name=AHFS2015>{{cite web|url=https://www.drugs.com/monograph/albuterol.html|title=Albuterol|last=|first=|date=|website=Drugs.com|publisher=The American Society of Health-System Pharmacists|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151208181121/http://www.drugs.com/monograph/albuterol.html|archive-date=2015-12-08|dead-url=no|accessdate=Dec 2, 2015|df=}}</ref>。本品可用於治療[[氣喘|氣喘]]相關病症，例如氣喘急性發作、{{tsl|en|exercise-induced bronchoconstriction|運動誘發性支氣管收縮}}，以及[[慢性阻塞性肺病|慢性阻塞性肺病]]（COPD）<ref name=AHFS2015/>。本品亦可用於治療[[高鉀血症|高血鉀]]<ref name=Mah2005>{{cite journal|last2=Smith|first2=WA|last3=Lo|first3=DS|last4=Tsoi|first4=K|last5=Tonelli|first5=M|last6=Clase|first6=CM|date=18 April 2005|title=Emergency interventions for hyperkalaemia.|journal=The Cochrane Database of Systematic Reviews|volume=|issue=2|pages=CD003235|doi=10.1002/14651858.CD003235.pub2|pmid=15846652|via=|last1=Mahoney|first1=BA}}</ref>。沙丁胺醇常配合{{tsl|en|metered-dose inhaler|定量吸入器}}或{{tsl|en|nebulizer|霧化器}}使用，但亦可製成藥錠或[[靜脈注射|靜脈注射]]<ref name=AHFS2015/><ref name=starkey>{{cite journal|last2=Mulla|first2=H|last3=Sammons|first3=HM|last4=Pandya|first4=HC|date=September 2014|title=Intravenous salbutamol for childhood asthma: evidence-based medicine?|url=https://derbyhospitals-nhs.archive.knowledgearc.net/bitstream/handle/123456789/297/%2814%29%20Arch%20Dis%20Child.pdf?sequence=1&isAllowed=y|journal=Archives of Disease in Childhood|volume=99|issue=9|pages=873–7|doi=10.1136/archdischild-2013-304467|pmid=24938536|via=|last1=Starkey|first1=ES|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170908175627/https://derbyhospitals-nhs.archive.knowledgearc.net/bitstream/handle/123456789/297/%2814%29%20Arch%20Dis%20Child.pdf?sequence=1&isAllowed=y|archivedate=2017-09-08|df=}}</ref>。吸入劑型約15分鐘內即可見效，效用可持續2至6小時<ref name=AHFS2015/>。

<!-- Side effects and mechanism -->
常見副作用包含搖晃、頭痛、[[心跳过速|心跳过速]]、暈眩，以及焦慮等等<ref name=AHFS2015/>。嚴重副作用則包含惡化{{tsl|en|bronchospasm|支氣管痙攣}}、[[心律不整|心律不整]]，以及[[低鉀血症|低血鉀]]<ref name=AHFS2015/>。[[妊娠|妊娠]]和[[哺乳|哺乳]]期間可以給藥，但其安全性尚未完全明瞭<ref name=AHFS2015/><ref>{{cite book|last1=Yaffe|first1=Sumner J.|title=Drugs in pregnancy and lactation : a reference guide to fetal and neonatal risk|date=2011|publisher=Wolters Kluwer Health/Lippincott Williams & Wilkins|location=Philadelphia|isbn=9781608317080|page=32|edition=9th|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=OIgTE4aynrMC&pg=PA32|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151208061118/https://books.google.ca/books?id=OIgTE4aynrMC&pg=PA32|archivedate=2015-12-08|df=}}</ref>。本品屬於速效型[[Β2肾上腺素受体激动药|β<sub>2</sub>肾上腺素受体激动药]]，可促進[[平滑肌|平滑肌]]舒張<ref name=AHFS2015/>。

<!-- History, society, and culture -->
沙丁胺醇最早於1967年於英國發現，並於1969年上市<ref>{{cite book|last1=Landau|first1=Ralph|title=Pharmaceutical innovation : revolutionizing human health|date=1999|publisher=Chemical Heritage Press|location=Philadelphia|isbn=9780941901215|page=226|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=IH4lPs6S1bMC&pg=PA226|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151208072936/https://books.google.ca/books?id=IH4lPs6S1bMC&pg=PA226|archivedate=2015-12-08|df=}}</ref>。1982年美國核准用於醫藥<ref name=AHFS2015/>。本品列名於[[世界卫生组织基本药物标准清单|世界卫生组织基本药物标准清单]]。為基礎公衛體系必備藥物之一<ref name=WHO19th>{{cite web|title=WHO Model List of Essential Medicines (19th List)|url=http://www.who.int/medicines/publications/essentialmedicines/EML_2015_FINAL_amended_NOV2015.pdf?ua=1|work=World Health Organization|accessdate=8 December 2016|date=April 2015|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161213052708/http://www.who.int/medicines/publications/essentialmedicines/EML_2015_FINAL_amended_NOV2015.pdf?ua=1|archivedate=13 December 2016|df=}}</ref>。本品為[[通用名药物|通用名药物]]<ref name=AHFS2015/>。2014年，每瓶含有200劑量的吸入器於[[发展中国家|发展中国家]]的價格約介於1.12至2.64美金之間<ref name=ERC2014>{{cite web|title=Salbutamol|url=http://mshpriceguide.org/en/single-drug-information/?DMFId=708&searchYear=2014|website=International Drug Price Indicator Guide|accessdate=5 December 2015}}</ref>。在美國，每月劑量約界於25至50美金之間<ref name=Ric2015>{{cite book|last1=Hamilton|first1=Richart|title=Tarascon Pocket Pharmacopoeia 2015 Deluxe Lab-Coat Edition|date=2015|publisher=Jones & Bartlett Learning|isbn=9781284057560|page=448}}</ref>。
