'''[[沙烏地阿拉伯|沙烏地阿拉伯]]'''在[[瓦哈比派|瓦哈比派]][[伊斯蘭教法|伊斯蘭教法]]的規範下，擁有嚴格的'''[[死刑|死刑]]制度'''，執行數量也位居世界前列。根据[[人权观察|人权观察]]组织统计，2015年执行次数高达158，<ref>{{cite web|title= Saudi Arabia ends 2015 with one final execution|url= https://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/middle-east/saudi-arabia-ends-2015-with-one-final-execution-a6793506.html|publisher= The Independent |accessdate=October 20, 2017}}</ref>，根据[[国际特赦组织|国际特赦组织]]统计，2016年则为至少154次<ref>{{cite web|title= Death sentences and executions in 2016|url=https://www.amnesty.org/en/documents/act50/5740/2017/en/ |publisher= amnesty.org |accessdate=August 21, 2017}}</ref>，2017年为146次<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.amnesty.org/en/latest/news/2018/04/death-penalty-facts-and-figures-2017/|title=The Death Penalty in 2017: Facts and Figures|publisher=[[Amnesty_International|Amnesty International]]|date=2018-04-12|accessdate=2018-07-16}}</ref> 。

行刑時採取公開的方式，最常見办法是将受刑人用劍[[斬首|斬首]]<ref name= BBCexecutioner>{{cite web |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/world/middle_east/2966790.stm |title=Saudi executioner tells all |date=5 June 2003 |publisher=BBC News |accessdate=11 July 2011}}</ref>和[[石刑|用乱石投击至死]]<ref name= FRD306>{{cite book |title=Saudi Arabia A Country Study |last=Federal Research Division |year=2004 |isbn=9781419146213 |page=304}}</ref>，其次是把受刑人釘死於[[十字架|十字架]]上<ref name= Miethe>{{cite book |title=Punishment: a comparative historical perspective  |last=Miethe |first=Terance D. |coauthors=Lu, Hong |year=2004 |isbn=9780521605168 |page=63}}</ref>。

2007年至2010年間，共有345起執行公開處決的報告<ref name= Punishment>U.S. State Department Annual Human Rights Reports for Saudi Arabia 2007-2010: {{cite web |url=http://www.state.gov/g/drl/rls/hrrpt/2010/nea/154472.htm |title=2010 Human Rights Report: Saudi Arabia |date = 8 April 2011  |publisher = U.S. State Department |accessdate = 11 July 2011}}; {{cite web |url = http://www.state.gov/g/drl/rls/hrrpt/2009/nea/136079.htm |title = 2009 Human Rights Report: Saudi Arabia |date = 11 March 2010 |publisher = U.S. State Department |accessdate = 11 July 2011}}; {{cite web |url=http://www.state.gov/g/drl/rls/hrrpt/2008/nea/119126.htm |title=2008 Human Rights Report: Saudi Arabia |date=25 February 2009 |publisher=U.S. State Department |accessdate=11 July 2011}}; {{cite web |url=http://www.state.gov/g/drl/rls/hrrpt/2007/100605.htm |title=2007 Human Rights Report: Saudi Arabia |date=11 March 2008 |publisher=U.S. State Department |accessdate=11 July 2011}}</ref>，最近一起因巫術而死刑定罪的報告出現於2007年，但最後並未成功執行<ref name= Punishment/>。2007年至2010年間沒有石刑的報告<ref>{{cite book |title=Islamic law and legal system: studies of Saudi Arabia |last=Vogel |first=Frank E. |year=1999 |isbn=9789004110625 |page=246}}</ref>，但1981至1992年間共有4起執行石刑報告<ref name= Punishment/>。

=== 伊斯蘭教法 ===
觸犯下列三項教法，可判處死刑<ref name= Otto166>{{cite book |title=Sharia Incorporated: A Comparative Overview of the Legal Systems of Twelve Muslim Countries in Past and Present |last=Otto |first=Jan Michiel |coauthors= |year=2010 |isbn=9789087280574 |page=166}}</ref> ：

:::::::::*[[Hudud|Hudud]]：根據《[[古蘭經|古蘭經]]》中的具體犯罪<ref name= Otto166/>。變節、通姦和[[雞姦|雞姦]]可判處死刑<ref>{{cite book |title=Comparative Criminal Justice Systems |last=Dammer, |first=Harry R. |coauthors=Albanese, Jay S. |year=2010 |isbn=9780495809890 |page=56}}</ref>。

:::::::::*[[基沙斯|Qisas]]：「[[以眼還眼|以眼還眼]]」的報復性法律<ref name= Otto166/>。謀殺罪屬於此法，但可以由被害者家屬選擇受刑方式，分別是死刑或索取巨額賠償金<ref name= washingtonpost>{{cite news |title=Saudis Face Soaring Blood-Money Sums |url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/content/article/2008/07/26/AR2008072601785.html |newspaper=The Washington Post |date=27 July 2008 |accessdate=11 July 2011}}</ref>。

:::::::::*[[Tazir|Tazir]]：一般的類別，包括國家的一般法規，其中部分可處以死刑，例如[[販毒|販毒]]<ref name= Otto166/>。

在沙烏地，有3種定罪的方式<ref name= Kritzer>{{cite book |title=Legal Systems of the World: A Political, Social, and Cultural Encyclopedia |last=Kritzer  |first=Herbert M. |year=2002  |isbn=9781576072318  |page=1415 }}</ref>：第一個是不在被逼問的情況下的自白<ref name= Kritzer/>；第二個是兩名男性所說出的證詞，但若是觸犯Hudud，自白供認也是必需的<ref name= Kritzer/>；最後一個則是[[宣誓|宣誓]]自己沒有犯罪<ref name= Kritzer/>，這在當地宗教社會中為一個很嚴肅的行為<ref name= Kritzer/>，拒絕宣誓將被視為認罪的表現<ref>{{cite book |title=King Faisal and the modernisation of Saudi Arabia | last=Beling | first=Willard A. | year=1980 | isbn=0709901372 | page=117}}</ref>。
