'''沙鼠屬'''（學名'''''Meriones'''''）是[[沙鼠|沙鼠]]中的一類，常被人們當成[[寵物|寵物]]飼養。這類生物分佈於[[北非|北非]]到[[蒙古|蒙古]]之間。

==分類==
<!--Pavlinov et al. (1990) considered the genus to belong to the [[Tribe_(biology)|(sub)tribe]] Rhombomyina, a group of mostly Asian gerbils.  Tong's (1989) hypothesized relationship is consistent and Pavlinov et al.'s (1990) taxonomy was adopted by Musser and Carleton (2005).  McKenna and Bell (1997) use the subtribe name Merionina (presumably arguing that it has priority) for the same group.  

In particular, ''Meriones'' is thought by Pavlinov et al. (1990) to be sister ([[Cladistics|cladistically]] closest) to the genus ''[[Brachiones|Brachiones]]''.  Tong (1989), however, suggests that ''[[Psammomys|Psammomys]]'' is its sister genus.  -->
