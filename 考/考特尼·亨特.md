'''考特尼·亨特''' (Courtney Hunt，1964年出生)  是一名美国女性电影导演和编剧。她于2008年执导的处女作《[[冰原之心|冰原之心]]》赢得[[圣丹斯影展|圣丹斯影展]]评审团大奖<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.screendaily.com/reviews/us-americas/features/frozen-river/4037881.article|title=Frozen River - Reviews - Screen|work=[[Screen_International|Screen International]]|first=David|last=D'Arcy|date=March 20, 2008}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.vulture.com/2008/12/indie_spirit_tk.html|title='Rachel' and 'River' Smell Like Independent Spirit|work=[[Vulture.com|Vulture.com]]|first=Mark|last=Graham|date=December 2, 2008}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://articles.chicagotribune.com/2008-01-27/news/0801260689_1_audience-award-documentary-prize-sundance-film-festival|title=Sundance honors 'River' and 'Water'|work=[[Chicago_Tribune|Chicago Tribune]]|first=Michael|last=Phillips|date=January 27, 2008}}</ref>。

==事业==
2008年，她的執導電影《[[冰原之心|冰原之心]]》由[[梅莉莎·李歐|梅莉莎·李歐]]主演<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2008/08/01/movies/01froz.html|title=Only a Few More Smuggling Days Left Before Christmas? It's Not a Wonderful Life|work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|first=Stephen|last=Holden|date=August 1, 2008}}</ref>，获得[[第81届奥斯卡金像奖|第81届奥斯卡金像奖]]最佳原创剧本和最佳女主角两项提名。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.theguardian.com/film/2009/feb/06/first-sight-courtney-hunt-oscars|title=First sight: Courtney Hunt|work=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]|first=Cath|last=Clarke|date=February 5, 2009}}</ref>

她将执导[[丹尼爾·克雷格|丹尼爾·克雷格]]主演的惊悚片《[[完全真相|完全真相]]》<ref>{{cite web|url=http://variety.com/2014/film/news/renee-zellweger-joining-daniel-craig-in-the-whole-truth-1201127423/|title=Renee Zellweger Joining Daniel Craig in 'The Whole Truth'|work=[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]]|first=Dave|last=McNary|date=March 6, 2014}}</ref>，但克雷格因檔期衝突而退出，男主角改由[[基努·李維|基努·李維]]演出。
