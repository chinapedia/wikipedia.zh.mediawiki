《'''男孩'''》({{lang-en|'''Boy'''}})是爱尔兰摇滚乐团[[U2樂團|U2]]的首張專輯，1980年10月20日發行，由[[史提夫·李利懷特|史提夫·李利懷特]]監製。《男孩》在發行後獲得廣泛好評，從此開啟了U2的輝煌史<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.u2.com/music/index.php?album_id=3&type=lp | title = ''Boy'' Review | publisher = Hot Press | accessdate = 2007-09-06|date=October 1980}}; {{cite web | url = http://www.u2.com/music/index.php?mode=full&news_id=1073&news_type=review | title = ''Boy'' New Music Express review | publisher = New Music Express | date = 1980-10-25 | accessdate = 2007-09-06}}; {{cite web | url = http://www.u2.com/music/index.php?mode=full&news_id=1074&news_type=review | title = ''Boy'' Billboard review | publisher = [[告示牌_(雜誌)|Billboard]] | date = 1980-09-30 | accessdate = 2007-09-06}}; {{cite web | url = http://www.u2.com/music/index.php?mode=full&news_id=1075&news_type=review | title = ''Boy'' The Washington Post review | publisher = [[The_Washington_Post|The Washington Post]] | date = 1980-09-30 | accessdate = 2007-09-06}}</ref>。隨著專輯熱烈好評後，U2展開了生涯首場歐美巡迴演出─Boy Tour<ref>de la Parra (2003), pages 16,17</ref>，此後專輯的巡迴演出也成了U2的慣例。2003年，專輯被《[[滾石雜誌|滾石雜誌]]》獲選為「音樂史上五百大專輯」第417名<ref>{{cite web|title= "The RS 500 Greatest Albums of All Time"|url=http://www.rollingstone.com/news/story/5938174/the_rs_500_greatest_albums_of_all_time/|publisher=滾石雜誌|accessdate = 2009-05-15}}</ref>。
