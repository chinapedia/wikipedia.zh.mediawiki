2013|work=The Futon Critic|accessdate=May 21, 2013}}</ref>。在2013年5月8日，FOX電視公司訂了這部[[单镜头模式|單鏡攝影]]喜劇13集<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/fox-comedy-series-orders-chris-519678|title=Fox Comedy Series Orders: Chris Meloni Entry, 'Brooklyn Nine-Nine,' 'Enlisted,' 'Us & Them'|date=May 8, 2013|last=Abrams|first=Natalie|work=[[电视指南_(美国杂志)|电视指南 (美国杂志)]]|accessdate=May 21, 2013}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.deadline.com/2013/05/fox-picks-up-andy-samberg-chris-meloni-comedies-us-them-and-enlisted-almost-doubles-new-series-volume/|title=Fox Picks Up Andy Samberg & Chris Meloni Comedies, ==摘要==
本劇的故事背景是在1991年的南加州，本劇的內容聚焦在傑克‧唐列維，一個一本正經的退役軍人。當他的老婆決定要去讀法學院的時候，他成為全職家庭主夫。他使用非正統的教育方式來讓他的孩子，法蘭奇和瑞秋乖乖聽話。

==演員==
*[[克里斯托弗·米洛尼|克里斯多夫・馬洛尼]] 飾演傑克‧唐列維醫生（Dr. Jack Dunlevy） a blunt talking, former military man, now a doctor, who, after many years, is assuming the parent responsibilities now that his wife is going back to law school.
* {{tsl|en|Rachael Harris|瑞秋‧哈里斯}}　飾演喬安‧唐列維（Joanne Dunlevy）, a loving wife and mom, who is going back to law school. She longs to have her home be the neighborhood "hangout house."
*[[Connor_Buckley|Connor Buckley]] as Frankie Dunlevy, a typical, awkward freshman teenager, who is usually the focus point of all of Jack's parenting mistakes.
*{{tsl|en|Claudia Lee|克勞蒂亞‧李}} 飾演瑞秋‧唐列維（Rachel Dunlevy）, a super conceded, over confident, super popular senior in high school. She can't stand any of her brother, Frankie's, friends.

*[[Kevin_Hernandez_(actor)|Kevin Hernandez]] 飾演喬治（George） Frankie's best friend since 2nd grade. He has a huge crush on Rachel.

*[[Tyler_Foden|Tyler Foden]] 飾演麥基（Mikey）, one of Frankie's best friends, who comes from a broken home. This is his second year as a freshman.
*[[Mell_Bowser|Mell Bowser]] 飾演克雷格（Craig）
==評價==
《男人成長錄》在[[網路電影資料庫|網路電影資料庫]]（IMDB）得到8.5分（滿分為10分），且[[Metacritic|Metacritic]]根據24個「讚賞」的評價給了該劇65分（滿分為100分）<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.metacritic.com/tv/surviving-jack |title=Surviving Jack Season 1 |work=Metacritic |accessdate=April 22, 2014}}</ref>。在另外一個評論網站[[爛番茄|爛番茄]]上這齣劇有著68%的成績，並依據28個評論獲得平均6.2的評價（滿分為10分）<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.rottentomatoes.com/tv/surviving-jack/ |title=Surviving Jack: Season 1 (2014) |publisher=Rotten Tomatoes |accessdate=April 22, 2014}}</ref>。
