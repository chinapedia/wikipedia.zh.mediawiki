'''牛津大學伊斯蘭研究中心'''（{{lang-en|'''Oxford Centre for Islamic Studies'''}}，縮寫：'''OCIS'''）是[[牛津大學|牛津大學]][[Recognised_Independent_Centre|已認可的獨立中心]]，成立於1985年，目的是爲了鼓勵[[伊斯蘭研究|伊斯蘭研究]]。其贊助人是[[查爾斯_(威爾斯親王)|查爾斯王子]]。<ref>{{cite web|title=About OCIS|publisher=Oxford Centre for Islamic Studies|url=http://www.oxcis.ac.uk/about.html|date=2008-12-15|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071028192204/http://www.oxcis.ac.uk/about.html|archivedate=2007-10-28}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=HRH visits the Oxford Centre for Islamic Studies new building|publisher=The Prince of Wales|date=9 February 2005|url=http://www.princeofwales.gov.uk/newsandgallery/news/hrh_visits_the_oxford_centre_for_islamic_studies_new_buildin_566.html|accessdate=2008-12-15|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20070619191733/http://www.princeofwales.gov.uk/newsandgallery/news/hrh_visits_the_oxford_centre_for_islamic_studies_new_buildin_566.html|archivedate=2007年6月19日}} </ref>

在牛津中東部[[牛津大學莫德林學院|莫德林學院]]附近，該研究中心還有另一棟建築，<ref>[http://www.oxcis.ac.uk/newbuilding.html New building] {{Wayback|url=http://www.oxcis.ac.uk/newbuilding.html |date=20131220100717 }}, Oxford Centre for Islamic Studies, UK.</ref> 其風格糅合了傳統牛津建築及伊斯蘭教藝術，<ref>[http://www.oxcis.ac.uk/newbuilding2.html New building 2] {{Wayback|url=http://www.oxcis.ac.uk/newbuilding2.html |date=20140107082411 }}, Oxford Centre for Islamic Studies, UK.</ref> 其中還包含一個[[清真寺|清真寺]]。<ref>[http://www.oxcis.ac.uk/newbuilding3.html New building 3] {{Wayback|url=http://www.oxcis.ac.uk/newbuilding3.html |date=20130109025126 }}, Oxford Centre for Islamic Studies, UK.</ref>
