'''羅斯維爾'''（{{lang en|'''Roswell'''}}）是[[美國|美國]][[新墨西哥州|新墨西哥州]]東南部[[沙維什縣_(新墨西哥州)|沙維什縣 (新墨西哥州)]]的城市，鄰近聖克萊爾(St. Clair)湖。羅斯維爾經常跟[[UFO|UFO]]（不明飛行物體）及[[外星人|外星人]]拉上關係<ref>[http://www.iufomrc.com/ Roswell International UFO Museum & Research Center]</ref>，當中最為人熟悉的是[[羅斯威爾飛碟墜毀事件|羅斯威爾飛碟墜毀事件]]。<ref>Carey, Tom, and Schmitt, Don. [https://web.archive.org/web/20040413230419/http://www.scifi.com/ufo/roswell/ UFOlogy Resource Center: ''The Roswell Report''], via [[Syfy|SciFi.com]]. Archived from [http://www.scifi.com/ufo/roswell/ the original] on April 13, 2004.</ref>此外羅斯威爾也是[[好萊塢|好萊塢]]巨星[[黛米·摩尔|黛米·摩尔]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.okmagazine.com/news/demi-moores-long-lost-siblings-we-can-save-her|title=Demi Moore's Long-Lost Siblings: We Can Save Her|publisher=Star via OK!| date=February 11, 2012}}</ref>和知名[[鄉村音樂|鄉村音樂]]歌手[[約翰·丹佛|約翰·丹佛]]的出生地。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.wargs.com/other/denver.html |title='&#39;Ancestry of John Denver'&#39; compiled by William Addams Reitwiesner |publisher=Wargs.Com |accessdate=May 9, 2011}}</ref>

==地理==
根據[[美國2000年人口普查|2000年人口普查]]，本城面積為{{convert|29.0|sqmi|sqkm|2}}，其中{{convert|28.9|sqmi|sqkm|2}}為陸地，{{convert|0.04|sqmi|sqkm|2}}為水域。<ref name ="Gazetteer files">{{cite web|title=US Gazetteer files 2010|url=http://www.census.gov/geo/www/gazetteer/files/Gaz_places_national.txt|publisher=[[United_States_Census_Bureau|United States Census Bureau]]|accessdate=2013-01-25}}</ref>

===氣候===
羅斯維爾擁有[[半乾旱氣候|半乾旱氣候]]，其夏季炎熱，冬季寒冷。本城最低紀錄溫度為1905年2月13日的{{convert|-29|F|0}}<ref>[http://www.weather.com/weather/wxclimatology/daily/USNM0267?climoMonth=2 February Daily Averages for Roswell, NM ''Weather.com'' Retrieved June 12, 2012]</ref>，而最高紀錄溫度則為1994年6月27日的{{convert|114|F|0}}。<ref>[http://www.weather.com/weather/wxclimatology/daily/USNM0267?climoMonth=6 June Daily Averages for Roswell, NM ''Weather.com'' Retrieved June 12, 2012]</ref>
