'''羅緞'''是一種緯線比經線粗、因而顯示出棱紋的[[平紋布|平紋]][[織物|織物]]，<ref name="america">{{cite book |title= Textiles in America, 1650-1870|last= Montgomery|first= Florence M.|author2=Linda Eaton|year= 2007|publisher= W. W. Norton & Company|isbn= 0-393-73224-X|page= 252|url= http://books.google.com/books?id=1emELgbrnS0C&dq|accessdate=July 10, 2009}}</ref><ref>Kate Heinz Watson, ''Textiles and Clothing'' American School of Home Economics, Chicago: 1907: pp 91 [http://www.gutenberg.org/files/21534/21534.txt]</ref> 它的表面不光滑，但是卻十分耐用。<ref>{{cite book |title= Household Sewing with Home Dressmaking|last= Banner|first= Bertha|year= 1898|publisher= Longmans, Green, and co|page= 106|url= http://books.google.com/books?id=IhMlyt7IuGsC&dq|accessdate=July 10, 2009}}</ref> 黑色的羅緞最為常見，但也有其他顏色的羅緞。

羅緞可以用[[絲|絲]]、[[羊毛|羊毛]]、[[尼龍|尼龍]]等各種材質織成，<ref name="idiot">{{cite book |title= The Complete Idiot's Guide to Crafts With Kids|last= Lockwood|first= Georgene|year= 1998|publisher= Alpha Books|isbn= 0-02-862406-8|page= 86|url= http://books.google.com/books?id=pZtLSzCJPRIC&pg=PA86&dq|accessdate=July 10, 2009}}</ref> 也有混合各種次材料織成的羅緞。<ref name="america"/> 它在17世紀時就廣泛運用與西方的各種衣物織造中了。<ref>{{cite book |title= Fashion and fancy|last= de Winkel |first=Marieke |year=2006 |publisher= Amsterdam University Press|isbn= 90-5356-917-0|url= http://books.google.com/books?id=WEQy3xkbU9cC&dq|accessdate=July 10, 2009}}</ref>
