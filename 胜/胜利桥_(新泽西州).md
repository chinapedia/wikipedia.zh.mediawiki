'''胜利桥'''（{{lang-en|Victory Bridge}}）是美国[[新泽西州|新泽西州]]的一个跨过[[拉里坦河|拉里坦河]]的桥梁，位于[[米德尔塞克斯县_(新泽西州)|米德尔塞克斯县]]，是[[35号新泽西州州道|35号新泽西州州道]]的过河通道。<ref>[http://www.state.nj.us/transportation/press/2005/102705.shtm Lettiere announces completion of Route 35 Victory Bridge and Victory Circle Project] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20061206113432/http://www.state.nj.us/transportation/press/2005/102705.shtm |date=2006-12-06 }}, [[New_Jersey_Department_of_Transportation|New Jersey Department of Transportation]] press release dated October 27, 2005. "The original Route 35 Victory Bridge was built in 1926, connecting the municipalities of Perth Amboy City and Sayreville Borough in Middlesex County. The 360-foot structure was the longest swing span bridge in New Jersey at the time it was built. The original bridge was commemorated to World War I veterans of New Jersey."</ref>
