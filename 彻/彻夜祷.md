== 仪轨 ==
在举行彻夜祷时，作为彻夜祷一部分的晚祷或晨祷时间会较他们分别各自举行时的时长有所变化。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://biserica.org/Publicatii/ServiceBook/Gvespers.htm|title=The Order of Great Vespers|work=The Priest's Service Book|publisher=Diocese of the South|accessdate=2007-03-06| archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20070330103441/http://biserica.org/Publicatii/ServiceBook/Gvespers.htm| archivedate= 30 March 2007 <!--DASHBot-->| deadurl= no}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://biserica.org/Publicatii/ServiceBook/Smatins.htm|title=The Order of Matins on Sundays and Feast Days|work=The Priest's Service Book|publisher=Diocese of the South|accessdate=2007-03-06}}</ref> 在一般教堂中，常常省略部分礼仪。(比如简略甚至略去诵经及晨祷时的[[圣人传|圣人传]]诵读)。因此，一般的彻夜祷大约持续两至两个半小时。
