'''英国航空2276号班机'''是一班由[[拉斯维加斯|拉斯维加斯]][[麦卡伦国际机场|麦卡伦国际机场]]飞往[[伦敦盖特威克机场|伦敦盖特威克机场]]的定期国际旅客航班，该航班2015年9月8日在[[拉斯维加斯|拉斯维加斯]][[麦卡伦国际机场|麦卡伦国际机场]]起飞时着火，导致机组人员中止起飞，乘客和机组被迫疏散。 该航班搭载了157名乘客和13名机组人员，乘客包括[[利氏家族|利氏家族]]一家 {{tlx|來源請求}}。飞机的左一 [[GE90|GE90]]引擎碎片飞出。<ref>{{cite web|title=NTSB Issues Update on the British Airways Engine Fire at Las Vegas|url=http://www.ntsb.gov/news/press-releases/Pages/PR20150910.aspx|website=NTSB|accessdate=2015-09-10}}</ref>

飞机已在2016年2月被维修好，随后于3月2日再次投入运营。

== 事件过程 ==
[[File:Boeing_777-236ER_‘G-VIIO’_British_Airways_(27343290862).jpg|thumb]]]]
当地时间15点53分，飞机离开T3航站楼E3登机口，16时12分与07L跑道起飞，就在起飞过程中，引擎着火。 <ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.flightstats.co.uk/HistoricalFlightStatus/flightStatusByFlight.do?id=596641778&airline=BA&flightNumber=2276&departureDate=2015-09-08|title=Flight Info|work=FlightStats|accessdate=2015-09-09}} (Login required)</ref>

当飞行员注意到这一灾难性的情况后，立即终止起飞，疏散乘客<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.fox5vegas.com/story/29984963/british-airways-flight-catches-fire-at-mccarran-airport|title=13 hospitalized after British Airways fire in Las Vegas|work=Fox 5 Vegas|accessdate=2015-09-09|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160302204613/http://www.fox5vegas.com/story/29984963/british-airways-flight-catches-fire-at-mccarran-airport|archive-date=2016-03-02|dead-url=yes}}</ref> 所有乘客及机组成员都逃脱了，只有一些在疏散过程中受了轻伤。当飞行员决定中断起飞时，飞机的速度已达到90英里/小时<ref>{{Cite web|title = What happened to BA Flight 2276?|url = http://www.itv.com/news/2015-09-09/what-happened-to-ba-flight-2276/|website = ITV News|access-date = 2016-02-23}}</ref> ，远远低于183MPH的起飞速度。

飞行员发出求救信号后5分钟，机场的紧急救援人员就扑灭了大火。 14人在疏散过程中受轻伤，随后被送往日落医院（Sunrise Medical Center）治疗.<ref name=phipps>{{Cite web|title = British Airways plane catches fire at Las Vegas airport|url = http://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2015/sep/09/british-airways-plane-catches-fire-at-las-vegas-airport|website = The Guardian|accessdate = 2015-09-09|first = Claire|last = Phipps}}</ref><ref name="auto1">{{cite web|title=British Airways fire: Jet's suppression system didn't work, source says|url=http://www.cnn.com/2015/09/09/us/las-vegas-british-airways-fire/index.html|publisher=CNN|date=2015-09-09|accessdate=2015-09-09}}</ref> 大火烧穿了货舱门并损坏了引擎。

[[联邦航空管理局|联邦航空管理局]]（FAA）认定大火是由左侧的[[GE90|GE90]]引擎引起的。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.ainonline.com/aviation-news/air-transport/2015-09-08/engine-failure-causes-fire-british-airways-boeing-777 | title=Engine Failure Causes Fire on British Airways Boeing 777 |first=Charles |last=Alcock |date=2015-09-08 |publisher=AIN Online|accessdate=2015-09-09}}</ref> 由于飞机逆风起飞，风将火焰吹向飞机的燃油仓。飞机停在了原地，但大火对飞机的结构造成了很大的破坏。<ref>{{cite web|first=Greg|last=Waldron|url=https://www.flightglobal.com/news/articles/pictures-unclear-whether-ba-777-engine-failure-was-416536/|title=Unclear whether BA 777 engine failure was contained|work=flightglobal.com|accessdate=2015-09-09}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Kaminski-Morrow|first1=David|title=Weather data suggests crosswind at time of BA 777 fire|url=https://www.flightglobal.com/news/articles/weather-data-suggests-crosswind-at-time-of-ba-777-fi-416546/|accessdate=2015-09-09|work=Flightglobal|date=2015-09-09}}</ref> 该飞机装备了引擎灭火器，但并未起效。<ref name="auto1"/>

07L跑道被迫关闭了4小时，部分进港航班被取消<ref>{{cite web|title=Accident: British Airways B772 at Las Vegas on Sep 8th 2015, rejected takeoff due to engine fire|url=http://avherald.com/h?article=48c10434&opt=0|website=The Aviation Herald|accessdate=2015-09-09}}</ref>
