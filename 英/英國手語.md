'''英國手語'''（'''British Sign Language'''，[[縮寫|縮寫]]：'''BSL'''）是一門在[[英國|英國]]使用的[[手語|手語]]，主要有約125,000英國成人和約20,000兒童會使用這種語言。<ref>IPSOS Mori GP Patient Survey 2009/10</ref> 2011年據威爾士和英格蘭的聾人報告，兩地使用英國手語為主要語言的人數為15,000。<ref>[http://www.ons.gov.uk/ons/dcp171778_297002.pdf 2011 Census: Quick Statistics for England and Wales, March 2011], Accessed 17 February 2013</ref>

英國聾啞人社群使用手語的記錄可以追溯至1570年，英國手語就是通過對這些原始手語進行改良而成的。<ref>[http://www.britishscienceassociation.org/web/news/reportsandpublications/magazine/magazinearchive/SPAArchive/SPAJune06/Feature6June06.htm Deaf people and linguistic research] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110604000057/http://www.britishscienceassociation.org/web/News/ReportsandPublications/Magazine/MagazineArchive/SPAArchive/SPAJune06/Feature6June06.htm |date=2011-06-04 }} ,Professor Bencie Woll ,Director of the Deafness, Cognition and Language Research Centre based at University College London. '''British Science Association'''. Accessed October 2010.</ref><ref>Kyle & Woll (1985).''[http://books.google.com/books?id=Gcy4MhmLhdkC&printsec=frontcover&dq=Sign+Language:+the+study+of+deaf+people+and+their+language&hl=en&ei=Si-uTL2gB5SL4gbT5430Bg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CDYQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q&f=false Sign Language: the study of deaf people and their language]'' Cambridge University Press, p. 263</ref> 直到1940年代為止英國聾啞學校裡還主要強調教授[[讀唇法|讀唇法]]和手指拼寫，而不鼓勵使用手語。1970年代開始寬容度才逐漸提高，不過由於老一輩和年輕一輩所學不同而造成了交流問題。<ref>[http://books.google.com/books?id=Gcy4MhmLhdkC&printsec=frontcover&dq=Sign+Language:+the+study+of+deaf+people+and+their+language&hl=en&ei=Si-uTL2gB5SL4gbT5430Bg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=book-preview-link&resnum=1&ved=0CDsQuwUwAA#v=onepage&q&f=false Sign Language: The Study of Deaf People and Their Language]</span>  J. G. Kyle,  B. Woll, G. Pullen,  F. Maddix, Cambridge University Press, 1988. </span>ISBN <span dir="ltr">0521357179</span>
</ref>

英國手語與[[美國手語|美國手語]]差異極大，相對於幾乎完全一致的[[口語|口語]]（即[[英語|英語]]）來說，手語中的完全相同的表達只有31%，同族的表達有44%。<ref>McKee, D. & G. Kennedy (2000). Lexical Comparison of Signs from American, Australian, British, and New Zealand Sign Languages. In K. Emmorey and H. Lane (Eds), "The signs of language revisited: an anthology to honor Ursula Bellugi and Edward Klima". Lawrence Erlbaum Associates, Inc.</ref> 此外英國手語與[[愛爾蘭手語|愛爾蘭手語]]也不一樣。英國手語使用[[主體-評論|主體-評論]]結構，<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.cohearentvision.org.uk/deaf-users/grammatical-structure-of-british-sign-language/ |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2013-03-20 |0=2013-04-23 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6GHhQOcWx?url=http://www.cohearentvision.org.uk/deaf-users/grammatical-structure-of-british-sign-language/ |archivedate=2013-05-01 }}</ref> 標準語序是[[賓主動語序|賓主動]]，名詞短語中心語前置。<Ref>[http://books.google.com/books?id=lUsCbazTICIC&printsec=frontcover&source=gbs_atb#v=onepage&q&f=false]</ref>
