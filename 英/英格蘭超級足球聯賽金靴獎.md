'''英格蘭超級聯賽金靴獎'''（{{lang-en|'''Premier League Golden Boot'''}}），简称英超金靴奖，是一项授予[[英格蘭超級足球聯賽|英格蘭超級足球聯賽]]神射手的[[足球|足球]]奖项，以表彰在单个英超赛季中取得最多进球的球员。因赞助商的缘故，该奖项从1994年到2001年被称为嘉宁金靴奖（{{lang|en|Carling Golden Boot}}），从2001到2014被称为{{link-en|巴克莱信用卡|Barclaycard}}金靴奖（{{lang|en|Barclaycard Golden Boot}}），在2013年被称为'''[[巴克莱银行|巴克莱]]金靴奖'''（{{lang|en|Barclays Golden Boot}}）<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.premierleague.com/fapl.rac?command=forwardOnly&nextPage=enSeasons |title=Premier League Awards |publisher=Premier League |accessdate=1 January 2014 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20061210142537/http://www.premierleague.com/fapl.rac?command=forwardOnly&nextPage=enSeasons |archivedate=10 December 2006 |deadurl=yes }}</ref><ref name=history>{{cite web |title=History of the Premier League |url=http://www.premierleague.com/en-gb/about/history.html |publisher=Premier League |accessdate=1 January 2014 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120421110805/http://www.premierleague.com/en-gb/about/history.html |archivedate=21 April 2012 }}</ref>。该奖项的获得者除了得到一座奖杯外，通常会获得每球1000英镑的奖金以捐献给他们选择的慈善机构<ref name=ronaldo>{{cite news |title=Ronaldo & Ferguson win top awards |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/m/man_utd/7401795.stm |work=BBC Sport|publisher=BBC|date=14 May 2008 |accessdate=3 January 2014 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Van Nistelrooy has final say |url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/sport/2404029/Van-Nistelrooy-has-final-say.html |date=14 May 2003 |accessdate=1 January 2014 |newspaper=The Daily Telegraph }}</ref>，而[[2012–13赛季英格兰超级联赛|2012–13赛季]]的金靴得主[[罗宾·范佩西|罗宾·范佩西]]以26球收到了30000英镑<ref name=retains/><ref>{{cite news|title=Van Persie picks up Premier League Golden Boot|url=http://www.goal.com/en-gb/news/2896/premier-league/2013/05/22/3996508/van-persie-picks-up-premier-league-golden-boot|date=22 May 2013|accessdate=15 January 2014|first=Joe|last=Doyle|work=Goal.com|publisher=Perform Group}}</ref>。
