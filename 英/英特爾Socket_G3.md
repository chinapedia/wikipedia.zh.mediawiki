'''Intel Socket G3'''， 也稱為'''rPGA 946B/947'''<ref name="datasheet">[http://www.intel.com/content/dam/www/public/us/en/documents/datasheets/4th-gen-core-family-mobile-m-h-processor-lines-vol-1-datasheet.pdf Datasheet for Haswell M CPUs, see section 8.1, "Package Mechanical Specifications"]</ref>或'''FCPGA 946'''<ref name="chip list">[http://ark.intel.com/Search/Advanced?s=t&SocketsSupported=FCPGA946 Intel ARK list of CPUs with socket FCPGA946]</ref>，是[[英特爾|英特爾]]設計的[[CPU插座|處理器插座]]及對應處理器的管脚陣列，支援[[Haswell微架構|Haswell微架構]]、[[Broadwell微架構|Broadwell微架構]]系列中可插拔式的移動處理器。相容於單品項管理於型號字尾有一個「M」的處理器。<ref name="chip list"/>

Socket G3設計是用於取代[[Socket_G2|Socket G2]]（也稱為rPGA 988B）。Socket G3插座有947個脚位，而處理器基板上的插針陣列的引腳有946針或947針。<ref name="datasheet"/><ref>[http://www.hardwareluxx.de/index.php/artikel/hardware/notebooks/26507-test-intel-core-i7-4700mq-haswell-im-notebook.html?start=1 Test of PGA946 CPUs Intel Core i7-4700MQ]</ref>

使用Socket G3的晶片組是英特爾的8系列[[PCH|PCH]]晶片組的行動版本，代號「Lynx Point」。<ref>{{cite web|last=Knight |first=Shawn |url=http://www.techspot.com/news/50783-leaked-intel-slides-detail-haswells-lynx-point-chipset.html |title=Leaked Intel slides detail Haswell's Lynx Point chipset |publisher=TechSpot |date= |accessdate=2014-01-19}}</ref>
