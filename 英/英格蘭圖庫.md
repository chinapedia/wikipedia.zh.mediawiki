英格蘭圖庫是2001年2月日英国所有新增的登录建筑的在线摄影记录，包含了超过323000张彩色图像。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.buildingconservation.com/directory/images-england |publisher=Building Conservation |title=English Heritage-Images of England  |accessdate=22 July 2011}}</ref>
==目的==
成立英格兰图库的目的是拍摄当时英国的所有登录建筑并在网上保存。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/arts/gallery/2007/sep/26/imagesofenlgand  |publisher=The Guardian |title=Images of England |date=26 September 2007 |accessdate=22 July 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web  |url=http://www.leicester.gov.uk/your-council-services/ep/planning/conservation/listed-buildings/images-of-england/  |title=Images of England: English Heritage National Monuments Record  |accessdate=22 July 2011  |deadurl=yes  |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110222193652/http://www.leicester.gov.uk/your-council-services/ep/planning/conservation/listed-buildings/images-of-england/  |archivedate=22 February 2011  }}</ref> It is part of the [[Historic_England_Archive|Historic England Archive]] of England’s historic environment.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://archive.historicengland.org.uk/ |title=Historic England Archive |accessdate=25 April 2015}}</ref>它是Historic England Archive中英国的历史环境的一部分。<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.historicengland.org.uk/listing/the-list/ |title=The List |publisher=[[Historic_England|Historic England]] |accessdate=25 April 2015}}</ref>
