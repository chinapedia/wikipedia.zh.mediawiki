本文讲述英国的[[埃博拉出血热|埃博拉出血热]]病例。截至目前该国共有三宗埃博拉感染病例，一宗因{{link-en|实验室安全|Laboratory safety|实验室事故}}导致，其他两宗出自治疗过[[2014年西非埃博拉病毒疫情|西非埃博拉病毒疫情]]受害者的归国医护人员。

== 1976年针刺病例==
1976年11月5日，[[威尔特郡|威尔特郡]]{{link-en|波登唐|Porton Dow}}前微生物研究机构实验室技术人员杰弗里·普拉特，处理非洲的埃博拉病毒样本时，不慎被污染的针头扎伤<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mirror.co.uk/news/uk-news/ebola-last-british-man-survive-4103288|title=Ebola: Last British man to survive deadly virus says public must be warned of danger |date=2014-08-25|publisher=Daily Mirror|author=Sam Rkaina}}</ref>。他得到了[[干扰素|人体干扰素]]和恢复期血清的治疗，病情正常并完全康复<ref>{{cite journal | author = Emond RT, Evans B, Bowen ET ''et al.'' | year = 1977 | title = A case of Ebola virus infection | url = | journal = British Medical Journal | volume = 2 | issue = 6086| pages = 541–544 | doi=10.1136/bmj.2.6086.541 | pmid=890413 | pmc=1631428}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.cdc.gov/vhf/ebola/outbreaks/history/chronology.html |work=CDC |title=Outbreaks Chronology: Ebola Virus Disease}}</ref>。

== 归国医护人员 ==
2014年12月29日，从塞拉利昂经由[[穆罕默德五世国际机场|穆罕默德五世国际机场]]和[[伦敦希思罗机场|伦敦希思罗机场]]回到[[格拉斯哥|格拉斯哥]]的英国救援人员宝莲·卡夫基（Pauline Cafferkey），在格拉斯哥{{link-en|加特内韦尔总医院|Gartnavel General Hosptial}}确诊患上埃博拉<ref name="BBC-30628349">{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-scotland-30628349|title=Ebola case confirmed in Glasgow hospital|work=BBC News|date=2014-12-29}}</ref><ref name=guardian2014dec29>{{cite web|url=http://www.theguardian.com/world/2014/dec/29/ebola-case-confirmed-glasgow-healthcare-worker|title=Ebola case confirmed in Glasgow|publisher=The Guardian|date=2014-12-29|author=Severin Carrell, Libby Brooks and Lisa O'Carroll}}</ref><ref name="Ebola_UK_29_12">{{cite news|title=Hero nurse Pauline Cafferkey could have contracted deadly Ebola at Christmas Day service|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/uknews/scotland/11317226/Ebola-in-Scotland-Hero-nurse-Pauline-Cafferkey-spoke-movingly-of-saving-lives.html|publisher=The Telegraph|date=2014-12-30}}</ref>。她曾在塞拉利昂{{link-en|凯瑞镇|Kerry Town}}的埃博拉治疗中心工作<ref name=guardiandec29>{{cite web|url=http://www.theguardian.com/world/2014/dec/29/ebola-case-confirmed-glasgow-healthcare-worker|title=Ebola case confirmed in Glasgow|publisher=The Guardian|date=2014-12-29|author=Severin Carrell, Libby Brooks and Lisa O'Carroll}}</ref>。

格拉斯哥的治疗未果后，她被{{link-en|诺斯霍特皇家空军|RAF Northolt}}的护送下，前往伦敦{{link-en|皇家義診醫院|Royal Free Hospital}}的{{link-en|高级别隔离病房|High-level isolation unit}}接受长期治疗<ref name=guardian2014dec29/><ref name=BBC-30629397/>。苏格兰政府发言人表示病例发现时，处在非常早的感染阶段，一般民众的风险“低到可以忽略不计<ref name=guardian2014dec29/>”。接触者追踪已在跟她乘坐航班从伦敦前往格拉斯哥的乘客身上开展<ref name=BBC-30629397>{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.com/news/uk-scotland-30629397|title=Ebola nurse Pauline Cafferkey transferred to London unit|date=2014-12-30|publisher=BBC News}}</ref>。医护人员表示她当时的状态，跟阶段的预期一样的好<ref>{{cite news |url= http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-30648879 |title= Ebola: Nurse Pauline Cafferkey 'as well as hoped for' |date= 2015-01-01 |work= BBC News |accessdate= 2015-01-01}}</ref>。

2015年1月4日，皇家免费医院表示病人的状态已经恶化到“危急”状态<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-30666265|title=BBC News - UK Ebola nurse Pauline Cafferkey 'in critical condition'|work=BBC News|accessdate=2015-01-01 |date= 2015-01-03}}</ref> ，后来她的健康稳定下来，1月12日解除危急状态<ref name=BBC-30657485>{{cite web|last1=Gallagher|first1=James|title=UK Ebola nurse Pauline Cafferkey has 'stabilised'|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/health-30657485|website=BBC News|accessdate=2015-10-05}}</ref><ref name=12Jan>{{cite news |url= http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/health-30783537 |title= Ebola nurse no longer critically ill |work= BBC News |date=2015-01-12|accessdate=2015-01-12}}</ref>。卡夫基输了威廉·普利的血，治疗时还用了试验性药物<ref name=12Jan/>。1月24日，被宣布无感染的卡夫基出院<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-30967337|title=Ebola nurse: Pauline Cafferkey 'happy to be alive'|publisher=BBC News|date=2015-01-24}}</ref>。

== 入境防控的争议 ==
鉴于宝莲·卡夫基顺利通过边境管制，从希思罗机场搭乘国内航班飞抵格拉斯哥的事实，目前英国入境关口的量体温并询问一系列问题的筛查方案遭到口诛笔伐。当宝莲登上飞机时，她的病症还没有发展，很难诊断<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-scotland-30637049|title=BBC News - Doctor travelling with Ebola nurse: Screening 'chaotic'|work=BBC News|date=2014-12-30}}</ref>。2014年12月，{{link-en|英国公共卫生部|Public Health England}}计划审查筛查程序<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.theguardian.com/world/2014/dec/30/ebola-airport-screening-reviewed|title=Ebola screening to be reviewed after doctor attacks ‘inadequate’ measures|author=Josh Halliday|work=the Guardian|date=2014-12-30}}</ref>。

== 2015年1月针刺病例 ==
2015年1月下旬，一名英国医护人员送往皇家免费医院接受进行观察，他因在塞拉利昂照顾埃博拉病人时不慎被{{link-en|被针刺伤|Neddlestick injury}}<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.channel4.com/news/ebola-british-military-health-worker-needle-royal-free|title= British health worker returned to UK for Ebola monitoring | publisher=Channel 4 News|date=2015-01-31}}</ref><ref name=guardian20150131/>。截至发稿，他没有出现埃博拉症状<ref name=guardian20150131>{{cite web|url=http://www.theguardian.com/world/2015/jan/31/ebola-military-healthcare-worker-returns-uk-needle-injury|title=Ebola: military healthcare worker returns to UK after needle injury | date=2015-01-31 | publisher=The Guardian}}</ref>。

几天后，另一名被针头扎伤的英国医护人员从塞拉利昂撤离后，也住在皇家免费医院接受观察。其他在该医院接受观察的人员至今仍未有埃博拉症状<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.theguardian.com/world/2015/feb/02/second-ebola-worker-uk-needlestick-royal-free-diagnosis|title=Second UK military Ebola worker flies back home after needlestick injury | date =2015-02-02| publisher= The Guardian}}</ref>。
