'''偽齒鳥屬'''（[[屬|屬]]名：{{lang|la|''Pelagornis''}}，字面意思即為「來自大海的鳥」）又譯'''遠洋鳥'''，是生存於[[漸新世|漸新世]]至[[上新世|上新世]]時期的[[偽齒鳥科|偽齒鳥科]]鳥類，演化關係上可能接近現今的[[鸛|鸛]]或[[鵜鶘|鵜鶘]]<ref>{{ cite journal | author = Bourdon, E. | title = "Osteological evidence for sister group relationship between pseudo-toothed birds (Aves: Odontopterygiformes) and waterfowls (Anseriformes)" | journal = Naturwissenschaften | volume = 92 | issue = 12 | year = 2005 | pages = 586–591 | issn = 0028-1042 | pmid = 16240103 | doi = 10.1007/s00114-005-0047-0 }}</ref><ref>{{ cite book | author = Mayr, G. | title = "Paleogene Fossil Birds" | publisher = Springer-Verlag, Heidelberg & New York | year = 2009 | isbn = 3-540-89627-9 }}</ref>，以體型而言是極其巨大的[[海鳥|海鳥]]。偽齒鳥屬在五大洲當中除了亞洲外皆有化石發現記錄。
