'''偽解析函數'''（Pseudoanalytic function）是美國數學家{{le|Lipman Bers|Lipman Bers}}在1950年至1956年提出的一種广义[[解析函数|解析函数]]<ref>{{Citation | last1=Bers | first1=Lipman | title=Partial differential equations and generalized analytic functions | jstor=88348 | mr=0036852 |url=http://www.pnas.org/content/37/1/42.full.pdf | format=pdf | pmc=1063147 | year=1950 | journal=Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences of the United States of America | issn=0027-8424 | volume=36 | pages=130–136 | doi=10.1073/pnas.36.2.130 | pmid=16588958}}
</ref><ref>{{Citation | last1=Bers | first1=Lipman | title=Partial differential equations and generalized analytic functions. Second Note | jstor=88213 | mr=0044006 |url=http://www.pnas.org/content/36/2/130.full.pdf | format=pdf |pmc=1063297 | year=1951 | journal=Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences of the United States of America | issn=0027-8424 | volume=37 | pages=42–47 | doi=10.1073/pnas.37.1.42 | pmid=16588987}}
</ref><ref>{{Citation | last1=Bers | first1=Lipman | title=Theory of pseudo-analytic functions | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=79dWAAAAMAAJ | publisher=Institute for Mathematics and Mechanics, New York University, New York | mr=0057347 | year=1953}}
</ref><ref>{{Citation | last1=Bers | first1=Lipman | title=An outline of the theory of pseudoanalytic functions | url=http://www.ams.org/journals/bull/1956-62-04/S0002-9904-1956-10037-2/S0002-9904-1956-10037-2.pdf | format=pdf | mr=0081936 | year=1956 | journal=Bulletin of the American Mathematical Society | issn=0002-9904 | volume=62 | pages=291–331 | doi=10.1090/s0002-9904-1956-10037-2}}</ref>
，可以滿足較弱型式的[[柯西－黎曼方程|柯西－黎曼方程]]。而美國數學家{{le|阿貝·吉爾巴特|Abe Gelbart}}也和Lipman Bers建立了[[流體動力學|流體動力學]]上的偽解析函數理論<ref name="nyt">{{citation|url=http://www.nytimes.com/1994/09/12/obituaries/abraham-gelbart-mathematician-82.html|newspaper=New York Times|title=Abraham Gelbart, Mathematician, 82|date=September 12, 1994}}.</ref><ref name="rag">{{citation|url=http://www.ams.org/notices/199501/people.pdf|first=Allyn|last=Jackson|title=Mathematics People: Abraham Gelbart 1911–1994|journal=Notices of the AMS|date=January 1995|page=59|volume=42|issue=1}}.</ref><ref>{{citation|url=http://www.ams.org/notices/199501/bers.pdf|first=William|last=Abikoff|title=Remembering Lipman Bers: Lipman Bers|journal=Notices of the AMS|date=January 1995|pages=8–18|volume=42|issue=1}}.</ref>
