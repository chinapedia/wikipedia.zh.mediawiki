'''紅翅黑鸝'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Agelaius phoeniceus'''''}}，英文名：{{lang|en|'''red-winged blackbird'''}}），亦作'''紅翅烏鶇'''、'''美洲红翼鸫'''，是[[黑鸝屬|黑鸝屬]]的一種鳥類，主要分布於[[北美洲|北美洲]]。分有多個族群，北部的族群在冬季飛往南部過冬，此外在[[薩爾瓦多|薩爾瓦多]]西部、[[洪都拉斯|洪都拉斯]]西北部和[[哥斯達黎加|哥斯達黎加]]西北部有獨立的族群。數量極多，是北美洲數量最多的陸禽，學者對這種鳥也有極為透徹的研究<ref>{{ cite web |author=Yasukawa, Ken |coauthors=Searcy, William A.|url=http://bna.birds.cornell.edu/bna/species/184/articles/introduction |work=Birds of North America Online |title=Red-winged Blackbird (''Agelaius phoeniceus'') |publisher=Cornell Lab of Ornithology |location=Ithaca |year=1995 |editor=A. Poole |accessdate=2012-11-13}}</ref><ref name=BirdsOfPenn>{{cite book|last=McWilliams|first=Gerald M.|title=The Birds of Pennsylvania |year=2000 |publisher=Cornell University Press |location=Ithaca, NY |isbn=0801436435 |coauthors=Brauning, Daniel W.}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Dolbeer|first=Richard A.|year=2008|url=http://icwdm.org/handbook/birds/Blackbirds/BlackbirdsBiology.aspx |title=Blackbirds and their Biology |work=Internet Center for Wildlife Damage Management |accessdate=16 April 2013}}</ref>。紅翅黑鸝的[[兩性異形|兩性異形]]特征十分明顯，雄性通體黑色、有紅色的肩部和黃色色帶，雌性通體深褐色，無其他明顯特征。紅翅黑鸝是雜食動物，以植物種子和昆蟲為食。

== 分類==
紅翅黑鸝是[[黑鸝屬|黑鸝屬]]（''Agelaius''）下的十一個種之一，而該屬所在的[[擬黃鸝科|擬黃鸝科]]（Icteridae）下所有生物均只生活在[[南美洲|南美洲]]和[[北美洲|北美洲]]<ref name="ITIS">{{cite web| title = ''Agelaius phoeniceus''| publisher = Integrated Taxonomic Information System| url = http://www.itis.gov/servlet/SingleRpt/SingleRpt?search_topic=TSN&search_value=179045&source| accessdate = 2008-06-30}}</ref>。[[卡爾·林奈|卡爾·林奈]]在《[[自然系統|自然系統]]》中將其學名定爲''Oriolus phoeniceus''<ref>{{cite book |last=Linnaeus |first=C |authorlink=Carolus Linnaeus |title=Systema naturae per regna tria naturae, secundum classes, ordines, genera, species, cum characteribus, differentiis, synonymis, locis. Tomus I. Editio duodecima, reformata |publisher=Holmiae. (Laurentii Salvii). |year=1766 |page=161 |language=la |url=http://dz1.gdz-cms.de/index.php?id=img&no_cache=1&IDDOC=265100 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150319115418/http://dz1.gdz-cms.de/index.php?id=img&no_cache=1&IDDOC=265100 |archivedate=2015-03-19 }}</ref>，但後來在1816年由[[路易·讓·皮埃爾·維埃洛特|路易·讓·皮埃爾·維埃洛特]]移入黑鸝屬<ref>{{cite book|last=Vieillot |first=L.P. |year=1816 |title=Analyse D'Une Nouvelle Ornithologie Elementaire |trans_title=A New Analysis of Elementary Ornithology |page=33 |language=fr}}</ref>。其屬名來自[[古希臘語|古希臘語]]的拉丁轉寫詞''agelaios''，意思是“一羣中的”，種加詞''phoeniceus''來自拉丁語，意思是“深紅色”<ref>{{cite web| last = Neff| first = John| title = Red-Winged Blackbird (''Agelaius phoeniceus'')| publisher = Northern State University| year = 1997| url = http://www.northern.edu/natsource/BIRDS/Redwin1.htm| accessdate = 2008-06-30| archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20080219215107/http://www.northern.edu/natsource/BIRDS/Redwin1.htm| archivedate = 2008-02-19| deadurl = yes}}</ref>。

紅翅黑鸝有多個亞種，大多數看起來極爲相似。只有加利福尼亞州和墨西哥中部的''A. p. gubernator''之間色彩區別明顯。實際上它們與紅翅黑鸝的關係還存疑<ref name= jaramillo>{{cite book |last=Jaramillo |first=Alvaro |coauthors=Burke, Peter |title=New World Blackbirds: The Icterids |year =1999 |publisher=Christopher Helm |location=London |isbn=0-7136-4333-1 |pages=258–269}}</ref>。

== 描述==
[[File:Red_Wing.jpg|thumb]]
其常用名來自雄性的黑色羽毛和紅色肩部，後者無論是飛行中還是棲息时都很明顯<ref name=PetersonRockiesEast>{{cite book |last=Peterson |first=Roger Tory |title = A Field Guide to the Birds East of the Rockies |publisher=Houghton Mifflin Company |year=1980 |location=Boston |page=253 |isbn=5-550-55149-7}}</ref>。除去有[[三色黑鸝|三色黑鸝]]的美國西部以外，紅翅黑鸝在大多數地區都很容易辨認<ref name = jaramillo/>。雌性通體黑褐色，羽毛並不鮮豔。雌性比雄性小，雌性體長{{convert|17|-|18|cm|in}}、重約{{convert|41.5|g|oz}}，雄性體長{{convert|22|–|24|cm|in}}、重約{{convert|64|g|oz}}<ref name=USGS>{{cite web |last=Gough |first = Gregory |title=''Agelaius phoeniceus'' |publisher=USGS Patuxent Wildlife Research Center |year=2003 |url=http://www.mbr-pwrc.usgs.gov/id/framlst/i4980id.html |accessdate=2008-06-29}}</ref>。最小的雌性個體體重低至{{convert|29|g|oz}}，最重的雄性可達{{convert|82|g|oz}}<ref>{{cite book|title=CRC Handbook of Avian Body Masses |editor=John B. Dunning Jr. |publisher=CRC Press |year=1992 |isbn=978-0-8493-4258-5}}</ref>。翅膀長度{{convert|8.1|-|14.4|cm|in}}，尾巴長{{convert|6.1|-|10.9|cm|in}}，[[嘴峰|嘴峰]]長{{convert|1.3|-|3.2|cm|in}}，[[跗骨|跗骨]]長{{convert|2.1|cm|in}}<ref name=jaramillo />。兩性的喙都很尖銳，連同眼睛、跗蹠在內均是黑色的<ref name=Cornell/>。
{{listen|filename=Agelaius-phoeniceus-001.ogg|title=''Cong-a-lee!'' call|description= 一隻雄性紅翅黑鸝的叫聲}}

幼鳥羽毛和成年雌性相似，但下半身更淺，且羽毛邊緣呈淺黃色<ref name=Cornell>{{cite web | title=''Agelaius phoeniceus'' |work=All About Birds |publisher=Cornell Lab of Ornithology |year=2003 |url=http://www.birds.cornell.edu/AllAboutBirds/BirdGuide/Red-winged_Blackbird_dtl.html |accessdate=2008-06-29}}</ref>。紅翅黑鸝有多種聲音，一種是發音像是用喉音發出的“check”、一種是含糊的哨子樣的“terrr-eeee”。雄性在展示羽毛時會發出尖厲的“oak-a-lee”聲<ref name=PetersonEastCentral>{{cite book |last=Peterson |first=Roger Tory |title=A Field Guide to the Birds: Eastern and Central North America |publisher=HMCo Field Guides |year=1999 |url=http://books.google.com/?id=r6yakrkA74AC&pg=PA230 |isbn=0-395-96371-0 |page=230 }}</ref><ref>{{cite book |last=Sibley |first=David Allen |year=2000 |title=The Sibley Guide to Birds |publisher=Alfred Knopf |page=513 |isbn=0-679-45122-6}}</ref>。雌性也會歌唱，一般聲音類似“chit chit chit chit chit chit cheer teer teer teerr”<ref name= jaramillo/>。

== 分佈 ==
紅翅黑鸝自[[阿拉斯加|阿拉斯加]]南部到[[尤卡坦半島|尤卡坦半島]]、[[加利福尼亞州|加利福尼亞州]]沿海到[[加拿大|加拿大]]東部都有分佈。加拿大等北部地區是繁殖區，這些地區的紅翅黑鸝都是候鳥，而南部則大多是留鳥。每年九月到十月間是遷徙季節，但有時八月也會出現紅翅黑鸝遷徙的跡象。<ref name=Rosenthal>{{cite web |last=Rosenthal |first=A. |title=''Agelaius phoeniceus'' |work=Animal Diversity Web |publisher=University of Michigan Museum of Zoology |year=2004 |url=http://animaldiversity.ummz.umich.edu/site/accounts/information/Agelaius_phoeniceus.html |accessdate=2008-06-28}}</ref>

紅翅黑鸝生活在開闊的草地上，尤其偏愛濕地，這其中又以生長[[香蒲屬|香蒲]]之地為最。不過在草原和荒廢地上也常能找到它們。<ref name=Rosenthal/>
