== 紅石 ==
'''紅石'''是《[[Minecraft|Minecraft]]》遊戲中的一個虛構資源，是組成紅石電路的一個重要元素。在遊戲中，紅石是一種[[紅色|紅色]]的[[礦物|礦物]]，開採或精煉後可獲得紅石粉<ref>{{Cite web | author = Cale Hunt | url = https://www.windowscentral.com/beginners-guide-redstone-minecraft-windows-10-edition | publisher = Windows Central | title = Beginner's guide to redstone in Minecraft | date = 2016-11-12 | accessdate = 2018-10-19}}</ref>，在遊戲中是利用紅石粉鋪設紅石電路或加工成其他電子元件<ref name="npr.org">{{Cite web | title = 'Schoolifying' Minecraft Without Ruining It | author = Anya Kamenetz | date = 2017-08-09 | url = https://www.npr.org/sections/ed/2017/08/09/539518179/schoolifying-minecraft-without-ruining-it | website = npr.org | quote = The Minecraft material "redstone," which simulates electrical circuits}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web | url = https://www.theguardian.com/technology/2015/jul/05/whats-next-for-minecraft-mojang-future-features-minecon-2015 | title = What's next for Minecraft? Developer Mojang talks future features | quote = redstone can be dropped as dust to create circuits that transmit power | date = 2015-07-05 | website = theguardian.com}}</ref>。

在自定義地圖中，紅石扮演重要的角色，利用紅石實現的邏輯控制，玩家可以建立新的規則、難題甚至包含有任務的自定義地圖<ref name="Custom maps">{{cite web|last=Savage|first=Phil|title=The 25 best Minecraft custom maps|url=http://www.pcgamer.com/2012/10/20/the-25-best-minecraft-custom-maps/|work=[[PC_Gamer|PC Gamer]]|publisher=[[Future_plc|Future plc]]|accessdate=October 28, 2012|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121023211322/http://www.pcgamer.com/2012/10/20/the-25-best-minecraft-custom-maps/|archive-date=2012-10-23|dead-url=no}}</ref>，但早期紅石只有幾種簡單元件要非常複雜的結構才能完成某些操作，但由於自定義地圖的流行，[[Mojang|Mojang]]也對紅石系統做了許多更新，比如直接比較得比較器，還有能用紅石來控制能執行命令的遊戲物件以用更簡單的方式完成更複雜的自定義地圖<ref name="RPS – 1.3july">{{cite web|url=http://www.rockpapershotgun.com/2012/07/06/minecraft-1-3-adding-lan-adventure-mode-in-august/|title=Minecraft 1.3 Adding LAN, Adventure Mode In August|work=[[Rock,_Paper,_Shotgun|Rock, Paper, Shotgun]]|first=Nathan|last=Grayson|date=July 6, 2012|accessdate=January 4, 2013|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121112203224/http://www.rockpapershotgun.com/2012/07/06/minecraft-1-3-adding-lan-adventure-mode-in-august/|archive-date=2012-11-12|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name="Gallegos">{{cite web|last=Gallegos|first=Anthony|title=Minecraft Adding New Block|url=http://www.ign.com/articles/2012/07/27/minecraft-adding-new-block|work=[[IGN|IGN]]|accessdate=October 28, 2012|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121216094902/http://www.ign.com/articles/2012/07/27/minecraft-adding-new-block|archive-date=2012-12-16|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

=== 遊戲模組 ===

== 紅石與[[數位電路|數位邏輯]] ==

遊戲中的紅石可以製成紅石磚塊與紅石火把，他們可以釋放一種叫做紅石能量的能量，粉末紅石可以傳遞這些能量，但是這種能量傳送時會有線性衰減，但紅石火把會因為其他紅石能量輸入而熄滅<ref>{{cite book |author = 王育貞, 張孝謙, 盧品霖 |title= Minecraft（當個創世神）DIY大事典：我的世界: 自己的紅石機關自己做  |year=2016 |publisher=Sharp Point Press |isbn=978-957-10-6411-6 |page=19}}</ref>。利用這個特性就可以做成數位電路<ref name="twvs.tnc">{{cite thesis |url=http://www.shs.edu.tw/works/essay/2015/03/2015030819470013.pdf |title=The Relationship among Minecraft Redstone Circuit and Digital Logic |publisher=[[國立曾文高級農工職業學校|National Tseng-Wen Senior Agricultural & Industrial Vocational School]] |work = Department of Electrical Engineering |first=Yi-Xun |last=Chen |date=March 8, 2015|accessdate=March 9, 2016}}</ref>，數位電路中的訊號有高電位1、低電位0、中間值x和高阻抗z，紅石的磚塊與紅石的火把可以做為高電位1的訊號原、熄滅的紅石火把、衰減至零的紅石訊號、沒有接紅石源的可以作為低電位0<ref>{{Cite web | url = https://www.wired.com/2010/10/virtual-computers/ | title = Geeky Gamers Build Working Computers out of Virtual Blocks | author = Priya Ganapati | date = 2010-10-12 | website = wired.com}}</ref>，紅石線相接可以傳遞訊號也可以做出中間值x和高阻抗z，即實現三態邏輯電路{{#tag:ref|Tom Peerdeman. UVA 2014<ref name="Tom_Peerdeman_UVA">{{cite web |url=https://esc.fnwi.uva.nl/thesis/centraal/files/f1408977246.pdf |title=Minecraft to C for the Raspberry Pi |publisher=[[阿姆斯特丹大學|Universiteit Van Amsterdam]] |author=Tom Peerdeman |date=July 1, 2014|accessdate=March 9, 2016}}</ref>, chapter 2. Overview of the redstone components|name="Tom_Peerdeman_UVA1"}}。

=== 硬體描述語言 ===
{{main|硬體描述語言}}
由於Minecraft的紅石系統也可以被設計得十分複雜，因此在社群中也發展出了關於紅石電路的[[電子設計自動化|邏輯電路設計自動化]]相關工具及腳本，甚至有專門用於描述Minecraft的紅石系統的硬體描述語言<ref>{{cite web |url=https://github.com/kkroening/RedGen |title=RedGen -- a tool that automates the process of constructing redstone circuits in Minecraft. |author=kkroening |date=2014-06-07 |publisher=github |accessdate=2016-03-07 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180611003902/https://github.com/kkroening/RedGen |archive-date=2018-06-11 |dead-url=no }}</ref>，甚至也有試圖直接將描述、設計電子系統、數位電路的[[硬體描述語言|硬體描述語言]]--Verilog{{#tag:ref| Zainalabedin Navabi 2007<ref name="Navabi0">{{cite book|author=Zainalabedin Navabi|title=Verilog数字系统设计——RTL综合、测试平台与验证（第二版）（英文名：Verilog Digital System Design: Register Transfer Level Synthesis, Testbench, and Verification, Second Edition）|publisher=电子工业出版社|isbn=978-7-121-04767-1}}</ref>, p. 3-7|name="Navabi"}}<ref name="nguyen2016pershing">{{Cite paper|url=https://www.ocf.berkeley.edu/~qmn/assets/nguyen_pershing_2016.pdf|title=PERSHING: An Automatic Place-and-Route Tool for Minecraft Redstone Circuits | author=Nguyen, Quan | year=2016}}</ref>直接編譯成Minecraft物件的專案<ref>{{cite web | title=MCVerilog - A Verilog to Minecraft Compiler | url=http://devpost.com/software/mcverilog | publisher=devpost | accessdate=2016-01-27 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160313202104/http://devpost.com/software/mcverilog | archive-date=2016-03-13 | dead-url=no }}</ref>，但是這類專案發展到一半就都沒有下文了。比較知名的紅石設計者多半沒有靠這類工具就直接做出了文字顯示器，甚至還試圖將處理器、ALU、CPU還有RAM都做出來<ref>{{cite web | title=Minecraft player creates word processor out of redstone | url=http://www.pcgamer.com/minecraft-player-creates-word-processor-out-of-redstone/ | publisher=pcgamer | date=2015-01-07 | accessdate=2016-01-27 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160123233004/http://www.pcgamer.com/minecraft-player-creates-word-processor-out-of-redstone/ | archive-date=2016-01-23 | dead-url=no }}</ref>。

== 影響 ==

由於Minecraft的流行，再加上紅石系統比現實數位邏輯電路來的簡單，因此Minecraft的紅石電路是讓孩子們開始電路或數位邏輯的一個網關<ref name="verge">{{cite web|url=http://www.theverge.com/2014/9/15/6152085/why-parents-love-minecraft|title=Why parents are raising their kids on Minecraft|author=Ben Popper|date=15 September 2014|publisher=Vox Media|work=The Verge|access-date=2016-03-12|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160310074819/http://www.theverge.com/2014/9/15/6152085/why-parents-love-minecraft|archive-date=2016-03-10|dead-url=no}}</ref>，經常被用作於電子學與數位邏輯電路的入門教材<ref name="learn">{{cite web | title=Minecraft Circuits in Real Life | url=http://solderingsunday.com/minecraft-circuits-in-real-life-4/ | publisher=solderingsunday | accessdate=2016-01-27 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160313203342/http://solderingsunday.com/minecraft-circuits-in-real-life-4/ | archive-date=2016-03-13 | dead-url=no }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web | url = https://www.cnet.com/special-reports/minecraft/mindcraft-helping-students-learn/ | title = Minecraft in the classroom | website = cnet.com | date = 2016-03-30 | author = Stephen Shankland}}</ref><ref name="npr.org"/>，甚至還有發行電路紅石實驗包使初學者能利用Minecraft的紅石概念更簡單的學習使用[[麵包板|麵包板]]和電路板。有時甚至會將Minecraft作為再製作物理測試前測試電子產品的好方法<ref>{{cite web | title=Learning Logic Gates with Minecraft | author=popewill | url=http://www.instructables.com/id/Learning-Logic-Gates-with-Minecraft/ | publisher=instructables | date=2015-01-07 | accessdate=2016-01-27 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160313201355/http://www.instructables.com/id/Learning-Logic-Gates-with-Minecraft/ | archive-date=2016-03-13 | dead-url=no }}</ref>。紅石系統不只是Minecraft遊戲內容，也讓許多沒接觸過電子學的人學習了數位邏輯與電子電路<ref>{{cite web |url=http://anyelse.cc/article/49764.html |title=吐血推薦，學生黨必看！玩這幾款遊戲時，別人問起來可以說「我在學習」 |publisher=anyelse.cc |author=张敏 |date=2015-08-06 |accessdate=2016-03-09 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160313202627/http://anyelse.cc/article/49764.html |archivedate=2016-03-13 }}</ref>。

搭配模組後紅石系統可以變得更強大，紅石線路可以更簡單，因此更適合作為教學用途，例如ComputerCraftEdu專案採用了2011的ComputerCraft模組內的電腦與紅石擴充作為教育目的<ref name="GamasutraTeaching">{{cite web|url=http://gamasutra.com/view/news/247888/TeacherGaming_mods_Minecraft_to_teach_basic_coding_skills.php|title=Gamasutra – TeacherGaming mods Minecraft to teach basic coding skills|author=Alex Wawro|publisher=|access-date=2016-03-12|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160310003156/http://gamasutra.com/view/news/247888/TeacherGaming_mods_Minecraft_to_teach_basic_coding_skills.php|archive-date=2016-03-10|dead-url=no}}</ref>。
