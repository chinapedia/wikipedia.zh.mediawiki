<span>'''《記憶斷層》'''</span> 是 2003 年的一部心理驚悚片，由 Roland Suso Richter執導，編劇為 Michael Cooney 根據自己的戲劇《''Point of Death》''所寫。這部片和Alan Dean Foster的科幻小說''《The I Inside》''無關。

== 劇情 ==
賽門‧凱博在醫院中醒來，腦中困惑、也不知道自己為何身在此處。在經過醫生的諮詢之後，他發現自己患有[[失憶症|<nowiki/>]][[失憶症|失憶症]]並不記得過去兩年間發生的所有事情。於是，他開始調查自己到底發生了什麼事，並逐漸將自己神秘的過去拼湊起來。

* The film has generally received luke-warm reviews from online critics. It holds a 33% rating on Rotten Tomatoes. Review sites have commended the film for a rather involving first two-thirds, with the mystery slowly being revealed layer by layer, drawing in the viewer. However, some critics found the ending a let down, calling it a "gotcha" gimmick<ref name="bey1">Nix, [http://www.beyondhollywood.com/reviews/theiinside.htm "The I Inside" Review] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20060212003609/http://www.beyondhollywood.com/reviews/theiinside.htm |date=2006-02-12 }}, ''Beyond Hollywood''</ref> that causes "the past 70 or so minutes (to go) out the window".<ref name="bey1">Nix, [http://www.beyondhollywood.com/reviews/theiinside.htm "The I Inside" Review] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20060212003609/http://www.beyondhollywood.com/reviews/theiinside.htm |date=2006-02-12 }}, ''Beyond Hollywood''</ref>

* Christopher Null of ''Filmcritic.com'' 提出了一個特別但是有趣的觀點： "The movie is based on a play with a much different title, one that actually gives away the surprise ending."<ref name="fil1">Christopher Null, {{cite web |url=http://filmcritic.com/misc/emporium.nsf/60e74e041ca9cd6b8625626f0062219f/352020feadc3337c88256fc70061538a?OpenDocument |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2006-06-14 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20130123054400/http://filmcritic.com/misc/emporium.nsf/60e74e041ca9cd6b8625626f0062219f/352020feadc3337c88256fc70061538a?OpenDocument |archivedate=2013-01-23 }}, ''Filmcritic.com''</ref>
