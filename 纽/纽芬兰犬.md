'''纽芬兰犬'''（{{lang-en|Newfoundland}}）是一种大型[[工作犬|工作犬]]，被毛厚直，颜色为黑色、棕色、米黄色、白底黑斑和灰色（罕见）。在其原产国[[加拿大|加拿大]]，只有黑色、白色或黑白被认为是正统颜色。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ckc.ca/en/Choosing-a-Dog/Choosing-a-Breed/Working-Dogs/Newfoundland|title=Newfoundland - CKC|publisher=}}</ref>它们最初被用于[[纽芬兰|纽芬兰]]省渔民的工作犬。<ref name="Walsh1878">{{cite book|author=John Henry Walsh|title=The dogs of the British Islands: being a series of articles on the points of their various breeds, and the treatment of the diseases to which they are subject|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=_jhJAAAAMAAJ&pg=PA173|year=1878|publisher="The Field" Office|page=173}}</ref><ref name="Smith1999">{{cite book|author=William Jardine, Charles Hamilton Smith|title=The Naturalist's Library: Mammalia, Dogs|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=-_fCPaMItxwC&pg=PA132|date=1 January 1999|publisher=Elibron.com|isbn=978-1-4021-8033-0|page=132}}</ref> 它们体型巨大，聪明，力量惊人，性格冷静和忠诚。它们的肌肉结实，有双层被毛，脚掌宽阔，并天生会游泳，非常擅长水中救援工作。<ref name="mail">{{cite news |url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/worldnews/article-1306009/Bonewatch-The-doggy-lifeguards-leap-helicopters-save-stranded-swimmers.html |title=Bonewatch: The doggy lifeguards that leap from helicopters to save stranded swimmers |date=August 27, 2010 |publisher=[[每日邮报|每日邮报]] |accessdate=January 15, 2011}}</ref>俄罗斯总统普京曾把其作为礼物，送给一名10岁女孩<ref>{{cite web|title=俄总统新闻秘书：普京已赠送吉尔吉斯斯坦女孩一只纽芬兰犬|url=http://www.inform.kz/chn/article/2799733|website=哈萨克国际通讯社|accessdate=2015-07-24}}</ref>。

==体貌特征==
[[File:Newfoundland_brown.jpg|thumb]]
===外形===
纽芬兰犬有宽阔的脚掌和防水的被毛<ref name="tkc">[http://www.thekennelclub.org.uk/item/57 Newfoundland Breed Standard] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111117065907/http://www.thekennelclub.org.uk/item/57 |date=2011-11-17 }} [[The_Kennel_Club|The Kennel Club]], 'Exceptionally gentle, docile nature' .. 'webbed' ... 'oily nature, water-resistant'</ref> ，成年雄性一般{{convert|60|-|70|kg|lb|abbr=on}},雌性一般{{convert|45|-|55|kg|lb|abbr=on}}，肩高一般{{convert|22|-|30|in|cm|abbr=on|order=flip}} ，已知有身高达到1米<ref>[http://video.sina.com.cn/p/news/w/v/2009-10-10/105560410102.html 1米高纽芬兰犬挑战世界最高狗纪录]</ref>，体重超过120kg的巨型纽芬兰犬存在。其身体非常强壮<ref>[http://edu.sina.com.cn/a/2012-08-21/1412218461.shtml 英国纽芬兰犬滚落45米悬崖仅擦伤鼻子]</ref>。
===性格===
纽芬兰犬冷静，温顺，身体强壮，忠诚，是理想的工作犬，被称为“巨人绅士”，国际犬舍俱乐部（{{lang-en|International kennel clubs}}）称其有“甜美”的性格<ref name="tkc" /><ref name="akc">[http://www.akc.org/breeds/newfoundland/ Newfoundland Breed Standard] [[American_Kennel_Club|American Kennel Club]], 'a sweet-dispositioned dog that acts neither dull nor ill-tempered' ... 'Sweetness of temperament'</ref><ref name="ckc">[http://www.ckc.ca/en/Default.aspx?tabid=99&BreedCode=NWO CKC Breed Standards] Canadian Kennel Club, 'The Newfoundland's expression is soft and reflects the character of the breed—benevolent, intelligent, dignified but capable of fun. He is known for his sterling gentleness and serenity.'</ref>。它们叫声低沉，如果从小开始，很容易训练<ref>[http://discover.163.com/08/0104/15/41CGERRT000125LI.html 英国两岁纽芬兰犬能帮主人洗衣购物]</ref>。它们和家里的孩子相处非常融洽<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eJwSyv1DnT4 Little girl plays hide and seek with giant dog]</ref>，但因自身体型过于庞大，有可能伤到婴儿，它们非常适合陪伴，是理想的保姆犬。它们与其他动物也能和平相处，但因为身躯巨大，有可能造成伤害。尽管如此，在中華人民共和国很多城市（例如昆明和成都），因为其巨大的身躯，依然被列为禁止在城市饲养的犬种<ref>[http://news.sohu.com/20150702/n416019938.shtml 烈犬咬伤人主人未制止 被判赔偿近2万元 ]</ref><ref>[http://news.chengdu.cn/2015/0722/1709216.shtml 司机撞狗后被咬一口 狗的主人负主责]</ref>。

===健康===
纽芬兰犬的寿命一般在8-10年，其容易患先天性髋部骨骼疾病，肘部骨骼疾病和尿道结石，还有基因缺陷导致的主动脉瓣下狭窄，这种疾病会导致其猝死和早亡<ref name="Cassidy Data">{{cite web|title=Breed Longevity Data|url=http://users.pullman.com/lostriver/breeddata.htm |first1=Kelly M. |last1=Cassidy |work=Dog Longevity |date=February 1, 2008 |accessdate=September 18, 2012}}</ref>。
