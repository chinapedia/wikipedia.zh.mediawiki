'''一氟化铝'''是化学式为AlF的[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]。这种难以捉摸的物质可以通过金属[[铝|铝]]与[[三氟化铝|三氟化铝]]在高温下反应制得，但冷却时又迅速[[歧化|歧化]]成原来的反应物。<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Dyke | first1 = C.Kirby | last2 = Morris | first2 = B.W.J.Gravenor | year = 1984 | title = A study of aluminium monofluoride and aluminium trifluoride by high-temperature photoelectron spectroscopy | url = | journal = Chemical Physics | volume = 88 | issue = 2| page = 289 | doi = 10.1016/0301-0104(84)85286-6 }}</ref> 很多一卤化铝的衍生物可以通过一些特殊的[[配体|配体]]来使它们稳定。<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Dohmeier | first1 = C. | last2 = Loos | first2 = D. | last3 = Schnöckel | first3 = H. | year = 1996 | title = Aluminum(I) and Gallium(I) Compounds: Syntheses, Structures, and Reactions | url = | journal = Angewandte Chemie International Edition in English | volume = 35 | issue = 2| pages = 129–149 | doi = 10.1002/anie.199601291 }}</ref>也有报道称，AlF在常温下能稳定存在，在潮湿空气中也是稳定的，而其他一卤化铝易歧化：<ref>{{cite book|title=《无机化学丛书》第二卷：铍、碱土金属、硼铝镓分族|pages=P432|author=张青莲|isbn=7-03-002238-6|location=北京|publisher=科学出版社|date=1991年11月}}</ref>

:3AlX → 2Al + AlX<sub>3</sub>

这种物质已经在[[星际物质|星际物质]]中被发现，因为其中分子浓度很小，[[分子间作用力|分子间作用力]]很微弱。<ref>{{cite journal | author=L. M. Ziurys, A. J. Apponi,  T. G. Phillips | title=Exotic fluoride molecules in IRC +10216: Confirmation of AlF and searches for MgF and CaF | journal=Astrophysical Journal | year=1994 | volume=433 | issue=2 | pages=729–732 | bibcode=1994ApJ...433..729Z | doi=10.1086/174682 }}</ref>
