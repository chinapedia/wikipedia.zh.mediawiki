'''第70屆[[坎城影展|坎城影展]]'''於2017年5月17日至5月28日在[[法國|法國]][[坎城|坎城]][[節慶宮|節慶宮]]舉辦。<ref name="Cannes2017">{{cite web |url=http://www.festival-cannes.com/en/actualites/articles/rendez-vous-in-2017 |title=Cannes Film Festival: Rendez-vous in 2017! |accessdate=2016-10-19 |work=Cannes}}</ref> [[西班牙|西班牙]][[導演|導演]][[佩德羅·阿莫多瓦|佩德羅·阿莫多瓦]]擔任評審團主席。<ref name="Pedro">{{cite web |url=http://www.festival-cannes.com/en/actualites/articles/pedro-almodovar-president-of-the-jury-of-the-70th-festival-de-cannes |title=Pedro Almodóvar President of the Jury of the 70th Festival de Cannes |accessdate=2017-01-31 |work=Cannes}}</ref>法國導演[[阿諾·戴普勒尚|阿諾·戴普勒尚]]執導的《{{link-en|當舊愛回來過|Ismael's Ghosts}}》獲選為影展開幕片<ref name="Main"/><ref name="IW"/>。

該屆[[金棕櫚獎|金棕櫚獎]]頒給瑞典導演[[鲁本·奥斯特伦德|鲁本·奥斯特伦德]]的《[[抓狂美術館|抓狂美術館]]》，並列為影展閉幕片<ref name="main-prizes">{{cite web |url=https://variety.com/2017/film/festivals/cannes-film-festival-2017-award-winners-palme-d-or-1202446856/ |title=2017 Cannes Film Festival Award Winners Announced |first=Peter |last= Debruge |date=28 May 2017 |accessdate=28 May 2017 |work=Variety}}</ref><ref name="BBC-PD">{{cite web |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/entertainment-arts-40077490 |title=Cannes Film Festival: The Square wins Palme d'Or |date=28 May 2017 |accessdate=28 May 2017 |work=BBC News}}</ref><ref>{{citeweb|url= https://www.theguardian.com/film/2017/may/28/cannes-2017-ruben-ostlund-wins-palme-dor-for-the-square|title=Cannes 2017: Ruben Östlund wins Palme d'Or for The Square|date=28 May 2017|work=Guardian|accessdate=31 May 2017}}</ref>。
