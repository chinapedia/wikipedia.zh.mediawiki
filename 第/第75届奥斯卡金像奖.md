'''第75届奥斯卡颁奖典礼'''是[[美国电影艺术与科学学院|美国电影艺术与科学学院]]旨在奖励[[2002年电影|2002年最优秀电影]]的一场晚会，于[[太平洋时区|太平洋时区]]2003年3月23日下午17点30分（[[北美东部时区|北美东部时区]]晚上20点30分）在[[美国|美国]][[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]][[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]][[好莱坞|好莱坞]]的[[杜比剧院|杜比剧院]]举行，共计颁发了24座[[奥斯卡金像奖|奥斯卡金像奖]]（也称[[学院奖|学院奖]]）。晚会通过[[美国广播公司|美国广播公司]]在[[美国电视|美国]]直播，由吉尔·凯茨担任[[制作人|制作人]]，路易斯·{{lang|en|J}}·霍维茨执导<ref>{{cite news | title = Oscar watch: Horvitz to direct kudocast | archivedate = 2014-04-07 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140413150506/http://variety.com/2002/film/news/oscar-watch-horvitz-to-direct-kudocast-1117876532/ | url = http://variety.com/2002/film/news/oscar-watch-horvitz-to-direct-kudocast-1117876532/ | accessdate = 2014-07-05 | work = Variety | publisher = PMC | date = 2002-11-24}}</ref>。男演员[[史提夫·马丁|史提夫·马丁]]继2001年举办的[[第73届奥斯卡金像奖|第73届奥斯卡颁奖典礼]]以来再度担任主持人<ref>{{cite news | last = Feiwell | first = Jill | title = Oscar taps old pal Martin | url = http://variety.com/2002/film/news/oscar-taps-old-pal-martin-1117875719/ | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140407155016/http://variety.com/2002/film/news/oscar-taps-old-pal-martin-1117875719/ | accessdate = 2014-07-04 | work = Variety | publisher = PMC | date = 2002-11-07 | archivedate = 2014-04-07 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。三个星期前的3月1日，女演员[[凯特·哈德森|凯特·哈德森]]在[[比佛利山_(加利福尼亚州)|比佛利山]]的[[比佛利山威尔希尔酒店|比佛利山威尔希尔酒店]]主持颁发了[[奥斯卡科技成果奖|奥斯卡科技成果奖]]<ref>{{cite news | title = Oscar Watch: Kate Hudson | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140704143450/http://variety.com/2003/film/news/oscar-watch-kate-hudson-1117880736/ | url = http://variety.com/2003/film/news/oscar-watch-kate-hudson-1117880736/ | accessdate = 2014-07-04 | work = Variety | publisher = PMC | date = 2003-02-18 | archivedate = 2014-07-04 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。

《[[芝加哥_(电影)|芝加哥]]》赢得了包括[[奥斯卡最佳影片奖|最佳影片]]在内的6项大奖，是这个夜晚的最大赢家<ref>{{cite news | last = Elliott | first = David | title = 'Chicago's' Best | work = U-T San Diego | publisher = MLIM Holdings | date = 2003-03-24 | url = http://legacy.utsandiego.com/news/features/20030324-9999_mz1d24oscar.html | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140413141139/http://legacy.utsandiego.com/news/features/20030324-9999_mz1d24oscar.html | deadurl = no | accessdate = 2014-07-04 | archivedate = 2014-04-13}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | last = Rickey | first = Carrie | title = 'Chicago' wins big; Polanski surprises The musical won six awards, including best picture. "The Pianist" won three, including best director. War casts shadow on Oscars Actor Actress Supporting Actor Honorary Supporting Actress | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140413142725/http://articles.philly.com/2003-03-24/news/25473316_1_supporting-actor-trophy-oscars-documentary-prize | archivedate = 2014-04-13 | deadurl = no | accessdate = 2014-07-04 | date = 2003-03-24 | url = http://articles.philly.com/2003-03-24/news/25473316_1_supporting-actor-trophy-oscars-documentary-prize | work = The Philadelphia Inquirer | publisher = Philadelphia Media Network}}</ref>。其它获奖电影包括胜出三项的《[[钢琴家_(电影)|钢琴家]]》，获奖两项的《[[弗里达|弗里达]]》和《[[指环王：双塔奇兵|指环王：双塔奇兵]]》，以及胜出一项的《[[改编剧本|改编剧本]]》、《[[科伦拜校园事件_(纪录片)|科伦拜校园事件]]》（{{lang|en|''Bowling for Columbine''}}）、《[[祖布祖布斯！|祖布祖布斯！]]》、《[[8英里|8英里]]》、《[[时时刻刻|时时刻刻]]》、《[[何处是我家|何处是我家]]》、《[[毁灭之路|毁灭之路]]》、《[[千与千寻|千与千寻]]》、《[[对她说|对她说]]》、《迷人的男子》（{{lang|da|''Der Er En Yndig Mand''}}）和《双雄》（{{lang|en|''Twin Towers''}}）。晚会的电视直播平均吸引了约3300万美国观众收看，是奥斯卡颁奖晚会收视成绩最差的一次。

== 获奖和提名 ==
第75届奥斯卡金像奖提名名单于2003年2月11日由学院主席[[弗兰克·皮尔森|弗兰克·皮尔森]]（{{lang|en|Frank Pierson}}）和女演员[[玛丽莎·托梅|玛丽莎·托梅]]一起在[[比佛利山_(加利福尼亚州)|比佛利山]]的[[塞缪尔·戈尔德温剧院|塞缪尔·戈尔德温剧院]]公布<ref>{{cite news | title = Oscar Watch: Marisa Tomei | url = http://variety.com/2003/film/news/oscar-watch-marisa-tomei-1117880068/ | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140407140341/http://variety.com/2003/film/news/oscar-watch-marisa-tomei-1117880068/ | accessdate = 2014-07-04 | work = Variety | publisher = PMC | date = 2003-02-05 | archivedate = 2014-04-07 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。《[[芝加哥_(电影)|芝加哥]]》以多达13项提名领跑，是历史上第8部获得这么多项奥斯卡金像奖提名的电影<ref>{{cite book | last = Osborne | first = Robert | title = 85 Years of the Oscar: The Complete History of the Academy Awards | year = 2013 | isbn = 0-7892-1142-4 | location = New York, United States | edition = | oclc = | publisher = Abbeville Publishing Group | page = 423 | postscript = .}}</ref>，《[[纽约黑帮|纽约黑帮]]》以10项紧随其后<ref>{{cite news | last = Ross | first = Bob | title = And This Year's Nominees Are... | work = The Tampa Tribune | publisher = Tampa Media Group, Inc. | date = 2003-02-12}}</ref>。
