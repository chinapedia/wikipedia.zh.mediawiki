'''第58届格莱美奖'''（{{lang-en|The 58th Annual Grammy Awards}}）颁奖典礼于2016年2月15日晚在[[美国|美国]][[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]][[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]][[斯台普斯中心|斯台普斯中心]]举办，颁奖典礼由[[CBS_(电视网)|CBS]]现场直播。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.grammy.com/news/the-recording-academy-cbs-announce-58th-grammy-awards-date|title=The Recording Academy, CBS Announce 58th GRAMMY Awards Date|work=Grammy.com|date=2015-05-11|accessdate=2015-12-08}}</ref>奖项由{{le|美国国家录音艺术科学学院|National Academy of Recording Arts and Sciences}}主办，奖励前一年内发行的优秀音乐作品以及有突出成就的艺人。本届格莱美奖中的作品须在2014年10月1日至2015年9月30日之间发行方可获得报名资格。<ref name="noms">{{cite web|url=http://www.grammy.com/files/58th-final-58thpresslist12042015.pdf|publisher=Grammy.com|title= The 58th Grammy Awards Final Nominations List|work=National Academy of Recording Arts and Sciences|date=2015-12-07|accessdate=2015-12-07}}</ref>本届格莱美奖的举办日期是[[第45屆葛萊美獎|2003年]]以来最晚的一次，也是首次在周一晚举行。这一日期是2016年的[[华盛顿生日|总统日假期]]，也是连续三天休息日的最后一日，格莱美奖选定在这一天举办是希望能在假期末吸引到更多观众。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2015/05/the-grammy-awards-monday-2016-1201424370/|title=CBS Shifts Grammy Awards To Monday For 2016|work=Deadline|date=2015-05-11|accessdate=2015-12-08}}</ref>本届格莱美奖的颁奖典礼继续由[[LL_Cool_J|LL Cool J]]主持，这也是他连续第五年主持格莱美奖颁奖典礼。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cbsnews.com/news/ll-cool-j-to-host-the-2016-grammy-awards/|title=LL Cool J to host the 2016 Grammy Awards|work=CBS|date=2015-12-16|accessdate=2016-01-19}}</ref>同时，本届格莱美奖打破以往在[[美国西海岸|美国西海岸]][[太平洋时区|太平洋时区]]延后直播的惯例，首次在[[美國時區|全美国不同时区]]内同步直播。<ref>{{Cite web|url = http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/grammys/6844245/grammys-to-air-live-on-west-coast-for-first-time|title = Grammys to Air Live Nationwide for First Time|accessdate = 2016-01-21|author = Colin Stutz|date = 2016-01-19|publisher = Billboard}}</ref>本届颁奖典礼吸引了2495万观众收看，这一收视数据是[[第51屆葛萊美獎|2009年]]以来的最低点。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://fortune.com/2016/02/16/grammy-2016-ratings/|title=Grammys' Audience Plummets to a New Low|work=Fortune|date=2016-02-16|accessdate=2016-02-16}}</ref>

本届奖项的最终提名于2015年12月7日公布，其中最重要的四大一般类奖项在CBS的晨间直播节目中公布，全部83个奖项的提名则随后在格莱美奖的官方网站上公布。<ref name="nom announce">{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/grammys/6769206/grammy-award-nominations-schedule-time-channel|title=Grammy Award Nominations to Get Rolling Dec. 7 on 'CBS This Morning'|work=Billboard|date=2015-11-18|accessdate=2015-12-07}}</ref>其中[[肯德里克·拉马尔|肯德里克·拉马尔]]获得11项提名，成为本届格莱美奖中获提名最多的艺人，[[泰勒·斯威夫特|泰勒·斯威夫特]]和[[The_Weeknd|The Weeknd]]各自获得7项提名位居其次。[[马克斯·马丁|马克斯·马丁]]获得5项提名紧随其后，他也是本届格莱美奖中获提名最多的非表演艺人。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.grammy.com/news/kendrick-lamar-taylor-swift-the-weeknd-top-58th-grammy-nominations|title=Kendrick Lamar, Taylor Swift, The Weeknd Top 58th GRAMMY Nominations|work=Grammy.com|date=2015-12-07|accessdate=2015-12-07}}</ref><ref name="Billboard nom">{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/grammys/6785974/grammy-nominations-2016-full-list|title=Grammy Nominations 2016: See the Full List of Nominees|work=Billboard|date=2015-12-07|accessdate=2015-12-07}}</ref>肯德里克·拉马尔的11项提名中有5项获奖，使他成为当晚获奖最多的艺人。泰勒·斯威夫特和{{le|阿拉巴馬雪克樂團|Alabama Shakes}}分别获得3项大奖位居其次。<ref>{{Cite news|url = http://abcnews.go.com/Entertainment/wireStory/grammys-feature-adele-gaga-rihanna-swift-36947546|title = Taylor Swift Wins Top Grammy Award, Kendrick Lamar Wins 5|last = FEKADU|first = MESFIN|date = 2016-02-16|work = ABC News|accessdate = 2016-02-16}}</ref>

本届颁奖典礼的直播过程中多次出现音响故障，{{le|山姆·亨特 (歌手)|Sam Hunt (musician)|山姆·亨特}}、[[凯莉·安德伍德|凯莉·安德伍德]]和[[愛黛兒|阿黛尔]]等人的现场表演都受到了不同程度的影响，引发了观众的批评和不满。<ref>{{Cite news|url = http://www.thewrap.com/twitter-erupts-over-bad-grammys-sound/|title = Twitter Erupts Over Bad Grammys Sound|last = Ge|first = Linda|date = 2016-02-16|work = The Wrap|accessdate = 2016-02-16}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url = http://www.mirror.co.uk/tv/tv-news/adele-explains-grammys-performance-sounded-7378463|title = Adele explains why her Grammys performance sounded out of tune - and treats herself to a burger|last = WAREING|first = CHARLOTTE|date = 2016-02-16|work = Mirror|accessdate = 2016-02-16}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url = http://time.com/4225052/grammys-2016-adele-all-i-ask/|title = The Internet Knew Something Was Off About Adele’s Performance at the Grammys|last = Begley|first = Sarah|date = 2016-02-16|work = Time|accessdate = 2016-02-16}}</ref>CBS提供的网络直播也故障频发，常常接受不到信号。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://fortune.com/2016/02/16/cbs-live-stream-grammy-feed-woes/|title=Grammy Report: Taylor Wins, CBS Live Stream Loses|work=Fortune|date=2016-02-16|accessdate=2016-02-17}}</ref>直播时的广告时间中，[[格温·史蒂芬妮|格温·史蒂芬妮]]在颁奖现场演唱了《Make Me Like You》，同时将表演过程拍摄成了[[音乐录影带|音乐录影带]]并在直播中播放，成为历史上的首次。<ref>{{Cite news|url = http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/grammys/6874152/gwen-stefani-make-me-like-you-live-video-grammy|title = Gwen Stefani to Broadcast TV's First Live Music Video During Grammy Night|last = Lynch|first = Joe|date = 2016-02-16|work = Billboard|accessdate = 2016-02-16}}</ref>[[肯德里克·拉马尔|肯德里克·拉马尔]]的表演被认为是当晚最出色的表演，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/entry/kendrick-lamar-grammys-performance_us_56ba3866e4b0b40245c4447a?9hjgu8fr|title=Kendrick Lamar's 2016 Grammys Performance Is The Only One Worth Watching|work=Huffington Post|date=2016-02-16|accessdate=2016-02-17}}</ref>而此前关注度甚高的[[阿黛尔|阿黛尔]]的表演则因音响故障而效果不如预期。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/entry/grammys-2016-highlights-winners-top-moments_us_56c239d5e4b08ffac12619f7|title=Grammy Awards 2016 Highlights And Top Moments|work=Huffington Post|date=2016-02-16|accessdate=2016-02-17}}</ref>[[Lady_Gaga|Lady Gaga]]致敬[[大卫·鲍伊|大卫·鲍伊]]的表演长达6分钟，演唱了10首鲍伊的经典作品，也被认为是当晚的亮点之一。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/music/what-to-listen-to/grammy-awards-2016-live/|title=Grammy Awards 2016: Lady Gaga steals show with incredible Bowie tribute|work=Telegraph|date=2016-02-16|accessdate=2016-02-17}}</ref>
