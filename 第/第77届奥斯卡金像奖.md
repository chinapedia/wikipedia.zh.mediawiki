本届颁奖典礼通过美国广播公司在美国直播，其播出时段里平均有4214万观众收看，与[[第76届奥斯卡金像奖|前一年]]相比降低了3%<ref name="Ratings">{{cite news | title = Fox, ‘Idol’ find sweeps groove | last = Kiseell | first = Rick | date = 2005-03-02 | work = Variety | publisher = PMC | accessdate = 2015-02-18 | archivedate = 2015-02-05 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150205115036/http://variety.com/2005/scene/news/fox-idol-find-sweeps-groove-1117918745/ | url = http://variety.com/2005/scene/news/fox-idol-find-sweeps-groove-1117918745/ | deadurl = no}}</ref>。从尼尔森收视率调查的数据看，节目的表现也不及去年，吸收了25.4%的家庭观众，收视率超过38%<ref name="Ratings" /><ref name="Adage">{{cite news | last1 = Atkinson | first1 = Claire | title = Final Oscar Ratings Contradict Early Reports | url = http://adage.com/article/media/final-oscar-tv-ratings-contradict-early-reports/45224/ | accessdate = 2015-02-19 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150205112245/http://adage.com/article/media/final-oscar-tv-ratings-contradict-early-reports/45224/ | archivedate = 2015-02-05 | work = Advertising Age | publisher = Crain Communications | date = 2005-03-01 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。此外，节目在18至49岁年龄段收获的收视率为15.1/34，这意味着当时美国所有该年龄段人群中有15.1%观看了本届奥斯卡颁奖典礼，而当时有看电视的该年龄段观众群则有34%收看本节目<ref name="Ratings" />。
