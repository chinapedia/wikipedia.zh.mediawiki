'''第56届柏林国际电影节'''（''{{lang-de|Die 56. Internationalen Filmfestspiele Berlin}}''）于[[德國|德國]]时间2006年2月9日到2月19日举行<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.berlinale.de/en/archiv/jahresarchive/2006/01_jahresblatt_2006/01_Jahresblatt_2006.html |title=56TH BERLIN INTERNATIONAL FILM FESTIVAL FEBRUARY 9 - 19, 2006 |accessdate=10 June 2014 |work=berlinale.de | archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131104212749/http://www.berlinale.de/en/archiv/jahresarchive/2006/01_jahresblatt_2006/01_Jahresblatt_2006.html| archivedate=4 November 2013 <!--DASHBot-->| deadurl= no}}</ref>。开幕片为{{link-en|馬可·伊凡斯|Marc Evans}}导演的《[[雪季過客|雪季過客]]》<ref>{{cite web|url=https://query.nytimes.com/gst/fullpage.html?res=9F05E7DF143EF93BA35751C0A9609C8B63 |title=Arts, Briefly |accessdate=10 June 2014}}</ref>，評審團主席則由[[英国|英国]]女演员[[夏綠蒂·蘭普琳|夏綠蒂·蘭普琳]]擔任<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.screendaily.com/charlotte-rampling-to-be-jury-president-at-berlinale-2006/4025346.article|title=Charlotte Rampling to be jury president at Berlinale 2006 |accessdate=10 June 2014}}</ref>。
