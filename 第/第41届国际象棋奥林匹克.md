'''第41届[[国际象棋奥林匹克|国际象棋奥林匹克]]'''是由[[世界国际象棋联合会|世界国际象棋联合会]]主办的[[国际象棋|国际象棋]]赛事，于2014年8月1日至14日在[[挪威|挪威]][[特罗姆瑟|特罗姆瑟]]举行。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chessbase.com/newsdetail.asp?newsid=6718 |title=41st Chess Olympiad to be staged in – Tromsø! | Chess News |publisher=ChessBase|date= |accessdate=2014-01-24}}</ref>

共有1570位棋手参与了本届国际象棋奥林匹克，其中包括881位男棋手与689位女棋手。<ref>[http://www.chessdom.com/tromso-chess-olympiad-men-live/ Tromso Chess Olympiad Men LIVE!], Chessdom.com, 29 July 2014.</ref><ref>[http://www.chessdom.com/tromso-chess-olympiad-women-live/ Tromso Chess Olympiad Women LIVE!], Chessdom.com, 29 July 2014.</ref>公开组共有来自172个国家的177支队伍参与，女子组则有来自131个国家的136支队伍参与。

最终，中国队首次夺得国际象棋奥林匹克公开组冠军，俄罗斯队则在女子组比赛中继续第三届折桂。第三台的中国棋手[[余泱漪|余泱漪]]与第一台的格鲁吉亚棋手[[娜娜·扎格尼泽|娜娜·扎格尼泽]]则分别是两个组别中表现最出色的棋手。<ref>[http://en.chessbase.com/post/tromso-final Tromso Final], ChessBase, 14 August 2014.</ref>本届赛事也是史上最强的女棋手[[波尔加·朱迪|波尔加·朱迪]]的最后一场职业比赛，她在赛事间歇期宣布了从职业比赛中退役。<ref>[http://www.thetimes.co.uk/tto/puzzles/chess/article4174361.ece Queen who exposed the arrogance of kings], ''[[The_Times|The Times]]'', 13 August 2014.</ref><ref>[http://www.theweekinchess.com/chessnews/events/41st-olympiad-tromso-2014/judit-polgar-to-retire-from-professional-chess Judit Polgar to retire from professional chess], ''[[The_Week_in_Chess|The Week in Chess]]'', 13 August 2014.</ref><ref>[http://en.chessbase.com/post/judit-polgar-to-retire-from-competitive-chess Judit Polgar to retire from competitive chess], ChessBase, 13 August 2014.</ref>
