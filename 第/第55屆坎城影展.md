'''第55屆[[坎城影展|坎城影展]]'''於2002年5月15日至5月26日在[[法國|法國]][[坎城|坎城]]舉辦，開幕片為美國導演[[伍迪·艾倫|伍迪·艾倫]]執導的《{{link-en|好萊塢結局|Hollywood Ending}}》，閉幕片則為法國導演[[克勞德·雷路許|克勞德·雷路許]]執導的《{{link-fr|紳士與淑女|And Now... Ladies and Gentlemen}}》。<ref>{{cite web|title=FESTIVALS: 2002 Cannes Film Festival Special Screenings Lineup|url=http://www.indiewire.com/article/festivals_2002_cannes_film_festival_special_screenings_lineup|accessdate=2013-11-12}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Hollywood Ending gets its international fest premiere before Cannes at San Francisco|url=http://www.screendaily.com/hollywood-ending-gets-its-international-fest-premiere-before-cannes-at-san-francisco/408937.article|accessdate=2013-11-12}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Lelouch ‘Ladies’ to end Cannes|url=http://variety.com/2002/film/news/lelouch-ladies-to-end-cannes-1117865628/|accessdate=2013-11-12 | work=Variety}}</ref><ref name="a">{{cite web|title=CANNES 2002 – A PREVIEW|url=http://www.urbancinefile.com.au/home/view.asp?a=6154&s=features|accessdate=2013-11-12}}</ref>

本屆最高榮譽[[金棕櫚獎|金棕櫚獎]]頒給[[波蘭|波蘭]]/[[法國|法國]]導演[[羅曼·波蘭斯基|羅曼·波蘭斯基]]執導的《[[钢琴家_(电影)|戰地琴人]]》<ref>{{cite web|title=CANNES FILM FESTIVAL WINNERS ANNOUNCED|url=http://www.hollywood.com/news/brief/1109393/cannes-film-festival-winners-announced|accessdate=2013-11-12}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=2002 Cannes Film Festival|url=http://www.infoplease.com/ipa/A0906761.html|accessdate=2013-11-12}}</ref><ref name=IMDB>{{cite web|title=Cannes Film Festival: 2002|publisher=[[Internet_Movie_Database|Internet Movie Database]]|url=http://www.imdb.com/Sections/Awards/Cannes_Film_Festival/2002|accessdate=2009-02-08|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090209093140/http://www.imdb.com/Sections/Awards/Cannes_Film_Festival/2002|archivedate=2009年2月9日|deadurl=yes}}</ref>；另外[[伍迪·艾倫|伍迪·艾倫]]也獲頒榮譽金棕櫚，以表彰他在電影上的傑出成就。<ref>{{cite web|title=A Honorary Palme at the opening ceremony of the Festival de Cannes|url=http://www.festival-cannes.fr/en/article/58029.html|accessdate=3 September 2015}}</ref>
