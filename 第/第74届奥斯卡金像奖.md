== 获奖和提名 ==
<!-- Please do not add any fact or statement below unless you have a credible source verifying it. Otherwise, it will be promptly removed. -->
第74届奥斯卡金像奖提名名单于2002年2月12日由学院主席[[弗兰克·皮尔森|弗兰克·皮尔森]]（{{lang|en|Frank Pierson}}）和女演员[[马西娅·盖伊·哈登|马西娅·盖伊·哈登]]（{{lang|en|Marcia Gay Harden}}）一起在[[比佛利山_(加利福尼亚州)|比佛利山]]的[[塞缪尔·戈尔德温剧院|塞缪尔·戈尔德温剧院]]公布<ref name="BBCOscar">{{cite news | archivedate = 2014-01-16 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140116132007/http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/in_depth/entertainment/2002/oscars_2002/1814014.stm | url = http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/in_depth/entertainment/2002/oscars_2002/1814014.stm | title = Film World Awaits Oscar nominations | date = 2002-02-12 | accessdate = 2014-03-15 | work = BBC News | publisher = BBC}}</ref>。《指环王：护戒使者》以13项提名领跑，是电影史上第7部获得这么多项提名的电影，《美丽心灵》和《红磨坊》均以8项提名排在第二位<ref name="ANA">{{cite book | last = Osborne | first = Robert | title = 85 Years of the Oscar: The Complete History of the Academy Awards | year = 2013 | isbn = 0-7892-1142-4 | location = New York, United States | edition = | oclc = | publisher = Abbeville Publishing Group | postscript = . | page = 423}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | last = Means | first = Sean | title = 'Lord of the Rings' in Hobbit Heaven With 13 Oscar Nominations | accessdate = 2014-02-24 | work = The Salt Lake Tribune | publisher = MediaNews Group | date = 2002-02-13 | page = A1}}</ref>。

获奖名单在2002年3月24日举行的颁奖典礼上公布<ref>{{cite news | last = Means | first = Sean | title = Hollywood Makes History | accessdate = 2014-01-22 | work = The Salt Lake Tribune | date = 2002-03-25 | page = A1 | publisher = MediaNews Group}}</ref>。最佳影片奖由[[梦工厂|梦工厂]]出品的《美丽心灵》获得，该公司之前两年还分别因《[[美国丽人|美国丽人]]》和《[[角斗士_(电影)|角斗士]]》连续获得[[第72届奥斯卡金像奖|第72]]和[[第73届奥斯卡金像奖|第73届奥斯卡]]最佳影片奖，成为历史上第二家在该奖项上达成三连冠的制片公司<ref>{{cite news | last = Collins | first = Keith | title = Pix precedents | url = http://variety.com/2003/film/awards/pix-precedents-1117878915/ | accessdate = 2014-03-15 | work = Variety | publisher = PMC | date = 2003-01-16 | archivedate = 2014-01-16 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140116151253/http://variety.com/2003/film/awards/pix-precedents-1117878915/}}</ref>。[[丹泽尔·华盛顿|丹泽尔·华盛顿]]成为继[[西德尼·波蒂埃|西德尼·波蒂埃]]以来的第一位夺得[[奥斯卡最佳男主角奖|影帝]]桂冠的[[非裔美国人|黑人男演员]]<ref name="LA Times Oscar" />。[[哈莉·贝瑞|哈莉·贝瑞]]成为历史上首位[[非裔美国人|非裔]][[奥斯卡最佳女主角奖|影后]]<ref name="LA Times Oscar" />。[[朱迪·丹奇|朱迪·丹奇]]和[[凯特·温丝莱特|凯特·温丝莱特]]均因在《长路将尽》中饰演[[爱尔兰|爱尔兰]][[作家|作家]][[艾瑞斯·梅铎|艾瑞斯·梅铎]]而分别获得[[奥斯卡最佳女主角奖|女主角]]和[[奥斯卡最佳女配角奖|女配角奖]]提名，成为历史上第二对在同一部电影中扮演同一人物并双双获得提名的女演员<ref name="ANA" />。

== 典礼资讯 ==
[[File:Whoopi_Goldberg_at_a_NYC_No_on_Proposition_8_Rally.jpg|130px]]担任第74届奥斯卡颁奖典礼的主持人。]]
美国电影艺术与科学学院希望为颁奖典礼找到新的主办地点，因为[[多萝西·钱德勒大厅|多萝西·钱德勒大厅]]一方面座位数量已现捉襟见肘之像，另一方面可以用来彩排的时间也不是很充分。同时在[[神殿礼堂|神殿礼堂]]举行晚会也存在着与其他庆典有所冲突，导致宾客可能不能方便地找到目的场地的问题<ref name=Pond>{{citation | last = Pond | first = Steve |title=The Big Show: High Times and Dirty Dealings Backstage at the Academy Awards |year=2005 |isbn=0-571-21193-3 |location=New York, United States |edition= |OCLC=  |publisher=Faber and Faber}}</ref>{{rp|160}}。1997年8月，学院与加拿大开发公司特里泽克哈恩（{{lang|en|TrizecHahn}}）开始就在[[好莱坞大道|好莱坞大道]]和高地大道交界，[[中国戏院|中国戏院]]邻近修筑一幢新的娱乐综合大楼展开蹉商<ref>{{cite news | last = Zehrq | first = Leonard | title = TrizecHahn in talks to house the Oscars Wants ceremony in Hollywood project | work = The Globe and Mail | publisher = The Globe and Mail Inc. | date = 1997-08-29}}</ref>。双方于7个月后达成为期20年的协议，让颁奖典礼可以在新的场地举行，该场地距1929年举办[[第1届奥斯卡金像奖|首届颁奖晚会]]的[[好莱坞罗斯福酒店|好莱坞罗斯福酒店]]也很近<ref name="Hurray">{{cite news | last = Newton | first = Jim | title = Mayor Leads a Hurray for Hollywood | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1998/apr/03/local/me-35558 | accessdate = 2014-03-16 | work = Los Angeles Times | publisher = Tribune Company | date = 1998-04-03 | archivedate = 2014-02-21 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140221113716/http://articles.latimes.com/1998/apr/03/local/me-35558}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | last = Feiwell | first = Jill | title = Oscar will have Kodak moment | archivedate = 2014-02-22 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140222164040/http://variety.com/2001/film/news/oscar-will-have-kodak-moment-1117801021/ | url = http://variety.com/2001/film/news/oscar-will-have-kodak-moment-1117801021/ | accessdate = 2014-03-16 | work = Variety | publisher = PMC | date = 2001-06-07}}</ref>。这也成为继1960年在[[庞特吉斯剧院|庞特吉斯剧院]]举行的[[第32届奥斯卡金像奖|第32届颁奖典礼]]以来首次在好莱坞举办的奥斯卡颁奖晚会<ref name="Hurray" />。

从奥斯卡回归好莱坞的角度出发，学院于2001年9月聘请电影制片人兼[[索尼影視娱乐|索尼影視娱乐]]总裁劳拉·泽斯金担任晚会电视直播的监制<ref name="Producer" />。学院主席弗兰克·皮尔森对此表示：“这场晚会的监制工作即使不是娱乐界最具挑战的工作，那也能排得上号了。劳拉·泽斯金过往的表现说明她拥有担当此任所需要的一切智慧和经验”<ref>{{cite news | title = Oscar gets new producer, new regulations | date = 2001-09-10 | url = http://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=2199&dat=20010910&id=-IkyAAAAIBAJ&sjid=nOYFAAAAIBAJ&pg=6481,6673396 | accessdate = 2014-03-16 | work = Lawrence Journal-World | publisher = The World Company}}</ref>。这也成为历史上首次由一位女性独立完成奥斯卡颁奖典礼的监制工作。四个月后，乌比·戈德堡获选担任晚会的主持人，对此泽期金在接受《[[洛杉矶时报|洛杉矶时报]]》采访时表示，她认为戈德堡拥有足够的“温情、幽默、人性和社会良知，我觉得这些品质都是今年晚会上必不可少的。我很期待与乌比合作，呈现出一个既具娱乐性又有意义的夜晚”<ref name="Whoopi">{{cite news | url = http://articles.latimes.com/2002/jan/08/local/me-21128 | title = Whoopi Goldberg Will Host Oscar Ceremony | last = Munoz | first = Lorenza | work = Los Angeles Times | publisher = Tribune Company | date = 2002-01-10 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131221153113/http://articles.latimes.com/2002/jan/08/local/me-21128 | archivedate = 2013-12-21 | deadurl = no | accessdate = 2014-03-16}}</ref>。

=== 新增动画长片奖 ===
从本届颁奖典礼开始，美国电影艺术与科学学院新增了一个奖励[[动画|动画]]长片的竞争性奖项<ref>{{cite news | last = Solomon | first = Charles | title = New Oscar Category Will Change Animation | archivedate = 2014-02-21 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140221130315/http://articles.latimes.com/2000/oct/11/entertainment/ca-34499/2 | deadurl = no | url = http://articles.latimes.com/2000/oct/11/entertainment/ca-34499/2 | accessdate = 2014-03-16 | work = Los Angeles Times | publisher = Tribune Company | date = 2000-10-11}}</ref>。据学院通讯主任约翰·帕夫利克（{{lang|en|John Pavlik}}）表示，一部影片至少要有75%属动画制作才能参与该奖项角逐<ref>{{cite news | last = Wloszczyna | first = Susan | title = Even 'toons must follow the rules | url = http://usatoday30.usatoday.com/life/enter/movies/2001-10-31-toon-rules.htm | accessdate = 2014-03-16 | work = USA Today | publisher = Gannett Company | date = 2001-10-31 | archivedate = 2014-02-02 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140202190437/http://usatoday30.usatoday.com/life/enter/movies/2001-10-31-toon-rules.htm}}</ref>，并且一个日历年内要有至少8部符合资格的电影上映才会颁发这一奖项，提名电影数量为三个，如果符合资格的电影达到12部以上，那么提名数量也将相应增加到5个<ref>{{cite news | last = Longino | first = Bob | title = New Oscar slot heating up as battle of beasties | work = The Atlanta Journal-Constitution | publisher = Cox Enterprises | date = 2001-12-09}}</ref>。这一奖项诞生前，一共有三部[[华特迪士尼影片|迪斯尼公司]]电影获得过[[奥斯卡特别成就奖|奥斯卡特别成就奖]]，分别是1937年的《[[白雪公主与七个小矮人|白雪公主与七个小矮人]]》、[[1988年电影|1988年]]的《[[谁陷害了兔子罗杰|谁陷害了兔子罗杰]]》和[[1995年电影|1995年]]的《[[玩具总动员|玩具总动员]]》<ref>{{cite news | last = Wloszczyna | first = Susan | title = 'Toons get their very own Oscar category | url = http://usatoday30.usatoday.com/life/movies/2001-10-31-animation.htm | accessdate = 2014-03-16 | work = USA Today | publisher = Gannett Company | date = 2001-10-31 | archivedate = 2014-01-01 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140101182840/http://usatoday30.usatoday.com/life/movies/2001-10-31-animation.htm}}</ref>。

=== 提名电影票房表现 ===

=== 专业评价 ===
本场晚会所获得的媒体评价褒贬不一，有些较为负面。《[[今日美国|今日美国]]》电视评论员罗伯特·比安科（{{lang|en|Robert Bianco}}）批评晚会“过于自恋且缺乏特色，几乎自始至终都很沉闷”<ref>{{cite news | last = Bianco | first = Robert | title = Academy Awards 'return to normalcy' | url = http://usatoday30.usatoday.com/life/movies/oscar2002/2002-03-25-bianco-review.htm | accessdate = 2014-03-16 | work = USA Today | publisher = Gannett Company | date = 2002-03-25 | archivedate = 2014-01-16 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140116151126/http://usatoday30.usatoday.com/life/movies/oscar2002/2002-03-25-bianco-review.htm}}</ref>。《[[波士顿环球报|波士顿环球报]]》（{{lang|en|''Boston Globe''}}）专栏作家马修·吉尔伯特（{{lang|en|Matthew Gilbert}}）哀叹“电视上最受关注的鼻涕虫爬回镇上来了”。他还称：“就像往常一下，先是一堆技术类奖项在节目中途组成[[百慕大三角|百慕大三角]]，然后再用一大堆电影片断和歌舞曲目来让我们的大脑感到麻木”<ref>{{cite news | last = Gilbert | first = Matthew | title = Despite Touches of Grace, It was an Oscar Crawl | accessdate = 2014-01-15 | work = Boston Globe | publisher = The New York Times Company | date = 2002-03-25 | page = D11}}</ref>。《[[萨克拉门托蜜蜂报|萨克拉门托蜜蜂报]]》（{{lang|en|''The Sacramento Bee''}}）的里克·基什曼（{{lang|en|Rick Kishman}}）感慨道：“这是（历史上）首次最佳男、女主角奖获奖者都是[[非裔美国人|非裔美国人]]……然而，观众却不得不坚持看完这好几个小时来表示（自己对此的）关切。”他还讽刺节目中太多的蒙太奇和致敬段落拖累了节目的进程<ref>{{cite news | last = Armstrong | first = Mark | title = Longest Oscars, Lowest Ratings | url = http://www.eonline.com/news/43050/longest-oscars-lowest-ratings | accessdate = 2014-03-16 | work = E! | publisher = NBC Universal | date = 2002-03-25 | archivedate = 2014-01-16 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140116143342/http://www.eonline.com/news/43050/longest-oscars-lowest-ratings}}</ref>。

其他媒体对节目的评价更为正面。《[[橙县纪事报|橙县纪事报]]》（{{lang|en|''Orange County Register''}}）影评人亨利·希恩（{{lang|en|Henry Sheehan}}）称赞了戈德堡的主持表现，称她引入《红磨坊》段落的做法构思巧妙，效果也很好，并且连续不断的笑料把节目引上了正轨<ref name ="OC Register" />。《[[丹佛邮报|丹佛邮报]]》电视专栏作家乔安妮·奥斯特鲁夫（{{lang|en|Joanne Ostrow}}）对颁奖典礼赞不绝口，称这全长近五小时的电视转播壮丽、高效、华丽，在一些惊艳服饰的帮助下有历史性的美感。她还认为，丹泽尔·华盛顿和哈利·贝瑞的获奖致辞加上向西德尼·波蒂埃致敬的桥段让这个夜晚有了历史性的意义，让人感慨万千<ref>{{cite news | last = Ostrow | first = Joanne | title = 74th telecast first one of real color | accessdate = 2014-01-14 | work = Denver Post | publisher = MediaNews Group | date = 2002-03-25 | page = D1}}</ref>。《[[西雅图邮讯报|西雅图邮讯报]]》的约翰·莱韦斯克（{{lang|en|John Levesque}}）称制片人泽斯金制作出了自己所记得最棒的一场奥斯卡颁奖典礼，并且将之与过去的晚会相比，认为这个夜晚特别清新、明快和与众不同<ref>{{cite news | last = Levesque | first = John | title = Movie awards show finally makes good TV | archivedate = 2014-01-16 | url = http://www.seattlepi.com/ae/tv/article/Movie-awards-show-finally-makes-good-TV-1083620.php | accessdate = 2014-03-16 | work = Seattle Post-Intelligencer | publisher = Hearst Corporation | date = 2002-03-24 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140116105739/http://www.seattlepi.com/ae/tv/article/Movie-awards-show-finally-makes-good-TV-1083620.php}}</ref>。
