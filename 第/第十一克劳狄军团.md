'''第十一克劳狄军团'''（{{lang-en|Legio XI Claudia}}）<ref>Adkins,Lesley Adkins,Roy A.（1998）.''Handbook to Life in Ancient Rome''.[[Oxford_University_Press|Oxford University Press]],USA.ISBN 9780195123326.</ref>[[古罗马|古罗马]][[军队|军队]]建制名称。由[[尤利乌斯·凯撒|尤利乌斯·凯撒]]于[[公元前|公元前]]58年建立并存在至5[[世纪|世纪]]。该军团曾参加[[罗马内战|罗马内战]]、[[腓立比战役|腓立比战役]]等一系列相关军事活动，<ref name="cronica">{{cite web | url=http://www.cimec.ro/scripts/ARH/Cronica/detail.asp?k=787 | title=''Archaeological Excavation Report'' | publisher=CIMEC | date=1992 | accessdate=2013-05-04 | author=Vlădescu, Cristian M. & Co.}}</ref><ref>C. Clermont-Ganneau, ''Archaeological Researches in Palestina during the Years 1873-74'', London 1899, pp. 463-470.</ref>具有重要地影响与积极意义。
