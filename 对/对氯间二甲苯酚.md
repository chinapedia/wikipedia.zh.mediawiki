'''氯二甲酚'''（{{lang-en|'''Chloroxylenol'''}}），又稱'''對氯間二甲苯酚'''（'''para-chloro-meta-xylenol'''，'''PCMX'''），是一種防腐劑和消毒劑，主要用於皮膚消毒和清洗外科器械<ref name="WHO2008">{{cite book|url=http://apps.who.int/medicinedocs/documents/s16879e/s16879e.pdf|title=WHO Model Formulary 2008|date=2009|publisher=World Health Organization|accessdate=8 January 2017|isbn=9789241547659|page=324}}</ref>。它還可用於多種家用消毒劑和傷口清潔劑<ref name="Gri2016">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=EyypCwAAQBAJ&pg=SA99-PA902|title=Rook's Textbook of Dermatology, 4 Volume Set|last2=Barker|first2=Jonathan|last3=Bleiker|first3=Tanya|last4=Chalmers|first4=Robert|last5=Creamer|first5=Daniel|publisher=John Wiley & Sons|isbn=9781118441176|page=128.38|language=en|last1=Griffiths|first1=Christopher}}</ref>。它比其他一些可用的藥劑效果較差<ref name="Di2007">{{cite journal|title=A review of anti-septic agents for pre-operative skin preparation.|date=2007|journal=Plastic surgical nursing : official journal of the American Society of Plastic and Reconstructive Surgical Nurses|issue=4|volume=27|pages=185-9; quiz 190-1|pmid=18165724|last1=Digison|first1=MB}}</ref><ref name="Mah2014">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=VloMBAAAQBAJ&pg=PA67|title=Textbook of Diagnostic Microbiology|last2=Lehman|first2=Donald C.|last3=Jr|first3=George Manuselis|date=2014|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences|isbn=9780323292627|edition=5|page=67|language=en|last1=Mahon|first1=Connie R.}}</ref>。它一般由溶液形式提供<ref name="WHO2008" />。

副作用一般很少，但可能包括對皮膚的刺激<ref name="WHO2008" /><ref name="Di2007" />。它可以與水或酒精混合使用<ref name="WHO2008" />。對於革蘭氏陽性菌來說，'''氯二甲酚'''是最有效的<ref name="WHO2008" />。它的工作原理是細胞壁的破壞和停止酶的功能<ref name="Mah2014" />。

'''氯二甲酚'''最早是在1927年被提出<ref name="La1986">{{cite journal|title=An approach for selection of health care personnel handwashing agents.|last2=Talbot|first2=GH|date=August 1986|journal=Infection control : IC|issue=8|volume=7|pages=419-24|pmid=3091524|last1=Larson|first1=E}}</ref>。它是世界衛生組織的基本藥物清單，是基本衛生系統中最重要的藥物<ref name="WHO19th">{{cite web|url=http://www.who.int/medicines/publications/essentialmedicines/EML_2015_FINAL_amended_NOV2015.pdf?ua=1|title=WHO Model List of Essential Medicines (19th List)|accessdate=8 December 2016|date=April 2015|work=World Health Organization}}</ref>。在開發中國家的批發成本中每5%溶液約為每公升2至6.20美元<ref name="ERC2014">{{cite web|url=http://erc.msh.org/dmpguide/resultsdetail.cfm?language=english&code=CHX4S&s_year=2014&year=2014&str=4%2E8%25&desc=Chloroxylenol&pack=new&frm=SOLUTION&rte=N/A&class_code2=15.2.&supplement=&class_name=Disinfectants|title=Chloroxylenol|accessdate=8 December 2016|website=International Drug Price Indicator Guide}}{{dead link|date=2017年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>。它以多種製劑銷售，並以多種品牌名稱出售，如：Dettol（'''滴露'''）<ref name="Gri2016" /><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.drugs.com/ingredient/chloroxylenol.html|title=Chloroxylenol - brand name list from Drugs.com|website=www.drugs.com}}</ref>。

==社会和文化==
对氯间二甲苯酚溶液有多种品牌名称，最著名的是“滴露”。滴露消毒液含 4.8% 对氯间二甲苯酚，<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.mhra.gov.uk/home/groups/spcpil/documents/spcpil/con1412920124477.pdf |title=Summary of Product Characteristics / Dettol Liquid |date=22 November 2010 |publisher=MHRA |accessdate=9 November 2014 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=http://webarchive.nationalarchives.gov.uk/20150108224624/http%3A//www.mhra.gov.uk/home/groups/spcpil/documents/spcpil/con1412920124477.pdf |archivedate=8 January 2015 |df= }}</ref>其余为作为溶剂的[[松节油|松节油]]、[[异丙醇|异丙醇]]、[[蓖麻油|蓖麻油]]、皂基和水。向滴露溶液加水会得到白色的乳浊液。
