'''对羟基苯甲酸酯'''（{{lang-en|Paraben}}）是一類用在[[化妆品|化妆品]]及[[药品|药品]]工業中的[[防腐剂|防腐剂]]，有时也会被用在[[食品添加剂|食品添加剂]]中，它的鹽及化合物最初則用作[[殺細菌劑|殺細菌]]及[[殺真菌劑|殺真菌劑]]。在[[洗髮水|洗髮水]]、商業[[潤膚膏|潤膚膏]]、[[刮鬍膏|刮鬍膏]]、[[人體潤滑劑|人體潤滑劑]]、[[外用|外用]]藥品、噴霧溶劑、[[化妝品|化妝品]]<ref>[http://www.fda.gov/cosmetics/productandingredientsafety/selectedcosmeticingredients/ucm128042.htm (www.fda.gov)] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111030154312/http://www.fda.gov/cosmetics/productandingredientsafety/selectedcosmeticingredients/ucm128042.htm |date=2011-10-30 }}{{en}}</ref>和[[牙膏|牙膏]]中均可找到此類化合物。

此類化合物如此常見，是因為它們為有效且低成本的防腐劑，而且已用了一段歷史，其天然代替品如[[葡萄柚籽提取物|葡萄柚籽提取物]]卻不如它們般有效<ref name="von Woedtke T">{{cite journal |author=von Woedtke T, Schluter B, Pflegel P, Lindequist U, Julich WD |title=Aspects of the antimicrobial efficacy of grapefruit seed extract and its relation to preservative substances contained |publisher=Institute of Pharmacy, Ernst Moritz Arndt University, |location=Greifswald, Germany |journal=Pharmazie |date=1999 June |volume=54|issue=6 |pages=452–6 |pmid=10399191}}{{en}}</ref>。然而，它們的使用卻愈來愈有爭議性，因它們曾在[[乳癌|乳癌]]的[[腫瘤|腫瘤]]中被找到（平均1克組織中含20納克）<ref name="pmid14745840">{{cite journal |pmid=14745840 |author=Harvey PW, Everett DJ |title=Significance of the detection of esters of p-hydroxybenzoic acid (parabens) in human breast tumours |volume=24 |issue=1 |pages=1–4 |doi=10.1002/jat.957 |journal=Journal of Applied Toxicology |date=Jan 2004}}{{en}}</ref>。它們表現出[[仿雌激素|仿雌激素]]的作用，可能因此參與乳癌的發展<ref name="pmid14745840"/>。儘管如此，它們與乳癌間的直接關係卻暫時未獲證實<ref name="Golden R, Gandy J, Vollmer G 2005 435–58">{{cite journal |author=Golden R, Gandy J, Vollmer G |title=A review of the endocrine activity of parabens and implications for potential risks to human health |journal=Critical Reviews in Toxicology |year=2005 |volume=35 |issue=5 |pages=435–58 |doi=10.1080/10408440490920104 |pmid=16097138}}{{en}}</ref>。另外，它們仿雌激素的特性可能会令女童青春期提前变得更普遍。<ref>{{cite news| url=http://www.nytimes.com/2012/04/01/magazine/puberty-before-age-10-a-new-normal.html | work=The New York Times | first=Elizabeth | last=Weil | title=Puberty Before Age 10: A New 'Normal'? | date=March 30, 2012}}{{en}}</ref>。

== 化學性質 ==
对羟基苯甲酸酯是[[对羟基苯甲酸|对羟基苯甲酸]]的[[酯|酯]]。常見的品種包括其[[对羟基苯甲酸甲酯|甲酯]]（[[E編碼|E編碼]]：E218）、[[对羟基苯甲酸乙酯|乙酯]]（E214）、[[对羟基苯甲酸丙酯|丙酯]]（E216）、[[对羟基苯甲酸丁酯|丁酯]]和[[对羟基苯甲酸己酯|己酯]]（E209）。此頁右上角的圖為此類化合物的通用結構，R為一烷羥，可為[[甲基|甲基]]、[[乙基|乙基]]、[[丙基|丙基]]或[[丁基|丁基]]。

== 天然存在 ==
有些对羟基苯甲酸酯在植物中存在，如[[藍莓|藍莓]]中存在[[對羥基苯甲酸甲酯|對羥基苯甲酸甲酯]]<ref>{{cite journal |title=Antimicrobial Agents From Higher Plants. Antimicrobial Agents From [[Peganum_harmala|Peganum harmala]] Seeds |author=Al-Shamma A, Drake S, Flynn DL, Mitscher LA, Park YH, Rao GSR, Simpson A, Swayze JK, Veysoglu T, Wu STS |journal=J Nat Prod |year=1981 |volume=44|issue=6 |pages=745–747 |doi=10.1021/np50018a025 |pmid=7334386}}{{en}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |author=Bais HP, Vepachedu R, Vivanco JM, |title=Root specific elicitation and exudation of fluorescent beta-carbolines in transformed root cultures of Oxalis tuberosa |journal=Plant Physiology and Biochemistry |volume=41 |issue=4 |date=1 April 2003 |pages=345–353 |doi=10.1016/S0981-9428(03)00029-9}}{{en}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |title=In Vitro Cellular and Developmental Biology |journal=Plant |volume=37 |issue=6 |year=2001 |pages=730–741}}{{en}}</ref>，作為其抗微生物劑。

==合成==
对羟基苯甲酸酯可由[[對羟基苯甲酸|對羟基苯甲酸]]加上適當的[[醇|醇]]（如[[甲醇|甲醇]]、[[乙醇|乙醇]]和[[1-丙醇|1-丙醇]]）的[[酯化反應|酯化反應]]製成。對羟基苯甲酸則可以[[苯酚|苯酚]]鉀和[[二氧化碳|二氧化碳]]，經[[科爾貝－施密特反應|科爾貝－施密特反應]]製成。

== 毒理學 ==
對[[齧齒目|齧齒目]]動物之急性、次慢性及慢性反應的研究指出对羟基苯甲酸酯實際上無毒<ref name=safety>{{cite journal |author=Soni MG, Carabin IG, Burdock GA |title=Safety assessment of esters of p-hydroxybenzoic acid (parabens) |journal=[[食品與化學品毒理學|食品與化學品毒理學]] |year=2005 |volume=43 |issue=7 |pages=985–1015 |doi=10.1016/j.fct.2005.01.020 |pmid=15833376}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |author=Soni MG, Taylor SL, Greenberg NA, Burdock GA |title=Evaluation of the health aspects of methyl paraben: a review of the published literature |journal=食品與化學品毒理學 |year=2002 |volume=40 |issue=10 |pages=1335–73 |doi=10.1016/S0278-6915(02)00107-2 |pmid=12387298}}</ref>，它們被急速吸收、[[代謝|代謝]]，然後排泄<ref name=safety/>。其主要[[代謝物|代謝物]]為[[對羟基苯甲酸|對羟基苯甲酸]]、對羥基馬尿酸、對羥基苯甲基[[葡萄糖醛酸鹽|葡萄糖醛酸鹽]]和對羰基苯基硫酸鹽<ref>{{cite journal |author=Elder RL |year=1984 |title=Final report on the safety assessment of methylparaben, ethylparaben, propylparaben, and butylparaben |journal=[[J_Am_Coll_Toxicol|J Am Coll Toxicol]] |volume=3 |pages=147–209}}</ref>。

=== 過敏反應 ===
對羥基苯甲酸酯對擁有正常皮膚的人的大多數部份不會造成刺激和敏感，可是對少數有對羥基苯甲酸酯[[過敏|過敏]]的人卻會刺激皮膚甚至導致[[刺激性皮膚炎|刺激性皮膚炎]]和[[酒槽鼻|酒槽鼻]]<ref>{{cite journal |author=Nagel JE, Fuscaldo JT, Fireman P. Paraben allergy |journal=[[J_Am_Med_Assoc|J Am Med Assoc]] |date=April 11, 1977 |volume=237 |issue=15 |pages=1594–5 |pmid=576658 |doi=10.1001/jama.237.15.1594 |title=Paraben allergy.}}</ref>。

=== 乳癌 ===
在20個乳房肿瘤組織的小样本中，對羥基苯甲酸酯的平均濃度為每克20納克<ref name="pmid14745840"/><ref name="JAT">{{cite journal |author=Darbre PD, Aljarrah A, Miller WR, Coldham NG, Sauer MJ, Pope GS |title=Concentrations of parabens in human breast tumours |journal=J Appl Toxicol|date=2004 Jan-Feb |volume=24|issue=1|pages=5–13 |pmid=14745841 |doi=10.1002/jat.958}}</ref>。這些發現連同某些對羥基苯甲酸酯所展現出來的部份近似[[雌激素|雌激素]]（一種參與乳癌發展的[[荷爾蒙|荷爾蒙]]<ref name="pmid14745840"/>）的特性，使有些科學家總結出對羥基苯甲酸酯與乳癌的發生有關<ref name="New Scientist">{{cite web |publisher=New Scientist |url=http://www.newscientist.com/article/dn4555-cosmetic-chemicals-found-in-breast-tumours.html |date=12 January 2004 |author=Vince G |title=Cosmetic chemicals found in breast tumours}}</ref><ref name=CBS>{{Cite news| last =Lagorio| first = Christine| title = The Cancer-Antiperspirant "Myth": Could Shaving And Antiperspirant Be Related To Breast Cancer?| newspaper = CBS News| date =Dec 5, 2005| url = http://www.cbsnews.com/stories/2005/12/05/eveningnews/main1098995.shtml| accessdate = 2 Jan 2009}}</ref>，並要求研究這種因果關聯存在與否<ref name=Harveyetal>{{cite journal |author=Harvey PW, Darbre P  |title=Endocrine disruptors and human health: Could estrogenic chemicals in body care cosmetics adversely affect breast cancer incidence in women?  A review of evidence and call for further research |journal=Journal of Applied Toxicology |year=2004 |volume=24 |issue=3 |pages=167–76 |pmid= 15211609 |doi=10.1002/jat.978 }}{{en}}</ref>。根據一份英國研究指出，由分子生物學家Philippa Darbre帶領的團隊在腫瘤找到的對羥基苯甲酸酯顯示它們來自用於皮膚的物質，例如腋下防臭劑、乳膏和體用噴霧，並指出此結果解釋了為何近60%的乳房腫瘤都在乳房的上外方近腋下的部分找到。<ref name="New Scientist"/>[[應用毒理學期刊|應用毒理學期刊]]編輯菲利普·哈維（Philip Harvey）則認為，雖然研究未證實對羥基苯甲酸酯導致那些腫瘤的形成，但與乳癌個案的增加有確實關係<ref name="New Scientist"/>。另外，[[西北大學_(伊利諾州)|美國伊利諾州西北大學]]的一個研究則指出年輕時期被確診乳癌與經常使用止汗劑、防臭劑和腋下脫毛膏有關<ref name="EJCP">{{Cite journal|last=McGrath| first=Kris |title=An earlier age of breast cancer diagnosis related to more frequent use of antiperspirants/deodorants and underarm shaving |journal=European Journal of Cancer Prevention |volume=12 |issue=6 |pages=479–85 |date=2003 Dec |pmid=14639125|doi=10.1097/00008469-200312000-00006 | url=http://terranaturals.com/pdf/mcgrath_full.pdf | format=PDF}}{{en}}</ref>。

儘管如此，尚未有證據證明對羥基苯甲酸酯與乳癌的因果關係。[[美國癌症協會|美國癌症協會]]總結出未有足夠的科學證據證實使用如止汗劑的用品會增加個人患上乳癌的風險，但亦指出需要更大型的研究查明對羥基苯甲酸酯如何影響患癌風險（如有）<ref>[http://www.cancer.org/docroot/MED/content/MED_6_1x_Antiperspirants.asp?sitearea=MED The American Cancer Society] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20040816090129/http://www.cancer.org/docroot/MED/content/MED_6_1x_Antiperspirants.asp?sitearea=MED |date=2004-08-16 }} Antiperspirants and Breast Cancer Risk{{en}}</ref>。

=== 雌激素活動 ===
[[動物實驗|動物實驗]]總結出對羥基苯甲酸酯有弱的[[雌激素|雌激素]]活動，如同[[仿雌激素|仿雌激素]]<ref name=byfordetal>{{cite journal |author=Byford JR, Shaw LE, Drew MG, Pope GS, Sauer MJ, Darbre PD |title=Oestrogenic activity of parabens in MCF7 human breast cancer cells |journal=J Steroid Biochem Mol Biol |date=2002 Jan |volume=80 |issue=1 |pages=49–60 |pmid=11867263 |doi=10.1016/S0960-0760(01)00174-1}}</ref>。

=== 日光暴露 ===
有研究指出[[對羥基苯甲酸甲酯|對羥基苯甲酸甲酯]]在皮膚上會與[[紫外線|紫外線]]B反應，加速皮膚老化和破壞DNA<ref>{{cite journal |author=Osamu Handa, Satoko Adachi, Tomohisa Takagi ''et al.'' |doi=10.1016/j.tox.2006.07.018 |title=Methylparaben potentiates UV-induced damage of skin keratinocytes |journal=[[Toxicology_(journal)|Toxicology]] |volume=227 |issue=1-2 |date=3 October 2006 |pages=62–72 |pmid=16938376}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |author=Yoshinori Okamoto, Tomohiro Hayashi, Shinpei Matsunami, Koji Ueda, Nakao Kojima |doi=10.1021/tx800066u |title=Combined activation of methyl paraben by light irradiation and esterase metabolism toward oxidative DNA damage |pmid=18656963 |journal=[[Chemical_Research_in_Toxicology|Chemical Research in Toxicology]] |date=July 26, 2008 |volume=21 |issue=8 |pages=1594–9}}</ref>。

== 爭議 ==
以上研究所致的科學辯論已造成大眾爭議<ref>{{cite journal |title=Can rumors cause cancer? |author=Jones J |journal=[[Journal_of_the_National_Cancer_Institute|Journal of the National Cancer Institute]] |volume=92 |issue=18 |pages=1469–71 |date=September 20, 2000 |doi=10.1093/jnci/92.18.1469 |pmid=10995800
}}</ref><ref name="breastcancer org">{{cite web |publisher=Breastcancer.org |url=http://www.breastcancer.org/risk/myths/antipersp.jsp |title=Antiperspirants do NOT cause breast cancer |date=August 15, 2008 |accessdate=April 15, 2010 }}; {{cite web |publisher=Breastcancer.org |url=http://www.breastcancer.org/risk/factors/ask_expert/2005_08/question_09.jsp |title=Link between antiperspirants and breast cancer? |date=October 2, 2008 |accessdate=May 3, 2012 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111212112542/http://www.breastcancer.org/risk/factors/ask_expert/2005_08/question_09.jsp |archivedate=2011年12月12日 }}; {{cite web |publisher=Breastcancer.org |url=http://www.breastcancer.org/risk/factors/no_evidence.jsp |title=Common Fears With No Evidence: Antiperspirants and Bras |date=March 20, 2012 |accessdate=May 3, 2012 }}</ref>，當中包括討論持續使用對羥基苯甲酸酯作為防腐劑可能的致癌風險和其雌激素效果<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Harvey | first1 = PW | last2 = Everett | first2 = DJ | title = Significance of the detection of esters of p-hydroxybenzoic acid (parabens) in human breast tumours. | journal = Journal of applied toxicology : JAT | volume = 24 | issue = 1 | pages = 1–4 | year = 2004 | pmid = 14745840 | doi = 10.1002/jat.957}}</ref><ref name="org consumer">{{cite web |author=Rita Arditti |publisher=Organic Consumers Association |date=June 9, 2004 |url=http://www.organicconsumers.org/bodycare/breastcancer090604.cfm |title=Cosmetics, parabens, and breast cancer |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070319223937/http://www.organicconsumers.org/bodycare/breastcancer090604.cfm |archivedate=2007年3月19日 |df= }}</ref>。

化妝品工業的主流根據此類化合物的長期使用和安全紀錄及科學研究，相信它們與多數化妝品成份無異，可以安全使用<ref name=antiperspirantsinfo>{{cite web |publisher=[[Unilever|Unilever]] |url=http://www.antiperspirantsinfo.com |title=The truth about antiperspirants and breast cancer}}</ref><ref name=antiperspirantsinfo2>{{cite web |publisher=[[ChemistryViews|ChemistryViews]] |url=http://www.chemistryviews.org/details/ezine/1452093/Underarm_Hygiene_Does_Not_Cause_Breast_Cancer.html |title=Underarm Hygiene Does Not Cause Breast Cancer}}{{en}}</ref>。而關注化妝品成份的公眾權益機構則相信需要更多的研究，以證明此類化合物的安全性（稱為「[[預防原則|預防原則]]」）<ref name="org consumer"/>。對[[內分泌干擾素|內分泌干擾素]]的擔憂亦使顧客和公司尋求無此類化合物的替代品<ref>{{cite news |author=Lebovits SC |publisher=The Boston Globe |url=http://www.boston.com/business/technology/articles/2008/05/26/cosmetics_firms_heed_calls_for_organics/ |title=Cosmetics firms heed calls for organics |date=May 26, 2008}}{{en}}</ref>。
