《'''铁幕来鸿'''》是一部美国[[黑色电影|黑色电影]]<ref>Kutner, Jerry C., ''[http://www.brightlightsfilm.com/54/noirgolden.htm Beyond the Golden Age - Film Noir Since the '50s]'', brightlightsfilm.com, 1994, 2006, retrieved 31 December 2008: "Among the films of 1970, John Huston's The Kremlin Letter was as unself-consciously noir as his '40s and '50s work."</ref>，[[约翰·休斯顿|约翰·休斯顿]]执导，[[理查德·布恩|理查德·布恩]]、[[奥森·威尔斯|奥森·威尔斯]]、[[马克斯·冯·叙多|马克斯·冯·叙多]]、[[毕比·安德森|毕比·安德森]]、[[帕特里克·奥尼尔|帕特里克·奥尼尔]]和[[乔治·桑德斯_(演员)|乔治·桑德斯]]出演。1970年由[[20世纪福克斯|20世纪福克斯]]发行。<ref name="Holcom">hollywood.com, ''[https://archive.is/20130125140557/http://www.hollywood.com/movie/The_Kremlin_Letter/234163 The Kremlin Letter]'', retrieved 31 December 2008</ref>剧本是休斯顿和[[格拉迪斯·希尔|格拉迪斯·希尔]]合写，根据[[诺埃尔·贝恩|诺埃尔·贝恩]]的小说忠实改编。<ref>nytimes.com, ''[http://query.nytimes.com/gst/fullpage.html?res=9B02EFDC1338F932A05754C0A96E958260 Noel Behn, 70, Novelist, Producer and Screenwriter]'', 31 July 1998, retrieved 31 December 2008</ref>评论家称该片的摄影“美丽”、“动人”<ref name="tvguide">tvguide.com, ''[http://movies.tvguide.com/kremlin-letter/review/103382 The Kremlin Letter]'', retrieved 31 December 2008</ref><ref name="variety">variety.com, ''[http://www.variety.com/review/VE1117792387.html?categoryid=31&cs=1 The Kremlin Letter]'', 1 January 1970, retrieved 31 December 2008</ref>，讲述了一个高度复杂、真实，充满了难以接受的阴谋和间谍活动的故事<ref name="variety"/>，这个故事设定在1969-1970年交替的冬天，正是[[冷战|冷战]]高峰期。当年该片遭到票房失败和评论界冷遇，但之后的几十年受到评论界持续的赞誉。法国导演[[让-皮埃尔·梅尔维尔|让-皮埃尔·梅尔维尔]]称赞该片是“杰作”、“为电影设立了标准”。<ref name="hudson">Hudson, Bob, "[http://linguaromana.byu.edu/Hudson4.html J.-P. Melville's Quest for the Absolute: Persistent Perfectionism and Realistic Obsession in His "Last" Films] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090103155927/http://linguaromana.byu.edu/Hudson4.html |date=2009-01-03 }}," ''Lingua Romana: a journal of French, Italian and Romanian culture'', fall 2005 (volume 4, issue 1), retrieved 31 December 2008</ref>
