《'''铁杉树丛'''》（{{lang-en|'''''Hemlock Grove'''''}}，又译：毒林）是一部[[美国|美国]][[电视剧|电视剧]]，第一季共13集，于2013年4月19日起在[[Netflix|Netflix]]上提供第一季全季观看<ref name=pressrel>{{Cite web|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2013/01/09/hemlock-grove-eli-roths-gothic-thriller-developed-by-brian-mcgreevy-and-lee-shipman-available-only-on-netflix-april-19-626311/20130109netflix01/ |title="Hemlock Grove" Eli Roth's Gothic Thriller Developed by Brian McGreevy & Lee Shipman Available Only on Netflix April 19|date=January 9, 2013|last=Tartaglione|first=Nancy|work=[[Deadline.com|Deadline.com]]|accessdate=January 9, 2013}}</ref>。2013年6月19日Netflix宣布续订第二季10集<ref>{{cite news|last=Seat42f|title=Hemlock Grove Renewed|url=http://www.seat42f.com/hemlock-grove-renewed.html|accessdate=19 June 2013}}</ref>。

== 简介 ==
一个女孩的尸体被抛弃在戈德弗雷家族的钢厂附近，由于警方遲遲不能破案，而17岁的[[吉普赛|吉普赛]]少年彼得·鲁曼切克（Peter Rumancek）和戈德弗雷家族的罗曼·戈弗雷（Roman Godfrey）也成为嫌疑人。为了洗清嫌疑两人决定一同去调查，但是他们很快就发现了惊讶的事情，那就是凶手可能是镇上任何一个人或动物<ref>[http://tvfantasy.net/2013/03/08/newsletter-2013-03-07-batb-supernatural-glee-go-on-arrow-and-more/ 3月7日简讯：侠胆雄狮、邪恶力量、欢乐合唱团、生活向前冲、绿箭侠及其他]</ref>。

== 开发和制作 ==
2011年12月Deadline报道Netflix将预订一部名为《Hemlock Grove》的剧集，并表示第一季将制作13集<ref name=neardeal>{{Cite web|url=http://www.deadline.com/2011/12/netflix-nears-13-episode-order-for-horror-drama-hemlock-grove-from-eli-roth-and-gaumont-international-television/ |title=Netflix Nears Order For Eli Roth Horror Drama 'Hemlock Grove' From Eli Roth And Gaumont International Television|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|work=Deadline.com|date=December 12, 2011|accessdate=July 5, 2012}}</ref><ref>[http://tvfantasy.net/2011/12/13/december-13-newsletter/ 12月13日简讯]</ref>，Netflix于2012年3月发布新闻稿确认此事<ref name=03-21-12>{{Cite web|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2012/03/21/only-on-netflix-premier-horror-suspense-director-eli-roth-reanimates-the-gothic-novel-with-hemlock-grove-981305/20120321netflix01/|title=Only on Netflix: Premier Horror Suspense Director Eli Roth Reanimates the Gothic Novel With "Hemlock Grove"|date=March 21, 2012|work=The Futon Critic|accessdate=July 3, 2012}}</ref>。伊莱·罗斯将担任本剧执行制片人同时他将执导试播集<ref name=neardeal/><ref>[http://www.shocktillyoudrop.com/news/173201-wondercon-interview-eli-roth-on-hemlock-grove-werewolf-transformation?utm_source=twitterfeed&utm_medium=twitter WonderCon Interview: Eli Roth On Hemlock Grove's Werewolf Transformation, Italian Horror Connection & Mainstream Gore]{{Dead link|date=2018年6月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=no }}</ref>，三月中旬德兰·萨拉菲安（Deran Sarafian）确认将担任本剧执行制片人以及剧集导演，他将执导第一季6集<ref>[http://collider.com/hemlock-grove-panel-recap-wondercon/ WonderCon 2013: HEMLOCK GROVE Panel Recap Featuring Eli Roth and Famke Janssen]</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.deadline.com/2012/03/deran-sarafian-signs-with-wme-joins-hemlock-grove-as-executive-producer/ |title=Deran Sarafian Signs With WME, Joins 'Hemlock Grove' As Executive Producer|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|date=March 16, 2012|work=Deadline.com|accessdate=July 5, 2012}}</ref>。

本片将在[[多伦多|多伦多]]拍摄<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://playbackonline.ca/2012/06/04/hemlock-grove-shoot-books-torontos-cinespace-stage|title=Hemlock Grove shoot books Toronto’s Cinespace stage|last=Vlessing|first=Etan|date=June 4, 2012|work=[[Playback_(website)|Playback]]|accessdate=February 19, 2013}}</ref>，于2012年7月13日在多伦多佩里港开机<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.durhamregion.com/news/article/1392348--film-crews-to-return-to-port-perry |title=Film crews to return to Port Perry|last=Hall|first=Chris|date=March 16, 2012|work=durhamregion.com|accessdate=July 14, 2012}}</ref>。

根据报道第一季的预算约为4500万美元<ref name=pullplug>{{Cite web|url=http://www.post-gazette.com/stories/ae/tv-radio/tv-series-pulls-plug-on-filming-here-635575/?p=0|title=TV series pulls plug on filming here|date=May 12, 2012|last=Owen|first=Rob|work=Pittsburgh Post-Gazette|accessdate=July 3, 2012}}</ref>。

=== 选角 ===
2012年3月Netflix宣布[[芳姬·詹森|芳姬·詹森]]和[[比爾·史柯斯嘉|比爾·史柯斯嘉]]将出演本剧，詹森将饰演奥利维娅·戈德弗雷（Olivia Godfrey）而史柯斯嘉将饰演前者角色的儿子罗曼·戈德弗雷（Roman Godfrey）<ref name=03-21-12/>，4月兰顿·利伯伊伦确认将饰演彼得·鲁曼切克一角色<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/terra-nova-landon-liboiron-netflix-hemlock-grove-eli-roth-307348 |title=Terra Nova's' Landon Liboiron Joins Netflix's 'Hemlock Grove' (Exclusive)|date=April 3, 2012|last=Goldberg|first=Lesley|work=[[The_Hollywood_Reporter|The Hollywood Reporter]]|accessdate=July 5, 2012}}</ref>。

4月4日佩内洛普·米切尔确认将饰演罗曼·戈德弗雷的堂妹莉萨·戈德弗雷<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.deadline.com/2012/04/series-hemlock-grove-pilots-malibu-country-louis-ckferesten-add-to-casts/ |title=Series 'Hemlock Grove', Pilots 'Malibu Country' & Louis CK/Feresten Add To Casts|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|date=April 2, 2012|work=Deadline.com|accessdate=July 5, 2012}}</ref>。

4月10日弗蕾娅·廷利确认将饰演克里斯蒂娜·文达尔<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.deadline.com/2012/04/latarsha-rose-cast-in-bets-mary-jane-freya-tingley-in-netflixs-hemlock-grove/ |title=Latarsha Rose, Omari Hardwick Join 'Mary Jane'; Freya Tingley To 'Hemlock Grove'|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|date=April 10, 2012|work=Deadline.com|accessdate=July 5, 2012}}</ref>。

4月12日[[多格雷·斯科特|多格雷·斯科特]]表示自己将在本剧饰演诺曼·戈德弗雷（Norman Godfrey），这个角色是奥利维娅的大伯以及莉萨的父亲<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.deadline.com/2012/04/dougray-scott-to-star-in-netflixs-hemlock-grove/ |title=Dougray Scott To Star In Netflix's 'Hemlock Grove'|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|date=April 12, 2012|work=Deadline.com|accessdate=July 5, 2012}}</ref>。

7月2日莉莉·泰勒确认将饰演彼得·鲁曼切克的母亲；而坎迪丝·麦克卢尔饰演肖瑟博士<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://tvline.com/2012/07/02/lili-taylor-hemlock-grove/ |title=Exclusive: Six Feet Under Vet Lili Taylor Joins Netflix Thriller Series Hemlock Grove|last=Roots|first=Kimberly|date=July 2, 2012|work=[[TVLine|TVLine]]|accessdate=July 5, 2012}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.deadline.com/2012/07/kandyse-mcclure-joins-cast-of-hemlock-grove/ |title=Kandyse McClure Joins Cast Of 'Hemlock Grove'|date=July 2, 2012|work=Deadline.com|accessdate=July 5, 2012}}</ref>。

7月8日亚伦·道格拉斯确认将饰演警长斯沃恩<ref>[http://www.deadline.com/2012/07/netflix-castings-house-of-cards-hemlock-grove/ Netflix Castings: ‘House Of Cards’, ‘Hemlock Grove’]</ref>，随后蒂约·霍恩确认将出演德斯蒂娜·鲁曼切克<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/hemlock-grove-netflix-tiio-horn-355974?utm_source=feedburner&utm_medium=feed&utm_campaign=Feed%3A+thr%2Ftelevision+%28The+Hollywood+Reporter+-+Television%29 |title='18 to Life' Alum to Play Gypsy Witch-Prostitute on Netflix's 'Hemlock Grove' (Exclusive)|last=Goldberg|first=Lesley|date=March 16, 2012|work=The Hollywood Reporter|accessdate=July 14, 2012}}</ref>。
