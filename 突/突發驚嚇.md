'''突發驚嚇'''（Jump scare）是一種常用於恐怖電影和恐怖電子遊戲的手法，旨在透過影像或事件的突然轉變來使觀眾大驚失色，通常會伴隨著巨大或駭人的聲音<ref>{{cite book |url= https://books.google.com/books?id=feXpAgAAQBAJ&lpg=PT7&ots=3iS5AKU3dw&dq=%22jump%20scare%22&lr&pg=PT14#v=onepage&q&f=false |title= Horror Films FAQ |year= 2013 |author= John Muir |publisher= Hal Leonard Corporation |isbn= 1-4803-6681-1 |accessdate= 2015-01-01}}</ref><ref name="verge" />。突發驚嚇被描述為「構成恐怖電影基本零件之一」<ref name="verge">{{cite web |url= http://www.theverge.com/2012/10/31/3574592/art-of-the-jump-scare-horror-movies |title= 'Why won't you die?!' The art of the jump scare |date= 2012-10-31 |accessdate= 2014-12-24 |work= The Verge |author= Bryan Bishop}}</ref>。突發驚嚇通常會出現在電影中的一段平靜、安靜、觀眾不期待會發生任何驚人內容的時間帶裡<ref>{{cite book |url= https://books.google.com/books?id=y_CmTysJi6AC&lpg=PA251&dq=jump%20scare&pg=PA52#v=onepage&q&f=false |title= Genre Filmmaking: A Visual Guide to Shots and Style for Genre Films |page= 52 |year= 2013 |author= Danny Draven |publisher= Taylor & Francis |isbn= 1-136-07078-8}}</ref>，或是為了回報觀眾長期的懸念而出現。<ref name=healthy>{{cite book |url= https://books.google.com/books?id=oliyEnpQibUC&lpg=PT166&dq=jump%20scare&pg=PT166#v=onepage&q=jump%20scare&f=false |title= The Healthy Edit: Creative Techniques for Perfecting Your Movie |year= 2013 |author= John Rosenberg |publisher= Taylor & Francis |isbn= 1-136-04073-0 |accessdate= 2014-12-24}}</ref>

一些評論家認為用突發驚嚇來嚇唬觀眾是一件很偷懶的事<ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.gamesradar.com/horror-games-dont-rely-jump-scares/ |title= 10 horror games that don't rely on jump scares |author= Lucas Sullivan |work= GamesRadar |accessdate= 2014-12-24}}</ref>，並認為恐怖作品在近幾年來因為過度依賴對話導致江河日下，這是現代恐怖電影的陳腔濫調。<ref>{{cite web |url= http://moviepilot.com/posts/2015/02/08/why-modern-horror-movies-don-t-get-it-right-more-often-2672187?lt_source=external,manual |title= Why Modern Horror Movies Don't Get it Right More Often |date= 2015-02-08 |first= Pedro |last= Diaz |work= Movie Pilot |accessdate= 2015-02-24 |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20150224165703/http://moviepilot.com/posts/2015/02/08/why-modern-horror-movies-don-t-get-it-right-more-often-2672187?lt_source=external,manual |archivedate= 2015-02-24 |deadurl= yes }}</ref>

== 使用於電影 ==
1980年代以前，突發驚嚇在恐怖電影裡是一個相對少見的事件，但在80年代初、因為[[砍殺電影|砍殺電影]]這個子類型受到歡迎而越來越常見。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://wheresthejump.com/do-modern-horror-movies-contain-more-jump-scares-than-older-movies/ |title=Do Modern Horror Movies Contain More Jump Scares Than Older Movies? - Where's The Jump?}}</ref>

1976年的電影《[[魔女嘉莉_(1976年電影)|魔女嘉莉]]》是第一部有現行突發驚嚇的作品<ref>{{cite web|url=http://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3197530/10-great-jump-scares-in-horror/2/|title=10 Great Jump Scares in Horror! - Bloody Disgusting!|date=2012-10-18|work=bloody-disgusting.com}}</ref>，當電影結束時出現的場景「某個已消亡的惡黨死而復生」被認為是1980年電影《十三號星期五》中突發驚嚇的靈感來源。<ref>{{cite book |url= https://books.google.com/books?id=Qlo6l0VGJNAC&pg=PA354&lpg=PA354&dq=%22as+close+as+I+could+steal+from+Carrie+without+being+arrested.%22&source=bl&ots=4LIk3mIrj_&sig=hLUUsSkbiSkN86D2yUx3L0iCB5E&hl=en&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwjOj5vAvbfLAhUovYMKHQzvCuwQ6AEIHTAA#v=onepage&q=%22as%20close%20as%20I%20could%20steal%20from%20Carrie%20without%20being%20arrested.%22&f=false |title= Reel Terror: The Scary, Bloody, Gory, Hundred-Year History of Classic Horror |page= 354 |year= 2012 |publisher= Macmillan |author= David Konow |isbn= 1-250-01359-3 |accessdate= 2016-03-10}}</ref>

1979年的電影《奪命電話》使用突發驚嚇手法同時為主角與觀眾突然揭示反派的現身，電影劇本作者William Cheng 認為這種手法能「突然抽掉主角和觀眾間的保護牆，讓觀眾在家裡也能感覺到入侵者更接近他們。」<ref>{{cite book |url= https://books.google.com/books?id=6KnFAgAAQBAJ&lpg=PA100&dq=%22jump%20scare%22&pg=PA100#v=onepage&q&f=false |title= Sound Play: Video Games and the Musical Imagination |page= 100 |year= 2014 |publisher= Oxford University Press |author= William Cheng |isbn= 0-19-996997-3 |accessdate= 2015-01-01}}</ref>

2009年的《[[地獄魔咒|地獄魔咒]]》內使用了突發驚嚇<ref name=healthy />，導演[[山姆·雷米|山姆·雷米]]表示他正是想製作一個能讓觀眾如「嚇一跳」的字面意思上跳起來的恐怖電影。<ref>{{cite web| last = Blair| first = Iain| title = Director's Chair: Sam Raimi Drag me to Hell| url = http://www.postmagazine.com/ME2/dirmod.asp?tier=4&mid=8F3A7027421841978F18BE895F87F791&sid=&nm=&id=9A2AE6F97AE946859A3C884BDE603E22&type=Publishing&mod=Publications%3A%3AArticle| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090802031958/http://www.postmagazine.com/ME2/dirmod.asp?sid=&nm=&type=Publishing&mod=Publications%3A%3AArticle&mid=8F3A7027421841978F18BE895F87F791&tier=4&id=9A2AE6F97AE946859A3C884BDE603E22| archivedate=2009-08-02| work = Post Magazine| date = 2009-07-01 | accessdate  = 2013-07-09}}</ref>

== 使用於電子遊戲 ==
1996年的《[[惡靈古堡_(遊戲)|生化危机]]》經常以第一個使用突發驚嚇的電子遊戲案例而被引用。當玩家在穿過遊戲中一些走廊和大廳時，背景音樂音量會逐漸降低，接著殭屍狗便會伴隨巨大聲響張牙舞爪地襲來。

《{{link-en|曙光 (電子遊戲)|Daylight (video game)}}》被稱為「一卡車的突發驚嚇」，評論家雖然讚譽遊戲將突發驚嚇運用得當，但也認為隨著玩家的遊戲時間變長，突發驚嚇的效果也不足以嚇到玩家了。<ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.polygon.com/2014/4/29/5662172/daylight-review |title= Daylight review: jump scare tactics |date= 2014-04-29 |accessdate= 2014-12-24 |first= Griffin |last= McElroy |work= Polygon}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.edge-online.com/reviews/daylight-review/ |title= Daylight review |date= 2014-05-01 |accessdate= 2015-01-01 |work= EDGE}}</ref>

2014年的《[[玩具熊的五夜後宮|玩具熊的五夜後宮]]》被評價為「現場直播用的傑作」，部份的原因便是使用了突發驚嚇。<ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.polygon.com/2014/11/13/7217503/five-nights-at-freddys-2-horror-streaming-indie |title= Why Five Nights at Freddy's 2 is a viral success |date= 2014-11-13 |accessdate= 2015-01-22 |first= Danielle |last= Riendeau |work= Polygon}}</ref>

== 在網際網路上 ==
在網際網路上，有一些旨在嚇唬使用者的圖像、影片、程式使用了突發驚嚇的手法，這種手法可能會以一張巨大的臉配合大聲尖叫來表現。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.geek.com/geek-cetera/what-you-dont-want-to-happen-when-you-computer-prank-someone-1295100/ | title=What you don’t want to happen when you computer prank someone | publisher=geek.com | date=2010-11-17 | accessdate=2015-07-08 | author=Osborne, Doug}}</ref><ref name="menning">{{cite web | url=http://www.urlesque.com/2010/10/13/screamers-the-history-of-the-scary-maze-prank/ | title=Screamers – The History of the Scary Maze Prank and its Cousins | publisher=urlesque.com | date=2010-10-13 | accessdate=2013-12-16 | author=Menning, Chris | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131217010347/http://www.urlesque.com/2010/10/13/screamers-the-history-of-the-scary-maze-prank/ | archivedate=2013-12-17 | deadurl=yes }}</ref>

網際網路突發驚嚇的早期例子是Jeremy Winterrowd設計的《The Maze》<ref name="menning" />，這個程式偽裝成一款電腦遊戲，玩家運用滑鼠操控藍色方塊按著指定的路線前進，並且不能觸到週遭的牆壁，牆壁會越來越狹窄、而部份玩家為了看清畫面、就會讓臉更貼近螢幕。遊戲起初，玩家會在碰到牆壁時返回遊戲選單，但如果玩家到了第三個關卡的某個階段，無論玩家有沒有碰到牆壁，電影《[[大法師|大法師]]》裡的角色{{link-en|蕾根·麥可尼爾|Regan MacNeil}}（[[琳達·布萊爾|琳達·布萊爾]]飾）都會突然出現在螢幕上以嚇唬玩家。

在YouTube等影片分享網站興起後，嚇人的影片從電子郵件連鎖信的形式轉變為記錄反應的影片，使用者在觀看他人推薦給它的影片前會用攝影機進行拍攝，並將自己的觀影過程（多半是被嚇到的過程）上傳並分享。一個值得矚目的嚇人影片是2006年由名為「Can't We All Just Get Along?」的用戶上傳至YouTube上的<ref>{{cite av media|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oh87njiWTmw|title=Scary Maze prank – The Original|date=2006-05-20|publisher=|via=YouTube}}</ref>，在那段影片裡，一群男孩在玩遊戲《Maze》，當上述的駭人景象出現後，男孩驚聲尖叫並大力拍打電腦螢幕，之後跑到拍攝者旁嚎啕大哭。至2017年，該影片已經有2700萬次瀏覽。<ref name=Middleton>Middleton, Jason. Documentary’s Awkward Turn: Cringe Comedy and Media Spectatorship. Routledge, 2013.</ref>
