'''蔡厝港体育场'''（{{lang-en|Choa Chu Kang Stadium}}），位于新加坡[[蔡厝港|蔡厝港]]规划区内，于2011年开放。球场可以容纳4268名观众，目前是[[新加坡职业足球联赛|新加坡职业足球联赛]][[勇士足球會|勇士足球會]]的主场。<ref name="ActiveSG"/><ref name="Soccerway 2012"/>

==球场设施==
球场的草坪是由[[瑞士|瑞士]]供应商提供，并且配有自动浇水系统，也被[[国际足球联合会|国际足球联合会]]比准举办国际比赛。<ref name="AsiaOne, Asias Leading News Portal, Get Latest News at AsiaOne 2009">{{cite web | title=THE WAY AHEAD | website=AsiaOne, Asia's Leading News Portal, Get Latest News at AsiaOne | date=2009-03-24 | url=http://www.asiaone.com/News/The%2BNew%2BPaper/Story/A1Story20090324-130601.html  | accessdate=2018-07-20 | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171030165449/http://www.asiaone.com/News/The%2BNew%2BPaper/Story/A1Story20090324-130601.html | archivedate=2017-10-30 | deadurl=no }}</ref>体育场也有也有一条8赛道的[[400米|400米]]塑胶跑步道。体育场傍边还有游泳池，健身室，网球场等设施。<ref name="ActiveSG1">{{cite web | title=Choa Chu Kang Sports Centre | website=ActiveSG | url=https://www.myactivesg.com/facilities/choa-chu-kang-sports-centre | accessdate=2018-07-20 | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170715015250/https://www.myactivesg.com/facilities/choa-chu-kang-sports-centre | archivedate=2017-07-15 | deadurl=no }}</ref>

==重要赛事==
蔡厝港体育场于[[2010年夏季青年奧林匹克運動會|2010年夏季青年奧林匹克運動會]]作为[[2010年夏季青年奧林匹克運動會足球比賽|足球比賽]]的训练场所。<ref>{{Cite web|title=Competition Venues for Singapore 2010 Youth Olympic Games |publisher=Singapore Youth Olympic Games Organising Committee |date=29 April 2009 |url=http://www.singapore2010.sg/public/sg2010/en/en_news/en_media_releases/en_20090429_competition_venues_for_singapore_2010.html |accessdate=4 August 2010 |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20120915081923/http://www.singapore2010.sg/public/sg2010/en/en_news/en_media_releases/en_20090429_competition_venues_for_singapore_2010.html |archivedate=15 September 2012 |deadurl=yes |df= }}</ref>
