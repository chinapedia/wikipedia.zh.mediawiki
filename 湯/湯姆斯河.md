'''湯姆斯河'''（{{lang en|'''Toms River'''}}）是[[美國|美國]][[新澤西州|新澤西州]][[海洋縣|海洋縣]]的縣治。{{GR|6}}<ref name=NACo>[http://www.uscounties.org/cffiles_web/counties/county.cfm?id=34029 Ocean County, NJ]{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}, [[National_Association_of_Counties|National Association of Counties]]. Accessed January 21, 2013.</ref>根據[[美國2010年人口普查|2010年人口普查]]，本城共有人口91,239人<ref name=Census2010/><ref name=Districts2011/><ref name=LWD2010/><ref name=Census2010XLS>[http://2010.census.gov/news/xls/st34-final_newjersey.xls The Counties and Most Populous Cities and Townships in 2010 in New Jersey: 2000 and 2010] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110221020910/http://2010.census.gov/news/xls/st34-final_newjersey.xls |date=2011-02-21 }}, United States Census Bureau. Accessed February 15, 2012.</ref> ，相對[[美國2000年人口普查|2000年]]來說上升了1,533人，即上升了1.7%。而2000年的人口相對[[美國1990年人口普查|1990年]]來說則上升了13,335人，即上升了17.5%。<ref>[http://lwd.dol.state.nj.us/labor/lpa/census/2010/2010data/table7cm.xls Table 7. Population for the Counties and Municipalities in New Jersey: 1990, 2000 and 2010] {{Wayback|url=http://lwd.dol.state.nj.us/labor/lpa/census/2010/2010data/table7cm.xls |date=20130520191436 }}, [[New_Jersey_Department_of_Labor_and_Workforce_Development|New Jersey Department of Labor and Workforce Development]], February 2011. Accessed July 11, 2012.</ref>
