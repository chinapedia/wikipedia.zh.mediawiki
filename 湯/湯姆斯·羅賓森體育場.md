'''湯姆斯·羅賓森體育場'''是一座位於[[巴哈馬|巴哈馬]][[拿索_(巴哈馬)|拿索]]的綜合性體育場。目前大多使用於舉辦足球比賽。體育場可以容納15,000人，可以擴大至容納30000人。<ref>http://www.thebahamasweekly.com/publish/bis-news-updates/National_Stadium_will_provide_economic_opportunities_for_The_Bahamas_printer.shtml ... capacity to be expanded to accommodate 23,000 seats</ref>


體育場的名字[[湯姆·羅賓森|湯姆斯·A. "湯姆" 羅賓森]]，是一位[[田徑|田徑]]明星，曾代表[[巴哈馬|巴哈馬]]數屆奧運會。<ref>http://www.jonesbahamas.com/news/123/ARTICLE/20122/2009-07-24.html{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} ...the Track and Field stadium in Nassau was named in his honour.</ref><ref>http://www.iaaf.org/news/kind=100/newsid=52345.html ...The stadium in Nassau was named after him in 1981</ref>
