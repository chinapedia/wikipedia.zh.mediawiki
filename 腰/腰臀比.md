'''腰臀比例'''（{{lang|en|Waist–hip ratio}} '''WHR'''）是指腰圍和臀圍的比例，數值等於腰圍除以臀圍。

==量度==
嚴格來說，腰圍是在末根[[肋骨|肋骨]]與[[上腸骨|上腸骨]]中間的水平量度，而臀圍則在雙腿併攏時以[[大粗隆|大粗隆]]的水平量度<ref>{{cite journal|title=Waist circumference action levels in the identification of cardiovascular risk factors: prevalence study in a random sample|pages=1401–1405|issn=0959-8138|journal=BMJ (Clinical research ed.)|volume=311|issue=7017|date=1995-11-25|url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/8520275|accessdate=2019-02-13|author=T. S. Han, E. M. van Leer, J. C. Seidell, M. E. Lean|pmid=8520275|pmc=PMC2544423}}</ref>。實際上，以腰部最小圓周處（通常位於肚臍上方）作為腰圍值更方便簡單，而同樣地臀圍可採用臀部或髖關節最寬部分的數值<ref>[http://www.healthcalculators.org/calculators/waist_hip.asp Waist To Hip Calculator] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110408000742/http://www.healthcalculators.org/calculators/waist_hip.asp |date=2011-04-08 }} at University of Maryland Medical System. Retrieved Dec 2010</ref>。如果腰部因懷孕或肥胖而凸出，腰圍則以肚臍以上1英寸垂直水平處的圓周值代替。<ref name=Brown>[http://www.jstor.org/stable/3702758] {{cite journal |author=Brown JE, Potter JD, Jacobs DR, ''et al.'' |title=Maternal waist-to-hip ratio as a predictor of newborn size: Results of the Diana Project |journal=Epidemiology |volume=7 |issue=1 |pages=62–6 |pmid=8664403 |doi= |url=|date=January 1996}}</ref>

==健康指標==
腰臀比例被用作衡量健康和出現嚴重健康風險的指標，研究發現重量集中在腰部的人較重量集中在臀部的人面臨更多的健康風險。腰臀比例是用來測量肥胖，因而可作為其他較嚴重健康狀況的指標。男性和女性的標準比例分別是0.9和0.7，與一般健康和生育能力有極大關連，腰臀比例約0.9的男性較少出現[[前列腺癌|前列腺癌]]和[[睾丸癌|睾丸癌]]<ref name="MarloweApicellaReed2005"/>，而腰臀比例約0.7的女性具有最佳的[[雌激素|雌激素]]水平，較少出現[[糖尿病|糖尿病]]、[[心血管疾病|心血管疾病]]和[[卵巢癌|卵巢癌]]<ref name='LiveScience'>{{Cite journal| first= | last=| coauthors=| contribution=The Rules of Attraction in the Game of Love| title=The Rules of Attraction in the Game of Love| editor-first=| editor-last=| coeditors=| publisher=| place=| pages=| date=| year=| id= | contribution-url=http://www.livescience.com/health/060213_attraction_rules.html| format=}}</ref>。

相對於腰圍和[[身高體重指數|身高體重指數]]（BMI），腰臀比例更能有效預測中老年人的死亡率<ref>{{cite journal |author=Price GM, Uauy R, Breeze E, Bulpitt CJ, Fletcher AE |title=Weight, shape, and mortality risk in older persons: elevated waist-hip ratio, not high body mass index, is associated with a greater risk of death |journal=Am. J. Clin. Nutr. |volume=84 |issue=2 |pages=449–60 |pmid=16895897 |laysummary=http://www.medicalnewstoday.com/medicalnews.php?newsid=49218|date=August 2006}}</ref>。如果以腰臀比例取代BMI用來定義[[肥胖|肥胖]]，全球被歸類為心臟病高危的人數將增加三倍<ref>{{cite journal |author=Yusuf S, Hawken S, Ounpuu S, ''et al.'' |title=Obesity and the risk of myocardial infarction in 27,000 participants from 52 countries: a case-control study |journal=Lancet |volume=366 |issue=9497 |pages=1640–9 |pmid=16271645 |doi=10.1016/S0140-6736(05)67663-5|date=November 2005}}</ref>。[[體脂肪率|體脂肪率]]被認為是一個更精確的重量值。在這三個測試中，只有腰臀比例需要考慮身體結構的差異，因此不同BMI的人可能有相同腰臀比例，或者相同BMI的人可能有不同腰臀比例。縱觀出現在不同古代文明畫像的女性，她們絕大部分的腰臀比例介乎0.6至0.7，顯示低腰臀比例較受歡迎<ref name="Ratio">{{cite journal |author=Singh D |title=Female mate value at a glance: relationship of waist-to-hip ratio to health, fecundity and attractiveness |journal=Neuro Endocrinol. Lett. |volume=23 Suppl 4 |issue= |pages=81–91 |pmid=12496738 |url=http://faculty.bennington.edu/~sherman/sex/whr-singh2002.pdf|date=December 2002}}</ref>。其他研究則發現，腰圍而非腰臀比例是心血管危險因素<ref>{{cite journal |author=Dobbelsteyn CJ, Joffres MR, MacLean DR, Flowerdew G |title=A comparative evaluation of waist circumference, waist-to-hip ratio and body mass index as indicators of cardiovascular risk factors. The Canadian Heart Health Surveys |journal=Int. J. Obes. Relat. Metab. Disord. |volume=25 |issue=5 |pages=652–61 |pmid=11360147 |doi=10.1038/sj.ijo.0801582|date=May 2001}}</ref>、體脂分佈<ref>{{cite journal |author=Ketel IJ, Volman MN, Seidell JC, Stehouwer CD, Twisk JW, Lambalk CB |title=Superiority of skinfold measurements and waist over waist-to-hip ratio for determination of body fat distribution in a population-based cohort of Caucasian Dutch adults |journal=Eur. J. Endocrinol. |volume=156 |issue=6 |pages=655–61 |pmid=17535865 |doi=10.1530/EJE-06-0730|date=June 2007}}</ref>和[[2型糖尿病|2型糖尿病]]高血壓<ref>{{cite journal |author=Picon PX, Leitão CB, Gerchman F, ''et al.'' |title=[Waist measure and waist-to-hip ratio and identification of clinical conditions of cardiovascular risk: multicentric study in type 2 diabetes mellitus patients] |language=pt |journal=Arq Bras Endocrinol Metabol |volume=51 |issue=3 |pages=443–9 |pmid=17546244|date=April 2007}}</ref>的良好指標。

==智商==
孩子的認知能力與母親的腰臀比例有一定關係。研究人員利用[[美國國家衛生統計中心|美國國家衛生統計中心]]的數據，發現孩子在認知測試中的表現與母親的腰臀比例有關<ref name=Lassek>{{cite journal|journal=Evolution and Human Behavior|title=Waist-hip ratio and cognitive ability: is gluteofemoral fat a privileged store of neurodevelopmental resources?|last=Lassek|first=W.|coauthors=Gaulin S.|doi=10.1016/j.evolhumbehav.2007.07.005 |volume=29 |issue=1 |pages=26–34|date=January 2008}}</ref>，得分最高孩子的母親有寬臀和低腰臀比例，從而推測胎兒受益於臀部脂肪，這些脂肪含有多不飽和脂肪酸長鏈，對胎兒的大腦發展有關鍵的作用<ref name=Lassek/>。此外，有證據顯示低腰臀比例的兒童較少出現未成年生育常見的認知功能缺陷。
