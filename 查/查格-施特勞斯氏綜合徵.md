'''查格-施特勞斯綜合徵'''({{lang|en|Churg–Strauss syndrome (CSS)}}、也稱為"嗜酸性韋格納肉芽腫"[EGPA]"(eosinophilic granulomatosis with polyangiitis)或"過敏性肉芽腫"(allergic granulomatosis){{sfn|Adu|Emery|Madaio|2012|p=125}})是一種[[自體免疫性疾病|自體免疫性疾病]]狀況，如有氣道過敏([[過敏體質|過敏體質]](Atopy))過敏史的人會導致小型和中型的血管([[血管炎|血管炎]])[[炎症|炎症]]。它通常體現出三個階段。早期([[前驅症狀|前驅症狀]](Prodrome))階段的特點是氣道炎症；幾乎所有的患者都會經歷[[哮喘|哮喘]]和/或[[過敏性鼻炎|過敏性鼻炎]]。第二階段的特徵是異常高的"嗜酸性粒細胞"(eosinophils)([[嗜伊紅細胞增多|嗜伊紅細胞增多]](Hypereosinophilia))，這會導致組織損傷，最常傷到[[肺|肺]]部和[[消化系统|消化道]]。第三階段包括[[血管炎|血管炎]]，其最終可以導致[[壞死|細胞死亡]]，並且可以造成生命的威脅性。

查格-施特勞斯綜合徵的有效治療需要[[免疫抑制|抑制免疫系統]]的藥物。通常是[[糖皮质激素|糖皮质激素]]，其次是其他的藥劑如[[環磷酰胺|環磷酰胺]]、或[[硫唑嘌呤|硫唑嘌呤]]。

"查格-施特勞斯綜合徵"它賦予著於1951年最先發現這個病症的兩位醫生"查格-施特勞斯"的名字。

===過敏性階段===
前驅階段的特點是過敏。<!--Almost all patients experience asthma and/or allergic rhinitis,{{sfn|Churg|Thurlbeck|1995|p=425}} with more than 90% having a history of asthma that is either a new development, or the worsening of pre-existing asthma,<ref name=clim701>{{cite book|last=Rich|first=Robert R.|title=Clinical Immunology: Principles and Practice|year=2012|publisher=[[Elsevier|Elsevier Health Sciences]]|isbn=9780723437109|page=701|author2=Fleisher, Thomas A. |author3=Shearer, William T. |author4=Schroeder, Harry |author5=Frew, Anthony J. |author6= Weyand, Cornelia M. }}</ref> which may require systemic [[corticosteroid|corticosteroid]] treatment.<ref name=mayo-s/> On average, asthma develops from three to nine years before the other signs and symptoms.<ref name=mayo-s/>

The allergic rhinitis may produce symptoms such as [[rhinorrhea|rhinorrhea]] and [[nasal_obstruction|nasal obstruction]], and the formation of [[nasal_polyp|nasal polyp]]s that require surgical removal, often more than once.{{sfn|Churg|Thurlbeck|1995|p=425}} [[Sinusitis|Sinusitis]] may also be present.{{sfn|Churg|Thurlbeck|1995|p=425}}-->

===嗜酸性粒細胞階段===
第二階段特徵是在血液和組織中的含有[[嗜酸細胞增多綜合徵|異常高水平的嗜酸性粒細胞]](Hypereosinophilia、一種[[白血球|白血球]])。<ref name=mayo-s>{{cite web|title=Churg-Strauss syndrome - Symptoms|url=http://www.mayoclinic.com/health/churg-strauss-syndrome/DS00855/DSECTION=symptoms|publisher=[[Mayo_Clinic|Mayo Clinic]]|accessdate=30 June 2013}}</ref><!--The symptoms of hypereosinophilia depend on which part of the body is affected, but most often it affects the lungs and digestive tract.<ref name=mayo-s/> The signs and symptoms of hypereosinophilia may include weight loss, night sweats, asthma, cough, abdominal pain, and gastrointestinal bleeding.<ref name=mayo-s/> [[Fever|Fever]] and [[malaise|malaise]] are often present.{{sfn|Churg|Thurlbeck|1995|p=426}}

The eosinophilic stage can last months or years, and its symptoms can disappear, only to return later.<ref name=mayo-s/> Patients may experience the third stage simultaneously.<ref name=mayo-s/>-->

===血管炎階段===<!--
The third and final stage, and hallmark of Churg–Strauss syndrome, is [[vasculitis|inflammation of the blood vessels]], and the consequent reduction of blood flow to various organs and tissues.<ref name=mayo-s/> Local and systemic symptoms become more widespread and are compounded by new symptoms from the vasculitis.{{sfn|Churg|Thurlbeck|1995|p=426}}

Severe complications may arise. [[Thrombosis|Blood clots]] may develop within the damaged arteries in severe cases, particularly in arteries of the abdominal region, which is followed by [[infarction|infarction]] and [[necrosis|cell death]], or slow [[atrophy|atrophy]].{{sfn|Churg|Thurlbeck|1995|p=426}} Many patients experience severe abdominal complaints; these are most often due to [[peritonitis|peritonitis]] and/or ulcerations and [[Gastrointestinal_perforation|perforations]] of the gastrointestinal tract, but occasionally due to [[Cholecystitis|acalculous cholecystitis]] or granulomatous [[appendicitis|appendicitis]].{{sfn|Churg|Thurlbeck|1995|p=426}}

The most serious complication of the vasculitic stage is [[cardiovascular_disease|heart disease]], which is the cause of nearly one-half of all deaths in patients with Churg–Strauss syndrome.{{sfn|Churg|Thurlbeck|1995|p=426}} Among heart disease-related deaths, the most usual cause is [[myocarditis|inflammation of the heart muscle]] caused by the high level of eosinophils, although some are deaths to inflammation of the [[coronary_artery|arteries that supply blood to the heart]] or [[Cardiac_tamponade|pericardial tamponade]].{{sfn|Churg|Thurlbeck|1995|p=426}} [[Renal|Kidney]] complications have been reported as being less common.{{sfn|Rich|Fleisher|Shearer|Schroeder|2012|p=701}}-->

==診斷==
診斷標記包括[[嗜酸性粒細胞|嗜酸性粒細胞]]、及受感染組織的[[肉芽腫|肉芽腫]](Granuloma)，以及抗[[中性粒细胞|中性粒细胞]]之[[抗中性白細胞胞質抗體|抗中性白細胞胞質抗體]](Anti-neutrophil cytoplasmic antibody)。1990年[[美國風濕病學院|美國風濕病學院]](American College of Rheumatology)為"查格-施特勞斯氏綜合徵"的診斷列出了這些標準：
*哮喘。
*嗜酸性粒細胞與[[白血球|白血球]]計算之差值大於10％。
*[[周圍神經病變|單神經病變]](Peripheral neuropathy)或[[多發性神經病變|多發性神經病變]](Polyneuropathy)的顯現。
*不固定的肺浸潤
*顯現[[鼻竇|鼻竇]]異常。
*血管外嗜酸性粒細胞的組織學證據。
以分類效果而言，患者在六項標準中如超過四項則可以判定患有查格-施特勞斯氏綜合徵(CSS)。六項標準的任何四項或更多項顯現則產生85％的靈敏度及99.7％的特異性。<ref>{{cite journal |author=Masi AT, Hunder GG, Lie JT, et al. |title=The American College of Rheumatology 1990 criteria for the classification of Churg-Strauss syndrome (allergic granulomatosis and angiitis) |journal=Arthritis Rheum. |volume=33 |issue=8 |pages=1094–100 |date=August 1990 |pmid=2202307 |url=http://www.rheumatology.org/practice/clinical/classification/churg.asp|doi=10.1002/art.1780330806}}</ref>

==治療==<!--
Treatment for Churg–Strauss syndrome includes glucocorticoids (such as [[prednisolone|prednisolone]]) and other immunosuppressive drugs (such as [[azathioprine|azathioprine]] and [[cyclophosphamide|cyclophosphamide]]). In many cases, the disease can be put into a type of chemical remission through drug therapy, but the disease is chronic and lifelong.

A [[systematic_review|systematic review]] conducted in 2007 indicated all patients should be treated with high-dose steroids, but in patients with a five-factor score of one or higher, cyclophosphamide pulse therapy should be commenced, with 12 pulses leading to fewer relapses than six. Remission can be maintained with a less toxic drug, such as azathioprine or [[methotrexate|methotrexate]].<ref>{{cite journal |author=Bosch X, Guilabert A, Espinosa G, Mirapeix E |title=Treatment of antineutrophil cytoplasmic antibody associated vasculitis: a systematic review |journal=JAMA |volume=298 |issue=6 |pages=655–69 |year=2007 |pmid=17684188 |doi=10.1001/jama.298.6.655}}</ref>-->
