'''满洲龟'''（[[学名|学名]]：''{{lang|la|Manchurochelys}}''）是已灭绝的[[真曲颈龟下目|真曲颈龟下目]]的一属，存於[[白垩纪|白垩纪]]时期的[[中国|中国]]东北<ref>{{en}}''Chinese Fossil Vertebrates'' by Spencer G. Lucas. Retrieved on 2008-08-24.</ref>，发现於辽西[[义县组|义县组]][[尖山沟层|尖山沟层]]<ref>{{en}}[http://209.85.173.104/search?q=cache:f40uDzLYSKMJ:219.238.6.200/getfile%3Fcategory%3Darticle%26code%3D04yd0038%26file-name%3D05yd0298.pdf+Manchurochelys&hl=en&ct=clnk&cd=19&gl=ca&lr=lang_en Jianshangou Bed of the Yixian Formation in West Liaoning, China；]{{dead link|date=2018年3月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} Nanjing Institute of Geology and Palaeontolgoy, Chinese Academy of Sciences, 2004-02-06. Retrieved on 2008-08-25.</ref>，是一种罕见的化石<ref>{{en}}{{cite web |url=http://technology.newscientist.com/article/mg18224530.700-fossils-seized.html |title=Fossils Seized |publisher=''NewScientistTech'' |date=2004-06-26 |accessdate=2008-08-26}}</ref>。

[[Image:Manchurochelys_liaoxiensis.JPG|left]]
满洲龟属的学名''{{lang|la|Manchurochelys}}''是於1942年由[[遠藤隆次|遠藤隆次]]和[[鹿間時夫|鹿間時夫]]命名，包含[[辽西满洲龟|辽西满洲龟]]、[[满洲满洲龟|满洲满洲龟]]和[[东海满洲龟|东海满洲龟]]3个物种，与现代的[[鳄龟科|鳄龟科]]（Chelydridae）有亲缘关系<ref>{{en}}''Dinosaurs: Photo Captions'' by the American Museum of Natural History. {{cite web |url=http://www.hmns.org/files/marketing/dinosphotocaptions.pdf |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2008-08-26 |0=2007-10-28 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071028002213/http://www.hmns.org/files/marketing/DinosPhotoCaptions.pdf |archivedate=2007-10-28 }}</ref>。目前满洲龟被分到[[中国龟科|中国龟科]]下，不过有观点认为分入[[大贝氏龟科|大贝氏龟科]]（Macrobaenidae）<ref>{{en}}[https://web.archive.org/web/20060918184317/http://www.yale.edu/peabody/collections/vp/vp_FT/FTN_Feb05.pdf Fossil Turtle Newsletter.] Retrieved on 2008-08-26.</ref>更恰当。
