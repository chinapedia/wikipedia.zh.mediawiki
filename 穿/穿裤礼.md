'''穿裤礼'''指男孩幼年时首次穿裤的仪式。从16世纪中期<ref>Melanie Scheussler suggests a date of post-1540 for England, France, and the Low Countries; see Scheussler, "'She Hath Over Grown All that She Ever Hath': Children's Clothing in the Lisle Letters, 1533-40", in Netherton, Robin, and Gale R. Owen-Crocker, editors, ''Medieval Clothing and Textiles'', Volume 3, p. 185.  Before roughly this date various styles of long robes were in any case commonly worn by adult males of various sorts, so boys wearing them could probably not be said to form a distinct phenomenon.</ref> 至20世纪初，西方的男童在幼年时(2岁左右-8岁左右)<ref>Baumgarten, Linda: ''What Clothes Reveal: The Language of Clothing in Colonial and Federal America'', p. 166</ref> 通常穿着[[长袍|长袍]]或[[连衣裙|连衣裙]]，直到长大后才穿裤。穿裤礼是男童人生中重要的庆祝仪式。这意味着父亲在抚养孩子方面会更多的参与。<ref>Baumgarten, p. 168</ref>

== 庆祝 ==
在19世纪，男童在穿裤后，通常会拍照留念，通常是跟他的父亲合影。
