'''洛遜街'''（[[英語|英語]]：Robson Street）是[[加拿大|加拿大]][[卑詩省|卑詩省]][[溫哥華|溫哥華]][[溫哥華市中心|市中心]]和[[西端區_(溫哥華)|西端區]]一條主要街道，東南起[[卑詩體育館|卑詩體育館]]，往西北經[[:en:Vancouver_Public_Library|溫哥華公共圖書館總館]]、[[洛遜廣場|洛遜廣場]]和[[溫哥華美術館|溫哥華美術館]]等地標，到[[史丹利公園|史丹利公園]]旁的[[洛斯特湖|洛斯特湖]]為止。洛遜街是以1880和90年代先後出任卑詩省財政廳長和[[不列顛哥倫比亞省省長|省長]]的[[:en:John_Robson|約翰·洛遜]]命名。<ref name="Walker">{{Cite book| last = Walker | first = Elizabeth | authorlink =   | coauthors =   | title = Street Names of Vancouver | publisher = Vancouver Historical Society | date = 1999 | location =   | pages = 106  | url =   | doi =   | id =   | isbn = 0-9692378-7-1}}</ref>

溫哥華的[[路面電車|路面電車]]系統於1895年伸延至洛遜街，街道兩旁自此也出現各種商舖。<ref>[http://www.robsonstreet.ca/history/ Robson Street Business Association | History] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070610170028/http://www.robsonstreet.ca/history/ |date=2007-06-10 }}</ref>[[二戰|二戰]]後洛遜街的西北端成為德國裔居民的熱門聚居地點，而傑維斯街至[[布勒街|布勒街]]的一段洛遜街更得了「Robsonstrasse」的稱號<ref>{{Cite book| last = City of Vancouver | first = | authorlink =   | coauthors =   | title = West End Commercial Areas Policy Plan | publisher =  | date = 1986 | location =   | pages = 7  | url = http://vancouver.ca/commsvcs/guidelines/W006.pdf  | doi =   | id =   | isbn = }}</ref>（[[德語|德語]]「strasse」解作「街道」）。洛遜街其後陸續發展成溫哥華市的主要購物街，沿途林立多個名牌的商舖<ref>{{cite news | title = Vancouver's retail rates among world's top five | work = 《[[溫哥華太陽報|溫哥華太陽報]]》 | publisher = | date = 2006-02-06 | url = http://www.canada.com/vancouversun/news/story.html?id=447af12f-73fc-4618-af2a-c547bb46f71e&k=83599 | accessdate = 2010-09-18 }}</ref>，更成為溫市景點之一。洛遜街沿途零售商舖租金於2010年達每平方英尺196.08[[美元|美元]]，在全加拿大排名第三（僅次於[[多倫多|多倫多]]的[[:en:Bloor_Street|布落街]]和[[滿地可|滿地可]]的[[聖凱瑟琳街|聖凱瑟琳街]]），在全世界則名列第51位。<ref>{{cite news | title = Toronto, Montreal retail real estate the most expensive in Canada | work = 《金融郵報》 | publisher =  | date = 2010-06-08 | url = http://www.ottawacitizen.com/sports/Toronto+Montreal+retail+real+estate+most+expensive+Canada/3125020/story.html | accessdate = 2010-09-18 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20100611144734/http://www.ottawacitizen.com/sports/Toronto+Montreal+retail+real+estate+most+expensive+Canada/3125020/story.html | archivedate = 2010-06-11 }}</ref>

[[溫哥華加人|溫哥華加人]]冰球隊在[[:en:1994_Stanley_Cup_Finals|1994年史丹利杯決賽]]中敗於[[紐約遊騎兵|紐約遊騎兵]]隊後，洛遜街夾書羅街一帶[[:en:1994_Stanley_Cup_riot|爆發了騷亂]]，造成超過200人受傷，財物損失達110萬元。<ref>[http://www.macleans.ca/culture/books/article.jsp?content=20051222_140516_2124 4 Canadian sports riots | Macleans.ca - Culture - Books]</ref>
