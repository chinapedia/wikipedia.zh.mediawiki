== 分類 ==
方蟹科過往分成多個亞科，但這些亞科現時均已獨立出來成為一個科，再組成[[方蟹總科|方蟹總科]]，怪方蟹科是當中其中一個成員<ref name="Grave" />。現時本屬包括有下列三個物種：
* ''X. ngatama'' McLay, 2007
* ''X. novaeinsularis'' Takeda & Kurata 1977<ref>Takeda & Kurata, 1977 : ''Crabs of the Ogasawara Islands IV. A collection made at the new volcanic island, Nishino-shima-shinto, in 1975.'' Bulletin of the National Science Museum, Tokyo, series A (Zoology), vol. 3, n. 2, {{p.|91–111}}.</ref>
* [[烏龜怪方蟹|烏龜怪方蟹]] ''X. testudinatus'' Ng, Huang & Ho, 2000
