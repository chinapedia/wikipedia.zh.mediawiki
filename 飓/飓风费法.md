'''飓风费法'''（{{lang-en|Hurricane Fefa}}）是[[1991年太平洋飓风季|1991年太平洋飓风季]]的一场大型飓风，虽然风暴基本没有造成破坏，但其名称仍然退役，永远都不会再在[[太平洋飓风季|东太平洋风暴]]命名时采用。气旋源于7月29日[[卡波圣卢卡斯|卡波圣卢卡斯]][[罗盘方位|东南偏南]]方向约1575公里海域的一股[[东风波|东风波]]{{#tag:ref|[[东风波|东风波]]指的是沿[[信风|信风]]移动的[[低压槽|低压槽]]<ref name="glos" />。|group="注"}}，是本季第6场热带风暴{{#tag:ref|根据美国国家飓风中心的定义，热带风暴是指持续风速在每小时63至118公里之间的热带气旋<ref name="glos" />。|group="注"}}、第4场飓风{{#tag:ref|根据[[国家飓风中心|美国国家飓风中心]]的定义，飓风是指持续风速在每小时119公里或以上的[[北大西洋热带气旋|大西洋]]和东北太平洋[[热带气旋|热带气旋]]，这一标准和西北太平洋的台风相同<ref name="glos">{{cite report | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 2013-03-25 | accessdate = 2014-06-15 | title = Glossary of NHC Terms | archivedate = 2014-06-01 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140601020814/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/aboutgloss.shtml | deadurl = no | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/aboutgloss.shtml}}</ref>。|group="注"}}和第2场大型飓风{{#tag:ref|大型飓风指[[最大持续风速|最大持续风速]]可以在[[萨菲尔-辛普森飓风等级|萨菲尔-辛普森飓风等级]]中达到三级或以上的风暴<ref name="glos" />。|group="注"}}。系统向西北偏西方向移动，因外界总体环境有利而增强，于8月2日达到[[最大持续风速|风力时速]]195公里的最高强度{{#tag:ref|如无特别说明，所有风速数据均为1分钟持续风速。|group="注"}}。费法转朝西面的[[夏威夷州|夏威夷州]]前进，然后逐渐减弱，最终在[[夏威夷岛|夏威夷岛]]附近逐渐消散。

途经夏威夷期间，费法产生强烈的拍岸浪、中等强度阵风和局部暴雨，但没有报道表明飓风有造成人员丧生或财产损失。风暴还产生[[闪电|闪电]]，导致夏威夷岛上两人受伤。

== 气象历史 ==
{{storm path|Fefa 1991 track.png}}
1991年7月17日，一股[[东风波|东风波]]离开[[非洲|非洲]]向西移动，先后经过[[大西洋|大西洋]]和[[加勒比海|加勒比海]]，由于外界环境不利而没有得到发展，穿越[[中美洲|中美洲]]后，东风波于7月25日进入东[[太平洋|太平洋]]。7月28日，系统云系和[[对流|对流]]结构有所改善，并在不久后发展出中层环流。系统继续组织，随后发展出下层环流，于7月29日在[[下加利福尼亚半岛|下加利福尼亚半岛]]最南端的卡波圣卢卡斯（{{lang|en|Cabo San Lucas}}）[[罗盘方位|东南偏南]]方向约1575公里海域发展成第七{{lang|en|E}}号热带低气压。<ref name="tcr1">{{cite web | author = Max Mayfield | year = 1991 | title = Hurricane Fefa Tropical Cyclone Report Page 1 | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2015-08-20 | archivedate = 2012-10-18 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121018162536/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/prenhc/prelim01.gif | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/prenhc/prelim01.gif | deadurl = no}}</ref>但是，[[国家飓风中心|美国国家飓风中心]]在实际操作中是从卫星图像确认下层环流形成后才开始发布热带气旋公告，这时距热带低气压形成已经过了12小时<ref name="disc1">{{cite web | author = Max Mayfield | year = 1991 | title = Tropical Depression Seven-E Discussion One | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2015-08-20 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/tropdisc/nep0791.001 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121018182450/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/tropdisc/nep0791.001 | archivedate = 2012-10-18}}</ref>。

由于所在洋面[[海面温度|水温]]较高，还有上层外流这样的利好条件<ref name="disc1" />，低气压迅速增强，形成仅12小时后就达到热带风暴标准并获名“费法”（{{lang|en|Fefa}}）<ref name="tcr1" />。在此期间，风暴中心东面和南面上空发展出弯曲的[[雨带|云带]]<ref name="disc1" />。受北面的[[高压脊|高压脊]]影响，气旋向西北偏西方向移动并缓慢强化<ref name="pasch3">{{cite web | author = Pasch | year = 1991 | title = Tropical Storm Fefa Discussion Three | publisher = NHC | accessdate = 2015-08-20 | archivedate = 2012-10-18 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121018182454/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/tropdisc/nep0791.003 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/tropdisc/nep0791.003}}</ref>。费法起初的增强速度较为缓慢，成为热带风暴后，其中心位于深层对流的西部边缘<ref name="mayfield6">{{cite web | author = Max Mayfield | year = 1991 | title = Tropical Storm Fefa Discussion Six | publisher = NHC | accessdate = 2015-08-20 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/tropdisc/nep0791.006 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121018182501/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/tropdisc/nep0791.006 | archivedate = 2012-10-18}}</ref>。7月31日，中心上空的对流开始增长<ref name="rappaport7">{{cite web | author = Rappaport | year = 1991 | title = Tropical Storm Fefa Discussion Seven | publisher = NHC | accessdate = 2015-08-20 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/tropdisc/nep0791.007 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121018182513/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/tropdisc/nep0791.007 | archivedate = 2012-10-18}}</ref>，估计气旋于当晚在下加利福尼亚半岛最南端西南方向约1145公里洋面升级成飓风<ref name="tcr1" />。

气象机构起初预计费法会在成为飓风后达到[[最大持续风速|持续风速]]每小时130公里的最高强度<ref name="gerrish8">{{cite web | author = Gerrish | year = 1991 | title = Hurricane Fefa Discussion Eight | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121018182548/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/tropdisc/nep0791.008 | archivedate = 2012-10-18 | deadurl = no | publisher = NHC | accessdate = 2015-08-20 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/tropdisc/nep0791.008}}</ref>。但是，飓风实际上却在8月1日清晨发展出庞大但不规则的[[风眼|风眼]]后迅速增强<ref name="avila13">{{cite web | author = Avila | year = 1991 | title = Hurricane Fefa Discussion Thirteen | publisher = NHC | accessdate = 2015-08-20 | archivedate = 2012-10-18 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121018182554/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/tropdisc/nep0791.013 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/tropdisc/nep0791.013 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。8月2日，风眼结构有进一步改善，气旋在卡波圣卢卡斯西南偏西方向约1510公里洋面达到风力时速195公里的最高强度<ref name="tcr1" />。从[[德沃夏克分析法|德沃夏克分析法]]得出的数值来看，飓风的最强风速有可能超过每小时195公里<ref name="tcr2">{{cite web | author = Max Mayfield | year = 1991 | title = Hurricane Fefa Tropical Cyclone Report Page 2 | archivedate = 2012-10-18 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121018162536/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/prenhc/prelim01.gif | deadurl = no | format = GIF | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2015-08-20 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/prenhc/prelim01.gif}}</ref>。此后不久，风暴因行经海域水温降低而开始减弱<ref name="avila17">{{cite web | author = Avila | year = 1991 | archivedate = 2012-10-18 | title = Hurricane Fefa Discussion Seventeen | publisher = NHC | accessdate = 2015-08-20 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121018182559/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/tropdisc/nep0791.017 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/tropdisc/nep0791.017}}</ref>。受北面正在形成的[[高气压|高气压]]影响，气旋略朝西侧转向，并因水温较低并遭遇干燥空气导致逐渐弱化<ref name="avila21">{{cite web | author = Avila | year = 1991 | title = Hurricane Avila Discussion 21 | publisher = NHC | accessdate = 2015-08-20 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/tropdisc/nep0791.021 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121018182559/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/tropdisc/nep0791.017 | archivedate = 2012-10-18}}</ref>，风速于8月3日降至每小时135公里<ref name="tcr2" />。

[[File:TS_Fefa_07_aug_1991_1656Z.jpg|right]]

费法略朝西面转向后沿水温较低的海域平行前进，飓风结构因此保持良好，风眼依然清晰<ref name="avila22">{{Cite web | author = Avila | year = 1991 | title = Hurricane Avila Discussion Twenty-Two | publisher = NHC | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121018182612/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/tropdisc/nep0791.022 | archivedate = 2012-10-18 | deadurl = no | accessdate = 2006-12-09 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/tropdisc/nep0791.022}}</ref>。虽然行经洋面温差增大，而且风暴还与北面的[[层积云|层积云]]联系起来，但费法还是于8月4日再度增强<ref name="pasch23">{{cite web | author = Pasch | year = 1991 | title = Hurricane Fefa Discussion Twenty-Three | publisher = NHC | accessdate = 2015-08-20 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/tropdisc/nep0791.023 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121018182750/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/storm_wallets/epacific/ep1991/fefa/tropdisc/nep0791.023 | archivedate = 2012-10-18}}</ref>，在[[夏威夷州|夏威夷州]][[希洛_(夏威夷州)|希洛]]东南偏东方向约2070公里海域达到风力时速170公里的第2波最高强度。在此强度下保持约18小时后，飓风已进入中太平洋飓风中心预警责任区，并因冷心[[低压槽|低压槽]]产生的[[风切变|风切变]]逐渐增多而出现小幅减弱。风切变继续增长，气旋强度的消退速度加快，于8月6日在[[夏威夷岛|夏威夷岛]]以东约1060公里海域降级成热带风暴。<ref name="tcr2" />气象机构起初预计费法会转向西北，从[[夏威夷群岛|夏威夷群岛]]以北海域经过。但风暴实际上继续向接近正西方向前进，并在此期间稳步弱化。气旋逼近夏威夷州期间，[[飓风猎人|飓风猎人]][[侦察机|侦察机]]3次飞进费法进行观测。8月7日晚，系统在夏威夷岛以东不远处海域降级成热带低气压，并于8月8日开始消散，登岸后退化成东风波。系统残留向西北偏西方向移动，从夏威夷群岛以南洋面掠过，最终于8月9日在[[考艾岛|考艾岛]]西北方向逐渐消散。<ref name="cphc">{{cite web | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150123040357/http://www.prh.noaa.gov/cphc/summaries/1991.php#Fefa | author = Central Pacific Hurricane Center | year = 1991 | title = The 1991 Central Pacific Tropical Cyclone Season | accessdate = 2015-08-20 | url = http://www.prh.noaa.gov/cphc/summaries/1991.php#Fefa | archivedate = 2015-01-23 | deadurl = no}}</ref>

== 影响 ==
[[File:Hurricane_Fefa_11_aug_1991_STS043.jpg|thumb]]
飓风费法长时间向西前进，产生高达4.5米的涌浪，冲击夏威夷岛的东向海滩。涌浪将瓦砾和海水带到[[普纳鲁吾黑沙滩|普纳鲁吾黑沙滩]]附近的沿海公路上，致使多条公路在风暴过后封闭<ref name="cphc" />。[[茂宜岛|茂宜岛]]西南部也出现强烈的拍岸浪<ref name="usgs">{{cite web | author = Charles H. Fletcher III, Eric E. Grossman, Bruce M. Richmond, and Ann E. Gibbs | year = 2002 | title = Atlas of Natural Hazards in the Hawaiian Coastal Zone | publisher = United States Geological Survey | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120327071806/http://pubs.usgs.gov/imap/i2761/sections/1_Intro.pdf | deadurl = no | accessdate = 2015-08-20 | url = http://pubs.usgs.gov/imap/i2761/sections/1_Intro.pdf | archivedate = 2012-03-27}}</ref>。虽然行经夏威夷群岛时只有弱热带低气压强度，但气旋还是在夏威夷岛和茂宜岛局部地区产生时速达80公里的大风<ref name="cphc" />。

经过夏威夷岛前，费法残余环流的东北部分发展出强烈[[雷暴|雷暴]]。雷暴在行经[[冒纳凯阿火山|冒纳凯阿火山]]和[[可哈拉山|可哈拉山]]的上山坡时已有进一步发展，在部分地区产生倾盆大雨，可哈拉和[[哈玛库亚|哈玛库亚]]（{{lang|en|Hamakua}}）出现山洪爆发。风暴没有造成人员丧生，但产生的[[闪电|闪电]]致使两人受伤。[[夏威夷火山國家公園|夏威夷火山國家公園]]一名男子在散步时被闪电击伤。希洛还有一名男子因闪电击中电话线路而被烧伤。<ref name="cphc" />没有报道表明这场风暴造成了财产损失<ref name="jsc">{{cite web | author = Maura White | year = 2006 | title = Oblique View of Hurricane Fefa, Pacific Ocean | publisher = JSC Digital Image Collection | accessdate = 2015-08-20 | archivedate = 2012-02-05 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120205060537/http://images.jsc.nasa.gov/luceneweb/caption_direct.jsp?photoId=STS043-74-005 | url = http://images.jsc.nasa.gov/luceneweb/caption_direct.jsp?photoId=STS043-74-005 }}</ref>。

风暴名称“费法”之后予以退役，从[[1997年太平洋飓风季|1997年]]开始以“费利西亚”（{{lang|en|Felicia}}）取代。由于风暴并没有造成多少损失，所以其退役的确切原因不得而知，有可能是发音上存在问题，例如会在另一种语言中代表某种无法为社会接受的含义，或是这一名称代表人类史上的另一场重大灾难。<ref name="wmo">{{cite web | url = https://www.wmo.int/pages/prog/www/tcp/documents/TCP30_Edition2012.pdf#page=101 | title = Regional Association IV (North America, Central America and the Caribbean) Hurricane Operational Plan | date = 2012-06-11 | work = [https://www.wmo.int/pages/prog/www/tcp/ Tropical Cyclone Programme] | publisher = World Meteorological Organization | location = Geneva, Switzerland | volume = 2012 Edition  | at = p. 9-6 TABLE IV: Names of Eastern North Pacific Ocean Storms Retired into Hurricane History | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140201195130/http://www.wmo.int/pages/prog/www/tcp/documents/TCP30_Edition2012.pdf#page=101 | archivedate = 2014-02-01 | deadurl = no | accessdate = 2015-08-17}}</ref><ref name="AOML FAQ">{{cite web | author = Atlantic Oceanographic and Meteorological Laboratory | year = 2009 | title = Subject: B3) What names have been retired in the Atlantic and East Pacific basin? | accessdate = 2015-08-11 | url = http://www.aoml.noaa.gov/hrd/tcfaq/B3.html | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150701194456/http://www.aoml.noaa.gov/hrd/tcfaq/B3.html | archivedate = 2015-07-01 | deadurl = no}}</ref>
