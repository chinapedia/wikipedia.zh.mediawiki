'''飓风阿道夫'''（{{lang-en|Hurricane Adolph}}）是有纪录以来首场在5月达到[[萨菲尔-辛普森飓风等级|萨菲尔-辛普森飓风等级]]下四级飓风标准的东太平洋[[热带气旋|热带气旋]]，也是[[2001年太平洋飓风季|2001年太平洋飓风季]]的第1个热带低气压、第1场获得命名的风暴、第1场飓风以及首场大型飓风。虽然风暴没有造成人员伤亡和财产损失，但其名称之后还是因与[[阿道夫·希特勒|阿道夫·希特勒]]的名字相近而退役，今后不会再在东太平洋风暴命名时采用<ref name="Padgett May 1 Report">{{cite web | author = Gary Padgett | year= 2001 | title = Monthly Global Tropical Cyclone Summary for May 01 | accessdate = 2015-08-04 | archivedate = 2013-01-17 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20130117025027/http://typhoon2000.ph/garyp_mgtcs/may01.txt | format = TXT | url = http://www.typhoon2000.ph/garyp_mgtcs/may01.txt}}</ref>。

系统于5月25日成为热带低气压，3天后达到飓风标准。经过[[快速增强|快速增强]]，阿道夫达到[[最大持续风速|持续风速]]每小时230公里的最高强度，和同年的[[2001年飓风朱丽叶|飓风朱丽叶]]并列成为本季最强烈的飓风<ref name="2001 Pacific hurricane season summary">{{cite web | author = Franklin, Avila, Beven, Lawrence, Pasch, and Stewart | year = 2001 | title = 2001 Pacific Hurricane Season Summary | accessdate = 2015-08-05 | archivedate = 2014-08-12 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140812095342/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2001/tws/MIATWSEP_nov.html | deadurl = no | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2001/tws/MIATWSEP_nov.html}}</ref>。行经[[海面温度|水温]]较低海域后，气旋于6月1日消散，只对陆地构成很短暂的威胁。

== 气象历史 ==
{{storm path|Adolph 2001 track.png}}
5月7日，一股[[东风波|东风波]]离开[[非洲|非洲]]海岸，在穿越[[大西洋|大西洋]]期间没有呈现出显著发展迹象。5月18日，[[哥斯达黎加|哥斯达黎加]]和[[巴拿马|巴拿马]]上空开始有低气压中心围绕东风波组织起来。5月22日，低气压进入[[太平洋|太平洋]]，气象机构从两天后开始用[[德沃夏克分析法|德沃夏克分析法]]估算系统强度。低气压结构起初杂乱无章，但中心附近很快有[[对流|对流]]聚集，于5月25日在[[墨西哥|墨西哥]][[阿卡普尔科|阿卡普尔科]]西南偏南方向约400公里海域发展成第一{{lang|en|E}}号热带低气压。<ref name="Adolph TCR">{{cite web | author = Stacy R. Stewart | year = 2001 | title = Hurricane Adolph Tropical Cyclone Report | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131005123006/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/2001adolph.html | accessdate = 2013-10-05 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/2001adolph.html | archivedate = 2013-10-05 | deadurl = yes}}</ref>

受高空弱转向气流影响，热带低气压以非常缓慢的速度向[[罗盘方位|东北偏东]]方向移动<ref name="Adolph Discussion 4">{{cite web | author = Avila | year = 2001 | title = Hurricane Adolph Discussion #4 | accessdate = 2015-08-05 | archivedate = 2013-10-12 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131012170151/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2001/dis/ep012001.discus.004.html | deadurl = no | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2001/dis/ep012001.discus.004.html}}</ref>。多个[[热带气旋路径预测|计算机模型]]在预测低气压动向时产生截然不同的结果，其中1个认为气旋最终将[[登陆_(气象学)|登陆]]墨西哥<ref name="Adolph Discussion 2">{{cite web | author = Beven | year = 2001 | title = Hurricane Adolph Discussion #2 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131012164111/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2001/dis/ep012001.discus.002.html | deadurl = no | accessdate = 2015-08-05 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2001/dis/ep012001.discus.002.html | archivedate = 2013-10-12}}</ref>。由于外界环境有利，低气压发展出大规模深层对流组成的[[中心密集云区|中心密集云区]]<ref name="Adolph Discussion 3">{{cite web | author = Lawrence | year = 2001 | title = Hurricane Adolph Discussion #3 | accessdate = 2015-08-05 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131012202757/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2001/dis/ep012001.discus.003.html | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2001/dis/ep012001.discus.003.html | archivedate = 2013-10-12}}</ref>。系统稳步增强，于5月26日在阿尔普尔科西南偏南方向约362公里洋面强化成热带风暴并获名“阿道夫”（{{lang|en|Adolph}}）。风暴所处海域[[风切变|风切变]]少，[[海面温度|海面温度]]高，[[国家飓风中心|美国国家飓风中心]]为此预计气旋会在两天内升级成飓风<ref name="Adolph Discussion 5">{{cite web | author = Avila | year = 2001 | title = Hurricane Adolph Discussion #5 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131012201421/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2001/dis/ep012001.discus.005.html | deadurl = no | accessdate = 2015-08-05 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2001/dis/ep012001.discus.005.html | archivedate = 2013-10-12}}</ref>。5月27日，由于东侧和东南方向有[[高压脊|高压脊]]形成，风暴转向北上<ref name="Adolph Discussion 9">{{cite web | author = Pasch | year = 2001 | title = Hurricane Adolph Discussion #9 | accessdate = 2015-08-05 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131012213803/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2001/dis/ep012001.discus.009.html | deadurl = no | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2001/dis/ep012001.discus.009.html | archivedate = 2013-10-12 }}</ref>，朝墨西哥逼近。卫星图像上开始出现小型飓风中常见的长条型[[风眼|眼状特状]]。风眼变得更加清晰，周围的对流逐渐增强，阿道夫于5月27日升级成飓风。5月28日，风暴从距墨西哥约265公里海域经过，这也是系统距陆地最近的一刻。此后不久，气旋因受中层高压脊影响转向西进<ref name="Adolph TCR" />。受外流良好、风切变少，以及行经海域水温高这样的多重利好因素影响<ref name="Adolph Discussion 10">{{cite web | author = Franklin | year = 2001 | title = Hurricane Adolph Discussion #10 | accessdate = 2015-08-05 | archivedate = 2013-10-12 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2001/dis/ep012001.discus.010.html | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131012200030/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2001/dis/ep012001.discus.010.html | deadurl = no}}</ref>，飓风[[快速增强|快速增强]]，[[气压|气压]]每小时下降1.46[[巴|毫巴]]<ref name="Padgett May 1 Report" />。阿道夫的规模也逐渐缩小<ref name="Adolph Discussion 10" />，于5月29日达到[[最大持续风速|风力时速]]230公里的最高强度<ref name="Adolph TCR" />。经德沃夏克分析法测算，飓风的{{lang|en|T}}数值为7.0，刚刚达到[[萨菲尔-辛普森飓风等级|萨菲尔-辛普森飓风等级]]下的[[五级太平洋飓风列表|五级飓风]]标准<ref name="Padgett May 1 Report" />。但由于缺乏[[飓风猎人|飓风猎人]][[侦察机|侦察机]]提供的风暴数据，这一强度数值可能不够可靠<ref name="Adolph Discussion 12">{{cite web | author = Pasch | year = 2001 | title = Hurricane Adolph Discussion #3 | accessdate = 2015-08-05 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2001/dis/ep012001.discus.012.html | archivedate = 2013-10-12 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131012225041/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2001/dis/ep012001.discus.012.html}}</ref>。
[[File:Hurricane_Adolph_30_may_2001.jpg|thumb]]

达到最高强度后，阿道夫因[[眼墙置换|眼墙置换]]而减弱，移动速度也随之放缓，到5月30日时，风眼已被云层遮蔽，清晰度降低，风速降至每小时185公里<ref name="Adolph Discussion 17">{{cite web | author = Pasch | year = 2001 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140812095618/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2001/dis/ep012001.discus.017.html | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2014-08-12 | title = Hurricane Adolph Discussion #3 | accessdate = 2015-08-05 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2001/dis/ep012001.discus.017.html}}</ref>。风暴继续减弱，风眼层次结构逐渐变得模糊，对流强度出现波动<ref name="Padgett May 1 Report" />。6月1日，对流从逐渐拉长的中心中暴露出来，气旋降级成热带风暴<ref name="Adolph Discussion 27">{{cite web | author = Avila | year = 2001 | title = Hurricane Adolph Discussion #27 | accessdate = 2015-08-05 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2001/dis/ep012001.discus.027.html | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140812095431/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2001/dis/ep012001.discus.027.html | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2014-08-12}}</ref>。由于途经洋面水温逐渐降低，大气环境也渐趋稳定，风暴的弱化速度进一步加快，于6月1日在[[下加利福尼亚州|下加利福尼亚州]]西南偏南方向约740公里海域消散<ref name="Adolph TCR" />。残留云系接下来又持续了数天才完全消散<ref name="Adolph TCR" />。

== 影响和纪录 ==
[[File:Hurricane_Adolph_TRMM_2001.JPG|thumb]]

| url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2001/dis/ep012001.discus.006.html | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140812095441/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2001/dis/ep012001.discus.006.html | 虽有气象部门发布热带风暴警告，并预报会有轻度影响，但没有任何陆地测得阿道夫产生的热带风暴强度大风，只有一艘名为“瑟拉号”（{{lang|en|Seurat}}）的船于5月29日在海上测得时速72公里大风<ref name="Mariners Weather Log">{{cite web | author1 = Jack Beven | author2 = Daniel P. Brown | author3 = Eric J. Holweg | year = 2001 | title = Mariners Weather Log of 2001 | accessdate = 2015-08-05 | url = http://www.vos.noaa.gov/MWL/dec2001.pdf | format = PDF | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20130215065402/http://www.vos.noaa.gov/MWL/dec2001.pdf | archivedate = 2013-02-15 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。除部分地区出现降雨和大浪外<ref name="Padgett May 1 Report" />，风暴没有造成任何人员伤亡或损失<ref name="Adolph TCR" />。

阿道夫于5月29日达到四级飓风强度，创下5月东太平洋热带气旋强度的新纪录，直至[[2014年太平洋飓风季|2014年]]才被最高风速达每小时250公里的飓风阿曼达超越。阿道夫也成为有纪录以来首场在5月达到四级飓风强度的太平洋飓风<ref name="Padgett May 1 Report" /><ref name="Adolph TCR" />。

虽然风暴基本没有造成影响，但其名称还是出于政治因素考量退役（与[[阿道夫·希特勒|阿道夫·希特勒]]的名字相似）<ref>{{cite news | title = Storm brewing over hurricane named Israel | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140611025545/http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1P1-44813885.html | via = Highbeam Research | url = http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1P1-44813885.html | newspaper = The Jerusalem Post | accessdate = 2015-08-03 | date = 2001-05-21 | author = Wohlhelernter, Elli | archivedate = 2014-06-11 | deadurl = yes }}{{subscription required}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | title = Storm blows over as 'Hurricane Israel' is retired | date = 2001-06-06 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140611025547/http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1P1-45044545.html | newspaper = The Jerusalem Post | url = http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1P1-45044545.html | accessdate = 2015-08-03 | archivedate = 2014-06-11 | deadurl = yes }}{{subscription required | via = Highbeam Research}}</ref><ref name="aoml">{{cite book | accessdate = 2015-08-01 | author = Landsea, Christopher W | author2 = Dorst, Neal | publisher = United States National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration | author5 = Hurricane Research Division | date = 2014-06-20 | chapter = Subject: B3) What storm names have been retired? | title = Tropical Cyclone Frequently Asked Questions: | url = http://www.aoml.noaa.gov/hrd/tcfaq/B3.html | archivedate = 2015-07-01 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150701194456/http://www.aoml.noaa.gov/hrd/tcfaq/B3.html | deadurl = no}}</ref>。
