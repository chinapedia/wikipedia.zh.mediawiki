'''飓风乔治的气象历史'''从1998年9月15日开始，同年10月1日结束，一共持续了17天。[[飓风乔治|飓风乔治]]源于1998年9月中旬离开[[非洲|非洲]]海岸的一股[[东风波|东风波]]{{#tag:ref|[[东风波|东风波]]指的是沿[[信风|信风]]移动的[[低压槽|低压槽]]<ref name="glo">{{cite report | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 2013-03-25 | accessdate = 2014-06-15 | title = Glossary of NHC Terms | archivedate = 2014-06-01 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140601020814/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/aboutgloss.shtml | deadurl = no | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/aboutgloss.shtml}}</ref>。|group="注"}}，系统向西移动，于两天后催生出[[低气压|低气压区]]并很快强化成[[热带气旋|热带低气压]]。9月16日，低气压升级成热带风暴并获名“乔治”，并且次日就达到飓风强度。接下来的几天时间里，风暴中发展出[[风眼|风眼]]，周围有持续存在的深层[[对流|大气对流]]围绕。飓风向[[罗盘方位|西北偏西]]方向移动，在此期间因强烈的[[外流_(气象学)|外流]]和温暖的[[海面温度|海面温度]]而继续强化，于9月20日达到[[最大持续风速|最大持续风速]]每小时250公里的最高强度，仅略低于[[萨菲尔-辛普森飓风等级|萨菲尔-辛普森飓风等级]]下的五级飓风标准，[[气压|气压]]也降至937[[巴|毫巴]]（[[帕斯卡|百帕]]，27.67[[英寸汞柱|英寸汞柱]]）。<ref name="TCR">{{cite web | author = John L. Guiney | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1999-01-05 | accessdate = 2014-07-13 | title = Hurricane Georges Preliminary Report | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/1998georges.html | archivedate = 2014-07-13 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140713194504/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/1998georges.html}}</ref>

接下来的5天时间里，乔治行经[[大安的列斯群岛|大安的列斯群岛]]并先后5次登陆，其中4次有三级飓风强度，一次为一级飓风强度。进入[[加勒比海|加勒比海]]后不久，风暴略有减弱，但又在穿越[[波多黎各|波多黎各]]前重新增强成大型飓风{{#tag:ref|大型飓风指[[最大持续风速|最大持续风速]]达到每小时至少179公里的[[热带气旋|热带气旋]]，在[[萨菲尔-辛普森飓风等级|萨菲尔-辛普森飓风等级]]中可以达到三级或以上<ref name="glo" />。|group="注"}}。经过又一次小幅减弱后，气旋在[[多明尼加|多明尼加共和国]]附近迅速组织，重新发展出清晰的风眼和良好的外流，令乔治在登陆前达到风力时速195公里强度。途经[[伊斯帕尼奥拉岛|伊斯帕尼奥拉岛]]期间，系统对流受到严重扰乱，但乔治仍然保持着飓风强度。9月23日，风暴以飓风最低标准强度从[[古巴|古巴]]东南部登陆。<ref name="TCR" />

到了9月25日，乔治已进入[[墨西哥湾|墨西哥湾]]并增强成二级飓风。风暴在海湾内重新组织，再度形成完整的风眼，环流中心也有深层对流围绕。9月27日，飓风风速达到每小时175公里。次日再度登陆前数小时，风暴略有减弱，从[[密西西比州|密西西比州]][[比洛克西_(密西西比州)|比洛克西]]附近以风力时速165公里强度上岸。乔治的前进速度在登陆后减缓，并短暂形成顺时针环形路径，然后保持向东飘流。气旋逐渐减弱，到9月29日下午时已降级成热带低气压。两天后，乔治在佛罗里达州大西洋近海完全消散。<ref name="TCR" />

== 形成和强化 ==
[[File:Hurricane_Georges_17_sept_1998_1735Z.jpg|180px]]

1998年9月13日晚，一股[[东风波|东风波]]离开[[非洲|非洲]]西海岸。次日，系统发展出大规模深层[[对流|对流]]区，气象部门因此开始启用[[德沃夏克分析法|德沃夏克分析法]]对之进行分析。到了9月15日，行经东风波附近的多艘船只报告这里已经发展出[[低气压|表面环流]]，[[国家飓风中心|美国国家飓风中心]]估计，1998年大西洋飓风季的第七号热带低气压于[[协调世界时|协调世界时]]中午12点在[[佛得角|佛得角]]西南偏南方向约555公里海域发展形成。<ref name="TCR" />由于北面有中层[[高压脊|高压脊]]向西沿伸，第七号热带低气压总体向正西方向移动<ref>{{cite web | author = Lixion A. Avila | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-15 | accessdate = 2015-03-12 | title = Tropical Depression Seven Discussion One | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023091551/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.001 | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.001}}</ref>。这天接下来的时间里，系统周围有[[雨带|带状特征]]发展出来，并且深层对流也围绕[[风眼|环流中心]]集中。成为热带低气压约24小时后，美国国家飓风中心将系统升级成热带风暴并以“乔治”（{{lang|en|Georges}}）命名。<ref name="TCR" />

由于受到持续存在的[[对流层|对流层]]中到上层高压脊的影响，乔治在接下来的10天时间里总体保持向[[罗盘方位|西北偏西]]方向移动<ref name="TCR" />。系统发展出强劲的外流并逐渐增强<ref>{{cite web | author = Lixion A. Avila | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-16 | accessdate = 2015-03-12 | title = Tropical Storm Georges Discussion Six | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.006 | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023091605/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.006}}</ref>，并且较高的[[海面温度|海面温度]]也使系统得以进一步发展<ref>{{cite web | author = John L. Guiney | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-16 | accessdate = 2015-03-12 | title = Tropical Depression Six Discussion Four | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023091610/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.004 | deadurl = no | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.004}}</ref>。东向[[风切变|风切变]]对风暴外流构成不利影响，但之前由两片深层对流区环绕的系统中心仍然位于[[雷暴|雷暴]]活动区的下方<ref>{{cite web | author = John L. Guiney | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-17 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023091633/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.008 | accessdate = 2015-03-12 | title = Tropical Storm Georges Discussion Eight | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.008 | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2012-10-23}}</ref>。到了9月17日凌晨时分，环流中已发展出[[风眼|风眼墙]]，表明乔治的强度正在接近[[萨菲尔-辛普森飓风等级|飓风标准]]<ref>{{cite web | author = Lixion A. Avila | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-17 | accessdate = 2015-03-13 | title = Tropical Storm Georges Discussion Nine | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023091650/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.009 | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.009}}</ref>。

== 达到最高强度并逼近小安的列斯群岛 ==
9月17日晚，卫星图像显示风暴中已有[[风眼|带状眼型特征]]出现，美国国家飓风中心因此在{{lang|en|[[协调世界时|UTC]]}}这天下午18点将乔治升级成飓风<ref name="TCR" />。次日，飓风上空开始有[[反气旋|反气旋]]形成，风暴外流因此增强<ref>{{cite web | author = Edward N. Rappaport | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023091656/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.011 | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-18 | accessdate = 2015-03-12 | title = Hurricane Georges Discussion Eleven | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.011 | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。几小时后，乔治成为二级飓风，风眼周围的持续风速提高到每小时155公里<ref name="TCR" />。美国国家飓风中心认为，没有任何因素阻止风暴进一步发展，所以乔治很可能会在到达[[小安的列斯群岛|小安的列斯群岛]]前达到四级飓风标准<ref>{{cite web | author = Lixion A. Avila | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-17 | accessdate = 2015-03-12 | title = Hurricane Georges Discussion Fourteen | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.014 | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023091701/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.014 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。9月18日晚，系统中开始发展出同心风眼墙，飓风短暂停止增强<ref>{{cite web | author = Edward N. Rappaport | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-18 | accessdate = 2015-03-13 | title = Hurricane Georges Discussion Fifteen | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.015 | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023091745/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.015}}</ref>并进入[[眼墙置换循环|眼墙置换循环]]，开始形成另一个尺寸更大的风眼<ref>{{cite web | author = Jarvinen | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-19 | accessdate = 2015-03-13 | title = Hurricane Georges Discussion Sixteen | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023091755/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.016 | deadurl = no | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.016}}</ref>。
[[File:Hurricane_Georges_near_peak_intensity.jpg|thumb]]

9月19至20日，乔治进入[[快速增强|爆发性增强期]]，风力时速在24小时里提高了85公里之多。这一周期结束时，乔治已达到[[最大持续风速|最大持续风速]]每小时250公里，最低[[气压|气压]]937[[巴|毫巴]]（[[帕斯卡|百帕]]，27.68[[英寸汞柱|英寸汞柱]]）的最高强度，仅略低于五级飓风标准。一架[[飓风猎人|飓风猎人]][[侦察机|侦察机]]飞入达到最高强度的气旋，测得飞行高度层的风力高达每小时270公里，经[[投落送|投落送]]测得的地表风力时速更达280公里。<ref name="TCR" />其中一个投落送测得低层风眼墙的风速有每小时307公里，但气象部门认为这一数据过高，不能代表乔治的实际强度，因此未予采纳。侦察机还发现飓风的热带风暴强度风场范围出现大幅扩张，美国国家飓风中心因此向小安的列斯群岛发布[[热带气旋警告|热带风暴警告]]。<ref>{{cite web | author = Max Mayfield | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-20 | accessdate = 2015-03-12 | title = Hurricane Georges Discussion Nineteen | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023091814/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.019 | deadurl = no | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.019}}</ref>。乔治达到最高强度时，其风眼直径约为55公里<ref>{{cite web | author = Serge F. Kovaleski | publisher = ''The Washington Post'' | date = 1998-09-22 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121026075737/http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1P2-677002.html | accessdate = 2015-03-12 | title = Hurricane Georges Pummels Puerto Rico, Virgin Islands | url = http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1P2-677002.html | deadurl = yes | archivedate = 2012-10-26 | via = HighBeam Research }}</ref>。

9月20日达到最高强度后不久，乔治由于受到上层风切变的不利影响而开始减弱，风眼中有云层填入，从卫星图像上失去踪影<ref name="TCR" />，风暴关联的对流也变得不及之前对称<ref>{{cite web | author = Pasch | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-20 | accessdate = 2015-03-12 | title = Hurricane Georges Discussion Twenty-One | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023091829/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.021 | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.021}}</ref>。但是，乔治在9月21日里仍然保持着大型飓风强度<ref name="TCR" />。由于风切变已经限制住飓风的部分外流，系统继续减弱<ref>{{cite web | author = John L. Guiney | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-21 | accessdate = 2015-03-12 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023095557/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.023 | title = Hurricane Georges Discussion Twenty-Three | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.023 | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。{{lang|en|UTC}}9月21日早上4点30分，乔治以风力时速185公里强度登陆[[安地卡岛|安地卡岛]]，并且数小时后又直接从[[圣基茨岛|圣基茨岛]]上空穿过<ref name="TCR" />。经过圣基茨岛后不久，乔治降级为二级飓风，风速降至每小时175公里<ref>{{cite web | author = Miles B. Lawrence | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-21 | accessdate = 2015-03-12 | title = Hurricane Georges Discussion Twenty-Four | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.024 | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023095603/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.024}}</ref>。

== 加勒比群岛 ==
[[File:Hurr-georges-19980922-g8vis.gif|180px]]

9月21日晚，风暴逼近[[波多黎各|波多黎各]]，风切变开始减少，乔治因此再度组织起来<ref>{{cite web | author = Pasch | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023095610/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.025 | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-21 | accessdate = 2015-03-12 | title = Hurricane Georges Discussion Twenty-Five | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.025 | deadurl = no}}</ref>，但由于接近陆地，其外流无法得到充分发展<ref name="TCR" />。{{lang|en|UTC}}晚上22点左右，气旋再次达到三级飓风强度，并以风力时速185公里强度登陆波多黎各，然后就开始再度弱化。数小时后，风暴进入[[莫纳海峡|莫纳海峡]]并开始重新组强<ref name="TCR" />。乔治在逼近[[多明尼加|多明尼加共和国]]期间出人意料地发展出清晰的风眼并开始增强，风速达到每小时195公里，卫星图像还显示风暴已重回四级飓风强度<ref>{{cite web | author = Hurricane Specialists | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-22 | accessdate = 2015-03-12 | title = Hurricane Georges Discussion Twenty-Nine | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023095622/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.029 | deadurl = no | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.029}}</ref>。{{lang|en|UTC}}9月22日中午12点30分，乔治以三级飓风强度登陆多明尼加共和国<ref name="TCR" />。

气象部门估计，伊斯帕尼奥拉岛的山区地形会是风暴减弱的显著因素，但即便是在登陆6小时后，乔治的风眼仍然清晰可见，外流也保持强劲，带状特征紧急组织在气旋周围<ref>{{cite web | author = Lixion A. Avila | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-22 | accessdate = 2015-03-12 | title = Hurricane Georges Discussion Thirty | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023095628/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.030 | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.030 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。不过到了9月23日，系统中心已经变得模糊，飓风猎人无法飞入风暴执行侦察任务，直到乔治回到海上才得以继续。飓风的核心受到伊斯帕尼奥拉岛上海拔3000米山脉的严重干扰，<ref>{{cite web | author = Pasch | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-23 | accessdate = 2015-03-12 | title = Hurricane Georges Discussion Thirty-One | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023095632/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.031 | deadurl = no | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.031}}</ref>气旋组织结构变得非常混乱，但途经伊斯帕尼奥拉岛期间，系统一直保持着飓风强度<ref name="TCR" />。
[[File:Hurricane_Georges_25_sept_1998_1935Z.jpg|180px]]上空的飓风乔治]]

9月23日下午，风暴进入[[向风海峡|向风海峡]]<ref name="TCR" />，并且保持着大规模环流，并带有眼状特征和层次分明的雨带<ref>{{cite web | author = Lixion A. Avila | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-23 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023095637/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.034 | accessdate = 2015-03-12 | title = Hurricane Georges Discussion Thirty-Four | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.034 | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2012-10-23}}</ref>。乔治在这天接下来的时间里基本没有发生变化，然后以风力时速120公里强度在[[关塔那摩湾|关塔那摩湾]]附近进行第5次登陆<ref name="TCR" />。由于受到东北方向的中到上层[[高气压|高气压区]]和[[尤卡坦半岛|尤卡坦半岛]]上空的中到下层低气压共同影响，气旋开始略朝西北转向<ref>{{cite web | author = Pasch | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-23 | accessdate = 2015-03-12 | title = Hurricane Georges Discussion Thirty-Five | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023095642/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.035 | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.035}}</ref>。途经[[古巴|古巴]]期间，乔治的中心已经模糊不清，但仍然保持着层次分明的外流格局<ref name="TCR" />。到9月24日，风暴已进入古巴以北洋面并开始再度发展出风眼墙<ref>{{cite web | author = Lixion A. Avila | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-24 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023095700/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.037 | accessdate = 2015-03-12 | title = Hurricane Georges Discussion Thirty-Seven | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.037 | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2012-10-23}}</ref>。

== 墨西哥湾及消亡 ==
9月25日，飓风逼近[[佛罗里达礁岛群|佛罗里达礁岛群]]，其中已再度发展出风眼，在卫星图像上也变得更加清晰<ref>{{cite web | author = Pasch | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-25 | accessdate = 2015-03-12 | title = Hurricane Georges Discussion Thirty-Nine | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.039 | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023095707/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.039 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。气旋再度强化，于{{lang|en|UTC}}下午15点30分左右以风速每小时160公里强度从[[基韦斯特岛|基韦斯特岛]]附近登陆<ref name="TCR" />。飓风转向西北偏西，预测模型表明乔治会在沿[[美国墨西哥湾沿岸地区|墨西哥湾沿岸地区]]登陆前再度达到三级飓风标准<ref>{{cite web | author = Lixion A. Avila | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-25 | accessdate = 2015-03-12 | title = Hurricane Georges Public Advisory Thirty-Nine-B | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023095722/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/pub/PBAL0798.039 | deadurl = no | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/pub/PBAL0798.039}}</ref>。由于风眼向西面摆动，风暴的前进速度较为缓慢，导致行进轨迹很不规则。估计垂直风切变的风速高达每小时55公里，使得外流被限制在飓风以内，大部分力道较强的对流也都局限在环流东侧。<ref>{{cite web | author = John L. Guiney | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-26 | accessdate = 2015-03-12 | title = Hurricane Georges Discussion Forty-Three | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023095730/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.043 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.043}}</ref>
[[File:Hurricane_Georges_28_sept_1998_2043Z.jpg|thumb]][[比洛克西_(密西西比州)|比洛克西]]附近登陆数小时后的飓风乔治]]

9月26至27日，气旋转向西北并继续缓慢移动<ref name="TCR" />。9月26日下午，系统的风场变得不对称，只有东侧才有飓风强度风力存在<ref>{{cite web | author = Edward N. Rappaport | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-26 | accessdate = 2015-03-13 | title = Hurricane Georges Discussion Forty-Five | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023095737/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.045 | deadurl = no | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.045}}</ref>。这天晚上，中心附近的风力时速已降至175公里，略低于大型飓风标准<ref name="TCR" />。这时，系统已经不大可能再出现爆发性增强<ref>{{cite web | author = Lixion A. Avila | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-27 | accessdate = 2015-03-12 | title = Hurricane Georges Discussion Forty-Eight | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.048 | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023095742/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.048}}</ref>。

9月28日，乔治的中心气压继续下降，最低降至961毫巴（百帕，28.39英寸汞柱）<ref name="TCR" />。[[路易斯安那州|路易斯安那州]]的[[新奥尔良|新奥尔良]]这时已经可以看到飓风，雷达图像则显示系统中的风眼墙已经不完整<ref>{{cite web | author = John L. Guiney | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-27 | accessdate = 2015-03-13 | title = Hurricane Georges Discussion Fifty | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.050 | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023095755/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.050 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。风暴继续缓慢前进<ref name="TCR" />，风眼墙周围的风速略有下降<ref>{{cite web | author = Miles B. Lawrence | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-28 | accessdate = 2015-03-13 | title = Hurricane Georges Discussion Fifty-One | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023095801/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.051 | deadurl = no | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.051 | archivedate = 2012-10-23}}</ref>。{{lang|en|UTC}}上午11点30左右，乔治以风力时速160公里强度最后一次从[[密西西比州|密西西比州]]的[[比洛克西_(密西西比州)|比洛克西]]附近登陆<ref name="TCR" />。上岸数小时后，气旋降级成1级飓风并基本停止前进<ref>{{cite web | author = Pasch | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023095807/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.053 | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-28 | accessdate = 2015-03-13 | title = Hurricane Georges Discussion Fifty-Three | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.053 | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。由于风暴移动缓慢，整个[[美国东南部|美国东南部]]极高的降雨量<ref name="TCR" />，其中又以[[佛罗里达州|佛罗里达州]]的曼森（{{lang|en|Munson}}）最高，达977毫米<ref name="WPCMAX">{{cite book | author = Roth, David M | author2 = Hydrometeorological Prediction Center | date = 2012-11-16 | chapter = Tropical Cyclone Rainfall Point Maxima | accessdate = 2015-03-13 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131203114006/http://www.wpc.ncep.noaa.gov/tropical/rain/tcmaxima.html | url = http://www.wpc.ncep.noaa.gov/tropical/rain/tcmaxima.html | publisher = United States National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration's National Weather Service | title = Tropical Cyclone Point Maxima | archivedate = 2013-12-03}}</ref>。

到9月29日晚，乔治已降级成热带风暴，其行进路线也在密西西比州南部上空形成很小的顺时针环<ref>{{cite web | author = Miles B. Lawrence | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-29 | accessdate = 2015-03-12 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023095823/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.055 | title = Tropical Storm Georges Discussion Fifty-Five | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.055 | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。随后，风暴开始加速向西北偏西方向移动<ref>{{cite web | author = Edward N. Rappaport | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-29 | accessdate = 2015-03-13 | title = Tropical Storm Georges Discussion Fifty-Six | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023095828/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.056 | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.056}}</ref>。{{lang|en|UTC}}中午12点左右，乔治降级成热带低气压<ref name="TCR" />，美国国家飓风中心也发布了针对这一天气系统的最后一份公告，[[美国气象预报中心|水文气象预报中心]]也在几乎同一时间开始发布公告<ref>{{cite web | author = Pasch | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 1998-09-29 | accessdate = 2015-03-13 | title = Tropical Depression Georges Discussion Fifty-Seven | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023095833/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.057 | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/dis/NAL0798.057}}</ref>。低气压在墨西哥湾沿岸行进期间保持着层次分明的环流，但环流中心一直位于内陆上空。大量热带水分进入风暴，沿途降下倾盆大雨<ref>{{cite report | author = Mausser | publisher = Hydrometeorological Prediction Center | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150313020306/http://www.wpc.ncep.noaa.gov/tropical/tropical_advisories.php?storm=GEORGES&adnum=58&dt=1998092917&status=td | accessdate = 2015-03-13 | date = 1998-09-29 | title = Remnants of GEORGES Advisory Number 58 | archivedate = 2013-03-13 | deadurl = no | url = http://www.wpc.ncep.noaa.gov/tropical/tropical_advisories.php?storm=GEORGES&adnum=58&dt=1998092917&status=td}}</ref>。到了9月30日，下层环流开始同上层环流分离<ref>{{cite report | author = Eckert | publisher = Hydrometeorological Prediction Center | date = 1998-09-30 | accessdate = 2015-03-13 | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2015-03-13 | archiveurl = 62}}</ref>。水文气象预报中心于10月1日清晨发布针对低气压的最后一份公告，这时系统已接近[[大西洋|大西洋]]<ref>{{cite report | author = Eckert | publisher = Hydrometeorological Prediction Center | date = 1998-10-01 | accessdate = 2015-03-13 | = http://www.wpc.ncep.noaa.gov/tropical/tropical_advisories.php?storm=GEORGES&adnum=63&dt=1998100105&status=td}}</ref>，并在数小时后完全消散<ref name="TCR" />。
