'''飓风胡安'''（{{lang-en|Hurricane Juan}}）是一场于2003年9月下旬对[[加拿大|加拿大]]造成重大损失的强烈[[热带气旋|热带气旋]]，也是[[2003年大西洋飓风季|2003年大西洋飓风季]]的第十个获得命名的风暴和第六场飓风，于9月24日由一股穿越[[亚热带|亚热带]][[大西洋|大西洋]]的[[东风波|东风波]]在[[百慕大|百慕大]]东南方向发展形成。系统北上进入[[墨西哥湾|墨西哥湾]]后因遇到[[墨西哥湾暖流|墨西哥湾暖流]]而逐步加强，于9月27日达到[[萨菲尔-辛普森飓风等级|萨菲尔-辛普森飓风等级]]下的二级飓风强度，并于同日达到[[最大持续风速|持续风速]]每小时165公里的最高强度，然后在向[[新斯科舍|新斯科舍]]海岸前进的过程中因海水温度降低而有所减弱。9月29日清晨，胡安以风速每小时160公里的二级飓风强度在[[哈利法克斯|哈利法克斯]]的沙德湾（{{lang|en|Shad Bay}}）和普罗斯佩克特（{{lang|en|Prospect}}）之间[[登陆_(气象学)|登陆]]<ref name="tcr">{{cite web | author = Lixion Avila | date = 2004-05-12 | title = Hurricane Juan Tropical Cyclone Report | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2014-03-17 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/2003juan.shtml | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131002000208/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/2003juan.shtml | archivedate = 2013-10-02 | deadurl = yes}}</ref>。风暴在从南向北穿越新斯科舍的过程中一直保持着飓风强度，然后在[[爱德华王子岛|爱德华王子岛]]上空减弱为热带风暴。9月29日晚，风暴在[[圣罗伦斯湾|圣罗伦斯湾]]北部的[[安蒂科斯蒂岛|安蒂科斯蒂岛]]附近由另一股[[温带气旋|温带低气压]]吸收。

这场飓风在穿过新斯科舍中部和进入爱德华王子岛的过程中造成了大范围的破坏，风暴中心以东和以西区域受到的影响较小，大部分损失都是由这些区域遭受的狂风引起。胡安所过之处共造成8人死亡，经济损失超过1.5亿美元（2003年[[美元|美元]]，相当于{{#time:Y}}年的{{Formatprice|{{Inflation|US|150000000|2003}}}}美元），报告中认为这是继1893年以来对新斯科舍造成破坏最严重的风暴<ref name="summary">{{cite web | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131206170111/http://www.novaweather.net/Hurricane_Juan_files/Juan_Summary.pdf | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2013-12-06 | url = http://www.novaweather.net/Hurricane_Juan_files/Juan_Summary.pdf | author = Chris Fogarty | year = 2003 | title = Hurricane Juan Storm Summary | work = Canadian Hurricane Centre | publisher = Environment Canada | accessdate = 2014-03-17 | format = PDF}}</ref><ref name="juan2003summary">{{cite web | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131206175452/http://www.ec.gc.ca/ouragans-hurricanes/default.asp?lang=en&n=B1A7B85A-1 | publisher = Environment Canada | author = Peter Bowyer | year = 2003 | title = Hurricane Juan 2003 Storm Summary | accessdate = 2014-03-17 | url = http://www.ec.gc.ca/ouragans-hurricanes/default.asp?lang=en&n=B1A7B85A-1 | archivedate = 2013-12-06 | deadurl = no}}</ref>，其名称“胡安”（{{lang|en|Juan}}）也因此予以退役，永不会再在[[北大西洋热带气旋|大西洋风暴]]命名中使用。

== 气象历史 ==
{{storm path|Juan 2003 track.png}}
2003年9月14日，一股大型东风波伴随着一片广阔的[[低气压|低气压区]]离开[[非洲|非洲]]海岸向西移动，其结构起初由于上层[[风切变|风切变]]的不利影响而杂乱无章<ref name="tcr" /><ref name="sept17two">{{cite web | author = Lixion Avila | date = 2003-09-17 | title = September 17 Tropical Weather Outlook | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2014-03-17 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/text/TWOAT/2003/TWOAT.200309171515 | archivedate = 2012-10-26 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121026003104/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/text/TWOAT/2003/TWOAT.200309171515 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。到了9月20日，系统周围的[[对流|对流]]因与一片大型上层低气压的相互作用而激增，不过外界环境存在的不利因素令其活动仍然保持混乱。系统整体围绕上层低气压向西北方向移动并发展出中层环流。9月23日，系统在位于[[百慕大|百慕大]]以南约725公里海域与一片[[天气图|锋区]]互动<ref name="tcr" />，组织性得以改善<ref name="sept23two">{{cite web | author = Lixion Avila | publisher = National Hurricane Center| date = 2003-09-23 | title = September 23 Tropical Weather Outlook | accessdate = 2014-03-17 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/text/TWOAT/2003/TWOAT.200309231502 | archivedate = 2012-10-26 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121026003109/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/text/TWOAT/2003/TWOAT.200309231502}}</ref>。这天晚些时候，系统中发展出了下层环流，不过由于附近存在的锋区，系统没有归类为[[热带气旋|热带低气压]]。9月24日，系统中心已经发展出了深层对流，还迅速发展出[[雨带|带状特征]]和明显的[[外流_(气象学)|外流]]。由于其组织性已明显改善，[[国家飓风中心|美国国家飓风中心]]于当天晚些时候将位于百慕大东南方向约555公里的系统归类为这年的第十五号热带低气压。不过在操作上，该部门起初并没有发布针对这一[[气旋|气旋]]的公告，根据飓风季之后的分析确认，美国国家飓风中心发布针对系统的首份公告时低气压已经形成了27个小时<ref name="tcr" />。

低气压起初同时具备[[热带气旋|热带]]和[[亚热带气旋|亚热带特征]]，一方面仍然与附近的冷锋相连接，另一方面暖心和对流的组织性又令其足以归类为热带气旋。气象部门一度预测低气压只会缓慢加强，所能达到的最高强度风速约为每小时105公里<ref name="td15disc1">{{cite web | author = Lixion Avila | date = 2003-09-25 | title = Tropical Depression Fifteen Discussion Fifteen | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2014-03-17 | archivedate = 2012-10-17 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2003/dis/al152003.discus.001.shtml? | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121017225452/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2003/dis/al152003.discus.001.shtml}}</ref>。不过低气压实际上却稳步组织起来，在9月25日清晨达到热带风暴强度，美国国家飓风中心因此将其命名为“胡安”<ref name="tcr" />。由于东面有一片[[副热带|副热带]][[高压脊|高压脊]]正在发展，热带风暴胡安开始以每小时16公里的速度向西北方向移动<ref name="juan2">{{cite web | author = Lixion Avila | date = 2003-09-25 | title = Tropical Storm Juan Discussion Two | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2014-03-17 | archivedate = 2012-10-22 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121022231501/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2003/dis/al152003.discus.002.shtml | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2003/dis/al152003.discus.002.shtml?}}</ref>。9月26日，风暴中发展出[[风眼|眼状特征]]，环流中心周围非常深层的对流也有所增长<ref name="juan4">{{cite web | author = Stacy Stewart | date = 2003-09-26 | title = Tropical Storm Juan Discussion Four | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2014-03-17 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121022231506/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2003/dis/al152003.discus.004.shtml | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2012-10-22 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2003/dis/al152003.discus.004.shtml?}}</ref>。云层格局继续巩固，胡安于9月26日晚在百慕大东南方向约270公里外洋面达到飓风强度<ref name="tcr" />。飓风接下来进入一片水温较高，风切变也较弱的水域并因此得以进一步加强，到了9月27日，风暴在位于新斯科舍[[哈利法克斯|哈利法克斯]]以南约1020公里海域达到风速最小时165公里的最高强度。这时飓风的[[风眼|风眼]]非常鲜明，周围是一片层次分明的圆形[[中心密集云层|中心密集云层]]层层环绕<ref name="avila10">{{cite web | author = Lixion Avila | date = 2003-09-27 | title = Hurricane Juan Discussion Ten | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2014-03-17 | archivedate = 2012-10-20 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121020211051/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2003/dis/al152003.discus.010.shtml | deadurl = no | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2003/dis/al152003.discus.010.shtml?}}</ref>。

[[File:Juan_at_landfall03.JPG|thumb]]
飓风胡安的最高强度保持了超过24小时。向西北方向移动了较长一段时间后，风暴转向加速北上<ref name="tcr" />。9月28日，飓风的风眼清晰程度有所降低，飓风强度也有小幅下降。由于前进速度较快，因此胡安经过的海域虽然水温明显下降，但风暴并未大幅减弱，到了9月29日在沙德湾和普罗斯佩克特之间登陆时，其风速仍然达到了每小时160公里<ref name="tcr" /><ref name="pasch14">{{cite web | author = Richard Pasch | date = 2003-09-28 | title = Hurricane Juan Discussion Fourteen | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2014-03-17 | archivedate = 2012-10-22 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121022231511/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2003/dis/al152003.discus.014.shtml | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2003/dis/al152003.discus.014.shtml? | deadurl = no}}</ref>。登陆后胡安继续快速穿越南部[[加拿大海洋省份|加拿大海洋省份]]，并在这一过程中迅速减弱，虽然穿越[[新斯科舍半岛|新斯科舍半岛]]过程中还有飓风强度，但到了9月29日清晨进入爱德华王子岛上空时就已降级成热带风暴。当天下午，风暴在圣罗伦斯湾西北上空由一片大型[[温带气旋|温带低气压]]吸收<ref name="tcr" />。

== 防灾措施 ==
9月26日，[[加拿大飓风中心|加拿大飓风中心]]发布了针对胡安的首份公告，预计这场风暴可能会给加拿大大西洋省份带去一些风和雨<ref name="CHC2612">{{cite web | author = Parkes | year = 2003 | title = Canadian Hurricane Centre Information Statement, 1200 UTC September 26 | publisher = Environment Canada | accessdate = 2014-03-17 | archivedate = 2013-10-06 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131006003920/http://www.ec.gc.ca/ouragans-hurricanes/E5761678-DCC3-45DF-816B-AD47F7C97C18/WT31-261200.txt | deadurl = no | url = http://www.ec.gc.ca/ouragans-hurricanes/E5761678-DCC3-45DF-816B-AD47F7C97C18/WT31-261200.txt}}</ref>。随着飓风在9月27日逼近，加拿大大西洋省份的地方媒体播放的警告也作出了相应变更。加拿大飓风中心在公告中表明，胡安有可能会造成停电和显著的风害，其产生的暴雨和[[风暴潮|风暴潮]]还可能引发洪灾，预计风暴登陆区域的公众和[[灾害管理|应急官员]]获知需要对可能来临的灾难做好准备<ref name="CHC2718">{{cite web | author = Parkes | year = 2003 | title = Canadian Hurricane Centre Information Statement, 1800 UTC September 27 | publisher = Environment Canada | accessdate = 2014-03-17 | url = http://www.ec.gc.ca/ouragans-hurricanes/E5761678-DCC3-45DF-816B-AD47F7C97C18/WT31-271800.txt | archivedate = 2013-10-06 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131006003927/http://www.ec.gc.ca/ouragans-hurricanes/E5761678-DCC3-45DF-816B-AD47F7C97C18/WT31-271800.txt}}</ref>。

== 影响 ==
=== 新斯科舍 ===
[[File:Barringtonhurricane.jpg|left]]街道上的废墟。]]
飓风胡安在新斯科舍登陆时的[[最大持续风速|最大持续风速]]经测量为每小时160公里<ref name="tcr" />。[[哈利法克斯港|哈利法克斯港]]周围的城市区域首当其冲，受到了风暴最强持续风速和阵风的吹袭，有非正式的估算中认为飓风产生的阵风时速高达230公里<ref name="classify">{{cite web | author = Peter Bowyer | year = 2003 | title = Classifying Hurricane Juan | publisher = Environment Canada | accessdate = 2014-03-17 | archivedate = 2013-10-02 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131002234945/http://www.ec.gc.ca/ouragans-hurricanes/default.asp?lang=En&n=258CBC16-1 | url = http://www.ec.gc.ca/ouragans-hurricanes/default.asp?lang=en&n=258CBC16-1}}</ref>。哈利法克斯港入口的气象[[浮标|浮标]]在纪录到超过20米高的狂浪后从系泊处崩断。港口有人居住的海岸上出现了严重的侵蚀，特别是贝德福德盆地，这里的铁轨和居民财物受到了最多的海浪冲击。海港内的风暴潮达到1.5到2米高，创下哈利法克斯港的新纪录<ref name="surge">{{cite web | author = Peter Bowyer | year = 2003 | title = The Storm Surge and Waves at Halifax with Hurricane Juan | publisher = Environment Canada | accessdate = 2014-03-17 | archivedate = 2013-10-02 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131002234844/http://www.ec.gc.ca/ouragans-hurricanes/default.asp?lang=En&n=BAAEAC12-1 | url = http://www.ec.gc.ca/ouragans-hurricanes/default.asp?lang=en&n=BAAEAC12-1}}</ref>。不过由于风暴行进速度快，并且其南面还存在较为干燥的空气，因此降水相对来说很少，没有出现任何地方报告的降雨量超过52毫米<ref name="rainfall">{{cite web | year = 2003 | title = Rainfall amounts from Hurricane Juan | publisher = Environment Canada | accessdate = 2014-03-17 | url = http://www.novaweather.net/Hurricane_Juan_files/rainfall.txt | archivedate = 2012-09-08 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120908085321/http://www.novaweather.net/Hurricane_Juan_files/rainfall.txt | deadurl = no}}</ref>。

胡安令整个区域出现了大范围的结构和植被破坏，特别是在哈利法克斯及其周边地区。报告表明有大量树木被刮倒，堵塞了许多街道并压断了多条供电线路。许多家庭和商户遭受了财产损失，特别是结构性的屋顶损坏，还有一些强度较弱的建筑被毁<ref name="summary" />。新斯科舍估计有31%的居民家中受到了一定程度的破坏，27%的家庭破坏程度足以要求保险索赔<ref name="climatesmart">{{cite web | year = 2003 | title = Climate SMART: Be cool, reduce global warming, pollution and climate risks | publisher = Halifax Regional Municipality | accessdate = 2014-03-17 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131226162042/http://www.halifax.ca/climate/change2.html | archivedate = 2013-12-26 | url = http://www.halifax.ca/climate/change2.html | deadurl = yes }}</ref>。哈利法克斯市中心有垃圾桶大小的侵蚀控制石块从哈利法克斯港被大浪冲到浮桥、停车场和码头上。风暴来袭期间予以疏散的维多利亚总医院遭受了水害，屋顶也受到破坏，还有多幢高楼公寓楼和多户型住宅也出现了同样的情况<ref name="usatoday">{{cite news | author = Associated Press | title = Juan hits Canada's eastern coast | publisher = USA Today | accessdate = 2014-03-17 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121023082205/http://usatoday30.usatoday.com/weather/hurricane/2003-09-29-hurricanejuan-canada_x.htm | url = http://usatoday30.usatoday.com/weather/hurricane/2003-09-29-hurricanejuan-canada_x.htm | date = 2003-09-30 | archivedate = 2012-10-23 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。还有许多广告牌和指示牌被摧毁，数十辆车被树木及其他废物砸毁<ref name="summary" />。市内的[[波因特普莱森特公园|波因特普莱森特公园]]和[[哈利法克斯公共花园|哈利法克斯公共花园]]都有大量树木被毁并关闭了数月之久。

哈利法克斯港入口的三布罗岛上拥有整个[[北美|北美]]最古老的灯塔，但这座灯塔也在胡安的摧毁下变得满目疮痍，到2007年都未能修复<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.heritagecanada.org/eng/news/s220_brief.htm | title = Brief to the Standing Committee on Fisheries and Oceans — Bill S-220 An Act to protect heritage lighthouses | publisher = Heritage Canada Foundation | accessdate = 2011-08-13 | deadurl = yes | archivedate = 2011-09-28 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20110928051706/http://www.heritagecanada.org/eng/news/s220_brief.htm}}</ref>。这场飓风给海港的航运造成了沉重打击，一般名为“拉林达号”（{{lang|en|Larinda}}）的来访休闲帆船在[[大西洋海事博物馆|大西洋海事博物馆]]旁的码头沉没，另有一艘海港游览双桅船“玛尔号”（{{lang|en|Mar}}）从哈利法克斯港对面的东部航道冲到了岸上。还有一艘海港游览帆船“席尔瓦号”（{{lang|en|Silva}}）从系泊处挣脱，还对海滨的码头电缆造成了大范围破坏。数十艘小型游艇也被冲到岸上，贝德福德盆地的多个游艇俱乐部都受到了严重破坏。南端[[集装箱|集装箱]]码头停靠的两艘货柜船上有数十个集装箱被风暴冲倒。哈利法克斯和达特茅斯（{{lang|en|Dartmouth}}）海滨的多个码头受到数额可观的破坏，达特茅斯铁路站场有多节车厢被冲入海港中，沿贝德福德盆地的铁路主线复线有多段靠近米尔维尤（{{lang|en|Millview}}）的铁轨被冲脱。风暴潮还在哈利法克斯港周边引发了沿海洪灾<ref name="surge" />，不过由于胡安的行进速度较快，产生的降雨量不高，因此内陆洪水也很轻微。<ref name="summary" />

风暴向西进入[[圣玛格丽特湾|圣玛格丽特湾]]（{{lang|en|St. Margarets Bay}}）和[[马洪湾|马洪湾]]（{{lang|en|Mahone Bay}}）时造成的破坏不及哈利法西斯港周边区域严重。此外，新斯科舍中心城区[[特鲁罗_(加拿大)|特鲁罗]]（{{lang|en|Truro}}）镇和[[科尔切斯特县|科尔切斯特县]]的所有乡村地区以及[[皮克图县|皮克图县]]（{{lang|en|Pictou County}}）西部都遭受了财产损失，还因树木倒塌引发了停电，风暴沿途东侧有许多谷仓和其它农业建筑受损，还包括皮克图港停放的“赫克托号”（{{lang|en|Hector}}）帆船复制品<ref name="summary" />。哈利法克斯和[[达特茅斯_(加拿大)|达特茅斯]][[都会区|都会区]]财物损失的严重程度令一些气象预告人员认为胡安很可能达到了三级飓风强度，但是多份报告中的持续风速都不能支持这一看法。新斯科舍中部的许多落叶树在风暴来袭时仍然有树叶存在，这在一定程度上放大了风害的影响<ref name="hithard">{{cite web | author = Murray Brewster | year = 2004 | title = Why Did Juan Hit So Hard? | publisher = Halifax Chronicle-Herald | accessdate = 2009-03-27 | deadurl = yes | url = http://c-ciarn.bio.ns.ca/documents/HurricaneJuan.pdf | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20090327040619/http://c-ciarn.bio.ns.ca/documents/HurricaneJuan.pdf | archivedate = 2009-03-27 | format = [[PDF|PDF]]}}</ref>。估计总体受到破坏的树木数量需要以百万来计算<ref name="summary" />。

胡安途经新斯科舍的过程中直接夺走了两人的生命，还间接导致四人死亡。其中直接因风暴丧生的两人都是被倒塌的树木砸死，一位是哈利法克斯的医务人员，另一位是恩菲尔德的机动车驾驭员<ref name="cleanup">{{cite news | author = CBC News | date = 2003-09-30 | title = Nova Scotians clean up after Juan | publisher = Canadian Broadcasting Corporation (CBC) | accessdate = 2014-03-18 | archivedate = 2012-11-04 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121104013416/http://www.cbc.ca/news/story/2003/09/29/juan030929.html | url = http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/nova-scotians-clean-up-after-juan-1.357097}}</ref>。间接遇难的四人中，有三人是因停电后使用蜡烛引发的住房火灾导致，还有一人是风暴过后的救灾工作人员<ref name="summary" />。

=== 加拿大大西洋省份其他地区 ===
[[File:Juan_2003_rainfall.gif|right]]
风暴穿过新斯科舍进入[[诺森伯伦海峡|诺森伯伦海峡]]时仍然有一级飓风强度，到进入圣罗伦斯湾时减弱成了热带风暴。[[夏洛特顿|夏洛特顿]]报告的阵风时速达到139公里<ref name="juan2003summary" />，圣罗伦斯湾的马格德林群岛报告的阵风时速也有107公里<ref name="tcr" />。

爱德华王子岛也报告受到了胡安的破坏，特别是在夏洛特顿周边，当地海滨的游艇和海堤受到了大浪的破坏，该市中心城区老城市森林也受到了严重破坏。整个岛上到处都有大范围树木受损的报道，许多谷仓和粮仓之类强度较弱的建筑物受到结构性损伤<ref name="summary" />。飓风还导致岛上多个地区停电<ref>{{cite news | author = Staff Writer | title = Hurricane Juan pounds provinces | date = 2003-09-30 | agency = Associated Press | newspaper = The Topeka-Capital Journal Online | accessdate = 2014-03-18 | url = http://cjonline.com/stories/093003/pag_juan.shtml | archivedate = 2012-10-07 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121007180359/http://cjonline.com/stories/093003/pag_juan.shtml | deadurl = yes }}</ref>。胡安至此行进的路径已经比较狭窄，这意味着其造成的破坏将非常局部化，新不伦瑞克和爱德华王子岛西部都只受以了很少的损失<ref name="juanedna">{{cite web | author = Fogarty, Chris | year = 2004 | title = A Comparison of the Wind Fields of Hurricane Edna (1954) and Hurricane Juan (2003) | publisher = Meteorological Service of Canada | accessdate = 2006-12-23 | url = http://deved.meted.ucar.edu/norlat/cases/case_novascotia_juan_edna/edna_juan_comp.pdf | format = PDF | deadurl = yes | archivedate = 2011-10-06 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20111006021347/http://deved.meted.ucar.edu/norlat/cases/case_novascotia_juan_edna/edna_juan_comp.pdf}}</ref>。岛上于9月29日举行的普选也予以中断，不过还是有超过80%的选民赶到了投票站<ref name="election">{{cite news | author = Canadian Press | year = 2003 | title = Hurricane doesn't stop election | publisher = CanWest Global Communications | accessdate = 2006-11-20}}</ref>。

有两位来自新不伦瑞克的渔夫在圣罗伦斯湾偏远的魁北克[[安蒂科斯蒂岛|安蒂科斯蒂岛]]水域捕鱼时因风暴丧生<ref name="summary" />。

== 善后 ==
[[File:JuanHFXwaterfront.jpg|thumb]]
风暴来袭几小时后，哈利法克斯都会区以及相邻的东汉普郡市政区和[[科尔切斯特县|科尔切斯特县]]宣布进入[[紧急状态|紧急状态]]。新斯科舍政府请求联邦政府调派600名[[加拿大军队|加拿大军人]]协助哈利法克斯都会区地方政府进行市区的清理以及公共事业职工的电力抢修工作<ref name="fallen">{{cite news | author = CBC News | title = Fallen trees hamper efforts to restore power in the wake of Juan | publisher = CBC | accessdate = 2014-03-18 | archivedate = 2014-03-18 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140318024945/http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/fallen-trees-hamper-efforts-to-restore-power-in-wake-of-juan-1.385421 | url = http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/fallen-trees-hamper-efforts-to-restore-power-in-wake-of-juan-1.385421 | date = 2003-10-01}}</ref>。许多公用事业员工，特别是[[新斯科舍电力公司|新斯科舍电力公司]]的雇员和海事电气与[[新不伦瑞克电力公司|新不伦瑞克电力公司]]的数十名工作人员根据企业间达成的互助协定连续工作了近三个星期，并且这一期间都没有得到充足的休息<ref name="summary" />。

满目疮痍的波因特普莱森特公园在风暴过后关闭进行了长时间的清理工作，一直到9个月后的2004年6月才再度开放，公园的海岸线受损，还失去了近85%的树木。2005年，政府开始对其展开复兴和再造林工程<ref name="ptpleasant">{{cite web | year = 2005 | title = Point Pleasant Park: Forestification | publisher = Halifax Regional Municipality | accessdate = 2006-12-23 | url = http://www.halifax.ca/real_property/pointpleasant/ | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20070516124247/http://www.halifax.ca/real_property/pointpleasant/ | archivedate = 2007-05-16}}</ref>。

新斯科舍政府在风暴来袭后承诺拨款1000万[[加拿大元|加拿大元]]援助救灾，还有许多私人迅速在灾后进行捐款<ref name="nsrelief">{{cite web | author = CTV.ca News Staff | year = 2003 | title = N.S. pledges $10M in relief for Hurricane Juan | publisher = CTVglobemedia | accessdate = 2014-03-18 | deadurl = yes | archivedate = 2012-03-04 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120304234123/http://www.ctv.ca/CTVNews/CTVNewsAt11/20031010/ns_juan_031010/ | url = http://www.ctv.ca/CTVNews/CTVNewsAt11/20031010/ns_juan_031010/}}</ref>。爱德华王子岛也在飓风袭击后立即承诺提供20万加拿大元的救灾款<ref name="peirelief">{{cite web | author = MacFayden, Elmer | year = 2003 | title = Province announces $200,000 to address immediate needs of Islanders affected by Hurricane Juan | publisher = Government of Prince Edward Island | accessdate = 2012-03-19 | url = http://www.gov.pe.ca/news/getrelease.php3?number=3299 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120319150134/http://www.gov.pe.ca/news/getrelease.php3?number=3299 | deadurl = yes | archivedate = 2012-03-19}}</ref>，联邦政府也宣布会提供救灾款项<ref name="juanhome">{{cite web | author = Canadian Hurricane Centre | year = 2003 | title = Hurricane Juan 2003 Special Reports (link removed) | publisher = Meteorological Service of Canada | accessdate = 2014-03-18 | archivedate = 2013-10-02 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131002234629/http://www.ec.gc.ca/ouragans-hurricanes/default.asp?lang=En&n=F0E43FF7-1 | url = http://www.ec.gc.ca/ouragans-hurricanes/default.asp?lang=En&n=F0E43FF7-1}}</ref>。当时的[[多伦多|多伦多]]市长[[梅尔·拉斯曼|梅尔·拉斯曼]]（{{lang|en|Mel Lastman}}）也捐出了5万加拿大元用于替换爱德华王子岛上受损的树木<ref name="torontofunds">{{cite news | year = 2005 | title = Toronto funds trees for PEI | publisher = Canada NewsWire | accessdate = 2006-12-23}}</ref>。

由于这场风暴对加拿大造成了严重影响，其名称“胡安”也于2004年4月予以退役，永远都不会再在[[北大西洋热带气旋|大西洋飓风]]命名时使用。加拿大环境部表示，申请将这一名称退役是“出于对其造成的生命损失、经济冲击和两个省中树木的大范围毁损”<ref>{{cite web | quote = in consideration of the lost and damaged lives, the impact to economy, and the widespread destruction of trees throughout two provinces | archivedate = 2013-10-02 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131002234941/http://www.ec.gc.ca/ouragans-hurricanes/default.asp?lang=En&n=3E279151-1 | url = http://www.ec.gc.ca/ouragans-hurricanes/default.asp?lang=en&n=3E279151-1 | author = Peter Bowyer | publisher = Canadian Hurricane Centre | date = 2004-04-29 | accessdate = 2007-01-30 | title = Should "Hurricane Juan" be retired?}}</ref>。这也是加拿大气象局在历史上首次申请将一个飓风名称退役<ref>{{cite news | url = http://atlantic-web1.ns.ec.gc.ca/newsreleases/default.asp?lang=En&n=561C8A5C-1 | date = 2004-04-30 | accessdate = 2008-01-19 | publisher = Environment Canada | title = 'Juan' retired from World Meteorological Organization hurricane name list | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20080119024953/http://atlantic-web1.ns.ec.gc.ca/newsreleases/default.asp?lang=En&n=561C8A5C-1 | archivedate = 2008-01-19}}</ref>。这个名称之后以“华金”（{{lang|en|Joaquin}}）代替，计划在[[2009年大西洋飓风季|2009年大西洋飓风季]]期间首度启用<ref name="wmo1">{{cite web | author = World Meteorological Organization | year = 2004 | title = Final Report of the 2003 Hurricane Season | accessdate = 2014-03-18 | archivedate = 2013-11-04 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131104140940/http://www.wmo.int/pages/prog/www/tcp/documents/HC26-English.pdf | url = http://www.wmo.int/pages/prog/www/tcp/documents/HC26-English.pdf | format = PDF}}</ref>。
