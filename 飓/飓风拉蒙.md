'''飓风拉蒙'''（{{lang-en|Hurricane Ramon}}）是1987年10月在[[南加利福尼亚州|南加利福尼亚州]]降下暴雨的一场高强度[[太平洋飓风季|太平洋飓风]]，也是活跃程度高于平均水平的[[1987年太平洋飓风季|1987年太平洋飓风季]]形成的第19场获命名的风暴，由10月初的[[热带扰动|热带扰动]]发展而成。10月5日，[[曼萨尼约|曼萨尼约]]西南方向数百英里海域有热带风暴形成，直接跳过热带低气压阶段。拉蒙起初朝西面移动，之后转向[[罗盘方位|西北偏西]]。10月7日，气旋达到飓风强度，再于两天后达到[[最大持续风速|风力时速]]220公里的最高强度。拉蒙接下来转向西北，并因行经洋面水温降低而急剧减弱，于10月11日降级成热带风暴，次日又进一步弱化成热带低气压，并且不久后便完全消散。

虽然自始至终远离陆地，但拉蒙还是在[[下加利福尼亚半岛|下加利福尼亚半岛]]产生小雨。风暴残留之后在[[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]]降下暴雨并引发洪灾，间接导致五人因交流意外遇难。内陆方向远至[[犹他州|犹他州]]都出现降水。

== 气象历史 ==
{{Storm path|Ramon 1987 track.png}}
飓风拉蒙源于10月初[[墨西哥|墨西哥]]中部上空大规模[[高压脊|高压脊]]南面的[[热带扰动|热带扰动]]。由于行经洋面[[海面温度|水温]]较高，达到约29[[摄氏温标|℃]]，这股扰动天气逐渐组织，[[东太平洋飓风中心|东太平洋飓风中心]]于[[协调世界时|协调世界时]]10月5日下午18点把中心位于[[阿卡普尔科|阿卡普尔科]]西南方向约925公里海域的系统升级成热带风暴，并以“拉蒙”（{{lang|en|Ramon}}）命名。<ref name="1987phs">{{cite journal | author = R.L. Cross | year = 1988 | title = Eastern North Pacific Tropical Cyclones of 1987 | journal = Monthly Weather Review | publisher = American Meteorological Society | volume = 116 | accessdate = 2016-07-13 | url = http://journals.ametsoc.org/doi/pdf/10.1175/1520-0493.116.10.2106.1 | doi = 10.1175/1520-0493.116.10.2106.1 | bibcode = 1988MWRv..116.2106C}}</ref>气象机构起初预计气旋会向西移动，不会影响陆地，但实际情况并非如此<ref name="EPHC">{{cite journal | author1 = Eastern Pacific Hurricane Center | date = Fall 1988 | title = Annual data and verification tabulation, eastern North Pacific tropical storms and hurricanes, 1987 | author2 = E.B. Gunther | author3 = R.L. Cross | author4 = National Weather Service Western Region | publisher = [[美国国家气象局|National Weather Service]]}}</ref>。拉蒙在热带风暴强度下约保持了24小时，然后就转向[[罗盘方位|西北偏西]]并开始增强。{{lang|en|[[协调世界时|UTC]]}}10月7日中午12点，东太平洋飓风中心报称气旋已强化成飓风。约24小时后，风暴强度达到[[萨菲尔-辛普森飓风等级|萨菲尔-辛普森飓风等级]]下的二级飓风标准。拉蒙快速增强，于10月8日达到大型飓风强度。{{lang|en|UTC}}10月9日早上6点，气旋升级成四级飓风<ref name="EPAC HURDAT">{{cite web | author1 = [[国家飓风中心|National Hurricane Center]] | author2 = Hurricane Research Division | author3 = Central Pacific Hurricane Center | publisher = [[美国国家海洋和大气管理局|United States National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration]]'s National Weather Service | title = The Northeast and North Central Pacific hurricane database 1949–2015 | accessdate = 2016-07-13 | format = [[文本文件|.TXT]] | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160614024812/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/data/hurdat/hurdat2-nepac-1949-2015-050916.txt | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/data/hurdat/hurdat2-nepac-1949-2015-050916.txt | archivedate = 2016-06-14 | deadurl = no}}</ref>，并在几乎同一时间达到[[最大持续风速|持续风速]]每小时220公里的最高强度<ref name="1987phs" />，之后在此强度下保持的时长约为24小时<ref name="EPAC HURDAT" />。

保持最高强度约一天后，飓风开始减弱。10月10日晚，拉蒙在转向西北期间小幅弱化，但次日清晨就已降级成二级飓风。<ref name="EPAC HURDAT" />受[[高速气流|高速气流]]和行经洋面[[海面温度|水温]]逐渐降低的共同影响，气旋开始快速消退<ref name="1987phs" />。{{lang|en|UTC}}10月11日早上6点，风暴强度降至一级飓风标准，再于当晚降级成热带风暴，10月12日进一步减弱成热带低气压，最终于{{lang|en|UTC}}当天早上6点完全消散<ref name="EPAC HURDAT" />。拉蒙的残留湿气之后进入[[南加利福尼亚州|南加利福尼亚州]]上空，产生创纪录的降雨量<ref name="1987phs" />。

== 防灾措施和影响 ==
[[File:Hurricane_Ramon_(1987).JPG|thumb]]
由于难以确认风暴动向，飓风拉蒙离加利福尼亚州位置尚远时，[[橙县_(加利福尼亚州)|橙县]]南部、[[圣迭戈县_(加利福尼亚州)|圣迭戈县]]、[[圣贝纳迪诺县|圣贝纳迪诺县]]西部和[[河滨县|河滨县]]就收到山洪爆发预警<ref name="news3">{{cite news | title = Snow, Record Cold Hit U.S. | page = 1 | newspaper = The Gainesville Sun | date = 1987-10-13 | accessdate = 2016-07-13 | url = https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=DT9WAAAAIBAJ&sjid=CuoDAAAAIBAJ&pg=3562,3437942&dq=hurricane+california&hl=en}}</ref><ref name="news1">{{cite news | author = McLean | title = West gets much-needed rain | date = 1987-10-13}}</ref><ref name="news2">{{cite news | author = Jack Jones | title = Hurricane Could Spawn Some Raine | newspaper = [[洛杉矶时报|The Los Angeles Times]] | date = 1987-10-10}}</ref>。此外，包括[[圣安娜山|圣安娜山]]、[[拉古纳山|拉古纳山]]和[[约书亚树国家公园|约书亚树国家纪念区]]在内的大范围地区进入警戒状态<ref name="news4">{{cite news | title = Storm Brings Record Rain | page = 5 | url = https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=XyFZAAAAIBAJ&sjid=zUYNAAAAIBAJ&pg=4856,1678895&dq=hurricane+california&hl=en | newspaper = The Union Democrat | date = 1987-10-13 | accessdate = 2016-07-13}}</ref>。风暴还在海上时就令[[下加利福尼亚半岛|下加利福尼亚半岛]]太平洋海岸受到大浪冲击，半岛最南端的[[卡波圣卢卡斯|卡波圣卢卡斯]]（{{lang|en|Cabo San Lucas}}）测得0.91米的轻浪<ref name="news2" />。拉蒙的外围[[雨带|雨带]]在半岛局部地区产生降水<ref name="LN">{{cite news | title = Cold on the Plains, Snow on Vermont | date = 1987-10-13 | agency = [[合众国际社|United Press International]]}}</ref>。

气旋残留令南加利福尼亚州普降暴雨<ref name="1987phs" />，降雨量最高的[[彭德顿营|彭德顿营]]达到54毫米<ref>{{cite report | title = A History of Significant Weather Events | url = http://www.wrh.noaa.gov/sgx/document/weatherhistory.pdf | year = 2005 | publisher = National Weather Service Forecast Office San Diego, California | work = National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration | pages = 37 | accessdate = 2016-07-13 | format = [[便携式文档格式|PDF]] | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160120153201/http://www.wrh.noaa.gov/sgx/document/weatherhistory.pdf | archivedate = 2016-01-20}}</ref>。[[圣地亚哥_(加利福尼亚州)|圣地牙哥]]共降下13毫米雨量<ref>{{cite news | title = The Weather Today | newspaper = The Union Democrat | date = 1987-10-12 | accessdate = 2016-07-13 | page = 6 | url = https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=XiFZAAAAIBAJ&sjid=zUYNAAAAIBAJ&pg=6888,1559563&dq=hurricane+california&hl=en}}</ref>，引发街道洪灾<ref name="5deaths">{{cite news | title = 5 dead in S. California is Storm's last Punch | newspaper = San Jose Mercury News | date = 1987-10-13}}</ref>。圣迭戈县降下瓢泼大雨，有两人分别死于交通事故<ref>{{cite news | title = Dying Hurricane Sprinkles Rains to Southland | newspaper = Daily News of Los Angeles | date = 1987-10-13}}</ref>，更北面的[[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]]市也有三人因交通意外遇难<ref name="news4" />。暴雨覆盖范围还向西延伸到河滨县的[[赫米特_(加利福尼亚州)|赫米特]]<ref name="news4" />，当地发生车祸，导致三人受伤<ref name="LN" />。不过，风暴产生的降水也不无益处，帕洛马山此前已持续一周、受灾面积达6500万平方米的森林火灾得到控制<ref name="news4" />。橙县和圣迭戈县有多条街道封闭，一间图书馆的屋顶漏水，工作人员不得不将书掩盖起来保护<ref>{{cite report | publisher = National Climatic Data Center | year = 1988 | title = Storm Data and Unusual Weather Phenomena | accessdate = 2013-11-11 | page = 15 | format = PDF | archivedate = 2013-11-11 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131111181239/http://www1.ncdc.noaa.gov/pub/orders/IPS-1F528B5F-6F60-40A7-A768-EFBA93B8BBDB.pdf | url = http://www1.ncdc.noaa.gov/pub/orders/IPS-1F528B5F-6F60-40A7-A768-EFBA93B8BBDB.pdf | deadurl = yes}}</ref>。此外，泉溪河的河水溢出河道<ref>{{cite news | title = San Jose Mercury News (CA) – October 13, 1987 – 8B California News Record San Diego Area Rains Help Control Big Brush Fire | date = 1987-10-13 | newspaper = San Jose Mercury News}}</ref>。气旋的残留湿气还在[[内华达州|内华达州]]、[[亚利桑那州|亚利桑那州]]、犹他州和[[科罗拉多州|科罗拉多州]]西南部产生降水<ref>{{cite news | newspaper = The Dispatch | title = Floyd Loses Its Punch | accessdate = 2016-07-13 | page = 6 | url = https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=KzseAAAAIBAJ&sjid=AL8EAAAAIBAJ&pg=6010,5419299&dq=hurricane+california&hl=en | date = 1987-10-13}}</ref>。这些湿气有助于缓解[[盐湖城|盐湖城]]已持续37天的旱情。整场风暴一共间接导致五人丧生，均为交通事故引发。<ref name="LN2">{{cite news | title = Domestic News | date = 1987-10-13 | agency = [[美联社|Associated Press]]}}</ref>
