'''[[飓风伊万|飓风伊万]]对小安的列斯群岛和南美洲的影响'''為共计45人死亡，经济损失超过12.47亿[[美元|美元]]（2005年美元，相当于{{#time:Y}}年的{{Formatprice|{{Inflation|US|1147000000|2004|r=2}}}}美元），其中又以[[格林纳达|格林纳达]]最为严重，是近半个世纪以来当地受[[热带气旋|飓风]]破坏最为严重的一次。这场风暴源于10月5日的一股[[东风波|东风波]]，之后经爆发性增强成为仆街大型飓风，于9月7日以每小时205公里风速在[[小安的列斯群岛|小安的列斯群岛]]以南近海掠过。当时飓风中心向外延伸260公里外风力仍有热带风暴强度，向外延伸110公里外风力则有飓风强度<ref name="adv22">{{cite web | year = 2004 | author = Avila | title = Hurricane Ivan Public Advisory Twenty-Two | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2014-04-29 | archivedate = 2014-04-23 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140423214952/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2004/pub/al092004.public.022.shtml | deadurl = no | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2004/pub/al092004.public.022.shtml?}}</ref>，[[风眼|风眼]]的北部也在格林纳达上空经过。

受创最为严重的格林纳达遭受了价值高达11亿美元（2004年美元，相当于{{#time:Y}}年的{{Formatprice|{{Inflation|US|1100000000|2004|r=2}}}}美元）的破坏，相当于该国两年的{{lang|en|[[国内生产总值|GDP]]}}。飓风令超过1.4万户民房受损，其中30%被彻底摧毁，导致约1.8万人无家可归，岛上共有39人遇难。[[委内瑞拉|委内瑞拉]]北部因伊万致使至少3人丧生，还造成了中等程度破坏。[[特立尼达岛|特立尼达岛]]和[[巴巴多斯|巴巴多斯]]各有一人死亡，风暴名称“伊万”（{{lang|en|Ivan}}）也因此予以退役<ref name="wmo">{{cite web | author = World Meteorological Organization | year = 2005 | title = Twenty-Seventh Session RA IV Hurricane Committee | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20080625022834/http://www.wmo.ch/pages/prog/www/TCP_vO/Final%20HC%2027%20Report-English.pdf | archivedate = 2008-06-25 | deadurl = yes | accessdate = 2008-06-25 | url = http://www.wmo.ch/pages/prog/www/TCP_vO/Final%20HC%2027%20Report-English.pdf | format = [[PDF|PDF]]}}</ref>。

== 防灾措施 ==
风暴持续初期，[[国家飓风中心|美国国家飓风中心]]对其行进路径的预计比实际情况更为偏北。9月5日晚，巴巴多斯政府向该国管辖地区发布了[[热带气旋警告|飓风观察预警]]。之后不久，[[圣卢西亚|圣卢西亚]]收到了飓风观察预警，格林纳达及其附属地区都收到了热带风暴观察预警。随着飓风朝南小安的列斯群岛逼近，越来越多的岛屿收到了观察预警，到伊万最终经过该[[群岛|群岛]]前24小时，巴巴多斯、[[圣文森特和格林纳丁斯|圣文森特和格林纳丁斯]]、圣卢西亚、[[多巴哥岛|多巴哥岛]]和格林纳达都处在飓风警告生效状态下，[[特立尼达岛|特立尼达岛]]也处于热带风暴警告生效状态。由于伊万的实际路径比之前的预计偏南，偏北面一些岛屿上的飓风警告相应降级为热带风暴警告，到9月7日晚飓风风眼从格林纳达附近经过时，圣文森特和格林纳丁斯、特立尼达岛、多巴哥岛以及格林纳达都处于飓风警告生效状态。之后委内瑞拉北海岸到[[哥伦比亚|哥伦比亚]]的[[瓜希拉半岛|瓜希拉半岛]]之间地区都收到了热带风暴警告<ref name="tcr">{{cite web | author = Stacy Stewart | year = 2004 | title = Hurricane Ivan Tropical Cyclone Report | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2014-04-30 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140421050902/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/2004ivan.shtml | archivedate = 2014-04-21 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/2004ivan.shtml?}}</ref>。

生活在[[法尔孔州|法尔孔州]]、[[苏克雷州|苏克雷州]]和[[玛格丽塔岛|玛格丽塔岛]]沿海地区的委内瑞拉公民转移到了更加安全的地点<ref name="reut97">{{cite news | author = Linda Hutchinson-Jafar | date = 2004-09-07 | title = Hurricane Ivan devastates isle of Grenada | agency = Reuters Foundation | accessdate = 2014-04-30 | url = http://reliefweb.int/report/barbados/hurricane-ivan-devastates-isle-grenada | archivedate = 2014-04-30 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140430145531/http://reliefweb.int/report/barbados/hurricane-ivan-devastates-isle-grenada}}</ref>，成千上万的居民因这场飓风而撤离。[[迈克蒂亚西蒙·玻利瓦尔国际机场|迈克蒂亚西蒙·玻利瓦尔国际机场]]以及另外三所内地机场予以关闭。[[委内瑞拉石油公司|委内瑞拉石油公司]]暂时关闭了位于[[库拉索|库拉索]]的炼油厂，还有两个港口短暂封闭，导致石油交货两次受到延迟<ref>{{cite news | publisher = VENews | date = 2004-09-10 | title = Hurricane Ivan moves away; Rangel says situation under control}}</ref>。[[瓦莱罗能源|瓦莱罗能源]]关闭了设立在[[阿鲁巴|阿鲁巴]]的最大规模炼油厂<ref>{{cite news | author = Geoffrey Smith | date = 2004-09-09 | title = Hurricane Ivan tears into Caribbean isles: 20 reported dead | publisher = Bloomberg}}</ref>。[[特立尼达及托巴哥|特立尼达及托巴哥]]的石油公司在飓风来袭前停止采油，并将海上钻井平台的工作人员撤离，[[大西洋液化天然气|大西洋液化天然气]]公司中止了天然气出口<ref name="reut97" />。托巴哥开设了七个避难所，约有560人前去躲避，其中大部分是低洼地区居民。此外，风暴途经当天，大部分学校和商户都予以关闭<ref name="ifrc910">{{cite web | author = International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies | year = 2004 | title = Caribbean: Hurricane Ivan Preliminary Appeal No. 21/04 | publisher = ReliefWeb | accessdate = 2014-04-30 | archivedate = 2013-10-23 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131023090519/http://reliefweb.int/report/aruba-netherlands/caribbean-hurricane-ivan-preliminary-appeal-no-2104 | url = http://reliefweb.int/report/aruba-netherlands/caribbean-hurricane-ivan-preliminary-appeal-no-2104 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。该国的两所主要机场也予关闭，还有一家航空公司取消了所有的航班<ref name="bbc97">{{cite news | publisher = BBC | date = 2004-09-07 | title = Hurricane Ivan leaves four possible deaths, trail of destruction}}</ref>。

== 影响 ==
=== 委内瑞拉、特立尼达和多巴哥，以及{{lang|en|ABC}}群岛===
飓风伊万给委内瑞拉北部沿海带去了强降雨和约4米高的[[风暴潮|风暴潮]]，有60套民房受到破坏<ref name="dpa98">{{cite news | agency = Deutsche Presse Agentur | date = 2004-09-08 | title = Hurricane Ivan Kills 24 on Grenada | publisher = ReliefWeb | accessdate = 2014-04-30 | url = http://reliefweb.int/report/aruba-netherlands/hurricane-ivan-kills-24-grenada | archivedate = 2013-11-14 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131114134117/http://reliefweb.int/report/aruba-netherlands/hurricane-ivan-kills-24-grenada | deadurl = no}}</ref>。玛格丽塔岛上出现的持续风速最强，达到每小时42公里，[[法尔孔州|法尔孔州]]、[[瓦尔加斯州|瓦尔加斯州]]、[[阿拉瓜州|阿拉瓜州]]、[[卡拉波波州|卡拉波波州]]和[[安索阿特吉州|安索阿特吉州]]受到的影响最为严重<ref name="vez910">{{cite news | publisher = Ministario del Poder Popular para la Comunicación y la Información, Gobierno Boliariano de Venezuela | date = 2004-09-10 | title = Cinco muertos y 127 damnificados dejó huracán Iván}}</ref>。汹涌的海浪令十艘船只倾覆，多个海滩被迫封闭，有一人在冲浪时淹死<ref>{{cite web | publisher = Europe Press | date = 2004-09-09 | title = El huracán Iván provoca un muerto en la costa de Venezuela | accessdate = 2014-01-07 | url = http://www.lukor.com/not-por/0409/09043720.htm | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140107083304/http://www.lukor.com/not-por/0409/09043720.htm | archivedate = 2014-01-07 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。内陆地区还有两人因河流泛滥溺水而死<ref name="eu99" />，[[卡拉卡斯|卡拉卡斯]]附近另有一人死于强风导致的墙壁倒塌<ref name="afp99" />。风雨交加之下，共有21套房屋被毁，还掀开了多个屋顶，1376人受到影响，其中80人无家可归。此外还出现了停电和缺水的报道<ref name="eu99">{{cite web | date = 2004-09-09 | title = Cuatro muertes en Venezuela por paso de Iván | accessdate = 2011-07-16 | url = http://www.el-universal.com/2004/09/09/pol_ava_09A491145.shtml | work = El Universal (Caracas) | archivedate = 2011-07-16 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20110716190943/http://www.el-universal.com/2004/09/09/pol_ava_09A491145.shtml}}</ref>。整场飓风共造成该国127人受伤<ref name="vez910" />，报告的死亡人数则在3<ref name="wmo" />到5人不等<ref name="vez910" />。起初还有新闻报道称近海的三艘船上共有28人失踪<ref name="dpa98" />。

多巴哥出现了时速74公里的中等强度阵风<ref name="wmo" />，刮倒了一些树木，导致7个村庄停电<ref name="trini98" />，超过30%的地区失去了电力供应<ref name="ifrc910" />。该岛有20个村庄受到不同程度和形式的破坏，至少45户民宅失去了屋顶<ref name="tcr" />。飓风直接对当地近千人产生影响，致使22人流离失所<ref name="wmo" />，还有一位女性因被风刮倒的树木砸中导致伤重不治<ref name="afp99">{{cite web | author = Agence France-Presse | year = 2004 | title = Hurricane Ivan kills at least 14 in Caribbean | accessdate = 2014-05-01 | archivedate = 2012-09-15 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120915022736/http://reliefweb.int/report/aruba-netherlands/hurricane-ivan-kills-least-14-caribbean | url = http://reliefweb.int/report/aruba-netherlands/hurricane-ivan-kills-least-14-caribbean | publisher = Reliefweb | deadurl = no}}</ref>。海浪估计高达20米<ref name="trini98">{{cite web | author = Earl Manmohan | date = 2004-09-08 | title = Tobago Suffers Blows | work = Trinidad Express | accessdate = 2014-05-01 | archivedate = 2013-09-23 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20130923035019/http://www.trinidadandtobagonews.com/forum/webbbs_config.pl/noframes/read/2376 | url = http://www.trinidadandtobagonews.com/forum/webbbs_config.pl?md=read;id=2376 | deadurl = no}}</ref>，导致至少一幢房屋倒塌并掉落大海<ref name="tcr" />。非正式统计数据显示，岛上在风暴经过期间的降雨量最高达到411毫米<ref name="tcr" />，引发了几起[[泥石流|泥石流]]。加上此外出现的一些农作物受损的报道，多巴哥受到的总体损失约为490万美元（2004年美元，相当于{{Formatprice|{{Inflation|US|4900000|2004|r=2}}}}{{#time:Y}}年美元）<ref name="wmo" />，不过与其邻近的特立尼达岛受到的破坏很小<ref name="ifrc98" />。

仍在继续增强的飓风伊万于9月9日在[[ABC群岛|ABC群岛]]以北约130公里处经过，狂风吹飞了屋顶的瓦片，还产生了强烈的涌浪，多个沿海设施受到影响。风暴中逐渐发展的[[雨带|螺旋状雨带]]给阿鲁巴带去了倾盆大雨，导致了洪灾和价值110万美元（2004年美元，相当于{{Formatprice|{{Inflation|US|1100000|2004|r=2}}}}{{#time:Y}}年美元）的结构性破坏。<ref>{{cite web|archivedate=2012-06-17 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120617005124/http://www.meteo.an/Include/Climate2/documents/HurricanesandTropicalStorms.pdf |url=http://www.meteo.an/Include/Climate2/documents/HurricanesandTropicalStorms.pdf |title=Hurricanes and tropical storms in the Netherlands Antilles and Aruba |format=PDF |page=19 |publisher=Meteorological Service of the Netherlands Antilles and Aruba |accessdate=2014-05-01 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web | archivedate = 2014-04-30 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140430163510/http://www.trouw.nl/tr/nl/4324/nieuws/archief/article/detail/1756925/2004/09/10/Orkaan-Ivan-gedraagt-zich-op-Cura-ccedil-ao-als-gewone-storm.dhtml | work = Trouw | deadurl = no | url = http://www.trouw.nl/tr/nl/4324/nieuws/archief/article/detail/1756925/2004/09/10/Orkaan-Ivan-gedraagt-zich-op-Cura-ccedil-ao-als-gewone-storm.dhtml | title = Orkaan Ivan gedraagt zich op Curaçao als gewone storm | date = 2004-09-10 | accessdate = 2014-05-01 | publisher = De Persgroep Nederland}}</ref>

=== 格林纳达 ===
[[Image:Grenada_ivan.jpg|right]]
飓风伊万从格林纳达之南近海掠过，给岛上带去了狂风，[[萨林斯角国际机场|萨林斯角国际机场]]测得的持续风速达到每小时195公里，阵风时速更高达215公里，风暴途经期间的降雨量达到134毫米<ref name="wmo" />。该国的六个[[格林纳达行政区划|区]]中偏南面的四个所受打击最为严重，这四个区中包含全国80%的人口<ref name="usaid">{{cite web | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120210170953/http://www.usaid.gov/locations/latin_america_caribbean/environment/IEEs/grenada/IEE04_76.pdf | author = John Wilson | year = 2004 | title = Hurricane Ivan Grenada Community Revitalization | publisher = United States Agency for International Development | accessdate = 2012-02-10 | deadurl = yes | archivedate = 2012-02-10 | url = http://www.usaid.gov/locations/latin_america_caribbean/environment/IEEs/grenada/IEE04_76.pdf | format = PDF}}</ref>。

强风对格林纳达的超过1.4万个家庭造成影响<ref name="tcr" />，全国90%的房屋受损，其中30%被彻底摧毁<ref name="usaid331" />。[[首都|首都]][[圣乔治_(格林纳达)|圣乔治]]受到严重破坏<ref name="tcr" />，所有的主要建筑不是受损就是被毁<ref name="ifrc911" />。整个岛上85%的建筑物在伊万途经期间受损或被毁<ref>{{cite web | author = Direct Relief International | year = 2006 | title = Grenada: Direct Relief's programme activities update Jun 2006 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131017202828/http://reliefweb.int/report/grenada/grenada-direct-reliefs-programme-activities-update-jun-2006 | publisher = ReliefWeb | accessdate = 2014-05-01 | archivedate = 2013-10-17 | deadurl = no | url = http://reliefweb.int/report/grenada/grenada-direct-reliefs-programme-activities-update-jun-2006}}</ref>，其中包括该国的紧急行动中心<ref name="reut97" />和一幢建于17世纪的[[监狱|监狱]]，许多囚犯因此得以在风暴来袭期间短暂逃离<ref name="tcr" />。全国有75所小学或中学受损或被毁，仅有两所仍然可以展开教学工作<ref name="dpi930">{{cite web | author = United Nations Department of Public Information | year = 2005 | title = Grenada Rebuilds | publisher = ReliefWeb | accessdate = 2014-05-01 | archivedate = 2013-10-14 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131014111705/http://reliefweb.int/report/grenada/grenada-rebuilds | deadurl = no | url = http://reliefweb.int/report/grenada/grenada-rebuilds}}</ref>。整个格林纳达的电力和自来水供应全部中断<ref name="usaid331">{{cite web | author = United States Agency for International Development | year = 2005 | title = Grenada Hurricane Ivan Reconstruction Program Quarterly Report 31 Mar 2005 | publisher = ReliefWeb | accessdate = 2014-05-01 | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2014-05-01 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140501022757/http://reliefweb.int/report/grenada/grenada-hurricane-ivan-reconstruction-program-quarterly-report-31-mar-2005 | url = http://reliefweb.int/report/grenada/grenada-hurricane-ivan-reconstruction-program-quarterly-report-31-mar-2005}}</ref>。

[[旅游业|旅游业]]也受到负面影响，估计有60%的酒店房间受损<ref name="dpi930" />。狂风吹倒了岛上80%的[[肉豆蔻|肉豆蔻]]<ref name="tcr" />，其它农作物损失在60%到90%不等<ref name="dpi930" />。估计有1.8万人在这场飓风过后流离失所，还有约700人受伤<ref name="usaid331" />。伊万是继[[1955年大西洋飓风季|1955年]]的[[飓风珍妮特|飓风珍妮特]]以来对格林纳达破坏最为严重的飓风<ref name="tcr" />，该国共有39人遇难，经济损失达11亿美元（2004年美元，相当于{{Formatprice|{{Inflation|US|1100000000|2004|r=2}}}}{{#time:Y}}年美元）<ref name="wmo" />，相当于其两年的[[国内生产总值|国内生产总值]]<ref name="dpi930" />，其中约45%属于房屋毁损<ref name="usaid" />。

=== 圣文森特和格林纳丁斯、巴巴多斯和其他地区 ===
圣文森特和格林纳丁斯沿岸部分区域的浪高达到6米<ref name="reut97" />，冲毁了两套民房<ref name="tcr" />，风暴潮摧毁了19户家宅，另有40套受损<ref name="ifrc98" />。[[尤宁岛|尤宁岛]]（{{lang|en|Union Island}}）上有一家医院的屋顶受损，该岛北部受到海浪的严重破坏<ref name="bbc97" />。棕榈岛（{{lang|en|Palm Island}}）和[[卡里亚库和小马提尼克|卡里亚库和小马提尼克]]也受到了中等程度的破坏<ref name="ifrc910" />。岛上超过三分之二的区域因狂风导致停电<ref name="tcr" />，[[香蕉|香蕉]]作物也受到破坏<ref>{{cite news | author = Eric Green | date = 2004-09-16 | title = World Bank Join U.S. in Aiding Caribbean Hurricane Victims; Aid will go to nations hit by Hurricanes Frances, Ivan | publisher = Washington File}}</ref>。该国共计遭受了4000万美元的损失（2004年美元，相当于{{Formatprice|{{Inflation|US|40000000|2004|r=2}}}}{{#time:Y}}年美元）<ref name="tcr" />。

巴巴多斯出现的[[最大持续风速|最大持续风速]]为每小时112公里，阵风时速达到149公里<ref name="wmo" />，许多树木和屋顶受损<ref name="ap97" />。共计有531套房屋受损<ref name="gob113">{{cite web | author = Government of Barbados | year = 2006 | title = Barbados: Hurricane Ivan housing recovery project winding-up | publisher = ReliefWeb | accessdate = 2014-05-01 | url = http://reliefweb.int/report/barbados/barbados-hurricane-ivan-housing-recovery-project-winding | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120919235729/http://reliefweb.int/report/barbados/barbados-hurricane-ivan-housing-recovery-project-winding | archivedate = 2012-09-19 | deadurl = no}}</ref>，其中43套被完全摧毁<ref name="wmo" />。此外还有4间酒店受到一定程度的破坏<ref name="hotel">{{cite news | author = Kitty Bean Yancey | date = 2004-09-17 | title = Grenada, Grand Cayman, Grand Bahama feel the pain | work = USA Today | accessdate = 2014-05-01 | deadurl = no | url = http://usatoday30.usatoday.com/travel/news/2004-09-16-island-by-island_x.htm | archivedate = 2014-05-01 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140501032125/http://usatoday30.usatoday.com/travel/news/2004-09-16-island-by-island_x.htm}}</ref>。岛上大部分区域停电<ref name="reut97" />，但在政府部门的迅速响应下很快就得以恢复<ref name="ifrc98" />。风暴带来的降水较少，大部分地区降雨量不足25毫米<ref name="wmo" />。风暴潮和海浪导致海岸受到侵蚀，大部分沿海公路严重受损。该国有一人丧生<ref name="tcr" />，受到的损失估计超过500万美元（2004年美元，相当于{{Formatprice|{{Inflation|US|5000000|2004|r=2}}}}{{#time:Y}}年美元）<ref name="wmo" />。

圣卢西亚南部沿海地区因强风和大浪而出现中等程度破坏，多套房屋受损，香蕉作物也受到损失<ref name="wmo" />，还出现了一些屋顶受到轻度破坏的报道<ref name="ifrc98" />。全岛受到经济损失共计约为260万美元（2004年美元，相当于{{Formatprice|{{Inflation|US|2600000|2004|r=2}}}}{{#time:Y}}年美元）<ref name="tcr" />，还有三人因飓风受到重伤，不过幸运的是没有出现人员伤亡<ref name="bbc97" />。[[多米尼克|多米尼克]]出现的风速达到每小时69公里<ref name="tcr" />。[[马提尼克|马提尼克]]和[[瓜德罗普|瓜德罗普]]西南部受到飓风所产生大浪的轻度破坏<ref name="wmo" />。

与其他地区不同的是，[[开曼群岛|开曼群岛]]在飓风伊万来袭前没有中断电力供应。9月12日凌晨1:40左右，供电系统完全瘫痪。发电厂和供电线路都受到破坏，其中输电线路、配电站和配电网受到的影响较为严重。此外，距海岸457米的海底电缆也受到了破坏。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.eclac.cl/portofspain/noticias/paginas/0/34530/L.25rev1-Part2.pdf | title = The Impact of the Hurricane | page = 33 | accessdate = 2014-05-01 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140502032543/http://www.eclac.cl/portofspain/noticias/paginas/0/34530/L.25rev1-Part2.pdf | archivedate = 2014-05-02 }}</ref>

== 善后 ==
