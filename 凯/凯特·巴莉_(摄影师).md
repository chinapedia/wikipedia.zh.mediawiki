'''凯特·巴莉''' (1967年4月8日 – 2013年11月11日)，是英国籍的时尚摄影师，她为[[Vogue|Vogue]]和[[周日时代杂志|周日时代杂志]]工作过。

== 生平 ==
巴里是英国女演员和歌手[[简·柏金|简·柏金]]和作曲家[[约翰·巴里|约翰·巴里]]的女儿。 她的外婆是演员[[朱迪·坎贝尔|朱迪·坎贝尔]]，舅舅是导演[[安德鲁·柏金|安德鲁·柏金]]。 还小的时候从[[英国|英国]]搬到了[[法国|法国]]，由她的母亲和[[赛日·甘斯布|赛日·甘斯布]]抚养长大。<ref name="independent">{{Cite news|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/obituaries/kate-barry-photographer-9004324.html|title=Kate Barry: Photographer - Obituaries|date=13 December 2013|work=[[The_Independent|The Independent]]|accessdate=2013-12-16|location=London|author=Pierre Perrone}}</ref> 童年时她与亲生父亲并无联系并且直到13岁一直相信亲生父亲已经死了。当她母亲和赛日·甘斯布分手时生父才与她建立联系。<ref name="mail">{{Cite web|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/tvshowbiz/article-2522965/Drink-drugs-decadent-childhood-As-tributes-pour-following-tragic-death-Kate-Barry-reveal-troubled-life-Jane-Birkins-girl.html|title=Drink, drugs and a decadent childhood: The troubled life of Jane Birkin's daughter Kate Barry who this week plunged to her death from her Paris apartment|accessdate=2015-09-21|date=2013-12-13|work=Daily Mail (U.K.)|last1=Levy|first1=Geoffrey}}</ref>

她有两个同母异父的妹妹，法国演员和歌手[[夏洛特·甘斯布|夏洛特·甘斯布]]和歌手 {{link-en|卢·多利伦|Lou Doillon}}。 她有两个同父异母的姐妹Suzy 和 Sian 还有一个兄弟 Jon-Patrick.。她的儿子是Roman de Kermadec。

青年时巴里吸食毒品成瘾。在戒除之后她在巴黎建立了一个戒毒中心。<ref name="mail">{{Cite web|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/tvshowbiz/article-2522965/Drink-drugs-decadent-childhood-As-tributes-pour-following-tragic-death-Kate-Barry-reveal-troubled-life-Jane-Birkins-girl.html|title=Drink, drugs and a decadent childhood: The troubled life of Jane Birkin's daughter Kate Barry who this week plunged to her death from her Paris apartment|accessdate=2015-09-21|date=2013-12-13|work=Daily Mail (U.K.)|last1=Levy|first1=Geoffrey}}</ref>

== 事业 ==
巴里以她为名人拍摄的亲密的写真而闻名。<ref name="wwd.com">{{Cite web|url=http://www.wwd.com/fashion-news/fashion-features/photographer-kate-barry-dead-at-46-7311601?src=nl/mornReport/20131213|title=Photographer Kate Barry Dead at 46|accessdate=13 December 2013|author=Diderich, Joelle|date=13 December 2013|publisher=WWD}}</ref> 她经常与母亲和妹妹们工作，并且拍摄了卡拉·布鲁尼《[[有人告诉我|有人告诉我]]》的专辑封面。<ref name="independent">{{Cite news|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/obituaries/kate-barry-photographer-9004324.html|title=Kate Barry: Photographer - Obituaries|date=13 December 2013|work=[[The_Independent|The Independent]]|accessdate=2013-12-16|location=London|author=Pierre Perrone}}</ref>

== 死亡 ==
2013年11月11日，巴里在自己位于巴黎16号区的公寓从4楼坠楼而死，外界猜测为自杀。<ref name="mail">{{Cite web|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/tvshowbiz/article-2522965/Drink-drugs-decadent-childhood-As-tributes-pour-following-tragic-death-Kate-Barry-reveal-troubled-life-Jane-Birkins-girl.html|title=Drink, drugs and a decadent childhood: The troubled life of Jane Birkin's daughter Kate Barry who this week plunged to her death from her Paris apartment|accessdate=2015-09-21|date=2013-12-13|work=Daily Mail (U.K.)|last1=Levy|first1=Geoffrey}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.standard.co.uk/news/world/frances-culture-minister-pays-tribute-to-british-photographer-kate-barry-8999772.html|title=France's culture minister pays tribute to British photographer Kate Barry|date=2013-12-12|work=Evening Standard (London)|accessdate=2013-12-12|last1=Allen|first1=Peter}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.standard.co.uk/news/world/kate-barry-photographer-daughter-of-jane-birkin-and-bond-film-composer-john-barry-dies-in-fall-from-8999474.html|title=Kate Barry, photographer daughter of Jane Birkin and Bond film composer John Barry, dies in fall from Paris apartment|date=2013-12-12|work=Evening Standard (London)|last2=Allen|first2=Peter|accessdate=2013-12-12|last1=Randhawa|first1=Kiran}}</ref> 她葬于巴黎。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://wwd.com/fashion-news/fashion-features/kate-barry-laid-to-rest-in-paris-7321031/|title=Kate Barry Laid to Rest in Paris|accessdate=2013-12-20|date=2013-12-19|last1=Diderich|first1=Joelle|website=WWD}}</ref>
