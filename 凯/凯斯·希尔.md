'''基夫·約翰·希路'''（{{lang-en|'''Keith John Hill'''}}，{{bd|1969年|5月17日|catidx=Hill, Keith}}）生於[[英格蘭|英格蘭]][[大曼徹斯特郡|大曼徹斯特郡]][[博尔顿|博尔顿]]，是一名前職業足球員，司職[[後衛_(足球)|中堅]]，退役後擔任[[教练|領隊]]，曾任教[[英冠|英冠]]的[[巴恩斯利足球俱乐部|班士利]]，現時執教[[英甲|英甲]]球會[[罗奇代尔足球俱乐部|羅奇代爾]]。

==生平==
===球員===
Hill began his career with [[Blackburn_Rovers_F.C.|Blackburn Rovers]], moving on to [[Plymouth_Argyle_F.C.|Plymouth Argyle]] in [[1992-93_in_English_football|1992]] in a joint deal with [[Craig_Skinner|Craig Skinner]], with [[Nicky_Marker|Nicky Marker]] moving in the opposite direction.

Hill spent four years with Plymouth, moving on to [[Rochdale_A.F.C.|Rochdale]] in [[1996-97_in_English_football|1996]]. After five years at [[Spotland|Spotland]], he joined [[Cheltenham_Town_F.C.|Cheltenham Town]], but he only spent a year there, playing only five games, punctuated by a two-month loan with [[Wrexham_A.F.C.|Wrexham]]. He spent a year with [[Nationwide_Conference|Conference]] side [[Morecambe_F.C.|Morecambe]], before retiring in [[2002-03_in_English_football|2003]].
