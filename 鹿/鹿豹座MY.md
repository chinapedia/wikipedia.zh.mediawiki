'''鹿豹座MY'''（'''MY Camelopardalis'''，縮寫'''MY Cam'''），是位於[[疏散星團|疏散星團]][[阿里坎特1|阿里坎特1]]（Alicante 1）內的一組[[聯星|聯星]]，在天球上位於[[鹿豹座|鹿豹座]]，距離地球約{{convert|13000|ly|pc|abbr=on|lk=on}}。它是已知質量最巨大的聯星系統之一<ref name=NatGeo-2014-12-09> {{cite news |url= http://news.nationalgeographic.com/news/2014/12/141209-starstruck-monster-star-merger-space-astronomy-science/ |title= Astronomers Spy Monster Star Merger on the Move |author= Andrew Fazekas |date= 9 December 2014 }} </ref>。鹿豹座MY是星團阿里坎特1中最明亮的成員星<ref name=ScienceDaily-2014-12-05> {{cite news |url= http://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2014/12/141205093754.htm |title= Astronomers observe two stars so close to each other that they will end up merging into a supermassive star |date= 5 December 2014 |publisher= ScienceDaily |author= Asociación RUVID }} </ref>。

==概要==
鹿豹座MY由兩顆質量分別為32和38倍[[太陽質量|太陽質量]]的恆星組成<ref name="Phys.org-2014-12-06"> {{cite web |url= http://phys.org/news/2014-12-astronomers-stars-merging-supermassive-star.html |title= Astronomers observe two stars so close to each other that they will end up merging into a supermassive star |date= 6 December 2014 |publisher= Phys.org |accessdate= 6 December 2014 }} </ref>，是[[密接聯星|密接聯星]]，也是[[食雙星|食雙星]]。兩者的軌道週期為1.2日，軌道速度為1000000 km/h<ref name=NatGeo-2014-12-09/>。這兩顆恆星大約在200萬年前形成<ref name=Independent141210> {{cite web |url= http://www.independent.co.uk/news/science/two-huge-stars-are-colliding-in-space--and-might-soon-join-in-a-huge-explosion-that-will-shed-light-on-the-beginning-of-the-universe-9915875.html |title= Two huge stars are colliding in space — and might soon join in a huge explosion that will shed light on the beginning of the universe |date= 10 December 2014 |publisher= [[The_Independent|The Independent]] |accessdate= 10 December 2014 }} </ref><ref name="10.1051/0004-6361/201424345"> {{cite journal|url=http://www.aanda.org/10.1051/0004-6361/201424345|title=MY Camelopardalis, a very massive merger progenitor|journal=Astronomy & Astrophysics|volume=572|date=2014-12-01|issn=0004-6361|language=en|accessdate=2018-04-02|doi=10.1051/0004-6361/201424345|author=J. Lorenzo, I. Negueruela, A. K. F. Val Baker, M. García, S. Simón-Díaz, P. Pastor, M. Méndez Majuelos}} </ref>。

鹿豹座MY的演化趨勢將是兩顆恆星合併為一顆質量達到太陽60倍的巨大恆星。這被認為是極高質量的單一恆星行成的其中一個方式<ref name=NatGeo-2014-12-09/>。

天文學家最初發現鹿豹座MY時把它當成單一恆星的[[變星|變星]]，而非聯星系統<ref name=NatGeo-2014-12-09/>。2004年時鹿豹座MY才被確認是聯星系統<ref name=ScienceDaily-2014-12-05/>。
