'''頭狀骨'''（'''capitate bone'''）是人類[[掌骨|掌骨]]中最大的一塊，為腕骨中央的骨頭。較圓的一端位於[[手舟骨|手舟骨]]及[[月骨|月骨]]所組成的凹槽上<ref name="Gray's">''[[Gray's_Anatomy|Gray's Anatomy]]'' (1918).  See infobox.</ref>。

許多[[哺乳類|哺乳類]]動物身上也可以找到這塊骨頭，與[[爬蟲類|爬蟲類]]及[[兩棲類|兩棲類]]身上的第三遠端腕骨為[[同源|同源]]構造。

==構造==
頭狀骨是腕骨中最大的一塊<ref name=EATHORNE2005>{{cite journal|last=Eathorne|first=SW|title=The wrist: clinical anatomy and physical examination--an update.|journal=Primary care|date=Mar 2005|volume=32|issue=1|pages=17–33|pmid=15831311|doi=10.1016/j.pop.2004.11.009}}</ref>，位於腕骨遠端列。頭狀骨的旁邊接的是無名指掌骨，，尺骨側連接的是[[鉤骨|鉤骨]]，橈側連接[[三角骨|三角骨]]，腕側結月骨跟手舟骨。<ref name=GRAYS2005>{{cite book|last=Drake|first=Richard L.|title=Gray's anatomy for students|year=2005|publisher=Elsevier/Churchill Livingstone|location=Philadelphia|isbn=978-0-8089-2306-0|author2=Vogl, Wayne |author3=Tibbitts, Adam W.M. Mitchell  |author4=illustrations by Richard |author5= Richardson, Paul }}</ref>{{rp|708–709}}

===表面===
The ''superior surface'' is round, smooth, and articulates with the [[lunate_bone|lunate bone]].<ref name="Gray's" />

The ''inferior surface'' is divided by two ridges into three facets, for articulation with the second, third, and fourth [[metacarpal_bone|metacarpal bone]]s, that for the third being the largest.<ref name="Gray's" />

The ''dorsal surface'' is broad and rough.<ref name="Gray's" />

The ''palmar surface'' is narrow, rounded, and rough, for the attachment of ligaments and a part of the [[adductor_pollicis_muscle|adductor pollicis muscle]].<ref name="Gray's" />

The ''lateral surface'' articulates with the lesser multangular by a small facet at its anterior inferior angle, behind which is a rough depression for the attachment of an [[Interosseous_intercarpal_ligaments|interosseous ligament]]. Above this is a deep, rough groove, forming part of the neck, and serving for the attachment of ligaments; it is bounded superiorly by a smooth, convex surface, for articulation with the [[scaphoid_bone|scaphoid bone]].<ref name="Gray's" />

The ''medial surface'' articulates with the [[hamate_bone|hamate bone]] by a smooth, concave, oblong facet, which occupies its posterior and superior parts; it is rough in front, for the attachment of an interosseous ligament.<ref name="Gray's" />

===變形===
The capitate bone variably articulates with the metacarpal of the index finger.<ref name=EATHORNE2005 />

頭狀骨的骨化始於1至5個月大時。<ref name=CHLT>{{cite journal|last1=Balachandran|first1=Ajay|last2=Kartha|first2=Moumitha|last3=Krishna|first3=Anooj|last4=Thomas|first4=Jerry|last5=K|first5=Prathilash|last6=TN|first6=Prem|last7=GK|first7=Libu|last8=B|first8=Krishnan|last9=John|first9=Liza|title=A Study of Ossification of Capitate, Hamate, Triquetral & Lunate in Forensic Age Estimation|journal=Indian Journal of Forensic Medicine & Toxicology|date=2014|volume=8|issue=2|pages=218–224|doi=10.5958/0973-9130.2014.00720.8|url=http://www.indianjournals.com/ijor.aspx?target=ijor:ijfmt&volume=8&issue=2&article=052|accessdate=18 August 2014|issn=0973-9130}}</ref>

==功能==
為構成腕骨的一部分。<ref name=GRAYS2005 />{{rp|708}}

==歷史==
辭源來自於[[拉丁文|拉丁文]]的「頭」（''{{lang-la|capitātus}}''）。<ref>{{cite web|last=Harper|first=Douglas|title=Capitate|url=http://www.etymonline.com/index.php?allowed_in_frame=0&search=capitate&searchmode=none|work=Online Etymology Dictionary|accessdate=5 January 2014}}</ref>
