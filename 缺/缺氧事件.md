'''缺氧事件'''或'''大洋缺氧事件'''指的是海洋中[[氧氣|氧氣]]缺乏的一段時期，期間海水中的[[硫化氫|硫化氫]]（{{chem|H|2|S}}）含量大幅增加<ref>{{cite journal|authors= Timothy W. Lyons, Ariel D. Anbar, Silke Severmann, Clint Scott, and Benjamin C. Gill|title= Tracking Euxinia in the Ancient Ocean: A Multiproxy Perspective and Proterozoic Case Study|journal= Annual Review of Earth and Planetary Sciences|date= January 19, 2009|volume=37|pages=507–53|doi=10.1146/annurev.earth.36.031207.124233|url= http://www.annualreviews.org/doi/abs/10.1146/annurev.earth.36.031207.124233|accessdate= April 11, 2014|bibcode = 2009AREPS..37..507L }}</ref>。它在地質史上曾多次發生，同時伴隨而來的是[[生物集群灭绝|生物集群灭绝]]<ref>{{cite journal|last= Wignall|first=Paul B.|author2=Richard J. Twitchett|title=Oceanic Anoxia and the End Permian Mass Extinction|journal=Science|date=24 May 1996|volume= 272|series= 5265|pages= 1155–1158|doi=10.1126/science.272.5265.1155|url=http://www.sciencemag.org/content/272/5265/1155.short|accessdate=12 September 2011|issue=5265|pmid=8662450|bibcode = 1996Sci...272.1155W }}</ref>。因此[[生物地層學|生物地層學]]上將缺氧時間當成時間劃分的一個重要指標<ref>{{cite book|author= Peters, Walters |author2=Modowan K.E. |title= The Biomarker Guide, Volume 2: Biomarkers and Isotopes in the Petroleum Exploration and Earth History|year=2005|publisher=Cambridge University Press|isbn= 978-0-521-83762-0|pages=749|url= https://books.google.com/books?id=qk8anEDZykwC&pg=PA749&lpg=PA749&dq=Anoxic+OCEAN+caused+by+greenhouse+gases#v=onepage&q=Anoxic%20OCEAN%20caused%20by%20greenhouse%20gases&f=false}}</ref>。它的成因可能是氣候變暖、溫室氣體增加及洋流速度變緩等，而釋放出大量二氧化碳的[[火山作用|火山作用]]是其主要外部成因<ref name="Kump 2008">
