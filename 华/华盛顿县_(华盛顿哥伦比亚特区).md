'''华盛顿县'''曾是[[哥伦比亚特区|哥伦比亚特区]]内的行政区划之一，范围包括华盛顿特区内，来自[[马里兰州|马里兰州]]的地区除去华盛顿市外的剩余部分，主要城市有[[乔治城_(华盛顿哥伦比亚特区)|乔治城]]等<ref>{{cite book |title= Washington Past and Present |editor= Proctor, John Clogett |publisher= Lewis Historical Publishing Company Inc. |year= 1930 |location= New York |volume= 1 |page= 52 }}</ref>。该县于1801年建立，在1871年废除，与华盛顿市合并<ref>{{cite book|last=Wilcox|first=Delos Franklin|title=Great cities in America: their problems and their government|year=1910|publisher=The Macmillan Company|pages=27–30|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=jY4SAAAAIAAJ}}</ref>。而来自弗吉尼亚州的地区则组成了[[阿灵顿县_(弗吉尼亚州)|阿灵顿县]]和[[亚历山德里亚_(弗吉尼亚州)|亚历山德里亚市]]<ref>{{cite journal|last=Richards |first=Mark David |date=Spring–Summer 2004 |title=The Debates over the Retrocession of the District of Columbia, 1801–2004 |journal=Washington History |publisher=Historical Society of Washington, D.C. |pages=54–82 |url=http://www.dcvote.org/pdfs/mdrretro062004.pdf |accessdate=2009-01-16 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090118053203/http://www.dcvote.org/pdfs/mdrretro062004.pdf |archivedate=2009-01-18 }}</ref>。尽管华盛顿县是华盛顿特区的一部分，马里兰州法律仍适用于该县<ref>{{cite web|url=http://memory.loc.gov/cgi-bin/ampage?collId=llsl&fileName=002/llsl002.db&recNum=140 |title=Statutes at Large, 6th Congress, 2nd Session |accessdate=2008-07-10 |work=A Century of Lawmaking for a New Nation: U.S. Congressional Documents and Debates, 1774 - 1875 |publisher=[[Library_of_Congress|Library of Congress]] }}</ref>。
