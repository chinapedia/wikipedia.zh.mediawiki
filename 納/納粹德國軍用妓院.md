'''納粹德國軍用妓院'''（{{lang-de|Wehrmachtsbordell}}），[[二戰|二戰]]時[[德國佔領區|德國佔領區]]內由軍方在民間廣泛設立的妓院，通常由民間旅館或該國既有妓院充公改建而成，至1942年有五百多所軍用妓院。與[[納粹德國集中營妓院|納粹德國集中營妓院]]的女囚合計，共34,140人被逼良為娼。<ref name="Herbermann">{{cite book | author =Nanda Herbermann, Hester Baer, Elizabeth Roberts Baer | title =The Blessed Abyss: Inmate #6582 in Ravensbruck Concentration Camp for Women | year =2000 | editor = | pages =33–34 | publisher =[[Wayne_State_University|Wayne State University]] Press | location =Detroit | isbn=0-8143-2920-9 | url =https://books.google.com/books?id=3JM3AdnmE18C&pg=PA34&dq=%22it+is+impossible+to+speak+of+free+will+when+considering+the+circumstances%22&hl=en&ei=_RwrTYmtIov2tgPcpd3XBw&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CCYQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q=%22it%20is%20impossible%20to%20speak%20of%20free%20will%20when%20considering%20the%20circumstances%22&f=false | format =[[Google_Books|Google Books]] | accessdate = }}</ref>其中波蘭的軍用妓院，妓女大多從街上強擄而得。在法國的軍用妓院非常制度化，1941年，德國陸軍總司令[[瓦爾特·馮·布勞希奇|馮·布勞希奇]]提議年青士兵每周需有強制健康檢查已防止性疾病。法國妓女亦有定期的健康檢查。<ref name="polityka">Joanna Ostrowska, Marcin Zaremba, [http://archiwum.polityka.pl/art/do-burdelu-marsz,424445.html  "Do burdelu, marsz!" (Marching on to the brothel).] [[Polityka|Polityka]] magazine, No 22 (2707), May 30, 2009; pp. 70-72. {{pl icon}}</ref>
