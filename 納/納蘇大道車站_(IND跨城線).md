'''納蘇大道車站'''（{{lang-en|Nassau Avenue}}）是[[紐約地鐵|紐約地鐵]][[IND跨城線|IND跨城線]]的一個[[地鐵站|地鐵站]]，位於[[布魯克林|布魯克林]]{{link-en|綠點 (布魯克林)|Greenpoint, Brooklyn|綠點}}曼哈頓及納蘇大道交界，設有[[紐約地鐵G線|G線]]（任何時候停站）列車服務。

此站在1933年8月19日啟用，是IND跨城線第一期通車時的總站<ref name=NYTimes-INDQBLCrstwnOpen-1933>{{cite web|title=TWO SUBWAY UNITS OPEN AT MIDNIGHT; Links in City-Owned System in Queens and Brooklyn to Have 15 Stations.|url=https://query.nytimes.com/mem/archive/pdf?res=9E0CEFDE1231EF3ABC4052DFBE668388629EDE|publisher=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|accessdate=November 7, 2015|date=August 18, 1933}}</ref>。此站到1937年7月1日路線其餘部分通車後才不再是總站<ref name=":1" /><ref name=":0">{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=w8cDPQAACAAJ|title=Building the Independent Subway|last=Kramer|first=Frederick A.|date=January 1, 1990|publisher=Quadrant Press|isbn=9780915276509|language=en}}</ref>。

此地下車站設有兩個[[側式月台|側式月台]]和兩條軌道<ref name="MTA-GLineReview-Append-2013">{{cite web|title=Review of the G Line: Appendices|url=http://web.mta.info/nyct/service/G_Appendices_7_10_13.pdf|publisher=[[Metropolitan_Transportation_Authority|Metropolitan Transportation Authority]]|accessdate=October 28, 2015|date=July 10, 2013}}</ref><ref name="tracks">{{NYCS const|trackref|trackbook3}}</ref>。

車站以北設有剪式橫渡線，容許列車轉換方向<ref name=MTA-GLineReview-Append-2013/><ref name=MTA-GLineReview-2013 /><ref name="tracks" />。此轉轍器一直用至1937年7月1日跨城線其餘部分通車為止<ref name=":1">{{cite news|title=New Crosstown Subway Line Is Opened|url=https://bklyn.newspapers.com/image/52688792/?terms=crosstown%2Bsubway|accessdate=December 24, 2015|publisher=Brooklyn Daily Eagle|date=July 1, 1937}}</ref><ref name=":0" />。在那以前，納蘇大道是路線的南端總站<ref name=NYTimes-INDQBLCrstwnOpen-1933/><ref name=INDServicesChart>{{cite web|title=Independent Subway Services Beginning in 1932|url=http://www.thejoekorner.com/lines/Indhistory.htm|website=thejoekorner.com|accessdate=August 2, 2015|date=August 21, 2013}}</ref><ref name=BklynPaper-GExt-July2012>{{Cite news|first=Natalie |last=O'Neill |url=http://www.brooklynpaper.com/stories/35/29/dtg_gtrainsaved_2012_07_27_bk.html |title=G wiz! MTA plans to save the G train extension! |newspaper=[[The_Brooklyn_Paper|The Brooklyn Paper]] |date=July 19, 2012 |accessdate=July 21, 2012}}</ref>。此站以南，路線由曼哈頓大道轉往聯合大道，並在{{link-en|麥加倫公園|McCarren Park}}下方以對角線通過<ref name=MTA-GreenpointMap-2015 />。
