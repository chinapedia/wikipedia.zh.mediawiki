《'''铃儿响叮当'''》（'''Jingle Bells'''）是全世界最著名<ref>Browne, Ray B. and Browne, Pat. [https://books.google.com/books?id=U3rJxPYT32MC&pg=PA171&dq=%22jingle+bells%22+%22best+known%22 ''The Guide to United States Popular Culture''] Popular Press, 2001. ISBN 0879728213. p.171</ref>并且最经常传唱<ref>Collins, Ace. [https://books.google.com/books?id=Q2xV3ZhqEFsC&q=%22jingle+bells%22+%22best+known%22+%22winter+song%22&dq=%22jingle+bells%22+%22best+known%22+%22winter+song%22 ''Stories Behind the Best-Loved Songs of Christmas''] Zondervan, 2004. ISBN 0310264480. p.104.</ref>的美国冬季歌曲。它的作者是[[James_Lord_Pierpont|James Lord Pierpont]] (1822–1893) 。这首歌发行于1857年秋天，原名One Horse Open Sleigh。虽然它现在一般与[[圣诞节|圣诞节]]联系在一起，但是它原来是用于庆祝[[感恩节|感恩节]]的。<ref>{{cite web|title=Jingle Bells (Christmas Read-Aloud Stories, Carols, & more)|url=http://www.readersdigest.ca/christmas/kind_christmas/jingle_bells.html|publisher=Reader's Digest|accessdate=November 19, 2010|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070927205505/http://www.readersdigest.ca/christmas/kind_christmas/jingle_bells.html|archivedate=九月 27, 2007}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|journal=TIME|url=http://entertainment.time.com/2012/12/17/yule-laugh-yule-cry-10-things-you-didnt-know-about-beloved-holiday-songs/?iid=tl-article-recirc|title=Yule Laugh, Yule Cry: 10 Things You Didn’t Know About Beloved Holiday Songs| author=Wook Kim|date=December 17, 2012}} – [http://entertainment.time.com/2012/12/17/yule-laugh-yule-cry-10-things-you-didnt-know-about-beloved-holiday-songs/?iid=tl-article-recirc "Jingle Bells" (p. 2)]</ref>它曾经被声称是写给Sunday学校合唱团演唱的，但是历史学家对此有争议，因为在那个年代由儿童教堂合唱团来唱為“亵渎”。<ref>{{cite web|title=A Thanksgiving Carol |url=http://www.snopes.com/holidays/christmas/music/jinglebells.asp| publisher=snopes.com |date=December 18, 2014 |accessdate=December 23, 2014}}</ref>
