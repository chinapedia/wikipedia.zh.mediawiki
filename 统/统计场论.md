
'''统计场论'''（'''statistical field theory'''）是以[[场_(物理)|场]]为自由度的[[统计力学|统计力学]]，即体系的微观态以场构型来描述。对于[[高分子|高分子]]体系，统计场论也被称作[[高分子场论|高分子场论]]。统计场论广泛用于描述[[高分子物理|高分子物理]]或[[生物物理学|生物物理学]]体系，比如高分子薄膜、嵌段[[共聚物|共聚物]]<ref>{{cite journal | author= Baeurle SA, Usami T, Gusev AA | title= A new multiscale modeling approach for the prediction of mechanical properties of [[polymer-based_nanomaterials|polymer-based nanomaterials]] | journal=Polymer | year=2006 | volume=47 | pages=8604–8617 | doi=10.1016/j.polymer.2006.10.017 | issue= 26 }}</ref>和[[聚电解质|聚电解质]]<ref>{{cite journal | author= Baeurle SA, Nogovitsin EA | title= Challenging scaling laws of flexible polyelectrolyte solutions with effective renormalization concepts | journal=Polymer | year=2007 | volume=48 | pages=4883–4899 | doi = 10.1016/j.polymer.2007.05.080 | issue = 16 }}</ref>。
