'''迭戈·法布里尼'''（{{lang-it|'''Diego Fabbrini'''}}；{{bd|1990年|7月31日}}）是一位[[意大利|意大利]]足球運動員。在場上的位置是[[中場|中場]]。现效力於[[羅甲|羅甲]]球隊[[博托沙尼足球俱樂部|博托沙尼]]。他也曾代表意大利國家隊和青年足球隊參賽<ref>[http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/19238374 "England 2–1 Italy"] BBC Sport. 15 August 2012. Retrieved 1 August 2013.</ref>。

==球會生涯==
===恩波利===
法布里尼的職業首秀是在2009年8月，首個職業賽季共在[[意乙|意乙]]上場30次及打進1球。

===乌迪内斯===
2010年8月31日，[[乌迪内斯足球俱乐部|乌迪内斯]]以300萬歐元的價錢取得他和[[加比爾·安祖拿|加比爾·安祖拿]]的[[球员共有权|共有權]]<ref name=Empoli2011bilancio>Empoli FC SpA ''bilancio'' (financial report and accounts) on 30 June 2011 {{it icon}}, [http://www.registroimprese.it PDF purchased from Italian CCIAA]</ref>，法布里尼在2010/11球季會留在恩波利<ref name=Empoli2011bilancio/><ref>{{cite news |url=http://messaggeroveneto.gelocal.it/sport/2010/09/01/news/inler-resta-all-udinese-ma-e-promesso-sposo-nerazzurro-1.45329 |title=Inler resta all'Udinese, ma è promesso sposo nerazzurro |language=it |trans-title=Inler stays at Udinese, but he's promised to the ''nerazzurri'' |first=Massimo |last=Meroi |newspaper=Messagero Veneto |location=Udine |date=1 September 2010 |accessdate=27 January 2016}}</ref>。

2011/12球季，他回到乌迪内斯並在8月24日首次代表新東家上場，參加[[2011–12年歐洲冠軍聯賽|2011–12年歐洲冠軍聯賽]]資格賽對[[阿仙奴|阿仙奴]]的賽事。8月31日，球會購下他和[[達尼埃萊·莫里|達尼埃萊·莫里]]餘下的50%擁有權，總計240萬歐元<ref>Empoli FC SpA ''bilancio'' (financial report and accounts) on 30 June 2012 {{it icon}}, [http://www.registroimprese.it PDF purchased from Italian CCIAA]</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.lanazione.it/empoli/videocommenti/calcio/2011/09/01/572802-mori_segue.shtml |title=Mori segue la scia di Fabbrini ma andrà via a giugno |language=it |trans_title=Mori follows on the heels of Fabbrini but will leave in June |newspaper=La Nazione |location=Florence |date=1 September 2011 |accessdate=27 January 2016}}</ref>。2011年9月25日，法布里尼首次在[[意甲|意甲]]上場參加對[[卡利亚里足球俱乐部|卡利亚里]]的賽事。2012年5月2日對[[切塞纳足球俱乐部|切塞纳]]，他打進首顆意甲進球使球隊以1-0取勝。2012年11月，球會與法布里尼續約至2017年<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.udinetoday.it/sport/calciomercato-udinese-diego-fabbrini-rinnova-contratto.html |title=Udinese: Fabbrini rinnova contratto fino al 2017 |language=it |trans-title=Udinese: Fabbrini renews contract up to 2017 |newspaper=Udine Today |date=15 November 2012 |accessdate=27 January 2016}}</ref>。

2013年1月31日，意甲球會[[巴勒莫足球俱樂部|巴勒莫]]宣佈借入法布里尼至2012/13年季未<ref>{{cite news |publisher=U.S. Città di Palermo |language=it |date=31 January 2013 |accessdate=31 January 2013 |url=http://palermocalcio.it/it/1213/news/scheda.php?id=27982 |title=Ancora due acquisti; presi Faurlin e Fabbrini |trans_title=Two more signings; Faurlin and Fabbrini in}}</ref>。

===沃特福德===
2013年7月19日，七名乌迪内斯被轉讓至[[英冠|英冠]]球會[[沃特福德足球俱乐部|沃特福德]]，法布里尼是其中之一並簽約至2017年<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/23374082 |title=Watford confirm permanent signings of seven Udinese players |publisher=BBC Sport |date=19 July 2013 |accessdate=1 August 2013}}</ref>。首秀是8月3日作客伯明翰並以1-0小勝<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/23474781 |title=Birmingham 0–1 Watford |publisher=BBC Sport |date=3 August 2013 |accessdate=12 October 2013}}</ref>。他在英格蘭首顆進球是2013年8月17日以3-3戰平[[雷丁足球俱乐部|雷丁]]的賽事<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/23644826 |title=Reading 3–3 Watford |publisher=BBC Sport |date=17 August 2013 |accessdate=12 October 2013}}</ref>。

====外借期====
2014年1月31日，他被外借到意乙球會[[錫耶納足球俱樂部|錫耶納]]至季末<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.acsiena.it/2014/01/31/in-arrivo-fabbrini-dal-watford/ |title=In arrivo Fabbrini dal Watford |trans_title=Fabbrini arrives from Watford |language=it |publisher=A.C. Siena |date=31 January 2014 |accessdate=1 February 2014 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140201234929/http://www.acsiena.it/2014/01/31/in-arrivo-fabbrini-dal-watford/ |archivedate=1 二月 2014 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。

2015年1月15日，他被外借到同樣是英冠的[[米尔沃尔足球俱乐部|米尔沃尔]]，為期93天<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/30848320 |title=Diego Fabbrini: Millwall sign Watford forward on loan |publisher=BBC Sport |date=16 January 2015 |accessdate=27 January 2016}}</ref>，首場比賽是3-1不敵[[伊普斯维奇城足球俱乐部|伊普斯维奇城]]。在[[伊恩·霍洛韦|伊恩·霍洛韦]]領軍下，法布里尼共先發上場11次及打進1球，但霍洛韦被辭退後只得到數分鐘的表現機會。3月26日，他被母會召回並外借到[[伯明翰城足球俱樂部|伯明翰城]]至季末<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.watfordobserver.co.uk/sport/watfordfc/watfordfcnews/11883405.Fabbrini_joins_Birmingham_on_loan_after_Millwall_stay_cut_short/ |title=Watford forward Diego Fabbrini has joined Birmingham City on loan after his stay at Millwall was cut short |first=Tom |last=Bodell |newspaper=Watford Observer |date=26 March 2015 |accessdate=26 March 2015}}</ref>。

抵達伯明翰初期，法布里尼就在訓練期間撞傷斷鼻而需要休養。在意大利治療完畢後，4月11日對[[伍尔弗汉普顿流浪足球俱乐部|狼隊]]的比賽入替[[安德鲁·希恩尼尔|安德鲁·希恩尼尔]]，比賽期間需戴護罩<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.birminghammail.co.uk/sport/football/football-news/birmingham-city-hopeful-diego-fabbrini-9002218 |title=Birmingham City hopeful of Diego Fabbrini return for Wolves visit |first=Brian |last=Dick |newspaper=Birmingham Mail |date=8 April 2015 |accessdate=12 April 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.birminghammail.co.uk/sport/football/match-reports/updated-birmingham-city-2-wolves-9025905 |title=Updated: Birmingham City 2 Wolves 1 – report and stats as Blues guarantee their safety |first=Brian |last=Dick |newspaper=Birmingham Mail |date=11 April 2015 |accessdate=12 April 2015}}</ref>。外借期間共上場5次。

2015年7月27日，他外借加盟[[米德尔斯堡足球俱乐部|米德尔斯堡]]至2015/16季末<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.mfc.co.uk/news/article/2014/middlesbrough-fc-boro-deigo-fabbrini-watford-2574257.aspx |title=Confirmed: Italian midfielder Diego Fabbrini completes season long loan move from Watford |publisher=Middlesbrough F.C. |date=27 July 2015 |accessdate=27 January 2016}}</ref>。雖然合約條款中沒有中途召回的權限，但米德尔斯堡在2016年1月從[[南安普顿足球俱乐部|南安普顿]]借入[[加斯頓·拉美利斯|加斯頓·拉美利斯]]後，雙方同意終止法布里尼的租借合同<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.gazettelive.co.uk/sport/football/transfer-news/diego-fabbrini-verge-signing-birmingham-10795028 |title=Diego Fabbrini on verge of signing for Birmingham City after Boro end loan spell |first=Anthony |last=Vickers |newspaper=Evening Gazette |location=Middlesbrough |date=27 January 2016 |accessdate=27 January 2016}}</ref>。

===伯明翰===
2016年1月27日，法布里尼與伯明翰簽下三年半合約，轉會費為150萬鎊，是球會近5年來最昂貴的收購<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.bcfc.com/news/article/diego-fabbrini-birmingham-city-2923887.aspx |title=Fabb signing for Blues |publisher=Birmingham City F.C. |date=27 January 2016 |accessdate=27 January 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.birminghammail.co.uk/sport/football/transfer-news/diego-fabbrini-what-gary-rowett-10788258 |title=Diego Fabbrini: What Gary Rowett had to say when asked about Birmingham City's interest in the playmaker |first=Mat |last=Kendrick |newspaper=Birmingham Mail |date=26 January 2016 |accessdate=27 January 2016}}</ref>。3天後對[[布里斯托尔城足球俱乐部|布里斯托尔城]]的比賽，他首度再次代表伯明翰上場<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.birminghammail.co.uk/sport/football/match-reports/bristol-city-0-birmingham-city-10812017 |title=Bristol City 0 Birmingham City 0 |first=Brian |last=Dick |newspaper=Birmingham Mail |date=30 January 2016 |accessdate=30 January 2016}}</ref>。

==國家隊生涯==
2012年8月15日，法布里尼首次披上[[意大利國家足球隊|意大利國家足球隊]]的球衣參加對[[英格蘭足球代表隊|英格蘭]]的友誼賽，他在完場前6分鐘入替，最終以2-1戰敗完場<ref>[http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/19238374 "England 2–1 Italy"] BBC Sport. 15 August 2012. Retrieved 1 August 2013.</ref>。
