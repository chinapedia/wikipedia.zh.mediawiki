'''葛倫·愛德華·葛林華德'''（{{lang-en|Glenn Edward Greenwald}}，{{bd|1967年|3月6日}}），美國[[律師|律師]]、[[新聞從業員|新聞從業人員]]與作家。2012年8月至2013年10月間，曾任《{{tsl|en|Guardian US|美國衛報}}》（''Guardian US''）專欄作家<ref>{{cite web |last=Greenwald |first=Glenn |url=http://mediadecoder.blogs.nytimes.com/2012/07/19/glenn-greenwald-moves-from-salon-to-guardian-u-s |title=Glenn Greenwald Moves From Salon to Guardian U.S. |publisher=New York Times |date=2012-07-19 |accessdate=2012-12-09}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last=Greenwald |first=Glenn |url=http://www.salon.com/2012/07/19/home_news |title=I'll be writing in a new venue beginning next month |publisher=Salon.com |date=2012-07-19 |accessdate=2012-12-09}}</ref><ref name="GreenGuard">{{cite web|url=http://ggsidedocs.blogspot.com.br/2013/10/my-statement-and-guardians.html|title=Glenn Greenwald and the Guardian's statements|publisher=|date=|accessdate=|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131016043310/http://ggsidedocs.blogspot.com.br/2013/10/my-statement-and-guardians.html|archivedate=2013-10-16}}</ref>。2007年至2012年間，也曾為{{tsl|en|Salon (website)|Salon.com}}寫過專欄，並為英國《[[衛報|衛報]]》供稿<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/commentisfree/2011/dec/14/bradley-manning-deserves-a-medal |location=London |work=The Guardian |first=Glenn |last=Greenwald |title=Bradley Manning deserves a medal |date=2011-12-14}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/commentisfree/cifamerica/2011/jul/21/barack-obama-social-security-cuts tapes' |date=2011-10-07}}</ref>。葛林華德亦曾任[[宪法|宪法]]與[[公民权利和政治权利|公民权利和政治权利]]律師。在《Salon》網站上，葛林華德身兼專欄作家與部落客，關注政治與法律議題<ref name=SalonProfile />。他亦為包含《[[纽约时报|纽约时报]]》<ref name=NYT2009-04-28 /><ref name=ITT>{{cite web |first=Glenn |last=Greenwald |title=Author Profile:Glenn Greenwald |url=http://www.inthesetimes.com/community/profile/3753 |work=[[In_These_Times|In These Times]] |date=2006-07-21 Intercept|The Intercept}}》共同編輯<ref name=TI>{{cite web |url=https://firstlook.org/theintercept/staff/ |title=Staff – The Intercept |accessdate=2014-01-08}}</ref>。

葛林華德被《[[外交政策_(杂志)|外交政策]]》雜誌選為「2013年全球百大思想家」<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.foreignpolicy.com/2013_global_thinkers/public/greenwald |title=The Leading Global Thinkers of 2013 |publisher=Foreign Policy |date= |accessdate=2014-06-29}}</ref>。他的五本著作中，有四本曾被列入[[紐約時報暢銷書榜|《紐約時報》暢銷書排行榜]]。葛林華德亦是校園演說家，足跡遍布[[哈佛法学院|哈佛法学院]]、[[耶鲁法学院|耶鲁法学院]]、[[宾夕法尼亚大学|賓州大學]]、[[布朗大学|布朗大学]]、[[加州大学洛杉矶分校法学院|加州大学洛杉矶分校法学院]]、[[威斯康星大学麦迪逊分校|威斯康星大学麦迪逊分校]]與[[馬里蘭大學學院市分校|馬里蘭大學學院市分校]]。葛林華德也是廣播與電視節目常客。

葛林華德2009年時曾獲{{tsl|en|Izzy Award|依奇獎}}獨立新聞獎<ref name="Ithaca Award">{{cite web |url=http://www.ithaca.edu/news/release.php?id=2646 |title=Glenn Greenwald And Amy Goodman Share Inaugural Izzy Award For Independent Media |work=Ithaca News Release |publisher=[[Ithaca_College|Ithaca College]] |date=2009-03-05 |accessdate=2009-03-12 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090305151154/http://www.ithaca.edu/news/release.php?id=2646 |archivedate=2009-03-05 }}</ref>，2010年時曾獲{{tsl|en|Online News Association|線上新聞協會|線上新聞協會獎}}最佳評論獎<ref name="OJA Award">{{cite web |url=http://conference.journalists.org/2010conference/2010/10/30/online-journalism-awards-honor-the-best-of-the-best |title=Online Journalism Awards, 2010 |publisher=[[Online_Journalism_Awards|Online Journalism Awards]] |date=2010-10-31 |accessdate=2010-10-31}}</ref>。

2013年6月，葛林華德因透過《衛報》刊登了詳述美國與英國{{tsl|en|Global surveillance|全球監測}}計畫，並由[[爱德华·斯诺登|-{zh-cn:爱德华·斯诺登;zh-hk:愛德華·斯諾登;zh-mo:愛德華·斯諾登;zh-tw:愛德華·史諾登;}-]]揭露機密文件的系列報導而聲名遠播<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2013/08/07/glenn-greenwald-edward-snowden-documents_n_3716424.html?utm_hp_ref=media |work=[[Huffington_Post|Huffington Post]] |last=Boadle |first=Anthony |title=Glenn Greenwald: Snowden Gave Me 15-20,000 Classified Documents |date=August 6, 2013 |accessdate=August 9, 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.theguardian.com/commentisfree/2013/aug/18/david-miranda-detained-uk-nsa |location=London |work=The Guardian |first=Glenn |last=Greenwald |title=Glenn Greenwald: detaining my partner was a failed attempt at intimidation |date=2013-08-19}}</ref>。這系列葛林華德與其他人合作出版的報導，讓他獲頒[[普利策奖|普利策奖]]{{tsl|en|Pulitzer Prize for Public Service|普立茲獎公共服務獎|公共服務獎}}<ref>Fung, Catherine, [Glenn Greenwald Reacts To Pulitzer Prize http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2014/04/20/glenn-greenwald-pulitzer-reliable-sources_n_5182297.html], ''Huffington Post'', April 20, 2014</ref><ref name="pulitzer prize">{{cite press release |url=http://www.theguardian.com/media/2014/apr/14/guardian-washington-post-pulitzer-nsa-revelations |title=Guardian and Washington Post win Pulitzer prize for NSA revelations |date=2014-04-14}}</ref>。

葛林華德針對[[美国国家安全局|美国国家安全局]]（NSA）的報導，讓他在全世界獲得無數獎項，包含{{tsl|en|George Polk Awads|乔治·波尔克奖|-{zh-hans:乔治·波尔克奖;zh-tw:喬治·潑克獎;zh-hk:佐治·伯格獎}-}}國家安全報導獎<ref name="george polk award" />、2013年線上新聞獎<ref>{{cite news |author=Martin Pengelly in New York |url=http://www.theguardian.com/media/2013/oct/20/guardian-nsa-files-online-journalism-awards |title=Guardian wins two online journalism awards for NSA Files reporting |publisher=theguardian.com |date= |accessdate=2014-06-29}}</ref>，並以在巴西《{{tsl|en|O Globo}}》雜誌接露NSA亦針對巴西人民進行監控的報導，獲得{{tsl|en|Esso Journalism Award|艾索新聞獎|-{zh-hant:艾索新聞獎;zh-hans:埃索新闻奖;}-}}（是第一個獲得此獎的外國人士）<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.premioesso.com.br/site/noticias/release_2013_04.aspx |title=PrÃªmio Esso de Jornalismo 2013 |publisher=Premioesso.com.br |date= |accessdate=2014-06-29 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140620055819/http://www.premioesso.com.br/site/noticias/release_2013_04.aspx |archivedate=2014-06-20 }}</ref>，此外還有阿根廷《{{tsl|en|Perfil}}》雜誌2013年的國際言論自由獎<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.perfil.com/sociedad/Premios-Perfil-a-la-Libertad-de-Expresion-y-la-Inteligencia-2013-20131106-0038.html |title=Premios Perfil a la Libertad de Expresión y la Inteligencia 2013 |publisher=Perfil.com |date= |accessdate=2014-06-29}}</ref>，以及[[电子前哨基金会|电子前哨基金会]]2013年的先鋒獎<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.eff.org/awards/pioneer/2013 |title=EFF Pioneer Awards 2013 |publisher=Electronic Frontier Foundation |date=2013-09-19 |accessdate=2014-06-29}}</ref>。

葛林華德揭露NSA文件的過程，被拍成電影《[[第四公民|第四公民]]》，並一舉獲得2014年[[奥斯卡最佳纪录片奖|奥斯卡最佳纪录片奖]]。葛林華德在頒獎典禮上與本片導演罗拉·柏翠丝一同現身領獎<ref>http://www.newyorker.com/news/amy-davidson/is-citizenfour-worth-celebrating</ref>。

葛林華德現與其伴侶大衛·迈克尔·密蘭達同住於他的故鄉[[巴西|巴西]][[里约热内卢|里约热内卢]]<ref name=GreenwaldResponse /><ref name="Out.com">{{cite web |url=http://www.out.com/news-commentary/2011/04/18/glenn-greenwald-life-beyond-borders?page=0,0 |title=Glenn Greenwald: Life Beyond Borders |publisher=Out.com |date=2011-04-18 |accessdate=2012-12-09}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.listener.co.nz/culture/books/glenn-greenwald-interview |title=Glenn Greenwald interview |work=[[New_Zealand_Listener|New Zealand Listener]] |date=2012-02-04 |accessdate=2012-12-09}}</ref><ref name=Silverstein>{{cite news |first=Ken |last=Silverstein |url=http://www.harpers.org/archive/2008/02/hbc-90002466 |title=Six Questions for Glenn Greenwald on Campaign Coverage |work=[[Harper's_Magazine|Harper's Magazine]] |date=2008-02-21 |accessdate=2008-12-15}}</ref><ref name=GreenwaldAOTPInt>{{cite web |author=Art of The Possible |url=http://www.theartofthepossible.net/2008/05/05/glenn-greenwald-an-interview-with-the-art-of-the-possible |title=Interview with Glenn Greenwald |work=Art of the Possible Blog |date=2006-01-16 |accessdate=2008-12-13 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080508160950/http://www.theartofthepossible.net/2008/05/05/glenn-greenwald-an-interview-with-the-art-of-the-possible/ |archive-date=2008-05-08 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>。葛林華德表示，自己會選擇住在巴西，是因為美國的[[捍卫婚姻法案|捍卫婚姻法案]]拒絕承認同性婚姻，導致其伴侶無法獲得在美國與他共同居住的簽證<ref name="Out.com" />。葛林華德亦擔心若返回美國境內，會遭到逮捕<ref name="Ratner">{{cite news |url=http://therealnews.com/t2/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=31&Itemid=74&jumival=11896 |work=The Real News Network |first=Michael |last=Ratner |title=Twitter Storm between WikiLeaks and Glenn Greenwald |date=2014-11-04}}</ref>。
