'''雷格瑞·溫斯頓·史雷頓'''（Gregory W. Slayton）是位美國籍的作家、外交官、商界人士和慈善人士；偶爾於達特茅斯學院<ref name="dartmouth1">{{cite web|url=http://mba.tuck.dartmouth.edu/pecenter/about/team.html |title=Center for Private Equity and Entrepreneurship: The Center Team |publisher=Mba.tuck.dartmouth.edu |date= |accessdate=November 18, 2010}}</ref>
、[[哈佛大學|哈佛]]、[[史丹福大學|史丹福]]授課，並於中國北京[[對外經濟貿易大學|對外經濟貿易大學]]商學院教授領導能力的研究。

早年在[[矽谷|矽谷]]的工作成果為[[哈佛商學院|哈佛商學院]]整理成[[個案教學法|個案研究]]的基礎<ref>{{cite news|url=http://online.wsj.com/news/articles/SB940894656923712432 |title=WSJ A Boss Who Makes Others' Ideas Pay Off |publisher=Wall Street Journal |date=October 26, 1999 |accessdate=July 17, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.time.com/time/covers/0,16641,19990927,00.html |title=TIME Magazine Cover: The New Silicon Valley |publisher=Time.com |date=September 27, 1999 |accessdate=November 18, 2010}}</ref>；曾受美國總統[[小布希|小布希]]、[[歐巴馬|歐巴馬]]命為美國駐[[百慕達|百慕達]]大使。<ref name="usconsulate1">{{cite web |url=http://hamilton.usconsulate.gov/new_us_consul_sworn_in_by_condoleezza_rice.html |title=New US Consul sworn in by Condoleezza Rice |publisher=Hamilton.usconsulate.gov |date=August 31, 2005 |accessdate=November 18, 2010 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081204144719/http://hamilton.usconsulate.gov/new_us_consul_sworn_in_by_condoleezza_rice.html |archivedate=十二月 4, 2008 }}</ref>

自2012年起，完成著作－《我是好爸爸》（Be A Better Dad Today）後，開始宣揚當個「好爸爸運動」的理念。<ref>[http://www.christianretailing.com/index.php/newsletter/latest/25330-regal-title-becomes-best-selling-fatherhood-book-in-last-decade]</ref>
