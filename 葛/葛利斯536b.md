'''葛利斯536b'''（'''Gliese 536 b'''，或'''GJ 536 b'''<ref name=NasaExoplanetArchive/><ref name=Exoplanet.eu/><ref name="SuárezMascareño"/>），是一顆距離地球33光年的[[太陽系外行星|太陽系外行星]]<ref name="dailyMail">{{cite web|last1=Plummer|first1=Libby|title=New super-Earth is discovered nearby: Exoplanet is found orbiting a star 33 light years away - and there could be more.|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/sciencetech/article-3949178/New-super-Earth-discovered-Exoplanet-orbiting-nearby-star-more.html|website=Daily Mail|publisher=Daily Mail|accessdate=2016-11-19}}</ref><ref name="Science Daily">{{cite web|title=Scientists discover a nearby 'super-Earth' Its short orbital period could help with future studies of biological activity.|url=https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2016/11/161116102103.htm|website=Science Daily|accessdate=19 November 2016}}</ref><ref name="phys.org">{{cite web|title=Scientists discover a nearby superearth|url=http://phys.org/news/2016-11-scientists-nearby-superearth.html|website=Phys.org|accessdate=19 November 2016}}</ref>，是一顆環繞[[恆星光譜|光譜型]]M1[[紅矮星|紅矮星]][[葛利斯536|葛利斯536]]的[[超級地球|超級地球]]，軌道位於母恆星的[[適居帶|適居帶]]內，軌道週期8.7日<ref name=Exoplanet.eu/>。因為該行星的公轉週期相當短，將會是適合進一步研究系外行星上生物活動的理想目標<ref name="Science Daily"/>。
