'''青壯年'''（young adult）一般是指[[年齡|年齡]]在18歲至40歲（或41歲）的[[成人|成人]]，不過也有不同的定義（例如[[爱利克·埃里克森|爱利克·埃里克森]]提出的[[埃里克森社会心理发展阶段|埃里克森社会心理发展阶段]]等）<ref>{{Cite web|title= The Theoretical Basis for the Life Model-Research And Resources On Human Development|publisher=|url= http://www.lifemodel.org/download/Model%20Building%20Appendix.pdf|accessdate= 2009-08-11}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title= PSY 345 Lecture Notes – Ego Psychologists, Erik Erikson|publisher= |url= http://www.psychology.sunysb.edu/ewaters/345/2007_erikson/2006_erikson.pdf|accessdate= 2009-08-11|deadurl= yes|archiveurl= https://www.webcitation.org/5tWHzkelF?url=http://www.psychology.sunysb.edu/ewaters/345/2007_erikson/2006_erikson.pdf|archivedate= 2010-10-16}}</ref>。成人在青壯年之後的階段稱為[[中年|中年]]，年齡約為45歲至59歲間。65歲及65歲以上的稱為[[老年|老年]]<ref name="Martin Briner">Martin Briner, [http://www.dean.usma.edu/math/activities/cape/Constructivism/501erik.htm Erik Erikson page] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20060821183134/http://www.dean.usma.edu/math/activities/cape/Constructivism/501erik.htm |date=2006-08-21 }}, 1999, on Briner's [http://www.dean.usma.edu/math/activities/cape/Constructivism/501learn.htm site about learning theories] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20061007042838/http://www.dean.usma.edu/math/activities/cape/Constructivism/501learn.htm |date=2006-10-07 }}, USMA Department of Mathematical Sciences, Center for Assessment and Program Evaluation (CAPE), United States Military Academy at West Point. Accessed 24 November 2006.</ref>。
