'''吾家有子'''(Kirstie)是一部美國[[情境喜劇|情境喜劇]]，[[TV_Land|TV Land]]電視台製播，由[[Kirstie_Alley|Kirstie Alley]]主演，<ref name=info>{{cite web|last=Kondolojy|first=Amanda|title=TV Land Greenlights Original Sitcom 'Kirstie's New Show' Starring Kirstie Alley|url=http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2013/02/15/tv-land-greenlights-original-sitcom-kirsties-new-show-starring-kirstie-alley/169633/|work=TV by the Numbers|accessdate=July 23, 2013|date=February 15, 2013}}</ref> 於2013年12月4日首播。<ref name=debut>{{cite web|title=John Travolta Reunites with Kirstie Alley as a Guest Star on TV Land's New Sitcom "Kirstie"|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2013/07/23/john-travolta-reunites-with-kirstie-alley-as-a-guest-star-on-tv-lands-new-sitcom-kirstie-754210/20130723tvland01/|work=The Futon Critic|date=July 23, 2013}}</ref>

==故事大綱==
「吾家有子」描述一位女演員麥蒂在一次公演結束後，意外地見到她過去送養的小孩阿羅(Arlo)。<ref name=information>{{cite press release|title=TV Land's December Is One to Remember!|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2013/11/05/tv-lands-december-is-one-to-remember-231515/20131105tvland01/|work=The Futon Critic|accessdate=November 21, 2013|date=November 5, 2013}}</ref> 阿羅在養母過世之後，從養母的遺物中找到生母的資料，於是過來找麥蒂。劇情就在麥蒂與她的小孩阿羅，還有助理希爾瑪(Thelma)，司機法蘭克(Frank)等人的生活中展開。
