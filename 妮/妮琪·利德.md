'''妮琪·利德'''（{{lang-en|Nikki Reed}}，{{bd|1988年|5月17日|catIdx=R}}）<ref>[http://www.tvguide.com/celebrities/nikki-reed-168088/bio/ "Nikki Reed".] TVGuide.com. Retrieved January 15, 2015.</ref>是[[美國|美國]]女演員。她在2003年拍攝電影《[[芳齡十三|芳齡十三]]》出道。她最為人所知的角色是在《[[暮光之城：無懼的愛|暮光之城：無懼的愛]]》系列所扮演的金髮女Rosalie。

她父親是[[猶太人|猶太人]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/films/features/nikki-reed-what-nikki-did-next-562481.html|title=Nikki Reed: What Nikki did next|work=The Independent|date=May 6, 2004|accessdate=May 17, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|first=Danielle|last=Berrin|url=http://jewishjournal.com/uncategorized/80947/|title=Top ten Jewish things about The Twilight Saga’s ‘Eclipse’|work=Jewish Journal|date=July 2, 2010|accessdate=May 17, 2018}}</ref>，母親則擁有[[切羅基人|切羅基人]]及義大利人血統<ref>[http://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/films/features/nikki-reed-what-nikki-did-next-6170311.html "Nikki Reed: What Nikki did next".] The Independent (London). 2004-05-07.</ref>。利德的現任丈夫是《[[吸血鬼日记|吸血鬼日记]]》 男星[[伊恩·桑莫哈德|伊恩·桑莫哈德]]<ref>[http://www.people.com/article/ian-somerhalder-nikki-reed-engaged Schwartz, Alison.
