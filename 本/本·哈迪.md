'''班·瓊斯'''（{{lang-en|'''Ben Jones'''}}，{{bd|1991年|1月2日}}）或稱'''班·哈迪'''（{{lang-en|'''Ben Hardy'''}}），是一位[[英國|英國]]男演員<ref name="blackmorevale">{{cite news | url=http://www.blackmorevale.co.uk/Sherborne-actor-Ben-Jones-lands-role-EastEnders/story-18790047-detail/story.html | title=Sherborne actor Ben Jones lands role in EastEnders | work=Blackmore Vale Magazine | date=2013-04-24 | accessdate=2015-02-07 | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150923191742/http://www.blackmorevale.co.uk/Sherborne-actor-Ben-Jones-lands-role-EastEnders/story-18790047-detail/story.html | archivedate=2015年9月23日 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news | url=http://www.westerngazette.co.uk/Sherborne-pupil-debuts-Peter-Beale-Eastenders/story-19272925-detail/story.html | title=Former Sherborne pupil debuts as Peter Beale on Eastenders | work=Western Gazette | date=2013-06-13 | accessdate=2015-02-07}}</ref>。他最著名的作品是在2013年至2015年在[[BBC|BBC]]肥皂劇《[[東區人|東區人]]》中飾演彼得·比爾一角，而他也在2016年電影《[[X戰警：天啟|X戰警：天啟]]》中飾演天使<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.digitalspy.co.uk/movies/s2/eastenders/news/a631334/eastenders-star-ben-hardy-cast-in-x-men-apocalypse.html|title=EastEnders star Ben Hardy cast in X-Men: Apocalypse|work=Digital Spy}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/TheInSneider/status/583012077331046400|title=Jeff Sneider on Twitter|work=Twitter}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.empireonline.com/news/story.asp?NID=43949|title=Exclusive: Bryan Singer Confirms Ben Hardy Is Angel In X-Men: Apocalypse|publisher=Empire Magazine|last=Hewit|first=Chris|date=2015-04-09}}</ref>。

==早期生活==
哈迪的出生名為班·瓊斯<ref name="blackmorevale"/>，他出生於[[多塞特郡|多塞特郡]]的[[伯恩茅斯|伯恩茅斯]]，且曾就讀過{{tsl|en|Gryphon School|鷹頭獅學校}}。
