'''运河街'''（Canal Street）是英格兰西北部[[曼彻斯特市中心|曼彻斯特市中心]]的一条步行街，沿着Rochdale 运河的西侧，布满[[同志酒吧|同志酒吧]]和餐馆<ref>[http://www.canal-st.co.uk/history.php History: From Grime to Prime; by Mike Wolfe] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081006071214/http://www.canal-st.co.uk/history.php |date=2008-10-06 }} Retrieved on 2 April 2008</ref>，是曼彻斯特同志村的中心，布满来自全世界的同志游客。这条街的北到 Minshull街，南到公主街。也是欧洲最成功的同志村<ref name="Campbell">see: {{Cite news| last =Campbell| first = Beatrix  | author-link = Beatrix Campbell | title = Village people| newspaper = The Guardian| date = 7 August 2004| url =https://www.theguardian.com/world/2004/aug/07/gayrights.communities| postscript =| location=London}}</ref>。

运河街开辟于1804年开挖Rochdale 运河以后。1960年代以后运河航运衰退<ref>[http://www.penninewaterways.co.uk/rochdale/rc2.htm History of the Rochdale Canal] retrieved 2 April 2008</ref>和棉纺织业衰退，该区沦落为[[红灯区|红灯区]]<ref name="Independent">{{Cite news|author= Jenny Turner|title=The gay village, Canal Street, Manchester  |url=http://www.independent.co.uk/life-style/the-gay-village-canal-street-manchester-1336138.html|work= [[The_Independent|The Independent]]|date= 1996-06-09|accessdate= 2010-12-24 | location=London}}</ref>。
