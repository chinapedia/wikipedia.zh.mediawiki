'''達比克'''（{{lang-ar|دابق}}）是[[敘利亞|敘利亞]]北部一個村莊，位於[[阿勒頗|阿勒頗]]東北部約40公里，距敘利亞—[[土耳其|土耳其]]邊境不到10公里。據2004年的統計，達比克僅有3,364人。<ref name="CBS">[http://www.cbssyr.org/new%20web%20site/General_census/census_2004/NH/TAB02-23-2004.htm General Census of Population and Housing 2004]. [[Central_Bureau_of_Statistics_(Syria)|Syria Central Bureau of Statistics]] (CBS). Aleppo Governorate. Archived at [http://archive.is/lcEK]. {{ar icon}}</ref>

達比克並非以現實戰略意義而著稱，但它具有高度的象征意義。在[[聖訓|聖訓]]的[[伊斯蘭教末世論|伊斯蘭教末世論]]中，這裡是[[穆斯林|穆斯林]]與[[基督徒|基督徒]]決戰並得勝的地方。[[恐怖主義|恐怖主義]]聖戰組織“[[伊斯蘭國|伊斯蘭國]]”的電子期刊名為《[[達比克_(雜誌)|達比克]]》，亦即透露與[[西方世界|西方世界]]決戰之意。<ref>{{cite news|author1=Giles Fraser|title=To Islamic State, Dabiq is important – but it’s not the end of the world|url=http://www.theguardian.com/commentisfree/belief/2014/oct/10/islamic-state-dabiq-important-not-end-of-the-world|accessdate=16 November 2014|work=The Guardian|date=10 October 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|author1=EMMA GLANFIELD|title=The 1,300-year-old apocalyptic prophecy that predicted a war between an Islamic army and 'infidel horde' in Syria and is fuelling ISIS's brutal killers|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2786039/The-1-300-year-old-apocalyptic-prophecy-predicted-war-Islamic-army-infidel-horde-Syria-fuelling-ISIS-s-brutal-killers.html|accessdate=17 November 2014|work=MAILONLINE|date=9 October 2014}}</ref>
