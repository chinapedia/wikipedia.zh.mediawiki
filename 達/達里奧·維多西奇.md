'''達里奥·維多西奇'''（'''Dario Vidošić'''，{{bd|1987年|4月8日|catIdx=Vidošić}}）是[[澳大利亞|澳大利亞]]的一位職業足球員。在場上司職攻擊型[[中場|攻擊型中場]]。他現在效力於[[澳職|澳職]][[墨爾本城|墨爾本城]]<ref>{{cite web | url = http://au.fourfourtwo.com/news/280368,vidosic-exits-adelaide-for-europe.aspx | title = Vidosic exits Adelaide for Europe | publisher = FourFourTwo | date = 26 August 2013 | accessdate = 26 August 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.adelaidenow.com.au/sport/dario-vidosic8217s-700000-deal-a-record-for-aleague-transfer/story-fnii0fc4-1226705282686 | title = Dario Vidosic's $700,000 deal a record for A-League transfer | publisher = adelaidenow.com.au | date = 27 August 2013 | accessdate = 28 August 2013 | first = Val | last = Migliaccio}}</ref>。他也代表[[澳大利亞國家足球隊|澳大利亞國家足球隊]]參加比賽。他出生在[[克羅地亞|克羅地亞]]，父母也都是移民到澳大利亞的克羅地亞人。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.news.com.au/couriermail/story/0,23739,23107728-5014143,00.html |title=Rado Vidosic A-League's only part-time assistant coach|date= 25 January 2008|publisher=www.couriermail.com.au | first=Alex | last=Murdoch | accessdate = 26 August 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.couriermail.com.au/sport/dario-vidosic-selected-in-socceroos-squad-for-ireland-clash/story-e6frep5o-1225757904828 |title=Dario Vidosic selected in Socceroos squad for Ireland clash |date= 4 August 2009 |publisher=www.couriermail.com.au |first=Marco|last=Monteverde | accessdate = 26 August 2013}}</ref>他首次代表澳大利亞國家隊參加比賽是在2009年6月17日，之後參加了2010年和2014年世界杯的比賽。
