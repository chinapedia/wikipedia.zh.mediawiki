'''達比克攻勢'''是[[土耳其|土耳其]]軍隊及親土耳其的[[叙利亚反对派|叙利亚反对派]]在2016年9月28日發起的軍事行動，以攻佔[[阿勒颇省|阿勒颇省]]北部城鎮[[達比克|達比克]]為目標。<ref name=apocalyptic>{{cite web|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/middle-east/isis-syria-dabiq-army-forces-propaganda-centre-a7343211.html |script-title=en:Isis is preparing for an ‘apocalyptic’ battle to defend the village at the heart of its propaganda|date=3 October 2016|publisher=|accessdate=9 October 2016}}</ref><ref name=3km>[http://rudaw.net/english/middleeast/syria/021020161 Turkish-backed rebels 3km from strategic Dabiq, northern Syria]</ref>

==攻勢==
===首周===
[[File:Dabiqof.png|thumb]]

2016年10月3日，反對派部隊在Turkman Bareh村遇上地雷區，造成15－21名反對派人員陣亡，多人受傷。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-middle-east-37541058|title=Syrian rebels advance on symbolic IS-held town of Dabiq|date=2016-10-03|newspaper=BBC News|language=en-GB|access-date=2016-10-03}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/wires/afp/article-3819408/IS-mines-kill-21-Turkey-backed-rebels-Syria-monitor.html|title=IS mines kill 21 Turkey-backed rebels in Syria: monitor|publisher=|accessdate=6 October 2016}}</ref><ref>[http://www.syriahr.com/en/?p=51767 50 rebels killed or wounded by IS landmines in Turkman Bareh]</ref>10月4日至5日間，反對派攻佔Turkman Bareh及另外5個村莊。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://syria.liveuamap.com/en/2016/4-october-rebels-captured-3-villages-east-of-akhtaren-town/|title=Rebels captured 3 villages East of Akhtaren town|publisher=|accessdate=6 October 2016|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161006025658/http://syria.liveuamap.com/en/2016/4-october-rebels-captured-3-villages-east-of-akhtaren-town|archivedate=2016年10月6日}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://syria.liveuamap.com/en/2016/5-october-fsa-still-in-control-of-turkmen-bareh--and--capture|title=FSA still in control of Turkmen Bareh  and  capture Mazare' 'Alaa, Qabtan & Aziziyeh #EuphratesShield|publisher=|accessdate=6 October 2016}}</ref>

===次周===
10月6日，反對派攻佔Akhtarin鎮。<ref>[https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/turkish-backed-rebels-capture-important-town-isis-northern-aleppo/ Turkish-backed rebels capture important town from ISIS in northern Aleppo] Al-Masdar News (6 October 2016)</ref>伊斯蘭國在兩天後反攻，一度奪回該鎮及包括Turkman Bareh在內的另外4個村莊，<ref name="38killed">{{cite web|url=http://www.reuters.com/article/idUSKCN12905N|title=Turkey says 38 Islamic State militants killed in northern Syria|author=Nevzat Devranoglu, Daren Butler|publisher=Reuters|date=9 October 2016|accessdate=9 October 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.reuters.com/article/us-mideast-crisis-syria-islamic-state-idUSKCN1280C5|title=Islamic State hits back against Syrian rebels north of Aleppo: monitors|date=8 October 2016|publisher=|accessdate=9 October 2016|via=Reuters}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/isis-launches-counter-offensive-northern-aleppo/|title=ISIS launches counter-offensive in northern Aleppo|author=Leith Fadel|publisher=Al-Masdar News|accessdate=8 October 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.qasioun.net/en/news/show/38988/ISIS_Re_Seize_Akhtarin_And_Ola_Farm_Northern_Aleppo|title=ISIS Re-Seize Akhtarin And Ola Farm Northern Aleppo - Qasion News Agency|publisher=|accessdate=9 October 2016}}</ref>最終被擊退。<ref name="38killed"/><ref>{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/sayed_ridha/status/784704443094749185|title=Hassan Ridha on Twitter|publisher=|accessdate=9 October 2016}}</ref>土耳其軍方宣稱10月8日至9日間有38名伊斯蘭國分子在戰鬥和空襲中喪生。<ref name="38killed"/>

10月9日至11日間，反對派從伊斯蘭國手上攻佔17個村莊，距離達比克不到2.5公里。30名反對派人員及20名伊斯蘭國分子在戰鬥中喪生。<ref name=syriahr52134>[http://www.syriahr.com/en/?p=52134 50 killed during clashes around Dabeq between rebels and IS]</ref>

===第三周===
10月15日，反對派攻佔另外3個村莊，幾乎完全包圍達比克。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/battle-dabiq-imminent-rebel-forces-advance-isis-aleppo/|title=Battle of Dabiq imminent as rebel forces advance against ISIS in Aleppo|first=Izat|last=Charkatli|date=15 October 2016|publisher=|accessdate=18 October 2016}}</ref><ref>[http://www.syriahr.com/en/?p=52439 Dabiq clashes continue and violent clashes in Soran Azaz]</ref>10月16日，反對派攻佔達比克、附近的Sawran鎮及另外7個村莊。<ref name="secure">{{cite web|url=http://www.hurriyetdailynews.com/Default.aspx?pageID=238&nid=105031&NewsCatID=352|title=Turkey-backed Syria rebels secure Dabiq area, eye al-Bab - MIDEAST|publisher=|accessdate=18 October 2016}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-middle-east-37670998|title=Syria conflict: Rebels 'capture' IS stronghold of Dabiq|date=2016-10-16|newspaper=BBC News|language=en-GB|access-date=2016-10-16}}</ref><ref>[http://www.syriahr.com/en/?p=52474 After 24 of Siege, the IS pulls back from Dabeq strategic town]</ref>
