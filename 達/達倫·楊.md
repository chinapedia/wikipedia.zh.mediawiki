'''達倫·楊'''（'''Darren Young'''）<ref name="OWOW">{{cite web|url=http://www.onlineworldofwrestling.com/profiles/d/darren-young.html|title=Darren Young|accessdate=April 27, 2010|publisher=Online World of Wrestling}}</ref><ref name="Varsallone-pg2">{{cite web|url=http://www.miamiherald.com/2010/04/26/1599309_p2/young-believes-hes-nxt.html|title=Young believes he's NXT|date=April 26, 2010|last=Varsallone|first=Jim|accessdate=July 23, 2010|work=[[The_Miami_Herald|The Miami Herald]]|page=2|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/5rRU3hMWN?url=http://www.miamiherald.com/2010/04/26/1599309_p2/young-believes-hes-nxt.html|archivedate=2010-07-23|dead-url=yes}}</ref>，（1983年11月2日）<ref>[http://www.intelius.com/results.php?ReportType=1&qf=Fred&qmi=D&qn=Rosser&qcs=NJ&focusfirst=0 Intelius search]</ref>，知名[[美國|美國]][[摔角|摔角]]選手，本名為'''Fredrick Douglas "Fred" Rosser'''。

達倫·楊曾加入Chaotic Wrestling<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chaoticwrestling.com/profiles/fuller_sampson.htm|title=Big Rick Fuller & Fred "Bonecrusher" Sampson|year=2008|work=Chaotic Wrestling Profiles|publisher=ChaoticWrestling.com}}{{Dead link|date=2018年7月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>、[[東岸摔角協會|東岸摔角協會]]（East Coast Wrestling Association）<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ecwaprowrestling.com/main/bios/frederick_of_hollywood.html|title=Frederick of Hollywood|year=2008|work=ECWA Current Roster Biographies & Photo Gallery|publisher=ECWAProWrestling.com|access-date=2016-09-04|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081220014546/http://www.ecwaprowrestling.com/main/bios/frederick_of_hollywood.html|archive-date=2008-12-20|dead-url=yes}}</ref>、[[獨立摔角聯盟|獨立摔角聯盟]]（Independent Wrestling Federation）與[[國家摔角聯盟|國家摔角聯盟]]（National Wrestling Alliance），目前是[[世界摔角娛樂|世界摔角娛樂]]成員。

達倫·楊接受八卦網站TMZ採訪時，宣布自身是同性戀者，成為職業摔角界第一位公開出櫃的現役摔角選手<ref>http://www.pwinsider.com/article/79636/cena-responds-to-darren-young.html?p=1</ref>。
