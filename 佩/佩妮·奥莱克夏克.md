'''佩内洛普·“佩妮”·奥莱克夏克'''（{{lang-en|Penelope "Penny" Oleksiak}}，{{bd|2000年|6月13日||}}），生于多伦多，是一名主攻自由泳和蝶泳的加拿大游泳运动员。她在2015年世界青年游泳锦标赛上获得1金4银1铜。在2016年里约奥运会上，她表现优异，获得了[[2016年夏季奧林匹克運動會女子100公尺自由式比賽|女子100米自由泳]]冠军、[[2016年夏季奧林匹克運動會女子100公尺蝶式比賽|女子100米蝶泳]]银牌、[[2016年夏季奧林匹克運動會女子4×100公尺自由式接力比賽|女子4×100米自由泳接力]]和[[2016年夏季奧林匹克運動會女子4×200公尺自由式接力比賽|女子4×200米自由泳接力]]的铜牌，成为了截至2016年为止单届夏季奥运获得奖牌最多的加拿大运动员<ref>{{cite news|title=Simone Manuel first female African-American swimmer to win individual Olympic medal|url=http://www.espn.com/olympics/swimming/story/_/id/17279325/simone-manuel-united-states-wins-women-100-meter-freestyle-rio-olympics|publisher=[[ESPN|ESPN]]|date=2016-08-12|accessdate=2016-09-01}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|first=Rob|last=Longley|title=Penny Oleksiak wins Canada's first gold medal in Rio, cementing her star in Olympic history|url=http://news.nationalpost.com/sports/rio-2016/penny-oleksiak-wins-canadas-first-gold-medal-in-rio-cementing-her-star-in-olympic-history|publisher=National Post|date=2016-08-12|accessdate=2016-09-01}}</ref>，除此之外她在奥运期间打破了女子100米蝶泳的世界青少年纪录和全国纪录，还和美国运动员[[西蒙娜·曼努埃尔|西蒙娜·曼努埃尔]]共同打破女子100米自由泳的奥运纪录和美洲纪录<ref>{{cite web|url=http://olympics.cbc.ca/news/article/swimming-finals-thursday.html|title=Penny Oleksiak wins gold, captures historic 4th Olympic medal|last=Ng|first=Callum|date=2016-08-12|publisher=CBC Sports|access-date=2016-08-12}}</ref>。

==运动生涯==
奥莱克夏克9岁时受父亲的鼓励开始学习游泳，那时她也正练习[[体操|体操]]和竞技舞蹈<ref name=dn>{{cite web |url=http://sportsday.dallasnews.com/other-sports/olympics/2016/08/08/16-year-old-penny-oleksiak-stars-defenseman-jamies-sister-became-latest-olympic-star |title=How 16-year-old Penny Oleksiak, Stars defenseman Jamie's sister, became the latest Olympic star |work=Dallas News |author=Kevin Sherrington |date=2016-08-08}}</ref>。她曾向数家游泳俱乐部提出加入申请，但均遭到拒绝。最终她成功加入多伦多奥林匹克游泳俱乐部，并师从该队教练Gary Nolden<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://swimming.ca/en/news/2016/08/03/teenager-penny-oleksiak-still-growing-swimming-potential/ |title=Teenager Penny Oleksiak still growing into her swimming potential |date=2016-08-03 |publisher=Swimming Canada|author=Morris, Jim |access-date=2016-08-18}}</ref>。在她加入俱乐部的第一年中，她在[[多伦多大学|多伦多大学]]进行的一场比赛中的表现引起了加拿大奥运游泳队教练Ben Titley的注意<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.macleans.ca/olympics/the-amazing-penny-oleksiak-i-just-tried-to-hold-on/|title=The Amazing Penny Oleksiak: 'I just tried to hold on'|author=Gatehouse, Jonathon |date=2016-08-07|work=Maclean's|access-date=2016-08-18}}</ref>，这之后她也有与Titley合作。2014年，她参加了加拿大全国游泳锦标赛，并在她所在的年龄组中获得包括5金3银2铜在内的10枚奖牌<ref name=making>{{Cite web |url=http://www.sportsnet.ca/olympics/making-penny-oleksiak-canadas-best-ever-summer-olympian/ |title=The making of Penny Oleksiak|date=2016-08-17 |publisher=Sportsnet|author=Gatehouse, Jonathon |access-date=2016-08-18}}</ref>。

奥莱克夏克的下一个目标是获得2016年里约奥运资格。在奥运预选赛中，她获得了100米自由泳、100米蝶泳、4×100米自由泳接力、4×200米自由泳接力和4×100米混合泳接力的参赛资格<ref name="cbc"/><ref name=sec />。在奥运开始前，加拿大游泳队曾预测她将获得奥运奖牌<ref name=numbers>{{cite web|url=https://www.thestar.com/sports/amateur/2016/12/10/the-future-of-canadian-swimming-is-in-the-numbers.html|title=The future of Canadian swimming is in the numbers|work=Toronto Star|date=2016-12-10|access-date=2016-12-12}}</ref>。

===2016年===
2016年8月，奥莱克夏克代表加拿大参加了里约奥运。在第一个比赛日中，她在女子100米蝶泳项目的预赛中以56秒73的成绩打破国家纪录和世界青年纪录，并晋级半决赛。当地时间同日晚上，她还以加拿大第四棒队员的身份参加了4×100米自由泳接力的比赛，最终队伍获得铜牌，终结了加拿大女子游泳队20年来无奥运奖牌入账的纪录，这枚铜牌也是加拿大女子游泳队40年来的首枚奥运自由泳接力奖牌<ref name=Macleansrio>{{cite news |title=The Young and the Fast: Canada wins relay bronze in Rio |author=Gatehouse, Jonathon |url=http://www.macleans.ca/olympics/canadian-swimmers-win-relay-bronze/ |work=Maclean's |date=2016-08-06|accessdate=2016-08-06}}</ref>。第二天，她在100米蝶泳决赛中以56秒46的成绩再度刷新国家纪录和世界青年纪录，并摘得一枚银牌，成为史上第一位在连续两个比赛日中都有夏季奥运奖牌入账的加拿大人<ref>{{cite news |title=Canada's Penny Oleksiak wins 2nd medal of Rio Olympics |url=http://www.cbc.ca/sports/olympics/rio2016/swimming/swimming-night-two-1.3711206 |publisher=CBC Sports |date=2016-08-07|accessdate=2017-10-07}}</ref>。

2016年12月，奥莱克夏克参加了在加拿大[[温莎_(安大略省)|温莎]]举行的世界短池游泳锦标赛，这是她在奥运后的国际赛场首秀。她先在第三个比赛日中获得女子100米自由泳项目的铜牌<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.thestar.com/sports/amateur/2016/12/08/canadas-penny-oleksiak-finds-success-despite-distractions-at-world-championships.html|title=Canada's Penny Oleksiak finds success despite distractions at world championships|work=Toronto Star |date=2016-12-08|accessdate=2016-12-08}}</ref>，两天后，她又携手队友在女子4×200米自由泳接力项目中夺金<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cbc.ca/sports/olympics/summer/aquatics/oleksiak-relay-gold-short-course-swimming-world-1.3891307|title=Oleksiak anchors Canada to short-course relay gold|publisher=[[加拿大广播公司|CBC]]|date=2016-12-10|accessdate=2016-12-12}}</ref>。在最后一天的比赛中，她又获得了女子4×50米自由泳接力的金牌以及女子4×100米混合泳接力的银牌<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sportsnet.ca/olympics/canada-wins-gold-4x50m-freestyle-relay-short-course-worlds/|title=Canada wins gold in 4x50m freestyle relay at short-course worlds|agency=Canadian Press|publisher=Sportsnet|date=2016-12-11|accessdate=2016-12-12}}</ref>。尽管加拿大在女子4×100米自由泳接力项目中第三个到达终点，但最终该队被取消成绩<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.thestar.com/sports/amateur/2016/12/06/canadas-womens-relay-swim-team-dqd-for-lineup-snafu.html|title=Canada's women's relay swim team DQ'd for lineup snafu |work=Toronto Star |date=2016-12-06}}</ref>。

她在奥运以及短池世锦赛期间的优异表现也使得她获得了Lou Marsh奖<ref name=LouMarsh>{{cite web|url=https://www.theglobeandmail.com/sports/more-sports/swimmer-penny-oleksiak-wins-lou-marsh-trophy-as-canadas-top-athlete/article33308322|title= Swimmer Penny Oleksiak wins Lou Marsh Trophy as Canada's top athlete|publisher=Globe & Mail|date=2016-12-13}}</ref>以及Bobbie Rosenfeld奖<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sportsnet.ca/olympics/penny-oleksiak-voted-canadian-press-female-athlete-year/|title=Penny Oleksiak voted Canadian Press female athlete of year|date=2016-12-27|agency=Canadian Press|publisher=Sportsnet|accessdate=2017-05-02}}</ref>。

===2017年===
奥莱克夏克在2017年年初饱受伤病困扰<ref>{{cite news |title=Adapting to Fame, Dealing with Injury, Part of Olympic Champion Penny Oleksiak's Learning Curve |url=https://swimming.ca/en/news/2017/07/18/adapting-fame-dealing-injury-part-olympic-champion-penny-oleksiaks-learning-curve/ |publisher=Swimming Canada |date=2017-07-18|accessdate=2017-07-18}}</ref>，这也影响了她的发挥，尽管如此她仍然入选加拿大2017年游泳世锦赛阵容<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.swimming.ca/en/news/2017/04/07/penny-oleksiak-shows-fight-win-100-metre-freestyle-team-canada-trials/|title=PENNY OLEKSIAK SHOWS FIGHT TO WIN 100-METRE FREESTYLE AT TEAM CANADA TRIALS|work=Swimming Canada|date=2017-04-07|accessdate=2017-05-04}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.swimming.ca/en/news/2017/04/09/kylie-masse-surprises-hilary-caldwell-win-200-m-backstroke-team-canada-trials/|title=KYLIE MASSE SURPRISES HILARY CALDWELL TO WIN 200-M BACKSTROKE AT TEAM CANADA TRIALS|publisher=Swimming Canada|date=2017-04-09|accessdate=2017-05-04}}</ref>。

在世锦赛正赛中，奥莱克夏克并没有突出表现，在女子4×100米自由泳接力项目上，加拿大队获得第四名，之后她在100米蝶泳项目上也以56秒94的成绩与奖牌失之交臂<ref>{{Cite news |title=Penny Oleksiak misses podium, Masse close to world record |url=http://www.cbc.ca/sports/olympics/summer/aquatics/fina-swimming-worlds-oleksiak-1.4218755 |publisher=CBC Sports |date=2017-07-24|accessdate=2017-07-24}}</ref>，在女子100米自由泳和女子50米蝶泳项目上，她分别位列第六名和第五名<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cbc.ca/sports/olympics/summer/aquatics/oleksiak-100-free-final-world-aquatics-championships-1.4225992|title=Penny Oleksiak 6th in 100m freestyle final at aquatics worlds|work=CBC Sports|author=Harrison, Doug|date=2017-07-28|accessdate=2017-07-28}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://omegatiming.com/File/Download?id=000111010A0204EB04FFFFFFFFFFFF01|title= 17th FINA World Championships - Women's 50m Butterfly |publisher=Omega Timing|date=2017-07-29|accessdate=2017-07-30}}</ref>。她在世锦赛上所获得的仅有的两枚奖牌分别来自于男女混合4×100米混合泳接力和男女混合4×100米自由泳接力项目<ref>{{cite news |title= World record-breaking swimmer Masse adds bronze in mixed relay|url=http://www.cbc.ca/sports/olympics/summer/aquatics/mixed-medley-relay-world-championships-1.4222706 |publisher=CBC Sports |date=2017-07-26|accessdate=2017-07-26}}</ref><ref name=mixed>{{cite web|url=http://www.cbc.ca/sports/olympics/summer/aquatics/world-aquatics-championships-saturday-1.4227594|title=Canadian mixed relay team swims to bronze at aquatics worlds|publisher=CBC Sports|date=2017-07-29|accessdate=2017-07-30}}</ref>。

世锦赛结束后，奥莱克夏克参加了在[[美国|美国]][[印第安纳波利斯|印第安纳波利斯]]举行的世界青年游泳锦标赛<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.swimming.ca/en/news/2017/04/10/fisu-games-world-junior-teams-announced/|title=FISU GAMES, WORLD JUNIOR TEAMS ANNOUNCED|publisher=Swimming Canada|date=2017-04-10|accessdate=2017-05-04}}</ref>。尽管她没有参加个人项目，她依然为加拿大包揽全部三个女子接力项目以及全部两个混合接力项目的冠军<ref>{{cite news |title=Penny Oleksiak, Canada win relay gold at world junior swimming championships |url=https://www.thestar.com/sports/amateur/2017/08/23/penny-oleksiak-canada-win-relay-gold-at-world-junior-swimming-championships.html |work=Toronto Star|location=Toronto, Ontario, Canada |date=2017-08-23 |access-date=2017-08-23 }}</ref>。

==个人生活==
佩妮·奥莱克夏克是家里五个孩子中年龄最小的，她的其中一个哥哥[[Jamie_Oleksiak|Jamie Oleksiak]]是[[国家冰球联盟|NHL]][[达拉斯星|达拉斯星队]]的防守后卫<ref>{{cite news |title=Canada's Penny Oleksiak makes waves at Olympic swim trials |url=https://www.thestar.com/sports/amateur/2016/04/07/canadas-penny-oleksiak-makes-waves-at-olympic-swim-trials.html |author=Gillespie, Kerry  |work=[[多伦多星报|Toronto Star]] |date=2016-04-07}}</ref>。她的父亲曾从事过篮球、足球和田径运动；她的母亲也是一名游泳运动员；她的其中一个姐姐是[[东北大学_(美国)|美国东北大学]]赛艇队队员；而她的另一个哥哥则是一名曲棍球选手<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.thestar.com/sports/olympics/2016/08/08/get-to-know-penny-oleksiak-canadas-16-year-old-swimming-medallist.html |title=Get to know Penny Oleksiak, Canada's 16-year-old swimming medalist |work=Toronto Star|date=2016-08-08|access-date=2016-08-14}}</ref>。奥莱克夏克现就读于位于多伦多的国王公园中学（{{lang|en|Monarch Park Collegiate Institute}}）<ref name=sec>{{cite web |url=http://www.sportsnet.ca/Olympics/person-interest-swimming-Canada's-best-kept-secret-penny-oleksiak/ |title=Person of interest: Swimming Canada's best-kept secret, Penny Oleksiak |publisher=SportsNet |date=2016-08-09 |accessdate=2016-08-10}}</ref>。
