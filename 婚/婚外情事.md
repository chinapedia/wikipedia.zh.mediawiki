《'''婚外情事'''》（{{lang-en|'''''The Affair'''''}}）是一部由{{link-en|莎拉·特里姆|Sarah Treem}}與{{link-en|哈蓋·列維|Hagai Levi}}開創，於2014年10月12日在[[Showtime電視網|Showtime]]首播的[[美国|美国]]电视剧。<ref name="picked up">{{cite news|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|title=Showtime To Pick Up Drama 'The Affair', Philip Seymour Hoffman Comedy To Series|url=http://deadline.com/2014/01/showtime-to-pick-up-drama-the-affair-philip-seymour-hoffman-comedy-to-series-665322/|accessdate=2014-08-29|publisher=[[Deadline.com|Deadline.com]]|date=2014-01-16}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://deadline.com/2014/10/the-affair-premiere-showtime-sneaking-early-look-today-846592/ |title=‘The Affair’ Premiere: Showtime Sneaking Early Look Today |publisher=Deadline.com |date=2014-10-06 |accessdate=2014-10-12}}</ref>

本劇於[[第72屆金球獎|第72屆金球獎]]上榮獲了最佳劇情類影集獎及最佳劇情類影集女主角獎。
