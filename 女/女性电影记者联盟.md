'''女性电影记者联盟'''（{{lang-en|'''Alliance of Women Film Journalists'''}}）是2006年建立的一个[[非营利性组织|非营利性组织]]，总部在[[美国|美国]][[纽约州|纽约州]][[纽约市|纽约市]]，它致力于支持[[女性|女性]]在[[电影|电影]]行业的工作。<ref>[http://awfj.org/about/  Alliance of Women Film Journalists]</ref>它包括76名女性电影评论家、记者、印刷工、媒体人等。同时，它设立了年度奖项，表彰女性电影工作者。<ref>{{cite web|last=Mayer|first=Sophie|title=Women on film, online|url=http://www.bfi.org.uk/news-opinion/sight-sound-magazine/comment/women-film-online|publisher=British Film Institute|accessdate=2013-05-28}}</ref>

== 奖项 ==
它每年由成员投票表决表彰的女性电影工作者，其得奖名单近年来被《[[时代_(杂志)|时代]]》杂志、《[[今日美国|今日美国]]》、《[[纽约时报|纽约时报]]》等美国著名媒体引用和报道。<ref>{{cite web|title=Women film journalists hail 'Zero Dark Thirty,' Bigelow|url=http://www.usatoday.com/story/life/movies/2013/01/08/alliance-of-female-journalists-zero-dark-thirty-kathryn-bigelow/1817081/|publisher=USA Today|accessdate=2013-05-28}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Bad Boy: Adam Sandler Vehicle Is Named to Misogyny ‘Hall of Shame’|url=http://entertainment.time.com/2013/01/08/bad-boy-adam-sandler-vehicle-is-2012-movie-named-to-misogyny-hall-of-shame/|publisher=TIME|accessdate=2013-05-28}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=‘Zero Dark Thirty’ tops Alliance of Women Film Journalists kudos|url=http://variety.com/2013/film/news/zero-dark-thirty-tops-alliance-of-women-film-journalists-kudos-1118064320/|publisher=Variety Media LLC|accessdate=2013-05-28}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=The Diving Bell and the Butterfly (2007)|url=http://movies.nytimes.com/movie/395604/The-Diving-Bell-and-the-Butterfly/awards|publisher=The New York Times Company|accessdate=2013-05-28}}</ref>

2007年，它公布了“[http://awfj.org/2007/06/25/awfjs-top-100-films-list/  100部优秀电影作品名单]”。<ref>{{cite web|title=The Alliance of Women Film Journalists' Top 100 Films|url=http://www.buzzsugar.com/Alliance-Women-Film-Journalists-Top-100-Films-346444|accessdate=2013-05-28}}</ref>
