'''明信片纪念碑'''（Postcards）是美国[[纽约市|纽约市]][[史泰登岛|史泰登岛]]的一个户外雕塑。它于2004年9月11日落成揭幕<ref>{{cite news |title='Postcards' Gets Nod For S.I. 9/11 Memorial|url=http://articles.nydailynews.com/2003-06-18/local/18229088_1_postcards-world-trade-center-vietnam-war-memorial |publisher=[[New_York_Daily_News|New York Daily News]] |date=June 18, 2003 |first=Mary |last=Engels}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=9/11 Memorial Unveiled On Staten Island|url=http://www.ny1.com/content/top_stories/43204/9-11-memorial-unveiled-on-staten-island |publisher=[[NY1|NY1]] News |date=09/10/2004}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=9-11 Groundbreaking for Memorial On S.I. |url=http://www.nydailynews.com/archives/ny_local/2003/08/28/2003-08-28_9-11_groundbreaking_for_memo.html |publisher=New York Daily News |date=August 28, 2003 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080605043930/http://www.nydailynews.com/archives/ny_local/2003/08/28/2003-08-28_9-11_groundbreaking_for_memo.html |archivedate=June 5, 2008 }}</ref>，包括两个30英尺（9米）高的白色大理石“翅膀”<ref>[http://gothamist.com/2008/09/11/staten_islands_911_memorial.php#photo-1 Postcards, Staten Island's 9/11 Memorial] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150508214012/http://gothamist.com/2008/09/11/staten_islands_911_memorial.php |date=2015-05-08 }} [[Gothamist|Gothamist]], September 11, 2008.</ref>
，纪念在2001年[[九一一袭击事件|九一一袭击事件]]和[[1993年世界贸易中心爆炸案|1993年世界贸易中心爆炸案]]中遇害的274名史坦顿岛居民。<ref name="SIMemorial">{{cite web |url=http://statenislandusa.com/pages/memorial.html |title=Staten Island September 11 Memorial |publisher=StatenIslandUSA.com |accessdate=2009-12-29 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090912003107/http://statenislandusa.com/pages/memorial.html |archivedate=2009-09-12 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title= 'Postcards' 9/11 Memorial Vandalized|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2010/08/18/staten-island-postcards-s_n_686104.html |publisher=Huffington Post|date= August 18, 2010 }}</ref> 死者包括许多在[[世界贸易中心|世界贸易中心]]工作的人，参与救援工作的警察和消防队员，一名死于[[聯合航空93號班機恐怖襲擊|聯合航空93號班機恐怖襲擊]]的乘客，以及一名[[1993年世界贸易中心爆炸案|1993年世界贸易中心爆炸案]]中遇害的死者。
