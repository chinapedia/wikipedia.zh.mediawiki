'''明·費里曼'''（{{lang-en|Ming Freeman}}），是一名[[鋼琴家|鋼琴家]]、[[鍵盤手|鍵盤樂器演奏家]]、{{link-en|音樂總監|Music director}}、[[作曲家|作曲家]]、[[編曲家|編曲家]]、[[音樂製作人|製作人]]<ref>{{cite news |author=Harold Duckett |title=Yanni is much better live than on record |newspaper=Knoxville News Sentinel |date=2004-03-21 |page=B5}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |author=Michele Morgan Bolton |title=Yanni concert an awesome show |date=1998-06-29 |newspaper=Times Union |location=Albany, New York |page=C5}}</ref><ref>{{cite news| author=Michael Chase |title=Yanni show proves critics wrong |newspaper=The Capital Times |date=1998-04-23 |page=E4}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |author=John Young |title=Yanni lets musicians do their thing |newspaper=Pittsburgh Post-Gazette |date=1998-02-09 |page=A10}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |author=Jim Santella |title=Stellar soloists, master musicians carry Yanni concert to enjoyable levels |newspaper=The Buffalo News |date=1998-02-08 |page=B14}}</ref>。來自臺灣，是臺灣人及加拿大人的後裔<ref>{{cite web| url=http://www.cme-pro.com/proline/artist-detail.php-artist_id=20.htm |title=Ming Freeman |publisher=Central Music |accessdate=2016-10-31}}</ref>。其鍵盤演奏快速而富有韻律；及腰的長髮是其標誌<ref name="rediff">{{cite web| url=http://www.rediff.com/style/may/28yanni.htm| title=The world in your orchestra pit |author=Suparn Verma |work=LIFE/STYLE |publisher=Rediff |accessdate=2016-10-31}}</ref>。

他在11歲時即與兄弟們組成名爲「鵝媽媽」的樂團在臺灣的酒吧、電視節目、演唱會等表演。17歲時，他與兄弟們移居美國，成立「東西方」樂團繼續從事音樂表演。<ref name="雅乐尼音">{{cite book| author=张鸣雨 |title=雅乐尼音：雅尼音乐励志手卷 |publisher=长春出版社 |year=2013 |isbn=9787544527743 |language=zh-cn }}</ref><ref name="rediff"/>1988年起，他開始爲{{link-en|傑佛瑞·奧斯朋|Jeffrey Osborne}}、[[寶拉·阿巴杜|寶拉·阿巴杜]]、{{link-en|席娜·伊斯頓|Sheena Easton}}、{{link-en|葛蕾蒂絲·奈特|Gladys Knight}}、[[瓊尼·米歇爾|瓊尼·米歇爾]]等知名歌手擔任演唱會或錄音室的鍵盤手<ref name="雅乐尼音"/><ref name="rediff"/><ref>{{cite news |author=Alex Sanz |title=Abdul casts spell, sets new standard |newspaper=South Florida Sun-Sentinel |date=1991-11-08 |page=7}}</ref>。1994年，受到[[雅尼|雅尼]]邀請擔任主要鍵盤手，隨其樂團做巡迴表演至今，並因此而受到大衆矚目<ref name="雅乐尼音"/><ref name="rediff"/><ref>{{cite web| url=http://www.yanni.com/orchestra |title=Yanni's Orchestra Members |website=Official Yanni Website |accessdate=2016-10-31}}</ref>；在雅尼諸多的音樂會DVD中均可見到其身影<ref>{{cite news |author=Jan DeKnock |title=Yanni enchants Omaha audience |newspaper=Omaha World-Herald |date=2004-03-14 |page=B9}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |author=Jon Bream|title=Pick six |newspaper=Star Tribune |date=2003-06-08 |page=F12}}</ref>。
