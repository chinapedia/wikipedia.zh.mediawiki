'''明平云南之战'''，是[[汉族|汉人]]主导的[[明朝|明朝]]将[[蒙古族|蒙古人]]主导的[[元朝|元朝]]驱逐出[[中国|中国]]的最后阶段。战争双方都使用了[[穆斯林|穆斯林]]部队。 

1381年，明朝出动30万[[汉族|汉族]]和[[回族|回族]]部队，消灭元朝在[[云南|云南]]的残余势力。明朝将领[[傅友德|傅友德]]、[[沐英|沐英]]和[[蓝玉|蓝玉]]率军攻打蒙古和穆斯林军队。<ref>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=vIUmU2ytmIIC&pg=PA170&dq=zhu+yuanzhang+mosques&hl=en&ei=M2I_TNbcJ8P88AbJr-m-Cg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=3&ved=0CDIQ6AEwAg#v=onepage&q=zhu%20yuanzhang%20mosques&f=false|title=Cheng Ho and Islam in Southeast Asia|author=Tan Ta Sen, Dasheng Chen|year=2009|publisher=Institute of Southeast Asian Studies|location=|isbn=9812308377|page=170|pages=|accessdate=2010-06-28}}</ref>1382年1月6日，[[梁王|梁王]][[把匝剌瓦尔密|把匝剌瓦尔密]]兵败自杀。忠于明朝的回族部队占领了云南。沐英和他的家族后来获得在云南全省世袭驻军地位，一直到[[咒水之难|咒水之难]]随[[南明|南明]][[永历帝|永历帝]]入[[缅甸|缅甸]][[東吁王朝|東吁王朝]]北部（今[[克钦邦|克钦邦]]东北部的[[八莫|八莫]]）边境的[[沐天波|沐天波]]。<ref>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=hUEswLE4SWUC&pg=PA34&dq=lan+yu+yunnan+muslim&hl=en&ei=UVjMTLHaKIT6lwf05fjiCA&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CCsQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q&f=false|title=China's Muslim Hui community: migration, settlement and sects|author=Michael Dillon|year=1999|publisher=Curzon Press|location=Richmond|page=34/208|isbn=0700710264|accessdate=2010-06-28}}</ref>

战争结束后，明朝将军蓝玉和傅友德[[阉割|阉割]]了380名蒙古和回族穆斯林俘虏。<ref>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?ei=MtPrTYuxL8Tk0QH-iNyrAQ&ct=result&id=CC5tAAAAMAAJ&q=lan+yu+fu+youde|title=Journal of Asian history, Volume 25|author=|editor=|year=1991|publisher=O. Harrassowitz.|edition=|location=|page=127|isbn=|pages=|accessdate=2011-06-06}}</ref>他们许多人成为[[太监|太监]]，服务明朝皇帝。<ref>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=Ka6jNJcX_ygC&pg=PA14&dq=lan+yu+yunnan+muslim&hl=en&ei=UVjMTLHaKIT6lwf05fjiCA&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=8&ved=0CE4Q6AEwBw#v=onepage&q=lan%20yu%20yunnan%20muslim&f=false|title=The eunuchs in the Ming dynasty|author=Shih-shan Henry Tsai|year=1996|publisher=SUNY Press|location=|isbn=0791426874|page=14|pages=|accessdate=2010-06-28}}</ref> 其中一名太监就是[[郑和|郑和]]。<ref>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=XULERYYEJo0C&pg=PA37&dq=lan+yu+yunnan+muslim&hl=en&ei=UVjMTLHaKIT6lwf05fjiCA&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=7&ved=0CEgQ6AEwBg#v=onepage&q=lan%20yu%20yunnan%20muslim&f=false|title=Islam in China|author=Shoujiang Mi, Jia You|year=2004|publisher=五洲传播出版社|location=|page=37|isbn=7508505336|pages=|accessdate=2010-06-28}}</ref>

汉人士兵也参与了战争。他们与汉族、[[苗族|苗族]]与[[瑶族|瑶族]]妇女的后代被称为“[[屯堡人|屯堡人]]”，以区别于在以后的几个世纪移民到云贵的汉人。屯堡人今天仍然生活在贵州、云南。<ref>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=IOM8qF34s4YC&pg=PA340&dq=han+soldiers+married+miao+women&hl=en&ei=e9LMTJi5FYOdlgfrn-WtBw&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=3&ved=0CDcQ6AEwAg#v=onepage&q=han%20soldiers%20married%20miao%20women&f=false|title=An ethnohistorical dictionary of China|author=James Stuart Olson|year=1998|publisher=Greenwood Publishing Group|location=|isbn=0313288534|page=340|pages=|accessdate=2010-06-28}}</ref>

== 评论 ==
[[朱元璋|朱元璋]]：那云南我本不征伐他，他却如常生边衅，以此无乃何去征他，调了二十二万军马，和余丁二十七万，平定之后带战亡逃病折了我五万兵，一万里远，接连着吐番一带，用热多军马去守又无益于中国，征伐之事盖出于不得已。<ref>《[[高丽史|高丽史]]》卷136 列传49 辛禑</ref>
