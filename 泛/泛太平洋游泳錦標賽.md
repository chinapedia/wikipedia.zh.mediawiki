'''泛太平洋游泳錦標賽'''（{{lang|en|'''Pan Pacific Swimming Championships'''}}）是由泛太平洋泳聯總會主辦的泛太平洋性[[游泳|游泳]]賽事，1985年起开始举行<ref name="SwimAus201205">[http://swimming.org.au/news/index.cfm?fuseaction=NewsItem&NewsID=25280 Queensland to host 2014 Pan Pacific Championships] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120511015233/http://swimming.org.au/news/index.cfm?fuseaction=NewsItem&NewsID=25280 |date=2012-05-11 }}, published by [[Swimming_Australia|Swimming Australia]] on 2012-05-11; retrieved 2012-05-11.</ref>，每2年舉行一屆，在1985年至1999年曾經每2年舉行一屆，在2002年開始恢復每4年舉行一屆。舉辦時間都在8月份<ref>[http://www.omegatiming.com/index_home.htm#swimming/racearchives/2010/IrvinePPchampionships/index_by_events.htm 2010 Pan Pacs results page] from Omega Timing. Retrieved 2012-05-11.</ref><ref>[http://www.omegatiming.com/index_home.htm#swimming/racearchives/2006/victoria_2006/index.htm 2006 Pan Pacs results page] from Omega Timing. Retrieved 2012-05-11.</ref><ref>[http://www.usaswimming.org/ViewMiscArticle.aspx?TabId=927&Alias=rainbow&Lang=en&mid=2687&ItemId=2143 2002 Pan Pacific Championships] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141219160805/http://www.usaswimming.org/ViewMiscArticle.aspx?TabId=927&Alias=rainbow&Lang=en&mid=2687&ItemId=2143 |date=2014-12-19 }} page from the USA Swimming website (usaswimming.org); retrieved 2012-05-11.</ref>。泛太泳赛由日、美、加、澳4国轮流主办，邀请一些其他国家选手参赛。俄罗斯选手从未受邀参赛。
