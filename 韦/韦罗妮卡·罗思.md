'''韦罗妮卡·罗思'''（'''Veronica Roth'''，{{bd|1988年|8月19日||}}）美国[[小说家|小说家]]和[[短篇小说|短篇小说]]作家，她的首部小说《[[分歧者_(小说)|分歧者]]》（Divergent）和《[[反叛者_(小说)|反叛者]]》（Insurgent）登上了[[纽约时报畅销书排行榜|纽约时报畅销书]]榜首。她的第三本书《[[忠实者_(小说)|忠实者]]》（Allegiant）2013年10月发行，《分歧者三部曲》完成。<ref name="Results for Favorite Book of 2011">{{cite web |url=http://www.goodreads.com/award/choice/2011#56593-Favorite-Book-of-2011 |title=Results for Favorite Book of 2011 |publisher=Goodreads.com}}</ref><ref name=Goodreads2>{{cite web |url=http://www.goodreads.com/choiceawards/best-young-adult-fantasy-books-2012#74593-Best-Young-Adult-Fantasy-&-Science-Fiction |title=Best Young Adult Fantasy & Science Fiction, 2012 award |publisher=Goodreads.com |accessdate=23 October 2013}}</ref>

== 生平 ==
罗思出生于纽约，在伊利诺斯州[[巴林顿|巴林顿]]长大。<ref name=evanstonref1>{{cite web |url=http://evanstonroundtable.com/main.asp?SectionID=4&SubSectionID=4&ArticleID=4435 |title='Divergent': A Bestseller Built in Evanston |author=Natalie Wainwright |date=5 July 2011}}</ref>母亲芭芭拉·罗思是一个画家，<ref name=evanstonref1/><ref>{{cite web |url=https://365barrington.com/2013/10/06/248-veronica-roth-divergent-trilogy-interview/ |title=365BarringtonTV Interviews DIVERGENT Author Veronica Roth |author=Lizluby |date=6 October 2013 |accessdate=23 October 2013}}</ref>她是三个孩子中最小的一个。她五岁时父母离异，母亲改嫁给一个金融顾问弗兰克·罗思<ref name=ChiTribune/><ref name=evanstonref1/>。她的哥哥和姐姐住在芝加哥地区。<ref name=evanstonref1/>她在巴林顿高中毕业后，<ref name="bar1">{{cite web |url=http://www.northwestern.edu/newscenter/stories/2010/05/roth.html |author=Leopold, Wendy |date=5 May 2010  |title=A Book Contract Already! Weinberg undergraduate gets a three-book deal from HarperCollins |publisher=Northwestern.edu |accessdate=29 August 2013}}</ref>进入卡尔顿学院，一年后转到[[西北大学|西北大学]]，<ref name=ChiTribune/><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.laprovinciadicremona.it/cultura/letture/le-novita-editoriali/veronica-roth-divergent-1.176008 |title=Veronica Roth - 'Divergent' (Italian) (|url=http://translate.google.com/translate?hl=en&sl=it&tl=en&u=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.laprovinciadicremona.it%2Fcultura%2Fletture%2Fle-novita-editoriali%2Fveronica-roth-divergent-1.176008)}}{{dead link|date=October 2013}}</ref>开始写作计划。现与丈夫摄影师纳尔逊居住在芝加哥地区。<ref name=":0">{{cite web|last=Bock|first=Cathy and David|title=Veronica and Nelson Married |url=http://cathydavidphoto.com/chicago-photographer-weddings/evanston-illinois-wedding-photography-womens-club |publisher=Cathy and David Photographers}}</ref> 

罗思以她的小说三部曲:《[[分歧者_(小说)|分歧者]]》（Divergent）、《[[反叛者_(小说)|反叛者]]》（Insurgent）、《[[忠实者_(小说)|忠实者]]》（Allegiant）出名，最后一部于2013年10月22日发表。<ref name="Veronica Roth 'Divergent' video reveal: Book 3 is ...">{{cite web|url=http://www.usatoday.com/story/life/books/2013/04/17/veronica-roth-divergent-reveal-video/2091139/ |author=USA Today|title=Veronica Roth 'Divergent' video reveal: Book 3 is ... |publisher=''USA Today'' |date=April 17, 2013 |accessdate=10 July 2012}}</ref>以此她获得读书社交网站[[Goodreads|Goodreads]]“Goodreads 2011年选择奖”和2012年最佳年轻成人科幻类小说以及「Goodreads 2012年最佳作者」。<ref name="Results for Favorite Book of 2011"/><ref name=Goodreads2/><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.goodreads.com/choiceawards/best-goodreads-author-2012#74595-Best-Goodreads-Author |title=Best Goodreads Author in 2012 |publisher=Goodreads.com |accessdate=23 October 2013}}</ref> 她在[[西北大学|西北大学]]大四寒假时写出《[[分歧者_(小说)|分歧者]]》第一部，<ref name=ChiTribune>{{cite web |url=http://articles.chicagotribune.com/2013-10-21/entertainment/chi-veronica-roth-profile-20131021_1_veronica-roth-allegiant-anderson |title=The next YA superstar? |author=Christopher Borrelli |publisher=Chicago Tribune |date=22 October 2013 |accessdate=23 October 2013}}</ref>受到好评，第一部小说的成功使她名利双收。<ref name="Truitt, Brian">{{cite web |url=http://books.usatoday.com/bookbuzz/post/2012-03-29/exclusive-trailer-and-q38a-insurgent-by-veronica-roth/659768/1 |author=Truitt, Brian|title=Exclusive trailer and interview: 'Insurgent' by Veronica Roth |publisher=''USA Today'' |date=March 29, 2012 |accessdate=12 April 2012}}</ref><ref name="nytimes1">{{cite web |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2011/05/15/books/review/young-adult-books-divergent-by-veronica-roth.html?_r=1 |title=In This Dystopia, Teens Must Choose Wisely |publisher=''The New York Times'' |date=May 12, 2011 |accessdate=April 12, 2012 |author=Dominus, Susan}}</ref>

罗思将《分歧者》电影版权卖给了[[顶峰娱乐|顶峰娱乐]]，<ref name="Crowder, Courtney 2012">{{cite web | url=http://www.chicagotribune.com/entertainment/books/ct-books-0423-veronica-roth-20110422,0,4974900.story | title=Chicago novelist sells film rights | publisher=[http://www.chicagotribune.com ''Chicago Tribune''] |year=2012 |accessdate=April 13, 2012 |author=Crowder, Courtney}}</ref><ref name="Screencrave.com">{{cite web |url=http://screencrave.com/2011-07-21/comic-con-2011-veronica-roth-from-divergent-press-conference/ |title=Comic-Con 2011: Veronica Roth from Divergent Press Conference |publisher=Screencrave.com |author=Mali Elfman |date=2011-07-21 |accessdate=2012-03-10 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111208130947/http://screencrave.com/2011-07-21/comic-con-2011-veronica-roth-from-divergent-press-conference/ |archivedate=2011-12-08 }}</ref><ref name="Kung, Michelle">{{cite web |url=http://blogs.wsj.com/speakeasy/2011/07/23/divergent-author-veronica-roth-on-her-film-adaptation/ | title=‘Divergent’ Author Veronica Roth on Her Film Adaptation | publisher=[http://online.wsj.com/home-page ''The Wall Street Journal''] |date=July 23, 2011 | accessdate=13 April 2012 |author=Kung, Michelle}}</ref>[[分歧者|分歧者]]拍成电影後，在2014年3月的首映票房达到5600万美元，高居北美周末票房榜首位。
