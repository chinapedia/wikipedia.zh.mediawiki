'''热带风暴阿尔玛'''（{{lang-en|Tropical Storm Alma}}）是有纪录以来形成位置最偏东面的[[太平洋飓风季|东太平洋]][[热带气旋|热带气旋]]，于2008年5月29日在[[哥斯达黎加|哥斯达黎加]]近海的[[季风槽|季风槽]]内发展形成，是[[2008年太平洋飓风季|2008年太平洋飓风季]]首场获得命名的风暴。气象机构起初预计系统会维持在弱热带风暴强度，但气旋却[[快速增强|快速增强]]并发展出[[风眼|风眼]]，以[[最大持续风速|持续风速]]每小时100公里的最高强度从[[莱昂_(尼加拉瓜)|莱昂]]附近[[登陆_(气象学)|登陆]][[尼加拉瓜|尼加拉瓜]]，成为有纪录以来首场吹袭该国太平洋海岸的热带风暴。哥斯达黎加降下暴雨，引发洪灾和山体滑坡，造成两人死亡，经济损失约3500万美元。尼加拉瓜有3人丧生，其中1人溺毙，2人触电身亡。[[洪都拉斯|洪都拉斯]]还有5人因一次很可能由风暴引起的航空事故丧生，另有1人被洪水卷走导致遇难。

== 气象历史 ==
{{storm path|Alma 2008 track.png}}
2008年5月末，多个[[热带气旋预测模型|计算机飓风模型]]预测[[中美洲|中美洲]]西南方向海域会发展出大规模[[低气压|低气压区]]<ref name="etwd526">{{cite report | author = Nelson | year = 2008 | title = May 26 Tropical Weather Discussion for the Eastern Pacific Ocean | publisher = National Hurricane Center}}</ref>。5月26日，[[加勒比海|加勒比海]]有大型[[低压槽|低压槽]]经[[哥斯达黎加|哥斯达黎加]]一直延伸到东[[太平洋|太平洋]]，形成广阔的低气压区<ref name="atwd526">{{cite report | author = Berg | year = 2008 | title = May 26 Tropical Weather Discussion for the Atlantic Ocean | publisher = National Hurricane Center}}</ref>。系统发展出强烈但零散的[[对流|对流]]，并且有部分同[[热带辐合带|热带辐合带]]相关联<ref name="etwd526" />。由于所在海域的转向气流很微弱，这股[[热带扰动|扰动天气]]基本没有移动，其降雨带于5月27日开始组织<ref>{{cite report | author = Franklin/Landsea | year = 2008 | title = May 27 Tropical Weather Outlook | publisher = National Hurricane Center}}</ref>。系统内起初存在多个气旋式漩涡，其中最强劲的一个位于哥斯达黎加[[圣荷西_(哥斯达黎加)|圣荷西]]西南偏西方向约550公里洋面<ref>{{cite report | author = Nelson | year = 2008 | title = May 27 Tropical Weather Discussion for the Eastern Pacific Ocean | publisher = National Hurricane Center}}</ref>。系统的组织结构逐渐改善，发展出层次分明的环流和对流结构，[[国家飓风中心|美国国家飓风中心]]因此于[[协调世界时|协调世界时]]5月29日凌晨3点将位于哥斯达黎加[[卡沃布兰科自然保护区|卡沃布兰科]]（{{lang|en|Cabo Blanco}}）[[罗盘方位|西北偏西]]方向约165公里海域的系统归类为第一{{lang|en|E}}号热带低气压<ref name="disc1">{{cite web | author = Franklin/Rhome | year = 2008 | title = Tropical Depression One-E Discussion One | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2015-08-07 | archivedate = 2014-01-06 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140106070202/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2008/ep01/ep012008.discus.001.shtml | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2008/ep01/ep012008.discus.001.shtml? | deadurl = no}}</ref>。

受[[墨西哥湾|墨西哥湾]]的中层[[高压脊|高压脊]]影响，低气压总体保持北上，进入[[海面温度|水温]]较高，[[风切变|风切变]]很少的海域。气旋内的对流起初很少，并且稀少的[[雨带|雨带]]也远离中心。气象机构据此认为，系统强度将保持在弱热带风暴标准。<ref name="disc1" />但是，低气压很快在中心附近发展出强烈[[雷暴|雷暴]]，还有雨带在系统南部半圆内增长，{{lang|en|[[协调世界时|UTC]]}}5月29日下午15点，美国国家飓风中心将位于尼加拉瓜[[马纳瓜|马纳瓜]]西南方向约85公里洋面的低气压升级成热带风暴，并以“阿尔玛”（{{lang|en|Alma}}）为其命名。虽然之前的预测中认为气旋[[登陆_(气象学)|登陆]]前只会有小幅强化，但风暴这时的风速已经达到每小时75公里。<ref>{{cite web | author = Avila/Roberts | year = 2008 | title = Tropical Storm Alma Discussion Three | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2015-08-07 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140106051940/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2008/ep01/ep012008.discus.003.shtml | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2008/ep01/ep012008.discus.003.shtml? | archivedate = 2014-01-06}}</ref>美国国家飓风中心还在将阿尔玛升级成热带风暴1小时后根据卫星图像和{{lang|en|[[QuikSCAT|QuikSCAT]]}}（意为快速散射仪）卫星数据宣布，阿尔玛的风力时速已达到100公里<ref name="disc4">{{cite web | author = Avila | year = 2008 | title = Tropical Storm Alma Special Discussion Four | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2015-08-07 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140106072116/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2008/ep01/ep012008.discus.004.shtml | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2008/ep01/ep012008.discus.004.shtml? | archivedate = 2014-01-06 | deadurl = no}}</ref><ref name="AlmaTCR">{{cite web | author = Brown | year = 2008 | title = Tropical Cyclone Report: Tropical Storm Alma | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2012-10-21 | archivedate = 2012-10-21 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121021090155/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/pdf/TCR-EP012008_Alma.pdf | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/pdf/TCR-EP012008_Alma.pdf}}</ref>。气旋中有[[风眼|眼状特征]]形成，周围环绕着非常紧密的环状对流<ref name="disc5">{{cite web | author = Avila/Roberts | year = 2008 | title = Tropical Storm Alma Discussion Five | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2015-08-07 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2008/ep01/ep012008.discus.005.shtml? | archivedate = 2014-01-06 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140106062828/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2008/ep01/ep012008.discus.005.shtml | deadurl = no}}</ref>，于{{lang|en|UTC}}5月29日晚上19点以强烈热带风暴强度从[[莱昂_(尼加拉瓜)|莱昂]]附近登陆尼加拉瓜<ref name="pa5">{{cite web | author = Avila/Roberts | year = 2008 | title = Tropical Storm Alma Public Advisory Five | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2015-08-07 | archivedate = 2014-01-06 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140106040330/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2008/ep01/ep012008.public.005.shtml | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2008/ep01/ep012008.public.005.shtml?}}</ref>。阿尔玛登岸后迅速减弱，不过经过[[洪都拉斯|洪都拉斯]]南部山区后还是有小规模雷暴区存在<ref name="disc6">{{cite web | author = Knabb/Blake | year = 2008 | title = Tropical Storm Alma Discussion Six | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2015-08-07 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2008/ep01/ep012008.discus.006.shtml? | archivedate = 2014-01-06}}</ref>，最终于{{lang|en|UTC}}5月30日下午15点在洪都拉斯和[[危地马拉|危地马拉]]边境附近退化成低气压区<ref>{{cite web | author = Avila | year = 2008 | title = Tropical Depression Alma Discussion Eight | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2015-08-07 | archivedate = 2014-01-06 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140106044643/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2008/ep01/ep012008.discus.008.shtml | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2008/ep01/ep012008.discus.008.shtml?}}</ref>。5月31日，低气压区穿越中美洲进入[[洪都拉斯湾|洪都拉斯湾]]，在这里催生出[[2008年热带风暴亚瑟|热带风暴亚瑟]]<ref name="Alma becomes Arthur">{{cite web | author = Avila/Rhome | year = 2008 | title = Tropical Storm Arthur Special Discussion One | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2008-05-31 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2008/al01/al012008.discus.001.shtml? | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140106072119/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2008/al01/al012008.discus.001.shtml | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2014-01-06}}</ref>。

== 防灾措施和影响 ==
[[File:Alma_Arthur_(2008)_TRMM_rain_27may-4june08.jpg|right]]
哥斯达黎加政府在美国国家飓风中心发布针对低气压的首份公告时向全国太平洋海岸地区发布[[热带气旋警告|热带风暴警告]]<ref>{{cite web | author = Franklin/Rhome | year = 2008 | title = Tropical Depression One-E Public Advisory One | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2015-08-07 | archivedate = 2014-12-22 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20141222071817/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2008/ep01/ep012008.public.001.shtml | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2008/ep01/ep012008.public.001.shtml?}}</ref>。风暴在登陆约4小时前获名阿尔玛，哥斯达黎加、尼加拉瓜、洪都拉斯和[[萨尔瓦多|萨尔瓦多]]4国的所有沿海地区都进入热带风暴警告生效状态<ref name="pa3">{{cite web | author = Avila/Roberts | year = 2008 | title = Tropical Storm Alma Public Advisory Three | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2015-08-07 | archivedate = 2014-12-22 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20141222082818/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2008/ep01/ep012008.public.003.shtml | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2008/ep01/ep012008.public.003.shtml? | deadurl = no}}</ref>。接下来风暴的发展迹象表明阿尔玛的强度会远高于前期预测，尼加拉瓜和洪都拉斯海岸因此进入飓风警告生效状态<ref name="pa4">{{cite web | author = Avila | year = 2008 | title = Tropical Storm Alma Special Public Advisory Four | publisher = National Hurricane Center | accessdate = 2014-01-06 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2008/ep01/ep012008.public.004.shtml? | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140106074747/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2008/ep01/ep012008.public.004.shtml | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2014-01-06}}</ref>，气象部门一度预计风暴会升级成飓风<ref name="disc4" />。美国国家飓风中心在风暴登岸前发出警告，称系统产生的降水可能高达500毫米，导致[[泥石流|泥石流]]和山洪爆发<ref name="pa3" />。哥斯达黎加国家紧急情况委员会在气旋来袭前启用应急避难所，[[帕里托县|帕里托县]]有250人被迫逃離家园<ref name="elpais">{{cite news | author = El País | date = 2008-05-30 | title = Alma se disipa y deja atrás dos muertos y cientos de evacuados en Centroamérica | accessdate = 2011-08-11 | archivedate = 2011-08-11 | deadurl = yes | url = http://www.elpais.com/articulo/internacional/Alma/disipa/deja/muertos/cientos/evacuados/Centroamerica/elpepuint/20080530elpepuint_9/Tes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20110811054443/http://www.elpais.com/articulo/internacional/Alma/disipa/deja/muertos/cientos/evacuados/Centroamerica/elpepuint/20080530elpepuint_9/Tes}}</ref>。尼加拉瓜官员疏散了约5000人，还在风暴过后动员3000名军人协助救灾<ref name="elnuevo">{{cite news | author = Filadelfo Aleman | date = 2008-05-29 | title = Tormenta tropical Alma golpea Centroamérica | publisher = El Nuevo Herald | accessdate = 2015-08-07 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150807085118/http://www.nacion.com/mundo/Tormenta-tropical-Alma-golpea-Centroamerica_0_979302080.html | url = http://www.nacion.com/mundo/Tormenta-tropical-Alma-golpea-Centroamerica_0_979302080.html | via = La Nación | archivedate = 2015-08-07}}, [http://www.informador.com.mx/internacional/2008/15395/6/tormenta-tropical-alma-golpea-centroamerica.htm 由El Informador提供的另一链接]</ref>。

中美洲各地在阿尔玛登陆后普降暴雨，[[巴拿马|巴拿马]]西部的[[戴维_(巴拿马)|戴维市]]测得的48小时降雨量有141毫米。哥斯达黎加首都[[圣荷西_(哥斯达黎加)|圣荷西]]的48小时降雨量也有78毫米。<ref name="twd529">{{cite report | author = Berg | year = 2008 | title = May 29 Atlantic Tropical Weather Discussion | publisher = National Hurricane Center}}</ref>哥斯达黎加因降雨引起河流洪灾，对17个社区构成威胁。降水还引发大规模泥石流，造成至少8条道路封闭。风暴刮倒树木，还令输电线缆中断，该国有约4万2000人失去供电。<ref name="elpais" />哥斯达黎加有两人死亡，经济损失估计有200亿[[哥斯达黎加科朗|哥斯达黎加科朗]]（相当于2008年的3500万[[美元|美元]]）<ref>{{cite web|author=Werner Stolz and Gabriela Chinchilla |publisher=Instituto Meteorólogico Nacional |title=Tormenta Tropical Alma y sus efectos en Costa Rica (27-30 de mayo de 2008) |accessdate=2015-08-07 |url=http://www.imn.ac.cr/publicaciones/ttalma.PDF |format=PDF |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120216121013/http://www.imn.ac.cr/publicaciones/ttalma.PDF |archivedate=2012-02-16 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。

阿尔玛从莱昂登陆尼加拉瓜，该市在风暴过境期间停电，多幢建筑被毁，部分道路受损。狂风导致[[莱昂省_(尼加拉瓜)|莱昂省]]和[[奇南德加省|奇南德加省]]大部分地区断电。高压电缆被风暴刮断，致使1人触电身亡<ref name="elpais" />，还有1人也因类似事故遇难<ref name="UK reuters">{{cite news | title = Tropical Storm Alma dies out over Central America | publisher = Reuters UK | url = http://uk.reuters.com/article/homepageCrisis/idUKN30448773._CH_.242020080531 | date = 2008-05-31 | accessdate = 2009-06-01 | first = Mica | last = Rosenberg | deadurl = yes | archivedate = 2009-06-01 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20090601215146/http://uk.reuters.com/article/homepageCrisis/idUKN30448773._CH_.242020080531}}</ref>。海上有1人因小船遇上风暴而溺毙<ref>{{cite news | author = Agence France-Presse | date = 2008-05-29 | title = Un muerto y daños por paso de tormenta "Alma" | accessdate = 2014-01-06 | archivedate = 2014-01-06 | deadurl = yes | url = http://www.elnuevodiario.com.ni/nacionales/17054 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140106033113/http://www.elnuevodiario.com.ni/nacionales/17054}}</ref>。{{lang|en|TACA}}航空公司390号班机在[[德古斯加巴|德古斯加巴]]机场因地面湿滑冲出跑道，机上3名乘客和地面2人死亡，另有80余人受伤<ref name="AlmaTCR" />。据报道，[[洪都拉斯|洪都拉斯]]还有一名青年女子被汹涌的激流卷走而遇难<ref name="AlmaTCR" />。

== 纪录和退役 ==
[[File:Alma_29_May_08.jpg|right]]
热带风暴阿尔玛在[[经度|西经]]86.5<sup>o</sup>海域形成，是有纪录以来形成位置最偏东面的[[太平洋飓风季|太平洋热带气旋]]<ref name="disc1" />。截至2014年，除从大西洋进入太平洋的天气系统外，另外只有7个系统是在西经90<sup>o</sup>以东发展形成<ref name="hurdat">{{cite web | author = National Hurricane Center | author2 = Hurricane Research Division | author3 = Central Pacific Hurricane Center | publisher = United States National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration's National Weather Service | date = 2014-07-07 | accessdate = 2015-08-03 | title = The Northeast and North Central Pacific hurricane database 1949–2013 | archivedate = 2014-10-05 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20141005025406/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/data/hurdat/hurdat2-nencpac-1949-2013-070714.txt | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/data/hurdat/hurdat2-nencpac-1949-2013-070714.txt | deadurl = no}} A guide on how to read the database is available [http://www.aoml.noaa.gov/hrd/hurdat/tracks-hurdat2-epac-format-apr13.pdf here].</ref>。阿尔玛的登陆点比其它任何太平洋热带气旋都更偏东面，也是唯一登陆尼加拉瓜太平洋海岸的热带气旋<ref name="disc6" />。风暴于5月29日形成，[[2008年太平洋飓风季|2008年]]由此成为连续第9个有热带气旋在东太平洋形成的年份，刷新又一项纪录<ref name="hurdat" />。阿尔玛的残留促使大西洋的热带风暴亚瑟形成，但由于阿尔玛的环流一度消散，所以两者从严格意义上不属同一系统<ref name="Alma becomes Arthur" />。阿尔玛还是最后一场从太平洋进入大西洋的热带气旋，之前一次出现这种情况则要追溯到[[1989年太平洋飓风季|1989年]]，[[1989年飓风科斯梅|飓风科斯梅]]的残留进入大西洋后发展成[[1989年热带风暴阿利森|热带风暴阿利森]]<ref name="crossovers">{{cite web | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150701170816/http://www.aoml.noaa.gov/hrd/tcfaq/E15.html | archivedate = 2015-07-01 | url = http://www.aoml.noaa.gov/hrd/tcfaq/E15.html | author = Stephen Caparotta, D. Walston, Steven Yound, & Gary Padgett | publisher = Hurricane Research Division, Atlantic Oceanographic and Meteorological Laboratory | work = Frequently Asked Questions| accessdate = 2015-08-07 | title = Subject: E15) What tropical storms and hurricanes have moved from the Atlantic to the Northeast Pacific or vice versa?}}</ref>。

虽然风暴造成的人员伤亡和财产损失都不算高，但[[世界气象组织|世界气象组织]]还是将其名称“阿尔玛”退役，以“阿曼达”（{{lang|en|Amanda}}）替换，新名称于2014年首度采用。阿尔玛由此成为继1965年的[[1965年热带风暴黑兹尔|热带风暴黑兹尔]]以来首场因造成破坏导致名称退役的热带风暴。<ref>{{cite web | author = World Meteorological Organization | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150803100143/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/aboutnames.shtml | publisher = National Hurricane Center | date = 2009-04-22 | accessdate = 2009-04-22 | title = Worldwide Tropical Cyclone Names | deadurl = no | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/aboutnames.shtml | archivedate = 2015-08-03}}</ref><ref name="hurricaneOP">{{cite web | publisher = World Meteorological Organization | year = 2008 | accessdate = 2015-08-07 | archivedate = 2012-02-14 | deadurl = no | format = PDF | title = RA IV Hurricane Operational Plan | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120214023939/http://www.wmo.int/pages/prog/www/tcp/documents/TCP30_2008edition_EN_withoutRadarMaps.pdf | url = http://www.wmo.int/pages/prog/www/tcp/documents/TCP30_2008edition_EN_withoutRadarMaps.pdf}}</ref>。
