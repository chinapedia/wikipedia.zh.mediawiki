'''蘇丹航空139號班機空難'''是[[蘇丹航空|蘇丹航空]]一班的來回[[蘇丹港|蘇丹港]]至[[喀土穆|喀土穆]]定期航班，2003年7月8日，一架[[波音737|波音737]]-200C客機起飛約15分鐘後，[[發動機|發動機]]功率下降，導致飛行員試圖返回出發地機場緊急迫降，之後飛行員錯過機場跑道，飛機墜毀於[[蘇丹港|蘇丹港]]，機上有117名乘客遇難<ref>{{ASN accident|id= 20030708-0|accessdate= 2011-12-19}}</ref>。

==乘客==
機上有117名乘客遇難，大多是[[蘇丹人|蘇丹人]]<ref name="116 Are Killed in Plane Crash in Sudan; a Small Boy Survives">{{cite news|title= 116 Are Killed in Plane Crash in Sudan; a Small Boy Survives|publisher= [[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|date= 2003-7-9|url= http://www.nytimes.com/2003/07/09/world/116-are-killed-in-plane-crash-in-sudan-a-small-boy-survives.html|archiveurl= http://www.webcitation.org/68qEoSeWn?url=http://www.nytimes.com/2003/07/09/world/116-are-killed-in-plane-crash-in-sudan-a-small-boy-survives.html|archivedate= 2012-07-01|deadurl= yes}}</ref>，另外有三個[[印度人|印度人]]，一個[[英國人|英國人]]，一個[[中國人|中國人]]，一個[[阿拉伯聯合大公國人|阿拉伯聯合大公國人]]和[[埃塞俄比亞人|埃塞俄比亞人]]<ref name="115 killed in Sudanese plane crash">{{cite news|title= 115 killed in Sudanese plane crash|first= Mohamed Ali|last= Saeed|publisher= Middle East Online|date= {{date|2003-7-8}}|url= http://www.middle-east-online.com/english/?id=6289|archiveurl= http://www.webcitation.org/68qH2Cva7?url=http://www.middle-east-online.com/english/?id=6289|archivedate= 2012-07-01|deadurl= yes}}</ref>。
