'''蘇里南國家足球隊'''（'''Suriname national football team'''）是[[南美洲|南美洲]]國家[[蘇里南|蘇里南]]的國家足球代表隊，由[[蘇里南足球協會|蘇里南足球協會]]管理，是[[中北美洲及加勒比海足球協會|中北美洲及加勒比海足球協會]]的成員。蘇里南前身是[[荷蘭|荷蘭]]的[[殖民地|殖民地]]，在1975年前的代表隊稱為'''荷屬圭亞那足球代表隊'''。

雖然[[蘇里南|蘇里南]]地理上處於[[南美洲|南美洲]]北部，但蘇里南國家隊卻不是[[南美洲足球協會|南美洲足球協會]]的成員。

儘管蘇里南外戰成績不突出, 但該國出产多名优质球员，部分球员并且入选[[荷蘭国家足球队|荷蘭国家足球队]]，包括[[古烈治|古烈治]]、[[列卡特|列卡特]]、[[施多夫|施多夫]]及[[戴維斯|戴維斯]]

Although the former [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[colony|colony]] (named Dutch Guyana, until 1975) is located in [[South_America|South America]], it competes in [[CONCACAF|CONCACAF]], together with [[Guyana_national_football_team|Guyana]] and [[French_Guiana_national_football_team|French Guiana]]. Suriname won the CFU Championship (Digicel Caribbean Cup predecessor) in 1977, were runners-up in 1979  and reached fourth place in 1994 and 1996. Unlike in the [[Netherlands_Antilles|Netherlands Antilles]], another former Dutch colony, it is ruled that players who move abroad to play in the Netherlands are not eligible to play for Suriname.<ref>[http://www.fifa.com/worldcup/news/newsid=692090.html#surinames+little+secret Suriname's little secret] - FIFA.com</ref> Many Suriname-born players and Dutch-born players of Surinamese descent, like [[Ruud_Gullit|Ruud Gullit]], [[Frank_Rijkaard|Frank Rijkaard]], [[Edgar_Davids|Edgar Davids]], [[Clarence_Seedorf|Clarence Seedorf]], [[Aron_Winter|Aron Winter]], [[Romeo_Castelen|Romeo Castelen]], and [[Jimmy_Floyd_Hasselbaink|Jimmy Floyd Hasselbaink]] turned out to play for [[Netherlands_national_football_team|''Oranje'']]. In 2008 they have gotten off to their best start and will now enter the group stage of CONCACAF World Cup qualifying despite using only local players. With their shock 2-leg victory over up and coming Guyana they have now earned home dates with some CONCACAF "powers" including Costa Rica.-->
