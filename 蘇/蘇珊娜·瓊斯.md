'''蘇珊娜·瑪夏特·瓊斯'''（{{lang-en|'''Susannah Mushatt Jones'''}}，{{bd|1899年|7月6日|2016年|5月12日|catIdx=J}}）<ref>{{cite web|title=Validated Living Supercentenarians|url=http://www.grg.org/Adams/TableE.html|website=[[Gerontology_Research_Group|Gerontology Research Group]]|publisher=Gerontology Research Group|accessdate=October 8, 2015}}</ref>，[[美国|美国]][[超級人瑞|超級人瑞]]，終年116歲311天。她和意大利人瑞[[艾瑪·莫拉諾|艾瑪·莫拉諾]]曾是最後兩位出生於1890年代的在世者<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.theaustralian.com.au/news/health-science/worlds-oldest-known-person-jeralean-talley-dies-at-116/story-e6frg8y6-1227405277595/ |title=World's oldest known person Jeralean Talley dies|publisher=theaustralian|accessdate=2015-06-21}}</ref>。她收到了[[美國眾議院|美國眾議院]]<ref>{{cite web|last=[[Edolphus_Towns|Edolphus Towns]]|title=A TRIBUTE TO SUSANNAH MUSHATT JONES ON HER 113TH BIRTHDAY -- HON. EDOLPHUS TOWNS (Extensions of Remarks - July 11, 2012)|url=http://thomas.loc.gov/cgi-bin/query/z?r112:E11JY2-0042:/|publisher=Congressional Record 112th Congress (2011-2012)|accessdate=June 13, 2013}}</ref>和[[亚拉巴马州众议院|亚拉巴马州众议院]]送來的禮物表彰她“生存了三個世紀，成就了一個非凡的一生。”<ref name=AL>{{cite news|last=Representative Jackson |title=COMMENDING SUSANNAH MUSHATT JONES FOR OUTSTANDING ACHIEVEMENTS. |url=http://www.legislature.state.al.us/searchableinstruments/2008RS/Resolutions/HR372.htm |accessdate=June 13, 2013 |newspaper=[[Alabama_Legislature|The Alabama Legislature]] |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140106040825/http://www.legislature.state.al.us/searchableinstruments/2008RS/Resolutions/HR372.htm |archivedate=一月 6, 2014 }}</ref>

==生平==
蘇珊娜·馬斯哈特在1899年7月6日生於[[朗兹县_(亚拉巴马州)|朗兹县]]，她是父母11個孩子中排行老三，也是最年長的女兒<ref name= Jones/><ref name=AL/><ref name=nytimes>{{cite news|first=Michaelle |last=Bond |title=Oldest Woman in New York Celebrates Birthday No. 114 |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2013/07/13/nyregion/oldest-woman-in-new-york-celebrates-birthday-no-114.html?ref=nyregion&_r=0 |work=[[New_York_Times|New York Times]] |publisher= |date=July 12, 2013 |accessdate=July 12, 2013}}</ref><ref name=Waxman>{{cite news |last1=Waxman |first1=Olivia B. |title=Long-Life Secrets From An (Almost) 115 Year Old Woman |url=http://time.com/2873247/second-oldest-american-115-birthday/ |accessdate=July 8, 2014 |publisher=''[[Time_(magazine)|Time]]'' |date=July 3, 2014 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6QvJaNzud?url=http://time.com/2873247/second-oldest-american-115-birthday/ |archivedate=2014-07-08 |deadurl=no }}</ref>。馬斯哈特的家庭是[[非裔美國人|非裔美國人]]和[[佃農|佃農]]，他們三代都在同一個田地耕種（她的祖母是一個[[美國奴隸制度|奴隸]]，並活了117歲）<ref>[http://yellowhammernews.com/faithandculture/the-oldest-living-person-on-earth-has-one-wish-i-want-to-go-home-to-alabama/ "The oldest living person on Earth has one wish: 'I want to go home' to Alabama", by Cliff Sims, YellowHammer.com]</ref>。根據馬斯哈特的家庭說，她的家族也擁有美國原住民血統<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nbcnews.com/news/us-news/worlds-oldest-person-susannah-mushatt-jones-celebrates-116th-birthday-brooklyn-n387571|title=World's Oldest Person Susannah Mushatt Jones Celebrates 116th Birthday in Brooklyn|work=nbcnews.com}}</ref>。馬斯哈特自幼務農，但她決心擺脫這種艱辛的生活<ref name=AL/>。1922年3月4日，馬斯哈特高中畢業；根據高中畢業名冊記載，她在學校時研修了「法國的黑人音樂」這一科<ref name=Waxman/>。畢業後，馬斯哈特立志成為一位[[教師|教師]]<ref name=Waxman/>，並被[[塔斯基吉大學|塔斯基吉大學]]師範科錄取。可是馬斯哈特的家人無力替她支付學費，所以1923年，她到[[紐約|紐約]][[哈莱姆文艺复兴|哈莱姆文艺复兴]]成為初級[[助教|助教]]<ref name=AL/><ref name=NYT>{{cite news|last=Flengenheimer|first=Matt|title=A Milestone in Brooklyn: 112 Birthdays|url=http://cityroom.blogs.nytimes.com/2011/07/20/a-milestone-in-brooklyn-112-birthdays/|accessdate=June 13, 2013|newspaper=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=Underwood|first=Khalea and Bill Hutchinson|title=Susannah Mushatte Jones is the oldest person in New York City and still going strong at 113|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/mushatte-jones-oldest-person-new-york-city-strong-113-article-1.1113496|accessdate=June 13, 2013|newspaper=[[New_York_Daily_News|New York Daily News]]}}</ref>。

馬斯哈特在1928年嫁給了亨利·瓊斯，但夫婦倆在1933年離婚。她在2011年透露「我不知道他做甚麼工作」<ref>{{cite news|title=A Milestone in Brooklyn: 112 Birthdays|url=http://cityroom.blogs.nytimes.com/2011/07/20/a-milestone-in-brooklyn-112-birthdays/|accessdate=June 13, 2013}}</ref>。瓊斯沒有孩子，後來她在富裕的家庭幫忙照顧他們的孩子們（一週7[[美元|美元]]的薪水）<ref name=Parikh/>。在這時候，瓊斯支持她的家人遷至[[紐約|紐約]]<ref name=Waxman/><ref name=NYT/><ref name=Staff>{{cite news |last1=Staff |title=Oldest Person In New York Celebrates 114th Birthday |url=http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2013/07/12/oldest-person-in-new-york-celebrates-114th-birthday/ |accessdate=July 8, 2014 |publisher=[[WCBS-TV|WCBS-TV]] |date=July 12, 2013 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6QvMztash?url=http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2013/07/12/oldest-person-in-new-york-celebrates-114th-birthday/ |archivedate=2014-07-08 |deadurl=no }}</ref><ref name=Akhtar>{{cite news |last1=Akhtar |first1=Sam |title=113-Year-Old Celebrates Her Birthday At Vandalia Center |url=http://www.canarsiecourier.com/news/2012-07-19/Other_News/113YearOld_Celebrates_Her_Birthday_At_Vandalia_Cen.html |accessdate=July 8, 2014 |newspaper=Canarsie Courier |date=July 19, 2012 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6QvOirLfv?url=http://www.canarsiecourier.com/news/2012-07-19/Other_News/113YearOld_Celebrates_Her_Birthday_At_Vandalia_Cen.html |archivedate=2014年7月8日 |deadurl=yes |df= }}</ref>。瓊斯會使用一些她的薪水去建立卡爾霍恩學校（一間書院專門給[[非裔美國人|非裔美國兒童]]報讀）<ref name=Waxman/>。30年內，瓊斯在那間學校經常出現<ref name=Waxman/><ref name=Parikh/> 。1965年，瓊斯在[[退休|退休]]後和她的侄女一起居住。瓊斯替她的侄女照顧她的嬰兒孩子。瓊斯在過世時，她有超過100個侄女。<ref name=Staff/>

==健康，習慣和生活方式==
瓊斯是一名[[失明|失明]]人士且患有部分[[失聰|失聰]]；她不能說太多話且使用[[輪椅|輪椅]]<ref name=NYT/><ref name=Staff/>。瓊斯只需要使用[[高血壓|高血壓]]藥物和一瓶多重[[維生素|維生素]]<ref name=Waxman/>。在100歲時，瓊斯成為法律上的[[失明|失明]]人士。瓊斯拒絶承認自己曾經接受[[白内障|白内障]]手術、使用[[起搏器|起搏器]]以及承認自己沒有做過[[乳房攝影術|乳房攝影術]]和{{link-en|腸鏡|Colonoscopy}}<ref name=Staff/>。瓊斯需要一年三至四次和一名{{link-en|初級保健醫生|primary care physician}}會面<ref name=Waxman/>。瓊斯從不[[吸煙|吸煙]]及[[酗酒|酗酒]]<ref name=Staff/>，她每天睡10個小時且每天都會打盹兒<ref name=Waxman/>。瓊斯會以四片[[煙肉|煙肉]]、炒[[雞蛋|雞蛋]]和糝作為她的[[早餐|早餐]]<ref>{{cite news|url=http://time.com/2873247/second-oldest-american-115-birthday/|title=Susannah Mushatt Jones Is Second Oldest American|author=Olivia B. Waxman|work=TIME.com}}</ref>，而且每天都保持這個習慣。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/entry/worlds-oldest-person-bacon_56148d09e4b0cf9984d7a0eb|title=Susannah Mushatt Jones, World's Oldest Person, Eats Bacon Every Day|publisher=''[[The_Huffington_Post|The Huffington Post]]''|date=October 7, 2015|accessdate=October 8, 2015}}</ref>

==晚年及去世==
瓊斯在人生中最後五年在[[布魯克林區|布魯克林區]]渡過<ref name=nytimes/><ref name=Parikh>{{cite news|last1=Parikh|first1=Sapna|title=Brooklyn Woman 'Miss Susie' Celebrates 115th Birthday|url=http://7online.com/family/brooklyn-woman-celebrates-115th-birthday/169479/|accessdate=July 9, 2014|publisher=[[WABC-TV|WABC-TV]]|date=July 8, 2014}}</ref><ref name=Staff/><ref name=Akhtar/><ref name="Daily Mail">{{cite news |title=World's oldest person turns 116: Brooklyn woman who was the granddaughter of slaves and counts bacon as her secret to long life celebrates another year |url = http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-3151333/Worlds-oldest-person-celebrates-116th-birthday-New-York-City.html |publisher=[[Daily_Mail|Daily Mail]] |date=July 6, 2015 |accessdate=July 15, 2015 }}</ref>。112歲[[生日|生日]]時，瓊斯收到了來自時任紐約市長[[邁克爾·布隆伯格|邁克爾·布隆伯格]]和紐約州長[[安德魯·庫默|安德魯·庫默]]的禮物。慶祝後，瓊斯說道：「我希望我時刻都會喜歡它（禮物）」<ref name=nytimes/>。在113歲生日時，瓊斯被{{link-en|查理斯·拜恩|Charles Barron}}護送<ref name=Akhtar/>。

瓊斯過了6天才慶祝自己的114歲生日，她的家人，親友和[[布魯克林區|布魯克林區]][[律師|律師]]{{link-en|查理斯·海因斯|Charles Hynes}}學習她的長壽秘訣<ref name=Staff/>。115歲生日時，瓊斯的其中一位侄女路易斯·約卓告訴{{link-en|WABC-TV|WABC-TV}}瓊斯「容易疲累，但今天對她來說是個好日子」不過瓊斯在生日會上沒有說話。瓊斯的玄侄女——也是稱作蘇珊娜的嬰兒，當時她也在場<ref name=Parikh/>。

[[傑拉琳·塔利|傑拉琳·塔利]]過世後，瓊斯以{{age in years and days|1899|7|6|2015|6|17}}之齡成為[[最年長者|世界上最年長人士]]和最後2位生於1890年代的人（另一位為[[義大利|義大利]][[超級人瑞|超級人瑞]][[艾瑪·莫拉諾|艾瑪·莫拉諾]]）<ref name="Daily Mail" /><ref>[http://www.theaustralian.com.au/news/health-science/worlds-oldest-known-person-jeralean-talley-dies-at-116/story-e6frg8y6-1227405277595 World’s oldest-known person Jeralean Talley dies at 116] Retrieved November 1, 2015.</ref>。2015年7月3日，瓊斯的年齡被[[金氏世界紀錄|金氏世界紀錄]]認證，正式成為「[[最年長者|世界上最年長人士]]」。<ref name="Daily Mail" />

2016年5月12日，瓊斯在睡夢中安詳離世，享嵩壽{{Age in years and days|1899|7|6|2016|5|12}}。隨着瓊斯的死，最後1位生於1890年代的人和[[最年長者|世界上最年長人士]]由[[義大利|義大利]][[超級人瑞|超級人瑞]][[艾瑪·莫拉諾|艾瑪·莫拉諾]]接任<ref>{{cite web |url = http://www.nydailynews.com/news/national/brooklyn-susannah-mushatt-jones-oldest-woman-dies-age-116-article-1.2635657 |title=Brooklyn's Susannah Mushatt Jones, oldest person and last American born in the 1800s, dies at age 116 |publisher=nydailynews.com |date=May 13, 2016 |accessdate=May 13, 2016 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url = http://www.usatoday.com/story/news/world/2016/05/13/shes-only-person-left-born-1800s/84321322/|title=This woman is the only person left born in the 1800s |publisher=''[[USA_Today|USA Today]]'' |date = May 13, 2016 }}</ref>，而「最年長的[[美國人|美國人]]」頭銜由{{le|高蒂·邁克爾遜|Goldie Michelson}}接任。
