== 宣佈放棄參加2008年北京奧運馬拉松賽事 ==

2008年3月10日，基比沙拉錫宣布，由於[[北京市|北京市]][[空氣污染|空氣污染]]問題嚴重，故不參加[[2008年夏季奧林匹克運動會|2008年夏季奧林匹克運動會]]的[[2008年夏季奧林匹克運動會田徑比賽|馬拉松賽事]]，避免其[[哮喘|哮喘]]病發，但他仍會參加10,000米賽事。<ref name="apple_daily" /><ref name="sing_tao" /><ref>{{cite news | language = en | author =  | coauthors = | url = http://edition.cnn.com/2008/SPORT/03/10/athletics.marathon/ | title = Gebreselassie likely to miss Beijing marathon | work = World Sport | publisher = [[CNN.com|CNN.com]] | pages = | date = 2008-03-10 | accessdate = 2008-07-30}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | language = en | author = | coauthors = | url = http://www.taipeitimes.com/News/sport/archives/2008/03/19/2003406265 | title = Gebreselassie will not run in Beijing's Olympic marathon | work = 體育 | publisher = [[台北時報|台北時報]] | pages = | date = 2008-03-19 | accessdate = 2008-07-30}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | language = zh-hk | author = | coauthors = | url = http://hk.news.yahoo.com/article/080405/4/2zw9.html | title = 法總統提出席奧運3條件 | work = 兩岸 | publisher = [[明報|明報]] | pages = | date = 2008-04-05 | accessdate = 2008-07-30}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | language = en | author = Addis Ababa | coauthors = | url = http://news.yahoo.com/s/afp/20080725/wl_africa_afp/oly2008athleticsethgebrselassie_080725170621 | title = Gebrselassie says he's fit for final Olympic showdown | work = 非洲 | publisher = [[法國新聞社|法國新聞社]] | pages = | date = 2008-07-25 | accessdate = 2008-07-30}}</ref>
