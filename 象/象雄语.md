'''象雄语'''（{{Bo|ཞང་ཞུང|w=zhang zhung}}）是[[吐蕃|吐蕃]]在[[青藏高原|青藏高原]]崛起前[[象雄|象雄]]所用语言，使用者在今天西藏西部，现已灭绝。经证实出现在被称为《洞窟中的宝藏》（mDzod phug）的多语种文献及其他短文当中。

一小部分包含未被破译的语言的[[敦煌文献|敦煌文献]]中的内容被称为'''古象雄语'''，但其性质尚存争议。

== 洞窟中的宝藏（mDzod phug） ==
《洞窟中的宝藏》（{{bo|མཛོད་ཕུག|w=mdzod phug}}）是指由Shenchen Luga（{{bo|གཤེན་ཆེན་ཀླུ་དགའ|w=gshen chen klu dga'}}）于12世纪公开的一部[[伏藏|伏藏]]。<ref>Berzin, Alexander (2005). ''The Four Immeasurable Attitudes in Hinayana, Mahayana, and Bön.'' Study Buddhism. Source: [http://studybuddhism.com/en/advanced-studies/abhidharma-tenet-systems/comparison-of-buddhist-traditions/the-four-immeasurables-in-hinayana-mahayana-and-bon] (accessed: June 6, 2016)</ref>Martin（n.d.: p. 21）认定其对于研究象雄语有重要意义：{{quote|For students of Tibetan culture in general, the ''mDzod phug'' is one of the most intriguing of all Bön scriptures, since it is the only lengthy bilingual work in Zhang-zhung and Tibetan (some of the shorter but still significant sources for Zhang-zhung are signalled in Orofino 1990)."<ref>Martin, Dan (n.d.). "Comparing Treasuries: Mental states and other ''mdzod phug'' lists and passages with parallels in Abhidharma works of Vasubandhu and Asanga, or in Prajnaparamita Sutras: A progress report." 耶路撒冷大学. 来源：{{cite web |url=http://ir.minpaku.ac.jp/dspace/bitstream/10502/1286/1/SER15_004.pdf |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2010-03-01 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110628000301/http://ir.minpaku.ac.jp/dspace/bitstream/10502/1286/1/SER15_004.pdf |archivedate=2011-06-28 }} (accessed: Monday March 1, 2010)</ref>}}{{quote|通常对于西藏文化的学者来说，mDzod phug是所有[[苯教|苯教]]典籍中最神秘的一部，以其是唯一长篇的象雄语和藏语双语作品（一些更短但仍然意义重大象雄文献于1990年在奥罗菲诺被报告）}}

== 古象雄语 ==
F. W. Thomas提出三部用藏文书写的未被破解的[[敦煌文献|敦煌文献]]是以象雄语早期的形式书写的。这一鉴定得到了武内紹人的认同，他称其为“古象雄语”，并且补充了两份文献。<ref name="Takeuchi">{{cite book | author=Tsuguhito Takeuchi | chapter = The Old Zhangzhung Manuscript Stein Or 8212/188 | title = Medieval Tibeto-Burman Languages | editor = Christopher Beckwith | pages = 1–11 | location = Leiden | publisher = Brill | year = 2002 | isbn = 978-90-04-12424-0 }}</ref>然而Dan Martin对于鉴定这些文本的语言为象雄语的变种的学说提出了疑问。

这些文献的其中两份收于[[不列颠图书馆|不列颠图书馆]]斯坦因藏书书目中，另外三份收于[[法国国家图书馆|法国国家图书馆]]贝希和藏书书目中。在不同情况下，写在与卷轴相反一侧的有价值的文本都包含了早期汉传佛教的文献。<ref name="Takeuchi"/>用藏文书写的文献可追溯到8世纪末或9世纪初。武内和Nishida声称文献已部分破译，他们相信破译的部分是医学内容。<ref>{{cite book | chapter = The Present Stage of Deciphering Old Zhangzhung | first1 = Tsuguhito | last1 = Takeuchi | first2 = Ai | last2 = Nishida | pages = 151–165 | title = Issues in Tibeto-Burman Historical Linguistics | editor-first = Yasuhiko | editor-last = Nagano | series = Senri Ethnological Studies | volume = 75 | year = 2009 | chapter-url = https://minpaku.repo.nii.ac.jp/?action=repository_uri&item_id=2570&file_id=18&file_no=1 | format = PDF }}</ref>
