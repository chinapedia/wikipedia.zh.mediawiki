'''焦糖色素'''是可溶的[[食用色素|食用色素]]，是由[[糖类|糖类]]加熱後產生，一般會有[[酸|酸]]、[[碱|碱]]或是[[盐_(化学)|盐]]的存在，此一程序稱為{{le|焦糖化|caramelization}}，最後會有燒焦糖類的氣味，味道有點苦，顏色從淺黃色到深[[褐色|褐色]]。

焦糖色素是最早使用在飲料中的色素，也是最廣為使用的色素，在許多市售的飲料及食品中都有，例如[[面糊|面糊]]、[[啤酒|啤酒]]、黑面包、圓面包、巧克力<ref>{{cite web |title= Other Applications: Cocoa/Chocolate |work= Caramel Color Application Guide |publisher= Sethness |url= http://www.sethness.com/apps/ |accessdate= 25 Nov 2013}}</ref>、餅乾、[[咳嗽藥水|咳嗽藥水]]以及像[[白兰地|白兰地]]、[[兰姆酒|兰姆酒]]及[[威士忌|威士忌]]<ref>{{citation |url=https://www.masterofmalt.com/blog/post/can-you-taste-caramel-(e150a)-in-whisky.aspx |publisher=Master of Malt Blog |title=Can you taste E150a in whisky? |date=2011-03-18 |accessdate=2016-09-30}}</ref>，巧克力風味的糖果和涂料、[[奶黃|奶黃]]蛋糕或填充料、[[馬鈴薯片|馬鈴薯片]]<ref>{{cite web |title= Zapp’s Potato Chips Ingredient Lists |publisher= Zapps, Inc |url= http://www.zapps.com/Zapps-Potato-Chips-Ingredient-Lists |accessdate= 25 Nov 2013 |deadurl= yes |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131203005229/http://www.zapps.com/Zapps-Potato-Chips-Ingredient-Lists |archivedate= 2013-12-03 }}</ref>、混合點心、甜甜圈、[[水果蜜饯|水果蜜饯]]、[[冰淇淋|冰淇淋]]、醃黃瓜<ref>{{cite web |title= Sweet Pickle Chips |publisher= M.A. Gedney Co. |url= http://www.gedneyfoods.com/products/detail?id=7 |accessdate= 25 Nov 2013}}</ref>、[[醬汁|醬汁]]（[[醬油|醬油]]、[[蠔油|蠔油]]）、[[可乐|可乐]]、[[醋|醋]]等。焦糖色素廣為用在食品產業中，但其應用以及使用程度的限制會因國家而不同<ref>{{citation |url=http://www.ddwcolor.com/standards |publisher=DD Williamson |title=Food & Beverage Processing Regulatory Resources |accessdate=2012-01-19 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130429064243/http://www.ddwcolor.com/standards |archivedate=2013-04-29 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。
