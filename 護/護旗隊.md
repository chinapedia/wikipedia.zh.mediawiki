'''護旗隊'''（{{lang-en|Color Guard, Colour Guard}}）在軍事傳統和禮儀中，是指守護{{tsl|en|Colours, standards and guidons|軍團旗|軍旗、戎旗和軍團旗}}的衛隊。一般指派初級軍官為這項義務的{{tsl|en|Ensign (rank)|掌旗官}}（Ensign，少尉軍階），資深士官為這項義務的{{tsl|en|Colour sergeant|掌旗軍士}}（Colour sergeants，上士軍階）。<ref name=handbook>{{cite web|author=British Army|title=THE REGIMENTAL HANDBOOK OF THE DUKE OF LANCASTER'S REGIMENT|url=http://www.army.mod.uk/documents/general/Regimental_handbook.pdf|publisher=The Regimental Charity of The Duke of Lancaster's Regiment Preston 2007|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304044834/http://www.army.mod.uk/documents/general/Regimental_handbook.pdf|archivedate=2016-03-04}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=USCG|title=United States Coast Guard Ceremonial Honor Guard|url=http://www.uscg.mil/honorguard/|publisher=United States Department of Homeland Security}}</ref> 在英聯邦國家的禮儀中，另外會有{{tsl|en|Regimental sergeant major|團軍士長}}（Regimental sergeant major，準尉軍階）配戴[[軍刀|軍刀]]或[[步槍|步槍]]護送軍旗。<ref name=handbook />
