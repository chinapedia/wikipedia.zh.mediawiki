'''庫德斯坦王國'''（{{lang-ku|Keyaniya Kurdistanê}}）<ref>{{cite web|url=http://books.google.de/books?id=stl97FdyRswC&pg=PA384&lpg=PA384&dq=Encyclopedia+of+the+Peoples+of+Africa+and+the+Middle+East+Kingdom+Kurdistan&source=bl&ots=uZUrLwfvz5&sig=wkk1qRK7gmh2P8nYy3BSNt1rxJ4&hl=de&sa=X&ei=r7MNUfjYGOim4gTk5oGQCg&ved=0CEwQ6AEwAw#v=onepage&q=Kingdom%20Kurdistan&f=false|title=Encyclopedia of the Peoples of Africa and the Middle East, Kingdom of Kurdistan|publisher=|accessdate=23 October 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://books.google.de/books?id=KJbXNA76tewC&pg=RA1-PA507&lpg=RA1-PA507&dq=kingdom+of+kurdistan+1921&source=bl&ots=bmTyVRDy8t&sig=WqA1Iph5zwzbyhD6Dz32UDRsO0k&hl=de&sa=X&ei=lq4NUcTvFOml4gTC2oGADA&redir_esc=y#v=onepage&q=k%C3%B6nigreich%20kurdistan&f=false|title=Der Kurdistan-Irak-Konflikt: der Weg zur Autonomie seit dem ersten Weltkrieg|publisher=|accessdate=23 October 2014}}</ref>是在[[鄂圖曼帝國|鄂圖曼帝國]]瓦解之後在[[蘇萊曼尼亞|蘇萊曼尼亞]]出現的一個國家。這個國家存在於1922年九月至1924年七月，後來被併入[[美索不達米亞託管地|美索不達米亞託管地]]。
