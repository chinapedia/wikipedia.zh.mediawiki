'''基督教国教主义'''（'''Dominionism'''），是用来指保守[[基督教派|基督教派]]中通过[[政治|政治]]活动在世俗[[政府|政府]]中获得影响或控制的[[运动|运动]]，寻求一个国家被[[基督徒|基督徒]]所统治或被[[基督教|基督教]]的[[法律|法律]]统治。基督国教主义1980年代末起源于美国，但在加拿大也用来讨论类似的趋势，并且在几个欧洲国家也存在，该术语的使用和应用存在争议。<ref name="Diamond1989a">Diamond, Sara. 1989. ''Spiritual Warfare: The Politics of the Christian Right''. Boston: South End Press.</ref><ref name="Barron1992a">Barron, Bruce. 1992. ''Heaven on Earth? The Social & Political Agendas of Dominion Theology.'' Grand Rapids, Mich.: Zondervan.  ISBN 0-310-53611-1.</ref><ref name="Diamond1995a">Diamond, Sara. 1995. ''Roads to Dominion: Right-Wing Movements and Political Power in the United States.'' New York: Guilford Press.  ISBN 0-89862-864-4.</ref><ref name="Clarkson1997a">Clarkson, Frederick. 1997. ''Eternal Hostility: The Struggle Between Theocracy and Democracy.'' Monroe, Maine: Common Courage. ISBN 1-56751-088-4</ref><ref name="Berlet-Lyons2000a">Berlet, Chip and Matthew N. Lyons. 2000. ''Right-Wing Populism in America: Too Close for Comfort''. New York: Guilford Press.</ref>
