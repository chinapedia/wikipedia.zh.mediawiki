'''基督党'''（{{lang-en|Christian Party}}）是[[萨摩亚|萨摩亚]]的一个小[[政党|政党]]。该党领导人为Tuala Falenaoti Tiresa Malietoa<ref>[http://www.islandsbusiness.com/archives/index_dynamic/containerNameToReplace=MiddleMiddle/focusModuleID=130/focusContentID=3316/tableName=mediaRelease/overideSkinName=newsArticle-full.tpl "First woman to lead political party outlines policy"], ''Islands Business'', 2006-01-17</ref>。成员主要为女性。该党诞生于2006年选举之前，在4月2日的选举中并未赢得席位，但其为反对《右侧驾驶法案》（Right-Hand Drive Bill）而继续存在，然而该法案于2009年最终通过。<ref>{{cite book|title=Political Parties and Democracy: Volume IV: Africa and Oceania|first=Kay |last=Lawson|first2=Luc |last2=Sindjoun|first3=Marian|last3=Simms |publisher= |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=TZJxDwAAQBAJ&pg=PA195&lpg=PA195&dq=Christian+Party+Samoa&source=bl&ots=ShTFw7RZbx&sig=ACfU3U2SOhaDfOyCd1X550X7PhtQ2ED8hw&hl=zh-CN&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwjCy6DY1JjhAhWMvLwKHfNkA5oQ6AEwEXoECAgQAQ#v=onepage&q=Christian%20Party%20Samoa&f=false|date=2010-07-20|isbn=9780313353024 }}</ref>
