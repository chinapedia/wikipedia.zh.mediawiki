'''琳达·埃德娜·卡德里尼'''（{{lang-en|Linda Edna Cardellini}}，{{bd|1975年|6月25日||}}），美国女演员，出演过《[[怪胎与书呆|怪胎与书呆]]》的林赛·威尔、《[[急诊室的故事|急诊室的故事]]》的萨曼莎·塔吉特、真人电影《[[史酷比|史酷比]]》的维尔玛·迪克利、[[AMC|AMC]]电视剧《[[广告狂人|广告狂人]]》中唐·雷德珀的邻居西尔维娅·罗森和[[Netflix|Netflix]]原创剧《[[血脈_(美國電視劇)|血脈]]》的梅格·雷伯恩等角色。

== 早年 ==
卡德里尼生于[[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]][[红木城|红木城]]，是家庭主妇埃尔南·洛林和商人韦恩·大卫·卡德里尼<ref name="knockoutnerd">{{cite web|url=http://www.people.com/people/archive/article/0,,20137425,00.html|title=Knockout Nerd - Scooby-Doo, Linda Cardellini|work=[[People_(magazine)|People]]|date=July 1, 2002 |accessdate=June 9, 2013}}</ref>的第四个女儿<ref name="knockoutnerd"/>。卡德里尼有着意大利（她祖父）、爱尔兰、德国和英国血统<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.starpulse.com/news/Jason_Coleman/2012/02/10/interviewreview_linda_cardellini_on_re|title=Interview/Review: Linda Cardellini On Researching 'Return', Michael Shannon And Being A First-Time Mom|publisher=Starpulse.com|date=February 10, 2012|accessdate=June 9, 2013}}</ref><ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0004802/bio?ref_=nm_ov_bio_sm</ref>，在[[天主教|天主教]]家庭中长大<ref>{{cite web|last=Watkins |first=Gwynne |url=http://www.gq.com/blogs/the-feed/2013/06/mad-men-interview-linda-cardellini-sylvia-rosen.html |title=The Mad Men GQ+A: Linda Cardellini on Sylvia Rosen's Extramarital Bombshell: The Q |publisher=GQ |date=June 12, 2013 |accessdate=October 31, 2013}}</ref>。10岁她在学校的戏剧表演中唱歌，是她首次公开亮相。随后她出演了学校制作的数部影片，开始参加戏剧课程<ref name="NYDN">{{cite news |title=Young Actress Takes Shine To Playing A Movie Starlet|first=Donna |last=Petrozzello |work=New York Daily News |date=August 19, 1999 |url=http://articles.nydailynews.com/1999-08-19/entertainment/18114779_1_linda-cardellini-jonathan-frakes-rue-mcclanahan}}</ref>。她于1993年毕业于[[山景城_(加利福尼亚州)|山景城]]郊区的{{tsl|en|Saint Francis High School (Mountain View)|圣弗朗西斯天主教高中 (山景城)|圣弗朗西斯天主教高中}}（{{lang|en|Catholic St. Francis High School}}）<ref name="AskMen">[http://www.askmen.com/women/actress_200/212_linda_cardellini.html Linda Cardellini]. AskMen.com.</ref>，随后搬到洛杉矶寻求出演电视和电影角色<ref name="NYDN"/>。

== 私生活 ==
卡德里尼就读于[[洛约拉马利蒙特大学|洛约拉马利蒙特大学]][[传播学|传媒]]与[[美术|美术]]学院<ref>[http://www.lmu.edu/sites/alumnirelations/Events/Event_Photos/LA_Leaders__LMU_Distinguished_Alumni_Reception_2007.htm "LA Leaders, LMU Distinguished Alumni Reception 2007"]. [[Loyola_Marymount_University|Loyola Marymount University]]. February 22, 2007. Retrieved January 2, 2013.</ref>，2000年以戏剧艺术学位毕业。2007年，卡德里尼被大学认定为“杰出校友”<ref>[http://www.lmu.edu/sites/alumnirelations/Awards___Scholarships/Awards/Distinguished_Alumni_Awardees_1970-present.htm "Distinguished Alumni Awardees 1970-present"]. Loyola Marymount University. Retrieved January 2, 2013.</ref>。

卡德里尼在《怪胎与书呆》节目停播几年后与该剧演员[[杰森·席格尔|杰森·席格尔]]约会<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.latimes.com/entertainment/news/la-ca-segel13apr13,0,6976921.story|title=This breakup really broke up Jason Segel |work=[[Los_Angeles_Times|Los Angeles Times]] |date=April 13, 2008 |accessdate=June 7, 2013}}</ref>。

2011年10月，卡德里尼与男友史蒂芬·罗德里格斯宣布自己怀孕<ref>{{cite web|url=http://celebritybabies.people.com/2011/10/18/linda-cardellini-expecting-first-child/ |title=Linda Cardellini Expecting First Child – Moms & Babies – Moms & Babies - People.com |publisher=Celebritybabies.people.com |date=June 22, 2011 |accessdate=February 8, 2012}}</ref>。卡德里尼于2012年2月生下他们的女儿莉亚·罗斯<ref>{{cite web|url=http://celebritybabies.people.com/2012/03/06/linda-cardellini-welcomes-daughter-lilah-rose/|title=Linda Cardellini Welcomes Daughter Lilah-Rose |work=[[People_(magazine)|People]]|date=March 6, 2012 |accessdate=March 6, 2012}}</ref>。两人于2013年6月订婚<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2013/06/07/linda-cardellini-engaged-steven-rodriguez_n_3404140.html|title=Linda Cardellini Engaged: 'Freaks And Geeks' Actress And Boyfriend Steven Rodriguez Set To Wed |work=[[The_Huffington_Post|The Huffington Post]] |date=June 7, 2013|accessdate=June 7, 2013}}</ref>。
