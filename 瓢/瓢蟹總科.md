'''瓢蟹總科'''（'''Carpilioidea'''）是[[短尾下目|短尾下目]]下的一個[[總科|總科]]，下含唯一的現存科[[瓢蟹科|瓢蟹科]]和另外三個已滅絕科。<ref name="Grave">{{cite journal |journal = [[Raffles_Bulletin_of_Zoology|Raffles Bulletin of Zoology]] |year=2009 |volume = Suppl. 21 |pages=1–109 |title = A classification of living and fossil genera of decapod crustaceans |author=Sammy De Grave, N. Dean Pentcheff, Shane T. Ahyong ''et al.'' |url=http://rmbr.nus.edu.sg/rbz/biblio/s21/s21rbz1-109.pdf}}</ref> 按現在的地理環境，其分佈位於[[印度洋-太平洋海域|印度洋-太平洋海域]]及包括[[加勒比海|加勒比海]]在內的西[[大西洋|大西洋]]。<ref name="Xanth2000">{{cite journal |author=Carrie E. Schweitzer |year=2000 |title=Tertiary Xanthoidea (Crustacea: Decapoda: Brachyura) from the west coast of North America |journal=[[Journal_of_Crustacean_Biology|Journal of Crustacean Biology]] |volume=20 |issue=4 |pages=715–742 |doi=10.1651/0278-0372(2000)020[0715:TXCDBF]2.0.CO;2}}</ref> 其[[化石記錄|化石記錄]]可追溯到[[古新世|古新世]]。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Fossil Crustacea (excluding Cirripedia and Ostracoda) in the University of Bucharest Collections, Romania, including two new species |author=Carrie E. Schweitzer, Rodney M. Feldmann & Iuliana Lazǎr |journal=[[Bulletin_of_the_Mizunami_Fossil_Museum|Bulletin of the Mizunami Fossil Museum]] |volume=35 |year=2009 |pages=1–14 |url=http://www.city.mizunami.gifu.jp/odocs/sightseeing/mizunami/cultural_property/institution/bull35/pdf/01.pdf |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110612184515/http://www.city.mizunami.gifu.jp/odocs/sightseeing/mizunami/cultural_property/institution/bull35/pdf/01.pdf |archivedate=2011-06-12 }}</ref>
