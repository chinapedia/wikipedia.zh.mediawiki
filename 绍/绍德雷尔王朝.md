'''绍德雷尔王朝'''（[[波纳佩语|波纳佩语]]：Mwehin Sau Deleur，也拼作Chau-te-leur，意为“德雷尔领主时期”）是[[波纳佩岛|波纳佩岛]]上第一个有组织的政府架构，统治时间是约1100年－约1628年。<ref>{{cite book|title=The Lelu Stone Ruins (Kosrae, Micronesia): 1978-81 Historical and Archaeological Research |issue=10 |series=Asian and Pacific Archaeology |first=Ross H |last=Cordy |publisher=Social Science Research Institute, University of Hawaii at Manoa |year=1993 |isbn=0-8248-1134-8 |pages=14, 254, 258 |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=hQMNAQAAMAAJ |accessdate=2011-12-31}}</ref>这个时代承接建筑时期（又称繁衍时期），而后又由Mwehin Nahnmwarki时期所接续。德雷尔是[[波纳佩|波纳佩]]的古名。波纳佩现在是[[密克罗尼西亚联邦|密克罗尼西亚联邦]]的一个州，首都[[帕利基尔|帕利基尔]]在其辖区内。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Lost in the Weeds: Theme and Variation in Pohnpei Political Mythology |volume=35 |journal=Occasional Papers |chapter=5. Isokelekel |pages=34 et seq |first=Glenn |last=Petersen |publisher=Center for Pacific Islands Studies, School of Hawaiian, Asian & Pacific Studies, University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa |year=1990 |url=http://scholarspace.manoa.hawaii.edu/bitstream/handle/10125/15545/OP35.pdf |format=PDF |accessdate=2011-12-31}}</ref>
