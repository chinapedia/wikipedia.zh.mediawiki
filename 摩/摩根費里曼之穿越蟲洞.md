《'''摩根費里曼之穿越蟲洞'''》（{{lang-en|''Through the Wormhole With Morgan Freeman''}}）<ref>{{cite press release |url=https://corporate.discovery.com/discovery-newsroom/science-channel-and-morgan-freeman-invite-viewers/ |script-title=Science Channel and Morgan Freeman Invite Viewers to Journey THROUGH THE WORMHOLE |work=[[探索傳播|Discovery Communications]] |publisher=|via= |author= |language= |date= May 11, 2010 |accessdate=|archiveurl= |archivedate=}}</ref>，簡稱《穿越蟲洞》（Through the Wormhole），是一系列探討科學的電視節目，由[[摩根·費里曼|摩根·費里曼]]擔任旁白及主持人。於美國時間2010年6月9日在[[科學頻道|科學頻道]]首播<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2010/06/09/arts/television/09wormhole.html |title=Asking the Big Questions in a Dignified Manner |author=Neil Genzlinger |newspaper=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] |date=June 8, 2010}}</ref>，至2017年5月16日播畢，共8季<ref name="final episodes">{{cite press |url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2017/03/23/final-four-episodes-of-through-the-wormhole-with-morgan-freeman-beginning-april-25-at-10pm-on-science-channel-615114/20170323science01/ |title=Final Four Episodes of "Through the Wormhole with Morgan Freeman" Beginning April 25 at 10PM on Science Channel |publisher=[[科学频道|Science Channel]] |date=March 23, 2017 |accessdate=April 20, 2017|via=[[The_Futon_Critic|The Futon Critic]]}}</ref>。

==發展==
穿越蟲洞的執行製作Bernadette McDaid想要再塑造一個「搖滾明星物理學家」<ref name="McDaid">{{cite web|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/rant/2010/06/09/interview-through-the-wormhole-with-morgan-freeman-executive-producer-bernadette-mcdaid/20100609_throughthewormhole/|title=Interview: "Through the Wormhole with Morgan Freeman" Executive Producer Bernadette McDaid|last=Halterman|first=Jim|date=June 9, 2010|work=The Futon Critic}}</ref>，例如[[加來道雄|加來道雄]]和[[布萊恩·考克斯_(物理學家)|布萊恩·考克斯]]。「我們想要融合『搖滾明星物理學家』和『流行文化的巨星』」<ref name="McDaid"/>當科學頻道的總經理Deborah Myers聽說摩根·費里曼對於太空、宇宙及大的哲學問題有興趣時<ref name="McDaid"/>，她聯絡費里曼及他的製作人並共同打造這一系列作品<ref name="McDaid"/>。

2011年2月17日，宇宙學家{{link-en|西恩·卡羅|Sean M. Carroll}}在Twitter公開證實將會開拍穿越蟲洞第二季<ref>{{Twitter status|user = seanmcarroll|statusid = 38268104739917824|title = Taping Through the Wormhole (Season 2) today for @ScienceChannel. We start in a pool hall. Entropy is involved.|name = [[Sean_M._Carroll|Sean Carroll]]|date = February 17, 2011|accessdate = February 17, 2011}}</ref>。2011年5月17日， 探索頻道證實第二季將於2011年6月8日在科學頻道播放<ref>{{cite web|title=Through the Wormhole with Morgan Freeman Returns for an Awe-Inspiring Second Season this June on Science |url=http://corporate.discovery.com/discovery-news/through-the-wormhole-wht-morgan-freeman-returns-fo/|work=Discovery Corporate}}</ref>。

2012年1月3日，西恩·卡羅在其Twitter頁面放上一張圖片，提到將會開拍第三季<ref>{{Twitter status|user = seanmcarroll|statusid = 154285577200615424|title = Through the Wormhole crew in the house, taping an episode for Season 3. https://t.co/Lprkythb |name = Sean Carroll|date = January 3, 2012|accessdate = January 3, 2012}}</ref>。第三季於2012年3月6日在科學頻道播出。科學頻道之後在官網放上整季的簡介<ref>{{cite web |publisher=Discovery.com  |title=Season 3 partial episode list |url=http://press.discovery.com/us/sci/programs/through-wormhole-morgan-freeman-season-3/}}</ref>。

第四季於2013年3月20日在科學頻道播放<ref>{{cite web|title=Through the Wormhole - Science Channel|url=http://science.discovery.com/tv-shows/through-the-wormhole|accessdate=May 27, 2013}}</ref>。2013年10月9日，播出「增量集」（Enhanced Episodes）版本《摩根費里曼之超越蟲洞》（Beyond the Wormhole with Morgan Freeman）<ref>{{cite web|title=Science Channel Airs Enhanced Episodes of THROUGH THE WORMHOLE w/ MORGAN FREEMAN Tonight- BWWMoviesWorld|url=http://www.broadwayworld.com/bwwmovies/article/Science-Channel-to-Air-Enhanced-Episodes-of-THROUGH-THE-WORMHOLE-w-MORGAN-FREEMAN-Today-20131008|accessdate=October 9, 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Beyond the Wormhole With Morgan Freeman |url=http://tv.twcc.com/tv/beyond-the-wormhole-with-morgan-freeman/8295626 |website=TWC Central |accessdate=}}</ref>。

第五季於2014年3月5日在科學頻道播放。另外，海外將第七、八季併成第七季播出，播放季度與美國有別。
