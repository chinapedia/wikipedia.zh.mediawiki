'''摩揭陀俗語'''(Magadhi Prakrit (Ardhamāgadhī))是[[古印度|古印度]]的一种语言，属[[普拉克里特诸语言|普拉克里特诸语言]]之一。在[[梵语|梵语]]和[[巴利语|巴利语]]相继没落之后，摩揭陀俗语成为古印度的书面语言之一。

摩揭陀俗语使用范围为[[印度次大陆|印度次大陆]]的东部，包括今日[[印度东部|印度东部]]、[[孟加拉国|孟加拉国]]和[[尼泊尔|尼泊尔]]。[[佛教|佛教]]创始人[[释迦牟尼|释迦牟尼]]和[[耆那教|耆那教]]创始人[[摩訶毗羅|摩訶毗羅]]都是摩揭陀俗语的使用者。在[[印度列国时代|印度列国时代]]，该语言是[[摩揭陀|摩揭陀]]的宫廷语言，后来也是[[孔雀王朝|孔雀王朝]]的宫廷语言。[[阿育王诏书|阿育王诏书]]便是用该语言写成。<ref>Bashan A.L., ''The Wonder that was India'', Picador, 2004, pp.394</ref>

摩揭陀俗语后来发展成为[[印度-雅利安语支|印度-雅利安语支]]的东部诸语，包括[[阿萨姆语|阿萨姆语]]、[[孟加拉语|孟加拉语]]、[[奥里亚语|奥里亚语]]和[[比哈尔语|比哈尔诸语]]（包括[[博杰普尔语|博杰普尔语]]、[[迈蒂利语|迈蒂利语]]、[[摩揭陀语|摩揭陀语]]等语言）。<ref>South Asian folklore: an encyclopedia : Afghanistan, Bangladesh, India, By Peter J. Claus, Sarah Diamond, Margaret Ann Mills, Routledge, 2003, p. 203</ref><ref>Ray, Tapas S. (2007). [http://books.google.com/books?id=OtCPAgAAQBAJ&pg=PA444 "Chapter Eleven: "Oriya"]. In Jain, Danesh; Cardona, George. ''The Indo-Aryan Languages''. Routledge. p. 445. ISBN 978-1-135-79711-9.</ref>

==巴利語與摩揭陀俗語==
<!--[[Theravada|Theravada]] [[Buddhism|Buddhist]] tradition has long held that [[Pali|Pali]] was synonymous with Magadhi and there are many analogies between it and an older form of Magadhi called ''Ardhamāgadhī'' "Proto-Magadhi", which is preserved in the [[Jain_Agamas|Jain Agamas]]. Both Gautama Buddha and the [[Tirthankara|Tirthankara]] Mahavira preached in Magadha.

Ardhamāgadhī differs from later Magadhi Prakrit on similar points as Pāli. For example, Ardhamāgadhī preserves historical [l], unlike later Magadhi, where [l] changed into [r]. Additionally, in the noun inflection, Ardhamagadhi shows the ending [-o] instead of Magadhi Prakrit [-e] in many metrical places.
