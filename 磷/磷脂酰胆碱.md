'''磷脂酰胆碱'''是带有[[胆碱|胆碱]]头基的[[磷脂|磷脂]]。磷脂酰胆碱是生物膜的主要成分；通过机械加工或己烷萃取等方法可从[[蛋黄|蛋黄]]、[[大豆|大豆]]等来源中提取磷脂酰胆碱。磷脂酰胆碱属于[[卵磷脂|卵磷脂]]的一种。二棕榈酰磷脂酰胆碱（卵磷脂）是肺表面活性剂的主要成分，可用于测定{{link-en|卵磷脂/鞘磷脂比|L/S ratio}}以计算胎儿肺成熟值。动物与植物细胞中含有磷脂酰胆碱；大肠杆菌在内<ref name="pmid19940943">{{cite journal | author = Chen F, Zhao Q, Cai X, Lv L, Lin W, Yu X, Li C, Li Y, Xiong M, Wang XG | title = Phosphatidylcholine in membrane of Escherichia coli changes bacterial antigenicity. | journal = Can. J. Microbiol. | volume = 55 | issue = 11 | pages = 1328–34 |date=November 2009 | pmid = 19940943 | doi = 10.1139/w09-082 | url =  }}</ref>的大多数细菌的细胞膜中缺乏磷脂酰胆碱。<ref>{{cite book |last1=Jackowski |first1=Suzanne |last2=Cronan, jr. |first2=John E. |last3=Rock |first3=Charles O. |editor1-first=Dennis E. |editor1-last=Vance |editor2-first=J. |editor2-last=Vance |title=Biochemistry of Lipids, Lipoproteins and Membranes |publisher=Elsevier |year=1991 |pages=80–81 |chapter=Chapter 2: Lipid metabolism in procaryotes |isbn=0-444-89321-0}}</ref> 

== 功能 ==
磷脂酰胆碱是构成细胞膜的主要成分，通常存在于[[细胞膜|细胞膜]]外质膜面上。磷脂酰胆碱也是肺表面活性剂的主要成分。有人认为磷脂酰胆碱在生物膜之间通过{{link-en|磷脂酰胆碱转运蛋白|phosphatidylcholine transfer protein}}（{{lang|en|PTCP}}）转运。<ref name="pmid1883207">{{cite journal | author = Wirtz KW | title = Phospholipid transfer proteins. | journal = Ann. Rev. Biochem. | volume = 60| issue = 13| pages = 73–99|date=July 1991 | pmid = 1883207 | doi = 10.1146/annurev.bi.60.070191.000445 | url =  }}</ref>

磷脂酰胆碱还参与了膜介导[[细胞信号|细胞信号]]和{{lang|en|PTCP}}对其他酶的活化过程。<ref name="pmid17704541">{{cite journal | author = Kanno K, Wu MK, Agate DA, Fanelli BK, Wagle N, Scapa EF, Ukomadu C, Cohen DE | title = Interacting proteins dictate function of the minimal START domain phosphatidylcholine transfer protein/StarD2. | journal = J. Biol. Chem. | volume = 282 | issue = 42 | pages = 30728–36 |date=October 2007 | pmid = 17704541 | doi = 10.1074/jbc.M703745200 | url =  }}</ref>

==结构与物理性质==

[[Image:1-palmitoyl-2-oleoylphosphatidylcholine.svg|300px]]
 
磷脂酰胆碱包括一个胆碱头基、一个甘油磷酸与两个脂肪酸构成。其中包括一个[[饱和脂肪酸|饱和脂肪酸]]（如图中的[[棕榈酸|棕榈酸]]或蛋黄中的十九酸）和一个[[不饱和脂肪酸|不饱和脂肪酸]]（图中为油酸）。

{{link-en|磷脂酶D|Phospholipase D}}可催化磷脂酰胆碱的水解，生成磷脂酸与胆碱。
