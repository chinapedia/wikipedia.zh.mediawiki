'''升糖负荷'''（GL）是指食物摄入后将如何升高人的[[血糖|血糖]]水平。 1个单位的升糖负荷约相当于吃1克[[葡萄糖|葡萄糖]]的效果。<ref>{{cite web|title=Glycemic Load Defined|url=http://www.glycemic.com/GlycemicIndex-LoadDefined.htm|publisher=Glycemic Research Institute|accessdate=8 February 2013}}</ref> 

升糖负荷是用[[升糖指数|升糖指数]]加权的食物中的可吸收的碳水化合物的量。升糖负荷的计算为：食物中[[碳水化合物|碳水化合物]]的[[克|克]]数乘以[[升糖指数|升糖指数]]再除以100。例如[[西瓜|西瓜]]的升糖指数很高为72；但是西瓜的含糖量较低（约5%），因此西瓜的升糖负荷较低，100克西瓜的升糖负荷为5 x 72/100=3.6。 

对于1份食物，升糖负荷大于20被认为是高的；在11-19为中等的；低于10为低负荷。<ref>{{cite journal|last=Das|first=Sai Krupa|coauthors=et al.|title=Long-term effects of 2 energy-restricted diets differing in glycemic load on dietary adherence, body composition, and metabolism in CALERIE: a 1-y randomized controlled trial|journal=American Journal of Clinical Nutrition|date=April 2007|volume=85|issue=4|pages=1023–1030|pmid=17413101|url=http://ajcn.nutrition.org/content/85/4/1023.full|accessdate=8 February 2013|pmc=2920502}}</ref>

升糖负荷对于糖尿病饮食、代谢综合症、[[胰岛素抵抗|胰岛素抵抗]]、减肥等，比较有价值。研究表明餐后的血糖与[[胰岛素|胰岛素]]保持高峰值水平将增加患[[糖尿病|糖尿病]]风险。<ref>{{cite journal|last=Ludwig|first=Daniel S.|title=The glycemic index: physiological mechanisms relating to obesity, diabetes, and cardiovascular disease|journal=Journal of the American Medical Association|date=May 2002|volume=287|issue=18|pages=2414–2423|doi=10.1001/jama.287.18.2414|pmid=11988062}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last=Villegas|first=Raquel|coauthors=Liu, Simin; Gao, Yu-Tang; Yang, Gong; Li, Honglan; Zheng, Wei; Shu, Xiao Ou|title=Prospective Study of Dietary Carbohydrates, Glycemic Index, Glycemic Load, and Incidence of Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus in Middle-aged Chinese Women|journal=Archives of Internal Medicine|year=2007|volume=6167|issue=21|pages=2310–2316|doi=10.1001/archinte.167.21.2310|pmid=18039989|url=http://archinte.jamanetwork.com/article.aspx?articleid=413531|accessdate=8 February 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last=Krishnan|first=Supriya|coauthors=Rosenberg, Lynn; Singer, Martha; Hu, Frank B.; Djoussé, Luc; Cupples, L. Adrienne; Palmer, Julie R.|title=Glycemic Index, Glycemic Load, and Cereal Fiber Intake and Risk of Type 2 Diabetes in US Black Women|journal=Archives of Internal Medicine|year=2007|volume=167|issue=21|pages=2304–2309|doi=10.1001/archinte.167.21.2304|pmid=18039988|url=http://archinte.jamanetwork.com/article.aspx?articleid=413517|accessdate=8 February 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Simple Steps to Preventing Diabetes|url=http://www.hsph.harvard.edu/nutritionsource/diabetes-full-story/|work=The Nutrition Source|publisher=Harvard School of Public Health|accessdate=8 February 2013}}</ref>
