'''力健'''（{{lang-en|'''Life Fitness'''}}）是一家[[美國|美國]]的健身器材設計生產商，由雷·威爾遜（{{lang|en|Ray Wilson}}）和奧吉·涅托（{{lang|en|Augie Nieto}}）創建。他們於1970年，從基恩·P·迪米克（{{lang|en|Keene P. Dimmick}}）手中購入設計，製造出世上第一部電動固定式[[健身單車|健身單車]]及將之成功推出市場<ref>{{cite web|last=Clark |first=Cammy |url=http://www.miamiherald.com/2011/03/01/2090323/trendy-soulcycle-hits-south-florida.html |title=Trendy SoulCycle hits South Florida with competitor FlyWheel in hot pursuit |publisher=MiamiHerald.com |date=2011-02-28 |accessdate=2011-06-19}}</ref>。

==合併與收購==
* 1991年，[[:en:Bally_Total_Fitness|Bally Total Fitness]]以6千2百50萬美元將力健售予一班投資者<ref>{{cite web|url=http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1991-06-05/business/9102190899_1_life-fitness-bally-manufacturing-corp-debt |title=Bally Agrees To Sell Life Fitness Business |publisher=Chicago Tribune |date= |accessdate=2013-04-11}}</ref>。
* 1998年6月，{{link-en|賓士域集團|Brunswick Corporation}}購入力健的股份<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.rbbi.com/company/bc/lifefit.htm |title=Brunswick Acquires Life Fitness |publisher=Rbbi.com |date= |accessdate=2011-06-19}}</ref>。
