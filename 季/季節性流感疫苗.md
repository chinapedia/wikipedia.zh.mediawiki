'''季節性流感疫苗，'''常簡稱'''流感疫苗'''，是針對[[流行性感冒|流行性感冒]]的[[疫苗|疫苗]]。<ref name="WHO2012">{{Template:Cite journal|title=Vaccines against influenza WHO position paper – November 2012.|url=http://www.who.int/wer/2012/wer8747.pdf|date=Nov 23, 2012|journal=Wkly Epidemiol Rec|issue=46|volume=87|pages=461–76|pmid=23210147}}</ref> 因為[[流感病毒|流感病毒]]變化的速度很快，一年會發展新的流感疫苗兩次。<ref name="WHO2012">{{Template:Cite journal|title=Vaccines against influenza WHO position paper – November 2012.|url=http://www.who.int/wer/2012/wer8747.pdf|date=Nov 23, 2012|journal=Wkly Epidemiol Rec|issue=46|volume=87|pages=461–76|pmid=23210147}}</ref>大部分狀況下，疫苗有中度到高度的保護力；然而每年情況略有不同。<ref name="WHO2012">{{Template:Cite journal|title=Vaccines against influenza WHO position paper – November 2012.|url=http://www.who.int/wer/2012/wer8747.pdf|date=Nov 23, 2012|journal=Wkly Epidemiol Rec|issue=46|volume=87|pages=461–76|pmid=23210147}}</ref><ref name="Man2012">{{Template:Cite journal|title=Effectiveness and harms of seasonal and pandemic influenza vaccines in children, adults and elderly: a critical review and re-analysis of 15 meta-analyses|authorlink2=John P.A. Ioannidis|date=July 2012|journal=Hum Vaccin Immunother|issue=7|doi=10.4161/hv.19917|volume=8|pages=851–62|pmc=3495721|pmid=22777099|name-list-format=vanc|author1=Manzoli L|author2=Ioannidis JP|author3=Flacco ME|author4=De Vito C|author5=Villari P}}</ref>對65歲以上的長者，目前證據較缺乏。<ref name="Ost2012">{{Template:Cite journal|title=Efficacy and effectiveness of influenza vaccines: a systematic review and meta-analysis.|last2=Kelley|first2=NS|date=Jan 2012|journal=The Lancet. Infectious diseases|issue=1|doi=10.1016/S1473-3099(11)70295-X|volume=12|pages=36–44|pmid=22032844|last3=Sommer|first3=A|last4=Belongia|first4=EA|last1=Osterholm|first1=MT}}</ref><ref>{{Template:Cite journal|title=Vaccines for preventing influenza in the elderly|last2=Di Pietrantonj|first2=C|date=Feb 17, 2010|journal=The Cochrane database of systematic reviews|issue=2|doi=10.1002/14651858.CD004876.pub3|pages=CD004876|pmid=20166072|last3=Al-Ansary|first3=LA|last4=Ferroni|first4=E|last5=Thorning|first5=S|last6=Thomas|first6=RE|last1=Jefferson|first1=T|authorlink1=Tom Jefferson (epidemiologist)}}</ref> 平均來說，他們可以減少半日因病而上班必須請假的狀況。<ref>{{Template:Cite journal|title=Vaccines for preventing influenza in healthy adults.|last2=Di Pietrantonj|first2=C|date=13 March 2014|journal=The Cochrane database of systematic reviews|doi=10.1002/14651858.CD001269.pub5|volume=3|pages=CD001269|pmid=24623315|last3=Rivetti|first3=A|last4=Bawazeer|first4=GA|last5=Al-Ansary|first5=LA|last6=Ferroni|first6=E|last1=Jefferson|first1=T}}</ref>有打疫苗的孩童，可以保護他們周遭的人。<ref name="WHO2012">{{Template:Cite journal|title=Vaccines against influenza WHO position paper – November 2012.|url=http://www.who.int/wer/2012/wer8747.pdf|date=Nov 23, 2012|journal=Wkly Epidemiol Rec|issue=46|volume=87|pages=461–76|pmid=23210147}}</ref>

[[世界衛生組織|世界衛生組織]]和[[美國疾病控制與預防中心|美國疾病控制與預防中心]]都建議幾乎任何大於六個月的人，每年應該施打流感疫苗。<ref name="WHO2012">{{Template:Cite journal|title=Vaccines against influenza WHO position paper – November 2012.|url=http://www.who.int/wer/2012/wer8747.pdf|date=Nov 23, 2012|journal=Wkly Epidemiol Rec|issue=46|volume=87|pages=461–76|pmid=23210147}}</ref><ref name="WhoShouldVax">{{Template:Cite web|url=http://www.cdc.gov/flu/protect/whoshouldvax.htm|title=Who Should Get Vaccinated Against Influenza|accessdate=2015-12-08|date=2015-11-04|publisher=U.S. [[Centers_for_Disease_Control_and_Prevention|Centers for Disease Control and Prevention]]}}</ref>尤其是[[孕婦|孕婦]]、六個月大至五歲的孩童、有慢性疾病者、[[美國原住民|美洲原住民]]、以及醫療相關人員。<ref name="AHFS2015"/><ref name="WHO2012">{{Template:Cite journal|title=Vaccines against influenza WHO position paper – November 2012.|url=http://www.who.int/wer/2012/wer8747.pdf|date=Nov 23, 2012|journal=Wkly Epidemiol Rec|issue=46|volume=87|pages=461–76|pmid=23210147}}</ref>

疫苗大致上是安全的。在兒童，有5-10%會有[[發燒|發燒]]的反應，也有可能有肌肉痛、疲倦等症狀。在某些年份，疫苗可能在年長者引起[[格林－巴利综合征|格林－巴利综合征]]，機率約百萬分之一。疫苗不應該施打於對蛋嚴重[[過敏|過敏]]者、或有流感疫苗過敏史者。疫苗有不活化及減毒病毒兩種。孕婦應該使用不活化疫苗。在施打類型方面，有肌肉內注射、鼻噴劑、皮內注射等形式。<ref name="WHO2012">{{Template:Cite journal|title=Vaccines against influenza WHO position paper – November 2012.|url=http://www.who.int/wer/2012/wer8747.pdf|date=Nov 23, 2012|journal=Wkly Epidemiol Rec|issue=46|volume=87|pages=461–76|pmid=23210147}}</ref>

對抗流感的疫苗在1930年代開始，一直到1945年開始在美國廣為使用。<ref>{{Template:Cite book|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=uOE-3atWZssC&pg=PA49|title=Vaccines for pandemic influenza|date=2009|publisher=Springer|isbn=9783540921653|location=Dordrecht|page=49|last1=Compans|first1=Richard W.}}</ref><ref>{{Template:Cite book|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=vJKeBQAAQBAJ&pg=PA61|title=Vaccine Analysis: Strategies, Principles, and Control|date=2014|publisher=Springer|isbn=9783662450246|page=61}}</ref>它被列在[[世界卫生组织基本药物标准清单|世界衛生組織基本藥物標準清單]]，即其建議之對基礎[[健康照護系統|健康照護系統]]最重要的藥物。<ref>{{Template:Cite web|url=http://www.who.int/medicines/publications/essentialmedicines/EML2015_8-May-15.pdf|title=19th WHO Model List of Essential Medicines (April 2015)|accessdate=May 10, 2015|date=April 2015|publisher=WHO}}</ref>在2014年，其批發價約 5.25 美元。<ref>{{Template:Cite web|url=http://erc.msh.org/dmpguide/resultsdetail.cfm?language=english&code=INF5A&s_year=2014&year=2014&str=0%2E5%20ml&desc=Vaccine%2C%20Influenza&pack=new&frm=AMPOULE&rte=INJ&class_code2=19%2E3%2E&supplement=&class_name=%2819%2E3%2E%29Vaccines%3Cbr%3E|title=Vaccine, influenza|accessdate=6 December 2015|website=International Drug Price Indicator Guide}}</ref>在美國，則是少於 25 美元。<ref name="Ric2015">{{Template:Cite book|title=Tarascon Pocket Pharmacopoeia 2015 Deluxe Lab-Coat Edition|date=2015|publisher=Jones & Bartlett Learning|isbn=9781284057560|page=314|last1=Hamilton|first1=Richart}}</ref>
