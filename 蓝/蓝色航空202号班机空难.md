'''蓝色航空202号班机'''是[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]]低成本航空公司[[蓝色航空_(巴基斯坦)|蓝色航空]]（{{lang-en|Air Blue}}）由[[卡拉奇|卡拉奇]]飞往[[伊斯兰堡|伊斯兰堡]]的定期航班。2010年7月28日，该航班由一架[[空中客车A321|空中客车A321]]执飞，在降落前坠毁于伊斯兰堡北部。机上146名乘客和6名机组成员共152人全部遇难<ref>{{cite news|language=en|publisher=AP|title=Plane carrying 152 crashes in Pakistan; 5 dead|url=http://news.yahoo.com/s/ap/20100728/ap_on_re_as/as_pakistan;_ylt=AjtBYyIp6yrxSWJT8sykWOus0NUE;_ylu=X3oDMTNja2c4ZDd0BGFzc2V0A2FwLzIwMTAwNzI4L2FzX3Bha2lzdGFuBGNjb2RlA21vc3Rwb3B1bGFyBGNwb3MDMwRwb3MDMTAEcHQDaG9tZV9jb2tlBHNlYwN5bl90b3Bfc3RvcnkEc2xrA3BsYW5lY2Fycnlpbg--|date=2010-07-28|accessdate=2010-07-28}}</ref>，飞机坠毁原因是在恶劣天气下飞行员失误<ref>{{cite news|language=en|publisher=wordpress.com|title=Air Blue’s Air Bus Flight No 202 Crashes near Margalla Hill Islamabad: 156 passengers onboard|url=http://nayyab.wordpress.com/2010/07/28/air-blues-air-bus-flight-no-202-crashes-near-margalla-hill-islamabad-156-passengers-onboard/|date=2010-07-28|accessdate=2010-07-28}}</ref><ref>http://code7700.com/pdfs/investigation_report_abq_202.pdf</ref>。这是巴基斯坦迄今发生的最致命的空难事故。<ref>{{cite news | url = https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-south-asia-10797614 | title = Pakistan mourns victims of its worst-ever air crash | date = 2010-07-29 | agency = BBC News | accessdate = 2010-07-29}}</ref><ref name="aviation-safety1">{{cite web | title = ASN Aircraft accident Airbus A321-231 AP-BJB Islamabad-Benazir Bhutto International Airport (ISB) | url = http://aviation-safety.net/database/record.php?id=20100728-0 | publisher = [[Aviation_Safety_Network|Aviation Safety Network]] |accessdate = 2010-07-28| archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20100731083206/http://aviation-safety.net/database/record.php?id=20100728-0| archivedate= 2010-07-31 | deadurl= no}}</ref>
