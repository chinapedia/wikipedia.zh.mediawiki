{{對女性的暴力行為}}

'''对妇女的暴力行为''' （英文：Violence Against women，缩写：VAW）泛指针对[[女性|女性]]或对女性产生影响的暴力行为。有时，这种行为属于一种[[仇恨罪行|仇恨罪行]]<ref>{{Cite book |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=2YdCbgXjr-EC&pg=PA405 |chapter=Hate Crime Statutes: A Promising Tool for Fighting Violence Against Women |first=Marguerite |last=Angelari |title=Pornography, sex work, and hate speech |editor=Karen J. Maschke |publisher=Taylor & Francis |year=1997}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=DiBIZcx1nPcC&pg=PA58 |title=Hate Crimes: Causes, Controls, and Controversies |first=Phyllis B. |last=Gerstenfeld |year=2013 |publisher=Sage}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=2ZNnXj_SbOsC&pg=PA271 |title=Hate and bias crime: a reader |editor=Barbara Perry |year=2003 |publisher=Psychology Press |first=Beverly |last=McPhail |chapter=Gender-Bias Hate Crimes: A Review}}</ref>，指针对某一特定[[性别|性别]]的犯罪行为。对女性的暴力行为是带有性别歧视的，也就是说，施暴者因为施暴对象是女性而对其实施暴力，或是由于父系社会的观念。联合国[[消除对妇女的暴力行为宣言|消除对妇女的暴力行为宣言]]表明：<blockquote>“对妇女的暴力行为是历史上男女权利不平等关系的一种表现”“对妇女的暴力行为是严酷的社会机制之一，它迫使妇女陷入从属于男子的地位”<ref>{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.un.org/en/ga/search/view_doc.asp?symbol=A/RES/48/104&referer=http://www.un.org/depts/dhl/resguide/r48_resolutions_table_eng.htm&Lang=C |title=A/RES/48/104 - 消除对妇女的暴力行为宣言|publisher=United Nations General Assembly |accessdate=2014-08-06}}</ref></blockquote>
前任[[联合国秘书长|联合国秘书长]][[科菲·安南|科菲·安南]]在2006年发布在联合国妇女发展基金会（英文：United Nations Development Fund of Women，缩写：UNDFW）网站上的一篇报告中指出：<blockquote>对妇女、女童的暴力行为是一个普遍的问题。全世界超过三分之一的女性都遭受到不同的程度上的暴力行为，包括毒打、强奸，熟识者的暴力行为。<ref name=Moradian>Moradian, Azad. [http://en.tolerancy.org/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=176:2009-09-15-08-37-55&catid=43:events-a-reports&Itemid=90 ''Domestic Violence against Single and Married Women in Iranian Society.''] Tolerancy International. September 2009. Retrieved 16 Nov. 2011.</ref></blockquote>

针对女性的暴力可包括个体实施的暴力行为和国家实施的暴力行为。个体实施暴力的方式有[[强奸|强奸]]、[[家庭暴力|家庭暴力]]、[[性骚扰|性骚扰]]、强制使用避孕药、溺弃女婴、产前性别选择、孕期暴力和聚众暴力。除此之外，有些不良传统和习俗也是针对女性的暴力行为，例如[[荣誉谋杀|荣誉谋杀]]、嫁妆暴力、切割女性生殖器官、骗婚和[[逼婚|逼婚]]。国家使用暴力的方式有，战时实施战争强奸、[[性暴力|性暴力]]和性奴役行为；强制绝育、强制堕胎；警察和权贵人士实施的暴力行为；[[投石|投石]]和 [[鞭刑|鞭刑]]。犯罪集团有组织地实施非法买卖女性和强制卖淫活动，这些同样属于针对女性的暴力行为。<ref name="Prügl, E. 2013">Prügl, E. (Director) (November 25, 2013). Violence Against Women. Gender and International Affairs Class 2013. Lecture conducted from The Graduate Institute of International and Development Studies (IHEID), Geneva, Switzerland.</ref>

[[世界卫生组织|世界卫生组织]] (WHO)，在其针对女性暴力的研究中，将女性一生分为五个阶段：“1) 出生前，2) 婴儿时期，3) 少女时期，4) 青春期和成人后，5) 晚年”<ref>(1997). [http://www.who.int/gender/violence/v4.pdf Violence against women: Definition and scope of the problem.] World Health Organization, 1, 1-3. Retrieved November 30, 2013.</ref>。

这些年来，随着各种大会、[[欧盟|欧盟]]组织提出女性暴力这一问题，故而越来越受到国际上的关注。欧盟法中包括[[性骚扰|性骚扰]]<ref>Directive 2002/73/EC - equal treatment of 23 September 2002 amending Council Directive 76/207/EEC on the implementation of the principle of equal treatment for men and women as regards access to employment, vocational training and promotion, and working conditions [http://eur-lex.europa.eu/LexUriServ/LexUriServ.do?uri=CONSLEG:1976L0207:20021005:EN:PDF]</ref>和[[贩卖人口|贩卖人口]]<ref>DIRECTIVE 2011/36/EU OF THE EUROPEAN PARLIAMENT AND OF THE COUNCIL of 5 April 2011 on preventing and combating trafficking in human beings and protecting its victims, and replacing Council Framework Decision 2002/629/JH      http://eur-lex.europa.eu/LexUriServ/LexUriServ.do?uri=OJ:L:2011:101:0001:0011:EN:PDF</ref>相关法规。
