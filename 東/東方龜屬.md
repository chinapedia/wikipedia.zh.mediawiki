'''東方龜屬'''（'''''Heosemys'''''）是[[地龜科|地龜科]]下的一個[[屬|屬]]，其成員都是淡水龜，1964年，東方龜屬才從[[地龜屬|地龜屬]]分離出來。

== 種 ==

東方龜屬下有四[[種|種]]：<ref name="hartner">{{cite journal|last1=Artner|first1=H.|year=2008|title=The World's Extant Turtle Species|journal=Emys|volume=15|issue=3|publisher=[http://www.chelonia2002.com Chelonia 2002 - Turtle Center]|url=http://www.chelonia2002.com/ownimages/artnerchecklist.pdf|accessdate=March 27, 2011|author=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110912072018/http://www.chelonia2002.com/ownimages/artnerchecklist.pdf|archive-date=2011-09-12|dead-url=yes}}</ref><ref name="checklist">Turtle taxonomy Working Group (Rhodin, A.G.J., van Dijk, P.P, Iverson, J.B., and Shaffer, H.B.).2010. [https://www.webcitation.org/5uzfktoIh?url=http://www.iucn-tftsg.org/wp-content/uploads/file/Accounts/crm_5_000_checklist_v3_2010.pdf Turtles of the world, 2010 update: annotated checklist of taxonomy, synonymy, distribution, and conservation status]. In: Rhodin, A.G.J., Pritchard, P.C.H., van Dijk, P.P., Saumure, R.A., Buhlmann, K.A., Iverson, J.B., and Mittermeier, R.A. (Eds.). Conservation Biology of Freshwater Turtles and Tortoises: A Compilation Project of the IUCN/SSC Tortoise and Freshwater Turtle Specialist Group. ''Chelonian Research Monographs'' No. 5. pp. 000.85-000.164, {{doi|10.3854/crm.5.000.checklist.v3.2010}}</ref>

* [[扁東方龜|扁東方龜]] <small>(Boulenger, 1903)</small>, ''Heosemys depressa''
* [[大東方龜|大東方龜]] <small>(Gray, 1860)</small>, ''Heosemys grandis''
* [[刺東方龜|刺東方龜]] <small>(Gray, 1831)</small>, ''Heosemys spinosa''
* [[廟龜|廟龜]] <small>(Anderson, 1875)</small>, ''Heosemys annandalii''

一些其他的龜也一度曾被分在該屬下，例如[[白頭龜屬|白頭龜屬]]下的[[白頭龜|白頭龜]]，[[粗頸龜屬|粗頸龜屬]]下的[[雷島東方龜|雷島東方龜]]以及[[蔗林龜屬|蔗林龜屬]]下的[[蔗林龜|蔗林龜]]。
