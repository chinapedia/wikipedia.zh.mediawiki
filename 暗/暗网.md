'''暗网'''（{{lang-en|Dark web}}）是存在于[[黑暗网络|黑暗网络]]、[[覆盖网络|覆盖网络]]上的[[万维网|万维网]]内容，只能用特殊軟體、特殊授權、或對電腦做特殊設定才能访问<ref>{{cite news|last1=Greenberg|first1=Andy|title=Hacker Lexicon: What Is the dark web?|url=https://www.wired.com/2014/11/hacker-lexicon-whats-dark-web/|publisher=Wired|accessdate=2015-08-27|date=2014-11-19|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150830233549/http://www.wired.com/2014/11/hacker-lexicon-whats-dark-web|archivedate=2015-08-30}}</ref><ref name=pcadvisor>{{cite news|last1=Egan|first1=Matt|title=What is the dark web? How to access the dark website – How to turn out the lights and access the dark web (and why you might want to)|url=http://www.pcadvisor.co.uk/how-to/internet/what-is-dark-web-how-access-dark-web-3593569/|accessdate=2015-06-18|date=2015-01-12|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150619001447/http://www.pcadvisor.co.uk/how-to/internet/what-is-dark-web-how-access-dark-web-3593569/|archivedate=2015-06-19}}</ref>。暗网构成了[[深网|深网]]的一小部分，深网网络没有被[[网络搜索引擎|网络搜索引擎]]索引，有时“深网”这一术语被错误地用于指代暗网<ref name="dictionary">{{cite news|last1=Solomon|first1=Jane|title=The Deep Web vs. The dark web|url=http://blog.dictionary.com/dark-web/|accessdate=2015-05-26|date=2015-05-06|deadurl=no|archiveurl=http://webarchive.loc.gov/all/20150509023641/http://blog.dictionary.com/dark-web/|archivedate=2015-05-09}}</ref><ref name="dark_web">{{cite news|last1=Greenberg|first1=Andy|title=Hacker Lexicon: What Is the dark web?|url=https://www.wired.com/2014/11/hacker-lexicon-whats-dark-web/|accessdate=2015-06-06 |date=2014-11-19 |work=Wired|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150607062159/http://www.wired.com/2014/11/hacker-lexicon-whats-dark-web/|archivedate=2015-06-07}}{{cite web|url=http://www.brightplanet.com/2014/03/clearing-confusion-deep-web-vs-dark-web/|title=Clearing Up Confusion – Deep Web vs. dark web|website=BrightPlanet|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150516160539/http://www.brightplanet.com/2014/03/clearing-confusion-deep-web-vs-dark-web/|archivedate=2015-05-16}}</ref><ref name="npr.org">{{cite news|last1=NPR Staff|title=Going Dark: The Internet Behind The Internet|url=https://www.npr.org/sections/alltechconsidered/2014/05/25/315821415/going-dark-the-internet-behind-the-internet|accessdate=2015-05-29|date=2014-05-25|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150527071850/http://www.npr.org/sections/alltechconsidered/2014/05/25/315821415/going-dark-the-internet-behind-the-internet|archivedate=2015-05-27}}</ref><ref>The dark web Revealed. ''Popular Science''. pp. 20–21</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Greenberg|first1=Andy|title=Hacker Lexicon: What Is the dark web?|url=https://www.wired.com/2014/11/hacker-lexicon-whats-dark-web/|accessdate=2015-06-06|date=2014-11-19 |work=Wired|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150607062159/http://www.wired.com/2014/11/hacker-lexicon-whats-dark-web/|archivedate=2015-06-07}}</ref>。

构成暗网的黑暗网络包括{{link-en|F2F|Friend-to-friend}}的小型[[点对点网络|点对点网络]]以及由公共组织和个人运营的大型流行网络，如[[Tor|Tor]]、[[自由网|自由网]]、[[I2P|I2P]]和{{link-en|Riffle (匿名網路)|Riffle (anonymity network)|Riffle}}。暗网用户基于常规网络[[明文|未加密]]的性质将其称为[[明網|明網]]<ref>{{cite web|title=Clearnet vs hidden services – why you should be careful|url=http://www.deepdotweb.com/jolly-rogers-security-guide-for-beginners/clearnet-vs-hidden-services-why-you-should-be-careful/|website=[[DeepDotWeb|DeepDotWeb]]|accessdate=2015-06-04|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150628204337/https://www.deepdotweb.com/jolly-rogers-security-guide-for-beginners/clearnet-vs-hidden-services-why-you-should-be-careful/|archivedate=2015-06-28}}</ref>。Tor暗网可以称为'''洋葱区域'''（onionland）<ref name="darknetthing">{{cite news|last1=Chacos|first1=Brad|title=Meet Darknet, the hidden, anonymous underbelly of the searchable Web|url=http://www.pcworld.com/article/2046227/meet-darknet-the-hidden-anonymous-underbelly-of-the-searchable-web.html|accessdate=2015-08-16|date=2013-08-12|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150812065847/http://www.pcworld.com/article/2046227/meet-darknet-the-hidden-anonymous-underbelly-of-the-searchable-web.html|archivedate=2015-08-12}}</ref>，其使用网络[[頂級域|頂級域]]后缀[[.onion|.onion]]和[[洋葱路由|洋葱路由]]的流量匿名化技术。

zh-hant:名詞}-经常被混淆<ref>{{cite news|last1=Daily Mail Reporter|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2454735/The-disturbing-world-Deep-Web-contract-killers-drug-dealers-ply-trade-internet.html|title=The disturbing world of the Deep Web, where contract killers and drug dealers ply their trade on the internet|accessdate=2015-05-25|date=2013-10-11|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20131030190858/http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2454735/The-disturbing-world-Deep-Web-contract-killers-drug-dealers-ply-trade-internet.html|archivedate=2013-10-30}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://fusion.net/story/145885/nasa-is-indexing-the-deep-web-to-show-mankind-what-google-wont/|title=NASA is indexing the 'Deep Web' to show mankind what Google won't|website=Fusion|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150630010143/http://fusion.net/story/145885/nasa-is-indexing-the-deep-web-to-show-mankind-what-google-wont/|archivedate=2015-06-30}}</ref>，有人建议应该区分它们<ref name="npr.org"/><ref name="confusion">{{cite web|url=http://www.brightplanet.com/2014/03/clearing-confusion-deep-web-vs-dark-web/|title=Clearing Up Confusion – Deep Web vs. Dark Web|website=BrightPlanet|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150516160539/http://www.brightplanet.com/2014/03/clearing-confusion-deep-web-vs-dark-web/|archivedate=2015-05-16}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Solomon|first1=Jane|title=The Deep Web vs. The Dark Web|url=http://blog.dictionary.com/dark-web/|accessdate=2015-05-26|date=2015-05-06|deadurl=no|archiveurl=http://webarchive.loc.gov/all/20150509023641/http://blog.dictionary.com/dark-web/|archivedate=2015-05-09}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Greenberg|first1=Andy|title=Hacker Lexicon: What Is the Dark Web?|url=https://www.wired.com/2014/11/hacker-lexicon-whats-dark-web/|accessdate=2015-06-06|date=2014-11-19|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150607062159/http://www.wired.com/2014/11/hacker-lexicon-whats-dark-web/|archivedate=2015-06-07}}</ref>。

== 定义 ==
{{main|黑暗网络}}

黑暗网络网站只能通过[[Tor|Tor]]（“洋葱路由”项目）和[[I2P|I2P]]（“隐形网计划”）等网络访问<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://hplusmagazine.com/2015/06/29/the-deep-web-and-its-darknets/|title=The Deep Web and Its Darknets – h+ Media|date=2015-06-29|website=h+ Media|access-date=2016-11-18|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150706093936/http://hplusmagazine.com/2015/06/29/the-deep-web-and-its-darknets/|archivedate=2015-07-06}}</ref>。黑暗网络用户广泛使用Tor浏览器和Tor可访问的站点，站点可以通过“.onion”域名识别<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://eds.b.ebscohost.com/eds/detail/detail?sid=390cc066-699e-4f33-860a-9d2dd97d9285@sessionmgr107&vid=0&hid=126&bdata=JnNpdGU9ZWRzLWxpdmU=#AN=117120750&db=edo|title=The 21st Century DarkNet Market: Lessons from the Fall of Silk Road: Start Your Search!|website=eds.b.ebscohost.com|access-date=2016-10-26|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170605201049/http://eds.b.ebscohost.com/eds/detail/detail?sid=390cc066-699e-4f33-860a-9d2dd97d9285@sessionmgr107&vid=0&hid=126&bdata=JnNpdGU9ZWRzLWxpdmU=#AN=117120750&db=edo|archive-date=2017-06-05|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。Tor专注于提供对互联网的匿名访问，而I2P则专注于匿名网站托管<ref>{{Cite journal|last1=Crenshaw,|first1=Adrian.|year=|title=Darknets and hidden servers: Identifying the true ip/network identity of i2p service hosts, Black Hat (2011)|url=|journal=|volume=|pages=|via=}}</ref>。分层加密系统使得黑暗网络用户的身份和位置保持匿名，无法被追踪。黑暗网络加密技术通过大量中间服务器发送用户数据，保护了用户身份并保障匿名。传输的信息只能由路径中的后续节点解密，而这些节点通向出口节点。因为系统过于复杂，所以几乎不可能再生节点路径并逐层解密信息<ref name=":0">{{Cite journal|title=Cryptopolitik and the Darknet|journal=Survival (00396338)|volume= 58| issue = 1|pages= 7–38. 32p|doi=10.1080/00396338.2016.1142085|year=2016 | last1 = Moore | first1 = Daniel}}</ref>。由于高层次的加密，网站无法跟踪其用户的地理位置和IP，用户也无法获取网站主机的有关信息。因此，黑暗网络用户之间的通信是高度加密的，允许用户以保密方式交流、发博客以及共享文件。<ref>[https://www.academia.edu/20813843/DARKNET_SOCIAL_MEDIA_AND_EXTREMISM_ADDRESSING_INDONESIAN_COUNTERTERRORISM_ON_THE_INTERNET Darknet, Social Media, and Extremism: Addressing Indonesian Counterterrorism on the Internet] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170604003640/http://www.academia.edu/20813843/DARKNET_SOCIAL_MEDIA_AND_EXTREMISM_ADDRESSING_INDONESIAN_COUNTERTERRORISM_ON_THE_INTERNET |date=2017-06-04 }}</ref>

黑暗网络还被用于非法活动，如非法交易、非法论坛以及恋童癖者和恐怖分子的媒介交流<ref name=":1">{{Cite journal|title=The Dark Net. Rolling Stone, 0035791X, 11/5/2015, Issue 1247|journal=|volume=}}</ref>。与此同时，传统网站为Tor浏览器提供了访问方式，以便与用户建立联系。例如，[[ProPublica|ProPublica]]推出了一个Tor用户专用的新版网站<ref name=":23">{{Cite web|url=http://eds.b.ebscohost.com/eds/detail/detail?sid=ad822f46-344d-47ac-8c12-09663ebad15f@sessionmgr103&vid=0&hid=127&bdata=JnNpdGU9ZWRzLWxpdmU=#AN=113873661&db=bth|title=A Marketer's Guide to the Dark Web: Start Your Search!|website=eds.b.ebscohost.com|access-date=2016-10-27|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170604045343/http://eds.b.ebscohost.com/eds/detail/detail?sid=ad822f46-344d-47ac-8c12-09663ebad15f@sessionmgr103&vid=0&hid=127&bdata=JnNpdGU9ZWRzLWxpdmU=#AN=113873661&db=bth|archive-date=2017-06-04|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。

===杰米·巴特利特===
杰米·巴特利特（Jamie Bartlett）是《[[每日电讯报|每日电讯报]]》的记者和技术[[網誌|博主]]，与[[萨塞克斯大学|萨塞克斯大学]]合作的Demos社区媒体分析中心主任。在他的-{zh-hans:书; zh-hant:書籍}-《-{zh-cn:暗网;zh-tw:黑暗網路}-》（''The Dark Net''）中<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/books/reviews/the-dark-netinside-the-digital-underworld-by-jamie-bartlett-book-review-9696473.html|title=The Dark Net:Inside the Digital Underworld by Jamie Bartlett, book review|last=Burrell|first=Ian|date=2014-08-28|website=Independent|publisher=|access-date=|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150620142200/http://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/books/reviews/the-dark-netinside-the-digital-underworld-by-jamie-bartlett-book-review-9696473.html|archivedate=2015-06-20}}</ref>，巴特利特描绘了暗网的世界及其在不同背景下对人类行为的影响。例如，本书开头讲述了一个年轻女孩的故事，她通过在-{zh-hans:网上; zh-hant:網路上}-发裸照来寻求正面反馈，以建立自尊。她最终被社交媒体网站所追踪，她的朋友和家人被她的裸照所淹没。这个故事突出了暗网所允许的[[人际关系|人际关系]]的多样性，但也提醒读者如何参与像暗网这样罕见地与大型网络完全分离的[[覆盖网络|覆盖网络]]。巴特利特的主要目标是探索暗网及其对社会的影响。他探索了不同的[[次文化|亚文化]]，其中一些对社会有积极意义，有些则对社会有负面影响。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.wnyc.org/story/growth-dark-subcultures-internet/|title=The Growth of Dark Subcultures On the Internet, The Leonard Lopate Show|date=2015-06-02|website=WNYC|publisher=|access-date=|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161020043736/http://www.wnyc.org/story/growth-dark-subcultures-internet/|archivedate=2016-10-20}}</ref>

2015年6月，巴特利特在[[TED大会|TED大会]]上发表题为“神秘的黑暗网络如何变成主流”的演讲，向观众介绍了暗网背后的想法<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.ted.com/talks/jamie_bartlett_how_the_mysterious_dark_net_is_going_mainstream#t-705685|title=How the mysterious dark net is going mainstream|last=Bartlett|first=Jamie|date=June 2015|website=ted.com|publisher=|access-date=|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161020042728/https://www.ted.com/talks/jamie_bartlett_how_the_mysterious_dark_net_is_going_mainstream#t-705685|archivedate=2016-10-20}}</ref>。
