'''丙酸镍'''是镍(II)的丙酸盐，化学式为(CH<sub>3</sub>CH<sub>2</sub>COO)<sub>2</sub>Ni。

==制备==
丙酸镍可由[[丙酸钡|丙酸钡]]和[[硫酸镍|硫酸镍]]反应得到：<ref>R.I Bickley, H.G.M Edwards, R Gustar ''et al''. Synthesis and vibrational spectroscopic characterisation of nickel (II) propionate tetrahydrate, Ni(CH<sub>3</sub>CH<sub>2</sub>COO)<sub>2</sub>·4H<sub>2</sub>O, and its aqueous solution. ''Journal of Molecular Structure'', 248 (1991) 237-250</ref>
: (C<sub>2</sub>H<sub>5</sub>COO)<sub>2</sub>Ba + NiSO<sub>4</sub> → BaSO<sub>4</sub>↓ + (C<sub>2</sub>H<sub>5</sub>COO)<sub>2</sub>Ni
