'''血小板衍生生長因子受體'''（Platelet-derived growth factor receptors，PDGF-R）為[[血小板衍生生長因子|血小板衍生生長因子]]（PDGF）蛋白質家族的[[受體|受體]]，位於[[細胞膜|細胞膜]]表面，屬於{{le|酪胺酸激酶受體|receptor tyrosine kinase}}的一種。PDGF的[[PDGFA|次單元A]]和[[PDGFB|次單元B]]在調控細胞[[細胞增殖|增殖]]、[[細胞分化|分化]]、[[細胞生長|生長]]、[[細胞發育|發育]]上扮演相當重要的角色。如果調控不正常，可能會引發[[癌症|癌症]]等多種疾病。<ref name="pmid2538922">{{Cite journal| author = Williams LT | title = Signal transduction by the platelet-derived growth factor receptor | journal = Science | volume = 243 | issue = 4898 | pages = 1564–70 |date=March 1989 | pmid = 2538922 | doi = 10.1126/science.2538922| url = | issn = }}</ref>

PDGF-R有兩種型態，分別為[[PDGFRA|A]]和[[PDGFRB|B]]，兩種型態來自於不同基因。<ref name="pmid2543106">{{Cite journal| author = Heldin CH, Westermark B | title = Platelet-derived growth factor: three isoforms and two receptor types | journal = Trends Genet. | volume = 5 | issue = 4 | pages = 108–11 |date=April 1989 | pmid = 2543106 | doi = 10.1016/0168-9525(89)90040-1| url = | issn = }}</ref>由於PDRF-R必須由兩者結合成為雙體之後才能作用，因此PDGFRA和PDGFRB可以互相組合或自我組合為均聚物（homodimerizes，即A配A、B配B）或雜二聚物（heterodimerizes，即A配B）<ref name="pmid1315403">{{Cite journal| author = Heldin CH, Ostman A, Eriksson A, Siegbahn A, Claesson-Welsh L, Westermark B | title = Platelet-derived growth factor: isoform-specific signalling via heterodimeric or homodimeric receptor complexes | journal = Kidney Int. | volume = 41 | issue = 3 | pages = 571–4 |date=March 1992 | pmid = 1315403 | doi = 10.1038/ki.1992.84| url = | issn = }}</ref>
