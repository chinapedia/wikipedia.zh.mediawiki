'''血清白蛋白'''（{{lang-en|Serum albumin}}），常简称为'''白蛋白'''（{{lang-en|albumin}}）是一种由人类[[基因|基因]] ''ALB'' 所编码的[[球状蛋白质|球状蛋白质]]<ref name="pmid6292049">{{cite journal | author = Hawkins JW, Dugaiczyk A | title = The human serum albumin gene: structure of a unique locus | journal = Gene | volume = 19 | issue = 1 | pages = 55–8 | year = 1982 | pmid = 6292049 | doi = 10.1016/0378-1119(82)90188-3| url = | issn = }}</ref><ref name="pmid6192711">{{cite journal | author = Harper ME, Dugaiczyk A | title = Linkage of the evolutionarily-related serum albumin and alpha-fetoprotein genes within q11-22 of human chromosome 4 | journal = Am. J. Hum. Genet. | volume = 35 | issue = 4 | pages = 565–72 |date=July 1983 | pmid = 6192711 | pmc = 1685723 | doi = | url = | issn = }}</ref><ref name="entrez">{{cite web | title = Entrez Gene: albumin| url = http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/sites/entrez?Db=gene&Cmd=ShowDetailView&TermToSearch=213| accessdate = }}</ref>。

血清白蛋白由[[肝臟|肝臟]]细胞生产，通常溶解在[[血浆|血浆]]中，是[[哺乳动物|哺乳动物]]最常见的[[血浆蛋白|血浆蛋白]]。白蛋白对维持血液的[[膨胀压|膨胀压]]有重要作用。
