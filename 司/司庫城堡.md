'''司庫城堡'''（{{lang-hr|Gradina Kamerlengo}}，{{lang-en|Kamerlengo Castle}}）是位於[[克羅埃西亞|克羅埃西亞]]城市[[特羅吉爾|特羅吉爾]]的一座[[城堡|城堡]]。司庫城堡由[[威尼斯共和國|威尼斯共和國]]修建<ref>[http://books.google.com.au/books?id=3bGvqYP_LokC&pg=PA144&dq=Kamerlengo+Castle&hl=en&ei=fbvwS6KKBIugkQWs5tHWBg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=5&ved=0CDkQ6AEwBA#v=onepage&q=Kamerlengo%20Castle&f=false The Other Futurism:] Futurist Activity in Venice, Padua  & Verona ''by'' Willard Bohn</ref>。它建于15世纪中期<ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=ue-4o37AficC&pg=PA158&dq=Kamerlengo+Castle&client=safari&cd=1#v=onepage&q=Kamerlengo%20Castle&f=false Frommer's Croatia] By Karen Torme Olson & Sanja Bazulic Olson</ref>。
