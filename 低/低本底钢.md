'''低本底钢'''（{{lang-en|Low-background steel}}），又稱'''低背景鋼'''，是在1940年代和1950年代第一代[[原子弹|原子弹]]爆炸前生产的[[钢材|钢材]]。由于在1945年，随着[[三位一体核试验|三位一体核试验]]、[[广岛市原子弹爆炸|广岛]]、[[长崎市原子弹爆炸|长崎]]核轰炸，以及[[冷战|冷战]]早期一系列[[核试验|核武器试验]]的进行，世界[[背景輻射|背景辐射量]]有明显升高。现代生产的钢铁由于普遍使用[[大气|大气]]气体而被[[放射性同位素|放射性同位素]]污染，低背景鋼的称呼由此而来。因为其没有受到过此类污染，这种钢材被使用在高精度放射性同位素检测设备上。 

== 放射性同位素污染 ==
从1856年到二十世纪中叶，炼钢采用的是[[贝塞麦转炉炼钢法|贝塞麦转炉炼钢法]]，这种方法通过向高炉中鼓入空气来将[[生铁|生铁]]转换为钢。到20世纪中叶，许多炼钢厂已经改为使用氧气吹顶制钢流程，这种方法使用[[氧气|纯氧]]代替了空气。但是此两种方法都使用了容易被空气中微粒子污染的大气气体。当今的大气中携带有各種放射性同位素，比如[[钴-60|钴-60]]，就会沉积在钢铁中，使其表现出微弱的放射性。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.straightdope.com/columns/read/2971/is-steel-from-scuttled-german-warships-valuable-because-it-isn-t-contaminated-with-radioactivity | title = Is steel from scuttled German warships valuable because it isn't contaminated with radioactivity? | work = The Straight Dope | date = December 10, 2010 | first = Cecil | last = Adams}}</ref> 

1963年，世界上的人为背景辐射达到了高峰，高于自然水平0.15[[毫西弗|毫西弗]]，这一年通过了[[部分禁止核试验条约|部分禁止核试验条约]]。从那时候开始，人为背景辐射程开始呈几何级数下降，到每年高于自然水平0.005毫西弗。<ref name=UNSCEAR>{{citation | url = http://www.unscear.org/unscear/en/publications/2008_1.html | author = [[United_Nations_Scientific_Committee_on_the_Effects_of_Atomic_Radiation|United Nations Scientific Committee on the Effects of Atomic Radiation]] (UNSCEAR) | title = Sources and Effects of Ionizing Radiation (UNSCEAR 2008 Report): Volume I | publisher = United Nations | location = New York | year = 2010 | isbn = 978-92-1-142274-0 | page = 6 | quote = The estimated annual per caput effective dose of ionizing radiation due to global fallout from atmospheric nuclear weapons testing was highest in 1963, at 0.11 mSv, and subsequently fell to its present level of about 0.005 mSv (see figure II). This source of exposure will decline only very slowly in the future as most of it is now due to the long-lived radio-nuclide carbon-14.}}</ref> 

但是由于废旧钢铁[[供应链|供应链]]的存在，从其中回收的钴60仍然污染了近代生产的钢铁。<ref>{{citation | url = http://www.iaea.org/Publications/Booklets/SealedRadioactiveSources/pdfs/handout_scrap.pdf | title = Reducing Risks in the Scrap Metal Industry - Sealed Radioactive Sources | publisher = [[International_Atomic_Energy_Agency|International Atomic Energy Agency]] (IAEA) | date = September 2005 | location = Austria | number = IAEA/PI/A.83 / 05-09511 | pages = 2-6 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20060614013429/http://www.iaea.org/Publications/Booklets/SealedRadioactiveSources/pdfs/handout_scrap.pdf | archivedate = 2006年6月14日 | deadurl = yes }}</ref>但2000年代以後人為活動產生的背景輻射接近自然界的水平，在控制鋼材煉製原料來源（主要方法是不使用回收鋼材）、改進煉製工藝的情況下，仍可製造出符合要求的低本底鋼。<ref>{{citation | url = http://www.jspf.or.jp/Journal/PDF_JSPF/jspf2011_03/jspf2011_03-167.pdf | title = 低放射化フェライト鋼製造技術の現状と課題 | publisher = プラズマ・核融合学会 | date = January 2011 | location = Japan }}</ref>

== 应用 ==
低背景钢主要应用在以下设备上：
* [[盖革计数器|盖革计数器]]
* 医学设备：全身计测装置和肺计数器
* 科学装备：[[光子学|光子学]]
* 航空航天传感器
由于这些是用于测试放射性排放量的设备，需要在非常低放射性的环境下才能正常工作。低背景计数舱室是用低背景钢制成，具有非常强的辐射屏蔽能力，用于测量细微核排放。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.lm.doe.gov/land/sites/co/rocky_flats/haer/base/Buildings/122.htm | title = Rocky Flats Plant, Emergency Medical Services Facility - HAER No. CO-83-S (Rocky Flats Plant, Building 122) | publisher = [[United_States_Department_of_Energy|U.S. Department of Energy]], Office of Legacy Management | first1 = D. Jayne | last1 = Aaron | first2 = Judith | last2 = Berryman | year = 1997}}</ref>

现今，低背景钢的主要来源是在三位一体核试验之前沉入水中的各种船舶，其中最有名为在一战时期，[[斯卡帕湾|斯卡帕湾]][[公海艦隊自沉事件|凿沉的德国战列舰]]。 <ref name="Butler">{{cite book |last=Butler | first = Daniel Allen |title=Distant Victory: the Battle of Jutland and the Allied Triumph in the First World War |page=229 |publisher=Praeger Security International (Greenwood Publishing Group) | location = Westport, Connecticut, USA | year = 2006 | isbn = 0-275-99073-7 |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=cqgrz37DgMcC&lpg=PP1&ots=1z1Ed0S5SP&dq=Butler.%20Distant%20Victory&pg=PA229#v=onepage&q&f=false}}</ref>
