'''直肠阴道瘘'''（{{lang-en|Rectovaginal fistula}}），是一类妇科疾病，指[[肠道|肠道]]与[[生殖道|生殖道]]之间形成的异常通道<ref name="pmid16221302">{{cite journal |author=Kumaran SS, Palanivelu C, Kavalakat AJ, Parthasarathi R, Neelayathatchi M |title=Laparoscopic repair of high rectovaginal fistula: Is it technically feasible? |journal=BMC Surg |volume=5|pages=20 |year=2005 |pmid=16221302 |pmc=1266383 |doi=10.1186/1471-2482-5-20 |url=http://www.biomedcentral.com/1471-2482/5/20}}</ref>。

表现为[[阴道|阴道]]内排出粪便。瘘孔大者粪便经阴道排出，便稀时更为明显。瘘孔小，粪便干结成形时，阴道内不时有排气现象。[[窥阴器|窥阴器]]扩开阴道可见后壁有瘘孔，瘘孔极小者可见小的红色肉芽组织<ref>{{cite journal|last=Landry|first=E|coauthors=Frajzyngier, V; Ruminjo, J; Asiimwe, F; Barry, TH; Bello, A; Danladi, D; Ganda, SO; Idris, S; Inoussa, M; Kanoma, B; Lynch, M; Mussell, F; Podder, DC; Wali, A; Mielke, E; Barone, MA|title=Profiles and experiences of women undergoing genital fistula repair: findings from five countries.|journal=Global public health|date=2013|volume=8|issue=8|pages=926-42|doi=10.1080/17441692.2013.824018|pmid=23947903|pmc=3805436}}</ref>。

肛诊同时阴道小孔处放探针，或[[直肠|直肠]]内注入[[美蓝|美蓝]]液，阴道内置干纱布，视有无蓝色浸液均可帮助诊断。它可能由[[性病淋巴肉芽肿|性病淋巴肉芽肿]]感染引起<ref name="pmid10449269">{{cite journal |author=Lynch CM, Felder TL, Schwandt RA, Shashy RG |title=Lymphogranuloma venereum presenting as a rectovaginal fistula |journal=Infect Dis Obstet Gynecol |volume=7 |issue=4 |pages=199–201 |year=1999 |pmid=10449269 |pmc=1784745 |doi=10.1155/S1064744999000344}}</ref>，或者手术副作用。
