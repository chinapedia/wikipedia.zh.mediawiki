'''分子與細胞蛋白質組學期刊'''({{lang|en|Molecular & Cellular Proteomics}})是一份由[[美國生物化學與分子生物學協會|美國生物化學與分子生物學協會]](American Society for Biochemistry and Molecular Biology)出版於2002年的[[同行評審|同行評審]]之單月[[科学期刊|科学期刊]]。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Seven Years of Good Luck|date=2009-01-01|doi=10.1074/mcp.e900001-mcp200|language=en|journal=Molecular & Cellular Proteomics|volume=8|issue=1|url=http://www.mcponline.org/content/8/1/1|pages=1–1|issn=1535-9476|accessdate=2018-04-02|author=Ralph A. Bradshaw, Alma L. Burlingame}}</ref> 它涵蓋了對[[蛋白質|蛋白質]]結構及其功能特性的研究，特別是關於蛋白質的發展。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://mcponline.org/misc/mission.dtl |title=Mission Statement |format= |work=Molecular & Cellular Proteomics |accessdate=2011-06-23 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081225131153/http://www.mcponline.org/misc/mission.dtl# |archive-date=2008-12-25 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>

==內容與歷史==
該期刊還出版其他的內容，比如"HUPO的意見"(HUPO views)，這些文章來自於[[人類蛋白質組組織|人類蛋白質組組織]](HUPO)<ref>[http://www.mcponline.org/cgi/content/full/6/6/951 Ralph A. Bradshaw and Alma L. Burlingame,"Welcome, HUPO". ''Molecular & Cellular Proteomics'' 6:951, 2007.]</ref><ref>[http://www.mcponline.org/cgi/content/full/6/6/951-a|Peipei Ping, Rolf Apweiler and John Bergeron,MCP and HUPO: An Era of New Partnership, ''Molecular & Cellular Proteomics'' 6:951-952, 2007.]{{dead link|date=2017年11月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>、HUPO會議<ref>[http://www.mcponline.org/content/vol6/issue6/ MCP Supplement: HUPO 4th Annual World Congress, August 29-September 1, 2005, Munich]</ref>以及"國際質譜研討會生命科學組"(International Symposium On Mass Spectrometry In The Life Sciences)等所發表的論文。<ref>[http://www.mcponline.org/content/vol7/issue4/ Special Issue: 8th International Symposium On Mass Spectrometry In The Life Sciences]</ref>

截至2010年1月，該期刊僅在網上出版且不再提供印刷版。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://mcponline.org/site/subscriptions/non-member-faq.xhtml |title=Frequently Asked Questions about Individual (Non-Member) Subscriptions |work=Molecular & Cellular Proteomics |accessdate=2012-02-08 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110424002056/http://www.mcponline.org/site/subscriptions/non-member-faq.xhtml# |archive-date=2011-04-24 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>[[總編輯|總編輯]]是"阿拉瑪·L.·蒲安臣"(Alma L. Burlingame)及"拉爾夫·A.·布拉德肖"(Ralph A. Bradshaw)。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://mcponline.org/content/11/2/local/ed-board.pdf |title=MCP Masthead, February 2012 issue |format= |work=Molecular & Cellular Proteomics |accessdate=2012-02-08}}</ref>所有文章出版後都可以免費提供1年。文章在接受後都可以在其網站上立即免費獲得。
