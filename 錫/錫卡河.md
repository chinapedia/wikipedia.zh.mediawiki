'''錫卡河'''（{{lang-en|Thika River}}）是[[非洲|非洲]]的一条[[河流|河流]]，屬於[[塔納河|塔納河]]的[[支流|支流]]，發源自[[阿伯德爾山脈|阿伯德爾山脈]]，流經[[肯亞|肯亞]]中部，为肯尼亚重要的水电输送河流，是肯尼亞首都和最大城市[[内罗毕|内罗毕]]最主要的水源地。

==词源==
錫卡一词来源有两个解释，一说是来自{{link-en|基库尤语|Gikuyu language}}单词''guthika''，意思是“埋葬”，另一说是来自[[马赛语|马赛语]]''sika''，意思是“从边缘擦去某物”。<ref name="Thika">{{cite web|url=http://www.thikamunicipal.go.ke/bginfo.php|title=Municipal council of Thika: Background information|publisher=Municipal Council of Thika|accessdate=2008-09-28|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081029063219/http://www.thikamunicipal.go.ke/bginfo.php|archivedate=2008-10-29}}</ref>

==流域==
锡卡河发源自[[阿伯德爾山脈|阿伯德爾山脈]]，汇入[[塔納河|塔納河]]，最终注入[[印度洋|印度洋]]。 <ref name="reservoir">{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=XDtP9_hNKAAC&pg=PA131&dq=Thika+river&ei=4uOdSYjDOpD4MdjV8aQJ#PPA131,M1|title=Water Resources and Reservoir Engineering |pages=131–140|author=British Dam Society Conference|publisher= Thomas Telford |year=1992|accessdate=2009-02-20 | isbn=978-0-7277-1692-7}}</ref>  
锡卡河上的地标是锡卡瀑布，位于[[锡卡|锡卡]]镇附近。锡卡瀑布落差25米，所在区域保持着自然原生态，从肯尼亚最悠久的酒店之一的蓝后酒店（ Blue Post Hotel）步行可达。<ref name="ambros">{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=v7ha1PXs1OcC&pg=PA162&dq=Thika+river&ei=4uOdSYjDOpD4MdjV8aQJ#PPA159,M1|title=Kenya: An Up-to-date Travel Guide with 153 Color Photos and 14 Maps |pages=159–162|author=Eva Ambros|publisher= Hunter Publishing |year=1999|accessdate=2009-02-20 | isbn=978-3-88618-167-4}}</ref> 。

==生态==
在20世纪初，殖民地猎人就注意到，沿锡卡河深水区有[[河马|河马]]和[[鳄鱼|鳄鱼]]。锡卡瀑布附近的森林里有[[狒狒|狒狒]]和[[豹|豹]]栖息，瀑布上游的旷野上有[[羚羊|羚羊]]和[[斑马|斑马]]奔腾，一起生存的动物还有[[犀牛|犀牛]]、[[狮子|狮子]]和[[鬣狗|鬣狗]]等。<ref name="macqueen">{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=ERUUAAAAIAAJ&pg=PA275&dq=Thika+river&ei=4uOdSYjDOpD4MdjV8aQJ|title=In Wildest Africa: The Record of a Hunting and Exploration Trip Through Uganda, Victoria Nyanza, the Kilimanjaro Region and British East Africa, with an Account of an Ascent of the Snowfields of Mount Kibo, in East Central Africa, and a Description of the Various Native Tribes|pages=273–274|author=Peter MacQueen|publisher= George Ball and Sons|year=1909|accessdate=2009-02-20}}</ref>  随着沿河流定居的人口的增加，狩猎区转到河流上游阿伯德尔山森林或河流下游。

==水源地==
肯尼亞首都内罗毕80%用水来自锡卡河。<ref name="usda">{{cite book|url=http://www.ars.usda.gov/research/publications/publications.htm|title=HYDROLOGIC AND ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACTS OF CONSERVATION PRACTICES IN OKLAHOMA AGRICULTURAL WATERSHEDS (abstract)|pages=1|author=Moriasi, Daniel et al.|publisher=United States Department of Agriculture, Agricultural Research Service|year=2007|accessdate=2009-02-20|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090216082029/http://ars.usda.gov/research/publications/publications.htm|archivedate=2009-02-16}}</ref>

锡卡河流域人口稠密，加上森林滥伐和过渡放牧，给河流带来了水土流失问题。泥沙降低了河水流量、库容量和水质。农药残留也是水质恶化的原因之一。<ref name="usda" />

==水电开发==
锡卡河流过的塔納盆地是肯尼亚干旱区流域工程建设最广泛的地方。<ref name="hydrology">{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=ZmdYFTlcSgEC&pg=PA421&dq=Thika+river&ei=nsmeSY-KM5GoM9a18aQJ#PPA421,M1|title=Hydrology and Water Resources of Africa |pages=421–422|author=Mamdouh Shahin|publisher= Springer |year=2002|accessdate=2009-02-20 | isbn=978-1-4020-0866-5}}</ref>  锡卡河发出的[[水力发电|水电]]是肯尼亚电力的重要组成部分。锡卡河上第一个水电开发项目是[[第一次世界大战|第一次世界大战]]结束后不久由[[古列尔莫·马可尼|古列尔莫·马可尼]]提出的，为当时计划中的内罗毕无线电通信基站供应电力。<ref name="Hugill">{{cite book|url=http://www.ars.usda.gov/research/publications/publications.htm?seq_no_115=215780|title=Global Communications since 1844|pages=118–119|author=Peter J. Hugill|publisher= JHU Press|year=1999|accessdate=2009-02-20}}</ref> 马克尼的计划没有得到实施，直到1994年，库容量7千万立方米的锡卡河大坝才修建完成。<ref name="usda" /><ref name="Hugill" /> 锡卡河还是马辛加水库（Masinga Reservoir）的水源之一，该水库为塞文福克斯(Seven Forks Dams)大坝服务<ref name="usda" />。以上这些大坝供应了肯尼亚四分之三的电力<ref name="usda" />。
