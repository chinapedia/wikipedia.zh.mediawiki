Davie |year=2002 |series=Zoological Catalogue of Australia |volume=19 |title=Crustacea: Malocostraca: Eucarida (Part 2), Decapoda: Anomura, Brachyura |publisher=[[CSIRO_Publishing|CSIRO Publishing]] |isbn=9780643067929 |chapter=Chirostylidae |pages=29–32 |url=http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=jr9SR1Cr5BAC&pg=PA632}}</ref>。
