'''耳房'''是指[[中国传统建筑|中国传统建筑]]设计中在主[[房屋|房屋]]旁边加盖的小房屋，其高度和体积小于主房，犹如在主房两侧的[[耳朵|耳朵]]，故名耳房。

在[[四合院|四合院]]中，[[正房|正房]]两侧可建耳房，<ref>[http://www.zhongguowenhua.org/%E6%B0%91%E4%BF%97/%E5%9B%9B%E5%90%88%E9%99%A2/%E8%80%B3%E6%88%BF.html 耳房] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070928031558/http://www.zhongguowenhua.org/%E6%B0%91%E4%BF%97/%E5%9B%9B%E5%90%88%E9%99%A2/%E8%80%B3%E6%88%BF.html |date=2007-09-28 }}，中国文化网</ref> [[厢房|厢房]]也可设耳房，有的建成平顶，称为“[[盝顶|盝顶]]”。<ref>[http://www.zhongguowenhua.org/%E6%B0%91%E4%BF%97/%E5%9B%9B%E5%90%88%E9%99%A2/%E5%8E%A2%E6%88%BF.html 厢房] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070928031614/http://www.zhongguowenhua.org/%E6%B0%91%E4%BF%97/%E5%9B%9B%E5%90%88%E9%99%A2/%E5%8E%A2%E6%88%BF.html |date=2007-09-28 }}，中国文化网。</ref>。耳房也可建在[[城楼|城楼]]上，例如[[天安门|天安门]]城楼两侧就建有耳房。<ref>[http://www.tiananmen.org.cn/capinfo_product/NC_Foreground/NCFore_cms/list.asp?id=168&column_id=316&column_layer=040316&title_name=%E8%80%B3%E6%88%BF 耳房] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070928200850/http://www.tiananmen.org.cn/capinfo_product/NC_Foreground/NCFore_cms/list.asp?id=168&column_id=316&column_layer=040316&title_name=%E8%80%B3%E6%88%BF |date=2007-09-28 }}，载于“[[天安门|天安门]]地区管理委员会”网页2002年7月19日。</ref>
