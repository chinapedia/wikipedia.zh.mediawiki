==历史==
1993年成人影片导演约恩（Joone）成立了DP公司，最初是制作转载在CD-ROM上的成人电脑游戏。<ref name=Fantasy>{{cite web |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2003/08/17/movies/17KENN.html?ei=5007&en=42e4fb19dab28bd1&ex=1376452800&partner=USERLAND&pagewanted=print&position= |title=The Fantasy of Interactive Porn Becomes a Reality |accessdate=2009-04-11 |author=Dana Kennedy |date=2003-08-17 |work= |publisher=[[纽约时报|纽约时报]]}}</ref>随着相关法律的改变，色情行业逐渐从地下事业变得合法起来。<ref>{{cite book|title=The Industrial Vagina: The Political Economy of the Global Sex Trade|publisher=[[Taylor_&_Francis|Taylor & Francis]]|author=Jeffreys, Sheila |year=2008|url=http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=-9U7Eqp8NigC|isbn=0415412331|pages=73}}</ref>DP公司的创举是使得色情信息可以通过个人电脑来获取。<ref name=Fox>{{cite web |url=http://www.foxnews.com/story/0,2933,182370,00.html |title=Porn Production Company Picks Blu-Ray |accessdate=2009-04-11 |author= |date=2006-01-20 |work= |publisher=福克斯新闻 |archive-url=https://www.webcitation.org/6Hyu13Nfp?url=http://www.foxnews.com/story/2006/01/20/porn-production-company-picks-blu-ray/ |archive-date=2013-07-09 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>2003年DP开始研究用于电脑的全息显示技术，希望能起到让色情演员进入到观众的起居室内的效果。<ref name=Fantasy/><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2007/01/22/business/media/22porn.html?pagewanted=1&_r=4&ref=technology |title=In Raw World of Sex Movies, High Definition Could Be a View Too Real |accessdate=2009-04-11 |author=Matt Richtel |date=2007-01-22 |work= |publisher=[[New_York_Times|New York Times]]}}</ref><ref>參見 [[虛擬調情|虛擬調情]]</ref>2005年DP开始拍摄高清的色情电影，在[[HD_DVD|HD DVD]]和[[Blu-ray|蓝光DVD]]的竞争中DP选择了后者。<ref name=Fox/>

目前，数位游乐场旗下拥有[[杰西·簡|杰西·簡]]、[[凯登·克罗斯|凯登·克罗斯]]、[[史托雅|史托雅]]、[[雷丽·斯蒂尔|雷丽·斯蒂尔]]等著名成人女星。
