'''蘭斯蒂芬城堡'''（{{lang-cy|Llansteffan Castle}}）是位於[[英國|英國]][[威爾斯|威爾斯]][[卡馬森郡|卡馬森郡]]城市蘭斯蒂芬的一座[[城堡|城堡]]，其歷史可以追溯至[[鐵器時代|鐵器時代]]<ref>{{cite journal|last=Guilbert and Schweiso|first=G C and J J|title=Llanstephan Castle. An interim discussion of the 1971 excavation|journal=The Carmarthenshire Antiquity|year=1972|volume=8|pages=75–90}}</ref>。現在的城堡修建於諾曼入侵威爾斯時期，於1146年開始修建<ref name="C and C">{{cite book|last1=Lloyd|first1=Thomas|last2=Orbach|first2=Julian|last3=Scourfield|first3=Robert|title=Carmarthenshire and Ceredigion|year=2006|publisher=Yale University Press|location=London|isbn=0-300-10179-1|pages=331-332|url=http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=wEkcJb2lHx8C&pg=PA331&lpg=PA331&dq=%22Llansteffan+castle%22&source=bl&ots=Tx2P64hIbQ&sig=1wB7OCwS_9VzrEZzshcZtKCYwX4&hl=en&sa=X&ei=Z6U7UL7SN4Sa0QXQ0YGgBw&ved=0CEQQ6AEwBA#v=onepage&q=%22Llansteffan%20castle%22&f=false}}</ref>。城堡現在由Cadw管理<ref>{{cite web| url= http://cadw.wales.gov.uk/daysout/llansteffancastle/?lang=en | title = Llansteffan Castle | work = Days Out | publisher = Cadw | accessdate = 29 Aug 2012}}</ref>。
