'''皮斯河吊桥'''（{{lang-en|Peace River Suspension Bridge}}）是[[加拿大|加拿大]][[卑诗省|卑诗省]]Taylor附近的一座跨过[[皮斯河|皮斯河]]的悬索桥，于1943建成通车，在当时皮斯河吊桥是[[阿拉斯加公路|阿拉斯加公路]]最长的桥梁，长约2,130英尺<ref>[http://fsj.net/peacehistory.html A Gallop through our Peace Region History] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080412030043/http://fsj.net/peacehistory.html |date=2008-04-12 }} URL accessed on March 6, 2008.</ref>，在1957年10月16日倒塌<ref>[http://collections.ic.gc.ca/north_peace/transport/02.07.html Collapsed Peace River Bridge] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20050409094945/http://collections.ic.gc.ca/north_peace/transport/02.07.html |date=2005-04-09 }} ''History of the North Peace''. </ref><ref name="history">[http://www.districtoftaylor.com/history.shtml Taylor's History] ''District of Taylor''. </ref>此后又在同一位置建了[[皮斯河大桥|皮斯河大桥]]。
