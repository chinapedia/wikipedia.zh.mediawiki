'''各各他浸礼会教堂'''（Calvary Baptist Church）是一座古老的多元化的[[美北浸礼会|美北浸礼会]]教堂，<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.dcbaptist.org/directory.php |title=Directory of Churches |publisher=District of Columbia Baptist Convention |accessdate=2010-06-06 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100608094935/http://www.dcbaptist.org/directory.php |archivedate=2010-06-08 }}</ref><ref name="About">{{cite web|url=http://calvarydc.org/learn/about-calvary/ | title=About Calvary | publisher=Calvary Baptist Church }}</ref>，位于美国[[华盛顿哥伦比亚特区|华盛顿哥伦比亚特区]][[潘恩区|潘恩区]]社区。2012年7月断绝与[[美南浸信会|美南浸信会]]的关系。<ref name="sbc-abp-2012">{{cite news | agency = Associated Baptist Press | author = Bob Allen | date = July 26, 2012 | url = http://www.abpnews.com/ministry/congregations/item/7653-church-severs-sbc-ties#.UBsGV8ie7Mg | title = Historic DC church severs SBC ties | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131112024156/http://www.abpnews.com/ministry/congregations/item/7653-church-severs-sbc-ties#.UBsGV8ie7Mg | archivedate = 2013年11月12日 }}</ref><ref name="butler-sbc-severed-2012">{{cite news | author = Rev. Dr. Amy Butler | newspaper = The Washington Post | url = http://www.washingtonpost.com/local/calvary-baptist-pastor-why-we-severed-ties-with-the-southern-baptist-convention/2012/07/26/gJQAy11xBX_story.html | date = July 26, 2012 | title = Calvary Baptist pastor: Why we severed ties with the Southern Baptist Convention}}</ref>

各各他浸礼会是1907年[[美北浸礼会|美北浸礼会]]成立时的创始教会<ref name="Wilbur">{{cite book |last1= Wilbur |first1= William Allen  |title= Chronicles of Calvary Baptist Church |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=MT_SAAAAMAAJ&printsec=frontcover&source=gbs_v2_summary_r&cad=0#v=onepage&q&f=false |year=1914|publisher= Judd & Deitwiller, Inc. |location= Washington, D.C. }}</ref>，19世纪末、20世纪初浸会主日学运动的领导教堂<ref>{{cite book | author = Samuel Harrison Greene |title= The Twentieth Century Sunday School |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=X7Y_AAAAYAAJ&printsec=frontcover&source=gbs_v2_summary_r&cad=0#v=onepage&q&f=false |year=1903|publisher=Sunday School Board, Southern Baptist Convention |location= Nashville, Tennessee }}</ref> 。
