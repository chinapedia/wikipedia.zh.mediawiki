'''长剑'''（longsword，也有long sword或者long-sword）也可称为'''一手半剑'''（hand and a half sword），是一種歐洲劍具，典型特征是有十字形护手，剑柄够长，可以双手持也可单手持的双刃直剑，一般长度为{{convert|90|to|110|cm|in}}<ref name=length>{{cite book|last=Loades|first=Mike|title=Swords and Swordsmen|year=2010|publisher=Pen & Sword Books|location=Great Britain|isbn=978-1-84884-133-8}}</ref>。在欧洲出现时间为13-17世纪，最盛行年代为[[中世纪晚期|中世纪晚期]]和[[文艺复兴|文艺复兴]]时期（约1350至1550年）。

==和变种剑的关系==
它和[[变种剑|变种剑]]（Bastard sword）的关系扑朔迷离。[[Joseph_Swetnam|Joseph Swetnam]]说变种剑的长度介于短剑和长剑之间，<ref name="historical-academyii"/>Randall Cotgrave的定义也差不多，某本字典显示了épée bâtarde的定义是“一把短的或至少是不够长的剑”。<ref name="pbm.com">[http://www.pbm.com/~lindahl/cotgrave/100small.html Cotgrave's 1611 French/English Dictionary]</ref>法语的''{{lang|fr|épée bâtarde}}''和英语的''bastard sword''起源于15-16世纪，一开始是指所有“非正规的，起源不明的剑”，但有证据表明到了16世纪中期却开始指所有特别长的剑。<ref>{{fr icon}} {{cite book|quote=Qui n'étoit ni Françoise , ni Espagnole, ni proprement Lansquenette, mais plus grande que pas une de ces fortes épées.（[A sword] which was neither French, nor Spanish, nor properly ''[[Landsknecht|Landsknecht]]'' [German], but larger than any of these great swords. | editor=Le Duchat, Jacob | title=Oeuvres | authorlink=François Rabelais | author=Rabelais, François | year=1741 | url=http://books.google.com/books?id=UCY_AAAAcAAJ&pg=PA129&dq=%22%C3%A9p%C3%A9e+b%C3%A2tarde%22&hl=en&ei=PTILTry5HczF8QPo15mJAQ&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=9&ved=0CF0Q6AEwCA#v=onepage&q=%22%C3%A9p%C3%A9e%20b%C3%A2tarde%22&f=false | page=129 (footnote 5)}}</ref>
[[亨利八世|亨利八世]]组织的“防御大师”比赛于1540年举办时，它把“双手剑”和“杂种剑”分成两种武器。<ref>{{cite book | last=Strutt | first=Joseph | title=The Sports and Pastimes of the People of England from the Earliest Period: Including the Rural and Domestic Recreations, May Games, Mummeries, Pageants, Processions and Pompous Spectacles | date=1801 | publisher=Methuen & Company | url=http://books.google.com/books?id=eJwSAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA211&dq=%22long+sword%22++%22bastard+sword%22&hl=en&ei=T0ILTqC0MYKd-wbBruzOAg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CCwQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q=%22long%20sword%22%20%20%22bastard%20sword%22&f=false | page=211}}</ref>尽管还有一些证据可表明杂种剑用来表示比较短的剑，19世纪的文物藏品表明了“杂种剑”毫无岐义地指的是很大的剑。<ref name=oakeshott>{{cite book | last=Oakeshott | first=Ewart | title=European Weapons and Armour: From the Renaissance to the Industrial Revolution | date=1980 | publisher=Boydell & Brewer, Incorporated | isbn=9780851157894 | pages=129–135}}</ref>

近代早期德语里长剑''{{lang|de|langes schwert}}''和短剑''{{lang|de|kurzes schwert}}''这两个词，据15世纪的手册表明并不是代表武器，而是代表双手用剑，还是一手持剑柄一手持剑身（类似于“牙突”）的技巧。直到16世纪晚期''{{lang|de|langes schwert}}''才是指代的一种剑。当代的长剑（"long-sword"）一词是在21世纪随着古德国流击剑术复兴才出现的，是翻译自德语''{{lang|de|langes schwert}}''。<ref>

"一手半剑"一词出现得晚得多，要到19世纪晚期才出现。<ref>As attested in {{cite book | title=Exhibition of the Royal House of Tudor | date=1890 | publisher=New Gallery | url=http://books.google.com/books?ei=BjQLTsC8A8ao8AOSiJ2EAQ&ct=result&id=cGgPAAAAYAAJ&dq=%2B%22hand-and-a-half%22+%2Bsword&q=%2B%22hand-and-a-half%22+#search_anchor | location=London}}</ref>指的是既能单手用也能双手用的剑。在20世纪前半，"杂种剑"经常指的是这种剑，而"长剑"（long sword或long-sword）即使使用过，也都指的是文艺复兴到近世时使用的[[护手刺剑|刺剑]]。<ref>See, for example,  {{cite book | title=A General Guide to the Wallace Collection | date=1933 | publisher=H.M. Stationery Office | page=149 | url=http://books.google.com/books?id=CpYKAQAAIAAJ&q=%22long+sword%22++%22bastard+sword%22&dq=%22long+sword%22++%22bastard+sword%22&hl=en&ei=F0ILTouUIoOf-wb59KDPAg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CCwQ6AEwAA}}</ref>
