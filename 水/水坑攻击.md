'''水坑攻击'''（{{lang-en|Watering hole}}）是一种[[攻擊_(電腦)|计算机入侵]]手法，其针对的目标多为特定的团体（组织、行业、地区等）。攻击者首先通过猜测（或观察）确定这组目标经常访问的网站，并入侵其中一个或多个，植入[[恶意软件|恶意软件]]，最后，达到感染该组目标中部分成员的目的。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Gragido|first1=Will|title=Lions at the Watering Hole – The "VOHO" Affair|url=https://blogs.rsa.com/lions-at-the-watering-hole-the-voho-affair/|website=The RSA Blog|publisher=[[EMC_Corporation|EMC Corporation]]|date=20 July 2012}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.co.kr/books?id=DDiOCgAAQBAJ&lpg=PA57&vq=watering%20hole&pg=PA58#v=snippet&q=watering%20hole&f=false|title=Cyber Guerilla|last=Haaster|first=Jelle Van|last2=Gevers|first2=Rickey|last3=Sprengers|first3=Martijn|date=2016-06-13|publisher=Syngress|isbn=9780128052846|language=en|page=57}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.co.kr/books?id=VBuDBAAAQBAJ&lpg=PA123&vq=%22watering%20hole%22&pg=PA123#v=snippet&q=%22watering%20hole%22&f=false|title=Internet Technologies and Information Services, 2nd Edition|last=Miller|first=Joseph B.|date=2014-08-26|publisher=ABC-CLIO|isbn=9781610698863|language=en|page=123}}</ref>

由于此种攻击借助了目标团体所信任的网站，攻击成功率很高，即便是那些对[[spear_phishing|鱼叉攻击]]或其他形式的[[phishing|钓鱼攻击]]具有防护能力的团体，
