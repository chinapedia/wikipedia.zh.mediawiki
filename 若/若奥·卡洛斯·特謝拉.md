'''若奥·卡洛斯·特謝拉'''（{{lang-pt|João Carlos Teixeira}}；{{bd|1993年|1月18日}}）是一位[[葡萄牙|葡萄牙]]足球運動員。在場上的位置是[[中場|中場]]。現效力[[葡超|葡超]]球隊[[維多利亞體育會|甘馬雷斯]]，曾效力於[[波图足球俱乐部|波图]]<ref>{{cite news|title=Liverpool manager Kenny Daglish says club are interested in Sporting Lisbon midfielder Joao Carlos Teixeira|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/sport/football/teams/liverpool/9025145/Liverpool-manager-Kenny-Daglish-says-club-are-interested-in-Sporting-Lisbon-midfielder-Joao-Carlos-Teixeira.html|accessdate=11 February 2014|newspaper=The Telegraph|date=19 January 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Liverpool agree £830,000 deal for Sporting Lisbon midfielder Teixeira|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/sport/football/article-2088597/Liverpool-sign-Joao-Carlios-Teixeira-Sporting-Lisbon-830-000.html|accessdate=11 February 2014|newspaper=Daily Mail|date=19 January 2012}}</ref>。他也代表葡萄牙各級國青隊參賽。
