'''昆士蘭音樂學院'''（Queensland Conservatorium Griffith University；簡稱QCGU）位於[[昆士蘭州|昆士蘭州]][[首府|首府]][[布里斯本|布里斯本]]市區、布里斯本河以南的[[南岸_(昆士蘭州)|南岸]]。這所音樂學院前身於1957年2月18日創校時，即是個獨立的音樂學院；早期以「南布里斯本市政廳」為校園，自舊地於1975年被劃歸予[[昆士蘭科技大學|昆士蘭科技大學]]後，該校於1996年遷至[[布里斯本中央商業區|中央商業區]]內的現址，是[[格里菲斯大学|格里菲斯大学]]的六大校區之一<ref name="griffith.edu.au">[http://www.griffith.edu.au/school/qcgu/alumni/content_alumni.html#tunein] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080720201436/http://www.griffith.edu.au/school/qcgu/alumni/content_alumni.html |date=2008-07-20 }} (Alumni|Queensland Conservatorium) Accessed: 14 June 2007.</ref>；與另一校區[[昆士蘭藝術學院|昆士蘭藝術學院]]相距約400公尺。

昆士蘭音樂學院自創校以後，一直是個保有獨立招生、辦學地位的高等教育機構；直至1980年代晚期與[[格里菲斯大學|格里菲斯大學]]合併成一體系，引進[[:en:Dawkins_Revolution|道金斯革新計劃]]。1996年，音樂學院遷至位於南岸的現址，並且定名為'''格里菲斯大學昆士蘭音樂學院'''。該院學生以『The Con』來暱稱母校 <ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.carlngray.com.au/panorama/qcgu2/index.htm |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2007-06-14 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070608040030/http://www.carlngray.com.au/panorama/qcgu2/index.htm |archivedate=2007-06-08 }} (The Brisbane Pan Project) Accessed: 14 June 2007.</ref>。1999年，音樂學院開設了「流行音樂學士」課程；並且自2000年起，該學位課程在該校的[[黃金海岸_(澳大利亞)|黃金海岸]]校區的資訊系所內開始招生授課 <ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.griffith.edu.au/school/qcgu/bpm/ |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2007-06-14 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070629131832/http://www.griffith.edu.au/school/qcgu/bpm/ |archivedate=2007-06-29 }} (Bachelor of Popular Music) Accessed: 14 June 2007.</ref>。2003年，附屬於音樂學院的研究中心開幕，成為該校的30所研究中心之一。<!--該研究中心的宗旨是The Research Centre aims to investigate the dynamics of contemporary musical environments and is headed by Professor Huib Schippers as its director.<ref>[http://www.griffith.edu.au/centre/qcrc/] (Queensland Conservatorium Research Centre) Accessed: 14 June 2007.</ref>。

Facilities include the Conservatorium Theatre—also used for smaller productions by [[Opera_Queensland|Opera Queensland]]— which seats a maximum of 727 and has one of the highest fly towers in Australia, the Ian Hangar Recital Hall which seats 200 and the Basil Jones Orchestra Hall.<ref>[http://www.griffith.edu.au/school/qcgu/venues/list.html] (Venue Specifications|Queensland Conservatorium) Accessed: 14 June 2007.</ref> There are also music production, post production, multimedia lab and recording facilities both in the South Bank and Gold Coast Campuses.<ref>[http://www.griffith.edu.au/school/qcgu/bpm/studios.htm#2] (Bachelor of Popular Music) Accessed: 14 June 2007.</ref>

The Queensland Conservatorium also includes the Young Conservatorium, an external, classical based music program for pre-schoolers to year 12 students run by Ralph Hultgren, head of pre-tertiary studies. In the past 12 months the "Young Con" has engaged more than 1500 students in performances.<ref>[http://www.griffith.edu.au/school/qcgu/youngcon/] (Young Conservatorium) Accessed: 14 June 2007.</ref>

In 2007 the Conservatorium celebrated its 50 year anniversary with Alumni reunions and visiting artists from around the world.<ref>[http://www3.griffith.edu.au/03/ertiki/tiki-read_article.php?articleId=3681] (Griffith University| News) Accessed: 14 June 2007.</ref>

The current Director is Professor Peter Roennfeldt who graduated with a Doctor of Musical Arts from The University of Cincinnati and the Associate Director is Professor Huib Schippers who graduated with a PhD from the University of Amsterdam.<ref>[http://www.griffith.edu.au/school/qcgu/about/staff/] (Staff profiles| Queensland Conservatorium) Accessed: 14 June 2007.</ref>
