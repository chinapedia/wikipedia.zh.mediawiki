'''凱瑟琳·哈德威克'''（'''Catherine Hardwicke''' {{birth date|1955|10|21}}）是一位美國電影導演、編劇、美術指導，著名作品包括奧斯卡提名《[[芳齡十三|芳齡十三]]》（與[[妮琪·利德|妮琪·利德]]合寫）<ref>{{cite web |url=http://movies.about.com/od/thirteen/Thirteen_2003.htm |title=Thirteen Movie - Evan Rachel Wood, Nikki Reed, Photos, Trailer, Credits, Review |publisher=Movies.about.com |date=2003-08-20 |accessdate=2012-11-08 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120901203416/http://movies.about.com/od/thirteen/Thirteen_2003.htm |archivedate=2012-09-01 }}</ref>、《[[聖誕頌_(電影)|聖誕頌]]》、《[[暮光之城：無懼的愛|暮光之城：無懼的愛]]》和《[[血紅帽|血紅帽]]》，其中《暮光之城》的周末票房為有史以來女導演之最。<ref>{{cite news|title='Twilight' debuts in No. 1 slot at box office |url=http://www.cnn.com/2008/SHOWBIZ/Movies/11/23/boxoffice.ap/ |publisher=CNN |date=November 23, 2008 |accessdate=November 23, 2008 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081211091707/http://www.cnn.com/2008/SHOWBIZ/Movies/11/23/boxoffice.ap/ |archivedate=December 11, 2008 }}</ref>
