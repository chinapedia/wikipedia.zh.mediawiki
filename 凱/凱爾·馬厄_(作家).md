'''凱爾·馬厄'''（Kevin Maher；{{bd|1972年}}）是一名[[愛爾蘭|愛爾蘭]]作家，以記者及[[電影評論者|電影評論者]]的身份廣為人知，其文章刊登於《[[泰晤士報|泰晤士報]]》、《[[衛報|衛報]]》、《[[獨立報|獨立報]]》及《[[觀察家報|觀察家報]]》。他的首本小說《The Fields》於2013年由[[列根·亞瑟圖書|列根·亞瑟圖書]]出版<ref>{{cite web |url= http://blogs.spectator.co.uk/books/2013/04/interview-with-a-writer-kevin-maher/ |title= Interview with a writer: Kevin Maher |last1= O'Malley |first1= JP |date= 2013-04-05 |website= http://blogs.spectator.co.uk |publisher= ''[[The_Spectator|The Spectator]]'' |accessdate=2014-09-12}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.gq-magazine.co.uk/entertainment/articles/2013-04/24/kevin-maher-the-fields-interview |title=A life well read: Kevin Maher talks similarities between fact and fiction |last1=Burton |first1=Charlie |date=13-04-24 |website=http://www.gq-magazine.co.uk |publisher=''[[GQ|GQ]]'' |accessdate=2014-09-12 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130522011131/http://www.gq-magazine.co.uk/entertainment/articles/2013-04/24/kevin-maher-the-fields-interview |archivedate=2013-05-22 }}</ref>，獲列於2013年的[[水石11獎|水石11獎]]，該獎旨在推廣首次著書作者的文學作品。
