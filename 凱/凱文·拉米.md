'''凯文·拉米'''（{{lang-en|'''Kevin Larmee'''}}，{{bd|1946年}}）<ref name="jsheehan">James Sheehan, “Larmee: in from the street,” ''East Informer'', October 1985.</ref>是一位美国画家，以参加了1980年代在纽约东村举行的艺术活动而为人熟知。

== 早年生活 ==
出生于[[新泽西州|新泽西州]]{{link-en|里奇菲尔德帕克|Ridgefield Park, New Jersey}}，成长于伊利诺斯州[[芝加哥|芝加哥]]的郊区。<ref name="jmandell">Jonathan Mandell, “Urban Artifacts,” [[New_York_Daily_News|New York Daily News]], August 18, 1985.</ref>1965-1967年間就读于当时位于伊利诺斯州芒特卡勒尔的[[夏默学院|夏默学院]]，並於1967-1968年間就读于{{link-en|圣弗朗西斯科艺术学院|San Francisco Art Institute}}。至1969-1970年后，又重新返回夏默学院進修。<ref name="eight">{{Cite web|url=https://ir.stonybrook.edu/xmlui/bitstream/handle/11401/2953/Art_Piece_107.pdf?sequence=1|title=Eight Urban Painters: Contemporary Artists of the East Village|publisher=The Fine Arts Center Art Gallery (SUNY Stony Brook)|page=5|date=1986-07-09|accessdate=2014-12-18}}</ref>

之后凯文·拉米在1979年与妻子Susan Isono移居[[纽约|纽约]]，和他们在1985年出生的儿子布莱斯·拉米{{tsl|en|Blaise Larmee}}居住在SoHo区。<ref name="jsheehan"/>

== 职业 ==
=== 绘画 ===
在1980年代初，伴随着对众多新画廊的引进，纽约的艺术街开始从SoHo区转移到东村。<ref name="Helen A. Harrison">Helen A. Harrison, “Urban Anxieties Reflected In Paintings From East Village,” [[New_York_Times|New York Times]], August 17, 1986.</ref>1984年，感到自己的工作没有得到艺术界的足够重视，凯文·拉米开始在深夜将他的大型壁画置于曼哈顿艺术中心附近建筑物的墙壁上。<ref name="Victoria Donohoe">Victoria Donohoe, "[http://articles.philly.com/1986-10-18/news/26061298_1_paintings-gallery-exhibits-fresh-art Show Emphasizes New Genre Painting,”] [[Philadelphia_Inquirer|Philadelphia Inquirer]], October 18, 1986.</ref><ref name="Stephanie Arena">Stephanie Arena, “Kevin Larmee,” Pulp, July/August 1990.</ref><ref name="Matthew Rose">Matthew Rose, “Kevin Larmee,”{{tsl|en| Arts Magazine}}, May 1986.</ref>他会定期检查并维护这些壁画并防止被涂鸦。<ref name="jmandell">Jonathan Mandell, “Urban Artifacts,” [[New_York_Daily_News|New York Daily News]], August 18, 1985.</ref>这些壁画在被清除或者损坏前，有时会保留几个月之久。 <ref name="Stephanie Arena">Stephanie Arena, “Kevin Larmee,” Pulp, July/August 1990.</ref><ref name="Robyn Perry">, Robyn Perry,“[http://www.artillerymag.com/archives/v3i3_09/current/feature4.html Private Eye,"] {{Wayback|url=http://www.artillerymag.com/archives/v3i3_09/current/feature4.html |date=20150923174128 }}Artillery Magazine, Vol. 3, No. 3, January/February 2009.</ref>

凯文·拉米很快在美国纽约及其他地方的画廊展出，并继续创造街头艺术。<ref name="Helen A. Harrison">Helen A. Harrison, “Urban Anxieties Reflected In Paintings From East Village,” [[New_York_Times|New York Times]], August 17, 1986.</ref><ref name="Victoria Donohoe">Victoria Donohoe, "[http://articles.philly.com/1986-10-18/news/26061298_1_paintings-gallery-exhibits-fresh-art Show Emphasizes New Genre Painting,”] [[Philadelphia_Inquirer|Philadelphia Inquirer]], October 18, 1986.</ref><ref name="Matthew Rose">Matthew Rose, “Kevin Larmee,”{{tsl|en| Arts Magazine}}, May 1986.</ref> 凯文·拉米成为由画廊艺术家组建的街头艺术运动的一份子，<ref name="jmandell">Jonathan Mandell, “Urban Artifacts,” [[New_York_Daily_News|New York Daily News]], August 18, 1985.</ref>在上世纪80年代作为下东城B大道的画廊代表者。画廊在近十年后结束。<ref name="Stephanie Arena">Stephanie Arena, “Kevin Larmee,” Pulp, July/August 1990.</ref><ref name="Robyn Perry">, Robyn Perry,“[http://www.artillerymag.com/archives/v3i3_09/current/feature4.html Private Eye,"] {{Wayback|url=http://www.artillerymag.com/archives/v3i3_09/current/feature4.html |date=20150923174128 }}Artillery Magazine, Vol. 3, No. 3, January/February 2009.</ref><ref name="Carter Ratcliff">Carter Ratcliff, “The Art of Innocence,” [[Elle|Elle]], Volume II, Number 6, February 1987.</ref>凯文·拉米在布鲁克林艺术博物馆第二十四届全国版画展会上展出了作品《今天美国的版画》，1986年走遍国家的各种博物馆。<ref name="“Exhibitions">[https://www.brooklynmuseum.org/opencollection/exhibitions/770 Exhibitions: National Print Exhibition, 24th Biennial: Public and Private: American Prints Today,”] Brooklyn Museum, February 7, 1986.</ref>他在1985年创作的香烟石版画是布鲁克林艺术博物馆的永久收藏之一，<ref name=" Cigarette">[https://www.brooklynmuseum.org/opencollection/objects/114316 Cigarette], Brooklyn Museum. Accessed May 27, 2014.</ref>{{link-en|大急流城艺术博物馆|Grand Rapids Art Museum}},{{link-en|印第安纳波利斯艺术博物馆|Indianapolis Museum of Art}}和{{link-en|托莱多艺术博物馆|Toledo Museum of Art}}等也都有收藏他的作品。<ref name="Museum References for Kevin Larmee">[http://www.askart.com/artist/artist/127096/artist.aspx Museum References for Kevin Larmee], askart.com. Retrieved May 27, 2014.</ref>1989年，凯文·拉米和家人搬到芝加哥，在那里继续绘画。<ref name="Barbara B. Buchholz">Barbara B. Buchholz, [http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1997-04-18/entertainment/9704180427_1_paintings-matisse-handmade-paper “Elke Claus’ Vivid ‘Information’,”][[Chicago_Tribune|Chicago Tribune]], April 18, 1997.</ref>

=== 风格 ===
凯文·拉米上世纪80年代的版画背景通常是夜间的城市环境，将一到两个孤立个体置于空无一人的街道，地铁站台<ref name="Victoria Donohoe">Victoria Donohoe, "[http://articles.philly.com/1986-10-18/news/26061298_1_paintings-gallery-exhibits-fresh-art Show Emphasizes New Genre Painting,”] [[Philadelphia_Inquirer|Philadelphia Inquirer]], October 18, 1986.</ref>或有波光的水纹前，<ref name="Lynn Snowden">Lynn Snowden, “Luminescence Through Darkness,” East Village Eye, November 1985.</ref>将强烈的色彩，多层次图案和人物结合起来。<ref name="Barbara B. Buchholz">Barbara B. Buchholz, [http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1997-04-18/entertainment/9704180427_1_paintings-matisse-handmade-paper “Elke Claus’ Vivid ‘Information’,”][[Chicago_Tribune|Chicago Tribune]], April 18, 1997.</ref>他的这一技巧被描述为“宽松的画笔和粗糙的质感”。<ref name="Victoria Donohoe">Victoria Donohoe, "[http://articles.philly.com/1986-10-18/news/26061298_1_paintings-gallery-exhibits-fresh-art Show Emphasizes New Genre Painting,”] [[Philadelphia_Inquirer|Philadelphia Inquirer]], October 18, 1986.</ref>纽约时报在1986年写到，他在地铁站台上的版画“收集移动列车的能量来摆动。”<ref name="Helen A. Harrison">Helen A. Harrison, “Urban Anxieties Reflected In Paintings From East Village,” [[New_York_Times|New York Times]], August 17, 1986.</ref>他的街头版画的代表作是手夹香烟的金发男人，一个男人站着，手夹香烟，空洞的眼睛盯着地面，身后是纽约的天际线。 <ref name="Matthew Rose">Matthew Rose, “Kevin Larmee,”{{tsl|en| Arts Magazine}}, May 1986.</ref> 受印象派的影响，他的作品往往有大量的油漆和纹理，使用平面人物来创作。<ref name="Stephanie Arena">Stephanie Arena, “Kevin Larmee,” Pulp, July/August 1990.</ref><ref name=" Jacqueline Hall">Jacqueline Hall, “Divergent Lancaster shows reveal three faces of art,"{{tsl|en|The Columbus Dispatch}}, July 20, 1986.</ref>
