'''窮鄉僻壤'''（{{lang-en|Backcountry}}），通常指偏僻荒遠的地方。<ref>{{cite web|title=教育部國語辭典簡編本|url=http://dict.moe.gov.tw/cgi-bin/jdict/GetContent.cgi?DocNum=26193&GraphicWord=&QueryString=%E5%83%BB|publisher=教育部|accessdate=2015-01-27}}{{dead link|date=2018年4月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>例如地理位置偏遠、交通建設未開發、缺少物資、外來訪客罕見的地區。

==出處==
*明·李時勉《北京賦》：「窮陬僻壤，無一物之不遂。」<ref>{{cite web|title=基隆市武崙國小成語詞典 - 窮鄉僻壤|url=http://idiom.wlps.kl.edu.tw/index.php?id=7907|accessdate=2015-01-27}}</ref>

==風險==
窮鄉僻壤包含許多風險，包括崎嶇的地形、危及生命的[[氣候|氣候]]、[[山崩|山崩]]、[[雪崩|雪崩]]和[[野生動物|野生動物]]。<ref>{{cite news |title=More people dying in avalanches as more take to B.C.'s backcountry |author= |url=http://www.canada.com/theprovince/news/story.html?id=5c815072-3d8d-4125-8b0a-9d03626e2dd7 |newspaper=Canada.com |date=December 30, 2008 |accessdate=July 6, 2011}}</ref>在一些新聞報導裡，因為登山或滑雪等，發生事故時，進入窮鄉僻壤的救援，就會獲得新聞媒體的關注度。<ref>{{cite news |title=Stranded Backcountry Skier Is Rescued After Eight Days |author= |url= http://www.nytimes.com/2005/04/26/national/26skier.html |newspaper= |date=April 26, 2005 |accessdate=July 6, 2011 |work=The New York Times}}</ref>某些司法管轄區已經討論將對在特別危險的時刻人類進入窮鄉僻壤的限制。<ref>{{cite news |title=Latest avalanche has Colorado looking at backcountry limits |author= |url=http://news.google.com/newspapers?id=a45TAAAAIBAJ&sjid=doYDAAAAIBAJ&pg=5375,1472700&dq=backcountry&hl=en |newspaper=The Bulletin |date=February 22, 1993 |accessdate=July 6, 2011}}</ref>
