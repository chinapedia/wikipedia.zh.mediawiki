'''迈克尔·罗伯特·索恰'''（{{lang-en|'''Michael Robert Socha'''}}, {{bd|1987年|12月13日}}），英國演員。代表作品包含電影《[[這就是英格蘭|這就是英格蘭]]》及《{{tsl|en|Summer (2008 film)|夏天 (2008年電影)|夏天}}》<ref name="Scott">{{cite web |last=Scott |first=Jennifer |url=http://www.thisisnottingham.co.uk/Glamour-Boy-Michael-s-amazing-acting-journey/story-12259684-detail/story.html |title=Michael Socha, Glamour, Nottingham Playhouse |publisher=This is Nottingham |date=2009-02-05 |accessdate=2012-04-01 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120913010925/http://www.thisisnottingham.co.uk/Glamour-Boy-Michael-s-amazing-acting-journey/story-12259684-detail/story.html |archivedate=2012-09-13 }}</ref>，以及電視劇《{{tsl|en|This Is England '86|這就是英國 '86}}》《{{tsl|en|This Is England '88|這就是英國 '88}}》、《{{tsl|en|This Is England '90|這就是英國 '90}}》、《[[魔性人心|魔性人心]]》<ref name="Broke">{{cite web |url=http://www.thisisderbyshire.co.uk/broke-watching-daytime-telly-fame-came-calling/story-11624513-detail/story.html |title=ACTOR Michael Socha has revealed that he was broke and sitting at home watching the Jeremy Kyle Show when he got a call that would make 2010 his best year yet. |publisher=This is Derbyshire |date=2010-09-04 |accessdate=2012-04-01 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121001193600/http://www.thisisderbyshire.co.uk/broke-watching-daytime-telly-fame-came-calling/story-11624513-detail/story.html |archivedate=2012-10-01 }}</ref>、《[[奇境传说|奇境传说]]》及[[英國廣播公司第三台|BBC Three]]迷你影集《{{tsl|en|Our World War (TV_series)|我們的世界大戰}}》。
