'''苯基乙醇胺N-甲基转移酶'''（{{lang-en|Phenylethanolamine N-methyltransferase}}；PNMT）是一个存在于[[肾上腺髓质|肾上腺髓质]]的酶，其将[[去甲肾上腺素|去甲肾上腺素]]转换为[[肾上腺素|肾上腺素]]。

此酶受[[皮质醇|皮质醇]]的正影响，后者由[[肾上腺皮质|肾上腺皮质]]产生出来。

[[S-腺苷-L-甲硫氨酸|S-腺苷-L-甲硫氨酸]]（SAM）是一个需要用的辅因子<ref name="pmid1426768">{{cite journal | author = Wong DL, Lesage A, Siddall B, Funder JW | title = Glucocorticoid regulation of phenylethanolamine N-methyltransferase in vivo | journal = The FASEB Journal : Official Publication of the Federation of American Societies for Experimental Biology | volume = 6 | issue = 14 | pages = 3310–5 | pmid = 1426768 | doi = | url = http://www.fasebj.org/cgi/content/abstract/6/14/3310 | issn = |date=November 1992}}</ref>。
