'''苯甲酸甲酯'''（'''Methyl benzoate'''）是一种有机化合物，结构简式C<sub>6</sub>H<sub>5</sub>CO<sub>2</sub>CH<sub>3</sub>。外观为无色液体，难溶于水，与很多有机溶剂混溶，具有浓郁香气，用于配制香精和人造精油，也用作有机溶剂，吸引昆虫的农药。 

==合成与反应==
苯甲酸甲酯由[[苯甲酸|苯甲酸]]与[[甲醇|甲醇]]在强酸（如盐酸）催化下合成。<ref>{{cite book |title=Organic Chemistry, 7th Edition|publisher =Thompson - Brooks/Cole|author = John McMurry|year =2008 |isbn=1-4390-4972-6}}. Page 623</ref>氢氧化钠催化下水解生成甲醇和苯甲酸钠，后者酸化得苯甲酸。

==存在==
苯甲酸甲酯可以从淡水蕨类{{en-link|人厌槐叶苹|Salvinia molesta|人厌槐叶苹}}分离。<ref>{{Cite journal|pmid=18177906|year=2008|last1=Choudhary|first1=MI|last2=Naheed|first2=N|last3=Abbaskhan|first3=A|last4=Musharraf|first4=SG|last5=Siddiqui|first5=H|title=Phenolic and other constituents of fresh water fern Salvinia molesta|volume=69|issue=4|pages=1018–23|doi=10.1016/j.phytochem.2007.10.028|journal=Phytochemistry|last6=Atta-Ur-Rahman}}</ref>其能吸引不同品种的雄性{{en-link|兰花蜜蜂|orchid bees|兰花蜜蜂}}，在研究上用苯甲酸甲酯来引诱和捕捉这些蜜蜂。<ref>{{cite journal | author = Schiestl, F.P.; Roubik, D.W. | year = 2003 | title = Odor Compound Detection in Male Euglossine Bees | journal = [[Journal_of_Chemical_Ecology|Journal of Chemical Ecology]] | volume = 29 | pages = 253–257 | doi = 10.1023/A:1021932131526 | pmid=12647866 | issue = 1}}</ref>
