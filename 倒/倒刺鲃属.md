'''倒刺鲃属'''（[[学名|学名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Spinibarbus'''''}}）为[[鲤科|鲤科]][[鱼类|鱼类]]的一個。

==物種==
本屬共有七個物種<ref>{{FishBase genus|genus=Spinibarbus|month=10|year=2011}}</ref>：
* ''Spinibarbus babeensis'' <small>Nguyen, 2001	</small>
* [[倒刺鲃|倒刺鲃]] ''Spinibarbus denticulatus'' <small>(Oshima, 1926)</small>
* [[光倒刺鲃|光倒刺鲃]]  ''Spinibarbus hollandi'' <small>Oshima, 1919	  </small>
* ''Spinibarbus maensis'' <small>Nguyen, Duong & Tran, 2007	</small>
* ''Spinibarbus nammauensis'' <small>Nguyen & Nguyen, 2001</small>  
* ''Spinibarbus ovalius'' <small>Nguyen & Ngo, 2001</small>
* [[中华倒刺鲃|中华倒刺鲃]] ''Spinibarbus sinensis'' <small>(Bleeker, 1871)</small><ref name='id5997'>{{cite web| url =http://www.bioinfo.cn/db05/BjdwSpecies.php?action=view&id=5997| title =中华倒刺鲃| work =中国动物物种编目数据库| publisher =中国科学院微生物研究所| author =中国科学院动物研究所| accessdate =2009-04-11| deadurl =yes| archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20160305060040/http://www.bioinfo.cn/db05/BjdwSpecies.php?action=view&id=5997| archivedate =2016-03-05}}</ref>
