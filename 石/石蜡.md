'''石蜡'''是固态高级[[烷烃|烷烃]]混合物的俗名，分子式为C<sub>n</sub>H<sub>2n+2</sub>，其中n=20~40。
[[File:Paraffin.jpg|thumb]]

石蜡可由天然或人造[[石油|石油]]的含蜡馏分用冷榨或溶剂脱蜡、发汗等方法制得。

石蜡通常是白色、有轻微气味的蜡状固体，熔点在47 °C-64 °C，密度约0.8 g/cm<sup>3</sup><ref>Kaye, George William Clarkson; Laby,Thomas Howell. [http://www.kayelaby.npl.co.uk/general_physics/2_2/2_2_1.html Mechanical properties of materials]. ''Kaye and Laby Tables of Physical and Chemical Constants''. National Physical Laboratory. Retrieved on 2008-03-06. </ref>。它不溶于水，但可溶于[[醚|醚]]、[[苯|苯]]和某些[[酯|酯]]中。石蜡不与常见的[[化学试剂|化学试剂]]反应，但可以燃烧。

纯石蜡是很好的[[绝缘体|绝缘体]]，其[[电阻#电阻率|电阻率]]为10<sup>13</sup>-10<sup>17</sup>欧姆·米<ref>[http://www.kayelaby.npl.co.uk/general_physics/2_6/2_6_3.html Electrical insulating materials]. ''Kaye and Laby Tables of Physical and Chemical Constants''. National Physical Laboratory (1995). Retrieved on 2007-04-23. </ref>，比除了某些[[塑料|塑料]]（尤其是[[特富龙|特富龙]]）外的大多数材料都要高。

石蜡也是很好的储热材料，其[[比热容|比热容]]为2.14–2.9 J·g<sup>–1</sup>·K<sup>–1</sup>，[[熔化热|熔化热]]为200–220 J·g<sup>–1</sup>。<ref>[http://www.diracdelta.co.uk/science/source/s/p/specific%20heat%20capacity/source.html Specific Heat Capacity] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070804192935/http://www.diracdelta.co.uk/science/source/s/p/specific%20heat%20capacity/source.html |date=2007-08-04 }}. ''Diracdelta.co.uk Science and Engineering Encyclopedia''. Dirac Delta Consultants Ltd, Warwick, England. Retrieved on 2007-08-18. </ref>

石蜡是[[蜡烛|蜡烛]]的主要成分。
