'''石膏底料'''（{{lang-it|'''Gesso'''}} {{IPA-it|ˈdʒɛsːo}}；意大利語本意就是[[石膏|石膏]]）是一種白色的[[繪畫|繪畫]]用混合[[粘合劑|粘合劑]]，成分主要可能是[[白堊|白堊]]、石膏等，<ref>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=I1DFuGQeG10C&pg=PA32&dq=%22Gesso+is%22&hl=en&ei=_wKqTLn3CoP6lwe1q8DkDQ&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=2&ved=0CC8Q6AEwAQ#v=onepage&q=%22Gesso%20is%22&f=false |title=The materials and techniques of medieval painting By Daniel Varney Thompson. page 32 |publisher=Books.google.com |date= |accessdate=2014-04-09}}</ref> 在繪畫時可用石膏底料來做木版畫、布面畫等的底層塗料。石膏底料的顏色一般是白色的。因為吸收性好，無論是在[[蛋彩畫|蛋彩畫]]還是[[油畫|油畫]]中都可以使用。在一些立體雕塑上有時也會用到這種底料。<ref>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=OHa9dxrh5g0C&pg=PA148&dq=Gesso+gold+leaf&hl=en&ei=Kg-qTJaqBMKBlAf7ic21DQ&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=4&ved=0CEMQ6AEwAw#v=onepage&q=Gesso%20gold%20leaf&f=false |title=Lettering & Calligraphy Workbook By Diagram Group, page 148 |publisher=Books.google.com |date= |accessdate=2014-04-09}}</ref>

==丙烯酸石膏底料==
現代的“[[丙烯顏料|丙烯酸]]石膏底料”也常見使用。<ref>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=DCN21wUqnS8C&pg=PA28&dq=Acrylic+gesso+is+on+of+the+most+popular+ground&hl=en&sa=X&ei=5fk1UY_BEoy20QHC8ICwBw&ved=0CDMQ6AEwAQ#v=onepage&q=Acrylic%20gesso%20is%20on%20of%20the%20most%20popular%20ground&f=false |title=Elizabeth Tolley, Oil Painter's Solution Book - Landscapes: Over 100 Answers to Your Oil Painting, page 28 |publisher=Books.google.com |date= |accessdate=2014-04-09}}</ref> 這種底料使用[[碳酸鈣|碳酸鈣]]和丙烯酸类高聚物乳液和其他一些顏料和物質混合而成，實際上已經和石膏沒有關係了。再加上其吸收性沒有真的石膏底料那麼好，因此並不能用來繪製蛋彩畫。<ref>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=CU56YgWzIM8C&pg=PA33&dq=%22Acrylic+gesso%22+non-absorbent+tempera&hl=en&sa=X&ei=3gw9Ue6LKrCC0QH7j4CgAw&ved=0CDcQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q=%22Acrylic%20gesso%22%20non-absorbent%20tempera&f=false |title=Altoon Sultan, Luminous Brush: Painting with Egg Tempera, page 33 |publisher=Books.google.com |date= |accessdate=2014-04-09}}</ref> 它可以用來提高藝術作品的保存年限。<ref>[http://books.google.com/books?id=o23u0SuRLxsC&pg=PA321&dq=%22Italian+gesso%22&hl=en&ei=vxWqTKYIxZuWB4yr7YAO&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=10&ved=0CFIQ6AEwCQ#v=onepage&q=%22Italian%20gesso%22&f=false ''The painter's handbook: a complete reference'', by Mark David Gottsegen.
