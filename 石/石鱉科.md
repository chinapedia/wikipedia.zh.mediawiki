'''石鳖科'''（[[學名|學名]]：'''Chitonidae'''）是[[多板纲|多板纲]][[新石鳖目|新石鳖目]]的一個科，本科物種均為在海洋生活的[[软体动物门|软体动物]] 。

==分類==
本科現時有15個現存亞科及一個化石亞科，分別如下：
* [[石鱉亞科|石鱉亞科]] Chitoninae <small>[[康斯坦丁·萨米埃尔·拉菲内克-施马尔茨|Rafinesque]], 1815</small>
**[[石鳖属|石鳖属]] ''Chiton'' <small>[[Linnaeus|Linnaeus]], 1758</small> – the type genus of the family
** ''[[Amaurochiton|Amaurochiton]]'' <small>Thiele, 1893</small>
** ''[[Radsia|Radsia]]'' <small>Gray, 1847</small>
** ''[[Sypharochiton|Sypharochiton]]'' <small>Thiele, 1893</small>
** ''[[Nodiplax|Nodiplax]]'' <small>Beu, 1967</small>
** ''[[Rhyssoplax|Rhyssoplax]]'' <small>Thiele, 1893</small>
** ''[[Teguloaplax|Teguloaplax]]'' <small>[[Tom_Iredale|Iredale]] & Hull, 1926</small>
** ''[[Mucrosquama|Mucrosquama]]'' <small>[[Tom_Iredale|Iredale]], 1893</small>
* Subfamily [[Toniciinae|Toniciinae]] <small>Pilsbry, 1893</small>
** ''[[Tonicia|Tonicia]]'' <small>Gray, 1847</small>
** [[錦石鱉屬|錦石鱉屬]] ''Onithochiton'' <small>Gray, 1847</small>
* [[海胆石鳖亚科|海胆石鳖亚科]] Acanthopleurinae <small>{{link-en|Dall|W. H. Dall}}, 1889</small>
**[[海胆石鳖属|海胆石鳖属]] ''Acanthopleura'' <small>Guilding, 1829</small>
**[[駝石鱉屬|駝石鱉屬]] ''Liolophura''
** ''[[Enoplochiton|Enoplochiton]]'' <small>Gray, 1847</small>
** ''[[Squamopleura|Squamopleura]]'' <small>Nierstrasz, 1905</small>
