'''寬臼獸科'''（[[學名|學名]]Eurymylidae）是一[[科|科]]已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的[[單齒類|單齒類]]。大部份學者都認為牠們是所有現今[[齧齒目|齧齒目]]的基底，且是齧齒目最後共同祖先的祖先。但是認識較多的[[原古兔屬|原古兔屬]]、[[曉鼠屬|曉鼠屬]]、[[晨光獸屬|晨光獸屬]]及[[菱臼獸屬|菱臼獸屬]]似乎是一個非齧齒目直接祖先的[[單系群|單系群]]。<ref name=Meng2003>{{Cite journal en|author=Meng, J., Y. Hu, C. Li|year=2003|title=The osteology of ''Rhombomylus'' (Mammalia, Glires): implications for phylogeny and evolution of Glires|journal=Bulletin of the American Museum of Natural History|volume=275|pages=1-247|url=http://hdl.handle.net/2246/442}}</ref>另外有指曉鼠屬、晨光獸屬及菱臼獸屬是屬於同一[[分支|分支]]，擁有獨特的[[頭顱骨|頭顱骨]]及[[齒式|齒式]]特徵。應分類為Rhombomylidae。<ref name=Huang2004>{{Cite journal en|author=Huang, X., C. Li, M.R. Dawson, and L. Liu|year=2003|title=''Hanomys malcolmi'', a new simplicidentate mammal from the Paleocene of central China: its relationships and stratigraphic implications|journal=Bulletin of Carnegie Museum of Natural History|volume=36|issue=1|pages=81-9}}</ref>寬臼獸科只在[[亞洲|亞洲]]被發現。
