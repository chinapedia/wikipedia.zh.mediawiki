'''外頸動脈'''（{{lang|en|external carotid artery}}）源自於[[總頸動脈|總頸動脈]]，為頭頸部的一條重要[[动脉|动脉]]。總頸動脈有[[內頸動脈|內頸動脈]]及外頸動脈兩條分支，外頸動脈負責供應頭頸部的血流<ref>{{cite web|title=Carotid artery|url=http://www.webmd.com/heart/picture-of-the-carotid-artery|website=WebMD|accessdate=2015-07-28}}</ref>。

==解剖==
外頸動脈源自於[[甲狀軟骨|甲狀軟骨]]上緣，向前上行，之後向後上行至[[下顎骨|下顎骨]]頸部後方，並在{{tsl|en|parotid gland|腮腺}}處分為[[淺顳動脈|淺顳動脈]]及[[上頜動脈|上頜動脈]]，之後管徑急速縮小。

==功能==
[[File:External_carotid_artery.png|thumb]]
外頸動脈的分支包含：
* In the {{tsl|en|carotid triangle||carotid triangle}}:<ref>[http://ect.downstate.edu/courseware/haonline/labs/l25/120100.htm Human Anatomy - Lab 25 Step 12<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>
** {{tsl|en|Superior thyroid artery||Superior thyroid artery}}, arising from its anterior aspect
** {{tsl|en|Ascending pharyngeal artery||Ascending pharyngeal artery}} - arising from medial, or deep, aspect
** {{tsl|en|Lingual artery||Lingual artery}} - arising from its anterior aspect
** {{tsl|en|Facial artery||Facial artery}} - arise from its anterior aspect
** {{tsl|en|Occipital artery||Occipital artery}} - arising from its posterior aspect
* {{tsl|en|Posterior auricular artery||Posterior auricular artery}} - arising from posterior aspect

外頸動脈的終末分支共有兩條：
* [[上頜動脈|上頜動脈]]
* [[淺顳動脈|淺顳動脈]]
