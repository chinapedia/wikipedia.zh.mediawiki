'''電漿粒團'''是一種將[[電漿|電漿]]和[[磁場|磁場]]連接在一起的結構，並曾經被用來解釋像是[[球閃電|球閃電]]的自然現象<ref>Silberg, Paul A., "Ball Lightning and Plasmoids", (1962) ''Journal of Geophysical Research'', Vol. 67, p.4941</ref>、在[[磁層|磁層]]內的磁泡 <ref>Hones, E. W., Jr., "The magnetotail - Its generation and dissipation", (1976) ''Physics of solar planetary environments''; ''Proceedings of the International Symposium on Solar-Terrestrial Physics'', Boulder, Colo., June 7-18, 1976. Volume 2.</ref>，和彗尾內的物體,<ref>Roosen, R. G.; Brandt, J. C., "Possible Detection of Colliding Plasmoids in the Tail of Comet Kohoutek" (1976), ''Study of Comets'', Proceedings of IAU Colloq. 25, held in Greenbelt, MD, 28 October - 1 November, 1974. Edited by B. D. Donn, M. Mumma, W. Jackson, M. A'Hearn, and R. Harrington. National Aeronautics and Space Administration SP 393, 1976., p.378</ref>、在太陽風<ref>Lemaire, J.; Roth, M., [http://adsabs.harvard.edu/cgi-bin/nph-bib_query?bibcode=1981P%26SS...29..843L&db_key=AST&data_type=HTML&format=%20&high=42ca922c9c15961 Differences between solar wind plasmoids and ideal magnetohydrodynamic filaments] ''Planetary and Space Science'', Volume 29, Issue 8, p. 843-849</ref><ref>Wang, S.; Lee, L. C.; Wei, C. Q.; Akasofu, S.-I., A mechanism for the formation of plasmoids and kink waves in the heliospheric current sheet (1988) ''Solar Physics'' (ISSN 0038-0938), vol. 117, no. 1, 1988, p. 157-169.</ref>、在太陽的大氣層<ref>Cargill, P. J.; Pneuman, G. W., "[http://adsabs.harvard.edu/cgi-bin/nph-bib_query?bibcode=1986ApJ...307..820C&db_key=AST&data_type=HTML&format=%20&high=42ca922c9c15961 The energy balance of plasmoids in the solar atmosphere]" (1986), ''Astrophysical Journal'', Part 1 (ISSN 0004-637X), vol. 307, Aug. 15, 1986, p. 820-825.</ref>，和在[[太陽圈電流片|太陽圈電流片]]。在實驗室內製造的電漿粒團包括[[逆轉磁場配置|逆轉磁場配置]]、[[Spheromak|Spheromak]]s、和[[密集電漿焦點|密集電漿焦點]]。

電漿粒團這個詞彙是在1956年由[[Winston_H._Bostick|Winston H. Bostick]] (1916-1991)創造的，它的意思是"電漿-磁場個體"<ref name="bostick1956">Bostick, Winston H., "[http://adsabs.harvard.edu/cgi-bin/nph-bib_query?bibcode=1956PhRv..104..292B&db_key=PHY&data_type=HTML&format=%20 Experimental Study of Ionized Matter Projected across a Magnetic Field]", (1956) ''Physical Review'', vol. 104, Issue 2, pp. 292-299</ref>：

<blockquote>
電漿的散發不是無定形的一團，而是一種[[環形曲面|環形曲面]]的形式，我們將這個超環面結構稱為電漿粒團，它的意思是電漿-磁場個體。'''電漿粒團'''這個詞彙將使用於所有的電漿-磁場個體。
</blockquote>

== 電漿粒團的特徵 ==

Bostick寫道<ref name="bostick1956" />：

<blockquote>
電漿粒團看起來是朝著磁場方向被延長的電漿圓柱體，電漿粒團擁有可以測量的磁矩、可以測量的平均速度、一個橫斷的電場和可以測量的大小。電漿粒團彼此之間可以有交互作用，它們的軌道可能也被互相的扭曲。電漿粒團如果被拋出進入10<sup>−3</sup>mm汞柱壓力的氣體時，可以被做成螺旋狀和停止。電漿粒團也可能會互相摧毀成為碎片。但沒有足夠的證據支持它們會分裂和旋轉的假說。
</blockquote>

== 宇宙論的應用 ==

Bostick嘗試將它的電漿粒團理論應用在[[天文物理|天文物理]]的現象上。他在1958年發表的論文<ref>Bostick, Winston H., "[http://articles.adsabs.harvard.edu//full/seri/IAUS./0008//0001090.000.html Possible Hydromagnetic Simulation of Cosmical Phenomena in the Laboratory]" (1958) ''Cosmical Gas Dynamics'', Proceedings from IAU Symposium no. 8. Edited by Johannes Martinus Burgers and Richard Nelson Thomas. International Astronomical Union. Symposium no. 8, p. 1090</ref>，應用電漿[[電漿定標|相似的轉換]]使用電漿槍（密集電漿焦點裝置）讓一對電漿粒團槍互射，以這樣的交互作用模擬星系形成的一種早期模型<ref>W. L. Laurence, "Physicist creates universe in a test tube," ''New York Times'', p. 1, Dec. 12, 1956.</ref><ref>Bostick, W. H., "[http://adsabs.harvard.edu/cgi-bin/nph-bib_query?bibcode=1986ITPS...14..703B&db_key=PHY&data_type=HTML&format=%20&high=42ca922c9c11925 What laboratory-produced plasma structures can contribute to the understanding of cosmic structures both large and small]" (1986) ''IEEE Transactions on Plasma Science'' (ISSN 0093-3813), vol. PS-14, Dec. 1986, p. 703-717.</ref>。
