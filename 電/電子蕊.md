'''電子蕊'''（{{lang-en|'''Electronicore'''}}）是一個混合[[後硬核|後硬核]]和/或[[金屬核|金屬核]]與電子音樂的音樂類型。

==特色==
一些現代的後硬蕊樂團，開始將這種音樂與[[電子音樂|電子音樂]]結合，<ref name=ISSaa>{{cite web |last=Birchmeier |first=Jason |title= I See Stars - Biography |url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/i-see-stars-p1122695 |work=[[Allmusic|Allmusic]] |publisher=[[Rovi_Corporation|Rovi Corporation]] |accessdate=April 25, 2011}}</ref><ref name=ISS/><ref name=SkyAA>{{cite web |last=Birchmeier |first=Jason |title= Sky Eats Airplane - Biography |url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/sky-eats-airplane-p1001468/biography |work=[[Allmusic|Allmusic]] |publisher=[[Rovi_Corporation|Rovi Corporation]] |accessdate=April 25, 2011}}</ref>創造出所謂的電子蕊或混音蕊（Synthcore）<ref name=AWZUM>{{cite web |last=Pio |first=Gabriel [http://thenewreview.net/staff#gabriel31 (Staff member)] |title=I See Stars - ''The End of the World Party'' |url=http://thenewreview.net/reviews/i-see-stars-the-end-of-the-world-party |publisher=TheNewReview.net |accessdate=April 25, 2011 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110223162300/http://thenewreview.net/reviews/i-see-stars-the-end-of-the-world-party |archivedate=二月 23, 2011 }}</ref><ref name=UTGR/>樂團利用[[金屬蕊|金屬蕊]]的BreakDown、[[吼腔|吼腔]]結合[[混音|混音]]、電子音效與Auto-Tune處理。<ref name=AWZUM/><ref name=UTGR>{{cite web |last=Duffy |first=Grace [http://underthegunreview.net/staff/ (Staff member)] |title=REVIEW: I SEE STARS – END OF THE WORLD PARTY |url=http://underthegunreview.net/2011/03/01/review-i-see-stars-end-of-the-world-party/ |publisher=Under the Gun Reviews |accessdate=April 25, 2011}}</ref><ref name=AASaa/>混合的電子音樂種類包括[[迴響貝斯|迴響貝斯]]、[[鐵克諾音樂|鐵克諾音樂]]、[[出神音樂|出神音樂]]。
