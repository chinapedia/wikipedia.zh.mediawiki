'''陳恩藩'''（Francis Chan，{{bd|1967年|8月31日}})是一名[[美籍華裔|美籍華裔]]牧師。現擔任加州聖米谷(SimiValley)基石教會(Cornerstone Community Church)的主任牧師<ref name=jama20070327>{{cite web |url=http://www.jamaglobal.com/wp/interview-with-francis-chan/ |title=Interview with Francis Chan |publisher=Jesus Awakening Movement for America/All Nations |date=Mar 27, 2007 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120220070024/http://www.jamaglobal.com/wp/interview-with-francis-chan/ |archivedate=2012-02-20 }}</ref> ，也是加州永恆聖經學院(Eternity Bible College)的創辦人。<ref>{{cite web |first=Francis |last=Chan |date= |title=about EBC: a message from our founder |url=http://www.eternitybiblecollege.com/about/message.html |publisher=Eternity Bible College |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090301060902/http://www.eternitybiblecollege.com/about/message.html |archivedate=2009-03-01 }}</ref>陳恩藩牧師在2013年由英國的[[沃特金斯書店|沃特金斯書店]](Watkins Books)出版的《沃特金斯評論》(Watkins Review)所公布[[世界百大精神人物|一百位在世最具精神影響力人物]]的榜單名列第16名。<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.watkinsbooks.com/review/watkins-spiritual-100-list-2013 |title=Watkins' Spiritual 100 List for 2013 |access-date=2013-12-23 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140105052003/http://www.watkinsbooks.com/review/watkins-spiritual-100-list-2013 |archive-date=2014-01-05 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>
