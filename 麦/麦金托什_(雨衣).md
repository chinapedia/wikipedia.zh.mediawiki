'''麦金托什'''（{{lang-en|Mackintosh}}）是一種防水外套或[[雨衣|雨衣]]，得名于发明防水材料的苏格兰化學家[[查尔斯·麦金托什|查尔斯·麦金托什]]，雨衣面料用[[煤焦油|煤焦油]]、[[清油|清油]]与[[橡胶|橡胶]][[溶液|溶液]]粘合两层橡胶而不透水。<ref>{{cite web |author= |url=http://thescotsman.scotsman.com/s2.cfm?id=1097652002 |title=Mrs Mac is back - The Scotsman |publisher=Thescotsman.scotsman.com |date=2002-10-03 |accessdate=2013-11-06 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071030230723/http://thescotsman.scotsman.com/s2.cfm?id=1097652002 |archivedate=2007-10-30 }}</ref>
