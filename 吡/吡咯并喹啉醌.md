'''吡咯并喹啉醌<ref>[http://www.term.gov.cn/pages/homepage/result2.jsp?id=334513&subid=10001798&subject=%E6%BF%80%E7%B4%A0%E4%B8%8E%E7%BB%B4%E7%94%9F%E7%B4%A0&subsys=%E7%94%9F%E7%89%A9%E5%8C%96%E5%AD%A6%E4%B8%8E%E5%88%86%E5%AD%90%E7%94%9F%E7%89%A9%E5%AD%A6]{{dead link|date=2017年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>'''（{{lang-en|Pyrroloquinoline quinone}}，简称为'''PQQ'''）是由J·G·豪格（J.G. Hauge）发现的在细菌中存在的继[[烟酰胺|烟酰胺]]和[[黄素|黄素]]以外的第三种氧化还原辅因子（尽管他们事先假设它是[[萘醌|萘醌]]）<ref>{{cite journal | author = Hauge JG | title = Glucose dehydrogenase of bacterium anitratum: an enzyme with a novel prosthetic group | journal = J Biol Chem | year = 1964 | volume = 239 | pages = 3630–9 | pmid = 14257587 }}</ref>。安东尼与札特曼亦在[[醇脱氢酶|醇脱氢酶]]中发现了这种未知的氧化还原辅因子并将它命名为“methoxatin”<ref>{{cite journal | author = Anthony C, Zatman LJ | title = The microbial oxidation of methanol. The prosthetic group of the alcohol dehydrogenase of Pseudomonas sp. M27: a new oxidoreductase prosthetic group | journal = Biochem J | year = 1967 | volume = 104 | issue = 3 | pages = 960–9 | pmc = 1271238 | pmid = 6049934 }}</ref>。
