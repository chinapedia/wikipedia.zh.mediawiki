'''鲁奇·桑维'''（{{lang-en|'''Ruchi Sanghvi'''}}，{{bd|1982年|1月20日|catIdx=S}}）是社交網站[[Facebook|Facebook]]第一個雇用的女[[電腦工程|電腦工程師]]<ref name="huff">{{cite news | url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2011/09/13/ruchi-sanghvi-facebook-female-engineer_n_961148.html | title=Ruchi Sanghvi, Facebook's First Female Engineer: 'It Was Difficult To Break Into The Boys' Club' | publisher=huffingtonpost.com | accessdate=October 7, 2011 | date=2011-09-13}}</ref><ref name="insider">{{cite web|url=http://articles.businessinsider.com/2011-09-15/tech/30159050_1_engineers-facebook-break |title=It Was Hard For Facebook's First Female Engineer "To Break Into The Boys' Club" |publisher=Business Insider |accessdate=October 7, 2011 |author=Lichaa, Zachary |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120402131113/http://articles.businessinsider.com/2011-09-15/tech/30159050_1_engineers-facebook-break |archivedate=四月 2, 2012 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://womany.net/read/article/3629 |title=勇闖 Facebook 的第一位女工程師 |publisher=Womany.net |date= |accessdate=2014-07-07}}</ref>。

鲁奇·桑维在Facebook時是核心產品經理，預見了{{link-en|Facebook平台|Facebook Platform}}及其動態彙總（News Feed）的功能，她負責公司的平台產品策略及新產品的雛形。鲁奇·桑维是Facebook早期的工程師，也是動態彙總功能的早期開發者之一。鲁奇·桑维在進入Facebook前曾是[[甲骨文公司|甲骨文公司]]實時通訊部門的軟體工程師。鲁奇·桑维在[[卡內基美隆大學|卡內基美隆大學]]獲得電子計算機工程的學士及碩士學位。

鲁奇·桑维在2010年底離開了Facebook<ref name=youtube1>{{cite web|url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=64AaXC00bkQ&feature=plcp |title=Ruchi Sanghvi: From Facebook to facing the unknown |publisher=YouTube |date=2012-03-20 |accessdate=2014-07-07}}</ref>，在2011年和另外兩位創始人成立了公司Cove，這個公司在2012年賣給[[Dropbox|Dropbox]]，鲁奇·桑维加入了Dropbox，在2013年10月離開<ref name=allthingsd>{{cite web|url=http://allthingsd.com/20131009/prominent-dropbox-executive-ruchi-sanghvi-is-leaving-the-company/|title = Prominent Dropbox Executive Ruchi Sanghvi Is Leaving the Company|last = Gannes|first = Liz|date = October 9, 2013|accessdate = June 30, 2014|publisher = ''AllThingsD''}}</ref><ref name=livemint>{{cite web|url=http://www.livemint.com/Companies/XmqafmHKXq3U45sB3LdsYL/I-just-happen-to-be-a-woman-who-is-aggressive-Ruchi-Sanghvi.html|title = I just happen to be a woman who is aggressive: Ruchi Sanghvi Facebook’s first woman engineer on the US immigration Bill, her reasons for investing in firms such as Flipkart, and being a member of FWD.us|last = D'Monte|first = Leslie|last2 = Khan|first2 = Zahra|date = November 29, 2013|accessdate = June 30, 2014|publisher = ''Livemint''}}</ref>。

== 生平和職業成就 ==
=== 早年 ===
鲁奇·桑维在[[印度|印度]]的[[浦那|浦那]]長大，在年輕時她有意在畢業後就參與她父親的事業<ref name=fwd-us-stories>{{cite web|url = http://www.fwd.us/stories|title = Stories|publisher = FWD.us|accessdate = 2013-04-17|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20130413231013/http://www.fwd.us/stories#|archive-date = 2013-04-13|dead-url = yes}}</ref><ref name=marieclaire-profile>{{cite web|url=http://www.marieclaire.com/career-money/advice/ruchi-sanghvi|title = Ruchi Sanghvi|publisher = ''Marie Claire''|accessdate = 2013-04-23}}</ref>。鲁奇·桑维在[[卡內基美隆大學|卡內基美隆大學]]獲得電子計算機工程的學士及碩士學位<ref name=huff/><ref name=cmu>{{cite web|url=http://www.cmu.edu/homepage/computing/2010/fall/engineering-facebook.shtml|title = Engineering Facebook|publisher = Carnegie Mellon University|accessdate = 2013-04-23}}</ref>。

=== Facebook時期 ===
鲁奇·桑维在大學畢業後原打算在紐約工作，但被狹小的辦公小间嚇到了，後來她前往[[矽谷|矽谷]]的[[甲骨文公司|甲骨文公司]]，而她當時正和大學同學Aditya Agarwal約會，他也在矽谷工作<ref name=livemint/>。

2005年起鲁奇·桑维和Aditya Agarwal開始在[[Facebook|Facebook]]工作，鲁奇·桑维是Facebook第一個女工程師<ref name=huff/><ref name=cmu/><ref name=kirkpatrick>Kirkpatrick, David (2010-06-08). The Facebook Effect (pp. 132). Simon & Schuster.</ref>。

鲁奇·桑维是參與Faceboo第一版動態彙總（News Feed）功能的幾位工程師之一，動態彙總功能是在2006年9月上線的，鲁奇·桑维寫了一個部落格的留言，宣佈此功能的問世<ref name=news-feed-launch>{{cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/blog/blog.php?post=2207967130|title = Facebook Gets a Facelift|last = Sanghvi|first = Ruchi|publisher = Facebook (blog)|date = 2006-09-05|accessdate = 2013-04-23}}</ref>。原始的動態彙總是由演算法計算，定期更新一用戶的朋友們活動的簡述。在當時這個功能相當地新穎，[[Twitter|Twitter]]只在幾個月前提出此一功能。

動態彙總問世後造成了不少的回應及批評，其中有一些是直接針對鲁奇·桑维個人<ref name=kirkpatrick/><ref name=arizona-wildcat>{{cite web|url=http://www.wildcat.arizona.edu/article/2006/09/student_users_say_new_facebook_feed_borders_on_stalking|title = Student users say new Facebook feed borders on stalking |last = Moore|first = Brandon|accessdate = 2013-04-23|date = 2006-09-08|publisher = ''Arizona Daily''}}</ref>。後來導入新的隱私控制，決定那些個人資料可以出現在朋友的頁面上，這些批評才漸漸平息，隱私控制的代碼是在一個忙碌的48小時編程會議中完成的，包括鲁奇·桑维及Chris Cox、Adam Bosworth等工程師都有參與編程，並透過Faceboo主要創始人[[馬克·扎克伯格|馬克·扎克伯格]]道歉的部落格文章公佈<ref name=kirkpatrick/><ref name=zuck-contrite>{{cite web|url=https://blog.facebook.com/blog.php?post=2208562130|title = An Open Letter from Mark Zuckerberg|date = 2006-09-08|accessdate = 2013-04-23|publisher = Facebook (blog)|last = Zuckerberg|first = Mark}}</ref>。

鲁奇·桑维在2006年時成為{{link-en|Facebook平台|Facebook Platform}}的領導者<ref name=crunchbase/>。

=== Cove及Dropbox ===
鲁奇·桑维在2010年底離開Facebook，在2011年和Aditya Agarwal共同開始一個稱為Cove的計劃<ref name=youtube1/>，2012年時檔案同步及分享的公司[[Dropbox|Dropbox]]宣佈併購了Cove，鲁奇·桑维和Aditya Agarwal也會加入<ref>{{cite web|url=http://techcrunch.com/2012/02/27/dropbox-buys-cove-to-bring-former-facebookers-ruchi-sanghvi-and-aditya-agarwal-to-the-team/|title = Dropbox Buys Cove To Bring Former Facebookers Ruchi Sanghvi And Aditya Agarwal To The Team|publisher = ''TechCrunch''|last = Tsotsis|first = Alexia|accessdate = 2013-04-23|date = 2012-02-27}}</ref>。鲁奇·桑维成為副營運長，負責產品、行銷、市場溝通及其他工作。2013年10月鲁奇·桑维離開Dropbox，轉型為Dropbox的顧問<ref name=allthingsd>{{cite web|url=http://allthingsd.com/20131009/prominent-dropbox-executive-ruchi-sanghvi-is-leaving-the-company/|title = Prominent Dropbox Executive Ruchi Sanghvi Is Leaving the Company|last = Gannes|first = Liz|date = October 9, 2013|accessdate = June 30, 2014|publisher = ''AllThingsD''}}</ref><ref name=livemint/>。

=== 投資者及顧問 ===
鲁奇·桑维是許多[[矽谷|矽谷]]公司的投資者及顧問<ref name=crunchbase>{{cite web|url=http://www.crunchbase.com/person/ruchi-sanghvi|title = Ruchi Sanghvi|publisher = [[CrunchBase|CrunchBase]]''|accessdate = 2013-04-23}}</ref>，包括私密性高的相片分享服務[[Path_(私密社交網站)|Path]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://techcrunch.com/2010/11/14/path-photo-sharing/|title = After Months Of Buzz, Path Launches: It's Photo Sharing Where You Can Be Yourself|publisher = ''TechCrunch''|date = 2010-11-14|accessdate = 2013-04-23|last = Kincaid|first = Jason}}</ref>、協同工作軟體[[Asana|Asana]]、以及她曾工作過的，資料儲存及分享軟體公司[[Dropbox|Dropbox]]<ref name=livemint/><ref name=allthingsd/>。

== 個人生活 ==
鲁奇·桑维的夫婿是Aditya Agarwal，他是鲁奇·桑维在[[卡內基美隆大學|卡內基美隆大學]]的同學，後來也一起進入[[Facebook|Facebook]]、Cove和[[Dropbox|Dropbox]]等公司工作<ref name=livemint>{{cite web|url=http://www.livemint.com/Companies/XmqafmHKXq3U45sB3LdsYL/I-just-happen-to-be-a-woman-who-is-aggressive-Ruchi-Sanghvi.html|title = I just happen to be a woman who is aggressive: Ruchi Sanghvi Facebook’s first woman engineer on the US immigration Bill, her reasons for investing in firms such as Flipkart, and being a member of FWD.us|last = D'Monte|first = Leslie|last2 = Khan|first2 = Zahra|date = November 29, 2013|accessdate = June 30, 2014|publisher = ''[[Mint_(newspaper)|Livemint]]''}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/pages/Ruchi-Aditya-wedding/248466307840|title = Ruchi-Aditya wedding|publisher = [[Facebook|Facebook]]|accessdate = June 30, 2014}}</ref>。

==獎項==
鲁奇·桑维在2011年時，因為她在Facebook的貢獻，得到TechFellow 2011年時的「最佳工程師領袖獎」<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.techfellows.com/engineering-leadership/2011/ruchi-sanghvi/|title = Ruchi Sanghvi|publisher = TechFellows|accessdate = 2013-04-23}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://on.aol.com/video/techfellow-awards--ruchi-sanghvi-517287387|title = TechFellow Awards: Ruchi Sanghvi|last = Tsotsis|first = Alexia|publisher =''TechCrunch''|date =2012-03-04|accessdate = 2013-04-23}}</ref>。

== 政治 ==
鲁奇·桑维是{{link-en|FWD.us|FWD.us}}的創始人之一，FWD.us是一個在[[矽谷|矽谷]]成立的[[501(c)条款#501(c)(4)|501(c)(4)條款]]組織，以推動美國{{link-en|移民改革|Immigration reform}}、提升教育及推動技術突破為主要工作<ref name=fwd-us-supporters>{{cite web|url=http://fwd.us/our_supporters |title=Our supporters |publisher=FWD.us |accessdate=2013-04-17 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130416045611/http://www.fwd.us/our_supporters |archivedate=2013-04-16 }}</ref>。這個組織在2013年4月11日成立<ref name=fwd-us-techcrunch1>{{cite web|url=http://techcrunch.com/2013/04/11/fwd-us/|title = Zuckerberg And A Team Of Tech All-Stars Launch Political Advocacy Group FWD.us|last = Constine|first = Josh|date= 2013-04-11|accessdate = 2013-04-17|publisher = ''TechCrunch''}}</ref>。

鲁奇·桑维個人的故事列在FWD.us的網頁中<ref name=fwd-us-stories>{{cite web|url = http://www.fwd.us/stories|title = Stories|publisher = FWD.us|accessdate = 2013-04-17|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20130413231013/http://www.fwd.us/stories#|archive-date = 2013-04-13|dead-url = yes}}</ref>。

鲁奇·桑维在2013年接受印度報紙《{{link-en|鑄幣 (報紙)|Mint (newspaper)|鑄幣}}》訪問時，曾提到她為何加入FWD.us：「矽谷是非常理想化的世界，FWD.us有使命從理想世界出發，在真實世界作一些努力。FWD.us的使命是推動知識經濟，移民只是其中之一，其他部份是了解兩黨的政策，以幫助教育系統在STEM（科學、技術、工程及數學）的研究。移民是一個目前正熱的議題，我對參議院通過的議案感到滿意，雖然華盛頓已明顯分為兩派，我希望移民改革議案可以通過。」<ref name=livemint/>
