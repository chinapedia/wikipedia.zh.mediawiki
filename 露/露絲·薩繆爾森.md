'''露絲·克柏森·薩繆爾森'''（{{lang-en|Ruth Culbertson Samuelson}}；{{bd|1959年|11月4日|2017年|1月23日}}<ref>[http://www.newscenter1.tv/story/34290256/former-south-dakota-legislator-kenny-mcnenny-passes-away Former South Dakota legislator, Kenny McNenny, passes away]</ref>），是美國的[[共和黨_(美國)|共和黨]]政治人物，前[[北卡罗来纳州众议院|北卡罗来纳州众议院]]議員。

==生平==
薩繆爾森於[[北卡羅來納州|北卡羅來納州]][[夏洛特_(北卡羅來納州)|夏洛特]]出生，長大在[[北卡罗来纳大学教堂山分校|北卡罗来纳大学教堂山分校]]畢業。2000年起，她是[[梅克倫堡縣_(北卡羅萊納州)|梅克倫堡縣]]委員會第五選區委員；直到2004年她轉戰單一選區委員會議席，惟在緊湊的競選中敗選<ref>{{cite web | url = http://charmeck.org/mecklenburg/county/BOE/data/Documents/general04.pdf | title = Mecklenburg County North Carolina November 2, 2004 Election Results | publisher = Mecklenburg County North Carolina Board of Elections | location = Charlotte, North Carolina | page = 103 | accessdate = 12 November 2013}}</ref>。

2006年11月，薩繆爾森以[[共和黨_(美國)|共和黨]]黨籍身分接替連任五屆的州眾議員{{le|W·埃德溫·麥克馬漢|W. Edwin McMahan|埃德·麥克馬漢}}出選北卡罗来纳州众议院，並以67%選票擊敗[[民主黨_(美國)|民主黨]]的寶拉·麥克斯溫（Paula McSwain）當選第一百零四選區議員<ref>{{cite web | url = http://charmeck.org/mecklenburg/county/BOE/data/Documents/general06.pdf | title = Mecklenburg County North Carolina 7 November 2006 Election Results | publisher = Mecklenburg County North Carolina Board of Elections | location = Charlotte, North Carolina | pages = 130–131 | accessdate = 12 November 2013}}</ref>，任職自2007年至2015年<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nccppr.org/drupal/content/article-ii/legislator-reports/3943/2011-2012-report-for-rep-ruth-samuelson|title=2011-2012 Report for Rep. Ruth Samuelson  - NCCPPR|publisher=|accessdate=23 January 2017|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170315083647/http://www.nccppr.org/drupal/content/article-ii/legislator-reports/3943/2011-2012-report-for-rep-ruth-samuelson|archivedate=2017年3月15日}}</ref><ref name="charlotteobserver.com">{{cite web|url=http://www.charlotteobserver.com/news/local/article128167464.html|title=Ruth Samuelson, former lawmaker, dies at 56|publisher=|accessdate=23 January 2017}}</ref>。

2013年10月15日，她宣布不會尋求第五個任期，也將會在2014年大選後離職<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.charlotteobserver.com/2013/10/15/4390946/ruth-samuelson-says-she-wont-run.html | script-title = en:Ruth Samuelson says she won’t run for re-election in 2014 | first = Jim | last = Morrill | date = 15 October 2013 | publisher = The Charlotte Observer | location = Charlotte, North Carolina | quote = -{Charlotte Republican Rep.
