'''斑鼠科'''（[[學名|學名]]Alagomyidae）是一[[科|科]]生存於[[古新世|古新世]]晚期及[[始新世|始新世]]早期[[亞洲|亞洲]]及[[北美洲|北美洲]]的[[啮齿目|啮齿目]]。<ref name=McKennaBell1997>{{Cite book en|author=McKenna, Malcolm C., and Bell, Susan K.|year=1997|title=Classification of Mammals Above the Species Level|publisher=Columbia University Press|location=New York|pages=631 pp|id=ISBN 0-231-11013-8}}</ref>斑鼠科被認為是基底的啮齿目，是現有形態共同祖先以外的分支。<ref name=Meng1994>{{Cite journal en|author=Meng, J., A.R. Wyss, M.R. Dawson, and R. Zhai|year=1994|title=Primitive fossil rodent from Inner Mongolia and its implications for mammalian phylogeny|journal=Nature|volume=370|pages=134-136}}</ref>由於牠們的[[種系發生學|種系發生學]]位置及古舊的[[齒式|齒式]]，故可以作為研究啮齿目起源及關係的重要角色。<ref name=Meng1994/><ref name=MengWyss2001>{{Cite journal en|author=Meng, J., and A.R. Wyss|year=2001|title=The morphology of ''Tribosphenomys'' (Rodentiaformes, Mammalia): phylogenetic implications for basal Glires|journal=Journal of Mammalian Evolution|volume=8|issue=1|pages=1-71}}</ref>
