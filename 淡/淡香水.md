'''淡香水'''（'''Eau de toilette'''）是一類氣味較為輕微的[[香水|香水]]。<ref>[http://www.thefreedictionary.com/toilet+water The Free Dictionary definition]</ref><ref>[http://www.macmillandictionary.com/thesaurus/british/rose-water/rose-water_3/Perfume MacMillan Dictionary]</ref><ref>[http://dictionary.cambridge.org/topics/personal-care/toiletry-and-perfumes/definition-of-toilet-water Cambridge Advanced Learner's Dictionary definition]</ref>也常被稱作“芳香水”并含有一定量的酒精。<ref>Cristiani, p. 117</ref>通常會直接在沐浴後塗抹於皮膚上。<ref>[http://www.proz.com/kudoz/english/cosmetics_beauty/2455435-toilet_water.html toilet water term meaning]</ref><ref>[http://www.antiquebottles.com/poch/15.html Distinguishing Colognes, Perfumes, Scents, & Toilet Waters]</ref>

淡香水命名時常會加上其中的主要成份，例如天竺葵水中的天竺葵、薰衣水中的薰衣草等。<ref>Ebert, p. 304</ref>因為常用花作為原料，因此也常被稱作“花香水”。<ref>Lawless, p. 39</ref><ref>Baker, p. 262</ref><ref>Fettner, p. 102</ref><ref>Cox, p. 118</ref>

== 歷史 ==

14世紀[[波蘭的伊莉莎白_(匈牙利女王)|波蘭的伊莉莎白]]（1305-1380）發明了一種混合酒精的芳香油來塗抹皮膚。<ref name="Sherrow125"/>這大約就是“匈牙利水”，即最早的淡香水，<ref name="Stoddart154"/>也是[[古龍水|古龍水]]的前身。<ref>Müller, p. 348</ref>傳說年屆七十且身體欠佳的女王在使用了這種香水後竟恢復了健康。此外，匈牙利水還含有[[迷迭香|迷迭香]]成份。<ref name="HistoryofPerfume">[http://www.everythinkaboutperfume.com/history.html The History of Perfume] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150208043220/http://www.everythinkaboutperfume.com/history.html |date=2015-02-08 }}</ref>

法國國王[[路易十四|路易十四]]也使用過淡香水為自己的襯衫染香，其材料包括[[沉香|沉香]]、[[麝香|麝香]]、橘子花、[[玫瑰水|玫瑰水]]及其他香料。<ref name="Sherrow125">Sherrow, p. 125 ''King Louis XIV (1638-1715) had his shirts scented with toilet water that included aloewood, rosewood, orangle flower, musk, and spices. The concoction was called "heavenly water" ...''</ref>

== 酒精 ==

[[File:Perfume_formulas_1910.jpg|thumb]]

一般會使用一些溶劑來溶解香油，但其必要性存在爭議。而大多數溶劑都是乙醇或乙醇與水的混合物，乙醇也可以起到擴散劑的作用，使得香水的香味更容易被人嗅到。相對含有3-5%芳香油、80-90%的酒精和15%的水的[[古龍水|古龍水]]來說，淡香水的酒精濃度低得多，一般只有60-80%，而芳香油則一般有2-8%。<ref name="Groom329">Groom, p. 329</ref>因此也被當做是一種稀釋過的古龍水或[[濃香水|濃香水]]。<ref>[http://www.thefreedictionary.com/eau+de+toilette ''eau de toil'' definition from the online Free Dictionary]</ref><ref>[http://thesaurus.com/browse/toilet+water Thesaurus online dictionary]</ref>傳統上古龍水都含有柑橘油和芳香物質，但淡香水並沒有類似要求。<ref>Grolier, p. 154</ref><ref>''Consumer reports'', pp. 409-411</ref>

== 健康 ==

一些淡香水被認為可以滋潤皮膚並有一些其他的醫療價值。<ref>Better Nutrition magazine, Nov 1999, p. 34</ref><ref>Hiss, pp. 918-919</ref><ref>Frank, p. 414</ref> 1905年的《Medical Record》曾報導說噴灑淡香水可以恢復人們的精力。<ref>Dewey, p. 55</ref><ref>[http://books.google.com/books?id=bclNAAAAMAAJ&pg=PA333&dq=perfume+%22toilet+water%22+define&hl=en&ei=mId1TJbbMIGclgfV1-zJBQ&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=7&ved=0CGAQ6AEwBg#v=onepage&q=perfume%20%22toilet%20water%22%20define&f=false Interstate druggist, Volume 7, page 333]</ref> 14至16世紀有一類淡香水被認為有驅除[[黑死病|黑死病]]的功效。<ref name="Stoddart154">Stoddart, p. 154</ref><ref>[http://www.british-history.ac.uk/report.aspx?compid=58687 Dictionary of Traded Goods and Commodities, 1550-1820 by Nancy Cox and Karin Dannehl]</ref>
