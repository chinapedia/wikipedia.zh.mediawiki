'''拉爾夫·德萊昂·格雷羅·托雷斯'''（{{lang-en|Ralph Deleon Guerrero Torres}}；{{bd|1976年|8月6日|}}），是一名[[北马里亚纳群岛|北马里亚纳群岛]]政治人物。他在{{le|2014年北馬里亞納群島總督選舉|Northern Mariana Islands gubernatorial election, 2014}}當選為{{le|北马里亚纳群岛副总督|Lieutenant Governor of the Northern Mariana Islands}}<ref name=results>{{cite web|title=Commonwealth Election Commission - Election 2014 Results |url=http://www.votecnmi.gov.mp/election-2014/election-2014-results |accessdate=3 May 2015 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141130161053/http://www.votecnmi.gov.mp/election-2014/election-2014-results |archivedate=November 30, 2014 |deadurl=unfit }}</ref>。

2015年12月29日，[[北马里亚纳群岛总督|總督]]{{le|埃洛伊·伊诺斯|Eloy Inos}}因心臟外科手術失血過多逝世，托雷斯繼任為總督<ref name=deathst>{{cite web|title= Gov. Eloy Inos Passes Away |url=http://www.saipantribune.com/index.php/breaking-news-gov-eloy-inos-passes-away/|first= Mark |last= Rabago |date= 29 December 2015 |work= [[Saipan_Tribune|Saipan Tribune]] |accessdate= 29 December 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.pacificnewscenter.com/local/7239 |first= Thomas |last= Manglona |work=Pacific News Center |title= CNMI Governor Eloy Inos Dies |date= 29 December 2015 |accessdate= 28 December 2015}}</ref>。按照{{le|北馬里亞納群島聯邦憲法|Northern Mariana Islands Commonwealth Constitution|憲法}}，{{le|北馬里亞納群島參議院|Northern Mariana Islands Senate}}參議院議長{{le|維克多·洪松|Victor Hocog}}繼任為副總督<ref>[http://www.mvariety.com/cnmi/cnmi-news/local/82659-update-ralph-torres-victor-hocog-will-be-sworn-in-at-3-p-m-tuesday-at-the-multi-purpose-center New governor, lt. governor sworn in] Marianas Variety December 29, 2015</ref>。

托雷斯批准了一項「公共法律19-42」法案，令市民在購買手槍時需繳付1,000美元{{le|貨物稅|Excise|消費稅}}，是美國首項手槍稅<ref>[http://www.atr.org/1000-gun-tax-pushed-role-model-states $1,000 Gun Tax pushed as "model for the states"]</ref>。
