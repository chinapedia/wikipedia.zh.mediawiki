'''拉赞·扎伊图娜'''（{{lang-en|'''Razan Zaitouneh'''}}，{{lang-ar|'''رزان زيتونة'''}}，{{bd|1977年|4月29日}}），叙利亚女性[[人权|人权]][[律师|律师]]。在[[2011年叙利亚反政府示威|2011年叙利亚反政府示威]]中担任[[地方协调委员会|地方协调委员会]]（LCC）的负责人，她因受到叙利亚政府的指控而寻求躲避<ref>{{Cite web | url = http://www.rferl.org/content/syrian_activist_in_hiding_razan_zeitouneh_zaitouneh_syria_assad/24352910.html| title = Syrian Activist In Hiding: 'If We Didn't Believe We Will Win, We Couldn't Bear All This' | publisher = Radio Liberty | accessdate = 27 October 2011| date = 7 October 2011}}</ref>，而她丈夫则遭到逮捕<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/world/2011/may/21/syria-women-unrest-repression|title=Syria's defiant women risk all to protest against President Bashar al-Assad|work=The Guardian|accessdate=27 October 2011}}</ref>。2011年10月27日，她获得了2011年[[萨哈罗夫奖|萨哈罗夫奖]]<ref>{{Cite web| url = http://www.europarl.europa.eu/en/headlines/content/20111014FCS29297/1/html/Three-finalists-for-Sakharov-Prize-2011-honouring-human-rights-activists| title = Three finalists for Sakharov Prize 2011 honouring human rights activists| publisher = [[European_Parliament|European Parliament]]| accessdate = 27 October 2011| deadurl = yes| archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20111023223729/http://www.europarl.europa.eu/en/headlines/content/20111014FCS29297/1/html/Three-finalists-for-Sakharov-Prize-2011-honouring-human-rights-activists| archivedate = 2011年10月23日}}</ref>。之前她曾被「联络战争中的女性」（Reach All Women in War）组织授予[[安娜·波利特科夫斯卡娅|安娜·波利特科夫斯卡娅]]奖<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.rawinwar.org/content/view/153/|title=Razan Zaitouneh (Syria): Winner of the 2011 Anna Politkovskaya Award|work=Reach All Women in War|accessdate=27 October 2011|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111113213238/http://www.rawinwar.org/content/view/153/|archivedate=2011年11月13日}}</ref>。
