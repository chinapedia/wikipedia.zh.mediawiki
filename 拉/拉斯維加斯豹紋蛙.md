'''拉斯維加斯豹紋蛙'''（''Rana fisheri''），'''維加斯谷蛙'''，是[[美國|美國]]特有的一種[[蛙|蛙]]。牠們於[[美國|美國]][[拉斯維加斯|拉斯維加斯]]及[[內華達州|內華達州]]的[[克拉克縣_(內華達州)|克拉克縣]]和莫戈永山西北部地區[[海拔|海拔]]370及760米出現。<ref name="Linsdale">{{Cite journal en|author=Linsdale, J. M.|year=1940|title=Amphibians and reptiles of Nevada|journal=Proceeding of the American Academy of Arts and Sciences|volume=73|issue=8|pages=197-257}}</ref><ref name="Stebbins1951">{{Cite book en|author=Stebbins, R. C.|year=1951|title=Amphibians of western North America|publisher=University of California Press|location=Berkeley, California|pages=ix+539 pp.}}</ref><ref name="Stebbins1985">Stebbins, R. C. 1985. A field guide to western reptiles and amphibians. Houghton Mifflin Co., Boston, MA.. Mild. Nat. 77:323-355.</ref><ref name="Jennings1995">Jennings, R.D., Riddle, B.R. and Bradford, D. 1995. Rediscovery of Rana onca, the relict leopard frog, in southern Nevada with comments on the systematic relationships of some leopard frogs (Rana pipiens complex) and the status of populations along the Virgin River. Unpublished report.</ref>

在克拉克縣曾發現了10個拉斯維加斯豹紋蛙的標本，並存放在[[密歇根大學|密歇根大學]]。<ref name="Platz1984">Platz, J. E. 1984. Status report for Rana onca Cope. Unpublished report prepared for Office of Endangered Species, U. S. Fish and Wildlife Service, Albuquerque, New Mexico. iv+27 pp.</ref><ref name="CBD-SUWA-2002">Center for Biological Diversity and Southern Utah Wilderness Alliance. 2002. Petition to list the relict leopard frog (Rana onca) as an endangered species under the Endangered Species Act. ([http://www.biologicaldiversity.org/swcbd/SPECIES/relictfrog/Petition.PDF Available online])</ref>由於曾許久未被再次觀察到，在2011年以前，它們被認爲已經[[滅絕|滅絕]]。<ref name="IUCN"/><ref name="Jennings1995"/>而在2011年，一項基因分析表明，拉斯維加斯豹紋蛙的DNA與分佈在[[莫戈永山|莫戈永山]]西北部地區的{{tsl|en|Rana chiricahuensis|奇裏卡瓦豹紋蛙}}(''Rana chiricahuensis'')完全一致，而後者目前仍是一個[[現生種|現生種]]。<ref name="Hekkala2011">{{cite journal|doi=10.1007/s10592-011-0229-6|title=Resurrecting an extinct species: Archival DNA, taxonomy, and conservation of the Vegas Valley leopard frog|year=2011|last1=Hekkala|first1=Evon R.|last2=Saumure|first2=Raymond A.|last3=Jaeger|first3=Jef R.|last4=Herrmann|first4=Hans-Werner|last5=Sredl|first5=Michael J.|last6=Bradford|first6=David F.|last7=Drabeck|first7=Danielle|last8=Blum|first8=Michael J.|journal=Conservation Genetics|volume=12|issue=5|pages=1379}}</ref><ref name="bhanoo">Bhanoo, S. N. [http://www.nytimes.com/2011/06/21/science/21obfrog.html?ref=science A frog endangered but extinct no more.] ''New York Times'' June 17, 2011. Accessed June 17, 2011.</ref>這意味它們並沒有絕滅，且與奇裏卡瓦豹紋蛙的莫戈永山西北部地區種羣屬同一物種。<ref name=mach>{{cite web|url=http://machineslikeus.com/news/breathing-life-extinct-species|title=Breathing life into an extinct species|publisher=Machines Like Us|date=June 17, 2011|accessdate=June 17, 2011|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110721051908/http://machineslikeus.com/news/breathing-life-extinct-species|archivedate=2011年7月21日}}</ref>根據命名優先權，位於莫戈永山西北部地區的種羣，重新定義歸類至拉斯維加斯豹紋蛙，而分佈於南部和東部地區的種羣則繼續沿用奇裏卡瓦豹紋蛙的學名。<ref name="Hekkala2011"/>
