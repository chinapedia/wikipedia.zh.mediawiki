'''降解体'''（Degradosome）是存在于大多数[[细菌|细菌]]中的一种多亚基[[蛋白质复合物|蛋白质复合物]]，其功能为参与[[核糖体RNA|核糖体RNA]]的加工以及[[信使RNA|信使RNA]]的降解，<ref name="Carpousis">{{en}}{{cite journal en|author=Carpousis AJ|title=The RNA degradosome of ''Escherichia coli'': an mRNA-degrading machine assembled on RNase E|journal=Annu Rev Microbiol|year=2007|volume=61|pages=71-87|pmid=17447862}}</ref><ref name="Carpousis2">{{en}}{{cite journal en|author=Carpousis AJ|title=The ''Escherichia coli'' RNA degradosome: structure, function and relationship in other ribonucleolytic multienzyme complexes|journal=Biochem Soc Trans|year=2002|volume=30|pages=150-155|pmid=12035760}}</ref>故又可称为'''RNA降解体'''。

==发现==
1994年，[[瑞士|瑞士]][[日内瓦大学|日内瓦大学]]的Henry Krisch所领导的实验室和[[英国|英国]][[剑桥大学|剑桥大学]]的Christopher Higgins所领导的实验室分别发表文章提出了细菌中存在着一种可以降解RNA的蛋白质复合物，也就是降解体；<ref>{{en}}{{cite journal en|author=Carpousis AJ, Van Houwe G, Ehretsmann C, Krisch HM|title=Copurification of ''E. coli'' RNase E and PNPase: evidence for a specific association between two enzymes important in RNA processing and degradation|journal=Cell |year=1994|volume=76|pages=889–900}}</ref><ref>{{en}}{{cite journal en|author=Causton H, Py B, McLaren RS, Higgins CF|title=mRNA degradation in ''Escherichia coli'': a novel factor which impedes the exoribonucleolytic activity of PNPase at stem-loop structures|journal=Mol Microbiol |year=1994|volume=14|pages=731–741}}</ref><ref>{{en}}{{cite journal en|author=Py B, Causton H, Mudd EA, Higgins CF|title=A protein complex mediating mRNA degradation in ''Escherichia coli''|journal=Mol Microbiol |year=1994|volume=14|pages=717–729}}</ref>他们是在对[[大肠杆菌|大肠杆菌]]的RNase E和PNPase的研究和鉴定过程中得到这一发现。<ref name="Carpousis"/>

==组成==
降解体的主要成分为[[核糖核酸酶|核糖核酸酶E]]（RNase E）、[[多核苷酸磷酸化酶|多核苷酸磷酸化酶]]（PNPase）、[[RNA螺旋酶|RNase螺旋酶B]]（RhlB）和[[烯醇酶|烯醇酶]]（enolase）。<ref>{{en}}{{cite journal en|author=Miczak A, Kaberdin VR, Wei CL, Lin-Chao S|title=Proteins associated with RNase E in a multicomponent ribonucleolytic complex|journal=Proc. Natl. Acad. Sci. USA|year=1996|volume=93|pages=3865–3869}}</ref><ref>{{en}}{{cite journal en|author=Py B, Higgins CF, Krisch HM, Carpousis AJ|title=A DEAD-box RNA helicase in the ''Escherichia coli'' RNA degradosome|journal=Nature |year=1996|volume=381|pages=169–172}}</ref>
