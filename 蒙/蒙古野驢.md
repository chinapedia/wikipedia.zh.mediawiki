'''蒙古野驢'''（''Equus hemionus hemionus''，{{mong|m=ᠬᠤᠯᠠᠨ|k=хулан}}），或稱'''戈壁野驢'''，是[[亞洲野驢|亞洲野驢]]的一個[[亞種|亞種]]。<ref name = oakenfull2000>{{Cite journal en|author=Oakenfull EA, Lim HN and Ryder AO|year=2000|title=A survey of equid mitochondrial DNA: Implications for the evolution, genetic diversity and conservation of ''Equus''|journal=Cons Genet|volume=1|pages=341-355}}</ref> 牠們分佈在[[蒙古|蒙古]]及[[中國|中國]]北方，以往亦曾在[[哈薩克|哈薩克]]出沒，但因獵殺而在當地[[滅絕|滅絕]]了。<ref>{{Cite book en|author=Clark, B. and Duncan, P.|year=1992|chapter=Asian Wild Asses - Hemiones and Kiangs (''E. hemionus'' Pallas and ''E. kiang'' Moorcroft)|editor=P. Duncan (ed.)|title=Zebras, Asses, and Horses. An Action Plan for the Conservation of Wild Equids|publisher=IUCN/SSC Equid Specialist Group|location=IUCN, Gland, Switzerland|pages=17-21}}</ref>

蒙古野驢的分佈地於1990年代大大的減少。於1994年至1997年估計就有33000-63000隻蒙古野驢，分佈在差不多整個蒙古南部。<ref>{{Cite journal en|url=http://journals.cambridge.org/action/displayAbstract?fromPage=online&aid=78469|author=Reading, R. P., H. M. Mix, B. Lhagvasuren, C. Feh, D. P. Kane, S. Dulamtseren, and S. Enkhbold|year=2001|title=Status and distribution of khulan (''Equus hemionus'') in Mongolia|journal=Journal of Zoology, London|volume=254|pages=381-389}}</ref>但到了2003年就只有約20000隻，分佈在蒙古南部的17萬平方公里。<ref>{{Cite book en|author=Mongolian Ministry of Nature and Environment|year=2003|title=Status and distribution of the khulan in Mongolia in 2003|publisher=Mongolian Ministry of Nature and Environment, Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia}}</ref>雖然所有資料都非正式統計<ref>{{Cite book en|author=Buckland, S.T., D.R. Anderson, K.P. Burnham, J.L. Laake, D.L. Borchers and L. Thomas|year=2001|title=Introduction to Distance Sampling|pages=432|publisher=Oxford University Press|location=Oxford UK and New York USA}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book en|author=Kaczensky P. and C. Walzer|year=2002a, 2002b, 2003a, 2003b|title=Przewalski horses, wolves and khulans in Mongolia. Bi-annual progress reports|url=http://www.takhi.org}}</ref>，但肯定牠們在過去70年就已失去了50%的分佈地。

蒙古野驢的數量下降是因被烹吃及與其他[[家畜|家畜]]爭食所致，並被列為[[瀕危物種|瀕危物種]]。<ref name = iucn/>自1953年起，牠們在蒙古就已經全面受到保護。牠們被列在《[[瀕危野生動植物種國際貿易公約|瀕危野生動植物種國際貿易公約]]》附錄二中。<ref>[http://www.cms.int/documents/appendix/cms_app2.htm CMS 2002. Convention on Migratory Species. Appendix II.] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20060513030552/http://www.cms.int/documents/appendix/cms_app2.htm |date=2006-05-13 }} </ref>但是，由於[[人類|人類]]人口膨漲及嚴冬的[[氣候|氣候]]<ref>{{Cite book en|author=United Nations Disaster Management Team (UNDMT): National Civil Defence and State Emergency Commission Ulaanbaatar|year=2000|title=DZUD 2000-Mongolia: An evolving ecological, social and economic disaster: A rapid needs assessment report|publisher=United Nations Disaster Management Team (UNDMT): National Civil Defence and State Emergency Commission Ulaanbaatar}}</ref>，牠們與牧民之間的爭競就更為激烈。

蒙古野驢被烹吃的問題日益嚴重。在一些地區，牠們的肉是很廉價。單於2005年的問卷調查，就顯示每年有多達4500匹蒙古野驢被烹吃，即總數量的20%。再者於1990年代初，政策容許城市居民回歸為[[遊牧民族|遊牧民族]]，引發野外地區的人口及家畜暴增。<ref>{{Cite journal en|author=Fernandez-Gimenez, M. E.|year=1999|title=Sustaining the Steppes: A Geographical History of Pastoral Land Use in Mongolia|journal=Geographical Revue|volume=89|issue=3|pages=315-342}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal en|author=Bedunah, D. J. and S. M. Schmidt|year=2004|title=Pastoralism and protected area management in Mongolia's Gobi Gurvansaikhan National Park|journal=Development and Change|volume=35|issue=1|pages=167-191}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal en|author=Mearns, R., D.
