'''蒙羅維亞'''（{{lang-en|'''Monrovia'''}}）為位在[[美国|美国]][[堪薩斯州|堪薩斯州]][[艾奇遜縣_(堪薩斯州)|艾奇遜縣]]的[[非建制地區|非建制地區]]<ref>{{GNIS|478218|name=Monrovia, Kansas}}</ref>。

==歷史==
蒙羅維亞於1856年設立，為艾奇遜縣最古老的城鎮之一<ref>{{cite book|title=History of the State of Kansas: Containing a Full Account of Its Growth from an Uninhabited Territory to a Wealthy and Important State|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=5t09AQAAMAAJ&pg=PA404|year=1883|publisher=A. T. Andreas|page=404}}</ref>。該聚居地因{{le|密蘇里太平洋鐵路|Missouri Pacific Railroad}}從未擴張至此地區而無法滿足此地區設立者的期望<ref>{{cite book | url=http://books.google.com/books?id=Qi9cXyTWt9EC&pg=PA299#v=onepage&q&f=true | title=Kansas: A Cyclopedia of State History, Volume 2 | publisher=Standard Publishing Company | author=Blackmar, Frank Wilson | year=1912 | pages=299}}</ref>。

蒙羅維亞的郵政局於1857年設立，直到1955年結束營運<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.kshs.org/geog/geog_postoffices/search/county:AT | title=Kansas Post Offices, 1828-1961 (archived) | publisher=Kansas Historical Society | accessdate=4 June 2014 | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131009125756/http://www.kshs.org/geog/geog_postoffices/search/county:AT | archivedate=9 十月 2013 }}</ref>。
