'''冷凍乾燥'''（freeze drying；lyophilisation；cryodesiccation）是一個利用[[冷凍|冷凍]]方式[[乾燥_(食物處理)|乾燥]]食材的方法，常用來[[食物保存|保存]]易腐壞的食物，或是使得食物更容易運送。<ref name=":2">{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/?id=yq3snHZu5coC&pg=PA209&lpg=PA209&dq=Fundamentals+and+Tendencies+in+Freeze-Drying+of+Foods#v=onepage&q=Fundamentals%20and%20Tendencies%20in%20Freeze-Drying%20of%20Foods&f=false|title=Advances in Food Dehydration|last=Ratti|first=Cristina|date=2008-11-21|publisher=CRC Press|isbn=9781420052534|location=|pages=209–235|language=en}}</ref>冷凍乾燥會先冷凍食物，再將周圍壓力降低，使得食物中凍結的冰直接[[昇華|昇華]]，由固態變為氣態。<ref name=":0">{{Cite book|url=https://www.worldcat.org/oclc/960758611|title=Food processing technology : principles and practice|last=Fellows|first=P. (Peter),|date=2017|publisher=Woodhead Publishing/Elsevier Science|year=|isbn=0081005237|edition=4th|location=Kent|pages=929–940|chapter=Freeze drying and freeze concentration|oclc=960758611}}</ref>
