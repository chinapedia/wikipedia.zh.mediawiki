'''鹰门'''（{{lang-en|Eagle Gate}}），[[盐湖城|盐湖城]]独特的地标<ref> {{cite book |title= Salt Lake City: 1890-1930 |last1= Topping |first1= Gary |last2= Ferguson |first2= Melissa Coy |year= 2009 |publisher= Arcadia Publishing |location= |isbn= 0738570745 |oclc= |page= 16 |url= http://books.google.es/books?id=EM-KrVqv9joC |accessdate= 2009-09-16 }}</ref>位于州街和南圣殿街的十字路口，毗邻[[圣殿广场|圣殿广场]]<ref> {{ cite book |title= The Desert States |first= Michael Schelling |last= Durham |authorlink= |year= 1990 |publisher= Stewart, Tabori & Chang |location= |isbn= 1556701098 |oclc= |page= 365 |url= http://books.google.es/books?id=QZ95AAAAMAAJ |accessdate= 2009-09-16 }}</ref>。它建于1859年，纪念[[杨百翰|杨百翰]]在City Creek Canyon口的产业的入口。<ref>{{cite web |title= Brigham Young Monument and Eagle Gate |url= http://www.lds.org/placestovisit/location/0,10634,1783-1-1-1,00.html |date=  |work= Places to visit |publisher= LDS Church |accessdate= 16 September 2009 |deadurl= yes |archiveurl= https://archive.is/20020202185930/http://www.lds.org/placestovisit/location/0,10634,1783-1-1-1,00.html |archivedate= 2002年2月2日 }}</ref> 原来顶部是一只木鹰，曾更改过数次，现为4,000磅的[[铜|铜]]鹰，翼展20英尺。由拉尔夫·拉姆齐雕刻<ref>{{cite book |title= Church Chronology: A Record of Important Events Pertaining to the History of |last= Jenson |first= Andrew |authorlink= |year= 2004 |publisher= Kessinger Publishing |location= |isbn= 1417968540 |oclc= |page= 16 |url= http://books.google.es/books?id=XEpUpEqDCfgC |accessdate= 2009-09-16 }}</ref> 原来的木鹰在北主街300号犹他先驱之女博物馆展出。
