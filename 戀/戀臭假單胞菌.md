'''戀臭假單孢菌'''（'''''Pseudomonas putida'''''）是一種[[腐生营养|腐生营养]][[土壤|土壤]][[桿菌|桿菌]]，屬於[[格蘭氏陰性菌|格蘭氏陰性菌]]。基于16S [[核糖體RNA|rRNA基因]]分析，恋臭假单孢菌被分类学上证实为假单胞菌属（狭义），并连同其他几个品种放置在恋臭假单孢菌群中，向其中借其名称。<ref>{{cite journal |author=Anzai, ''et al.'' |title=Phylogenetic affiliation of the pseudomonads based on 16S rRNA sequence |journal=Int J Syst Evol Microbiol |volume=50 | issue = 4 |pages=1563–89 |date=Jul 2000 |pmid=10939664 |last2=Kim |first2=H |last3=Park |first3=JY |last4=Wakabayashi |first4=H |last5=Oyaizu |first5=H |doi=10.1099/00207713-50-4-1563}}</ref>

他是世界上第一個被賦予專利的生物體。這個賦予活體專利的行為造成了爭議，後來[[美國聯邦最高法院|美國聯邦最高法院]]判決發明者{{le|Ananda Mohan Chakrabarty|Ananda Mohan Chakrabarty}}勝訴，這個史無前例的判決也成了美國的判例（《{{le|Diamond v. Chakrabarty|Diamond v. Chakrabarty}}》）

戀臭假單孢菌有許多代謝途徑，可以分解許多有機分子，包括降解[[甲苯|甲苯]]等有機分子<ref>{{Cite journal|doi=10.1111/j.1365-2958.1993.tb01222.x|pmid=7934920|title=Transcriptional control of the Pseudomonas putida TOL plasmid catabolic pathways|year=1993|last1=Marqués|first1=Silvia|last2=Ramos|first2=Juan L.|journal=Molecular Microbiology|volume=9|issue=5|pages=923–9}}</ref>。牠被廣泛應用於{{le|生物修復技術|bioremediation}}，或是用於微生物分解漏油等等。除了因為它強大的生物分解能力之外，比起其他{{le|假單孢菌屬|Pseudomonas}}的種類它更安全無害，而不像[[綠膿桿菌|綠膿桿菌]]（''P. aeruginosa''）那樣是一個具有機會性的人類病原體。


==應用==

===生物修復===
戀臭假單孢菌多條代謝途徑的特性，讓它成為開發[[生物修復|生物修復]]技術的寵兒；舉例來說，在被[[萘|萘]]污染的土地上，戀臭假單孢菌可被用作{{le|土壤接種物|bioremediation}}來療癒土地<ref>{{Cite journal|pmid=16329969|year=2005|last1=Gomes|first1=NC|last2=Kosheleva|first2=IA|last3=Abraham|first3=WR|last4=Smalla|first4=K|title=Effects of the inoculant strain Pseudomonas putida KT2442 (pNF142) and of naphthalene contamination on the soil bacterial community|volume=54|issue=1|pages=21–33|doi=10.1016/j.femsec.2005.02.005|journal=FEMS microbiology ecology}}</ref>。

戀臭假單孢菌還可以將[[苯乙烯|苯乙烯]]轉化成[[生物可分解塑膠|生物可分解塑膠]][[Polyhydroxyalkanoates|PHA]]<ref>[http://www.livescience.com/technology/060307_styrofoam_cup.html Immortal Polystyrene Foam Meets its Enemy | LiveScience<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|pmid=  16649270|year=  2006|last1=  Ward|first1=  PG|last2=  Goff|first2=  M|last3=  Donner|first3=  M|last4=  Kaminsky|first4=  W|last5=  O'Connor|first5=  KE|title=  A two step chemo-biotechnological conversion of polystyrene to a biodegradable thermoplastic|volume=  40|issue=  7|pages=  2433–7|journal=  Environmental science & technology|doi=  10.1021/es0517668}}</ref>。這被認為是回收[[保麗龍|保麗龍]]（發泡[[聚苯乙烯|聚苯乙烯]]）的有效方法，因為保麗龍幾乎完全無法被生物分解。

===Oligonucleotide Usage Signatures of the ''Pseudomonas putida'' KT2440 Genome===
Di- to [[pentanucleotide|pentanucleotide]] usage and the list of the most abundant octa- to tetradecanucleotides are useful measures of the bacterial [[genomic|genomic]] signature. The ''Pseudomonas putida'' KT2440 chromosome is characterized by strand symmetry and intra-strand parity of complementary oligonucleotides. Each tetranucleotide occurs with similar frequency on the two strands. Tetranucleotide usage is biased by G+C content and physicochemical constraints such as base stacking energy, dinucleotide propeller twist angle or trinucleotide bendability. The 105 regions with atypical [[oligonucleotide|oligonucleotide]] composition can be differentiated by their patterns of oligonucleotide usage into categories of horizontally acquired gene islands, multidomain genes or ancient regions such as genes for ribosomal proteins and RNAs. A species-specific extragenic [[palindromic_sequence|palindromic sequence]] is the most common repeat in the genome that can be exploited for the typing of ''P. putida'' strains. In the coding sequence of ''P. putida'' LLL is the most abundant tripeptide.<ref name=Cornelis>{{cite book | author = Cornelis P (editor). | title = Pseudomonas: Genomics and Molecular Biology | edition = 1st | publisher = Caister Academic Press | year = 2008 | url=http://www.horizonpress.com/pseudo | isbn = 1-904455-19-0}}</ref>
