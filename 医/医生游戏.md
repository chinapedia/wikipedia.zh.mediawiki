'''医生游戏'''或'''看醫生遊戲'''是用来描述孩子们互相检查彼此的[[生殖器|生殖器]]官的一个[[词组|词组]]。<ref>{{Cite web|url = http://life.familyeducation.com/sexuality/toddler/53839.html|title = Is Your Preschooler Playing Doctor?|accessdate = 4 September 2009|work = FamilyEducation}}</ref> 它起源于孩子们之间假扮[[医生|医生]]或病人来进行检查的游戏。然而，无论它是否涉及[[性角色扮演|角色扮演]]，这种说法一直被用来表示任何类似的这种“检查”。<div><ref>{{Cite web|first = Lynn Blinn|last = Pike|title = Sexuality and Your Child: For Children Ages 3 to 7|url = http://extension.missouri.edu/xplor/hesguide/humanrel/gh6002.htm|publisher = University of Missouri Extension|date = January 2001|accessdate = 4 September 2009}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|first = Kevin|last = Kennedy|title = Sexual abuse? or just playing 'Doctor'?|url = http://www.medhelp.org/forums/ChildBehavior/messages/33006.html|work = MedHelp|publisher = Self-published|date = 27 October 2004|accessdate = 4 September 2009}}</ref><ref name="msnbc">{{Cite web|first = Victoria|last = Clayton|title = Playing doctor: How to teach kids about inappropriate touch|url = http://www.msnbc.msn.com/id/5444897/|work = Growing Up Healthy|publisher = msnbc.com|date = 6 August 2004|accessdate = 4 September 2009}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|first = Marilyn|last = Heins|title = Sex Play: parenting strategies|url = http://www.parentkidsright.com/pt-sexplay.html|archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20080214/http://www.parentkidsright.com/pt-sexplay.html|work = ParentKidsRight|year = 2004|archivedate = 2008年2月14日|accessdate = 4 September 2009|deadurl = yes}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title = Why Your Child Plays Doctor|url = http://www.fisher-price.com/us/parentingsolutions/article_view.asp?articleid=36591|work = Advice from Experts|publisher = Fisher-Price|year = 2005|accessdate = 4 September 2009|deadurl = yes|archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20050405172835/http://www.fisher-price.com/us/parentingsolutions/article_view.asp?articleid=36591|archivedate = 2005年4月5日|df = }}</ref></div>

医生游戏区别与[[兒童性虐待|兒童性虐待]]，因为后者是冲着故意的[[性刺激|性刺激]]而进行的，包括[[性高潮|性高潮]]。前者则是因为孩子们对身体[[解剖|解剖]]的好奇。<ref name="loseke">{{Cite book|last = Loseke|first = Donileen R.|author2 = Gelles, Richard J.|author3 = Cavanaugh, Mary M.|title = Current Controversies on Family Violence|publisher = Sage Publications Inc|year = 2005|location = Thousand Oaks, CA|isbn = 0-7619-2106-0|page = {{page needed|time=May 2014}}}}</ref> 儿童心理学家认为，在儿童3岁到6岁之间，医生游戏是一个正常的阶段，只要儿童双方都主动愿意参与，并且年龄相近。 然而，当家长发现他们的孩子在做这种事，会感到有些不适。<ref>{{Cite web|url = http://www.canadianparents.com/article/i-caught-them-playing-doctor|title = I Caught Them Playing Doctor!|accessdate = 4 September 2009|work = FamilyEducation|deadurl = yes|archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20090116190323/http://www.canadianparents.com/article/i-caught-them-playing-doctor|archivedate = 2009年1月16日}}</ref>育儿专家经常建议家长们，把这种发现当作一个很好的机会去冷静地教孩子关于性、个人隐私以及尊重他人隐私的事情。 <ref name="msnbc"/>
