'''阔沙蚕属'''（學名：''Platynereis''）是[[環節動物門|環節動物門]][[多毛綱|多毛綱]][[葉鬚蟲目|葉鬚蟲目]][[沙蠶科|沙蠶科]]之下的一個屬<ref>{{Cite journal| last2 = Tessmar-Raible| last7 = Ferrier| last4 = Wincker| last1 = Raible | first1 = F. | first2 = K.| first3 = K. | first4 = P.| last3 = Osoegawa| last6 = Balavoine | first5 = C.| first6 = G.| last5 = Jubin | first7 = D. | first8 = V. | first9 = P.| last10 = Weissenbach | first10 = J.| last11 = Bork | first11 = P.| last12 = Arendt | first12 = D. | title = Vertebrate-type intron-rich genes in the marine annelid ''Platynereis dumerilii '' | journal = Science| volume = 310 | issue = 5752 | pages = 1325–1326 | date=2005-11 | last8 = Benes | issn = 0036-8075 | pmid = 16311335| last9 = De Jong | doi = 10.1126/science.1119089|bibcode = 2005Sci...310.1325R }}</ref>。

[[杜氏阔沙蚕|杜氏阔沙蚕]]（''[[Platynereis_dumerilii|Platynereis dumerilii]]'' <small>(Audouin et Milne Edwards, 1834)</small>）這個物種在[[發育生物學|發育生物學]]被用於研究[[胚胎發育|胚胎發育]]（{{lang|en|embryogenesis}}），因為牠們的胚胎基本上都是透明的，所以要繼續研究也很方便<ref name=Fischer2010 >{{Cite journal | last1 = Fischer | first1 = A. |title=Genetic and Genomic Tools for the Marine Annelid Platynereis dumerilii |first1=Juliane|last1=Zantke |first2=Stephanie|last2=Bannister |first3=Vinoth Babu Veedin |last3=Rajan |first4=Florian |last4=Raible |first5=Kristin |last5=Tessmar-Raible }}</ref>發現本屬物種的遺傳基因組與[[脊椎動物|脊椎動物]]的脊索的遺傳基因的差異，居然比起同屬[[原口动物|原口动物]]的其他各物種之間的差異更少<ref>{{cite news |url=http://orientaldaily.on.cc/cnt/china_world/20140917/00180_045.html |title=沙蠶基因演變人類脊椎 |publisher=[[東方日報_(香港)|東方日報 (香港)]]|date=2014-09-17 |accessdate=2016-03-20 |language=zh-hant }}</ref><ref name=Zantke2014 />。
