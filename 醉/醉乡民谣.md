《'''醉乡民谣'''》（{{lang-en|'''''Inside Llewyn Davis'''''}}）是一部2013年美国音乐题材剧情片，[[科恩兄弟|科恩兄弟]]编剧执导，[[奥斯卡·伊萨克|奥斯卡·伊萨克]]、[[凱莉·穆里根|凱莉·墨里根]]與[[賈斯汀·提姆布萊克|賈斯汀·提姆布萊克]]主演。故事改编自美国民谣歌手[[Dave_Van_Ronk|Dave Van Ronk]]（1936-2002年）逝世后2005年出版的回忆录 ''The Mayor of MacDougal Street''<ref>{{cite news |author=Russ Fischer |url=http://www.slashfilm.com/coen-bros-film-based-folk-musician-dave-van-ronk/#more-106431 |title=The Coen Bros. New Script is Based on the 60′s NYC Folk Scene |work=[[醉乡民谣/Film|醉乡民谣//Film]] |date=2011-06-25 |accessdate=2012-02-11}}</ref>。获得[[第66屆坎城影展|第66屆坎城影展]][[评审团大奖_(戛纳电影节)|评委团大奖]]，美国于2013年12月6日有限上映<ref name="Cannes2013">{{cite web|url=http://www.festival-cannes.fr/en/article/59652.html |title=2013 Official Selection|date=2013-04-18|accessdate=2013-04-18|work=Cannes}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.indiewire.com/article/heres-the-screening-schedule-for-the-2013-cannes-competition-lineup|title=Here's The Screening Schedule For The 2013 Cannes Competition Lineup|work=The Playlist|publisher=[[IndieWire|IndieWire]]|date=2013-05-09|accessdate=2013-05-09|last=Knegt|first=Peter}}</ref><ref name="NYT Caanes">{{cite news|last=Dargis|first=Manhola|title=Singing a Happier Tune in Cannes|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2013/05/20/movies/coen-brothers-inside-llewyn-davis-is-popular-at-cannes.htm|accessdate=2013-05-20|newspaper=New York Times|date=2013-05-19}}</ref>，12月20日正式上映<ref>{{cite web | url=http://variety.com/2013/film/news/coen-brothers-inside-llewyn-davis-release-date-1200448664/ | title=Coen Brothers’ ‘Inside Llewyn Davis’ Dated For Dec. 6 | work=[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]] | publisher=[[Reed_Business_Information|Reed Business Information]] | date=2013-05-03 | accessdate=2013-05-07 | last=McNary | first=Dave}}</ref>。臺灣於2014年2月14日上映。
