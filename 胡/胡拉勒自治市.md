'''胡拉勒自治市'''是[[澳大利亞|澳大利亞]][[雪梨|雪梨]]大都會內東部的一個[[地方政府區域|地方政府]]。

== 沿革 ==
<!--The traditional Aboriginal owners of Woollahra were the Cadigal and Birrabirragal clans of the Dharug people<ref name="wooll">A Brief History of Woollahra, Woollahra Municipal Council, http://www.woollahra.nsw.gov.au/library/local_history  retrieved 2008-04-02</ref>.-->1788年，歐洲移民遷進[[雪梨|雪梨]]以後，有一支探勘隊前往胡拉勒的南部勘察，<ref>A Narrative of the Expedition to Botany Bay by Watkin Tench, 1788 p55</ref>其中或許有原住民擔任嚮導。這一條勘察路線，後來開闢為老南頭路（Old South Head Road）。1790年，當地建立起一座[[驛站|驛站]]。<ref name="wooll">A Brief History of Woollahra, Woollahra Municipal Council, http://www.woollahra.nsw.gov.au/library/local_history  retrieved 2008-04-02</ref>胡拉勒爾後於1860年成為自治市；1968年轄境擴大。<ref name="wooll">A Brief History of Woollahra, Woollahra Municipal Council, http://www.woollahra.nsw.gov.au/library/local_history  retrieved 2008-04-02</ref>自1895年至1948年間，地方政府所轄的一部份政區獲獨立升格為「沃克呂茲自治市」（Vaucluse Municipality）。<ref name="wooll">A Brief History of Woollahra, Woollahra Municipal Council, http://www.woollahra.nsw.gov.au/library/local_history  retrieved 2008-04-02</ref>雪梨地區有相當多數的猶太裔人聚居於此，他們早期分別是來自於俄羅斯、南非、以色列、德國、波蘭和匈牙利的[[猶太人|猶太人]]。2010年，當地有位老人－Don Ritchie因50年來駐守於當地懸崖邊，成功勸導160位人士打消輕生的念頭而獲得胡拉勒市府表揚。<ref>[http://www.nzherald.co.nz/world/news/article.cfm?c_id=2&objectid=10651629 Australian 'angel' saves lives at suicide spot]{{dead link|date=2018年4月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>

== 議會 ==
胡拉勒自治市議會有議席15座，全市分5選區，各區選出議員3名代表市民和納稅人問政；市長非直選。<ref name=EC>{{cite web|url = http://www.lg.elections.nsw.gov.au/LGE2008/result.Woollahra.html|title = Woollahra Municipal Council|work = 2008 Election results| publisher = Electoral Commission NSW| accessdate = 2009-08-03}}</ref>
