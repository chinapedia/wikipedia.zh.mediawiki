《'''野性花园：食虫植物栽培'''》（'''''The Savage Garden: Cultivating Carnivorous Plants'''''）是由[[彼得·达马托|彼得·达马托]]创作的[[食虫植物|食虫植物]]栽培指南。其出版于1998年<ref name=DAmato>D'Amato, P. 1998. ''The Savage Garden: Cultivating Carnivorous Plants''. Ten Speed Press, Berkeley.</ref><ref>[http://www.worldcat.org/title/savage-garden-cultivating-carnivorous-plants/oclc/473718060 The savage garden : cultivating carnivorous plants]. WorldCat.</ref>，再版于2004年<ref>[http://www.worldcat.org/title/savage-garden-cultivating-carnivorous-plants/oclc/473718060/editions?referer=di&editionsView=true Formats and editions of The savage garden : cultivating carnivorous plants]. WorldCat.</ref>。2013年将出版一个修订版。<ref>[http://www.necps.org/icps2012/speakers.html ICPS 2012 Speakers List: The 9th Annual International Carnivorous Plant Society Meeting and Show] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120815230359/http://www.necps.org/icps2012/speakers.html |date=2012-08-15 }}. New England Carnivorous Plant Society.</ref> 

1999年，该书荣获[[美国园艺协会|美国园艺协会]]年度图书奖<ref name=AHS>[http://www.ahs.org/awards/book_awards/1999_book_award_winners.htm 1999 AHS Book Awards]. American Horticultural Society.</ref>和美国花园作家协会的毛笔·铲子奖。其被称为“食虫植物种植者的圣经”<ref>Van Cleef, L. 2002. [http://www.sfgate.com/cgi-bin/article.cgi?f=/g/a/2002/02/06/greeng.DTL Animal-Eating Plants: Carnivorous-plant growing tips from a local expert]. == 同行评议 ==
[[巴里·赖斯|巴里·赖斯]]在1998年9月的《[[食虫植物通讯|食虫植物通讯]]》中提及了这本书：{{#tag:ref|1998年9月《[[食虫植物通讯|食虫植物通讯]]》中[[巴里·赖斯|巴里·赖斯]]的原文：<ref name=Rice />{{Cquote|There are already several full-sized books on growing carnivorous plants. Granted, Slack's works are very hard to find, but Cheers wrote a fine volume. Do we really need another? In answer, I tell you to run, not walk, to the nearest bookseller and buy D'Amato's new book, The Savage Garden.}}|name=Rice1|group="注"}}<ref name=Rice>Meyers-Rice, B. 1998. Book review. ''[[食虫植物通讯|Carnivorous Plant Newsletter]]'' '''27'''(3): 72–73.</ref>

{{Cquote|已经有几本关于食虫植物种植的书籍。诚然，[[阿德里安·斯莱克|斯莱克]]的作品很难找到，但[[戈登·切尔斯|切尔斯]]写了一本非常好的作品。我们还需要另一本吗？现在我告诉你要跑而不是走，到最近书店里买彼得·达马托的新书——《野性花园》。
}}

巴里·赖斯对这本书的栽培信息高度称赞，但他也发现了书中的一些错误：{{#tag:ref|巴里·赖斯指出错误的原文：<ref name=Rice />{{Cquote|The Savage Garden does have flaws. Some are minor but oddly consistent spelling errors (e.g. "''N. bicalcurata''," "''U. reinformis''," "''U. humboltii''," "thripes," and others). More significant is D'Amato's confusing use of a flurry of unpublished cultivar names. It is hoped these are mended in future editions.}}|name=Rice2|group="注"}}<ref name=Rice />

{{Cquote|《野性花园》的确有些瑕疵。部分是些拼写错误（如：“''N. bicalcurata''”、“''U. reinformis''”、“''U. humboltii''”、“thripes”等）。最主要的是，彼得·达马托混淆使用了未发表的栽培种名称。希望这些错误能在未来的修订版中更正。
}}

巴里·赖斯总结说：“《野性花园》是一部详实，准确，有趣且只要19.95美元的便宜货。”<ref name=Rice />

美国园艺协会的评论员为《野性花园》写道：“彼得·达马托的作品是权威，激情和幽默的最佳结合。”<ref name=AHS />《[[洛杉矶时报|洛杉矶时报]]》将其称为“详尽无遗的作品”<ref>Smaus, R. 1998. [http://articles.latimes.com/1998/sep/06/realestate/re-19970 Stop Breaking Ground, Brush Up on Books]. ''Los Angeles Times'', September 06, 1998.</ref>《[[里契蒙每日快报|里契蒙每日快报]]》的杰里·威廉也积极评价了这本书。<ref>Williams, J. 1998. Book reviews. ''Richmond Times-Dispatch'', August 23, 1998.</ref>
