'''世界三大瀑布'''指[[巴西|巴西]]、[[阿根廷|阿根廷]]交界的[[伊瓜苏瀑布|伊瓜苏瀑布]]（世界上最宽）、东非[[赞比亚|赞比亚]]和[[津巴布韦|津巴布韦]]交界的[[维多利亚瀑布|维多利亚瀑布]]以及美国加拿大交界的[[尼亚加拉瀑布|尼亚加拉瀑布]]。这三个瀑布无论从宽度、面积、流量都远超其他瀑布，并且都是跨国瀑布。世界排名第四的跨国瀑布是[[中国|中国]]与[[越南|越南]]交界的[[德天瀑布|德天瀑布]]。

<br>2.^ a b c d e [http://www.dams.org/docs/kbase/studies/cszzanx.pdf|World Commission on Dams website]{{dead link|date=2017年11月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}: "Case Study — Kariba Dam-Zambezi River Basin" Annex 13 & 14 Victoria Falls Mean Monthly Flows. 網站存取自二零零七年三月一日。此網站提供了每立方米/秒為單位的月平均流量(i.e., the total volume of water passing in each calendar month divided by the number of seconds in the month), 指水文學裡衝量的標準，用作指示流量的季節變化。 A figure of around 9,000 m³/s (318,000 cu ft) is quoted by many websites for Victoria Falls but this is the mean maximum instantaneous rate, which is only achieved for a little amount of days per year. The figure of 536 million m³/minute (18.9 billion cu ft/min) on some websites (eg ZNTB) is an error for 536 million litres/minute (equivalent to 9100 m³/s or 142 million U.S. gallons/min). "十年最高" 是指十年內各個月份最高流量值的平均數。
