'''帕偉鄂本'''(Pawel L. Urban)，國際聞名化學家，專長研究領域為質譜學及生化分析。目前是[[國立清華大學|國立清華大學]]化學系[[分析化學|分析化學]]領域的教授。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://pubs.acs.org/doi/pdf/10.1021/acssynbio.6b00238|title="Introducing Our Authors" entry from ACS Synthetic Biology journal (2016)}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chem.nthu.edu.tw/files/13-1078-122085.php?Lang=zh-tw|title=國立清華大學化學系}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.urbanlab.tw|title=Urban Lab 網站}}</ref> 他畢業於[[約克大學_(英國)|約克大學 (英國)]]。他的研究興趣包括[[質譜法|質譜法]]和生化分析。

== 學術活動 ==
帕偉鄂本開發了多項分析化學方法:如水凝膠微量採樣法、<ref>{{cite web|url=http://pubs.acs.org/doi/abs/10.1021/ac4039338|title=Dutkiewicz E.P., Lin J.-D., Tseng T.-W., Wang Y.-S., Urban P.L. 2014, Hydrogel micropatches for sampling and profiling skin metabolites. Analytical Chemistry 86:2337-2344}}</ref>氣泡萃取法、<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Lim|first1=XiaoZhi|title=Fizzy extraction brings out semivolatile compounds|journal=Chemical & Engineering News|date=September 2, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last1=Chang|first1=Cheng-Hao|last2=Urban|first2=Pawel L|title=Fizzy Extraction of Volatile and Semivolatile Compounds into the Gas Phase|journal=Analytical Chemistry|doi=10.1021/acs.analchem.6b02074}}</ref>化學反應成像系統，<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.chemistryworld.com/research/tracking-complex-reactions-in-space-and-time/7607.article|title=Helen Bache, "Tracking complex reactions in space and time", highlight in Chemistry World magazine}}</ref>和質譜微陣列<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Urban|first1=Pawel L|last2=Jefimovs|first2=Konstantins|last3=Amantonico|first3=Andrea|last4=Fagerer|first4=Stephan R.|last5=Schmid|first5=Thomas|last6=Madler|first6=Stefanie|last7=Puigmarti-Luis|first7=Josep|last8=Goedecke|first8=Nils|last9=Zenobi|first9=Renato|title=High-density micro-arrays for mass spectrometry|journal=Lab on a Chip|date=2010|volume=10|pages=3206–3209|doi=10.1039/c0lc00211a}}</ref>等分析方法。他已經發表了許多研究論文<ref>{{cite web|url=http://pubs.rsc.org/en/content/articlelanding/2014/an/c4an02013h#!divAbstract|title=Urban P.L. 2015, Universal electronics for miniature and automated chemical assays. Analyst 140:963-975}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nature.com/nature/journal/v532/n7599/full/532313d.html|title=Urban P. 2016, Self-built labware stimulates creativity. Nature 532:313}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://orcid.org/0000-0003-3471-045X|title=ORCID}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/?term=Urban%20PL%5BAuthor%5D&cauthor=true&cauthor_uid=25633338|title=PubMed}}</ref>並且也出版了兩本專書，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://lubimyczytac.pl/ksiazka/208763/promienie-niezgody|title=Promienie niezgody. (波蘭文寫的)}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://as.wiley.com/WileyCDA/WileyTitle/productCd-1118887328.html|title=Time-Resolved Mass Spectrometry: From Concept to Applications}}</ref>其中一本是有關於以時間解析質譜為主題的書。他目前是科學報告''Scientific Reports''、<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nature.com/srep/about/editorial-board|title=Scientific Reports - 編輯委員會}}</ref>硬件''HardwareX''、<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.journals.elsevier.com/hardwarex/editorial-board|title=HardwareX - 編輯委員會}}</ref>''Heliyon''<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.heliyon.com/editorial-team/board/applied-sciences|title=Heliyon - 編輯委員會}}</ref>及''PeerJ''<ref>{{cite web|url=https://peerj.com/PLUrban/|title=PeerJ - 編輯委員會}}</ref>的編輯委員會成員，並曾擔任皇家學會所出版的期刊中一期的客座編輯''Philosophical Transactions of the Royal Society A''。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://rsta.royalsocietypublishing.org/content/374/2079?current-issue=y|title=Philosophical Transactions of the Royal Society A - theme issue on Quantitative Mass Spectrometry}}</ref>
