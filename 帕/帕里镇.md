'''帕里镇'''（{{bo|z=ཕག་རི|w=phag ri}}，[[藏语|藏语]]意为“群山旋转的地方”<ref>{{cite web | language = zh-cn | publisher = 凤凰网 | title =海拔4500米的无名高地哨所：来当兵不是来挣钱的| url = http://news.ifeng.com/mil/2/detail_2011_10/20/10006811_0.shtml| date = 2011-10-20 | accessdate = 2011-11-16 }}</ref>）位于[[中国|中国]][[西藏自治区|西藏自治区]][[亚东县|亚东县]]东部，海拔4370米，是中国海拔最高的城镇。地处边境地区，东距[[不丹|不丹]]边境线只有60公里<ref>{{cite web | language = zh-cn | publisher = 西藏新闻网 | title = 亚东县帕里镇村民户户用上自来水 | url = http://info.tibet.cn/news/xzxw/shjj/t20070104_195815.htm | date = 2007-01-04 | accessdate = 2011-11-16 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160304115827/http://info.tibet.cn/news/xzxw/shjj/t20070104_195815.htm | archivedate = 2016-03-04 }}</ref>。帕里曾是西藏的商贸重镇，20世纪初，帕里成为西藏的一个“[[宗_(西藏)|宗]]”。 1964年初亚东口岸关闭后，商贸活动开始衰落<ref>{{cite web | language = zh-cn | publisher = 新华网 | title = 帕里：商贸重镇上的历史烟云 | url = http://tibet.news.cn/gdbb/2009-12/02/content_18389042.htm | date = 2009-12-02 | accessdate = 2011-11-16 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20110926062531/http://tibet.news.cn/gdbb/2009-12/02/content_18389042.htm | archivedate = 2011-09-26 }}</ref>。
