'''帕维尔·艾利亚诺夫'''（{{lang-uk|Павло Володимирович Ельянов}}，{{bd|1983年|5月10日}}），[[乌克兰|乌克兰]][[国际象棋|国际象棋]]棋手、[[国际象棋特级大师|特级大师]]。

艾利亚诺夫在[[国际象棋奥林匹克|国际象棋奥林匹克]]比赛中两次获得团体金牌，一次获得个人银牌。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.olimpbase.org/players/3xppjdje.html|title=Men's Chess Olympiads: Pavel Eljanov|last=Bartelski|first=Wojciech|publisher=OlimpBase|accessdate=1 January 2012}}</ref>此外，他还曾是[[鲍里斯·格尔凡德|鲍里斯·格尔凡德]]与世界冠军[[马格努斯·卡尔森|马格努斯·卡尔森]]的助手。<ref>{{cite web|title=Boris Gelfand: 'There are not enough places for everyone'|url=http://en.chessbase.com/post/boris-gelfand-there-are-not-enough-places-for-everyone-|publisher=ChessBase|date=31 October 2007|accessdate=24 September 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Eljanov Pavel|url=http://www.chessnc.com/biography/person-555.html|website=Chess Network Company|accessdate=24 September 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Doggers|first1=Peter|title=Boris Gelfand: "I was by no means inferior in this match" (interview, part 1 of 2)|url=http://www.chessvibes.com/?q=reports/boris-gelfand-i-was-by-no-means-inferior-in-this-match-interview-part-1-of-2|publisher=ChessVibes|date=6 June 2012|accessdate=24 September 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Crowther|first=Mark|title=World Chess Championship closing ceremony: Putin speaks and Carlsen receives his prizes|url=http://www.theweekinchess.com/chessnews/events/world-chess-championship-2014/world-chess-championship-closing-ceremony-putin-speaks-and-carlsen-receives-his-prizes|publisher=The Week in Chess|accessdate=24 November 2015|date=2014-11-25}}</ref>
