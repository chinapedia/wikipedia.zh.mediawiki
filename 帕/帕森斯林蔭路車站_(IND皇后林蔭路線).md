'''帕森斯林蔭路車站'''（{{lang-en|Parsons Boulevard}}）是[[紐約地鐵|紐約地鐵]][[IND皇后林蔭路線|IND皇后林蔭路線]]的快車[[地鐵站|地鐵站]]，位於[[皇后區|皇后區]]{{link-en|帕森斯林蔭路|Parsons Boulevard}}及山邊大道<ref name=MTAJamaicaMap-2015>{{cite web|title=MTA Neighborhood Maps: Jamaica|url=http://web.mta.info/maps/neighborhoods/qns/Q10_Jamaica_2015.pdf|website=[[大都會運輸署|mta.info]]|publisher=[[大都會運輸署|Metropolitan Transportation Authority]]|accessdate=2015-06-30|date=2015}}</ref>，設有[[紐約地鐵F線|F線]]（任何時候停站）、[[紐約地鐵E線|E線]]（僅繁忙時段停站）列車服務。E線同時停靠因為其主要總站[[牙買加中心-帕森斯/射手車站_(射手大道線)|牙買加中心-帕森斯/射手]]車站容量受限，位於此站以南四個街區。

== 歷史 ==
此地下車站在1937年4月24日開放，是獨立地鐵系統皇后林蔭路線自[[克猶花園-聯合公路車站_(IND皇后林蔭路線)|克猶花園-聯合公路]]延長至[[169街車站_(IND皇后林蔭路線)|169街]]<ref name="WheelsDroveNY-2012">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=qfZ0VxuLoc0C&pg=PA416|title=The Wheels That Drove New York: A History of the New York City Transit System|author1=Roger P. Roess|author2=Gene Sansone|date=2012-08-23|publisher=Springer Science & Business Media|isbn=978-3-642-30484-2|pages=416–417}}</ref><ref name=":03">{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=w8cDPQAACAAJ|title=Building the Independent Subway|last=Kramer|first=Frederick A.|date=1990|publisher=Quadrant Press|year=|isbn=978-0-915276-50-9|location=|pages=|language=en}}</ref><ref name="NYTImes-QBL169Ext-19372">* {{cite web|url=https://query.nytimes.com/mem/archive/pdf?res=9504E0DA1030EE32A25754C1A9659C946694D6CF|title=Subway Link Opens Soon: City Line to Jamaica Will Start About April 24|date=1937-03-17|website=[[紐約時報|nytimes.com]]|publisher=[[紐約時報|The New York Times]]|accessdate=2015-06-27}}
* {{cite web|url=https://query.nytimes.com/mem/archive/pdf?res=9505E7DE133AE23ABC4153DFB266838C629EDE|title=Trial Run to Jamaica on Subway Tomorrow: Section From Kew Gardens to 169th Street Will Open to Public in Two Weeks|date=1937-04-09|website=[[紐約時報|nytimes.com]]|publisher=[[紐約時報|The New York Times]]|accessdate=2015-06-30}}</ref>。當169街車站仍為路線總站時，F線列車已經以此為總站，因為該站沒有足夠的儲車設施。因此路線計劃延長至184廣場，並在[[牙買加-179街車站_(IND皇后林蔭路線)|179街]]車站設立兩個島式月台、足夠的出入口和四列十節列車的儲車軌。設施容許快車和慢車列車服務此站<ref name=":0">{{Cite book|url=http://hdl.handle.net/2027/mdp.39015020928621|title=Report including analysis of operations of the New York City transit system for five years, ended June 30, 1945|last=|first=|publisher=Board of Transportation of the City of New York|year=1945|isbn=|location=New York City|pages=}}</ref>。延長線後來在1950年12月11日完工，受{{link-en|美國大蕭條|Great Depression in the United States|大蕭條}}和[[第二次世界大戰|第二次世界大戰]]影響延誤。E線和F線列車都延長到新車站<ref>{{cite web|url=https://query.nytimes.com/mem/archive/pdf?res=9E02E0DE1438E73ABC4E53DFB467838B649EDE|title=Subway Link Opens Monday|date=1950-12-06|website=[[紐約時報|nytimes.com]]|publisher=[[紐約時報|The New York Times]]|accessdate=2015-06-30}}</ref><ref name="INDServicesChart">{{cite web|url=http://www.thejoekorner.com/lines/Indhistory.htm|title=Independent Subway Services Beginning in 1932|date=2013-08-21|website=thejoekorner.com|accessdate=2015-08-02}}</ref><ref name="NYTImes-179open-19502">* {{cite web|url=https://query.nytimes.com/mem/archive/pdf?res=9E05E0DD133EE532A05751C1A9649D946192D6CF|title=New Subway Link Opening in Queens|date=1950-12-12|website=[[紐約時報|nytimes.com]]|publisher=[[紐約時報|The New York Times]]|accessdate=2015-06-30}}
* {{cite web|url=https://query.nytimes.com/mem/archive/pdf?res=9402E1D61538E53ABC4A53DFBE66838D659EDE|title=Subway Extension In Queens Is Voted|date=1946-08-02|website=[[紐約時報|nytimes.com]]|publisher=[[紐約時報|The New York Times]]|accessdate=2015-06-30}}</ref>。

根據2015－2019年度[[大都會運輸署|MTA]]資金計劃，此站連同其他30座紐約地鐵車站，將進行徹底翻新並關閉達6個月。更新包括蜂窩服務、Wi-Fi、充電站、改善指示牌和車站燈光<ref>{{cite web|url=http://gothamist.com/2016/01/08/subway_facelift_shutdown.php|title=MTA Will Completely Close 30 Subway Stations For Months-Long "Revamp"|publisher=Gothamist|accessdate=2016-07-18|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160801200338/http://gothamist.com/2016/01/08/subway_facelift_shutdown.php|archivedate=2016-08-01}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.governor.ny.gov/sites/governor.ny.gov/files/atoms/files/MTAStations.pdf|title=MTAStations|last=|first=|date=|website=governor.ny.gov|publisher=Government of the State of New York|access-date=2016-07-18}}</ref>。

此地下車站設有兩個[[島式月台|島式月台]]和四條軌道。F線任何時候都停靠外側慢車軌道<ref>{{NYCS const|timetable|f}}</ref>，而不頻密的繁忙時段E線列車則在中央快車軌道停靠<ref>{{NYCS const|timetable|e}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.subwaynut.com/ind/parsonsf/parsonsf44.jpg|title=A limited rush hour service E train stopped on the express track beneath its sign|last=Cox|first=Jeremiah|date=2012-01-03|website=subwaynut.com|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|access-date=2017-08-15}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.subwaynut.com/ind/parsonsf/parsonsf6.jpg|title=A close up of the 'parsons' text on a column at Parsons Blvd.|last=Cox|first=Jeremiah|date=2008-06-02|website=subwaynut.com|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|access-date=2017-08-15}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.subwaynut.com/ind/parsonsf/parsonsf8.jpg|title=The orange wall trim that's three tiles wide and the 1/2 tile black border with Parsons letting one full tile below it along the local track walls at Parsons Blvd.|last=Cox|first=Jeremiah|date=2008-06-02|website=subwaynut.com|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|access-date=2017-08-15}}</ref>。

月台上方設有一個全長夾層，並在兩端連接兩個車站入口。設站在牙買加方向月台比曼哈頓方向月台有更多樓梯，因為曼哈頓方向側設有職員辦公室。

{{clear left}}

=== 出口 ===
{{stack|float=left|[[File:Parsons_IND_SB_jeh.JPG|thumb]]}}
全時間入口位於車站北面，設有轉棍閘機和票務處，並在帕森斯林蔭路和山邊大道西南角或東南角分別設有兩個街梯，並在西北角設有一條樓梯<ref name=MTAJamaicaMap-2015 /> 。

南端入口無職員駐守，設有全高式轉棍閘機和三條街梯，通往153街和山邊大道交界的北、西南和東南角<ref name=MTAJamaicaMap-2015 />。其票務處在2003年拆除。
