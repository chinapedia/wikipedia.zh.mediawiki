《甜蜜的复仇》（Three Cheers for Sweet Revenge）是美国[[另类摇滚|另类摇滚]]乐队[[我的另类罗曼史|我的另类罗曼史]]的第二部[[录音室专辑|录音室专辑]]，发布于2004年6月8日。<ref>{{Template:Cite web|url=http://www.rollingstone.com/music/albumreviews/three-cheers-for-sweet-revenge-20040708|title=Three Cheers for Sweet Revenge|accessdate=7 April 2015|publisher=Rolling Stone|last1=Miller|first1=Kirk|website=[[Rolling_Stone|Rolling Stone]]}}</ref>这是我的另类罗曼史第一个通过Reprise唱片公司发布的专辑<ref name="Contactmusic">{{Template:Cite web|url=http://www.contactmusic.com/my-chemical-romance|title=My Chemical Romance|accessdate=7 April 2015|publisher=Contactmusic.com|quote=My Chemical Romance signed with Reprise in 2003. After a major tour with Avenged Sevenfold, the band started to work on their second album. The album went platinum in less than a year.|website=[[Contactmusic.com|Contactmusic.com]]}}</ref>[他们的第一部专辑《子弹换爱》（[[I_Brought_You_My_Bullets,_You_Brought_Me_Your_Love|I Brought You My Bullets, You Brought Me Your Love]]）通过Eyeball Records唱片公司发布<ref>{{Template:Cite web|url=http://drinkingsouls.com/music/i-brought-you-my-bullets-you-brought-me-your-love/|title=I Brought You My Bullets, You Brought Me Your Love|accessdate=7 April 2015|publisher=Drinkingsouls.com|website=Drinking Souls}}</ref>]。这是该乐队由麦特·派利席尔最后担任鼓手的专辑，之后鼓手换成了巴柏·布莱亚。<ref name="RollingStoneBiographyMCR">{{Template:Cite web|url=http://www.rollingstone.com/music/artists/my-chemical-romance/biography|title=My Chemical Romance Biography|accessdate=12 April 2015|quote=On the strength of tracks like the pummeling 9-11 lament "Skylines and Turnstiles," the band quickly signed with Reprise Records. 2004's Three Cheers for Sweet Revenge (Number 28) went platinum and featured singles "I'm Not Okay (I Promise)" (Number 86, 2004) and "Helena" (Number 33, 2005). Pelissier left the band shortly after the release of Three Cheers and was replaced by Bob Bryar, a sound tech for the Used.|author1=Joel Hoard|website=[[Rolling_Stone|Rolling Stone]]}}</ref>这张专辑在发布不到一年内被认证为白金专辑。这部专辑已经在美国售出超过100万份。

在《甜蜜的复仇》中，我的另类罗曼史表现的声音比他们在上个专辑《子弹换爱》中来的更干净。<ref name="Zemler">{{Template:Cite web|url=http://www.altpress.com/reviews/entry/threecheersforsweetrevenge|title=Three Cheers for Sweet Revenge|accessdate=7 April 2015|date=22 June 2010|last1=Zemler|first1=Emily|website=[[Alternative_Press|Alternative Press]]}}</ref>这个专辑对于乐队和厂牌来说都是一个成功，尽管亦有负面评价。相比较上个专辑《子弹换爱》，《甜蜜的复仇》给我的另类罗曼史带来了更多的粉丝。杰洛德·威称这部专辑为“伪概念的恐怖故事”。

此专辑包含四个已发布的单曲："[[I'm_Not_Okay_(I_Promise)|I'm Not Okay (I Promise)]]"、"[[Helena|Helena]]"、 "[[The_Ghost_of_You|The Ghost of You]]"，和在英国发布的 "[[Thank_You_for_the_Venom|Thank You for the Venom]]"。专辑中的一些非单曲发布的作品也被赞扬是乐队的很好作品，比如"Hang 'Em High" 和 "The Jetset Life is Gonna Kill You"。

乐队主唱[[杰洛德·威|杰洛德·威]]为专辑封面作画。<ref>{{Template:Cite web|url=http://www.mtv.com/artists/my-chemical-romance/biography/|title=About My Chemical Romance|accessdate=12 April 2015|publisher=[[Viacom|Viacom]]|quote=The following year, they released Three Cheers for Sweet Revenge, a platinum-selling album that featured cover art by Way himself.|author1=Andrew Leahey|author2=Alex Henderson|website=[[MTV.com|MTV.com]]}}</ref>
