《'''离巨星二十英尺'''》（''20 Feet from Stardom''）是一部2013年[[摩根·内维尔|摩根·内维尔]]导演、[[Gil_Friesen|Gil Friesen]]监制的美国音乐题材[[纪录片|纪录片]]<ref>{{cite news|last=Caplan|first=B.|title=Twenty Feet From Stardom Director Morgan Neville on His Own 'Cinderella Moment'|url=http://blogs.miaminewtimes.com/cultist/2013/03/director_morgan_neville_on_pos.php|newspaper=Miami New Times|date=March 13, 2013}}</ref>。讲述几位幕后女声Darlene Love, Merry Clayton, Claudia Lennear, Lisa Fischer, Tata Vega和Judith Hill等伴唱女歌手的故事，他们曾为20世纪最著名的流行歌手们当绿叶，并见证了流行音乐史。<ref>{{cite news |last=Van Syckle|first=Katie|title=Backup Singer Documentary 'Twenty Feet From Stardom' Set for Summer Release |url=http://www.rollingstone.com/music/news/backup-singer-documentary-twenty-feet-from-stardom-set-for-summer-release-20130128|newspaper=Rolling Stone|date=January 28, 2013}}</ref>獲選[[第85屆國家評論協會獎|第85屆國家評論協會獎]]五佳紀錄片，並获得[[第86届奥斯卡金像奖|第86届奥斯卡金像奖]]最佳纪录片。

==发行==
2013年1月17日于[[圣丹斯电影节|圣丹斯电影节]]上首映，后被[[韦恩斯坦电影公司|韦恩斯坦电影公司]]买下发行权，并于同年6月14日在美国上映<ref>{{cite web|last=Lee|first=Stephan|title=MOVIE PREVIEW Twenty Feet from Stardom (2013)|url=http://www.ew.com/ew/article/0,,20483133_20693475,00.html|work=Entertainment Weekly|accessdate=April 28, 2013}}</ref>，影片的国际发行方为 Elle Driver / Wild Bunch<ref>{{cite web|last=Fleming Jr.|first=Mike |title=Elle Driver Buys Foreign On 'Twenty Feet From Stardom' |url=http://www.deadline.com/2013/01/elle-driver-buys-foreign-on-twenty-feet-from-stardom/|publisher=Deadline Hollywood |deadurl=no |accessdate=June 20, 2013}}</ref>。此外，影片也参加过许多影展，如South by Southwest影展、True/False影展、Full Frame纪录片影展、夏威夷国际电影节、费城音乐节、RiverRun国际影展、旧金山电影节、西雅图国际电影节、Montclair影展等等<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.metacritic.com/movie/twenty-feet-from-stardom |title=20 Feet from Stardom |publisher=Metacritic |date=June 14, 2013 |accessdate=August 27, 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/review/twenty-feet-stardom-sundance-review-413598 |first=Justin |last=Lowe |title=Twenty Feet From Stardom |work=The Hollywood Reporter |date=January 18, 2013 |accessdate=August 27, 2013}}</ref><ref>{{IMDb title|2396566|20 Feet from Stardom}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=McNary |first=Dave |url=http://variety.com/2013/film/reviews/twenty-feet-from-stardom-1117949005 |first=Peter |last=Debruge |title=Twenty Feet from Stardom |work=Variety |date=January 18, 2013 |accessdate=August 27, 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.google.com/search?client=safari&rls=en&q=twenty+feet+from+stardom+festivals&ie=UTF-8&oe=UTF-8 |publisher=Google |title=Google search |deadurl=no |accessdate=June 20, 2013}}</ref>。

==反响==
影片获得一致的好评，[[Metacritic|Metacritic]]上24家媒体综评83分<ref name="Metacritic">{{cite web |title=20 Feet from Stardom Reviews |url=http://www.metacritic.com/movie/twenty-feet-from-stardom |publisher=[[Metacritic|Metacritic]] |accessdate=December 20, 2013}}</ref>。美国票房483万美元，加上海外成绩累计580万<ref name='BOMJ'/>。
