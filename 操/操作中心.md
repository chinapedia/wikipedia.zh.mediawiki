'''操作中心'''（{{lang-en|Action Center}}）是包含在[[Windows_Phone_8.1|Windows Phone 8.1]]、[[Windows_10|Windows 10]]和[[Windows_10_行動裝置版|Windows 10 Mobile]]中的通知中心。2014年7月在Windows Phone 8.1中被首次引入，2015年7月29日被引入Windows 10桌面版。<ref name="Engadget-Windows-10-Action-Center">{{Template:Cite news|url=http://www.engadget.com/2014/10/21/windows-10-action-center/|title=Windows 10 brings Windows Phone's notification center to the desktop.|last=Fingas|first=Jon|date=21 October 2014|newspaper=[[Engadget|Engadget]]}}</ref>

操作中心在Windows 10中取代了超级按钮（Charms）。<ref>{{Template:Cite web|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/technology/microsoft/windows/11781913/Windows-10-review-a-fresh-Start.html|title=Windows 10 review: a fresh Start for Microsoft|date=4 August 2015|last1=Curtis|first1=Sophie|website=[[The_Daily_Telegraph|The Daily Telegraph]]}}</ref><ref>{{Template:Cite web|url=http://www.computerworld.com/article/2876761/windows-10-deep-dive-review-finally-a-unified-operating-system.html|title=Windows 10 deep-dive review: Finally, a unified operating system|date=29 January 2015|publisher=[[IDG|IDG]]|last1=Gralla|first1=Preston|website=[[Computerworld|Computerworld]]}}</ref><ref>{{Template:Cite web|url=http://windowsitpro.com/windows-10/new-windows-10-features-you-need-know-about|title=The New Windows 10 Features You Need to Know About|date=28 January 2015|publisher=[[Penton_(company)|Penton]]|last1=Savill|first1=John|website=[[Windows_IT_Pro|Windows IT Pro]]}}</ref>

== 特点 ==
操作中心允许4个快速设置按钮，Windows 10中用户还可扩展以显示所有快速设置按钮。通知会按应用分类，用户可以向右滑动以清除通知。Windows 10中的操作中心还可控制通知设置。在移动版本中，用户可以从上向下滑动以调出操作中心，<ref name="ZDNet-Windows-Phone8.1-Action-Center-Launch">{{Template:Cite news|url=http://www.zdnet.com/article/windows-phone-8-1-action-center-provides-a-balanced-alert-system/|title=Windows Phone 8.1 Action Center provides a balanced alert system. Apple commonly copies other mobile operating systems and makes apps and utilities better. In this case, Microsoft did the same thing with notifications and may have just come up with the best approach.|last=Miller|first=Matthew|date=17 April 2014|newspaper=ZDNet}}</ref>在Windows Phone 8.1中更增加了进一步的功能，包括如更改音量等简单设置。新的通知区域设计允许用户完成如更改[[无线网络|无线网络]]、开关[[蓝牙|蓝牙]]和[[飞行模式|飞行模式]]，以及从屏幕顶部的可定制框访问“驾驶模式”等设置，而在下部横向放置的四个可定制框中包括最近文本信息和社交集成。<ref>[http://www.wpcentral.com/notification-center-windows-phone-81-reveal First screenshots of the Notification Center for Windows Phone 8.1 reveal new design | Windows Phone Central]</ref><ref>[http://tech.firstpost.com/news-analysis/windows-phone-8-1-get-action-center-show-screenshots-217954.html With 8.1 update and Action Center, Windows Phone users will finally have a notifications hub - Tech2]</ref><ref>{{Template:Cite web|url=http://www.engadget.com/2014/02/19/windows-phone-81-notification-action-center/|title=Here's Windows Phone 8.1's notification center in action (video)|accessdate=2014-08-21|date=2014-02-19|last=Gorman|first=Michael|publisher=Engadget.com}}</ref>在桌面版本中，用户可以点击任务栏中的操作中心图标或从右向左滑动以调出操作中心。

微软在[[Build_(开发者大会)|Build 2016]]上宣布，[[Microsoft_Cortana|Cortana]]将可以镜像Windows 10 Mobile和Windows 10之间的通知，<ref>{{Template:Cite web|url=http://mspoweruser.com/cortana-now-2-years-old/|title=Cortana is now 2 years old.|date=2 April 2016|last=Surur|first=Suril|work=MSPowerUser}}</ref><ref>{{Template:Cite web|url=http://www.slashgear.com/cloud-based-windows-10-action-center-will-also-be-on-android-01434250/|title=Cloud-based Windows 10 Action Center will also be on Android.|date=1 April 2016|last=Torres|first=JC|work=SlashGear}}</ref>Cortana也将能同步[[Android|Android]]设备的通知到Windows 10操作中心。<ref>{{Template:Cite web|url=http://phandroid.com/2016/04/01/microsoft-windows-10-anniversary-update-notification-mirroring-sync-actionable/|title=Windows 10 Anniversary Update not only mirrors Android notifications, but lets you interact with them too [VIDEO].|date=1 April 2016|last=Chavez|first=Chris|work=Phandroid}}</ref><ref>{{Template:Cite web|url=http://arstechnica.com/gadgets/2016/04/the-windows-10-anniversary-update-can-mirror-android-notifications/|title=The Windows 10 Anniversary Update can mirror Android notifications.
