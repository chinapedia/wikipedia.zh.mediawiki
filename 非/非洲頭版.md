'''《非洲頭版》'''（[[英文|英文]]：{{lang|en|FrontPage Africa}}）是由文字工作者[[羅德尼·希|羅德尼·希]]2005年成立的[[賴比瑞亞|賴比瑞亞]]日報，截至2012年，其發行量達1,500份。《非洲頭版》的調查報導獲得國際肯定，美國[[基督科學箴言報|基督科學箴言報]]讚其調查動作「領先全國」<ref name=CSM>{{cite web |url=http://www.csmonitor.com/World/Making-a-difference/2012/0529/Mae-Azango-exposed-a-secret-ritual-in-Liberia-putting-her-life-in-danger/(page)/2 |title=Mae Azango exposed a secret ritual in Liberia, putting her life in danger |author=Jina Moore |date=29 May 2012 |work=Christian Science Monitor |deadurl=no |archivedate=23 九月 2012 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6AtX3fb9a?url=http://www.csmonitor.com/World/Making-a-difference/2012/0529/Mae-Azango-exposed-a-secret-ritual-in-Liberia-putting-her-life-in-danger |accessdate=2013-11-20 }}</ref>。該報以調查少女賣淫、政府腐敗、警務人員涉及強暴等事件為主<ref name=CSM />；2012年，該報記者[[美·阿贊戈|美·阿贊戈]]完成一則女性[[割禮|割禮]]的相關報導，引起全國軒然大波，導致官方宣布暫停該傳統儀式。同年，該報出版了一份報告，內容指控{{Link-en|賴比瑞亞最高法院|Supreme Court of Liberia}}成員私自挪用國際援助資金<ref name=CSM />。

==早期歷史==

2005年，賴比瑞亞記者羅德尼·希創立了《非洲頭版》，在此之前，羅德尼已在幾個[[美國|美國]]報紙工作多年，經驗豐富。《非洲頭版》一開始只有發行網路版，後來發行實體報後，在2008年擴大發行至1,500份，並有多個版面<ref>{{cite web |url=http://pulitzercenter.org/reporting/liberia-front-page-africa-investigative-newspaper-print |title=Liberia: FrontPage Africa, an Investigative Daily |author=Jina Moore |date=19 March 2012 |publisher=The Pulitzer Center |accessdate=2013-11-20}}</ref>。

2010年，《非洲頭版》、羅德尼和該報記者[[山沃·法拉赫|山沃·法拉赫]]（{{lang|en|Samwar Fallah}}）被賴比瑞亞前農業部長[[克里斯多福·托爾|克里斯多福·托爾]]（{{lang|en|Christopher Toe}}）控告誹謗，並求償200萬元，原因是《非洲頭版》曾報導他挪用高達數百萬元的公共資金。對此[[世界报业协会|世界報業協會]]曾表示，該報對該案件的是非曲直沒有意見，只是單純的陳述，該前農業部長所提出的求償是懲罰性的要求<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1G1-221768105.html |title=WAN-IFRA condemns libel judgement in Liberia |date=19 March 2010 |publisher=World Association of Newspapers {{Subscription required|via=HighBeam Research}} |accessdate=2013-11-20 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150924152208/http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1G1-221768105.html |archive-date=2015-09-24 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>。

==2012年女性割禮的報導==

該報頭版在2012年的[[國際婦女節|國際婦女節]]刊出一則記者美·阿贊戈所寫的報導，內文描述一名婦女如何在另5名女子參與的儀式中被割去陰蒂<ref name=DB>{{cite web |url=http://www.thedailybeast.com/articles/2012/03/23/liberian-writer-mae-azango-forced-into-hiding-for-story-on-female-genital-cutting.html |title=Liberian Writer Mae Azango Forced Into Hiding for Story on Female Genital Cutting |author=Danielle Shapiro |date=23 March 2010 |work=The Daily Beast |deadurl=no |archivedate=23 九月 2012 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6AtUqIzGD?url=http://www.thedailybeast.com/articles/2012/03/23/liberian-writer-mae-azango-forced-into-hiding-for-story-on-female-genital-cutting.html |accessdate=2013-11-20 }}</ref><ref name=CSM/>；此文所描述的細節對執行該儀式的神秘組織「{{Link-en|薩德社會|Sande society}}」來說屬於祕密，因此在文章發布後，阿贊戈不斷收到死亡威脅；該報社編輯[[韋德·威廉斯|韋德·威廉斯]]（{{lang|en|Wade Williams}}）趕緊要求阿贊戈找尋避難所<ref name=DB />。

阿贊戈所受到的威脅包括，將抓住她並進行同樣的儀式<ref name=AP />；但當地警方並未採取積極行動，於是阿贊戈將9歲的女兒託給親戚照顧，自己則尋找避難處躲藏<ref name=DB /><ref name=G />。

總部設於[[美國|美國]]的新聞非政府組織[[保護記者委員會|保護記者委員會]]，呼籲賴比瑞亞總統[[埃伦·约翰逊·瑟利夫|埃倫·約翰遜·瑟利夫]]協助阿贊戈並予以保護<ref name=AP>{{cite web |url=http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1A1-c5e1de8e76fd444b9dd9c73dd29ab56c.html |title=Liberia reporter gets death, mutilation threats |date=14 March 2012 |publisher=Associated Press {{Subscription required|via=HighBeam Research}} |accessdate=23 September 2012 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150924043652/http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1A1-c5e1de8e76fd444b9dd9c73dd29ab56c.html |archive-date=2015-09-24 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>；[[國際特赦組織|國際特赦組織]]和[[無國界記者|無國界記者]]組織也發表聲明支持<ref name=G>{{cite web |url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/media/greenslade/2012/apr/30/journalist-safety-liberia |title=Journalist who revealed genital mutilation in Liberia forced into hiding |author=Roy Greensdale |date=30 April 2012 |work=The Guardian |archivedate=23 九月 2012 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6AtVvfMCz?url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/media/greenslade/2012/apr/30/journalist-safety-liberia |accessdate=2013-11-20 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>；[[哥倫比亞大學|哥倫比亞大學]]新聞學院<ref>{{cite web |url=http://spj.jrn.columbia.edu/2012/03/15/letter-to-the-liberian-government/ |title=Letter to the Liberian Government |author=Jake Heller and Mohamed Ademo |date=15 March 2012 |publisher=Columbia Journalism School |archivedate=23 九月 2012 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6AtWnRfzk?url=http://spj.jrn.columbia.edu/2012/03/15/letter-to-the-liberian-government/ |accessdate=2013-11-20 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>和國際新聞工作者聯合會<ref>{{cite web |url=http://allafrica.com/stories/201203161470.html |title=Liberia: IFJ Calls On Govt to Protect Threatened Female Journalist |date=16 March 2012 |publisher=allafrica.com |archivedate=23 九月 2012 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6AtYLWGwF?url=http://allafrica.com/stories/201203161470.html |accessdate=2013-11-20 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>也同聲支持；賴比瑞亞廣播電台主持人[[提提·蓋布瑞爾|提提·蓋布瑞爾]]（{{lang|en|Tetee Gebro}}）也在節目中播放阿贊戈的報導<ref name=DB/>。

在國內外的輿論壓力下，賴比瑞亞總統埃倫·約翰遜·瑟利夫宣布該國暫停此一傳統儀式<ref name=CPJ>{{cite web |url=http://cpj.org/awards/2012/mae-azango-liberia.php |title=Mae Azango, Liberia |publisher=Committee to Protect Journalists |archivedate=23 九月 2012 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6AtTijiAV?url=http://cpj.org/awards/2012/mae-azango-liberia.php |accessdate=2013-11-20 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.theworld.org/2012/03/female-genital-circumcision-temporarily-stopped-in-liberia/ |title=Female Circumcision Temporarily Stopped in Liberia |author=Bonnie Allen |date=March 29, 2012 |work=The World |archivedate=23 九月 2012 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6AtU9Ve2T?url=http://www.theworld.org/2012/03/female-genital-circumcision-temporarily-stopped-in-liberia/ |accessdate=2013-11-20 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>，此為第一次有賴比瑞亞的政治人物批評女性割禮<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.newnarratives.org/news/nns-proudest-moment-reporting-prompts-leaders-to-end-female-genital-cutting/ |title=NN's Proudest Moment: Reporting Prompts Leaders To Herald End Of Female Genital Cutting |date=1 April 2012 |publisher=New Narratives |archivedate=23 九月 2012 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6AtWRzj3r?url=http://www.newnarratives.org/news/nns-proudest-moment-reporting-prompts-leaders-to-end-female-genital-cutting/ |accessdate=2013-11-20 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。
