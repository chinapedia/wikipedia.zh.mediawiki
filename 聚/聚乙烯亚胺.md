'''聚乙烯亚胺'''（Polyethylenimine，PEI）分为线性和分枝状两种，线性聚乙烯亚胺包含的全是[[仲胺|仲胺]]，而分枝状聚乙烯亚胺中有[[伯胺|伯胺]]、[[仲胺|仲胺]]和[[叔胺|叔胺]]基。任何分子量的线性聚乙烯亚胺在[[常温|常温]]下是固体，而分枝状聚乙烯亚胺是液体。线性PEI可溶於热水中或低pH的甲醇、乙醇或氯仿中，但不溶於冷水、[[苯|苯]]、[[乙醚|乙醚]]和[[丙酮|丙酮]]；[[聚合物|聚合物]]链的一端是[[甲基|甲基]]，另一端是[[氨基|氨基]]；[[熔点|熔点]]为73－75℃；可贮存在室温中。

== 应用 ==
=== 附着力促进剂 ===
[[File:Aziridine.svg|thumb]]的结构]]
聚乙烯亚胺在[[细胞培养|细胞培养]]中可增强黏附力较弱的细胞的黏附力。PEI是阳离子聚合物，细胞外表面的负电荷附着到覆盖有PEI的培养皿底面，为细胞和平板之间提供了更强的附着力。不过，聚乙烯亚胺有很强的细胞毒性。<ref name= "Vancha 2004">{{cite journal | author = Vancha AR | coauthors= et al. | title = Use of polyethyleneimine polymer in cell culture as attachment factor and lipofection enhancer | journal = BMC Biotechnology | volume = 4 | year = 2004 | doi=10.1186/1472-6750-4-23 | pmid = 15485583 | pages = 23 | pmc = 526208}}</ref>

=== 转染试剂 ===
聚乙烯亚胺是历史上继[[多聚赖氨酸|多聚赖氨酸]]之後发现的第二种聚合物转染试剂<ref>Boussif, O. et al. A Versatile Vector for Gene and Oligonucleotide Transfer into Cells in Culture and in vivo: Polyethylenimine. Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences 92, 7297-7301, doi:10.1073/pnas.92.16.7297 (1995)</ref>。PEI能将DNA缩合成带正电荷的微粒，这些微粒可以黏合到带有负电荷的细胞表面残基，并通过[[胞吞|胞吞]]作用进入细胞。一旦进入细胞，胺的[[质子化|质子化]]导致[[反离子|反离子]]大量涌入以及[[渗透势|渗透势]]降低。上述变化导致的滲透膨脹使囊泡释放聚合物与DNA形成的复合物（polyplex）进入细胞质。复合物拆解後，DNA就能自由的融合到细胞核中。<ref name="Rudolf 2000"> {{ cite journal |year= 2000| title= In vivo gene delivery to the lung using polyethylenimine and fractured polyamidoamine dendrimers.| journal= Journal of Gene Medicine | volume= 2(4)| pages= 269–78 | pmid= 10953918 |last1= Rudolph |first1= C |last2= Lausier |first2= J |last3= Naundorf |first3= S |last4= Müller |first4= RH |last5= Rosenecker |first5= J |issue= 4 |url=http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/1521-2254(200007/08)2:4%3C269::AID-JGM112%3E3.0.CO;2-F/abstract;jsessionid=8C7767410C9D50AE050BE50424F84D1C.d02t01 }}</ref><ref name="Aknic 2004"> {{ cite journal |year= 2004| title= Exploring polyethylenimine-mediated DNA transfection and the proton sponge hypothesis. | journal= Journal of Gene Medicine | volume= 7(5)| pages= 657–663 | doi=10.1002/jgm.696 |pmid= 15543529 |last1= Akinc |first1= A |last2= Thomas |first2= M |last3= Klibanov |first3= AM |last4= Langer |first4= R |issue= 5}}</ref>PEI有很强的细胞毒性<ref>Hunter, A. C. Molecular hurdles in polyfectin design and mechanistic background to polycation induced cytotoxicity. Advanced Drug Delivery Reviews 58, 1523-1531 (2006)</ref>，作用机理分为两种<ref>Moghimi, S. M. et al. A two-stage poly(ethylenimine)-mediated cytotoxicity: implications for gene transfer/therapy. Molecular Therapy 11, 990-995 (2005)</ref>，一种是使细胞膜破碎导致（即刻的）[[细胞坏死|细胞坏死]]，另一种是在[[胞吞|胞吞]]後导致线粒体膜破碎，最终导致（延迟的）[[细胞凋亡|细胞凋亡]]。

线性PEI可通过水解聚(2-乙基-2-[[噁唑啉|噁唑啉]])合成<ref>Brissault, B. et al. Synthesis of Linear Polyethylenimine Derivatives for DNA Transfection. Bioconjugate Chemistry 14, 581-587 (2003)</ref>并以jetPEI的名称销售<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.polyplus-transfection.com/transfection-reagents/high-throughput-screening-jetpei/ |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2010-04-02 |0=2010-03-02 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100302173423/http://www.polyplus-transfection.com/transfection-reagents/high-throughput-screening-jetpei/ |archivedate=2010-03-02 }}</ref>。目前这一代的[[in-vivo|in-vivo]]-jetPEI使用定制的聚(2-乙基-2-[[噁唑啉|噁唑啉]])复合物作为前体。<ref>http://www.wipo.int/pctdb/en/wo.jsp?WO=2009016507&IA=IB2008002339&DISPLAY=DOCS</ref>
