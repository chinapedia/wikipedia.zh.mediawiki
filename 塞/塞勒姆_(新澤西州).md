'''塞勒姆'''（{{lang-en|Salem}}）是[[美國|美國]][[新澤西州|新澤西州]][[坎伯蘭縣_(新澤西州)|坎伯蘭縣]]的縣治。{{GR|6}}<ref>Walsh, Daniel. [http://nl.newsbank.com/nl-search/we/Archives?p_product=AC&p_theme=ac&p_action=search&p_maxdocs=200&p_topdoc=1&p_text_direct-0=129A5108F8016700&p_field_direct-0=document_id&p_perpage=10&p_sort=YMD_date:D&s_trackval=GooglePM "History and nature to merge on byway / Officials unveil scenic bayshore route for drivers"], ''[[The_Press_of_Atlantic_City|The Press of Atlantic City]]'', July 23, 2009. Accessed November 29, 2011. "The county has rolled out an advertising campaign that includes radio, print and television commercials, along with a new Web site, and county leaders have sought to sell outsiders on New Jersey's least-populated and most-rural county."</ref>根據[[美國2010年人口普查|2010年人口普查]]，本城共有人口5,857人<ref name=Census2010/><ref name=Districts2011/><ref name=LWD2010/>，相對[[美國2000年人口普查|2000年]]來說減少了1,026人，即下降了14.9%。而2000年的人口相對[[美國1990年人口普查|1990年]]來說則減少了1,026人，即下降了14.9%。<ref>[http://lwd.dol.state.nj.us/labor/lpa/census/2010/2010data/table7cm.xls Table 7. Population for the Counties and Municipalities in New Jersey: 1990, 2000 and 2010] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130520191436/http://lwd.dol.state.nj.us/labor/lpa/census/2010/2010data/table7cm.xls |date=2013-05-20 }}, New Jersey Department of Labor and Workforce Development, February 2011. Accessed August 17, 2012.</ref>其名稱「塞勒姆」源自希伯來字「[[平安_(希伯来语)|沙洛姆]]」（shalom），意思為「和平」。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.preservationsalem.org/PSIHIST.HTM|work=The City of Salem Master Plan Historic Preservation Element|title=A Brief and Partial History of the City of Salem|publisher=Preservation Salem County, Inc.|accessdate=September 15, 2010|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20041022074621/http://www.preservationsalem.org/PSIHIST.HTM|archivedate=2004年10月22日}}</ref>

本城於1693年10月成立，於1798年2月21日成立市政機關。<ref name=Story>Snyder, John P. [http://www.state.nj.us/dep/njgs/enviroed/oldpubs/bulletin67.pdf#page=204 ''The Story of New Jersey's Civil Boundaries: 1606-1968''], Bureau of Geology and Topography; Trenton, New Jersey; 1969. p. 217. Accessed August 17, 2012.</ref>

==地理==
塞勒姆位於塞勒姆河岸。<ref>{{Cite book| title = New Jersey Atlas & Gazetteer| publisher = DeLorme| year = 2005| location = Yarmouth, Maine| isbn =0-89933-324-9 }}</ref>根據[[美國2000年人口普查|2000年人口普查]]，本城面積為{{convert|2.815|sqmi|sqkm|2}}，其中{{convert|2.343|sqmi|sqkm|2}}為陸地，{{convert|0.472|sqmi|sqkm|2}}為水域，佔總面積的3.92%。{{GR|1}}<ref name=CensusArea/><ref name ="Gazetteer files">{{cite web|title=US Gazetteer files 2010|url=http://www.census.gov/geo/www/gazetteer/files/Gaz_places_national.txt|publisher=United States Census Bureau|accessdate=2013-01-25}}</ref>
