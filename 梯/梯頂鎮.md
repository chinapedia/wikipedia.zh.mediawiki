'''梯頂鎮'''（{{lang-my|တီတိန်မြို့နယ်}}，{{lang-en|Tedim Township}}）為[[緬甸|緬甸]][[欽邦|欽邦]][[法蘭縣|法蘭縣]]的鎮區<ref name="MIMU001">[http://www.burmalibrary.org/docs6/MIMU001_A3_SD%20&%20Township%20Overview.pdf "Myanmar States/Divisions & Townships Overview Map"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101203160828/http://www.burmalibrary.org/docs6/MIMU001_A3_SD%20%26%20Township%20Overview.pdf |date=2010-12-03 }} Myanmar Information Management Unit (MIMU)</ref>。2014年人口87,623人<ref name=citypopulation/>，區域面積2,409.6平方公里<ref name=citypopulation/>。[[梯頂|梯頂]]為該鎮之首府。
