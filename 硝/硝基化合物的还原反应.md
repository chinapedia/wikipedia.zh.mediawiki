'''硝基化合物的还原反应'''是一种可通过诸多不同试剂和不同反应条件进行的[[化学反应|化学反应]]。由于硝基的还原反应非常容易发生，因此历史上[[硝基化合物|硝基]]是首批被发现可发生[[氧化还原|还原]]反应的基团之一。

脂肪族硝基化合物与芳香族硝基化合物在还原反应上表现出较大的差异，因此可将这类反应分类为：脂肪族硝基化合物的还原反应与芳香族硝基化合物的还原反应，第二级分类则通过还原试剂的种类进行分类。

==脂肪族硝基化合物==
===还原为[[烃|烃]]===
[[Image:Reduction_NitroalkaneToAlkane.png|180px]]

'''氢化脱硝'''反应（硝基被[[氢|氢]]所取代）很难达成，需要通过在[[硅胶|硅胶]]上分散的[[钯|钯]]试剂并在高温的条件下才可发生。<ref>{{cite journal | author = M. J. Guttieri and W. F. Maier | title = Selective cleavage of carbon-nitrogen bonds with platinum | year = 1984 | journal = [[J._Org._Chem.|J. Org. Chem.]] | volume = 49 | issue = 16 | pages = 2875–2880 | doi = 10.1021/jo00190a006}}</ref>

=== 还原为[[胺|胺]]===
[[Image:Reduction_NitroalkaneToAmine.png|180px]]

脂肪族硝基化合物可以通过下列几种试剂还原为脂肪族的胺类化合物：

* 使用催化量的[[二氧化铂|二氧化铂]]（PtO<sub>2</sub>）<ref>{{cite journal | author = A. T. Nielsen | title = The Isomeric Dinitrocyclohexanes. II. Stereochemistry | year = 1962 | journal = [[J._Org._Chem.|J. Org. Chem.]] | volume = 27 | issue = 6 | pages = 1998–2001 | doi = 10.1021/jo01053a019}}</ref>或[[雷尼镍|雷尼镍]]进行催化氢化反应。<ref>{{OrgSynth | author = Dauben, Jr., H. J.; Ringold, H. J.; Wade, R. H.; Pearson, D. L.; Anderson, Jr., A. G. | title = Cycloheptanone | collvol = 4 | collvolpages = 221 | year = 1963 | prep = cv4p0221}}</ref>
* [[铁|铁]]粉在醋酸，加热的条件下进行<ref>{{cite journal | author = Senkus, M. | title = Ind. Eng. Chem. | year = 1948 | volume = 40 | pages = 506}}</ref>
* [[二碘化钐|二碘化钐]]<ref>{{cite journal | author = A. S. Kende and J. S. Mendoza | title = Controlled reduction of nitroalkanes to alkyl hydroxylamines or amines by samarium diiodide | year = 1991 | journal = [[Tetrahedron_Letters|Tetrahedron Letters]] | volume = 32 | issue = 14 | pages = 1699–1702 | doi = 10.1016/S0040-4039(00)74307-3}}</ref>

α,β-不饱和硝基化合物可通过下列方法还原为饱和胺类化合物：
* 在[[钯碳|钯碳]]条件下进行催化氢化反应
* 金属铁
* [[氢化锂铝|氢化锂铝]]<ref>{{cite journal | author = A. Burger, M. L. Stein and J. B. Clements | title = Some Pyridylnitroalkenes, Nitroalkanols, and Alkylamines | year = 1957 | journal = [[J._Org._Chem.|J. Org. Chem.]] | volume = 22 | issue = 2 | pages = 143–144 | doi = 10.1021/jo01353a010}}</ref> （注：反应中通常可以发现羟氨和肟的副产物）

===还原为[[羟胺|羟胺]]===
[[Image:Reduction_NitroalkaneToHydroxylAmine.png|180px]]

脂肪族硝基化合物可通过[[乙硼烷|乙硼烷]]还原为羟氨类化合物。<ref>{{cite journal | author = H. Feuer, R. S. Bartlett, B. F. Vincent and R. S. Anderson | title = Diborane Reduction of Nitro Salts. A New Synthesis of N-Monosubstituted Hydroxylamines | year = 1965 | journal = [[J._Org._Chem.|J. Org. Chem.]] | volume = 30 | issue = 9 | pages = 2880–2882 | doi = 10.1021/jo01020a002}}</ref>

===还原为[[肟|肟]]===
[[Image:Reduction_NitroalkaneToOxime.png|180px]]

硝基化合物通常可被一些低价无机盐还原为肟类化合物，如：[[氯化锡|氯化锡]]<ref>{{cite journal | author = Braun, V. J.; Sobecki, W. | title = Über primäre Dinitro-, Nitronitrit- und Dialdoxim-Verbindungen der Fettreihe | journal = [[Chemische_Berichte|Ber.]] | year = 1911 | volume = 44 | issue = 3 | pages = 2526–2534 | doi = 10.1002/cber.19110440377}}</ref>或[[二氯化铬|二氯化铬]]。<ref>{{cite journal | author = J. R. Hanson and E. Premuzic | title = Applications of chromous chloride--II : The reduction of some steroidal nitro-compounds | year = 1967 | journal = [[Tetrahedron_(journal)|Tetrahedron]] | volume = 23 | issue = 10 | pages = 4105–4110 | doi = 10.1016/S0040-4020(01)97921-9}}</ref> 另外，通过控制氢气的量进行的硝基化合物的催化氢化反应，也同样可以得到肟类化合物<ref>{{cite journal | author = C. Grundmann | title = Über die partielle Reduktion von Nitro-cyclohexan | year = 1950 | journal = [[Angewandte_Chemie|Angewandte Chemie]] | volume = 62 | issue = 23-24 | pages = 558–560 | doi = 10.1002/ange.19500622304}}</ref>

==芳香族硝基化合物==
芳香硝基化合物的还原反应通常收率很高，并可用于多种有机化合物的合成反应，相比脂肪族硝基化合物的还原反应，芳香族硝基化合物的还原反应更具应用意义。

===还原为[[苯胺|苯胺]]===
{{See also|Bechamp还原反应}}
[[Image:Reduction_NitroareneToAniline.png|180px]]

通过芳香硝基化合物来合成芳香胺，有诸多方法：
* 利用[[钯碳|钯碳]]，[[二氧化铂|二氧化铂]]或[[雷尼镍|雷尼镍]]催化剂进行催化氢化反应<ref>{{OrgSynth | author = Bavin, P. M. G. | title = 2-Aminofluorene | collvol = 5 | collvolpages = 30 | year = 1973 | prep = cv5p0030}}</ref> <ref>{{OrgSynth | author = Allen, C. F. H.; VanAllan, J. | title = 2-Amino-''p''-cymene | collvol = 3 | collvolpages = 63 | year = 1955) | prep = cv3p0063}}</ref>
* 利用[[铁|铁]]单质，在含有醋酸的溶剂中进行还原反应<ref>{{OrgSynth | author = Fox, B. A.; Threlfall, T. L. | title = 2,3-Diaminopyridine | collvol = 5 | collvolpages = 346 | year = 1973 | prep = cv5p0346}}</ref> （注：铁粉因其价格低廉，对于大多数官能团耐受且条件温和，常用于实验室内的硝基化合物的还原反应。但铁粉因为环境污染及后处理纯化的不易，在工业生产中用途并不广泛，参见[[Bechamp还原反应|Bechamp还原反应]]）。
* [[连二亚硫酸钠|连二亚硫酸钠]]<ref>{{OrgSynth | author = Redemann, C. T.; Redemann, C. E. | title = 5-Amino-2,3-dihydro-1,4-phthalazinedione | collvol = 3 | collvolpages = 69 | year = 1955 | prep = cv3p0069}}</ref>
* [[硫化钠|硫化钠]]或[[硫化氢|硫化氢]]
* [[二氯化锡|二氯化锡]]<ref>{{cite encyclopedia | last1 = Faul | first1 = Margaret M. | last2 = Thiel | first2 = Oliver R. | title = Encyclopedia of Reagents for Organic Synthesis | encyclopedia = [[Encyclopedia_of_Reagents_for_Organic_Synthesis|Encyclopedia of Reagents for Organic Synthesis]] | year = 2005 | doi = 10.1002/047084289X.rt112.pub2 | chapter = Tin(II) Chloride | isbn = 9780470842898 }}</ref>
* [[三氯化钛|三氯化钛]]
* [[锌|锌]]单质
* [[钐|钐]]<ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1016/S0040-4039(00)00917-5 | journal = [[Tet._Lett.|Tet. Lett.]] | author = Basu, M. 还可以通过[[硫化钠|硫化钠]]还原二硝基芳香化合物，以制备硝基芳香胺类化合物。<ref>{{OrgSynth | author = Hartman, W. W.; Silloway, H. L. | title = 2-Amino-4-nitrophenol | collvol = 3 | collvolpages = 82 | year = 1955 | prep = cv3p0082}}</ref>

硝基化合物可利用[[金属氢化物|金属氢化物]]的还原性，发生还原反应制备偶氮化合物。
