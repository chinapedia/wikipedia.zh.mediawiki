《'''黎巴嫩'''》 ({{lang-he|''לבנון''}})是2009年由以色列導演[[山謬·毛茲|山謬·毛茲]]所執導的首部電影，並獲得[[威尼斯影展|威尼斯影展]][[金獅獎|金獅獎]]，是第一部獲得該獎項的以色列電影。劇本是根據導演年輕時參與[[第五次中東戰爭|第五次中東戰爭]]時的經驗所啟發的<ref name="'Lebanon' wins Golden Lion at Venice">{{cite web|url=http://www.rte.ie/arts/2009/0912/venice.html|title='Lebanon' wins Golden Lion at Venice|date=2009-09-12|accessdate=2009-09-13|publisher=[[Raidió_Teilifís_Éireann|RTÉ]]}}</ref><ref name="NYT tank">{{citation |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2010/08/01/movies/01lebanon.html |title='Lebanon', Samuel Maoz's Tank's-Eye View of War |journal=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] |last=Erlanger |first=Stephen |date=July 30, 2010 |accessdate=August 3, 2010 }}.</ref>，全片視角侷限在一個坦克車內，塑造封閉緊張的戰場氣氛，外國媒體認為它帶有強烈的反戰思想，在以國也引起許多爭議，不過該片在以色列奧斯卡獎一共入圍了十個獎項。<ref name="bfi">{{cite web |url=http://www.bfi.org.uk/whatson/bfi_southbank/events/the_2009_satyajit_ray_award_winner_lebanon_qa_with_samuel_maoz |title=The 2009 Satyajit Ray Award Winner: Lebanon |accessdate=2010-04-18 |work=BFI |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100415184411/http://www.bfi.org.uk/whatson/bfi_southbank/events/the_2009_satyajit_ray_award_winner_lebanon_qa_with_samuel_maoz |archivedate=2010-04-15 }}</ref>
