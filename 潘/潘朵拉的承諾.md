《'''潘朵拉的承諾'''》（{{lang-en|'''''Pandora's Promise'''''}}）是一部2013年由[[羅伯特·史東|羅伯特·史東]]所執導的美國[[核電存廢問題|核電存廢問題]][[紀錄片|紀錄片]]。本片的中心議題為[[核能發電|核能發電]]，核能發電是一種相對安全且乾淨的發電方式，是減緩[[全球暖化|全球暖化]]的一個[[全球暖化的能源緩和技術|對策]]，但在美國受到部分[[環境保護主義者|環境保護主義者]]以歷史上的核事故為由反對。<ref name=WaPo /><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.google.com/hostednews/afp/article/ALeqM5h0sXC00bs6M_Jb4gB85wl-La-P2w |title=Pro-nuclear activists get pulpit at Sundance |last1=Raynaldy |first1=Romain |date=19 Jan 2013 |publisher=[[Agence_France-Presse|Agence France-Presse]] |accessdate=27 September 2013}}</ref><ref>{{YouTube|id=IIMbj67Mz9s|title=Pandora's Promise - Q&A at the IFC Center's Stranger Than Fiction Series|link=no}} (Pandora's Promise's channel)</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.slate.com/articles/health_and_science/science/2013/06/pandora_s_promise_producer_nuclear_energy_is_necessary_to_fight_climate.html |title=Orthodox Environmentalists Don’t Want You to See My Environmental Film |last1=Stone |first1=Robert |date=20 June 2013 |website=[[Slate_(magazine)|Slate (magazine)]] |accessdate=27 September 2013}}</ref>本片的名稱源自於[[希臘神話|希臘神話]]的[[潘朵拉|潘朵拉]]，這也是紀錄片的標語「她在[[潘朵拉的盒子|盒子]]底部找到了希望」的來源。

== 片中人物 ==
本片邀請到數位曾經強烈反核<ref name=WaPo /><ref>{{YouTube|id=pLkBdD-sM8E|title=Pandora's Promise: Talks at Google}} ([[AtGoogleTalks|AtGoogleTalks]] channel)</ref>，後來卻改觀的著名環保人士，包含：[[斯圖爾特·布蘭特|斯圖爾特·布蘭特]]、[[格溫妮絲·奎文|格溫妮絲·奎文]]、[[馬克·萊納斯|馬克·萊納斯]]、[[理查德·羅德|理查德·羅德]]及[[邁克爾·雪倫伯格|邁克爾·雪倫伯格]]。<ref name=Vulcan>{{cite web |url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/paul-allen-lends-support-pro-559512 |title=Paul Allen Lends Support to Pro-Nuclear Doc 'Pandora's Promise' |last1=Kilday |first1=Gregg |date=29 May 2013 |website=[[The_Hollywood_Reporter|The Hollywood Reporter]] |accessdate=25 September 2013}}</ref>

並對致力於美國[[反核運動|反核運動]]的[[海倫·寇蒂卡|海倫·寇蒂卡]]提出質疑<ref name=WaPo>{{cite web |url=http://articles.washingtonpost.com/2013-06-13/news/39945376_1_nuclear-power-film-fukushima |title=‘Pandora’s Promise’ movie review |last1=O’Sullivan |first1=Michael |date=13 June 2013 |website=[[The_Washington_Post|The Washington Post]] |accessdate=25 September 2013}}</ref><ref name="npr">{{cite web|url=http://www.npr.org/templates/story/story.php?storyId=198444052|title=Film Rankles Environmentalists By Advocating Nuclear Power|author=[[Robert_Siegel|Robert Siegel]]|date=3 July 2013|website=[[NPR|NPR]]|accessdate=20 June 2014}} </ref>。反核人士[[哈維·威瑟曼|哈維·威瑟曼]]也在片頭短暫出現。片中引用了[[珍·芳達|珍·芳達]]、[[拉爾夫·納德|拉爾夫·納德]]和[[艾默里·洛文斯|艾默里·洛文斯]]演說的部分歷史片段。
