'''德永氏船蛸'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Argonauta tokunagai''）為一種[[滅絕|滅絕種]][[章魚|章魚]]。本種於1913年發表描述，基於自[[中新世|中新世]]中期[[Huzina_Formation|Huzina Formation]][[日本|日本]]出土的化石材料上。<ref>Martill, D.M. & M.J. Barker (2006). [http://www.blackwell-synergy.com/doi/abs/10.1111/j.1475-4983.2006.00578.x?journalCode=pala A paper nautilus (Octopoda, ''Argonauta'') from the Miocene Pakhna Formation of Cyprus.] ''Palaeontology'' '''49''' (5): 1035-1041.</ref>

尚未描述而與本[[分類單元|分類單元]]有近緣關係的的化石[[船蛸屬|船蛸屬]]種類，會暫時標出 ''Argonauta'' cf. ''tokunagai'' （和德永氏船蛸對照）與 ''Argonauta "tokunagai"'' （「德永氏」船蛸）。<ref>Yanagisawa, Y. 1990. {{PDFlink|[http://www.gsj.jp/Pub/Bull/vol_41/41-03_02.pdf Age of fossil Argonautidae (Cephalopoda) from Hokuriku Province (central Honshu, Japan) based on diatom biostratigraphy.]|1.90 [[Mebibyte|MiB]]<!-- application/pdf, 1997660 bytes -->}} ''Bull. Geol. Surv. Japan'' '''41'''(3): 115-127.</ref>
