'''桥本真司'''（{{lang-ja|橋本 真司}}，{{bd|1968年|10月6日||}}）是[[日本|日本]]游戏制作公司[[史克威尔艾尼克斯|史克威尔艾尼克斯]]旗下一名[[游戏制作人|游戏制作人]]。他目前担任史克威尔艾尼克斯控股公司执行副总裁，以及该公司的[[1st_Production_Department|第一生产部]]执行干部<!-- is a Japanese [[game_producer|game producer]] at [[Square_Enix|Square Enix]]. He currently serves as corporate executive of the company's [[1st_Production_Department|1st Production Department]].--><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.4gamer.net/games/120/G012012/20110118006/ |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/5vr0zwlBW?url=http://www.4gamer.net/games/120/G012012/20110118006/ |archivedate=2011-01-19 |title=「Final Fantasy XIII-2」が2011年発売予定，「Agito」は「Final Fantasy 零式」と名称変更して2011年夏発売。「Square Enix 1st Production Department Premiere」をTwitterで実況 |date=2011-01-18 |language=[[Japanese|Japanese]] |work=4Gamer.net |publisher=Aetas, Inc. |accessdate=2011-01-19 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。他曾是多部作品的制作人，其中包括游戏[[最终幻想系列|最终幻想系列]]和[[王国之心系列|王国之心系列]]<!-- He has been the producer for several titles, including games in the [[Final_Fantasy|Final Fantasy]] and [[Kingdom_Hearts|Kingdom Hearts]] series.--><ref name="ten">{{cite web|url=http://ps3.ign.com/articles/782/782629p1.html|title=Ten Years of FFXIII|publisher=[[IGN|IGN]]|first=Anoop|last=Gantayat|date=2007-04-23|accessdate=2008-05-30}}</ref>。

===最终幻想===
桥本真司曾是《[[最终幻想VII|最终幻想VII]]》商品推广负责人<!--He was the promotions producer for [[Final_Fantasy_VII|Final Fantasy VII]].--><ref name="ten"/>。在2008年[[E3|E3]]展会上当被问及最终幻想VII重制的可能性时他曾说，史克威尔艾尼克斯公司确实注意到了爱好者们的对此事的高度兴趣，而目前他们正优先忙于下一部作品的制作<!--
When asked at [[E3|E3]] 2008 about the possibility of a remake of [[Final_Fantasy_VII|Final Fantasy VII]], he said that [[Square_Enix|Square Enix]] is aware fans would like that, and that they are very busy making other titles first.--><ref name="seven">{{cite web|url=http://whatifgaming.com/whatifgaming-e3-08-final-fantasy-xiii-sit-down-with-shinji-hashimoto|title=Whatifgaming E3-'08: Final Fantasy XIII Sit Down With Shinji Hashimoto|publisher=Whatifgaming|first=Usman|last=Ihtsham|date=2008-07-18|accessdate=2013-02-29}}</ref>。当最终幻想X-2和王国之心项目结束时，制作团队在“[[最终幻想VII补完计划|最终幻想VII补完计划]]”中所获得的经验之上催生建立了“[[新的水晶故事_最终幻想|新的水晶故事]]”系列开发计划<!--As [[Final_Fantasy_X-2|Final Fantasy X-2]] and [[Kingdom_Hearts|Kingdom Hearts]] were being completed, the learning experience the team had during the [[Compilation_of_Final_Fantasy_VII|Compilation of Final Fantasy VII]] project spawned the [[Fabula_Nova_Crystallis|Fabula Nova Crystallis]] series which was supposed to build on it.--><ref name="2up">{{cite web|url=http://www.1up.com/news/ffxiii-interview|title=FFXIII Interview: Nomura, Kitase, Hashimoto and Toriyama: Compilation of games puts new spin on FF series|publisher=[[1UP.com|1UP.com]]|author=Luke Smith|date=2006-06-07|accessdate=2013-02-29}}</ref>。当谈及《[[最终幻想XIII|最终幻想XIII]]》，桥本真司提到史克威尔艾尼克斯公司一直在尝试努力让该公司制作的游戏产品在各个地区更好的本地化，以缩小日本市场发布版本与世界其他地区市场版本之间的差距<!--While discussing [[Final_Fantasy_XIII|Final Fantasy XIII]], Hashimoto mentioned that [[Square_Enix|Square Enix]] has been attempting to make localization of their game releases close the release gap between Japan and the rest of the world.--><ref name="release">{{cite web|url=http://www.1up.com/news/e3-2008-xiii-interview|title=E3 2008: One More Final Fantasy XIII Interview|publisher=[[1UP.com|1UP.com]]|author=Shane Bettenhausen|date=2008-07-16|accessdate=2013-02-29}}</ref>。

===王国之心===
当史克威尔与[[迪士尼公司|迪士尼公司]]碰巧在东京的同一幢大楼内办公的时候，桥本真司偶然在电梯中与迪士尼的高管交谈，由此为契机，双方开始构思一款名叫《王国之心》的史克威尔、迪士尼合作游戏<!--When Square was sharing a building in Tokyo with the Disney corporation, Hashimoto found himself conversing in an elevator with a Disney executive, and there they conceived of a Disney/Square Enix video game eventually called [[Kingdom_Hearts|Kingdom Hearts]].--><ref name="heart">{{cite web|url=http://www.pcworld.idg.com.au/slideshow/373607/10_strange_facts_about_kingdom_hearts_franchise/|title=10 strange facts about the Kingdom Hearts franchise|publisher=[[PC_World|PC World]] Australia|author= McKinley Noble|date=2008-01-20|accessdate=2013-02-29}}</ref>。桥本真司指出《[[王国之心II|王国之心II]]》中出现的新功能是王国之心系列[[王国之心|首部作品]]的成功和迪士尼公司对史克威尔艾尼克斯制作出优质游戏的信心倍增共同带来的结果<!--Hashimoto has states that the new features in [[Kingdom_Hearts_II|Kingdom Hearts II]] were the result of the success of the first game and [[Disney|Disney]]s increased trust in [[Square_Enix|Square Enix]] to pull off an excellent product.--><ref name="hearts">{{cite web|url=http://m.ign.com/articles/2005/03/21/hashimoto-talks-kingdom-hearts-ii|title=Hashimoto Talks Kingdom Hearts II|publisher=IGN|author=Jeremy Dunham|date=2005-03-21|accessdate=2013-02-29}}</ref>。

===其他游戏===
《[[前线任务：进化|前线任务：进化]]》的制作过程中，桥本真司曾表示在开发过程中遇到的挑战之一是平衡机器人{{notetag|《前线任务》中出现的人型装甲机器人“Wanzer”}}或者[[机甲|机甲]]的即时战斗速度，使游戏能在实体机甲尺寸大小下依然能有足够快的运行速度。<!--
While working on [[Front_Mission_Evolved|Front Mission Evolved]], one of the challenges was balancing the speed of the real time battles the wanzers, or [[mech|mech]]s, were having so that the game was realistic to the mechs size, but also still fast enough to be engaging.--><ref name="evolved">{{cite web|url=http://www.gamingnexus.com/Article/Front-Mission-Evolved-Interview/Item2746.aspx|title=Front Mission Evolved Interview|publisher=Gaming Nexus|author=Charles Husemann|date=2008-10-19|accessdate=2013-02-29}}</ref>
