'''贾斯汀·安东尼·纳普'''（{{lang-en|Justin Anthony Knapp}}，{{bd|1982年|11月18日}}）<ref name="imm">{{Cite web |url=http://www.indianapolismonthly.com/features/king-of-corrections/ |title=King of Corrections |work=[[Indianapolis_Monthly|Indianapolis Monthly]] |last=Comisky |first=Daniel S. |date=July 26, 2012}}</ref>，网名'''Koavf'''，生于美国[[印第安纳州|印第安纳州]][[印第安纳波利斯|印第安纳波利斯]]，[[维基百科编者|维基百科编者]]。他是第一个在[[维基百科|维基百科]]编辑次数达到100万次的维基人<ref>{{cite web|title=The hardest working man on Wikipedia April 19, 2012|url=http://www.dailydot.com/news/wikipedian-first-1-million-edits/|publisher=[[Daily_Dot|Daily Dot]]|accessdate=September 3, 2012}}</ref>。截至2015年7月，纳普的编辑次数接近150万<ref name=wapo>{{cite web | url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/the-intersect/wp/2015/07/22/you-dont-know-it-but-youre-working-for-facebook-for-free/ | title=You don’t know it, but you’re working for Facebook. For free. | work=Washington Post | date=22 July 2015 | accessdate=16 August 2015 | author=Dewey, Caitlin}}</ref>。2012年4月18日至2015年11月1日，纳普在{{le|维基百科：最活跃的维基百科贡献者|Wikipedia:List of Wikipedians by number of edits|最活跃的维基百科贡献者}}中排名第一。

==教育与经历==
纳普于1997进入圣约基督高中（Covenant Christian High School）<ref name=imm/>。他在[[印第安纳大学与普渡大学印第安纳波利斯联合分校|印第安纳大学与普渡大学印第安纳波利斯联合分校]]获哲学和政治学学位<ref name=telegraph/><ref name="nuvo">{{cite web |url = http://www.nuvo.net/indianapolis/wikis-million-edit-man-is-lifelong-hoosier/Content?oid=2450776|title = Wiki's Million Edit Man is lifelong Hoosier|work = Nuvo|date = 23 May 2012|accessdate = 5 April 2015|author = Pogue, Paul F.P.}}</ref>。截至2013年，他于印第安纳大学（Indiana University）攻读护理专业<ref name=week>{{cite web | url=http://theweek.com/articles/468191/6-superdedicated-employees | title=6 super-dedicated employees | work=The Week | date=30 January 2013 | accessdate=30 January 2015 | author=Hansen, Lauren}}</ref>。为维持生计，纳普变换过不少工作，包括派送披萨、杂货店和[[危机热线|危机热线]]工作。<ref name="wapo2">{{cite web |url = https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/the-intersect/wp/2015/07/22/you-dont-know-it-but-youre-working-for-facebook-for-free/|title = You don’t know it, but you’re working for Facebook. For free.|work = Washington Post|date = 22 July 2015|accessdate = 16 August 2015|author = Dewey, Caitlin}}</ref><ref name="BI3">{{cite web |url = http://www.businessinsider.com/justin-knapp-on-future-of-wikipedia-2014-9|title = This Guy Has Edited Wikipedia More Than 1.3 Million Times — And He Doesn't Believe In The Decline Of The Free Encyclopedia|work = [[Business_Insider|Business Insider]]|date = 19 September 2014|accessdate = 21 September 2014|author = Lubin, Gus}}</ref>

==生涯==
===维基百科===
刚加入维基时，纳普还是一名大三的学生，他回忆道“我当时还没有个人电脑，我就在学校把文章打印出来，然后带回家读。”<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url = http://www.modernweekly.com/trend/5418|title = 维基百科的超级编辑|accessdate = 2016-01-26|author = |date = 2016-01-15|publisher = 周末画报}}</ref>

2012年4月19日，纳普宣布了他在维基百科的第100万次编辑<ref name=telegraph/>。自2005年3月注册账号以来，他平均每天编辑385次。对于他的表现，纳普说：“突然并且非自愿地失业才会这样对你”<ref name=telegraph>{{cite web|title=First man to make 1 million Wikipedia edits|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/technology/wikipedia/9215151/First-man-to-make-1-million-Wikipedia-edits.html|work=The Telegraph|accessdate=3 September 2012}}</ref>他的维基百科用户名“'''Koavf'''”的被扩写为“Vext粉丝之王”（King of all Vext Fans） ，这出自20世纪90年代的漫画书《{{Le|Vext|Vext}}》<ref name="nuvo2">{{cite web |url = http://www.nuvo.net/indianapolis/wikis-million-edit-man-is-lifelong-hoosier/Content?oid=2450776|title = Wiki's Million Edit Man is lifelong Hoosier|work = Nuvo|date = 23 May 2012|accessdate = 5 April 2015|author = Pogue, Paul F.P.}}</ref>。

在他走红后，一些“小团体”找到他，请求纳普在维基上写他们公司的文章，并给予他酬劳，但被纳普拒绝。<ref name=":0" />[[File:Great_Lakes_2011_Training.jpg|thumbnail]]
纳普对[[乔治·奥威尔|乔治·奥威尔]]{{Le|乔治·奥威尔作品|George Orwell bibliography|作品}}的编辑具有突出贡献<ref name="week2">{{cite web |url = http://theweek.com/articles/468191/6-superdedicated-employees|title = 6 super-dedicated employees|work = The Week|date = 30 January 2013|accessdate = 30 January 2015|author = Hansen, Lauren}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url = http://gizmodo.com/5903743/seven-years-one-million-edits-zero-dollars-wikipedias-flat-broke-superstar-editor|title = Seven Years, One Million Edits, Zero Dollars: Wikipedia's Flat Broke Superstar|work = Gizmodo|date = 20 April 2012|accessdate = 13 August 2015|author = Horn, Leslie}}</ref>，他还做过一些对专辑分类的编辑<ref>{{cite web |url = http://www.bbc.com/news/magazine-18833763|title = Wikipedia: Meet the men and women who write the articles|work = BBC News|date = 14 July 2012|accessdate = 18 February 2015}}</ref>。2012年，维基百科联合创始人[[吉米·威爾斯|吉米·威尔士]]向他的工作表示祝贺，并授予他该网站的最高成就奖<ref>{{cite web|title = Hardest working man on the internet passes one million Wikipedia edits|url = http://www.engadget.com/2012/04/20/one-million-wikipedia-edits/|publisher = Engadget.com|accessdate = 3 September 2012}}</ref>，将每年的4月20日定为贾斯汀·纳普日<ref name="declared2">{{cite news|title = Wikipedia Volunteer Editor Reaches 1 Million Edits|url = http://mashable.com/2012/04/23/wikipedia-volunteer-editor/|accessdate = 24 October 2012|newspaper = Mashable|date = 23 April 2012|author = Alissa Skelton}}</ref>。 在2014年一次与《Business Insider》的采访中，纳普说他的维基百科编辑并没有“代表日”，他还认为这样的编辑次数“未必是个问题”，最终百科内容会变得完整<ref name="BI2">{{cite web |url = http://www.businessinsider.com/justin-knapp-on-future-of-wikipedia-2014-9|title = This Guy Has Edited Wikipedia More Than 1.3 Million Times — And He Doesn't Believe In The Decline Of The Free Encyclopedia|work = [[Business_Insider|Business Insider]]|date = 19 September 2014|accessdate = 21 September 2014|author = Lubin, Gus}}</ref>。

===社会活动===
在2005年第60届[[联合国|联合国]]大会上，纳普为[[撒哈拉人|撒哈拉人]]发言并提及了[[西撒哈拉|西撒哈拉]]的现状<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.un.org/News/Press/docs/2005/gaspd314.doc.htm |title=REPRESENTATIVES OF MEMBER STATES, NON-SELF-GOVERNING TERRITORIES, PETITIONERS ADDRESS FOURTH COMMITTEE, AS IT CONTINUES GENERAL DEBATE ON DECOLONIZATION: Statements Focus on Questions of Gibraltar , Western Sahara , Guam |work=[[United_Nations|United Nations]] |deadurl=no |date=October 6, 2005 |accessdate=February 14, 2013 }}</ref>。 2013年，他为“{{Le|复兴第四修正案| Restore the Fourth}}”的示威运动进行社区组织<ref name="restore">{{Cite web |url=http://www.indystar.com/article/20130703/NEWS/307030098/Protesters-march-against-government-surveillance-nationwide-rally?nclick_check=1 |title=Protesters to March Against Government Surveillance in Nationwide Rally |work=[[Indianapolis_Star|Indianapolis Star]] |publisher=[[Gannett|Gannett]] |date=July 3, 2013 |last=Ritger |first=Carla}}</ref>。
