== 重量单位 ==
* 在{{le|古希腊计量单位|Ancient Greek units of measurement}}中，德拉克马（drachm）为重量单位，相当于6[[奥波勒斯|奥波勒斯]]或{{frac|100}}希腊[[米纳|米纳]]，约合4.37[[克|克]]。<ref name="Mastronarde1993">{{cite book|author=Donald J. Mastronarde|title=Introduction to Attic Greek|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=wRMtX6K7szcC&pg=PA222|accessdate=30 June 2012|date=19 March 1993|publisher=University of California Press|isbn=978-0-520-07844-4|page=222}}</ref>
* 在{{le|古罗马计量单位|Ancient Roman units of measurement}}中，重量单位德拉克马，或称德拉姆（dram），等于{{frac|96}}罗马磅，约合3.41克。<ref name="DICTIONARY OF GREEK AND ROMAN ANTIQUITIES.">{{cite book|last=Smith|first=William|title=DICTIONARY OF GREEK AND ROMAN ANTIQUITIES.|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=nqiyh-kZ8LkC&pg=PA1062|accessdate=1 July 2012|edition=3rd American|year=1886|publisher=Harper & Brothers|location=New York|page=1062}}</ref><ref name="Ramsay1883">{{cite book|last=Ramsay|first=William Wardlaw|title=An elementary manual of Roman antiquities|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=z4IBAAAAQAAJ&pg=PA206|accessdate=1 July 2012|edition=7th|year=1883|publisher=Charles Griffin and Company|location=London|page=206}}</ref>
* 在奥斯曼帝国，迪拉姆（Dirham），等于{{frac|400}}{{le|奥克 (单位)|Oka (mass)|奥克}}，约合3.207克。
