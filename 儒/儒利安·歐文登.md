'''儒利安·歐文登'''（'''Julian Ovenden'''，{{bd|1975年|11月29日}}<ref>{{cite web|url=http://search.ancestry.com/cgi-bin/sse.dll?rank=1&new=1&MSAV=0&gss=angs-c&gsfn=Julian&gsln=Ovenden&uidh=fk7&pcat=34&h=51533083&recoff=3+5&db=ONSBirth84&indiv=1&ml_rpos=9|title=England & Wales, Birth Index, 1916–2005| publisher=Ancestry.com}}</ref>）是[[英國|英國]]的一位演員和歌手，出生在[[雪菲爾|雪菲爾]]<ref name="Hoggard">{{cite news | author=Liz Hoggard | title=Marguerite's man | url=http://www.thisislondon.co.uk/theatre/article-23486069-marguerites-man.do | work=The Evening Standard | date=2008-05-20 | accessdate=2011-02-20 | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100114164005/http://www.thisislondon.co.uk/theatre/article-23486069-marguerites-man.do | archivedate=2010-01-14 }}</ref>。他曾接受歌劇歌手的訓練，<ref>{{cite news | author=Dalya Alberge | title=Opera: it sounds like being the next big thing in movies | url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/music/2010/oct/31/cosi-fan-tutte-stephen-evans-mozart | work=The Observer | date=2010-10-30 | accessdate=2011-02-20}}</ref>，並曾就讀於韋伯·道格拉斯戲劇學院。
