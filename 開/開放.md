'''開放'''是一個整體概念或哲學，特點是強調{{link-en|透明度 (行為)|transparency (behavior)|透明度}}和自由，無限制地獲取任何知識和[[信息自由|自由訊息]]<ref name=Peters>{{cite web|last=Peters|first=Michael|title=The Idea of Openness: Open Education and Education for Openness|url=http://eepat.net/doku.php?id=open_education_and_education_for_openness|work=The Encyclopaedia of Educational Philosophy and Theory|accessdate=1 May 2014|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.fo/20140501132743/http://eepat.net/doku.php?id=open_education_and_education_for_openness|archivedate=2014年5月1日}}</ref>。[[秘密|秘密]]可說是開放的相反。<ref name=Peters />

== 用於政府 ==
{{Main article|開放政府}}
開放政府是一種執政的原則，它認為公民有權取得任何政府文件，以便有效地公開監督政府<ref name="ruma">{{cite book |editor1-first=Daniel |editor1-last=Lathrop |editor2-first=Laurel |editor2-last=Ruma |title= Open Government: Transparency, Collaboration and Participation in Practice |publisher= O'Reilly Media |date=February 2010  |url = https://openlibrary.org/books/OL24435672M/Open_Government |isbn= 978-0-596-80435-0}}</ref>，即公民有權知道政府一切的運作和活動<ref name="Birkinshaw">{{cite journal|last=Birkinshaw|first=Patrick|title=FREEDOM OF INFORMATION AND OPENNESS: FUNDAMENTAL HUMAN RIGHTS?|journal=Administrative Law Review|volume=58|issue=1|pages=177–218|url=http://web.b.ebscohost.com/ehost/detail?sid=ac4899af-cdac-4b67-8b67-d0f7951e70b3%40sessionmgr115&vid=1&hid=124&bdata=JnNpdGU9ZWhvc3QtbGl2ZQ%3d%3d#db=bth&AN=20477744&anchor=AN0020477744-10|accessdate=1 May 2014}}{{Dead link|date=2018年7月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>，由於可信賴的信息是責任政治的必要條件，自由地取得政府的訊息也有助於保障其他權利。<ref name = "Birkinshaw"/>

== 用於創作 ==

== 用於學術 ==
{{Main article|開放獲取}}
開放獲取是指不限制經過[[同行評審|同行評審]]的學術研究的在線訪問。開放獲取主要針對[[學術期刊|學術期刊]]文章，但也在提供越來越多的論文<ref>{{cite journal|last=Schöpfel|first=Joachim|author2=Prost, Helene|title=Degrees of secrecy in an open environment. The case of electronic theses and dissertations|journal=ESSACHESS – Journal for Communication Studies|year=2013|volume=6|issue=2|url=http://www.essachess.com/index.php/jcs/article/view/214|issn=1775-352X}}</ref>、書籍章節<ref name="earlham.edu">Suber, Peter. [http://www.earlham.edu/~peters/fos/overview.htm "Open Access Overview"]. Earlham.edu. Retrieved on 2011-12-03.</ref>和學術著作<ref>{{cite journal| journal=Library Journal|title=Directory of Open Access Books Goes Live|author=  Meredith Schwartz |date =April 13, 2012 |url=http://lj.libraryjournal.com/2012/04/academic-libraries/directory-of-open-access-books-goes-live/}}</ref>。

== 用於資訊科技 ==
{{Main article|開放原始碼}}
開放原始碼是資訊科技系統中所提供的功能，特點在於可互操作性、可移植性和可擴展性<ref name="CPO">{{cite web|last1=Steven|first1=Vettermann|title=Code of Openness|url=http://www.prostep.org/en/cpo.html|website=CPO|publisher=ProSTEP iViP|accessdate=10 January 2017}}</ref>，這使得生產模組、通訊管道、互動社群獲得改善。
