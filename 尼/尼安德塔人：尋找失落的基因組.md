'''尼安德塔人：尋找失落的基因組'''（{{lang-en|''Neanderthal Man: In Search of Lost Genomes''}}）是[[瑞典|瑞典]][[演化人類學|演化人類學]]者[[斯万特·帕博|斯万特·帕博]]（Svante Pääbo）所著的一部书<ref name="Neanderthal Man: In Search of Lost Genomes">{{cite book|last1=Pääbo|first1=Svante|title=Neanderthal Man: In Search of Lost Genomes|date=2014|publisher=Basic Books|isbn=978-0-465-02083-6|accessdate=6 July 2014}}</ref>。本书讲述了帕博對生活在欧洲和近东的[[尼安德塔人|尼安德塔人]]DNA的探索。此书以[[回忆录|回忆录]]的形式写成<ref name="Most of Us Are Part Neanderthal">{{cite web|last1=Mithen|first1=Steven|title=Most of Us Are Part Neanderthal|url=http://www.nybooks.com/articles/archives/2014/apr/03/most-us-are-part-neanderthal/|website=nybooks.com|publisher=The New York Review of Books|accessdate=6 July 2014}}</ref>，畅談了其25年來的研究歷程。

==反响==
[[科学普及|科普作家]]{{le|卡尔·齐默|Carl Zimmer}}在《[[紐約時報|紐約時報]]》撰文称其为“令人着迷的书”（"a fascinating account"）<ref name="Missing Links">{{cite web|last1=Zimmer|first1=Carl|title=Missing Links|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2014/04/20/books/review/neanderthal-man-by-svante-paabo.html|website=nytimes.com|publisher=The New York Times|accessdate=7 July 2014}}</ref> The book made the paper's Sunday Book Review Editors' Choice List.<ref name="Editors' Choice">{{cite web|title=Editors' Choice|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2014/04/27/books/review/editors-choice.html|website=nytimes.com|publisher=The New York Times|accessdate=7 July 2014}}</ref> 《[[衛報|衛報]]》的Peter Forbes, remarking on the book's characterization of the research process, stated that:

"His book is also valuable for showing just how modern research teams work together and, sometimes, fall out. Along the way, a key collaborator became a competitor and, midstream, Pääbo abandoned one collaborating gene-sequencing company for another. The race for the Neanderthal genome is reminiscent of the human genome story of 10 years previously, as recounted in James Shreeve's ''The Genome War''."<ref name="Neanderthal Man: In Search of Lost Genomes by Svante Pääbo – review">{{cite web|last1=Forbes|first1=Peter|title=Neanderthal Man: In Search of Lost Genomes by Svante Pääbo – review|url=http://www.theguardian.com/books/2014/feb/19/neanderthal-man-search-lost-genomes-svante-paabo|website=theguardian.com|publisher=The Guardian|accessdate=7 July 2014}}</ref>
