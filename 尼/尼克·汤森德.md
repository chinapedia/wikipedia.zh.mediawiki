'''尼古拉斯·彼德·汤森德'''（{{lang-en|'''Nicholas Peter Townsend'''}}，{{bd|1994年|11月1日|catIdx=Townsend, Nick}}）是一名[[英格蘭|英格蘭]][[足球|足球]][[運動員|運動員]]，司職[[守門員_(足球)|守門員]]，現時效力[[英甲|英甲]]球會[[巴恩斯利足球俱乐部|巴恩斯利]]。

==球會生涯==
===伯明翰===
汤森德在2011年7月取得獎學金加入伯明翰青訓系統<ref name=FAscholar>{{cite web |url=http://www.thefa.com/~/media/Files/TheFAPortal/governance-docs/registrations/july-2011.ashx |format=PDF |title=List of Players Registered as Scholars in Accordance with Rule C.3 Between 01/07/2011 and 31/07/2011 |publisher=The Football Association |accessdate=11 August 2015}}</ref>。2013年1月，由於球隊三號守門員[[大衛·盧卡斯|大衛·盧卡斯]]離隊，他獲發一隊球衣背碼<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.bcfc.com/news/article/lucas-joins-fleetwood-572043.aspx |title=Lucas joins Fleetwood |first=Andy |last=Walker |publisher=Birmingham City F.C. |date=1 January 2013 |accessdate=11 August 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130104141615/http://www.bcfc.com/news/article/lucas-joins-fleetwood-572043.aspx |archive-date=2013-01-04 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>，並在足總盃第三輪對[[列斯聯足球會|列斯聯]]時擔任[[科林·多伊尔|科林·多伊尔]]的替補<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.birminghammail.co.uk/sport/football/football-news/lee-clark-says-time-of-keith-1222331 |title=Lee Clark: 'We are looking to the next few weeks for Keith Fahey to rejoin us' |first=Colin |last=Tattum |newspaper=Birmingham Mail |date=19 January 2013 |accessdate=11 August 2015}}</ref>。之後他被外借到[[牛津城足球會|牛津城]]並成為先發成員，共在[[英格蘭足球議會北部聯賽|北議聯]]上場9次及[[牛津郡高級盃|牛津郡高級盃]]上場2次<ref name=OCstats>{{cite web |url=https://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/pub?key=0Av02KuLaNo0WdFZJM0k1UG1jcUd0SVZnSG9NS1p4UlE&output=html |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6Pz1Iv5Fw?url=https://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/pub?key=0Av02KuLaNo0WdFZJM0k1UG1jcUd0SVZnSG9NS1p4UlE |archivedate=2014-05-31 |deadurl=no |title=The matches: 2012 |publisher=Chris Byrne |website=Oxford City FC – History & Statistics |accessdate=11 August 2015 }}<!-- This may not *look* desperately reliable, but the pages were part of Oxford City FC's official website http://www.oxfordcityfc.com/ accessed via navbar Our Club >>> Our history >>> The records, which takes you to the History & Statistics website, then click on The matches >>> 2012. The links disappeared when oxfordcityfc.com was redesigned, but the stats site remains, as of May 2015, at http://citystats.moonfruit.com/ --></ref>。季後他獲得一年職業合約，外另一年選擇權<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.birminghammail.co.uk/sport/football/football-news/birmingham-city-chris-burke-says-3902254 |title=Chris Burke: 'I have contributed this season but I have not met my own expectations' |first=Colin |last=Tattum |newspaper=Birmingham Mail |date=21 May 2013 |accessdate=11 August 2015}}</ref>。

2014年2月，他被外借到[[英格蘭足球議會全國聯賽|全國聯]]球會[[林肯城足球俱乐部|林肯城]]並成為先發陣容，共上場14次。2014/15球季，他在伯明翰的合約獲得延長<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.bcfc.com/news/article/20140430-decision-made-on-future-of-seven-youngsters-1518889.aspx |title=Decision made on future of seven youngsters |first=Andy |last=Walker |publisher=Birmingham City F.C. |date=30 April 2014 |accessdate=11 August 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150923183310/http://www.bcfc.com/news/article/20140430-decision-made-on-future-of-seven-youngsters-1518889.aspx |archive-date=2015-09-23 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>，並獲得30號球衣背碼<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.bcfc.com/news/article/20140709-201415-squad-numbers-announced-1730940.aspx |title=2014/15 Blues squad numbers announced |publisher=Birmingham City F.C. |date=9 July 2014 |accessdate=11 August 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170301221846/http://www.bcfc.com/news/article/20140709-201415-squad-numbers-announced-1730940.aspx |archive-date=2017-03-01 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>。由於沒有職業球會對他有興趣，意味著汤森德須再次外借回非聯賽。汤森德回到林肯城，依舊成為球隊首席守門員並在聯賽上場17次。在林肯城出戰首場足總盃前，由於伯明翰主教練[[李爾·奇勒|李爾·奇勒]]被辭退，球會不希望在新教練上任前就讓汤森德參加足總盃而使他無法代表伯明翰在盃賽上場，這迫使林肯城召回外借的門將保羅·法曼。盃賽結束後，汤森德回到板凳上，沒有再代表林肯城上場，於是伯明翰在1月召回他<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.bcfc.com/news/article/lincoln-city-nick-townsend-birmingham-city-2182188.aspx |title=Townsend back from the Imps |publisher=Birmingham City F.C. |date=5 January 2015 |accessdate=11 August 2015}}</ref>。

回到伯明翰城，他在U21發展隊獲得穩定的上場機會，並以隊長身份帶領球隊贏得伯明翰高級盃<ref name=Contract2015/><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.bcfc.com/news/article/blues-win-the-senior-cup-2433455.aspx |title=Blues win the Senior Cup |first=Peter |last=Lewis |publisher=Birmingham City F.C. |date=30 April 2015 |accessdate=11 August 2015}}</ref>。2015年夏天，他與球會簽下兩年新合約，外加一年行使權<ref name=Contract2015>{{cite web |url=http://www.bcfc.com/news/article/nick-townsend-birmingham-city-2454113.aspx |title=Nick Townsend pens new Blues contract |publisher=Birmingham City F.C. |date=15 May 2015 |accessdate=11 August 2015}}</ref>。由於[[湯馬士·古斯錫|湯馬士·古斯錫]]和[[亚当·莱格兹丁斯|亚当·莱格兹丁斯]]的加盟，他被降至第三守門員。於是汤森德被外借到英甲球會[[巴恩斯利足球俱乐部|巴恩斯利]]至11月1日。

===巴恩斯利===
加盟巴恩斯利的首場聯賽雖然坐冷板，但三天後的聯賽盃到訪[[斯坎索普联足球俱乐部|斯坎索普联]]的比賽中獲得上場機會，在[[點球大戰|點球大戰]]中由於未能救中任何一球，使球隊以7-6出局<ref name=BarnsleyDebut>{{cite news |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/33768619 |title=Scunthorpe 1–1 Barnsley |publisher=BBC Sport |date=11 August 2015 |accessdate=11 August 2015}}</ref>。9月1日，他正式轉會至巴恩斯利<ref name=TownsendOut>{{cite news |url=http://www.bcfc.com/news/article/birmingham-city-blues-nick-townsend-barnsley-2662540.aspx |title=Official: Nick Townsend joins Barnsley on permanent deal |publisher=Birmingham City F.C. |date=1 September 2015 |accessdate=1 September 2015}}</ref>。9月12日主場對[[斯温登足球俱乐部|斯温登]]的比賽，由於守門員[[亞當·戴維斯|亞當·戴維斯]]被棄用，他首次在職業聯賽上場並以4-1取勝。
