'''尼夫赫人'''（{{lang-ru|Нивхи}}、吉里迷）舊稱'''吉利亚克人'''，是[[西伯利亞|西伯利亞]]東南地區[[原住民|原住民族]]，分布於[[黑龍江|黑龍江]]下游至入海口以及[[庫頁島|庫頁島]]北部，即今[[俄羅斯聯邦|俄羅斯聯邦]]的[[哈巴罗夫斯克边疆区|哈巴罗夫斯克边疆区]]和[[萨哈林州|萨哈林州]]。這一地區多為稀疏[[針葉林|針葉林]]帶，夏季溫和，冬季寒冷多雪，尼夫赫人長期以[[捕魚|捕魚]]、[[狩獵|狩獵]]、養犬為生，夏季居於沿海，冬季則遷至內陸。<ref name=GallTL>Gall, pp.2-4 </ref>

學者認為，尼夫赫人可能源于[[更新世|更新世]]后期從[[外貝加爾山脈|外貝加爾山脈]]地區遷徙至此的[[新石器|新石器]]居民。<ref> Fitzhugh, William , and Durbreui  pp.39,40 </ref>千百年來，他們歷經其他民族的影響，從[[通古斯人|通古斯人]]的移民，到[[中國|中國]][[滿清|滿清]]的統治，至19世紀中葉，[[沙俄|沙俄]][[哥薩克|哥薩克]]兼并了尼夫赫人的土地并在此殖民，使他們成為常被忽略的少數民族。<ref name="Bassett, p.1">Bassett, p.1</ref><ref name="Culture: Nivkh Gilyak">Jesup Exhibition: [http://www.amnh.org/exhibitions/Jesup/G12.html " Culture: Nivkh (Gilyak)"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080212061144/http://www.amnh.org/exhibitions/Jesup/G12.html |date=2008-02-12 }} - American Museum of Natural History - New York, New York</ref>尼夫赫人信仰[[薩滿教|薩滿教]]，在冬季“熊祭”期間尤其重要；也有一些人皈依了[[俄羅斯東正教|俄羅斯東正教]]。現在，尼夫赫人居住在俄式風格的房屋中，飲食也俄化。<ref name="Chaussonnet, pp. 34,35">Chaussonnet, pp. 34,35 </ref>

根據俄羅斯2002年的人口統計，尼夫赫總人口為5287人，多數使用[[俄語|俄語]]，僅有10%左右的族人仍使用本民族語言——[[尼夫赫語|尼夫赫語]]。尼夫赫語是一種[[孤立語言|孤立語言]]，有時歸類于[[古西伯利亞語言|古西伯利亞語]]，可分為四種方言。与阿伊努语也有相似之处<ref name="Mattissen, p.515">Mattissen, p.515</ref>

== 族稱 ==
“尼夫赫”是族人的自稱，在[[尼夫赫語|尼夫赫語]]中意為“人”。俄語轉寫的單數“<span lang="ru">Нивх</span>”和復數“<span lang="ru">Нивхи</span>”分別拉丁化為“{{lang|en|Nivkh}}”和“{{lang|en|Nivkhi}}”，廣泛用于西方文獻中。在17至18世紀，俄羅斯殖民者最早稱之為“吉利亚克人”（{{lang|en|Gilyak、Giliaks、Giliatski}}），該詞的詞源有爭議，可能源于周圍通古斯民族對尼夫赫人的他稱，或是對周邊某一通古斯民族的名稱“{{lang|en|Kile}}”的誤用。<ref name="GallTL"/>

中國史籍對尼夫赫人早有記載。[[唐代|唐代]][[靺鞨|靺鞨]]有'''郡利部'''，朝廷設黑水府治其地。後[[渤海國|渤海國]]興盛，降伏靺鞨<ref>[[新唐書|新唐書]]卷二一九靺鞨傳：“<span style="font-size:75%; font-weight:normal;">開元十年，其酋倪屬利稽來朝，玄宗即拜勃利州刺史。於是安東都護薛泰請置黑水府，以部長爲都督、刺史，朝廷爲置長史監之，賜府都督姓李氏，名曰獻誠，以雲麾將軍領黑水經略使，隸幽州都督。訖帝世，朝獻者十五。大曆世凡七，貞元一來，元和中再。……初，黑水西北又有思慕部，益北行十日得郡利部，東北行十日得窟說部，亦號屈設，稍東南行十日得莫曳皆部，又有拂涅、虞婁、越喜、鐵利等部。其地南距渤海，北、東際於海，西抵室韋，南北袤二千里，東西千里。拂涅、鐵利、虞婁、越喜時時通中國，而郡利，屈設、莫曳皆不能自通。……後渤海盛，靺鞨皆役屬之，不復與王會矣。</span>”</ref>。[[遼代|遼代]]稱其部為'''阿里眉'''屬[[五國部|五國部]]<ref>[[契丹國志|契丹國志]]卷二二</ref>。[[金代|金代]]稱之為'''吉里迷'''<ref>[[金史|金史]]卷二四地理志</ref>，[[元代|元代]]作吉里迷、'''乞烈賓'''<ref>[[元史|元史]]世祖紀、卷九八兵志</ref>、'''吉烈滅'''<ref>別里哥帖穆爾神道碑，金華集卷二五</ref>，[[清代|清代]]則定名為'''費雅喀'''。阿伊努人叫他們'''尼古奔'''或'''史米倫格'''。有人說他們是唐代的[[流鬼國|流鬼國]]。

== 起源 ==
根據現有的考古資料，尼夫赫人的起源難以查考，他們傳統的捕魚、獵取沿海哺乳動物的生活方式以及狗拉雪橇的使用均與[[堪察加半島|堪察加半島]]的[[堪察加人|堪察加人]]和[[科里亞克族|科里亞克族]]很相似，原始信仰卻接近美洲西北的[[薩利希語系|薩利希語]]民族。<ref name="Chaussonnet, pp. 34,35"/>從體質人類學和遺傳學角度看來，尼夫赫人與周邊民族都不相同（蘇聯人類學家稱他們是[[蒙古人種|蒙古人種]]中的黑龍江庫頁類型）。目前的考古學模型主張他們的極低生存模式起源于[[外貝加爾山脈|外貝加爾山]]區域的一種[[細石器時代|細石器]]文化，并在晚[[更新世|更新世]]甚至更早的時期隨移民擴展到黑龍江和庫頁島地區。<ref> Fitzhugh, William, and Durbreui  pp.39,40 </ref>這些細石器文化也可能是最早進入美洲的文化。<ref>Fitzhugh, William, and Durbreui, p.35; [http://www.npolar.no/ansipra/english/Indexpages/Ethnic_groups.html#19 "Indigenous Peoples of the Russian North, Siberia and Far East" ] by Arctic Network for the Support of the Indigenous Peoples of the Russian Arctic </ref>在冰河時期，細石器文化很好地適應了西伯利亞地區的嚴酷氣候環境。隨著氣候轉暖，冰川消退，通古斯民族從南部遷移而來，并支配了當地原住民。尼夫赫人被看作是他們中適應了氣候改變而未被同化的唯一遺存。<ref> Chaussonnet, p35 </ref>對庫頁島北部遺存進行的[[放射性碳定年法|放射性碳測年]]結果表明，最早的遺址是[[特米河|特米河]]口的“Imchin Site 2”，屬于[[新石器時代|新石器時代]]，距今7000至6500年（公元前4950-4570年）。<ref> Kuzmin, Vasilevski, Gorbunov, Burr, Jull, Orlova Shubina, p.355,359-360
</ref>

俄羅斯旅行家馬克認為，他們生活在黑龍江下游至河口一帶，與通古斯語系民族一起生活，但是他們與通古斯人無關，他們與[[千島群島|千島群島]]上的種群關係較大。尼夫赫人與通古斯人相比，色素深，輪廓突出，有人將他們歸入黑龍江庫頁島類型。

== 歷史 ==

更新世晚期，氣候寒冷，庫頁島尚與[[歐亞大陸|歐亞大陸]]相連，尼夫赫先民即至此定居，冰期結束后，海平面上升，[[韃靼海峽|韃靼海峽]]將庫頁島與大陸阻隔，尼夫赫人因此分為兩個支系。<ref>Fitzhugh, William, and Durbreuil p32 </ref>[[唐玄宗|唐玄宗]]开元十年（722年）唐廷就曾在尼夫赫人居住的地區建立了羈縻統治。[[唐朝|唐朝]]人稱居住在黑水（黑龍江）下游的部族為[[黑水靺鞨|黑水靺鞨]]，並在此設立[[黑水都督府|黑水都督府]]，從屬于[[幽州|幽州]]節度使，[[唐宪宗|唐宪宗]]之后废弃。[[遼朝|遼朝]]時其地屬[[東京道|東京道]]。至晚在[[金朝|金朝]]，尼夫赫人居住的地區就被正式納入[[中原王朝|中原王朝]]疆域。金時吉里迷之地隸屬于[[胡里改路|胡里改路]]，[[元朝|元朝]]时則先后屬于[[遼陽等处行中书省|遼陽等处行中书省]]的[[开元路_(元朝)|开元路]]和[[水達達路|水達達路]]（設狗站）。到[[明朝|明朝]]，該地于永乐七年（1409年）设置[[奴儿干都司|奴儿干都司]]管轄，后在[[明宣宗|明宣宗]]宣德九年（1434年）废弃，[[清朝|清朝]]时则屬于[[吉林|吉林]]之三姓副都統轄區和[[黑龍江將軍|黑龍江將軍]]轄區。《[[皇清職貢圖|皇清職貢圖]]》提到[[松花江|松花江]]极东沿海岛屿也有其族人散居。三姓副都統衙門每年派員向費雅喀人徵收貂皮。[[满族|满族]]“乌勒本”（{{lang-mnc|ᡠᠯᠠᠪᡠᠨ|z=ulabun}}）中记载了具有满族血统的尼夫赫首领包鲁嘎汗的故事，反映了[[后金|后金]]同尼夫赫人的早期接触。

17世纪晚期，[[俄罗斯沙皇国|沙皇俄国]]殖民者开始侵入。[[瓦西里·波亞爾科夫|瓦西里·波亞爾科夫]]於1643年記述了“吉利亚克人”，即指尼夫赫人。<ref name=KolM/>尼夫赫人不斷受到騷擾，對[[哥薩克|哥薩克]]深惡痛絕，稱之為“kinrsh”（魔鬼）。<ref>Shternberg,  p.183</ref>1689年的中俄《[[尼布楚条约|尼布楚条约]]》规定[[外东北|外东北]]归中国所有，但19世纪中叶以后通過1858年的中俄《[[璦琿條約|璦琿條約]]》和1860年的中俄《[[北京條約|北京條約]]》，[[俄罗斯帝国|俄罗斯帝国]]最終吞并該地區。<ref name=KolM>Kolga, pp.269-273</ref>1857年至1906年，俄國人在庫頁島建立的殖民點收納了大量罪犯和流放的政治犯，其中，人類學家{{tsl|en|Lev Sternberg|列夫·史坦伯格}}，最早記錄了尼夫赫人的民族志。移民數量很快超過尼夫赫人，一些族人開始受雇看守監獄或追捕逃犯。<ref name="Culture: Nivkh Gilyak"/>移民也帶入了傳染病，[[天花|天花]]、[[鼠疫|鼠疫]]、[[流感|流感]]等疾病的流行造成期间原住民人口的減少。<ref name="SmoA">Smolyak, p.175</ref>俄國當地官員還通過征收重稅，限制尼夫赫人在沿海和河流的捕魚活動。1898年起，過度捕撈引起的魚類減少使尼夫赫人多次陷入饑荒，迫使族人改變生活方式，引入俄式食物。<ref name=KAMrev/>同時，逐漸崛起的[[日本帝国|日本帝国]]也積極向該地區擴張。1905年，[[日俄戰爭|日俄戰爭]]結束，《[[朴次茅斯和约|朴次茅斯和约]]》规定日俄以北緯50度為界分治庫頁島。1880年代起，日本捕魚企業便得到許可前來捕魚，直到1948年方止。<ref name=KAMrev>Kaminaga, p.269</ref>清[[光緒|光緒]]年間，不少原住民（費雅喀、赫哲）以及滿人移民為躲避俄人迫害，紛紛逃回清國境內。

1922年[[蘇聯|蘇聯]]成立，蘇維埃政府改變了沙俄的政治措施，蘇聯官方用族人的自稱“尼夫赫”取代了“吉利亚克”的原稱呼<ref name=SNTrev> Shternberg and Grant, pp.184-194</ref>，成立過短暫存在的尼夫赫自治地區，并保障族人的大量捕魚權，直至1960年代廢止。<ref name=SNTrev/>然而，蘇聯的經濟政策對尼夫赫人是毀滅性的，他們被強迫於[[集體農莊|集體農莊]]從事工農業勞動。<ref name=KolM/>漁獵為生的尼夫赫人相信耕地是一種罪惡，對農業非常抵觸。<ref name=KolM/>因此，在大規模的俄羅斯族勞動力涌入之後，尼夫赫人淪為次等階層。<ref name=SNTrev/>

一系列外來影響之后，尼夫赫人的生活方式終于被不可逆轉地改變，傳統的漁獵、采集经济不復存在。<ref name=SNTrev/>蘇聯當局將其視為從[[原始社會|原始社會]]轉入[[社會主義|社會主義]]社會的樣板而宣傳。尼夫赫語被禁止在學校和公共場所使用，俄語則強制推行，族人俄化加速。許多尼夫赫民間故事、信仰、宗族關係都被新一代遺忘。<ref name=SNTrev/>1945年，苏联收复库页岛南部，至1948年，許多尼夫赫人連同半數以上的[[鄂罗克人|鄂罗克人]]、全部[[阿伊努人|阿伊努人]]和[[大和人|大和人]]被迫遷往[[北海道|北海道]]，后來方有部分返回。<ref> Chaussonner p.35 </ref>

新环境中的尼夫赫人逐漸依賴於集體制經濟。1962年，蘇聯當局將尼夫赫人重新安置<ref name=SNTrev/>，把眾多小村落的尼夫赫人集中到數個大的居民點，到1986年，薩哈林地區的尼夫赫居民點從原先的82個減少到13個。<ref>Mote, p.140.</ref>

1991年蘇聯解體，國家资助的集體農莊制度无法维持下去而廢止，尼夫赫人的處境更加艱難。<ref>Shternberg and Grant,  p.196 </ref>目前，庫頁島北部的尼夫赫人又受到欧洲企業的大型石油開發計劃的威脅。2005年1月以來，尼夫赫人推選{{tsl|en|Alexey Limanzo|阿列克謝·利曼佐}}領導展開非暴力抗議活動，爭取民族權益。<ref>[http://www.sakhalin.environment.ru/en/detail.php?slice=8b4cb37fba47da1c76cf3e44aa940cd2&sitemid=221211 "Oil majors attempt to suppress Sakhalin indigenous peoples' protest"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070314164639/http://www.sakhalin.environment.ru/en/detail.php?slice=8b4cb37fba47da1c76cf3e44aa940cd2&sitemid=221211 |date=2007-03-14 }} - ''Sakhalin Environment Watch ''（SEW） - (c/o www.sakhalin.environment.ru) - January 19, 2005</ref>

== 語言 ==
{{main|尼夫赫語}}
尼夫赫語（<span lang="ru">Нивхгу диф</span>）或稱吉利亚克語是一種[[孤立語言|孤立語言]]，有時歸類于[[古西伯利亞語言|古西伯利亞語]]。尼夫赫語的許多詞匯與[[阿伊奴語|阿伊奴語]]、[[朝鲜語|朝鲜語]]和[[阿爾泰語系|阿爾泰語系]]的語言相似，但并沒有規則的語音對應關係，因此這些相似的詞匯很可能是[[借詞|借詞]]。<ref name="Mattissen, p.515" />一說尼夫赫語與[[新羅語|新羅語]]、[[伽耶語|伽耶語]]相似。{{来源请求|time=2009-03-13}}

尼夫赫語可分為四種方言，分別為黑龍江方言，北庫頁方言，南庫頁方言和東庫頁方言。黑龍江方言與東庫頁方言使用者之間通話困難，北庫頁方言介于二者之間。<ref>[http://www.ethnologue.com/14/show_language.asp?code=NIV http://www.ethnologue.com/14/show_language.asp?code=NIV] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070821032412/http://www.ethnologue.com/14/show_language.asp?code=NIV |date=2007-08-21 }} 民族語網站對尼夫赫語的簡介</ref>

== 社會 ==
在《[[開元新志|開元新志]]》中，尼夫赫有四個部落：'''囊家儿'''、'''福里期'''、'''兀剌'''和'''纳衣'''。他們流行[[妻姊妹婚|妻姊妹婚]]，以妹為[[妾|妾]]。衣狗皮，食狗肉，夏天穿魚皮。尼夫赫人女多男少，十歲即結婚，女性先與狗結一次婚，再與人結婚。[[大明一統志|大明一統志]]说尼夫赫人分四部：累龍江下游一部，黑龍江入海口以北的海島一部，庫頁島上一部，[[鄂霍次克海|鄂霍次克海]]以北一部。與[[野人女真|野人女真]]雜居。夏天於海邊生活，冬季於山中生活。

=== 經濟 ===
尼夫赫人的傳統經濟是捕魚、捕獵海獸、林中狩獵和采集業，以及飼養大群的狗。狗是他們唯一的牲畜（戶中每人養3-5隻）。

捕魚是尼夫赫人最主要的食物來源。捕魚在全年都會進行，多捕撈[[洄游|洄游]]魚類，如[[鮭魚|鮭魚]]和[[鱘魚|鱘魚]]，并制作的魚干作為冬季的主要食物。近海的尼夫赫人還會捕獵海洋哺乳動物，特別是海豹和海獅，具體方法是劃船接近海獸，然后投出魚叉獵取。林中狩猎也是一个重要的生产部门，主要獵物有[[鹿|鹿]]、[[狐貍|狐貍]]、[[水獺|水獺]]、[[貂|貂]]等。獵鹿常在早春，獵取幼狐從夏季開始，捕貂則在深秋。<ref name="EAK">E·A·柯列伊诺维奇. 1973年</ref>狗的飼養非常引人注目，17世紀時，一些家族甚至飼養300-500只狗，可供捕獵和食用。<ref>{{en}}[http://www.npolar.no/ansipra/english/Indexpages/Ethnic_groups.html#19 http://www.npolar.no/ansipra/english/Indexpages/Ethnic_groups.html#19] 挪威基地研究所網站對尼夫赫人的介紹</ref>他們需要的糧食由最近的[[璦琿|璦琿]]地區使馬的[[達斡爾族|達斡爾族]]提供。

=== 村落 ===
尼夫赫人從事漁獵、采集的半游牧生活，有夏季和冬季的永久性定居點，通常由二至八個家系組成，以四家最為多見。較大規模的村落多集中在黑龍江口。這些定居點可持續使用多年，有時會被[[洪水|洪水]]沖垮。如1915年和1968年的洪水就破壞了許多尼夫赫人村落。深秋季節，村中男勞力均出門狩獵，女人則在林中采集食物。到冬天，人們移居到河畔的冬季居住點，捕撈[[鮭魚|鮭魚]]。他們很好客，在黑龍江上游居住的[[那乃人|那乃人]]在嚴冬常到尼夫赫人的村落停留。<ref name=SmoA/>

== 宗教 ==

尼夫赫人的信仰基于[[萬物有靈|萬物有靈]]，特別是通過薩滿教。俄羅斯殖民者到來后，盡力傳播[[俄羅斯東正教|俄羅斯東正教]]，許多尼夫赫人至少在形式上皈依了東正教。<ref> Reid, pp.156-157</ref>

=== 原始崇拜和熊祭 ===

古亞細亞民族信仰的一個特色是崇拜[[逆戟鯨|逆戟鯨]]。過去，尼夫赫人认为逆戟鲸是為人们驅送鲑鱼和海豹的神靈。俄罗斯人到來之前，他們从不捕食逆戟鲸，後來受俄罗斯人影响，尼夫赫人也逐渐開始捕食逆戟鲸。<ref name="EAK" />

尼夫赫人還崇拜火神，火神老夫婦住在爐火中央，因此對火的使用曾有許多禁忌。在春季出發捕猎海豹、秋季出發狩猎山兽之前，或在婴儿出生、族人去世之时，都要祭祀火神。<ref name="GuoZhang">郭天红，张嘉宾。尼夫赫人的原始崇拜和熊祭。黑龙江民族丛刊. 2004年第6期（总第83期）</ref>他們向水與水神祭祀，是其他民族中不多的。

世居黑龙江流域的诸民族，如[[鄂温克族|鄂温克族]]、[[鄂伦春族|鄂伦春族]]、[[赫哲族|赫哲族]]（那乃族）、[[乌尔奇人|乌尔奇人]]，以及生活在庫頁島的[[阿伊努人|阿伊努人]]等都曾奉行[[熊图腾崇拜|熊图腾崇拜]]。<ref name="EAK" />熊是作為神、祖先、圖騰、食物贈予者出現的聖獸。熊也是[[朝鮮人|朝鮮人]]、[[貊族|貊族]]的圖騰之一，[[檀君|檀君]]即為熊女所生，像徵韓民族其中一族源是北亞民族。{{fact|time=2009-3-25}}而在尼夫赫人、乌尔奇人和阿伊努人中，除這些習俗外，还舉行“熊祭”（或稱“熊節”、“熊靈祭”）。<ref name="EAK" />具體過程，是捕捉熊崽带回村中，由婦女照顧孩童般喂养大，<ref>Black, p. 94</ref>待幼熊长到2～3岁时，於一二月間，举行特殊的仪式，将熊杀死，然后按照一定規矩吃掉熊肉，并将熊头、熊骨处理好。<ref>Shternberg and Grant, p.160</ref>熊在北亞民族中被稱為父親，祖父，老人，爺爺。尼夫赫人相信每一氏族也有他們的熊，與他們有血緣關係。

清代学者[[曹廷杰|曹廷杰]]在其著作《西伯利东偏纪要》中記載，由于“济勒迷”（尼夫赫人）的影响，“不剃发黑斤”（[[乌尔奇人|乌尔奇人]]）也有了“弄熊为戲”的風俗。<ref name="GuoZhang">郭天红，张嘉宾。尼夫赫人的原始崇拜和熊祭。黑龙江民族丛刊. 2004年第6期（总第83期）</ref>

蘇聯存在期間，熊祭遭到限制，近年來又作為一個文化而非宗教節日而重新興起。<ref>Gall, pp.4-6</ref>他們也有犬崇拜，通古斯語族常說狗國可能指他們。

他们死后一般[[风葬|风葬]]或[[水葬|水葬]]。

=== 薩滿教 ===
對尼夫赫人來說，[[薩滿|薩滿]]的主要作用是驅邪治病。以源自動植物的材料制作藥品，并用護身符來防病。薩滿通常收取食物、石英等作為報償。<ref name=FREref> Friedrich and Diamond, p.283</ref>

===氏族===
Nivkh clans (''khal''）were a group of people united by marriage ties, a common derived deity, arranging marriages, and responsible for group dispute resolution. The clan is divided into three exogamous sub-clans. <ref name=CzaM> Czaplicka and Collins, pp.52-54 </ref>A clan would cooperate with other members on hunts and fishing when away from the village. <ref name=CzaM/> A Nivkh  clan believed they had "one (common) ''akhmalk'' or ''imgi'', one fire, one mountain man…one bear, one devil, one tkhusind (ransom, or clan penalty), and one sin." <ref name="Sternberg and Grant, p.154">Sternberg and Grant, p.154</ref>

===婚姻===
Marriage tended to be [[exogamic|exogamic]] unlike many paleo-Siberian groups.  Although within the clan, marriage is [[endogamic|endogamic]] while sub-clans are exogamic. <ref name=CzaM/> Nivkh marriage customs were very complicated and controlled by the clan. <ref name=CzaM/> Cross-cousin marriage seems to be the original custom with the clan a latter necessity when the clan was unable to marry individuals without breaking taboo. <ref name=CzaM/> The bride-price was probably introduced by the Neo-Siberians. <ref name=CzaM/> The dowry was shared by the clan. The number of men generally exceeded the number of women. It was hard to gain wives, as they were few and expensive. This would lead to the wealthier men having more than one wife and the poor men without. <ref name="Sternberg and Grant, p.154"/>

==宗教==
尼夫赫人的信仰基于[[萬物有靈|萬物有靈]]，特別是通過薩滿教。俄羅斯殖民者到來后，盡力傳播[[東正教|東正教]]。
Nivkh's traditional religion was based on [[animist|animist]] beliefs, especially via [[shamanism|shamanism]], before colonial Russians made efforts to convert the population to [[Eastern_Orthodox_Christianity|Eastern Orthodox Christianity]].<ref> Reid, pp.156-157</ref> Nivkh animists believe the island of Sakhalin is a giant beast lying on its belly with the trees of the island as its hair. When the beast is upset, it awakens and trembles the earth causing earthquakes. <ref name=CHArev/> Nivkh have a pantheon of vaguely defined gods (''yz, yzng'') that presided over the mountains, rivers, seas and sky.<ref>Friedrich and Diamond, p.285; Shternberg and Grant, p.158</ref> Nivkhs' have extensive folklore, songs, and mythos of how humans and the universe were created, and of how fantastic heroes, spirits and beasts battled with each other in ancient times. Some Nivkhs have converted to [[Russian_Orthodoxy|Russian Orthodoxy]] or other religions, though many still practice traditional beliefs. Fire is especially venerated. It is the symbol of the unity of the clan. Fire is considered a deity of their ancestors, protecting them from evil spirits and guarding their clan from harm. <ref>Shternberg and Grant, p.155-158</ref> An open flame would be 'fed' a leaf of tobacco, spices, or a tipple of vodka in order to please the spirits for protection.  Nivkhs would also frequently offer items to the deities by 'feeding'. <ref> Czaplicka and Collins, p.56; Shternberg and Grant, p.155-158 </ref> The sea would be 'fed' an item of importance in order that the sea god protect the travelers.

==環境==
The [[Russian_Far_East|Russian Far East]] has a cold and harsh climate. In the fish-rich [[Amur_River|Amur River]] estuary in the districts of Nixhne-Amruskii and Takhtinskii, winters have high winds and heavy snows with mid winter usually averaging from -28° to -20°C (-18° to -4°F). Summers are wet and moderately warm ranging between 16° and 20°C (61° to 68°F). The areas [[biome|biome]] is characterized as [[Taiga|Taiga]] and evergreen [[Pinophyta|coniferous forest]]s consisting of larch, yew, birch, maple, lilac, honeysuckle, and extensive low-lying swamp grasses. Higher elevations have spruce, fir, ash, lime, walnut and mountain tops have cedar and lichens. Bears, foxes, [[sable|sable]]s, hares, [[Siberian_tiger|Siberian tiger]]s, [[elk|elk]]s, [[grouse|grouse]], and deer typical near the Amur outlet which usually floods during the rainy season <ref>  Gall, p.1</ref><ref name=Fitzhugh-LE>Fitzhugh, William, and Durbreuil p.35 </ref>

Northern Sakhalin is harsher ecologically with mostly [[Taiga|Taiga]]. Winters are longer, with a mean temperature of -19°C (-5°F), however short summers are warmer averaging 15°C (59°F) due to warmer Pacific Ocean currents moving around the island. Heavy snows blanket the island of Sakhalin (''Yh-mif'' in [[Nivkh_language|Nivkh]]) during winter, due to monsoon winds blowing from Siberia, drawing humidity as they pass over the [[Sea_of_Okhotsk|Sea of Okhotsk]], [[Sea_of_Japan|Sea of Japan]], and the [[Strait_of_Tartary|Strait of Tartary]]. Barren [[tundra|tundra]] dominates the north, with sparse trees such as larch, birch and various grasses, while moving southward, spruce and fir are seen.  Bears, foxes, otters, [[lynx|lynx]], and [[reindeer|reindeer]] are common wildlife.<ref>Gall, p.2</ref><ref name=Fitzhugh-LE /> The Island's major rivers are the Tym' and Poronai, rich in fish especially Salmon. Before Russian colonization, Nivkh villages could be found on these rivers approximately every 5 km.<ref>Shternberg and Grant, XXXV</ref>

The Strait of Tartar is currently only 20 kilometers (12.5 miles) wide and is shallow enough that the divide is covered by an [[ice_road|ice bridge]] during the winter that can be traversed by foot or dog sled. At the glacial maximum of the Ice Age, sea levels were 100 meters (300 feet) lower than they are today. The [[Eurasia|Eurasia]] continent was connected to Sakhalin via the Strait of Tatar and [[Hokkaidō|Hokkaidō]] via the [[Soya_Strait|Soya Strait]] of which humans migrated. This connection explains the similarities of trees, plants, and animals including now extinct [[mammoth|mammoth]]s.  The receding ice age warmed the area allowing greater tree cover and wildlife, thus new resources for the Nivkhs to exploit. The opening of the Soya and then the shallower Tartar Strait allowed warm pacific currents to bathe the island and the lower Amur River.<ref>Fitzhugh, William, and  Durbreuil  pp.32-40 </ref>

==Technology==
===Dwellings===
Nivkhs lived in two types of self-built winter dwellings. Most ancient of these was the ''ryv'' (or ''to''). The dwelling was a round dugout about 7.5 meters (23 feet) in diameter, shored up by wooden poles and covered with packed dirt and grass. <ref name=GalT> Gall, pp.4-11 </ref> The ''ryv'' had a fireplace in the center and a smoke hole for light and smoke escape. The other type of dwelling used for winter is the ''chad ryv'' similar to the [[Nanai|Nanai]] ''dio'' which was modeled after Manchurian and Chinese dwellings of the Amur. <ref name=GalT/> The ''chad ryv'' were one-room structures with a [[gable|gable]] roof and a kan (Korean furnace) for heating. <ref name=SmoA/> A nearby shed held sledges, skis, boats, and dogs. <ref name=SmoA/>

===服飾===
尼夫赫人的傳統穿著為裙裝（男用稱“skiy，女用稱“hukht”），用三個扣於身體左側。<ref name=GalT/>冬衣為魚皮、海豹皮和其他陸生哺乳動物如狗的皮毛制作。Women's ''hukht'' extended below the knee and were light multicolored with intricate embroideries and various ornaments sewed on the sleeves, [[collar|collar]] and [[hem|hem]]. <ref name=GalT/> Ornaments were coins, bells, or beads made of wood, glass, or metal mostly originating from Manchurian and Chinese traders. <ref name=GalT/>  Men's ''skiy'' were darker colored, shorter,  and had pockets built into the sleeves. Men's clothing were less elaborate with ornaments on the sleeve and left [[lapel|lapel]]. Men would also wear a loose kilt called a ''kosk'' when hunting or traveling on dog sled.<ref name=GalT/> Boots were made of fish-, seal-, or deerskin, being very water tight. Fur hats (''hak''）were worn in winter, with the furry tails and ears of the animals used often adorning the back and crown of the hat. Summer hats (''hiv hak'') were conical made from birch-bark. After soviet collectivization, Nivkh mostly wear mass-produced Western clothing, but traditional clothing is worn for holidays and cultural events. <ref name=GalT/>

==飲食==
過去，尼夫赫人的主要食物是魚肉，如[[Pacific_Salmon|Pacific Salmon]], ([[Chum_Salmon|Chum]], [[Pink_Salmon|Pink]], Trout), Red Eye, [[burbot|burbot]] and pike found in rivers and streams. Salt water fishing provided [[saffron_cod|saffron cod]], [[flatfish|flatfish]], and [[Goby|marine goby]] caught in the [[littoral|littoral]] coasts of the [[Strait_of_Tartary|Strait of Tartary]], [[Sea_of_Okhotsk|Sea of Okhotsk]], and the [[Pacific_Ocean|Pacific Ocean]], though [[Fishing_capacity|over fishing]] by Russian and Japanese [[fishing_trawler|trawler]]s have depleted many of these fish stocks. Additionally, Chinese and Russian industrial pollution such as [[phenols|phenols]] and heavy metals in the Amur River have devastated fish stocks and damaged the [[Estuary|Estuaries']] soil. <ref> [http://www.npolar.no/ansipra/english/Indexpages/Ethnic_groups.html#19 "Indigenous Peoples of the Russian North, Siberia and Far East: Nivkh" ] by Arctic Network for the Support of the Indigenous Peoples of the Russian Arctic] </ref>傳統的保存方法''Yukola'', involving slicing the fish in a particular way and drying the strips by hanging them in the frigid air, without salt, was used before foreign influences.<ref name=SEIC>Smolyak, pp.174; [http://sakhalin-2.ru/en/documents/doc_38_sia_chp6.pdf "Indigenous Peoples' Consultation Programme :Social Impact Assessment"]. Sakhalin Energy Investment Company. p.13 - ([[Adobe_Acrobat|Adobe Acrobat]] *.PDF document)</ref> The preservation process created a lot of dried fish waste, unpalatable for human consumption but utilized for dog food. <ref name=SmoA> Smolyak, pp.174-180</ref> Pulverizing dried fish and mixing it with fish skins, water, seal fat, and berries until the mixture had a sour cream consistency is a favorite Nivkh dish called ''mos'''.<ref> [http://museum.sakh.com/eng/10.shtml "The Indigenous Peoples"] - The Sakhalin Regional Museum - Sakh.com</ref> Nivkhs would hunt seal (Larha, Reinged, Reibbon, Sea-lions), duck, sable, and otters. They would gather various berries, wild [[leek|leek]]s, [[lily|lily]]bulbs, and nuts.<ref name=SEIC />12世紀起，在與漢人、女真人、日本人等發生接觸之后，尼夫赫人開始使用[[糖|糖]]、[[鹽|鹽]]、[[小米|小米]]、[[蔬菜|蔬菜]]和[[茶葉|茶葉]]等。19世紀，俄羅斯人又引入了面粉、面包、馬鈴薯、伏特加、黃油等飲食。<ref>Gall, p.5</ref>
