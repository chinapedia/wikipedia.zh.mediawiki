'''尼尔·麦克唐纳'''（{{lang-en|'''Neal McDonough'''}}，{{bd|1966年|2月13日|||catIdx=M}}）美国知名男演员。<ref>{{cite news| url=http://pqasb.pqarchiver.com/bostonherald/access/68122029.html?dids=68122029:68122029&FMT=ABS&FMTS=ABS:FT&type=current&date=Jul+10%2C+1993&author=&pub=Boston+Herald&desc=OBITUARY+Catherine+McDonough%2C+owned+hotel+%26+store%2C+66&pqatl=google | work=Boston Herald | title=OBITUARY Catherine McDonough, owned hotel & store, 66 | date=July 10, 1993}}</ref>

==生平==
1966年，麦克唐纳出生在[[美国|美国]][[马萨诸塞州|马萨诸塞州]][[波士顿|波士顿]]，<ref name="FilmRef">{{cite web|url=http://www.filmreference.com/film/27/Neal-McDonough.html|title=Neal McDonough Biography (1966–)|work=filmreference.com}}</ref><ref name="McDonough1">{{cite web | last =Drew| first=April| title=McDonough Stars in The Guardian| publisher =IrishAbroad| year=2006| url =http://www.irishabroad.com/news/irishinamerica/entertainment/McDonoughStarsinTheGuardian.asp| accessdate =20 January 2007|work=IrishVoice}}</ref>母亲凯瑟琳，父亲弗兰克·麦克唐纳，他们都是[[爱尔兰|爱尔兰]]移民，信奉[[罗马天主教|罗马天主教]]。<ref name="IrishCentral">{{cite web|url=http://www.irishcentral.com/ent/Desperate-Housewives-star-Neal-McDonough-fired-from-new-show-for-refusing-sex-scenes--89765492.html|title='Housewives' Catholic star Neal McDonough fired, refused sex scenes|work=www.IrishCentral.com|last=Kelly|first=Antoinette|date=2 April 2010}}</ref>麦克唐纳毕业于[[雪城大学|雪城大学]]和[[倫敦音樂與戲劇藝術學院|倫敦音樂與戲劇藝術學院]]。

==家庭==
2003年，麦克唐纳和瑞文·罗伯逊结婚，两人育有5个孩子。
