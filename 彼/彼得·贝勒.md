'''彼得·贝勒'''（{{lang-en|'''Peter Bellew'''}}），是一名擁有超過30年經驗的[[爱尔兰|爱尔兰]]籍航空业老手<ref name=":6">{{Cite web|url=http://www.malaysiandigest.com/features/619781-who-is-peter-bellew-malaysia-airlines-new-ceo.html|title=Who Is Peter Bellew, Malaysia Airlines New CEO?|access-date=2016-07-07|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160704033513/http://www.malaysiandigest.com/features/619781-who-is-peter-bellew-malaysia-airlines-new-ceo.html|archive-date=2016-07-04|dead-url=yes}}</ref><ref name=":5">{{Cite web|url=http://www.irishexaminer.com/business/peter-bellew-joins-malaysia-air-board-as-christoph-muller-departs-394231.html|title=Peter Bellew joins Malaysia Air board as Christoph Müller departs|access-date=2016-07-07}}</ref>，前任[[马来西亚航空|马来西亚航空]][[首席执行长|首席执行长]]兼[[董事总经理|董事总经理]]。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.thestar.com.my/business/business-news/2016/06/30/bellew-new-group-md-and-ceo-of-malaysia-airlines/|title=Bellew new group MD and CEO of Malaysia Airlines - Business News {{!}} The Star Online|website=www.thestar.com.my|access-date=2016-07-07}}</ref><ref name=":4">{{Cite web|url=http://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/business/peter-bellew-named-ceo-of/2918804.html|title=Peter Bellew named CEO of Malaysia Airlines|access-date=2016-07-07}}</ref>

2016年7月1日，他从原任马来西亚航空[[首席运营长|首席运营长]]（COO）调职成为[[首席执行长|首席执行长]]（CEO）以及[[董事总经理|董事总经理]]（MD）以顶替[[克里斯托·穆勒|克里斯托·穆勒]]（{{lang|en|Christoph R. Mueller}}）辞职后的空缺。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.theguardian.com/world/2016/apr/20/malaysia-airlines-ceo-christoph-mueller-resigns|title=Malaysia Airlines CEO Christoph Mueller resigns|last=Lumpur|first=Associated Press in Kuala|date=2016-04-20|website=the Guardian|access-date=2016-07-07}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.thestar.com.my/business/business-news/2016/04/20/the-show-must-go-on/|title=MAS CEO and MD Mueller quits in Sept ahead of his 3-year contract - Business News {{!}} The Star Online|website=www.thestar.com.my|access-date=2016-07-07}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://money.cnn.com/2016/04/20/news/companies/malaysia-airlines-ceo-quits-mueller/index.html|title=Malaysia Airlines CEO Christoph Mueller quits in middle of turnaround plan|last=Mullen|first=Jethro|date=2016-04-20|website=CNNMoney|access-date=2016-07-07}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://time.com/4300336/christoph-mueller-malaysia-airlines-mas-resign/|title=Malaysia Airlines CEO Resigns After Less Than a Year|last=Jenkins|first=Nash|website=TIME.com|access-date=2016-07-07}}</ref>
