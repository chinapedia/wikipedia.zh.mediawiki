'''霍里奇'''（Horwich，{{IPAc-en|ˈ|h|ɒr|ɪ|tʃ}}）是[[英格蘭|英格蘭]][[大曼徹斯特|大曼徹斯特]]地區的一個城市<ref name="GM Gazetteer H">{{citation|url=http://www.gmcro.co.uk/Guides/Gazeteer/gazzg.htm|title=Greater Manchester Gazetteer|publisher=Greater Manchester County Record Office|accessdate=12 June 2010|archivedate=18 七月 2011|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110718144437/http://www.gmcro.co.uk/Guides/Gazeteer/gazzg.htm|deadurl=yes}}</ref><ref name="map">{{citation|url=http://www.genuki.org.uk/big/eng/LAN/Horwich/ParishMap.shtml|title=Horwich Township Map|publisher=genuki.org |accessdate=6 June 2010}}</ref>，位於[[蘭開夏郡|蘭開夏郡]]。據2001年英國人口普查，霍里奇有人口19,312人<ref>{{citation|url=http://neighbourhood.statistics.gov.uk/dissemination/LeadAreaSearch.do?a=3&c=Horwich&i=1001&m=0&enc=1&areaSearchText=Horwich&areaSearchType=16&extendedList=true&searchAreas=Search|title=Horwich CP (Parish)|author=United Kingdom Census 2001|publisher=neighbourhood.statistics.gov.uk|accessdate=12 April 2007|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110525193621/http://neighbourhood.statistics.gov.uk/dissemination/LeadAreaSearch.do?a=3&c=Horwich&i=1001&m=0&enc=1&areaSearchText=Horwich&areaSearchType=16&extendedList=true&searchAreas=Search|archivedate=25 五月 2011}} </ref>。2011年增加到25,068人<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.neighbourhood.statistics.gov.uk/dissemination/LeadKeyFigures.do?a=7&b=13689167&c=horwich+and+blackrod&d=14&e=62&g=6340758&i=1001x1003x1032x1004&m=0&r=1&s=1451836091135&enc=1|title=Bolton Ward (Horwich and Blackrod) population 2011|accessdate=3 January 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.neighbourhood.statistics.gov.uk/dissemination/LeadKeyFigures.do?a=7&b=13689168&c=horwich&d=14&e=62&g=6341268&i=1001x1003x1032x1004&m=0&r=1&s=1451836383166&enc=1|title=Bolton Ward (Horwich North East) population 2011|accessdate=3 January 2015}}</ref>。
