'''霍桑大桥'''（{{lang-en|Hawthorne Bridge}}）是[[波特蘭_(俄勒岡州)|俄勒冈州波特兰市]]的一座跨越[[威拉米特河|威拉米特河]]的[[桁架桥|桁架]]升降桥，也是[[美国|美国]]最古老的垂直升降桥<ref name="Wortman">{{Cite book|title=The Portland Bridge Book|last=Wood Wortman|first=Sharon|last2=Wortman, Ed|publisher=Urban Adventure Press|year=2006|isbn=0-9787365-1-6|edition=3rd|pages=61–68}}</ref> 和波特兰最古老的公路桥<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.portlandoregon.gov/transportation/article/386265|title=2011 Bicycle Counts Report|accessdate=April 24, 2013|format=PDF|publisher=Portland Bureau of Transportation}}</ref><ref name="Wortman">{{Cite book|title=The Portland Bridge Book|last=Wood Wortman|first=Sharon|last2=Wortman, Ed|publisher=Urban Adventure Press|year=2006|isbn=0-9787365-1-6|edition=3rd|pages=61–68}}</ref>，于2012年列入[[國家史蹟名錄|國家史蹟名錄]]<ref name="nrhp-weekly">{{Cite web|url=http://www.nps.gov/history/nr/listings/20121123.htm|title=Weekly list of actions taken on properties: 11/13/12 through 11/16/12|accessdate=April 24, 2013|date=November 23, 2012|publisher=[[National_Park_Service|National Park Service]]}}</ref><ref name="oreg-2012nov">{{Cite news|url=http://www.oregonlive.com/portland/index.ssf/2012/11/four_multnomah_county_bridges.html|title=Four Multnomah County bridges listed on National Register of Historic Places|last=Tims|first=Dana|date=November 20, 2012<!--(print edition of Nov. 21, page B1)-->|newspaper=The Oregonian|accessdate=December 9, 2014}}</ref>。
