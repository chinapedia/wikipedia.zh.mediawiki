'''泰国银行'''（{{lang-th|ธนาคารแห่งประเทศไทย}}，简称'''BOT'''）系[[泰王国|泰王国]]之[[中央银行|中央银行]]，总部设于[[曼谷|曼谷]]<ref name=lianxifangshi/>。其成立之初为'''泰国国家银监局'''，后到1942年4月28日政府发布《1942年泰国银行法案》（{{lang-en|Bank of Thailand Act, B.E.2485}}），法案规定将所有中央银行之职能全部划归新设之泰国银行，同年12月10日，泰国银行正式开始运作<ref name=jianlishijian/>。

== 历史 ==

{{参见|泰国历史|第二次世界大戰期間的泰國}}

[[File:Bangkhunprom_Palace.JPG|thumb]]

16世紀初，歐洲列強开始來到[[中南半岛|中南半岛]]。至19世紀末，[[英国|英国]]及[[法国|法国]]已經分別在[[暹羅|暹羅]]東西兩邊分别建立[[英屬印度|英屬印度]]和[[法屬印度支那|法屬印度支那]]。19世纪中期，即[[泰國君主列表|泰王]][[拉瑪五世|拉瑪五世]]及[[拉瑪六世|拉瑪六世]]统治时期，当时西方列强数次试图为[[暹羅|暹羅]]建立一个央行以发行[[货币|货币]]。然而[[泰王|泰王]]相信这些权力必须保留给泰国独立行使，而当时也缺乏经验及专业知识去设立，因此建立计划被搁置。<ref name="lishi1">{{Cite web|url =http://www.bot.or.th/English/AboutBOT/History/Pages/History.aspx|format =|title =History of the Bank of Thailand|author =|date =|publisher =泰国银行官网|language =en|accessdate =2014-11-19|quote =The reign of King Rama IV was characterised by fostering relations and trade of Siam with foreign powers. A significant milestone of these relations was the signing of a diplomatic and commercial treaty with England by King Rama IV. The so-called “Bowring Treaty” opened doors of Siam to the world, and also led to numerous similar treaties with other countries. With increasing ties to the West under the reigns of Kings Rama V and VI, there were several attempts by these western powers to establish a central bank for Siam to issue bank notes which would bring about substantial benefits.  Yet these attempts did not materialise as the Siamese believed that these powers intended to reap the benefits for themselves.  However, it was believed that in principle, having a central bank would serve as a central medium for trade and economy, but the project was shelved due to the lack of experience and expertise at that time.|deadurl =yes|archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20141113013516/http://www.bot.or.th/English/AboutBOT/History/Pages/History.aspx|archivedate =2014-11-13}}</ref>
<br>二十世纪三十年代，[[美国|美国]]爆发[[經濟大恐慌|經濟大恐慌]]並席捲了全世界，直接导致了[[暹罗|暹罗]]1932年6月的[[:en:Siamese_revolution_of_1932|不流血宪政革命]]，而[[暹罗|暹罗]]自此成為[[君主立憲制|君主立憲制]]國家。此时新成立的政府也因经济危机，认为有必要建立一个[[中央银行|中央银行]]以作为一个关键机构来实施国家的[[经济政策|经济政策]]。但是此时的[[暹罗|暹罗]]国内并没有一个完善的银行体系，也没有足够的资金及经验，因此“国家银行建立法案”在起草阶段就被放弃，没有公布。<ref name="lishi2">{{Cite web|url =http://www.bot.or.th/English/AboutBOT/History/Pages/History.aspx|format =|title =History of the Bank of Thailand|author =|date =|publisher =泰国银行官网|language =en|accessdate =2014-11-19|quote =Shortly after that, the plan to have central bank was revived, when Phraya Phahon Phon Phayuhasena seized power on 20 June 1933.  However, the Minister of Finance consulted with James Bexter, the fiscal adviser to the Government at that time, who felt that it was not time to establish a central bank, given the lack of experts on banking issues in Siam, not to mention the lack of capital and the lack of a commercial banking system in the country. The Government pushed forward the project to establish a central bank again in 1935. The Cabinet resolved to have the Ministry of Finance to propose a bill on the Establishment of the National Bank, B.E. 2478 (1935), drafted by Luang Voranitipricha. The bill proposed to incorporate the Siam Commercial Bank Ltd as the national bank.  With just a mere eight articles, the bill was regarded as incomprehensive and lacking in details by the Comptroller-General, hence it was not promulgated.|deadurl =yes|archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20141113013516/http://www.bot.or.th/English/AboutBOT/History/Pages/History.aspx|archivedate =2014年11月13日}}</ref>
<br>到了1935年10月26日，[[泰国国会|泰国国会]]通过了《泰国国家银监局建立法案》（{{lang-en|Act on the Establishment of the Thai National Banking Bureau}}）。随后'''泰国国家银监局'''於1940年5月13日开始运营。但此时[[二战|二战]]已经爆发一年之久，[[日本|日本]]於1941年12月7日發動[[太平洋戰爭|太平洋戰爭]]，由于已经与[[泰國|泰國]]事先簽訂了《{{link-en|日泰同盟條約|Treaty between Thailand and Japan (1940)}}》，日本并没有入侵泰国，但是日本向泰国政府要求建立一个由日本籍人士担任顾问的中央银行，泰国政府拒绝了日本的要求，但不久后於1942年4月28日发布了《1942年泰国银行法案》（{{lang-en|Bank of Thailand Act, B.E.2485}}），废除了原泰国国家银监局，将所有中央银行之职能全部划归新设之泰国银行<ref name="lishi6">{{Cite web|url =http://www.bot.or.th/English/AboutBOT/History/Pages/History.aspx|format =|title =History of the Bank of Thailand|author =|date =|publisher =泰国银行官网|language =en|accessdate =2014-11-19|quote =The Thai National Banking Bureau started its operations on 13 May 1940. The official opening ceremony was held on 24 June 1940, which coincided with the National Day then.  It was only after more than a year’s operation of the Bureau when the Asia-Pacific Theatre of the Second World War erupted.  Japanese troops landed on Thai shores on 8 December 1941. The invading Japanese demanded that the Thai Government set up a central bank comprising Japanese advisers and department heads. Of course the Thai Government could not accept such conditions and requested that Prince Viwat draft a bill to change the status of the Thai National Banking Bureau to a central bank, with immediate effect.  Hence the “Bank of Thailand Act” was promulgated on 16 April 1942. The inauguration ceremonies were held on 10 December 1942, which was Constitution Day. On the following day, the Bank of Thailand started its operations at the former offices of the Hong Kong and Shanghai Banking Corporation on Siphraya Road. Prince Viwat assumed the position of the first Governor.|deadurl =yes|archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20141113013516/http://www.bot.or.th/English/AboutBOT/History/Pages/History.aspx|archivedate =2014年11月13日}}</ref>。同年12月10日，'''泰国银行'''正式开始运作，沿袭至今<ref name=jianlishijian/>。
