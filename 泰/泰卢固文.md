'''泰盧固文'''是[[婆羅米系文字|婆羅米系]][[元音附標文字|元音附標文字]]，用來書寫[[泰盧固語|泰盧固語]]，它是在南印度[[安得拉邦|安得拉邦]]和一些臨近邦中使用的中南部[[達羅毘荼語系|達羅毘荼語]]。泰盧固文派生自[[泰盧固-卡納達文|泰盧固-卡納達文]]<ref>
{{cite web| last =Bhat| first =Raghunath| authorlink =| title =PALAEOGRAPHIC ART OF INDIA AND SOUTH EAST ASIA| work =| publisher =IGNCA| date =2001| url =http://ignca.gov.in/nl001809.htm| format =| doi =| accessdate =2007-07-19| deadurl =yes| archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20070927121209/http://ignca.gov.in/nl001809.htm| archivedate =2007年9月27日| df =}}</ref>并與[[卡納達文|卡納達文]]同時獨立發展因此看起來很相像。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tdil.mit.gov.in/TelugulScriptDetailsApr02.pdf|title=Telugu script details|access-date=2010-04-10|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070926090446/http://tdil.mit.gov.in/TelugulScriptDetailsApr02.pdf|archive-date=2007-09-26|dead-url=yes}}</ref>

==派生自婆羅米文==
[[孔雀王朝|孔雀王朝]]所使用的[[婆羅米文|婆羅米文]]最終抵達了[[奎師那河|奎師那河]]三角洲并引發了在盛裝佛陀舍利的瓦罐上的[[Bhattiprolu文|Bhattiprolu文]]。<ref>Antiquity of Telugu language and script: http://www.hindu.com/2007/12/20/stories/2007122054820600.htm</ref><ref>[http://www.buddhavihara.in/ancient.htm Ananda Buddha Vihara] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070930085421/http://www.buddhavihara.in/ancient.htm |date=2007-09-30 }}</ref>佛教從[[Ghantasala|Ghantasala]]和[[默蘇利珀德姆|默蘇利珀德姆]]（[[托勒密|托勒密]]地圖的古代Maisolos和[[Periplus|Periplus]]圖的Masalia）的臨近港口傳播到東亞<ref>[http://www.wuys.com/news/Article_Show.asp?ArticleID=8303 The Great Stupa at Nagarjunakonda in Southern India]</ref>。Bhattiprolu文的傳播還引發了現代[[泰文|泰文]]<ref>[http://www.sepiamutiny.com/sepia/archives/002554.html My Thais - Sepia Mutiny]</ref>、[[緬甸文|緬甸文]]、[[爪哇文|爪哇文]]和[[巴厘文|巴厘文]]<ref>[http://www.omniglot.com/writing/balinese.htm Balinese alphabet, language and pronunciation]</ref>，它們看來很像泰盧固文。Bhattiprolu婆羅米文在公元5世紀演化成了古泰盧固-卡納達文。<ref>The Blackwell Encyclopedia of Writing Systems by Florian Coulmas, p. 228</ref><ref>Vishwabharath by K. N. Murthy and G. U. Rao, http://tdil.mit.gov.in/TelugulScriptDetailsApr02.pdf {{Wayback|url=http://tdil.mit.gov.in/TelugulScriptDetailsApr02.pdf |date=20070926090446 }}</ref><ref>Indiain Epigraphy: a guide to the study of inscriptions in Sanskrit, Prakrit, and the other Indo-Aryan languages, by Richard Solomon, Oxford University Press, 1998, p.40, ISBN 0195099842</ref><ref>Indian Epigraphy by Dineschandra Sircar, Motilal Banarsidass, 1996, p.46, ISBN 8120811666</ref><ref>The Dravidian Languages by Bhadriraju Krishnamurti, 2003, Cambridge University Press, pp.78-79, ISBN 0521771110</ref><ref>Comparative Dravidian linguistics: Current perspectives by Bhadriraju Krishnamurti. Oxford: Oxford University Press, 2001. ISBN 0198241224</ref><ref>K. Raghunath Bhat, {{cite web |url=http://ignca.gov.in/nl001809.htm |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2007年7月19日 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070927121209/http://ignca.gov.in/nl001809.htm |archivedate=2007年9月27日 |df= }}</ref>
