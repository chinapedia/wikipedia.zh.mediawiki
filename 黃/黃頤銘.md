== 早年生活 ==
黃頤銘生於[[美國|美國]][[華盛頓特區|華盛頓特區]]，其父親路易斯·黃（{{lang|en|Louis Huang}}）與母親潔西卡·黃（{{lang|en|Jessica Huang}}）皆為[[臺灣|臺灣]]移民。<ref name=NPR-FOtB-2013>{{cite news|last1=Martin|first1=Rachel|title='Fresh Off The == 職業生涯 ==
黃頤銘初入職場的第一份工作是在[[紐約|紐約]]的{{le|查德本·派克律師事務所|Chadbourne & Parke}}擔任律師，主要負責[[公司法|公司法]]。他曾在2006及2007年擔任暑期實習生，之後在2008年被公司法部門聘用擔任正職。不到一年便發生了[[2007年–2008年環球金融危機|金融危機]]，黃頤銘因此被解僱，並開始表演[[獨角喜劇|獨角喜劇]]及擔任[[大麻_(藥用)|大麻]]販子。<ref name=NYTimes-Defies-Profile-2013>{{cite news|last=Stein|first=Joshua David|title=Chef Who Refuses to Be Defined by His Wok|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2013/01/24/fashion/eddie-huang-defies-description.html|accessdate=2013-01-26|newspaper=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|date=2013-01-23|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130124183539/http://www.nytimes.com/2013/01/24/fashion/eddie-huang-defies-description.html|archivedate=2013-01-24|deadurl=no}}</ref>

=== 服裝設計師 ===
2006至2009年間，黃頤銘經營一間名叫「胡德曼服飾」（{{lang|en|Hoodman Clothing}}）的街頭服飾公司，這間公司最初也被稱作「波道夫·胡德曼」（{{lang|en|Bergdorf Hoodman}}）。<ref name=UPROXX-Hoodman-2008>{{cite web|last1=TSS Crew|title=Hoodman Clothing|url=http://uproxx.com/smokingsection/2008/12/hoodman-clothing/|website=UPROXX|accessdate=2015-04-21|date=2008-12-01|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150402141243/http://uproxx.com/smokingsection/2008/12/hoodman-clothing/|archivedate=2015-04-21|deadurl=no}}</ref><ref name=SiliconPrairie-BigOmaha-Kaneko-2012>{{cite news|last1=Koch|first1=Macy|title=Eddie Huang: "I'm about getting paper, but I need a 'why'"|url=http://siliconprairienews.com/2012/05/eddie-huang-i-m-about-getting-paper-but-i-need-a-why/|accessdate=2015-04-21|work=Silicon Prairie News|date=2012-05-11|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150402115023/http://siliconprairienews.com/2012/05/eddie-huang-i-m-about-getting-paper-but-i-need-a-why/|archivedate=2015-04-02|deadurl=no}}</ref>在此期間，他與一位在台灣認識的{{le|平面設計師|Graphic designer}}寧·莊（{{lang|en|Ning Juang}}）合作設計衣服。<ref name=AllHipHop-Hoodman-2007>{{cite web|last1=Paine|first1=Jake|title=Hoodman Clothing: Politics as Usual|url=http://allhiphop.com/2007/06/20/hoodman-clothing-politics-as-usual/|website=AllHipHop|accessdate=2015-04-21|date=2007-06-20|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304105451/http://allhiphop.com/2007/06/20/hoodman-clothing-politics-as-usual/|archivedate=2016-03-04|deadurl=no}}</ref>

=== 廚師與餐館老闆 ===
黃頤銘從小在家看著媽媽做菜長大，因此對食物十分感興趣。他也向許多在他父親餐廳任職，具有不同文化背景及烹調風格的廚師學習烹飪技巧。他學習管理餐廳，以及如何成為一位好的「跑單員」（即餐廳中負責在前台與後台間傳遞資訊的人員），以確保出菜的順序及時間，盡可能高效率且快速地將最好的品質呈現給顧客。他從在奧蘭多地區開過數家餐廳的父親那，學得做為一位跑單員該有的技巧。<ref name=FoodRepublic-Bao-2011>{{cite news|last1=Nordee|first1=Emily|title=Talking With Eddie Huang: The bad-boy restaurateur takes a Bao|url=http://www.foodrepublic.com/2011/03/28/talking-eddie-huang|accessdate=2015-04-21|work=Food Republic|date=2011-03-28|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150512024551/http://www.foodrepublic.com/2011/03/28/talking-eddie-huang|archivedate=2015-05-12|deadurl=no}}</ref>2011年，黃頤銘登上Chow.com年度美食界具影響力人物的Chow 13榜。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://eatocracy.cnn.com/2011/11/03/chow-13-honorees-a-sneak-peek/ |title=Chow 13 honorees - a sneak peek |work=CNN.com |publisher=Cable News Network |date=2011-11-03 |accessdate=2016-04-23 |language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151001082323/http://eatocracy.cnn.com/2011/11/03/chow-13-honorees-a-sneak-peek/|archivedate=2015-10-01|deadurl=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last=Birdsall |first=John |url=http://www.chowhound.com/food-news/95112/the-2011-chow-13/2/#wp_content |title=The 2011 CHOW 13 |work=Chowhound |publisher=CBS Interactive |date=2011-11-03 |accessdate=2016-04-23 |language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160306100216/http://www.chowhound.com/food-news/95112/the-2011-chow-13/2/#wp_content|archivedate=2016-03-06|deadurl=no}}</ref>

==== 餐廳 ====
2009年12月，黃頤銘在[[曼哈頓下城|曼哈頓下城]]的[[下東城|下東城]]成立一間[[台灣小吃|台式]][[割包|割包]]餐廳BaoHaus。<ref>{{cite news|last=Turner|first=David|url=http://www.rollingstone.com/culture/features/rap-snacks-inside-the-hip-hop-restaurant-boom-20151006|title= Rap Snacks: Inside the Hip-Hop Restaurant Boom|work= ''Rolling Stone''|date=2015-10-08|accessdate=2016-04-23|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160408104254/http://www.rollingstone.com/culture/features/rap-snacks-inside-the-hip-hop-restaurant-boom-20151006|archivedate=2016-04-08|deadurl=no}}</ref>2011年7月，他將他的第一家店遷至[[東村_(紐約市)|東村]][[14街|14街]]238號，並且擴展菜單內容。<ref name=NYEater-Baohaus-2011>{{cite web|url=http://ny.eater.com/archives/2011/07/eddie_huang_opening_east_village_baohaus_this_month.php|title=Eddie Huang Opening East Village Location of BaoHaus|work=New York Eater|date=2011-07-14|accessdate=2014-03-01|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120322063536/http://ny.eater.com/archives/2011/07/eddie_huang_opening_east_village_baohaus_this_month.php|archivedate=2012-03-22|deadurl=no}}</ref>另一家餐廳「宵夜」（{{lang|en|Xiao Ye}}）卻沒有辦法複製BaoHaus的成功，並在販售[[Four_Loko|Four Loko]]的爭議下關門大吉。<ref name=NYObserver-XioYe-2015>{{cite news|last=Freeman|first=Nate|title=Xiao Ye, Eddie Huang's Bastion of Four Loko Has Shut Down|url=http://observer.com/2010/11/xiao-ye-eddie-huangs-bastion-of-four-loko-has-shut-down|date=2010-11-30|newspaper=The New York Observer|accessdate=2015-04-01|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160308080428/http://observer.com/2010/11/xiao-ye-eddie-huangs-bastion-of-four-loko-has-shut-down/|archivedate=2016-03-08|deadurl=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last=Yang |first=Wesley |url=http://cn.nytstyle.com/people/20150226/t26huang/ |title=《初來乍到》原型黃頤銘，打破亞裔模範迷思 |work=紐約時報中文網 |publisher=The New York Times |date=2015-02-08 |accessdate=2016-04-23 |language=zh-cn|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150617024514/http://cn.nytstyle.com/people/20150226/t26huang/|archivedate=2015-06-17|deadurl=no}}</ref>

=== 作家 ===

黃頤銘曾在2011年底主持{{le|烹飪頻道|Cooking Channel}}的《廉價美食》節目，也上過幾集《{{le|獨家美食|Unique Eats}}》。之後，他離開烹飪頻道，為《[[Vice_(雜誌)|Vice]]》主持《初來乍到》節目，隨後更名為《黃的世界》（{{lang|en|''Huang's World''}}）。<ref name=NYTimes-FOtB-review-2013 /><ref name=HighSnobiety-HuangsWorld-2014>{{cite web|url=http://www.highsnobiety.com/2014/08/02/eddie-huang-to-premiere-huangs-world-on-munchies|title=Eddie Huang to Premiere 'Huang's World' on MUNCHIES|last1=Banks|first1=Alec|date=2014-08-02|website=Highsnobiety|accessdate=2014-11-06|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160307103607/http://www.highsnobiety.com/2014/08/02/eddie-huang-to-premiere-huangs-world-on-munchies/|archivedate=2016-03-07|deadurl=no}}</ref>

==== 《菜鳥新移民》 ====

===黑人婦女言論===
2015年5月，黃頤銘在《[[馬赫脱口秀|馬赫脱口秀]]》發表有關黑人婦女的言論引發批評。他說：「在美國，我認為亞裔的男子氣概一直被過度弱化，基本上，我們被看作是黑人婦女一般。」隨後，他利用自己的[[Twitter|Twitter]]帳號@MrEddieHuang與@BlackGirlDanger交流，並對自己的言論被看作是{{le|厭黑人女性症|Misogynoir|仇視黑人女性}}作辯解。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Chu|first1=Arthur|title=Eddie Huang self-destructs: Why the "Fresh Off the Boat" author's descent into misogyny is so depressing|url=http://www.salon.com/2015/05/15/eddie_huang_self_destructs_why_the_fresh_off_the_boat_authors_descent_into_misogyny_is_so_depressing_partner/|work=Salon|date=2015-05-15|accessdate=2015-05-15|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160314170832/http://www.salon.com/2015/05/15/eddie_huang_self_destructs_why_the_fresh_off_the_boat_authors_descent_into_misogyny_is_so_depressing_partner/|archivedate=2016-03-14|deadurl=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Ting|first1=Jenevieve|title=We Need to Talk About Eddie Huang's Misogyny|url=http://msmagazine.com/blog/2015/05/11/we-need-to-talk-about-eddie-huangs-misogyny/|work=Ms.|date=2015-05-11|accessdate=2015-05-11|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150913142331/http://msmagazine.com/blog/2015/05/11/we-need-to-talk-about-eddie-huangs-misogyny/|archivedate=2015-09-13|deadurl=no}}</ref>

===唱美國國歌笑場===
2017年黃頤銘在美國麻州錄製<<黃的世界>>，應邀於美國業餘球隊Yarmouth–Dennis Red Sox主場唱美國國歌，由於在唱國歌時因為緊張頻頻笑場，導致唱完美國國歌後與現場主辦單位白人發生嚴重口角，被指責唱歌嬉笑不尊重美國國歌，黃頤銘極力澄清他不是專業歌手已努力演唱並無藐視之義，但還是當場被主辦單位以髒話怒罵趕出球場。
