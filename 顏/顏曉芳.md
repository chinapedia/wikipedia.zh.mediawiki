'''顏曉芳'''（{{lang-en|Gan Siow Huang}}），[[新加坡共和國空軍部隊|新加坡共和國空軍部隊]][[將領|將領]]，也是[[新加坡武裝部隊|新加坡陸海空三軍]]首位[[女性軍人|女性將官]]<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/singapore/saf-promotes-first-female/1942582.html |title=Chan Luo Er, SAF promotes first female to Brigadier-General rank |date=2015-06-26 |publisher=Channel NewsAsia |accessdate=2016-10-08 |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20161124140334/http://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/singapore/saf-promotes-first-female/1942582.html |archivedate=2016-11-24 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>，於2015年7月1日以40歲之齡獲授[[准將|准將]]階級<ref name="first">{{cite web |url=http://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/saf-gets-its-first-female-general |author=Jeremyn Chow |title=SAF gets its first female general |publisher=The Straits Times |accessdate=2016-10-08 |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20161124140456/http://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/saf-gets-its-first-female-general |archivedate=2016-11-24 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。顏曉芳於軍中擔任過作戰部隊、情報、人力等機構的主官或參謀人員，並曾赴[[英國|英國]]、[[美國|美國]]進修，晉昇將軍後亦繼續擔當司令職務。2016年10月，[[新加坡國會|新加坡國會]]議員{{Link-en|劉燕玲|Low Yen Ling}}對[[女權|女權]]組織演說時，稱顏氏為「突破『[[玻璃天花板|黃銅天花板]]』、成為新加坡部隊首位女將的顯赫例子」<ref>{{cite web | url = https://app.msf.gov.sg/Press-Room/Speech-by-Ms-Low-Yen-Ling-at-CEDAW-Forum-2015 | title = Speech by Ms Low Yen Ling, Parliamentary Secretary for Trade & Industry and Education at the CEDAW Forum 2015 “See Doors Open: Celebrating 20 Years of CEDAW" | publisher = Ministry of Social and Family Development | accessdate = 2016-10-30 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20161030225000/https://app.msf.gov.sg/Press-Room/Speech-by-Ms-Low-Yen-Ling-at-CEDAW-Forum-2015 | archivedate = 2016-10-30 }}</ref>。

== 學歷 ==
顏曉芳曾在[[維多利亞初級學院|維多利亞初級學院]]求學，後於1993年獲得{{Link-en|新加坡武裝部隊女性優異獎學金|Singapore Armed Forces Merit Scholarship (Women)}}，成為該獎項在新加坡最早的4位女性得主之一<ref name="Madam">{{Cite news |title=Ready For Take-Off, Madam General |publisher=[[海峽時報|The Straits Times]] |author=Jermyn Chow |date=2015-07-02 |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20161124162955/http://www.stjobs.sg/career-resources/personalities/ready-for-take-off-madam-general/a/216534 |archivedate=2016-11-24 |accessdate=2016-11-24 |url=http://www.stjobs.sg/career-resources/personalities/ready-for-take-off-madam-general/a/216534 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>，赴[[英國|英國]][[倫敦政經學院|倫敦政經學院]]攻讀理學學士學位，並在2003年修習{{Link-en|紐西蘭國防學院|New Zealand Defence College}}指揮參謀課程<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.mindef.gov.sg/imindef/publications/pointer/journals/2006/v32n1/features/feature4.html  | title = Chin, Gan and Ng, Making a Difference: RSAF's Role in Peacetime Operations |publisher=Journal of the Singapore Armed Forces (2006, Vol 32, No.1) |accessdate=2016-10-08 |archivedate=2016-10-09 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161009165757/https://www.mindef.gov.sg/imindef/publications/pointer/journals/2006/v32n1/features/feature4.html}}</ref>。2010年，她亦經由[[美國|美國]][[麻省理工學院|麻省理工學院]][[MIT史隆管理學院|史隆管理學院]]的研究員計畫成為[[工商管理碩士|工商管理碩士]]<ref>{{cite web | url = http://mitsloan.mit.edu/fellows/fellows-present-past/class-of-2010/ | title = Profile of Siow Huang Gan (Class of 2010), MIT Sloan Fellows Program, |accessdate=2016-10-08 |archivedate=2016-10-09 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161009210026/http://mitsloan.mit.edu/fellows/fellows-present-past/class-of-2010/ }}</ref>。

== 軍歷 ==
顏曉芳從軍後，經培訓成為{{Link-en|武器系統官|Weapon systems officer}}，在軍中歷任數項指揮官、參謀職務，包括第203中隊長、空中偵察與控制團（{{lang|en|Air Surveillance and Control Group}}）團長、[[新加坡國防部|新加坡國防部]]三軍人力處（{{lang|en|Joint Manpower Department}}）處長等職<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.todayonline.com/singapore/first-woman-be-made-brigadier-general-saf |title=Lynnette Tan, Sense of purpose drives SAF's first woman General |publisher={{Link-en|新加坡今日報|Today (Singapore newspaper)|TODAY}} |accessdate=2016-10-08 |archivedate=2016-11-24 |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20161124140342/http://www.todayonline.com/singapore/first-woman-be-made-brigadier-general-saf |deadurl=yes }}</ref><ref name="聯合早報">{{Cite news |title=颜晓芳成为我国首位女准将 |author=黄顺杰 |publisher=[[联合早报|联合早报]] |date=2015-06-26 |url=http://www.zaobao.com.sg/realtime/singapore/story20150626-496201 |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20161124164511/http://www.zaobao.com.sg/realtime/singapore/story20150626-496201 |archivedate=2016-11-24 |accessdate=2016-11-24 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>{{ref label|術語|註1|}}。

顏曉芳於2013年獲授{{Link-en|公共行政管理勳章軍事章|Pingat Pentadbiran Awam (Tentera)}}<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.pmo.gov.sg/national-day-awards/recipients?title=gan+siow+huang&year%5Bvalue%5D%5Byear%5D=&field_award_tid_1=All |title=Gan Siow Huang National Day Award (2013) |publisher=[[新加坡總理公署|Prime Minister's Office of Singapore]] |accessdate=2016-10-08 |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20161124165209/http://www.pmo.gov.sg/national-day-awards/recipients?title=gan+siow+huang&year%5Bvalue%5D%5Byear%5D=&field_award_tid_1=All |archivedate=2016-11-24 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。2015年6月26日，時為[[上校|上校]]的她透過新加坡國防部長[[黃永宏|黃永宏]]取得晉升證書，並自7月1日軍人節起擢升為准將<ref name="聯合早報"/>。昇上將官後，顏曉芳結束三軍人力處長一職之任期，改調為空軍情報處（{{lang|en|Air Intelligence Department}}）處長兼特別任務（{{lang|en|Special Projects}}）司長<ref name="Madam"/><ref name="聯合早報"/>，2016年又轉任空軍運作指揮部（{{lang|en|Air Power Generation Command}}）司令<ref>{{Cite web |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161124155958/https://www.gov.sg/sgdi/ministries/mindef/departments/sf/departments/af |archivedate=2016-11-24 |title=SAF FORMATIONS: Air Force |publisher=Singapore Government Directory |url=https://www.gov.sg/sgdi/ministries/mindef/departments/sf/departments/af |accessdate=2016-11-24}}</ref>{{ref label|術語|註1|}}。

== 個人生活 ==
顏曉芳的夫婿姓李，為[[新加坡共和國海軍部隊|新加坡共和國海軍部隊]]退役軍官，1994年憑武裝部隊獎學金赴英留學時與顏氏結識。兩人於1998年成婚後，膝下共育有三個女兒<ref>{{cite web |url=http://news.asiaone.com/print/News/Latest%2BNews/Singapore/Story/A1Story20130722-438997.html |title=High-flier: Pioneer scholarship holder also proud mum |publisher=AsiaOne |date=2013-07-23 |accessdate=2016-10-08 |archivedate=2016-11-24 |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20161124140313/http://news.asiaone.com/print/News/Latest%2BNews/Singapore/Story/A1Story20130722-438997.html |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。除軍職之外，顏曉芳亦兼任2016、17年度{{Link-en|新加坡女童軍|Girl Guides Singapore}}執行委員會委員<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.girlguides.org.sg/cos/o.x?c=/ggs/pagetree&func=view&rid=4720 | title = Singapore Girl Guides Executive Commitee | accessdate = 2016-10-24 | archivedate = 2016-11-24 | archiveurl = https://archive.is/20161124140355/http://www.girlguides.org.sg/cos/o.x?c=/ggs/pagetree&func=view&rid=4720 | deadurl = yes }}</ref>。
