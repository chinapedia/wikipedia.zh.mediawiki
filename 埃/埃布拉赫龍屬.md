'''埃布拉赫龍屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|''Ebrachosaurus''}}）是[[堅蜥目|堅蜥目]]的一屬<ref>Carroll, R.L. (1988). ''Vertebrate Paleontology and Evolution''. WH Freeman and Company, New York ISBN 0-7167-1822-7</ref>，化石發現於[[德國|德國]]的Blasensandstein地層，地質年代為[[三疊紀|三疊紀]]晚期。屬名是以化石發現地的[[埃布拉赫市|埃布拉赫市]]（Ebrach）為名<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Kuhn | first1 = O. | year = 1936 | title = Weitere Parasuchier und Labyrinthodonten aus dem Blasensandstein des mittleren Keuper von Ebrach | url = | journal = Palaeontographica | volume = 83 | issue = | pages = 61–98 }}</ref>。Blasensandstein地層發現的其他動物化石包含：[[離片錐目|離片錐目]]（{{lang|la|Temnospondyli}}）的[[寬額螈|寬額螈]]（''Metoposaurus''）、[[植龍目|植龍目]]的{{lang|la|''Francosuchus''}}<ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1016/j.jsames.2005.04.002 | last1 = Langer | first1 = M. C. | year = 2005 | title = Studies on continental Late Triassic tetrapod biochronology. II. The Ischigualastian and a Carnian global correlation | url = | journal = Journal of South American Earth Sciences | volume = 19 | issue = 2| pages = 219–239 }}</ref>。埃布拉赫龍常被歸類於其近親[[鍬鱗龍|鍬鱗龍]]的[[異名|異名]]<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Benton | first1 = M.J. | last2 = Walker | first2 = A.D. | year = 1985 | title = Palaeoecology, taphonomy, and dating of Permo-Triassic reptiles from Elgin, north-east Scotland | url = | journal = [[Palaeontology_(journal)|Palaeontology]] | volume = 28 | issue = | pages = 207–234 }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1080/02724634.1999.10011122 | last1 = Heckert | first1 = A. B. | last2 = Lucas | first2 = S. G. | year = 1999 | title = A new aetosaur (Reptilia: Archosauria) from the Upper Triassic of Texas and the phylogeny of aetosaurs | url = | journal = [[Journal_of_Vertebrate_Paleontology|Journal of Vertebrate Paleontology]] | volume = 19 | issue = 1| pages = 50–68 }}</ref><ref>Heckert, A. B., and Lucas, S. G. (2000). Taxonomy, phylogeny, biostratigraphy, biochronology, paleobiogeography, and evolution of the Late Triassic Aetosauria (Archosauria:Crurotarsi). ''Zentralblatt für Geologie und Paläontologie'' Teil I 1998 Heft 11-12, p. 1539-1587.</ref>。在[[第二次世界大戰|第二次世界大戰]]期間，埃布拉赫龍的標本遭到轟炸摧毀<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Lucas | first1 = S. G. | year = 2007 | title = Global Triassic tetrapod biostratigraphy and biochronology: 2007 status | url = | journal = New Mexico Museum of Natural History and Science Bulletin | volume = 41 | issue = | pages = 229–240 }}</ref>，所以目前無法確定牠們與其他堅蜥類的分類關係<ref>Parker, W. G. (2003). Description of a new specimen of ''Desmatosuchus haplocerus'' from the Late Triassic of Northern Arizona. Unpublished MS thesis. Northern Arizona University, Flagstaff. 315 pp. [http://www.miketaylor.org.uk/dino/nm/Parker2003-thesis-chapter8.pdf]</ref>。
