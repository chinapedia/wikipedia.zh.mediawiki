'''埃莉諾·蘭伯特'''（{{lang-en|'''Eleanor Lambert Berkson'''}}）（{{bd|1903年|08月10日|2003年|10月7日}}）是[[纽约时装周|紐約時裝週]]的創辦人，美國時尚界及公關界的核心人物。<ref name="Stahl Case Study: Eleanor Lambert Demand Creator">{{Cite web|url=http://www.valuewalk.com/2013/11/eleanor-lambert-demand-creator/|title=Stahl Case Study: Eleanor Lambert Demand Creator|accessdate=20 November 2013|date=18 November 2013|publisher=[[ValueWalk|ValueWalk]] LLC}}</ref><ref name="Eleanor Lambert believed in destiny">{{Cite web|url=http://www.cfda.com/about/history#!|title=Eleanor Lambert believed in destiny|accessdate=20 November 2013|author=Tiffany,  John|publisher=Council of Fashion Designers of America}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/914905/Eleanor-Lambert|title=Eleanor Lambert|accessdate=20 November 2013|publisher=Encyclopaedia Britannica}}</ref><ref name="Eleanor Lambert, Empress of Fashion, Dies at 100">{{Cite web|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2003/10/08/nyregion/eleanor-lambert-empress-of-fashion-dies-at-100.html|title=Eleanor Lambert, Empress of Fashion, Dies at 100|accessdate=21 November 2013|author=NEMY, ENID|date=October 8, 2003|publisher=The New York Times}}</ref>

== 個人生平 ==
蘭伯特出生於[[印第安纳州|印第安纳州]]的[[克劳福兹维尔_(印第安纳州)|克劳福兹维尔]]，<ref name="Eleanor Lambert believed in destiny" /><ref name="Eleanor Of Seventh Avenue: Where Fashion Week Came From">{{Cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/gioia-diliberto/eleanor-of-seventh-avenue_b_268619.html|title=Eleanor Of Seventh Avenue: Where Fashion Week Came From|accessdate=20 November 2013|author=Diliberto, Gioia|date=2009-09-09|publisher=Huffington Post}}</ref>於{{link-en|約翰·赫倫藝術學院|John Herron School of Art}}及[[芝加哥藝術學院|芝加哥藝術學院]]進修[[時裝|時裝]]。<ref name="Eleanor Lambert believed in destiny" /><ref name="Eleanor Of Seventh Avenue: Where Fashion Week Came From" />蘭伯特渴望成為一名雕塑家，卻進了廣告行業。<ref name="Telegraph10Oct2003">{{Cite web|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/obituaries/1443712/Eleanor-Lambert.html|title=Eleanor Lambert Obituary|accessdate=20 November 2013|date=10 October 2003|publisher=The Telegraph newspaper}}</ref>她在曼哈頓從事[[廣告代理商|廣告代理]]，主要為藝術家以及藝術畫廊服務。<ref name="Eleanor Lambert believed in destiny" /><ref name="Eleanor Of Seventh Avenue: Where Fashion Week Came From" />

她有過兩段婚姻：20世紀20年代，與第一任丈夫'''威爾斯·康納'''（{{lang-en|Wills Conner}}）結婚。<ref name="Eleanor Lambert, Empress of Fashion, Dies at 100" />1936年離婚後，與第二任丈夫'''西摩爾·伯克森'''（{{lang-en|Seymour Berkson}}）<ref name="Eleanor Lambert believed in destiny" />結婚，直至1959年西摩爾逝世。<ref name="Telegraph10Oct2003">{{Cite web|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/obituaries/1443712/Eleanor-Lambert.html|title=Eleanor Lambert Obituary|accessdate=20 November 2013|date=10 October 2003|publisher=The Telegraph newspaper}}</ref>她與西摩爾育有一個兒子，是著名詩人{{link-en|比爾·伯克森|Bill Berkson}}。<ref name="Telegraph10Oct2003">{{Cite web|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/obituaries/1443712/Eleanor-Lambert.html|title=Eleanor Lambert Obituary|accessdate=20 November 2013|date=10 October 2003|publisher=The Telegraph newspaper}}</ref>2003年10月7日，埃莉諾·蘭伯特於紐約市曼哈頓逝世。<ref name="Eleanor Lambert, Empress of Fashion, Dies at 100" />

== 事業 ==
[[File:Photograph_of_movie_stars_posing_with_President_Truman_and_his_family_at_the_White_House,_(front_row,_left_to_right)..._-_NARA_-_199330.tif|缩略图]]與[[杜魯門|杜魯門]]總統一家的合影：<br/>

]]
20世紀30年代中期，蘭伯特作為[[惠特尼美术馆|惠特尼美术馆]]首席媒體總監協助建立了[[現代藝術博物館|現代藝術博物館]]（MoMA），<ref name="Stahl Case Study: Eleanor Lambert Demand Creator">{{Cite web|url=http://www.valuewalk.com/2013/11/eleanor-lambert-demand-creator/|title=Stahl Case Study: Eleanor Lambert Demand Creator|accessdate=20 November 2013|date=18 November 2013|publisher=[[ValueWalk|ValueWalk]] LLC}}</ref><ref name="Eleanor Lambert believed in destiny" /><ref name="Eleanor Of Seventh Avenue: Where Fashion Week Came From" />展出了包括[[杰克逊·波洛克|傑克遜·波洛克]]、{{link-en|雅各布·愛潑斯坦|Jacob Epstein}}、[[野口勇|野口勇]]為代表的眾多藝術家的作品。<ref name="Eleanor Lambert believed in destiny" /><ref name="Eleanor Of Seventh Avenue: Where Fashion Week Came From" />

20世紀40年代，蘭伯特創立了「'''{{link-en|國際最佳着裝名單|International Best Dressed List}}'''」，1943年創立了「'''{{link-en|科蒂美國時尚評論家獎|Coty Award}}'''」，發起[[纽约时装周|紐約時裝週]]。<ref name="Stahl Case Study: Eleanor Lambert Demand Creator">{{Cite web|url=http://www.valuewalk.com/2013/11/eleanor-lambert-demand-creator/|title=Stahl Case Study: Eleanor Lambert Demand Creator|accessdate=20 November 2013|date=18 November 2013|publisher=[[ValueWalk|ValueWalk]] LLC}}</ref><ref name="Eleanor Of Seventh Avenue: Where Fashion Week Came From" /><ref name="time">{{Cite web|url=http://content.time.com/time/specials/packages/article/0,28804,2110513_2110630_2110690,00.html|title=Eleanor Lambert|accessdate=20 November 2013|author=Skarda, Erin|date=April 2, 2012|publisher=Time magazine}}</ref><ref name="Author to Discuss Fashion Industry Impresario Eleanor Lambert, Feb. 2">{{Cite web|url=http://www.loc.gov/today/pr/2012/12-004.html|title=Author to Discuss Fashion Industry Impresario Eleanor Lambert, Feb. 2|accessdate=20 November 2013|date=January 3, 2012|publisher=Library of Congress}}</ref>1959年及1967年，[[美國國務院|美國國務院]]安排她到俄羅斯、德國、義大利、澳洲、日本、英國以及瑞士推廣美國時尚文化。<ref name="Stahl Case Study: Eleanor Lambert Demand Creator">{{Cite web|url=http://www.valuewalk.com/2013/11/eleanor-lambert-demand-creator/|title=Stahl Case Study: Eleanor Lambert Demand Creator|accessdate=20 November 2013|date=18 November 2013|publisher=[[ValueWalk|ValueWalk]] LLC}}</ref><ref name="time">{{Cite web|url=http://content.time.com/time/specials/packages/article/0,28804,2110513_2110630_2110690,00.html|title=Eleanor Lambert|accessdate=20 November 2013|author=Skarda, Erin|date=April 2, 2012|publisher=Time magazine}}</ref>

1962年，蘭伯特成立{{link-en|美國時裝設計師協會|Council of Fashion Designers of America}}（縮寫：{{lang|en|CFDA}}），是CFDA終身榮譽會員。<ref name="Eleanor Lambert believed in destiny" />1965年，蘭伯特被[[林登·约翰逊|約翰遜]]總統任命為「{{link-en|國家藝術基金會|National Endowment for the Arts}}」的「{{link-en|國家藝術組織|National Council on the Arts}}」成員。<ref name="Eleanor Lambert believed in destiny" />

2001年，CFDA創立“埃莉諾·蘭伯特獎”（{{lang-en|The Eleanor Lambert Award}}），用以表彰對世界時尚行業作出傑出貢獻的人。<ref name="Eleanor Lambert believed in destiny" />

在她去世前幾個月，蘭伯特將“{{link-en|國際最佳着裝名單|International Best Dressed List}}”交給了[[名利场_(杂志)|名利場]]的四位編輯。<ref name="Eleanor Lambert believed in destiny" />2003年9月，蘭伯特於2004春夏紐約時裝週公開露面後不久逝世，享嵩壽100歲。<ref name="Eleanor Lambert believed in destiny" /><ref name="Eleanor Lambert grave">{{Cite web|url=http://www.findagrave.com/cgi-bin/fg.cgi?page=gr&GRid=7959373|title=Eleanor Lambert (1903-2003)|accessdate=21 November 2013|author=Moody, Ron|date=October 8, 2003|publisher=Find a Grave.com}}</ref>她的孫子摩西·伯克森（{{lang-en|Moses Berkson}}）在她去世後拍了一部關於蘭伯特的[[紀錄片|紀錄片]]《埃莉諾——美國時尚教母》（{{Lang-en|Eleanor - Godmother of American Fashion}}）。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://vimeo.com/74978706|title=Eleanor - Godmother of American Fashion|accessdate=2016-11-22|author=Moses Berkson|date=|work=Vimeo|publisher=|quote=A short documentary film I directed about my Grandmother, Eleanor Lambert.}}</ref>
