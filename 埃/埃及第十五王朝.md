'''埃及第十五王朝'''是[[古埃及|古埃及]]历史上的一个王朝，為[[西克索人|西克索人]]所立，定都於[[阿瓦里斯|阿瓦里斯]]<ref>Holladay, John S. Jr. (1997) "The Eastern Nile Delta During the Hyksos and Pre-Hyksos Periods: Toward a Systemic/Socioeconomic Understanding", in {{cite book|author=Eliezer D. Oren|title=The Hyksos: new historical and archaeological perspectives|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=oiKLQgAACAAJ|year=1997|publisher=University Museum, University of Pennsylvania|isbn=978-0-924171-46-8|pages=183–252}}</ref>。本王朝存在時間從前1674年至前1535年，屬於[[第二中間期|第二中間期]]。現今人們常將其和[[埃及第十六王朝|第十六王朝]]與[[埃及第十七王朝|埃及第十七王朝]]談論在一起<ref name="ShadowShadowShadowShadowShadowShadowShadowShadowShadowShadowShadowShadowShadowShadowShadowShadow">{{cite book |editor-last=Shaw |editor-first=Ian |title=The Oxford History of Ancient Egypt |year=2000 |publisher=Oxford University Press |isbn=0-19-815034-2 |page=481 }}</ref>。
