== 背景 ==
'''埃里克·加纳'''（{{lang-en|Eric Garner}}，1970年9月15日－2014年），[[非裔美国人|非裔美国人]]<ref name="Sullivan">{{cite news | last =  Sullivan | first =  C. | date = July 18, 2014 | title = Man dies after suffering heart attack during arrest| url =  http://nypost.com/2014/07/18/man-dies-after-suffering-heart-attack-during-arrest/ | newspaper = New York Post | location = New York City | publisher = Jesse Angelo | accessdate = August 10, 2014 }}</ref><ref name="Goodman">{{cite news | last =  Goodman | first =  David | date = August 4, 2014 | title = Difficult Decisions Ahead in Responding to Police Chokehold Homicide| url =  http://www.nytimes.com/2014/08/05/nyregion/after-eric-garner-chokehold-prosecuting-police-is-an-option.html | newspaper = The New York Times | location = New York City | publisher = Arthur Ochs Sulzberger, Jr. | accessdate = August 10, 2014 }}</ref>，曾在[[纽约市公园和休闲局|纽约市公园和休闲局]]担任[[园艺学|园艺师]]，后因健康问题辞职。加纳的妻子艾萨瓦·加纳（{{lang|en|Esaw Garner}}）<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/brooklyn/funeral-today-eric-garner-article-1.1877381|title=Eric Garner, who died after NYPD chokehold, laid to rest|work=NY Daily News}}</ref>为人慷慨，与人融洽，被朋友们称为“社区调解员”<ref name=":0">{{cite web| url=http://news.yahoo.com/friends-man-nyc-chokehold-case-gentle-giant-205839377.html| title=Friends: Man in NYC chokehold case 'gentle giant'| work=[[Yahoo!_News|Yahoo! News]]| date=July 21, 2014| accessdate=July 21, 2014| author=Blidner, Rachelle}}</ref>。加纳有三个孩子和六个孙子。去世的时候他有一个三个月大的孩子<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.silive.com/news/index.ssf/2014/07/he_was_a_great_dad_says_family.html|title="He was a great dad," says family of man who died while being arrested in Tompkinsville|work=SILive.com}}</ref>。

自1980年起，加纳被纽约市警察局逮捕三十次，罪名包括斗殴、拘捕和盗窃罪。一位警察表示他曾多次因涉嫌销售无牌香烟而被捕<ref>{{cite web|url=http://online.wsj.com/articles/new-york-city-police-officer-wont-face-criminal-charges-in-eric-garner-death-1417635275?mod=WSJ_hpp_LEFTTopStories|title=New York City Police Officer Won’t Face Criminal Charges in Eric Garner Death|author=Rebecca Davis O'Brien, Michael Howard Saul and Pervaiz Shallwani|date=December 4, 2014|work=WSJ|accessdate=December 4, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Donnelly|first1=Frank|title=Misdemeanor cases over alleged untaxed cigarettes preceded fatal police incident with Eric Garner|url=http://www.silive.com/northshore/index.ssf/2014/07/eric_garner_who_died_in_police.html|accessdate=August 8, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=DeNinno|first1=Nadine|title=Eric Garner Died After NYPD Put Him In A Chokehold, Slammed His Head On Ground During Arrest [VIDEO]|url=http://www.ibtimes.com/eric-garner-died-after-nypd-put-him-chokehold-slammed-his-head-ground-during-arrest-video-1633114|publisher=International Business Times|accessdate=July 19, 2014}}</ref>。2007年，加纳曾向联邦法院提交手写诉状<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.scribd.com/doc/253681968/Eric-Garner-Lawsuit-NYPD-Violated-Him-in-Eric-s-own-hand-writing-7-years-ago|title=Garner v. == 死亡 ==
2014年7月17日下午4点45分，埃里克·加纳在史泰登岛汤普金斯维尔碰到[[便衣警员|便衣警员]]贾斯汀·达米柯（{{lang|en|Justin Damico}}）。根据包括曾用视频录下事件的加纳朋友拉姆齐·奥尔塔（{{lang|en|Ramsey Orta}}）在内的目击者所述<ref name="orta"/><ref>[http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2014/12/03/ramsey-orta-indictment-eric-garner_n_6264746.html A Grand Jury Did Indict One Person Involved In Eric Garner's Killing -- The Man Who Filmed It], Nick Wing, ''[[Huffington_Post|Huffington Post]]'', December 3, 2014</ref>，加纳死前曾从一场争斗中挣扎出来，引起了奥尔塔和警察丹尼尔·潘塔雷昂的注意<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.msnbc.com/msnbc/what-killed-eric-garner|title=Eric Garner was killed by more than just a chokehold|work=MSNBC}}</ref>。之后加纳对警察说，“滚开（乱码），凭什么？每当你看见我，就像招惹我？我已经厌倦了。我保证不会有下次了。为什么你......？这里每个人都会告诉你我什么都没干，什么也没卖。因为每次你看见我，都想要开骚扰我，你想阻止我（乱码）出售香烟。我在乎我的工作，警察，我在乎我的工作啊。请远离我。我再最后一次告诉你，请远离我<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cnn.com/2014/12/04/us/garner-last-words/|title=Eric Garner's haunting last words - CNN.com|author=Susanna Capelouto, CNN|date=December 4, 2014|work=CNN}}</ref>。“

潘塔雷昂靠近加纳的身后，试图铐住他，但加纳扑打着他的手臂挣脱出，并称“请别碰我<ref name="gothamist">[http://gothamist.com/2014/07/20/nypd_strips_badge_gun_from_cop_invo.php#photo-1 NYPD Strips Badge, Gun From Cop Involved In Fatal Chokehold] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150501013056/http://gothamist.com/2014/07/20/nypd_strips_badge_gun_from_cop_invo.php |date=2015-05-01 }}, ''[[Gothamist|Gothamist]]'', July 20, 2014.</ref>。”潘塔雷昂随后从身后掐住加纳的脖子，向后拉以试图把加纳撞向地面。加纳撞向地面时，与警察撞向一面玻璃窗，但玻璃窗没有打碎，随后其他制服警察包围了加纳。片刻过后，加纳一言不发地挪动着膝盖和前臂。此时，三名制服警察和两名便利警察将其包围。几秒钟后，录像显示潘塔雷昂把手臂从加纳脖子上移开，用手推把加纳的脸推向人行道<ref name="TheChief"/><ref name="Robbins"/><ref name="Time">[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=N__5p_dNW3U "Behind the Eric Garner Video of a Deadly Confrontation With Police", ]''[[Time_(magazine)|Time]]''<span>, 31 July 2014</span></ref>。脸朝下躺在人行道上的加纳多次声称“我不能呼吸”<ref name="usa 20141203"/><ref name=HuffLynch/><ref name=":1"/>。逮捕过程由女性非裔美国纽约市警察局警长柯姿·亚多尼（{{lang|en|Kizzy Adoni}}）监督，期间她没有为加纳求情。亚多尼在最初的警方报告中表示，“犯罪者的情况似乎并不严重，他似乎不那么糟<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.americanthinker.com/articles/2014/12/sgt_kizzy_adoni_meet_sgt_stacey_koon.html|title=Sgt. Kizzy Adoni, Meet Sgt. Stacey Koon|date=December 8, 2014| accessdate=December 21, 2014| author=Cashill, Jack}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://pix11.com/2014/07/31/exclusive-police-report-in-eric-garners-death-conflicts-with-videos-witnesses/|title=EXCLUSIVE: Police report in Eric Garner’s death conflicts with videos - New York's PIX11 / WPIX-TV|work=New York's PIX11 / WPIX-TV}}</ref>。”

加纳一动不动的躺着，被戴上手铐，救护车抵达前有好几分钟反应迟钝，这一幕在第二段视频中可以看到<ref name=WashingtonPost/><ref name="SIlive.com">{{cite web|url=http://www.silive.com/news/index.ssf/2014/07 cops_dug_through_garners_pocke.html|title=New video shows Eric Garner motionless on ground as police wait for EMS|work=[[Staten_Island_Advance|Staten Island Advance]]|date=July 19, 2014|accessdate=July 19, 2014|author=Koeske, Zack|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140809235916/http://www.silive.com/news/index.ssf/2014/07|archivedate=2014年8月9日}}</ref>。除了一名警察当时表示加纳没有了呼吸，其他警察都没有尝试给加纳复苏<ref name="second"/>。警察辩称没有决定给加纳进行心肺复苏术的原因是他当时还有呼吸，给还在自主呼吸的人士做心肺复苏术是不恰当的<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=s4AC5WS91rs|title=Staten Islander Eric Garner's Death - Panel Discussion on NY1|work=YouTube|accessdate=December 13, 2014}}</ref>救护车最终赶到现场，车里的两名医务人员和两名急救人员没有进行任何急救<ref name=":3">{{cite web|url=http://nypost.com/2014/07/20/4-ems-workers-barred-from-duty-after-chokehold-death/|title=4 EMS workers barred from duty after chokehold death|work=New York Post}}</ref>，或是及时把加纳放到担架上<ref name=":3"/>。据警方透露，加纳被送到里士满大学医疗中心时心脏病发作<ref>{{cite news|last1=Bloom|first1=Deborah E.|last2=Imam|first2=Jareen|title=New York man dies after chokehold by police|url=http://www.cnn.com/2014/07/20/justice/ny-chokehold-death/|accessdate=21 December 2014|publisher=CNN|date=8 December 2014}}</ref>。加纳抵达医院一小时后被宣布到院前死亡<ref>{{cite web|title=Transcript: Mayor de Blasio Holds Media Availability With Police Commissioner Bratton On The Death Of Eric Garner| date=July 18, 2014 | accessdate=December 6, 2014 |publisher=Office of [[the_Mayor_of_New_York_City|the Mayor of New York City]]|url=http://www1.nyc.gov/office-of-the-mayor/news/360-14/transcript-mayor-de-blasio-holds-media-availability-police-commissioner-bratton-the-death}}</ref>。

加纳的葬礼于2014年7月23日在[[布鲁克林|布鲁克林]]伯特利浸信会教堂举行。艾尔·夏普顿在葬礼的将画上呼吁对涉事警察采取更严厉的惩罚措施<ref>{{cite web| url=http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2014/07/23/funeral-for-staten-island-man-who-died-in-police-custody/| title=Sharpton To Officers In Eric Garner Case: ‘When Does Your Morality Kick In?'| work=[[CBS_New_York|CBS New York]]| date=July 23, 2014|accessdate=July 24, 2014}}</ref>。

== 后果 ==
=== 调查 ===
7月20日，曾掐住加纳脖子的警察丹尼尔·潘塔雷昂被停职，配枪和徽章被收缴<ref>{{cite news| url= http://abcnews.go.com/US/nypd-cops-chokehold-caused-injury-mans-throat/story?id=24640347 |title= NYPD Cop's Chokehold May Not Have Caused Serious Injury to Man's Throat| publisher=ABC News |first1= Josh |last1= Margolin |first2= Ron |last2= Cliborne |first3= Mark |last3= Crudele |date= 20 July 2014 |accessdate= 29 July 2014}}</ref>。警察贾斯汀·达米柯允许保留徽章和配枪，但被停职<ref>{{cite news| url = http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/nyc-crime/wife-man-filmed-chokehold-arrested-article-1.1893790#cAOs5mib4wBv4MOC.97 | title= NYPD No. 3's order to crack down on selling loose cigarettes led to chokehold death of Eric Garner |first1= John |last1= Marzulli |first2= Rocco |last2= Parascandola |first3= Thomas |last3= Tracy |newspaper= [[Daily_News_(New_York)|Daily News]] |date= 7 August 2014}}</ref>。四名负责将加纳运抵医院的急救人员和医护人员于7月21日停职<ref name=Time/>，两名医护人员目前已复职。其余两名急救人员目前在医院从事非医疗工作，等待里士满大学医疗中心调查事件<ref>{{cite web|url=http://bigstory.ap.org/article/ab96cf8f886243ec89b8138da80c98f2/internal-probe-moving-swiftly-chokehold-case|title=NYC police probe moving swiftly in chokehold case|work=The Big Story}}</ref><ref>{{cite web| url=http://www.silive.com/news/index.ssf/2014/07/emts_and_paramedics_who_respon.html| title = EMTs and paramedics who responded to Eric Garner have been suspended without pay by hospital| work=[[Staten_Island_Advance|Staten Island Advance]]| date=July 21, 2014| accessdate = July 22, 2014|last= Annese |first= John M.}}</ref>。

用手机录下朋友被逮捕三个星期后，拉姆齐·奥尔塔因被控持有武器而被捕<ref name=orta>Goodman, J. David. [http://www.nytimes.com/2014/08/04/nyregion/after-recording-eric-garner-chokehold-ramsey-orta-gets-charged-with-gun-possession.html "Man Who Filmed Fatal Police Chokehold Is Arrested on Weapons Charges: After Recording Eric Garner Chokehold, Ramsey Orta Gets Charged With Gun Possession,"] ''New York Times'' (Aug 3, 2014).</ref>。[[阿尔·夏普顿|阿尔·夏普顿]]发表声明起诉奥尔塔的同时让他担任证人可能构成利益冲突。检察官后来质疑用筹集款项保释奥尔塔是否合法。奥尔塔于2015年4月10日保释出狱<ref>{{cite news|last=Lewis|first=Taylor|date=April 13, 2015|title=Man who filmed Eric Garner's death released from jail|url=http://www.essence.com/2015/04/13/man-who-filmed-eric-garners-death-released-jail/|newspaper=Essence|accessdate=April 17, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|date=April 13, 2015|title=Ramsey Orta, Man Who Filmed NYPD Chokehold of Eric Garner, Is Released from Jail|url=http://www.democracynow.org/blog/2015/4/13/ramsey_orta_man_who_filmed_nypd|newspaper=DemocracyNow|accessdate=April 17, 2015}}</ref>。

8月1日，纽约市法医办公室发现加纳死因是“窒息”<ref name="TimeNathan">{{cite news|last1=Nathan|first1=Giri|title=Eric Garner Died from Chokehold While in Police Custody|url=http://time.com/3071288/eric-garner-chokehold-death-nypd-medical-examiner/|accessdate=21 December 2014|work=Time|date=1 August 2014}}</ref>。哮喘、心脏疾病和肥胖被列为影响因素<ref>{{cite news| title= Medical examiner says chokehold by police officer caused death of NYC man; ruled homicide | publisher= Fox News| agency= Associated Press | url= http://www.foxnews.com/us/2014/08/01/medical-examiner-says-chokehold-by-police-officer-caused-death-nyc-man-ruled/ | date= 1 August 2014 | accessdate= 4 December 2014}}</ref>。气管和颈骨没有损伤<ref name=windpipe>{{cite news| title= Man in chokehold had no throat damage | url=http://nypost.com/2014/07/19/man-in-chokehold-death-had-no-throat-damage-autopsy/|newspaper=[[The_New_York_Post|The New York Post]]|accessdate=13 December 2014}}</ref>。8月1日，法医发言人朱莉·卜赛尔（{{lang|en|Julie Bolcer}}）宣布加纳的死亡已排除他杀<ref name="pearson">{{cite news| url=http://bigstory.ap.org/article/medical-examiner-says-chokehold-police-officer-caused-death-nyc-man-ruled-homicide| title = Official: Police chokehold caused NYC man's death| work=Associated Press| date=August 1, 2014| accessdate=August 1, 2014| last= Pearson |first= Jake}}</ref>。到了2014年12月3日，美国司法部发起对加纳死亡的进一步调查<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.redeyechicago.com/news/chi-eric-garner-chokehold-grand-jury-verdict-20141203,0,5325686.story|title=Department of Justice to investigate NY chokehold death: U.S. official| agency=''Tribune'' wire report|date=December 3, 2014|work=redeyechicago.com}}</ref>。

=== 第一波抗议 ===
阿尔·夏普顿组织了7月19日下午在史泰登岛的抗议，谴责警方掐死加纳，称这样做没有理由<ref>{{cite web| url=http://www.latimes.com/nation/nationnow/la-na-nn-sharpton-events-20140719-story.html| title = Rev. Al Sharpton leads calls for justice in NYPD chokehold death|work=[[Los_Angeles_Times|Los Angeles Times]]| date=July 19, 2014| accessdate=July 19, 2014| author=Queally, James}}</ref>。

7月29日，WalkRunFly Productions和诗人丹尼尔·J·瓦特在[[时代广场|时代广场]]组织了一场抗议活动，受到许多诗人和百老汇演员的参与<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FpfTos6NroM#t=113 I Can't Breathe], YouTube, WalkRunFly Productions, July 29, 2014.</ref>。阿尔·夏普顿计划原定于8月23日举行抗议，让参与者驶过维拉萨诺·纳罗斯桥后前往案发地点和地方律师小丹尼尔·多诺瓦先生的办公室<ref>Allen, Jonathan. [http://news.yahoo.com/bridge-protest-over-nyc-mans-arrest-death-proceed-171924935.html Bridge protest over NYC man's arrest death to proceed], ''[[Reuters|Reuters]]'', August 9, 2014.</ref>。该设想被阿尔改为在加纳遇害的史泰登岛的湾街游行。警方估计超过2500人参与游行<ref>{{cite news|url=http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2014/08/23/thousands-to-call-for-action-over-death-of-eric-garner-at-staten-island-march-and-rally/|title=Rev. Al Sharpton Leads March, Rally Over Eric Garner’s Death On Staten Island|publisher=CBSNewYork/AP|date=August 23, 2014|accessdate=August 27, 2014}}</ref><ref>BBC News [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-us-canada-28917198 Eric Garner death: Thousands rally in Staten Island] [[BBC|BBC]] August 24, 2014, Accessed August 27, 2014</ref>。

=== 大陪审团 ===
8月19日，史泰登岛地方律师小丹尼尔·M·多诺瓦宣布潘塔雷昂的案子将由[[大评审团|大评审团]]审议，表示考虑到法医的调查结果，他的办公室决定“适合向里士满县大陪审团提交加纳死亡的证据<ref name="timeline">{{cite web|url=http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Timeline-Eric-Garner-Chokehold-Death-Arrest-NYPD-Grand-Jury-No-Indictment-284657081.html|title=Timeline: Eric Garner Death|work=NBC New York}}</ref>。”9月29日，大陪审团开始审理加纳一案证据<ref name="timeline"/>，23名成员组成的评估小组中有14名白人，9名非白人，至少有5名黑人<ref name="timeline"/>。11月21日，潘塔雷昂向大陪审团作出长达约两小时的证词<ref name="timeline"/>。案发两个月后的12月3日，大陪审团决定不起诉潘塔雷欧<ref name="timeline"/>。史泰登岛法官要求把自己撤换出大陪审团的案件笔录<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/police-union-chief-calls-talks-de-blasio-article-1.2048354|title=Judge withdraws from hearing in Eric Garner case|work=NY Daily News}}</ref>。
