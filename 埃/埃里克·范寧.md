'''埃里克·范寧'''（Eric Fanning，{{bd|1968年|7月2日}}）是[[美國|美國]]的一位政治家。2015年11月3日，他獲得提名擔任[[美國陸軍部長|美國陸軍部長]]，是第22任美國陸軍部長<ref>{{cite book|publisher=Government Printing Office| url=http://www.gpo.gov/fdsys/pkg/CHRG-113shrg87878/pdf/CHRG-113shrg87878.pdf |title= Hearings before the Senate Armed Service Committee |accessdate=March 15, 2016| date=February 28, 2013| pages=529ff., 683–6}}</ref><ref name=schogol>{{cite news|last=Schogol|first=Jeff|title=Donley retires; Under Secretary Fanning becomes acting AF secretary|url=http://www.airforcetimes.com/article/20130621/NEWS05/306210037/Donley-retires-Under-Secretary-Fanning-becomes-acting-AF-secretary|accessdate=August 23, 2013|newspaper=Air Force Times|date=June 21, 2013}}</ref>。他也是首位擔任美國國防部要職的同性戀人士<ref name=dart>{{cite news|last=Bornstein|first=Daniel|title=Obama taps alum. for top Air Force position|url=http://thedartmouth.com/2012/08/07/news/Airforce|accessdate=April 19, 2013|newspaper=The Dartmouth|date=August 7, 2013|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120811040432/http://thedartmouth.com/2012/08/07/news/Airforce|archive-date=2012-08-11|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。
