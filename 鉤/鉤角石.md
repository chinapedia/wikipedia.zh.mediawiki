'''鉤角石'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Hamites'''''}}），又名'''含米特菊石'''、'''鉤菊石'''，是生存在[[白堊紀|白堊紀]][[海洋|海洋]]中的一[[屬|屬]][[菊石|菊石]]。游泳能力較差。牠們自[[下白堊紀|下白堊紀]][[阿普第階|阿普第階]]開始[[演化|演化]]，生存至[[上白堊紀|上白堊紀]]的[[森諾曼階|森諾曼階]]。幾可肯定牠們是[[並系群|並系群]]，但是[[塔菊石超科|塔菊石超科]]並不能完全適合較進化類別。為了要確認鉤角石的分支，牠們被分成一連串的新屬或[[亞屬|亞屬]]。<ref>{{Cite journal en|author=Szives O. & Monks N.|year=2002|title=Heteromorphs of the Tata Limestone Formation (Aptian - Lower Albian), Hungary|journal=Palaeontology|volume=44|pages=1137-49|url=http://homepage.mac.com/nmonks/files/hungarian_heteros.pdf|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=http://archive.wikiwix.com/cache/20060918030343/http://homepage.mac.com/nmonks/files/hungarian_heteros.pdf|archivedate=2006-09-18}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal en|author=Monks N|year=2002|title=Cladistic analysis of a problematic ammonite group: the Hamitidae (Cretaceous, Albian - Turonian) and proposals for new cladistic terms|journal=Palaeontology|volume=44|pages=687-707|url=http://homepage.mac.com/nmonks/files/hamitidae.pdf|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=http://archive.wikiwix.com/cache/20060912004310/http://homepage.mac.com/nmonks/files/hamitidae.pdf|archivedate=2006-09-12}}</ref>

== 特徵 ==
鉤角石殼的形狀起先呈打開的螺旋狀，繼而形成一個大鉤或[[回形針|回形針]]狀。<ref name=reconstr>{{Cite web|author=Neale Monks|title=Fossil Cephalopod Reconstructions|url=http://homepage.mac.com/nmonks/Projects/ammonitereconstr.html|accessdate=2009-12-27|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081013091524/http://homepage.mac.com/nmonks/Projects/ammonitereconstr.html|archivedate=2008-10-13|deadurl=yes}}</ref>鉤角石殼上沒有那些螺旋紋裝飾，成熟時由於孔穴的限制，它們就會由1-2條較粗的[[肋骨|肋骨]]為界。這種特徵在其他[[菊石目|菊石目]]菊石也可以見到，估計屬於一種[[兩性異形|兩性異形]]的特徵。<ref>{{Cite journal en|author=Monks N|year=1999|title=Cladistic analysis of Albian heteromorph ammonites|journal=Palaeontology|volume=42|pages=907-25|url=http://homepage.mac.com/nmonks/files/cladistics_albian_heteros.pdf|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=http://archive.wikiwix.com/cache/20060920143013/http://homepage.mac.com/nmonks/files/cladistics_albian_heteros.pdf|archivedate=2006-09-20}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal en|author=Monks N|year=2002|title=Cladistic analysis of a problematic ammonite group: the Hamitidae (Cretaceous, Albian - Turonian) and proposals for new cladistic terms|journal=Palaeontology|volume=44|pages=687-707|url=http://homepage.mac.com/nmonks/files/hamitidae.pdf|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=http://archive.wikiwix.com/cache/20060912004310/http://homepage.mac.com/nmonks/files/hamitidae.pdf|archivedate=2006-09-12}}</ref>

鉤角石開放式的殼因[[阻力|阻力]]的問題而不方便游泳，但其實有關牠們的生存方式所知有限。一般假設牠們是以[[浮游生物|浮游生物]]為食，或許像[[水母|水母]]般捕捉細小獵物。從一些被[[蟹|蟹]]所破壞的殼中發現，牠們可能會有一些時間活動或棲息在接近[[海床|海床]]的位置。<ref>{{Cite journal en|author=Trueman A|year=1941|title=The ammonite body-chamber, with special reference to the buoyancy and mode of life of the living animal|journal=Quarterly Journal of the Geological Society of London|volume=384|pages=339-83}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal en|author=Monks N|year=2000|title=Mid Cretaceous heteromorph ammonite shell damage|journal=Journal of Molluscan Studies|volume=66|pages=283-5}}</ref>

== 種系發生 ==
鉤角石本身包含了多個不同形態，包括一些似乎較伙進化的在內。牠們在[[阿普弟階|阿普弟階]]快速的分化為多個不同分支，包括[[桿菊石|桿菊石]]、[[塔菊石|塔菊石]]及[[船菊石|船菊石]]。<ref>{{Cite journal en|author=Monks N|year=1999|title=Cladistic analysis of Albian heteromorph ammonites|journal=Palaeontology|volume=42|pages=907-25|url=http://homepage.mac.com/nmonks/files/cladistics_albian_heteros.pdf|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=http://archive.wikiwix.com/cache/20060920143013/http://homepage.mac.com/nmonks/files/cladistics_albian_heteros.pdf|archivedate=2006-09-20}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal en|author=Monks N|year=2000|title=Phylogeny of the Scaphitidae|journal=Journal of Molluscan Studies|volume=66|pages=205-16}}</ref>例如[[演化|演化]]出塔菊石的分支，其殼先有一個螺旋狀，再形成筆直狀。故此塔菊石擁有比鉤角石更先進的形態。<ref>{{cite journal en|author=Monks N|year=2001|title=The functional morphology of ''Hamites'' and ''Stomohamites'' and the origins of the Turrilitidae|journal=Bollettino Malacologico|volume=36|pages=195-200}}</ref>
