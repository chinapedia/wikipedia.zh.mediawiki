'''特里西納·安德烈·「特麗」·塞維爾'''（{{lang-en|'''Terrycina Andrea "Terri" Sewell'''}}<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.campaignmoney.com/political/campaigns/terrycina-andrea-sewell.asp?cycle=10 |title=Terrycina Andrea Sewell - $1,802,819 raised, '10 election cycle, Alabama (AL), Democrat Party, Congress |publisher=Campaignmoney.com |date= |accessdate=2012-08-29}}</ref>；{{bd|1976年|7月26日}}<ref name="thehill">{{cite web|url=http://thehill.com/homenews/campaign/126297-new-members-2010-alabama |title=New Members 2010 - Alabama - The Hill - covering Congress, Politics, Political Campaigns and Capitol Hill |publisher=TheHill.com |date= |accessdate=2012-08-29}}</ref>），是一位美國[[民主黨_(美國)|民主黨]][[政治人物|政治人物]]，自2011年通過{{le|2010年阿拉巴馬州美國眾議院議員選舉|United States House of Representatives elections in Alabama, 2010|眾議院選舉}}擊敗[[共和黨_(美國)|共和黨]]的唐·張伯倫（Don Chamberlain）成為[[阿拉巴馬州|阿拉巴馬州]][[美國眾議院|聯邦眾議院]][[阿拉巴馬州第七國會選區|第七國會選區]]的議員<ref name="results">{{cite news|url=http://elections.nytimes.com/2010/results/house|title=New York Times Election Results 2010 | work=The New York Times}}</ref><ref>[http://www.montgomeryadvertiser.com/article/20101102/NEWS/101102052/GOP+s+Roby+defeats+Bright+in+Alabama+s+7th+District+ GOP's Roby defeats Bright in Alabama's 7th District] ''[[Montgomery_Advertiser|Montgomery Advertiser]]'' November 2, 1010</ref>。塞維爾是民主黨在阿拉巴馬州首位黑人女性國會議員<ref>{{cite web |url=http://atlantapost.com/2010/11/03/black-lawmakers-break-new-ground-suffer-losses/ |title=Black Lawmakers Break New Ground, Suffer Losses | Madame Noire | Black Women's Lifestyle Guide | Black Hair | Black Love |publisher=Atlantapost.com |date=2010-11-03 |accessdate=2012-08-29 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20120707163932/http://atlantapost.com/2010/11/03/black-lawmakers-break-new-ground-suffer-losses/ |archivedate=2012-07-07 }}</ref>，她和[[瑪莎·羅比|瑪莎·羅比]]亦是首兩名通過定期選舉成為{{le|美國國會阿拉巴馬州代表議員|United States congressional delegations from Alabama|阿拉巴馬州國會代表}}的女性。
