'''玛丽·特罗斯特内兹灭绝营'''（{{lang-de|Vernichtungslager Maly Trostinez}}）<ref name="Smilovitsky" /><ref name="Yad Vashem" />，是纳粹德国在[[奧斯蘭總督轄區|奧斯蘭總督轄區]][[白俄罗斯|白俄罗斯]][[明斯克|明斯克]]郊外特罗斯特内兹（{{lang-be|Трасцянец}}）建立的集中营。运营于1942年7月到1943年10月间，明斯克最后的一批犹太人被杀害并埋葬于此地。<ref name="Smilovitsky">{{cite web | url=http://www.jewishgen.org/belarus/newsletter/ilya_ehrenburg.htm | title=Ilya Ehrenburg on the Holocaust in Belarus: Unknown Testimony | publisher=East European Jewish Affairs | work=Vol. 29, No. 1–2 | date=Summer–Winter 1999 | accessdate=1 September 2013 | author=Dr. Leonid Smilovitsky | pages=61–74 | quote=Ilya Ehrenburg's ''Black Book'' cites the official data that in all, 206,500 people were murdered at Trostenets, of whom 150,000 were killed at the Blagovshchina Forest between September 1941 and October 1943, and another 50,000 at the Shashkovka Forest between October 1943 and June 1944.}}</ref><ref name="Yad Vashem">{{cite web | url=http://www.yadvashem.org/odot_pdf/microsoft%20word%20-%206636.pdf | title=Maly Trostinets | publisher=Shoah Resource Center, The International School for Holocaust Studies | date=2013 | accessdate=1 September 2013 | author=Yad Vashem | format=PDF file, direct download 19.5 KB}}</ref>
