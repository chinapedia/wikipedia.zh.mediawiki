'''玛丽·娄·吉普森'''（{{lang-en|Mary Lou Jepsen}}，{{bd|1965年|}}）是[[Google_X_实验室|Google X 实验室]]的展示部门的负责人。她也是一家为便携式电脑设计和制造低成本，低耗量LCD屏幕的制造商[[Pixel_Qi|Pixel Qi]]的[[企业家|创始人]]。她曾是[[OLPC|One Laptop per Child]](OLPC)的[[企业家|联合创始人]]和第一位[[首席技术官|首席技术官]]。<ref> http://www.linkedin.com/pub/mary-lou-jepsen/0/1/3b2</ref>

== Solve for X ==
她是[[Google|Google]]的"Solve for X"项目里早期贡献者之一，其项目点子是"设想思维脑图的眼睛/Imaging the Mind's Eye"。 <ref>{{cite web|last=Nasir|first=Farhat|title=Google X? Google Launches “Solve for X” For Discussing Radical Ideas to Solve World Problems!|url=http://hitechanalogy.com/google-x-google-launches-solve-for-x-for-discussing-radical-ideas-to-solve-world-problems/|publisher=Hitechanalogy|accessdate=7 February 2012|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131119032431/http://hitechanalogy.com/google-x-google-launches-solve-for-x-for-discussing-radical-ideas-to-solve-world-problems/|archivedate=2013年11月19日}}</ref> 

== Google X Lab ==
2013年，她加入 [[Google_X_实验室|Google X 实验室]]。其头衔是“展示部门的负责人/Head of the Display Division”。<ref>http://www.linkedin.com/search/fpsearch?fname=Mary&lname=Jepsen&pplSearchOrigin=SEO_SN&trk=SEO_SN&csrfToken=ajax%3A6212865922249445506&domainCountryName=#facets=fname%3DMary%26lname%3DJepsen%26searchLocationType%3DY%26pplSearchOrigin%3DSEO_SN%26viewCriteria%3D1%26sortCriteria%3DR%26facetsOrder%3DCC%252CN%252CG%252CI%252CPC%252CED%252CL%252CFG%252CTE%252CFA%252CSE%252CP%252CCS%252CF%252CDR%26page_num%3D2%26infScroll%3Dtrue%26openFacets%3DCC%252CN%252CG</ref>
