'''曼尼普尔毛主义共产党'''（{{lang-bn|মণিপুরের মাওবাদী কমিউনিস্ট পার্টি}}）是[[印度|印度]][[曼尼普尔邦|曼尼普尔邦]]的一个[[毛派|毛派]]<ref>{{cite news |url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/guwahati/KCPs-ultra-Left-turn-worries-Manipur/articleshow/10016542.cms |title=KCP's ultra-Left turn worries Manipur |newspaper=[[The_Times_of_India|The Times of India]] |first=Caesar |last=Mandal |location=Kolkata |date=17 September 2011 |accessdate=9 June 2014}}</ref>政党，其目标是“通过武装革命战争建立一个共产主义社会”。<ref name="Shillong">{{cite news |url=http://www.theshillongtimes.com/2011/09/21/maoism-in-manipur/ |title=Maoism in Manipur |newspaper=The Shillong Times |date=21 September 2011 |accessdate=9 June 2014 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140714173939/http://www.theshillongtimes.com/2011/09/21/maoism-in-manipur/ |archivedate=2014年7月14日 }}</ref> 该党表示，将致力于使曼尼普尔人民从“印度殖民统治”中解放出来。
