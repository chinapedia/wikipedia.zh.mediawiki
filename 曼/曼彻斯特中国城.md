'''曼彻斯特中国城'''（Chinatown of Manchester）位于英格兰[[曼彻斯特市中心|曼彻斯特市中心]]，是[[英国|英国]]的第二大[[唐人街|唐人街]]，欧洲第三大唐人街<ref>{{cite book| last = Christiansen| first = Flemming| title = Chinatown, Europe: An Exploration of Overseas Chinese Identity in the 1990s| publisher = Routledge | year = 2003| pages = 202 | isbn = 0-7007-1072-8 }}</ref>。[[牌坊|牌坊]]建于1987年<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mymanchester.org/manchester/community-chinatown.htm |title=Manchester Chinatown |publisher=MyManchester |accessdate=2017-01-27 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071022114731/http://www.mymanchester.org/manchester/community-chinatown.htm |archivedate=2007-10-22 |deadurl=yes |df= }}</ref>。这里有许多亚洲餐厅、商店和超市<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.chinatownology.com/chinatown_manchester.html |title=Manchester Chinatown |publisher=chinatownology.com |accessdate= 2017-01-27}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.ilovemanchester.com/2014/10/16/a-taste-of-chinese-culture-manchester-style.aspx |title=A Taste Of Chinese Culture – Manchester Style |date=2014-10-16 |publisher=I Love MCR |accessdate= 2017-01-27}}</ref>。

曼彻斯特中国城西到[[莫斯利街|莫斯利街]]，东到波特兰街，南到公主街，北到夏洛特街<ref name="Geog">{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.manchesterchinesearchive.org.uk/index.php?p=84 |title=Manchester Chinatown |publisher=Manchester Chinese Archive |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140702075646/http://manchesterchinesearchive.org.uk/index.php?p=84 |archivedate=2014-07-02 |date= |accessdate=2017-01-27 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。有些地图显示中国城南到牛津街<ref name="Map 2002">{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.manchester2002-uk.com/maps/chinatown-map.html |title=Street Plan Map of Manchester China Town |publisher=Manchester2002-UK.com |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160505154616/http://www.manchester2002-uk.com/maps/chinatown-map.html |archivedate=2016-05-05 |date= |accessdate=2017-01-27 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>，东面是运河街一带的同志村，北面是[[皮卡迪利花园|皮卡迪利花园]]。
