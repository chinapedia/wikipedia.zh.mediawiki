'''曼西鎮'''（{{lang-my|မန်စီမြို့နယ်}}，{{lang-en|Mansi Township}}）為[[緬甸|緬甸]][[克欽邦|克欽邦]][[八莫縣|八莫縣]]的鎮區<ref name="MIMU001">[http://www.burmalibrary.org/docs6/MIMU001_A3_SD%20&%20Township%20Overview.pdf "Myanmar States/Divisions & Townships Overview Map"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101203160828/http://www.burmalibrary.org/docs6/MIMU001_A3_SD%20%26%20Township%20Overview.pdf |date=2010-12-03 }} Myanmar Information Management Unit (MIMU)</ref>。2014年人口52,945人，區域面積2,898.8平方公里<ref name=citypopulation/>。該鎮下分230個村莊。[[曼西|曼西]]為該鎮之首府。而曼西鎮的森林受到人為[[非法砍伐|非法砍伐]]的危機<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.illegal-logging.info/approach.php?a_id=80|title=Myanmar (Burma)|publisher=Illegal Logging Info|accessdate=September 25, 2010|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100820050437/http://www.illegal-logging.info/approach.php?a_id=80|archivedate=2010年8月20日}}</ref>。
