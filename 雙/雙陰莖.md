'''双阴茎'''（{{lang|la|'''Diphallia'''}}，亦有從英語{{lang|en|'''penile duplication'''}}簡寫作'''PD'''）是指[[男性|男性]]天生就有二個[[阴茎|阴茎]]，是一种[[男性外生殖器|男性外生殖器]]異常。醫學上首宗有記錄的雙陰莖的個案發生於1609年由[[瑞士|瑞士]]醫生[[Johannes_Jacob_Wecker|Johannes Jacob Wecker]]發表<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.jiaps.com/article.asp?issn=0971-9261;year=2000;volume=5;issue=1;spage=18;epage=21;aulast=Sharma;type=0|title=Concealed diphallus: a Case report and review of the literature.|publisher=Journal of Indian Association of Pediatric Surgeons|accessdate=2010-03-07|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20101218070734/http://www.jiaps.com/article.asp?issn=0971-9261%3Byear%3D2000%3Bvolume%3D5%3Bissue%3D1%3Bspage%3D18%3Bepage%3D21%3Baulast%3DSharma%3Btype%3D0|archivedate=2010-12-18|deadurl=yes}}</ref>。根據美國的統計數字<ref>{{cite web|url=http://today.reuters.com/News/CrisesArticle.aspx?storyId=DEL270522| title=Indian man wants op to remove extra organ| publisher=Reuters| accessdate=2006-08-18| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070122012925/http://today.reuters.com/News/CrisesArticle.aspx?storyId=DEL270522 |archivedate=2007-01-22 |language=en }}</ref>，每550万人就有一名双阴茎者<ref>{{cite book|title=性医学|year=1982|publisher=科学技术文献出版社}}</ref>。而截至現在，全球亦只出現過約1000個案例<ref>{{cite web |url=http://hypervocal.com/culture/2012/blanche-dumas-juan-baptista-dos-santos-two-penises-vaginas/ |title=Man With 2 Penises, Woman With 2 Vaginas Were the Sexiest Couple of the 19th Century] (contains photos with nudity) |language=en |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130215082228/http://hypervocal.com/culture/2012/blanche-dumas-juan-baptista-dos-santos-two-penises-vaginas/ |archivedate=2013-02-15 }}</ref>。

雙陰莖通常都會伴隨其他先天問題出現，例如：[[腎臟|腎臟]]、[[脊椎|脊椎]]等，有雙陰莖的人同時罹患[[脊柱裂|脊柱裂]]的風險也較高。
