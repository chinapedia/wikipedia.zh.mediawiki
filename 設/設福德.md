'''設福德'''（{{lang-en|Shefford}}）是[[英國|英國]]的一個城市和民政教區，位於[[貝德福德郡|貝德福德郡]]的{{Link-en|中央貝德福德郡|Central Bedfordshire}}區。2001年，設福德有人口4,928人。2007年增加至5,770人<ref>[http://www.neighbourhood.statistics.gov.uk/dissemination/viewFullDataset.do?instanceSelection=03070&productId=779&$ph=60_61&datasetInstanceId=3070&startColumn=1&numberOfColumns=8&containerAreaId=790320 Office for National Statistics: ''Census 2001: Parish Headcounts: Mid Bedfordshire''] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130526233238/http://www.neighbourhood.statistics.gov.uk/dissemination/viewFullDataset.do?instanceSelection=03070&productId=779&$ph=60_61&datasetInstanceId=3070&startColumn=1&numberOfColumns=8&containerAreaId=790320 |date=2013-05-26 }} Retrieved 31 October 2010</ref><ref>[http://www.centralbedfordshire.gov.uk/council-and-democracy/statistics/population.aspx Bedfordshire County Council: ''Population Estimates and Forecasts: 2007'']</ref>。
