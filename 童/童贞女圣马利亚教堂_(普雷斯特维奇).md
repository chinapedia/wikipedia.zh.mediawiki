'''童贞女圣马利亚教堂'''（{{lang-en|'''Church of St Mary the Virgin'''}}）位于[[英国|英国]][[大曼彻斯特郡|大曼彻斯特郡]]普雷斯特维奇教堂路，是一座圣公会教区教堂兼博尔顿副主教的宅邸。<ref>{{Citation|title=St Mary, Prestwich|url=http://www.achurchnearyou.com/prestwich-st-mary/|publisher=[[英國國教會|英國國教會]]|accessdate=15 October 2011}}</ref>教堂被记录在英国国家遗产名录内，是一座I级[[登录建筑|登录建筑]]。<ref name="nhl">{{NHLE|num=1067252|desc=Church of St Mary, Bury|access-date=15 October 2011|mode=cs2}}</ref>英格兰系列的建筑物的作者称其为“a major church”。<ref name="pev">{{Citation|last=Hartwell|first=Clare|title=Lancashire: Manchester and the South-East|year=2004|last2=Hyde|last3=Pevsner|first2=Matthew|first3=Nikolaus|authorlink=|author2-link=|author3-link=Nikolaus Pevsner|series=The Buildings of England|pages=564–566|edition=|location=New Haven and London|publisher=[[耶魯大學出版社|耶魯大學出版社]]|isbn=0-300-10583-5}}</ref>

== 构造 ==
[[File:John_Brooks_monument-St_Mary's_churchyard,_Prestwich.JPG|链接=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:John_Brooks_monument-St_Mary's_churchyard,_Prestwich.JPG]]
教堂的主体部分由红砂岩建成，屋顶铺满了深色瓦片。它的计划包括一个五开间殿与天窗、双跨圣坛有天窗上升比殿、南北通道、南北门廊和西部的高塔。<ref name="nhl2">{{NHLE|num=1067252|desc=Church of St Mary, Bury|access-date=15 October 2011|mode=cs2}}</ref><ref name="vch">{{citation|title=The Parish of Prestwich with Oldham: Church|url=http://www.british-history.ac.uk/report.aspx?compid=53001|year=1911|series={{tsl|en|Victoria County History||Victoria County History}}|work=A History of the County of Lancaster|volume=5|pages=67–76|publisher=University of London & History of Parliament Trust|editor1-last=Farrer|editor2-last=Brownbill|editor1-first=William|editor2-first=J.|accessdate=15 October 2011}}</ref>教堂附近的墓地是II级登录建筑，埋有一战期间死亡的29人、二战期间死亡的19人以及棉花企业家兼银行家威廉·布鲁克斯的儿子约翰·布鲁克斯（1788年至1849年）的尸体。墓碑是由约翰·托马斯雕刻，约花费了£3000。<ref>{{NHLE|num=1067254|desc=Monument to John Brooks to west of Church of St Mary, Prestwich|access-date=24 March 2015|mode=cs2|fewer-links=x}}</ref>
