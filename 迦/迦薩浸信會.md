'''迦薩浸信會'''是一間[[浸信宗|浸信宗]]的[[基督教會|基督教會]]，在[[巴勒斯坦領土|巴勒斯坦]][[加沙地帶|加沙地帶]]的[[加沙市|加沙市]]。<ref name='Alford 2007-10-08'>{{cite news | first = Deann | last = Alford | title = Christian Bookstore Manager Martyred in Gaza City | date = 2007-10-08 | url = http://www.christianitytoday.com/ct/2007/octoberweb-only/141-12.0.html | work = Christianity Today | accessdate = 2010-09-20}}</ref><ref name='Haaretz 2007-10-07'>{{cite news | title = Palestinian Christian activist stabbed to death in Gaza | date = 2007-10-07 | publisher = [[Haaretz|Haaretz]] | url = http://www.haaretz.com/news/palestinian-christian-activist-stabbed-to-death-in-gaza-1.230592 | work = Associated Press | accessdate = 2010-09-20}}</ref><ref name='Silver 2007-10-08'>{{cite news | first = Eric | last = Silver | title = Gaza's Christian bookseller killed | date = 2007-10-08 | url = http://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/middle-east/gazas-christian-bookseller-killed-396283.html | work = The Independent | accessdate = 2010-09-20}}</ref>這間教會是加沙地帶僅有的三間基督教會之一，是當地唯一的[[新教|新教]]及[[福音派|福音派]]教會。

迦薩浸信教會及其約二百名會眾，受當地[[巴以衝突|巴以衝突]]的持續暴力及混亂所害。有數名教會成員在巴勒斯坦的派系衝突中傷亡。一名教會領袖在2007年被身份不明的武裝份子殺害後，加沙政府勸喻數名教會領袖，為自身安全離開加沙。因為當地的局勢及[[以色列|以色列]]一直施加的往來限制，教會牧師及領導在外流亡，只能偶然回去。

==教會概況和活動==
這間教會在1950年代建立。<ref name='Belz 2007-05-08'>{{cite journal | title = Gaza’s sturdy but small Christian population confronts Islamic militancy and the Israeli blockade | journal = WORLD Magazine | date = 2007-05-08 | first = Mindy | last = Belz | volume = 25 | issue = 9| id = | url = http://www.worldmag.com/articles/16649 | accessdate = 2010-09-20}}</ref>教會牧師是Hanna Massad。<ref name='Alford 2005-06-17'>{{cite journal | title = Love in the Land of Enmity | journal = Christianity Today | date = 2005-06-17 | first = Deann | last = Alford | volume = 49 | issue = 7| id = | url = http://www.christianitytoday.com/ct/2005/july/9.44.html | accessdate = 2010-09-20}}</ref>

加沙地帶的基督徒有2500人。這間教會成立至今，會眾人數在150至250之間，是加沙地帶僅有的三間基督教會之一，<ref name='Jansezian 2007-06-10'>{{cite news | first = Nicole | last = Jansezian | title = Gaza’s forgotten Christians | date = 2007-06-10 | url = http://www.israeltoday.co.il/default.aspx?tabid=132&view=item&idx=1413 | work = Israel Today Magazine | accessdate = 2010-09-20 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20100612121320/http://www.israeltoday.co.il/default.aspx?tabid=132&view=item&idx=1413 | archivedate = 2010-06-12 }}</ref><ref name='Asharq Alawsat 2007-10-20'>{{cite news | first = Mounir | last = Abu Rizk | title = Christians in Gaza: An Integral Part of Society | date = 2007-10-20 | url = http://www.aawsat.com/english/news.asp?section=3&id=10613 | work = Asharq Alawsat | accessdate = 2010-09-20}}</ref>並且是加沙地帶唯一的[[福音派|福音派]]教會。當地其餘兩間教會是[[希臘正教|希臘正教]]的[[聖波斐利教堂|聖波斐利教堂]]和[[天主教|天主教]]的[[迦薩聖家堂|迦薩聖家堂]]。<ref name='Asharq Alawsat 2007-10-20'/><ref name='BBC 2005-12-21'>{{cite news | title = Middle East Christians: Gaza pastor | date = 2005-12-21 | url = http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/4514822.stm | work = BBC News | accessdate = 2010-09-20}}</ref>在2006年教會開設了加沙地帶的第一間公眾基督教圖書館。<ref name='Blake 2006-11-23'/>

教會大樓有六層高。<ref name='MNN 2007-02-07'>{{cite news | title = Gaza Baptist Church seized by Fatah | date = 2007-02-07 | url = http://www.mnnonline.org/article/9569 | work = Mission Network News | accessdate = 2010-09-20 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20110727091933/http://www.mnnonline.org/article/9569 | archivedate = 2011年7月27日 | df =  }}</ref>地下兩層是公用圖書館，有基督教和非基督教書籍。<ref name='BNL 2006-11-22'>{{cite news | title = Embattled Christians In Gaza Open Library Amid Violence | date = 2006-11-22 | url = http://www.bosnewslife.com/2584-2584-embattled-christians-in-gaza-open-library-ami | work = BosNewsLife | accessdate = 2010-09-20}}</ref>四樓用作宣教事工；五樓是宿舍，給外地到訪同工寄宿；六樓是敬拜會堂。<ref name='Blake 2006-11-23'>{{cite news | first = Daniel | last = Blake | title = First Christian Public Library Opens in Gaza | date = 2006-11-23 | url = http://www.christiantoday.com/article/first.christian.public.library.opens.in.gaza/8454.htm | work = Christianity Today | accessdate = 2010-09-20}}</ref>

加沙佔少數的基督徒，傳統上和當地佔大多數的穆斯林關係良好。<ref name='AP 2007-12-23'/>於2007年社會秩序大亂之前，教會開展青少年事工，開辦圖書館和數間診所。至2010年5月，教會仍然營辦一間學校，有約250名學生，當中很多是穆斯林。<ref name='Belz 2007-05-08'/>

==近期歷史==

===以色列炸彈攻擊===
在2007年2月或之前，教會的公眾圖書館，在三次事件中遭人縱火破壞。<ref name='MNN 2007-02-07'/>2008年12月一次以色列空襲中，教會大樓被附近炸彈爆炸損毀。<ref name='Allen 2009-01-06'>{{cite news | first = Bob | last = Allen | title = Gaza Baptist Church caught in crossfire | date = 2009-01-06 | url = http://www.abpnews.com/content/view/3749/103/ | work = Associated Baptist Press | accessdate = 2010-09-20 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20110514235544/http://www.abpnews.com/content/view/3749/103/ | archivedate = 2011-05-14 }}</ref><ref name='Kelly 2009-01-05'>{{cite news | first = Mark | last = Kelly | title = Gaza Baptist Church's building sustains damage in Israeli air strike | date = 2009-01-05 | url = http://www.bpnews.net/bpnews.asp?id=29605 | work = Baptist Press | accessdate = 2010-09-20 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120320033026/http://www.bpnews.net/bpnews.asp?id=29605 | archivedate = 2012-03-20 }}</ref>

===法塔赫和哈馬斯衝突===
加沙市以低層建築為主，於是迦薩浸信會大樓不尋常的高度，使其在[[法塔赫和哈馬斯衝突|法塔赫和哈馬斯衝突]]中，多次被法塔赫和哈馬斯部隊徵用作瞭望臺。<ref name='Jansezian 2007-06-10'/>因此教會數名同工被困在雙方交火中。有一次一位教會圖書館員，在敵對勢力槍戰中被擊中。<ref name='Jansezian 2007-06-10'/>另一次同類事件中，一位22歲新婚的教會公車司機被殺。<ref name='Jansezian 2007-06-10'/>教會在2007年2月被法塔赫警察搜查和短暫佔用。<ref name='Alford 2007-02-07'>{{cite news | first = Deann | last = Alford | title = Fatah Police Seize Gaza Baptist Church | date = 2007-02-07 | url = http://www.christianitytoday.com/ct/2007/februaryweb-only/106-34.0.html | work = Christianity Today | accessdate = 2010-09-20}}</ref>

===以色列的封鎖及往來限制===
Ayyad被害後，教會包括Massad牧師在內的七名領袖，遵從政府勸喻離開加沙。Massad牧師一家搬到[[約旦|約旦]]，其餘六人有五人搬到靠近[[伯利恆|伯利恆]]的[[西岸地區|西岸地區]]。此後只有Massad獲以色列當局准許回去。搬到伯利恆的五人被禁止離開該地區，因此有些人多年不能和家人見面。<ref name='Belz 2007-05-08'/>

Massad稱，[[2007年起對加沙的封鎖|以色列對加沙的封鎖]]，導致了「人民中很多有無路可走和絕望的感覺……更多貧困和更多苦難。」<ref name=abp_7jun2010>[http://www.abpnews.com/content/view/5213/53/ "American Baptist leader says Gaza flotilla reveals need for Mideast peace"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100611074119/http://www.abpnews.com/content/view/5213/53 |date=2010-06-11 }}, Bob Allen, Associated Baptist Press, 2010-06-07.</ref>當地生活費用上漲，醫療儀器短缺，但是絕大多數的當地人同意，相比起來更難忍受的，是以色列施加的往來限制。Massad總結巴勒斯坦基督徒的困境，就像生存於「兩團火之間，一團是穆斯林的迫害，一團是以色列的佔領。」<ref name='Belz 2007-05-08'/>
