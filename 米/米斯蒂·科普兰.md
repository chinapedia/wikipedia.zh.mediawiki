'''米斯蒂·丹妮爾·科普蘭'''（Misty Danielle Copeland，{{bd|1982年|9月10日|catIdx=Copeland, Misty}}）<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?t=16&v=saMB0Mr1qdE|title=MINKUS - "DON QUIXOTE" - BALLET ~ MISTY COPELAND - 15 - 1997 - VOB|publisher=[[YouTube|YouTube]]|accessdate=30 June 2015}}</ref>，是美國三大[[古典芭蕾|古典芭蕾]]{{link-en|芭蕾舞團|Ballet company|舞團}}之一{{link-en|美國芭蕾舞劇團|American Ballet Theatre}}（ABT）的首席[[芭蕾舞者|芭蕾舞者]]<ref>{{cite news | url = http://www.guardian.co.uk/stage/2007/feb/18/dance | title = One step closer to perfection: The best of Balanchine lights up London – but Stravinsky in Birmingham must not be missed | accessdate = 2008-08-26 | date = 2007-02-18 | publisher = Guardian News and Media Limited | work = [[The_Observer|The Observer]] | author = Jennings, Luke}}</ref>。她在2015年6月30日正式被晉升，同時也是ABT在其75年歷史以來的首位[[非裔美国人|非洲裔]]女性首席舞者<ref name=MCIPtPDaABT>{{cite web|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2015/07/01/arts/dance/misty-copeland-is-promoted-to-principal-dancer-at-american-ballet-theater.html|title=Misty Copeland Is Promoted to Principal Dancer at American Ballet Theater|last=Cooper|first=Michael|publisher=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|date=30 June 2015|accessdate=30 June 2015}}</ref><ref name=MCPtPDaABT>{{cite web|url=http://www.wsj.com/articles/misty-copeland-promoted-to-principal-dancer-at-american-ballet-theatre-1435679617?mod=rss_US_News|title=Misty Copeland Promoted to Principal Dancer at American Ballet Theatre|last=Catton|first=Pia|publisher=[[The_Wall_Street_Journal|The Wall Street Journal]]|date=30 June 2015|accessdate=30 June 2015}}</ref><ref name=amny>{{cite news|url=http://www.amny.com/entertainment/misty-copeland-named-american-ballet-theatre-s-first-black-principal-dancer-1.10596221|work=A.M. New York|title=Historic 1st for ballet company|first=Sheila Anne|last=Feeley|page=3|date=July 1, 2015|accessdate=July 1, 2015}}</ref>。此次晉升的一個多月前，她剛被《[[時代雜誌|時代雜誌]]》選為[[时代百大人物|百大人物]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/blogs/style-blog/wp/2015/04/16/misty-copeland-on-2015-time-100/|title=Misty Copeland on 2015 Time 100|accessdate=2015-04-28|date=2015-04-16|work=[[The_Washington_Post|The Washington Post]]|author=Kaufman, Sarah}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://artsbeat.blogs.nytimes.com/2015/04/16/misty-copeland-makes-the-cover-of-time-magazine|title=Misty Copeland Makes the Cover of Time Magazine|accessdate=2015-04-28|date=2015-04-16|work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|author=Cooper, Michael}}</ref>。

她曾被譽為「芭蕾[[神童|神童]]」，因為她13歲才開始學習，此前沒有受過任何相關的訓練。13至15歲期間，她的母親和芭蕾舞老師為了爭取擔任她的[[法定監護人|法定監護人]]打過官司；在此同時，她已經是一名芭蕾舞獎項的常勝軍，與此領域的專業人士<ref name="CHfBR">{{cite news | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1998/aug/28/local/me-17365 | title = Custody Hearing for Ballerina Rescheduled | accessdate = 2008-08-24 | date = 1998-08-28 | work = [[Los_Angeles_Times|Los Angeles Times]]}}</ref>。1998年，法院判決她取得了[[未成年人獨立資格|未成年人獨立資格]]（[[:en:Emancipation_of_minors|Emancipation of minors]]），以及對她母親的[[禁止令|禁止令]]（[[:en:Restraining_order|Restraining order]]）<ref name="Tdsiat">{{cite web | url = http://www.southcoasttoday.com/article/19981101/LIFE/311019931/101107/ARCHIVE | title = Teen dancer stumbles in adults' tug-of-war | accessdate = 2014-12-12 | date = 1998-11-01 | work = SouthCoast Today | work = [[The_Standard-Times_(New_Bedford)|The Standard-Times]] | author = Hastings, Deborah}}</ref>。之後，她與母親中止了訴訟，並搬出家中，至一位前ABT成員的老師處繼續學習<ref name="TSiMtC">{{cite web | url = http://www.la.com/ci_11394770?IADID=Search-www.la.com-www.la.com | title = This Swan is More than Coping | accessdate = 2011-01-17 | date = 2008-03-27 | publisher = LA.com | author = Farber, Jim | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20110713180910/http://www.la.com/ci_11394770?IADID=Search-www.la.com-www.la.com | archivedate = 2011-07-13 }}</ref>。

1997年，她奪得[[洛杉磯音樂中心|洛杉磯音樂中心]]聚光獎（Spotlight Award）中的「[[南加州|南加州]]最佳舞者獎」；在參加2次於ABT的夏季課程後的2000年，她成為其下的Studio Company成員；2001年，她升為ABT舞團的一員；2007年，晉升為{{link-en|獨舞者|Soloist (ballet)}}<ref name="MC">{{cite web | url = http://www.abt.org/dancers/detail.asp?Dancer_ID=56 | title = Misty Copeland | accessdate = 2008-08-24 | publisher = Ballet Theatre Foundation, Inc.}}</ref>。在技巧上，她被認為是古典芭蕾的舞蹈家<ref name="DRABTOoaEETIS">{{cite news | url = http://www.nytimes.com/2004/11/04/arts/dance/04ball.html? | title = Dance Review – American Ballet Theater: Out of an Ensemble Emerge Two Individual Spirits | accessdate = 2008-08-26 | date = 2004-11-04 | publisher = [[The_New_York_Times_Company|The New York Times Company]] | work = [[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] | author = Kisselgoff, Anna}}</ref>；在成為獨舞者的2007至2015年夏天，她更被形容已成為當代最精緻的舞者之一<ref name="FBSCaBQ">{{cite news | url = http://www.nytimes.com/2007/05/19/arts/dance/19abt.html? | title = For Ballet's Shifting Casts, a Big Question: Who Will Lift It to the Realm of Poetry?  | accessdate = 2008-08-26 | date = 2007-05-19 | publisher = [[The_New_York_Times_Company|The New York Times Company]] | work = [[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] | author = Dunning, Jennifer}}</ref>。
