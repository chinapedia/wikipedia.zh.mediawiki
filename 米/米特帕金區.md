'''米特帕金區'''（Meatpacking District），又譯肉類加工區，是位於[[紐約市|紐約市]][[曼哈頓|曼哈頓]]的一個地區，其範圍南起西14街，北至岡薩沃特街。西起哈德遜河，東至[[哈德遜街|哈德遜街]]<ref name="voice">{{cite news|url=http://www.villagevoice.com/nyclife/0252,mcpherson,40756,15.html|title=Close-Up on: The Meatpacking District|work=[[Village_Voice|Village Voice]]|date=December 24, 2002|first=Coco|last=McPherson|accessdate=2008-02-28}}</ref>，不過在一些定義中範圍會擴大<ref name="Gawker">{{cite news|url=http://gawker.com/news/meatpacking-district/hit-piece-meatpacking-district-202992.php|title=Close-Up on: The Meatpacking District|work=[[Gawker|Gawker]]|date=September 25, 2006|first=Chris|last=Mohney|accessdate=2008-02-28|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080116152130/http://gawker.com/news/meatpacking-district/hit-piece-meatpacking-district-202992.php|archivedate=2008年1月16日}}</ref>。米特帕金區自19世紀中期開始開發，曾是一個度假勝地<ref name=desrep>Shockley, Jay [http://www.nyc.gov/html/lpc/downloads/pdf/reports/gansevoortpt1.pdf "Gansevoort Market Historic District Designation Report part 1"] {{WebCite|url=https://www.webcitation.org/6BqkWJjeW?url=http://www.nyc.gov/html/lpc/downloads/pdf/reports/gansevoortpt1.pdf |date=20121101125706 |dateformat=iso }}, [[New_York_City_Landmarks_Preservation_Commission|New York City Landmarks Preservation Commission]] (September 9, 2003)</ref>。19世紀後期，這裡成為一個肉類和農產品加工中心。不過隨著工業的蕭條，該地區在二戰後曾是犯罪的溫床。1990年代之後，米特帕金區開始改造，成為一個歷史保護區和潮流文化區。
