'''赤藓醇'''（{{lang-en|Erythritol}}，'''(2''R'',3''S'')-丁烷-1,2,3,4-四醇'''）或稱'''赤藻糖醇'''，是一种存在於葡萄、梨子等果實或醬油、味噌、清酒等發酵食品中的天然[[糖醇|糖醇]](或者[[多元醇|多元醇]])。具有砂糖60-80%的糖度。赤藻糖醇是由葡萄糖發酵而成。幾乎不含熱量，也不會引起齲齒。由於能弱化齒垢中細菌彼此的結合力，因此具有分解齒垢的作用。它已经在美国获准使用。<ref name="cfsan.fda.gov">[http://www.fda.gov/Food/FoodIngredientsPackaging/GenerallyRecognizedasSafeGRAS/GRASListings/ucm154185.htm FDA/CFSAN: Agency Response Letter: GRAS Notice No. GRN 000076<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>它是由{{link-en|约翰·斯登豪斯|John Stenhouse}}于1848年首先发现的。<ref>The discovery of erythritol, which Stenhouse called "erythroglucin", was announced in: {{cite journal |author=Stenhouse, John |title=Examination of the proximate principles of some of the lichens |journal=Philosophical Transactions of the Royal Society of London |volume=138 |issue= |pages=63–89; see especially p. 76 |date=January 1, 1848 }}</ref>
