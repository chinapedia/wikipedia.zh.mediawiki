'''妊娠劇吐'''（{{lang|en|Hyperemesis gravidarum}}，HG）是{{le|妊娠併發症|complication of pregnancy}}的一種。其特徵為足以造成[[體重下降|體重下降]]與[[脫水|脫水]]的嚴重[[噁心|噁心]]和[[嘔吐|嘔吐]]<ref name=Drug2013>{{cite journal|title=Management of hyperemesis gravidarum.|journal=Drug Ther Bull|date=2013 Nov|volume=51|issue=11|pages=129-9|doi=10.1136/dtb.2013.11.0215|pmid=24227770}}</ref>。徵象和症狀包含一天內多次的劇吐還有暈眩感，其嚴重程度超越[[害喜|害喜]]。症狀通常會在懷孕二十週後稍微改善，但也可能整個懷孕期間都為此而苦<ref name=Women2010>{{cite web|title=Pregnancy|url=http://www.womenshealth.gov/pregnancy/you-are-pregnant/pregnancy-complications.html|website=Office on Women's Health|accessdate=5 December 2015|date=September 27, 2010}}</ref>。

<!-- Cause and diagnosis -->
造成妊娠劇吐的原因尚未確定<ref name=BMC2010/>，但已知的危險因子有初次懷孕、{{le|多胞胎|multiple pregnancy}}、[[肥胖症|肥胖症]]、[[妊娠滋養層細胞疾病|妊娠滋養層細胞疾病]]、[[進食障礙|進食障礙]]病史，或是先前曾發生或有妊娠劇吐的家族病史<ref name=BMC2010/><ref name=Fer2013>{{cite book|last1=Ferri|first1=Fred F.|title=Ferri's clinical advisor 2013 5 books in 1|date=2012|publisher=Elsevier Mosby|location=St. Louis, Mo.|isbn=9780323083737|page=538|edition=1st ed.|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=OR3VERnvzzEC&pg=PA538}}</ref>。本病通常根據其症狀確診。妊娠劇吐已經在實務上被定義為每日嘔吐超過三次、體重下降百分之五或三公斤、及在尿液中出現[[酮類|酮類]]<ref name=BMC2010>{{cite journal|last1=Jueckstock|first1=JK|last2=Kaestner|first2=R|last3=Mylonas|first3=I|title=Managing hyperemesis gravidarum: a multimodal challenge.|journal=BMC medicine|date=15 July 2010|volume=8|pages=46|pmid=20633258}}</ref>。確診前應排除[[泌尿道感染|泌尿道感染]]與[[甲狀腺功能亢進|甲狀腺功能亢進]]等可能造成相似症狀的疾病<ref name=AFP2007>{{cite journal|last1=Sheehan|first1=P|title=Hyperemesis gravidarum--assessment and management.|journal=Australian family physician|date=September 2007|volume=36|issue=9|pages=698-701|pmid=17885701}}</ref>。

<!-- Prevention and treatment -->

本病療法包括補充流質、攝取清淡食物<ref name=Women2010/>。建議包括服用{{le|電解質補充飲料|electrolyte-replacement drinks}}、[[硫胺|硫胺]]、還有高蛋白質食品<ref name=BMC2010/><ref name=Gab2012>{{cite book|last1=Gabbe|first1=Steven G.|title=Obstetrics : normal and problem pregnancies|date=2012|publisher=Elsevier/Saunders|location=Philadelphia, PA|isbn=9781437719352|page=117|edition=6th ed.|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=-3ufSTqeb6cC&pg=PA117}}</ref>。有一些女性需要[[靜脈注射|通過靜脈注射補充電解質及營養素]]<ref name=Women2010/>。藥物治療方面，建議使用[[吡哆醇|吡哆醇]]或是[[甲氧氯普胺|甲氧氯普胺]]<ref name=AFP2007/>，若無效則可用{{le|普樂明|Prochlorperazine}}、{{le|克暈片錠|dimenhydrinate}}、[[卓弗蘭|卓弗蘭]]治療<ref name=AFP2007/><ref name=BMC2010/>。一些患者可能需要入院治療。其他替代療法如[[心理療法|心理療法]]，以及傳統[[中醫|中醫]]則有對[[內關|內關]]穴進行{{le|穴位按壓|acupressure}}的治療法可能具有療效<ref name=BMC2010/><ref>{{Cite journal|title = Success of acupuncture and acupressure of the Pc 6 acupoint in the treatment of hyperemesis gravidarum|url = http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15004444|journal = Forschende Komplementärmedizin Und Klassische Naturheilkunde = Research in Complementary and Natural Classical Medicine|date = 2004-02-01|issn = 1424-7364|pmid = 15004444|pages = 20–23|volume = 11|issue = 1|doi = 10.1159/000077192|first = D.|last = Habek|first2 = A.|last2 = Barbir|first3 = J. C.|last3 = Habek|first4 = D.|last4 = Janculiak|first5 = M.|last5 = Bobić-Vuković}}</ref>。


關於懷孕時嘔吐的歷史紀錄，最早可以追溯到西元兩千年前，但直到1852年才由{{link-fr|安托尼·杜布瓦|Antoine Dubois}}首度以醫學文獻的形式清楚描述妊娠劇吐<ref>{{cite book|last1=Davis|first1=Christopher J.|title=Nausea and Vomiting : Mechanisms and Treatment|date=1986|publisher=Springer Berlin Heidelberg|location=Berlin, Heidelberg|isbn=9783642704796|page=152|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=ufoqBAAAQBAJ&pg=PA152}}</ref>。 根據統計，妊娠劇吐影響0.3–2.0%懷孕女性，過去也曾經是懷孕婦女的常見死因，但現今因為完善的治療，其造成的死亡已經非常罕見<ref name=Goodwin2008>{{cite journal|last=Goodwin|first=TM|title=Hyperemesis gravidarum.|journal=Obstetrics and gynecology clinics of North America|date=September 2008|volume=35|issue=3|pages=401–17, viii|pmid=18760227|doi=10.1016/j.ogc.2008.04.002}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|last1=Kumar|first1=Geeta|title=Early Pregnancy Issues for the MRCOG and Beyond|date=2011|publisher=Cambridge University Press|isbn=9781107717992|page=Chapter 6|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=lfSUAwAAQBAJ&pg=PT61}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|last1=DeLegge|first1=Mark H.|title=Handbook of home nutrition support|date=2007|publisher=Jones and Bartlett|location=Sudbury, Mass.|isbn=9780763747695|page=320|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=KtSF221KP-0C&pg=PA320}}</ref>。被妊娠劇吐影響的女性有較低的[[流產|流產]]風險，但伴隨著較高的[[早產|早產]]機率。某些女性會因為妊娠劇吐而選擇[[墮胎|墮胎]]<ref name=Gab2012/>。
