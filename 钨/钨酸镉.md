'''钨酸镉'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，[[化学式|化学式]]为CdWO<sub>4</sub>，或简写为'''CWO'''。

==用途==
钨酸镉可用于[[闪烁体探测器|闪烁体探测器]]来检测[[γ射线|γ射线]]或[[中子辐射|中子辐射]]。<ref name="Burachas">{{cite journal| doi=10.1016/0168-9002(95)00675-3| author=Burachas S. F. | title=Large volume CdWO<sub>4</sub> crystal scintillators| journal=Nucl. Instrum. and Methods in Phys. Research A | volume=369| issue=1| pages=164–168 | year=1996 |ref=Burachas|display-authors=etal}}</ref>它也是一些[[荧光透视|荧光透视]]仪器中[[钨酸钙|钨酸钙]]的替代品。<ref>{{cite web | title=Patterson Hand-Held Fluoroscope (ca. 1940s) | publisher=''Oak Ridge Associated Universities'' | year=1999 | url=http://www.orau.org/ptp/collection/Radiology/pattersonhandfluorotwo.htm | accessdate=2008-04-26}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|last=Kroeger|first=F. A.| title=Some Aspects of the Luminescence of Solids| publisher=Elsevier | year=1948}}</ref>
