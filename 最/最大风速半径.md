'''最大风速半径'''（{{lang-en|radius of maximum wind}}，简称{{lang|en|RMW}}）指的是[[气旋|气旋]]中心到其最强烈风带之间的距离，是一个用于大气动力学和[[热带气旋预测|热带气旋预测]]的参数<ref name="HSU">{{cite web|author1=S. A. Hsu |author2=Adele Babin |url=http://www.nwas.org/ej/hsu/hsu_babin_2005.pdf |title=Estimating the Radius of Maximum Winds Via Satellite During Hurricane Lili (2002) Over the Gulf of Mexico |accessdate=2014-02-23 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120206133851/http://www.nwas.org/ej/hsu/hsu_babin_2005.pdf |archivedate=2012-02-06 }}</ref>。[[热带气旋|热带气旋]]的最大风速半径内降雨量通常也最高。使用这个参数，可以对气旋的最大潜在强度和所产生[[风暴潮|风暴潮]]的规模做出估算。随着[[最大持续风速|最大持续风速]]的提高，最大风速半径通常会减少。近年来，这个参数已经开始在[[龙卷风|龙卷风]]的描述中使用。此外，在对[[建筑物|建筑物]]进行设计，防止因[[气压|气压]]变化而出现损伤时，也可以调用最大风速半径来进行计算。<ref>{{cite web | author1 = Donald W. Burgess | author2 = Michael A. Magsig | archivedate = 2013-03-21 | deadurl = no | url = http://www.wdtb.noaa.gov/modules/sls00/M3RADAR.pdf | title = Understanding WSR-88D Signatures for the 3 May 1999 Oklahoma City Tornado | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20130321215633/http://www.wdtb.noaa.gov/modules/sls00/M3RADAR.pdf | accessdate = 2014-02-23}}</ref>

== 测定 ==
大西洋出现气旋的最大风速半径传统上是由[[侦察机|侦察机]]来进行测量<ref name="HSU" />。并且也可以通过在[[天气图|天气图]]上测量气旋中心与系统最大压力梯度之间的距离来确定<ref>{{cite web|author1=Brian W. Blanchard |author2=S. A. Hsu |url=http://www.nwas.org/ej/pdf/2006-EJ4.pdf |title=ON THE RADIAL VARIATION OF THE TANGENTIAL WIND SPEED OUTSIDE THE RADIUS OF MAXIMUM WIND DURING HURRICANE WILMA (2005) |publisher=Louisiana State University |work=Coastal Studies Institute |accessdate=2014-02-23 |location=Baton Rouge, Louisiana |archivedate=2012-09-05 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120905133143/http://www.nwas.org/ej/pdf/2006-EJ4.pdf |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。使用[[气象卫星|气象卫星]]数据，通过测量[[卫星图像|红外卫星图像]]上顶端云层温度最低的位置到[[风眼|风眼]]中温度最高部分之间的距离，也可以测定最大风速半径。这种方法可行的原理在于，[[热带气旋|热带气旋]]中最强烈的气往往位于其最深层[[对流|对流]]中，在红外卫星图像上，最深层对流也就是温度最低的顶端云层<ref name="HSU" />。此外，多普勒[[气象雷达|气象雷达]]的速度数据也可以用来测算龙卷风和靠近海岸热带气旋的这一参数。

== 龙卷风 ==
[[File:05june-rapiddow-wide.gif|thumb]]一场[[龙卷风|龙卷风]]及其关联中气旋的雷达图像，左边的反向率数据显示出龙卷风关联的风眼墙，右边的速度数据显示出其最强风的位置。]]
{{See also|龙卷风}}
对于龙卷风最大风速半径的了解非常重要，因为密封建筑物内的气压变化可能导致建筑出现结构性损伤。大部分建筑物每28立方米体积就会有一平方英尺的开口以便平衡结构内部和外部之间的空气压力。气压变化幅度大约相当于最大风速半径数值的一半，龙卷风的最大风速半径通常都是其中心或风眼向外约46米到150米之间。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://nuclearpowerradiation.tpub.com/STD-10202002/STD-102020020175.htm | title = Natural Phenomena Hazards Design and Evaluation Criteria for Department of Energy: E.2.2 Additional Adverse Affects of Tornadoes | author = United States Department of Energy | date = | accessdate = 2014-02-23 | page = E7}}</ref>通过雷达测风仪实际测得的龙卷风中最宽的一个是1999年在[[俄克拉荷马州|俄克拉荷马州]]北部[[洛根县_(奥克拉荷马州)|洛根县]]和[[佩恩县_(奥克拉荷马州)|佩恩县]]交界处小镇[[马尔霍尔|马尔霍尔]]（{{lang|en|Mulhall}}）出现的，该州1999年出现了龙卷风的[[1999年俄克拉荷马州龙卷风爆发|大爆发]]，其最大风速半径超过800米<ref name="Mulhall">{{cite journal | last = Wurman | first = Joshua | authorlink = | coauthors = C. Alexander, P. Robinson, Y. Richardson | title = Low-Level Winds in Tornadoes and Potential Catastrophic Tornado Impacts in Urban Areas | journal = Bulletin of the American Meteorological Society | volume = 88 | issue = 1 | pages = 31-46 | publisher = American Meteorological Society | date = 2007-01 |url=http://journals.ametsoc.org/doi/abs/10.1175/BAMS-88-1-31 | accessdate = 2014-02-23 |doi=10.1175/BAMS-88-1-31 |bibcode = 2007BAMS...88...31W }}</ref>。

== 热带气旋 ==
{{See also|热带气旋|风暴潮}}
经过对中心最低气压在909[[巴|毫巴]]（[[帕斯卡|百帕]]，26.8[[英寸汞柱|英寸汞柱]]）到993毫巴（百帕，29.3英寸汞柱）之间的所有[[热带气旋|飓风]]进行测量后计算，其最大风速半径的平均值为47公里<ref>{{cite journal | author = S. A. Hsu and Zhongde Yana | journal = Journal of Coastal Research | jstor = 4298820 | title = A Note on the Radius of Maximum Winds for Hurricanes | volume = 12 | number = 2 | date = 1998 Spring | pages = 667-668 | publisher = Coastal Education & Research Foundation, Inc.}}</ref>。从热带气旋的强度来看，其最大持续风速最大，最大风速半径也就越小<ref name="Wil">{{cite web | url = http://severe.worldweather.org/iwtc/document/Topic_1_Hugh_Willoughby.pdf | author = Hugh Willoughby |title = Sixth International Workshop on Tropical Cyclones Topic 1 : Tropical Cyclone Structure and Structure Change | publisher = Severe Weather Information Centre | date = 2006-10-06 | accessdate = 2014-02-23 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120219052749/http://severe.worldweather.org/iwtc/document/Topic_1_Hugh_Willoughby.pdf | archivedate = 2012-02-19 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。经过观察还确认，强烈热带气旋产生降雨量最多的通常都在最大风速半径以内<ref>{{cite journal | url = http://www.mri-jma.go.jp/Publish/Technical/DATA/VOL_14/14_abs.pdf | title = The Study on the Changes of the Three-dimensional Structure and the Movement Speed of the Typhoon through its Life Time | author = Teruo Muramatsu | journal = Tech. Rep. Meteorol. Res. Inst. Number 14 | year = 1985 | page = 3 | accessdate = 2014-02-23 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20130208145541/http://www.mri-jma.go.jp/Publish/Technical/DATA/VOL_14/14_abs.pdf | archivedate = 2013-02-08 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。

最大风速半径有助于确认热带气旋会直接袭击的地点。如果一个热带气旋接近陆地到最大风速半径范围内，那么就可以认为这个热带气旋对该地进行了直接袭击<ref>{{cite web | author = National Weather Service | publisher = National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration | date = 2009-06-25 | url = http://www.weather.gov/glossary/index.php?letter=d | title = Glossary:  D | accessdate = 2009-11-20 | quote = For locations on the left-hand side of a tropical cyclone's track (looking in the direction of motion), a direct hit occurs when the cyclone passes to within a distance equal to the cyclone's radius of maximum wind. For locations on the right-hand side of the track, a direct hit occurs when the cyclone passes to within a distance equal to twice the radius of maximum wind. | archivedate = 2013-10-19 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131019225430/http://w1.weather.gov/glossary/index.php?letter=d}}</ref>。最大风速半径还有在热带气旋最大潜在强度的计算公式中使用，这个公式就是依靠热带气旋最大风速半径附近的风速来确认其最终潜力<ref>{{cite web | author = Kerry A. Emanuel | url = http://wind.mit.edu/~emanuel/pcmin/pclat/pclat.html | title = Maximum Intensity Estimation |accessdate=2014-02-23 | publisher = Massachusetts Institute of Technology | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121101095733/http://wind.mit.edu/~emanuel/pcmin/pclat/pclat.html | archivedate = 2012-11-01}}</ref>。

热带气旋产生最强烈风暴潮的范围通常会与最大风速半径重合。因为热带气旋内最强烈的风都处在最大风速半径内，这一区域内产生的海浪也最为强烈，并且海浪还会以最终以风暴中心为圆心向外漂移<ref>{{cite web|author1=Edward J. Walsh |author2=C. Wayne Wright |url=http://www.onr.navy.mil/sci_tech/32/reports/docs/05/mmwalsh5.pdf |title=Hurricane Wave Topography and Directional Wave Spectra in Near Real-Time |accessdate=2009-03-27 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090327040702/http://www.onr.navy.mil/sci_tech/32/reports/docs/05/mmwalsh5.pdf |deadurl=yes |archivedate=2009-03-27 }}</ref>。热带气旋会把三倍于最大风力半径范围内的海水混合，并且由于[[上升流|上升流]]的作用而降低海洋表面的海水温度<ref name="Wil" />。

对于热带气旋最大风速半径还存在多个未知领域，例如是否能够对其加以预测就是其中之一<ref>{{cite journal | url = https://ams.confex.com/ams/27Hurricanes/techprogram/paper_108632.htm | title = What Sets a Hurricane's Radius of Maximum Wind? | author1 = Agnieszka A. S. Mrowiec | author2 = S. T. Garner | author3 = O. Pauluis | journal = 27th Conference on Hurricanes and Tropical Meteorology | publisher = American Meteorological Society | date = 2006-04-25 | archivedate = 2012-02-20 | deadurl = no | accessdate = 2014-02-23 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120220013211/http://ams.confex.com/ams/27Hurricanes/techprogram/paper_108632.htm}}</ref>。
