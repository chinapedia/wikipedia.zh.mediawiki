[[热带气旋|热带气旋]]对应的'''最大持续风速'''（{{lang-en|Maximum sustained wind}}）是一个用于衡量风暴强度的常见[[指标|指标]]。一个发展成熟的热带气旋，其最大持续风速可以在[[风眼|风眼墙]]中探测出来。与[[风|阵风]]不同的是，持续风速是根据采样一段时间内的风速计算平均值来得出。对速的测量已经制订了全球统一的标准，在距[[地球|地球]]表面10米处进行，而最大持续风速则代表了热带气旋内部任何位置在一[[分钟|分钟]]或十分钟时间段内的最高平均风速。由于地球表面和[[大气层|大气层]]之间的[[磨擦|磨擦]]，风速有很大的变化范围，如果热带气旋位于陆地上空的山峰或山脉附近也同样会导致这样的情况。

对于海上的热带气旋，可以利用[[气象卫星|卫星]][[卫星图像|图像]]来确定其最高持续风速。如果有陆地、船只、[[飓风猎人|飓风猎人]][[侦察机|侦察机]]的观察报告以及[[气象雷达|雷达]]图像，那么气象部门同样可以对最大持续风速做出估算。这个数值可以通过[[萨菲尔-辛普森飓风等级|萨菲尔-辛普森飓风等级]]加以应用，有助于对热带气旋可能造成的破坏作出预计。

== 定义 ==
{{see also|最大风速半径}}
最大持续风速通常出现在热带气旋内距风暴中心还有一段距离的位置，这段距离被称为[[最大风速半径|最大风速半径]]，并且通常都在风眼墙中，距离进一步拉大后，风速又会开始减慢<ref>{{cite web|author1=Brian W. Blanchard |author2=S. A. Hsu |url=http://www.nwas.org/ej/pdf/2006-EJ4.pdf |title=ON THE RADIAL VARIATION OF THE TANGENTIAL WIND SPEED OUTSIDE THE RADIUS OF MAXIMUM WIND DURING HURRICANE WILMA (2005) |publisher=Louisiana State University |work=Coastal Studies Institute |accessdate=2014-02-23 |location=Baton Rouge, Louisiana |archivedate=2012-09-05 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120905133143/http://www.nwas.org/ej/pdf/2006-EJ4.pdf |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。大部分气象部门都会使用[[世界气象组织|世界气象组织]]推荐的持续风速定义，即在距地表十米高的位置测量十分钟并取平均值。不过，[[美国国家气象局|美国国家气象局]]的测量方式有所不同，其测量高度仍然是十米，但测量的时间范围则缩短到了一分钟<ref name="NWSM Defs">{{cite web | author = Tropical Cyclone Weather Services Program | title = Tropical cyclone definitions | url = http://www.weather.gov/directives/sym/pd01006004curr.pdf | format = PDF | date = 2006-06-01 | accessdate = 2014-02-23 | publisher = National Weather Service | archivedate = 2013-11-11 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131111165801/http://www.nws.noaa.gov/directives/sym/pd01006004curr.pdf}}</ref>。这是一个非常重要的区别，因为一分钟持续风速的数值要比十分钟持续风速的数值大14%<ref>{{cite web | publisher = United States Navy | url = http://www.nrlmry.navy.mil/pubs/TC_Forecasters_Guide/chap6/se200.htm | title = SECTION 2. INTENSITY OBSERVATION AND FORECAST ERRORS | accessdate = 2014-02-23}}</ref>。

== 数值测定 ==
大部分地区都会把[[德沃夏克分析法|德沃夏克分析法]]作为确定热带气旋最大持续风速的主要方法，这种方法是以[[气象卫星|气象卫星]]的观测数值来进行判断<ref name="ODT">{{cite web | author1 = Christopher S. Velden |author2 = Timothy L. Olander | author3 = Raymond M. Zehr | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140223085207/http://tropic.ssec.wisc.edu/misc/adt/odt/odt.html | title= Objective Dvorak Technique | url = http://tropic.ssec.wisc.edu/misc/adt/odt/odt.html | publisher = University of Wisconsin–Madison | accessdate = 2014-02-23 | archivedate = 2014-02-23 | deadurl = no}}</ref>，通过对螺旋[[雨带|带]]的规模和风眼与风眼墙之间的温度差来确定热带气旋的最大持续风速和[[气压|气压]]<ref>{{cite web | author = Chris Landsea | date = 2010-06-08 | url = http://www.aoml.noaa.gov/hrd/tcfaq/H1.html | title = Subject: H1) What is the Dvorak technique and how is it used? | publisher = Atlantic Oceanographic and Meteorological Laboratory | accessdate = 2014-02-23 | archivedate = 2014-01-25 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140125190925/http://www.aoml.noaa.gov/hrd/tcfaq/H1.html | deadurl = no}}</ref>。[[低气压|低气压区]]中心的[[气压|中心气压]]值为估计值。飓风的强度则是根据[[登陆_(气象学)|登陆]]时间和最大强度一起得出<ref name="NHC SSHS">{{cite web | author = National Hurricane Center | title = Saffir-Simpson Hurricane Scale Information | publisher = National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration | date = 2006-06-22 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140111191521/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/aboutsshws.php | archivedate = 2014-01-11 |url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/aboutsshws.php | accessdate = 2014-02-23 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。对精细卫星图像上单独云层的追踪还可以用来进一步估计热带气旋的表面风速<ref>{{cite web | author1 = A. F. Hasler | author2 = K. Palaniappan | author3 = C. Kambhammetu | author4 = P. Black | author5 = E. Uhlhorn | author6 = D. Chesters | url = http://journals.ametsoc.org/doi/abs/10.1175/1520-0477(1998)079%3C2483%3AHRWFWT%3E2.0.CO%3B2 | title = High-Resolution Wind Fields within the Inner Core and Eye of a Mature Tropical Cyclone from GOES 1-min Images | accessdate = 2014-02-23}}</ref>。

如果可以的话，科研人员也会使用船舶和陆地的观测数据。[[大西洋|大西洋]]以及[[太平洋|太平洋]]的中部和东部仍然会动用侦察机飞入热带气旋来确定飞行高度层的风力，这个数据加以调整后可以对最大持续风速做出相当可靠的估算。经过之前十年使用[[全球定位系统|全球定位系统]][[投落送|投落送]]后得出的大量数值已经证实，将飞行高度层取样的风速减少10%以后，就可以用来对接近地表的最大持续风速作出估算<ref name="Franklin">{{cite journal | author1 = James L. TUTTLE | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120921172543/http://cat.inist.fr/?aModele=afficheN&cpsidt=1801644 | author2 = R. GALL | url = http://cat.inist.fr/?aModele=afficheN&cpsidt=1801644 | title = A single-radar technique for estimating the winds in tropical cyclones | accessdate = 2014-02-23 | volume = 80 | year = 1999 | journal = Bulletin of the American Meteorological Society | issn = 0003-0007 | archivedate = 2012-09-21 | deadurl = no | pages = 653-668}}</ref>。

== 变动 ==
{{See also|大气边界层}}
大气与地球表面的磨擦导致地表风速会降低二十个百分点<ref>{{cite web | author = Jeff Haby | archivedate = 2012-06-29 | url = http://www.theweatherprediction.com/habyhints/10/ | title = The Importance of Friction | work = theweatherprediction.com | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120629074352/http://www.theweatherprediction.com/habyhints/10/ |deadurl=no | accessdate = 2014-02-23}}</ref>，地表的粗糙程度也会对风速产生很大影响。陆地上风速最高的通常是山峰或山脉的峰顶，而山峰和峰顶的阻挡又反过来使得山谷和山坡背的风速更慢<ref>{{cite web | archivedate = 2012-10-21 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121021080031/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/jht/05-07reports/JHT07_Miller_midyear.pdf | deadurl = no | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/jht/05-07reports/JHT07_Miller_midyear.pdf | title = Mapping of Topographic Effects on Maximum Sustained Surface Wind Speeds in Landfalling Hurricanes | accessdate = 2014-02-23}}</ref>。与水上相比，陆地上热带风旋的最大持续风速平均要低八个百分点<ref>{{cite web|first=James L. |last=Franklin |date=2002-08-04 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131003074034/http://www.aoml.noaa.gov/hrd/hurdat/august01/Franklin_response%282%29.txt |url=http://www.aoml.noaa.gov/hrd/hurdat/august01/Franklin_response(2).txt |title=Subject: Re: Offshore vs nearshore sonde composite |accessdate=2014-02-23 |archivedate=2013-10-03 |deadurl=no }}</ref>。更具体地来说，城市或粗糙地形上空的风速与高层[[地转风|地转风]]风速的差距幅度可以达到40%到50%，而在水面或冰面上，差距幅度则是在10%到30%之间<ref name="Franklin" /><ref>{{cite book | last = Harrison | first = Roy | title = Understanding Our Environment | publisher = Royal Society of Chemistry | location = Cambridge | year = 1999 | isbn = 0-85404-584-8 | page = 11}}</ref><ref name=Russell>{{cite book | last = Thompson | first = Russell | title = Atmospheric Processes and Systems | publisher = Routledge | location = New York | year = 1998 | isbn = 0-415-17145-8 | pages = 102-103 | accessdate = 2014-02-23 | url= http://books.google.com.hk/books/about/Atmospheric_Processes_and_Systems.html?id=jSsu8eVx298C}}</ref>。

== 与热带气旋强度分级间的关系 ==
大部分地区都是用最大持续风速来确定热带气旋的强度。例如大西洋和东风太平洋就是使用[[萨菲尔-辛普森飓风等级|萨菲尔-辛普森飓风等级]]，该等级可以用来对陆地上因热带气旋而导致的[[风暴潮|风暴潮]]和破坏做出估算<ref name="USA Today">{{cite news | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20130925065610/http://usatoday30.usatoday.com/weather/hurricane/whscale.htm | author = Williams, Jack | title = Hurricane scale invented to communicate storm danger | publisher = USA Today | date = 2005-05-17 | accessdate = 2014-02-23 | url= http://usatoday30.usatoday.com/weather/hurricane/whscale.htm | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2013-09-25}}</ref>。大部分地区的热带气旋强度分级（如热带低气压、热带风暴、飓风、台风、超强台风、低气压、深层低气压、强烈热带气旋）都是根据[[气旋|气旋]]的最大持续风速确定，只有[[澳大利亚|澳大利亚]]例外，该国海域不会使用最大持续风速来确定热带气旋的强度级别，而是使用阵风风速<ref>{{cite web | publisher = Bureau of Meteorology | url = http://www.bom.gov.au/catalogue/warnings/WarningsInformation_TC_Ed.shtml#Cat | title = Bureau of Meteorology: Tropical Cyclone Information Resources | accessdate = 2007-09-27 | archivedate = 2007-09-27 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20070927233700/http://www.bom.gov.au/catalogue/warnings/WarningsInformation_TC_Ed.shtml}}</ref>。
