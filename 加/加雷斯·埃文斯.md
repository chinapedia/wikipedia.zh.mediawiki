'''加雷斯·休·埃文斯'''（Gareth Huw Evans）是一名出生于[[威尔士|威尔士]]的电影导演，主要拍摄动作片<ref>{{cite news|title= GARETH EVANS AND IKO UWAIS TALK MERANTAU|work= [[Twitch_Film|Twitch Film]]|date= |url= http://twitchfilm.com/interviews/2009/02/gareth-evans-and-iko-uwais-talk-merantau.php|accessdate= 2011-09-22|deadurl= yes|archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20120309052649/http://twitchfilm.com/interviews/2009/02/gareth-evans-and-iko-uwais-talk-merantau.php|archivedate= 2012-03-09}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title= Gareth Evans – The Raid Interview at TIFF 2011|work= Tribute.ca|date=|url= http://www.tribute.ca/tiff/index.php/2011/09/08/gareth-evans-the-raid-interview-at-tiff-2011/|accessdate=2011-10-01}}</ref>。

他毕业于[[格拉摩根大学|格拉摩根大学]]（University of Glamorgan）的电影制作专业<ref>{{cite news|title= From the valleys of Wales to the hills of Hollywood: Fledgling director Gareth Evans' meteoric rise to fame as critics praise 'The Raid'|work= [[Daily_Mail|Daily Mail]]|date=|url= http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2105397/From-valleys-Wales-hills-Hollywood-Fledgling-director-Gareth-Evans-meteoric-rise-fame-critics-praise-The-Raid.html#ixzz1nEfw6GtS|accessdate=2012-02-23}}</ref>。

2011年他导演的警匪动作片《[[全面突袭|全面突袭]]》获得了[[多伦多电影节|多伦多电影节]]的午夜疯狂奖<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.deadline.com/tag/gareth-evans/ |title=Sony Pictures Classics Releasing 'The Raid' |date=November 29, 2011}}</ref>。
