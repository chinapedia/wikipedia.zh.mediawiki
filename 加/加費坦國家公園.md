'''加費坦國家公園'''（{{lang-sv|Garphyttans nationalpark}}）是[[瑞典|瑞典]]的一座國家公園，位於瑞典的中部地區<ref>{{cite web|url=http://sverigesnationalparker.se/en/choose-park---list/garphyttan-national-park/#.VqbNaVKy4Qw |title=Garphyttan national park | Choose Park - List | Sweden's national parks |publisher=Sverigesnationalparker.se |date= |accessdate=2016-01-26}}</ref>。加費坦國家公園成立於1909年，面積{{convert|1.11|km2|mi2|abbr=on}}<ref name="naturvårdsverket">{{cite web|title=Garphyttan National Park |url=http://www.naturvardsverket.se/en/In-English/Menu/Enjoying-nature/National-parks-and-other-places-worth-visiting/National-Parks-in-Sweden/Garphyttan-National-Park/ |publisher=[[Environmental_Protection_Agency_(Sweden)|Naturvårdsverket]] |accessdate=2009-02-26 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090404174552/http://www.naturvardsverket.se/en/In-English/Menu/Enjoying-nature/National-parks-and-other-places-worth-visiting/National-Parks-in-Sweden/Garphyttan-National-Park/ |archivedate=2009-04-04 }}</ref>，是瑞典第一批國家公園之一<ref name="naturvårdsverket" /> but was in existence as early as 1857.<ref>{{cite journal|title= Botaniska notiser: för år|journal=[[Botaniska_Notiser|Botaniska Notiser]]|volume=103|author=Lunds botaniska förening|publisher=O. Nordstedt och A. Falck|year=1857}}</ref>。
