'''加布里埃拉·達勒漫'''（{{lang-en|Gabrielle Daleman}}，{{bd|1998年|1月13日|catIdx=Daleman, Gabrielle}}）是一位[[加拿大|加拿大]]女子[[花樣滑冰|花樣滑冰]]選手。

== 个人生活 ==
達勒漫生于加拿大[[多伦多|多伦多]]，她的弟弟Zack同样也是一名花样滑冰运动员<ref name=YR140103>{{cite news |url= http://www.yorkregion.com/news-story/4298460-rogozine-daleman-lead-local-skaters-to-nationals/ |title= Rogozine, Daleman lead local skaters to nationals |first= David |last= Li |work= YorkRegion.com |date= 2014-01-03 |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20140801045936/http://www.yorkregion.com/news-story/4298460-rogozine-daleman-lead-local-skaters-to-nationals/ |archivedate= 2014-08-01 |deadurl= no }}</ref>。她曾就读于Pickering College<ref name=YR140103/><ref name=COC-GD>{{cite web |url= http://olympic.ca/team-canada/gabrielle-daleman/ |title= Gabrielle Daleman |publisher=Canadian Olympic Committee |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20140402041327/http://olympic.ca/team-canada/gabrielle-daleman/ |archivedate= 2014-04-02 |deadurl= no }}</ref>。
