'''加布里埃爾·鄧南遮''' （Gabriele d'Annunzio，原名'''Gaetano Rapagnetta'''；1863年3月12日－1938年3月1日），[[意大利|義大利]][[诗人|詩人]]、[[记者|記者]]、[[小说家|小說家]]、[[戲劇家|戲劇家]]和[[冒險者|冒險者]]。 <span data-segmentid="167" class="cx-segment">1889年至1910年期间，在意大利文学界占有重要地位。他經常被称为'''诗人'''（Il Vate）或 '''先知'''（Il Profeta ）。 <span data-segmentid="175" class="cx-segment">主要作品有《玫瑰三部曲》。</span></span>

<span data-segmentid="175" class="cx-segment">自</span><span data-segmentid="167" class="cx-segment">1914年至1924年期间，鄧南遮在政</span><span data-segmentid="175" class="cx-segment">治</span><span data-segmentid="167" class="cx-segment">中占有重要地位。鄧南遮</span><span data-segmentid="175" class="cx-segment">常被視作[[贝尼托·墨索里尼|墨索里尼]]的先驅者，在政治上頗受爭議。其</span><span data-segmentid="176" class="cx-segment">思想和美学影响了</span>[[国家法西斯党|<span data-segmentid="175" class="cx-segment">義</span><span data-segmentid="176" class="cx-segment">大利法西斯</span>]]<span data-segmentid="176" class="cx-segment">和[[希特勒|希特勒]]。</span>

<span data-segmentid="180" class="cx-segment">文學方面，鄧南遮与[[頹廢主義運動|颓废主义运动]]關聯密切。該運動与[[法国|法国]][[象征主义|象征主义]]和[[英国|英国]][[唯美主义|唯美主义]]相互影響。鄧南遮</span><span data-segmentid="184" class="cx-segment">的</span><span data-segmentid="180" class="cx-segment">一些</span><span data-segmentid="184" class="cx-segment">作品</span><span data-segmentid="180" class="cx-segment">承繼了[[浪漫主義|浪漫主義]]傳統</span><span data-segmentid="184" class="cx-segment">，與[[自然主義|自然主義]]針鋒相對，既感性，又帶神秘感。 鄧南遮無論是在文學方面，或是在政治行動上，都</span><span data-segmentid="186" class="cx-segment">受到[[尼采|尼采]]的影响。 他和幾名女性的風流韻事，如</span>{{Link-en|露易莎‧卡薩提|Luisa Casati}}<span data-segmentid="188" class="cx-segment">，引起了公眾的注意。</span>

<span data-segmentid="191" class="cx-segment">[[第一次世界大战|第一次世界大战]]期间，[[義大利人|義大利人]]对邓南遮的印象，從文学家转变为民族战争英雄。<ref>[http://ilpiccolo.gelocal.it/polopoly_fs/1.13266527.1460193009!/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/detail_558/image.jpg D'Annunzio and "Carnaro" irredentism]</ref> 鄧年遮加入[[義大利陸軍|義]]</span>[[義大利陸軍|大利陆军]]<span data-segmentid="191" class="cx-segment">的</span><span data-segmentid="193" class="cx-segment">[[阿迪蒂突击队|阿迪蒂突擊隊]]，參與諸多軍事行动，如</span>{{Link-en|Flight over Vienna|Flight over Vienna}}{{找中文}}<span data-segmentid="193" class="cx-segment">。 以義大利[[民族主義|民族主義]]者之姿，</span><span data-segmentid="197" class="cx-segment">反对[[巴黎和会|巴黎和会]]。鄧南遮在[[阜姆|阜姆]]建立了短命的</span>{{Link-en|義大利克瓦內爾臨時政府|Italian Regency of Carnaro}}<span data-segmentid="197" class="cx-segment">，並自稱「[[Duce|領袖]]」。</span> <span data-segmentid="202" class="cx-segment">宪法使</span><span data-segmentid="197" class="cx-segment">「</span><span data-segmentid="202" class="cx-segment">音乐</span><span data-segmentid="197" class="cx-segment">」</span><span data-segmentid="202" class="cx-segment">成为国家的基本原则，</span><span data-segmentid="197" class="cx-segment">並在本質上施行[[社團主義|組合主]]</span>[[社團主義|義]]<span data-segmentid="202" class="cx-segment">。<ref>{{Cite book|title=La sinistra fascista|last=Parlato|first=Giuseppe|publisher=Il Mulino|year=2000|location=Bologna|pages=88|language=it}}</ref></span>

== <span data-segmentid="277" class="cx-segment">向阜姆進军</span> ==
<span data-segmentid="289" class="cx-segment">第一次战争加强了鄧南遮的[[极端民族主义|极端民族主义]]和[[领土收复主义|領土收復主義]]的想法。鄧南遮展開广泛的行動，希望義大利是以一流歐洲強權之姿，与戰時盟友一同作战。 </span><span data-segmentid="293" class="cx-segment">1919年9月12日，<span data-segmentid="289" class="cx-segment">因不滿</span>巴黎</span><span data-segmentid="289" class="cx-segment">和</span><span data-segmentid="293" class="cx-segment">会上</span><span data-segmentid="289" class="cx-segment">移交人口大多為義大利人的阜姆</span><span data-segmentid="293" class="cx-segment">的要求，</span><span data-segmentid="289" class="cx-segment">鄧南遮率領</span><span data-segmentid="293" class="cx-segment">2000名意大利民族主义</span><span data-segmentid="289" class="cx-segment">者</span><span data-segmentid="293" class="cx-segment">占领城市，迫使</span><span data-segmentid="289" class="cx-segment">美英法的</span><span data-segmentid="293" class="cx-segment">占领部队撤出。<ref>H.R. Kedward, ''Fascism in Western Europe 1900–45'', p 40 New York University Press New York, 1971</ref> 鄧南遮等人</span><span data-segmentid="297" class="cx-segment">试图让意大利吞并<span data-segmentid="289" class="cx-segment">阜姆</span>，但遭到義大利政府拒绝。</span><span data-segmentid="298" class="cx-segment">意大利</span><span data-segmentid="297" class="cx-segment">政府</span><span data-segmentid="298" class="cx-segment">封锁阜姆，要求投降。</span>

<span data-segmentid="301" class="cx-segment">鄧南遮随后宣布在<span data-segmentid="289" class="cx-segment"><span data-segmentid="299" class="cx-segment">阜姆</span></span>成立獨立國家「{{Link-en|義大利克瓦內爾臨時政府|Italian Regency of Carnaro}}<span data-segmentid="289" class="cx-segment"><span data-segmentid="299" class="cx-segment">」( Italian Regency of Carnaro)</span></span>；《克瓦內爾宪章》影響了后来意大利的法西斯体系，包含鄧南遮自稱「领袖」(Duce)。一部份的</span><span data-segmentid="304" class="cx-segment">[[意大利皇家海军|意大利皇家海军]]加入了鄧南遮。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://query.nytimes.com/gst/abstract.html?res=9D07E7D7163CE533A25752C1A9649D946195D6CF|title=D'ANNUNZIO PAYS DESERTING SAILORS; Hands Out 10,000 Francs to Crew of Destroyer—Its Officer Bound to Gun.WRANGEL TROOPS NEAR BYMany in Rome Look Hopefully to Giolitti to Find a Way Outof Flume Crisis|date=11 December 1920|work=The New York Times|accessdate=3 May 2010}}</ref> 鄧南遮嘗試為其他被壓迫的民族</span><span data-segmentid="306" class="cx-segment">，</span><span data-segmentid="304" class="cx-segment">如鄧南遮在1920年試圖武裝的[[愛爾蘭人|愛爾蘭人]]，提供國聯之外的另一種選擇；並</span><span data-segmentid="306" class="cx-segment">寻求与巴尔干地区的各种[[分离主义|分离主义]]团体结盟，特別是在巴爾幹的義大利分離主義份子，但是没有取得多大成功。<ref>Mark Phelan, 'Prophet of the Oppressed Nations: Gabriele D'Annunzio and the Irish Republic, 1919–1921, ''History Ireland''vol. 21, no, 5(Sept/Oct 2013, pp. 44–50.</ref><ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=bnsITEI0l4IC&printsec=frontcover&dq=ekrem+vlora&hl=en&sa=X&ei=-xwBU8D4BMf20wGD8YCYBw&ved=0CCsQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q=beqir%20vokshi&f=false|title=Lebenserinnerungen: 1912 bis 1925|last=Ekrem Vlora|publisher=Walter de Gruyter|year=1973|isbn=9783486475715|page=154|language=de|trans-title=Memoirs: 1912–1925}}</ref></span> <span data-segmentid="312" class="cx-segment">直到1920年12月，在意大利海军的轰炸下，</span><span data-segmentid="306" class="cx-segment">無視[[拉巴洛條約_(1920年)|拉巴洛條約]]</span><span data-segmentid="312" class="cx-segment">而向意大利宣战的鄧南遮，最終投降。</span>

== <span data-segmentid="316" class="cx-segment">晚年</span> ==
<span data-segmentid="319" class="cx-segment">在向阜姆進军之後，鄧南遮隱居[[加尔达湖|加爾達湖]]，在寫作中渡過晚年。 </span><span data-segmentid="321" class="cx-segment">虽然</span><span data-segmentid="319" class="cx-segment">鄧南遮對墨索里尼有很大的影響</span><span data-segmentid="321" class="cx-segment">，但他从未直接卷入意大利法西斯的政治活动。</span><span data-segmentid="319" class="cx-segment">1922，</span><span data-segmentid="321" class="cx-segment">向羅馬進军之前，鄧南遮因不明原因從窗邊跌落，受傷但未死。</span>

<span data-segmentid="327" class="cx-segment">1924年，鄧南遮受國王[[伊曼紐三世|伊曼紐三世]]授予貴族頭銜(Principe di Montenevoso</span>{{找中文}}<span data-segmentid="327" class="cx-segment">)。 </span><span data-segmentid="332" class="cx-segment">1938年，因中风去世。 </span><span data-segmentid="334" class="cx-segment">墨索里尼国葬</span><span data-segmentid="332" class="cx-segment">鄧南遮</span><span data-segmentid="334" class="cx-segment">，</span><span data-segmentid="332" class="cx-segment">葬於</span><span data-segmentid="334" class="cx-segment">[[義大利勝利莊園|义大利胜利庄园]]。</span>

== <span data-segmentid="342" class="cx-segment">政治地位</span> ==
<span data-segmentid="344" class="cx-segment">鄧南遮通常被認為是義大利法西斯主義的先驅者。</span><span data-segmentid="346" class="cx-segment">在阜姆</span><span data-segmentid="344" class="cx-segment">的時候</span><span data-segmentid="346" class="cx-segment">，</span><span data-segmentid="344" class="cx-segment">鄧南遮</span><span data-segmentid="346" class="cx-segment">与工团主义者Alceste De Ambris</span>{{找中文}}<span data-segmentid="344" class="cx-segment">共同起草《克瓦內爾宪章》</span><span data-segmentid="346" class="cx-segment">。</span> <span data-segmentid="350" class="cx-segment">Alceste提供了法律和政治框架，</span><span data-segmentid="346" class="cx-segment">鄧南遮則增添了一些文學修飾</span><span data-segmentid="350" class="cx-segment">。</span><span data-segmentid="351" class="cx-segment">Alceste De Ambris是一群意大利海员的领袖，叛变后</span><span data-segmentid="350" class="cx-segment">，率艦投靠鄧南遮</span><span data-segmentid="351" class="cx-segment">。</span><span data-segmentid="352" class="cx-segment">宪法建立了一个</span><span data-segmentid="351" class="cx-segment">組合</span><span data-segmentid="352" class="cx-segment">主义的国家</span><span data-segmentid="351" class="cx-segment">。九個</span><span data-segmentid="352" class="cx-segment">團體代表代表不同的经济部门(工人</span><span data-segmentid="351" class="cx-segment">、雇傭主、</span><span data-segmentid="352" class="cx-segment">专业人员等)，第十个(鄧南遮的發名)則代表「优秀」的人类(英雄、诗人、先知、超人)。憲章</span><span data-segmentid="354" class="cx-segment">还宣布，音乐是国家的基本原则。</span>

<span data-segmentid="355" class="cx-segment">墨索里尼从鄧南遮身上模仿和学习了獨裁者的文化。鄧南遮就像是</span><span data-segmentid="357" class="cx-segment">意大利法西斯主义的</span><span data-segmentid="355" class="cx-segment">施洗約翰一</span><span data-segmentid="357" class="cx-segment">般，</span><span data-segmentid="355" class="cx-segment">義大利</span><span data-segmentid="357" class="cx-segment">法西斯</span><span data-segmentid="355" class="cx-segment">所使用的</span><span data-segmentid="357" class="cx-segment">仪式</span><span data-segmentid="355" class="cx-segment">與符號幾乎就在鄧南遮</span><span data-segmentid="357" class="cx-segment">向阜姆</span><span data-segmentid="355" class="cx-segment">進军期間發明出來</span><span data-segmentid="357" class="cx-segment">，<ref name="Ledeen">{{Cite book|title=''Preface''|last=Ledeen|first=Michael Arthur|publisher=Transaction Publishers|year=2001|isbn=9780765807427|edition=2, illustrated}}</ref><ref name="Paxton">{{Cite book|title=Taking Root|last=Paxton|first=Robert O.|publisher=Random House, Inc.|year=2005|isbn=9781400040940|edition=reprint|series=Vintage Series|pages=59–60}}</ref></span> <span data-segmentid="360" class="cx-segment">包括</span><span data-segmentid="357" class="cx-segment">陽台樣式、</span><span data-segmentid="360" class="cx-segment">罗马</span><span data-segmentid="357" class="cx-segment">式敬禮、模仿《[[伊里亞德|伊里亞德]]》[[阿基里斯|阿基里斯]]的哭喊聲、</span><span data-segmentid="362" class="cx-segment">与群眾間充滿戏剧性和修辞的对话</span><span data-segmentid="357" class="cx-segment">、宗教符號的政治運用</span><span data-segmentid="362" class="cx-segment">。<ref name="Ledeen" /> 此外，</span><span data-segmentid="363" class="cx-segment">还包括</span><span data-segmentid="362" class="cx-segment">在<span data-segmentid="357" class="cx-segment">阜姆</span></span><span data-segmentid="363" class="cx-segment">的政府體制：組合主義國家的經濟模式、戲劇把戲、情緒化的民族主義儀式；還有黑杉军，即[[阿迪蒂突击队|阿迪蒂突擊隊]]，擁有纪律、野兽般的反应和对异己的强大武装镇压能力。<ref>''The United States and Italy'', H. Stuart Hughes, Harvard University Press, Cambridge, MA, 1953, pp. 76 and 81–82.</ref> <span data-segmentid="365" class="cx-segment">蓖麻油，是一種泻药。</span></span><span data-segmentid="365" class="cx-segment">据说</span><span data-segmentid="363" class="cx-segment">，鄧南遮</span><span data-segmentid="365" class="cx-segment">甚至开创了强</span><span data-segmentid="363" class="cx-segment">灌</span><span data-segmentid="365" class="cx-segment">大量[[蓖麻油|蓖麻油]]的酷刑，用以羞辱、殘廢或杀死反對者。而墨索里尼的[[黑衫軍|黑衫军]]承繼了這個手法。<ref>Cecil Adams, [http://www.straightdope.com/classics/a4_028.html Did Mussolini use castor oil as an instrument of torture?], ''The Straight Dope'', 22 April 1994. Accessed 6 November 2006.</ref><ref>Richard Doody, {{Cite web|url=http://worldatwar.net/nations/other/fiume/|title=Stati Libero di Fiume – Free State of Fiume|accessdate=2002-08-24|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090308033039/http://worldatwar.net/nations/other/fiume/|archivedate=8 March 2009}}, ''The World at War''.</ref><ref>Cali Ruchala, {{Cite web|url=http://www.diacritica.com/degenerate/6/dannunzio7.html|title="Superman, Supermidget": the Life of Gabriele D'Annunzio, Chapter Seven: The Opera|accessdate=2006-11-06|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20050210064018/http://www.diacritica.com/degenerate/6/dannunzio7.html|archivedate=10 February 2005}}, ''Degenerate'' magazine, Diacritica (2002).</ref></span>

鄧南遮擁護義大利向外擴張的外交政策，甚至歡迎[[第二次義大利衣索比亞戰爭|義大利對衣索比亞的侵略]]。

=== <span data-segmentid="378" class="cx-segment">与墨索里尼的竞争關係</span> ===
<span data-segmentid="388" class="cx-segment">尽管墨索里尼的法西斯主义深受《克瓦內爾宪章》的影响，但鄧南遮及Alceste De Ambris都不想積極參與義大利法西斯運動。兩人都拒绝了法西斯主義者参加1921年5月15日选举的徵招。 </span><span data-segmentid="391" class="cx-segment">在向罗马进军之前，Alceste De Ambris甚至</span><span data-segmentid="388" class="cx-segment">批評</span><span data-segmentid="391" class="cx-segment">法西斯运动</span><span data-segmentid="388" class="cx-segment">。</span>

<span data-segmentid="395" class="cx-segment">1922年8月13日，鄧南遮因从窗户跌落而严重受伤；與</span>{{Link-en|弗朗西斯科|Francesco Saverio Nitti}} <span data-segmentid="395" class="cx-segment">及墨索里尼共同举行的「全国和解会议」的計劃遭到取消。</span> <span data-segmentid="397" class="cx-segment">这一事件从未得到解释，</span><span data-segmentid="395" class="cx-segment">而</span><span data-segmentid="397" class="cx-segment">一些历史学家认为这是一起谋杀</span><span data-segmentid="395" class="cx-segment">鄧南遮</span><span data-segmentid="397" class="cx-segment">的</span><span data-segmentid="395" class="cx-segment">計劃</span><span data-segmentid="397" class="cx-segment">。</span> <span data-segmentid="398" class="cx-segment">尽管</span><span data-segmentid="397" class="cx-segment">鄧南遮</span><span data-segmentid="398" class="cx-segment">在这次事件后退出了</span><span data-segmentid="397" class="cx-segment">公眾目光</span><span data-segmentid="398" class="cx-segment">，但</span><span data-segmentid="397" class="cx-segment">墨索里尼</span><span data-segmentid="398" class="cx-segment">仍然定期向</span><span data-segmentid="397" class="cx-segment">鄧南遮</span><span data-segmentid="398" class="cx-segment">发放</span><span data-segmentid="397" class="cx-segment">慰問</span><span data-segmentid="398" class="cx-segment">金，作为不再重新参与政治舞台的贿赂。</span> <span data-segmentid="399" class="cx-segment">当一位密友问及此事时，据称墨索里尼说</span><span data-segmentid="398" class="cx-segment">：「</span><span data-segmentid="399" class="cx-segment">当你有一颗蛀牙时，你有两种可能性</span><span data-segmentid="398" class="cx-segment">：</span><span data-segmentid="399" class="cx-segment">要么拔掉牙齿，要么</span><span data-segmentid="398" class="cx-segment">塞</span><span data-segmentid="399" class="cx-segment">满金子。</span><span data-segmentid="398" class="cx-segment">對</span><span data-segmentid="399" class="cx-segment">付鄧南遮</span><span data-segmentid="400" class="cx-segment">，我选择了后者。</span><span data-segmentid="399" class="cx-segment">」</span><span data-segmentid="400" class="cx-segment"><ref>''he Vittoriale degli Italiani'', Fred Licht, The Journal of the Society of Architectural Historians, Vol. 41, No. 4 (Dec. 1982), pp. 318–324</ref></span>

<span data-segmentid="401" class="cx-segment">不過，直到1938年去世之前，鄧南遮仍一直试图干预政治。 </span><span data-segmentid="402" class="cx-segment">他在1933年写信给墨索里尼，试图说服他不要参与[[轴心国|轴心国]]与[[阿道夫·希特勒|希特勒]]的协议。 </span><span data-segmentid="403" class="cx-segment">1934年，在希特勒和墨索里尼会面后，</span><span data-segmentid="402" class="cx-segment">鄧南遮</span><span data-segmentid="403" class="cx-segment">试图破坏兩者之间的关系，甚至写了一本关于希特勒的讽刺小册子。</span>
