'''奔宁山脉断裂带'''是一个自西北向东南的[[断裂带|断裂带]]。它形成于[[奔宁山脉|奔宁山脉]]的西南边界上，位于[[坎布里亚郡|坎布里亚郡]]。它是于[[二叠纪|二叠纪]]形成，为正断层，具有半[[地堑|地堑]]形状，盆地边界是[[伊甸谷|伊甸谷]] 。它连接到[[凹陷断层|凹陷断层]]的东南端。<ref name="Hughes">{{cite web |url = http://nora.nerc.ac.uk/3674/1/RR02001.pdf|title = Permian and Triassic rocks of the Appleby district (part of Sheet 30, England and Wales)|publisher = Natural Environment Research Council|work = Integrated Geoscience Surveys (North) Research Report RR/02/01|date = 2003|accessdate = 19 March 2015|author = Hughes R.A.}}</ref>[[奥陶纪|奥陶纪]]和[[志留紀|志留紀]]时代露出地面的岩石堆积在断层两岸，形成[[克罗斯山|克罗斯山]]。<ref name="Geodiversity">{{cite web|url = http://www.northpennines.org.uk/Lists/DocumentLibrary/Attachments/175/GeodiversityAudit.pdf|title = North Pennines Area of Outstanding Beauty and European Geopark: A Geodiversity Audit|publisher = North Pennines AONB|date = 2010|accessdate = 19 March 2015|deadurl = yes|archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150402130613/http://www.northpennines.org.uk/Lists/DocumentLibrary/Attachments/175/GeodiversityAudit.pdf|archivedate = 2015年4月2日}}</ref>
