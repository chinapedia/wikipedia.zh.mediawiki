'''格蘭大道-新城車站'''（{{lang-en|Grand Avenue–Newtown}}）是[[紐約地鐵|紐約地鐵]][[IND皇后林蔭路線|IND皇后林蔭路線]]的一個慢車[[地鐵站|地鐵站]]，位於[[皇后區|皇后區]][[埃姆赫斯特_(皇后區)|埃姆赫斯特]]{{link-en|格蘭街 (布魯克林)|Grand Street (Brooklyn)|格蘭大道}}、百老匯及{{link-en|皇后林蔭路|Queens Boulevard}}交界東北角的私人住宅地下，設有[[紐約地鐵R線|R線]]（任何時候停站（深夜除外））<ref>{{NYCS const|timetable|r}}</ref>、[[紐約地鐵E線|E線]]（僅深夜停站）<ref>{{NYCS const|timetable|e}}</ref>、[[紐約地鐵M線|M線]]（僅平日停站）列車服務<ref>{{NYCS const|timetable|m}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://web.mta.info/maps/service_guide.pdf|title=Subway Service Guide September 2015|last=|first=|date=September 2015|website=mta.info|publisher=Metropolitan Transportation Authority|access-date=September 20, 2016}}</ref>。

此站設有兩個[[側式月台|側式月台]]和四條軌道.<ref name="tracks">{{NYCS const|trackref|trackbook3}}</ref>。快車和慢車軌道之間有軌道牆分隔<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=http://www.nycsubway.org/perl/show?70883|title=Trackway Walls at Grand Avenue|last=E.|first=Bill|date=July 31, 2007|website=www.nycsubway.org|publisher=|access-date=December 13, 2016}}</ref>。車站設有全長夾層，但閘機和詢問處都位於夾層中央，跨線橋只位於最東面的樓梯<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.subwaynut.com/ind/grandr/grandr19.jpg|title=A sign indicating that the crossover to the other platform is at the easternmost end of the platform.|last=Cox|first=Jeremiah|date=June 5, 2008|website=subwaynut.com|publisher=|access-date=December 13, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.subwaynut.com/ind/grandr/grandr15.jpg|title=Two High Entrance Turnstiles, and Two High Exit Only turnstiles on the very narrow portion of the mezzanine that serves as an overpass between the two platforms.|last=Cox|first=Jeremiah|date=June 5, 2008|website=subwaynut.com|publisher=|access-date=December 13, 2016}}</ref>。
