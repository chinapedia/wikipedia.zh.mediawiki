'''葛拉罕·摩爾'''（'''Graham Moore'''，{{bd|1981年|10月18日}}）是一位美国作家与编剧，成名于2010年发表的处女作书籍 ''The Sherlockian''，位列[[纽约时报畅销书榜|纽约时报畅销书榜]]<ref>[http://www.newsofmillcreek.com/content/fiction-book-group-sherlockian-mill-creek-library Fiction Book Group: "The Sherlockian," Mill Creek Library | News of Mill Creek<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>。编剧代表作为2014年历史传记片《[[模仿游戏|模仿游戏]]》<ref>[http://www.deadline.com/2012/09/teddy-schwarzmans-black-bear-pictures-wins-graham-moore-black-list-script-imitation-game/ Black Bear Pictures Wins Graham Moore Black List Script 'Imitation Game' - Deadline.com<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>，为其赢得[[第87届奥斯卡金像奖|第87届奥斯卡金像奖]]、[[美国编剧工会奖|美国编剧工会奖]]、[[卫星奖|卫星奖]]最佳改编剧本奖，以及[[第72届金球奖|第72届金球奖]]最佳剧本提名、[[英国电影学院奖|英国电影学院奖]]最佳改编剧本提名。

==生平==
摩尔生于[[芝加哥|芝加哥]]的一个[[犹太人|犹太人]]家庭<ref name="buzzfeed">{{cite web|url=http://www.buzzfeed.com/eahanks/benedict-cumberbatch-alan-turing-graham-moore |title=How Benedict Cumberbatch And Alan Turing Helped A Writer Find Success In Hollywood |publisher=[[BuzzFeed|BuzzFeed]] |first=E.A. |last=Hanks |accessdate=November 11, 2014 }}</ref>，父母亲都是[[律师|律师]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://articles.chicagotribune.com/2009-07-20/news/0907190101_1_michelle-obama-valerie-jarrett-obama-administration |title=Michelle Obama's confidant-in-chief: Susan Sher |publisher=''Chicago Tribune'' |date=July 20, 2009 |first=Mike |last=Dorning |accessdate=November 12, 2014 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://voices.washingtonpost.com/reliable-source/2010/12/its_all_about_mom_at_first-tim.html|title=It's all about mom at first-time novelist Graham Moore's book party at the veep's house|work=Washington Post|date=December 2, 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mediabistro.com/fishbowldc/author-graham-moores-presidential-perks_b26252 |title=Author Graham Moore’s Presidential Perks |publisher=Mediabistro |date=December 2, 2010 |first=Matt |last=Dornic |accessdate=November 11, 2014 }}</ref>，在其幼时父母离婚。1999年毕业于[[哥伦比亚大学|哥伦比亚大学]]的[[宗教|宗教]]历史专业<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.ucls.uchicago.edu/alumni/alumni-weekend/young-alumni-professional-achievement-award/index.aspx |title=RISING STAR PROFESSIONAL ACHIEVEMENT AWARD |publisher=University of Chicago Laboratory Schools |accessdate=November 11, 2014 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141111093100/http://www.ucls.uchicago.edu/alumni/alumni-weekend/young-alumni-professional-achievement-award/index.aspx |archivedate=2014年11月11日 }}</ref>。后来曾在[[音乐|音乐]]行业任[[音频工程师|音频工程师]]。

目前他居住在[[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]]。<ref>[http://www.thesherlockian.com/about-graham-moore/ The Sherlockian » About Graham Moore<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>

在第87届奥斯卡金像奖获奖发言中，摩尔表示在他16岁时曾有过[[自杀|自杀]]倾向。<ref >{{cite web|url=http://ent.163.com/15/0224/08/AJ73NLBR000300B1.html | language= | title=奥斯卡最佳改编剧本得主受访 透露曾有自杀倾向 |publisher= Mtime时光网 |date=2015-02-24 |accessdate= }}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Graham Moore Gives The Oscars' Most Moving Acceptance Speech|first=Jessica|last=Goodman|work=The Huffington Post|date=February 22, 2015|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2015/02/22/graham-moore-oscars_n_6733082.html}}</ref>
