'''格氏貂'''（''Martes gwatkinsii''）是唯一生活在南[[印度|印度]]的[[貂|貂]]。牠們分佈在[[尼爾吉里丘陵|尼爾吉里丘陵]]及[[西高止山脈|西高止山脈]]。

==特徵==
格氏貂與[[黃喉貂|黃喉貂]]相似，但體型較大，其前額亦明顯凹陷。牠們背部深色，[[喉嚨|喉嚨]]呈黃色至橙色。牠們體長55-65厘米，尾巴長40-45厘米，重2.1公斤。

==分佈==
格氏貂分佈在[[尼爾吉里丘陵|尼爾吉里丘陵]]、[[果達古|果達古]]南部及[[喀拉拉邦|喀拉拉邦]]北部。<ref>{{Cite journal en|author=Christopher, G. & Jayson, E.A.|year=1996|title=Sightings of Nilgiri marten (''Martes gwatkinsii'' Horsfield) at Peppara Wildlife Sanctuary and Silent Valley National Park, Kerala, India|journal=Small Carnivore Conservation|volume=15|pages=3-4}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal en|author=Madhusudan, M.D.|year=1995|title=Sighting of the Nilgiri marten (''Martes gwatkinsii'') at Eravikulam National Park, Kerala, India|journal=Small Carnivore Conservation|volume=13|pages=6-7}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal en|author=Gokula, V. & Ramachandran, N.K.|year=1996|title=A record of the Nilgiri marten (''Martes gwatkinsii'' Horsfield)|journal=J. Bombay nat. Hist. Soc.|volume=93|pages=82}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal en|author=Muduppa, D.|year=1999|title=Lesser known carnivores of the Western Ghats IN ENVIS Bulletin : Wildlife, Protected areas: Mustelids, Viverrids and Herpestides of India|volume=2|issue=2|pages=65-70|journal=Wildlife Institute of India, Dehra Dun}}</ref>

==生態及行為==
對於格氏貂所知很少。牠們是日間活動的，棲於樹上，有時也會落到地下。有指牠們吃[[鳥類|鳥類]]、細小的[[哺乳動物|哺乳動物]]及[[昆蟲|昆蟲]]。<ref>{{Cite journal en|author=Hutton, A.F.|year=1944|title=Feeding habits of the Nilgiri marten|journal=J. Bombay nat. Hist. Soc.|volume=48|pages=374-375}}</ref>
