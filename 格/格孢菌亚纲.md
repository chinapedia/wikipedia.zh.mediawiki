'''格孢菌亚纲'''（[[学名|学名]]：Pleosporomycetidae）是[[子囊菌门|子囊菌门]][[座囊菌纲|座囊菌纲]]下的亚纲，共包括纵裂菌目（Hysteriales）、Jahnulales、Mytilinidiales、格孢菌目（Pleosporales）四个目。<ref>Schoch CL, Shoemaker RA, Seifert KA, Hambleton S, Spatafora JW, Crous PW, 2006. A multigene phylogeny of the Dothideomycetes using four nuclear loci. Mycologia 98, 1041–1052.</ref><ref>Schoch CL, Crous PW, Groenewald JZ, Boehm EW, Burgess TI, de Gruyter J, de Hoog GS, Dixon LJ, Grube M, Gueidan C, Harada Y, Hatakeyama S, Hirayama K, Hosoya T, Huhndorf SM, Hyde KD, Jones EB, Kohlmeyer J, Kruys A, Li YM, Lucking R, Lumbsch HT, Marvanova L, Mbatchou JS, McVay AH, Miller AN, Mugambi GK, Muggia L, Nelsen MP, Nelson P, Owensby CA, Phillips AJ, Phongpaichit S, Pointing SB, Pujade-Renaud V, Raja HA, Plata ER, Robbertse B, Ruibal C, Sakayaroj J, Sano T, Selbmann L, Shearer CA, Shirouzu T, Slippers B, Suetrong S, Tanaka K, Volkmann-Kohlmeyer B, Wingfield MJ, Wood AR, Woudenberg JH, Yonezawa H, Zhang Y, Spatafora JW, 2009. A class-wide phylogenetic assessment of Dothideomycetes. Stud Mycol 64, 1-15S10.[http://www.studiesinmycology.org/cgi/content/abstract/64/1/1]</ref>这一亚纲的一大重要特征是该亚纲[[真菌|真菌]]的[[子囊|子囊]]间存在着拟侧丝（pseudoparaphysis）。<ref>Eriksson OE, 1981. The families of bitunicate ascomycetes. Opera Botanica 60, 1-220.
</ref>拟侧丝是一种从子囊座中心顶部向下生长并在基部与细胞融合的不孕性丝状体。
