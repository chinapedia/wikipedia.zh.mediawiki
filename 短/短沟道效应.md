'''短沟道效应'''（{{lang-en|'''short-channel effects'''}}）是当[[金属氧化物半导体场效应管|金属氧化物半导体场效应管]]的导电沟道长度降低到十几纳米、甚至几纳米量级时，晶体管出现的一些效应。这些效应主要包括[[阈值电压|阈值电压]]随着沟道长度降低而降低、[[漏致势垒降低|漏致势垒降低]]、载流子表面散射、[[速度饱和|速度饱和]]、离子化和[[热载流子注入|热电子效应]]。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://ecee.colorado.edu/~bart/book/book/chapter7/ch7_7.htm|title=Advanced MOSFET issues|publisher=[[科羅拉多大學波德分校|University of Colorado]]|accessdate=2015-03-01}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.mcc.uiuc.edu/summerschool/2002/Umberto%20Ravaioli/Ravaioli_files/Winstead-Ravaioli.pdf | format= pdf | title= Simulation of Schottky Barrier MOSFET’s with a Coupled Quantum Injection/Monte Carlo Technique |work = [[IEEE|IEEE]] TRANSACTIONS ON ELECTRON DEVICES, VOL. 47, NO. 6, |date= JUNE 2000 |page= {{{pages|p.1241}}} |publisher= [[伊利诺伊大学厄巴纳-香槟分校|uiuc]] |author= Brian Winstead |coauthors = Umberto Ravaioli , ''Senior Member, IEEE''  |language=en}}</ref>
