'''''稻草假单胞菌'''''是一类杆状[[革兰氏阴性菌|革兰氏阴性菌]]，它所包含的菌株以前属于赭色假单胞菌（''P. ochracea''）。<ref>{{cite journal |author=Uchino |title=Emendation of ''Pseudomonas straminea'' Iizuka and Komagata 1963 |journal=Int J Syst Evol Microbiol |volume=50 | issue = 4 |pages=1513–9 |date=Jul 2000 |pmid=10939658 |last2=Kosako |first2=Y |last3=Uchimura |first3=T |last4=Komagata |first4=K |doi=10.1099/00207713-50-4-1513|display-authors=etal}}</ref>根据16S [[rRNA|rRNA]]分析，目前稻草假单胞菌属于[[绿脓杆菌|绿脓杆菌]]。<ref>{{cite journal |author=Anzai |title=Phylogenetic affiliation of the pseudomonads based on 16S rRNA sequence |journal=Int J Syst Evol Microbiol |volume=50 |pages=1563–89 |date=Jul 2000 |pmid=10939664 |last2=Kim |first2=H |last3=Park |first3=JY |last4=Wakabayashi |first4=H |last5=Oyaizu |first5=H |doi=10.1099/00207713-50-4-1563 |issue=4|display-authors=etal}}</ref>
