'''圣嘉勒堂'''（Church of St Clare, Liverpool）是[[天主教利物浦总教区|天主教利物浦总教区]]的的一个堂口<ref>{{citation |url= http://www.archdiocese-of-liverpool.co.uk/parishes/02liverpoolsouth.html |title= Liverpool South (PASTORAL AREA 2) |accessdate= 7 October 2008 |publisher= The Archdiocese of Liverpool |deadurl= yes |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20080907114953/http://www.archdiocese-of-liverpool.co.uk/parishes/02liverpoolsouth.html |archivedate= 2008年9月7日 }}</ref>，位于英格兰[[利物浦|利物浦]]塞夫顿公园地区的阿伦德尔大道和约克大道的转角。1952年列为I级[[登录建筑|登录建筑]]<ref name=images>{{NHLE |num= 1205333|desc= Church of St Clare, Liverpool|access-date= 19 August 2012 |mode=cs2}}</ref>。它是利物浦总教区唯一一个列为I级登录建筑的天主教堂。<ref name=lee>{{citation |url= http://www.stcl.pwp.blueyonder.co.uk/default.htm/liturgy_commission1.htm#ch2 |title= Architectural Features : Detail |accessdate= 2008-10-08 |last= Lee |first= Christopher |publisher= St Clare's Church, Liverpool |pages=  }}{{dead link|date=2017年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>夏布尔和波拉德认为它是“当时该国最有想象力的教堂之一”<ref name=pev>{{Citation | last = Sharples | first = Joseph| last2 = Pollard| first2 = Richard| year = 2004| title = Liverpool| series = Pevsner Architectural Guides| publication-place = New Haven and London|  publisher = Yale University Press| pages = 286–288| isbn = 0-300-10258-5}}</ref>。

==历史==
教堂由棉花经纪人弗朗西斯和詹姆斯·雷诺兹兄弟建于1888-90年，耗资7,834英镑({{Inflation|UK|7834|1890|r=-4|fmt=eq|cursign=£}}).{{Inflation-fn|UK}} 建筑师是伦纳德·斯托克斯，弗朗西斯·雷诺兹的教子。<ref name=pev/><ref>{{citation|url= http://www.st-clares.org.uk/|title= Home|accessdate= 7 October 2008|publisher= St Clare's Church, Liverpool|deadurl= yes|archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20081118020333/http://www.st-clares.org.uk/|archivedate= 2008年11月18日}}</ref>它被认为是斯托克斯的“第一个真正杰出的教堂设计”。<ref name=dnb>Howell, Peter, ‘Stokes, Leonard Aloysius Scott (1858–1925)’, ''[[Oxford_Dictionary_of_National_Biography|Oxford Dictionary of National Biography]]'', [[Oxford_University_Press|Oxford University Press]], 2004 [http://www.oxforddnb.com/view/article/48197], accessed 2008-10-08</ref>该堂于1889年3月25日奠基，1890年6月3日祝圣，同年7月20日开始礼拜。
