'''圣体堂'''（Blessed Sacrament Catholic Church）是[[天主教洛杉矶总教区|天主教洛杉矶总教区]]的一个堂区，位于美国加州[[好莱坞|好莱坞]]中心的[[日落大道|日落大道]]。堂区成立于1904年，教堂建于1928年，但直到1954年才完成。意大利文艺复兴风格，被认为是洛杉矶教区最美丽的教堂之一。20世纪20年代到50年代经典好莱坞时代的许多演员都是该堂的教友，包括[[宾·克罗斯比|宾·克罗斯比]]、[[约翰·福特|约翰·福特]]、[[艾琳·邓恩|艾琳·邓恩]]、[[洛丽泰·杨|洛丽泰·杨]]和[[里卡多·蒙特尔班|里卡多·蒙特尔班]]。该堂位于多族裔的城市社区，以同性恋和济贫等事工著称。一些影视作品也在此拍摄。 

==历史==
圣体堂成立于1904年，第一个教堂容纳250人<ref name=Jubille>{{cite news|title=Hollywood Church Starts Its Golden Jubilee Today |publisher=Los Angeles Times|date=1954-10-15}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Catholics Celebrate Church's 50th Birthday: Cardinal McIntyre Presides at Mass at Hollywood Blessed Sacrament Anniversary |publisher=Los Angeles Times|date=1954-10-18|url=http://pqasb.pqarchiver.com/latimes/access/434753482.html?dids=434753482:434753482&FMT=ABS&FMTS=ABS:AI&date=Oct+18%2C+1954&author=&pub=Los+Angeles+Times&desc=Catholics+Celebrate+Church's+50th+Birthday&pqatl=google}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Hollywood: Consecration Service; Republican Rally|publisher=Los Angeles Times|date=1904-10-23|url=http://pqasb.pqarchiver.com/latimes/access/322814422.html?dids=322814422:322814422&FMT=ABS&FMTS=ABS:AI&date=Oct+23%2C+1904&author=&pub=Los+Angeles+Times&desc=HOLLYWOOD.&pqatl=google}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=TO DEDICATE FINE CHURCH|publisher=Los Angeles Times|date=1904-10-22|url=http://pqasb.pqarchiver.com/latimes/access/322812482.html?dids=322812482:322812482&FMT=ABS&FMTS=ABS:AI&date=Oct+22%2C+1904&author=&pub=Los+Angeles+Times&desc=TO+DEDICATE+FINE+CHURCH.&pqatl=google}}</ref> 随着1910年代电影业的发展，好莱坞的人口迅速增加，到1919年，老教堂每个星期天都“接近爆炸”。<ref name=Tracy/>
[[File:Facade_of_Blessed_Sacrament_Catholic_Church_(Los_Angeles,_California).JPG|thumb]]

1921年，堂区出资75000美元在日落大道购地。<ref name=Tracy>{{cite web|author=Kathy Tracy|title=Blessed Sacrament Parish History - 2004 Centennial Edition|publisher=Blessed Sacrament Catholic Church|year=2004|url=http://blessedsacramenthollywood.org/history/index.htm|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20130414075007/http://blessedsacramenthollywood.org/history/index.htm|archivedate=2013-04-14}}</ref>新教堂为意大利文艺复兴风格，在1928年6月投入使用<ref name=Edifice>{{cite news|title=CATHOLICS PLAN EDIFICE: Construction to Start January 1 on Church for Parish of Blessed Sacrament in Hollywood|publisher=Los Angeles Times|date=1925-12-13|url=http://pqasb.pqarchiver.com/latimes/access/361809002.html?dids=361809002:361809002&FMT=CITE&FMTS=CITE:AI&date=Dec+13%2C+1925&author=&pub=Los+Angeles+Times&desc=CATHOLICS+PLAN+EDIFICE&pqatl=google}}</ref>。它拥有高223英尺的钟楼，华丽的外观和1,400个座位，迅速成为好莱坞地标。<ref>{{cite news|title=Hollywood Church Will Hold First Open House: Blessed Sacrament Priests and Nuns to Act as Guides for Sunday Visitors|publisher=Los Angeles Times|date=1967-05-20|url=http://pqasb.pqarchiver.com/latimes/access/521670872.html?dids=521670872:521670872&FMT=ABS&FMTS=ABS:AI&date=May+20%2C+1967&author=&pub=Los+Angeles+Times&desc=Hollywood+Church+Will+Hold+First+Open+House&pqatl=google}}</ref>

但是室内装饰因[[大萧条|大萧条]]而耽延<ref name=Tracy/>，直到1954年才由赫尔特·杜鲁多完成。麦科伊神父主持期间（1932年到1957年），该堂教友增加到6000人。

近几十年来，好莱坞地区经历了经济衰退，大量无家可归和穷人生活在该地区。1987年10月，发生枪手向参加教堂狂欢节的数百人开火的暴力事件，<ref>{{cite news|title=Gunfire Wounds 4 at L.A. Church|publisher=San Jose Mercury News|date=1987-10-12|url=http://nl.newsbank.com/nl-search/we/Archives?p_product=SJ&s_site=mercurynews&p_multi=SJ&p_theme=realcities&p_action=search&p_maxdocs=200&p_topdoc=1&p_text_direct-0=0EB72C76B269AF9F&p_field_direct-0=document_id&p_perpage=10&p_sort=YMD_date:D&s_trackval=GooglePM}}</ref> 两个成年人和两个孩子受伤。<ref>{{cite news|author=Richard Holguin|title=Boy, 14, Killed, 7 Wounded in Compton, Hollywood Shootings|publisher=Los Angeles Times|date=1987-10-12|url=http://pqasb.pqarchiver.com/latimes/access/58719638.html?dids=58719638:58719638&FMT=FT&FMTS=ABS:FT&type=current&date=Oct+12%2C+1987&author=RICHARD+HOLGUIN&pub=Los+Angeles+Times+(pre-1997+Fulltext)&desc=Boy%2C+14%2C+Killed%2C+7+Wounded+in+Compton%2C+Hollywood+Shootings}}</ref>
