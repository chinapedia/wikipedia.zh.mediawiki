'''圣玛丽瑞肯高中（St. Mary's Ryken High School)''', 位于美国马里兰州Leonardtown镇，距离美国首都华盛顿约一个半小时车程。该校男女同校且为天主教学校。<ref name="xbss.org">{{cite web|url=http://www.xbss.org/html/schools.html |title=Xaverian Brothers Sponsored Schools - Schools |publisher= |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20061004213823/http://xbss.org/html/schools.html |archivedate=2006-10-04 }}</ref>  <ref>[http://site.adw.org/school-profiles-and-links Archdiocese of Washington] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130312062324/http://site.adw.org/school-profiles-and-links |date=2013-03-12 }}</ref> <ref>[http://www.msde.state.md.us/nonpublic/nsab_directory/DisplayLocations.asp?County=St.%20Mary%27s Maryland State Department of Education]</ref> 该校毕业生SAT平均分1530分，自建校以来该校中国国际毕业生共有三人进入美国排名前50大学。<ref name="somdnews">{{cite web |url=http://ww2.somdnews.com/stories/09172010/entetop162309_32313.shtml |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2015-02-26 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150227013413/http://ww2.somdnews.com/stories/09172010/entetop162309_32313.shtml |archivedate=2015-02-27 }}</ref> 该校对美国学生学费为13100美金 ，对国际学生学费为52000美金。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.smrhs.org/page.cfm?p=3000|title=St. Mary's Ryken High School: Apply to St. Mary's Ryken|publisher=|access-date=2015-02-26|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150227011215/http://www.smrhs.org/page.cfm?p=3000|archive-date=2015-02-27|dead-url=yes}}</ref>
