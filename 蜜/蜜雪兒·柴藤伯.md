'''蜜雪兒·雀柏格'''（{{lang-en|Michelle Trachtenberg}}，{{bd|1985年|10月11日}}）是一位[[美國|美國]]女演員。蜜雪兒小时候是一名童星，后因出演美劇《[[花邊教主_(電視劇)|花邊教主]]》中的[[:en:Georgina_Sparks|Georgina Sparks]]及《[[捉鬼者巴菲|捉鬼者巴菲]]》中的[[:en:Dawn_Summers|Dawn Summers]]而為人熟悉。此外她在奇幻电影《[[回到17岁|回到17岁]]》中也有亮相。

== 早年生活 ==
雀柏格出生于[[纽约|纽约]]<ref name=CBSFight>{{Cite news|url= http://www.cbsnews.com/stories/2005/03/18/earlyshow/leisure/celebspot/main681555.shtml|title= Find And Fight For Your Dream|work=cbsnews.com|accessdate= July 21, 2010|author=Tatiana Morales|date= March 18, 2005}}</ref>，是[[光导纤维|光纤]]工程师迈克·雀柏格(Michael Trachtenberg)和银行经理拉娜(Lana)的小女儿。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.filmreference.com/film/22/Michelle-Trachtenberg.html | title = Michelle Trachtenberg Biography (1985–) | work=Film Reference}}</ref>她父亲是德国人，母亲是俄罗斯人。她还有[[犹太人|犹太人]]血统<ref>{{Cite episode | series = [[:en:Kathy_(TV_series)|Kathy]] | title = March 14, 2013 episode|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SeX4JW6OW1Y}}</ref>，祖父母居住在[[以色列|以色列]]。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.contactmusic.com/new/home.nsf/webpages/buffy4x01x04x04 |title=James Marsters and Michelle Trachtenberg interview |accessdate=March 14, 2007 |work=[[:en:contactmusic.com|contactmusic.com]]}}</ref>她和姐姐艾瑞尼(Irene)一同在[[:en:Sheepshead_Bay,_Brooklyn|羊头湾]]长大<ref>{{Cite news | last = Butler | first = Karen | url = http://www.brooklynpaper.com/stories/28/12/28_12iceprincess.html | title = ON 'ICE': Sheepshead Bay native talks of tough workouts for lead in 'Ice Princess' | work=[[:en:The_Brooklyn_Paper|The Brooklyn Paper]] | date = March 19, 2005 | accessdate = November 16, 2009 | quote = When Michelle Trachtenberg was a young girl growing up in Sheepshead Bay, she dreamed of being a championship ice skater like her childhood idols, Michelle Kwan and Oksana Baiul.}}</ref>，她后就读于本地的P.S.99小学和[[:en:The_Bay_Academy_for_the_Arts_and_Sciences|海岬艺术与科学学校]]。她后移居至[[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]]，就读于位于[[:en:Sherman_Oaks,_Los_Angeles,_California|谢尔曼欧卡地区]]的[[:en:Notre_Dame_High_School_(Sherman_Oaks,_California)|圣母高中]]。<ref>{{cite web|title=Gossip Girl Transformations - Michelle Trachtenberg|url=http://www.instyle.com/instyle/package/general/photos/0,,20208061_20207867_20473221,00.html#20473228|work=[[:en:InStyle|InStyle]]|accessdate=February 11, 2013}}</ref>
