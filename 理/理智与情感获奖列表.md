《[[理智与情感_(电影)|理智与情感]]》（{{lang-en|''Sense and Sensibility''}}）是一部由[[英国电影|英]][[美国电影|美]]合拍的[[1995年电影|1995年电影]]，由[[李安|李安]]执导，[[女演员|女演员]][[艾玛·汤普森|艾玛·汤普森]]将[[简·奥斯汀|简·奥斯汀]]1811年的[[理智与情感|同名小说]]改编成剧本。汤普森和另一位女演员[[凯特·温丝莱特|凯特·温丝莱特]]一起饰演了达什伍德姐妹。[[索尼影视娱乐|索尼影视娱乐]]下属子公司[[哥伦比亚电影公司|哥伦比亚电影公司]]制作并发行了这部电影<ref>{{cite web | url = http://usatoday30.usatoday.com/life/special/l96os010.htm | title = Big boost for small movies | work = USA Today | first = Andy | last = Seiler | date = 1997-03-04 | accessdate = 2014-03-30}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = http://money.cnn.com/magazines/fortune/fortune_archive/1996/09/30/217442/index.htm | title = Alas, Poor Sony After Investing $7 billion in its Movie and TV Business, and Running Through a Huge Cast of Top Executives, Sony Still Hasn't Figured Out How to Play the Hollywood Game | publisher = CNNMoney.com | first = Marc | last = Gunther | coauthors = Eryn Brown | date = 1996-09-30 | accessdate = 2014-03-30}}</ref><ref>{{cite book | first = William B. | last = Russell | coauthors = Stewart Waters | title = Reel Character Education: A Cinematic Approach | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120419045119/http://www.metacritic.com/movie/sense-and-sensibility}}</ref>。影片还登上了超过100个影评人或媒体评选的年度十大佳片榜单<ref name="tcmfmiller">{{cite web | url = http://www.tcm.com/this-month/article/62625%7C0/Sense-and-Sensibility.html | title = Sense and Sensibility | publisher = Turner Classic Movies | first = Frank | last = Miller | accessdate = 2014-01-27 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20130701174531/http://www.tcm.com/this-month/article/62625%7C0/Sense-and-Sensibility.html | archivedate = 2013-07-01 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。

《理智与情感》上映后获得了多个不同组织颁发的各类奖项肯定，获得肯定的范围主角包括主角演员的表演和汤普森的剧本。影片获得了包括[[奥斯卡最佳影片奖|最佳影片奖]]在内的七项[[奥斯卡金像奖|奥斯卡金像奖]]提名，不过最终只获得了[[奥斯卡最佳原著改编奖|原著改编奖]]<ref name="oscar1996">{{cite web |archivedate=2013-09-27 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20130927005125/http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/oscarlegacy/1990-1999/68nominees.html |url=http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/oscarlegacy/1990-1999/68nominees.html |title=Nominees & winners for the 68th Academy Awards |publisher=Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences |accessdate=2014-01-29}}</ref>，汤普森之前还曾因[[1992年电影|1992年电影]]《[[霍华德庄园|霍华德庄园]]》获[[第65届奥斯卡金像奖|第65届奥斯卡]][[奥斯卡最佳女主角奖|女主角奖]]<ref name="Oscars1993">{{cite news |url=http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/legacy/ceremony/65th-winners.html |title=The 65th Academy Awards (1993) Nominees and Winners |accessdate=2013-12-24 |work=''Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences'' |publisher=AMPAS |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131224030023/http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/legacy/ceremony/65th-winners.html |archivedate=2013-12-24 |deadurl=no}}</ref>，她也由此成为截止[[第86届奥斯卡金像奖|2014年]]唯一一位获得过奥斯卡表演和编剧类奖项的电影人<ref>{{cite news | archivedate = 2013-12-23 | deadurl = no | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1996-03-26/news/mn-51422_1_sarandon-win | title = 'Braveheart' is top film; Cage, Sarandon win | work = Los Angeles Times | first = Robert W. | last = Welkos | date = 1996-03-26 | accessdate = 2014-01-29 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131023035359/http://articles.latimes.com/1996-03-26/news/mn-51422_1_sarandon-win}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | url = http://www.independent.co.uk/news/people/news/emma-thompson-how-jane-austen-saved-me-from-going-under-1929510.html | title = Emma Thompson: How Jane Austen saved me from going under | work = The Independent | first = Andrew | last = Johnson | date = 2010-03-28 | accessdate = 2014-01-29 | archivedate = 2012-12-11 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121211171616/http://www.independent.co.uk/news/people/news/emma-thompson-how-jane-austen-saved-me-from-going-under-1929510.html}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|archivedate=2014-03-22 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140322084958/http://affairsmagazine.com/wordpress2/2011/01/26/best-writing-adapted-screenplay/ |url=http://affairsmagazine.com/wordpress2/2011/01/26/best-writing-adapted-screenplay/ |title=Best Writing: Adapted Screenplay |work=Affairs Magazine |first=Laura |last=Kessenich |date=2011-01-26 |accessdate=2014-03-30 }}</ref>。汤普森的剧本还赢得了包括[[广播影评人协会|广播影评人协会奖]]、[[金球奖|金球奖]]、伦敦影评人协会奖在内的11项荣誉。在[[第49届英国电影学院奖|第49届]][[英国电影学院奖|英国电影学院奖]]角逐中，影片获得了[[英国电影和电视艺术学院|英国电影和电视艺术学院]]的12项提名，最终拿下了3项大奖，分别是[[英国电影学院奖最佳影片|最佳影片奖]]，汤普森赢得的[[英国电影学院奖最佳女主角|最佳女主角奖]]以及温丝莱特获得的[[英国电影学院奖最佳女配角奖|最佳女配角奖]]。

众演员也获得了多项表演类奖项，其中汤普森就因演出获得了七项提名。温丝莱特在女主角和女配角奖项上都有所斩获，如《[[伦敦标准晚报|标准晚报]]》（{{lang|en|''Evening Standard''}}）奖最佳女主角和[[金球奖最佳电影女配角|金球奖最佳电影女配角]]。除奥斯卡金像奖外，电影还获得了多项提名，并拿下[[波士顿影评人协会奖最佳影片|波士顿影评人协会奖最佳影片]]、[[金球奖最佳戏剧类影片|金球奖最佳戏剧类影片]]和[[评论家选择奖|广播影评人协会]][[评论家选择奖最佳影片|最佳影片]]等奖项。《理智与情感》也成为20世纪90年代获奖最多的奥斯汀作品改编电影<ref>{{cite book | first = Sue | last = Parrill | title = Jane Austen on film and television: a critical study of the adaptations | publisher = McFarland & Company, Inc., Publishers | location = Jefferson | year = 2002 | page = 4 | isbn = 0-7864-1349-2 | accessdate = 2014-03-31 | url = http://books.google.com/books?id=l6dsN6KfXUkC&printsec=frontcover&dq=sense+and+sensibility+1995+film&hl=en#v=onepage&q=sense%20and%20sensibility&f=false}}</ref>。{{lang|en|[[RealNetworks|RealNetworks]]}}的玛丽安·约翰森（{{lang|en|MaryAnn Johanson}}）也认为本片可以在所有1995年的电影中排名第五<ref>{{cite web | archivedate = 2011-08-20 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20110820061438/http://www.film.com/movies/the-10-best-movies-of-1995#fbid=7TXI_8jJsDt | deadurl = yes | url = http://www.film.com/movies/the-10-best-movies-of-1995#fbid=7TXI_8jJsDt | title = The 10 Best Movies of 1995 | publisher = Film.com | first = MaryAnn | last = Johanson | date = 2008-03-03 | accessdate = 2014-03-31 }}</ref>。到颁奖季结束时，《理智与情感》一共获得了60项提名，并在其中30项上得以胜出。
