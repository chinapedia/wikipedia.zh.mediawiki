'''福德漢姆路車站'''（{{lang-en|Fordham Road}}）是[[紐約地鐵|紐約地鐵]][[IND匯集線|IND匯集線]]的快車[[地鐵站|地鐵站]]，位於[[紐約市|紐約市]]其中一個最大的購物區，{{link-en|大廣場 (布朗克斯)|Grand Concourse (Bronx)|大廣場}}及{{link-en|福德漢姆路|Fordham Road}}，設有[[紐約地鐵D線|D線]]（任何時候停站）及[[紐約地鐵B線|B線]]（僅繁忙時段停站）列車服務。

福德漢姆路按空間來說是匯集線的最大車站。南行[[島式月台|島式月台]]在車站北端變寬，並設有一堵牆分成兩半，製造出兩個「側式月台」。北行島式月台與地鐵其他車站類似。<ref name=NYTimes-QBL-PlansChanged-June1930>{{cite web|title=PLANS ARE CHANGED FOR QUEENS SUBWAY: Traffic Crossings at Nassau and Woodhaven Boulevards Altered to Avoid Congestion. VIADUCT PROJECT DROPPED Main Driveway to Be Depressed, Side Routes to Be at Grade-- New Bids Due Soon. How Plans Were Changed. Elimination Plans Received.|url=https://query.nytimes.com/gst/abstract.html?res=9D01E7D9113AEE32A25751C2A9609C946194D6CF|publisher=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|accessdate=September 1, 2015|date=June 22, 1930}}</ref>。

== 位置 ==
車站位於福德漢姆路商業改善區，是市內第三大的購物區，由[[第三大道|第三大道]]和{{link-en|韋布斯特大道|Webster Avenue}}，西至{{link-en|傑羅姆大道|Jerome Avenue}}<ref name=BxTimes-FordhamSearsClose-Feb2014>{{cite web|last1=Goodstein|first1=Steven|title=Fordham Sears to close|url=http://www.bxtimes.com/stories/2014/35/35-sears-2014-08-29-bx_2014_35.html|publisher=[[Bronx_Times-Reporter|Bronx Times-Reporter]]|accessdate=November 12, 2015|date=February 9, 2014}}</ref><ref name=ExperienceFordhamRoad>{{cite web|title=EXPERIENCE Fordham Road|url=http://www.fordhamroadbid.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/map2014_BID_HR_6-FINAL.pdf|publisher=[[Fordham_Road|Fordham Road]] Business Improvement District|accessdate=November 12, 2015}}</ref><ref name=BxTimes-FordhamRdBID-Plaza-2014>{{cite web|last1=Rocchio|first1=Patrick|title=The Fordham Road Business Improvement district will expand to include One Fordham Plaza|url=http://www.bxtimes.com/stories/2014/33/33-fordham-2014-08-15-bx_2014_33.html|work=[[Bronx_Times-Reporter|Bronx Times-Reporter]]|accessdate=November 12, 2015|date=August 14, 2014}}</ref><ref name=FordhamRdBID-MasterPlan-2008>{{cite web|author1=The RBA Group|title=Fordham Road Streetscale Master Plan|url=http://www.fordhamroadbid.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/FordhamRoadMPfinal.pdf|publisher=[[Fordham_Road|Fordham Road]] Business Improvement District|accessdate=November 12, 2015|location=[[The_Bronx|Bronx]], [[New_York_(state)|New York]]|date=June 30, 2008}}</ref><ref name=BxTimes-FordhamBIDBoost-2008>{{cite web|last1=Asaba|first1=Jeni|title=Fordham Rd. BID to boost local Bronx image|url=http://www.bxtimes.com/stories/2008/26/doc485bf22c5c5d4807088724.html|work=[[Bronx_Times-Reporter|Bronx Times-Reporter]]|accessdate=November 12, 2015|date=June 25, 2008}}</ref>。福德漢姆路和大廣場的西北角自1933年至1992年間是{{link-en|Alexander's|Alexander's}}百貨店（福德漢姆廣場2號） <ref>{{cite web|last1=McDowell|first1=Edwin|title=REGIONAL MARKET: The Bronx; Fordham Revival Where Alexander's Once Ruled|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2002/07/31/business/regional-market-the-bronx-fordham-revival-where-alexander-s-once-ruled.html|publisher=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|accessdate=December 6, 2015|date=July 31, 2002}}</ref><ref name=NYTimes-Alexanders-May1992>{{cite web|last1=Strom|first1=Stephanie|title=ALEXANDER'S SHUTS ALL ITS 11 STORES; PLANS LIQUIDATION|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1992/05/16/business/alexander-s-shuts-all-its-11-stores-plans-liquidation.html?pagewanted=all|publisher=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|accessdate=December 6, 2015|date=May 16, 1992}}</ref>。此站距離{{link-en|福德漢姆廣場 (布朗克斯)|Fordham Plaza, Bronx|福德漢姆廣場}}（購物區東端）和[[福德漢姆大學|福德漢姆大學]]的玫瑰丘校園較近<ref name=MTAMap-VanCourtland-Botanic-FordhamPlz-2015>{{cite web|title=MTA Neighborhood Maps: Van Cortlandt Park / NY Botanical Garden|url=http://web.mta.info/maps/neighborhoods/bx/BX4_van_cort_pk_nybg_2015.pdf|publisher=[[Metropolitan_Transportation_Authority|Metropolitan Transportation Authority]]|accessdate=September 24, 2015|date=2015}}</ref>。
