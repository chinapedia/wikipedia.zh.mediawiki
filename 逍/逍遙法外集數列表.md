《[[逍遙法外_(電視劇)|逍遙法外]]》（{{lang-en|''How to Get Away with Murder''}}）是一部於2014年9月25日在[[美國廣播公司|ABC]]開播，<ref name="premiere">{{cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/abc-sets-fall-premiere-dates-718576|title=ABC Sets Fall Premiere Dates|publisher=[[荷里活報道|好萊塢報導]]|date=2014-07-15 | accessdate=2014-07-16|author=Philiana Ng}}</ref>由[[彼得·諾沃克|彼得·諾沃克]]開創的美國電視影集。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/abc-orders-how-get-away-702523|title=ABC Orders 'How to Get Away With Murder,' 'Black-ish,' 'American Crime,' 'Selfie,' 'Galavant,' More to Series|last=Goldberg|first=Lesley|date=2014-05-08|work=好萊塢報導|accessdate=2014-05-12}}</ref>

第一季最初獲得13集預訂，<ref>{{cite web|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie |url=http://www.deadline.com/2014/05/abc-picks-up-shonda-rhimes-how-to-get-away-with-murder-anthony-anderson-american-crime/|title=ABC New Series Pickups: 'Selfie', 'Forever', Galavant', 'Whispers', 'How To Get Away With Murder', 'American Crime', 'Black-ish', Jeff Lowell Comedy|publisher=[[Deadline.com|Deadline.com]]|date=2014-05-08|accessdate=2014-05-12}}</ref>後因為開播收視告捷，故獲得ABC全季集數15集預訂。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2014/10/09/how-to-get-away-with-murder-and-black-ish-get-full-season-orders-at-abc/312767/|title='How to Get Away With Murder' and 'black-sh'Get Full Season Orders at ABC|work=[[TV_by_the_Numbers|TV by the Numbers]]|last=Kondolojy|first=Amanda|date=2014-10-09|accessdate=2014-10-24|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141024083749/http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2014/10/09/how-to-get-away-with-murder-and-black-ish-get-full-season-orders-at-abc/312767/|archivedate=2014-10-24}}</ref>ABC通常是於每年5月份才會宣布續訂與否，然而在拍攝本劇第1季季終時，已透過女主角[[維奧拉·戴維斯|薇拉·戴維絲]]確定獲得第2季續訂。2015年5月7日，ABC官方正式宣布續訂第2季。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://variety.com/2015/tv/news/abc-forever-cristela-resurrection-cancelled-1201490485/|title=ABC Cancels ‘Forever,’ ‘Cristela,’ ‘Resurrection’ & ‘The Taste’|last=Wagmeister|first=Elizabeth|date=2015-05-07|work=[[綜藝_(雜誌)|綜藝雜誌]]|accessdate=2015-05-08}}</ref>5月12日，宣布第二季同樣將有15集。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/TVGuide/status/489095092636876801|title=#Scandal will do a full 22-episode season, while #HowToGetAwayWithMurder will only do 15 or 16 episodes #TCA14|publisher=[[電視指南_(雜誌)|電視指南]]|date=2014-07-15|accessdate=2014-07-16}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2014/10/how-to-get-away-with-murder-blackish-full-season-1-orders-abc-848758/|title=‘How To Get Away With Murder’ & ‘Black-Ish': Full-Season Orders At ABC - Deadline|work=Deadline|date=2014-10-09|accessdate=2014-11-01}}</ref>2016年3月3日，ABC宣布續訂第3季。<ref name="Renewals S3">{{cite web|url=http://variety.com/2016/tv/news/abc-renewals-the-goldbergs-1201721897/|title=ABC Renews 15 Shows Including: 'Quantico,' 'The Bachelor' & Comedy Slate|last=Wagmeister|first=Elizabeth|work=綜藝雜誌|date=2016-03-03|accessdate=2016-03-03}}</ref>2017年2月10日，ABC宣布續訂第4季。<ref name="Renewals S4">{{cite web|url=http://tvline.com/2017/02/10/greys-anatomy-renewed-scandal-htgawm-renewals/|title=''Grey's Anatomy'', ''Scandal'' and ''HTGAWM'' Renewed for 2017-18 Season|last=Swift|first=Andy|work={{le|TVLine|TVLine}}|date=2017-02-10|accessdate=2017-02-10}}</ref>2018年5月11日，ABC宣布續訂第5季。<ref name="Renewals S5">{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2018/05/how-to-get-away-with-murder-renewed-for-season-5-by-abc-1202379660/|title=‘How To Get Away With Murder’ Renewed For Season 5 By ABC|work=Deadline.com|author=Nellie Andreeva|date=2018-05-11|accessdate=2018-05-11}}</ref>
