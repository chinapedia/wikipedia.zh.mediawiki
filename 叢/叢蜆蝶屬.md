'''叢蜆蝶屬'''（學名：'''''Xenandra'''''）是[[蜆蝶科|蜆蝶科]][[蜆蝶亞科|蜆蝶亞科]][[樹蜆蝶族|樹蜆蝶族]]裡的一個[[屬|屬]]。共有10個物種，分佈於[[新熱帶界|新熱帶界]]<ref>Tree of Life Web Project. 2008. Xenandra C. Felder & R. Felder 1865. Andara Capronnier 1874 currently viewed as a subjective junior synonym, Westwoodia Houlbert 1918 invalid junior homonym of Westwoodia Brullé 1846 (Hymenoptera) , Uestiudia Oiticica 1955 replacement name, currently viewed as a subjective junior synonym. Version 05 June 2008 (under construction). http://tolweb.org/Xenandra/105310/2008.06.05 in The Tree of Life Web Project, http://tolweb.org/ {{en}}</ref>。
