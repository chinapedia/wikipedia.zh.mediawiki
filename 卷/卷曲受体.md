'''卷曲受体'''（{{lang-en|Frizzled}}）是[[G蛋白偶联受体|G蛋白偶联受体]]中的一个家族<ref name="pmid14977528">{{cite journal | author = Malbon CC | title = Frizzleds: new members of the superfamily of G-protein-coupled receptors | journal = Front. Biosci. | volume = 9 | issue = | pages = 1048–58 | year = 2004 | pmid = 14977528 | doi = 10.2741/1308 }}</ref>作为[[Wnt信号通路|Wnt信号通路]]和其他信号通路中的受体。当其被激活，会引起[[细胞质基质|基质]]中信号通路下游[[散乱蛋白|散乱蛋白]]的激活。

==物种分布==
卷曲受体的基因在从海绵到人类的一系列动物基因组中都有发现<ref name="pmid15239825">{{cite journal | author = Huang HC, Klein PS | title = The Frizzled family: receptors for multiple signal transduction pathways | journal = Genome Biol. | volume = 5 | issue = 7 | pages = 234 | year = 2004 | pmid = 15239825 | doi = 10.1186/gb-2004-5-7-234 | pmc = 463283 }}</ref>。

==功能==

卷曲受体在调节胚胎发育，细胞极性，神经[[突触|突触]]的形成，细胞增殖，以及发育和成体生物的许多其它的过程中扮演重要角色。这些调控涉及三个信号通路，包括经典Wnt/β-catenin通路、Wnt/calcium通路和平面细胞极性（PCP）通路<ref name="pmid15239825">{{cite journal | author = Huang HC, Klein PS | title = The Frizzled family: receptors for multiple signal transduction pathways | journal = Genome Biol. | volume = 5 | issue = 7 | pages = 234 | year = 2004 | pmid = 15239825 | doi = 10.1186/gb-2004-5-7-234 | pmc = 463283 }}</ref>。人类中{{le|FZD4|FZD4|卷曲受体4}}基因上的突变与一种影响[[视网膜|视网膜]]和[[玻璃体|玻璃体]]的罕见疾病{{le|家族性渗出性玻璃体视网膜病变|familial exudative vitreoretinopathy}}相关。

卷曲受体（fz）作用于{{le|果蝇属|Drosophila|果蝇}}表皮细胞的细胞骨架，产生表皮毛和刚毛的平行阵列<ref name="PUB00001934">{{cite journal |author=Adler PN, Vinson C, Park WJ, Conover S, Klein L |title=Molecular structure of frizzled, a Drosophila tissue polarity gene |journal=Genetics |volume=126 |issue=2 |pages=401–16 |year=1990 |pmid=2174014 |pmc=1204194}}</ref><ref name="PUB00004042">{{cite journal |author=Adler PN, Conover S, Vinson CR |title=A Drosophila tissue polarity locus encodes a protein containing seven potential transmembrane domains |journal=Nature |volume=338 |issue=6212 |pages=263–264 |year=1989 |pmid=2493583 |doi=10.1038/338263a0}}</ref>。在FZ突变体中，整个生物个体的刚毛方向被改变<ref name="PUB00004042" /> 。

在翅的发育中，Fz有两个功能：it is required for the proximal-distal transmission of an intracellular polarity signal; and it is required for cells to respond to the polarity signal. Fz produces an mRNA that encodes an integral membrane protein with 7 putative transmembrane (TM) domains. This protein should contain both extracellular and cytoplasmic domains, which could function in the transmission and interpretation of polarity information.<ref name="PUB00004042" /> This signature is usually found downstream of the Fz domain ({{InterPro|IPR000024}})

==富半胱氨酸结构域==
卷曲受体蛋白包含一个富半胱氨酸结构域，这种结构域常见于[[受体酪氨酸激酶|受体酪氨酸激酶]]<ref name="PUB00001039">{{cite journal |doi=10.1016/S0960-9822(98)70262-3 |author=Nusse R, Xu YK |title=The Frizzled CRD domain is conserved in diverse proteins including several receptor tyrosine kinases |journal=Curr. Biol. |volume=8 |issue=12 |pages=R405–R406 |year=1998 |pmid=9637908}}</ref><ref name="PUB00005055">{{cite journal |doi=10.1002/pro.5560070718 |author=Saldanha J, Singh J, Mahadevan D |title=Identification of a Frizzled-like cysteine rich domain in the extracellular region of developmental receptor tyrosine kinases |journal=Protein Sci. |volume=7 |issue=7 |pages=1632–1635 |year=1998 |pmid=9684897 |pmc=2144063}}</ref><ref name="PUB00005486">{{cite journal |doi=10.1016/S0968-0004(98)01290-0 |author=Hofmann K, Pihlajaniemi T, Bucher P, Rehn M |title=The frizzled motif: in how many different protein families does it occur? |journal=Trends Biochem. Sci. |volume=23 |issue=11 |pages=415–417 |year=1998 |pmid=9852758}}</ref>等蛋白。在''[[黑腹果蝇|黑腹果蝇]]''中，一些组织极性基因编码的卷曲受体蛋白作为Wnt信号通路的细胞表面受体。

卷曲受体属于七跨膜类受体（7TMR）并有一个与Wnt配体结合相关联的富半胱氨酸胞外结构域。而卷曲受体与多种受体酪氨酸激酶在富半胱氨酸结构域上有着序列相似性，这些受体酪氨酸激酶在发育中扮演着重要作用，比如[[MuSK蛋白|肌肉特异性受体酪氨酸激酶]]（MuSK）、神经元特异性激酶（NSK2）和[[ROR1|ROR1]]与[[ROR2|ROR2]]。这一胞外结构域的结构是已知的，主要由α螺旋组成，包括十个半胱氨酸残基形成的五个二硫键。
