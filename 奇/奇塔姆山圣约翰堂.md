'''奇塔姆山圣约翰堂'''（Church of St John the Evangelist, Cheetham）位于英格兰[[曼彻斯特|曼彻斯特]]奇塔姆山滑铁卢路，是一座[[圣公会|圣公会]]教堂，属于曼彻斯特教区北曼彻斯特總鐸區<ref>{{Citation| url = http://www.achurchnearyou.com/cheetham-st-john-the-evangelist/| title = St John the Evangelist, Cheetham| accessdate = 2012-06-16| publisher = [[Church_of_England|Church of England]]| deadurl = yes| archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121104055251/http://www.achurchnearyou.com/cheetham-st-john-the-evangelist/| archivedate = 2012-11-04}}</ref>。1974年列为II*级[[登录建筑|登录建筑]]<ref name=nhl>{{NHLE |num= 1254832|desc= Church of St John the Evangelist, Cheetham|access-date= 2012-06-16|mode=cs2}}</ref>。

==历史==
该堂建于1869-1871年，由建筑师Paley and Austin设计，造价估计1万英镑（相当于今日{{Inflation|UK|10000|1871|r=-4}}英镑）{{Inflation-fn|UK}}，可容纳600人。{{sfn|Brandwood|Austin|Hughes|Price|2012|pp=93–94, 224}}

==建筑==
圣约翰堂用砂岩建造，建筑风格结合了罗马式和英国哥特式。钟楼高四层，金字塔塔顶。<ref name=nhl/>{{sfn|Hartwell|Hyde|Pevsner|2004|p=388}}
