'''奇特拉庫特縣'''（{{lang-en|Chitrakoot district}}，{{lang-hi|चित्रकूट जिला}}）是[[印度|印度]]的一個[[縣|縣]]，位於該國北部，由[[北方邦|北方邦]]負責管轄，首府[[奇特拉科奥特达姆|奇特拉科奥特达姆]]。該地面積{{convert|3216|km2}}<ref name="census2011">{{en}} {{cite web | url= http://www.census2011.co.in/census/district/542-chitrakoot.html | title= Chitrakoot District : Census 2011 data | access-date= 2018-09-10 | archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20180111174545/http://census2011.co.in/census/district/542-chitrakoot.html | archive-date= 2018-01-11 | dead-url= no }}</ref>。該地是北方邦人口第二少的縣<ref name="districtcensus" />，同時是全國250個最落後的地區之一<ref name=brgf>{{cite web|author=Ministry of Panchayati Raj |date=2009-09-08 |title=A Note on the Backward Regions Grant Fund Programme |publisher=National Institute of Rural Development |url=http://www.nird.org.in/brgf/doc/brgf_BackgroundNote.pdf |accessdate=2011-09-27 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120405033402/http://www.nird.org.in/brgf/doc/brgf_BackgroundNote.pdf |archivedate=2012-04-05 }}</ref>。
