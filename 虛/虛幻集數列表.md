《[[虛幻_(電視劇)|虛幻]]》（{{lang-en|''UnREAL''}}）是一部於2015年6月1日在{{link-en|Lifetime|Lifetime (TV network)}}開播的[[美國|美國]][[黑色幽默|黑色喜劇]][[戲劇|劇情類]]影集。<ref name="futon">{{Cite web|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/showatch/unreal/listings/|title=Shows A-Z - unreal on lifetime|work=The Futon Critic|accessdate=2015-08-10}}</ref>2013年6月30日，Lifetime預訂了本劇試播集。<ref name="Deadline 2013-07">{{Cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2013/07/lifetime-orders-marti-noxon-drama-pilot-based-on-indie-short-sequin-raze-553206/ |title=Lifetime Orders Marti Noxon Drama Pilot Based On Indie Short ''Sequin Raze'' |last=Andreeva |first=Nellie |publisher=[[Deadline.com|Deadline.com]] |date=2013-07-30 |accessdate=2014-02-09}}</ref>

2015年7月6日，Lifetime宣布續訂本劇第2季10集。<ref name="season2">{{Cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2015/07/unreal-renewed-season-2-lifetime-1201471240/|title=‘UnReal’ Renewed For Season 2 By Lifetime|last=Hipes|first=Patrick|publisher=[[Deadline.com|Deadline.com]]|date=2015-07-06|accessdate=2015-08-10}}</ref>2016年6月2日，Lifetime於第2季首播前，提前續訂第3季10集。<ref name="season3">{{Cite web|url=http://variety.com/2016/tv/news/unreal-season-3-renewed-1201787654/|title=‘UnReal’ Renewed for Season 3 Ahead of Season 2 Premiere|last=Prudom|first=Laura|publisher=[[綜藝_(雜誌)|綜藝雜誌]]|date=2016-06-02|accessdate=2016-06-02}}</ref>2017年4月7日，證實第3季將不會在2017年夏季首播，預計安排於2018年初播出。<ref name="Season 3 Delay">{{Cite web|url=http://tvline.com/2017/06/04/unreal-season-3-delayed-premiering-2018-lifetime/|title=''UnREAL'' Season 3 Delayed Until 2018|author=Dave Nemetz|publisher={{le|TVLine|TVLine}}|date=2017-06-04|accessdate=2017-06-04}}</ref>2017年7月28日，Lifetime在[[電視評論家協會|電視評論家協會]]上提前宣布續訂第4季，<ref name="Season 4">{{Cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2017/07/unreal-renewed-season-4-lifetime-mary-kills-season-2-people-tca-1202137915/|title=‘UnReal’ Renewed For Season 4 On Lifetime; Rookie ‘Mary Kills People’ Also Picked Up – TCA|author=Erik Pedersen|publisher=Deadline.com|date=2017-07-28|accessdate=2017-07-28}}</ref>但集數相比往常的10集，刪減了2集為8集。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://tvline.com/2017/08/28/unreal-season-4-reduced-order-8-episodes-lifetime/|title=''UnREAL'' Season 4 Episode Order Cut|author=Michael Ausiello|publisher=TVLine|date=2017-08-28|accessdate=2017-08-31}}</ref>
