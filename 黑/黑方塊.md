《'''黑方塊'''》是俄羅斯前衛藝術家[[馬列維奇|馬列維奇]]創作的一幅作品，最早的一幅創作於1915年，展出於當年的[[0.10展|0.10展]]<ref>[http://www.moma.org/collection/theme.php?theme_id=10202 John Milner, ''Suprematism'', MoMA, Grove Art Online, Oxford University Press, 2009]</ref>，隨後還有其他四個類似的版本。雖然畫家本人説最後一幅實際上早就完成于1913年，其真正的創作時間應該是1930年前後<ref>[http://www.hermitagemuseum.org/wps/portal/hermitage/what-s-on/temp_exh/1999_2013/hm4_1_30/?lng=en ''Kasimir Malevich. Black Square'', Hermitage Museum]</ref><ref name="THE MAKING OF BLACK SQUARE">{{cite web|last1=Kudriavtseva|first1=Catherine I.|title=The Making of Kazimir Malevich's Black Square|url=http://digitallibrary.usc.edu/cdm/ref/collection/p15799coll127/id/375461|publisher=University of Southern California|accessdate=15 May 2015}}</ref><ref name="PAINTING AS EMPTY SPACE">{{cite web|last1=Meinhardt|first1=Johannes|title=The Painting As Empty Space|url=http://allanmccollum.net/allanmcnyc/Johannes_Meinhardt.html|accessdate=15 May 2015}}</ref>。《黑方塊》在[[現代藝術|現代藝術]]史上有重要的地位，被認爲是對傳統藝術的反叛。 <ref name="ALMOST NOTHING">{{cite web|last1=Mazzoni|first1=Ira|title=Everything and Nearly Nothing: Malevich and His Effects|url=http://db-artmag.com/archiv/07/e/thema-mazzoni.html|publisher=DeutscheBank/Art|accessdate=15 May 2015}}</ref><ref name="THE PROPHET">{{cite web|last1=Schjehldahl|first1=Peter|title=The Prophet: Malevich's Revolution|url=http://www.newyorker.com/magazine/2003/06/02/the-prophet-2|publisher=The New Yorker|accessdate=15 May 2015}}</ref><ref name="THE SHAPE OF THINGS">{{cite web|last1=Schjehldahl|first1=Peter|title=The Shape of Things:After Kazimir Malevich |url=http://www.newyorker.com/magazine/2011/03/14/shapes-of-things|publisher=The New Yorker|accessdate=15 May 2015}}</ref>
