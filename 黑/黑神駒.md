《'''黑神駒'''》（Black Beauty）為英國作家[[安娜·休厄爾|安娜·休厄爾]]於1877年完成之小說。小說出版後立即成為[[暢銷書|暢銷書]]，出版五個月後，休厄爾逝世<ref name="Webster">Merriam-Webster (1995). "Black Beauty". ''Merriam Webster's Encyclopedia of Literature''.</ref>。《黑神駒》賣出5000萬本，是史上最暢銷的書籍之一<ref name=times>[http://entertainment.timesonline.co.uk/tol/arts_and_entertainment/books/children/article3459873.ece The Times] on ''Black Beauty'': "Fifty million copies of Black Beauty have been sold in the years since Anna Sewell's publisher paid her £20 for the story." (29 February 2008)</ref>。

《黑神駒》毫不避諱[[動物福利|動物福利]]，也教導對待人的方法，例如仁慈、同情和尊重。《黑神駒》為[[兒童文學|兒童文學]]先驅之一<ref name="clarissa">'[http://www.collectingbooksandmagazines.com/ponybook.html Pony Books: A Brief Introduction by Clarissa Cridland]' web page on collectingbooksandmagazines.com website, viewed 11 December 2011</ref> 。2003年，這部小說被[[英國廣播公司|英國廣播公司]]（BBC）列入[[大閱讀|大閱讀]]調查<ref>[http://www.bbc.co.uk/arts/bigread/top100_2.shtml "BBC – The Big Read"]. BBC. April 2003, Retrieved 18 October 2012</ref>。
