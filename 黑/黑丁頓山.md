'''黑丁頓山'''位於[[牛津市|牛津市]]東部的[[黑丁頓區|黑丁頓區]]西部，海拔160米，是[[牛津市|牛津市]]區與[[黑丁頓區|黑丁頓區]]的連接通道之一。這裡擁有[[黑丁頓|黑丁頓]]最大的公園[[南公園|南公園]]。<ref>[http://www.headington.org.uk/history/headington_hill/index.htm/ Headington Hill section on Headington Community website] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080421111628/http://www.headington.org.uk/history/headington_hill/index.htm |date=2008-04-21 }}</ref>
