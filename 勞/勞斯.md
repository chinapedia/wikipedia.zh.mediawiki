'''勞斯'''（Louth，{{IPAc-en|audio=En-louth.ogg|ˈ|l|aʊ|θ}}）是[[英格蘭|英格蘭]][[林肯郡|林肯郡]]的一個城市和民政教區<ref name=EX283>OS Explorer map 283:Louth and Mablethorpe: (1:25 000):
ISBN 978 0319238240</ref>。當地的地標有聖詹姆斯教堂和勞斯博物館。勞斯有眾多獨立的零售商<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.lincolnshirelife.co.uk/posts/view/luscious-louth | title = Luscious Louth | accessdate = 9 November 2013 | last = Burton | first = Melanie | work = Lincolnshire Life}}</ref>。2012年，勞斯被BBC鄉村生活選為英國最喜歡的集鎮<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.countryfile.com/countryside/countryfile-magazine-awards-results-2012 | title = Countryfile Magazine Awards: The results 2012 | accessdate = 9 November 2013 | work = CountryFile | publisher = [[Countryfile|Countryfile]]}}</ref>。
