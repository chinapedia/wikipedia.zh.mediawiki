'''高柠檬酸'''（{{lang-en|Homocitric acid}}）是一种化学式为HOC(CO<sub>2</sub>H)(CH<sub>2</sub>CO<sub>2</sub>H)(C<sub>2</sub>H<sub>4</sub>CO<sub>2</sub>H)的[[有机化合物|有机化合物]]，  This [[tricarboxylic_acid|tricarboxylic acid]] occurs naturally as a component of the iron-molybdenum cofactor of certain [[nitrogenase|nitrogenase]] proteins.<ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1146/annurev.biochem.71.110601.135406 | title = Greatmetalloclustersinenzymology | year = 2002 | last1 = Rees | first1 = Douglas C. | journal = Annual Review of Biochemistry | volume = 71 | pages = 221–46 | pmid = 12045096}}</ref>  Biochemists often refer to this cofactor as homocitrate, which is the conjugate bases that predominate in neutral aqueous solutions of this species.

高柠檬酸比[[柠檬酸|柠檬酸]]在分子中间多了一个[[亚甲基|亚甲基]]基团，因此称为“高”（{{lang-la|''homo''}}）。但与柠檬酸不同的是，高柠檬酸是一种[[手性|手性]]分子，存在两种对应异构体。此外，高柠檬酸还能自发形成[[内酯|内酯]]：
