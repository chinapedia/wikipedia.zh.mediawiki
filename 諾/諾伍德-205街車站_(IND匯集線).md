'''諾伍德-205街車站'''（{{lang-en|Norwood–205th Street}}），過去稱作「205街車站」（{{lang|en|205th Street}}），是[[紐約地鐵|紐約地鐵]][[IND匯集線|IND匯集線]]的北端[[總站|總站]]。位於[[布朗克斯|布朗克斯]]{{link-en|諾伍德 (布朗克斯)|Norwood, Bronx|諾伍德}}205街及拜恩布里奇大道交界，設有[[紐約地鐵D線|D線]]（任何時候停站）列車服務。
== 歷史 ==
車站作為1920年代末IND匯集線第六和第七段的一部份興建<ref name=NYTimes-INDCncrse-205StRouting-June1929/><ref>{{cite news|url=https://query.nytimes.com/mem/archive/pdf?res=9806E7DF1731E13ABC4151DFB066838B629EDE|title=$2,691,028 IS LOW BID ON SECTION OF SUBWAY; Di Marco & Reimann Are Below Seven Others in Seeking Contract on City's Project.|last=|first=|date=1930-06-29|website=|newspaper=[[紐約時報|The New York Times]]|accessdate=2015-11-04}}</ref>。車站大部份長度都建於已經存在的私人地段下方，並在東端下穿東205街<ref name=NYTimes-INDEastSide-April1929>{{cite news|title=CITY SOON TO LAUNCH $600,000,000 SUBWAY FOR THE EAST SIDE; Delaney to Submit Plans for New System Including the Bronx in Two Months.|url=https://query.nytimes.com/mem/archive/pdf?res=9A03E7DB1630E33ABC4D53DFB2668382639EDE|newspaper=[[紐約時報|The New York Times]]|accessdate=2015-11-04|date=1929-04-05}}</ref>。車站在1933年7月1日連同匯集線地鐵其餘部份一同啟用<ref name=NYTimes-INDCncrseOpen-1933>{{cite news |title=New Bronx Subway Starts Operation |url=https://select.nytimes.com/gst/abstract.html?res=F00D14FE385C16738DDDA80894DF405B838FF1D3 |newspaper=The New York Times |date=1933-07-01 |accessdate=2010-02-13}}</ref><ref name=BklynEagle-INDCncrseOpen-1933>{{cite news|title=Bronx-Concourse New Subway Link Opened at 12:57 A.M.: Adds 21 1/2 Miles to City's System−Connects With Manhattan Line at 145th|url=https://www.newspapers.com/image/59843693/?terms=concourse%2Bsubway%2Bbronx|via={{link-en|Newspapers.com|Newspapers.com}}|newspaper={{link-en|布魯克林鷹報|Brooklyn Eagle|Brooklyn Eagle}}|accessdate=2015-10-26|page=20|date=1933-07-01}}</ref>。1937年7月1日此站安裝了一部扶手電梯，也是扶手電梯首次在[[布朗克斯|布朗克斯]]出現<ref name=NYTimes-IND205Escalator1-1937/><ref name=NYTimes-IND205Escalator2-1937/>。

1954年8月23日，一列[[紐約地鐵D線|D線]]列車在車站以東衝越軌道末端的止衝擋並撞上了隧道的牆壁<ref name="NYTimes-IND205Crash1-1954" /><ref name="NYTimes-IND205Crash2-1954" />。列車長被困隧道7小時，而被救出後還需要截除左腳<ref name=NYTimes-IND205Crash1-1954>{{cite news|title=IND Motorman Trapped 7 Hours In Wreck, Loses Foot in the Rescue|url=https://query.nytimes.com/mem/archive/pdf?res=9803E0D9113EE53BBC4C51DFBE66838F649EDE|newspaper=[[紐約時報|The New York Times]]|accessdate=2015-11-04|date=1954-08-24}}</ref><ref name=NYTimes-IND205Crash2-1954>{{cite news|title=IND Accident Traced: Subway Motorman's Crash Is Laid to 'Man Failure'|url=https://query.nytimes.com/mem/archive/pdf?res=9C03E3D91531E43ABC4D51DFBE66838F649EDE|publisher=[[紐約時報|The New York Times]]|accessdate=2015-11-04|date=1954-08-25}}</ref>。

=== 出口 ===
[[File:205_Street_vc.jpg|thumb]]
此站設有兩個閘機區。全時間的位於西端，設有閘機、詢問處以及兩條前往東206街及拜恩布里奇大道的東北和西北角的樓梯<ref name=BxCB7-Sanitation-April2010/><ref name=MTAMap-VanCourtland-Botanic-FordhamPlz-2015/>。因應附近鄰里不同的地形，此閘機區在1937年安裝了一條扶手電梯，在夾層與月台之間差別25英呎<ref name=NYTimes-IND205Escalator1-1937>{{cite news|title=Subway Din Mars A Fete in Bronx: Halley Starts to Dedicate a New Escalator but the Trains Drown out His Voice|url=https://query.nytimes.com/mem/archive/pdf?res=9F07E0DA1F3AE23ABC4953DFB166838C629EDE|newspaper=[[紐約時報|The New York Times]]|accessdate=2015-11-04|date=1937-07-01}}</ref><ref name=NYTimes-IND205Escalator2-1937>{{cite news|title=New Escalator Ready: Bronx Demonstration Wednesday in 205th St. Subway Station|url=https://query.nytimes.com/mem/archive/pdf?res=9A06E1DE1031EE3ABC4F51DFB066838C629EDE|newspaper=[[紐約時報|The New York Times]]|accessdate=2015-11-04|date=1937-06-27}}</ref>。其餘則需要在月台層步行三段樓梯上行到閘機區<ref name=News12-205St-2013>{{cite web|title=MTA officials: Broken escalator at Norwood & 205th Street Station won't be fixed until April|url=http://bronx.news12.com/news/mta-officials-broken-escalator-at-norwood-205th-street-station-won-t-be-fixed-until-april-1.6667347|publisher={{link-en|新聞12網絡|News 12 Networks|News 12}}|accessdate=2015-11-04|date=2013-12-24}}</ref>。

另一個閘機區位於車站東端，以一個坡度到達月台。該閘機區無人管理，設有全高式閘機和兩條前往東205街和佩里大道西北角和東南角的樓梯<ref name=BxCB7-Sanitation-April2010/><ref name=MTAMap-VanCourtland-Botanic-FordhamPlz-2015>{{cite web|title=MTA Neighborhood Maps: Van Cortlandt Park / NY Botanical Garden|url=http://web.mta.info/maps/neighborhoods/bx/BX4_van_cort_pk_nybg_2015.pdf|publisher=[[大都會運輸署|Metropolitan Transportation Authority]]|accessdate=2015-09-24|date=2015}}</ref>。此處的詢問處在2005年7月30日關閉<ref>{{cite web|title=The D Line 205th Street token booth closes in the Bronx|url=http://bronx.news12.com/news/the-d-line-205th-street-token-booth-closes-in-the-bronx-1.8260331|publisher={{link-en|新聞12網絡|News 12 Networks|News 12}} Bronx|accessdate=2015-11-04|date=2005-08-01}}</ref>並在後來拆除<ref name=NorwoodNews-205StBooth-2015>{{cite web|last1=Hall|first1=Imani|title=5-2 Warns of "Swipers" At Local Stations|url=http://www.norwoodnews.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/NorwoodNews_5_29_web.pdf#page=3|publisher=Norwood News|accessdate=2015-11-04|date=2014-06-11}}</ref>。

== 軌道配置 ==
此站並非設計為匯集線或D線列車的總站，兩條軌道原初都預期可以{{link-en|紐約地鐵計劃擴展|Proposed New York City Subway expansion|東延}}沿伯克大道越過{{link-en|布朗克斯公園|Bronx Park}}以及[[IRT白原路線|IRT白原路線]]服務布朗克斯東北部地區<ref name=NYTimes-OurGreatSubway-IND2ndSystem-1929>{{cite news|last1=Duffus|first1=R.L.|title=OUR GREAT SUBWAY NETWORK SPREADS WIDER; New Plans of Board of Transportation Involve the Building of More Than One Hundred Miles of Additional Rapid Transit Routes for New York|url=https://query.nytimes.com/gst/abstract.html?res=9C0CE4D71530E73ABC4A51DFBF668382639EDE|newspaper=[[紐約時報|The New York Times]]|accessdate=2015-08-19|date=1929-09-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=City Board Votes New Subway Links|url=https://query.nytimes.com/gst/abstract.html?res=980CEED91030EE32A2575AC1A9659C946694D6CF|newspaper=[[紐約時報|The New York Times]]|accessdate=2015-07-03|date=1937-03-19}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=$101,200,000 Asked for 1930 Work on Tubes: Projects Include Jay, Fulton, Crosstown and Queens City Subways|url=https://www.newspapers.com/image/58234468/?terms=culver%2Bramp%2Bsubway|via={{link-en|Newspapers.com|Newspapers.com}}|newspaper={{link-en|布魯克林鷹報|Brooklyn Eagle|Brooklyn Eagle}}|accessdate=2015-09-16|date=1930-01-14}}</ref>。此計劃因缺乏資金而延誤，最終因為1941年[[紐約市|紐約市]]購入幾近破產的{{link-en|紐約、威斯徹斯特及波士頓鐵路|New York, Westchester and Boston Railway}}並改為[[IRT代里大道線|地鐵]]而告吹<ref name=Raskin-RoutesNotTaken-2013 />。另一個{{link-en|行動計劃|Program for Action|1970年代的計劃}}包括將匯集線延長至[[干丘路車站_(IRT白原路線)|白原路]]，但因為財政困難而導致計劃流產<ref name=Raskin-RoutesNotTaken-2013 /><ref name="int-arch">{{cite web | title=Full text of "Metropolitan transportation, a program for action. Report to Nelson A. Rockefeller, Governor of New York." | website=Internet Archive | date=1967-11-07 | url=https://archive.org/stream/metropolitantran00newy/metropolitantran00newy_djvu.txt | accessdate=2015-10-01}}</ref>。

由於興建時預期可繼續延長，兩條軌道繼續沿205街向東延長約700英呎到{{link-en|威布斯特大道|Webster Avenue}}，在混凝土牆壁中止<ref name=NYTimes-IND205Crash1-1954 /><ref name=NYTimes-IND205Crash2-1954 /><ref name=Raskin-RoutesNotTaken-2013>{{cite book|author=Joseph B. Raskin|title=The Routes Not Taken: A Trip Through New York City's Unbuilt Subway System|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=5b6cAQAAQBAJ&pg=PT112&lpg=PT112&dq=jerome+concourse+subway+bronx&source=bl&ots=QOvJhhHWMk&sig=wxsUbv3zpnZhBZvhIP2Fhy8MWqg&hl=en&sa=X&ved=0CE8Q6AEwCDgeahUKEwikhNHypuPIAhWGej4KHSPfA0I#v=onepage&q=jerome%20concourse%20subway%20bronx&f=false|accessdate=2015-08-12|date=2013-11-01|publisher=Fordham University Press|isbn=978-0-8232-5369-2}}</ref>，而此站並無任何職員宿舍。職員在此站以南的[[貝德福德公園林蔭路車站_(IND匯集線)|貝德福德公園林蔭路]]交換。另外，此站以西並無任何剪式橫渡線，此處有一條中央軌道西行前往[[紐約地鐵車廠#匯集車廠|匯集車廠]]<ref name="tracks" /><ref name=tracks-469 />。因此，以此站為總站的列車需要在南面軌道落客，然後繼續東行前往軌道末端。之後利用該處的剪式橫渡線回到車站北面軌道上客，前往[[曼哈頓|曼哈頓]]和[[布魯克林|布魯克林]]<ref name=NYTimes-IND205Crash1-1954 /><ref name="tracks" /><ref name=Raskin-RoutesNotTaken-2013 /><ref name=tracks-469>{{NYCS const|trackref|469}}</ref>。受制於軌道配置，列車將要從南行軌道倒車進入車廠，而從車廠開出的列車有時需要在北面軌道開始營運<ref name="tracks" /><ref name=tracks-469/>。

== 車站周邊 ==
車站位於若干{{link-en|諾伍德 (布朗克斯)|Norwood, Bronx#Landmarks|諾伍德地標}}相鄰，包括[[紐約公共圖書館|紐約公共圖書館]]的{{link-en|紐約公共圖書館分館列表|List of New York Public Library branches#Libraries in the Bronx|莫肖盧分館}}、{{link-en|蒙特菲奧爾醫療中心|Montefiore Medical Center}}以及{{link-en|紐約市健康及醫院公司|New York City Health and Hospitals Corporation#List of hospital facilities|北中央布朗克斯醫院}}，位於車站以北東210街，{{link-en|聖布倫丹教堂 (布朗克斯)|St. Brendan's Church (Bronx, New York)|聖布倫丹教堂和學校}}、{{link-en|瓦倫丁-瓦倫屋|Valentine–Varian House}}以及前身為{{link-en|威廉斯布里奇水庫|Williamsbridge Reservoir}}的{{link-en|威廉斯布里奇橢圓|Williamsbridge Oval}}<ref name=MTAMap-VanCourtland-Botanic-FordhamPlz-2015 />。
