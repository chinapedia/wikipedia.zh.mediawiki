'''諾基亞215'''是由[[微軟移動|微軟移動]]製造的[[諾基亞|諾基亞]][[雙頻|雙頻]][[GSM|GSM]]手機。這款手機有黑色，白色和淺綠色。 <ref>[http://www.pocket-lint.com/news/130304-nokia-130-dual-sim-dumbphone-is-super-cheap-but-still-offers-access-to-microsoft-cloud-services ''Nokia 130 dual-sim dumbphone is super cheap but still offers access to Microsoft cloud services''; pocket-lint.com; August 11, 2014.]</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.microsoft.com/en-us/news/press/2014/aug14/08-11nokia.aspx# |title=''Microsoft Devices Group announces ultra-affordable mobile phone with music and video player for 19 euros''; microsoft.com; August 11, 2014. |access-date=2015-12-14 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140814005813/http://www.microsoft.com/en-us/news/press/2014/aug14/08-11nokia.aspx# |archive-date=2014-08-14 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>

==技術指標==
諾基亞215擁有一個VGA[[攝像頭|攝像頭]]，[[揚聲器|揚聲器]]，[[多媒體|多媒體]]播放，MMS短信，[[網絡瀏覽器|網絡瀏覽器]]和[[電子郵件|電子郵件]]客戶端。它也帶有[[Facebook|Facebook]]和[[Twitter|Twitter]]應用程式，以及[[Opera_Mini|Opera Mini]]網頁瀏覽器，而且還内置微軟服務，如[[Bing|Bing]]和[[MSN_Mobile#MSN天氣|MSN天氣]]。<ref name=Microsoftlumiaconversations>{{cite web |url=http://lumiaconversations.microsoft.com/2015/01/05/nokia-215/ |title=Meet Nokia 215: our most affordable Internet-ready entry-level phone yet |accessdate=January 5, 2015 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150105222119/http://lumiaconversations.microsoft.com/2015/01/05/nokia-215/ |archivedate=一月 5, 2015 }}</ref>電池通話時間長達20小時。待機時間為29天。<ref name=Nokia215>{{cite web |url=http://www.microsoft.com/en/mobile/phone/215/specifications/ | title=Nokia 215 Specifications | accessdate=January 6, 2015}}</ref>其尺寸為116×50×12.9毫米，並使用2G網絡基礎設施，並通過微型SIM卡激活。可在其地址簿中存儲多達1000個聯繫人電話。
諾基亞215在2015年第一季度向中東，非洲，亞洲和歐洲消費者發布。<ref name=Microsoftnews>{{cite web |url=http://microsoft-news.com/microsoft-tells-us-what-we-can-do-with-the-new-29-nokia-215/ |title=Microsoft Tells Us What We Can Do With The New $29 Nokia 215 |accessdate=January 5, 2015}}</ref>
