'''玛丽·贝丝·帕特森'''（{{lang-en|'''Mary Beth Patterson'''}}，艺名'''贝丝·迪托'''，{{bd|1981年|2月19日|catIdx=D}}）出生于[[阿肯色州|阿肯色州]][[瑟西_(阿肯色州)|瑟西]]<ref>{{cite web|title=Beth Ditto|url=http://www.emusic.com/listen/#/artist/beth-ditto/11730295/|publisher=emusic|accessdate=2012-07-15}}</ref>，是一位美国[[唱作人|创作歌手]]，[[独立摇滚|独立摇滚]]乐队[[流言蜚語|流言蜚語]]的主唱。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.gossipyouth.com/lebio.html |title=Bio |accessdate=2008-03-06 |publisher=The Gossip official website |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071022041410/http://www.gossipyouth.com/lebio.html |archivedate=2007-10-22 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>

==个人生活==
迪托是一个[[女同性恋|女同性恋]]，众所周知她十分支持[[LGBT|LGBT]]和[[女权主义|女权主义]]。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://music.guardian.co.uk/pop/story/0,,2098486,00.html|title=What would Beth Ditto do?|accessdate=2008-03-06|date=2007-06-08|author=Beth Ditto|publisher=The Guardian | location=London}}</ref>

2013年7月，迪托和女友Kristin Ogata在[[夏威夷州|夏威夷州]]的[[茂宜島|茂宜島]]举行婚礼。迪托身穿Jean Paul Gaultier婚纱光着脚，Ogata则穿了外套，衬衫和短裤。<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/music/news/the-gossips-beth-ditto-is-talk-of-the-town-singer-marries-longterm-girlfriend-kristin-ogata-8730852.html |title=The Gossip's Beth Ditto is talk of the town: Singer marries long-term girlfriend Kristin Ogata |newspaper=The Independent |date=2013-07-24 |accessdate=2013-07-25 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2013/07/24/beth-ditto-marries-kristin-ogata_n_3643966.html |title=Beth Ditto Of Gossip Marries Kristin Ogata, Longtime Girlfriend, In Hawaiian Wedding |newspaper=The Huffington Post |first=Cavan |last=Sieczkowski |date=2013-07-24 |accessdate=2013-07-25 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/tvshowbiz/article-2375832/The-Gossips-Beth-Ditto-marries-girlfriend-Kristin-Ogata.html |title=Wedding belles!
