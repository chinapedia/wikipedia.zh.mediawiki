'''贝尔尼纳快车'''是连接[[瑞士|瑞士]][[库尔|库尔]]与[[意大利|意大利]]波斯基亚沃和蒂拉诺的特快列车，从北到南穿越瑞士阿尔卑斯山。其大部分线路通过[[世界遗产|世界遗产]][[雷蒂亚铁路|雷蒂亚铁路]]阿尔布拉/贝尔尼纳路段风景。 

贝尔尼纳快车由[[雷蒂亚铁路|雷蒂亚铁路]]公司运营。这并不是一个高速火车，而是为一段长时间的旅程提供服务。贝尔尼纳快车由全景车厢组成，提供多语言的解说。该列车很受旅行者欢迎，在蒂拉诺可以转乘邮政巴士经过意大利的[[科莫湖|科莫湖]]到达瑞士的[[卢加诺|卢加诺]]。<ref name=offial/>

2008年，贝尔尼纳快车线路中的阿尔布拉/贝尔尼纳路段被列入[[世界遗产|世界遗产]]。<ref name=offial>[http://www.rhb.ch/index.php?id=33?&L=4 雷蒂亚铁路-贝尔尼纳快车]</ref>该路段乘坐时间为4小时，经过196座桥梁，通过55个隧道，<ref name=offial/>最高处海拔为2253米。

阿尔布拉铁路线于1898-1904年建造，一直由雷塔恩铁路公司运营。贝尔尼纳铁路线于1908-1910年建造，贝尔尼纳铁路公司独立运营直到1940年，之后被雷蒂亚铁路公司接管。

=== 阿尔布拉线 ===

Main article: [[阿尔布拉铁路|阿尔布拉铁路]]

The train leaves the city of [[Chur|Chur]] (585 m) in [[Graubünden|Graubünden]] and follows the course of the [[Rhine|Rhine]] to [[Bonaduz|Bonaduz]] (655 m). From there it enters the Domleschg Valley and follows the [[Hinterrhein_(river)|Posterior Rhine]] from [[Rhäzüns|Rhäzüns]] (658 m) to [[Thusis|Thusis]] (697 m). The train continues toward [[Tiefencastel|Tiefencastel]] (851 m) following the [[Albula_river|Albula river]] and then crosses the [[Landwasser_Viaduct|Landwasser Viaduct]] before arriving at [[Filisur|Filisur]] (1032 m). Shortly after Filisur the train passes its first [[Spiral_(railway)|spiral]] tunnel then continues to [[Bergün/Bravuogn|Bergün/Bravuogn]]. Between Bergün/Bravuogn (1373 m) and [[Preda|Preda]] (1789 m), at the end of the valley, the train has to achieve a difference in height of about 400 meters with an horizontal distance of 5 km without using [[Rack_railway|rack-and-pinion]], but with many spirals. Then the train enters the [[Albula_Tunnel|Albula Tunnel]] at 1,815 m under the [[Albula_Pass|Albula Pass]]. It emerges in the Val Bever, where it reaches [[Bever,_Switzerland|Bever]] (1,708 m) on the [[Engadin|Engadin]] plain. The train continues toward [[Samedan|Samedan]] (1,721 m) and arrives at the [[St._Moritz|St. Moritz]] station (1,775 m).
