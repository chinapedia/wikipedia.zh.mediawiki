'''克瑞斯蒂娜·朱迪斯·佩瑞'''（'''Christina Judith Perri'''，{{bd|1986年|8月19日|||CatIdx=María}}）<ref>{{cite web|title=Christina Perri|url=http://www.wkrq.com/?s=christina+perri|publisher=[[:en:WKRQ|WKRQ]]|accessdate=January 23, 2011}}</ref><ref name="Beatweek">{{cite web|title=Christina Perri interview: Beatweek 2011 Rising Star talks Lovestrong|url=http://www.beatweek.com/coverstory/8522-christina-perri-interview-beatweek-2011-rising-star-talks-lovestrong-and-more/|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20120527091909/http://www.beatweek.com/coverstory/8522-christina-perri-interview-beatweek-2011-rising-star-talks-lovestrong-and-more/|archivedate=2012-05-27}}</ref>是一名美国[[唱作人|唱作人]]。她的第1首单曲《Jar of Hearts》在美国国内的销量达3百万份。她后因亲自创作并演唱过《[[暮光之城4：破晓|暮光之城4：破晓]]》主题曲《{{link-en|千年之恋 (克瑞斯蒂娜·佩瑞歌曲)|A Thousand Years (Christina Perri song)|千年之恋}}》而进一步为人们熟知。代表作主要还包括《Burning Gold》、《Shot Me In the Heart》、《Butterfly》和《Sea of Lovers》等。

克瑞斯蒂娜已发售2张个人[[录音室专辑|录音室专辑]]，还曾在[[黛米·洛瓦托|黛米·洛瓦托]]2014年世界巡演中担任过暖场歌手。

== 早年生活 ==
佩瑞出身于美国[[宾夕法尼亚州|宾夕法尼亚州]][[费城|费城]]近郊{{link-en|本萨棱镇|Bensalem Township, Pennsylvania}}。她母亲名叫Mary，父亲名叫Dante Perri。<ref>http://articles.philly.com/2010-08-08/entertainment/24970320_1_jar-hearts-stardom</ref>她有一个哥哥叫Nick Perri，做过吉他手。<ref name="usat">{{cite news|url=http://www.usatoday.com/life/music/news/2010-07-10-christina-perri_N.htm  |title=Christina Perri pries open the lid to fame with 'Jar of Hearts' |author=Lopez, Korina |accessdate=2010-07-25 | work=USA Today | date=July 13, 2010}}</ref>他父亲来自意大利。<ref name=clayton>{{cite web|url=http://claytonperry.com/2010/10/28/interview-christina-perri-singer-songwriter-and-producer/ |title=Interview: Christina Perri – Singer, Songwriter and Producer « Clayton Perry's Interview Exclusives |publisher=Claytonperry.com |date= |accessdate=2012-10-16 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140111183053/http://claytonperry.com/2010/10/28/interview-christina-perri-singer-songwriter-and-producer/ |archivedate=2014-01-11 }}</ref><ref>http://archive.radioswisspop.ch/cgi-bin/pip/html.cgi?lang=en&m=entity&v=b&w=Christina+Perri</ref>佩瑞于2004年毕业于雷安主教高中(Archbishop Ryan High School)。<ref>{{cite web|last=Derakhshani|first=Tirdad|title=Bensalem native Christina Perri's sudden success after 'Jar of Hearts'|url=http://articles.philly.com/2010-08-08/entertainment/24970320_1_jar-hearts-stardom|publisher=Philadelphia Inquirer|accessdate=August 8, 2010}}</ref>她曾通过看Shannon Hoon的视频自学吉他。<ref name="RollingStoneBandOfTheWeek">{{cite web|last=Edwards|first=Gavin|title=Band of the Week|url=http://www.rollingstone.com/music/news/51942/226324|publisher=Rolling Stone}}</ref>年少的时候，她常会参演一些音乐剧，并参与剧中的歌唱。
