|title=Christopher Pincher MP 
|publisher=BBC |work=BBC Democracy Live |date= |accessdate=25 July 2010}}</ref>）是一位[[英格蘭|英格蘭]]政治人物，他的黨籍是[[保守黨_(英國)|保守黨]]。自2010年開始，他擔任[[塔姆沃思_(英國國會選區)|塔姆沃思]]選區選出的英國下議院議員<ref name="Labour MP of 14 years loses">{{cite news |title=Labour MP of 14 years loses seat to Tories in Tamworth |first= |last= |url=http://www.birminghampost.net/news/politics-news/2010/05/07/labour-mp-of-14-years-loses-seat-to-tories-in-tamworth-65233-26395057/ |newspaper=[[Birmingham_Post|Birmingham Post]] |date=7 May 2009 |accessdate=11 June 2011}}</ref>。他在1987年加入保守黨<ref name="Parliament biography">{{cite web|url=http://www.parliament.uk/biographies/christopher-pincher/35491|title=Christopher Pincher|author=|date=|work=|publisher=[[Parliament_of_the_United_Kingdom|Parliament]]|accessdate=15 July 2011|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110701162625/http://www.parliament.uk/biographies/christopher-pincher/35491|archivedate=2011年7月1日}}</ref>。
