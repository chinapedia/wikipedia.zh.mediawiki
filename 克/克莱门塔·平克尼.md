== 早年 ==
平克尼生于[[南卡罗来纳州|南卡罗来纳州]][[博福特_(南卡罗来纳州)|博福特]]，母亲赛菲亚·艾肯斯（{{lang|en|Theopia Aikens}}，[[名字|娘家姓]]叫布鲁姆斯，1945－2005）是一名儿童早教专家，父亲名叫约翰·平克尼（{{lang|en|John Pinckey}}）<ref name=PostCourier-MotherObit-2005>{{cite news|title=Theopia Aikens Obituary|url=http://archiver.rootsweb.ancestry.com/th/read/SCMARION/2005-11/1132060874|accessdate=2015-06-18|work=The Post and Courier|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150618153427/http://archiver.rootsweb.ancestry.com/th/read/SCMARION/2005-11/1132060874|archive-date=2015-06-18|dead-url=yes}}</ref><ref name=FamilySearch-US-SSDI-2005>{{cite web|title=Theopia Stevenson Aikens - United States Social Security Death Index|url=https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:JK4R-QNR|website=FamilySearch|accessdate=2015-06-18}}</ref><ref name=SCLegislature-PinckneyBio>{{cite web |url=http://www.scstatehouse.gov/member.php?code=1479545277 |title=Senator Clementa C. Pinckney - Member Biography |work=South Carolina Legislature |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6ZN6TaFcG?url=http://www.scstatehouse.gov/member.php?code=1479545277 |archivedate=2015-06-18 |accessdate=2015-06-18 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。平克尼13岁开始在教会布道，18岁便被任命为牧师<ref name=Heavy-Pinckney5Fact-2015/>。1995年，平克尼自{{tsl|en|Allen University|艾倫大學}}取得學士學位，随后于1999年在[[南卡罗来纳大学|南卡罗来纳大学]]获得[[公共行政硕士|公共行政硕士]]学位<ref name=Heavy-Pinckney5Fact-2015 /><ref name=VoteSmart-Bio>{{cite web|title=Clementa Pinckney's Biography|url=https://votesmart.org/candidate/biography/11972/clementa-pinckney#.VYKAJRNVhBc|website=Vote Smart|accessdate=2015-06-18}}</ref>。之后，他从[[信义宗南方神学院|信义宗南方神学院]]获得硕士学位<ref name=ReligionNews-USAToday-Legend-2015>{{cite news|last1=Diebel|first1=Matthew|title=Slain S.C. pastor Clementa Pinckney was 'a giant, a legend'|url=http://www.religionnews.com/2015/06/18/slain-s-c-pastor-clementa-pinckney-was-a-giant-a-legend/|accessdate=2015-06-19|work=USA Today|date=2015-06-18}}</ref><ref name=EmanuelAMEChurch-Rev-Bio>{{cite web|title=Reverend Clementa Pinckney - Pastor's Page |url=http://www.emanuelamechurch.org/revpinckney.php |website=Emanuel African Methodist Episcopal Church |accessdate=2015-06-19 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150821222515/http://www.emanuelamechurch.org/revpinckney.php |archivedate=2015-08-21 }}</ref>。平克尼还在[[韦斯利神学院|韦斯利神学院]]获得教牧学博士学位<ref>{{cite web|last1=McAllister-Wilson|first1=David|title=Mourning the Loss of the Rev. Pinckney and Church Members|url=https://www.wesleyseminary.edu/NewsandEvents/News/tabid/201/ID/225/Mourning-the-Loss-of-the-Rev-Pinckney-and-Church-Members.aspx|website=Wesley Seminary|accessdate=2015-06-18|date=2015-06-18|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150619050529/https://www.wesleyseminary.edu/NewsandEvents/News/tabid/201/ID/225/Mourning-the-Loss-of-the-Rev-Pinckney-and-Church-Members.aspx|archivedate=2015-06-19}}</ref>。

== 生涯 ==
=== 宗教生涯 ===
平克尼曾在南卡罗来纳州的[[博福特_(南卡罗来纳州)|博福特]]、[[查尔斯顿_(南卡罗来纳州)|查尔斯顿]]和[[哥伦比亚_(南卡罗来纳州)|哥伦比亚]]傳道<ref name=BeaufortGazette-Mourning-2015>{{cite news|last1=Lurye|first1=Rebecca|title=Sen. Clementa Pinckney mourned in Jasper County hometown|url=http://www.islandpacket.com/2015/06/18/3801166_sen-clementa-pinckney-mourned.html?rh=1|accessdate=2015-06-18|work=The Beaufort Gazette|date=2015-06-18|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150619004709/http://www.islandpacket.com/2015/06/18/3801166_sen-clementa-pinckney-mourned.html?rh=1|archivedate=2015-06-19}}</ref>。2010年，平克尼成为查尔斯顿[[以馬內利非裔衛理公會教堂|以馬內利非裔衛理公會教堂]]的牧师。[[沃尔特·斯科特枪击案|沃尔特·斯科特枪击案]]过后，平克尼成为州内多座教堂的牧师<ref name=PostCourier-BecamePastorMotherAME-2010>{{cite news|last1=Parker|first1=Adam|title=Leading from the pulpit|url=http://www.postandcourier.com/article/20101128/ARCHIVES/311289929|accessdate=2015-06-18|work=The Post and Courier|date=2010-11-28}}</ref><ref name=PostCourier-FollowingFootsteps-2010>{{cite news|last1=Parker|first1=Adam|title=Mother Emanuel AME pastor follows in footsteps of 19th-century minister-lawmaker|url=http://www.postandcourier.com/article/20101128/ARCHIVES/311289918|accessdate=2015-06-18|work=The Post and Courier|date=2010-11-28}}</ref>，这在当地引发了一些争议<ref name=Heavy-Pinckney5Fact-2015 />。

=== 议员生涯 ===
1996年，23岁的平克尼首次当选为南卡罗来纳州大会议员，成为南卡罗来纳州最年轻的非洲裔议员<ref name=SavannahMorningNews-20Under40-1999>{{cite news|title=Local News - 20 Under 40: Clementa Pinckney 12/29/99|url=http://savannahnow.com/stories/122999/LOCpinckney.shtml#.VYKHFRNVhBc|accessdate=2015-06-18|work=Savannah Morning News|date=1999-12-29|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150627041639/http://savannahnow.com/stories/122999/LOCpinckney.shtml#.VYKHFRNVhBc|archive-date=2015-06-27|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。他曾就职于南卡罗来纳州众议院，直至2000年入选南卡罗来纳州参议员。

身为州参议员，鉴于一名手无寸铁的黑人男子沃尔特·斯科特被[[北查尔斯顿_(南卡罗来纳州)|北查尔斯顿]]警察八次朝背部开枪后击毙的事件，平克尼曾倡导为要求警察及其他警务人员佩戴随身摄像头立法<ref name=Heavy-Pinckney5Fact-2015 />。

== 私生活 ==
1999年，平克尼在[[佐治亚州|佐治亚州]][[奥古斯塔_(佐治亚州)|奥古斯塔]]与珍妮弗·本杰明结婚<ref name=SavannahMorningNews-Wedding-1999>{{cite news|title=Wedding bells don't signal end of Pinckney's political career|url=http://savannahnow.com/stories/102099/CMNbells.shtml#.VYJ95RNVhBd|work=Savannah Morning News|date=1999-10-20|access-date=2015-06-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150712123128/http://savannahnow.com/stories/102099/CMNbells.shtml#.VYJ95RNVhBd|archive-date=2015-07-12|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。夫妇俩生活在[[南卡罗来纳州|南卡罗来纳州]][[里奇兰_(南卡罗来纳州)|里奇兰]]，育有两女爱莉安娜和马拉娜<ref name=SCLegislature-PinckneyBio />。平肯尼是历史上的黑人联谊会[[阿尔法联谊会|阿尔法联谊会]]的成员<ref name=WatchTheYard-Alpha-2015>{{cite web|last1=Watch The Yard|title=Alpha Phi Alpha Brother, Rev Clementa C. Pinckney Among Those Killed In Charleston Church Shooting|url=http://www.watchtheyard.com/alphas/alpha-phi-alpha-rev-clementa-pinckney-killed/|website=Watch The Yard|accessdate=2015-06-18|date=2015-06-18|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150619015803/http://www.watchtheyard.com/alphas/alpha-phi-alpha-rev-clementa-pinckney-killed/|archivedate=2015-06-19}}</ref>。

2015年6月24日，平克尼的遗体将被放在南卡罗来纳州议会参议院办公室圆形大厅供公众吊唁。此外，里奇兰的圣约翰AMC教堂和查尔斯顿的[[以馬內利非裔衛理公會教堂|以馬內利非裔衛理公會教堂]]也有公众吊唁仪式。葬礼定于2015年6月26日在查尔斯顿学院TD体育场举行<ref name=Guardian-FuneralPlans-2015>{{cite news|last1=
Staff and agencies in South Carolina|first1=|title=Charleston shooting: Clementa Pinckney’s casket to lie at State House|url=http://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2015/jun/21/charleston-shooting-senators-funeral-planned-amid-confederate-flag-protests|accessdate=2015-06-21|work=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]|agency=[[The_Associated_Press|The Associated Press]]|date=2015-06-21}}</ref><ref name=TheState-FuneralPlans-2015>{{cite news|last1=Monk|first1=John|title=Pinckney’s casket to be in State House Wednesday; other funeral plans announced|url=http://www.thestate.com/news/politics-government/article25072327.html|accessdate=2015-06-21|work=The State|date=2015-06-20}}</ref>。

== 逝世 ==
{{main|2015年南卡罗莱纳州查尔斯顿枪击案}}
2015年6月17日，平克尼遇害当天的上午在查尔斯顿参与了[[民主党_(美国)|民主党]]候选人[[希拉里·克林顿|希拉里·克林顿]]的竞选活动<ref name=BlueNationReview-Shooting-2015>{{cite news|last1=Drury|first1=Shawn|title=BREAKING: Mass Shooting In Historic Black SC Church, 9 Dead|url=http://bluenationreview.com/breaking-shooting-at-historic-church-in-charleston-leaves-8-dead/|accessdate=2015-06-18|work=Blue Nation Review|date=2015-06-17}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Sarlan|first1=Benjy|title=Nine dead in Charleston church massacre|url=http://www.msnbc.com/msnbc/charleston-police-church-shooting|accessdate=2015-06-19|work=[[MSNBC|MSNBC]]}}</ref>。晚上，他主持了[[以馬內利非裔衛理公會教堂|以馬內利非裔衛理公會教堂]]的圣经研讨会和祈祷。枪手迪伦·卢福（{{lang|en|Dylann Roof}}）向教友开枪，杀害了包括平克尼在内的九人<ref name=NYTimes-Shooting-2015>{{cite news|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2015/06/19/us/charleston-church-shooting.html|title=Charleston Church Shooting Leaves 9 Dead; Gunman Is Sought|work=The New York Times|date=2015-06-18|accessdate=2015-06-18}}</ref>。尽管[[美国联邦调查局|美国联邦调查局]]的调查把这起大规模枪击事件看作仇恨罪案<ref name=Heavy-Pinckney5Fact-2015 /><ref>{{cite news|last1=MSNBC Staff|title=Tragedy strikes Charleston, South Carolina|url=http://www.msnbc.com/msnbc/tragedy-strikes-charleston-south-carolina|accessdate=2015-06-18|work=[[MSNBC|MSNBC]]|date=2015-06-18}}</ref>，但许多人认为案件是带有恐怖主义的种族动机行为，并批评执法部门和媒体没有正确认识案件<ref name=Heavy-CharlestonShootingTerrorism-2015>{{cite web|last1=Cheney-Rice|first1=Zak|title=There's One Word the Media Isn't Using When Talking About the Charleston Shooter|url=http://heavy.com/news/2015/06/clementa-pinckney-dead-charleston-church-shooting-victim-black-church-pastor-ame-shot-injury/|website=Heavy.com|accessdate=2015-06-18}}</ref><ref name=HuffPo-CharlestonShootingTerrorism-2015>{{cite web|last1=Workneh|first1=Lilly|title=Why Recognizing The Charleston Church Shooting As An Act Of Racially Motivated Terrorism Is Only The First Step|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2015/06/18/charleston-church-shooting-race_n_7609640.html|website=Huffington Post|accessdate=2015-06-18}}</ref>。
