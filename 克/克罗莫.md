'''克罗莫'''（{{lang-en|Chromo}}）是[[美国|美国]][[科羅拉多州|科羅拉多州]][[阿丘利塔縣_(科羅拉多州)|阿丘利塔縣]]的一个[[非建制地區|非建制地區]]和[[美國郵政署|美国邮政点]]，该地的邮政局的[[美國郵區編號|邮政编码]]为81128。<ref name="ZIPcode"><cite class="citation web">[http://zip4.usps.com/zip4/citytown.jsp "邮政编码查询"]  {{webarchive|url=https://www.webcitation.org/5uRWp2Zfd?url=http://zip4.usps.com/zip4/citytown.jsp |date=2010-11-23 }} ([[JavaScript|JavaScript]]/[[HTML|HTML]]). </cite></ref>

==历史==
1985年开始，有一所名为克罗莫（Chromo）的邮政局就开始投入运作。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.postalhistory.com/postoffices.asp?state=CO |title=Post offices |publisher=Jim Forte Postal History |accessdate=24 June 2016 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160306120236/http://www.postalhistory.com/postoffices.asp?state=CO |archivedate=6 March 2016 |df= }}</ref> 克罗莫城镇附近有克罗莫山脉，克罗莫镇因地理位置处于山脉遍而此得名。<ref name=dawson>{{cite book|last=Dawson|first=John Frank|title=Place names in Colorado: why 700 communities were so named, 150 of Spanish or Indian origin|url=https://babel.hathitrust.org/cgi/pt?id=mdp.39015051116740;view=1up;seq=19|year=1954|publisher=The J. Frank Dawson Publishing Co.|location=Denver, CO|page=13}}</ref>

==气候==
克罗莫地带的[[氣候|氣候]]根据[[柯本气候分类法|柯本气候分类法]]属于[[温带大陆性湿润气候|温带大陆性湿润气候]]。气温于夏季以及冬季时候相差很大，冬天有时候非常冷、夏季则潮湿温热。<ref name="Weatherbase">{{cite web | title=Chromo, Colorado Köppen Climate Classification (Weatherbase) | website=Weatherbase | url=http://www.weatherbase.com/weather/weather-summary.php3?s=806292&cityname=Chromo%2C+Colorado%2C+United+States+of+America&units=%20Climate%20Summary%20for%20Chromo,%20Colorado | access-date=2018-08-31}}</ref>
