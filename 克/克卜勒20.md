'''克卜勒20'''（{{lang en|'''Kepler-20'''}}）是一個距離地球950光年的恆星<ref name=Nature-20111220 />，位於[[天琴座|天琴座]]，它的行星系統有5顆行星<ref name="NASA-20111220">{{cite web |last=Johnson |first=Michele |title=NASA Discovers First Earth-size Planets Beyond Our Solar System|url=http://www.nasa.gov/mission_pages/kepler/news/kepler-20-system.html|publisher=[[NASA|NASA]] |date=20 December 2011 |accessdate=2011-12-20}}</ref>，其中兩顆為[[類地行星|類地行星]]。<ref>[http://www.theguardian.com/science/2011/dec/20/planets-earth-like-exoplanet-solar-system Two new planets are most Earth-like ever seen – but hot as hell - The Guardian]</ref>

==恆星狀況==
克卜勒20的[[視星等|視星等]]為12.51<ref>[http://www.ancientsuns.com/space-facts/space-news/kepler-20ef.php Kepler-20e and Kepler-20f — 2 Earth-sized Planets - Ancient Suns]</ref>，因此不能僅以肉眼觀看，必須以口徑{{Convert|15|cm|in|sigfig=1|abbr=on}}以上望遠鏡觀測<ref name=sherrod_koed2003/>。該恆星的光度稍低於太陽，半徑是[[太陽半徑|太陽半徑]]的94%，質量是[[太陽質量|太陽質量]]的91%。克卜勒20光球層的[[有效溫度|有效溫度]]是稍低於太陽的5,466 K，因此其顏色為黃色，[[恆星光譜|恆星光譜]]分類是G8。<ref name=csiro/>截至2011年，目前尚不知該恆星是否為[[主序星|主序星]]，而它的[[金屬量|金屬量]]與太陽相當。它的年齡可能比太陽稍老，雖然誤差範圍相對較大<ref name=exoplanet/>。

2011年12月20日<ref>[http://articles.latimes.com/2011/dec/20/science/la-sci-planets-20111221 Two Earth-sized planets found orbiting star Kepler-20 - Los Angeles Times]</ref>，[[克卜勒太空望遠鏡|克卜勒太空望遠鏡]]團隊報告了首次發現體積與地球相若的行星[[克卜勒20e|克卜勒20e]]<ref name="Kepler20e-20111220">{{cite web |author=[[NASA|NASA Staff]] |title=Kepler: A Search For Habitable Planets - Kepler-20e |url=http://kepler.nasa.gov/Mission/discoveries/kepler20e/ |date=20 December 2011 |publisher=[[NASA|NASA]] |accessdate=2011-12-23 }}</ref>和[[克卜勒20f|克卜勒20f]]<ref name="Kepler20f-20111220">{{cite web |author=[[NASA|NASA Staff]] |title=Kepler: A Search For Habitable Planets - Kepler-20f |url=http://kepler.nasa.gov/Mission/discoveries/kepler20f/ |date=20 December 2011 |publisher=[[NASA|NASA]] |accessdate=2011-12-23 }}</ref>環繞[[類太陽恆星|類太陽恆星]]<ref name="Nature-20111220">{{cite web |last=Hand |first=Eric |title=Kepler discovers first Earth-sized exoplanets |url=http://www.nature.com/news/kepler-discovers-first-earth-sized-exoplanets-1.9688 |date=20 December 2011 |publisher=[[自然_(期刊)|Nature]] |accessdate=2011-12-20 }}</ref><ref name="NASA-20111220" />克卜勒20公轉<ref>[http://kepler.nasa.gov/news/nasakeplernews/index.cfm?FuseAction=ShowNews&NewsID=172 Kepler-20 system: 5 planets including two that are Earth-size - NASA]</ref>。

根據[[美國航空航天局|美國航空航天局]]的報告，這兩顆[[類地行星|類地行星]]距離[[適居帶|適居帶]]過遠<ref name="Nature-20111220" />，表面溫度分別是760和427°C，而這兩顆行星的軌道半徑都小於[[水星|水星]]軌道<ref name="Moskowitz">{{cite news |last=Moskowitz |first=Clara |title=First Ever 'Earth-Sized' Alien Planets Discovered |url=http://www.foxnews.com/scitech/2011/12/20/first-ever-earth-sized-alien-planets-discovered/|date=20 December 2011 |publisher=[[福斯新聞頻道|Fox News Channel]]|accessdate=2011-12-20 }}</ref>。克卜勒20在之前已發現有三個海王星大小的行星：[[克卜勒20b|克卜勒20b]]、[[克卜勒20c|克卜勒20c]]和[[克卜勒20d|克卜勒20d]]。而該系統中距離母恆星距離由近到遠是 b-e-c-f-d。而這些行星的軌道所在範圍相當於水星環繞太陽軌道之內<ref name="NYT-20111220">{{cite news |last=Overbye |first=Dennis |title=Two Earth-Size Planets Are Discovered |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2011/12/21/science/space/nasas-kepler-spacecraft-discovers-2-earth-size-planets.html |date=20 December 2011 |publisher=[[New_York_Times|New York Times]] |accessdate=2011-12-21 }}</ref><ref name="Space-20111220">{{cite web|last=Tate |first=Karl |url=http://www.space.com/13987-earth-size-alien-planets-kepler-22e-infographic.html |title=At Last, Earth-Sized Alien Worlds (Infographic) |publisher=Space.com |date=20 December 2011 |accessdate=2011-12-21}}</ref>。

該行星系統和太陽系的不同之處是該系統的氣體巨行星和類地行星是交錯排列的。<ref>[http://arxiv.org/abs/1112.4550 Two Earth-sized planets orbiting Kepler-20 - Cornell University Library]</ref>

===克卜勒20e和f===
克卜勒20e和f都是[[太陽系外行星|系外]][[類地行星|類地行星]]<ref>[http://www.nature.com/nature/journal/vnfv/ncurrent/full/nature10780.html Two Earth-sized planets orbiting Kepler-20 - Nature]</ref>，它們的質量與地球相若：e的質量為地球質量的0.39–1.67倍<ref name="kepler20e">[http://kepler.nasa.gov/Mission/discoveries/kepler20e/ Kepler-20e - NASA]</ref>，而f的質量則為地球質量的0.66–3.04倍<ref name="kepler20f">[http://kepler.nasa.gov/Mission/discoveries/kepler20f/ Kepler-20f - NASA]</ref>。而它們的規模更與地球非常相近：e的半徑為地球半徑的0.868倍<ref name="kepler20e"/>，而f的半徑則為地球半徑的1.034倍<ref name="kepler20f"/>，這顯示e和f都可能是一個[[超級地球|超級地球]]。<ref>[http://www.nasa.gov/mission_pages/kepler/news/kepler-20-system.html NASA Discovers First Earth-size Planets Beyond Our Solar System - NASA]</ref>e是第一個被發現圍繞著類太陽恆星公轉，且規模比地球小的太陽系外行星<ref name="kepler20e"/>；而f則是至今人類已知大小最接近地球的太陽系外行星<ref name="kepler20f"/>。e和f的[[半長軸|半長軸]]分別為0.0630天文單位<ref name="kepler20e"/>和0.1370天文單位<ref name="kepler20f"/>，而因為過於接近母星，所以它們的表面溫度介乎於705 K和1040 K之間。<ref name="kepler20e"/>

===克卜勒20b、c和d===
克卜勒20b、c和d都是[[太陽系外行星|系外]][[氣體巨行星|氣體巨行星]]，它們的質量均大於地球很多：b、c和d的質量分別為地球質量的8.7倍<ref name="kepler20b">[http://kepler.nasa.gov/Mission/discoveries/kepler20b/ Kepler-20b - NASA]</ref>、16.1倍<ref name="kepler20c">[http://kepler.nasa.gov/Mission/discoveries/kepler20c/ Kepler-20c - NASA]</ref>和20.1倍<ref name="kepler20d">[http://kepler.nasa.gov/Mission/discoveries/kepler20d/ Kepler-20d - NASA]</ref>。而它們的規模則介乎於地球和[[海王星|海王星]]之間：b、c和d的半徑分別為地球半徑的1.91倍<ref name="kepler20b"/>、3.07倍<ref name="kepler20c"/>和2.75倍<ref name="kepler20d"/>。它們的大小均比海王星（半徑為地球半徑的3.883倍）小，但比地球大，因此被歸類為[[迷你海王星|迷你海王星]]。<ref>[http://iopscience.iop.org/0004-637X/749/1/15 KEPLER-20: A SUN-LIKE STAR WITH THREE SUB-NEPTUNE EXOPLANETS AND TWO EARTH-SIZE CANDIDATES - IOP science]</ref>b是至今被發現體積最大，且密度接近地球密度的系外行星<ref name="kepler20b"/>。b、c和d的[[半長軸|半長軸]]分別為0.04537天文單位<ref name="kepler20b"/>、0.0930天文單位<ref name="kepler20c"/>和0.3453天文單位<ref name="kepler20d"/>，而因為過於接近母星，所以它們的表面溫度最高能達5,466 K<ref name="kepler20b"/>。而因為其表面溫度如此高<ref>[http://www.cosis.net/abstracts/EGS02/04469/EGS02-A-04469.pdf Hot Neptunes: A Key To Giant Planet Formation]</ref><ref>[http://www.cosis.net/abstracts/EGU2008/07657/EGU2008-A-07657.pdf Oligarchic formation of hot Neptunes]</ref>，所以它們被歸類為[[熱海王星|熱海王星]]。<ref>{{citation |url=http://www.centauri-dreams.org/?p=1263 |title=Transiting 'Hot Neptune' Found |date=2007-05-16 |publisher=[[Tau_Zero_Foundation|Centauri Dreams]]}}</ref><ref>[http://arxiv.org/abs/1112.4514 Kepler-20: A Sun-like Star with Three Sub-Neptune Exoplanets and Two Earth-size Candidates - Cornell University Library]</ref>
