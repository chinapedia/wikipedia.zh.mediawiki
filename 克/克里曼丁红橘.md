'''克里曼丁红橘'''（[[学名|学名]]：''Citrus ×clementina''）是一种[[柑橘|柑橘]]类水果，由地中海红橘（''Citrus ×deliciosa''）或[[橘子|橘子]]与[[甜橙|甜橙]]杂交而成<ref name=genealogy>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1038/nbt.2906 | volume=32 | title=Sequencing of diverse mandarin, pummelo and orange genomes reveals complex history of admixture during citrus domestication | journal=Nature Biotechnology | pages=656–662 | pmid=24908277 | pmc=4113729}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1007/s00122-006-0255-9 | volume=112 | title=Assessing genetic diversity and population structure in a citrus germplasm collection utilizing simple sequence repeat markers (SSRs) | journal=Theoretical and Applied Genetics | pages=1519–1531}}</ref>，命名于1902年。<ref name="Edible">{{cite book
|title=Edible: An Illustrated Guide to the World's Food Plants|publisher=[[National_Geographic_(magazine)|National Geographic]] |page=73 | year=2008 |isbn=978-1-4262-0372-5 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=HORIzBx17DYC&pg=PA73#v=onepage&q&f=false}}</ref>其呈橙色、果面光滑，囊瓣7至14瓣。果皮十分易剥，与[[瓯柑|瓯柑]]类似。商业化生产的克里曼丁红橘皆为无籽品种，故有时被看作无籽瓯柑。其果肉则汁多、味甜。.<ref name="Edible"/>而精油则与其他柑橘相似，以[[柠檬烯|柠檬烯]]为主，同时还含有[[香叶烯|香叶烯]]、[[芳樟醇|芳樟醇]]、[[α-蒎烯|α-蒎烯]]等成分。<ref name="Ziegler">{{cite book |title=Flavourings: production, composition, applications, regulations |page=203 |author=Ziegler, Herta |year=2007 | publisher= Wiley, John & Sons, Incorporated | isbn=978-3-527-31406-5|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=9J8USxfwkT0C&lpg=PA201&dq=clementines&pg=PA203#v=onepage}}</ref>
