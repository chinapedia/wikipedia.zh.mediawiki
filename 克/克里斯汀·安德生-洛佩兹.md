'''克里斯汀·安德生-洛佩兹'''（{{lang-en|'''Kristen Anderson-Lopez'''}}）是一名[[美国|美国]]女配音员和歌曲创作人<ref name=imdb>{{cite web|title=Kristen Anderson-Lopez|url=http://www.imdb.com/name/nm2844448/|work=[[IMDB|IMDB]]|accessdate=13 February 2012}}</ref><ref name=TIME>{{cite web|url=http://entertainment.time.com/2014/03/02/oscars-frozen-egot/|title=EGOT! Frozen's Robert Lopez on Scoring His Oscar|author=Lily Rothman|publisher=[[Time_(magazine)|Time]]|date=02-03-2014|accessdate=02-03-2014}}</ref>。

== 事业 ==
克里斯汀与其丈夫[[罗伯特·洛佩兹|罗伯特·洛佩兹]]以及[[Henry_Jackman|Henry Jackman]]三人一起创作了2011年迪士尼电影{{引书号转换|小熊維尼大電影}}（''Winnie the Pooh''）的音乐<ref name=imdbpoohfull>{{cite web|title=Full cast and crew for Winnie the Pooh (2011)|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1449283/fullcredits|work=[[IMDB|IMDB]]|accessdate=13 February 2012}}</ref>，并获得[[安妮奖|安妮奖]]最佳动画音乐提名<ref name=AnnieLAT>{{cite news|last=King|first=Susan|title='Rango' wins the Annie Award for animated feature|url=http://latimesblogs.latimes.com/movies/2012/02/rango-wins-annie-award-best-animated-feature.html#more|accessdate=13 February 2012|newspaper=[[Los_Angeles_Times|Los Angeles Times]]|date='Rango' wins the Annie Award for animated feature}}</ref><ref name=AnnieTHR>{{cite news|last=Giardina|first=Carolyn|title='Rango' Wins Annie Award for Best Animated Feature|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/race/annie-awards-rango-jennifer-yuh-nelson-287137|accessdate=13 February 2012|newspaper=[[The_Hollywood_Reporter|The Hollywood Reporter]]|date=February 4, 2012}}</ref>，她也为片中角色Kanga配音<ref name="imdbpooh">{{cite web|title=Winnie the Pooh (2011)|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1449283/|work=[[IMDB|IMDB]]|accessdate=13 February 2012}}</ref>。另外，也为音乐剧《[[海底總動員|海底總動員]]》写歌<ref name=findingnemo>{{cite web|title=Finding Nemo – The Musical  Entertainment at Disney's Animal Kingdom® Park|url=http://disneyworld.disney.go.com/parks/animal-kingdom/entertainment/finding-nemo-the-musical/|work=[[Walt_Disney_World|Walt Disney World]]|accessdate=13 February 2012}}</ref>。2013年她与丈夫共同为迪士尼动画《[[冰雪奇缘|冰雪奇缘]]》谱写主题曲《[[Let_It_Go_(迪士尼歌曲)|Let It Go]]》<ref>[http://insidemovies.ew.com/2012/06/11/disneys-frozen-kristen-bell-and-idina-menzel-to-voice-new-animated-tale/ Disney's 'Frozen': Kristen Bell and Idina Menzel to voice animated tale] Retrieved December 1, 2013</ref>，赢得第86届[[奥斯卡最佳原创歌曲奖|奥斯卡最佳原创歌曲奖]]。<ref name=TIME />

目前她正与丈夫和[[Alex_Timbers|Alex Timbers]]从事一个爱情音乐剧<ref name=UpHerePlaybill>{{cite news|last=Hetrick|first=Adam|title=Robert Lopez, Kristen Anderson-Lopez and Alex Timbers Developing Romantic Musical Up Here|url=http://www.playbill.com/news/article/155969-Robert-Lopez-Kristen-Anderson-Lopez-and-Alex-Timbers-Developing-Romantic-Musical-Up-Here|accessdate=13 February 2012|newspaper=[[Playbill|Playbill]]|date=27 Oct 2011|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120130102633/http://www.playbill.com/news/article/155969-Robert-Lopez-Kristen-Anderson-Lopez-and-Alex-Timbers-Developing-Romantic-Musical-Up-Here|archivedate=2012年1月30日}}</ref>，以及迪士尼音乐剧{{引书号转换|''Bob the Musical''}}<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.playbill.com/news/article/186324-Allan-Loeb-Enlisted-for-Disneys-Bob-the-Musical-Featuring-a-Score-by-Robert-Lopez-and-Kristen-Anderson-Lopez?tsrc=hph |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2014-03-03 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140109223814/http://www.playbill.com/news/article/186324-Allan-Loeb-Enlisted-for-Disneys-Bob-the-Musical-Featuring-a-Score-by-Robert-Lopez-and-Kristen-Anderson-Lopez?tsrc=hph |archivedate=2014-01-09 }}</ref>的相关工作。

==个人生活==
她丈夫为三次[[托尼奖|托尼奖]]得主<ref>{{cite web|title=2004 Tony Award Winners|url=http://www2.broadwayworld.com/tonyawardsyear.cfm?year=2004|work=BroadwayWorld.com|accessdate=13 February 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=|first=|title=Who's Nominated? - All Categories |url= http://www.tonyawards.com/en_US/nominees/index.html|work=tonyawards.com|accessdate=3 May 2011| date= May 3, 2011}}</ref>、作曲家和作词人[[罗伯特·洛佩兹|罗伯特·洛佩兹]]，两人于2003年结婚，兩個女兒凱蒂（Katie）和安妮（Annie）都曾在《[[冰雪奇缘|冰雪奇缘]]》中献声唱歌<ref name=usatoday_frozen>{{cite web|last=Alexander|first=Bryan|title='Frozen': Family affair for married songwriters|url=http://www.usatoday.com/story/life/movies/2013/11/25/robert-and-kristen-anderson-lopez/3652505/|publisher=USA Today|accessdate=4 December 2013}}</ref>。
