'''克雷格·大衛'''（{{lang-en|Craig David}}，全名Craig Ashley David，{{bd|1981年|5月5日||}}），[[英國|英國]]流行[[歌手|歌手]]。他擅唱[[節奏藍調|節奏藍調]]（R&B）[[音樂|音樂]]。他在1999年出道至今，共發行了5張[[錄音室專輯|錄音室專輯]]：《Born to Do It》、《Slicker Than Your Average》、《The Story Goes...》、《Trust Me》和《Signed Sealed Delivered》。

== 生平 ==
=== 早年生活 ===
大卫生于[[漢普郡|漢普郡]][[南安普敦|南安普敦]]霍利鲁德，<ref>[http://homepage.ntlworld.com/alan.stuart/music/craig/david.html The A to Z of Craig David] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130820210115/http://homepage.ntlworld.com/alan.stuart/music/craig/david.html |date=2013-08-20 }}</ref>其母为蒂娜（Tina，原名Loftus），Superdrug的销售助理，其父为乔治·大卫，一名木工。<ref name="timesref">{{Cite news|last=Cowan|first=Amber|coauthors=|title=I'm hungry for love . . .|pages=|publisher=Times Online|date=2005-08-20|url=http://women.timesonline.co.uk/tol/life_and_style/women/body_and_soul/article556682.ece|accessdate=2010-03-25}}</ref><ref name="latimesref">{{Cite news|last=Stucliffe|first=Phil|coauthors=|title=Will We Dance to His Tunes?|pages=|publisher=Los Angeles Times|date=2001-04-15|url=http://articles.latimes.com/2001/apr/15/entertainment/ca-51090|accessdate=2010-03-25}}</ref>大卫的父亲来自[[格林纳达|格林纳达]]，其母是英裔[[犹太人|犹太人]]。<ref name="jcref">{{Cite news|last=Lester |first=Paul |coauthors= |title=A Star Called David |pages= |publisher=[[The_Jewish_Chronicle|The Jewish Chronicle]] |date=2008-02-01 |url=http://website.thejc.com/home.aspx?AId=57854&ATypeId=1&search=true2&srchstr=loftus&srchtxt=0&srchhead=1&srchauthor=0&srchsandp=0&scsrch=0 |accessdate=2010-03-25 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111005064416/http://website.thejc.com/home.aspx?AId=57854&ATypeId=1&search=true2&srchstr=loftus&srchtxt=0&srchhead=1&srchauthor=0&srchsandp=0&scsrch=0 |archivedate=2011-10-05 }}</ref>大卫的双亲在他8岁时离异，他由母亲抚养长大。<ref name="mirrorref">{{Cite news|last=Salt|first=Henry|coauthors=|title=Article: Interview - Craig David: Careless Wispas|pages=|publisher=The Mirror|date=2002-11-02|url=http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1G1-94068819.html|accessdate=2010-03-25|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121103113827/http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1G1-94068819.html|archive-date=2012-11-03|dead-url=yes}}</ref>

大卫的父亲在一个名叫“Ebony Rockers”的[[雷鬼乐|雷鬼乐隊]]里担任贝斯手。大卫在小时候曾经随父亲一起前往当地的舞厅，并首次登台。
