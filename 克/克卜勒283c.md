'''克卜勒283c'''（'''Kepler-283c'''）是一顆環繞[[恒星光谱|K型恆星]][[克卜勒283|克卜勒283]]的[[太陽系外行星|太陽系外行星]]。

== 概要 ==
克卜勒283c的半徑為地球的1.82倍，屬於[[超級地球|超級地球]]。它的軌道週期93日，位於母恆星的[[適居帶|適居帶]]內<ref>[http://exoplanetarchive.ipac.caltech.edu/cgi-bin/DisplayOverview/nph-DisplayOverview?objname=Kepler-283+c&type=CONFIRMED_PLANET Kepler-283c, NASA Exoplanet Archive.] NASA Exoplanet Science Institute</ref><ref>[http://www.space.com/25528-most-earthlike-alien-planets-ranking.html 5 Exoplanets Most Likely to Host Alien Life]</ref>。克卜勒283c的表面溫度大約是{{convert|238.5|K|C}}<ref>[http://exoplanetarchive.ipac.caltech.edu/cgi-bin/DisplayOverview/nph-DisplayOverview?objname=K01298.02&type=KEPLER_CANDIDATE KO1298.02, NASA Exoplanet Archive.] NASA Exoplanet Science Institute</ref>。{{link-en|波多黎各大學阿雷西博分校|University of Puerto Rico at Arecibo}}的行星適居性實驗室（Planetary Habitability Laboratory）已不再將克卜勒283c收入該實驗室的適居系外行星目錄<ref>[http://phl.upr.edu/projects/habitable-exoplanets-catalog Habitable Exoplanets Catelog, Planetary Habitability Laboratory.] University of Puerto Rico at Arecibo</ref>
