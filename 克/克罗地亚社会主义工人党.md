'''克罗地亚社会主义工人党'''（{{lang-hr|Socijalistička radnička partija Hrvatske}}，缩写为{{lang|hr|SRP}}）是[[克罗地亚|克罗地亚]]的一个[[共产主义|共产主义]]政党。该党被认为是目前克罗地亚[[左翼|左翼]]政党的代表。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nacional.hr/articles/view/11031/|title=RH će biti zemlja 4 milijuna staraca i još toliko Europljana s vikendicama|language=hr|trans_title=Croatia will be a country of 4 million elderly and as many Europeans with holiday homes|publisher=Nacional (weekly)|author=Robert Bajruši|date=22 June 2004|accessdate=30 June 2012|archivedate=2012-06-30|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/68oYMGBsl?url=http://www.nacional.hr/clanak/11031/rh-ce-biti-zemlja-4-milijuna-staraca-i-jos-toliko-europljana-s-vikendicama}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.bhdani.com/arhiva/258/intervju.shtml |title=Greška 404 - Dani |publisher=Bhdani.com |date= |accessdate=2013-10-20 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130518113250/http://bhdani.com/arhiva/258/intervju.shtml |archive-date=2013-05-18 |dead-url=yes }}</ref> 该党内部有一个名为“[[工人斗争_(克罗地亚)|工人斗争]]”的团体，是[[第四国际|第四国际]]常驻观察员。
