'''克里斯蒂安·佩措尔德'''（Christian Petzold，{{bd|1960年|9月14日}}）是一名[[德国|德国]]导演和编剧，生于[[希爾登|希爾登]]。

== 作品 ==
=== 电影 ===
* 2000：《{{link-de|心的居所|Die innere Sicherheit}}》(Die innere Sicherheit)
* 2003：《{{link-de|蝴蝶振翅之後|Wolfsburg (Film)}}》(Wolfsburg)
* 2005：《{{link-de|柏林遊魂|Gespenster (Film)}}》(Gespenster)
* 2007：《{{link-de|耶萊|Yella (Film)}}》(Yella)
* 2008：《{{link-de|愛在愛情空窗期|Jerichow (Film)}}》(Jerichow)
* 2012：《[[芭芭拉|為愛出走]]》(Barbara)
* 2014：《{{link-de|回不去的時光|Phoenix (Film)}}》(Phoenix)

=== 电视 ===
* 1995: ''Pilotinnen'' (With [[Eleonore_Weisgerber|Eleonore Weisgerber]], [[Nadeshda_Brennicke|Nadeshda Brennicke]], [[Udo_Schenk|Udo Schenk]], Annedore von Donop, Barbara Frey)
* 1996: ''Cuba Libre'' (With [[Richy_Müller|Richy Müller]], [[Marquard_Bohm|Marquard Bohm]], Eleonore Weisgerber)
* 1998: ''[[Die_Beischlafdiebin|Die Beischlafdiebin]]'' (With [[Constanze_Engelbrecht|Constanze Engelbrecht]], Richy Müller, Wolfram Berger)
* 2002: [[Something_to_remind_me|Something to remind me]] (German: ''Toter Mann'') (Scenario Jean-Baptiste Filleau, with Nina Hoss, [[André_Hennicke|André Hennicke]], [[Sven_Pippig|Sven Pippig]], [[Kathrin_Angerer|Kathrin Angerer]])
