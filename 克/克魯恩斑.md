'''克魯恩斑'''<ref group=note>傳統的伊拉克[[曼德語族|曼德語]]（已滅絕），英文的發音接近{{IPAc-en|'|k|r|u:|n}}；或現代的伊朗曼德語，英文的發音接近{{IPAc-en|'|k|r|V|n}}。</ref>是冥王星暗黑色[[冥王星地理#指節套環|指節套環]]系列中最西邊的一節。它的名字源自[[:en:Krun|Krun]]<!-- {{IPA-mid|kʰrʌn|}} - might be [u], checking with native speakers-->，是[[曼德語族|曼德語族]]冥府最大的五位惡魔之首腦<ref>{{cite web|last1=Chang|first1=Kenneth|title=NASA’s New Horizons Spacecraft Completes Flyby of Pluto|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2015/07/15/science/space/nasa-new-horizons-spacecraft-reaches-pluto.html|publisher=The New York Times|accessdate=14 July 2015|date=14 July 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Rogers|first1=Adam|title=The New, Nerdy Mythology of Pluto's Place Names|url=http://www.wired.com/2015/07/new-nerdy-mythology-plutos-place-names/|publisher=Wired|accessdate=14 July 2015|date=14 July 2015}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title = Mandaeans on Pluto|url = http://www.ancienthistorymagazine.com/pw/ancient-history-magazine/blog/mandaeans-on-pluto/|website = Ancient History Magazine|accessdate = 2015-07-23|last = Lendering|first = Jona}}</ref>。

[[File:Krun_on_Pluto.jpg|thumb]]東葉的東南。]]
克魯恩斑是繼[[克蘇魯區|克蘇魯區]]和[[炎魔斑|炎魔斑]]之後第三大的暗區。它延伸到接近西經180度附近，也就是冥王星上正對著[[冥衛一|Charon]]的背面。
