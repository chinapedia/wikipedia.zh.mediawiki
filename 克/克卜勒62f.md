'''克卜勒62f'''<ref name="Borucki" /><ref name="NASA-20130418" /><ref name="NYT-20130418"> {{cite news |last=Overbye |first=Dennis |title=2 Good Places to Live, 1,200 Light-Years Away|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2013/04/19/science/space/2-new-planets-are-most-earth-like-yet-scientists-say.html|date=18 April 2013 |publisher=[[New_York_Times|New York Times]] |accessdate=18 April 18 }}</ref>（{{lang en|'''Kepler-62f'''}}），亦稱'''KOI-701.04'''，是一顆環繞[[天琴座|天琴座]]恆星[[克卜勒62|克卜勒62]]的[[太陽系外行星|系外]][[超級地球|超級地球]]，是已知距離母恆星最遠的行星，由[[NASA|NASA]]的[[克卜勒太空望遠鏡|克卜勒太空望遠鏡]]以偵測行星通過恆星前方造成亮度下降的凌日法發現。<ref>[http://arxiv.org/abs/1304.5058 Water Planets in the Habitable Zone: Atmospheric Chemistry, Observable Features, and the case of Kepler-62e and -62f], L. Kaltenegger, D. Sasselov, S. Rugheimer, 18 Apr 2013</ref>它很可能是一顆位於[[適居帶|適居帶]]中的[[類地行星|類地行星]]<ref name = "Borucki">{{Cite journal | last=Borucki | first=William J. | authorlink=威廉·博魯奇 | coauthors = ''et al.'' | title=Kepler-62: A Five-Planet System with Planets of 1.4 and 1.6 Earth Radii in the Habitable Zone | journal=Science Express| date=18 April 2013 | url = http://www.sciencemag.org/content/early/2013/04/17/science.1234702 |doi=10.1126/science.1234702 | accessdate=18 April 2013}}</ref><ref name="spacecomkepler62f">[http://www.space.com/24142-kepler-62f.html Kepler-62f: A Possible Water World]</ref><ref name="space.com kepler">[http://www.space.com/20723-earth-like-alien-planets-habitable-zone-infographic.html 3 Potentially Habitable 'Super-Earths' Explained (Infographic)]</ref>。

==被證實的太陽系外行星和主恆星==
作為一顆超級地球，克卜勒62f的半徑是地球半徑的1.4倍。<ref name="Borucki" />這顆行星所環繞的恆星，克卜勒62，是一顆比太陽略小和略冷的恆星。至今，人類已發現五個克卜勒62行星，而克卜勒62f就是位於最外圍的行星。<ref name="Borucki" />其母星克卜勒62的[[視星等|視星等]]為13.8，無法以肉眼觀察。而從克卜勒62f觀看克卜勒62的話，則恆星的大小約為地球上太陽大小的90%。<ref name="Borucki" />

==恆星狀況==
克卜勒62的[[恆星光譜|恆星光譜]]分類為K2V，質量為太陽質量的0.69倍，而半徑則是太陽半徑的0.62倍。其[[有效溫度|有效溫度]]為4925 ± 70 [[開氏溫標|K]]，年齡大約是70 ± 40億年<ref name=Borucki />。相較之下，太陽年齡為是45.7億年<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.universetoday.com/18237/how-old-is-the-sun/ |title=How Old is the Sun? |author=Fraser Cain |date=16 September 2008 |work= |publisher=Universe Today |accessdate=19 February 2011}}</ref>，有效溫度為5778 K<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.universetoday.com/18092/temperature-of-the-sun/ |title=Temperature of the Sun |author=Fraser Cain |date=15 September 2008 |work= |publisher=Universe Today |accessdate=19 February 2011}}</ref>。

==行星特質==
克卜勒62f距離地球約{{convert|1200|ly|pc|abbr=on}}，其半徑為地球半徑的1.41 ± 0.07倍，半長軸為0.718 [[天文單位|AU]]。這顆行星可能是一顆位於適居帶中的類地行星<ref name="Borucki"/>，並且很可能是一顆超級地球。其[[地球相似指數#高相似指数行星|地球相似指數]]為0.69，比[[火星|火星]]（0.66）還要高。<ref name=esi>{{cite web|title=Earth Similarity Index (ESI)|url=http://phl.upr.edu/projects/earth-similarity-index-esi|publisher=Planetary Habitability Laboratory}}</ref>

[[File:Relative_sizes_of_all_of_the_habitable-zone_planets_discovered_to_date_alongside_Earth.jpg|thumb]]、[[克卜勒62e|克卜勒62e]]、克卜勒62f和地球的體積比較。]] 

根據行星的年齡（70 ± 40億年）、[[辐射通量|辐射通量]]（地球的0.41 ± 0.05倍）和半徑（地球的1.41 ± 0.07倍），它可能是由岩石組成，並且部分表面被海洋覆蓋<ref name = "Borucki"/>。已被《[[天文物理期刊|天文物理期刊]]》接受的論文指出根據模型，它可能完全被海洋覆蓋<ref name = "gazette">{{cite web
 | title = Water worlds surface: Planets covered by global ocean with no land in sight
 | work = | publisher = [[哈佛大學報|Harvard Gazette]]
 | date = 2013-04-18
 | url = http://news.harvard.edu/gazette/story/2013/04/water-worlds-surface/
 | accessdate = 2013-04-19}}</ref>。如果它的密度和地球相同，它的質量可能是地球的1.41<sup>3</sup>或2.80倍。

== 文化影響 ==
於2013年5月9日，一個由兩位[[美国众议院|美国众议院]]小組委員會因天文學家們發現了克卜勒62f，而舉辦的國會聽證會討論了「太陽系外行星的發現：我們是否有其他的地球？」（{{lang|en|Exoplanet Discoveries: Have We Found Other Earths?}}）這個議題。<ref>[https://science.house.gov/hearing/subcommittee-space-and-subcommittee-research-joint-hearing-exoplanet-discoveries-have-we Subcommittee on Space and Subcommittee on Research Joint Hearing - Exoplanet Discoveries: Have We Found Other Earths?] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141207205111/https://science.house.gov/hearing/subcommittee-space-and-subcommittee-research-joint-hearing-exoplanet-discoveries-have-we |date=2014-12-07 }}</ref>《[[科學_(期刊)|科學]]》亦同時出版了一個關於發現系外行星的專刊。<ref name="SCI-20130503">{{cite journal |authors=Staff |title=Special Issue: Exoplanets |url=http://www.sciencemag.org/site/special/exoplanets/ |date=May 3, 2013 |journal=[[Science_(journal)|Science]] |accessdate=May 18, 2013 }}</ref>[[搜寻地外文明计划|搜寻地外文明计划]]也將克卜勒62f和其他克卜勒62行星列為候選外星生命家園之一。<ref name="Kepler-62 SETI">{{cite web |title=Has Kepler Found Ideal SETI-target Planets? |url=http://www.seti.org/seti_kepler_62 |date=19 April 2013 |work=[[SETI_Institute|SETI Institute]] |accessdate=17 September 2013 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131029212645/http://www.seti.org/seti_kepler_62 |archive-date=2013-10-29 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>
