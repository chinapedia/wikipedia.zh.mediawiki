'''马赞德兰语'''（مَزِروني），亦称：'''塔巴里语'''，属[[印度－伊朗语族|印度－伊朗语族]]西北伊朗语支，主要使用在[[伊朗|伊朗]][[马赞德兰省|马赞德兰省]]和[[戈勒斯坦省|戈勒斯坦省]]。与[[吉拉基语|吉拉基语]]、[[扎扎其語|扎扎其語]]，和[[俾路支语|俾路支语]]关系紧密。与[[波斯语|波斯语]]也有较远的关系。塔巴里语使用者大约四百多万人。<ref>Coon, "Iran:Demography and Ethnography" in Encyclopedia of Islam, Volume IV, E.J. Brill, pp. 10,8. Excerpt: "The Lurs speak an aberrant form of Archaic Persian" See maps also on page 10 for distribution of Persian languages and dialect</ref><ref>Kathryn M. Coughlin, "Muslim cultures today: a reference guide," Greenwood Publishing Group, 2006. p. 89: "...Iranians speak Persian or a Persian dialect such as Gilaki or Mazanderani"</ref>

==歷史==
馬贊德蘭語是現存的[[伊朗語支|伊朗語支]]諸語中書寫歷史最長一個，可以追溯到10至15世紀。其原因是[[阿拉伯人|阿拉伯人]]入侵之後，[[馬贊德蘭|馬贊德蘭]]獨立及半獨立統治者的統治。<ref>Windfuhr, G. L. 1989. New Iranian languages: Overview. In Rüdiger Schmitt, ed., ''Compendium linguarum Iranicarum''. Wiesbaden: L. Reichert. pp. 246–249.</ref>

用馬贊德蘭語書寫的文獻有《[[Marzban_Nameh|Marzban Nameh]]》和詩歌《Amir Pazevari》等。不過後來因為[[波斯人|波斯人]]的強勢，其使用者便漸漸減少了。<ref name="M. Borjian 2005">Borjian, Maryam. 2005. [http://www.tc.columbia.edu/students/sie/LCEjr05/pdfs/Borjian.pdf Bilingualism in Mazandaran: Peaceful Coexistence With Persian]  {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20060921001601/http://www.tc.columbia.edu/students/sie/LCEjr05/pdfs/Borjian.pdf |date=2006-09-21 }}. ''Language, Communities and Education''.  ''Languages, Communities & Education: A Volume of Graduate Student Research''. New York: [http://www.tc.edu/students/sie/LCEjr05/home.htm Society for International Education] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110727230834/http://www.tc.edu/students/sie/LCEjr05/home.htm |date=2011-07-27 }}, Teachers College, [[Columbia_University|Columbia University]]. pp. 65–73.</ref>
