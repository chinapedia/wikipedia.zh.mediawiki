'''马赫尔·阿卜杜勒-哈菲兹·哈贾尔'''（{{lang-ar|ماهر عبد الحفيظ حجار}}，{{bd|1968年||}}），是一名[[叙利亚|叙利亚]]政治家、国会议员，[[叙利亚人民意志党|叙利亚人民意志党]]人物，曾经竞选[[2014年敘利亞總統選舉|2014年敘利亞總統選舉]]<ref>{{cite news|title=Maher Abdul-Hafiz Hajjar Registers As First Presidential Candidate For Syria Election|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2014/04/23/syria-presidential-candidate_n_5197605.html|newspaper=[[Huffington_Post|Huffington Post]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Assad to face 2 others in Syrian presidential poll|url=http://www.utsandiego.com/news/2014/May/04/report-rebel-infighting-in-eastern-syria-kills-62/|newspaper=[[Associated_Press|Associated Press]]|date=4 May 2014}}</ref>。

他出生于一个穆斯林[[逊尼派|逊尼派]]家庭，毕业于{{le|阿勒颇大学|Aleppo University}}<ref>{{Cite news|title = MP Maher Abdul-Hafiz Hajjar Announces Candidacy for President|date = 24 April 2014|url = http://syrianobserver.com/News/News/MP+Maher+AbdulHafiz+Hajjar+Announces+Candidacy+for+President|authorlink = Syrian Observer|deadurl = yes|archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140505004426/http://syrianobserver.com/News/News/MP+Maher+AbdulHafiz+Hajjar+Announces+Candidacy+for+President|archivedate = 2014年5月5日}}</ref>。他曾经在1984年，参加叙利亚欧诺共产党，之后退出，加入2000年的人民意志党<ref>{{Cite news|title = Syria lawmaker becomes first presidential challenger|date = 23 April 2014|url = http://www.dailystar.com.lb/News/Middle-East/2014/Apr-23/254125-syria-lawmaker-becomes-first-presidential-challenger-tv.ashx#axzz30mOJ92h2|authorlink = Daily Star (Lebanon)}}</ref>。2007年，曾经参加叙利亚国会选举，但是落选。2012年再次竞选，并且获得席位。2014年，参加敘利亞總統選舉<ref>{{Cite news|title = Assad loyalists take up 'challenge’ in Syrian election race|date = 24 April 2014|url = http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/worldnews/middleeast/syria/10786543/Assad-loyalists-take-up-challenge-in-Syrian-election-race.html|authorlink = |publisher = The Telegraph}}</ref>，以无党派身份参加，获得3.2%的选票<ref>[http://kassiounpaper.com/politics1/item/9752-2014-04-27-06-50-10 تصريح ناطق رسمي باسم حزب الإرادة الشعبية (Ar)], ''Kassioun'', 27 April 2014</ref>。
