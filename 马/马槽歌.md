《'''马槽歌'''》（{{lang-en|"Away in a Manger"}}，或作《马槽圣婴》，或依歌词首句作《远远在马槽裡》）是[[英语世界|英语世界]]广泛使用的一首[[聖誕頌歌|聖誕頌歌]]，曲调轻柔优美。{{tsl|en|William_J._Kirkpatrick|威廉·柯克帕特里克}}作曲<ref name="中文歌词">{{Cite web |url = http://cclw.net/song/Christmas.htm |title = 圣诞类 |author= cclw.net |date = |publisher = cclw.net |language = zh-hans |accessdate = 2013-08-01 |quote= 《马槽歌》……译配 }}</ref>，该曲首次出版于19世纪末<ref name="Away in a Manger">[http://www.christmas-carols.org.uk/christmas-carols/away-in-a-manger Away in a Manger]{{dead link|date=2018年2月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}. Christmas-Carols.org.uk. Accessed 7th December 2009.</ref>。前两节的歌词于1884年5月首次刊登在一家[[波士顿|波士顿]]期刊《The Myrtle》上<ref name="myrtle">{{cite book |title=The Myrtle, vol. xxxiv no. 1 |publisher = Universalist Publishing House |location=Boston, MA |year=1884 |pages=6 |url = http://books.google.com/books?id=_3gpAAAAYAAJ}}</ref>，而第三节歌词在1892年时第一次印刷于《Gabriel's Vineyard Songs》上<ref>See the story in ''Our Hymnody: a manual of the Methodist hymnal'' (New York, etc., The Methodist Book Concern, 1937, p. 436)</ref>。
