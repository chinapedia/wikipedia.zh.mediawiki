'''马本'''是[[缅甸|缅甸]][[掸邦|掸邦]]北部的一个镇。它坐落于[[瑞丽江|瑞丽江]]，与[[克钦邦|克钦邦]]的城镇[[孟密|孟密]]、[[八莫|八莫]]和[[密支那|密支那]]道路互通。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.modins.net/myanmarinfo/media/transport.htm|title=Transport in Myanmar|publisher=MODiNS.Net|accessdate=2009-03-13}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://mission.itu.ch/MISSIONS/Myanmar/02nlm/n020326.htm#(%204%20)|title=Maj-Gen Ye Myint inspects regional development tasks|publisher=''[[New_Light_of_Myanmar|New Light of Myanmar]]''|date=26 March 2002|accessdate=2009-03-13}}{{dead link|date=2018年2月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>马本与中国云南省的边境仅{{convert|90|km}}，但距[[曼德勒|曼德勒]]北部约{{convert|200|km}}。<ref name=USGS>{{Cite web|url=http://minerals.usgs.gov/minerals/pubs/country/2004/bmmyb04.pdf|author=Yolanda Fong-Sam|title=The Mineral Industry of Burma|publisher=[[United_States_Geological_Survey|United States Geological Survey]] Minerals Yearbook — 2004|accessdate=2009-03-13}}</ref>

==经济==
在瑞丽江流域，马本镇是一个主要的大米产地。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.mizzima.com/news/inside-burma/623-regional-commander-bans-rice-export-to-china.html|author=Myo Gyi|title=Regional commander bans rice export to China|publisher=''[[Mizzima_News|Mizzima News]]''|date=3 June 2008|accessdate=2009-03-13|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090429042250/http://www.mizzima.com/news/inside-burma/623-regional-commander-bans-rice-export-to-china.html|archivedate=29 四月 2009}}</ref>从马本镇周围林地开采的[[柚木|柚木]]被列为中等品。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.teakboat.com/teak_log_grading.htm|title=Teak log Grading|publisher=teakboat.com|accessdate=2009-03-13|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20051220024305/http://www.teakboat.com/teak_log_grading.htm|archivedate=2005-12-20}}</ref>
在2002年的鸦片调查中，[[马本镇区|马本镇区]]被[[联合国国际麻醉品管制署|联合国国际麻醉品管制署]](UNDCP)确认全区禁毒，这一结果被[[联合国毒品和犯罪问题办公室|联合国毒品和犯罪问题办公室]](UNODC)发表在了2005年度[[世界毒品报告|世界毒品报告]]上。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.shaps.hawaii.edu/drugs/myanmar/2002/|title=Myanmar Central Committee for Drug Abuse Control: Opium Survey 2002|work=[[United_Nations_International_Drug_Control_Programme|UNDCP]]|accessdate=2009-03-13|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080327030550/http://www.shaps.hawaii.edu/drugs/myanmar/2002/|archivedate=2008-03-27}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.unodc.org/pdf/WDR_2005/volume_2_chap5_opium.pdf|title=Myanmar Shan State* Opium Poppy Cultivation 2003-2004|publisher=[[World_Drug_Report|World Drug Report]] 2005 Vol 2 Chap 5|pages= map 21|accessdate=2009-03-13}}</ref>

该地区也发现了有金矿床。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.freewebs.com/indawgyi/reports/golddistricts.htm#11|author=A H G Mitchell et al|title=PACRIM BERLI Seminar, November 1999: Geological Settings of Gold Districts in Myanmar|publisher=freewebs.com|accessdate=2009-03-13|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081026164736/http://www.freewebs.com/indawgyi/reports/golddistricts.htm#11|archivedate=2008-10-26}}</ref>2004年1月，[[国家和平与发展委员会|军政府]]与两家加拿大公司签署协议，允许其在Set Ga Done和Nga Mu Gyi两个区域开采金矿。<ref name=USGS/>
