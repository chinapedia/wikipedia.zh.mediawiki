== 概論 ==
印度農民自殺問題始於20世紀90年代，其中[[馬哈拉施特拉邦|馬哈拉施特拉邦]]首先出現農民自殺的個案，不久[[安得拉邦|安得拉邦]]也發生了類似事件<ref>[http://specials.rediff.com/news/2007/nov/22sld1.htm  Rediff News]</ref>。雖然印度政府未曾作出印度農民分佈的研究，但有說法指大多數農民自殺事件是發生在[[安得拉邦|安得拉邦]]、[[馬哈拉施特拉邦|馬哈拉施特拉邦]]、[[卡納塔克邦|卡納塔克邦]]、[[喀拉拉邦|喀拉拉邦]]和[[旁遮普邦|旁遮普邦]]<ref>{{cite news |title=Debts and Drought Drive India's Farmers to Despair |url= http://www.nytimes.com/2004/06/06/world/debts-and-drought-drive-india-s-farmers-to-despair.html|date=June 6, 2004|publisher=[[New_York_Times|New York Times]] |page= | first=Amy | last=Waldman | accessdate=2010-05-02}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=India acts over suicide crisis on farms |url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/asia/india-acts-over-suicide-crisis-on-farms-551762.html |date=2 July 2004 |publisher=[[The_Independent|The Independent]] |page= | location=London | first=Justin | last=Huggler | accessdate=2010-05-02}}</ref>。印度農民自殺問題頗為嚴峻，迫使了一些地方政府成立專責辦公室，處理農民自殺問題<ref name="VNSS Mission">[http://www.vnss-mission.gov.in/  VNSS Mission]</ref>。
