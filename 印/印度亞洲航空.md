'''印度亞洲航空'''（{{lang-en|AirAsia India}}；簡稱'''印度亞航'''）<ref>{{cite news|title=AirAsia incorporates company for Indian venture|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/business/india-business/AirAsia-incorporates-company-for-Indian-venture/articleshow/19303944.cms|accessdate=31 March 2013|newspaper=[[The_Times_of_India|The Times of India]]|date=31 March 2013|agency=[[Press_Trust_of_India|Press Trust of India]]|location=New Delhi}}</ref>是[[印度|印度]]的一間[[低成本航空公司|低成本航空公司]]，總部及樞紐機場皆設於[[班加羅爾|班加羅爾]]。<ref>{{cite news |url= http://www.nytimes.com/2007/12/23/travel/23prac.html?ref=yourmoney |title= Does Low Cost Mean High Risk? |date=23 December 2007 |accessdate=28 April 2010|work=The New York Times |first=Joshua |last=Kurlantzick}}</ref>於2013年2月19日宣布由三方合資成立，[[亞洲航空|亞洲航空]]持有49%股份，[[塔塔集團|塔塔集團]]持有30%，Telestra Tradeplace則持有剩餘21%股份。此合資公司成為塔塔集團60年後重回航空業的標的。<ref>{{cite web|title=AirAsia to tie up with Tata Sons for new airline in India|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/business/india-business/AirAsia-to-tie-up-with-Tata-Sons-for-new-airline-in-India/articleshow/18601005.cms|work=Times of India|accessdate=21 February 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite press release |url = http://economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/news-by-industry/transportation/airlines-/-aviation/airasia-applies-for-indian-airline-jv-with-tata-sons-and-telestra-tradeplace/articleshow/18591201.cms |title= Tata Sons, Telestra Tradeplace and Air Asia to form Air Asia India |work =Economic Times|date=20 February 2013}}</ref>2014年6月12日，印度亞航正式營運。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://profit.ndtv.com/news/corporates/article-airasia-india-tickets-on-sale-from-friday-389821?pfrom=home-lateststories |title=AirAsia India Tickets on Sale From Today - NDTVProfit.com |publisher=Profit.ndtv.com |date= |accessdate=2014-05-30}}</ref>

==機隊==
