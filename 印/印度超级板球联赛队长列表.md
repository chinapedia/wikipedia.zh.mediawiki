{{校对翻译}}
在[[板球|板球]]比赛中，队长是领导团队的一名球员，承担更多角色和责任。<ref name="AustraliaPyke2010">{{Cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=RJVCVBB1HYoC&pg=PA132|title=Cutting Edge Cricket|date=8 March 2010|publisher=[[Human_Kinetics_(publisher)|Human Kinetics]]|accessdate=18 August 2013|isbn=978-0-7360-7902-0|page=132|author1=Cricket Australia|author2=Frank Pyke|author3=Ken Davis}}</ref><ref name="DavisBuszard2011">{{Cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=l9bq_Yp9H0QC&pg=PA69|title=Cricket: 99.94 Tips to Improve Your Game|date=5 April 2011|publisher=Human Kinetics|accessdate=18 August 2013|isbn=978-0-7360-9078-0|pages=69|author1=Ken Davis|author2=Neil Buszard}}</ref>[[印度超級板球聯賽|印度超级板球联赛]]（英文缩写：IPL，简称：印超）是采行2020制进行比赛的超级联赛，<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.icc-cricket.com/about/71/icc-members/full-members/india|title=ICC Members- India|accessdate=18 August 2013|publisher=[[國際板球理事會|國際板球理事會]]|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130825041609/http://www.icc-cricket.com/about/71/icc-members/full-members/india|archivedate=2013年8月25日}}</ref>2008年，第一轮印超顺利举办，此后每年都举办一次。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://articles.economictimes.indiatimes.com/2011-01-07/news/28423450_1_player-auction-indian-premier-league-ipl-auction|title=IPL auction 2011: Teams to spend big for top cricketers|accessdate=18 August 2013|work={{tsl|en|The Economic Times||The Economic Times}}}}</ref> 在已办的6个赛季中共出现了34名队长，每支队伍至少有一名队长。<ref name="captains">{{Cite web|url=http://stats.espncricinfo.com/indian-premier-league-2012/engine/records/individual/most_matches_as_captain.html?id=117;type=trophy|title=Indian Premier League / Records / Most matches as captain|accessdate=18 August 2013|publisher={{tsl|en|ESPNcricinfo||ESPNcricinfo}}}}</ref>

板球队Chennai Super Kings的马亨德拉·辛格多尼担任队长的次数位居第一，共96次，获胜率为61.05%。<ref name="captains">{{Cite web|url=http://stats.espncricinfo.com/indian-premier-league-2012/engine/records/individual/most_matches_as_captain.html?id=117;type=trophy|title=Indian Premier League / Records / Most matches as captain|accessdate=18 August 2013|publisher={{tsl|en|ESPNcricinfo||ESPNcricinfo}}}}</ref>获胜率最高且参与过10轮比赛以上的队长是孟买印度人的罗希特夏尔马，获胜率为76.92%。<ref name="captains">{{Cite web|url=http://stats.espncricinfo.com/indian-premier-league-2012/engine/records/individual/most_matches_as_captain.html?id=117;type=trophy|title=Indian Premier League / Records / Most matches as captain|accessdate=18 August 2013|publisher={{tsl|en|ESPNcricinfo||ESPNcricinfo}}}}</ref>多尼担任队长时赢得比赛的次数最多（58次），而亚当吉尔克里斯特担任队长时输掉比赛的次数最多（39次）。<ref name="captains">{{Cite web|url=http://stats.espncricinfo.com/indian-premier-league-2012/engine/records/individual/most_matches_as_captain.html?id=117;type=trophy|title=Indian Premier League / Records / Most matches as captain|accessdate=18 August 2013|publisher={{tsl|en|ESPNcricinfo||ESPNcricinfo}}}}</ref> Mahela Jayawardene和Kumar Sangakkara担任了3支队伍的队长，数量最多。 Mahela Jayawardene是Delhi Daredevils、Kings XI Punjab和Kochi Tuskers Kerala的队长，Kumar Sangakkara是Deccan Chargers、Kings XI和Sunrisers Hyderabad。<ref name="captains">{{Cite web|url=http://stats.espncricinfo.com/indian-premier-league-2012/engine/records/individual/most_matches_as_captain.html?id=117;type=trophy|title=Indian Premier League / Records / Most matches as captain|accessdate=18 August 2013|publisher={{tsl|en|ESPNcricinfo||ESPNcricinfo}}}}</ref>

冒失鬼队的James Hopes以队长的身份参加了3次比赛，这些比赛的胜负都不得而知。其中2个比赛结果不知所踪，剩余一个[[比赛结果_(板球)|没有结果]]。<ref name="captains">{{Cite web|url=http://stats.espncricinfo.com/indian-premier-league-2012/engine/records/individual/most_matches_as_captain.html?id=117;type=trophy|title=Indian Premier League / Records / Most matches as captain|accessdate=18 August 2013|publisher={{tsl|en|ESPNcricinfo||ESPNcricinfo}}}}</ref>共6名球员担任了冒失鬼队、the Indians and the Warriors的队长，5名球员担任了Kings XI和Royal Challengers的队长；4名球员担任了Deccan Chargers的队长；3名球员担任了加尔各答骑士和拉贾斯坦邦皇家队的队长；2名球员担任了Super Kings和Tuskers的队长。<ref name="captains">{{Cite web|url=http://stats.espncricinfo.com/indian-premier-league-2012/engine/records/individual/most_matches_as_captain.html?id=117;type=trophy|title=Indian Premier League / Records / Most matches as captain|accessdate=18 August 2013|publisher={{tsl|en|ESPNcricinfo||ESPNcricinfo}}}}</ref>
