== 种下类群 ==
已有两个伯克猪笼草变种的描述：
*''Nepenthes burkei'' var. ''excellens'' Hort.[[維奇苗圃|Veitch]] ''ex'' Marshall (1890)
*''Nepenthes burkei'' var. ''prolifica'' Mast. (1890)<ref>Masters, M.T. 1890. [http://biodiversitylibrary.org/page/25924768 New or noteworthy plants. ''Nepenthes Burkeii'' Mast. var. ''prolifica''.] ''[[园丁纪事|The Gardeners' Chronicle]]'', series 3, '''8'''(190): 184.</ref>
