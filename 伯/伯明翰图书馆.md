'''伯明翰图书馆'''（{{lang|en|Library of Birmingham}}）是[[英国|英国]][[伯明翰|伯明翰]]市中心的一座[[公共图书馆|公共图书馆]]，他為[[英國|英國]]最大的公共圖書館<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.specfinish.co.uk/product-news/linder-and-prater-complete-on-new-birmingham-library/|work=SpecFinish|title=LINDER AND PRATER COMPLETE ON NEW BIRMINGHAM LIBRARY|accessdate=21 March 2014 | location=Leamington Spa | date=21 March 2014}}</ref>，歐洲最大的公共文化空間<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/books/2009/apr/03/libraries-architecture-birmingham|work=The Guardian|title=Library of Birmingham plans unveiled as recession opens a new chapter for civic buildings|accessdate=9 July 2010 | location=London | first=Robert | last=Booth | date=3 April 2009}}</ref><ref>The [[British_Library|British Library]] in London is larger, but is only open to the public by appointment</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.mecanoo.nl/tabid/217/DetailId/353/Library-of-Birmingham-on-BBC-Radio-4.aspx?img=newsletter|publisher=Mecanoo architecten|title=Library of Birmingham on BBC Radio 4|accessdate=8 May 2012 }}</ref>，歐洲最大的區域性圖書館<ref name="BBC-MoreThan">{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-england-birmingham-23814424|title=Library of Birmingham: 'It's about more than just books'|date=2013-08-30|work=[[BBC_Online|BBC Online]]|accessdate=2 September 2013}}</ref>，耗资1.888亿[[英镑|英镑]]，于2013年9月3日正式向公众开放。伯明翰图书馆接收了原[[伯明翰中心图书馆|伯明翰中心图书馆]]的所有收藏，并拥有全球规模第二大的[[莎士比亚|莎士比亚]]著作收藏。伯明翰图书馆是伯明翰市“20年大城市规划”的重要市政建设项目，图书馆及其毗邻的伯明翰REP剧院和交响音乐厅将成为该市的文化中心。<ref>{{cite web | language = zh-cn | publisher = 新华网 | title =欧洲最大公共图书馆将在伯明翰开放 | url =http://news.xinhuanet.com/world/2013-08/29/c_117144343.htm| date = 2013-08-29 | accessdate = 2013-09-08 }}</ref>
