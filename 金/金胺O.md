'''金胺O'''是一種二芳基甲烷螢光[[染料|染料]]，常態下為黃色針狀結晶。極易溶於水，易溶於[[乙醇|乙醇]]。

金胺O可用於[[抗酸|抗酸]][[細菌|細菌]]（例如''[[分支杆菌|Mycobacterium]]''）, 其方法類似[[抗酸染色|抗酸染色]]法。<ref name=Kommareddi_1984>{{cite journal |author=Kommareddi S, Abramowsky C, Swinehart G, Hrabak L |title=Nontuberculous mycobacterial infections: comparison of the fluorescent auramine-O and Ziehl-Neelsen techniques in tissue diagnosis |journal=Hum Pathol |volume=15 |issue=11 |pages=1085–9 |year=1984 |pmid=6208117 |doi=10.1016/S0046-8177(84)80253-1}}</ref> 也被當做[[希夫試劑|希夫試劑]]的螢光版來使用。<ref name=Khavkin_1980>{{cite journal |author=Khavkin T, Kudryavtseva M, Dragunskaya E, Polotsky Y, Kudryavtsev B |title=Fluorescent PAS-reaction study of the epithelium of normal rabbit ileum and after challenge with enterotoxigenic Escherichia coli |journal=Gastroenterology |volume=78 |issue=4 |pages=782–90 |year=1980 |pmid=6986320}}</ref>

在[[金胺-若丹明染色法|金胺-若丹明染色法]]中與[[若丹明B|若丹明B]]一起用來給[[結核桿菌|結核桿菌]]染色。<ref name=Truant_1962>{{cite journal |author=Truant J, Brett W, Thomas W |title=Fluorescence microscopy of tubercle bacilli stained with auramine and rhodamine |journal=Henry Ford Hosp Med Bull |volume=10 |issue= |pages=287–96 |year=1962 |pmid=13922644}}</ref><ref name=Arrowood_1989>{{cite journal |author=Arrowood M, Sterling C |title=Comparison of conventional staining methods and monoclonal antibody-based methods for Cryptosporidium oocyst detection |journal=J Clin Microbiol |volume=27 |issue=7 |pages=1490–5 |year=1989 |pmid=2475523 |pmc=267601}}</ref> 同時也可用作[[防腐劑|防腐劑]]。
