'''金巴斯集團'''（'''Compass Group plc'''）是一家總部位於英格蘭[[薩里郡|薩里郡]][[切特西|切特西]]的跨國餐飲業集團。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.compass-group.com/Talk-to-us.htm |title=Compass: Talk to us |publisher=Compass-group.com |date= |accessdate=2013-06-01 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160214215441/http://www.compass-group.com/Talk-to-us.htm |archivedate=2016-02-14 }} "Compass House Guildford Street Chertsey Surrey KT16 9BQ UK"</ref>它是世界上最大的合約制餐飲業公司，在超過50個國家運營。<ref>{{Cite news| url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/business/2009/jul/30/compass-ban-69-fish|title=Compass bans 69 endangered fish species from its restaurants|accessdate=27 August 2010|work=The Guardian |location=UK | first=James | last=Meikle | date=30 July 2009}}</ref>該集團已在[[倫敦證券交易所|倫敦證券交易所]]上市，且是[[富時100指數|富時100指數]]成分。
