'''金屬蕊'''（Metalcore）是一個[[金屬樂|金屬樂]]的子類型，結合了[[金屬樂|極端金屬]]與[[硬蕊龐克|硬蕊龐克]]。金屬蕊這個詞大約是在1990年代中期出現，由極端金屬的「金屬」與硬蕊的「蕊」組成，而這些類型樂團有[[Earth_Crisis|Earth Crisis]]、[[Deadguy|Deadguy]]、[[Integrity|Integrity]]。<ref>{{cite web |title= Shai Hulud, interview with Punknews.org - 05/28/08 |url= http://www.ruleeverymoment.com/media/interviews/interview.php?id=43 |accessdate= 2008-09-21 |quote= As far as coining the term 'metalcore' or coining a sound, I don’t think we did. There were bands before [[Shai_Hulud_(band)|Shai Hulud]] started that my friends and I were referring to as 'metalcore'. Bands like Burn, Deadguy, Earth Crisis, even Integrity. These bands that were heavier than the average hardcore bands. These bands that were more progressive than the average hardcore band. My friends and I would always refer to them as 'metalcore' because it wasn’t purely hardcore and it wasn’t purely metal. It was like a heavier hardcore band with hardcore ethics and attitude but clearly a metal influence. So we would joke around and say 'Hey, it’s metalcore. Cool!' But it was definitely a tongue-in-cheek term. |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20081016144924/http://www.ruleeverymoment.com/media/interviews/interview.php?id=43# |archive-date= 2008-10-16 |dead-url= yes }}</ref>這些早期的樂團，像是Integrity，最早在1988年表演，當時玩這類型的樂團有[[終極殺戮樂團|終極殺戮樂團]]（Killswitch Engage）、[[叛客魔咒樂團|叛客魔咒樂團]]（Underoath）、[[All_That_Remains|All That Remains]]、 [[混種魔獸樂團|混種魔獸樂團]]（Trivium）、[[As_I_Lay_Dying|As I Lay Dying]]、[[致命情人|致命情人樂團]]（Bullet for My Valentine）與[[The_Devil_Wears_Prada|The Devil Wears Prada]]。<ref name="Alternative Press">{{cite news
  | title =Blood Runs Deep: 23 Bands Who Shaped the Scene
  | pages =110, 118

==歷史==
===先驅 (1977–1984)===
{{main|硬蕊龐克}}
[[Black_Flag|Black Flag]]<ref>Blush, American Hardcore, part 2, "Thirsty and Miserable", p. 63, 66</ref>與[[Bad_Brains|Bad Brains]]<ref>Andersen, Mark and Mark Jenkins (2003). ''Dance of Days: Two Decades of Punk in the Nation's Capital''. "Positive Mental Attitude". p. 27. Akashic Books. ISBN 1-888451-44-0</ref>這些硬蕊的先鋒，相當欣賞樂團[[黑色安息日|黑色安息日]]，進而開始模仿他們。美國[[街頭龐克|街頭龐克]]樂團，如Discharge、The Exploited都從重金屬樂獲得啟發。<ref>Glasper, Ian (2004). ''Burning Britain: The History of UK Punk 1980-1984''. Cherry Red Books. p. 5. ISBN 1-901447-24-3</ref>樂團Misfits發行的《Earth A.D.》成為影響[[鞭撻金屬|鞭撻金屬]]最大的一張專輯。<ref>Blush, "Hits from Hell", ''American Hardcore'', p. 204</ref>然而，龐克與金屬的音樂文化在1980年代中期以前，仍有區別。

===混合鞭撻 (1984–1988)===
在金屬與硬蕊的交互影響下，混合鞭撻（Crossover Thrash）就這麼誕生了。1984年，在[[加州|加州]][[柏克萊|柏克萊]]的一間叫作''Ruthie's''的俱樂部。<ref>Blush, p. 115</ref>，「金屬蕊」這個詞原先就是來形容這些混合金屬與硬蕊的樂團。<ref>Felix von Havoc, ''Maximum Rock'n'Roll'' #198 [http://www.havocrex.com/press/article/1/20]. Access date: June 20, 2008</ref>硬蕊龐克團體Corrosion of Conformity<ref>Blush, p. 193</ref>、Dirty Rotten Imbeciles和Suicidal Tendencies<ref name="sotb">Christe, Ian: ''[[Sound_of_the_Beast:_The_Complete_Headbanging_History_of_Heavy_Metal|Sound of the Beast: The Complete Headbanging History of Heavy Metal]]'' (2003), p. 184</ref>也開始與一些鞭撻金屬樂團如[[金屬製品|金屬製品]]（[[:en:Metallica|Metallica]]）、[[超級殺手合唱團|超級殺手合唱團]]（[[:en:Slayer|Slayer]]）同台演出。這樣的背景影響了紐約硬蕊的[[光頭黨|光頭黨]]們，紐約硬蕊始自1984年，這些團體有Cro-Mags、Murphy's Law、[[Agnostic_Front|Agnostic Front]]<ref>Blush, p. 186</ref>與Warzone<ref>Blush, p. 188</ref>。其中受影響最大的樂團The Cro-Mags，被等同於Bad Brains、[[摩托頭|摩托頭]]（[[:en:Motörhead|Motörhead]]）和黑色安息日。<ref>Blush, p. 189</ref>Cro-Mags同樣融合了[[直刃族|直刃族]]（[[:en:straight_edge|straight edge]]），令人驚訝的還有''奎師那意識''（[[:en:International_Society_for_Krishna_Consciousness|Krishna consciousness]]）.<ref>"Cro-Mags were the first band to attract both Skinheads and metalhead audiences; their music at the point where Hardcore nihilism met Metal power." Blush, p. 189</ref>其他紐約直刃族們，包括Gorilla Biscuits、Crumbsuckers與[[Youth_of_Today|Youth of Today]]<ref>Blush, p. 194</ref>，也加入了Youth Crew風格。<ref>''Alternative Press'', July 7, 2008, p. 109</ref>到了1985年，Breakdown開始在硬蕊音樂發展，他是一種Bad Brains的[[雷鬼音樂|雷鬼]]與金屬背景的結合<ref name=mosh/>，使得觀眾做出「衝撞」的動作。Agnostic Front發行於1986年的專輯《Cause for Alarm》與[[歌德金屬|歌德金屬]]樂團Type O Negative成員Peter Steele合作，正是一個硬蕊與金屬交融的分水嶺。<ref>Blush, p. 192</ref>

===旋律金屬蕊 (1995–至今)===
1990年代早期，第三波的新生金屬蕊樂團出現了，這些樂團更著重在音樂[[旋律|旋律]]上。他們結合[[旋律死亡金屬|旋律死亡金屬]]、[[硬蕊龐克|硬蕊龐克]]、[[Emo|Emo]]。<ref name="Stylus Magazine">{{cite web|url=http://www.stylusmagazine.com/feature.php?ID=2073|title=Into the void: Stylus Magazine's Beginner's Guide to Metal - Article - Stylus Magazine|last1=Lee|first1=Cosmo|last2=Voegtlin|first2=Stewart|publisher=[[Stylus_Magazine|Stylus Magazine]]|accessdate=17 May 2010}}</ref>旋律金屬蕊（Melodic metalcore）的樂團有[[Atreyu|Atreyu]]、<ref name="Stylus Magazine"/><ref name=amg>Allmusic Review, Atreyu, ''Suicide Notes and Butterfly Kisses'' [{{Allmusic|class=album|id=r591507|pure_url=yes}}] Access date: June 24, 2008</ref><ref name=ToC>"Taste of Chaos", ''Revolver'', June 2008. p. 110. ==特徵==
金屬蕊以其使用Breakdown而闻名，其中之前是重型硬核。<ref>{{cite journal |title=Resistance |publisher=Resistance Records |issue=22–26 |year=2004 |page=111}}</ref>金屬蕊歌手通常会尖叫，<ref name="WhatIsMetalcore" /> 在20世纪80年代发展起来的声乐技术，是具有20世纪90年代的金屬蕊核心特征。
