'''山道年'''，也称散道宁、蛔蒿素，是从菊科植物[[蛔蒿|蛔蒿]]的花中提取的一种[[酮内酯|酮内酯]]化学物质，可用作{{tsl|en|Anthelmintic|驱虫药}}。过去被美英等国[[药典|药典]]列为{{tsl|en|Ascaricide|蛔虫药}}。现已被效力更强、毒性更低的药物所代替。<ref>Modern Drug Encyclopedia and Therapeutic Index, Sixth Edition, Drug Publications Inc, 1955</ref>

水解可成为{{tsl|en|santonic acid|山道年酸}} (C<sub>15</sub>H<sub>20</sub>O<sub>4</sub>)。<ref>[http://www.cem.msu.edu/~reusch/VirtualText/rearang2.htm Base-catalyzed rearrangements] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090207013929/http://www.cem.msu.edu/~reusch/VirtualText/rearang2.htm |date=2009-02-07 }}</ref><ref>Per the ''Encyclopædia Britannica'' (1911 edition)</ref><ref>Modern botanicals also list ''[[Artemisia_cina|Artemisia cina]]'' or '''Levant wormwood''' as the definitive source; this is also backed up by statements in formularies published in the heyday of santonin use such as ''King's American Dispensatory''.</ref><ref>[http://www.henriettesherbal.com/eclectic/kings/artemisia-pauc_sant.html King's American Dispensatory, 1898] (this work is now in the public domain)</ref><ref>[http://www.botanical.com/botanical/mgmh/w/worlav36.html Botanical.com] (article on Levant Wormseed, primary source of santonin)</ref><ref>original source, US Pharmacopoeia, 1898</ref>

==药用==
可处理蛔虫等线虫感染，对[[绦虫|绦虫]]无效。 

山道年中毒可造成黄视、绿视。服用之前与之后起码3天不能吃油脂，以免脂溶性的山道年摄入体内。 服药后，还应吃泻药以排出蛔虫。
