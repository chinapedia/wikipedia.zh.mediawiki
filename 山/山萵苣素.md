'''山萵苣素'''（Lactucin）是一種帶[[苦味|苦味]]的物質，呈白色結晶狀固體，屬於[[倍半萜內酯|倍半萜內酯]]類。它發現在某一些品種的[[生菜|生菜]]當中，可提煉製造成[[山萵苣膏|山萵苣膏]]，具有[[镇痛药|镇痛药]]和[[鎮靜劑|鎮靜劑]]特性。<ref>{{cite journal |author=Wesołowska A, Nikiforuk A, Michalska K, Kisiel W, Chojnacka-Wójcik E |title=Analgesic and sedative activities of lactucin and some lactucin-like guaianolides in mice |journal=J Ethnopharmacol |volume=107 |issue=2 |pages=254–8 |year=2006 |pmid=16621374 |doi=10.1016/j.jep.2006.03.003}}</ref>

它可以用作[[腺甘酸受體|腺甘酸受體]]的[[激动剂|受體素]]。<ref>{{Cite journal|last=Qasem|first=Jamal R|date=2015-04-01|title=Prospects of wild medicinal and industrial plants of saline habitats in the Jordan Valley|url=https://www.researchgate.net/publication/275339308_Prospects_of_wild_medicinal_and_industrial_plants_of_saline_habitats_in_the_Jordan_Valley|journal=Pakistan Journal of Botany|volume=47|issue=2|issn=0556-3321}}</ref>
