'''达伦·爱德华·兰多夫'''（{{lang-en|'''Darren Edward Randolph'''}}，{{bd|1987年|5月12日|catIdx=Randolph, Darren}}）是一名[[愛爾蘭|愛爾蘭]][[足球|足球]][[運動員|運動員]]，司職[[守門員_(足球)|守門員]]，現時效力[[英格蘭足球冠軍聯賽|英冠]]球會[[米德尔斯堡足球俱乐部|米德尔斯堡]]。

==球會生涯==

===早期===
兰多夫生於[[布瑞|布瑞]]並在阿德莫尔流浪展開足球生涯。2003年夏天加入[[查尔顿竞技足球俱乐部|查尔顿竞技]]<ref name="ardmorelink" />，而阿德莫尔流浪更把兰多夫的名字寫入名人堂<ref>{{cite web |title=Hall of Fame – Darren Randolph |url=http://www.ardmorerovers.com/a/hall-of-fame-28782.html |publisher=Ardmore Rovers F.C |accessdate=7 September 2013}}</ref>。

2004年兰多夫被外借到[[英格蘭足球議會南部聯賽|議會南部聯]]的[[威靈聯足球會|威灵联]]一個月，參加了四場聯賽及一場足總盃比賽<ref name=Welling>{{cite web |url=http://www.winningisnteverything.net/2004-05/stats2004-05.php |title=2004–05 Player stats |postscript=}} and {{cite web |url=http://www.winningisnteverything.net/2004-05/fixtures2004-05.php |title=2004–05 Fixture list |work=Winning Isn't Everything |accessdate=1 September 2013}}</ref>。2005/06季初，他被外借到[[英格蘭足球議會全國聯賽|議會全國聯]]的[[艾宁顿斯坦利足球俱乐部|艾宁顿斯坦利]]<ref>{{cite news |title=Stanley keeper crisis continues |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/a/accrington_stanley/4188786.stm |publisher=BBC Sport |date=26 August 2005 |accessdate=31 July 2013}}</ref>，並在季末與母會查爾頓續約至2009年<ref>{{cite news |title=Valley View |url=http://www.thisislocallondon.co.uk/sport/sporttopstories/760995.Valley_View/ |website=This is Local London |date=15 May 2006 |accessdate=7 September 2013 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150411160458/http://www.thisislocallondon.co.uk/sport/sporttopstories/760995.Valley_View/ |archivedate=11 四月 2015 }}</ref>。2006年8月兰多夫被外借到[[英格蘭足球甲級聯賽|英甲]]的[[吉林汉姆足球俱乐部|吉林汉姆]]一個月<ref>{{cite news |title=Gills loan for Addicks goalkeeper |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/g/gillingham/5263502.stm |publisher=BBC Sport |date=18 August 2006 |accessdate=31 July 2013}}</ref>。

===查尔顿竞技===
2006/07球季最後一場英超比賽對[[利物浦足球俱乐部|利物浦]]，兰多夫首次代表球會上場代替無法上場的[[史葛·卡臣|史葛·卡臣]]，以2-2戰平<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.independent.ie/regionals/wicklowpeople/sport/other-sports/randolph-impresses-on-charlton-debut-27739514.html |title=Randolph impresses on Charlton debut |newspaper=Wicklow People |date=17 May 2007 |accessdate=7 September 2013}}</ref>。2007/08球季，他是球隊二號守門員。2008年2月，他被外借至[[英格蘭足球乙級聯賽|英乙]]的[[貝里足球俱樂部|貝里]]<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.independent.ie/regionals/wicklowpeople/sport/other-sports/randolph-makes-loan-switch-to-bury-27837517.html |title=Randolph makes loan switch to Bury |newspaper=Wicklow People |date=14 February 2008 |accessdate=7 September 2013}}</ref> 並參加了十四場比賽，之後因查尔顿的守門員[[尼基·韦弗|尼基·韦弗]]停賽而被召回<ref>{{cite news |title=Sodje could miss rest of season |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/c/charlton_athletic/7334290.stm |publisher=BBC Sport |date=7 April 2008 |accessdate=31 July 2013}}</ref>。在下場比賽對[[南安普顿足球俱乐部|南安普顿]]以先發身份上場，在接高球時不慎使隊友[[帕特里克·麦卡锡|帕特里克·麦卡锡]]打進一烏龍球<ref>{{cite news |title=Charlton 1–1 Southampton |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/eng_div_1/7332080.stm |publisher=BBC Sport |date=12 April 2008 |accessdate=31 July 2013}}</ref>。

==== 外借至靴尔福特联 ====
兰多夫在2008年7月被外借到[[靴尔福特联足球俱乐部|靴尔福特联]]<ref>{{cite news |date=11 July 2008 |url=http://www.skysports.com/story/0,19528,11771_3806449,00.html |title=Bulls bag keeper |publisher=Sky Sports |accessdate=11 July 2008}}</ref>。他參加了球季首17場比賽的14場，包括作客[[列斯聯足球會|列斯聯]]的比賽中，在對方19次射中龍門下僅失1球。<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.yorkshireeveningpost.co.uk/match-reports/United-pass-the-Bulls-test.4536603.jp |title=United pass the Bulls test |work=Yorkshire Evening Post |date=29 September 2008 |accessdate=4 May 2009}}</ref>

2008年11月11日，兰多夫拒絕在[[英格蘭足總盃|英格蘭足總盃]]對[[达根汉姆和雷德布里奇足球俱乐部|达根汉姆和雷德布里奇]]上場，之後他被罰兩週薪金，而他表示不再願意為靴尔福特联比賽<ref>{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/h/hereford_united/7723034.stm |title=Randolph refused to play – Turner |publisher=BBC Sport |date=11 November 2008 |accessdate=9 September 2013}}</ref>。隔天球會從[[伍尔弗汉普顿流浪足球俱乐部|伍尔弗汉普顿流浪]]租借新守門員[[马特·穆雷|马特·穆雷]]，為期一個月<ref>{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/h/hereford_united/7724709.stm |title=Murray heads for Hereford on loan |publisher=BBC Sport |date=12 November 2008 |accessdate=13 November 2008}}</ref>。[[格萊姆・特納|格萊姆・特納]]說這情況是他近30年執教生涯前所未聞，他沒想到身份職業球員居然有這樣的行為，如果他是球會的合約球員，就會立即解約<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.herefordtimes.com/sport/3843900.Randolph_refused_point_blank_to_play_in_cup_tie/ |title=Randolph refused point blank to play in cup tie |author=Turner, Graham |work=Hereford Times |date=13 November 2008 |accessdate=1 September 2013}}</ref>。兰多夫對特納的言論表示不滿，並得到查爾頓教練[[阿兰·帕德鲁|阿兰·帕德鲁]]支持。他在靴尔福特联的生涯完結，並在2009年1月返回查爾頓<ref>{{cite news |title=Bulls complete three loan deals |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/h/hereford_united/7813287.stm |publisher=BBC Sport |date=6 January 2009 |accessdate=31 July 2013}}</ref>。

====返回查尔顿竞技====
2009年1月17日，回到查尔顿的第二場比賽對[[谢周三足球俱乐部|谢周三]]丟了四球，此後該球季他再沒有上場<ref>[http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/eng_div_1/7815960.stm Sheff Wed 4–1 Charlton] BBC Sport, 17 January 2009, retrieved 4 May 2009</ref>。這場比賽沒影響他在季後續約一年<ref>{{cite news |title=Addicks let six players go |url=http://www1.skysports.com/football/news/11684/5365810/ |publisher=Sky Sports |date=6 June 2009 |accessdate=31 July 2013}}<br>{{cite news |title=Randolph pens Addicks extension |url=http://www1.skysports.com/football/news/11684/5464353/ |publisher=Sky Sports |date=31 July 2009 |accessdate=31 July 2013}}</ref>。新球季他是球隊二號守門員，排在[[罗布·埃利奥特|罗布·埃利奥特]]之後，到11月的足總盃對[[諾維治維多利亞足球會|諾維治維多利亞]]才首次上場，雖然對手是在[[英格蘭足球議會北部聯賽|議會北部聯]]打滾的業餘球會，兰多夫在上半場作出一次精彩撲救，仍以1-0爆冷落敗，當地媒體形容為球會104年史上最尷尬的賽果<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.newsshopper.co.uk/sport/charlton/4727025.Northwich_stun_Addicks_in_Cup_humiliation/ |title=Northwich stun Addicks in Cup humiliation |author=Green, Paul |newspaper=News Shopper |location=Bromley |date=8 November 2009 |accessdate=7 September 2013}}</ref>。2010年3月兰多夫取回正選位置至季末，包括晉級附加賽半決賽，但球隊在[[點球大戰|點球大戰]]不敵[[斯温登足球俱乐部|斯温登]]<ref>{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/eng_div_2/8682146.stm |title=Charlton 2–1 Swindon (agg 3–3) |author=Fletcher, Paul |publisher=BBC Sport |date=17 May 2010 |accessdate=9 September 2013}}</ref>。

===马瑟韦尔===
[[File:Randolph,_Darren.jpg|150px]]的賽前熱身]]

縱使查爾頓向兰多夫開出新合約<ref>{{cite news |title=Addicks to retain Randolph |url=http://www1.skysports.com/football/news/11684/6176880/ |publisher=Sky Sports |date=28 May 2010 |accessdate=31 July 2013}}</ref>，他選擇投效[[蘇格蘭足球超級聯賽|蘇超]]球會[[马瑟韦尔足球俱乐部|马瑟韦尔]]，簽約三年<ref>{{cite news |title=Charlton goalkeeper Darren Randolph joins Motherwell |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/m/motherwell/8777563.stm |publisher=BBC Sport |date=1 July 2010 |accessdate=31 July 2013}}</ref>。他首場比賽是[[欧足联欧洲联赛|欧足联欧洲联赛]]第二輪次回合，以1-0擊敗[[布列达布利克竞技俱乐部|布列达布利克竞技]]<ref>{{cite news |title=Europa League: Motherwell 1 Breidablik 0 |url=http://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/sport/football/europa-league-motherwell-1-breidablik-1064425 |author=McDermott, Scott |newspaper=Daily Record |date=15 July 2010 |accessdate=2 September 2013}}</ref>，但球隊在資格賽最後一輪被[[欧登塞足球俱乐部|欧登塞足]]淘汰<ref>{{cite news |title=Motherwell complain over missiles thrown at Randolph |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/m/motherwell/8930005.stm |publisher=BBC Sport |date=19 August 2010 |accessdate=31 July 2013}}</ref>。兰多夫很快成為球隊先發守門員，在2011年2月27日以2-0擊敗[[凯尔特人足球俱乐部|凯尔特人]]的賽事中便打破[[约翰·鲁迪|约翰·鲁迪]]的15場不失球紀錄<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/sport/football/teams/motherwell/8521446/Darren-Randolph-pressure-is-all-on-Celtic-against-Motherwell-in-Scottish-Cup-final.html |title=Darren Randolph: pressure is all on Celtic against Motherwell in Scottish Cup final |author=Forsyth, Roddy |newspaper=Daily Telegraph |date=18 May 2011 |accessdate=7 September 2013}}</ref>。他助马瑟韦尔打進蘇超蘭足總盃決賽，但以3-0敗于凯尔特人<ref>{{cite news |title=Scottish Cup Final: Motherwell keeper Darren Randolph bemoans lady luck as Steelmen crash to defeat |url=http://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/sport/football/scottish-cup-final-motherwell-keeper-1103695 |author=Parks, Gordon |newspaper=Daily Record |date=23 May 2011 |accessdate=7 September 2013}}</ref>。

2011/12球季首四場比賽，兰多夫都能保持不失球<ref name=sb1112>{{cite web |url=http://www.soccerbase.com/players/player.sd?player_id=41744&season_id=141 |title=Games played by Darren Randolph in 2011/2012 |work=Soccerbase |publisher=Centurycomm |accessdate=1 September 2013}}</ref>。2012年4月28日以5-1大勝[[圣庄士东足球俱乐部|圣庄士东]]中更取得助攻<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/17796092 |title=Motherwell 5–1 St Johnstone |publisher=BBC Sport |date=28 April 2012 |accessdate=7 September 2013}}</ref>。他是唯一一位马瑟韦尔球員當選為PFA蘇格蘭年度最佳陣容<ref name=PFA1112>{{cite web |title=PFA Scotland SPL Team of the Year |author=Ross, Jack |url=http://pfascotland.co.uk/2012/news/pfa-scotland-spl-team-of-the-year/ |publisher=PFA Scotland |date=4 May 2012|accessdate=1 September 2013}}</ref>。

由於2011/12球季聯賽亞軍的[[流浪者足球俱乐部|流浪者]]被托管，[[欧洲冠军联赛|欧洲冠军联赛]]附加赛則落到第三名的马瑟韦尔<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/sport/football/article-2138690/Motherwell-qualify-Champions-League.html |title=Motherwell in the Champions League! McCall delight as Scottish underdogs join Europe's top table |author=Greechan, John |newspaper=Daily Mail |date=3 May 2012 |accessdate=7 September 2013}}</ref>，是球會史上首次參加該比賽。兰多夫首場歐冠比賽是對[[纳辛奈科斯足球俱乐部|纳辛奈科斯]]，球隊總比數以3-0出局<ref>{{cite news |title=Greek tragedy: Motherwell bow out of Champions League at first hurdle |url=http://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/sport/football/football-match-reports/champions-league-qualifier-panathinaikos-3-1239255 |newspaper=Daily Record |date=8 August 2012 |accessdate=7 September 2013}}</ref>，降級參加欧足联欧洲联赛<ref name=Soccerway>{{cite web |url=http://uk.soccerway.com/players/darren-randolph/5301/ |title=D. Randolph |website=Soccerway |publisher=Global Sports Media |accessdate=7 September 2013}}</ref>。

2012年11月24日對[[哈茨足球俱乐部|哈茨]]的比賽中，兰多夫接高球期間腳部不慎踢到對方球員[[卡勒姆·帕特森|卡勒姆·帕特森]]頭部，但[[裁判_(足球)|裁判]]沒有示牌，哈茨教練[[约翰·麦格林|约翰·麦格林]]說兰多夫走運<ref>{{cite news |title=John McGlynn Darren Randolph lucky to avoid red card |url=http://www1.skysports.com/football/news/11095/8277392/ |publisher=Sky Sports |date=25 November 2012 |accessdate=31 July 2013}}</ref>。數天後蘇超蘭足球協會秋後算帳，指控兰多夫的暴力行為並禁賽兩場<ref>{{cite news |title=Motherwell keeper Darren Randolph faces ban over challenge |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/20511803 |publisher=BBC Sport |date=27 November 2012 |accessdate=31 July 2013}}</ref>，马瑟韦尔提出上訴但維持原判<ref>{{cite news |title=Motherwell goalkeeper Darren Randolph two-match ban upheld |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/20524028 |publisher=BBC Sport |date=29 November 2012 |accessdate=31 July 2013}}</ref>。季後兰多夫與隊友[[迈克尔·希格顿|迈克尔·希格顿]]、[[尼基·劳|尼基·劳]]和[[肖恩·哈钦森|肖恩·哈钦森]]一起當選為PFA蘇格蘭年度最佳陣容<ref name=PFA1213>{{cite web |title=PFA Scotland Teams of the Year |url=http://pfascotland.co.uk/2013/news/pfa-scotland-teams-of-the-year/ |author=Ross, Jack |publisher=PFA Scotland |date=2 May 2013 |accessdate=1 September 2013}}</ref> 。马瑟韦尔以亞軍完成聯賽，並取得欧洲冠军联赛附加赛資格。

===伯明翰城===

==國家隊生涯==
