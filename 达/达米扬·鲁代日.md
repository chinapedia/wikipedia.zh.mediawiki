'''達姆揚·魯德斯'''（{{lang-hr|'''Damjan Rudež'''}}，{{bd|1986年|6月17日}}），[[克羅地亞|克羅地亞]]籃球運動員。他現在效力於[[NBA|NBA]]球隊[[印第安納步行者|印第安納步行者]]<ref>[http://www.nba.com/pacers/news/pacers-announce-three-player-signings Pacer Sign Free Agents C.J. Miles and Damjan Rudez; Re-sign Lavoy Allen]</ref><ref>[http://www.sportando.com/en/usa/nba/125366/pacers-to-sign-damjan-rudez-to-a-three-year-deal.html Pacers to sign Damjan Rudez to a three-year deal]</ref><ref>[http://sports.yahoo.com/news/sources--pacers-reach-agreement-with-european-shooter-damjan-rudez-143317388.html Sources: Pacers reach agreement with European shooter Damjan Rudez]</ref><ref>[http://espn.go.com/nba/statistics/player/_/stat/3-points/sort/threePointFieldGoalPct/seasontype/2/position/rookies]</ref>。他在場上的位置是小前鋒。他也代表[[克羅地亞國家籃球隊|克羅地亞國家籃球隊]]參賽，參加了2014年籃球世界杯。
