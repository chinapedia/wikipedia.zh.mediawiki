==分佈==
猬蝦分佈于幾乎整個泛熱帶地區，<ref name="Gulf"/> 在一些溫帶地區也有分佈。主要生活在從[[加拿大|加拿大]]到[[巴西|巴西]]的[[大西洋|大西洋]]海域<ref>{{cite web |url=http://sealifebase.org/summary/SpeciesSummary.php?genusname=Stenopus&speciesname=hispidus |title=''Stenopus hispidus'' (Olivier, 1811) banded coral shrimp |publisher=[[SeaLifeBase|SeaLifeBase]] |date=March 23, 2010 |accessdate=June 12, 2011}}</ref>（包括[[墨西哥灣|墨西哥灣]]）。<ref name="Gulf">{{cite book |editor=Darryl L. Felder & David K. Camp |year=2009 |title=Gulf of Mexico Origin, Waters, and Biota: Biodiversity |volume=Volume 1 |publisher=[[Texas_A&M_University_Press|Texas A&M University Press]] |isbn=978-1-60344-094-3 |chapter=Decapoda Crustacea of the Gulf of Mexico with Comments on the Amphionidacea |author=Darryl L. Felder, Fernando Álvarez, Joseph W. Goy & Rafael Lemaitre |pages=1019–1104 |url=http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=CphA8hiwaFIC&pg=PA1052}}</ref> 在[[澳大利亞|澳大利亞]]由北至南直至[[悉尼|悉尼]]的海域以及[[新西蘭|新西蘭]]附近也有分佈。<ref name="Aus"/>

這種蝦生活在沿海深度{{convert|210|m}}以上<ref name="Aus"/> 的[[珊瑚礁|珊瑚礁]]中。<ref name="Voss"/>

==描述==

猬蝦可長至{{convert|60|mm}}，<ref name="Aus"/> 色彩鮮豔、略透明，<ref name="Voss">{{cite book |author=Gilbert L. Voss |year= |title=Seashore Life of Florida and the Caribbean |publisher=[[Courier_Dover_Publications|Courier Dover Publications]] |isbn=978-0-486-42068-4 |chapter=The crustaceans |pages=78–123 |url=http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=scXKvA97b24C&pg=PA84}}</ref> 在體內有卵時可以直接看到。<ref name="dig"/>其頭胸甲、[[腹部|腹部]]和第三對[[步足|步足]]有紅白相間的條紋，并覆蓋有棘突。<ref name="Aus"/><ref name="Voss"/> 其[[觸鬚|觸鬚]]和其他步足則都是白色的。<ref name="Aus"/>

它是一種[[清潔蝦|清潔蝦]]，<ref name="Voss"/><ref name="HK">{{cite book |author=Brian Morton & John Edward Morton |year=1983 |title=The Sea Shore Ecology of Hong Kong |publisher=[[Hong_Kong_University_Press|Hong Kong University Press]] |isbn=978-962-209-027-9 |chapter=The coral sub-littoral |pages=253–300 |url=http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=r79kKRbkzMwC&pg=PA273}}</ref> 會使用其三對螯足清理魚類體表[[寄生蟲|寄生蟲]]、[[真菌|真菌]]和受損組織。<ref name="HK"/>
