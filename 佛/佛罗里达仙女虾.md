'''佛罗里达仙女虾'''([[学名|学名]]:''Dexteria floridana'')是{{le|丰年虫科|Chirocephalidae}}下的一种，是'''佛罗里达仙女虾属'''的[[单型种|单型种]]。<ref name="Rogers">{{cite journal |title=A morphological re-evaluation of the anostracan families Linderiellidae and Polyartemiidae, with a redescription of the linderiellid ''Dexteria floridana'' (Dexter 1956) (Crustacea: Branchiopoda) |journal=[[Hydrobiologia|Hydrobiologia]] |volume=486 |issue=1 |year=2002 |doi=10.1023/A:1021326129460 |pages=57–61 |author=D. Christopher Rogers}}</ref>其被美国生物学家{{le|拉尔夫·W·德克斯特|Ralph W. Dexter}}于1953年发现于[[美国|美国]]的[[佛罗里达州|佛罗里达州]][[盖恩斯维尔|盖恩斯维尔]]南部的一个水塘之中。是[[佛罗里达州|佛罗里达州]]的[[特有种|特有种]]。该物种于2011年10月5日被宣告[[灭绝|灭绝]]。<ref name="FL">{{cite news|url=http://www.biologicaldiversity.org/news/press_releases/2011/florida-extinct-species-10-05-2011.html|title=Two Florida species declared extinct: endangered species review too late to save South Florida rainbow snake, Florida fairy shrimp |publisher=[[Center_for_Biological_Diversity|Center for Biological Diversity]] |date=October 5, 2011 |accessdate=January 11, 2012}}</ref>但[[国际自然保护联盟|国际自然保护联盟]]仍将其列为[[极危|极危]]。<ref name=IUCN/>
