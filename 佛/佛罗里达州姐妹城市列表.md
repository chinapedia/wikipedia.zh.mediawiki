[[友好城市|姐妹城市]]是指将地域或政治上没有关联的城市联系起来，以期促进相互间的人员接触和文化联系，并且这样的联系并不局限于城市之间，在[[县|县]]、地区、[[州|州]]（[[省|省]]）和其他[[国家|国家]]以下的[[行政区|行政区]]之间都可以进行。

佛罗里达州的各个司法管辖区经常通过[[国际姐妹城协会|国际姐妹城协会]]与他国城市结成伙伴关系，该组织的目标是“通过相互尊重、理解与合作促进和平”<ref>{{Cite_web | title = Mission and Goals | publisher = Sister Cities International, Inc. | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20080705083104/http://www.sister-cities.org/sci/aboutsci/mission | archivedate = 2008-07-05 | url = http://www.sister-cities.org/sci/aboutsci/mission |accessdate =2008-07-05 | deadurl = yes}}</ref>。各姐妹城市之间可以通过各种各样的活动互动，如医疗保健、教育、商业和艺术等<ref name=sistersfaq>{{Cite_web | title = Frequently Asked Questions | publisher = Sister Cities International, Inc. | url = http://www.sister-cities.org/sci/aboutsci/faqs | accessdate = 2007-06-26 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20081013071442/http://www.sister-cities.org/sci/aboutsci/faqs | deadurl = yes | archivedate = 2008-10-13}}</ref>。
