'''国际航空联合会'''（[[法語|法語]]：{{lang|fr|Fédération Aéronautique Internationale}}，简称'''FAI'''）是一個[[飛行運動|飛行運動]]、[[航空工程|航空工程]]和[[航天|航天]][[世界紀錄|世界紀錄]]的世界管理機構，成立於1905年10月14日，總部位於[[瑞士|瑞士]][[洛桑|洛桑]]。規範所有[[航空運動|航空運動]]的標準，包含載人和非載人機具（例如[[模型飛機|模型飛機]]）。

==活動==
以下活動為國際航空聯盟所管轄的：
*[[特技飞行|特技飞行]]由FAI特技飛行委員會管理（"Commission Internationale de Voltige Aerienne" - CIVA）<ref name="CIVA website">[http://www.fai.org/aerobatics Official CIVA website] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303201457/http://www.fai.org/aerobatics/ |date=2016-03-03 }}</ref>
{{Anchor|CIAM}}
* [[模型飛機|模型飛機]]由FAI模型飛機委員會管理（"Commission Internationale d'Aero-Modelisme" -CIAM）<ref name="CIAM website">[http://www.fai.org/aeromodelling Official CIAM website] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081014063628/http://www.fai.org/aeromodelling/ |date=2008-10-14 }}</ref>
* [[航天記錄|航天記錄]]由FAI航天記錄委員會管理（"International Astronautic Records Commission - ICARE）<ref name="ICARE website">[http://www.fai.org/astronautics/ Official ICARE website] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111007231235/http://www.fai.org/astronautics/ |date=2011-10-07 }}</ref>
* [[氣球（航空器）|氣球]]由FAI氣球委員會管理（"Commission Internationale de l'Aérostation - CIA）<ref name="CIA website">[http://www.fai.org/ballooning/ Official CIA website] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170705084641/http://www.fai.org/ballooning/ |date=2017-07-05 }}</ref>
* [[通用航空|通用航空]]由FAI通用航空委員會管理（"General Aviation Commission - GAC）<ref name="GAC website">[http://www.fai.org/general_aviation/ Official GAC website] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110903213345/http://www.fai.org/general_aviation/ |date=2011-09-03 }}</ref>
* [[滑翔機|滑翔機]]由FAI滑翔機委員會管理（"International Gliding Commission - IGC"）<ref name="IGC website">[http://www.fai.org/gliding/ Official IGC website] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20021017022248/http://www.fai.org/Gliding/ |date=2002-10-17 }}</ref>
* [[懸掛式滑翔|懸掛式滑翔]]& [[飛行傘|飛行傘]]由FAI懸掛式滑翔和飛行傘委員會管理（"Commission Internationale de Vol Libre - CIVL）<ref name="CIVL website">[http://www.fai.org/hang_gliding/ Official CIVL website] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20061120115228/http://www.fai.org/hang_gliding/ |date=2006-11-20 }}</ref>
* [[Microlight|Microlight]]ing and [[Paramotor|Paramotor]]ing through the FAI Microlight & Paramotor Commission（"Commission Internationale de Microaviation - CIMA）<ref name="CIMA website">[http://www.fai.org/microlight/ Official CIMA website] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110809112128/http://www.fai.org/microlight/ |date=2011-08-09 }}</ref><ref name="CIMA wiki">[http://wiki.fai.org/display/cima/CIMA+Wiki+Home/ CIMA wiki]</ref>
* [[Parachuting|Parachuting]] through the FAI Parachuting Commission（"International Parachuting Commission - IPC ）<ref name="IPC website">[http://www.fai.org/parachuting/ Official IPC website] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170705181909/http://www.fai.org/parachuting |date=2017-07-05 }}</ref>
* [[Rotorcraft|Rotorcraft]] through FAI Rotorcraft Commission（"Commission Internationale de giraviation - CIG）<ref name="CIG website">[http://www.fai.org/rotorcraft/ Official CIG website] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081013083753/http://www.fai.org/rotorcraft |date=2008-10-13 }}</ref>

The FAI establishes the standards for records in the activities.  Where these are [[air_sports|air sports]], the FAI also oversees international competitions at world and continental levels, and also organizes the [[World_Air_Games|World Air Games]] and [[FAI_World_Grand_Prix|FAI World Grand Prix]].
