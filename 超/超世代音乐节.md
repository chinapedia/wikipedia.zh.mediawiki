== 历史 ==

===海滩 （1999-2000）===

超世代音乐节由[[罗素·法比奇|罗素·法比奇]]和[[亚历克斯·奥斯|亚历克斯·奥斯]]创立<ref name="mntimes" />。首届音乐节于1999年3月13日正式开幕，为期一天<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/473184/blood-on-the-dancefloor-winter-music-conference-vs-ultra-music-festival|title=Blood On the Dancefloor: Winter Music Conference Vs. Ultra Music Festival|website=Billboard|access-date=2016-06-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160629183234/http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/473184/blood-on-the-dancefloor-winter-music-conference-vs-ultra-music-festival|archive-date=2016-06-29|dead-url=no}}</ref>。首届阵容中包括[[保羅·凡戴克|保罗·范迪克]]、[[Rabbit_in_the_Moon|Rabbit in the Moon]]、[[Josh_Wink|Josh Wink]]与[[DJ_Baby_Anne|DJ Baby Anne]]等知名DJ<ref name=":3">{{Cite web|url=http://elitedaily.com/music/music-news/15-ultra-music-festival/|title=The 15 Things You Didn't Know About Ultra Music Festival|date=2013-03-14|website=Elite Daily|language=en-US|access-date=2016-06-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160809131113/http://elitedaily.com/music/music-news/15-ultra-music-festival/|archive-date=2016-08-09|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=http://www.6am-group.com/tbt-ultra-music-festival-1999|title=#TBT Series: How Much Has ULTRA Changed Since The First Ultra Beach Festival in 1999? [VIDEOS] {{!}} 6AM|date=2016-05-12|website=6AM|language=en-US|access-date=2016-06-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160616193917/http://www.6am-group.com/tbt-ultra-music-festival-1999|archive-date=2016-06-16|dead-url=no}}</ref>。这届音乐节在[[迈阿密海滩|迈阿密海滩]]上的[[柯林斯公园|柯林斯公园]]（Collins Park）进行且饱受欢迎。然而曾预计有一万人出席的法比奇与奥斯仍然亏损了10000至20000美元<ref name=":1" /><ref name="mntimes" />。

尽管如此，但在2000年的三月，超世代音乐节回到了相同的地方并且更加成功 。之后，音乐节又迎来了新一年<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.axs.com/how-miami-s-ultra-music-festival-became-a-worldwide-phenomenon-45274|title=How Miami's Ultra Music Festival became a worldwide phenomenon|website=AXS|access-date=2016-06-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160723132848/http://www.axs.com/how-miami-s-ultra-music-festival-became-a-worldwide-phenomenon-45274|archive-date=2016-07-23|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

官方不建议驾车前去，因为停车费极其昂贵。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.miamiherald.com/news/local/community/miami-dade/downtown-miami/article66600157.html|title=7 tips for surviving the Ultra Music Festival|website=miamiherald|access-date=2016-06-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160701125606/http://www.miamiherald.com/news/local/community/miami-dade/downtown-miami/article66600157.html|archive-date=2016-07-01|dead-url=no}}</ref> 相反，大多数游客乘坐[[迈阿密地铁|迈阿密地铁]]或[[路面地铁|路面地铁]]前往目的地。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.axs.com/the-ultra-music-festival-transportation-guide-45219|title=The Ultra Music Festival transportation guide|website=AXS|access-date=2016-06-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160625165701/http://www.axs.com/the-ultra-music-festival-transportation-guide-45219|archive-date=2016-06-25|dead-url=no}}</ref> 最近的路面地铁站为[[政府中心站|政府中心站]]（Government Center），此外，免费地铁（Metromover）还提供迈阿密市中心来返Brickell的路线。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.bayfrontparkmiami.com/pages/directions.html|title=Bayfront Park Miami, Directions and Parking|website=www.bayfrontparkmiami.com|access-date=2016-06-21|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160608102746/http://www.bayfrontparkmiami.com/pages/directions.html|archivedate=2016-06-08}}</ref> 截至2016年，在超音乐节的周末，火车和巴士服务已延长至凌晨两点。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.miamidade.gov/releases/2016-03-17-transit-service-during-ultra.asp|title=Metrorail and Metromover service during 2016 Ultra Music Festival|website=www.miamidade.gov|access-date=2016-06-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160919040823/https://www.miamidade.gov/releases/2016-03-17-transit-service-during-ultra.asp|archive-date=2016-09-19|dead-url=no}}</ref>
