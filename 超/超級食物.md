'''超級食物'''是一個行銷詞彙，意指各種宣稱有益健康的食物。[[營養師|營養師]]並不使用這個詞彙，許多營養師否認超級食物所宣稱的健康助益。<ref name= "Hill2007">{{cite web |title= Forget superfoods, you can't beat an apple a day |url= http://www.guardian.co.uk/uk/2007/may/13/health.healthandwellbeing1 |newspaper= [[觀察家報|觀察家報]]（The Observer） |first= Amelia |last= Hill |date= 2007-05-13}}</ref><ref name="ufic">{{cite web|url=http://www.eufic.org/article/en/artid/The-science-behind-superfoods/|title=The science behind superfoods: are they really super?|publisher=European Food Information Council|date=November 2012|accessdate=21 October 2014|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150214131813/http://www.eufic.org/article/en/artid/The-science-behind-superfoods|archivedate=2015年2月14日}}</ref>

[[image:blueberries_on_branch.jpg|thumb]]，被稱作超級食物的一種水果，其實沒有特別高的營養價值。<ref name = "Di Noia">{{cite journal | last1 = di Noia | first1 = Jennifer | title = Defining Powerhouse Fruits and Vegetables: A Nutrient Density Approach | journal = Preventing Chronic Disease | volume = 11 | url = http://www.cdc.gov/pcd/issues/2014/13_0390.htm | date = 2014-06-05 | publisher = National Center for Chronic Disease Prevention and Health Promotion (USA) | issn = 1545-1151 | doi = 10.5888/pcd11.130390 | accessdate = 2014-06-11 }}</ref><ref name="blue">{{cite web|url=http://nutritiondata.self.com/facts/fruits-and-fruit-juices/1851/2|title=Nutrition facts profile for blueberries per 100 g, USDA Nutrient Tables, SR-21|publisher=Conde Nast|date=2014|accessdate=21 October 2014}}</ref>]]

==定義==
麥克米倫字典中，超級食物（Superfood）定義為「對健康非常好、甚至可能對某些疾病有幫助的食物」<ref>[http://www.macmillandictionary.com/dictionary/american/superfood American English definition of superfood by Macmillan Dictionary]</ref>。牛津字典則定義為「被認為對健康有益的非常營養的食物」<ref>{{cite book|url=http://www.oxforddictionaries.com/|title=Superfood (search)|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|publisher=Oxford English Dictionary, online edition|date=2014|accessdate=21 October 2014}}</ref>。在2016年，臺灣的教育部國語詞典中沒有這個詞彙。

公益組織{{Link-en|英國癌症研究協會|Cancer Research UK}}表示「超級食物」只是個行銷手法，背後沒有多少科學基礎<ref name=cruk/>。網路醫生網站旗下的MedTerms網站則定義為「非醫學名詞，因傳播媒體普及，指可能有健康助益的性質（例如減少生病或改善生理或心理健康的任何面向）的食物。所謂的超級食物可能有特別高的抗氧化物、維生素或其他營養素。」<ref>[http://www.medterms.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=125459 Superfoods definition - MedTerms Medical Dictionary on]</ref>。

==使用規範==
歐盟在2007年禁止使用超級食物這個詞，除非附上精確的醫學宣稱並且有可靠的科學研究支持。<ref name="BBC-2">{{cite web |title=Superfood 'ban' comes into effect |date=2007-06-28 |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/health/6252390.stm |publisher=BBC News}}</ref>

2004年出現了「超級水果」這個詞<ref name="dm2013"/><ref name="gross2">{{cite book|title=Superfruits|author=Gross, Paul|publisher=McGraw-Hill|url=http://www.mhprofessional.com/product.php?isbn=007163505X|year=2010|isbn=9780071633871}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news |date=30 November 2006 |title=Amazon superfruits set to boom |url=http://www.functionalingredientsmag.com/article/Trends/amazon-superfruits-set-to-boom.aspx |work=Functional Ingredients |publisher=Penton Media |accessdate=23 June 2009 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100510020807/http://www.functionalingredientsmag.com/article/Trends/amazon-superfruits-set-to-boom.aspx |archivedate=2010年5月10日 }}</ref>，這個詞在科學界以及各大消費市場（如[[美國農業部|美國農業部]]、[[衛生福利部食品藥物管理署|食藥署]]、歐洲食品安全局）都沒有官方定義<ref name="sohn">{{Cite news|first=Emily |last=Sohn |date=10 March 2008 |title=Superfruits, super powers? |url=http://www.latimes.com/features/health/la-hew-superfruit,0,5060388.story |work=[[Los_Angeles_Times|Los Angeles Times]] |accessdate=23 June 2009}}</ref><ref name=gross>{{cite web|url=http://www.naturalproductsinsider.com/articles/2009/07/comprehensive-criteria-for-superfruit-status.aspx|title=Comprehensive criteria for superfruit status|author=Gross, Paul|publisher=Natural Products Insider, Informa Exhibitions LLC|date=9 July 2009|accessdate=19 November 2014|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141129063102/http://www.naturalproductsinsider.com/articles/2009/07/comprehensive-criteria-for-superfruit-status.aspx|archivedate=2014年11月29日|df=}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|last=Brown|first=Amy|title=Understanding Food: Principles and Preparation|year=2010|isbn=978-0-538-73498-1|accessdate=21 October 2011|page=331}}</ref>，因此任何人不需經過任何認證就可以稱自己產品為超級水果<ref name=sohn/><ref name=crawford>{{Cite book|first=Karl |last=Crawford |author2=Julian Mellentin  |title=Successful Superfruit Strategy: How To Build a Superfruit Business |publisher=Woodhead Publishing |location=Cambridge, England |year=2008 |isbn=978-1-84569-540-8|url=http://www.amazon.com/Successful-Superfruit-Strategy-K-Crawford/dp/1845695402}}{{Page needed|date=September 2010}}</ref><ref name="starling">{{Cite news|first=Shane |last=Starling |date=14 May 2008 |title=Superfruit success not grown on trees, say authors |url=http://www.foodnavigator.com/Financial-Industry/Superfruit-success-not-grown-on-trees-say-authors |publisher=Decision News Media |accessdate=22 June 2009}}</ref>。

==商業影響==
食物風潮本就來來去去。20世紀初，人們稱[[香蕉|香蕉]]是「被大自然密封在無菌的包裝中」，並且讚揚它是[[乳糜瀉|乳糜瀉]]的療方，可以說是超級食物的始祖。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Levinovitz|first1=Alan|title=The First Superfood|url=http://www.slate.com/articles/health_and_science/science/2015/04/the_first_superfood_doctors_believed_bananas_could_cure_celiac_disease.html|accessdate=23 April 2015|work=Slate|date=22 April 2015}}</ref>

在1996~2005年間，第一波的超級食物自大洋洲（[[諾麗果|諾麗果]]）、中國（[[枸杞|枸杞]]和[[沙棘|沙棘]]）、東南亞（[[山竹|山竹]]）、南美（[[阿薩伊果|阿薩伊果]]）、西亞（[[石榴|石榴]]）開始試圖進入西方消費市場。諾麗果、石榴和山竹等大多數消費者無法接受的鮮果成功地以以果汁的形式打入西方市場<ref name=dm2013/><ref>{{cite journal|journal=Phytother Res|year=2007|volume=21|issue=11|pages=1100–1|title=Evaluation of the ergogenic potential of noni juice|authors=Ma DL, West BJ, Su CX, Gao JH, Liu TZ, Liu YW|pmid=17604369|doi=10.1002/ptr.2188}}</ref><ref name=schardt2006>{{Cite journal |first=David |last=Schardt |date=November 2006 |title=Super Fruit: Squeezing cold cash out of three 'hot' juices |url=http://www.cspinet.org/nah/11_06/juice.pdf |journal=Nutrition Action Healthletter |publisher=Center for Science in the Public Interest |pages=9–11 |accessdate=23 June 2009 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090706054649/http://www.cspinet.org/nah/11_06/juice.pdf |archivedate=6 七月 2009 }}</ref>，在2005年左右開始飛快成長<ref>{{Cite news |first=Todd |last=Runestad |date=1 October 2007 |title=''Functional ingredients'' market overview |url=http://www.functionalingredientsmag.com/article/Business-Strategies/-em-functional-ingredients-em-market-overview.aspx |work=Functional Ingredients |publisher=Penton Media |accessdate=22 June 2009 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20101205064358/http://www.functionalingredientsmag.com/article/Business-Strategies/-em-functional-ingredients-em-market-overview.aspx |archivedate=2010年12月5日 }}{{可疑|date=June 2009}}</ref><ref name=schardt2006/>。第一波產品在2013年開始退流行，被北歐的森林莓果和非洲的[[猴麵包樹|猴麵包樹]]取代<ref name=gross2/><ref name=schardt2006/><ref name=dm2013/><ref name=dm2013/>。

超級食物在2007~2014年間成長為上百億美元的全球產業，每年有上萬種新產品進入市場，在2007~2008年間成長率達67%<ref name=mcnally20070810>{{Cite news|first=Alex |last=McNally |date=10 August 2007 |title=Superfoods market set to double by 2011 |url=http://www.nutraingredients.com/Consumer-Trends/Superfoods-market-set-to-double-by-2011 |publisher=Decision News Media|accessdate=22 June 2009}}{{可疑|date=June 2009}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Baby Boomers Fuel $130 Billion 'Superfood' Industry|publisher=Global Newswire|date=2014-06-25|url=https://globenewswire.com/news-release/2014/06/25/646606/10087115/en/Baby-Boomers-Fuel-130-Billion-Superfood-Industry.html}}</ref><ref name="dm2013">{{cite web|url=http://www.frost.com/prod/servlet/market-insight-print.pag?docid=123415588|author=Srinivasan S|title=Superfruits - Bespoke for Functionality or Fad?|publisher=Frost & Sullivan Market Insight|date=6 March 2008|accessdate=29 November 2015}}</ref><ref name=mcnally20070810/>，但在2011~2013間開始減緩，消費者開始質疑這些產品的效能和價格<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/health/article-2531441/Why-superfruits-just-costly-rip-youd-just-eating-humble-potato.html|publisher=The Daily Mail Online, Associated Newspapers Ltd., United Kingdom|title=Why those 'superfruits' may just be a costly rip-off... and you’d do just as well eating a humble potato!|author=Renton, Alex|date=30 December 2013|accessdate=29 November 2015}}</ref>。超級水果除了以果汁的形式販售，也有越來越多廠商開發以超級水果作為其他食品的添加物、保健食品、能量飲料、或是加入美妝產品中，如此可以遮掩食物不好吃的味道<ref name=dm2013/><ref>{{Cite news|first=Jess |last=Halliday |date=23 October 2007 |title=Superfruit flavours get ever more exotic |url=http://www.foodnavigator.com/Financial-Industry/Superfruit-flavours-get-ever-more-exotic |publisher=Decision News Media|accessdate=22 June 2009}}</ref>。行銷的成功於否取決於水果的品質、對健康有益的科學證據、行銷策略、智慧財產權的保護等等<ref name=crawford/><ref name=starling/>。

==健康影響==
超級食物不能取代健康而均衡的飲食，人們也不宜靠所謂的超級食物來降低癌症風險 <ref name=cruk>{{cite web|title='Superfoods' and cancer|url=http://www.cancerresearchuk.org/cancer-info/healthyliving/cancercontroversies/superfoods/|publisher=英國癌症研究 Cancer Research UK|accessdate=August 2013}}</ref>。營養師認為超級食物只是個行銷詞彙<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.healthline.com/health-news/how-superfoods-like-bulletproof-coffee-get-popular-012715|title=How 'Superfoods' Like Bulletproof Coffee Get Popular (Hint: It's Not Nutritional Science)|publisher=Healthline|date=January 2015|accessdate=10 Mar 2015}}</ref>，並且如果用在有些不宜多食的食物 ，反而可能有害。例如有些被稱為超級食物的海帶含有天然毒素，可能傷肝或提高癌症風險<ref name= "Hill2007"/>。{{Link-en|歐洲食品信息委員會|EUFIC}}和{{Link-nl|荷蘭食品中心|Voedingscentrum}}都表示超級食物健康和醫療宣稱通常缺少科學研究支持<ref name="ufic"/><ref name="nos">{{cite web|author=Jeroen Schutijser|url=http://nos.nl/artikel/619629-superfoods-bestaan-helemaal-niet.html|title=Superfoods bestaan helemaal niet (in Dutch)|publisher=Nederlandse Omroep Stichting|date=6 March 2014}}</ref>，大量使用超級食物反而易造成飲食不均衡<ref name="nos"/>。 2013年一篇對綠茶抗癌功能的綜合研究讓美國食藥屬公開警告相關的不實廣告。<ref>{{cite web|title=Letter Updating the Green Tea and Risk of Breast Cancer and Prostate Cancer Health Claim April 17, 2012; RE: Health Claim Petition: Green Tea and Reduced Risk of Cancer Health Claim (Docket No. FDA-2004-Q-0427) |author=Schneeman BO |date=April 2013 |url= http://www.fda.gov/Food/IngredientsPackagingLabeling/LabelingNutrition/ucm301644.htm|publisher= US Food and Drug Administration|accessdate=20 November 2013}}</ref>
