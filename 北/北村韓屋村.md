'''北村韓屋村'''是一座韩国村庄，地处[[首尔|首尔]][[鐘路區|鐘路區]]，邻近[[景福宮_(韓國)|景福宮]]、[[昌德宮|昌德宮]]和[[宗庙_(首尔)|宗庙]]。该村以[[韓屋|韓屋]]和传统韩国建筑风格的小屋闻明。2009年，北村韓屋村被[[聯合國教科文組織|聯合國教科文組織]]頒發[[亞太區文物古蹟保護獎|亞太區文物古蹟保護獎]]傑出項目獎以表揚其復修成果。<ref name="KTO">{{cite web |url=http://english.metro.seoul.kr/today/news/newsclip/040520/1993,29640,0,0,0.html |title=Bukchon Hanok Village |publisher=[[韩国观光公社|韩国观光公社]] |date=2004-05-20 |accessdate=2008-05-07 |author=O Youn-hee |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081220215038/http://english.metro.seoul.kr/today/news/newsclip/040520/1993,29640,0,0,0.html |archivedate=2008-12-20 }}</ref><ref name="encyber">{{cite web |url=http://www.encyber.com/search_w/ctdetail.php?masterno=741250&contentno=741250 |title=북촌한옥마을 北村韓屋─ |accessdate=2008-05-07 |language=ko |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080928161018/http://www.encyber.com/search_w/ctdetail.php?masterno=741250&contentno=741250 |archivedate=2008-09-28 }}</ref>
