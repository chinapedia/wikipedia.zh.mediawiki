'''北貝納區'''是[[澳大利亞|澳大利亞]]昆士蘭州的[[地方政府區域|地方政府]]；2008年，由6個地方政府合併而成；所統籌的政府預算有澳幣3200萬。

==區議會==
北貝納區議會有議席6座，分由6個選區，各選出1位議員；市長為直選。<ref>[http://www.ecq.qld.gov.au/elections/local/lg2008/NorthBurnettRegionalCouncil/results/councillor/summary.html North Burnett Region Councillor Election results] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20091006125257/http://www.ecq.qld.gov.au/elections/local/lg2008/NorthBurnettRegionalCouncil/results/councillor/summary.html |date=2009-10-06 }} <small>Retrieved 17 April 2008</small></ref>
