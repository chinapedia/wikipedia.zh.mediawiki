'''北海站'''（{{lang-ms|Stesen Kereta Api Butterworth}}）位于[[马来西亚|马来西亚]][[槟城州|槟城州]][[威省北海|北海]]，属于[[KTM城际铁路|KTM城际铁路]]和[[KTM通勤铁路北马区|KTM通勤铁路北马区]]。[[马来亚铁道公司|马来亚铁道公司]]是该车站的主要经营者。此站也是[[泰国|泰国]][[SRT国际快车|SRT国际快车]]的南端终点站。

此站附近有北海临时巴士总站和苏丹阿都哈林渡轮总站，还有免费接驳到渡轮的服务。此站是"零[[链_(量度单位)|公里]]"的起点，而[[西海岸铁路线|西海岸铁路线]]的里程就是从这里开始算起。

==历史==
<!--The station began operations in 1967 when the railway swing bridge over the [[Perai_River|Perai River]] was opened, allowing the rail line which had prior to this terminated at [[Perai|Perai]] (also known as Prai and Prye), to continue to Butterworth.

Before the railway line was extended to Butterworth, all rail services began from Perai, where ferries to Penang Island also departed. 

The railway line between Prai and [[Bukit_Mertajam|Bukit Mertajam]] was opened in July 1899, and as sections of the West Coast Line were completed, allowed trains to run from Prai to [[Seremban|Seremban]] by 1903, and [[Johor_Bahru|Johor Bahru]] by 1909. The line to [[Padang_Besar,_Malaysia|Padang Besar]] was completed in 1918, allowing the first [[International_Express|International Express]] to run between Prai and Bangkok's Thonburi Station that year.-->

==新车站==
2011年8月4日，旧北海站的建筑物被拆毁，列车服务则在距离旧车站30米的临时车站营运。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thestar.com.my/story/?file=%2f2011%2f8%2f23%2fnorth%2f9348511&sec=North | title=End of the line for train stop | accessdate=13 August 2015 |author=Derrick Vinesh |date=23 August 2011 |publisher=''[http://www.thestar.com.my/ The Star]''}}</ref>

==北岸箱运码头（NBCT）== 
北海站附近也设有货运车库和集装箱终站以便将货物从[[泰北|泰北]]（通过[[巴东勿刹站|巴东勿刹站]]）和柔佛南部通过海路运输至其他地方。
