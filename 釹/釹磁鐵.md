'''釹磁鐵'''<ref>常被訛誤為「[[銣|銣]]磁鐵」。</ref>（'''Neodymium magnet'''）也稱為'''釹鐵硼磁鐵'''（'''NdFeB magnet'''），是由[[釹|釹]]、[[鐵|鐵]]、[[硼|硼]]（Nd<sub>2</sub>Fe<sub>14</sub>B）形成的[[四方晶系|四方晶系]][[晶體|晶體]]。於1982年，[[住友金屬工業|住友特殊金屬]]的[[佐川真人|佐川真人]]發現釹磁鐵。這種磁鐵的磁能積（BH<sub>max</sub>）大於[[釤鈷磁鐵|釤鈷磁鐵]]，是當時全世界磁能積最大的物質。<ref>{{cite web| title =The 2012(28th)Japan Prize | work =Laureats of the Japan Prize | publisher =The Japan Prize Foundation | url =http://www.japanprize.jp/en/prize_past_2012_prize02.html | accessdate =2012-03-15 }}</ref>後來，[[住友金屬工業|住友特殊金屬]]成功發展[[粉末冶金法|粉末冶金法]]（powder metallurgy process），[[通用汽車公司|通用汽車公司]]成功發展{{le|旋噴熔煉法|Melt spinning}}，能夠製備釹鐵硼磁鐵。<ref>{{cite book | last =Pyrhonen | first =Juha | coauthors =et al. | title =Design of Rotating Electrical Machines | publisher =John Wiley & Sons | date =2009  | pages =pp. 202 | isbn =9780470740088}}</ref>這種磁鐵是現今磁性最強的[[永久磁鐵|永久磁鐵]]，也是最常使用的[[稀土磁鐵|稀土磁鐵]]<ref>{{cite web
|title = What is a Strong Magnet?| website = The Magnetic Matters Blog| publisher = Adams Magnetic Products| date = October 5, 2012
|url = http://www.adamsmagnetic.com/blogs/2012/what-is-a-strong-magnet/| accessdate = October 12, 2012}}</ref>。釹鐵硼磁鐵被廣泛地應用於電子產品，例如[[硬碟|硬碟]]、[[手機|手機]]、[[耳機|耳機]]以及用[[電池|電池]]供電的工具等。

为了避免[[腐蚀|腐蚀]]的损害，使用时需要在永磁材料表面做保护处理，例如用[[金|金]]、[[镍|镍]]、[[锌|锌]]、[[锡|锡]]进行电镀，以及表面喷涂[[环氧树脂|环氧树脂]]等。<ref>{{cite journal | volume = 20 | issue = 1–2 | year = 2007 | title = Corrosion of Nd-Fe-B permanent magnets | first1 = M. | last1 = Drak | first2 = L.A. | last2 = Dobrzanski | journal = Journal of Achievements in Materials and Manufacturing Engineering | url = http://www.journalamme.org/papers_vol20/1369S.pdf | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120402134024/http://www.journalamme.org/papers_vol20/1369S.pdf | archivedate = 2012-04-02 }}</ref>
