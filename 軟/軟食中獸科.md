'''軟食中獸科'''（Hapalodectidae）是已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的[[中爪獸目|中爪獸目]]，生存於[[古新世|古新世]]至[[始新世|始新世]]的[[北美洲|北美洲]]及[[亞洲|亞洲]]<ref name=McK&B>{{cite book en| author = McKenna, M. C, and S. K. Bell | year  = 1997 | title = Classification of Mammals Above the Species Level |  publisher = Columbia University Press | id = ISBN 023111012X}}</ref>，體型相對較細小，只有1-8公斤重。<ref name=Gunnell96>{{cite journal en | author = Gunnell, G.R. and P.D. Gingerich | year = 1989 | title = New hapalodectid ''Hapalorestes lovei'' (Mammalia, Mesonychia) from the early middle Eocene of northwestern Wyoming | journal = Contributions from the Museum of Paleontology, The University of Michigan | volume = 29 | issue = 15 | pages = 413-418 | url = http://hdl.handle.net/2027.42/48636}}</ref>軟食中獸科與著名的[[中爪獸科|中爪獸科]]稍為不同，牠們的[[牙齒|牙齒]]適合切開食物，尤其是肉類，而其他的中爪獸目（如[[中爪獸|中爪獸]]或[[中國中獸|中國中獸]]）的牙齒則適合咬碎骨頭。<ref name=Gunnell96/>軟食中獸科曾被認為是中爪獸科的[[亞科|亞科]]<ref name=SzalayGould>{{cite journal en | author = Szaalay, F.S. and S.J. Gould | year = 1966 | title = Asiatic Mesonychidae (Mammalia, Condylarthra) | journal = Bulletin of the American Museum of Natural History | volume = 132 | issue = 2 | pages = 127-174 | url = http://hdl.handle.net/2246/1112}}</ref>，但從[[河塘軟食中獸|河塘軟食中獸]]的[[頭顱骨|頭顱骨]]得知一些不同之處而足以成立自己的[[科|科]]。<ref name=Ting&Li>{{cite journal en| author = Ting, S. and C. Li | year = 1987 | title = The skull of ''Hapalodectes'' (?Acreodi, Mammalia), with notes on some Chinese Paleocene mesonychids | journal = Vertebrata PalAsiatica | volume = 25 | issue = | pages = 161-186}}</ref>河塘軟食中獸的眶後棒在眼窩後閉合<ref name=Ting&Li/>，但其他的中爪獸目則沒有這個特徵。對於軟食中獸科的[[骨骼|骨骼]]所知甚少，而顱後骨骼則只有[[肱骨|肱骨]]被描述。這根骨頭的形態顯示牠們較中爪獸目不怎麼適合陸上運動。<ref name=OLeary98>{{cite journal en | author = O'Leary, M.A. | year = 1998 | title = Morphology of the humerus of ''Hapalodectes'' (Mammalia, Mesonychia) | journal = American Museum Novitates | volume = 3242 | issue = | pages = 1-6 | url = http://hdl.handle.net/2246/3175}}</ref>
