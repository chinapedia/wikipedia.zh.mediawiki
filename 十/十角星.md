'''十角星'''，又稱十芒星，是指一種有十隻尖角，並以十條直線畫成的星星圖形。

==幾何學==
在幾何學中，十角星是邊自我相交的[[十邊形|十邊形]]。

正十角星只有一種，其[[施萊夫利符號|施萊夫利符號]]為{10/3}，與所述第二數字差別在繪製十角星時頂點間隔數。<ref>{{citation|title=Gems of Geometry|first=John|last=Barnes|publisher=Springer|year=2012|isbn=9783642309649|pages=28–29|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=7YCUBUd-4BQC&pg=PA28}}.</ref>

正十角星每邊為<math>\sqrt 5 - 2</math>，正十角星各邊的長度比例，以及在每個邊的交叉點比例在以下圖形所示。
[[File:Decagram_lengths.svg|320px]]

在幾何學上，只要擁有10個邊、10個角，並可用[[10邊形|10邊形]]容納的圖形即可稱為十角星，其符號以{10/n}表示。只有{10/3}的十角星為正十角星，但還有三種十角星也可被解釋為正十角星。

*{10/5} 由5個5{2}[[退化多邊形|退化多邊形]]組成。
*{10/4} 由兩個2{5/2}[[五角星|五角星]]組成。
*{10/2} 由兩個2{5}[[五邊形|五邊形]]組成。<ref>Regular polytopes, p 93-95, regular star polygons, regular star compounds</ref><ref>Coxeter, Introduction to Geometry, second edition, 2.8 ''Star polygons'' p.36-38</ref>

==與五角星及五邊形相關性==
十角星與五角星及五邊形有一定的關連性，當五角星或五邊形截斷邊角時，也可創造出十角星。<ref>The Lighter Side of Mathematics: Proceedings of the Eugène Strens Memorial Conference on Recreational Mathematics and its History, (1994), ''Metamorphoses of polygons'', {{le|布蘭科·格倫鮑姆|Branko Grünbaum|Grünbaum, B.}}.</ref><ref>*{{Cite journal | last1=Coxeter | first1=Harold Scott MacDonald | author1-link=哈羅德·斯科特·麥克唐納·考克斯特 | last2=Longuet-Higgins | first2=M. S. | last3=Miller | first3=J. C. P. | title=Uniform polyhedra | jstor=91532 | mr=0062446  | year=1954 | journal=Philosophical Transactions of the Royal Society of London. Series A. Mathematical and Physical Sciences | issn=0080-4614 | volume=246 |issue=916 | pages=411 | publisher=The Royal Society | doi=10.1098/rsta.1954.0003 | ref=harv}}</ref><ref>Coxeter, The Densities of the Regular polytopes I, p.43 If d is odd, the truncation of the polygon {p/q} is naturally {2n/d}. But if not, it consists of two coincident {n/(d/2)}'s; two, because each side arises from an original side and once from an original vertex. Thus the density of a polygon is unaltered by truncation.</ref>

==應用==
十角星常出現在伊斯蘭教使用的{{le|綺理花磚|Girih tiles}}上。<ref>{{citation|contribution=Polyhedral Modularity in a Special Class of Decagram Based Interlocking Star Polygons|title=Bridges 2012: Mathematics, Music, Art, Architecture, Culture|first=Reza|last=Sarhangi|year=2012|url=http://archive.bridgesmathart.org/2012/bridges2012-165.pdf|pages=165–174}}.</ref>
