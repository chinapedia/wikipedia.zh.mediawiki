'''猝倒病'''，俗稱'''倒苗'''、'''霉根'''、'''小腳瘟'''，是一種會導致植物突然倒伏的疾病，引發此病的病原體有很多种。它是一些農業作物在苗期易發的疾病之一<ref name=UniMinn>{{cite web | url=http://blog.lib.umn.edu/cgi-bin/mt-search.cgi?blog_id=9996&tag=damping%20off&limit=10 | title=Cold Wet Soils Help Root Rotting Fungi | publisher=University of Minnesota Extension | date=June 1, 2012 | accessdate=June 3, 2012 | author=Grabowski, M. | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120401144856/http://blog.lib.umn.edu/cgi-bin/mt-search.cgi?blog_id=9996&tag=damping%20off&limit=10 | archivedate=2012年4月1日 }}</ref>，最終可能會導致作物大面積死亡。<ref name=Bucz_Harr>Buczacki, S., and Harris, K., ''Pests, Diseases and Disorders of Garden Plants'', Collins, 1998, pp. 481–2.</ref>

患此病的植物幼苗會從莖部近地面处軟倒，受害部位可能會出現暗色病斑<ref name=Bucz_Harr/>，此時作物的葉子可能尚為綠色。而病因相似的情況下，如果植物已經發生[[木質化|木質化]]，病菌從其根部入侵，就如出現作物枯而不倒的症狀，此時稱'''立枯病'''。<ref name=園林>{{cite web | url=http://www.ylzb98.com/news.asp?id=2385 | title=苗木猝倒病、立枯病的发生与防治 | publisher=园林植保网 | date=2016-08-23  | accessdate=2016-09-23 }}</ref>

==病原==
導致猝倒病或立枯病的病原有很多，它們包括：
* [[鏈格孢菌|鏈格孢菌]]<ref name=Bucz_Harr/>
* [[葡萄孢菌|葡萄孢菌]]<ref name=Bucz_Harr/>
* [[鐮刀菌|鐮刀菌]]<ref name=UniMinn/>
* [[菜豆壳球孢菌|菜豆壳球孢菌]]
* [[叶点霉菌|叶点霉菌]]<ref name=Bucz_Harr/>
* [[疫病菌|疫病菌]]<ref name="rhs" />
* [[假单胞菌|假单胞菌]]<ref name=Bucz_Harr/>
* [[腐霉菌|腐霉菌]]<ref name=Bucz_Harr/>
* [[立枯丝核菌|立枯丝核菌]]，是全球各地最常見的猝倒病和立枯病的病原之一<ref name="rhs" />
* [[白絹病菌|白絹病菌]]<ref>{{Cite web|url = http://www.gardeningknowhow.com/plant-problems/disease/controlling-southern-blight.htm|title = What Is Southern Blight: Tips For Controlling Southern Blight Disease|website = Gardening Know How|language = en-US|access-date = 2016-04-05}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url = http://plantdiseasehandbook.tamu.edu/problems-treatments/problems-affecting-multiple-crops/southern-blight/|title = Southern Blight {{!}} Texas Plant Disease Handbook|website = Texas Plant Disease Handbook|access-date = 2016-04-05}}</ref>
* [[根串珠霉|根串珠霉]]<ref>{{Cite web|url = http://plantclinic.cornell.edu/factsheets/blackrootrot.pdf|title = Black Root Rot: Thielaviopsis basicola|last =|first =|date =|website = Plant Disease Diagnostic Clinic|publisher = Cornell University|access-date =}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url = https://edis.ifas.ufl.edu/pp143|title = Thielaviopsis Trunk Rot of Palm|last = Elliott|first = Monica L.|website = edis.ifas.ufl.edu|access-date = 2016-04-05}}</ref>

==預防==

預防此病應保證天地平整，不會積水，使得土壤足夠疏鬆透氣。應當及時拔除患病植物、清理附近土壤<ref name="rhs">{{cite web | url=https://www.rhs.org.uk/advice/profile?PID=151 | title=Damping off | publisher=Royal Horticultural Society | accessdate=14 April 2015}}</ref>。此外也可施用[[碱式氯化铜|碱式氯化铜]]及其他藥劑對土壤進行消毒，以此來抑制病原體的生長。<ref name=園林/>
