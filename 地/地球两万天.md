《'''地球两万天'''》（{{lang-en|20,000 Days on Earth}}）是一部2014年英国[[纪录片|纪录片]]，由[[伊恩·福赛斯和简·波拉德|伊恩·福赛斯和简·波拉德]]共同编剧和导演<ref>{{cite web |title=How Nick Cave Lost His V-Card, and More From the Rocker's Sundance Doc|url=http://www.rollingstone.com/movies/news/how-nick-cave-lost-his-v-card-and-more-from-the-rockers-sundance-doc-20140116|accessdate=April 14, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Drafthouse Films Will Release Nick Cave Doc '20,000 Days on Earth'|url=http://www.firstshowing.net/2014/drafthouse-films-will-release-nick-cave-doc-20000-days-on-earth/|accessdate=April 14, 2014}}</ref>，[[尼克·凯奇|尼克·凯奇]]同样参与编剧<ref>{{cite web |title=Sundance 2014: World Cinema Documentary Competition|url=http://www.indiewire.com/article/sundance-2014-world-cinema-documentary-competition|accessdate=January 18, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Sundance 2014: '20,000 Days On Earth' for Nick Cave as man & myth|url=http://articles.latimes.com/2014/jan/24/entertainment/la-et-mn-sundance-2014-20000-days-on-earth-for-nick-cave-as-man-myth-20140124|accessdate=April 14, 2014}}</ref>。影片2014年1月20日于[[圣丹斯电影节|2014年圣丹斯电影节]]世界纪录电影大赛竞赛单元首映，赢得电影节两项大奖<ref name="a">{{cite web |title=Sundance: ‘Whiplash’ & ‘Rich Hill’ Win Grand Jury Awards; Dramatic Directing Goes To Cutter Hodierne For ‘Fishing Without Nets’|url=http://www.deadline.com/2014/01/sundance-film-festival-winners-2014-awards-list/|accessdate=12 April 2014}}</ref><ref name="b">{{cite web |title='Whiplash' Owns the 2014 Sundance Film Festival Awards Netting Two Top Prizes|url=http://www.indiewire.com/article/the-2014-sundance-film-festival-winners-as-they-come-in|accessdate=12 April 2014}}</ref>。

圣丹斯首映后，[[阿拉莫库房电影院|Drafthouse Film]]获得电影发行权<ref>{{cite web|title=Berlin: Drafthouse Picks Up Nick Cave's '20,000 Days on Earth' for North America|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/berlin-drafthouse-picks-up-nick-678795|accessdate=12 April 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Berlin: Nick Cave’s ’20,000 Days on Earth’ Gets U.S. Distribution|url=http://variety.com/2014/film/news/berlin-nick-cave-20000-days-on-earth-1201095770/|accessdate=12 April 2014}}</ref>，电影于2014年9月17日在美国上映<ref>{{cite web|title='20,000 Days On Earth' Gets a Pleasantly Chaotic Poster Featuring Nick Cave|url=http://www.indiewire.com/article/20-000-days-on-earth-gets-a-pleasantly-chaotic-poster-featuring-nick-cave-20140723|accessdate=8 August 2014}}</ref>。

== 概述 ==
影片纪录了澳大利亚音乐家、词曲作家、作家、编剧、作曲家和演员[[尼克·凯奇|尼克·凯奇]]<ref>{{cite web|title=Movie review|url=http://md-eksperiment.org/etv_pages.php?album_id=191&category=kino|accessdate=5 October 2014}}</ref>录制其2013年专辑《[[冲天而去|冲天而去]]》前后的虚构24小时生活。

== 发行 ==
该片2014年2月于[[第64届柏林电影节|第64届柏林国际电影节]]“全景纪录片”单元首映<ref>{{cite web|title=Berlinale Lines Up 10 World Premieres for Panorama Dokumente Section|url=http://variety.com/2014/film/news/berlinale-lines-up-10-world-premieres-for-panorama-dokumente-section-1201066549/|accessdate=12 April 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=20,000 Days on Earth - Berlin Film Festival 2014|url=http://www.theupcoming.co.uk/2014/02/14/20000-days-on-earth-berlin-film-festival-2014/|accessdate=12 April 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=British film beefs up Berlin Film Festival|url=http://www.bfi.org.uk/news-opinion/news-bfi/features/british-film-beefs-up-berlin-film-festival|accessdate=12 April 2014}}</ref>，并亮相2014年[[真假电影节|真假电影节]]<ref>{{cite web|title=True/False movie review: ‘20,000 Days on Earth’|url=http://move.themaneater.com/blogs/move-m/2014/2/28/truefalse-movie-review-20000-days-earth/|accessdate=18 April 2014|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140419012802/http://move.themaneater.com/blogs/move-m/2014/2/28/truefalse-movie-review-20000-days-earth/|archivedate=2014年4月19日}}</ref>。该片还是[[悉尼电影节|2014年悉尼电影节]]2014年6月4日开幕当晚的电影<ref>{{cite web|title=Sydney Film Festival 2014 Announces Opening Night Film|url=http://metroscreen.org.au/sydney-film-festival-2014-announces-opening-night-film/|accessdate=12 April 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=20,000 Days on Earth offers chance to get close to Nick Cave|url=http://www.smh.com.au/entertainment/movies/20000-days-on-earth-offers-chance-to-get-close-to-nick-cave-20140409-36dag.html|accessdate=12 April 2014}}</ref>。

影片2014年9月19日于英国上映<ref>{{cite web|title=Nick Cave to perform live at Barbican screening of '20,000 Days On Earth' documentary|url=http://www.nme.com/news/nick-cave-and-the-bad-seeds/78458|accessdate=8 August 2014}}</ref>。

== 评价 ==
影片在2014年圣丹斯电影节的首映中获得普遍好评。影评聚合网站[[烂番茄|烂番茄]]基于38条评论，给予本片97%“新鲜度”的积极评价，平均得分7.4分（满分10分）<ref>{{cite web |title= 20,000 Days on Earth (2014)|url=http://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/20000_days_on_earth/|work=[[Rotten_Tomatoes|Rotten Tomatoes]]|publisher=[[Flixster|Flixster]]|accessdate=August 8, 2014}}</ref>。[[Metacritic|Metacritic]]从收集到的100篇影评中，基于15条影评给予本片“84”的平均得分，表示“普遍好评<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.metacritic.com/movie/20000-days-on-earth |title=20,000 Days on Earth (2014): Reviews |work=Metacritic |publisher=CNET Networks |accessdate=2014-05-29}}</ref>”。
