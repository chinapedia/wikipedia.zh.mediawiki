'''地幔楔'''（'''mantle wedge''')，是[[大洋板块|大洋板块]]在海沟（[[板块汇聚边界|板块汇聚边界]]）处向[[地幔|地幔]]俯冲时与上盘的[[大陆板块|大陆板块]]（非潜没板块）之间夹杂的三角楔形的地幔舌部。可用地震波速成像来辨识。<ref name="Weins 2008">{{cite journal|last=Weins|first=A. D. |author2=Conder, A. J. |author3=Faul H. U.|title=The seismic structure and dynamics of the mantle wedge|journal=Annual Review of Earth and Planetary Sciences|year=2008|volume=10.1146}}</ref>  

俯冲的洋壳携带了大量挥发性水分（水合矿物的分解与渗透的海水），水分的上升将降低其上的地幔楔的熔化温度。<ref>{{cite journal|last=Kelley|first=K.|author2=Plank, T. |author3=Newman, S. |author4=Stolper, E. |author5=Grove, T. |author6=Parman, S. |author7=Hauri, E. |title=Mantle melting as a function of water content beneath the Mariana arc|journal=Journal of Petrology|year=2010|volume=51|issue=8|pages=1711–1738|doi=10.1093/petrology/egq036}}</ref><ref name=Keken>{{cite journal|doi=10.1016/S0012-821X(03)00460-6|url=http://www.geo.lsa.umich.edu/~keken/papers/frontiers03/vanKeken_EPSL03.pdf|title=The structure and dynamics of the mantle wedge|year=2003|last1=Van Keken|first1=Peter E|journal=Earth and Planetary Science Letters|volume=215|issue=3–4|pages=323–338|bibcode=2003E&PSL.215..323V|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110721170638/http://www.geo.lsa.umich.edu/~keken/papers/frontiers03/vanKeken_EPSL03.pdf|archivedate=2011-07-21}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last=Kimura|first=J.|author2=Yoshida, T.|title=Contributions of slab fluid, mantle wedge and crust to the origin of quaternary lavas in the NE Japan arc|journal=Journal of petrology|year=2006|volume=47|issue=11|pages=2185–2232|doi=10.1093/petrology/egl041}}</ref>
地幔楔的流动导致的减压也有助于其熔化。熔融的地幔楔上升形成了火山活动。<ref name="Hirshmann 2012">{{cite journal|last=Hirshmann|first=M. M.|title=Ironing out the oxidation of earth's mantle|journal=Science Magazine|year=2012|volume=10.1126}}</ref>


[[弧前|弧前]]地幔扩展至俯冲带与地幔楔较冷的鼻部相遇之处，深度约为10–40 km.<ref name="Weins 2008"/>俯冲带之处的地震波衰减低，波速快。<ref name="Stachnik 2004">{{cite journal|last=Stachnik|first=J. C.|author2=Abers, A. G.|title=Seismic attenuation and mantle wedge temperatures in the Alaska subduction zone|journal=Journal of geophysical research|year=2004|volume=10|issue=B10304}}</ref>地幔楔区域的[[地震波断层成像|地震波断层成像]]显示为低速高衰减区域，Vp= 7.4 km·s<sup>−1</sup> ，Vs= 4 km·s<sup>−1</sup>.<ref name="Weins 2008"/> 没有火山弧的地幔楔就没有这种低速。这反映了地幔楔是否熔融。
