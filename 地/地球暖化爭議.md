'''地球暖化爭議'''是指[[全球变暖|全球变暖]]事件在道德、社會及政治等多個方面的爭議，對於「在近幾十年，人為造成的全球暖化存在，並正在發生」是事實的這點，在學術界當中是沒有爭議的<!-- {{Citation needed}} begin -->{{fix |link=Wikipedia:列明来源|text=来源请求|class=Template-Fact }}<!-- {{Citation needed}} end -->。，超過97%的氣候科學家認為「全球暖化存在，且人類活動極有可能是導致近半個世紀的全球暖化現象的主要原因」，<ref>https://climate.nasa.gov/scientific-consensus/</ref>。近年大眾對地球暖化的重視程度明顯提升<ref>{{Citation | first=Spencer | last=Weart | author-link=Spencer R. Weart | contribution=The Public and Climate Change | contribution-url=http://www.aip.org/history/climate/Public.htm | title=The Discovery of Global Warming | editor-first=Spencer | editor-last=Weart | editor-link=Spencer R. Weart |  url=http://www.aip.org/history/climate/index.html | publisher=[[American_Institute_of_Physics|American Institute of Physics]] | year=2006 | accessdate=2007-04-14 }}</ref>。人們對地球暖化原因的各項科學解釋高度關注，同時引起了政治與經濟上的辯論。雖然貧窮地區，特別是[[非洲|非洲]]的排放量很微小，但卻面對著全球变暖所帶來的極大風險<ref>{{cite news | title= Poor Nations to Bear Brunt as World Warms | first=Andrew | last=Revkin | date=2007-04-01 | publisher=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] | url= http://www.nytimes.com/2007/04/01/science/earth/01climate.html?ex=1333080000&en=6c687d64add0b7ba&ei=5088&partner=rssnyt&emc=rss| accessdate = 2007-05-02}}</ref>。同時，美國不滿[[發展中國家|發展中國家]]能夠免除[[京都協議書|京都協議書]]的約束，並且以這個為拒絕簽署協議書的理由<ref>{{cite web | title= China's emissions may surpass the US in 2007 | first=Catherine | last=Brahic | date=2006-04-25 | publisher=[[New_Scientist|New Scientist]] | url=http://environment.newscientist.com/article/dn11707-chinas-emissions-to-surpass-the-us-within-months.html | accessdate = 2007-05-02}}</ref>。在[[西方社會|西方社會]]中，相比[[美國|美國]]，[[歐洲|歐洲]]有很多人接納了人類對氣候的影響及努力對抗变暖<ref>{{cite news | title=More in Europe worry about climate than in U.S., poll shows | first=Thomas | last=Crampton | date=2007-01-04 | publisher=[[International_Herald_Tribune|International Herald Tribune]] | url=http://www.iht.com/articles/2007/01/04/news/poll.php | accessdate = 2007-04-14}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | title = Summary of Findings | work = Little Consensus on Global Warming. Partisanship Drives Opinion | publisher = [[Pew_Research_Center|Pew Research Center]] | date = 2006-07-12 | accessdate = 2007-04-14 | url = http://people-press.org/reports/display.php3?ReportID=280}}</ref>。

化石燃料組織及公司譬如由{{le|菲利普·庫尼|Philip Cooney}}及某些[[智庫|智庫]]－{{le|競爭企業協會|Competitive Enterprise Institute}}和[[卡托研究所|卡托研究所]]所代表的[[美國石油學會|美國石油學會]]及[[艾克森美孚|艾克森美孚]]利用一些運動來淡化全球变暖的[[風險|風險]]。<ref name="Newsweek August 13 2007">{{cite journal |last=Begley |first=Sharon |title=The Truth About Denial|journal=Newsweek |volume= |issue=August 13, 2007 |url= http://www.msnbc.msn.com/id/20122975/site/newsweek/|accessdate= 2007-08-08 }}</ref><ref name="Guardian 2">{{cite web | title=Royal Society tells Exxon: stop funding climate change denial | url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/environment/2006/sep/20/oilandpetrol.business | first=David | last=Adams | publisher=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]] | date=2006-09-20 | accessdate=2007-08-09}}</ref><ref name="MSNBC 01-12">{{cite news |title= Exxon cuts ties to global warming skeptics |url= http://www.msnbc.msn.com/id/16593606 |publisher= [[MSNBC|MSNBC]] |date= 2007-01-12 |accessdate= 2007-05-02}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title= Report: Big Money Confusing Public on Global Warming |url= http://abcnews.go.com/Technology/Business/story?id=2767979&page=1 |last= Sandell |first= Clayton |publisher= [[美国广播公司|ABC]] |date= 2007-01-03 |accessdate= 2007-04-27}}</ref>環保團體及公眾人物則舉行運動來強調當中的風險。最近，一些化石燃料公司已經減少了淡化運動<ref>{{cite news | url=http://www.usatoday.com/weather/climate/globalwarming/2007-05-18-greenpeace-exxon_N.htm |title= Greenpeace: Exxon still funding climate skeptics |date=2007-05-18 |publisher= [[USA_Today|USA Today]] |accessdate=2007-07-09}}</ref>，甚至呼籲制定避免全球变暖的政策。<ref>{{cite press release|url=http://www.ceres.org/news/news_item.php?nid=56|title=Global Warming Resolutions at U.S. Oil Companies Bring Policy Commitments from Leaders, and Record High Votes at Laggards|date=April 28, 2004| publisher=Ceres|accessdate=2007-07-27}}</ref>

這個議題促發了關於限制工業的[[溫室氣體|溫室氣體]]排放對經濟活動的影響的辯論。在[[美國|美國]]，對科學證據及報告作[[政治操控|政治操控]]也成了重要的議題。<ref>{{cite news |url= http://thehill.com/the-executive/global-warming-becomes-hot-topic-on-capitol-hill-2007-01-18.html |title= Global warming becomes hot topic on Capitol Hill |date= 2007-01-18 |last= Holzer |first= Jessica |publisher= [[The_Hill_(newspaper)|The Hill]] |accessdate=2007-05-02}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url= http://news.yahoo.com/s/nm/20070504/pl_nm/globalwarming_usa_dc_1;_ylt=AnqRsGYs.NTGIOSzjoZQzwzuOrgF |title= U.S. rejects 'high cost' global warming scenarios |date= 2007-05-04 |last= Zabarenko |first= Deborah |publisher= [[Reuters|Reuters]] |accessdate=2007-05-04}}</ref>其他國家則更會討論為了[[減排|減排]]而採取另外一些更潔淨[[能源|能源]]時所需要的成本。<ref>{{cite news |url= http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/europe/6432829.stm |title= EU agrees on carbon dioxide cuts |date= 2007-03-09 |publisher= [[BCC|BCC]] |accessdate=2007-05-04}}</ref>

其他爭論焦點則在於一些新興工業國家如印度及中國在控制排放的力度應該有多大。2007年，[[各国二氧化碳排放量列表|中國的二氧化碳排放量]]已经超越美國，中國辯稱因為中國人均排放量是美國的三分之一（2010年），所以在減排上沒有很大責任。美國辯稱如果他們必要承擔減排成本，中國也要。<ref>{{cite news| language=en| url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/science/nature/6610653.stm |title=Chinese object to climate draft|publisher=BBC|date=2007-01-05}}</ref><ref>{{cite news| language=en|url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/content/article/2007/06/05/AR2007060502546_2.html?hpid=topnews |title=In Battle for U.S. Carbon Caps, Eyes and Efforts Focus on China| author=Steven Mufson|publisher=Washington Post|date=2007-06-06}}</ref>[[印度|印度]]也快將成為工業排放的大國之一，將會與中國一樣面對同樣的問題。<ref>{{cite web|language=en|url=http://www.oregonlive.com/science/oregonian/index.ssf?/base/science/1184718333231440.xml&coll=7 |title=India's glaciers give grim message on warming|author=Somni Sengupta|date=2007-07-17|publisher=New York Times via oregonlive.com}}</ref>
