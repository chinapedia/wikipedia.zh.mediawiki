'''尤金'''（{{lang en|'''Eugene'''}}）是[[美國|美國]][[俄勒岡州|俄勒岡州]]西部[[雷恩縣_(俄勒岡州)|雷恩縣]]的[[城市|城市]]，城市自然景色秀麗，尤金也是雷恩縣的縣治。{{GR|6}}[[面積|面積]]40.6平方哩（105km²），其中陸地面積40.02平方哩（104.9 km²），水域面積0.04平方哩（0.1 km²）。位於北緯44度3分28秒，西經123度6分37秒。2006年估計尤金擁有人口148,595人。這個數字於2010年上升至156,185人。<ref>[http://factfinder2.census.gov/ 2010 census factfinder2]</ref>2012年則估計尤金擁有人口158,335人。<ref>{{cite web|url = http://www.pdx.edu/prc/sites/www.pdx.edu.prc/files/2012%20CertEst_city_town.pdf|title = Population estimates|publisher = Portland Research Center|accessdate = 2013-03-04}}</ref>而尤金都會區則共有人口351,715人。<ref name="2010 Census">{{cite web|url=http://www.census.gov/newsroom/releases/archives/2010_census/cb11-cn43.html|title=U.S. Census Bureau Delivers Oregon's 2010 Census Population Totals|date=February 23, 2011|publisher=2010.census.gov|accessdate=2012-12-31}}</ref><ref name="PSUCensus2010">{{cite web|title=2010 Census profiles: Oregon cities alphabetically D-G|url=http://www.pdx.edu/sites/www.pdx.edu.prc/files/media_assets/2010_PL94_cities_D-G_updated.pdf|publisher=[[Portland_State_University|Portland State University]]|format=PDF}}</ref>

城市建立在1846年。尤金是俄勒岡州人口第三多的城市，原本尤金是俄勒岡州人口第二多的城市，不過在在2004年[[塞勒姆|塞勒姆]]的人口超過了尤金。<ref>{{Cite news|last = Loh|first = Stephanie|title = Eugene no longer No. 2 city in Oregon|newspaper= [[Oregon_Daily_Emerald|Oregon Daily Emerald]]|date = July 13, 2006|url= http://dailyemerald.com/2006/07/13/eugene-no-longer-2-city-in-oregon/|accessdate = 2006-11-13}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|last= Russo|first= Edward|title = Eugene reclaims second in city size|newspaper = The Register-Guard|url = http://news.google.com/newspapers?id=RdNYAAAAIBAJ&sjid=nvADAAAAIBAJ&dq=eugene%20reclaims%20second%20in%20city%20size&pg=5698%2C5864738|accessdate = 2011-08-11|date=December 28, 2007|page= A1, A4}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Eugene overtakes Salem as 2nd-largest Oregon city |publisher=KGW Portland |url=http://www.kgw.com/lifestyle/stories/kgw_122807_lifestyle_eugene_population.5dcd37b7.html |accessdate=2007-12-28 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080102075749/http://www.kgw.com/lifestyle/stories/kgw_122807_lifestyle_eugene_population.5dcd37b7.html |archivedate=一月 2, 2008 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>尤金的名是紀念城市的創造者尤金·富蘭克林·斯金納（Eugene Franklin Skinner）的。{{GR|3}}

[[俄勒岡大學|俄勒岡大學]]位於此市。<ref>[http://oregonbachfestival.com/ Oregon Bach Festival]</ref>
