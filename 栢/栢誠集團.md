'''栢誠集團'''（{{lang-en|'''Parsons Brinckerhoff'''}}），1885年成立，全球員工人數大約14,000，是國際性的設計及工程顧問公司，據2013年研究所知，它的規模在美資的同業中排行第十<ref>"[http://enr.construction.com/toplists/Top-Design-Firms/001-100.asp The Top 500 Design Firms]," Engineering News-Record, April 25, 2013</ref>。

==簡史==
[[File:William_Barclay_Parsons.jpg|thumb]]]]
於1885年由{{link-en|威廉·巴克利·帕森斯|William Barclay Parsons}}（{{lang|en|William Barclay Parsons}}）在[[紐約市|紐約市]]創立，栢誠集團的首批工程包括紐約地鐵的原[[跨區捷運公司|跨區捷運]]（{{lang|en|IRT}}）路線<ref>Clifton Hood, 722 Miles: The Building of the Subways and How They Transformed New York (Baltimore: The Johns Hopkins University Press, 1993).</ref>、[[麻薩諸塞州|麻薩諸塞州]]的{{link-en|科德角運河|Cape Cod Canal}}<ref>William James Reid, The Building of the Cape Cod Canal (New York: George McKibbin and Son, Inc., 1961).</ref>和[[武漢|武漢]]至[[廣州|廣州]]的鐵路<ref>"'L' Engines on Chinese Road," New-York Tribune, January 15, 1905</ref>。1906年，高速公路工程師{{link-en|亨利·布林克霍夫|Henry M. Brinckerhoff}}（{{lang|en|Henry M. Brinckerhoff}}）將電氣化火車技術引進到公司，他亦是[[第三軌供電|第三軌供電]]的發明人之一<ref>Lisa Moses, "Henry M. Brinckerhoff," APWA Reporter, August 1981.</ref>。

在20世紀，栢誠集團曾經參與不少舉世知名的大型工程，包括：[[底特律-溫莎隧道|底特律-溫莎隧道]]<ref>S.A. Thoresen, "Constructing the Detroit-Windsor Tunnel," Civil Engineering, April 1931.</ref>、1939年紐約[[世界博覽會|世界博覽會]]<ref>John P. Hogan, "Construction Organization and Technique," Engineering News-Record, September 22, 1938</ref>及[[三藩市灣區捷運系統|三藩市]]<ref>Thomas R. Kuesel, "Bart subway construction: planning and costs," Civil Engineering, March 1969.</ref>、[[亞特蘭大都市區快速運輸局|亞特蘭大]]<ref>"First line of Atlanta's new transit system opens," Civil Engineering, July 1979.</ref>、[[新加坡地鐵|新加坡]]<ref>Rajam Krishnan and K.S. Chan, "Singapore on the Move," Civil Engineering, November 2003</ref>、[[臺北捷運|臺北]]<ref>Scott Danielson, "Enter the Dragon," Civil Engineering, November 1994</ref>等地的[[捷運系統|捷運系統]]。

==收購合併==
栢誠集團於2009年10月被[[鮑佛貝蒂公司|鮑佛貝蒂公司]]以6.26億美元全面收購。鮑佛貝蒂公司於2014年9月3日宣佈，將所有栢誠集團股份售予{{link-en|科進集團|WSP Global}}，作價12.4億美元<ref>http://online.wsj.com/articles/balfour-beatty-to-sell-parsons-brinckerhoff-to-wsp-global-1409778904</ref>。
