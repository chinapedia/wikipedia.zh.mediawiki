'''雄二烯酮 ''' （{{lang-en|androstadienone}}）， 又叫雄甾二烯酮， '''androsta-4,16,-dien-3-one'''，是人类男性[[费洛蒙|费洛蒙]]<ref>{{cite journal|author=周延鑫|url=http://web1.nsc.gov.tw/ct.aspx?xItem=8620&ctNode=40&mp=1|title=人類的性費洛蒙 - 國家科學委員會|journal=科学发展|year=2008|volume=421|page=48-53|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20120724042813/http://web1.nsc.gov.tw/ct.aspx?xItem=8620&ctNode=40&mp=1|archivedate=2012-07-24}}</ref>，这种化学物质被描述成对人类具有强烈的费洛蒙（信息素）相关的作用<ref>{{cite journal|author=Ivanka Savic,Hans Berglund and Per Lindström|url=http://www.pnas.org/content/102/20/7356.abstract|journal=PNAS|year=2005|volume=102|title=Brain response to putative pheromones in homosexual men |doi=10.1073/pnas.0407998102}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|author=Robert Sanders 
University of California Berkeley|url=http://www.medicalnewstoday.com/articles/62600.php|title= Chemical In Male Sweat Increases Cortisol And Sexual Arousal In Heterosexual Women|journal=medical news today|year=2007}}</ref><ref name="pmid17287500">{{cite journal | author = Wyart C, Webster WW, Chen JH, Wilson SR, McClary A, Khan RM, Sobel N | title = 嗅闻人类男性汗水中的单一成分就会改变女性体内的可体松荷尔蒙 Smelling a single component of male sweat alters levels of cortisol in women | journal = [[The_Journal_of_Neuroscience_:_the_Official_Journal_of_the_Society_for_Neuroscience|J Neurosci]] | volume = 27 | issue = 6 | pages = 1261–5 | pmid = 17287500 | doi = 10.1523/JNEUROSCI.4430-06.2007 | url = http://www.jneurosci.org/cgi/pmidlookup?view=long&pmid=17287500 |date=February 2007}}</ref>  雄二烯酮（费洛蒙酮）是雄性荷尔蒙[[睾固酮|睾固酮]]的[[代谢|代谢]]物。然而，雄二烯酮并不会产生任何雄性激素或同化激素的活性作用。 虽然研究报告显示雄二烯酮会明显的影响异性恋女人与同性恋男人的心情<ref>{{cite journal|author=Jacob S, McClintock MK.|url=http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/10712859 |title= Psychological State and Mood Effects of Steroidal Chemosignals in Women and Men|journal=Hormones and Behavior|year=2000|volume=37|page=57-78|PMID=10712859}}</ref>，并且很微妙的吸引别人的注意<ref>{{cite journal |author=Hummer TA, McClintock MK |title= Putative human pheromone androstadienone attunes the mind specifically to emotional information |journal=Horm Behav |volume=55 |issue=4 |pages=548–59 |pmid=19470369 |doi=10.1016/j.yhbeh.2009.01.002 |url=http://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0018-506X(09)00005-1|date=April 2009}}</ref>，但却不会明显的影响人类的行为。雄二烯酮（费洛蒙酮）目前被添加到香水中，据称能够增加异性的吸引力<ref>{{cite journal|author=Tamsin K. Saxton, Anna Lyndon, Anthony. Littlec and S. Craig Roberts|url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science?_ob=ArticleURL&_udi=B6WGC-4SRW16D-1&_user=10&_coverDate=11/30/2008&_rdoc=1&_fmt=high&_orig=search&_origin=search&_sort=d&_docanchor=&view=c&_acct=C000050221&_version=1&_urlVersion=0&_userid=10&md5=e23654d8f74826f81e9ac6dac1d62e64&searchtype=a|title=Evidence that androstadienone, a putative human chemosignal, modulates women’s attributions of men’s attractiveness|journal=Hormones and Behavior|year=2008|volume=54|issue=5|page=597-601|doi=10.1016/j.yhbeh.2008.06.001}}</ref>。
