'''索鎮'''（{{lang-my|ဆောမြို့နယ်}}；{{IPA-my|sʰɔ́ mjo̰nɛ̀}}；{{lang-en|Saw Township}}）為[[緬甸|緬甸]][[馬圭省|馬圭省]][[甘高縣|甘高縣]]的鎮區<ref name="MIMU001">[http://www.burmalibrary.org/docs6/MIMU001_A3_SD%20&%20Township%20Overview.pdf "Myanmar States/Divisions & Townships Overview Map"] Myanmar Information Management Unit (MIMU)</ref>。2014年人口68,697人<ref name=citypopulation/>，區域面積1,775.2平方公里<ref name=citypopulation/>。該鎮下分93個村莊。[[索_(緬甸)|索]]為該鎮之首府。Kyauktu為索鎮第二大城市<ref>[http://www.themimu.info/MapsInfo/Magway/MIMU154v01_100728_Saw%20Ts-Magway%20Div_A1.pdf "Saw Township - Magway Division"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101117090551/http://www.themimu.info/MapsInfo/Magway/MIMU154v01_100728_Saw%20Ts-Magway%20Div_A1.pdf |date=2010-11-17 }} map, Myanmar Information Management Unit (MIMU)</ref>。
