質子交換膜 ('''proton exchange membrane''')或稱高分子電解質膜 ('''polymer electrolyte membrane''') (簡稱'''PEM''') 是一種[[離子聚合物|離子聚合物]]的[[半透膜|半透膜]]，設計用於[[質子|質子]]傳導隔絕[[氧|氧]]或[[氫|氫]]<ref name="NasaTechBriefs">{{cite techreport | url=http://www.techbriefs.com/component/content/article/9-ntb/tech-briefs/physical-sciences/1440 | title=Alternative electrochemical systems for ozonation of water | accessdate=17 January 2015 | institution=[[NASA|NASA]] | date=20 March 2007 | work=[[NASA_Tech_Briefs|NASA Tech Briefs]] | number=MSC-23045 }}</ref>  主要應用於[[質子交換膜燃料電池|質子交換膜燃料電池]]的[[膜電極組|膜電極組]] (MEA)，分離反應物及傳導質子。

PEM材質可由純[[聚合物|聚合物]]膜或[[複合材料|複合]]膜製作。最常見的和市售的質子交換膜的材料是[[全氟磺酸|全氟磺酸]]的[[含氟聚合物|含氟聚合物]]（PFSA），[[杜邦|杜邦]]產品。<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://web.anl.gov/PCS/acsfuel/preprint%20archive/Files/49_2_Philadelphia_10-04_1065.pdf |title=Novel inorganic/organic hybrid electrolyte membranes |format=PDF |author=Zhiwei Yang|year=2004 |volume=49 |issue=2 |pages=599 |journal=Prepr. Pap.-Am. Chem. Soc., Div. Fuel Chem.|display-authors=etal}}</ref>
