'''劍魚座星系團'''是一個鬆散的[[星系集團|星系集團]]<ref name="Carrascoetal2001" />，不僅有[[螺旋星系|螺旋星系]]也有[[橢圓星系|橢圓星系]]<ref name="Fergusonetal1990">{{citation
 | url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1990AJ....100....1F
 | title = Population studies in groups and clusters of galaxies. III - A catalog of galaxies in five nearby groups
 | year = 1990
 | date = July 1990
 | last1 = Ferguson
 | first1 = Henry C.
 | last2 = Sandage
 | first2 = Allan
 | authorlink2 = Allan Sandage
 | journal = Astronomical Journal
 | issn = 0004-6256
 | volume = 100
 | pages = 1–31
 | doi = 10.1086/115486
}}</ref>。它通常被視為一個星系群，但已經接近星系團的大小<ref name="Firthetal2006" />，它的成員主要分布在[[劍魚座|劍魚座]]，並且是[[南半球|南半球]]最富有的星系群之一<ref name="Laurikainenetal2006">{{citation
 | url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2006AJ....132.2634L
 | title = The Near-infrared S0 Survey III: Morphology of 15 Southern Early-Type Disk Galaxies
 | authors = Laurikainen, Eija; Salo, Heikki; Buta, Ronald; Knapen, Johan; Speltincx, Tom; Block, David
 | year = 2006
 | date = December 2006

 | volume = 132
 | issue = 6
 | pages = 2634–2652
 | doi = 10.1086/508810
}}</ref>。在1975年，[[熱拉爾·佛科留斯|熱拉爾·佛科留斯]]最早確認這個星系群是在劍魚座的一大片複雜[[電離氫|電離氫]]區域<ref name="Carrascoetal2001" />，並標示為G16<ref name="Fergusonetal1990" />。

== 特徵 ==
利用NGC 1549 (以[[哈勃定律|哈勃常數]]為70) 粗略估計出這個星系團的距離大約是1,840萬[[秒差距|秒差距]]<ref name="Carrascoetal2001" />，Freedman等人在2001年使用[[造父變星|造父變星]]估計的距離則是1,530萬秒差距<ref name="Evstigneevaetal2007">{{citation
 | url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2007MNRAS.378.1036E
 | title = Searches for ultracompact dwarf galaxies in galaxy groups
 | doi = 10.1111/j.1365-2966.2007.11856.x
 | authors = Evstigneeva, E. A.; Drinkwater, M. J.; Jurek, R.; Firth, P.; Jones, J. B.; Gregg, M. D.; Phillipps, S.
 | year = 2007
 | date = July 2007
 | journal = Monthly Notices of the Royal Astronomical Society
 | volume = 378
 | issue = 3
 | pages = 1036–1042
}}</ref>。在2007年，以2001年Tonry等人的觀測為基礎<ref name="tonryetal2001">{{cite journal
  | author=J. L. Tonry, A. Dressler, J. P. Blakeslee, E. A. Ajhar, A. B. Fletcher, G. A. Luppino, M. R. Metzger, C. B. Moore
  | title=The SBF Survey of Galaxy Distances. IV. SBF Magnitudes, Colors, and Distances
  | journal=Astrophysical Journal
  | year=2001
  | volume=546
  | issue=2
  | pages=681–693
  | url=http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2001ApJ...546..681T
  | doi=10.1086/318301 }}</ref>，利用[[表面光度函數|表面光度函數]]測量6個星系成員的距離，得到的平均數值是1,910±80萬秒差距<ref name="DeGraaffetal2007" />。

在這個集團中心的星系是仍在進行[[交互作用星系|交互作用]]<ref name="Bridgesetal1990">{{citation
 | url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1990AJ.....99.1100B
 | title = Globular clusters in the interacting galaxies NGC 1549 and NGC 1553
 | year = 1990
 | date = April 1990
 | journal = Astronomical Journal
 | issn = 0004-6256
 | volume = 99
 | pages = 1100–1107, 1340, 1341
 | authors = Bridges, Terry J.; Hanes, David A.
 | doi = 10.1086/115399
}}</ref>的NGC 1549和[[NGC_1553|NGC 1553]]。以亮度排序，集團的統治成員依序為：螺旋星系[[NGC_1566|NGC 1566]]、[[透鏡星系|透鏡星系]] NGC 1553、和橢圓星系NGC 1549<ref name="Firthetal2006">{{citation
 | url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2006MNRAS.372.1856F
 | year = 2006
 | date = November 2006
 | title = Kinematics, substructure and luminosity-weighted dynamics of six nearby galaxy groups
 | authors = Firth, P.; Evstigneeva, E. A.; Jones, J. B.; Drinkwater, M. J.; Phillipps, S.; Gregg, M. D.
 | journal = Monthly Notices of the Royal Astronomical Society
 | volume = 372
 | issue = 4
 | pages = 1856–1868
 | doi = 10.1111/j.1365-2966.2006.10993.x
}}</ref>。這個集團在天空中跨越的範圍大約10平方度，相當於300萬秒差距平方<ref name="Carrascoetal2001" />。由於這個集團較明亮的星系向中心集中，因此翻不在相對較小的[[調和平均數|調和平均數]]半徑內 (23萬 ± 4萬秒差距)<ref name="Firthetal2006" />。集中在一起，整個集團的光度是太陽的7.8± 1.6 {{e|10}}[[太陽光度|''L''<sub>⊙</sub>]]<ref name="Firthetal2006" />。

依據[[中性氫區|中性氫區]]分布的區域，劍魚座包括自己在內，可以分為3個較小的群：NGC 1672群、NGC 1566群和 NGC 1433群<ref name="Kilbornetal2005" />，由天爐座長城將這3個小群連結成劍魚座星系團<ref name="Kilbornetal2005">{{citation
 | url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2005MNRAS.356...77K
 | title = A Wide-Field Hi Study of the NGC 1566 Group
 | year = 2005
 | date = January 2005
 | journal = Monthly Notices of the Royal Astronomical Society
 | volume = 356
 | issue = 1
 | pages = 77–88
 | authors = Virginia A. Kilborn; Bärbel S. Koribalski; Duncan A. Forbes; David G. Barnes; Ruth C. Musgrave
 | doi = 10.1111/j.1365-2966.2004.08450.x
}}</ref>。因為是在天爐座長城之內，因此他的距離與[[天爐座星系團|天爐座星系團]]相似<ref name="DeGraaffetal2007">{{cite journal
  | author = DeGraaff, Regina Barber; Blakeslee, John P.; Meurer, Gerhardt R.; Putman, Mary E.
  | title = A Galaxy in Transition: Structure, Globular Clusters, and Distance of the Star-Forming S0 Galaxy NGC 1533 in Dorado
  | journal = The Astrophysical Journal
  | year = 2007
  | date = December 2007
  | volume = 671
  | issue = 2
  | pages = 1624–1639
  | url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2007ApJ...671.1624D
  | doi = 10.1086/523640
 }}</ref>。劍魚座星系團是比較有趣的[[本星系群|本星系群]]，在[[星系分類|星系類型]]上依然受到盤面的控制 (也就是說，兩個最明亮的星系是螺旋星系NGC 1566和透鏡星系NGC 1553)在這一對有著相同的分類型態和他的成員星系有H I質量的[[交互作用星系|無交互作用星系]]<ref name="DeGraaffetal2007" />。這一群明顯的橫越了126億 ± 0.6<ref name="Firthetal2006" /> % 的[[宇宙年齡|宇宙的年齡]]，目前的分析推論這一群[[維理定理|未維理化]]，因此或許可以解釋旋臂和H I的豐度<ref name="DeGraaffetal2007" />。
