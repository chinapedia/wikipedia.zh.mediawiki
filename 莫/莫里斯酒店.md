'''莫里斯酒店'''（{{lang-fr|Hôtel Meurice}}）是[[法國|法國]]首都[[巴黎|巴黎]]第1區的一座五星級酒店，位於[[杜樂麗花園|杜樂麗花園]]的對面，協和廣場和盧浮宮之間<ref name="Post2001">{{cite book|last=Post|first=Melville Davisson|title=The Sleuth of St. James Street|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=IINzr2u-O7MC&pg=PA230|accessdate=6 January 2012|date=August 2001|publisher=Essential Library (xLibris)|isbn=978-1-4010-0466-8|page=230}}</ref>。酒店始建於1771年，在1815年開業<ref name="Phillips1891">{{cite book|last=Phillips|first=Morris|title=Abroad and at home: practical hints for tourists|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=JT8SAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA126|accessdate=7 January 2012|edition=Public domain|year=1891|publisher=Brentano's|pages=126–}}</ref>。酒店擁有超過400名員工和160個房間<ref>{{cite web| url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/luxury/travel/1393/the-best-hotels-in-paris.html| title=The best hotels in Paris| last=O'Ceallaigh| first=John| date=8 July 2014| publisher=The Telegraph| access-date=July 31, 2015}}</ref>。
