'''莫莉·斯密登-道恩斯'''({{lang-en|Molly Alice Smitten-Downes}},生于1985年4月2日)是一名英国歌手，常被[[:en:Mononymous_person|简称]]为'''莫莉'''。她曾被[[BBC|BBC]]选为代表[[英国在欧洲歌唱大赛之历年表现|英国]]参加[[2014年欧洲歌唱大赛|欧洲歌唱大赛]]的歌手。<ref>{{cite web|title=Eurovision Song Contest: Little-known artist set to be UK's hopeful|url=http://www.bbc.com/news/entertainment-arts-26414284|publisher=BBC}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=United Kingdom: ‘BBC selects Molly Smitten-Downes’|url=http://www.escdaily.com/united-kingdom-bbc-selects-molly-smitten-downes/}}</ref> 

== 早年生活 ==
莫莉出生于[[:en:Anstey,_Leicestershire|安斯迪地区]],<ref>"[http://www.leicestermercury.co.uk/ve-just-got-deliver-killer-vocal-don-t-bigger/story-20787130-detail/story.html Eurovision 2014: Leicestershire's Molly Smitten-Downes on why she's just got to deliver that killer vocal] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140502210039/http://www.leicestermercury.co.uk/ve-just-got-deliver-killer-vocal-don-t-bigger/story-20787130-detail/story.html |date=2014-05-02 }}", ''[[:en:Leicester_Mercury|Leicester Mercury]]'', 10 March 2014. Retrieved 2 May 2014</ref>在罗斯利地区([[:en:Rothley|Rothley]])长大，后就读于位于[[拉夫堡|拉夫堡]]的[[:en:Our_Lady's_Convent_School|Our Lady's Convent School]]。她在8岁时开始登台献艺。她在[[:en:Leicester_College|莱斯特学院]]和[[:en:Academy_of_Contemporary_Music|当代音乐学院]]学习过音乐，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.getsurrey.co.uk/whats-on/whats-on-news/former-academy-contemporary-music-student-6769299 |title=Former ACM student to represent UK in Eurovision Song Contest |publisher=GetSurrey.co.uk |accessdate=4 March 2014}}</ref>同时也是一名伴奏钢琴师。

== 事业生涯 ==
=== 2005–10: 加盟"Stunt" ===
莫莉曾是艺人舞曲合作项目"[[:en:Stunt_(group)|Stunt]]"的一员。2009年，莫莉与瑞典音乐人[[低频猎人|巴松特]]一起推出了歌曲《我会学着重新去爱》("I Will Learn to Love Again")，收录于专辑《贝斯一代》("[[:en:Bass_Generation|Bass Generation]]")中。

=== 2011–13：事业开端 ===
2011年12月18日，不插电[[迷你专辑|EP]]《同我远飞》("Fly Away with Me")发售。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/gb/album/shadows/id487415556?i=487415560 |title=iTunes - Music - Fly Away With Me - EP by Molly |publisher=Itunes.apple.com |date=2011-12-18 |accessdate=2014-03-01}}</ref>2011年8月21日，EP中的歌曲《暗影》("Shadows")也曾被合作艺人玛格(Marger)通过[[:en:Sonic360_Records|索尼克360唱片]]发售于[[iTunes|iTunes]]平台。<ref>{{cite web|author=MTV UK |url=http://www.mtv.co.uk/music/urban/2734-fresh-marger-feat-molly-shadows |title=Fresh: Marger Feat. Molly - 'Shadows' | Mtv Uk |publisher=Mtv.co.uk |date= |accessdate=2014-03-03}}</ref>她与瑞典制作人[[:en:Anders_Hansson_(songwriter)|安德斯·汉森]]合作的歌曲《光下》("Beneath The Lights")也收录于安德斯的作品集《梦想的敲击》("Dream Beats")中，并于2013年4月发售。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/gb/album/beneath-the-lights-single/id641453788|title=iTunes - Music - Beneath the Lights - Single by Molly & Dream Beats|accessdate=2014-03-02}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=Joseph 'JP' Patterson |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/blogs/introducing/2011/07/jps_underground_tracks_of_the_6.shtml |title=Introducing blog: JP's Underground Tracks of The Month |publisher=BBC |date= |accessdate=2014-03-03}}</ref>2012年，莫莉还与[[硬核|硬核]]制作人[[:en:Darren_Styles|达任·斯代勒]]合作过歌曲《未曾忘怀》("Never Forget")。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nme.com/nme-video/youtube/id/wbzXsoGroQk/search/darren-styles--chris-unknown-feat-molly |title=Free Music Videos, Video Interviews, Music Video News, Live Sessions and Clips |publisher=Nme.Com |date= |accessdate=2014-03-03}}</ref>

=== 2014–现今：欧洲歌唱大赛 ===
{{Main|:en:United Kingdom in the Eurovision Song Contest 2014}}
2014年3月3日，BBC宣布莫莉将会以歌曲《宇宙之子》("[[:en:Children_of_the_Universe|Children of the Universe]]")代表英国参加[[2014年欧洲歌唱大赛|2014年欧洲歌唱大赛]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://esctoday.com/77171/united-kingdom-molly-smitten-downes-copenhagen/ |title=United Kingdom in the Eurovision 2014: It's Molly Smitten-Downes to Copenhagen}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://esctoday.com/77182/tonight-song-presentation-uk/ |title=Molly's song for the Eurovision 2014, presentation 3 March 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.eurovision.tv/page/news?id=molly_smitten-downes_to_represent_the_uk|title=Molly Smitten-Downes to represent the UK|publisher=eurovision.tv|date=3 March 2014|accessdate=4 March 2014}}</ref>这首歌曲是莫莉自己创作的，她自信地表示希望这首歌能让全世界“冲破政治壁垒”("breaks down political barriers")。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/entertainment-arts-26431800|title=Molly Smitten-Downes: Eurovision will be a challenge|publisher=bbc.co.uk|date=4 March 2014|accessdate=7 March 2014}}</ref>2014年4月1日，莫莉与[[华纳音乐集团|华纳音乐英国部门]]签订了一张唱片合约。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://esctoday.com/79388/uk-mollys-album-deal-warner-music-uk/ |title=UK: Molly’s album deal with Warner Music UK |publisher=esctoday.com |date=1 April 2014 |accessdate=1 April 2014}}</ref>4月4日，莫莉宣布她将于4月28日正式发售歌曲《宇宙之子》。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.digitalspy.co.uk/music/news/a562398/mollys-eurovision-song-children-of-the-universe-gets-studio-version.html |title=Molly's Eurovision Song 'Children of the Universe' gets studio version |publisher=Digital Spy |date=4 April 2014 |accessdate=4 April 2014}}</ref>4月15日起，[[iTunes|iTunes]]在线商店中已有该歌曲的收费下载。4月23日，歌曲的官方视频也在[[YouTube|YouTube]]上公布。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://esctoday.com/81589/united-kingdom-children-universe-clip-released/ |title=United Kingdom: Children of the universe clip released |publisher=esctoday.com |accessdate=24 April 2014}}</ref>在5月10日的欧视决赛上，这首歌曲最终以40分的成绩排在第17位。

== 提名与奖项 ==
2012年，莫莉曾得过“未签约活跃艺人评选”([[:en:Live_and_Unsigned|Live and Unsigned]])之“最佳都市/流行表演”(Best Urban/Pop Act)的提名。2013年赢得了“不列颠未签约音乐大奖”(British Unsigned Music Awards)之最佳歌曲奖。<ref>{{cite web|title=BBC - Eurovision 2014 - Media centre|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/mediacentre/mediapacks/eurovision2014|publisher=BBC|accessdate=24 April 2014}}</ref>
