'''莫克清真寺''' ({{lang-fa|مسجد نصیر الملک – Masjed e Nasir ol Molk}}),俗称“粉红清真寺”<ref>''[http://muslimheritage.com/article/mosque-whirling-colours-mixture-architecture-and-art-nas%C4%ABr-al-mulk-mosque-shiraz-iran Mosque of Whirling Colours: A Mixture of Architecture and Art in Nasīr al-Mulk Mosque in Shiraz, Iran] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160511161559/http://muslimheritage.com/article/mosque-whirling-colours-mixture-architecture-and-art-nas%C4%ABr-al-mulk-mosque-shiraz-iran |date=2016-05-11 }}'', Cem Nizamoglu, MuslimHeritage.com</ref>，是[[伊朗|伊朗]][[设拉子|设拉子]]的一座[[清真寺|清真寺]]。

该清真寺的立面大量采用彩色玻璃，室内则大量采用粉红色的瓷砖，因而俗称为粉红清真寺<ref>[[CNN|CNN]]: ''[http://edition.cnn.com/2015/07/09/travel/iran-tourism-persepolis/ Why your next vacation could be in Iran]'', Frederik Pleitgen – July 14, 2015</ref>。

==历史==
该清真寺建于1876年到1888年<ref>''[http://www.demilked.com/nasir-al-mulk-pink-mosque-stained-glass-shiraz-iran/ Stunning Mosque In Iran Becomes A Magnificent Kaleidoscope When The Sun Rises]'', DeMilked Magazine</ref>。
