'''莫拉瑟勒比清真寺'''（{{lang-tr|Molla Çelebi Camii}}），是[[土耳其|土耳其]][[伊斯坦布尔|伊斯坦布尔]]的一座奥斯曼清真寺，位于[[贝伊奥卢|贝伊奥卢]]区，靠近[[博斯普鲁斯海峡|博斯普鲁斯海峡]]渡轮码头<ref name=Gov/>和[[多尔玛巴切清真寺|多尔玛巴切清真寺]]。<ref name=Gov>{{Cite web|url=http://www.ibb.gov.tr/sites/ks/en-US/1-Places-To-Go/mosques/Pages/molla-celebi-mosque.aspx|title=Molla Celebi Mosque|accessdate= 6 October 2012|publisher=Istanbul Metropolitan Municipality Government of Tureky}}</ref><ref name=Lonely>{{Cite web|url=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/travelblogs/1016/157179/Daily+Wanderlust%3A+Molla-Celebi+Mosque+in+Istanbul?destId=360887|title=Daily Wanderlust: Molla-Celebi Mosque in Istanbul|accessdate=6 October 2012|publisher=Lonely planet}}{{dead link|date=2018年4月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref name=Arch>{{Cite web|url=http://archnet.org/library/sites/one-site.jsp?site_id=14032|title=Molla Çelebi Mosque|accessdate=6 October 2012|publisher=ArchNet Digital Library|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130121125743/http://archnet.org/library/sites/one-site.jsp?site_id=14032|archivedate=21 一月 2013}}</ref><ref name= "Freely 2011">{{cite book|last= Freely |first= John |title= A History of Ottoman Architecture |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=vgp46TUFK7wC&pg=PA300&lpg=PA300&dq=Molla+%C3%87elebi+Mosque&source=bl&ots=ih_yu-_LiJ&sig=CzgeVkCAHpR4xCANqoTUlMfCiS0&hl=en&sa=X&ei=aslwUJmOBcOFiAKcn4Eo&ved=0CDAQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q=Molla%20%C3%87elebi%20Mosque&f=false|accessdate=6 October 2012|year=2011|publisher= WIT Press |isbn=9781845645069|page=300}}</ref>

该清真寺由土耳其著名建筑师[[米马尔·希南|米马尔·希南]]设计。根据政府文献，它建于1561-1562年<ref name=Gov/>，另一份文献则表明它建于1570-1584年<ref name=Arch/>。
