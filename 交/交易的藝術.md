'''《交易的藝術》'''（'''''Trump: The Art of the Deal'''''）是商人及美國第45任總統[[唐納·川普|唐納·川普]]在1987年出版的一本書籍，由川普和記者{{link-en|托尼·施瓦茨|Tony Schwartz (author)}}（Tony Schwartz）共同編寫。這本書曾在紐約時報暢銷書排行榜上排名首位達13週，並累積進入排行榜達48週。它是川普出版的第一本書<ref name="people">{{cite magazine |url=http://www.people.com/people/archive/article/0,,20098358,00.html |title=Picks and Pans Review: ''Trump: the Art of the Deal'' |work=[[People_(magazine)|People]] |author=Ralph Novak |date=February 29, 1988 |accessdate=November 21, 2014}}</ref>，並使得川普家喻戶曉<ref>{{Cite book|title=Speaking Freely: My Life in Publishing and Human Rights|last=Bernstein|first=Robert|publisher=The New Press|year=2016|isbn=|location=|pages=|via=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2016/05/18/magazine/the-trump-of-magazines-past.html|title=The Trump of Magazines Past|last=Ligman|first=Kyle|date=2016-05-18|newspaper=The New York Times|issn=0362-4331|access-date=2016-07-18}}</ref>。川普稱這是他第二喜歡的書，僅次於[[聖經|聖經]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.msnbc.com/all-in/watch/donald-trumps-favorite-book-505029187689|work=MSNBC|title=Donald Trump reveals his favorite book|access-date=2016-07-18}}</ref>。
