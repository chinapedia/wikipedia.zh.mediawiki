{{vgname|迷宫魔兽|en=Dungeon Master}}是一款由[[FTL_Games|FTL Games]]公司制作和发行的[[电子角色扮演游戏|角色扮演类游戏]]。游戏最初于1987年在Atari ST上发行。<ref name="gamefaqs">{{cite web | url = http://www.gamefaqs.com/snes/588299-dungeon-master/data| title = Release information | publisher = [[GameFAQs|GameFAQs]]| date = | accessdate = 2015-11-05 |language=en}}</ref>游戏后也在[[Amiga|Amiga]]、[[TurboGrafx-CD|TurboGrafx-CD]]、[[Sharp_X68000|Sharp X68000]]、[[PC-9801|PC-9801]]和[[FM_Towns|FM Towns]]等平台发行。

本游戏为早期网格化的3D游戏。相对于同一时期传统的回合制游戏，迷宫魔兽为一个实时游戏。

本游戏在最初发行当年共售出4万份。<ref name="Dragon #200">{{cite journal | last = Petersen | first = Sandy | authorlink = | coauthors = | title = Eye of the Monitor | journal = [[Dragon_(magazine)|Dragon]] | volume = | issue = #200 | pages = 73 | publisher = TSR, Inc.  | location = [[Lake_Geneva,_Wisconsin|Lake Geneva, Wisconsin]] | date = December 1993 | url = | format = | issn =}}</ref>
