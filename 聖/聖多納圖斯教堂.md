'''聖多納圖斯教堂'''（{{lang-hr|Crkva svetog Donata}}，{{lang-en|Church of St. Donatus}}）是位於[[克羅埃西亞|克羅埃西亞]]城市[[扎達爾|扎達爾]]的一座教堂<ref>{{hr icon}} [http://www.amzd.hr/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=27&Itemid=49 Arheološki Muzej Zadar - Sveti Donat]</ref>。教堂最早的名稱是聖三一教堂<ref>[http://www.inyourpocket.com/croatia/zadar/sightseeing__culture/essentialzadar/venue/4537-St_Donatus_and_39;_Church.html InYourPocket - Essential Zadar] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20071222051828/http://www.inyourpocket.com/croatia/zadar/sightseeing__culture/essentialzadar/venue/4537-St_Donatus_and_39%3B_Church.html |date=2007-12-22 }}</ref>，在15世紀改為現名<ref>{{hr icon}} [http://www.izaberi.hr/index.php?lang=hr&op=sd&tip=zna&id=0000060 Izaberi.hr - Crkva sv.Donat]</ref>。
