[[File:Anglican_prayer_beads-2006_04_08.jpg|缩略图]]'''聖公宗禱告念珠'''（{{lang-en|Anglican prayer beads}}），也稱作'''聖公宗玫瑰念珠'''，是一種由[[美國聖公宗|美國聖公宗]]（Episcopalians in the United States ）神職人員[[萊恩·包伍曼|萊恩·包伍曼]]（Rev. Lynn Bauman）所發起的連禱用的念珠。由於該念珠也推廣到其他基督新教教會如[[信義宗|信義宗]]、[[循道宗|衛理公會]]、以及[[長老宗|長老會]]間，故此也稱之為'''新教念珠'''或'''新教祈禱念珠'''。 <ref name="Vincent2017">{{cite book|title=Beads of Healing: Prayer, Trauma, and Spiritual Wholeness|last=Vincent|first=Kristen E.|date=2017-03-04|publisher=Upper Room|accessdate=2017-03-11|year=|isbn=9780835816373|location=|pages=11–12|language=En}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.umc.org/what-we-believe/prayer-beads-for-united-methodists|title=Prayer Beads for United Methodists|accessdate=2017-03-11|author=Fran Walsh|date=2016-04-20|publisher=The United Methodist Church|language=En|quote=At the United Methodist General Conference, every delegate and church leader received a set of prayer beads made especially for 2016 in Portland. The United Methodist artist who helped craft the design says Protestant prayer beads are an idea that is catching on.}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Christian Prayer Beads Central|url=http://netministries.org/frames.asp?cm=CM07928&st=.&name=Christian%20Prayer%20Beads%20Central&city=National%20Resource%20and%20Referral%20Center|publisher=Net Ministries|accessdate=2014-03-25}}</ref>

新教大多不用；[[浸礼宗|浸信會]]和[[福音派|福音派]]甚至反對念珠，引用{{圣经引用|馬太福音|6|7}}：“你們禱告，不可像外邦人用許多重複話，他們以為話多了必蒙垂聽。”

==格式==
此一念珠的造型類似於[[天主教|天主教]]的[[玫瑰念珠|玫瑰念珠]]，不過異於玫瑰念珠的五組一串，聖公宗念珠則是四組一串。每組有七粒小珠並以大珠分隔，加上第一顆大珠底下聯繫的另一顆大珠。所以一串念珠是由五顆大珠與二十八顆小珠共三十三顆以及一個[[十字架|十字架]]組成。
