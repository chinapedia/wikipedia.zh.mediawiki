San Nicolás de los Garza, sometimes known only as San Nicolás, is a city and coextensive municipality in Nuevo León, Mexico that is part of the Monterrey Metropolitan area. It has become basically a city for residencies and family houses, although it still has several factories that tend to relocate to the periphery of the metropolitan area. It is the third-largest city in the state, behind Monterrey and Guadalupe. -->按照人口数排列，聖尼古拉斯市在新萊昂州居于[[蒙特雷市|蒙特雷市]]、[[瓜达卢配市|瓜达卢配市]]之后排第三。
