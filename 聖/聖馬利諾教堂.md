'''聖馬利諾教堂'''（{{lang-it|Basilica di San Marino}}）<ref name="santiebeati">{{cite web|url=http://www.santiebeati.it/dettaglio/68950|title=San Marino,  3 settembre,  m. 301|work=santiebeati.it|accessdate=3 November 2010}}</ref>是[[聖馬利諾共和國|聖馬利諾共和國]]的一座[[羅馬天主教|羅馬天主教]]的[[教堂|教堂]]。教堂是[[聖馬利諾_(城市)|聖馬利諾城]]的主教堂，獻給聖馬利諾共和國的創建人聖馬利諾<ref name="Vatican">{{cite web|url=http://www.vatican.va/roman_curia/secretariat_state/archivio/documents/rc_seg-st_19920402_santa-sede-rep-s-marino_it.html|title=CONCORDATO FRA LA SANTA SEDE E LA REPUBBLICA DI SAN MARINO|date=December 1992|publisher=The Vatican|accessdate=3 November 2010}}</ref>。現在的教堂修建於1836年，但這裡在7世紀時就已經有教堂。建築為新古典主義風格，帶有科林斯柱式風格的門廊。聖馬利諾的遺物被供奉在教堂內<ref name=Religious>{{cite web|url=http://www.visitsanmarino.com/default.asp?id=90|title=Religious itineraries|accessdate=2010-11-03|publisher=RepublicDeSanMarino: Visitsanmarion|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110725130536/http://www.visitsanmarino.com/default.asp?id=90|archivedate=2011-07-25}}</ref><ref name=San>{{cite web|url=http://www.virtualtourist.com/travel/Europe/San_Marino/Things_To_Do-San_Marino-Historical_centre-BR-2.html|title=Historical centre, San Marino|accessdate=2010-11-03|publisher=Virtual Tourist|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130527174315/http://www.virtualtourist.com/travel/Europe/San_Marino/Things_To_Do-San_Marino-Historical_centre-BR-2.html|archivedate=2013-05-27}}</ref><ref name=Michelin>{{Cite book|author= Michelin Tyre Company, ltd|title= Italy, Volume 1992|pages=426-|accessdate=2010-11-03|url=http://books.google.co.in/books?id=Dd-shWp2yFIC&pg=PA426&dq=Basilica+di+San+Marino,+San+Marino&hl=en&ei=JG_QTLyQKIPEvQPiksSSBg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CC4Q6AEwAA#v=onepage&q=Basilica%20di%20San%20Marino%2C%20San%20Marino&f=false|publisher= Michelin Apa Publications|year=2007|isbn= 1-906261-13-X}}</ref>。
