'''聖瑪利亞聖殿主教座堂'''（St. Mary Cathedral Basilica）位于美国德州[[加尔维斯顿|加尔维斯顿]]，是[[天主教加尔维斯顿-休斯敦总教区|天主教加尔维斯顿-休斯敦总教区]] 的[[主教座堂|主教座堂]]，1979年，该堂升格为[[宗座圣殿|宗座圣殿]]<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20080320073844/http://www.marycath.org/index.htm St. Mary's Cathedral Basilica Website (archive.org)]</ref><ref>[http://www.archgh.org/cocathedral/cathedral-history.htm Archdiocese Cathedral History]  {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070125042116/http://www.archgh.org/cocathedral/cathedral-history.htm |date=2007-01-25 }}</ref>。

该堂建于1847年。同年加尔维斯顿教区成立。<ref name="diocese">{{cite web| title=History| url=http://www.archgh.org/About/History/| publisher=Archdiocese of Galveston-Houston| accessdate=2011-12-29}}{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref name="storms">{{Cite news| last=Dooley| first=Tara| title=At 155, Galveston's St. Mary's still battles the storms| newspaper=Houston Chronicle| date=January 4, 2003| url=http://www.chron.com/CDA/archives/archive.mpl?id=2003_3615026| accessdate=June 4, 2010}}</ref>

1973年，该堂成为国家历史地标。
