'''耶書''' (希伯来語ישו ，Yeshu) 指代 [[托塞夫塔|托塞夫塔]], [[巴比倫的塔木德|巴比倫的塔木德]]和[[Toledot_Yeshu|Toledot Yeshu]]記述的一個或者多個人。托塞夫塔 (''c'' 200) 和巴比倫的塔木德 (''c'' 500) rarely<ref name = "TM1998">Theissen, Gerd and Annette Merz. The historical Jesus: a comprehensive guide. Fortress Press. 1998. 譯自德文 (1996版)</ref>Adin Steinsaltz認為耶書可能指生活在公元1或者2世紀的1個人。<ref>[http://www.jewishjournal.com/world/article/jesus_death_now_debated_by_jews_20031010/ Jesus' Death Now Debated by Jews] by Eric J. Greenberg, The Jewish Week, USA, 2003年10月3日刊</ref>還有一些学者認為他就是指耶穌。

== 词源 ==
[[File:Yeshua_hebreo.jpg|left]]

{{main|Names and titles of Jesus in the New Testament}}

Yeshu一次源自希伯来语，是“拯救”、“传达”之意<ref>[[Brown_Driver_Briggs|Brown Driver Briggs]] Brown-Driver-Briggs Hebrew and English Lexicon; Hendrickson Publishers 1996 ISBN 1-56563-206-0</ref>，在犹太人第二圣殿时期一直保持着这种用法，耶書一词在亚拉姆语和希伯来语圣经中被广泛使用：希伯来圣经中多次同时使用Yeshu和Joshua。

== 鉴定耶書和耶穌的関系 ==
已知最早的[[巴比伦塔木德|巴比伦塔木德]]（[[:en:Babylonian_Talmud|:en:Babylonian_Talmud]] 公元500年）中出现的[[耶書|耶書]]被认为指的就是基督教中的耶穌，该说法源自中世纪，现代学者对此有各种不同观点<ref>''The Blackwell Companion to Jesus'' p220  Delbert Burkett - 2010 "That is to say, varying presuppositions as to the extent to which the ancient rabbis were preoccupied with Jesus and Christianity can easily predetermine which texts might be identified and interpreted as having him in mind."</ref>。部分当代学者认为这些段落指向基督教和基督徒描述的耶稣<ref>Robert E.
