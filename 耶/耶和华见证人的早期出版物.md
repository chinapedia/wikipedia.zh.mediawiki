{{耶和华见证人}}
[[耶和华见证人|耶和华见证人]]自1870年代开始出版了数不尽的圣经,书刊,杂志,小册子,传单和其它材料.以下列出的作品已经不再被[[宾夕法尼亚守望台圣经书社|守望台书社]]分发,尽管有一些仍可以在守望台图书馆光盘里找到。今天耶和华见证人守望台书社不接受诸多的圣经研究者会众为罗素神父工作的成果.他们不接受1917年以后守望台出版的作品的权威性.    

''1960年以后的出版物请参看[[耶和华见证人出版物列表|耶和华见证人出版物列表]]。''

'''1842年'''

[http://www.earlysda.com/miller/evidence1.html Evidence From Scripture and History of the Second Coming of Christ, About the Year 1843] by [[威廉·米勒(传教士)|威廉·米勒]].<ref>William Miller is acknowledged as the main leader of the Adventist movement in the 1840s. Storrs, Barbour, Stetson and Wendell (later close personal friends of Russell) had all been associated with Miller. Storrs is referenced in an 1842 issue of the Millerite journal  [http://0-astatine.llu.edu.catalog.llu.edu/docs/MC/MC-0102.pdf ''The Midnight Cry'']. (Storrs involvement with Miller is also explained in the October 15, 2000 ''Watchtower'', p. 28.) In 1881, Russell explained his view that Miller and his followers fulfilled the "Parable of the Ten Virgins" of Matthew chapter 25: "While we are neither 'Millerites' nor 'Adventists,' yet we believe that this much of this parable met its fulfillment in 1843 and 1844, when William Miller and others, Bible in hand, walked out by faith on its statements, expecting Jesus at that time." October-November 1881 ''Watch Tower'', [http://www.mostholyfaith.com/bible/Reprints/Z1881OCT.asp#R288  ''Reprints'', p. 288]. [http://www.earlysda.com/miller/evidence16-end.html#LECTURE%20XVII. Lecture XVII] of Miller's book detailed a 2,520 year chronological period from 677 B.C. to 1843 A.D., which is also shown in a teaching chart [http://www.cornerstonemag.com/cart/img/millerite/fig_1.jpg] used by Millerite preachers of that era.</ref>
