'''耶稣圣名教堂'''（{{lang-en|'''Church of the Holy Name of Jesus'''}}）是一座位于英国大曼彻斯特郡牛津街的教堂，由约瑟夫·汉森设计，于1869年至1871年期间建成​​。<ref>Harris, Penelope, ''The Architectural Achievement of Joseph Aloysius Hansom (1803-1882), Designer of the Hansom Cab, Birmingham Town Hall and Churches of the Catholic Revival'' (The Edwin Mellen Press, 2010).</ref>1989年，耶稣教堂被选为I级[[登錄建築|登錄建築]]，1963年晋级为II级登录建筑。<ref>[http://www.britishlistedbuildings.co.uk/en-454843-roman-catholic-church-of-the-holy-name-o/ British Listed Buildings] Retrieved 29 January 2013</ref>
