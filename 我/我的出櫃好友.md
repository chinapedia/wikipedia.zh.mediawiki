'''我的出櫃好友'''（{{lang|en|Date and Switch}}）為2014年美國[[喜劇電影|喜劇電影]]，由 Chris Nelson 導演，以及{{Tsl|en|Alan Yang}}編劇。本電影於2014年2月14日在戲院上映以及在[[隨選視訊|隨選視訊]]上發行。<ref>{{cite web |last=Truitt |first=Brian |url=http://www.usatoday.com/story/life/movies/2014/02/11/date-and-switch-movie-exclusive-clip/5396637/ |title='Date and Switch' puts a spin on old teen-movie trope |work=[[USA_Today|USA Today]] |publisher=[[Gannett_Company|Gannett Company]] |date=February 11, 2014 |accessdate=February 16, 2014}}</ref> 由[[Nicholas_Braun|Nicholas Braun]]，Hunter Cope，[[Dakota_Johnson|Dakota Johnson]]，以及{{tsl|en|Zach Cregger}}等主演。原始的名稱為「同志好友」(Gay Dude)。<ref>{{cite web |last=Kramer |first=Gary M. |url=http://blogs.indiewire.com/bent/straight-eye-for-the-queer-guy-on-chris-nelsons-date-and-switch-20140211 |title=Straight Eye For The Queer Guy: On Chris Nelson's "Date and Switch" |publisher=[[Indiewire|Indiewire]]. [[SnagFilms|SnagFilms]] |date=February 11, 2014 |accessdate=February 16, 2014 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140221220622/http://blogs.indiewire.com/bent/straight-eye-for-the-queer-guy-on-chris-nelsons-date-and-switch-20140211 |archive-date=2014-02-21 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>

==劇情大意==
馬帝（Matty）與麥克（Michael）兩個高中男生好朋友，他們約定好要在畢業舞會前找到女孩子並且上床。然而，馬帝突然對麥克說他是同性戀，打擊了兩個人之間的友誼。<ref name="VareityOffermanJoins">{{cite web|url=http://www.variety.com/article/VR1118041271|title=Hunter Cope joins Lionsgate's 'Gay Dude'|last=Sneider|first=Jeff|work=[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]]|publisher=[[Reed_Business_Information|Reed Business Information]]|date=August 12, 2011|accessdate=September 30, 2011|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/626ADhhLS?url=http://www.variety.com/article/VR1118041271|archivedate=2011-10-01|deadurl=no}}</ref>
