'''波特兰公立学校'''('''Portland Public Schools''')是[[奧勒岡州|奧勒岡州]]的学区, 总部在[[波特蘭_(奧勒岡州)|波特蘭]]。<ref>"[http://www.pps.k12.or.us/ Welcome to Portland Public Schools]." 波特兰公立学校. "Portland Public Schools 501 North Dixon Street Portland, Oregon, 97227-1807 - USA"</ref> PPS约有47,000名小学生和85个学校。<ref>"[http://www.pps.k12.or.us/about-us/index.htm About Portland Public Schools]." 波特兰公立学校.</ref>
