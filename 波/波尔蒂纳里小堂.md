'''波尔蒂纳里小堂'''（意大利语：''Cappella Portinari''）是[[意大利|意大利]][[米兰|米兰]][[圣欧斯托焦圣殿|圣欧斯托焦圣殿]]东端的一座[[文艺复兴|文艺复兴]]小堂。它在1460年动工，于1468年完成，委托兴建此堂的是[[佛罗伦萨|佛罗伦萨]]贵族皮格罗·波尔蒂纳里（1421年至1468年），他在1452年成为[[美第奇银行|美第奇银行]]驻米兰代表。<ref name="basilica"/><ref name="tci-milano">[http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=nshyVnFbYbIC&pg=PA370 ''Milano''], Guida d'Italia del Touring club italiano, 10th edn (Milan: Touring Editore, 1998), pp. 370–372.</ref>它被认为是在伦巴第的一座托斯卡纳建筑<ref>[http://www.hellomilano.it/Museums/Museums.htm Museums] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090418044406/http://hellomilano.it/Museums/Museums.htm |date=2009-04-18 }}, Hello Milano.</ref>。
这是一座家族教堂，内有由总主教若望·维斯康蒂在1340年修建的安葬[[维罗纳的圣伯铎|维罗纳的圣伯铎]]首级的银质坟墓。<ref name="basilica">[http://www.santeustorgio.it/cappella_portinari.html ‘Cappella Portinari’], Basilica di Sant’Eustorgio (official site).</ref><ref>Luca Beltrami, [http://www.archive.org/stream/italianwalldecor00victrich#page/15/ ‘The Chapel of St. Peter Martyr in the Church of Sant’ Eustorgio, Milan’], tr. by A. R Evans, in Victoria and Albert Museum, ''Italian Wall Decorations of the 15th and 16th Centuries: A Handbook to the Models Illustrating Interiors of Italian Buildings in the Victoria and Albert Museum, South Kensington'' (London: Chapman & Hall, 1901), pp. 13–32 (pp. 18 and 29).</ref>
