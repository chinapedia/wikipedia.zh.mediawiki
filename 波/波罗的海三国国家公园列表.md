==立陶宛==
[[File:Pilkosios_kopos1.jpg|thumb]]]]
立陶宛共有5个国家公园。They were established in 1991 after [[Lithuania|Lithuania]] declared independence from the [[Soviet_Union|Soviet Union]] in 1990. [[Lithuanian_SSR|Lithuanian SSR]] National Park established in 1974 was reorganized and renamed Aukštaitija National Park. Now they cover about 2.3 percent of Lithuania's territory.
