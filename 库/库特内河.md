|last=Palliser
|first=John
|title=Exploration - British North America - the journals, detailed reports, and observations relative to the exploration, by Captain Palliser, of that portion of British North America, which, in latitude, lies between the British boundary line and the height of land or watershed of the northern or ...
|page=162
|year=1863
|publisher=G.E. Eyre and W. Spottiswoode for H.M. Stationery Off.
|ISBN=
|accessdate=2010-03-28}}</ref>）是[[哥伦比亚河|哥伦比亚河]]的一条重要[[支流|支流]]，流经[[加拿大|加拿大]][[卑诗省|卑诗省]]的东南部和[[美国|美国]]西北部的[[爱达荷州|爱达荷州]]与[[蒙大拿州|蒙大拿州]]，全长{{convert|781|km|mi}}，起源于加拿大落基山脉，在[[卡斯尔加|卡斯尔加]]注入哥伦比亚河，最终注入[[太平洋|太平洋]]。
