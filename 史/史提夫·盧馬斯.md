'''史提芬·馬田·"史提夫"·盧馬斯'''（{{lang-en|'''Stephen Martin "Steve" Lomas'''}}，{{bd|1974年|1月18日|catIdx=Lomas, Steve}}）是一名[[北愛爾蘭|北愛爾蘭]]前[[職業|職業]]職業足球員，退役後擔任[[教練|教練]]工作，曾擔任[[英冠|英冠]]球會[[米尔沃尔足球俱乐部|米禾爾]]的[[領隊_(足球)|領隊]]。

盧馬斯由1991年至2010年的球員生涯主要出任[[中場|防守中場]]，曾分別效力[[英超|英超]]球會[[曼彻斯特城足球俱乐部|曼城]]和[[西汉姆联足球俱乐部|韋斯咸]]，其後才加盟[[英格蘭足球聯賽|低級別]]的[[女王公园巡游者足球俱乐部|昆士柏流浪]]和[[吉林汉姆足球俱乐部|基寧咸]]。2009年盧馬斯成為非聯賽球隊[[St_Neots_Town_F.C.|圣尼茨镇]]（St Neots Town）的球員兼領隊。而他亦曾代表[[北愛爾蘭足球代表隊|北愛爾蘭]]上陣45場國際賽及射入3球。

2011年盧馬斯獲[[蘇超|蘇超]]球會[[圣庄士东足球俱乐部|聖莊士東]]委任為領隊，於2011年和2012年分別帶領球隊晉身[[欧足联欧洲联赛|欧足联欧洲联赛]]。2013年6月重返[[英格蘭|英格蘭]]出掌[[英冠|英冠]]球會[[米尔沃尔足球俱乐部|米禾爾]]。

==生平==
===球會===
盧馬斯在[[德國|德國]][[汉诺威|汉诺威]]出生，而在[[北愛爾蘭|北愛爾蘭]]成長，並在[[倫敦德里郡|倫敦德里郡]]的[[Coleraine_F.C.|高利宁]]（Coleraine）接受足球訓練，年僅13歲為青年隊上陣時被時任[[曼彻斯特城足球俱乐部|曼城]]領隊[[比利·麥尼爾|比利·麥尼爾]]（Billy McNeill）相中，加盟後由青年隊起步，先成為青年隊[[隊長_(足球)|隊長]]，繼而成功晉身一隊<ref name="Five things">{{cite web |language=en |url=http://theawayend.net/articles/football/the-away-end-archive/929-five-things-you-didnt-know-about-st-johnstone-manager-steve-lomas |title=Five things you didn't know about St Johnstone manager Steve Lomas |publisher=''theawayend.net'' |date= |accessdate=2013-06-08 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160305053332/http://theawayend.net/articles/football/the-away-end-archive/929-five-things-you-didnt-know-about-st-johnstone-manager-steve-lomas |archive-date=2016-03-05 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>。盧馬斯在曼城最為人熟知是他的一個[[烏龍球|烏龍球]]導致球隊降班，於1995/96年球季煞科日，曼城主場對前列份子[[利物浦足球俱乐部|利物浦]]只可取得勝利才可保級，但開賽僅6分鐘，盧馬斯便笨拙地將[[史蒂夫·麦克马纳曼|史蒂夫·麦克马纳曼]]的傳中球撞入自己球門內而先落後，雖然最後球隊追平2-2和局完場，但賽前同分的護級對手[[南安普顿足球俱乐部|修咸頓]]和[[考文垂足球俱乐部|高雲地利]]同樣取得和局成績，於計算[[得失球差|得失球差]]後曼城宣佈降班<ref>{{cite web |language=en |url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/sport/blog/2012/aug/24/joy-of-six-liverpool-manchester-city |title=The Joy of Six: great Liverpool v Manchester City matches |publisher=''guardian.co.uk'' |date= |accessdate=2013-06-12}}</ref>；但人們忘記了上一場作客全憑盧馬斯一箭定江山以1-0擊敗[[阿士東維拉足球會|阿士東維拉]]才保留曼城在煞科日一線的保級希望<ref>{{cite web |language=en |url=http://www.transfermarkt.co.uk/en/aston-villa_manchester-city/index/spielbericht_1113008.html |title=Aston Villa Man City 0:1(0:0) |publisher=''transfermarkt.co.uk'' |date= |accessdate=2013-06-12}}</ref>。

===國家隊===
1994年4月21日盧馬斯首次代表[[北愛爾蘭足球代表隊|北愛爾蘭]]於[[1996年欧洲足球锦标赛|歐洲國家盃]]外圍賽在主場迎戰[[列支敦士登国家足球队|列支敦士登]]，盧馬斯射入球隊的第二球，協助北愛爾蘭大勝4-1<ref>{{cite web |language=en |url=http://int.soccerway.com/matches/1994/04/20/europe/european-championship-qualification/northern-ireland/liechtenstein/628870/ |title=NORTHERN IRELAND 4 - 1 LIECHTENSTEIN |publisher=''int.soccerway.com'' |date= |accessdate=2013-06-08}}</ref>。盧馬斯其後合共為北愛爾蘭上陣46場及射入3球，更成為國家隊的隊長。

===教練及領隊===
After leaving 基寧咸, Lomas took up a coaching role at [[诺维奇城足球俱乐部|諾域治]] before managing [[St_Neots_Town_F.C.|圣尼茨镇]].<ref>[http://www.stneotsfc.com/news/article229.html/ St Neots Town FC Official Website]</ref>{{dead link|date=November 2011}} from 23 March 2009, until July 2010, leading the club to their most successful season for 40 years.<ref>[http://www.stneotsfc.com/news/article618.html/ St Neots Town FC Official Website]</ref>{{dead link|date=November 2011}} He left the club briefly in January 2010, following the departure of chairman John Delaney, but returned after Delaney had been reinstated as Chairman. Lomas resigned from St Neots in July 2010 to pursue his ambition to manage at a league club. At the end of February 2011 he accepted the position of reserve team manager at [[西汉姆联足球俱乐部|韋斯咸]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.whufc.com/articles/20110228/lomas-returns_2236884_2304399 |title=Lomas returns | News | Latest News | News | West Ham United |publisher=Whufc.com |date= |accessdate=2011-03-04}}</ref>

In November 2011, Lomas signed a two-and-a-half year deal to manage [[蘇格蘭足球超級聯賽|蘇格蘭足球超級聯賽]] side [[圣庄士东足球俱乐部|聖莊士東]] following the departure of [[德雷克·麦克因斯|德雷克·麦克因斯]] to English [[英格蘭足球冠軍聯賽|英冠]] team [[布里斯托尔城足球俱乐部|布里斯托城]]. Shortly after signing the deal to become first team manager he was joined by former [[北愛爾蘭足球代表隊|北愛爾蘭]] goalkeeper and [[Lisburn_Distillery_F.C.|Lisburn Distillery]] manager [[Tommy_Wright_(Northern_Ireland_footballer)|湯美·胡禮]]（Tommy Wright） who had agreed to join the club as an assistant to Lomas.<ref>{{cite web|title=St Johnstone confirm Steve Lomas as manager|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/15526952.stm|publisher=BBC Sport|accessdate=3 November 2011}}</ref> In his first season as manager of St Johnstone, Lomas took the club to a top 6 finish for the first time since 1999.<ref>{{cite web|title=St Johnstone 2 - 1 Hearts: Saints secure top-six slot|url=http://www.scotsman.com/scotland-on-sunday/sport/st-johnstone-2-1-hearts-saints-secure-top-six-slot-1-2194733}}</ref>

After [[哈茨足球俱乐部|赫斯]] won the Scottish Cup, St Johnstone also qualified for the [[欧足联欧洲联赛|欧足联欧洲联赛]], entering the competition in the second qualifying round.<ref>{{cite web|title=Premier League and Football League: Ups and downs|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/mobile/football/17288116}}</ref> Initially there was confusion about who their opponents would be, because of an appeal to CAS by Turkish club, [[贝西克塔斯体操俱乐部|比錫達斯]], who had been banned from this season's European competition by UEFA.<ref>{{cite web|title=St Johnstone face Eskisehirspor unless Besiktas win appeal|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/sport/football/article-2164475/St-Johnstone-draw-Eskisehirspor-Europa-League-qualifying-round.html}}</ref> Turkish club, [[艾斯基沙希|艾斯基沙希]]（Eskisehirspor） were, however, confirmed as opponents of St Johnstone with the first leg to be played in Turkey on 19 July and the second leg at home at McDiarmid Park, Perth on 26 July 2012.<ref>{{cite web|title=Eskisehirspor confirmed as St Johnstone's Euro opponents|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/18747693}}</ref>

Despite a slow start to the 2012–13 season after many changes to the squad - 12 players were out of contract during the summer - St Johnstone had an excellent September, drawing with [[邓迪联足球俱乐部|登地聯]] and winning the next four matches, the first against [[凯尔特人足球俱乐部|些路迪]], ending [[罗斯郡足球俱乐部|羅斯郡]]'s 40 game unbeaten run and beating rivals [[邓迪足球俱乐部|登地]], as well as reaching the quarter finals of the [[蘇格蘭聯賽盃|蘇格蘭聯賽盃]]. On 4 October 2012, Lomas was named SPL Manager of the month, the first time a St Johnstone manager had won the award.<ref>{{cite web|title=St Johnstone's Steve Lomas named SPL Manager of the Month for September|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/19820221}}</ref> On beating the Manager of the Month "curse" in October to record 5th and 6th straight wins against [[圣米伦足球俱乐部|聖美倫]] and  [[基马诺克足球俱乐部|基爾馬諾克]], Steve Lomas achieved the best run of results at St Johnstone since 1971,<ref>{{cite web|title=St Johnstone boss Steve Lomas Praises record-equalling side|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/20018760}}</ref> reaching 2nd place in the SPL table behind Celtic.<ref>{{cite web|title=St Johnstone boss Steve Lomas: Second place is pleasing.. but it could be even better|url=http://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/sport/football/football-news/st-johnstone-boss-steve-lomas-1393942}}</ref>

In October 2012, Lomas' name began to be linked with vacant manager's positions in English clubs; first at [[伯恩茅斯足球俱乐部|般尼茅夫]]<ref>{{cite web|title=Bournemouth: Steve Cotterill return is unlikely|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/19844119}}</ref> then [[伯恩利足球俱乐部|般尼]]<ref>{{cite web|title=Lomas into Turf Frame|url=http://www.lancashiretelegraph.co.uk/sport/football/10003123.Lomas_into_Turf_frame/}}</ref> [[弗利特伍德镇足球俱乐部|費列活特]] <ref>{{cite web|title=Lomas Leading the way to replace Mellon at League Two Fleetwood|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/sport/football/article-2243058/Steve-Lomas-contender-new-Fleetwood-Town-manager.html}}</ref> [[唐卡斯特流浪足球俱乐部|唐卡士打]] <ref>{{cite web|title=St Johnstone Boss set for Doncaster move after Harry Redknapp Recommendation|url=http://www.dailyrocrd.co.uk/sport/football/football-news/st-Johnstone-boss-Steve-lomas-1524033}}</ref> and [[考文垂足球俱乐部|高雲地利]].

Lomas achieved a second successive top six SPL finish with a game to spare, and on the last game of the season on 19 May 2013, in his first full season in charge, a 2-0 victory over [[马瑟韦尔足球俱乐部|馬瑟韋爾]] gave St. Johnstone 3rd place in the SPL and [[欧足联欧洲联赛|欧足联欧洲联赛]] qualification, the first time in their history that St Johnstone had gained back to back [[European_football|European qualification]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Heaven and Perth|url=http://www.thescottishsun.co.uk/scotsol/homepage/sport/spl/4934397/Heaven-n-Perth.html}}</ref>
