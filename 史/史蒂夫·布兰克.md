'''史蒂夫·布兰克''' ({{lang-en|Steve Blank}}，{{bd|1953年|||catIdx=Blank}}) 是一个在加利福尼亚州佩斯卡德罗的[[硅谷|硅谷]]连续创业的企业家和学者。<ref name="FC">{{cite web|url=http://www.forbes.com/sites/jjcolao/2012/08/01/steve-blank-introduces-scientists-to-a-new-variable-customers/|title=Steve Blank Introduces Scientists to a new Variable: Customers|author=J.J. Colao |date=1 August 2012 |publisher=Forbes Magazine|accessdate=2 November 2012}}</ref>

史蒂夫·布兰克因开发了客户开发方法论，它启动了精益创业运动而知名。<ref name="NYT">{{cite web|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2010/04/25/business/25unboxed.html |title=The Rise of the Fleet-Footed Start-Up |author=Steve Lohr |date=24 April 2010|publisher=The New York Times |accessdate=2 November 2012}}</ref> 布兰克也是[[E.piphany|E.piphany]]的共同创始人。<ref name="SB">{{cite web|url=http://steveblank.com/about/ |title=About Steve |author=Steve Blank|year=2012 |publisher=Steve Blank |accessdate=2 November 2012}}</ref><ref name="IN">{{cite web |url=http://www.inc.com/author/steve-blank |title=The Lean Launchpad |author= |year=2012 |publisher=Inc. |accessdate=2 November 2012}}</ref>

布兰克有超过30年从事高科技产业。他建立或者参与8家[[创业公司|创业公司]]，其中4家是上市公司。<ref name="SB" /><ref name="HP">{{cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/steve-blank/ |title=Steve Blank |author=|year=2012 |publisher=The Huffington Post |accessdate=2 November 2012}}</ref>

布兰克[[AtGoogleTalks|在谷歌演讲]]，硅谷的神秘历史，分享了关于新兴硅谷创业公司创新的广阔内行视角。他发行了3本书：The Four Steps to the Epiphany, Not All Those Who Wander Are Lost和The Startup Owner's Manual。<ref name="WA">{{cite web|url=http://www.wamda.com/2012/10/struggling-to-find-a-business-model-for-your-idea-read-the-startup-owner-s-manual|title=Struggling to Find a Business Model for Your Idea? Read the Startup Owner's Manual |author=Kia Davis |date=1 October 2012 |publisher=Wamda|accessdate=2 November 2012}}</ref><ref name="ASGB">{{cite web|url=http://www.amazon.com/Steven-Gary-Blank/e/B002IBT21M/ref=ntt_dp_epwbk_0|title=Steven Gary Blank |author=Steve Blank |date= |publisher=Amazon|accessdate=2 November 2012}}</ref>

在[[斯坦福大学|斯坦福大学]]，布兰克教授客户开发的课程和著书，还是[[企业家精神|企业家精神]]的咨询副教授。<ref name="UCB">{{cite web|url=http://facultybio.haas.berkeley.edu/faculty-list/blank-steve|title=Faculty and Executive Leadership Directory: Steve G. Blank |author=|year=2012 |publisher=University of California, Berkely, Haas School of Business |accessdate=2 November 2012}}</ref><ref name="SUEC">{{cite web |url=http://ecorner.stanford.edu/author/steve_blank |title=Steve Blank: Serial Entrepreneur |author= |date=1 October 2008 |publisher=Ecorner: Stanford University's Entrepreneurship Corner |accessdate=2 November 2012 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121027190625/http://ecorner.stanford.edu/author/steve_blank |archivedate=2012年10月27日 }}</ref> 目前，他在Haas School of Business, [[柏克萊加州大學|柏克萊加州大學]], [[哥伦比亚大学|哥伦比亚大学]]和[[加州理工學院|加州理工學院]]授课。

==受欢迎==
布兰克在众多企业家和学生眼里公认为"硅谷教父"之一。他的书籍，博客和访谈常常在全球新闻出版社所引用和开设专栏，例如[[路透社|路透社]], [[CNBC|CNBC]], [[MSNBC|MSNBC]], [[华尔街日报|华尔街日报]], [[TechCrunch|TechCrunch]],[[纽约时报|纽约时报]], [[Inc._Magazine|Inc. Magazine]], [[福布斯|福布斯]], 和[[连线|连线]]。<ref name="FC" /><ref name="NYT" /><ref name="IN" /><ref name="WSJ">{{cite web|url=http://blogs.wsj.com/in-charge/2012/04/25/live-chat-when-to-pivot-when-to-persevere-with-your-business-idea/|title=Live Chat: When to Pivot, When to Persevere With Your Business Idea|author=Demetria |date=25 April 2012 |publisher=The Wall Street Journal|accessdate=2 November 2012}}</ref> 他主持过售罄一空的各种演讲会和关于[[企业家精神|企业家精神]]和客户开发方法论的大会。<ref name="COM">{{cite web |url= http://www.commonwealthclub.org/node/62981 |title=Steve Blank: How to Build a Great Company, Step by Step |author= |date=14 August 2012 |publisher=The Commonwealth Club of California |accessdate=2 November 2012}}</ref><ref name="XCO">{{cite web|url=http://www.meetup.com/SanDiego-Tech-Founders/events/16359145/|title=Steve Blank meets San Diego |author= |date=9 February 2011|publisher=Meetup |accessdate=2 November 2012}}</ref><ref name="XCO"/><ref name="TEC">{{cite web |url=http://tec20120503.eventbrite.com/ |title=[TEC-PaloAlto] Legendary Steve Blank Presents His New Book! |author= |date=3 May 2012 |publisher=Eventbrite |accessdate=2 November 2012}}</ref><ref name="EVB">{{cite web |url=http://steveblankrsa.eventbrite.com/ |title=Steve Blank Presentation at Rockstart Accelerator, Amsterdam |author= |date=6 July 2012 |publisher=Eventbrite |accessdate=2 November 2012}}</ref>
