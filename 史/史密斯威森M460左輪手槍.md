'''史密斯威森M460'''（{{Lang-en|'''Smith & Wesson Model 460'''}}）是一系列由[[美国|美国]]槍械製造商[[史密斯&威森|史密斯&威森]]所研製及生產的5發式“X型[[机匣|底把]]”[[扳機#雙動操作|雙動操作]]式大[[口徑|口徑]][[左輪手槍|左輪手槍]]，發射{{Link-en|.460 S&W馬格南|.460 S&W Magnum}}或火力相對較弱的{{Link-en|.454卡蘇爾|.454 Casull}}、{{Link-en|.45柯爾特|.45 Colt}}和{{Link-en|.45斯科菲爾德|.45 Schofield}}這四種[[子彈|子彈]]。

史密斯威森M460被設計為一把適用於非洲和阿拉斯加的狩獵和危險遊戲的防衛型左輪手槍。該左輪手槍建構於史密斯&威森公司中被稱為X型底把的最大最強的平台上，代表著史密斯&威森、Hornady和Cor-Bon的共同努力。<ref name="SupicaNahas2006">{{cite book|last1=Supica|first1=Jim|last2=Nahas|first2=Richard|title=Standard Catalog of Smith & Wesson|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=aUb20H1UFCIC&pg=PA353|date=20 December 2006|publisher=Gun Digest Books|location=Iola, Wisconsin|isbn=1-4402-2700-4|page=353}}</ref>

== 設計細節 ==
史密斯威森M460的基本設計是以其他的X型[[机匣|底把]]左輪手槍為基礎，也就是與其相對應的[[史密斯威森M500左輪手槍|史密斯威森M500]] {{Link-en|.500 S&W馬格南|.500 S&W Magnum}}[[口徑|口徑]]左輪手槍。<ref name=odl>{{cite web|url=http://www.outdoorlife.com/articles/gear/hunting/2007/09/gun-review-smith-wessons-460xvr-revolver|year=2007|publisher=Outdoor Life|title= Gun Review: Smith & Wesson's 460XVR Revolver}}</ref><ref name="Ramage2006">{{cite book|last1=Ramage|first1=Ken|last2=Taffin|first2=John|authorlink2=John Taffin|title=Handguns 2007|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=aBIFulEGU98C&pg=PA47|date=16 November 2006|publisher=Gun Digest Books|isbn=0-89689-415-0|page=47}}</ref> 

該槍可藉由槍管端部的孔和原廠的[[氖|氖]]瞄準具來區分出M460。除了.460 S&W馬格南子彈以外，該左輪手槍也能裝填{{Link-en|.454卡蘇爾|.454 Casull}}、{{Link-en|.45柯爾特|.45 Colt}}和{{Link-en|.45斯科菲爾德|.45 Schofield}}[[彈藥|彈藥]]。<ref name="SupicaNahas2006"/><ref>{{cite news|title=Smith & Wesson unveils monster .460 caliber gun: The ‘Backpack Cannon’|first= Cheryl K|last= Chumley |newspaper= The Washington Times|date= January 21, 2014|url=http://www.washingtontimes.com/news/2014/jan/21/smith-wesson-unveils-monster-460-caliber-gun-backp/}}</ref><ref>http://www.gunsandammo.com/video/new-smith-wesson-460xvr-model-929-performance-center-revolvers/ New Smith & Wesson 460XVR & Model 929 Performance Center Revolvers. January 22nd, 2014.</ref><ref name=odl/>史密斯威森指出，M460 XVR（XVR，全寫：'''X'''treme '''V'''elocity '''R'''evolver，意為：極限初速左輪手槍）是「當今世界初速最高的批量生產左輪手槍」，這是由於其發射200[[格令|格令]][[彈頭|彈頭]]的[[槍口初速|槍口初速]]和{{Link-en|槍口能量|Muzzle energy}}分別達到710.18[[米每秒|米／秒]]（2,330[[英尺每秒|英尺／秒]]）和3,253.968[[焦耳|焦耳]]（2,400英尺·磅），而成為世界上最強大的.45口徑批量生產左輪手槍。<ref name="SupicaNahas2006"/><ref name=odl/><ref name="BarnesMann2012">{{cite book|last1=Barnes|first1=Frank C.|last2=Mann|first2=Richard A.|title=Cartridges of the World: A Complete Illustrated Reference for More Than 1,500 Cartridges|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=bjhy8uv196IC&pg=PA362|date=5 October 2012|publisher=Gun Digest Books|location=Iola, Wisconsin|isbn=1-4402-3059-5|page=362}}</ref><ref name="Cunningham2012">{{cite book|last=Cunningham|first=Grant|title=Gun Digest Shooter's Guide to Handguns|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=y1WjqnF31iwC&pg=PA84|date= 2012|publisher=Gun Digest Books|location=Iola, wisconsin|isbn=1-4402-3276-8|page=84}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Beauty of the beast: S&W .460 XVR|first=Gordon |last=Hutchinson |url=http://www.louisianasportsman.com/details.php?id=96}}</ref>

這把左輪手槍的槍管膛線獨一無二，它一開始的[[膛線|膛線]][[膛線纏距|纏距]]為纏距較慢的1：100，然後逐漸地加快到1：20，以適應彈藥的高膛壓性質。<ref name="SupicaNahas2006"/>

當它在2005年首次亮相以後，S&W M460贏得了射擊工業學院年度手槍優秀獎。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Academy of excellence honors industry's best|first=Roy|last=Huntington |journal=Shooting Industry|publisher=FMG Publications|year=2005|volume=20|issue=5|page=98}}</ref>

== 衍生型 ==
史密斯&威森提供了這種手槍的多個衍生型。有些短槍管型號，就像M460 ES型旨在用作小型飛機應該會在有著如阿拉斯加熊的野獸的國家土地以上墜毀時的求生套件。<ref name="RadielovicPrasac2012">{{cite book|last1=Radielovic|first1=Marko|last2=Prasac|first2=Max|title=Big-Bore Revolvers|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=V89WYyn1GeEC&pg=PA225|date=31 August 2012|publisher=Gun Digest Books|location=Iola, Wisconsin|isbn=1-4402-2856-6|page=225|chapter=Survival Tools}}</ref>相對的，其他槍管如長達14英吋的槍管型則旨在用作主要狩獵武器。<ref name="Schoby2008">{{cite book|last=Schoby|first=Mike|title=Do-It-Yourself Dream Hunts: Plan Like An Outfitter And Hunt For Less|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=RExDLQvADAkC&pg=PA60|date=8 July 2008|publisher=Krause Publications|location=Iola, Wisconsin|isbn=0-89689-641-2|page=60}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|title=The Battle Of The Hand Cannons|first=Russ|last=Thurman |journal=Shooting Industry|publisher=FMG Publications|year=2005|volume=20|issue=2|page=35}}</ref>
* M460[[橄榄绿|橄榄绿]]攜帶組合型
* M460 ES應急求生套件型
* M460 V 5英吋槍管型，裝有[[砲口制動器|槍口制退器]]（V的意思是槍管長度為5英吋）
* M460 XVR 8{{frac|3|8}}英吋槍管型，裝有[[砲口制動器|槍口制退器]]（XVR的意思是極限初速左輪手槍）
* M460 XVR性能中心10{{frac|1|2}}英吋槍管型，裝有[[砲口制動器|槍口制退器]]、{{Link-en|槍背帶|Sling (firearms)}}旋轉環和頂部[[皮卡汀尼導軌|皮卡汀尼導軌]]
* M460 XVR性能中心12英吋槍管型，裝有{{Link-en|槍背帶|Sling (firearms)}}旋轉環和頂部與底部[[皮卡汀尼導軌|皮卡汀尼導軌]]
* M460 XVR性能中心14英吋槍管型，裝有[[砲口制動器|槍口制退器]]、{{Link-en|槍背帶|Sling (firearms)}}旋轉環、頂部與底部[[皮卡汀尼導軌|皮卡汀尼導軌]]和[[兩腳架|兩腳架]]
* M460骨頭收集者型，裝有[[槍管|槍管]]頂部導軌、[[無溝槽式彈巢|無溝槽式]][[弹巢|弹巢]]、[[砲口制動器|槍口制退器]]、鉻製淚滴型{{Link-en|擊錘|Hammer (firearms)}}、鍍鉻扳機和{{Link-en|槍背帶|Sling (firearms)}}旋轉環
