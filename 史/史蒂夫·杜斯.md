'''史蒂夫·杜斯'''（Steve Doocy，{{bd|1956年|10月19日}}）是[[美國|美國]]的一位電視新聞節目主持人和暢銷書作家。

==生平==
1956年出生在愛奧瓦州<ref>{{cite web |url=http://english.360elib.com/datu/P/EM303002.pdf |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2015-12-11 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151222084031/http://english.360elib.com/datu/P/EM303002.pdf |archivedate=2015-12-22 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://dannerfuneralhome.net/obituary/1507|title=James E. Doocy - Obituary - Danner Funeral Home|publisher=|accessdate=September 29, 2016}}</ref>，成長在堪薩斯州<ref name="CJ">{{cite news| url=http://events.cjonline.com/events/clubs-and-meetings/fox-news-host-steve-doocy-be-honored-distinguished-kansan-year| newspaper=Topeka Capital-Journal| title=Fox News host Steve Doocy to be honored as Distinguished Kansan of the Year| date=January 24, 2014| accessdate=August 4, 2015}}</ref>，畢業於堪薩斯大學並擁有新聞學學士學位。他曾服務於CBS和NBC等電視台，在1996年加盟福斯新聞台。杜斯是兩本紐約時報暢銷書的作者。他已經結婚並有三個孩子<ref>{{cite web| last=Spelling| first=Ian| url=http://www.bergen.com/Putting_Down_Roots_Fox__Friends_co-anchor_Steve_Doocy_is_right_at_home_in_Bergen.html |title=Putting Down Roots: Fox & Friends co-anchor Steve Doocy is right at home in Bergen| publisher=Bergen| date=August 10, 2011| accessdate=September 20, 2011}}</ref>，信奉羅馬天主教<ref>[http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jKyLAErDqoc Fox and Friends: "Timothy Cardinal Dolan on the meaning of Christmas"] December 24, 2013 | Minute 3:30 of interview: ''"I'm Catholic. I'm a lector in our church"''</ref>。
