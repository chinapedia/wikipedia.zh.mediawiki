'''史提夫·特維格'''（Stephen Twigg，{{bd|1966年|12月25日}}）是一位[[英格蘭|英格蘭]]政治人物<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.dfes.gov.uk/pns/DisplayPN.cgi?pn_id=2004_0229|title=Stephen Twigg appointed Minister for School Standards|accessdate=9 November 2007|work=Department for Children, Schools and Families|publisher=[[Department_for_Children,_Schools_and_Families|Department for Children, Schools and Families]]}}</ref>，他的黨籍是[[工黨_(英國)|工黨]]。自2010年開始，他擔任[[利物浦西德比_(英國國會選區)|利物浦西德比]]選區選出的英國下議院議員。他是一位已經出櫃的同性戀者<ref name=guardian1>{{cite news|title=Twigg puts down new roots|author=Polly Curtis|url=http://education.guardian.co.uk/schools/story/0,5500,1375256,00.html|date=16 December 2004|accessdate=27 July 2007|publisher=The Guardian|location=London|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090630013226/http://www.guardian.co.uk/education/2004/dec/16/schools.uk8|archivedate=2009年6月30日|deadurl=yes}}</ref>。
