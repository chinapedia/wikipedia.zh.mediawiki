'''烏赫爾布羅德槍擊案'''，是指2015年2月24日，於[[捷克|捷克]]當地時間下午12:30左右，發生在[[兹林州|兹林州]][[烏赫爾布羅德|烏赫爾布羅德]]鎮內的「友誼」（Družba）餐廳的槍擊事件<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.reuters.com/article/2015/02/24/us-czech-shooting-idUSKBN0LS1OJ20150224|title=Gunman kills eight in Czech restaurant then kills himself|agency=Reuters|accessdate=24 February 2015}}</ref>。該起事件一共造成包含兇手茲德內克（Zdeněk K.，63歲）在內的9人死亡，1人受傷<ref name=BBC中文網>{{cite news|title=捷克餐廳發生槍擊案8人喪生 槍手自殺|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/zhongwen/trad/world/2015/02/150224_czeck_shooting|accessdate=2015-02-25|newspaper=BBC中文網|date=2015-02-24}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://bigstory.ap.org/article/168fb416b841447d870ccc9890198bbc/mayor-8-dead-czech-restaurant-shooting|title=9 dead including gunman in Czech restaurant shooting|work=The Big Story|accessdate=24 February 2015}}</ref>。烏赫爾布羅德鎮位於首都[[布拉格|布拉格]]東南方約300[[公里|公里]]處，鄰近[[斯洛伐克|斯洛伐克]]邊界<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mail.com/int/news/world/3375656-mayor-8-dead-czech-restaurant-shooting.html#.2394-stage-set1-2|title=9 dead including gunman in Czech restaurant shooting |publisher=mail.com|accessdate=24 February 2015}}</ref>。
