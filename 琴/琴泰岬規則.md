'''琴泰岬規則'''（{{lang-en|Mull of Kintyre rule}}）是傳說中由[[英國電影分級委員會|英國電影分級委員會]]（BBFC）制定的[[猥褻物|猥褻物]]非正式規則。這一規定要求影視作品中陰莖的勃起角度不得比[[蘇格蘭|蘇格蘭]]的[[琴泰岬半島|琴泰岬半島]]更大<ref>{{cite news | last = Armstrong | first = Rebbeca | url = http://www.independent.co.uk/news/people/hit-and-run/hit-amp-run-seen-but-not-hard--the-big-penis-debate-1771105.html | title = The Censor Goes Public | work = The Independent | date = 2009-08-13 | accessdate = 2012-09-26 }}</ref>。據[[胡爾大學|胡爾大學]]的約翰博伊魯斯教授稱，BBFC是在1992年制定了這項規則<ref>{{Cite web |last=Hoyles |first=John |date=2000-09-29 |url=http://www.moviemail.com/film/dvd+blu-ray/In-the-Realm-of-the-Senses/#7659 |title=THE BBFC, PORNOGRAPHY AND CENSORSHIP(Customer Reviews) |publisher=Movie Mail UK |accessdate=2012-09-26 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130304061911/http://www.moviemail.com/film/dvd+blu-ray/In-the-Realm-of-the-Senses#7659 |archivedate=2013-03-04 }}</ref>。但另一方面，BBFC在2000年否定了有這一規則<ref>{{cite news | title = WH Smith suffers from video nasties <!-- | authorlink = John O'Reilly (author) --> | first = John | last = O'Reilly | url = http://www.v3.co.uk/vnunet/analysis/2132026/wh-smith-suffers-video-nasties | publisher = [[VNU|VNU Business Publications Ltd.]] | date = 2000-10-12 }}</ref>。
