'''齒緣癩頸龜屬'''（'''''Elseya'''''<ref name=gray67>Gray, J.E. (1867) Description of a new Australian tortoise (''Elseya latisternum''). Ann. Mag. Nat. Hist. (3) 20: 43-45.</ref>）是[[蛇頸龜科|蛇頸龜科]]下的一個[[屬|屬]]，該屬的[[龜|龜]]發現於[[澳大利亞|澳大利亞]]北部及東北部以及[[新幾內亞|新幾內亞]]的河流中。<ref name=TGL>Thomson, S., Georges, A. and C. Limpus, (2006). A New Species of Freshwater Turtle in the Genus ''Elseya'' (Testudines: Chelidae) from Central Coastal Queensland, Australia. ''[[Chelonian_Conservation_and_Biology|Chelon. Conserv. Biol.]]'' '''5'''(1): 74–86. [http://aerg.canberra.edu.au/reprints/2005_Thomson_etal_Burnett_Elseya.pdf PDF fulltext] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070829225524/http://aerg.canberra.edu.au/reprints/2005_Thomson_etal_Burnett_Elseya.pdf |date=2007-08-29 }}</ref> 它們的嘴部構造特殊並有齒槽。<ref name=TWG>Thomson, S., White, A. & Georges, A (1997) Re-Evaluation of ''Emydura lavarackorum'': Identification of a Living Fossil. Memoirs of the Queensland Museum (1997),42(1):327-336</ref> 其[[模式種|模式種]]為[[齒緣癩頸龜|齒緣癩頸龜]]。

== 分類學 ==

齒緣癩頸龜屬下有8[[種|種]]：

* [[齒緣癩頸龜|齒緣癩頸龜]]（'' Elseya dentata''  Gray, 1863<ref name=gray63>Gray, J.E. 1863. On the species of Chelymys from Australia, with the description of a new species. Annals and Magazine of Natural History. 3(12):98-99</ref>）
* [[布氏癩頸龜|布氏癩頸龜]]（''Elseya branderhorsti''  Ouwens, 1914<ref>Ouwens, P. A. 1914. List of Dutch East Indian Chelonians in the Buitenzong Zoological Museum. Contributions a la Faune des Indes Neelandaises. 1:29-32.</ref>）
* [[歐文癩頸龜|歐文癩頸龜]]（''Elseya irwini'' Cann, 1997<ref>Cann, J. 1997. Irwin's Turtle. Monitor 9(1):36-40.</ref>）
* [[拉氏癞颈龟|拉氏癞颈龟]]（''Elseya lavarackorum''  White & Archer, 1994<ref>White, A. & Archer, M. 1994. ''Emydura lavarackorum'', a new Pleistocene turtle ([[Pleurodira|Pleurodira]]: [[Chelidae|Chelidae]]) from fluviatile deposits at Riversleigh, Northwestern Queensland. Records of the South Australian Museum 160-167.</ref> ）
* [[白喉癞颈龟|白喉癞颈龟]]（'' Elseya albagula''  Thomson, Georges & Limpus, 2006<ref name=TGL/>）
* [[新几内亚癞颈龟|新几内亚癞颈龟]]（''Elseya novaeguineae'' Meyer, 1874.<ref>Meyer, A.B. 1874. ''Platemys novaeguineae'' sp. nov. Dr W.H. Peters legte vor: Eine mitteilung von Hrn. Adolf Bernhard Meyer uber die von ihm auf Neu-Guinea under den Inseln Jobi, Mysore und Mafoor im Jahre 1873 gesammelten Amphibien. Monatsber. Konig. Preuss. Akad. Wiss. Berlin. 39:128-140.</ref>）新幾內亞癩頸龜曾在2010年被重新劃分到[[寬胸癩頸龜屬|寬胸癩頸龜屬]]下，<ref name=gt10>Georges, A. & Thomson, S. 2010. [http://www.mapress.com/zootaxa/2010/f/zt02496p037.pdf Diversity of Australasian freshwater turtles, with an annotated synonymy and keys to species.] Zootaxa  2496: 1–37.</ref> 但是不久被移回。[[舒氏癩頸龜|舒氏癩頸龜]]（''Elseya schultzei'' Vogt, 1911）<ref>Vogt, T. 1911. ''Emydura schultzei'', sp. nov. Reptilien und Amphibien aus Neu Guinea. Sber. ges. naturf. Freunde, Berl. 9:410-412.</ref> 是新幾內亞癩頸龜的亞種。<ref>Artner, H. 2008. The world's extant turtle species, Part 1. Emys, 15, 4-32.</ref>
* †'' Elseya nadibajagu''  Thomson & Mackness, 1999<ref>Thomson, S. & Mackness, B. (1999) Fossil Turtles from the Early Pliocene Bluff Downs Local Fauna, with a description of a new species of ''Elseya''. Trans. R. Soc. S. Aust. 123(3), 101-105, 30 November 1999.</ref>
* †''Elseya uberima''  De Vis, 1897<ref>De Vis, C.W. 1897. The extinct freshwater turtles of Queensland. Annals of the Queensland Museum. 3:3-7.</ref>
