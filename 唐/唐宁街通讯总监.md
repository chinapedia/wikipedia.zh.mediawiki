'''唐宁街通讯总监''' （'''{{lang-en|Downing Street Director of Communications}}'''）是[[英国首相|英国首相]]的{{le|通讯主管|Director of communications}}。该职位由政治任命的[[英国政府特别顾问|特别顾问]]担任。2017年4月{{le|凯蒂·佩里尔|Katie Perrior}}辞职后，<ref name="PRWeek 2017">{{cite news|title=Katie Perrior resigns as Downing Street comms chief after PM announces snap election|url=http://www.prweek.com/article/1430771/katie-perrior-resigns-downing-street-comms-chief-pm-announces-snap-election|accessdate=21 April 2017|work=[[PRWeek|PRWeek]]|date=18 April 2017}}</ref>该职一直空缺到7月才由{{le|罗比·吉布|Robbie Gibb}}接任。<ref name="BBC News 2017">{{cite news|title=BBC Daily Politics editor Robbie Gibb to join No 10|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-politics-40517721|accessdate=6 July 2017|work=[[BBC_News_Online|BBC News Online]]|date=6 July 2017}}</ref>

== 历史 ==
这个职位设立于2000年，第一位担任者是曾经担任{{le|唐宁街新闻秘书|Downing Street Press Secretary}}和{{le|首相发言人|Prime Minister's Spokesman}}的[[阿拉斯泰尔·坎贝尔|阿拉斯泰尔·坎贝尔]]。最初该职位有权向[[英国公务员服务|公务员]]发命令，这项权力在坎贝尔2003年离开后被取消。<ref name="BBC 2003">{{cite news|title=No 10 press shake-up unveiled|trans_title=10号的新闻没有发布|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/uk_politics/3076574.stm|accessdate=8 April 2015|work=[[BBC_News_Online|BBC News Online]]|date=3 September 2003}}</ref>坎贝尔任该职时，还有权列席[[内阁|内阁]]会议。
