'''甲癬'''（onychomycosis），俗稱'''臭甲'''、'''灰指甲'''，泛指受到[[真菌|真菌]]感染的[[指甲|指甲]]，通常影響[[腳趾|腳趾]]，但[[手指|手指]]甲也有可能出現。兩成[[指甲病|指甲病]]是由甲癬所引起。

== 起因 ==
甲癬的成因是真菌感染，主要細為分[[酵母菌|酵母菌]]感染、[[霉菌|霉菌]]感染，以及[[皮癬菌|皮癬菌]]感染，包括引起[[足癬|足癬]]的真菌。

大約三分之一的[[糖尿病|糖尿病]]人<ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Gupta AK, Konnikov N, MacDonald P, Rich P, Rodger NW, Edmonds MW, McManus R, Summerbell RC |title= Prevalence and epidemiology of toenail onychomycosis in diabetic subjects: A multicentre survey |journal= The British Journal of Dermatology |volume= 139 |issue= 4 |pages= 665–671 |year= 1998 |pmid= 9892911 |doi= 10.1046/j.1365-2133.1998.02464.x}}</ref>、56%的[[牛皮癬|牛皮癬]]患者會感染甲癬<ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Gupta AK, Lynde CW, Jain HC, Sibbald RG, Elewski BE, Daniel CR, Watteel GN, Summerbell RC |title= A higher prevalence of onychomycosis in psoriatics compared with non-psoriatics: A multicentre study |journal= The British Journal of Dermatology |volume= 136 |issue= 5 |pages= 786–789 |year= 1997 |pmid= 9205520 |doi= 10.1046/j.1365-2133.1997.6771624.x}}</ref>。
== 病徵 ==
甲癬的病徵有：趾甲變形、色變到灰褐色、暗黃色、趾甲變厚而難剪，但甲面變得很薄、很脆；有怪味，會痛。

它常易與單純的指甲畸形混淆<ref name="AADfive">{{Citation |author1= American Academy of Dermatology |author1-link= American Academy of Dermatology |date= February 2013 |title= Five Things Physicians and Patients Should Question |publisher= [[American_Academy_of_Dermatology|American Academy of Dermatology]] |work= [[Choosing_Wisely|Choosing Wisely]]: an initiative of the [[ABIM_Foundation|ABIM Foundation]] |page= |url= http://www.choosingwisely.org/doctor-patient-lists/american-academy-of-dermatology/ |accessdate= 5 December 2013}}. Which cites:*{{cite journal |vauthors=Roberts DT, Taylor WD, Boyle J |title= Guidelines for treatment of onychomycosis |journal= The British Journal of Dermatology |volume= 148 |issue= 3 |pages= 402–410 |year= 2003 |pmid= 12653730 |doi= 10.1046/j.1365-2133.2003.05242.x |url= http://www.bad.org.uk/library-media/documents/Onychomycosis_2003.pdf}}</ref>，此外[[指甲银屑病|指甲银屑病]]、[[扁平苔癣|扁平苔癣]]、[[接触性皮炎|接触性皮炎]]、甲床腫瘤以及[[黄甲综合征|黄甲综合征]]產生的症狀都可能與灰指甲有相似之處，為避免誤診需要实验室檢查。<ref name=AFP2013/>

==治療==

[[File:A_patient&#039;s_left_foot_-_after_ten_weeks_of_Terbinafine_oral_treatment.png|thumb]]

治療灰指甲的方法很多，灰指甲大部分是由真菌引起的，治療灰指甲的藥物都是能夠消滅指甲真菌的，目前所採用的主要藥物和方式是手術拔除指甲、內服藥物、外用藥物和中醫治療灰指甲

口服藥物主要包括[[特比萘芬|特比萘芬]]、[[依曲康唑|依曲康唑]]和[[氟康唑|氟康唑]]。治愈後較易復發（10–50%）。<ref name=AFP2013>{{cite journal |vauthors=Westerberg DP, Voyack MJ |title= Onychomycosis: current trends in diagnosis and treatment. |journal= American Family Physician |volume= 88 |issue= 11 |pages= 762–70 |date= Dec 1, 2013 |pmid= 24364524}}</ref>
