'''甲草胺'''（{{lang-en|Alachlor}}）是一种氯[[乙酰苯胺|乙酰苯胺]]类[[除草剂|除草剂]]，无味白色固体。其最大用途是去除大田中的[[一年生植物|一年生杂草]]与阔叶杂草，在美国是使用量第二大的除草剂<ref name="Consumer Factsheet on: ALACHLOR">{{Template:Cite web|url = http://www.epa.gov/safewater/pdfs/factsheets/soc/alachlor.pdf|title = Consumer Factsheet on: ALACHLOR|publisher = United States Environmental Protection Agency (EPA|work = National Primary Drinking Water Regulations|accessdate = 4 September 2015}}</ref>，但在[[欧盟|欧盟]]禁止使用<ref name="Zakim and Boyer's Hepatology: A Textbook of Liver Disease">{{Template:Cite web|url = https://books.google.com/books?id=zjYof6MJZkkC&pg=PA486&lpg=PA486&dq=alachlor+as+a+herbicide+has+been+banned+in+the+European+Union&source=bl&ots=BdwX6pmpJh&sig=7NHjY_KLuvb-VBA1YjLcS9cfGjU&hl=en&sa=X&ved=0CEoQ6AEwBmoVChMI2cubuv_dxwIVyBU-Ch0LLQ-w#v=onepage&q=alachlor%20as%20a%20herbicide%20has%20been%20banned%20in%20the%20European%20Union&f=false|title = Zakim and Boyer's Hepatology: A Textbook of Liver Disease|publisher = Saunders, 1990|date = 1990|accessdate = 4 September 2015|author = Thomas D. Boyer, Michael Peter Manns, Arun J. Sanyal, David Zakim|pages = 486}}</ref>。

甲草胺是一种[[酶抑制剂|酶抑制剂]]，通过抑制[[香叶基香叶基焦磷酸|香叶基香叶基焦磷酸]] (GGPP) 环化[[酶|酶]]来阻断[[赤霉素|赤霉素]]的合成。其在美国的[[商業名稱|商品名]]有Alanex、Bronco、Cannon、Crop Star、Intrro、Lariat、Lasso、Micro-Tech和Partner<ref name="Arnold P. Appleby 2002">Arnold P. Appleby, Franz Müller, Serge Carpy “Weed Control“ in Ullmann's Encyclopedia of Industrial Chemistry 2002, Wiley-VCH, Weinheim. {{Template:Doi|10.1002/14356007.a28_165}}</ref>。
