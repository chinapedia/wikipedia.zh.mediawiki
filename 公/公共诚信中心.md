'''公共诚信中心'''（{{lang-en|'''Center for Public Integrity'''}}，缩写作{{lang|en|'''CPI'''}}）是一个位于美国的非盈利新闻调查组织，其初衷是「揭示有权力的公众以及私人机构的滥权、腐败和玩忽职守等行为，以迫使他们诚实、正直、负责地行使权力，并且以公众利益为先」<ref name="about">{{cite web |url=http://www.iwatchnews.org/about |title=About The Center for Public Integrity |accessdate=2012-06-09 |work=Center for Public Integrity |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120603161449/http://www.iwatchnews.org/about |archivedate=2012-06-03 }}</ref>。其初始成员只有50人，但CPI現在是美国最大的无党派非盈利的调查中心之一<ref name="huff">{{cite web |url = http://www.poynter.org/latest-news/mediawire/155573/cpi-reduces-staff-to-compensate-for-2-million-budget-hole/ |title = PI reduces staff to compensate for $2 million budget hole |accessdate=2012-06-09 |date=December 9, 2011 |publisher=Poynter Institute }}</ref>。它赢得了2014年的[[普利策奖|普利策奖]]——普利策调查性报道奖<ref name="Pulitzer.org">{{cite web |url = http://www.pulitzer.org/citation/2014-Investigative-Reporting|title=The 2014 Pulitzer Prize Winners Investigative Reporting |accessdate=2014-07-29 |work=[[普立茲獎|Pulitzer.org]] }}</ref>。
