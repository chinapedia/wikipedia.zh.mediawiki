'''公社马赛曲'''是[[巴黎公社|巴黎公社]]在1871年使用并制作的马赛曲的一种版本，为巴黎公社的国歌。

'''英语译词'''

French! no longer be slaves!,<br>  
Under the flag, let us rally,<br>  
Under our feet, break barriers,<br>  
Eighty-nine, wake up (重复)<br>  
Knock the last anathema<br>  
Those who, by a stupid pride,<br>  
Opened the dark coffin<br>  
Our brothers died without emblem.<br>  

副歌: Singing freedom,<br>  
Defend the city,<br>  
March, march, without a sovereign,<br>   
The people will have bread.<br>  

For twenty years you slumber,<br>  
French people, wake up,<br>  
The time ringing in your ears,<br>  
This is the hour of salvation for you. (重复)<br>  
People standing! that victory<br>  
Guide to battle your proud warriors,<br>  
Go to France its laurels,<br>  
His rank and ancient glory.<br>  
(副歌)

The miles you see these brave<br>  
Walk to immortality,<br>  
The master sold his slave,<br>  
And we sing the freedom. (aa)<br>  
No, more than kings, crowns more,<br>  
Enough blood, enough of mourning,<br>  
That forgetting in his cold shroud<br>  
Envelope scepters and thrones.<br>  
(副歌)

More tears in the cottages<br>  
When the conscript from the home;<br>  
Leave, leave, poor mothers<br>  
Near their son to kneel. (aa)<br>  
Progress! Thy bright light<br>  
Descend on all our children,<br>  
That man is free in his fields,<br>  
That the tax is no longer barrier.<br>  
(副歌)

Do not exalt your most new laws,<br>  
The people are deaf to your accents,<br>  
Enough solemn phrases,<br>  
Enough empty words. (aa)<br>  
French, Wimbledon,<br>  
It is the conquest of your rights,<br>  
These are your best plays<br>  
That can record the history.<br>  
(副歌)

People, that the honor be thy guide,<br>  
That justice be thy laws,<br>  
That the worker is no longer hungry<br>  
Mantle which covered our kings. (重复)<br>  
As his own dark night of the<br>  
Where chained royalty,<br>  
The Torch of Liberty<br>  
Rises and shines on the world!
