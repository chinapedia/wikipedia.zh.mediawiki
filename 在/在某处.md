《'''在某处'''》（英語：''Somewhere''）是一部2010年的[[美國電影|美國]]劇情片，[[索菲亚·科波拉|索菲亚·科波拉]]执导，[[斯蒂芬·多尔夫|斯蒂芬·多尔夫]]、[[艾丽·范宁|艾丽·范宁]]主演。在美国于2010年12月22日公映。2010年9月11日，该片获得[[第67届威尼斯国际电影节|第67届威尼斯国际电影节]][[金獅獎_(威尼斯影展)|金獅獎]]。

==剧情==
Johnny是一名Hollywood的演员，一直过着放纵与空虚的生活。直到有一天他遇上他的11岁的女儿Cleo（她是他与一名女人发生关系后意外怀孕的产物），在与她一起过了一段日子后，他开始审视自己的生活状态。

==制作==
据称该片中的许多场景来源于导演的童年生活。<ref name="Corriere">{{cite journal | last=Grassi | first=Giovanni | url=http://archiviostorico.corriere.it/2009/agosto/26/Sofia_Coppola_mio_nuovo_film_co_9_090826048.shtml | title=Sofia Coppola: il mio nuovo film sulle orme del Fellini più dark | journal=[[Corriere_della_Sera|Corriere della Sera]] | date=August 26, 2009 | accessdate=September 1, 2009 | archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/5qPRgpGyT?url=http://archiviostorico.corriere.it/2009/agosto/26/Sofia_Coppola_mio_nuovo_film_co_9_090826048.shtml | archivedate=2010-06-11 | author= | dead-url=no }}</ref>[[费德里科·费里尼|费德里科·费里尼]]的《[[勾魂懾魄|勾魂懾魄]]》（''Toby Dammit''）<ref name="Corriere" />和[[彼得·博格丹诺维奇|彼得·博格丹诺维奇]]（Peter Bogdanovich）的《[[纸月亮_(电影)|纸月亮]]》（''Paper Moon''）<ref>{{cite journal | last=Wilson | first=Owen | authorlink=Owen Wilson | url=http://www.interviewmagazine.com/film/stephen-dorff/5/ | title=Stephen Dorff | journal=[[Interview_(magazine)|Interview Magazine]] | date=August 2009 | accessdate=September 1, 2009 | archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/5qPRpMKMR?url=http://www.interviewmagazine.com/film/stephen-dorff/5/ | archivedate=2010-06-11 | author= | dead-url=no }}</ref>同样影响了该片。

2009年6月和7月，电影在洛杉矶和意大利开拍。<ref>{{cite news |title = Sofia Coppola books Marmont film |first = Tatiana |last = Siegel |url = http://www.variety.com/article/VR1118002519.html?categoryid=13&cs=1&nid=2564 |work = [[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]] |date = April 16, 2009 |accessdate = June 11, 2010 }}</ref>据称[[凤凰乐团|凤凰乐团]]参加了该片的配乐。<ref>{{cite news |title = Phoenix Talk Soundtrack To New Sofia Coppola Film, 'Somewhere' |first = James |last = Montgomery |url = http://www.mtv.com/news/articles/1637333/20100419/phoenix.jhtml |work = [[MTV_News|MTV News]] |date = April 19, 2010 |accessdate = June 11, 2010 |archiveurl = https://www.webcitation.org/5qPQDU0N5?url=http://www.mtv.com/news/articles/1637333/20100419/phoenix.jhtml |archivedate = 2010-06-11 |dead-url = no }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title = Grammy-winning band Phoenix rewarded for patience |first = Greg |last = Cochrane |url = http://news.bbc.co.uk/newsbeat/hi/music/newsid_10050000/newsid_10056700/10056729.stm |work = [[BBC_News|BBC News]] |date = February 8, 2010 |accessdate = June 11, 2010 |archiveurl = https://www.webcitation.org/5qPQJY1kW?url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/newsbeat/hi/music/newsid_10050000/newsid_10056700/10056729.stm |archivedate = 2010-06-11 |dead-url = no }}</ref>
