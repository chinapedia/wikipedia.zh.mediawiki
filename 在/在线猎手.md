Mitchell, Michele L. Ybarra |title= Online "predators" and their victims: Myths, realities, and implications for prevention and treatment |journal= [[American_Psychologist|American Psychologist]] |volume= 63 |issue= 2 |pages= 111–128 |id= |url= http://www.unh.edu/news/cj_nr/2008/feb/lw18internet.cfm |accessdate= 2008-04-21 |quote= |doi= 10.1037/0003-066X.63.2.111 |pmid= 18284279 |date=Feb–Mar 2008}}</ref>

网络上未成年人受害会包括[[儿童性诱拐|儿童性诱拐]]、[[人类性行为#法律議題|被成人要求性行为]]或性讨论、接收到不想要的[[色情|色情]]物质（如含有裸照的邮件等等），以及网上骚扰、威胁和其它不具有“自然‘性’”（not sexual in nature）却带来沮丧、恐惧和尴尬的冒犯性交流。<ref>"Snyder,H; Sickmund,M: Juvenile Offenders and Victims: 2006 National Report", p38. National Center for Juvenile Justice, March 2006.</ref>
