'''薩曼莎·魯斯維特'''，或译'''萨曼塔·卢思韦特''' （Samantha Louise Lewthwaite<ref name="Interpol">{{cite web|title=Lewthwaite, Samantha Louise: Identity Particulars|year=2013|work=Wanted List|publisher=[[Interpol|Interpol]]|accessdate=25 September 2013|url=http://www.interpol.int/Wanted-Persons/(wanted_id)/2013-52018}}</ref>，{{bd|1983年|12月5日|}} ）<ref name=Interpol/><ref name="Clements">{{cite news|title=Bomber had been thrown out of home|last=Clements|first=Andrea|work=[[The_Belfast_Telegraph|The Belfast Telegraph]]|location=Belfast|date=25 July 2005|page=1|url=http://www.belfasttelegraph.co.uk/imported/bomber-had-been-thrown-out-of-home-28218906.html|accessdate=25 September 2013}}</ref>，英國人，綽號“白寡婦”。

薩曼莎·魯斯維特是[[倫敦七七爆炸案|倫敦七七爆炸案]]自殺式爆炸襲擊者[[傑曼·林賽|傑曼·林賽]]（Germaine Lindsay）的妻子，也是[[恐怖主義|恐怖主義]]嫌疑人之一<ref>{{cite news|title=Was the 'white widow' among attackers?啊|last1=Pflanz|first1=Mike|last2=Laing|first2=Aislinn|last3=Rayner|first3=Gordon|last4=Whitehead|first4=Tom|work=[[The_Daily_Telegraph|The Daily Telegraph]]|location=London|date=24 September 2013|page=4}}</ref><ref name="Kirby">{{cite news|title=The 'quiet, likeable' man who was the King's Cross bomber|last=Kirby|first=Terry|work=[[The_Belfast_Telegraph|The Belfast Telegraph]]|location=Belfast|date=15 July 2005|page=1}}</ref><ref name=Telegraph>{{cite web | url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/uknews/terrorism-in-the-uk/9112824/Samantha-Lewthwaite-77-bomber-widow-previously-a-Home-Counties-girl.html | title=Samantha Lewthwaite: 7/7 bomber widow previously a 'Home Counties' girl | work=[[The_Daily_Telegraph|The Daily Telegraph]]|location=London | date=29 February 2012 | accessdate=24 September 2013 | last=Hough|first=Andrew}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Samantha Lewthwaite: shy schoolgirl to terrorism suspect|work=[[The_Sydney_Morning_Herald|The Sydney Morning Herald]]|location=Sydney|date=26 September 2013|accessdate=25 September 2013|url=http://www.smh.com.au/world/samantha-lewthwaite-shy-schoolgirl-to-terrorism-suspect-20130925-2udsn.html}}</ref>。

薩曼莎·魯斯維特是索馬利亞的[[伊斯蘭|伊斯蘭]]激進組織[[青年党_(索马里)|青年黨]]成員。她已被指控於[[2012年歐錦賽|2012年歐錦賽]]期間在蒙巴薩策劃使用[[手榴彈|手榴彈]]襲擊非[[穆斯林|穆斯林]]禮拜場所。

2014年11月，根據俄新社報導薩曼莎·魯斯維特在烏克蘭被民兵狙擊手擊斃，但[[福斯新聞頻道|福斯新聞頻道]]稱未獲證實。<ref>[http://www.foxnews.com/world/2014/11/12/white-widow-terrorist-killed-by-russian-sniper-reports-say/ 'White Widow' terrorist killed by Russian sniper, reports say]. [[Fox_News|Fox News]]. 12 November 2014</ref>
