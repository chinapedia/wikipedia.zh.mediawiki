'''薩摩亞全國聯賽'''（'''Samoa National League'''）是大洋洲島國薩摩亞國內最高級別足球聯賽。容量3500人的Toleafoa J.S Blatter Soccer Stadium是全國聯賽的唯一球場。<ref>[http://www.theguardian.com/football/2010/nov/10/which-stadiums-home-to-most-champions Which stadiums are home to the most champions?] at The Knowledge, Guardian Newspapers</ref>
