'''巴庫市街賽道'''（{{lang-en|Baku City Circuit}}，{{lang-az|Bakı Şəhər Halqası}}）<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.azernews.az/sports/89288.html|title=Baku City Circuit unveils logo|last=Orujova|first=Nigar|date=2015-10-30|publisher=Azernews|accessdate=2015-11-07}}</ref>是一條位於[[亞塞拜然|亞塞拜然]][[巴庫|巴庫]]的賽道。賽道長度為{{convert|6.003|km|mi}}，是目前一級方程式賽車賽程中第二長的賽道。<ref>{{cite web|title=F1: Bernie Ecclestone confirms Azerbaijan to replace axed Korea|url=http://www.auto123.com/en/racing-news/f1-bernie-ecclestone-confirms-azerbaijan-to-replace-axed-korea?artid=167344|work=www.auto123.com|accessdate=2014-05-29}}</ref>

==歷史==
===發展===
一級方程式賽車總裁兼首席執行官[[伯尼·埃克萊斯頓|伯尼·埃克萊斯頓]]在2013年12月認為這場比賽將在{{F1|2016}}舉行，但之後又表示，由於[[韓國大獎賽|韓國大獎賽]]主辦單位[[違約|違約]]，所以賽事將移至{{F1|2015}}。<ref>{{cite web|last=Prince|first=Max|title=Korea out, Azerbaijan in for 2015 Formula 1 calendar|url=http://www.roadandtrack.com/racing/racing-news/korea-out-azerbaijan-in-2015-f1|work=www.roadandtrack.com|date=2014-05-08|accessdate=2014-05-09}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Smith|first=Luke|title=Azerbaijan set to welcome Formula 1 in 2015; Korea scrapped|url=http://motorsportstalk.nbcsports.com/2014/05/08/azerbaijan-set-to-welcome-formula-1-in-2015-korea-scrapped/|work=motorsportstalk.nbcsports.com|publisher=[[NBC|NBC]]|date=2014-05-08|accessdate=2014-05-08}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Sylt|first=Christian|title=Exclusive: Korean GP dropped to make way for race in Azerbaijan|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/sport/motor-racing/exclusive-korean-gp-dropped-to-make-way-for-race-in-azerbaijan-9335087.html|work=www.independent.co.uk|publisher=[[The_Independent|The Independent]]|date=2014-05-07|accessdate=2014-05-08}}</ref>然而，根據2014年7月的消息指出這場賽事的登場將延後至2016年。<ref name="delay">{{cite web|title=Formula 1 - Azerbaijan Grand Prix debut delayed until 2016|url=https://uk.eurosport.yahoo.com/news/formula-1-azerbaijan-host-grand-prix-europe-2016-093717491--f1.html|website=uk.eurosport.yahoo.com|publisher=[[Reuters|Reuters]]|date=2014-07-25|accessdate=2014-07-25|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150708224213/https://uk.eurosport.yahoo.com/news/formula-1-azerbaijan-host-grand-prix-europe-2016-093717491--f1.html|archivedate=2015年7月8日|df=}}</ref>

===設計===
這條長約{{convert|6|km|mi|abbr=off}}、逆時針方向布局的賽道是由賽道設計師[[赫爾曼·提爾克|赫爾曼·提爾克]]所設計。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.formula1.com/news/headlines/2014/10/16459.html|title=Azerbaijan layout unveiled for Baku European Grand Prix in 2016|work=formula1.com|publisher=[[Formula_One_Group|Formula One Administration]]|date=2014-10-08|accessdate=2014-10-09}}</ref>這條賽道的起點毗鄰[[自由廣場_(巴庫)|自由廣場]]，之後環繞於{{le|政府大廈 (巴庫)|Government House, Baku|政府大廈}}周圍，接著向西至[[少女塔_(巴库)|少女塔]]。此時，車手將被導向一條狹窄的上坡路段，穿越並環繞{{le|老城 (巴庫)|Old City (Baku)|老城}}，接著來到一條長{{convert|2.2|km|mi|abbr=off}}的長直道——賽曆中最長的直道——沿著{{le|Neftchilar大道|Neftchilar Avenue}}回到起跑線。<ref>{{cite web|title=Азад Рагимов рассказал о примерной трассе бакинского этапа Формулы-1|url=http://www.azerisport.com/other/20140726125426178.html|website=www.azerisport.com|date=2014-07-26|accessdate=2014-07-26|language=ru}}</ref><ref name="delay"/>
