'''巴加'''（Baga）是位于[[尼日利亚|尼日利亚]]东北部[[博尔诺州|博尔诺州]]的一个小城镇，靠近[[乍得湖|乍得湖]]，距州府[[迈杜古里|迈杜古里]]约196公里。<ref name="study1">Mukhtar, Yakubu and Waziri A. Gazali. The Dynamics of Fish Trade in North-East Nigeria: A Case Study of Doron Baga, ''Berichte des Sonderforschungsbereichs'' 268, Band 14, Frankfurt a.M. 2000: 83-91</ref>在巴加镇6公里处有一个卖鱼的市场，该市场建立于2000年。<ref name="study1"/>巴加镇曾经处于乍得湖湖边，并且在20世纪六七十年代成为了一个渔业中心。但由于近来乍得湖面积的萎缩，使得当地渔民不得不放弃此地转移到其他地方或者是改行。<ref name="all1">Idris, Hamza et al. (30 June 2012)[http://allafrica.com/stories/201207111194.html?viewall=1 Nigeria: Northern Communities Losing Territory to Desert], ''[[Daily_Trust|Daily Trust]]'' (AllAfrica.com)</ref><ref name="slowd">Bomford, Andrew (14 April 2006). [http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/4906692.stm Slow death of Africa's Lake Chad], [[BBC|BBC]]("Just 30 years ago, water covered the whole area. Baga was a waterfront town. Now it is stranded many miles from the lake as the land around it becomes desert.")</ref><ref name="packup">Murray, Senan (15 January 2007). [http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/africa/6261447.stm Lake Chad fisherman pack up their nets], [[BBC|BBC]]</ref>

==恐怖袭击==
[[File:Lake_Chad_1973.jpg|right]]
2013年，尼日利亚政府军与极端组织[[博科圣地|博科圣地]]在巴加镇的战争中，造成了当地185人死亡，2000多座房屋被毁。
2015年初，[[博科圣地|博科圣地]]再次袭击当地，制造了“[[2015年巴加大屠杀|巴加大屠杀]]”，大约有2000多人遭杀害<ref>{{cite web | language = zh-cn | publisher = 网易新闻 | title =博科圣地在尼日利亚北部“屠城” 已杀2000余人| url = http://news.163.com/15/0110/18/AFKBGE8P00014JB5.html| date = 2015-01-10 | accessdate =  }}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.smh.com.au/world/boko-haram-massacre-thousands-says-amnesty-international-20150110-12loit.html|publisher=[[悉尼先驱晨报|悉尼先驱晨报]]|title=Boko Haram massacre thousands, says Amnesty International|date=10 January 2015|accessdate=11 January 2015}}</ref>，这是巴加历史上遭到的最大一起恐怖袭击，<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.amnesty.org/en/for-media/press-releases/nigeria-massacre-possibly-deadliest-boko-haram-s-history-2015-01-09-0|date=9 January 2015|accessdate=11 January 2015|title=Nigeria: Massacre possibly deadliest in Boko Haram’s history|publisher=[[国际特赦组织|国际特赦组织]]}}</ref>一些幸存的居民逃到了邻国[[乍得|乍得]]。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-africa-30672391|title=Boko Haram seizes army base in Nigeria town of Baga|publisher=BBC News|date=4 January 2015}}</ref>
