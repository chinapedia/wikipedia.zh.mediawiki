'''巴勒姆'''（Balham）是[[英格蘭|英格蘭]][[南倫敦|南倫敦]]的一個地區。巴勒姆分別屬於[[旺茲沃思倫敦自治市|旺茲沃思倫敦自治市]]和[[蘭貝斯倫敦自治市|蘭貝斯倫敦自治市]]。二戰期間巴勒姆曾遭到嚴重轟炸<ref>{{cite web|url=http://ww2today.com/14th-october-1940-disaster-at-balham-tube-station|title=14th October 1940: Disaster at Balham Tube station|work=ww2today.com}}</ref>。巴勒姆的中心地區有各種酒吧、餐館、商店等商業設施<ref>{{cite web|url=http://balham.com/listing/#map|title=Listings - Balham.com|work=Balham.com|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130503094455/http://balham.com/listing/#map|archivedate=2013-05-03}}</ref>。
