'''巴納德城堡'''（Barnard Castle）是[[英格蘭|英格蘭]][[達勒姆郡|達勒姆郡]]的一個城鎮，地名取自於附近的巴納德城堡。巴納德城堡是提斯達爾地區主要的城鎮，也是一個人氣的旅遊目的地<ref>Daily Mail (London)  10 January 2009 Saturday  1ST Edition  BRIC-A-BRAC BRITAIN;  NORMAN MILLER goes on a rummage to find the top ten towns for bargain hunters</ref>。當地最大的雇主是製藥巨頭[[葛蘭素史克|葛蘭素史克]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.devereuxarchitects.com/Work/Manufacturing/All-Manufacturing-Projects/Projects/GSK,-Barnard-Castle.aspx |website=Devereux Architects |title=GSK Barnard Castle |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140503190016/http://www.devereuxarchitects.com/Work/Manufacturing/All-Manufacturing-Projects/Projects/GSK%2C-Barnard-Castle.aspx |archivedate=2014-05-03 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/history/domesday/dblock/GB-404000-516000/page/7|title=BBC - Domesday Reloaded: A BARNARD CASTLE FACTORY|work=domesday}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.teesdalemercury.co.uk/Articles/2013/millions-of-pounds-to-be-invested-in-glaxo-plant-in-barnard-castle |title=Teesdale Mercury |work=teesdalemercury.co.uk |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140503192927/http://www.teesdalemercury.co.uk/Articles/2013/millions-of-pounds-to-be-invested-in-glaxo-plant-in-barnard-castle |archivedate=2014-05-03 }}</ref>。
