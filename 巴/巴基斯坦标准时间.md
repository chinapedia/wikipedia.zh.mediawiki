'''巴基斯坦标准时间'''是[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]]的标准时间，[[时区|时区]]上属於[[UTC+5|东五区]]，比[[协调世界时|协调世界时]]早5小时。2002年起该国开始采用[[夏令时|夏令时]]以节省电力，夏令时间比协调世界时早6小时，但这一制度快引发争议，遭到公众抵制，同年内便被取消。2007至2008年，巴基斯坦遭遇能源危机，电力短缺，该国政府于2008年再次开始采用夏令时，但依旧被公众抵制，因此夏时制于2010年再次被取消。<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://eeasia.unescap.org/PDFs/Gap-Analysis-Pakistan.pdf|title=Gap analysis on Energy Efficiency institutional arrangements in Pakistan|author=Asif Masood|page=44|year=2010|work=UN ESCAP (Economic and Social Commission for Asia and the Pacific)|publisher=United Nations|quote=... In 2002, Pakistan introduced Daylight {{sic|Saving|s Time}} (DST) ... met with public controversy and resistance was discontinued the same year. During the energy crisis of 2007–2008, the Government once again announced DST during summer season. It was implemented for almost two years before it was discontinued in 2010 because of the same public controversy and resistance ...|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130513024632/http://eeasia.unescap.org/PDFs/Gap-Analysis-Pakistan.pdf|archivedate=2013-05-13}}</ref>
