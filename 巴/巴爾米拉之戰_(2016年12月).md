2016年12月的'''巴爾米拉之戰'''是[[伊斯兰国|伊斯兰国]]向[[敘利亞|敘利亞]]古城[[巴尔米拉|巴尔米拉]]發起的進攻行動。<ref name="11Dec2016capture">{{cite web|url=https://www.alaraby.co.uk/english/news/2016/12/11/islamic-state-recaptures-palmyra-after-syria-army-withdrawal|title=Islamic State recaptures Palmyra after Syria army withdrawal|first=The New Arab &|last=agencies|publisher=}}</ref><ref name="Firstattack"/>伊斯蘭國曾於2015年攻佔巴爾米拉，但於2016年3月被敘利亞政府軍[[巴爾米拉攻勢_(2016年3月)|奪回]]。

==背景==
自從巴爾米拉於2016年3月被收復後，敘利亞政府把[[阿勒頗攻勢_(2016年9月－12月)|收復阿勒頗]]和收復[[大馬士革|大馬士革]]郊區列為主要任務，<ref name="Reenters">{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.com/news/world-middle-east-38275905 |title=Islamic State fighters 're-enter ancient Palmyra' in Syria |publisher=BBC|date=10 December 2016|accessdate=10 December 2016}}</ref>留守巴爾米拉的敘軍兵力有限。另一方面，美國支持的[[敘利亞民主力量|敘利亞民主力量]]於2016年11月開始的[[拉卡省北部攻勢|進攻拉卡]]行動流於「宣傳多於行動」，對伊斯蘭國的威脅不大，讓伊斯蘭國得以集中兵力進攻巴爾米拉一帶的敘軍。<ref>{{Cite news |url=http://sputniknews.cn/military/201612131021382899/ |title=美国务院不排除“伊斯兰国”武装分子或从摩苏尔抵达巴尔米拉地区 |publisher=俄羅斯衛星網 |date=2016-12-13}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news |url=http://sputniknews.cn/military/201612131021383186/ |title=美国否认在“伊斯兰国”反攻巴尔米拉之时停止支持进攻拉卡 |publisher=俄羅斯衛星網 |date=2016-12-13}}</ref>

== 巴爾米拉失守 ==
2016年12月8日，伊斯蘭國在霍姆斯省東北部發動進攻，被敘軍擊退，據報有60名武裝分子陣亡。<ref name="Firstattack">{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/isis-suffers-heavy-casualties-failed-offensive-north-palmyra/|title=ISIS suffers heavy casualties in failed offensive north of Palmyra|first=Leith|last=Fadel|date=8 December 2016|publisher=al-Masdar News|accessdate=10 December 2016}}</ref>同日稍後伊斯蘭國再度進攻，攻佔7個敘軍檢查站，至少34名親政府戰士陣亡，4人被俘。伊斯蘭國武裝分子推進至距離巴爾米拉不到4公里處。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://indianexpress.com/article/world/is-kills-26-syria-regime-fighters-in-homs-monitor-4417474/|title=IS kills 26 Syria regime fighters in Homs: monitor|date=8 December 2016|publisher=|accessdate=10 December 2016}}</ref><ref>[http://www.syriahr.com/en/?p=56585 -{The “Islamic State” advances in new places at Palmyra area in the eastern desert of Homs}-]</ref><ref>[http://www.syriahr.com/en/?p=56561 -{Violent and large-attack by the “Islamic State” in Palmyra on sites of the regime forces and on ancient Palace causes tens of casualties and injuries}-]</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/second-attack-proves-successful-isis-seize-huwaysis-northeast-homs/|title=Second attack proves successful for ISIS as they seize Huwaysis in northeast Homs|first=Leith|last=Fadel|date=8 December 2016|publisher=|accessdate=10 December 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.reuters.com/article/us-mideast-crisis-syria-islamic-state-idUSKBN13X2CM|title=Islamic State makes advances towards Palmyra: monitor|date=8 December 2016|publisher=|accessdate=10 December 2016|via=Reuters}}</ref>在巴爾米拉東北方的穀倉也被伊斯蘭國攻佔。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.reuters.com/article/mideast-crisis-syria-palmyra-idUSL5N1E43P1|title=Islamic State seizes more ground near Syria's Palmyra - monitoring group|date=9 December 2016|publisher=|accessdate=10 December 2016|via=Reuters}}</ref>在伊斯蘭國部隊於巴爾米拉附近集結的時候，[[聯合特遣隊－堅決行動|聯合特遣隊－堅決行動]]趁機出擊，大舉空襲伊斯蘭國的運輸隊，共摧毀168輛油罐車。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://aranews.net/2016/12/coalition-destroys-168-isis-oil-tankers-near-palmyra/|title=Coalition destroys 168 ISIS oil tankers near Palmyra - ARA News|date=2016-12-10|publisher=|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161213173651/http://aranews.net/2016/12/coalition-destroys-168-isis-oil-tankers-near-palmyra/|archivedate=2016年12月13日|df=}}</ref>

敘軍於12月9日反攻，有15名士兵於一處油田附近發生的伏擊中喪生，當日有報道宣稱至少49名政府軍士兵在過去24小時的戰鬥中陣亡。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hindustantimes.com/world-news/islamic-state-jihadists-kill-49-syria-regime-fighters-near-palmyra-monitor/story-NJyG2q8cq0tfAuAAqQLnEK.html |title=Islamic State jihadists kill 49 Syria regime fighters near Palmyra: monitor |date=2016-12-09 |publisher= |accessdate=2016-12-10}}</ref>當日伊斯蘭國攻佔T-4空軍基地東北方的一些地方。<ref name="keeps"/><ref>[http://www.syriahr.com/en/?p=56613 IS advances in Homs countryside over regime forces, and continued clashes in the area], 2016-12-09.</ref>

12月10日，敘軍援兵抵達巴爾米拉。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.reuters.com/article/us-mideast-crisis-syria-palmyra-idUSKBN13Z073|title=Islamic State militants enter Palmyra after heavy fighting: monitor|date=10 December 2016|publisher=|accessdate=10 December 2016|via=Reuters}}</ref>敘軍在巴爾米拉穀倉附近消滅至少45名武裝分子及擊毀3輛敵方坦克，但是仍未能阻止武裝分子進抵巴爾米拉的入口。俄敘空軍對巴爾米拉附近油田的伊斯蘭國據點展開空襲，摧毀了數輛裝甲車輛及一些其它車輛。<ref name="keeps"/><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/isis-reaches-palmyras-gates-syrian-army-attempts-hold-city/|title=ISIS reaches Palmyra's gates as the Syrian Army attempts to hold the city|first=Leith|last=Fadel|date=10 December 2016|publisher=|accessdate=10 December 2016}}</ref>敘軍一架[[MiG-23|MiG-23]]戰機墜毀，伊斯蘭國宣稱是被其擊落。<ref name="MiG23-down">{{cite web|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/wires/ap/article-4019876/The-Latest-IS-says-shot-Syrian-jet-near-Palmyra.html|title=The Latest: IS says it shot down Syrian jet near Palmyra|publisher=|accessdate=10 December 2016}}</ref>伊斯蘭國稍後攻佔城西的泰爾山，並且攻入巴爾米拉。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.xinhuanet.com/english/2016-12/11/c_135895802.htm|title=Islamic State re-enters Syria's Palmyra - Xinhua - English.news.cn|publisher=|accessdate=10 December 2016}}</ref>在該日之內，伊斯蘭國已佔領巴爾米拉的大部分，包括[[巴爾米拉城堡|巴爾米拉城堡]]。<ref>[http://www.syriahr.com/en/?p=56724 Russian warplanes target IS in Tadmur and IS takes almost full control on the city]</ref>

地面上的敘軍在俄羅斯空軍支援下<ref name="counterattack">{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/syrian-army-counterattacks-palmyra-outskirts-order-secure-city/|title=Syrian Army counterattacks on Palmyra outskirts in order to secure the city|first=Ivan|last=Yakovlev|date=2016-12-11 |publisher=}}</ref>擊退伊斯蘭國於12月10日夜間的進攻，到12月11日日出時伊斯蘭國武裝分子已被趕出巴爾米拉城外。<ref name="driveout">{{cite web|url=http://www.aljazeera.com/news/2016/12/russian-syrian-forces-drive-isil-palmyra-161211074224138.html|title=Russian and Syrian forces drive ISIL out of Palmyra|publisher=}}</ref>俄羅斯國防部宣稱支援敘軍作戰的俄空軍消滅超過300名武裝分子，擊毀11輛敵方坦克和[[裝步戰車|-{zh-cn:步兵战车;zh-tw:裝步戰車;zh-hk:步兵戰車;}-]]（IFV）以及31輛配備重機槍的其它車輛。<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://sputniknews.com/middleeast/201612111048417760-russia-palmyra-airstrikes/ |title=Russian Jets Deliver 64 Strikes on Militants Near Palmyra, Shielding the City |publisher=Sputnik |date=2016-12-11}}</ref>不過伊斯蘭國在當日稍後再次進攻巴爾米拉，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.reuters.com/article/us-mideast-crisis-syria-palmyra-idUSKBN13Z073|title=Syrian official says Palmyra falls again to Islamic State|date=11 December 2016|publisher=|via=Reuters}}</ref>最終敘軍被迫撤出巴爾米拉，伊斯蘭國再一次完全佔領該市。<ref name="11Dec2016capture"/>敘軍在撤出巴爾米拉前協助當地居民撤離，並繼續在巴爾米拉附近與伊斯蘭國交戰。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://sputniknews.com/middleeast/201612111048428715-palmyra-daesh-residents-evacuated-fight/|title=Palmyra Residents Evacuated, Syrian Army Fighting Daesh in Suburbs - Source |publisher=Sputnik |date=2016-12-11}}</ref>

== T-4空軍基地攻防戰 ==
伊斯蘭國部隊奪得巴爾米拉後，向西面的T-4空軍基地推進。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/isis-seizes-two-villages-west-palmyra/|title=ISIS seizes two villages west of Palmyra|first=Leith|last=Fadel|date=11 December 2016|publisher=}}</ref><ref>[http://www.syriahr.com/en/?p=56724 Russian warplanes target IS in Tadmur and IS takes almost full control on the city]</ref>12月12日，伊斯蘭國向T-4基地發起第一次進攻，首先以兩輛[[汽车炸弹|裝載炸彈的車輛]]衝向敘軍陣地，被敘軍在它們駛抵目標前成功引爆。經過數小時激戰，獲得俄空軍支援的敘軍成功擊退伊斯蘭國的進攻，數十名武裝分子陣亡。<ref name="first wave">{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/syrian-army-repels-first-isis-assault-t-4-airbase-prepares-second-wave/ |title=Syrian Army repels first ISIS assault on T-4 Airbase, prepares for a second wave|first=Ivan|last=Yakovlev |date=2016-12-12 |publisher=al-Masdar News |accessdate=2016-12-12}}</ref>與此同時，數百名[[國防軍_(敘利亞)|國防軍]]援兵抵達前線。<ref name="first wave" /><ref name="reinforcements">{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/reinforcements-arrive-near-palmyra-syrian-army-regroups-counter-offensive-photos/|title=Reinforcements arrive near Palmyra as Syrian Army regroups for counter-offensive (Photos) |first=Chris|last=Tomson |date=2016-12-12 |publisher=al-Masdar News|accessdate=2016-12-12}}</ref>

伊斯蘭國於12月13日再次進攻T-4基地。敘利亞陸軍和俄羅斯特種部隊的援軍同日稍後抵達基地。<ref>[http://www.syriahr.com/en/?p=56888 -{Airstrikes target the eastern countryside of Homs and the “Islamic state” targets T4 airbase again}-]</ref><ref>[http://www.syriahr.com/en/?p=56913 Russian backups for regime forces in Taifur military airport]</ref>伊斯蘭國分子攻佔一個位於通往[[盖尔亚廷|盖尔亚廷]]的公路上的檢查站。<ref>[http://www.syriahr.com/en/?p=56913 Continued clashes around al_Taifor military airport]</ref>12月14日敘軍收復在13日失守的通往蓋爾亞廷公路上的一些檢查站，<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.alhadathnews.net/archives/247176|title= بالفيديو: شاهد لماذا يعزف الجيش السوري داخل مطار التيفور؟ |publisher= Al-Hadath News|date=2016-12-14 |language=ar}}</ref>不過基地以西的al-Sharifah村被伊斯蘭國攻佔。<ref>[http://www.syriahr.com/en/?p=57015 -{The “Islamic State” achieves a new advancement in the eastern countryside of Homs and clashes continue near the T4 airbase}-]</ref>伊斯蘭國再向基地進攻，被守軍擊退。敘軍隨即在夜間反攻，把敵軍逐出基地外圍。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://rudaw.net/english/middleeast/syria/14122016|title=Syrian reinforcements prevent ISIS from taking Palmyra airbase |publisher=|accessdate=2016-12-18}}</ref>

12月16日敘軍發起另一次反攻，重新打通蓋爾亞廷與T-4基地之間的道路。<ref name=Hadath>{{cite news|url=http://www.alhadathnews.net/archives/248540|title= بالصور: حامية مطار التيفور.. واخر التطورات في تدمر |publisher= Al-Hadath News|date=2016-12-17 |language=ar}}</ref>伊斯蘭國於12月19日再向基地進攻，並宣稱擊落一架俄軍直升機。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/field-report-isis-reportedly-shot-russian-helicopter-near-t4-airbase/|title=Field report: ISIS reportedly shot down Russian helicopter near T4 airbase|first=Zen|last=Adra|date=2016-12-19 |publisher=|accessdate=2016-12-19}}</ref>守軍最終擊退進攻，36名伊斯蘭國分子陣亡。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.insidesourcenews.com/single-post/2016/12/20/ISIS-Launch-Large-Scale-Assault-on-the-T4-Airbase|title=ISIS Launch Large Scale Assault on the T4 Airbase|date=2016-12-19|publisher=The Inside Source|accessdate=2016-12-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161222152134/http://www.insidesourcenews.com/single-post/2016/12/20/ISIS-Launch-Large-Scale-Assault-on-the-T4-Airbase|archive-date=2016-12-22|dead-url=yes}}</ref>
