'''巴基斯坦国际航空公司PK661航班'''，在2016年12月7日在巴基斯坦的北部城市{{link-en|契特拉|Chitral}}飛往[[伊斯兰堡|伊斯兰堡]]時墜毀。失事客机机型为[[ATR_42|ATR 42-500]]，失事原因目前未明。<ref name=bbc>{{Cite web | url = http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-asia-38238699 | title = Pakistan International Airways flight 'crashes in north' | date = 2016-12-07 | accessdate = 2016-12-07 | publisher = BBC News | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20161207132821/http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-asia-38238699 | archive-date = 2016-12-07 | dead-url = no }}</ref><ref name=reuters>{{Cite web | url = http://www.reuters.com/article/us-pakistan-airplane-idUSKBN13W1F5 | title = Pakistani plane with more than 40 people aboard crashes in north: police | publisher = Reuters | accessdate = 2016-12-07 | date = 2016-12-07 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20161207122502/http://www.reuters.com/article/us-pakistan-airplane-idUSKBN13W1F5 | archive-date = 2016-12-07 | dead-url = no }}</ref><ref name="dawn"/> 

==失事客机==
失事的[[ATR_42|ATR 42-500]]客机注册编号为AP-BHO<ref name=ah>{{Cite web | url = http://avherald.com/h?article=4a1c2ee4&opt=0 | publisher = [[The_Aviation_Herald|The Aviation Herald]] | date = 2016-12-07 | accessdate = 2016-12-07|title = Crash: PIA AT42 near Abbottabad on Dec 7th 2016, engine problems | first = Simon | last = Hradecky}}</ref>，2007年首飞并交付给巴基斯坦国际航空使用。2009年在一次降落在[[拉合爾|拉合爾]]的过程中受损，修复后继续服役。<ref>{{citeweb|title=ATR 42/72 – MSN 663 – AP-BHO|url=http://www.airfleets.net/ficheapp/plane-atr-663.htm|publisher=Airfleets|accessdate=2016-12-07|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161208121709/http://www.airfleets.net/ficheapp/plane-atr-663.htm|archive-date=2016-12-08|dead-url=no}}</ref>

==乘客==

==调查==
事故发生后不久，调查人员迅速找回了飞机的[[飞行记录仪|飞行记录仪]]。巴基斯坦民航局（PCAA）对事故的初步报告据说已经确定该飞机的左发动机在海拔13,375英尺（4,077米）处发生故障。飞行员在16:12报告了[[发动机|发动机]]故障，随后是几分钟后飞机从地面雷达迅速失控下降和失踪。然而，即使在从雷达屏幕消失之后，[[无线电通信|无线电通信]]也继续用[[雷达|雷达]]进行<ref name=":0">{{cite news|url=http://www.dawn.com/news/1301373/initial-report-says-pk-661s-left-engine-malfunctioned|title=Initial report says PK-661’s left engine malfunctioned|last=Ashgar|first=Mohammed|work=Dawn|date=2016-12-09|accessdate=2016-12-09|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161209131516/http://www.dawn.com/news/1301373/initial-report-says-pk-661s-left-engine-malfunctioned|archive-date=2016-12-09|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

在12月11日[[木尔坦国际机场|木尔坦国际机场]]发生[[ATR_72|ATR 72]]事件后，PCAA下令所有属于PIA的ATR飞机停飞进行检查。五架ATR 42和五架ATR 72飞机受到该订单的影响<ref name=ASN121216>{{Cite web |url=http://news.aviation-safety.net/2016/12/12/pakistan-grounds-atr-fleet-inspections/ |title=Pakistan grounds ATR fleet for inspections |publisher={{tsl|en|Flight Safety Foundation||Flight Safety Foundation}} |website=Aviation Safety Network |date=2016-12-12 |access-date=2016-12-12 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161220130353/http://news.aviation-safety.net/2016/12/12/pakistan-grounds-atr-fleet-inspections/ |archive-date=2016-12-20 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。
