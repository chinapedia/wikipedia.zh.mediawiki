'''巴里·羅蘭'''（{{lang-en|Barry Rowland}}；{{bd|1961年|8月|}}），是一名[[英國|英國]]管理人、現任[[福克兰群岛行政长官|福克兰群岛行政长官]]黨魁<ref name="MercoPress">{{cite news|url=http://en.mercopress.com/2016/06/02/falkland-s-set-to-appoint-barry-rowland-as-new-chief-executive |title=Falkland’s set to appoint Barry Rowland as new Chief Executive |date=2 June 2016 |newspaper=[[MercoPress|MercoPress]] |publisher= |accessdate=8 August 2016}}</ref>。他曾擔任{{le|諾森伯蘭郡理事會|Northumberland County Council}}和{{le|新堡議會|Newcastle City Council}}的首席執行官<ref name="MercoPress"/>。

==簡歷==
羅蘭在[[達拉謨|達拉謨]]成長，於[[杜伦大学|杜伦大学]]取得[[工商管理碩士|工商管理碩士]]<ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.ncl.ac.uk/congregations/assets/documents/BarryRowlandCitationFinal_000.pdf |last= Bruce |first= Vicki |author-link= Vicki Bruce |title= Barry Alan Rowland |date=  |publisher= [[Newcastle_University|Newcastle University]] |accessdate= 8 August 2016 |deadurl= yes |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20151024020744/http://www.ncl.ac.uk/congregations/assets/documents/BarryRowlandCitationFinal_000.pdf |archivedate= 2015年10月24日 }}</ref>。自1979年進入新堡議會當管理實習生到2009年成為議會的首席執行官，他在30年間都在新堡議會工作<ref name="LGE">{{cite news |last=Regan |first=Danielle |date=22 August 2012 |title=Newcastle City Council’s chief executive, Barry Rowland, steps down |url=http://www.localgovernmentexecutive.co.uk/news/newcastle-city-council%E2%80%99s-chief-executive-barry-rowland-steps-down |newspaper=Local Government Executive |location= |access-date=8 August 2016 }}{{dead link|date=2018年2月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>，直到2012年於雙方同意下離職<ref name="MercoPress"/>。
