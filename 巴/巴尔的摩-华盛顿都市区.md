巴尔的摩-华盛顿都市区（Baltimore–Washington metropolitan area）是一个[[联合统计区|联合统计区]]（部分相邻的[[大都市统计区|大都市统计区]]被合称为联合统计区），它覆盖了[[巴尔的摩|巴尔的摩]]和[[华盛顿|华盛顿]]的都市区。<ref>[http://www.greaterbaltimore.org/tabid/534/Default.aspx CSA Median household income] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121207060434/http://www.greaterbaltimore.org/tabid/534/Default.aspx |date=2012-12-07 }}</ref><ref>[http://triangle.bizjournals.com/triangle/stories/2009/09/21/daily23.html?ana=tt3245 Raleigh–Durham area ranks third in U.S. for college degrees]</ref>
[[File:Map_of_Washington-Baltimore-Arlington_CSA,_2012.jpg|right]]-designated Washington-Baltimore-Arlington, DC-MD-VA-WV-PA Combined Statistical Area.]]
