'''吉·曼斯菲爾德'''（{{lang|en|James Mansfield, Sr.}}，{{bd|1939年|4月9日|2014年|1月29日}}），是爱尔兰商人，从事大量地产开发，包括{{link-en|西城酒店|Citywest Hotel}}，以及{{link-en|韦斯顿机场|Weston Airport}}。

曼斯菲爾德在他从事的地产贸易上有很大争议<ref name="rte-planning">{{cite news|url=http://www.rte.ie/news/2004/0421/planning.html|title=Mansfield disappointed at planning refusal|date=21 April 2004|work=RTÉ News}}</ref><ref name="iritimes-saudi">{{cite news|date=2 March 2010|author=Grainne Faller|title=The strange case of Citywest, 750 Saudi students and the disputed contract|url=http://www.irishtimes.com/newspaper/education/2010/0302/1224265424568.html|work=[[The_Irish_Times|The Irish Times]]}}</ref>。2011年，曼斯菲爾德的公司债务无法偿还，其资产转移到{{link-en|爱尔兰国家资产管理局|National Asset Management Agency}}<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.independent.ie/business/irish/namas-weston-airport-starting-to-turn-around-as-buyer-sought-2881162.html|work=Irish Independent|first=Emmet|last=Oliver|title=NAMA's Weston airport starting to turn around as buyer sought|date=20 September 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.rte.ie/news/2011/0420/mansfieldj.html|work=RTÉ News|title=NAMA appoints receiver to Mansfield businesses|date=20 April 2011}}</ref>。他患有{{link-en|多系统萎缩症|Multiple System Atrophy}}。2014年1月29日去世。
