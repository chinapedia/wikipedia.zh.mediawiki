=== 慈善事業 ===
吉娜投入各式各樣的慈善活動，這為她的演藝事業提升了不少知名度。<ref>{{Cite web|title = W.E.N.N  » Gina Rodriguez Planning Education Charity With Sisters|url = http://blog.wenn.com/all-news/gina-rodriguez-planning-education-charity-with-sisters/|website = blog.wenn.com|accessdate = 2015-12-07}}</ref> 於2015年，她開始投入各種組織，從{{link-en|CustomInk|CustomInk}}到Naja內衣，都與她的生活有著特殊的連結與經驗。<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|title = CustomInk Teams Up With Gina Rodriguez To Kick Off Its 5th Annual "Be Good to Each Other" Bullying Prevention Campaign|url = http://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/customink-teams-up-with-gina-rodriguez-to-kick-off-its-5th-annual-be-good-to-each-other-bullying-prevention-campaign-300140855.html|website = www.prnewswire.com|accessdate = 2015-12-07}}</ref><ref name=":2"/>

2015年3月16日，她宣布自己成為了西班牙裔獎學基金（{{lang|en|Hispanic Scholarship Fund, HSF}}）董事會的新成員。HSF為美國最大支助西班牙裔美國人接受高等教育的非營利組織。對於羅德里奎家來說教育極為重要，再加上她決心修得學位，故她接受了HSF的獎學金。<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|title = Actress Gina Rodriguez Joins Hispanic Scholarship Fund Board of Directors|url = http://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/actress-gina-rodriguez-joins-hispanic-scholarship-fund-board-of-directors-300050628.html|website = www.prnewswire.com|accessdate = 2015-12-07}}</ref> 身為董事會的一員，吉娜將協助HSF專注在無比有意義的事上，以確保HSF會繼續保有原來的價值與致力於教育的初衷。<ref>{{Cite web|url = https://hsf.net/en/about-hsf/people/board-of-directors/|title = Board of Directors|date = |accessdate = 2015-11-03|website = Board of Directors|publisher = The Hispanic Scholarship Fund|last = |first = |deadurl = yes|archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20151208214505/https://hsf.net/en/about-hsf/people/board-of-directors/|archivedate = 2015-12-08}}</ref> 吉娜表明這是個很好的機會對HSF表示感謝，並為拉丁裔學生開啟了一扇門讓他們追隨。<ref name=":0" />

2015年晚些，家長倡導教育權利聯盟（{{lang|en|The Parent Advocacy Coalition for Educational Rights, PACER}}）全國[[霸凌|霸凌]]預防中心與CustomInk攜手，並在許多名人包括吉娜的幫助下，籌措了資金以舉辦第5屆「彼此友好活動」（{{lang|en|Be Good to Each Other Campaign}}）。<ref name=":1" /> 2015年10月，大夥鼓勵民眾購買於CustomInk出售來自PACER的反霸凌“名人”T恤。<ref>{{Cite web|title = Stop Bullying T-Shirts - CustomInk's Be Good to Each Other Campaign|url = http://www.customink.com/stopbullying/2015?pc=PR-147031&utm_source=stopbullying&utm_medium=pr&utm_campaign=stopbullying%25200915&cm_mmc=PR-_-Stopbullying-_-0915-_-0915|website = CustomInk.com|accessdate = 2015-12-07}}</ref> 吉娜設計了其中一款T恤，而上面印著「善良是種風格」的字樣，其明確指出對善良、接受與反霸凌的立場，並表示她的父母一直教導她要有積極的思考與尊重他人的心。她希望藉此活動，繼續傳遞這樣的正面訊息給下一代。<ref name=":1" /><ref>{{Cite web|title = CustomInk’s 5th Annual Be Good to Each Other|url = https://www.booster.com/ginarodriguez?ref=CI_Other_BG2EO15&utm_source=%2520CI_Other&utm_medium=customink_promo&utm_campaign=BG2EO15&utm_term=ginarodriguez|website = www.booster.com|accessdate = 2015-12-07}}</ref>

同樣於2015年10月，吉娜宣布成為Naja的合作夥伴，而該公司於2014年成立，並致力於幫助女性與自然環境。由於Naja是垂直整合生產，故大大減少了生產週期，這讓公司可以省下更多錢來供應員工子女學校書本、午餐與制服，而靈活的工作環境更是優點之一。<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|title = Why Gina Rodriguez Is Entering The Lingerie Business|url = http://social.refinery29.com/2015/10/95239/gina-rodriguez-naja-lingerie|website = Refinery29|accessdate = 2015-12-07}}</ref> 在見識大學摯友身為單親媽媽的辛勞後，吉娜打算支持以錄用單親媽媽為優先的公司，並讓她們在家中工作好兼顧家庭。<ref>{{Cite web|title = Gina Rodriguez Gives Back As Co Founder Of Naja Lingerie|url = http://www.forbes.com/sites/sboyd/2015/10/07/gina-rodriguez-gives-back-as-co-founder-of-naja-lingerie/|website = Forbes|accessdate = 2015-12-07}}</ref> 她列舉了Naja的承諾，並製造各種身型與大小的女性內衣，以增加女性對身體的自信，欲透過此舉讓女性更愛惜自己的身體。<ref>{{Cite web|title = Gina Rodriguez Teams Up with Lingerie Brand Naja and Shares an Empowering Message for Women|url = http://www.instyle.com/news/gina-rodriguez-partners-naja-lingerie-empowering-message|website = InStyle.com|accessdate = 2015-12-07}}</ref> Naja在製造的過程中也很重視環保意識，其材料多由回收的塑膠瓶製成，並用更環保的[[數碼印刷|數碼印刷]]製造。<ref name=":2" />
