'''詹姆斯·艾伦“吉姆”帕蒂森'''（{{bd|1928年|10月1日||}}）是加拿大商业大亨，投资者和慈善家。他是[[温哥华|温哥华]][[吉姆·帕蒂森集团|吉姆·帕蒂森集团]]的举行首席执行官、主席和唯一所有者。他被认为是加拿大第四富有的人。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.canadianbusiness.com/lists-and-rankings/richest-people/top-100-richest-canadians-2015/|title=Canada’s Richest People 2015: The 100 Richest Canadians|accessdate=2016-01-09|language=en-US}}</ref>

== 早期的生活和教育 ==
帕蒂森的父母居住在[[萨斯喀彻温|萨斯喀彻温]]的农村小镇陆斯兰，他在临近[[萨斯卡通|萨斯卡通]]的医院出生。<ref name="globe">{{Cite news|url=http://www.theglobeandmail.com/report-on-business/rob-magazine/jimmy-has-the-last-laugh/article260838/print/ |title=Jimmy has the last laugh |work=The Globe and Mail |location=Toronto |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20130620021404/http://www.theglobeandmail.com/report-on-business/rob-magazine/jimmy-has-the-last-laugh/article4088239/?page=all |archivedate=2013-06-20 |quote=Pattison's father was already in the business back in Luseland, Sask., on the day in 1928 that his only son was born... |deadurl=yes }}</ref>帕蒂森在温哥华东区长大，他暑假打的第一份工是在一个教会儿童活动中吹[[小号|小号]]，后来又在中学的暑假采摘水果(覆盆子、樱桃和桃子)。帕蒂森在高中时有许多工作，包括在学校停车场卖甜甜圈，挨家挨户卖种子、送报纸，及在乔治亚酒店当侍从。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.canada.com/story.html?id=fba72335-84f0-4c10-8c9f-38948db20533|title=canada.com - Page Not Found|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140921055129/http://www.canada.com/story.html?id=fba72335-84f0-4c10-8c9f-38948db20533|archivedate=2014-09-21}}</ref>1947年他毕业于约翰·奥利弗中学。<ref>[http://www.vancourier.com/news/billionaire-returns-to-former-high-school-in-vancouver-1.387809 Vancouver Courier - Billionaire returns to former high school in Vancouver] - September 7, 2011</ref>
