'''香兰素胺'''（'''Vanillylamine'''），又称'''香草胺'''，是一种[[生物碱|生物碱]]，是[[辣椒素|辣椒素]][[生物合成|生物合成]]的中间产物<ref>{{cite journal | title = Biosynthesis of capsaicin and dihydrocapsaicin in Capsicum frutescens | author = Edward Leete and Mary C. L. Louden | journal = J. Am. Chem. Soc. | date = 1968 | volume = 90 | issue = 24 | pages = 6837–6841 | doi = 10.1021/ja01026a049| pmid = 5687710 }}</ref>。由[[香兰素|香兰素]]通过[[香兰素转氨酶|香兰素转氨酶]]而得到<ref name= MetaCyc >{{cite web | url = http://metacyc.org/META/NEW-IMAGE?type=PATHWAY&object=PWY-5710 | title = MetaCyc Pathway: capsaicin biosynthesis | publisher = MetaCyc }}</ref>。并进一步与8-甲基-6-壬酸通过[[辣椒素合成酶|辣椒素合成酶]]的催化而转化为[[辣椒素|辣椒素]]。而辣椒素也能在一定条件下通过酶催化水解为香兰素胺和8-甲基-6-壬酸<ref>{{cite journal | title = Capsaicin hydrolysis by Candida antarctica lipase | author = Dolores Reyes Duarte, Edmundo Castillo, Eduardo Bárzana and Agustín López-Munguía | journal = Biotechnology letters | date = 2000 | volume = 22 | issue = 22 | pages = 1811-1814 | doi = 10.1023/A:1005622704504}}，[http://www.academia.edu/download/46549024/a_3A100562270450420160616-30961-1vrru2f.pdf PDF]{{dead link|date=2018年5月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref>辛嘉英, 王艳, 张超越, 等.[http://www.spkx.net.cn/EN/article/downloadArticleFile.do?attachType=PDF&id=30316 辣椒素酯类物质研究进展][J]. 食品科学, 2013, 34(11): 352-357.</ref>。
