'''呼气虫屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Pneumodesmus''）是一類已灭绝的[[马陆|马陆]]，其下只有'''紐氏呼氣蟲'''（''Pneumodesmus newmani''）一個種，生存于4亿2800万年前的[[志留纪|志留纪]]晚期<ref name="Wilson"/><ref name="BBC">{{cite news |publisher=[[BBC_News|BBC News]] |date=January 25, 2004 |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/uk_news/scotland/3427499.stm |title=Fossil find 'oldest land animal' |access-date=2013-07-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131022063120/http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/uk_news/scotland/3427499.stm# |archive-date=2013-10-22 |dead-url=no }}</ref>，是最早的[[多足類|多足類]]物種，也是已知最早的陸生動物<ref name="CNN">{{cite news |url=http://edition.cnn.com/2004/TECH/science/01/27/environment.britain.fossil.reut/index.html |title=Fossil millipede found to be oldest land creature |publisher=[[CNN|CNN]] (from [[Reuters|Reuters]]) |date=January 27, 2004 |access-date=2013-07-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131023061250/http://edition.cnn.com/2004/TECH/science/01/27/environment.britain.fossil.reut/index.html# |archive-date=2013-10-23 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。其樣本於2004年在[[蘇格蘭|蘇格蘭]][[亞伯丁郡|亞伯丁郡]]{{link-en|斯通黑文|Stonehaven}}發現<ref name="BBC"/>。

==发现==
[[File:Pneumodesmus_newmani_-_MUSE.JPG|thumb]]
巴士司機、业余[[古生物學家|古生物學家]]迈克纽曼（Mike Newman）在亞伯丁郡斯通黑文附近、[[科維_(亞伯丁郡)|科維]]的一處沙灘的[[砂岩|砂岩]]中<ref>{{cite web |title=''Pneumodesmus newmani'' Exhibition |url=http://www.britnett-carver.co.uk/stonehaven/prods/21.html |publisher=Stonehaven Guide |accessdate=May 6, 2007 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080617094659/http://www.britnett-carver.co.uk/stonehaven/prods/21.html# |archive-date=2008-06-17 |dead-url=no }}</ref>，發現了單一、約長1公分的呼气虫化石斷片。该化石隨後為紀念紐曼而被命名为纽氏呼气虫（''Pneumodesmus newmani''）。其[[正模標本|正模標本]]位於[[愛丁堡|愛丁堡]]的[[蘇格蘭國家博物館|蘇格蘭國家博物館]]<ref>{{cite journal |url=http://www.paulselden.net/uploads/7/5/3/2/7532217/seldenread2008.pdf |journal=Bulletin of the British Myriapod & Isopod Group |volume=23 |year=2008 |title=The oldest land animals: Silurian millipedes from Scotland |author=Paul Selden & Helen Read |pages=36–37 }}</ref>。

==重要性==
呼气虫的重要性來自其表皮有些被認為是[[氣門|氣門]]的構造，這種[[氣體交換|氣體交換]]系統只能在陸地上發揮功能，使的呼氣蟲成為已知最早具有[[氣管|氣管]]系統的[[節肢動物|節肢動物]]，也是已知最早的在陸上生活、呼吸[[氧氣|氧氣]]的動物<ref name="CNN"/><ref name="myriapoda">{{cite web|url=http://www.myriapoda.org/diplopoda/millipede_fossils.html |title=Millipede Fossils |publisher=[[East_Carolina_University|East Carolina University]] |date=March 1, 2005 |author=Rowland Shelley & Paul Marek |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110527071726/http://www.myriapoda.org/diplopoda/millipede_fossils.html |archivedate=五月 27, 2011 }}</ref>。

[[多足類|多足類]]的[[遗迹化石|遗迹化石]]最早可追溯至[[奧陶紀|奧陶紀]]晚期，但紐氏呼氣蟲是最早的實體化石，其年代約為4亿2800万年前，即[[志留紀|志留紀]]的{{link-en|溫洛克期|Wenlock (Silurian)}}至{{link-en|勒德洛期|Ludlow Group}}。最早的[[蜈蚣|蜈蚣]]出現在1000萬年後<ref name="myriapoda"/>，而最早的陸上脊椎動物[[提塔利克魚|提塔利克魚]]則要再過5000萬年才出現<ref>{{cite web |url=http://science_boy.blogspot.com/2006/04/when-animals-first-conquered-land.html |title=When animals first conquered the land |date=April 9, 2006 |author=David Winter |access-date=2013-07-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131024213619/http://science_boy.blogspot.com/2006/04/when-animals-first-conquered-land.html# |archive-date=2013-10-24 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。志留紀時，現今蘇格蘭的陸塊是[[勞倫大陸|勞倫大陸]]的一部份，位於[[南半球|南半球]]的熱帶地區，與現在蘇格蘭的氣候不同<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/scotland/whereilive/coast/stages.shtml?walk=northeast&stage=2 |publisher=[[BBC_Scotland|BBC Scotland]] |title=Cowie |accessdate=May 6, 2007 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121108104939/http://www.bbc.co.uk/scotland/whereilive/coast/stages.shtml?walk=northeast&stage=2# |archive-date=2012-11-08 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。
