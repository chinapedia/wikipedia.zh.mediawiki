'''胸椎'''是[[脊椎|脊椎]]的一部分，位于[[颈椎|颈椎]]和[[腰椎|腰椎]]之间<ref>The thoracic vertebrae were historically called ''dorsal vertebrae''; cf. [https://archive.org/download/anatomydescripti1858gray/anatomydescripti1858gray.pdf].</ref>。人类有12块胸椎，大小介于颈椎和腰椎之间，从上至下逐渐增大{{citation needed|date=2019-04-20}}。
