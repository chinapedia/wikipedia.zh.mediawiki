'''别林斯高晋站'''（{{lang-ru|Беллинсгаузен}}）[[俄罗斯|俄罗斯]]（[[前苏联|前苏联]]）建立的[[南极科学考察站|南极科学考察站]]，位于[[乔治王岛|乔治王岛]]，1968年设。<ref>http://www.state.gov/g/oes/rls/rpts/82039.htm United States Antarctic Inspection Team 2006: Report of Inspections under Article VII of the Antarctic Treaty and Article 14 of the Protocol on Environmental Protection. March 21, 2007] (Includes detailed description of this and some nearby stations)</ref>

别林斯高晋站温度是[[南极洲|南极洲]]及其附近岛屿中最高的。其最低气温为-6.8°С（19.76° F），二月温度可达+1.1°С（34 ° F）。
