'''席尔'''（{{lang-en|Seal}}，{{bd|1963年|2月19日|||Seal}}），全名'''席爾·亨利·奧盧塞貢·奧盧米德·阿德奧拿·塞繆爾'''（{{lang|en|Seal Henry Olusegun Olumide Adeola Samuel}}）<ref name=musiclaw>[http://www.musiclawupdates.com/?p=2570 "Ongoing Management Commission gets Seal of Approval : Wadlow v Samuel"]. Music Law.</ref>，生於[[英国|英国]]伦敦[[帕丁顿|帕丁顿]]，尼日利亚-巴西裔英国[[靈魂樂|灵魂歌手]]和作曲家<ref>[http://www.thesun.co.uk/sol/homepage/showbiz/bizarre/4077981/Seal-and-Heidi-Klum-news-Couple-to-divorce-after-arguments.html "Seal: ‘My wife’s German and I’m British... of course we’d argue’ ]. The Sun. Retrieved 1 September 2012</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/music/2010/sep/16/seal-interview-pop|title=Seal: The reluctant pop star|last=Batey|first=Angus|date=16 September 2010|work=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]|accessdate=25 November 2012|location=London}}</ref>。全球唱片銷量超過3千萬張<ref>{{cite web|title=Acclaimed Singer and Songwriter Seal Returns With Second Set of Soul Classics, Soul 2, Due for Release in the U.S. on January 31st, 2012, From Warner Bros. Records; The First Single, "Let's Stay Together," Impacts Radio Today |url=http://finance.yahoo.com/news/Acclaimed-Singer-Songwriter-iw-214514235.html |work=[[Reprise_Records|Reprise Records]] |publisher=[[Yahoo!|Yahoo!]]|accessdate=30 October 2011|date=24 October 2011}}</ref>，他的首本名曲為《[[玫瑰之吻|玫瑰之吻]]》。他亦都是{{link-en|澳洲好聲音|The Voice (Australian TV series)}}2012年及2013年的評判<ref name=TheVoice2014>{{cite web |url=http://channelnine.ninemsn.com.au/article.aspx?id=8761509 |title=will.i.am and Kylie Minogue join The Voice in 2014 |author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.--> |year=2014 |website=Nine Network |publisher=ninemsn Pty Ltd |accessdate=29 April 2014}}</ref>。

2005年5月10日，席尔与德裔美国模特[[海蒂·克鲁姆|海蒂·克鲁姆]]在墨西哥结婚。两人育有兩子兩女，依大小排序分別為：女兒莱妮（{{lang|en|Leni}}）、兒子亨利（{{lang|en|Henry}}）和约翰（{{lang|en|Johan}}），以及女兒露（{{lang|en|Lou}}）。兩人於2012年1月宣告離婚。

虽然众说纷纭，他的面部疤痕其实是{{link-en|盤狀紅斑性狼瘡|Discoid lupus erythematosus}}的结果。

== 早年生活 ==
席尔原名奧盧塞貢·奧盧米德·阿德奧拿·塞繆爾（{{lang|en|Olusegun Olumide Adeola Samuel}}）<ref>[http://search.findmypast.co.uk/results/world-records/england-and-wales-births-1837-2006?lastname=samuel&mothersmaidenname=ogundeji "Results for England and Wales Births 1837-2006: Olusegun Olumide Adeola Samuel"]. Findmypast.co.uk. Retrieved 17 February 2015</ref>，生於英國[[帕丁顿|帕丁顿]]，父親弗朗西斯·塞繆爾（{{lang|en|Francis Samuel}}）是[[巴西人|巴西人]]，母親[[尼日利亞人|尼日利亞人]]<ref>{{cite news|last1=Batey|first1=Angus|title=Seal: The reluctant pop star|url=http://www.theguardian.com/music/2010/sep/16/seal-interview-pop |accessdate=16 September 2010|work=The Guardian|location=London}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news |url=http://www.boston.com/news/globe/living/articles/2003/09/07/seal_chooses_idealism_optimism_on_new_cd/ |title=Seal chooses idealism, optimism on new CD |last=Morse |first=Steve|date=9 July 2003 |work=[[The_Boston_Globe|The Boston Globe]] |accessdate=12 March 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.askmen.com/toys/interview_100/102_seal_interview.html |title=Bio |publisher=Askmen.com |date=19 February 1963 |accessdate=26 April 2011}}</ref>。他的名字為[[西非|西非]][[約魯巴語|約魯巴語]]，奧盧塞貢（{{lang|en|Olusegun}}）意思為「勝利之神」，奧盧米德（{{lang|en|Olumide}}）即「神已來臨」，阿德奧拿（{{lang|en|Adeola}}）可以解作「富有的皇冠」或「財富的皇冠」。他在[[西敏市|西敏市]]由[[寄養家庭|寄養家庭]]養大。
