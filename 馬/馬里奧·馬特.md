'''馬里奧·馬特'''（{{bd|1979年|4月9日}}）是一位[[奧地利|奧地利]]高山滑雪世界盃運動員和奧運金牌得主。<ref name=mmhotgl>{{cite magazine|url=http://skiracing.com/stories/mens_olympic_slalom/ |magazine=Ski Racing |title=Mario Matt holds on for gold, as Ligety blasts course |last=Etchells |first=Tim |date=February 22, 2014 |accessdate=February 22, 2014}}</ref>

馬特在34歲奪得第一面奧運獎牌，是2014年索契奧運曲道金牌<ref name=mmhotgl/>該面獎牌，使他成為最年長的奧運高山滑雪金牌得主。<ref name=NYT-2014-02-22-Slalom>{{cite news|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2014/02/23/sports/olympics/mario-matt-of-austria-becomes-oldest-olympic-slalom-champion.html?_r=0|publisher=New York Times|date=22 February 2014|title=Slalom Champion Sets an Age Record|author=Bill Pennington}}</ref>
