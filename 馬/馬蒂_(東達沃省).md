'''馬蒂市'''（{{lang-en|Mati}}），是[[菲律賓|菲律賓]][[東達沃省|東達沃省]]省會，也是東達沃省唯一的城市，位於[[棉蘭老島|棉蘭老島]]的東南部海岸。根據2010年人口普查資料顯示，馬蒂市人口有12萬6千143人口。<ref name=NSO10>{{cite web |url=http://www.census.gov.ph/sites/default/files/attachments/hsd/pressrelease/Davao.pdf |title=Total Population by Province, City, Municipality and Barangay: as of May 1, 2010 |work=2010 Census of Population and Housing |publisher=National Statistics Office |accessdate=16 April 2014 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131101215001/http://census.gov.ph/sites/default/files/attachments/hsd/pressrelease/Davao.pdf |archivedate=1 十一月 2013 }}</ref>馬蒂市總面積為588.63平方公里。<ref name=nscb>{{cite web |title=Province: DAVAO ORIENTAL |url=http://www.nscb.gov.ph/activestats/psgc/province.asp?provCode=112500000&provName=DAVAO%20ORIENTAL&regCode=11&regName=REGION%20XI%20%28Davao%20Region%29 |work=PSGC Interactive |publisher=National Statistical Coordination Board |accessdate=16 April 2014 |location=Makati City, Philippines}}</ref>
