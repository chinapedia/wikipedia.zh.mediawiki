'''斯圖爾特·安德魯'''（Philip Davies，{{bd|1972年|1月5日}}）是一位[[英格蘭|英格蘭]]政治人物，他的黨籍是[[保守黨_(英國)|保守黨]]。自2005年開始，他擔任[[希普利_(英國國會選區)|希普利]]選區選出的英國下議院議員。他是現在保守黨內最反叛的議員，曾經做出120次和保守黨黨鞭不同的投票<ref name=revolts>http://revolts.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Four-year-itch_cover_full.pdf</ref>，被批評為「殺掉一切他不喜歡的法案」<ref name=independent-20141214>{{cite news |url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/uk/politics/philip-davies-the-master-of-filibuster-and-friend-to-rogue-landlords-9924580.html |title=Philip Davies, the master of filibuster and friend to rogue landlords |author=Oliver Wright |newspaper=The Independent |date=14 December 2014 |accessdate=27 January 2015}}</ref><ref name=independent-20151101>{{cite news | url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/uk/politics/tory-mp-who-blocked-law-to-scrap-hospital-parking-charges-for-carers-says-he-was-speaking-up-for-a6716816.html | title=Tory MP Philip Davies who blocked law to scrap hospital parking charges for carers says he was 'speaking up for carers' | author=Jon Stone | publisher=The Independent | date=1 November 2015}}</ref><ref name=guardian-20151030>{{cite news|url=http://www.theguardian.com/society/2015/oct/30/tory-mps-filibuster-blocks-bill-to-give-carers-free-hospital-parking|title=Tory MP's filibuster blocks bill to give carers free hospital parking|work=The Guardian|author=Frances Perraudin|date=30 October 2015|accessdate=30 October 2015}}</ref> <ref name=independent-20151120>{{cite news|last1=Stone|first1=Jon|title=Tory MPs block bill to give first aid training to children by talking non-stop until debate ends|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/uk/politics/tory-mps-block-bill-to-give-first-aid-training-to-children-by-talking-until-time-runs-out-a6742251.html|accessdate=20 November 2015|work=The Independent|date=20 November 2015}}</ref>。
