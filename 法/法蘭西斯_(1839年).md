'''法蘭西斯'''是一艘於1839年、在[[南澳大利亚州|南澳大利亚州]]{{link-en|相遇灣|Encounter Bay}}所建的[[独桅纵帆船|独桅纵帆船]]。該船在1840年8月29日在{{link-en|南尼普頓島|Neptune Island}}附近海域失事。<ref>‘Frances’, [[Australian_National_Shipwreck_Database|Australian National Shipwreck Database]] at https://apps5a.ris.environment.gov.au/shipwreck/public/wreck/wreck.do?key=5369{{dead link|date=2018年3月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}, retrieved 03/07/2012.</ref><ref>[[Peter_Christopher_(author)|Christopher, P.]]; (1990), ''South Australian Shipwrecks: A Database (1802-1989)'', [[Society_for_Underwater_Historical_Research|Society for Underwater Historical Research]], North Adelaide, SA, pp 64. (ISBN 0 9588006 1 8)</ref>根據當時的報紙報導，尚存的船員上岸後有50天是靠著吃企鵝肉過活，之後送兩名
到[[林肯港|林肯港]]求助。而剩下在南尼普頓島的船員之後也全數救起。<ref>’Latest from Port Lincoln,’ ''Southern Australian'' (Adelaide, SA, 1838 - 1844), Tuesday 3 November 1840, page 3.</ref>該船的沈船遺址由[[大英國協王國|大英國協王國]]的{{link-en|1976年歷史沉船法|Historic Shipwrecks Act 1976}}所保護，而遺址位於{{coord|35|33|00|S|136|12|00|E|display=inline,title}}。至今，研究人員尚未尋獲該船遺址。<ref>‘Frances’, ''Australian National Shipwreck Database'' athttps://apps5a.ris.environment.gov.au/shipwreck/public/wreck/wreck.do?key=5369, retrieved 03/07/2012.</ref><ref>Baker, J. L. (2004), ''Towards a System of Ecologically Representative Marine Protected Areas in South Australian Marine Bioregions - Technical Report''. Prepared for Coast and Marine Conservation Branch, Department for Environment and Heritage, South Australia. page 74, at[http://www.environment.sa.gov.au/files/a0da1f2a-4568-4676-9650-9e2500c89c74/part_3.pdf], retrieved 21/07/2012.</ref>
