(Page available on-line [https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=WwJzAwAAQBAJ&pg=PA390&lpg=PA390&dq=faroe+islands+state+church&source=bl&ots=WqblMGRHqp&sig=BC0o4LtuKwp89TZnv91btuvgMsY&hl=en&sa=X&ei=1lnyVK21B8atU5CkgJgL&ved=0CD4Q6AEwBA#v=onepage&q=faroe%20islands%20state%20church&f=false here]).</ref>。在2007年7月29日之前，法羅群島教會是[[信義宗|信義宗]][[丹麥教會|丹麥教會]]的下屬教區之一。法羅群島83%的人口是法羅群島教會的信徒<ref>http://www.folkakirkjan.fo/default.aspx?pageid=6235&sectionid=207 Fólkakirkjan's official website (in Faroese)</ref>。

== 现任领导人 ==
