'''股癣'''（{{lang-en|tinea cruris}}）<ref name="Bolognia">{{ cite book | author = Rapini, R. P.; Bolognia, J. L.; Jorizzo, J. L. | title = Dermatology | publisher = Mosby | location = St. Louis | year = 2007 | isbn = 1-4160-2999-0 }}</ref>，是一种皮肤霉菌疾病，常出现于男性或女性的腹股沟和其他生殖器官附近，病原体以[[表皮癣菌|表皮癣菌]]、[[石膏样发癣茵|石膏样发癣茵]]较为常见<ref>{{ cite journal | journal = U.S. Pharmacist | title = Tinea Cruris in Men: Bothersome but Treatable | year = 2005 | volume = 30 | issue = 8 | pages = 13–17 }}</ref>。

病变多出现于股上部内侧，可向上下延伸，并波及[[外阴|外阴]]、[[会阴|会阴]]等处。初起时为小片针头大小的淡红色[[皮疹|皮疹]]，有时呈棕色或褐色，环形或弧形逐渐扩展，边缘明显，略高于正常皮肤表面<ref>{{ cite web | url = http://www.nlm.nih.gov/medlineplus/ency/article/000876.htm#Symptoms | title = Jock itch | publisher = NLM / NIH | work = MedlinePlus }}</ref>。病变可互相融合形成大片，损害中可有小的鳞屑，由于搔抓，可以溢液或结疤，也易继发化脓性感染。皮疹也可发生湿疹样变化或苔癣样硬化，多有[[脚癣|脚癣]]史<ref>{{ cite web | url = http://www.med.nyu.edu/patientcare/library/article.html?ChunkIID=11736 | title = Jock itch | publisher = NYU Langone Medical Center | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20071013165411/http://www.med.nyu.edu/patientcare/library/article.html?ChunkIID=11736 | archivedate = 2007-10-13 }}</ref>。治疗的方式通常包括[[抗霉药剂|抗霉药剂]]<ref>{{ cite journal | author = Nadalo, D.; Montoya, C.; Hunter-Smith, D. | title = What is the best way to treat tinea cruris? | journal = The Journal of Family Practice | year = 2006 | volume = 55 | issue = 3 | pages = 256–258 | pmid = 16510062 }}</ref>。
