'''庆祝枪声'''是在庆典中利用枪械对空中发射的庆祝方式，盛行在[[巴尔干半岛|巴尔干半岛]]、[[中东|中东]]、中亚的[[阿富汗|阿富汗]]、南亚的[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]]和[[印度北部|印度北部]]以及[[拉丁美洲|拉丁美洲]]地区。在波多黎各和美国的其他地区，这种庆祝方式是非法的，尤其是发生在假期的节日庆典。<ref name="dawn">{{cite news|url=http://dawn.com/2012/06/29/celebratory-gunfire/|title=Celebratory gunfire|work=Dawn|date=2012-06-29|accessdate=2012-08-15|first=Hassan Latif|last=Shaikh}}</ref>

一般上，这种庆祝的方式会出现在[[婚礼|婚礼]]、[[元旦|元旦]]、[[美国独立日|独立日]]，以及[[圣诞节|圣诞节]]和[[开斋节|开斋节]]的宗教假期。<ref>{{Youtube|IJEO3rmefmI|Arab Wedding Celebration with Guns}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title='Celebratory' gunfire at Saudi wedding brings down electricity cable killing 23 women and children|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2225691/Celebratory-gunfire-Saudi-wedding-brings-electricity-cable-killing-23-women-children.html|accessdate=2013-12-09|newspaper=Mail Online|date=2012-10-31}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=No Celebratory Gunfire This Fourth of July|url=http://miami.cbslocal.com/2013/07/03/no-celebratory-gunfire-this-fourth-of-july/|accessdate=2013-12-09|newspaper=Miami CBS|date=2013-07-03}}</ref><ref name=SETimes>{{cite web|url=http://www.balkantimes.com/cocoon/setimes/xhtml/en_GB/features/setimes/features/2006/01/27/feature-02|title=Campaign in Macedonia raises awareness of dangers posed by gunfire (SETimes.com) |accessdate=2007-08-01}}</ref> 这种庆祝方式可能会出现流弹导致人命伤亡，以及不必要的财物损失。<ref name=UPI>{{cite web|url=http://www.upi.com/NewsTrack/Top_News/2002/12/31/new_years_eve_gunfire_may_bring_jail_time/6707/|title=New Year's Eve gunfire may bring jail time|accessdate=2007-08-02|work=United Press International}}{{deadlink}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|author=Dean Nelson in New Delhi|title=Groom shot dead at wedding by uncle's stray celebratory bullet|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/worldnews/asia/india/7271134/Groom-shot-dead-at-wedding-by-uncles-stray-celebratory-bullet.html|accessdate=2013-12-08|newspaper=The Telegraph|date=2010-02-19}}</ref> [[美国疾病控制与预防中心|美国疾病控制与预防中心]]的一份研究发现，有80%的庆祝枪声导致头、脚和肩膀的受伤。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cdc.gov/MMWR/preview/mmwrhtml/mm5350a2.htm|title=New Year's Eve Injuries Caused by Celebratory Gunfire --- Puerto Rico, 2003|accessdate=2007-07-31}}</ref> 子弹也有可能会穿透屋顶，对屋瓦造成损害，引致屋顶漏水，大多数的损害都必须要加以修复。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.fsboadvertisingservice.com/library1.htm#BULLETS|title=Professionals Topics Library: Roofs & Bullets|accessdate=2007-08-02}}</ref>
