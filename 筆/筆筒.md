'''筆筒'''是放置[[筆|筆]]或其它長型文具的柱狀容器，有多種形狀與材質<ref>{{cite book |page=149 |chapter=Brush pots |title=Chinese Ivories: From the Shang to the Qing |author=Oriental Ceramic Society |publisher=British Museum |year=1984 |isbn=9780903421225}}</ref>。傳統的筆筒以[[木|木]]、[[岩石|石]]、[[陶器|陶]]、[[竹|竹]]等材料打造。

當筆筒用於收納[[毛筆|毛筆]]、水彩筆等類型的筆時，筆尖應該向上，避免筆毛受損。<ref>{{citation |title=History of Design: Decorative Arts and Material Culture |page=313 |isbn=0300196148 |author=Pat Kirkham, Susan Weber |year=2013}}</ref>

== 價值 ==
高品質的中國筆筒可以漲到很高的價格。2012年，一只竹製中國筆筒儘管邊緣有了修補過了痕跡，仍舊賣得了36萬英鎊。<ref name = "telegraph">{{cite web|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/culture/art/art-news/9293365/Chinese-art-collectors-scramble-to-buy-battered-pot-from-Bristol-charity-shop.html|title=Chinese art collectors scramble to buy battered pot from Bristol charity shop|newspaper=[[The_Daily_Telegraph|The Daily Telegraph]] |date=27 May 2012| accessdate=25 November 2015}}</ref>

在史金納亞洲藝術品拍賣會上，一件竹製中國筆筒以539500美金的價格賣出，筆筒上刻有山水風景和人物圖像。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.antiquesandthearts.com/brush-pot-is-hot-at-539500-at-skinner-asian-arts-auction/|title=Brush Pot Is Hot At 539,500 At Skinner Asian Arts Auction|work=antiquesandthearts.com|accessdate=11 November 2015}}</ref>
