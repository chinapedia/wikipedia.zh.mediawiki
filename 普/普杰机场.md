'''普杰机场'''（{{lang-en|'''Bhuj Airport'''}}，{{Airport codes|BHJ|VABJ}}）是位于[[印度|印度]][[古吉拉特邦|古吉拉特邦]][[喀奇县|喀奇县]]{{link-en|普杰|Bhuj}}市的一座国内机场。

普杰机场海拔78米，占地面积3.37平方千米，<ref name="technical">{{Cite web|title=Technical Information-Bhuj|url=http://www.aai.aero/allAirports/bhuj_technicalinfo.jsp|publisher=[[印度机场管理局|印度机场管理局]]|accessdate=4 February 2014|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140221140258/http://www.aai.aero/allAirports/bhuj_technicalinfo.jsp|archivedate=2014年2月21日}}</ref>  距离印巴边界48千米<ref name="hidebound">{{cite news|title= The Quake's Silent Ally: A Hidebound Bureaucracy |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2001/02/04/world/the-quake-s-silent-ally-a-hidebound-bureaucracy.html |first=John |last=Burns |date=4 February 2001|work=The New York Times|accessdate=4 February 2014}}</ref>。

普杰机场最初由{{link-en|印度空军普杰基地|Bhuj Rudra Mata Air Force Base}}附近两座油库和一条跑道构成，跑道一边是[[印度人航空|印度人航空]]公司油库，另一边是[[捷特航空|捷特航空]]公司油库。有一台大巴车运送乘客穿过印度空军普杰基地到达机场的航站楼。<ref name=gkim /><ref name=tsol>{{cite book|last=Bhatkal|first=Satyajit|title=The Spirit of Lagaan|date=2002|publisher=Popular Prakashan|isbn=9788179910030|pages=104}}</ref>

== 历史 ==
=== 1971年印巴战争 ===
普杰机场前身是一座简易机场，毁于[[1971年印巴战争|1971年印巴战争]]，被巴基斯坦空军投掷的[[凝固汽油弹|凝固汽油弹]]炸毁。在14天内，该机场被轰炸35次，经受了92枚炸弹和22枚火箭弹。<ref name="twinwar">{{cite book|last=Sagar |first=Krishna Chandra |title=The War of the Twins |url=http://books.google.co.in/books?id=XM5oZYYvEmYC&lpg=PP1&pg=PA212#v=onepage&q&f=false|year=1997|accessdate=4 February 2014|publisher=Northern Book Centre|isbn=8172110820|page=212}}</ref> 战后，机场重建，重建者为附近300名农村妇女。工程完成后，印度政府给这些村妇颁发了5万[[印度卢比|印度卢比]]作为奖励。<ref name="rebuilt">{{cite news|title=Women rebuilt Bhuj airstrip destroyed in '71 Pak attack |url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2010-07-25/rajkot/28319257_1_bhuj-strip-bunkers |date=25 July 2010|work=The Times of India|location=Bhuj|accessdate=3 February 2014}}</ref>

=== 2001年印度古吉拉特邦大地震  ===
普杰机场被2001年1月26日的[[2001年印度古吉拉特邦大地震|印度古吉拉特邦大地震]]损坏，震后花费4亿印度卢比修复。2003年，{{link-en|印度副总理| Deputy Prime Minister of India}}{{link-en|拉尔・克利须那・阿德瓦尼|L. K. Advani}}主持了修复一新的航站楼开幕典礼。<ref name="hinderadvani">{{cite news|title=Terror attacks will hinder peace initiative: Advani |url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2003-09-04/india/27217145_1_minister-l-k-advani-security-forces-peace-initiative |date=4 September 2003|work=The Times of India|location=Bhuj|accessdate=3 February 2014}}</ref><ref name="renamerow">{{cite news|title=Row over renaming of Bhuj airport |url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2005-11-05/ahmedabad/27865962_1_praful-patel-upa-government-civil-airport |first=Himanshu |last=Kaushik |date=5 November 2005|work=The Times of India |location=Ahmedabad |accessdate=3 February 2014}}</ref>

机场管制塔台毁于地震，设一临时终端用于地震救援，由印度空军三名军人手动操作，在4天的时间里，指挥了800余架次的起降。<ref name="threemen">{{cite news |title=How three men control India's busiest airport this week |url=http://expressindia.indianexpress.com/ie/daily/20010202/ina02044.html |first=Sonu |last=Jain |date=1 February 2001 |work=The Indian Express |location=Bhuj |accessdate=3 February 2014 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140221214954/http://expressindia.indianexpress.com/ie/daily/20010202/ina02044.html |archivedate=2014年2月21日 }}</ref> 跑道也在地震中受损，但在几个小时内就初步修复，到1月26日下午，飞机即可自行降落。从[[昌迪加尔|昌迪加尔]]市来的救灾设备在这里运入，灾民从这里运出，运往[[浦那|浦那]]等地方。<ref name=gkim>{{cite book|title=Gujarat (Kutch) India M7.7 Earthquake of January 26, 2001 and Napa M5.2 Earthquake of Sept. 3, 2000: Lifeline Performance|date=2001|publisher=ASCE Publications|isbn=9780784475065|pages=130, 145}}</ref><ref name="killer">{{cite news|title=THE KILLER EARTHQUAKE |url=http://www.frontline.in/static/html/fl1803/18030050.htm |first=Venkatesh |last=R |date=3 February 2001|coauthors=Praveen Swami |work=Frontline |location=Ahmedabad |accessdate=4 February 2014}}</ref> 从1月26日地震发生到2月5日，普杰机场起降飞机944架次，超过[[贾特拉帕蒂·希瓦吉国际机场|孟买机场]]和[[英迪拉·甘地国际机场|德里机场]]之和。<ref name=eeri>{{cite book|title=Earthquake Spectra: The Professional Journal of the Earthquake Engineering Research Institute, Volume 18, Issues 1–2|publisher=Earthquake Engineering Research Institute|pages=54, 386}}</ref> 印度空军向普杰和[[贾姆讷格尔|贾姆讷格尔]]派出直升机撤离灾民和搭建医疗营，<ref name="PIBIAF">{{cite news|title=INDIAN AIR FORCE : TOUCHING THE SKY WITH GLORY |url=http://pib.nic.in/feature/feyr2001/foct2001/f041020012.html |date=8 October 2001|agency=Press Information Bureau |accessdate=4 February 2014}}</ref> 货机将来自美国和巴基斯坦的救灾物资运到此机场。<ref name="seekfood">{{cite news|title=Earthquake survivors seek food and shelter |url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-19565/Earthquake-survivors-seek-food-shelter.html |work=Daily Mail|accessdate=4 February 2014}}</ref>

[[国际红十字会|国际红十字会]]的报告却与以上情况截然不同，报告称，机场停电并且没有无线电通讯，处于关闭状态。<ref name="redcross">{{cite news|title=In Action |url=http://www.redcross.int/EN/mag/magazine2001_2/india.html |first=Patrick |last=Fuller |publisher=Red Cross |date=February 2001|accessdate=4 February 2014}}</ref>

==基础设施==
普杰机场只有一座航站楼，处理所有离到港航班，<ref name="jetinfo">{{Cite web|title=Bhuj Airport Information |url=http://www.jetairways.com/EN/ID/AirportInformation/Bhuj.aspx |publisher=Jet Airways |accessdate=4 February 2014}}</ref> 可同时接纳350人的客流量<ref name="cruisingheights">{{Cite web |title=Crusing Heights |url=http://www.aai.aero/misc/ch8.pdf |date=[[印度机场管理局|印度机场管理局]] |publisher=April 2011 |accessdate=4 February 2014 |series=Chapter 34 |page=35 }}{{dead link|date=2018年3月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref> 航站楼1楼面积6682平方米，2楼1382平方米。机场有2个登机口，吞吐量为200人到港和200人离港。机场有4个乘机登记处，1个安检柜台，1个入口，1套X射线行李扫描器。<ref name="technical" />

普杰机场可起降的最大飞机为[[空中客车A320|空中客车A320]]，停机坪可同时停放2架[[波音737#737-800|波音737-800]]<ref name="cruisingheights" />。机场还有一个固定的直升机停机坪<ref name="kachchhelipad">{{cite web |title=Co-Ordinates of various helipad -Kutch Helipad |url=http://cad.gujarat.gov.in/kutch-helipad.htm |publisher= Directorate of Civil Aviation, Government of Gujarat |accessdate=4 February 2014 }}</ref>。

机场平均每月到港旅客4039人，离港旅客3636人。<ref name="mapwithairport">{{cite web |title=Bhuj Airport |url=http://cad.gujarat.gov.in/BhujAirPort.htm |publisher= Directorate of Civil Aviation, Government of Gujarat |accessdate=4 February 2014 }}</ref>

2013年，印度民用航空安全局发布的一份报告称，普杰机场缺少手握金属探测器和{{link-en|痕量爆炸物探测器|Explosives trace detector}}，因而面临安全威胁。该报告还称，机场[[闭路电视|闭路电视]]图像储存周期太短。<ref name="securitythreat">{{cite news|title=Small airports across India face major security threats |url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/indiahome/indianews/article-2381597/Small-airports-India-face-major-security-threats.html |first=Vikas |last=Mishra |date=30 July 2013|work=Daily Mail|accessdate=4 February 2014}}</ref>

普杰机场的空中交通管制机构还控制和指挥蒙德拉机场的空中交通，蒙德拉机场是一私人机场，为{{link-en|阿达尼集团|Adani group}}所有，距离普杰机场70公里。<ref name="getnod">{{cite news|title=Jamshedpur, Vijaynagar, Mundra airports get nod to go commercial |url=http://www.livemint.com/Companies/Q79R5DLxiR6NmTc9JiPSBN/Jamshedpur-Vijaynagar-Mundra-airports-get-nod-to-go-commer.html |first=Tarun |last=Shukla |date=30 August 2007|agency=[[Livemint|Livemint]]|accessdate=4 February 2014}}</ref>

2004年，为促进旅游业，政府在普杰市与普杰机场之间建设了一座“丘园”。丘园项目繁多，有骑马、游戏、划船等，有两个小湖和一架亚洲最大的蓝鲸骨骼，平均每个节假日吸引800到1000名游客。<ref name="hillgarden">{{cite news|title=Hill garden at Bhuj a big draw |url=http://www.dnaindia.com/india/report-hill-garden-at-bhuj-a-big-draw-1254998 |first=Maheshwari |last=DV |date=11 May 2009|work=[[每日新闻分析|每日新闻分析]] |location=Bhuj |accessdate=4 February 2014}}</ref>

==航空公司与航线==
[[File:Jet_Airways_aircraft_at_Bhuj_Airport.jpg|thumbnail]]
1984年，捷特航空公司开始运营孟买至普杰的航线，<ref name=routes>{{cite web|last=Maslen|first=Richard|title=Jet Airways Outlines ATR 72–600 Plans|url=http://www.routesonline.com/news/29/breaking-news/172666/jet-airways-outlines-atr-72-600-plans/|publisher=Routesonline.com|accessdate=4 February 2014}}</ref> 目前每天有两个航班<ref name="jetinfo" />。

1930年代，[[印度航空#历史|塔塔航空]]公司赢得政府合同，开始运营从巴基斯坦最大城市[[卡拉奇|卡拉奇]]经停普杰、[[艾哈迈达巴德|艾哈迈达巴德]]、[[孟买|孟买]]、[[海得拉巴_(印度)|海得拉巴]]、[[马德拉斯|金奈]]和[[蒂鲁吉拉帕利|蒂鲁吉拉帕利]]到[[斯里兰卡|斯里兰卡]]首都[[科伦坡|科伦坡]]的航线，两周一次航班。<ref name=tiyb>{{cite book|title=The Indian Year Book, Volume 23; Volumes 25–28|date=1941|publisher=Bennett, Coleman & Company|pages=603|url=http://books.google.co.in/books?id=NEIdAQAAMAAJ&q=}}</ref><ref name="hundredyears">{{cite news |title=One hundred years of flying high |url=http://www.thehindu.com/features/kids/article2584818.ece |first=Murali |last=Krishnaswamy |date=31 October 2011 |work=The Hindu |accessdate=4 February 2014}}</ref> 普杰机场早期就有航班经停艾哈迈达巴德到孟买的航班，不久，印度人航空公司取消了在艾哈迈达巴德的中转，理由是客流太少。<ref name="requestmore">{{cite news|title= Gujarat requests Indian Airlines to start more flights to Bhuj |url=http://expressindia.indianexpress.com/fe/daily/20000820/fec20038.html |first=Jyotsna |last=Bhatnagar |date=19 August 2000|work=The Indian Express|location=Ahmedabad|accessdate=3 February 2014}}</ref> 印度人航空公司以前还曾运营过孟买经停贾姆讷格尔到普杰的航线。<ref name=sah>{{cite book|last=Bradnock|first=Robert W.|title=South Asian Handbook|publisher=Trade & Travel Publications|location=1994|pages=443, 1019|url=http://books.google.co.in/books?id=le8uAQAAIAAJ&q=}}</ref> 已結業的翠鸟航空公司曾运营孟买到普杰的直航航线。<ref name=kingfisher>{{cite news|title=Kingfisher Airlines launches direct flights between Mumbai and Bhuj|url=http://travel.financialexpress.com/200708/aviationworld28.shtml|accessdate=4 February 2014|newspaper=The Financial Express|date=August 2007}}</ref>
