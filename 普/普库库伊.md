'''普库库伊'''（{{lang-en|Puu Kukui}}）是[[美国|美国]][[夏威夷|夏威夷]][[茂宜島|茂宜島]]西部毛伊县的火山峰。为火奴鲁阿火山带的最高峰，海拔1764米。普庫庫伊是全球最濕潤的地方之一<ref>{{cite book |first=Sonia P. |last=Juvik |year=1998 |title=Atlas of Hawaii |publisher=[[University_of_Hawaii_Press|University of Hawaii Press]] |isbn=978-0-8248-2125-8 |page=14}}</ref>，年雨量可高達9820毫米<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.prh.noaa.gov/hnl/hydro/pages/data_tbl_12.html |title=NOAA Hawai{{okina}}i rain gauge summary |work=Pacific Islands Water Science Center |publisher=[[United_States_Geological_Survey|United States Geological Survey]] |accessdate=2009-02-20}}</ref> ，是夏威夷州年年雨量次多之處（僅次於年雨量可達11500毫米的的{{link-en|懷厄萊阿萊峰|Mount Waiʻaleʻale}}）<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.prh.noaa.gov/hnl/hydro/pages/jul08sum.php |title=July 2008 Precipitation Summary |work=National Weather Service Forecast Office Honolulu, HI |publisher=[[National_Oceanic_and_Atmospheric_Administration|National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration]] |date=2008-09-04 |accessdate=2009-02-20 |quote=The USGS gage on Puu Kukui lived up to expectation as the second wettest spot in the state by having the second highest total of 26.67 inches (79 percent of normal) below only Mount Waialeale’s 30.30 inches. |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081002160520/http://www.prh.noaa.gov/hnl/hydro/pages/jul08sum.php |archivedate=2008-10-02 }} </ref>，無法流走的雨水形成許多沼澤。土壤深而厚實且呈酸性<ref>{{cite journal |url=http://www.nokaoimagazine.com/Features/v.11n.2/Rooted_in_Mystery.html |title=Rooted in Mystery |first=Shannon |last=Wianecki |journal=[[Maui_No_Ka_'Oi_Magazine|Maui Nō Ka {{okina}}Oi Magazine]] |volume=11 |issue=2 | date=March  2007 }}</ref>。此地也具有許多[[特有種|特有種]]生物，包括{{link-en|綠劍草|Argyroxiphium grayanum}}、{{link-en|多型鐵心木|metrosideros polymorpha}}的一個[[變種_(植物命名)|變種]]（''Metrosideros polymorpha var. pseudorugosa''）<ref>{{cite journal |url=http://www.biology.duke.edu/cunningham/pdfs/PercyMetrosideros2008.pdf |first=Diana M. |last=Percy |author2=Adam M. Garver |author3=Warren L. Wagner |author4=Helen F. James |author5=Clifford W. Cunningham |author6=Scott E. Miller |author7= Robert C. Fleischer  |title=Progressive island colonization and ancient origin of Hawaiian Metrosideros (Myrtaceae) |journal=Proceedings of the Royal Society B |volume=275 |year=2008 |doi=10.1098/rspb.2008.0191 |page=1484 |pmid=18426752 |issue=1642 |pmc=2602662}}</ref>以及許多種Hawaiian lobelioids。由於山頂的惡劣氣候與[[泥炭|泥炭]]，許多植物都十分矮小。另外此地僅限研究與保育人員進入。
