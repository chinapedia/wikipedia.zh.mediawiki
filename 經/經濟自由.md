'''經濟自由'''用於[[經濟|經濟]]研究和[[政策|政策]]討論。與[[自由|自由]]相同，有著不同的定義，當中沒有普遍接受的。<ref name=Bronfenbrenner1955>{{citation|last1=Bronfenbrenner|first=Martin|authorlink=Martin Bronfenbrenner|year=1955|journal=[[Ethics_(journal)|Ethics]]|title=[http://www.jstor.org/stable/2378928 Two Concepts of Economic Freedom]|volume=65|number=3|unused_data=|page=pp.157--170}}</ref><ref name=SenRationalityFreedom>{{citation|last=Sen|first=Amartya|authorlink=Amartya Sen|title=Rationality and Freedom|page=9}}</ref>一個接近經濟自由的定義來自[[自由意志主義|自由意志主義]]傳統強調的[[自由市場|自由市場]]和[[私有制|私有制]]，同時延伸到個人選擇的[[福利經濟學|福利經濟學]]研究，帶更大的經濟自由來自“大”（在一些法律定義中）的對可能的選擇之設定。另一個更冷靜的觀點強調分配公正和每個人的基本自由的背景。<ref>Freeman S. (2003). ''The Cambridge Companion to Rawls'' [http://books.google.com/books?id=ipMVcT5R2zwC&printsec=frontcover#PPA4,M1 p. 4]. Cambridge University Press.</ref>

今天，這個詞通常與[[古典自由主義|古典自由主義]]（或[[自由市場|自由市場]]）聯繫在一起，並且定義為自由[[生產|生產]]、[[貿易|貿易]]和[[消費|消費]]任何商品與服務，而不搶劫、詐騙或偷竊。這體現在[[法治|法治]]、[[財產權|財產權]]和[[合約自由|合約自由]]、以由外部和內部之開放性市場為特徵、保護財產權利和經濟干預之自由。<ref name="EncyclopediaPubChoice">{{cite encyclopedia|title=Economic Freedom and its Measurement|encyclopedia=The Encyclopedia of Public Choice |year=2004 |publisher=[[Springer_Science+Business_Media|Springer]] |isbn=978-0-7923-8607-0 |volume=2|pages=[http://books.google.com/books?id=YUVMr–aFYwYC&printsec=frontcover#PRA1–PA161,M1 161]–171 }}</ref>Surjit S. Bhalla. ''Freedom and economic growth: a virtuous cycle?''. Published in ''Democracy's Victory and Crisis''. (1997). Cambridge University Press. ISBN 0521575834 p. 205</ref><ref>David A. Harper. ''Foundations of Entrepreneurship and Economic Development''. (1999). Routledge. ISBN 0415153425 p.57, 64</ref>

[[經濟自由指數|經濟自由指數]]試圖衡量（自由市場）經濟自由和基於這些值之發現進行經驗主義研究來提高人民生活水平、經濟增長、收入均等、減少腐敗和減少政治暴力。<ref>{{Citation|last=Pei|first=Minxin|chapter=Political Institutions, Democracy, and Development| title= Democracy, Market Economics, and Development|publisher=World Bank Publications|publication-date=2001|isbn=9780821348628}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last1=Easton|first1=Stephen T.|last2=Walker|first2=Michael A.|title= Income, growth, and economic freedom|journal=American Economic Review|volume=87|issue=2|pages=328–332|publisher=American Economic Association|date=May 1997}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last1=Ayal|first1=Eliezer B.|last2=Karras|first2=Georgios|title=Components of economic freedom and growth: an empirical study|journal=Journal of Developing Areas|volume=32|issue=3|pages=327-338|publisher=Western Illinois University|date=Spring 1998}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last=Scully|first=Gerald|title=Economic Freedom, Government Policy, and the Trade-Off Between Equity and Economic Growth|journal=Public Choice|volume=113|issue=1-2|pages=77–96|publisher=Kluwer Academic Publishers|year=2002}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last= Berggren|first= Niclas|title= Economic Freedom and Equality: Friends or Foes?|journal=Public Choice|volume=100|issue=3-4|pages=203-223|publisher=Kluwer Academic Publishers|year=1999}}</ref>有時會用[[各國經濟自由度列表|經濟指數對各個國家排名]]，[[香港|香港]]和[[新加坡|新加坡]]經常領先。1985年到2005年間，只有少數國家沒有增加他們的[[經濟自由度指數|經濟自由度指數]]評分。<ref name="fraser">Economic Freedom of the World 2005, Fraser Institute</ref>一些實證分析表明，該指數沒有與其經濟增長密切相關<ref>De Haan, J. and C. L. J. Siermann (1998). Further Evidence on the Relationship Between Economic Freedom and Economic Growth, ''Public Choice''. 95: 363-380. </ref>，但崩潰組成部分的回歸分析表明，當其他方面影響它時，一些具體的自由有助於經濟增長。<ref name=Heckelman>Heckelman, Jac C., Stroup, Michael D. (2000). Which Economic Freedoms Contribute to Growth? ''Kyklos'' '''53'''(4):527-44</ref>

其他經濟自由概念包括免於贫困之自由（美國總統[[富蘭克林·羅斯福|富蘭克林·羅斯福]]1941年在美國國會大廈發表演說時提出的“言論自由、信仰自由、免於貧困及免於恐懼之自由”）<ref name=Bronfenbrenner1955 /><ref name=RooseveltFourFreedoms>{{citeweb
|url=http://www.fdrlibrary.marist.edu/od4freed.html|title=Franklin Roosevelt's Annual Address to Congress - The "Four Freedoms"|date=January 6, 1941|accessdate=November 10, 2008}}</ref>和進行集體談判之自由。<ref name=Jacoby1998>{{cite book|last = Jacoby|first = Daniel|authorlink = Daniel Jacoby|title = Laboring for Freedom: A New Look at the History of Labor in America (eBook)|publisher = [[ME_Sharpe|ME Sharpe]]|location = Armonk, NY|date = 1998|isbn = 9780585190303|page=8-9,148,166-167}}</ref>
