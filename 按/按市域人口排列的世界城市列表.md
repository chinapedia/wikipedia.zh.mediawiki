本条目列出了“[[市域|市域]]”概念定义下的世界上人口最多的城市。[[市域|市域]]（{{lang|en|city proper}}）是由法定或政治边界和定义的地方，是常以某种形式的地方政府为特征的行政上认可的城市。 <ref name="wup2007">{{cite web|url=http://esa.un.org/unup/index.asp?panel=6 |title=World Urbanization Prospects: The 2007 Revision Population Database |publisher=Esa.un.org |date= |accessdate=26 July 2010 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100822053414/http://esa.un.org/unup/index.asp?panel=6 |archivedate=22 August 2010 |df= }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://millenniumindicators.un.org/unsd/demographic/sconcerns/densurb/densurbanNotes.htm |title=United Nations Statistics Division – Demographic and Social Statistics |publisher=Millenniumindicators.un.org |date= |accessdate=26 July 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite book |title=Demographic Yearbook 2005, Volume 57|year=2008 |publisher= United Nations|isbn=92-1-051099-2 |page= 2; 756 |url= https://books.google.com/books?id=OwfGJtkpCbwC&pg=PA241}}</ref>[[市域|市域]]及其边界和人口的数据覆盖[[郊区|郊区]]。<ref name="wup2003">{{cite book |title=World Urbanization Prospects: The 2003 Revision |year=2004 |publisher= United Nations|isbn=92-1-151396-0 <!-- This is the correct ISBN. The number on the copyright page of the book is invalid. --> |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=oxOAckK2stYC}}</ref>

[[联合国|联合国]]出版物《世界城镇化展望》（World Urbanization Prospects）将[[市域|市域]]人口定义为“居住在城市行政边界内或由单一权力机构直接由城市管辖的人口。”（the population living within the administrative boundaries of a city or controlled directly from the city by a single authority）。该书还继续说到：“行政边界所定义的市域包括很大比例的在城市工作或学习的人口居住的[[郊区|郊区]]。”（city proper as defined by administrative boundaries may not include suburban areas where an important proportion of the population working or studying in the city lives.）<ref name="wup2003" />该定义并准确地描述城市的城区或大都会区。
使用该行政的定义时，市区小于辖区的城市（广域市）会上榜。中国城市管理了远远超出传统的“市区”的区域，延伸到了郊区和鄉村地区。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Alexander|first1=Ruth|title=The world's biggest cities: How do you measure them?|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/magazine-16761784|date=29 January 2012|publisher=BBC News|accessdate=16 March 2015}}</ref>
