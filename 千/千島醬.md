'''千島醬'''（{{lang-en|thousand Island dressing}}）是一種[[調味料|調味料]]，由[[雷莫拉醬|雷莫拉醬]]（remoulade）演變而來。千島醬的原料主要是[[美乃滋|美乃滋]]，可與[[橄欖油|橄欖油]]、[[檸檬汁|檸檬汁]]、[[橙汁|橙汁]]、[[辣椒粉_(西餐)|辣椒粉]]、[[辣醬油|辣醬油]]、[[芥末醬|芥末醬]]、[[醋|醋]]、[[奶油|奶油]]、[[辣椒醬|辣椒醬]]、[[番茄糊|番茄糊]]（tomato puree）、[[番茄醬|番茄醬]]或[[塔巴斯科辣椒醬|塔巴斯科辣椒醬]]等一起調和。<ref name="Honberger1914">Honberger, Maud Mitchell, ed. (1914). ''Tried Receipts of Pasadena.''</ref><ref name="weaver1917">Weaver, Louise Bennett; LeCron, Helen Cowles (1917). ''A thousand ways to please your husband, with Bettina's best recipes,'' p. 89. New York: Britton Publishing Company</ref> 另外，千島醬亦可加入剁碎的原料，如[[醃黃瓜|醃黃瓜]]、[[洋蔥|洋蔥]]、[[菜椒|菜椒]]、綠[[橄欖|橄欖]]、水煮[[蛋|蛋]]、[[香芹|香芹]]、[[西班牙甜椒|西班牙甜椒]]（pimento）、[[蝦夷蔥|蝦夷蔥]]、[[蒜|蒜]]或[[堅果|堅果]]（[[核桃|核桃]]或[[栗子|栗子]]）。<ref name="grimes1915">Grimes, Etta (1915). Home Economics: Some choice recipes. The Oregon Countryman, May 1915, p. 325.</ref><ref name="woodland1919">Woodland, Mrs. F.B. (1919). ''Stevenson Memorial Cook Book,'' p. 76. Sarah Hackett Stevenson Memorial Lodging House Association, Chicago</ref><ref name="hirtzler1919">Hirtzler, Victor (1919). ''The Hotel St. Francis Cook Book,'' p. 335. The Hotel Monthly Press, John Willy, Inc., Chicago.</ref>

== 起源 ==

[[File:Thousand-Island-Dressing.jpg|thumb]]

千島醬首次出現在1900年的一本[[食譜|食譜]]裡，當時在[[紐奧良|紐奧良]]一帶非常受歡迎。<ref name="1900cookbook">Breaded Veal Rounds and Thousand Island Dressing. In ''A Book of Famous Old New Orleans Recipes used in the South for more than 200 years,'' p. 21. Peerless Printing Company, New Orleans, 1900.</ref> 

根據《The Oxford Companion of Food and Drink》，千島醬的名稱推測起源於美國和加拿大間聖羅倫斯河上的[[千島群島_(北美洲)|千島群島]]<ref name="smith2007">Smith, Andrew F. (2007). ''The Oxford Companion of Food and Drink,'' p. 514. Oxford University Press US, ISBN 9780195307962</ref>，當地常耳聞的說法是，一位釣魚嚮導的妻子 Sophia LaLonde 做了千島醬當做丈夫 George 在岸邊吃晚餐的調味料<ref name="stiles2010">Stiles, Kaelyn; Altıok, Özlem; Bell, Michael M. (2010). The ghosts of taste: food and the cultural politics of authenticity. ''Agriculture and Human Values'' DOI: 10.1007/s10460-010-9265-y</ref>。一名加拿大女演員 May Irwin 嘗過千島醬之後，拿到了製作方法<ref name="mcneese2005">McNeese, Tim (2005). ''The St. Lawrence River.'' Infobase Publishing, ISBN  9780791082454</ref>，接著把作法給了另一位居住在千島群島的暑期度假居民 George Boldt ，Boldt 經營[[華道夫-阿斯多里亞酒店|華道夫-阿斯多里亞酒店]]，並把千島醬列入菜單內<ref name="1000islandsinn">Thousand Islands Inn, Clayton, NY. {{cite web |url=http://www.1000islands.com/inn/dressing.htm |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2011-06-18 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110611063820/http://www.1000islands.com/inn/dressing.htm |archivedate=2011-06-11 }}</ref>。1959年《[[國家地理雜誌|國家地理雜誌]]》一篇報導中指出，千島醬據說是 Boldt 的主廚發明的。<ref name="brown1959">Brown, Andrew H. (March 1959). New St. Lawrence Seaway opens the Great Lakes to the world. National Geographic Magazine, vol. 115, no. 3, p. 336</ref>

== 食用方法 ==
1950年代時，千島醬是[[三明治|三明治]]和[[沙拉|沙拉]]相當基本的調味料，常使用在美國的速食餐廳，有時也當做[[魯賓三明治|魯賓三明治]]的醬料<ref name="dispirito2010">DiSpirito, Rocco (2010). ''Now Eat This! 150 of America's Favorite Comfort Foods, All Under 350 Calories,'' p. 75. Random House, ISBN 9780345520906</ref>。[[麥當勞|麥當勞]][[大麥克|大麥克]]漢堡所使用的醬料就是由千島醬衍生的。
