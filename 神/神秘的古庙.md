'''神秘的古庙'''（{{lang-en|Legends of the Hidden Temple}}）是一档[[美国|美国]]的动作冒险类<ref name="arneberg">{{cite news|last=Arneberg|first=Marianne|title=Programmers Dive into Kids Shows: Programs Involving Children Hottest New Trend in Television|newspaper=Orlando Sentinel|date=1993-10-04|page=12|quote='We wanted to do an action-adventure game show - sort of like a live video game for television,' said Scott Stone}}</ref>儿童游戏节目。节目背景设定为一藏有失落的玛雅宝藏并有神庙守卫保护的古庙。<ref name="lotht-catherine"/>Kirk Fogg充当主持<ref name="eotvgs">{{cite encyclopedia |last=Schwartz |first=David |author= |authorlink= |coauthors=Steve Ryan, and Fred Wostbrock |editor= |encyclopedia=The Encyclopedia of TV Game Shows |title=Legends of the Hidden Temple |url= |accessdate= |edition=3rd|date= |year=1999 |month= |publisher=Facts on File |volume= |location= |isbn=0-8160-3847-3 |doi= |pages=124 |quote= }}</ref> 并作为团队指导，[[Dee_Bradley_Baker|Dee Bradley Baker]]为[[奥尔梅克|奥尔梅克]]石头像（Olmec colossal heads）配音，石像知晓隐藏在宝藏背后的秘密。<ref name="lotht-catherine"/>六支队的两个孩子（一男一女）将通过亲身闯关以及回答有关历史、神话和地理的问题来竞争获得一件古庙中的历史文物。经过三轮淘汰赛，只有一支队留下，他们需要在三分钟内完成决赛任务并获得大奖。

本节目由[[Stone_Stanley_Entertainment|Stone Stanley Productions]]与[[尼克国际儿童频道|尼克国际儿童频道]]制作，由[[Nickelodeon_Studios|Nickelodeon Studios]]录制。于1993年9月11日至1995年在尼克国际儿童频道首播<ref name=brooks-marsh />并在1999年2月28日开始重播。（1999–2007年在[[Nickelodeon_Games_and_Sports_for_Kids|Nick GAS]]上重播，偶尔也在[[TeenNick|TeenNick]]上重播）。

本节目在1995年赢得[[CableACE|CableACE]]游戏特别节目或系列奖（Best Game Show Special or Series）。<ref name="imdb-awards">{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0310482/awards|title="Legends of the Hidden Temple" (1993) - Awards|work=The Internet Movie Database|accessdate=2008-05-13}}</ref><ref name="eotvgs-awards">{{cite encyclopedia | title=Appendix E:  Game Show Award Winners and Nominees | last=Schwartz | first=David | coauthors=Steve Ryan, and Fred Wostbrock | encyclopedia=The Encyclopedia of TV Game Shows | year=1999 | edition=3rd | publisher=Facts on File | pages=305 | isbn=0-8160-3847-3}}</ref>

== 游戏过程 ==
游戏设计基于[[印第安纳·琼斯|印第安纳·琼斯]]系列电影<ref name="katz">{{cite news|title=Secrets of the 'Temple': Behind the scenes at Nickelodeon's hit game show|last=Katz|first=Frances|date=1995-04-09|work=Boston Herald|pages=12|accessdate=2009-03-13|quote='Kids love this show,' says co-producer Brendan Huntington, citing the combination of athletic skill and brainpower and the Indiana Jones-type setting as being particularly popular with kids aged 11–14 . . . 'We want the kids to feel like they really are right inside an "Indiana Jones" movie duking it out to the end,' says co-producer David Greenfield.}}</ref>，《[[Orlando_Sentinel|Orlando Sentinel]]》Marianne Arneberg描述游戏设计就像“《[[危险边缘|危险边缘]]》与《[[夺宝奇兵|夺宝奇兵]]》的混合体”。<ref name="arneberg" /> 游戏背景被设定为[[玛雅文化|玛雅文化]]。<ref name="katz2">Katz. "Much more threatening this season will be the timed chase through the Mayan ruins"</ref><ref name="scarberry">{{cite news | title=Classmates to Make Nickelodeon Debut | accessdate=30 June 2010 | last=Scarberry | first=Pat | date=1993-09-19 | newspaper=St. Petersburg Times | page=11 | quote=[''Legends''] encourages kids to use both mental and physical capabilities as they trek through a Mayan ruin searching for legendary artifacts.}}</ref> 节目包含了不同类型的挑战关卡： 一个广而浅<ref>Katz.  "'We still have the occasional kid fall into the moat because it looks like fun,' Huntington says. 'But the water's not that deep.'"</ref> 的水池（护城河）、一系列智力问答、一个大的两个半层楼的<ref>"Kids Can Audition for Nick Show."  "kids scramble through a 13-room, 2½ -story Mayan temple"</ref> 垂直立体迷宫。迷宫路口有一会说话的奥尔梅克石头像（名叫Olmec）。Olmec讲述背景知识以及挑战规则。 每一集都围绕一个特定的来自全世界的传说（有真实的亦有虚构的），包括“[[托马斯·爱德华·劳伦斯|阿拉伯的劳伦斯]]的头饰”、“[[本杰明·富兰克林|本杰明·富兰克林]]导电实验的钥匙”、“[[叶卡捷琳娜二世|叶卡捷琳娜二世]]缀满宝石的蛋”、“[[伊卡洛斯|伊卡洛斯]]的折翼”。 除了提供传说相关的宝物，传说也是历史问答的步骤关键，同时又于最后的迷宫设计有关。<ref>Arneberg. "The 40 episodes of Legends 'are all story driven' — the stunts are themed around a specific legend, as are questions contestants must answer during a segment of the show, Stone said."</ref>

每一集里，六支队伍的两名成员经过三轮比赛确定一族进入决赛。每支队伍的队服以颜色及花纹区别：红色美洲虎、蓝梭鱼、绿猴、橙绿鬣蜥、紫鹦鹉、银蛇。<ref name="lotht-catherine">Stone-Stanley Productions. "The Jewel-Encrusted Egg of Catherine the Great."  ''Legends of the Hidden Temple.'' Starring Kirk Fogg and Dee Bradley Baker as Olmec. 1995</ref>
