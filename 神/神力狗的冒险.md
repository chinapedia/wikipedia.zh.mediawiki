'''神力狗的冒险'''（{{lang-en|''The Adventures of Superpup''}}），是1958年失败的试播集，该试播集因《超人冒险》的成功而制作。<ref name='ST'>{{cite web | url = http://seattletimes.nwsource.com/html/movies/2003079436_superman25a.html | title = Who's gonna save us now? | accessdate = 2011-05-13 | last = Shannon | first = Jeff |date = June 25, 2006| work = [[The_Seattle_Times|The Seattle Times]]}}</ref>该试播集也是超人系列作品中不以人类为主角的电视作品。<ref name='DIVE'>{{cite web | url = http://www.di-ve.com/Default.aspx?ID=32&Action=1&NewsId=49288&currentPage=72 | title = The media on the media | accessdate = 2011-05-13 | last = Cilia | first = Tanja | date = January 21, 2008 | publisher = di-ve.com}}</ref>

==简介==
在剧中克拉克·肯特角色更名为“树皮特”（Bark Bent），在Daily Bugle工作。他的同事是记者Pamela Poodle。Pamela Poodle被邪恶的Sheepdip教授绑在一支被发射到太空的火箭，而神力狗必须去救她。<ref>Terrace, Vincent  (2009).  ''Encyclopedia of Television Shows, 1925 through 2007'' (Volume 1).  Jefferson, North Carolina:  McFarland & Company, Inc. ISBN 978-0-7864-3305-6.</ref>
