'''豹蛺蝶屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Argynnis''）是[[蛺蝶科|蛺蝶科]][[釉蛺蝶亞科|釉蛺蝶亞科]]中的一個屬，其物種分佈於[[古北界|古北界]]<ref>Wahlberg, Niklas and Andrew V. Z. Brower. 2006. Argynnis Fabricius 1807. Speyeria Scudder 1872 currently viewed as a subjective junior synonym, Argyreus Scopoli 1777 suppressed by the ICZN as having priority over Argynnis. Version 07 November 2006 (under construction). http://tolweb.org/Argynnis/70410/2006.11.07 in The Tree of Life Web Project, http://tolweb.org/</ref>。
