'''[[荷蘭|荷蘭]]'''以開放著稱，十分注重[[性傾向|性傾向]]等個人隱私的相關政策。人民普遍支持[[LGBT|LGBT]]族群擁有平等權利<ref>[http://www.minvws.nl/en/reports/dmo/2006/acceptance-of-homosexuality-in-the-netherlands.asp Ministry of health - Acceptance of homosexuality in the Netherlands]</ref>，部分基督徒及[[穆斯林|伊斯蘭教徒]][[外來移民|移民]]對性別平等仍採保守態度。調查顯示，至少三分之二的[[反同性戀|反同性戀]][[仇恨罪|仇恨罪]]是由穆斯林移民家庭的[[青年|青年]]所為<ref>http://www.torontosun.com/comment/columnists/ezra_levant/2010/10/08/15630946.html</ref>，其多數是[[摩洛哥|摩洛哥]]或[[土耳其人|土耳其人]]後裔。2011年10月调查表明，虽然[[荷兰|荷兰]]'''90%'''的[[荷兰人|荷兰族]]认为[[同性恋|同性恋]]并不违背道德，但仅有'''30%'''在荷[[土耳其人|土耳其人]]和'''25%'''的在荷[[摩洛哥人|摩洛哥人]]接纳此观点。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.rnw.nl/english/article/lesbians-flee-amsterdam-bible-belt |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2015-01-14 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150113045938/http://www.rnw.nl/english/article/lesbians-flee-amsterdam-bible-belt |archivedate=2015-01-13 }}</ref> 而据报道，印度裔的印度教徒多能接受[[荷兰人|荷兰族]]的观点。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.rnw.nl/english/article/homophobia-among-hindus-holland |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2015-01-14 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141014172659/http://www.rnw.nl/english/article/homophobia-among-hindus-holland |archivedate=2014-10-14 }}</ref>
