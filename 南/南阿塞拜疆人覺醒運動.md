'''南[[阿塞拜疆人|阿塞拜疆人]]民族覺醒運動'''（[[阿塞拜疆语|阿塞拜疆语]]: Güney Azerbaycan Milli Oyanış Herekatı，GAMOH），是一個以[[巴庫|巴庫]]為基地<ref>{{cite news |author=Jean-Christophe Peuch |title=Iran: Cartoon Protests Point To Growing Frustration Among Azerbaijanis |url=http://www.rferl.org/featuresarticle/2006/05/d11e08d4-32e7-4de5-9e8f-ab2971130928.html |work=[[自由欧洲电台／自由电台|自由欧洲电台／自由电台]] |date=May 31, 2006}}</ref>的阿塞拜疆人民族主義團體，主張伊朗阿塞拜疆人[[自决|自决]]和統一[[阿拉斯河|阿拉斯河]]兩岸的阿塞拜塞人。<ref>William O. Beeman, "The "Great Satan" vs. the "Mad Mullahs",University of Chicago Press, 2008. pg 135: "The Bush administration also flitered with supporting the Mujaheddin-Khalq (MEK) in their efforts to overthrow the Tehran government, supporting Mahmud Ali Chehregani, leader of an Azerbaijani separatist movement calling for a federated Iran".</ref><ref>{{cite news |author=Jean-Christophe Peuch |title=Iran: Cartoon Protests Point To Growing Frustration Among Azeris |url=http://www.rferl.org/featuresarticle/2006/05/d11e08d4-32e7-4de5-9e8f-ab2971130928.html |work=[[自由欧洲电台／自由电台|自由欧洲电台／自由电台]] |date=May 31, 2006}}</ref>

這團體建立在2002年，宣稱代表伊朗阿塞拜疆人的利益。
