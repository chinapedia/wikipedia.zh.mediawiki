== 種 ==

南美象龜屬下有5個種：<ref name="Turtle Taxonomy Working Group 2010"/><ref name="Turtle Taxonomy Working Group 2011"/>

* ''Chelonoidis carbonaria'' – [[紅腿象龜|紅腿象龜]]
* ''Chelonoidis chilensis'' – [[阿根廷象龜|阿根廷象龜]]：又名[[查科象龜|查科象龜]]
* ''Chelonoidis denticulata'' – [[黃腿象龜|黃腿象龜]]
* ''Chelonoidis nigra'' - [[加拉帕戈斯象龜|加拉帕戈斯象龜]]
