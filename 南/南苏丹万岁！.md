《'''南蘇丹萬歲！'''》（'''South Sudan Oyee!'''）是[[南蘇丹|南蘇丹]]的[[國歌|國歌]]。这首国歌是在2010年8月开始的国歌选定比赛中由[[苏丹人民解放军|苏丹人民解放军]]的南苏丹国歌委员会选定。<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://nationalanthems.me/south-sudan-south-sudan-oyee/ |title=存档副本 |access-date=2012-11-07 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121101121905/http://www.nationalanthems.me/south-sudan-south-sudan-oyee/ |archive-date=2012-11-01 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>整个国歌的选定工作要早于让南苏丹成为[[主权国家|主权国家]]的在2011年1月举行的[[南苏丹独立公投|南苏丹独立公投]]。<ref>http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-africa-10889886</ref>国歌名称中的“Oyee!”应该被译作“万岁！”。<ref name="BBC Jan 11" />

==中文译文==

啊，上帝，<br>
我们赞美并崇敬您，<br>
为您对南苏丹的恩典，<br>
丰饶的土地，<br>
以及让我们在和平中团结。<br>

啊，祖国，<br>
我们升起你飘扬的带伯利恒之星的旗帜，<br>
在欢乐中歌唱自由之歌，<br>
为了正义、自由与繁荣<br>
永远的发展。<br>

啊，伟大的爱国者们，<br>
让我们在尊敬中站起来，<br>
向烈士們致敬，<br>
他們的鲜血巩固我国根基，<br>
我们发誓将保护我们的国家。<br>

啊上帝，保佑南苏丹。<br>

==歷史==
国歌委员会共收到49件作品。<ref name="ToS">{{cite web |url=http://www.talkofsudan.com/opinions/item/5126-south-sudan-national-anthem-the-land-of-cush |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2011-01-09 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110716191205/http://www.talkofsudan.com/opinions/item/5126-south-sudan-national-anthem-the-land-of-cush |archivedate=2011-07-16 }}</ref>这首歌原本的标题为[[库施|古實的土地]]。这首歌由朱巴大学的师生作曲。<ref name="BBC Jan 11" />

=== ''古實的土地'' ===

;1
: Oh God!
: We praise and glorify you,
: For your grace upon Cush.
: The land of great warriors,
: And origin of world's civilizations.

;2
: Oh Cush!
: Arise and shine, raise your flag with the guiding star,
: And sings songs of freedom with joy.
: For peace, liberty and justice
: Shall forever more reign.


: ''So Lord!''
: ''Bless South Sudan!''

;3
: Oh black warriors
: Let us stand up in silence and respect.
: Saluting millions of martyrs whose blood
: Cemented our national foundation
: We vow to protect our nation.

;4
: Oh Eden!
: Land of milk and honey and hardworking people
: Uphold us united in peace and harmony
: The Nile, valleys, forests and mountains
: Shall be our sources of our joy and pride!


:   ''So Lord!''
:   ''Bless South Sudan!''
