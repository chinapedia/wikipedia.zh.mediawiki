'''镭补'''，又译作'''镭水'''，是一种{{link-en|专利药品|patent medicine}}，以{{link-en|放射性骗局|radioactive quackery}}的一种著名例子而广为人知。它是一种含有至少1毫[[居里_(单位)|居里]]（合37千[[贝克勒尔|贝克勒尔]]）的[[镭|镭]]226或228[[同位素|同位素]]的三重蒸馏水。
[[File:Radithor_bottle_(25799475341).jpg|thumb]]收藏的镭补瓶]]
==历史==
镭补是1918至1928年间，由位于{{link-en|新泽西的东奥兰治市|East Orange, New Jersey}}的Bailey Radium Laboratories公司生产的产品。公司的拥有者是{{link-en|威廉·J. A. 贝利|William J. A. Bailey}}博士，一个[[哈佛大学|哈佛大学]]的辍学生，<ref>{{cite journal |url=http://www.time.com/time/magazine/article/0,9171,743525,00.html |title=Medicine: Radium Drinks |date=Apr 11, 1932|magazine=[[Time_(magazine)|Time]] |accessdate=11 July 2010}}</ref>并且不是一个医生。<ref>[http://www.clpgh.org/exhibit/neighborhoods/northside/nor_n106.html Literary Digest, 16 April 1932] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101203072143/http://www.clpgh.org/exhibit/neighborhoods/northside/nor_n106.html |date=2010年12月3日 }}</ref>这种产品被宣传为“治愈活死人的良方”<ref>[http://www.museumofquackery.com/devices/radium.htm Radium Cures - museumofquackery.com]</ref>和“永恒的阳光”。

{{link-en|埃本·拜尔斯|Eben Byers}}，一个富有的美国运动员，实业家及[[耶鲁大学|耶鲁大学]]毕业生，在1932年因镭补引起的[[辐射中毒|辐射中毒]]而死去。<ref>{{cite news |author= |coauthors= |title=Death Stirs Action on Radium 'Cures'. Trade Commission Speeds Its Inquiry. Health Department Checks Drug Wholesalers. Autopsy Shows Symptoms. Maker of "Radithor" Denies It Killed Byers, as Does Victim's Physician in Pittsburgh. Walker Uses Apparatus. Friends Alarmed to Find Mayor Has Been Drinking Radium-Charged Water for Last Six Months. |url=http://select.nytimes.com/gst/abstract.html?res=FA0F16F93D5A13738DDDAB0894DC405B828FF1D3 |quote=Federal and local agencies, as well as medical authorities in various parts of the country, were stirred to action yesterday as a result of the death of Eben M. Byers, wealthy Pittsburgh steel manufacturer and sportsman, who died here Wednesday at the Doctors' Hospital from causes attributed to radium poisoning resulting from the drinking of water containing radium in solution. ... |newspaper=[[New_York_Times|New York Times]] |date=April 2, 1932 |accessdate=2011-10-01 }}</ref> 

[[华尔街日报|华尔街日报]]上的文章这样评论拜尔斯的死亡：“镭补一直很管用，直到他的下巴掉下来为止。”<ref>http://www.case.edu/affil/MeMA/MCA/11-20/1991-Nov.pdf</ref> 拜尔斯的死亡使得[[美国食品药品管理局|美国食品药品管理局]]强化了对专利药品的管制。
