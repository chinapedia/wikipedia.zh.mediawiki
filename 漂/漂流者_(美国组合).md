'''漂流者组合'''（{{lang-en|'''The Drifters'''}}）是一个美国[[嘟·喔普|嘟·喔普]]、[[节奏布鲁斯|节奏布鲁斯]]和[[灵魂乐|灵魂乐]]歌唱团体，起初为了给[[克莱德·麦克菲特|克莱德·麦克菲特]]伴奏而于1953年组建。团体的老板乔治·特里维尔（{{lang|en|George Treadwell}}）以低报酬雇佣歌手，导致漂流者的人员组成非常不稳定<ref name=autogenerated2>{{cite web| title = The Immortals: The First Fifty| work = Rolling Stone Issue 946| publisher = Rolling Stone| url =http://www.rollingstone.com/music/lists/100-greatest-artists-of-all-time-19691231/the-drifters-20110420}}</ref>。在他作为业主期间该组合经历了共60位歌手<ref>{{cite web| title = Treadwell Presents The Drifters| url = http://www.thedrifters.co.uk/index.php| deadurl = yes| archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131203194922/http://www.thedrifters.co.uk/index.php| archivedate = 2013-12-03}}</ref>。漂流者有两个出名的阵容，第一个是“经典版漂流者”，于1998年进入{{link-en|声乐团体名人堂| Vocal Group Hall of Fame}}<ref name=autogenerated3>{{cite web|title=The Drifters (Inducted 1998) |url=http://www.vocalgroup.org/inductees/the_drifters.html |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131031192308/http://www.vocalgroup.org/inductees/the_drifters.html |archivedate=2013-10-31 }}</ref>。第二个是以[[本·E·金|本·E·金]]为中心的阵容，以“本·E·金与漂流者”之名进入声乐团体名人堂<ref name=autogenerated1>{{cite web| title=Ben E. King and the Drifters (Inducted 2000)| url=http://www.vocalgroup.org/inductees/ben_e_king.html| deadurl=yes| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304113535/http://www.vocalgroup.org/inductees/ben_e_king.html| archivedate=2016-03-04}}</ref>。[[摇滚名人堂|摇滚名人堂]]在引入该组合时从经典版漂流者中选了四人，从第二版中选了二人，从后特里维尔时代的漂流者中选了一人<ref>{{cite web|url=http://rockhall.com/inductees/the-drifters/ |title=The Drifters: inducted in 1988 | The Rock and Roll Hall of Fame and Museum |publisher=Rockhall.com |date= |accessdate=2012-05-10}}</ref>。漂流者组合拥有十三首热门单曲。
