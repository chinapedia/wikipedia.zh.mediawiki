'''鐳射切割'''（{{lang-en|'''Laser cutting'''}}）是一種使用激光切割材料的技術，通常用於工業製造應用，但也開始被學校，小企業和業餘愛好者使用。激光切割的工作原理是通常通過光學系統引導高功率激光器的輸出。該激光光學和CNC（計算機數字控制）用於引導材料或產生的激光束。用於切割材料的典型商用激光器涉及遵循CNC或G代碼的運動控制系統要切割到材料上的圖案。聚焦的激光束指向材料，然後熔化，燃燒，蒸發，或被氣體噴射吹走，[1]留下具有高質量表面光潔度的邊緣。工業激光切割機用於切割平板材料以及結構和管道材料。。<ref>{{ cite book | last = Bromberg | first = Joan | title = The Laser in America, 1950-1970 | publisher = MIT Press | page = 202 | year = 1991 | url = https://books.google.com/books?id=P6Ta1MPiOM8C&pg=RA1-PA202 | isbn = 978-0-262-02318-4 |ref = harv }}</ref><ref>{{ cite book | last1=Oberg  | first1=Erik | last2=Jones  | first2=Franklin D. | last3=Horton | first3=Holbrook L. | last4=Ryffel | first4=Henry H. | title = Machinery’s Handbook | publisher = Industrial Press Inc | edition = 27th | year = 2004 | location = New York, NY | isbn = 978-0-8311-2700-8 }}</ref><ref>{{ cite book | first1 = Robert H. | last1 = Todd | first2 = Dell K. | last2 = Allen | first3 = Leo | last3 = Alting | year = 1994 | title = Manufacturing Processes Reference Guide | publisher = Industrial Press Inc. | url = https://books.google.com/books?id=6x1smAf_PAcC | isbn = 0-8311-3049-0 | ref = harv }}</ref>
