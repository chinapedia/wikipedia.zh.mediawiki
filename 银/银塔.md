'''银塔'''（Silver Towers）是位于纽约[[曼哈顿|曼哈顿]]地狱厨房的一座双子塔结构住宅楼。这座60层的建筑位于第十一大道西侧，介于41街、42街之间，靠近[[哈德逊河|哈德逊河]]，包括1,359套居住单元。<ref>http://www.aviewoncities.com/buildings/nyc/silvertowers.htm</ref><ref>{{cite news |last=Bagli |first=Charles V. |title=Developers Set to Make Far West Side Much Taller |newspaper=The New York Times |date=2007-02-04 |url=http://query.nytimes.com/gst/fullpage.html?res=9C00EED8123FF937A35751C0A9619C8B63 |accessdate=2009-07-26}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Silver Towers Brings Condo Luxuries To Renters |first=Tara Lynn |last=Wagner |url=http://ny1.com/1-all-boroughs-news-content/ny1_living/real_estate/90915/silver-towers-brings-condo-luxuries-to-renters/ |newspaper=NY1 |date=2008-12-19 |accessdate=2010-01-04 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20120711190725/http://ny1.com/1-all-boroughs-news-content/ny1_living/real_estate/90915/silver-towers-brings-condo-luxuries-to-renters/ |archivedate=2012-07-11 }}</ref>该项目包括一个长75英尺的游泳池，纽约市最大的住宅楼，以及西侧一个0.25英亩的公共公园。
