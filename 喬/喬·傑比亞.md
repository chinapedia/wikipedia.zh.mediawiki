'''小約瑟夫·「喬」·傑比亞'''（{{lang-en|Joseph "Joe" Gebbia Jr.}}，{{bd|1981年|8月21日}}）是一名[[美國|美國]][[設計師|設計師]]及[[創業|網路創業家]]，為[[Airbnb|Airbnb]]的共同創始人兼產品長。2009年，傑比亞獲選為《[[彭博商業周刊|彭博商業周刊]]》20位最佳青年科技創業家。<ref name="bloomberg09">{{Cite web|title = Best Young Tech Entrepreneurs 2009: AirBnB - BusinessWeek|url = http://www.bloomberg.com/ss/09/04/0421_best_young_entrepreneurs/9.htm|website = Businessweek.com|access-date = 2016-02-11|language=en |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150316220334/http://www.bloomberg.com/ss/09/04/0421_best_young_entrepreneurs/9.htm |archivedate=2015-03-16 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>他在2010年入選《{{le|Inc. (雜誌)|Inc. (magazine)|Inc.}}》30歲以下最具影響力人物，<ref>{{Cite web |title=2010 30 Under 30 List: Brian Chesky, Joe Gebbia, and Nathan Blecharczyk, Founders of AirBnB |url=http://www.inc.com/30under30/2010/profile-brian-chesky-joe-gebbia-nathan-blecharczyk-airbnb.html |website = Inc.com |date = 2010-07-19 |first=Christine |last=Lagorio-Chafkin |access-date = 2016-02-11 |language=en |archiveurl= |archivedate= |deadurl=}}</ref>並在2013年獲選《[[財星_(雜誌)|財星]]》的40歲以下最具影響力人物。<ref>{{Cite web|title = 40 UNDER 40 2013 |url = http://fortune.com/40-under-40/2013/brian-chesky-joe-gebbia-nate-blecharchzyk-6/|website = Fortune.com|access-date = 2017-03-09|language=en |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160719144557/http://fortune.com/40-under-40/2013/brian-chesky-joe-gebbia-nate-blecharchzyk-6/ |archivedate=2016-07-19 |deadurl=no }}</ref>

==早年生活==
1981年8月21日，喬·傑比亞生於[[美國|美國]][[喬治亞州|喬治亞州]][[亞特蘭大|亞特蘭大]]，<ref name="forbesmarch2">{{cite web|url=http://www.forbes.com/sites/forbespr/2015/03/02/forbes-29th-annual-worlds-billionaires-issue/|title=Forbes' 29th Annual World's Billionaires Issue|author=Forbes Corporate Communications|date=2015-03-02|work=Forbes|language=en |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160830092713/http://www.forbes.com/sites/forbespr/2015/03/02/forbes-29th-annual-worlds-billionaires-issue/ |archivedate=2016-08-30 |deadurl=no }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.cnbc.com/2014/10/06/brian-chesky-and-joe-gebbia.html |title=Brian Chesky and Joe Gebbia |last= |first= |date=2014-10-06 |website=CNBC |access-date=2016-05-07 |language=en |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160902170044/http://www.cnbc.com/2014/10/06/brian-chesky-and-joe-gebbia.html |archivedate=2016-09-02 |deadurl=no }}</ref>雙親為艾琳（{{lang|en|Eileen}}）與喬·傑比亞（{{lang|en|Joe Gebbia}}），<ref name=AboutJoe>{{cite web |url=http://joegebbiaworks.com/about-joe/ |title=Joe Gebbia - Brookhaven City Councilman |website=joegebbiaworks.com |accessdate=2016-05-08 |language=en |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170217140952/http://joegebbiaworks.com/about-joe/ |archivedate=2017-02-17 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>還有一名姐妹金柏莉（{{lang|en|Kimberly}}）。<ref name=AboutJoe />他在2005年從[[羅德島州|羅德島州]][[普洛威頓斯_(羅德島州)|普洛威頓斯]]的[[羅德島設計學院|羅德島設計學院]]畢業，取得[[平面設計|平面設計]]與[[工業設計|工業設計]]{{le|藝術學士|Bachelor of Fine Arts}}學位。<ref name="airbnbfounders">{{cite web|url=https://www.airbnb.co.uk/about/founders|title=Founders - Airbnb|work=airbnb.co.uk|language=en |archiveurl= |archivedate= |deadurl= }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.risd.edu/About/News/Gebbia_Thinks_Big/|title=Gebbia Thinks Big|website=RISD|access-date=2016-05-07|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170130040103/https://www.airbnb.co.uk/about/founders|archivedate=2017-01-30|deadurl=yes}}</ref>他在大學時期認識了{{le|布萊恩·切斯基|Brian Chesky}}，他們之後成為室友並一起創辦[[Airbnb|Airbnb]]。在[[美國東北部|美國東北部]]就學期間，傑比亞除了在羅德島設計學院追求他對創造的理念之外，也前往[[布朗大學|布朗大學]]及[[麻省理工學院|麻省理工學院]]學習商業課程。<ref name="RISD">{{Cite web|title = Gebbia Thinks Big|url = http://www.risd.edu/About/News/Gebbia_Thinks_Big/|website = www.risd.edu|date = 2012-10-25|access-date = 2016-02-11|language = en|archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160215101842/http://www.risd.edu/About/News/Gebbia_Thinks_Big/|archivedate = 2016-02-15|deadurl = yes}}</ref>

==職業生涯==
羅德島設計學院畢業後，<ref name="RISD"/>傑比亞在[[舊金山|舊金山]]取得一份{{le|編年史出版社|Chronicle Books}}的工作，<ref>{{Cite web|title = Joe Gebbia|url = http://www.fastcompany.com/person/joe-gebbia|website = Fast Company|access-date = 2016-02-11|language=en |archiveurl= |archivedate= |deadurl= }}</ref>而切斯基則前往[[洛杉磯|洛杉磯]]發展。傑比亞在2007年說服切斯基搬至舊金山，辭掉各自的工作一同開創新的事業，但在不久後，無業的兩人卻收到房東增漲房租的通知。<ref name=":0">{{Cite web |title = Starred: The Email That Launched Airbnb |url = http://www.fastcompany.com/1792024/starred-email-launched-airbnb |website = Fast Company |access-date = 2016-02-11 |language=en |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160807180435/http://www.fastcompany.com/person/joe-gebbia |archivedate=2016-08-07 |deadurl=no }}</ref>正當思考著要如何負擔房租的時候，他們得知[[美國工業設計師協會|美國工業設計師協會]]即將在舊金山舉行會議，但附近的飯店大多都已客滿。<ref>{{Cite web|title = AirBed & Breakfast for Connecting '07|url = http://www.core77.com/posts/7715/airbed-breakfast-for-connecting-07-7715|website = Core77|access-date = 2016-02-11|language=en |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170203150746/http://www.core77.com/posts/7715/airbed-breakfast-for-connecting-07-7715 |archivedate=2017-02-03 |deadurl=no }}</ref>傑比亞便決定在他們的公寓擺上氣墊床，將床位出租給參加會議的人。經過這次的經驗，兩人創立了名為「氣墊床加早餐食宿」（{{lang|en|AirBed & Breakfast}}）的住宿出租網站，<ref name=":0" />也正是[[Airbnb|Airbnb]]的前身。傑比亞的另一名室友：[[哈佛大學|哈佛大學]]畢業的技術架構師{{le|內森·布萊卡斯亞克|Nathan Blecharczyk}}在2008年2月加入，成為第三位共同創始人。<ref name="bloomberg09"/>

為這間新創公司籌措資金失敗後，他們在[[2008年美國總統選舉|2008年美國總統選舉]]期間，根據參選人[[巴拉克·奧巴馬|巴拉克·奧巴馬]]及[[約翰·馬侃|約翰·馬侃]]分別設計出「歐巴馬圓圈」（{{lang|en|Obama O’s}}）和「馬侃方塊」（{{lang|en|Cap’n McCain’s}}）兩款麥片盒，並且在全國代表大會上銷售以推動公司的創立。<ref>{{Cite web|title = 19_Airbnb|url = http://www.fastcompany.com/3017358/most-innovative-companies-2012/19airbnb |website = Fast Company|
date=2012-02-07 |first=Austin |last=Carr |access-date = 2016-02-11 |language=en |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161127150232/https://www.fastcompany.com/3017358/most-innovative-companies-2012/19airbnb |archivedate=2016-11-27 |deadurl=no }}</ref>[[Y_Combinator_(公司)|Y Combinator]]創辦人[[保羅·格雷厄姆|保羅·格雷厄姆]]被他們堅韌且不願放棄的創業過程打動，便邀請傑比亞及其團隊加入該公司旗下的計畫，並2009年給予他們第一筆種子基金。<ref>{{Cite web|title = Brian Chesky: The homeless entrepreneur|url = http://mg.co.za/article/2013-03-12-brian-chesky-the-homeless-entrepreneur|website = The M&G Online|first=Alistair |last=Fairweather |date=2013-03-12 |access-date = 2016-02-11 |language=en |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160927132316/http://mg.co.za/article/2013-03-12-brian-chesky-the-homeless-entrepreneur |archivedate=2016-09-27 |deadurl=no }}</ref>

==慈善事業==
傑比亞是他的母校羅德島設計學院董事會的一員，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.risd.edu/About/History_Mission_Governance/Leadership/|title=Board of Trustees - Leadership - About - RISD|work=risd.edu|language=en |archiveurl= |archivedate= |deadurl= }}</ref>他在2014年捐贈30萬美元給該校，成立一個5萬美元的獎學金及捐贈基金。<ref name="risdscholarships">{{cite web|url=http://www.risd.edu/about/news/2014/gebbia-gift/|title=Airbnb Cofounder Supports RISD Scholarships - News - About - RISD|work=risd.edu|language=en |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160927111937/http://www.risd.edu/About/History_Mission_Governance/Leadership/ |archivedate=2016-09-27 |deadurl=no }}</ref>

2016年6月1日，傑比亞加入了[[華倫·巴菲特|華倫·巴菲特]]與[[比爾·蓋茲|比爾·蓋茲]]發起的「捐贈誓言」（{{lang|en|The Giving Pledge}}）運動，成為其中一位承諾捐出自己大多數財產的富豪。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://fortune.com/2016/06/01/airbnb-cofounders-join-buffett-and-gates-giving-pledge/ |title=Airbnb Cofounders Join Buffett and Gates' 'Giving Pledge' |publisher=''Fortune'' |date=2016-06-01 |first=Leigh |last=Gallagher|accessdate=2016-10-10 |language=en |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170217134846/http://fortune.com/2016/06/01/airbnb-cofounders-join-buffett-and-gates-giving-pledge/ |archivedate=2017-02-17 |deadurl=no }}</ref>
