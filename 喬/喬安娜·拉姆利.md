'''喬安娜·拉蒙德·拉姆利''' [[大英帝國勳章|OBE]] [[皇家地理學會|FRGS]]（'''Joanna Lamond Lumley'''，{{bd|1946年|5月1日}}），英格蘭女演員、<ref>{{cite web|last=Passey |first=James |url=http://natgeotraveller.co.uk/how/interview/33681/ |title=The fabulous campaigner: Joanna Lumley / Interview / How / National Geographic Traveller (UK) |publisher=Natgeotraveller.co.uk |date=7 February 2012 |accessdate=21 October 2012}}</ref> 作家，也曾當過模特兒。曾靠在《[[三個偵探_(電視劇)|三個偵探]]》中的表現獲得[[英國電影學院獎|英國電影學院獎]]。

她亦以[[生存國際|生存國際]]、[[世界农场动物福利协会|世界农场动物福利协会]]等人道組織和動物福利組織的支持者而出名。<ref>Gore, Alex. [http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2093376/Silence-lambs-pigs-horse-600-animals-slaughtered-Joanna-Lumley-backed-animal-sanctuary-faces-eviction.html "Silence of the lambs, pigs and horse: 600 animals could be slaughtered as Joanna Lumley-backed animal sanctuary faces eviction"], ''[[The_Daily_Mail|The Daily Mail]]'', London, 29 January 2012. Retrieved on 3 March 2014.</ref> 2013年獲得[[國家電視獎|國家電視獎]]中的特殊貢獻獎。

==生平==
1946年5月1日，喬安娜·拉蒙德·拉姆利出生於[[英屬印度|英屬印度]]的[[斯利那加|斯利那加]]。她的父母在1941年結婚，母親是英格蘭人赛拉·碧翠斯·羅斯（Thyra Beatrice Rose，原姓Weir，1920–2005），父親是蘇格蘭-英格蘭混血兒、[[英属印度陆军|英属印度陆军]]少校詹姆斯·盧瑟福·拉姆利（James Rutherford Lumley，1917–1999）<ref name="Telegraph: Family Detective">[https://www.telegraph.co.uk/portal/main.jhtml?xml=/portal/2007/08/04/nosplit/ftdet104.xml Family detective: Joanna Lumley] {{cite news|last=Barratt|first=Nick|title=Family Detective: Joanna Lumley|work=The Daily Telegraph|date=4 August 2007|page=W16}}</ref><ref name="The Daily Telegraph">{{cite news|url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/newstopics/celebritynews/2988869/Joanna-Lumley-Ill-quit-Britain-if-there-is-no-justice-for-Gurkhas.html|work=The Daily Telegraph|location=London|title=Joanna Lumley: I'll quit Britain if there is no justice for Gurkhas|date=19 September 2008|accessdate=27 March 2010}}</ref>，外祖父萊斯利·韦尔（Leslie Weir，1883–1950）也曾在印度服役，軍銜為中校。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/tv/2017/07/19/joanna-lumleys-indian-journey-came-moving-end-review/|title=Joanna Lumley's Indian journey came to a very moving endÂ â€“ review|date=2017-07-19|work=The Telegraph|access-date=2019-01-23|language=en-GB|issn=0307-1235}}</ref> 

喬安娜·拉姆利在英格蘭[[肯特|肯特]]長大<ref name="Telegraph: Family Detective"/>，她曾在16歲時報考[[皇家戲劇藝術學院|皇家戲劇藝術學院]]，但未被錄取，最後就讀女子精修学校露西·克萊頓學院（Lucie Clayton College）<ref>{{cite news|url=http://news.scotsman.com/opinion/Joanna-Lumley-profile-She39s-nobody39s.5251834.jp|title=Joanna Lumley profile: She's nobody's Patsy|work=The Scotsman|location=Edinburgh|date=9 May 2009|accessdate=21 October 2012}}</ref>。

她曾當過三年攝影模特<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/p04wxq2r|title=BBC Arts - BBC Arts, A youthful Joanna Lumley talks about working with photographer Brian Duffy|website=BBC|language=en-GB|access-date=2018-12-06}}</ref><ref name='Telegraph 2010-01-08'>{{cite news|first=Stephen|last=Adams|title=1960s photographer Brian Duffy picks up camera 30 years on|date=8 January 2010|url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/culture/culturenews/6951311/1960s-photographer-Brian-Duffy-picks-up-camera-30-years-on.html|work=The Daily Telegraph|location=London|accessdate=15 January 2012}}</ref>。1969年首次拍攝電視廣告<ref>{{cite news|last=Sweney|first=Mark|title=Nimble Builds on Heritage|url=http://media.guardian.co.uk/advertising/story/0,,1871716,00.html|work=The Guardian (MediaGuardian)|date=14 September 2006|location=London|accessdate=27 March 2010}}</ref>，并開始進入演藝界。
