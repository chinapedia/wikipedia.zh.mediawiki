'''喬爾戈夫斯基1'''（'''Djorgovski 1'''）是一個[[球狀星團|球狀星團]]，這是{{link-en|斯坦尼斯拉夫·喬爾戈夫斯基|Stanislav George Djorgovski}}使用原始的[[IRAS|IRAS]]目錄尋找可能被遮蔽的[[球狀星團|球狀星團]]時發現的<ref name=Discovery>{{cite web|last1=Djorgovski|first1=S|title=Discovery of three obscured globular clusters|url=http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1987ApJ...317L..13D|website=SAO/NASA ADS Astronomy Abstract Service|accessdate=19 June 2015|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6ZOtcR2RK?url=http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1987ApJ...317L..13D|archivedate=2015-06-19|doi=10.1086/184903|dead-url=no}}</ref>。研究顯示這是一個''貧金屬'' －除了[[氫|氫]]和[[氦|氦]]，幾乎沒有其它的。喬爾戈夫斯基1其實室內銀河系中最缺乏金屬的星團之一<ref name=Hubble>{{cite web|title=Engulfed by stars near the Milky Way’s heart|url=https://www.spacetelescope.org/images/potw1126a/|website=Hubble Space telescope|accessdate=19 June 2015|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6ZOv08Z5w?url=https://www.spacetelescope.org/images/potw1126a/|archivedate=2015-06-19|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。
