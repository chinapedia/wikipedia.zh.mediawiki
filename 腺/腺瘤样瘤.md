'''腺瘤样瘤'''（{{lang-en|Adenomatoid tumor}}），是一种原生的[[间皮性|间皮性]][[肿瘤|肿瘤]]。它一般出现于生殖道，比如[[睾丸|睾丸]]<ref>{{cite journal |author=Williams SB, Han M, Jones R, Andrawis R |title=Adenomatoid tumor of the testes |journal=Urology |volume=63 |issue=4 |pages=779–81 |year=2004 |pmid=15072910 |doi=10.1016/j.urology.2003.11.035}}</ref>和[[副睾|副睾]]<ref name="titleAdenomatoid tumor of the epididymis">{{cite web |url=http://www.gfmer.ch/selected_images_v2/detail_list.php?cat1=6&cat3=139&stype=d |title=Adenomatoid tumor of the epididymis |accessdate=2007-12-15 |format= |work= |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070615013911/http://www.gfmer.ch/selected_images_v2/detail_list.php?cat1=6&cat3=139&stype=d |archivedate=2007-06-15 }}</ref>。但是，它也发现于[[胰腺|胰腺]]中<ref name="titleModern Pathology - Adenomatoid Tumor of the Pancreas: A Case Report with Comparison of Histology and Aspiration Cytology">{{cite journal |author=Overstreet K, Wixom C, Shabaik A, Bouvet M, Herndier B |title=Adenomatoid tumor of the pancreas: a case report with comparison of histology and aspiration cytology |journal=Mod Pathol. |volume=16 |issue=6 |pages=613–7 |pmid=12808068 |doi=10.1097/01.MP.0000072803.37527.C8 |url=http://www.nature.com/modpathol/journal/v16/n6/full/3880812a.html|date=Jun 2003}}</ref>。

在妇科中，它也发现于[[子宫|子宫]]和[[输卵管|输卵管]]里<ref>{{cite journal |author=Huang CC, Chang DY, Chen CK, Chou YY, Huang SC |title=Adenomatoid tumor of the female genital tract |journal=Int J Gynaecol Obstet |volume=50 |issue=3 |pages=275–80 |pmid=8543111 |doi= 10.1016/0020-7292(95)02453-J|url=http://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/002072929502453J|date=Sep 1995}}</ref>。
