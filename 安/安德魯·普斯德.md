'''安德魯·富蘭克林·「安迪」·普斯德'''（{{lang-en|Andrew Franklin "Andy" Puzder}}；{{bd|1950年|7月11日}}）是[[美國|美國]]連鎖餐飲企業CKE Restaurants的首席執行官，擁有[[華盛頓大學法學院_(聖路易斯)|華盛頓大學法學院]]的法學博士學位。他自2000年9月開始出任CKE餐飲業的行政總裁。他獲總統[[唐納·川普|唐納·川普]]提名為[[美國勞工部|美國勞工部]]長，後因無望在參議院取得足夠議員支持任命而宣佈退出<ref name=people20170216>{{Cite news |url=http://world.people.com.cn/n1/2017/0216/c1002-29085859.html |title=“钦点”劳工部长宣布退出 特朗普内阁再受重创 |publisher=人民網 |date=2017-02-16}}</ref>。

== 生平 ==
從商之前他是一位律師<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ckr.com/about_management.html#puzder|title=CKE Restaurants Bio: Andrew Puzder|publisher=[[CKE_Restaurants|CKE Restaurants]]}}</ref>，在1980年代曾是[[反墮胎|反墮胎]]權利運動的活躍分子，並且為反墮胎組織提供法律服務<ref name="Raasch">{{cite news|last1=Raasch|first1=Chuck|title=Trump to nominate former St. Louis lawyer Puzder as labor secretary|url=http://www.stltoday.com/news/local/govt-and-politics/trump-to-nominate-former-st-louis-lawyer-puzder-as-labor/article_5a259060-6f37-5722-8068-a7f43c33ea60.html|work=St. Louis Post-Dispatch|date=December 8, 2016}}</ref><ref name="Lewin">{{cite news|last1=Lewin|first1=Tamar|title=In Bitter Abortion Debate, Opponents Learn to Reach for Common Ground|url=http://www.nytimes.com/1992/02/17/us/in-bitter-abortion-debate-opponents-learn-to-reach-for-common-ground.html?pagewanted=all|work=The New York Times|date=February 17, 1992}}</ref>。

在[[2016年美國總統選舉|2016年美國總統選舉]]，[[唐納·川普|唐納·川普]]競選美國總統期間他就為川普捐款<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/politics/romney-loyalists-divisions-over-trump-spill-out-into-the-open-at-utah-summit/2016/06/11/700ab4a4-3000-11e6-9b37-42985f6a265c_story.html|title=Romney loyalists' divisions over Trump spill out into the open at Utah summit|publisher=Washington Post}}</ref>。2016年12月8日，他被侯任美國總統[[唐納·川普|唐納·川普]]提名為繼任美國勞工部長<ref>{{Cite news|first=Marianne |last=LeVine |first2=Cogan |last2=Schneier |url=http://www.politico.com/blogs/donald-trump-administration/2016/12/andrew-puzder-labor-secretary-trump-232368|title=Trump chooses Puzder as labor secretary|newspaper=Politico|date=December 8, 2016|access-date=2016-12-09}}</ref>；惟他在2017年2月15日決定退出提名<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/powerpost/now-6-republicans-are-on-the-fence-about-andrew-puzder/2017/02/15/e34cada6-f38b-11e6-8d72-263470bf0401_story.html?hpid=hp_hp-banner-main_puzder-135pm%3Ahomepage%2Fstory&utm_term=.7f121eba4b8f|title=Andrew Puzder withdraws nomination for labor secretary|date=February 15, 2017|accessdate=February 15, 2017|work=[[The_Washington_Post|The Washington Post]]|last1=O'Keefe|first1=Ed|last2=Marte|first2=Jonnelle}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://bigstory.ap.org/article/2d3b7517662e4c11883d526c6934f9b1/puzders-nomination-labor-secretary-serious-trouble|title=Puzder withdraws nomination to be Trump's labor secretary|date=February 15, 2017|accessdate=February 15, 2017|work=[[Associated_Press|Associated Press]]|last1=Kellman|first1=Laurie|last2=Lucey|first2=Catherine}}</ref>。
