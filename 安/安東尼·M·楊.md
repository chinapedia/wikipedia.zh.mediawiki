'''安東尼·M·楊'''（{{lang-en|Anthony (Tony) M. Young}}），是一名[[澳大利亚|澳大利亚]][[昆士兰大学|昆士兰大学]]的生物学家。他出版了许多关于真菌的书籍和关于澳大利亚[[蜡伞科|蜡伞科]]的学术论文和研究<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.anbg.gov.au/fungi/history-today.html|title=The study of Australian fungi: What is the state of knowledge today?|last=Lepp|first=Heino|date=9 July 2007|work=Australian Fungi Website, Australian National Herbarium|publisher=Australian National Botanic Gardens, Australian Government|accessdate=23 January 2010|location=Canberra, ACT}}</ref>。此外他对{{link-en|枝瑚菌属|Ramaria}}进行分析研究，并新发现了十五种真菌。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.environment.gov.au/biodiversity/abrs/biologue/2007/taxonomy-research/article01.html|title=ABRS National Taxonomy Research Grants Programme: ''Ramaria'' in Australia|last=Young AM, Fechner N|year=2007|work=ABRS website|publisher=Department of the Environment, Water, Heritage and the Arts, Australian Government|accessdate=23 January 2010}}</ref>
