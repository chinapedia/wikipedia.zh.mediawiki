'''安妮女王战争'''（Queen Anne's War，1702至1713年），是[[西班牙王位继承战争|西班牙王位继承战争]]在北美的延伸，也是[[北美殖民地戰爭|法国與印第安人战爭]]系列中的第二次，交战双方是法国和英格蘭，也就是後來的英国，<ref name="GB">In 1707, the kingdoms of England and [[Kingdom_of_Scotland|Scotland]] were unified as the [[Kingdom_of_Great_Britain|Kingdom of Great Britain]], sharing a single Parliament at Westminster under the [[Act_of_Union_1707|Act of Union 1707]].  After this, Scottish troops joined their English counterparts in all colonial wars.</ref>目的是为了争夺北美大陆的控制权。西班牙王位继承战争主要戰場在欧洲。北美戰區除了两个主要的交战國外，許多[[美洲原住民|美洲原住民]]部落也分别与法国或英国结盟，而西班牙則與法國合作。它也被称为'''第三次印第安战争'''，<ref>The first Indian War was [[King_Philip's_War|King Philip's War]], the second was [[King_William's_War|King William's War]] and the fourth was [[Father_Rale's_War|Father Rale's War]]. See [[Alan_Taylor_(historian)|Taylor, Alan]]. ''Writing Early American History''. Philadelphia: [[University_of_Pennsylvania_Press|University of Pennsylvania Press]], 2005; pg. 74.</ref>而法國人則稱之為'''第二次洲際戰爭'''。<ref>Denis Héroux, Robert Lahaise, Noë̈l Vallerand, "La Nouvelle-France", p.101 [https://books.google.fr/books?id=xBIVAAAAYAAJ&q=Seconde+Guerre+intercoloniales+nouvelle+france&dq=Seconde+Guerre+intercoloniales+nouvelle+france&hl=fr&sa=X&ei=Pie6VKLlGovhaoe2gYAF&ved=0CDEQ6AEwAw]</ref>
