== 背景和动机 ==
俄罗斯和土耳其官员在阿勒颇撤离期间就叙利亚停火问题举行了会谈<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.yahoo.com/news/turkey-russia-meet-wednesday-syrias-aleppo-turkish-official-091517656.html|title=Turkey talking to Russia on ceasefire, evacuation from Syria's Aleppo}}</ref>。俄罗斯、土耳其和伊朗举行会谈，谈判解决[[叙利亚内战|叙利亚内战]]的问题。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.reuters.com/article/us-mideast-crisis-syria-turkey-russia-idUSKBN1480ZO|title=Russia, Iran and Turkey to hold Syria talks in Moscow on Tuesday|date=2016-12-19|via=Reuters}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.businessinsider.com/syria-talks-between-iran-turkey-and-russia-will-still-go-on-in-moscow-despite-ambassadors-assassination-2016-12|title=Syria talks between Iran, Turkey, and Russia will still go on in Moscow despite ambassador's assassination}}</ref>

=== 动机 ===
此次刺杀的动机尚未明确。虽然看似是对俄罗斯参与[[阿勒颇战役|阿勒颇战役]]（[[叙利亚内战|叙利亚内战]]的一部分）的行动展开报复，也有人怀疑{{tsl|en|Islamic extremism|伊斯兰极端主义}}或{{tsl|en|Anti-Russian sentiment|俄罗斯恐怖主义}}是刺杀的原因。北约参与刺杀行动的谣言已经在政府官员和评论员之间流传。另外存疑的两个组织：[[伊斯兰国|伊斯兰国]]（IS）和[[征服沙姆陣線|征服沙姆陣線]]<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.cnbc.com/2016/12/19/who-shot-russias-ambassador-to-turkey-it-could-be-a-lot-of-people.html|title=Any number of groups could have wanted Russian ambassador dead|date=2016-12-19|work=CNBC|last1=Kemp|first1=Ted}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://nypost.com/2016/12/19/russian-ambassador-to-turkey-shot-in-targeted-attack/|title=Russian ambassador to Turkey killed in targeted attack|date=2016-12-19|work=New York Post|last1=Steinbuch|first1=Yaron}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/europe/russia-ambassador-shooting-turkey-nato-security-services-andrey-karlov-a7485296.html|title=Russian MP blames Nato conspiracy for killing of ambassador in Turkey|date=2016-12-19|work=The Independent|last1=Kentish|first1=Ben}}</ref>。土耳其曾遭疑支持这两个[[恐怖组织|恐怖组织]]。据报道，土耳其当局正在追查{{tsl|en|Gülenist Terrorist Organisation|葛兰恐怖组织}}与此次袭击事件的关系：在一次[[新闻发布会|新闻发布会]]上，[[土耳其总统|土耳其总统]][[雷杰普·塔伊普·埃尔多安|雷杰普·塔伊普·埃尔多安]]声称，凶手是该组织的成员<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.milliyet.com.tr/cumhurbaskani-erdogan-ankara-da-siyaset-2365707/|title=Erdoğan resmen açıkladı: Suikastçı FETÖ mensubu|work=MİLLİYET HABER - TÜRKİYE'NİN HABER SİTESİ|last1=sitesi|first1=milliyet.com.tr Türkiye'nin lider haber|accessdate=2016-12-21}}</ref><ref name="ABCNews20Dec">{{cite news|title=Who was the Ankara assassin?|url=http://www.abc.net.au/news/2016-12-20/ankara-russian-ambassador-shooting-who-is-mevlut-mert-aydintas/8135510|work=ABC News|date=2016-12-20|language=en-AU}}</ref>。IS和[[基地组织|基地组织]]名下的帐户在[[社交媒体|社交媒体]]上替此次袭击点赞<ref name="Social media Heavy">{{cite news|url=http://heavy.com/news/2016/12/isis-islamic-state-tahwid-allahu-akbar-assassination-attempt-russian-ambassador-to-turkey-andrei-karlov-twitter-video-claim/|title=ISIS ‘Celebrates’ Assassination of Russian Ambassador to Turkey Andrei Karlov|date=2016-12-19|work=Heavy.com|last1=Prince|first1=S.J.}}</ref>。凶手在行凶时说的话语，和叙利亚伊斯兰教叛乱组织Jabhat Fatah al-Sham（以前的[[征服沙姆陣線|征服沙姆陣線]]）的非官方国歌很相似<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/2016/12/19/turkish-police-officer-shot-dead-russian-ambassador-andrey-karlov/|title=Mevlut Mert Altintas: The policeman accused of killing Russian ambassador Andrey Karlov?|date=2016-12-20|work=The Daily Telegraph}}</ref>。

== 过程 ==
卡尔洛夫应邀出席土耳其的一个俄罗斯乡村摄影展<ref name="SoichetThompson">{{cite web|url=http://www.cnn.com/2016/12/19/europe/turkey-russian-ambassador-shot/index.html|title=Russia's ambassador to Turkey assassinated in Ankara|website=CNN|last1=CNN|first1=Catherine E. Shoichet and Nick Thompson|accessdate=2016-12-19}}</ref>，并发表现场讲话。摄影展名为“土耳其人眼中的俄罗斯”，于安卡拉詹卡亚区凯达斯·撒奈特·梅克玆（{{lang|en|Cagdas Sanat Merkezi}}）现代艺术中心举办<ref name="SoichetThompson" />。

奥尔塔廷斯利用警察证混进大厅，画廊与会者都以为他是卡尔洛夫的贴身保镖。大使开始演讲没多久，他向对着正在直播的电视镜头对空开了一枪，之后致敬圣战组织，并朝大使背部开了数枪，致其死亡，并误伤数人<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=http://www.cnn.com/2016/12/19/europe/turkey-russian-ambassador-shot/index.html|title=Gunman shouted 'Do not forget Aleppo!' as he shot ambassador|last=Shoichet|first=Catherine E.|last2=Thompson|first2=Nick|date=|website=CNN|publisher=|access-date=2016-12-20|last3=Grinberg|first3=Emanuella}}</ref>。

枪杀大使后，奥尔塔廷斯在房内踱步，砸碎展出的图片，并以阿拉伯语和土耳其语高喊：“[[大赞辞|真主万岁]]，不要忘记[[阿勒坡|阿勒坡]]，不要忘记[[叙利亚|叙利亚]]”<ref name=":0" /><ref name="Reuters" /><ref name="SoichetThompson" /><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.haaretz.com/middle-east-news/turkey/1.760063|title=Turkey: Russia's ambassador in Ankara killed in shooting attack by police officer|work=Haaretz|agency=Reuters and the Associated Press|accessdate=2016-12-19}}</ref><ref name="Independent">{{cite news|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/europe/russian-ambassador-dead-turkey-death-andrey-karlov-shot-ankara-back-murder-assassination-latest-a7484991.html|title=Russian ambassador to Turkey dead: Andrey Karlov dies after being shot in Ankara|date=2016-12-19|newspaper=The Independent}}</ref>。最终土耳其安全部队击毙奥尔塔廷斯<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sun-sentinel.com/news/la-fg-russian-ambassador-turkey-attacked-20161219-story.html|title=Off-duty police officer identified in fatal shooting of Russia's ambassador to Turkey|last2=King|first2=Laura|date=2016-12-19|website=Sun-Sentinel|access-date=2016-12-19|last1=Umar|first1=Farooq}}</ref>，而大使送至医院，但因伤势过重身亡<ref name=":0" />。[[File:Andrey_Karlov_2016.jpg|thumb]]

凶徒梅尔鲁特·莫特·奥尔塔廷斯（{{IPA-tr|ˈmevlyt ˈmæɾt ˈɑɫtɯntɑʃ|lang}}；1994年6月24日-2016年12月19日）是事发时休班的警察，生于土耳其西北部爱琴海地区[[艾登省|艾登省]][[瑟凯|瑟凯]]城的[[世俗|世俗]]家庭尽管瑟凯城是土耳其最古老的城镇之一，但在奥尔塔廷斯家人居住的周围，[[民族主义|民族主义]]和[[伊斯兰主义|伊斯兰主义]]十分盛行。诸如“上帝应该给予土耳其人”和“伊斯兰是唯一的道路”等口号是该地区常见的涂鸦<ref name="Reuters 23 Dec">{{cite news|last1=Gumrukcu|first1=Tuvan|title=Russian envoy's killer remembered as lonely boy, not angry jihadist|url=http://www.reuters.com/article/us-turkey-russia-diplomat-assailant-idUSKBN14C1FM|work=Reuters|date=2016-12-23}}</ref>。他自从2所大学將之劝退后<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.reuters.com/article/us-turkey-russia-diplomat-assailant-idUSKBN14C1FM|title=El Gumrukcu, Tuvan (23 December 2016).|website=Reuters}}</ref> ，2014年从伊兹密尔警察学校毕业<ref>{{cite web|url=http://gaceta.es/noticias/asesino-andrei-karlov-policia-turco-22-anos-19122016-1943|title=El asesino de Andrei Karlov, un policía turco de 22 años|date=2016-12-19 |website=La Gaceta|accessdate=2016-12-19}}</ref> 。他在安卡拉的防爆警察部队服役了2年半。直到2016年7月，他曾有8次参加土耳其总统埃尔多安的安保工作<ref name="telegraph">{{cite web|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/2016/12/21/russian-ambassadors-assassin-guarded-recep-tayyip-erdogan/|title=Russian ambassador's assassin 'guarded Recep Tayyip Erdogan'|date=2016-12-21|website=The Telegraph|accessdate=2016-12-24}}</ref>。
