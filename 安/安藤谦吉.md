'''安藤谦吉''' （安藤 謙吉；{{Bd|1950年|5月3日}}于[[岐阜市|岐阜市]]）是一名[[日本|日本]]举重运动员，他曾在[[1976年夏季奥林匹克运动会|1976年蒙特利尔奥运会]]中获得一枚铜牌。 <ref name=db-olymp>[http://www.databaseolympics.com/games/gamessport.htm?g=19&sp=WEI "1976 Summer Olympics – Montreal, Canada – Weightlifting"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080917043647/http://www.databaseolympics.com/games/gamessport.htm?g=19&sp=WEI |date=2008-09-17 }} – ''databaseOlympics.com'' (Retrieved on March 3, 2008)</ref><ref name=gbrathletics>[http://www.gbrathletics.com/olympic/weight.htm "Olympic Games Medallists – Weightlifting"] –  (Retrieved on March 3, 2008)</ref>
