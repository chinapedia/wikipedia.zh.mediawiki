'''安扎利滨海湖'''（[[波斯语|波斯语]]：تالاب انزلی）（也称 '''Anzali Mordab''', '''Anzali Bay''',   '''Pahlavi Mordab''', '''Pahlavi Bay''' 或 '''Anzali Liman'''）<ref name="Holčik1">[http://www.fao.org/docrep/006/AD192E/AD192E07.htm Holčik, Juraj and Oláh, János (1992) "Introduction" ''Fish, Fisheries and Water Quality in Anzali Lagoon and Its Watershed'' Food and Agriculture Organization, Rome], accessed 29 November 2008</ref> 是一个近海[[漓漫|漓漫]]，或称[[潟湖|潟湖]]，位于伊朗北部省份[[吉兰省|吉兰省]]的临里海城市[[安扎利港|安扎利港]]。该湖将安扎利港分成两半，也是Selke野生生物保护区和Siahkesheem河流湿地的所在处。<ref name="birdland">[http://www.birdlife.org/datazone/sites/index.html?action=SitHTMDetails.asp&sid=8078 "IR016: Anzali Mordab complex" Birdand International], accessed 29 November 2008</ref>

尽管受到污染<ref>Pourang, N. (1996) "Heavy metal concentrations in surficial sediments and benthic macroinvertebrates from Anzali wetland, Iran" ''Hydrobiologia'' 331(1/3): pp. 53–61, p.53, ''et.seq.''</ref>，该湖仍被认为是一个观鸟的好去处。<ref name="birdland">[http://www.birdlife.org/datazone/sites/index.html?action=SitHTMDetails.asp&sid=8078 "IR016: Anzali Mordab complex" Birdand International], accessed 29 November 2008</ref> 该湖湖水的盐度从支流处的[[淡水|淡水]]向入海口处的[[汽水_(水域)|汽水]]过渡。研究表明，19世纪和20世纪初时，该湖湖水的盐分含量明显高于现在。<ref name="Holčik2">[http://www.fao.org/docrep/006/AD192E/AD192E07.htm Holčik, Juraj and Oláh, János (1992) "2.3 Status of the Anzali Lagoon" ''Fish, Fisheries and Water Quality in Anzali Lagoon and Its Watershed'' Food and Agriculture Organization, Rome], accessed 29 November 2008</ref>

从20世纪30年代起，该湖的容量开始缩小至不及原先的1/4。<ref name="Holčik2">[http://www.fao.org/docrep/006/AD192E/AD192E07.htm Holčik, Juraj and Oláh, János (1992) "2.3 Status of the Anzali Lagoon" ''Fish, Fisheries and Water Quality in Anzali Lagoon and Its Watershed'' Food and Agriculture Organization, Rome], accessed 29 November 2008</ref> 然而，在最近的10年中（截至2007年），由于里海海面上升导致更多的水体交换，以及支流上游灌溉使得支流盐分提高，在这样的双重作用下，该湖湖水的盐度有所上升。<ref name="Holčik2">[http://www.fao.org/docrep/006/AD192E/AD192E07.htm Holčik, Juraj and Oláh, János (1992) "2.3 Status of the Anzali Lagoon" ''Fish, Fisheries and Water Quality in Anzali Lagoon and Its Watershed'' Food and Agriculture Organization, Rome], accessed 29 November 2008</ref><ref>[http://www.caspianenvironment.org/autoindex/index.php?dir=newsite/DocCenter/Reports/2007/Anzali%20lagoon/&file=workshop%205%20Report%20(for%20web).pdf Final Report of the 5th Meeting on Anzali Lagoon Adaptive Management Pilot Initiation Project (17 April 2007), Caspian Environment Program (CEP), p. 4], accessed 28 November 2008</ref>

该湖现已被列入《[[湿地公约|湿地公约]]》的保护名录。<ref>[http://ramsar.wetlands.org/Database/Searchforsites/tabid/765/language/en-US/Default.aspx Ramsar sites database] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090927161034/http://ramsar.wetlands.org/Database/Searchforsites/tabid/765/language/en-US/Default.aspx |date=2009-09-27 }}</ref>

== 渔业 ==
[[File:Anzali_lagoon_Barry_Kent.jpg|右]]
1950年以前，安扎利滨海湖的经济渔业产量约占整个吉兰省的70%，年捕捞量超过5,000吨。<ref name="Holčik1">[http://www.fao.org/docrep/006/AD192E/AD192E07.htm Holčik, Juraj and Oláh, János (1992) "Introduction" ''Fish, Fisheries and Water Quality in Anzali Lagoon and Its Watershed'' Food and Agriculture Organization, Rome], accessed 29 November 2008</ref> 商业捕捞在每年春季和秋季[[產卵|产卵]]期时进行——届时，[[庫圖擬鯉|库图拟鲤]]、梭鲈和[[欧鳊|欧鳊]]将从里海游入安扎利滨海湖。<ref name="Holčik1">[http://www.fao.org/docrep/006/AD192E/AD192E07.htm Holčik, Juraj and Oláh, János (1992) "Introduction" ''Fish, Fisheries and Water Quality in Anzali Lagoon and Its Watershed'' Food and Agriculture Organization, Rome], accessed 29 November 2008</ref> 然而，由于各种因素的影响，至1960年商业捕捞彻底终止时，该湖的年捕捞量已降低至不到100吨。<ref name="Holčik1">[http://www.fao.org/docrep/006/AD192E/AD192E07.htm Holčik, Juraj and Oláh, János (1992) "Introduction" ''Fish, Fisheries and Water Quality in Anzali Lagoon and Its Watershed'' Food and Agriculture Organization, Rome], accessed 29 November 2008</ref>

由于上游灌溉的增多，严重的淤积使得湖体缩小、水深降低，水源污染和水体[[富营养化|富营养化]]都迫使渔业陷入停滞。如今湖水表面逐渐被大型水生植物覆盖，主要是外来的[[细叶满江红|细叶满江红]]（Azolla filiculoides），这使得水体富营养化逐步加重，大片湖水因而缺乏足够多的溶解氧来维持鱼类的生存。<ref name="Holčik3">[http://www.fao.org/docrep/006/AD192E/AD192E07.htm Holčik, Juraj and Oláh, János (1992) "2.3.3 Azolla invasion" ''Fish, Fisheries and Water Quality in Anzali Lagoon and Its Watershed'' Food and Agriculture Organization, Rome], accessed 29 November 2008</ref><ref>Jamalzad, F.; Nezami, Sh. and Sefat, A. Darvish (2004) "Investigation of trophic states in the Anzali Lagoon using GIS" Intecol 7th International Wetlands Conference, Utrecht, Netherlands</ref>

== 地理 ==
安扎利湖临近[[伊朗|伊朗]][[吉兰省|吉兰省]]北部港口城市[[安扎利港|安扎利港]]，面积约15,000公顷，是伊朗为数不多的名列1975年《[[湿地公约|湿地公约]]》国际湿地名录的湿地之一。湿地被认为是生物多样性最为复杂的[[生态系统|生态系统]]；然而，由于行政机构的疏忽，安扎利湖的生态系统正在面临遭受极大破坏的危险。

安扎利湖作为城市供水的水源地和生活、工业污水的排放处，已经严重威胁到居住于此的许多动物种群，包括至少78种鸟类。此外，一些当地的行政机构还试图在毗邻湿地的一个半岛上修建运动场。这会对湿地造成致命一击，因而在环保主义者的抗议之下至今仍未能修建。

澳大利亚、加拿大、美国和许多欧洲国家在自然保护方面所作出的努力，体现了政府对自然栖息地保护问题的严肃对待，这些自然栖息地反过来也构成了当地长久不衰的旅游吸引力。
