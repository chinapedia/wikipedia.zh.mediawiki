'''安杰利卡'''（{{lang-en|Angelica}}）是位于[[美国|美国]][[纽约州|纽约州]][[阿利根尼县_(纽约州)|阿利根尼县]]的一个镇，地处该县中部。[[2010年美国人口普查|2010年美国人口普查]]时，该镇有1403人。<ref name>{{cite web|url=http://factfinder2.census.gov|publisher=[[United_States_Census_Bureau|United States Census Bureau]]|title=American FactFinder}}</ref>其名称由此地最早的白人定居者之一菲利普·丘奇（{{lang|en|Philip Church}}）以其母亲[[安杰莉卡·斯凯勒·丘奇|安杰莉卡·斯凯勒·丘奇]]的名字命名的。<ref>John W. Barber, [http://books.google.com/books?id=DoAt3cXaS4EC&pg=PA53&dq=angelica+town+%22new+york%22+named+for+%22angelica+church%22&hl=en&sa=X&ei=Jbt2UqCFFsupsASU6IGoBw&ved=0CHQQ6AEwCQ#v=onepage&q=angelica%20town%20%22new%20york%22%20named%20for%20%22angelica%20church%22&f=false Historical Collections of the State of New York], 1851, page 53</ref>安杰利卡是[[美国独立战争|美国独立战争]]时的将领[[菲力·斯凯勒|菲力·斯凯勒]]之女，也是美国开国元勋[[亚历山大·汉密尔顿|亚历山大·汉密尔顿]]的大姨。<ref>Henry Gannett, [http://books.google.com/books?id=9V1IAAAAMAAJ&pg=PA25&dq=angelica+town+%22new+york%22+named+for&hl=en&sa=X&ei=PLp2UvyGKbHlsAToxIDgCA&ved=0CC4Q6AEwAA#v=onepage&q=angelica%20town%20%22new%20york%22%20named%20for&f=false The Origin of Certain Place Names in the United States], 1905, page 25</ref><ref>John Warner Barber, Henry Howe, [http://books.google.com/books?id=2ZyGfk7FqlYC&pg=PA57&dq=angelica+town+%22new+york%22+named+for+%22angelica+church%22&hl=en&sa=X&ei=Jbt2UqCFFsupsASU6IGoBw&ved=0CF0Q6AEwBQ#v=onepage&q=angelica%20town%20%22new%20york%22%20named%20for%20%22angelica%20church%22&f=false Historical Collections of the State of New York], 1842, page 57</ref>
