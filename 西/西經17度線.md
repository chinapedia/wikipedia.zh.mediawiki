'''西經17度線'''是[[本初子午線|本初子午線]]向西17度的經線，是為西經17°。此經線由[[北極點|北極點]]起穿過[[北冰洋|北冰洋]]、[[格陵蘭|格陵蘭]]、[[冰島|冰島]]、[[大西洋|大西洋]]、[[非洲|非洲]]、[[南冰洋|南冰洋]]以及[[南極洲|南極洲]]直到[[南極點|南極點]]。此段半圓路徑長{{convert|20003932|km}}，於[[赤道|赤道]]與本初子午線距{{convert|1892|km}}遠。<ref name="noaa">{{cite web | language =en | url     = http://www.ngs.noaa.gov/PUBS_LIB/inverse.pdf
 | title   = Direct and Inverse Solutions of Geodesics on the Ellipsoid with application of nested equation
 | author  = T. Vincenty
 | date    = 1975-04
}}</ref><ref name="latlon">{{cite web | language =en | url = http://www.movable-type.co.uk/scripts/latlong-vincenty.html
 | title   = Vincenty formula for distance between two Latitude/Longitude points
 | website = Movable type scripts
 | accessdate = 2017-05-17
}}</ref>

西經17度線與[[東經163度線|東經163度線]]形成一個[[大圓|大圓]]。
