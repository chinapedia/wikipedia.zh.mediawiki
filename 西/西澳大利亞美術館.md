'''西澳大利亞美術館'''是[[澳洲|澳洲]][[西澳大利亞州|西澳大利亞州]][[伯斯|伯斯]]的一座[[美術館|美術館]]，位於伯斯文化中心之內，靠近[[西澳大利亞博物館|西澳大利亞博物館]]和[[西澳大利亞州立圖書館|西澳大利亞州立圖書館]]。西澳大利亞美術館開幕於1979年<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.artgallery.wa.gov.au/about_us/history.htm |title=Art Gallery of Western Australia History |accessdate=2006-06-25 |date= |work= |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20060619095146/http://www.artgallery.wa.gov.au/about_us/history.htm |archivedate=2006-06-19 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。西澳大利亞美術館收藏了超過15000件美術品<ref name=DCA>{{cite web |url=http://www.cultureandarts.wa.gov.au/agwa.asp |title=Department of Culture and the Arts - Art Gallery of Western Australia |accessdate=2008-01-05 |format= |date= |work= |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071102184913/http://www.cultureandarts.wa.gov.au/agwa.asp |archivedate=2007-11-02 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>，每年參觀者平均40萬人次<ref name=DCA />。西澳大利亞美術館最早和州立博物館及圖書館共同位於禧年大樓之內<ref name=RHP01>{{cite web |url=http://register.heritage.wa.gov.au/PDF_Files/A%20-%20A-D/Art%20Gallery%26Museum%28P-AD%29.PDF |title=Register of Heritage Places: Art Gallery & Museum Buildings |accessdate=2008-01-03 |format=PDF |date=2001-08-28 |work= |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080411053820/http://register.heritage.wa.gov.au/PDF_Files/A%20-%20A-D/Art%20Gallery%26Museum%28P-AD%29.PDF |archivedate=2008-04-11 }}</ref>。現在的美術館大樓修建於1977年<ref name=RHP06>{{cite web |url=http://register.heritage.wa.gov.au/PDF_Files/A%20-%20A-D/16722%20Art%20Gallery%20Complex%20%28I-AD%29.PDF |title=Register of Heritage Places: Art Gallery of Western Australia Complex |accessdate=2008-01-03 |format=PDF |date=2006-05-09 |work= |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080411053827/http://register.heritage.wa.gov.au/PDF_Files/A%20-%20A-D/16722%20Art%20Gallery%20Complex%20%28I-AD%29.PDF |archivedate=2008-04-11 }}</ref>。
