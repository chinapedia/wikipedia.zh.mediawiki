'''西經115度線'''是[[本初子午線|本初子午線]]向西115度的[[經線|經線]]，是為西經115°。此線經線由[[北極點|北極點]]起穿過[[北冰洋|北冰洋]]、[[北美洲|北美洲]]、[[太平洋|太平洋]]、[[南冰洋|南冰洋]]以及[[南極洲|南極洲]]直到[[南極點|南極點]]。此段半圓路徑長{{convert|20003932|km}}，於赤道與本初子午線距{{convert|12802|km}}遠。<ref name="noaa">{{cite web | url  = http://www.ngs.noaa.gov/PUBS_LIB/inverse.pdf
 | title   = Direct and Inverse Solutions of Geodesics on the Ellipsoid with application of nested equation
 | author  = T. Vincenty
 | date    = 1975-04
}}</ref><ref name="latlon">{{cite web | url = http://www.movable-type.co.uk/scripts/latlong-vincenty.html
 | title   = Vincenty formula for distance between two Latitude/Longitude points
 | website = Movable type scripts
 | accessdate = 2017-05-17
}}</ref>

於[[赤道|赤道]]與[[南緯60度線|南緯60度線]]之間，西經115度線是[[拉羅東加條約|南太平洋无核区]]<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.armscontrol.org/documents/rarotonga |title=South Pacific Nuclear Weapons Free Zone Treaty (Treaty of Rarotonga) |accessdate=2016年1月6日 |publisher={{le|軍備控制協會|Arms Control Association}} |language=en |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160131152314/http://www.armscontrol.org/documents/rarotonga |archivedate=2016年1月31日 }}</ref>與{{le|特拉特洛爾科條約|Treaty of Tlatelolco|拉丁美洲無核區}}的邊界。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.iaea.org/publications/documents/treaties/treaty-prohibition-nuclear-weapons-latin-america-tlatelolco-treaty |title=Treaty for the Prohibition of Nuclear Weapons in Latin America (Tlatelolco Treaty) |accessdate=2016年1月6日 |publisher=[[國際原子能總署|國際原子能總署]] |language=en }}</ref>

西經115度線與[[東經65度線|東經65度線]]形成一個[[大圓|大圓]]。
