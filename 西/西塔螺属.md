'''西塔螺屬'''（[[学名|学名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Sitalina'''''}}）<ref name='id11045'/>，又名'''圓口小蝸牛屬'''<ref name="TaiBIF" />，舊屬[[拟阿勇蛞蝓科|拟阿勇蛞蝓科]]<ref name='id11045'/>，後來認為本屬其實應該被歸入其姊妹分類元[[鼈甲蝸牛科|鼈甲蝸牛科]]（Helicarionidae）。這兩個科同樣都是[[腹足綱|腹足綱]][[柄眼目|柄眼目]]的[[軟體動物|軟體動物]]。

==物種==
以下為部分本屬的物種：
* [[角圓口小蝸牛|角圓口小蝸牛]] ''[[Sitalina_angulifera|Sitalina angulifera]]'' <small>(Pilsbry et Hirase, 1906)</small><ref name="shelldoc_00133" />
* [[濟州西塔螺|濟州西塔螺]] ''[[Sitalina_chejuensis|Sitalina chejuensis]]'' Kwon & Lee, 1991<ref name="Noseworthy 2007"/>
* ''[[Sitalina_circumcincta|Sitalina circumcincta]]'' (Reinhardt, 1883)<ref name="Noseworthy 2007"/>
* [[笠圓口小蝸牛|笠圓口小蝸牛]] ''[[Sitalina_hirasei|Sitalina hirasei]]'' <small>(Pilsbry, 1905)</small><ref name="shelldoc_00133" />
* ''[[Sitalina_insignis|Sitalina insignis]]''（{{lang|ja|カサネシタラガイ}}，NT）<ref name="jpenv"/>
* [[日本西塔螺|日本西塔螺]] ''[[Sitalina_japonica|Sitalina japonica]]'' <small>Habe, 1964</small><ref name="Noseworthy 2007"/>（{{lang|ja|ウメムラシタラガイ}}，NT）<ref name="jpenv"/>
* [[平圓口小蝸牛|平圓口小蝸牛]] ''[[Sitalina_latissima|Sitalina latissima]]'' <small>(Pilsbry, 1902)</small>（{{lang|ja|ヒラシタラガイ}}）<ref name="shelldoc_00133" /><ref name="jpenv"/>
* [[草笠西塔螺|草笠西塔螺]] ''[[Sitalina_Petasus-sinensis|Sitalina Petasus-sinensis]]'' <small>(Heude, 1885)</small><ref name='id11045' />
* [[蓬萊圓口小蝸牛|蓬萊圓口小蝸牛]] [[Sitalina_trochulus_formosana|''Sitalina trochulus'' subsp. ''formosana'']] <small>(Schmacker & Böettger, 1891)</small><ref name="shelldoc_00133" />
* [[臺灣圓口小蝸牛|臺灣圓口小蝸牛]] [[Sitalina_turrita_taiwanica|''Sitalina turrita'' subsp. ''taiwanica'']] <small>(Pilsbry & Hirase, 1906)</small><ref name="shelldoc_00133" />
