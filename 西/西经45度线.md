'''西經45度線'''是[[本初子午線|本初子午線]]向西45度的[[經線|經線]]，是為西經45°。此線經線由[[北極點|北極點]]起穿過[[北冰洋|北冰洋]]、[[格陵蘭|格陵蘭]]、[[大西洋|大西洋]]、[[南美洲|南美洲]]、[[南冰洋|南冰洋]]以及[[南極洲|南極洲]]直到[[南極點|南極點]]。此段半圓路徑長{{convert|20003932|km}}，於[[赤道|赤道]]與本初子午線距{{convert|5009|km}}遠。<ref name="noaa">{{cite web | language =en | url  = http://www.ngs.noaa.gov/PUBS_LIB/inverse.pdf
 | title   = Direct and Inverse Solutions of Geodesics on the Ellipsoid with application of nested equation
 | author  = T. Vincenty
 | date    = 1975-04
}}</ref><ref name="latlon">{{cite web | language =en | url = http://www.movable-type.co.uk/scripts/latlong-vincenty.html
 | title   = Vincenty formula for distance between two Latitude/Longitude points
 | website = Movable type scripts
 | accessdate = 2017-05-17
}}</ref>

於格陵蘭，此線是[[卡蘇伊特薩普|卡蘇伊特薩普]]、[[凱克卡塔|凱克卡塔]]與[[瑟莫蘇克|瑟莫蘇克]]、[[東北格陵蘭國家公園|東北格陵蘭國家公園]]的邊界。

西經45度線與東經135度線形成一個大圓，並且是[[UTC-3|UTC-3]]時區中間軸<ref>{{cite web | language =en | url = https://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/timezone/utc-3
 | title   = Current UTC-3, Time Zone
 | website = TimeAndDate.com
 | accessdate = 2017-05-17
}}</ref>。
