'''西蒙·柯蒂斯'''（Simon Curtis，{{bd|1960年|3月11日}}）是一名[[英国|英国]]电影导演与制片人，他执导的最知名作品是2011年的传记片《[[情迷梦露7天|情迷梦露7天]]》。

== 事业 ==
他最早是在伦敦的[[Royal_Court_Theatre|Royal Court Theatre]]开始踏入演艺行业的<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/media/inside-story-in-the-right-direction--the-cream-of-britains-television-directing-talent-848048.html|title=Inside Story: In the right direction - the cream of Britain's television directing talent|date=16 June 2008|work=[[The_Independent|The Independent]]|publisher=Independent Print Limited|accessdate=9 January 2012}}</ref>。第一份工作是担任剧作家[[Caryl_Churchill|Caryl Churchill]]编导的戏剧 ''Top Girls'' 的助理导演<ref name="Desk">{{cite web|url=http://www.theartsdesk.com/tv/my-job-directing-cranford|title=My Job Directing Cranford|last=Curtis|first=Simon|date=18 December 2009|publisher=The Arts Desk|accessdate=9 January 2012}}</ref>。后来他成为[[Danny_Boyle|Danny Boyle]]与[[Max_Stafford-Clark|Max Stafford-Clark]]的助理导演<ref name="SC">{{cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.co.uk/simon-curtis|title=Simon Curtis|work=[[The_Huffington_Post|The Huffington Post]]|publisher=[[AOL|AOL]]|accessdate=9 January 2012|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120121201623/http://www.huffingtonpost.co.uk/simon-curtis|archivedate=2012年1月21日}}</ref>。他参与过的主要戏剧作品有''Road''、 ''A Lie of the Mind''、''Roots''、 ''Dinner with Friends'' 和 ''The Rise and Fall of Little Voice''<ref name="SC"/>。

==个人生活==
1992年，他与美国女演员[[伊莉莎白·麥戈文|伊莉莎白·麥戈文]]结婚<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/femail/article-1324246/Downton-Abbey-star-Elizabeth-McGovern-leave-fiance-Sean-Penn.html|title=Why I had to escape Hollywood's clutches|date=29 October 2010|work=[[Daily_Mail|Daily Mail]]|publisher=[[Associated_Press|Associated Press]]|accessdate=10 December 2011}}</ref>。两人育有2个女儿，居住于伦敦的Chiswick<ref name="GIilbert">{{cite news|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/films/features/hollywood-never-suited-me-elizabeth-mcgovern-on-fleeing-la-and-downton-abbeyrsquos-lady-cora-2161323.html|title='Hollywood never suited me': Elizabeth McGovern on fleeing LA and Downton Abbey’s Lady Cora|last=GIilbert|first=Gerard|date=18 December 2010|work=[[The_Independent|The Independent]]|publisher=Independent Print Limited|accessdate=10 December 2011}}</ref>。
