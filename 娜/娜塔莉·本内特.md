'''娜塔莉·路易絲·本內特'''（{{lang-en|'''Natalie Louise Bennett'''}}，{{bd|1966年|2月10日|||catIdx=B}}），是[[澳洲|澳洲]]出生的英國[[政治家|政治家]]及[[新聞從業員|新聞從業員]]。她在2012年9月3日當選為[[英格蘭和威爾斯綠黨|英格蘭和威爾斯綠黨]]黨魁<ref name="new leader">{{cite web |url=http://greenparty.org.uk/news/2012/09/03/new-leader-and-deputy-leader-announcement/ |title=New Leader and Deputy Leader announcement |date=3 September 2012 |publisher=Green Party |accessdate=23 December 2012}}</ref><ref name="BBC1">{{cite news|title=Natalie Bennett elected new Green Party leader in England and Wales. She beat three other candidates to the position in a poll of Green Party members.|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-politics-19462474|accessdate=3 September 2012|newspaper=BBC}}</ref>。

==早年生活==
1966年2月10日，本內特在[[澳洲|澳洲]][[悉尼|悉尼]]郊區[[伊士活|伊士活]]出生<ref name="Who's Who 2014">{{cite web|title=BENNETT, Natalie Louise|url=http://www.ukwhoswho.com/view/article/oupww/whoswho/U257973|website=Who's Who 2014|publisher=A & C Black|accessdate=10 February 2015|date=November 2014}}</ref><ref name="Telegraph - Natalie Bennett - a little green around the gills">{{cite news|last1=Langley|first1=William|title=Natalie Bennett - a little green around the gills|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/politics/green-party/11442084/Natalie-Bennett-a-little-green-around-the-gills.html|accessdate=3 April 2015|work=The Daily Telegraph|date=1 March 2015}}</ref>，是約翰和喬伊·本內特（John and Joy Bennett）的女兒<ref>[http://www.ukwhoswho.com/view/article/oupww/whoswho/U257973 "Bennett, Natalie Louise"], ''Who's Who 2014'', A & C Black, an imprint of Bloomsbury Publishing plc, 2014; online edn, Oxford University Press, 2014 ; online edn, Nov 2014. Retrieved 9 May 2015 (subscription required).</ref> 。她的父母出身[[工人階級|工人階級]]——兼職秘書和一個木匠學徒，母親在1989年一場車禍中逝世<ref name="BBC - From 'Jillaroo' to Green Party leader">{{cite news|last1=Totaro|first1=Paola|title=Natalie Bennett: From 'Jillaroo' to Green Party leader|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/election-2015-32493939|accessdate=30 April 2015|work=BBC News|date=30 April 2015}}</ref>。

本內特後來獲得[[獎學金|獎學金]]，在[[新南威尔士州|新南威尔士州]][[宝活_(新南威尔士州)|宝活]]的女子[[独立学校|独立学校]]{{link-en|MLC學校|MLC School}}學習<ref name="Telegraph - Natalie Bennett - a little green around the gills" /><ref name=":0">Ferraro, Carmela. [http://inside.org.au/force-of-nature/ "Force of nature"]. Inside Story. 17 April 2013. Retrieved 27 April 2014.</ref>，並成為家庭中第一個進入大學的成員<ref name="BBC - From 'Jillaroo' to Green Party leader" />。大學期間她得到[[悉尼大學|悉尼大學]]{{link-en|農業理科學士|Bachelor of Science in Agriculture|榮譽農業理科學士}}學位、[[新英格蘭大學_(澳洲)|新英格蘭大學]]亞洲研究[[文学士|榮譽文学士]]及[[萊斯特大學|萊斯特大學]][[大众传播|大众传播]][[文学硕士|文学硕士]]<ref name="Who's Who 2014" /><ref name="bbc profile">{{cite web|last=Rath|first=Kayte|title=Profile: Green Party leader Natalie Bennett|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-politics-19465606|publisher=BBC News|accessdate=23 December 2012}}</ref>。
