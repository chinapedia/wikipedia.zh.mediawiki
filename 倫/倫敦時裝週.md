'''倫敦時裝週'''（{{lang-en|'''London Fashion Week'''}}，縮寫：{{lang|en|'''LFW'''}}）是[[倫敦|倫敦]]每年2月舉辦當年秋冬時裝週、9月舉辦次年春夏時裝週，一年舉辦兩次的服裝交易會，與[[紐約時裝週|紐約]]、[[米蘭時裝週|米蘭]]、[[巴黎時裝週|巴黎時裝週]]合稱為四大時裝週（{{lang-en|Big Four}}）。<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=H2xeBAAAQBAJ&pg=PA129|title=Fashion Journalism|last=Bradford|first=Julie|publisher=Routledge|year=2014|page=129}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=3XFMAQAAQBAJ&pg=PA115|title=The Fundamentals of Fashion Management|last=Dillon|first=Susan|publisher=A&C Black|year=2011|page=115}}</ref>

== 歷史和起源 ==
1983年10月，在{{link-en|商業、革新和技能部|Department for Business, Innovation and Skills}}（{{lang-en|Department for Business, Innovation and Skills}}，縮寫：{{lang|en|BIS}}）的協助下，{{link-en|倫敦發展署|London Development Agency}}（{{lang-en|London Development Agency}}，縮寫：{{lang|en|LDA}}）轄下的{{link-en|英國時裝委員會|British Fashion Council}}（{{lang-en|British Fashion Council}}，縮寫：{{lang|en|BFC}}）舉辦了第一屆倫敦時裝週（{{lang-en|London Fashion Week}}）。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.crazyaboutmagazines.com/ourshop/prod_514225-The-Face-magazine-John-Lydon-cover-December-1983-Issue-44.html|title=crazyaboutmagazines|last=Johnson|first=David|date=1983-12-01|work=The Face, December 1983, issue 44, page 44: "Eight For 84 – Identifying the best of Britain’s young designer talent".|accessdate=2016-11-10|location=London}}</ref>與紐約、米蘭、巴黎時裝週合稱為四大時裝週（{{lang-en|Big Four}}）。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.britishfashioncouncil.com/content.aspx?CategoryID=420|title=British Fashion Council website|accessdate=2010-03-10}}</ref>

作為一項惠及納稅人的交易活動，倫敦時裝週備受媒體的關注。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.whatdotheyknow.com/request/london_fashion_week_biannual_upd#comment-7716|title=Agreement for Funding Relative to Creative Sector Support - Designer Fashion. Parties: LDA/BFC website|accessdate=2010-06-07}}</ref>倫敦時裝週目前贊助商是[[梅賽德斯-賓士|梅賽德斯-賓士]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://voicesoffashion.co.uk/|title=London Fashion Week Official Sponsors|accessdate=28 August 2015|publisher=Voices of Fashion}}</ref> 、{{link-de|TONI&GUY|Toni&Guy}}。有5000多個媒體及時尚買手參與，成交額達4000萬英鎊<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.londonfashionweek.co.uk/news_details.aspx?ID=86|title=Record Figures for London Fashion Week|accessdate=2010-03-10|author=Caroline Rush|work=Crush Communications}}</ref>或1億英鎊不等。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.londonfashionweek.co.uk/uploads/documents/doc_1747.pdf|title=London Fashion Week factsheet|accessdate=2010-03-10}}</ref>以零售為重點、對公眾開放的倫敦時裝週末（{{lang|en|London Fashion Weekend}}）亦在同一地點舉行。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.londonfashionweekend.co.uk/|title=London Fashion Weekend website|accessdate=2010-03-10|publisher=London Fashion Weekend}}</ref>

[[File:Manisharora6.jpg|缩略图]]
主要會場，包括大多數頂尖設計師、時裝屋的走秀，會安排在[[倫敦市中心|倫敦市中心]]的[[萨默塞特府|薩默塞特府]]（{{lang|en|Somerset House}}）中庭的大型帳幕中，<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.thisislondon.co.uk/fashion/article-23746171-costelloe-is-head-and-shoulders-ahead-at-fashion-week.do |title=London Evening Standard article |accessdate=2010-03-10 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100415213741/http://www.thisislondon.co.uk/fashion/article-23746171-costelloe-is-head-and-shoulders-ahead-at-fashion-week.do |archivedate=2010-04-15 }}</ref>而展覽會安排在薩默塞特府的室內舉行，可以容乃超過150名設計師的作品。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.londonfashionweek.co.uk/designers|title=Designers|accessdate=2010-03-10|publisher=London Fashion Week}}</ref>其它由私人團體組織的走秀，會安排倫敦市中心其它地方（例如：Vauxhall Fashion Scout、On|Off）進行。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.artslondonnews.co.uk/20100219-fashion-week-day-1-outside |title=Arts London website |accessdate=2010-03-10 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100224061034/http://www.artslondonnews.co.uk/20100219-fashion-week-day-1-outside |archivedate=2010-02-24 }}</ref>

== 網絡直播 ==
2010年春季，倫敦時裝週在網上直播薩默塞特府的走秀，成為四大時裝週中，第一個在[[互聯網|互聯網]]直播走秀的時裝週。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/life-style/fashion/news/london-fashion-week-to-be-streamed-live-1904888.html|title=Independent newspaper article|last=Rice|first=Simon|date=2010-02-19|work=The Independent|accessdate=2010-03-10|location=London}}</ref>

== 倫敦男裝系列 ==
2012年夏季起，倫敦推出春夏系列及秋冬系列以外的倫敦男裝系列（{{lang|en|London Collections: Men}}）。2015年6月有77名獨立設計師參展，相對比第一屆倫敦男裝系列，增長了67%。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/luxury/mens-style/70664/london-collections-men-ss16-preview.html|title=LCM and London's menswear renaissance|last=Doig|first=Stephen|date=5 June 2015|newspaper=Telegraph|accessdate=28 August 2015}}</ref>
