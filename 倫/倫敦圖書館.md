'''倫敦圖書館'''（{{lang-en|London Library}}）是[[英國|英國]][[倫敦|倫敦]]的一座[[圖書館|圖書館]]，也是世界規模最大的圖書館之一，創建於1841年。倫敦圖書館自1845年以來就位於[[聖詹姆士廣場|聖詹姆士廣場]]<ref name="Map">"[http://www.westminster.gov.uk/fmn/index3.cfm?LayerID=24&LGSL=42,437,438,439,440,441,442,444,445,446,447,449,456,9999 Libraries] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090508051629/http://www.westminster.gov.uk/fmn/index3.cfm?LayerID=24&LGSL=42%2C437%2C438%2C439%2C440%2C441%2C442%2C444%2C445%2C446%2C447%2C449%2C456%2C9999 |date=2009-05-08 }}." ''[[City_of_Westminster|City of Westminster]]''. Retrieved on 21 January 2009.</ref>。
