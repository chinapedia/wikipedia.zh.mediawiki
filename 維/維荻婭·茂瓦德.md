'''維荻婭·茂瓦德'''（'''''Vidya Malvade'''''）是一個[[寶萊塢|寶萊塢]]的電影演員。

== 個人生活 ==
維荻婭曾經學習過法律和作過空中小姐，她的第一任丈夫曾是Alliance Air航空公司的飛行員巴嘎機長（Capt Bagga），但是他不幸死于一次墜機事故<!-- died when his plane, the Alliance Air Boeing, collided with a building at Patna. -->[https://web.archive.org/web/20070311192336/http://www.apunkachoice.com/scoop/bollywood/20040109-3.html] [http://bollywood.allindiansite.com/g/2005101906.html]{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}.
(詳情請看《[https://web.archive.org/web/20071122181009/http://civilaviation.nic.in/coi/ 飛行資訊]》)。

== 電影事業 ==
初出于2003年，現今演過最知名的電影是《[[加油印度！|加油印度！]]》，<!--with [Vikram Bhatt]]'s ''[[Inteha_(2003_film)|Inteha]]'' (2003), which flopped at the box office. After a string of unsuccessful movies and several advertisements, she recently  acted opposite [[Shahrukh_Khan|Shahrukh Khan]] in the [[Yash_Raj_Films|Yash Raj Film]] ''[[Chak_De_India|Chak De India]]'' (2007). She plays the role of a goalkeeper and the Captain of the Indian women's [[Hockey|Hockey]] team.-->在這部影片中[[沙·茹克·罕|沙·茹克·罕]]扮演印度女子曲棍球隊的教練，她則扮演守門員兼隊長。
