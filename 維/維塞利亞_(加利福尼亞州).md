'''維塞利亞'''（{{lang en|'''Visalia'''}}，{{IPAc-en|v|aɪ|ˈ|s|eɪ|l|j|ə}}，{{respell|vy|SAYL|yə}}）是[[美國|美國]][[加利福尼亞州|加利福尼亞州]][[圖萊里縣_(加利福尼亞州)|圖萊里縣]]的縣治{{GR|6}}，位於[[中央谷地|中央谷地]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tularecounty.ca.gov/economicdevelopment/index.cfm/agriculture/ |title=Agriculture - Tulare County Economic Development Office |publisher=Tularecounty.ca.gov |date= |accessdate=2012-10-24}}</ref>面積74.0平方公里，2006年人口113,487人。<ref>[http://factfinder.census.gov/servlet/SAFFPopulation?_event=Search&geo_id=16000US0616532&_geoContext=01000US%7C04000US06%7C16000US0616532&_street=%20&_county=Visalia+city&_cityTown=Visalia+city&_state=04000US06&_zip=%20&_lang=en&_sse=on&ActiveGeoDiv=geoSelect&_useEV=%20&pctxt=fph&pgsl=160&_submenuId=population_0&ds_name=null&_ci_nbr=null&qr_name=%20&reg=%3Anull&_keyword=%20&_industry=%20 Visalia, California - Population Finder - American FactFinder]</ref>這個數字於2010年上升至124,442人。<ref>[http://factfinder2.census.gov/ 2010 census factfinder2]</ref>本城於2010年是美國人口第198多的城市。<ref name=PopEstBugCities>{{cite web| url=http://www.census.gov/popest/cities/tables/SUB-EST2008-01.csv| title=Table 1: Annual Estimates of the Population for Incorporated Places Over 100,000, Ranked by July 1, 2005 Population: April 1, 2000 to July 1, 2005| format=[[comma-separated_values|CSV]]| work=2008 Population Estimates| publisher=[[United_States_Census_Bureau|United States Census Bureau]], Population Division| date=2006-06-20| accessdate=2007-01-26}}</ref> 

1852年建立堡壘<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.militarymuseum.org/FtVisalia.html |title=Historic California Posts: Fort Visalia |publisher=Militarymuseum.org |date= |accessdate=2012-10-24}}</ref>，1853年成為縣治。<ref>[http://www.calarchives4u.com/history/tulare/tul1913-ch1.txt California Genealogy & History Archives] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141218163442/http://www.calarchives4u.com/history/tulare/tul1913-ch1.txt |date=2014-12-18 }}</ref>1874年建市。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.carnegie-libraries.org/california/visalia.html |title=Visalia, Tulare County |publisher=Carnegie-libraries.org |date= |accessdate=2012-10-24}}</ref>
