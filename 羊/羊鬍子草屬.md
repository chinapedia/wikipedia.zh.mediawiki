'''羊胡子草'''（[[学名|学名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Eriophorum'''''}}）是[[莎草科|莎草科]]下的一[[属|属]]，包含約25個[[開花植物|開花植物]]品種。广泛分布于[[北半球|北半球]][[温带|温带]]的酸性[[沼泽|沼泽]]地带，特別是[[北極圈|北極圈]]的[[苔原|苔原]]地帶<ref name=fna>{{cite book |author=Ball, Peter W. & Daniel E. Wujek |year=2002 |editor=Flora of North America Editorial Committee |title=Cyperaceae |volume=23 |work=[[Flora_of_North_America|Flora of North America North of Mexico]] |publisher=[[Oxford_University_Press|Oxford University Press]] |isbn=978-0-19-515207-4 |chapter=''Eriophorum'' Linnaeus, Sp. Pl. 1: 52. 1753; Gen. Pl. ed. 5, 27. 1754 |pages=21–27 |url=http://www.efloras.org/florataxon.aspx?flora_id=1&taxon_id=112065}}</ref><ref name=fleur>Flora Europaea: [http://rbg-web2.rbge.org.uk/cgi-bin/nph-readbtree.pl/parent=/filename=feout/firstval=11/SID=83.1184789870?FAMILY_XREF=%20&GENUS_XREF=Eriophorum&SPECIES_XREF=%20&TAXON_NAME_XREF=%20&RANK=species ''Eriophorum'']</ref>。

为[[多年生草本植物|多年生草本植物]]，高约15-40厘米。其[[种子|种子]]成熟后分裂出状如棉絮的白色细丝，可以随风飞散，以利于传播种子<ref>{{cite book |last=Crawford |first=R. M. M. |title=Studies in Plant Survival |year=1989 |publisher=Blackwell Science|pages=54-55}}</ref>。
