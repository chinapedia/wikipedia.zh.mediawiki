《'''羊毛記'''》（{{lang-en|''Silo''}}）是由[[美国|美国]]作家[[休·豪伊|休·豪伊]]写的一套[[科幻|科幻]][[中篇小说|中篇小说]]與反烏托邦小說。（亦有[[单行本|单行本]]发行）。<ref>{{cite web |url= http://edition.cnn.com/2012/09/07/tech/mobile/kindle-direct-publish/index.html|title=Self-published e-book author: 'Most of my months are six-figure months'|publisher=CNN|date=September 7, 2012 |accessdate=September 8, 2012}}</ref>该系列作品的的电影版权已被[[二十世纪福克斯|二十世纪福克斯]]公司购得，<ref>{{cite web|last=Deahl|first=Rachel|title=Hugh Howey's 'Wool' Nabbed By 20th Century Fox|url=http://www.publishersweekly.com/pw/by-topic/book-news/page-to-screen/article/51934-hugh-howey-s-wool-nabbed-by-20th-century-fox.html|publisher=Publishers Weekly|accessdate=30 September 2012}}</ref>导演[[雷德利·斯科特|雷德利·斯科特]]和{{link-en|Steven Zaillian|Steven Zaillian}}表示有将此系列改编为电影的意向。<ref>{{cite web|last=Sutter|first=John|title=E-book author Hugh Howey claims 'six figure months' from Amazon Kindle Direct Publishing|url=http://www.abcactionnews.com/dpp/money/e-book-author-hugh-howey-claims-six-figure-months-from-amazon-kindle-direct-publishing|publisher=ABC News|accessdate=30 September 2012|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120916204009/http://www.abcactionnews.com/dpp/money/e-book-author-hugh-howey-claims-six-figure-months-from-amazon-kindle-direct-publishing|archivedate=2012年9月16日}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Boog|first=Jason|title=Hugh Howey’s Wool Series Gets Book Trailer Treatment|url=http://www.mediabistro.com/galleycat/hugh-howey-wool-book-trailer_b54289|publisher=GalleyCat|accessdate=30 September 2012|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120917055547/http://www.mediabistro.com/galleycat/hugh-howey-wool-book-trailer_b54289|archivedate=2012年9月17日|df=}}</ref>

== 写作背景 ==
休·豪伊自2011年起创作该系列，一开始他把《羊毛战记》作为独立的短篇故事来写，<ref>{{cite web|last=Wecks|first=Erik|title=Hugh Howey Interview Part 2: A Spoiler-Filled Discussion of The Wool Omnibus|url=http://www.wired.com/geekdad/2012/03/hugh-howey-interview-part-2/|publisher=Wired|accessdate=30 September 2012}}</ref>并将其发布在 Kindle Direct Publishing 平台上。在该系列获得了一定的人气之后，他开始进行更多的创作。<ref>{{cite web|last=Wecks|first=Erik|title=Hugh Howey Interview Part 1: Science Fiction, Indie Writing, and Success|url=http://www.wired.com/geekdad/2012/03/hugh-howey-interview-part-one-science-fiction-indie-writing-and-success/|publisher=Wired|accessdate=30 September 2012}}</ref>休·豪伊自2012年起开始售卖国际版权，电影改编权则由二十世纪福克斯公司获得。

休·豪伊和[[西蒙与舒斯特|西蒙与舒斯特]]签订了大约五十万美元的协议，出售《羊毛战记》系列书籍在美国和加拿大的印刷版权。休·豪伊依然保有在线发布《羊毛战记》后续作品的权利。<ref>{{cite web|last=Alter |first=Alexandra |url=http://online.wsj.com/article/SB10001424127887324678604578340752088305668.html |title=Wool: Sci-Fi's Underground Hit — WSJ.com |publisher=Online.wsj.com |date= |accessdate=2013-03-08}}</ref>
