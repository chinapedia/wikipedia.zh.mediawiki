'''唧筒座AG '''，也稱為'''HD 89353'''和''' HR 4049'''，是顆位於[[唧筒座|唧筒座]]的前[[漸近巨星分支|漸近巨星分支]]恆星。一顆非常[[貧金屬星|貧金屬星]]<ref name="Takeda">{{cite journal|title=Detection of Zinc in the Very Metal-Poor Post-AGB Star HR 4049|journal=Publications of the Astronomical Society of Japan|url=https://academic.oup.com/pasj/article/54/5/765/1522977|date=2002-10-25|volume=54|issue=5|doi=10.1093/pasj/54.5.765|pages=765–773|issn=0004-6264|language=en|accessdate=2018-04-02|author=Yoichi Takeda, Mudumba Parthasarathy, Wako Aoki, Yoshifusa Ita, Yoshikazu Nakada, Hideyuki Izumiura, Kunio Noguchi, Masahide Takada-Hidai, Bun’ei Sato, Akito Tajitsu, Satoshi Honda, Satoshi Kawanomoto, Hiroyasu Ando, Hiroshi Karoji}}</ref>，它被富含幾種分子的一層厚厚[[聯星拱星盤|聯星拱星盤]]環繞著<ref name="Malek">{{cite journal|title=The Gas-rich Circumbinary Disk of HR 4049. I. A Detailed Study of the Mid-infrared Spectrum|journal=The Astrophysical Journal|volume=780|issue=1|pages=41|url=http://stacks.iop.org/0004-637X/780/i=1/a=41|date=2014|doi=10.1088/0004-637x/780/1/41|issn=0004-637X|language=en|accessdate=2018-04-02|author=S. E. Malek, J. Cami}}</ref>，視星等5.53等，在理想的條件下隨時都可以用裸眼看見這顆恆星<ref name="SIMBAD">{{cite web |url =http://simbad.u-strasbg.fr/simbad/sim-id?Ident=HR+4049&NbIdent=1&Radius=2&Radius.unit=arcmin&submit=submit+id |title = AG Antliae|work = SIMBAD Astronomical Database|publisher=Centre de Données astronomiques de Strasbourg |accessdate = 15 June 2014}}</ref>。這顆恆星的距離大約是{{convert|813|pc|ly}}<ref name=SIMBAD/>，已經被證實是聯星 <ref name="Astron">{{cite journal|url=http://www.aanda.org/10.1051/0004-6361/201219282|title=Amorphous carbon in the disk around the post-AGB binary HR 4049|journal=Astronomy & Astrophysics|volume=551|date=2013-03-01|issn=0004-6361|language=en|accessdate=2018-04-02|doi=10.1051/0004-6361/201219282|author=B. Acke, P. Degroote, R. Lombaert, B. L. de Vries, K. Smolders, T. Verhoelst, E. Lagadec, C. Gielen, H. Van Winckel, C. Waelkens}}</ref>；然而，像許多單獨的特殊恆星一樣，這顆恆星有著獨特的頻譜。這顆恆星基於其[[巴耳末系|巴耳末系]]的譜線，是顆[[藍超巨星|藍超巨星]]，實際上它是顆位於前漸近巨星分支階段的老年低質量恆星<ref name="Waelkens">{{cite journal |title=HR 4049 - an old low-mass star disguised as a young massive supergiant |last1=Waelkens |first1=C. |last2=Lamers |first2=H. |last3=Waters |first3=R. |journal=ESO Messenger |date=September 1987 |volume=49 |pages=29–32 |bibcode=1987Msngr..49...29W}}</ref>。這顆恆星正在進行激烈的質量損失<ref name="Geballe">{{cite journal|title=Unusual features of the 1-4 micron spectrum of HR 4049|journal=The Astrophysical Journal|volume=340|pages=L29–L32|date=May 1989|doi=10.1086/185431|issn=0004-637X|language=en|url=http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1989ApJ...340L..29G|accessdate=2018-04-02|author=T. R. Geballe, K. S. Noll, D. C. B. Whittet, L. B. F. M. Waters}}</ref>，唯一改變的是在5.29和5.83星等間，以429天的週期改變光度<ref name=AAVSO>{{cite web|url=http://www.aavso.org/vsx/index.php?view=detail.top&oid=503 |title=AG Antliae |author=VSX|date=4 January 2010|work=The International Variable Star Index |publisher=American Association of Variable Star Observers|accessdate=15 June 2013}}</ref>。
