'''蘑菇香精'''（Lenthionine）是一个非常活泼的香气成分，它是[[香菇|香菇]]的主要风味物质之一。<ref>{{cite journal en|journal=Pure & Appl. Chem. |volume=66 |issue=10/11 |pages=2205–2206 |year=1994 |title=Chemistry in a salad bowl: Comparative organosulfur chemistry of garlic, onion and shiitake mushrooms |author=Eric Block and Russell Deorazio |url=http://www.iupac.org/publications/pac/1994/pdf/6610x2205.pdf |doi=10.1351/pac199466102205}}</ref>

据称它在香菇内是通过[[蘑菇酸|蘑菇酸]]（[[半胱氨酸亚砜|半胱氨酸亚砜]]）在[[S-烷基-L-半胱氨酸亚砜裂解酶|''S''-烷基-<small>L</small>-半胱氨酸亚砜裂解酶]]作用下产生的，但具体的生成机理仍然不清楚。这些反应只有在植物组织破坏后才发生。除了产生蘑菇香精，还生成[[聚噻噁烷|聚噻噁烷]]。

研究显示它有防止[[血栓|血栓]]形成的功效。<ref>{{cite journal en |year=2005 |pages=Presentation 54G–9 |journal=IFT Annual Meeting |title=Mechanism of inhibition of platelet aggregation by lenthionine, a flavor component from shiitake mushroom |author=T. Shibuya, S. Shimada, H. Sakurai, and H. Kumagai |url=http://ift.confex.com/ift/2005/techprogram/paper_29006.htm |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070311025520/http://ift.confex.com/ift/2005/techprogram/paper_29006.htm |archivedate=2007-03-11 }}</ref>
