'''凝結芽孢桿菌'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Bacillus coagulans''），又稱'''芽孢乳酸菌'''（''Lactobacillus sporogene''），是一種可以產生[[乳酸|乳酸]]的[[革蘭氏陽性菌|革蘭氏陽性菌]]。此種細菌為[[桿菌|桿菌]]，大小約為0.9*3-5微米，具有[[過氧化氫酶|過氧化氫酶]]，營養型態為[[兼性厭氧菌|兼性厭氧]]，生長到{{link-en|靜止期|Bacterial growth}}時革蘭氏染色可能呈陰性，[[IMViC|IMViC試驗]]的伏普（Voges Proskauer）與甲基紅（MR）結果均為陽性。

==名稱==
1915年，B.W.Hammer在[[愛荷華|愛荷華]]農業實業站分離了造成當地煉乳場生產[[淡奶|淡奶]]發生[[凝乳|凝乳]]現象的細菌，將其命名為凝結芽孢桿菌（''Bacillus coagulans''）<ref>Hammer, B. W. 1915. Bacteriological studies on the coagulation of evaporated milk. Iowa Agric. Exp. Stn. Res. Bull. 19:119-131</ref>。1932年Horowitz-Walssowa與Nowotelnow再次分離了這種菌，命名為芽孢乳酸菌''Lactobacillus sporogene''<ref>Horowitz-Wlassowa,L.M. and Nowotelnow, N.W.; :Über eine sporogenes Milchsaurebakterienart, Lactobacillus sporogenes n. sp. Zentralb. F. Bakt. II Abt. 87(1932)331.</ref>。這種細菌兼具[[乳桿菌屬|乳桿菌屬]]與[[芽孢桿菌屬|芽孢桿菌屬]]的特色，因此分類地位曾倍受爭議<ref name=autogenerated1>[http://www.food-info.net/uk/ff/sporogenes.htm Bacillus coagulans (Lactobacillus sporogenes) a probiotic ?]</ref>，1957年，第七版{{link-en|伯杰氏细菌鉴定手册|Bergey's Manual of Determinative Bacteriology}}依據生化特性將其歸入芽孢桿菌屬，但「芽孢乳酸菌」的名稱仍被廣泛使用<ref name="Julie">{{cite journal |vauthors=Jurenka JS |year=2012 |title=Bacillus coagulans: Monograph |journal =Alternative Medicine Review |volume=17 |issue=1 |pages=76-81 |PMID= 22502625}}</ref>。

雖然凝結芽孢桿菌可以產生[[乳酸|乳酸]]，商品行銷時也常被稱為一種[[乳桿菌屬|乳酸菌]]，但嚴格說起來它並不屬於乳酸菌，因為乳酸菌通常只包含[[乳桿菌屬|乳桿菌屬]]、[[鏈球菌屬|鏈球菌屬]]與{{link-en|雙岐桿菌屬|Bifidobacterium}}的細菌，這些細菌都不會形成[[內孢子|內孢子]]，不過凝結芽孢桿菌屬於[[芽孢桿菌屬|芽孢桿菌屬]]，會形成內孢子<ref name=autogenerated1 />。

==益生菌==
多數[[益生菌|益生菌]]在室溫下不穩定，經過冷凍、乾燥等加工過程後，製成藥品或食品添加物時多數細菌都已經失去活性，加上被食用後還會被[[消化道|消化道]]的[[胃酸|胃酸]]與[[膽汁|膽汁]]破壞，只有很小比例在抵達腸道時仍具有活性<ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Gilliland SE |year=2012 |title=Health and nutritional benefits from lactic acid bacteria
 |journal =FEMS Microbiology Letters
 |volume=87 |issue=1-2 |pages=175-188 |DOI= 10.1016/0378-1097(90)90705-U}}</ref>，但凝結芽孢桿菌是個例外，因為它能形成抗性很高的內孢子，不易在加工過程或消化道中被殺死，而可以順利抵達[[小腸|小腸]]後萌發、增殖。口服此菌後約4-6小時，此菌會於小腸萌發，且高達85%的攝取量都能順利抵達小腸<ref>{{cite book |author=Gandhi AB |year=1998 |title=Lactobacillus Sporogenes, an advancement in lactobacillus therapy. |publisher =The Eastern Pharmacist |pages=41-44 }}</ref>。在小腸萌發後，凝結芽孢桿菌會分泌[[乳酸|乳酸]]與短鏈[[脂肪酸|脂肪酸]]，進而影響[[人類微生物群系|腸道菌叢]]的組成<ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Adami A, Cavazzoni V|year=1999 |title=Occurrence of selected bacterial groups in the faeces of piglets fed with Bacillus coagulans as probiotic
 |journal =Journal of Basic Microbiology
 |volume=39 |issue=1 |pages=3-9 |PMID= 10071861}}</ref>，還可以分泌{{link-en|細菌素|Bacteriocin}}、{{link-en|凝固素|Coagulin}}等毒素以抑制致命的細菌生長<ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Hyronimus B, Le Marrec C, Urdaci MC |year=1998 |title=Coagulin, a bacteriocin-like inhibitory substance produced by Bacillus coagulans I4.
 |journal =FEMS Microbiology Letters
 |volume=85 |issue=1 |pages=42-50 |DOI= 10.1046/j.1365-2672.1998.00466.x}}</ref>。凝結芽孢桿菌屬於暫駐菌，在腸道內停留的時間不長，約七天後其內孢子便會隨糞便排出體外<ref>Majeed M, Bacillus coagulans: Probiotic of Choice, 
Sabinsa Corporation</ref>。有研究結果顯示凝結芽孢桿菌對治療[[抗藥性|抗藥性]]細菌造成的[[腹瀉|腹瀉]]有幫助<ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=La Rosa M, Bottaro G, Gulino N, Gambuzza F, Di Forti F, Inì G, Tornambè E |year=2003 |title=Prevention of antibiotic-associated diarrhea with Lactobacillus sporogens and fructo-oligosaccharides in children. A multicentric double-blind vs placebo study
 |journal =Minerva Pediatr
 |volume=55 |issue=5 |pages=447-52 |language=it |PMID= 14608267}}</ref>，也有助改善[[炎症性腸病|炎症性腸病]]的病情，可減少患者的排便次數<ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Dolin BJ |year=2009 |title=Effects of a proprietary Bacillus coagulans preparation on symptoms of diarrhea-predominant irritable bowel syndrome.
 |journal =Methods and findings in experimental and clinical pharmacology
 |volume=31 |issue=10 |pages=655 |DOI= 10.1358/mf.2009.31.10.1441078}}</ref>。另外凝結芽孢桿菌還可能在腸道中與[[膽固醇|膽固醇]]結合，以及抑制合成膽固醇所需的[[羟甲基戊二酸单酰辅酶A还原酶|羟甲基戊二酸单酰辅酶A还原酶]]，可能可以降低血液中的膽固醇含量<ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Mohan JC, Arora R, Khalilullah M|year=1990 |title=Preliminary observations on effect of Lactobacillus sporogenes on serum lipid levels in hypercholesterolemic patients
 |journal =The Indian Journal of Medical Research
 |volume=92 |issue=1 |pages=431-432 |PMID= 2079358}}</ref>。也有研究顯示某一型的凝結芽孢桿菌可以改善[[細菌性陰道炎|細菌性陰道炎]]<ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Ratna Sudha M, Yelikar KA, Deshpande S|year=2012 |title=Clinical Study of Bacillus coagulans Unique IS-2 (ATCC PTA-11748) in the Treatment of Patients with Bacterial Vaginosis
 |journal =Indian Journal of Microbiology
 |volume=52 |issue=3 |pages=396-399 |DOI= 10.1007/s12088-011-0233-z}}</ref>。
