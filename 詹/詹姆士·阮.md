'''詹姆斯·阮'''（{{lang-en|'''James Nguyen'''}}，{{bd|1966年|9月1日}}<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rottentomatoes.com/celebrity/770752987/|title=James Nguyen – Biography|location=Rotten Tomatoes|access-date=13 July 2016}}</ref>），越南导演，曾执导2010年发行的浪漫恐怖片《{{link-en|Birdemic：震惊与恐怖|Birdemic: Shock and Terror}}》及2013年发行的续集《{{link-en|Birdemic 2：复活|Birdemic 2: The Resurrection}}》。

== 生活和事业 ==
阮出生在[[越南|越南]][[岘港市|岘港市]]<ref>{{cite news |last=Savlov |first=Marc |date=2 July 2010 |title=When Bad Movies Attack: James Nguyen's underground oddity 'Birdemic' cries fowl |url=http://www.austinchronicle.com/screens/2010-07-02/1046582/ |work=The Austin Chronicle |access-date=14 July 2016 }}</ref>，他和他的家人曾于[[越南战争|越南战争]]期间西贡陷落前夕逃离了越南<ref>{{cite news |last=Itzkoff |first=Dave |title=A Cheesy Horror Turkey Becomes a High-Flying Cult Hit |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2010/03/25/movies/25birdemic.html |work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] |date=24 April 2010 |access-date=14 July 2016}}</ref>。阮从未接受过任何正式的制片训练，而是看着[[阿尔弗雷德·希区柯克|阿尔弗雷德·希区柯克]]执导的电影长大，其中包括1958年的《[[迷魂記|迷魂記]]》和1963年的《[[鸟|鸟]]》<ref>{{cite video |title=The Worst Movie Ever Made? |people=Amy Rosner (producer), Patrick Lee (editor) |url=http://www.cbsnews.com/video/watch/?id=6453386n |date=2 May 2010 |access-date=14 July 2016 |format=Television production |publisher=[[CBS_News|CBS News]]}}</ref>。后阮去到了[[硅谷|硅谷]]做软件推销员，他第一次拿起相机是在1999年<ref name=backstage>{{cite web |last=Riley |first=Jenelle |title=For the Birds |url=http://www.backstage.com/bso/news-and-features-features/for-the-birds-1004077669.story |publisher=Backstage |date=23 March 2010 |access-date=14 July 2016}}</ref>。

2003年，阮执导小成本爱情电影《朱莉和杰克》。2005年，阮执导的科幻惊悚副本，最终没有发行，他归咎于昂贵的情节串联，因此影响了制作决策。阮的成名作是其在2010年执导的电影《Birdemic：震惊​​和恐怖》。他是受到启发后，到[[美国|美国]][[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]][[半月湾|半月湾]]一边度假一边为这部电影编写剧本，而后在周边地区大量取景并拍摄了一些场景<ref>{{cite news |last=Miska |first=Brad |date=11 March 2010 |title=Video Interview with ‘Birdemic’ Director Part 2, New York Premiere Announced! |url=http://bloody-disgusting.com/news/19437 |publisher=Bloody Disgusting |access-date=14 July 2016 }}</ref>。拍摄电影的10,000美金预算由他自己的收入填补<ref>{{cite news |last=O'Neill |first=Phelim |date=26 May 2010 |title=Why I'm looking forward to Birdemic: Shock and Terror |url=https://www.theguardian.com/film/filmblog/2010/may/26/birdemic-james-nguyen-independent-film |work=The Guardian |access-date=14 July 2016 }}</ref>。阮利用从电影《鸟》获得的灵感编导了电影《Birdemic：震惊​​和恐怖》。<ref name="wsj">{{Cite news|url=http://blogs.wsj.com/speakeasy/2011/02/21/birdemic-director-on-shooting-his-sequel-in-3-d/|title=‘Birdemic’ Director on Shooting His Sequel… in 3-D|last=Gilchrist|first=Todd|date=21 February 2011|work=The Wall Street Journal|access-date=14 July 2016}}</ref>

自从Birdemic发行以来，就经常被列为有史以来最糟糕的电影之一<ref>{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/entertainment/8604858.stm |title=Is Birdemic worst film ever made? |publisher=BBC News |date=6 April 2010 |access-date=13 July 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/fabio-periera/review-embirdemic-shock-a_b_491140.html |author=Fabio Periera |title=Review: Birdemic: Shock and Terror |work=The Huffington Post |date=8 May 2010 |access-date=14 July 2016}}</ref>。2012年2月，阮开始制作电影的续集，并命名为《Birdemic 2：复活》。这部电影在2013年发行后打破了既有形象。
