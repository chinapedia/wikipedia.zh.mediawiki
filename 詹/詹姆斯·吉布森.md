'''詹姆斯·杰尔姆·吉布森'''（[[英语|英语]]：'''{{lang|en|James Jerome Gibson}}'''，{{bd|1904年|1月27日|1979年|12月11日|catIdx=G}}）是一位[[美国|美国]] [[心理学家|心理学家]]，被认为是20世纪[[视知觉|视知觉]]领域最重要的心理学家之一。1950年，他发表了经典著作《視覺世界的知覺》（The Perception of the Visual World），根据自己的实验，反对当时流行的[[行为主义|行为主义]]观点。 <!-- which pioneered the idea that animals 'sampled' information from the 'ambient' outside world. He also coined the term '[[affordance|affordance]]', meaning the interactive possibilities of a particular object or environment. This concept has been extremely influential in the field of [[design|design]] and [[ergonomics|ergonomics]]: see for example the work of [[Donald_Norman|Donald Norman]] who worked with Gibson, and has adapted many of his ideas for his own theories. --> 

在他后来的著作中，例如《视知觉生态论》（The Ecological Approach to Visual Perception，1979年），吉布森变得更为哲学化，如同从前批评行为主义一样地批评[[认知主义|认知主义]]。吉布森强烈地主张直接知觉<!-- , or '[[direct_realism|direct realism]]' (as pioneered by 苏格兰哲学家[[Thomas_Reid|Thomas Reid]]), as opposed to cognitivist 'indirect realism'. He termed his new approach [[生态心理学|生态心理学]]。他也批评[[information_processing|information processing]] view of cognition. Gibson is increasingly influential on 当代许多运动 in [[心理学|心理学]], particularly those considered to be [[post-cognitivist|post-cognitivist]]. -->

詹姆斯·吉布森的妻子[[埃莉诺·吉布森|埃莉诺·吉布森]]也是一位心理学家，他们在1932年结婚。
