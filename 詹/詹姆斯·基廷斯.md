'''詹姆斯·基廷斯'''（{{lang-en|James Keatings}}；{{bd|1992年|1月20日}}）是一位[[蘇格蘭|蘇格蘭]]足球運動員。在場上的位置是前鋒。他現在效力於[[蘇格蘭足球冠軍聯賽|蘇格蘭足球冠軍聯賽]]球隊[[希伯尼安足球俱樂部|希伯尼安足球俱樂部]]<ref>http://sport.stv.tv/football/clubs/hibernian/1322068-striker-james-keatings-signs-for-hibernian-just-days-after-leaving-hearts/</ref>。他也代表蘇格蘭U16<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.scottishfa.co.uk/football_player_profile.cfm?page=106&playerID=115489&squadID=17 | title=James Keatings – U16 Squad | publisher=Scottish Football Association | accessdate=3 February 2012}}</ref>、U17<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.scottishfa.co.uk/football_player_profile.cfm?page=106&playerID=115489&squadID=13 | title=James Keatings – U17 Squad | publisher=Scottish Football Association | accessdate=3 February 2012}}</ref>和U19國家足球隊參賽<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.scottishfa.co.uk/football_player_profile.cfm?page=106&playerID=115489&squadID=9 | title=James Keatings – U19 Squad | publisher=Scottish Football Association | accessdate=3 February 2012}}</ref>。
