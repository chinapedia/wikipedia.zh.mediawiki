'''草酸铅'''是[[铅|铅]](II)的[[草酸盐|草酸盐]]，化学式为PbC<sub>2</sub>O<sub>4</sub>。它是白色重质固体。<ref>"Lead Oxalate". American Elements: The World's Manufacturer of Engineered & Advanced Materials. http://www.americanelements.com/pboxl.html.</ref>

==制备==
该化合物有市售品。它可以通过[[硝酸铅|硝酸铅]]和[[草酸钠|草酸钠]]的[[复分解反应|复分解反应]]制备：<ref>{{cite journal | author = Grases, F. | title = Studies on Lead Oxalate Crystalline Growth | doi = 10.1006/jcis.1993.1035 | journal = Journal of Colloid and Interface Science | volume = 155 | pages = 265–270 | year = 1993 | last2 = Ruiz | first2 = J. | last3 = Costa-Bauzá | first3 = A. | issue = 2}}</ref>
:Pb<sup>2+</sup>(aq) + C<sub>2</sub>O<sub>4</sub><sup>2−</sup> → PbC<sub>2</sub>O<sub>4</sub> (s)

==溶解性==
草酸铅在水中的溶解度很小，但是在过量的[[草酸根|草酸根]]离子存在下，由于形成了Pb(C<sub>2</sub>O<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub><sup>2−</sup>配离子，溶解度增大。<ref>{{cite journal | author = Kolthoff, I.M. | title = The Solubility of lead Sulfate and of Lead Oxalate in Various Media | doi = 10.1021/j150419a004 | year = 1942 | last2 = Perlich | first2 = R. W. | last3 = Weiblen | first3 = D. | journal = Journal of Physical Chemistry | volume = 46 | issue = 5 | pages = 561}}</ref>
