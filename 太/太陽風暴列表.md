[[太陽風暴|太陽風暴]]是太陽引起的干，通常與來自[[太陽黑子|太陽黑子]]活動區的[[太陽閃焰|太陽閃焰]]引起的[[日冕雲|日冕雲]]和[[日冕大量拋射|日冕大量拋射]]相關聯，也有少數與[[日冕洞|日冕洞]]關。大多數活著的恆星都會對[[太陽物理學|太陽物理學]]領域研究的[[太空天氣|太空天氣]]產生干擾；這是與許多領域，像是[[恆星天文學|恆星天文學]]和[[行星科學|行星科學]]結合的一個學門。在[[太陽系|太陽系]]，[[太陽|太陽]]可以產生劇烈的[[磁暴|地磁]]和[[太陽質子事件|質子]]風暴，對無線電通訊設施衛星通訊和相關太空科技（包括[[全球衛星定位系統|全球衛星定位系統]]）的暫時或永久的損壞，使廣大區域的電力供應中斷造成[[停電|停電]]。強烈的太陽風暴也可能危害在高緯度、高海拔飛航的飛機與載人太空航具<ref>{{cite web |last = Phillips |first = Tony |title = Severe Space Weather--Social and Economic Impacts |work = NASA Science News |publisher = National Aeronautics and Space Administration |date = 21 Jan 2009 |url = http://science.nasa.gov/science-news/science-at-nasa/2009/21jan_severespaceweather/ |accessdate = 2014-05-07 |archiveurl = |archivedate = }}</ref>[[磁暴|磁暴]]是[[極光|極光]]現象發生的原因<ref>{{cite web |title = NOAA Space Weather Scales |publisher = NOAA Space Weather Prediction Center |date = 1 Mar 2005 |url = http://www.swpc.noaa.gov/NOAAscales/ |accessdate = 2014-05-07 |archiveurl = |archivedate = }}</ref>。已知最強烈的太陽風暴發生在1859年9月，稱為[[1859年太陽風暴|卡林頓事件]]<ref>{{cite web |last = Bell |first = Trudy E. |author2=T. Phillips |title = A Super Solar Flare |work = NASA Science News |publisher = National Aeronautics and Space Administration |date = 6 May 2008 |url = http://science.nasa.gov/science-news/science-at-nasa/2008/06may_carringtonflare/ |accessdate = 2014-05-07 |archiveurl = |archivedate = }}</ref>。最強大的太陽風暴造成的損失，對現代人類的文明構成極大的威脅<ref>{{cite book |last = Kappenman |first = John |title = Geomagnetic Storms and Their Impacts on the U.S. Power Grid |publisher = Metatech Corporation for Oak Ridge National Laboratory |series = META-R |volume = 319 |date = 2010 |location = Goleta, CA |url = http://www.ornl.gov/sci/ees/etsd/pes/pubs/ferc_Meta-R-319.pdf |oclc = 811858155 |deadurl = yes |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20130310230427/http://www.ornl.gov/sci/ees/etsd/pes/pubs/ferc_Meta-R-319.pdf |archivedate = 2013-03-10 }}</ref>。
