'''瓦氏效應'''（{{lang-en|Warburg effect}}），被用于两个不相关[[生物化学|生物化学]]的观测，一个在[[植物生理学|植物生理学]]中而另一个在[[肿瘤学|肿瘤学]]中，都是由于诺贝尔奖获得者[[奧托·海因里希·瓦爾堡|瓦爾堡]]所提出。

== 植物生理学 ==
在[[植物生理学|植物生理学]]中，瓦氏效应是指在高氧浓度下[[光合作用|光合作用]]出现减少<ref name="pmid13923215">{{cite journal | author = Turner JS, Brettain EG | title = Oxygen as a factor in photosynthesis | journal = Biol Rev Camb Philos Soc | volume = 37 | issue =  | pages = 130–70 | date = February 1962 | pmid = 13923215 | doi = 10.1111/j.1469-185X.1962.tb01607.x | url = ftp://171.66.68.104/pub/joeberry/Ref_Links_2.html/Biological%20Reviews%201962%20Turner.pdf }}{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref name="isbn0124316085">{{cite book | author = Zelitch I | title = Photosynthesis, Photorespiration, and Plant Productivity | publisher = Academic Press | location = New York | year = 1971 | pages = 253–255 | chapter = Chapter 8, Section E: Inhibition by O<sub>2</sub> (The Warburg Effect) | isbn = 0124316085 |chapterurl = http://books.google.com/books?id=kej9jDg5ZogC&lpg=PP1&dq=Photosynthesis%2C%20Photorespiration%2C%20and%20Plant%20Productivity&pg=PA253#v=onepage&q=Photosynthesis,%20Photorespiration,%20and%20Plant%20Productivity&f=false }}</ref> 。[[核酮糖-1,5-二磷酸羧化酶/加氧酶|RuBisCO]]能启动光合作用，而氧气是二氧化碳{{le|碳固定|Carbon fixation}}RuBisCO的竞争性抑制剂。另外，氧气刺激[[光呼吸|光呼吸]]从而降低光合作用产量。这两个机制一起导致了瓦氏效应<ref name="isbn3-540-58016-6">{{cite book | author = Schopfer P, Mohr H | title = Plant physiology | publisher = Springer | location = Berlin | year = 1995 | pages = 236–237 | isbn = 3-540-58016-6 | chapter = The leaf as a photosynthetic system | chapterurl = http://books.google.com/books?id=jTxfMQg38B4C&lpg=PA236&ots=5IiQLoTiSk&dq=RuBisCO%20warburg%20effect&pg=PA236#v=onepage&q=RuBisCO%20warburg%20effect&f=false }}</ref>。

== 肿瘤学 ==
{{medical}}
[[奧托·海因里希·瓦爾堡|瓦爾堡]]所提出的理論，認為癌細胞的生長速度遠大於正常細胞的原因來自於产生能量（例如[[三磷酸腺苷|ATP]]）的來源差別。癌細胞會偏向使用[[糖解作用|糖解作用]]取代一般正常細胞的有氧循環，所以癌細胞使用[[線粒體|線粒體]]的方式與正常細胞就會有所不同。而癌細胞主要使用糖解作用取代有氧循環的現象，就稱作瓦氏效應<ref>{{cite journal |author=Gatenby RA, Gillies RJ |title=Why do cancers have high aerobic glycolysis? |journal=Nat. Rev. Cancer |volume=4 |issue=11 |pages=891–9, 2004 |pmid=15516961 |doi=10.1038/nrc1478 |url=}}</ref>。這樣的現象也引起研究者的好奇，是否能藉由導引細胞恢復正常有氧循環，切斷癌細胞的能量供應來阻止癌細胞生長<ref name="pmid17426345">{{cite journal | author = Pan JG, Mak TW | title = Metabolic targeting as an anticancer strategy: dawn of a new era? | journal = Sci. STKE | volume = 2007 | issue = 381 | pages = pe14 , 2007 |pmid=17426345 | doi = 10.1126/stke.3812007pe14 | issn = }}</ref>，所以線粒體以及調控有氧循環與糖解作用間的過程也一直是癌症研究的重要問題<ref>{{cite journal | author = H. Pelicano, D. S. Martin, R. H. Xu and P. Huang | title = Glycolysis inhibition for anticancer treatment| journal = Oncogene | volume = 25 | issue = 34 | pages = 4633–4646, 2006 | doi = 10.1038/sj.onc.1209597}}</ref>。
