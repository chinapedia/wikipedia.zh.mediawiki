'''瓦洛厄河'''({{lang|en|Wallowa River}})位於[[美國|美國]][[奧勒岡州|奧勒岡州]]東北部，為[[格蘭德隆德河|格蘭德隆德河]](Grande Ronde River)的一個支流，大約 {{convert|55|mi|km}} 長。它在[[瓦洛厄山脈|瓦洛厄山脈]]的北部，[[哥倫比亞高原|哥倫比亞高原]](Columbia Plateau)上沖刷出一個山谷。

==地形==
此範圍由山脈東部的瓦洛厄河及其支流，位於山脈西部的[[彌念河|彌念河]](Minam River)所形成。而[[尹那哈河|尹那哈河]](Imnaha River)則從山脈的東部流入會合。

此區域的最高點為[[薩卡賈維亞峰|薩卡賈維亞峰]]，在海拔{{convert|9838|ft|m|abbr=off}}的位置。<ref>{{cite web | title = Elevations of Points Near Eagle Cap Wilderness | publisher = U.S. Forest Service | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20050321002445/http://www.fs.fed.us/r6/w-w/recreation/wilderness/elevations-ecw.shtml | url = http://www.fs.fed.us/r6/w-w/recreation/wilderness/elevations-ecw.shtml | archivedate = 2005年3月21日 | deadurl = yes }}</ref>薩卡賈維亞峰是奧勒岡的第六高山峰，也是在本州中，除了喀斯喀特山脈()以外的第一高峰。<ref>Engeman, Richard H. (2009). ''The Oregon Companion: A Historical Gazetteer of the Useful, the Curious, and the Arcane''. Portland, Oregon: Timber Press. p. 393. ISBN 0-88192-899-2. {{OCLC|236142647}}.</ref>

==地質==
[[File:Lostine_River_valley.jpg|thumb]](Lostine River)]]

許多[[地質學家|地質學家]]相信在奧勒岡州東北部的瓦洛厄山脈是[[島嶼地帶|島嶼地帶]]( Insular Belt)分離的碎塊。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.washington.edu/burkemuseum/geo_history_wa/Coast%20Range%20Episode.htm |title=The Coast Range Episode |accessdate=25 May 2008 |publisher=The Burke Museum of Natural History and Culture |year=2006 }}</ref> 瓦洛厄的中心部分由瓦洛厄岩基環繞著[[哥倫比亞河|哥倫比亞河]]玄武岩群所組成。<ref name = Krauskopf1943>{{cite journal|last = Krauskopf | first = K.B.| year = 1943| journal = American Journal of Science| volume = 241| issue = 10 | pages = 607–628|url = http://www.ajsonline.org/content/241/10/607.full.pdf+html | accessdate = 16 October 2012|doi = 10.2475/ajs.241.10.607|title = The Wallowa Batholith}}</ref>此外，在岩基和玄武岩層之間，也有許多較老的岩層。<ref name = USGSCVO2004>{{cite web | title = America's Volcanic Past - Oregon | publisher = United States Geological Survey | date = 26 October 2004 | first = Lyn | last = Topinka | url = http://vulcan.wr.usgs.gov/LivingWith/VolcanicPast/Places/volcanic_past_oregon.html | accessdate = 18 November 2007}}</ref>

瓦洛厄岩基是在[[侏儸紀|侏儸紀]]晚期和[[白堊紀|白堊紀]]早期（大約在一億六千萬年前和一億兩千萬年前）之間，由往上湧出的岩漿所造出的花崗岩所組成。<ref name = USGSCVO2004/> 此岩石的配置，在熱帶海時期引起地層表面的上升。這可由在山脈中所出現的板岩、石英岩及大理石得到證明；這些岩石是在三疊紀時期，由海洋沉積物所生成的岩石變形後所產生。<ref name = Warren1928>{{ cite journal | title = Physical and Economic Geography of Oregon: Chapter XIII The Wallowa Mountains and County | last = Smith | first = Warren | journal = "The Commonwealth Review of the University of Oregon" | volume = 10 | issue = 1 |date=January 1928 | url = http://ir.library.oregonstate.edu/xmlui/bitstream/handle/1957/12051/Phy_Eco_Geo.pdf?sequence=1 | format = PDF | accessdate = 22 March 2012 }}</ref> 在岩基的中心部分為花崗閃長岩，而其外圍的邊界地區則為石英閃長岩。<ref name=West1991>{{cite conference | title = The Origin of the Mafic Enclaves within the Wallowa Batholith, Oregon | last = West | first = Andrew | conference = 4th Keck Geology Consortium Undergraduate Research Symposium | location = Franklin and Marshall College | year = 1991 | url = http://keckgeology.org/node/1 | accessdate = 22 March 2012 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120309123409/http://keckgeology.org/node/1 | archivedate = 2012年3月9日 }}</ref> 花崗岩岩基由深溝所切斷， <ref name = Mbalu1991>{{cite conference | title = Contact Metamorphic Effects of the Wallowa Batholith on the Hurwal Formation, Wallowa Mountains, Oregon | last = Mbalue | first = Chuma | conference = 4th Keck Geology Consortium Undergraduate Research Symposium | location = Franklin and Marshall College | year = 1991 | url = http://keckgeology.org/files/pdf/symvol/4th/Oregon/mbalu.pdf | format = PDF | accessdate = 22 March 2012}}</ref>但這種情形很少見。<ref name = West1991/>

在[[更新世|更新世]]晚期，由於[[冰河|冰河]]的擠壓而塑造了一些山谷出來，而且也留下了一些[[冰磧石|冰磧石]]。<ref>{{cite journal | title = Varies climatic and topographic influences on the Late Pleistocene mountain glaciation in the western United State | last = Thackray | first = Glenn | journal = Journal of Quaternary Science | year = 2008 | volume = 23 | issue = 6–7 | pages = 671–681 | url = http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/jqs.1210/pdf | format = PDF | accessdate = 22 March 2012 | doi=10.1002/jqs.1210}}</ref> 這一系列的冰磧石是由長時間的[[冰河推進|冰河推進]]和後退所形成。[[冰川沉積物|冰川沉積物]]也是這些山谷的共同特徵。<ref name=Budlong2005>{{cite journal | title = The Bathymetry and Sediments of Wallowa Lake, Northeast Oregon | last1 = Budlong | first1 = Bryce | last2 = Collier | last3 = Davis | first3 = Calvin | last4 = Gilbert | first4 = David | last5 = Legerwood | first5 = Rob | last6 = Tassel | first6 = Jay | journal = Eastern Oregon Geology | volume = 2 | date = September 2005 | url = http://www3.eou.edu/geology/easternoregongeology.html | accessdate = 22 March 2012 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20060909220012/http://www3.eou.edu/geology/easternoregongeology.html | archivedate = 2006年9月9日 }}</ref> 冰河的溶解產生了相當大量的水，而這些水將沉澱物質沖刷到山谷的底部，形成了一層一層的沉積物，新的覆蓋在舊的沉積物之上面。<ref name=spokanecc>{{cite web|url=http://users.scc.spokane.edu/ABuddington/g210spr02/g3/geology.htm|title=General Geology|date=Spring 2002|publisher=Spokane Community College|work=Wallowa Lake|first1=Logan|last1=Ditto|first2=Bryan|last2=Donaldson|accessdate=9 September 2013}}</ref>

==氣候==
在山脈中，大部分的降水都是以[[雪|雪]]的型態落下，降雪的尖峰時期為每年三月附近。<ref name="Rheinheimer2007">{{cite web | title = Hydrological and ecologic impacts of climate change in the Grande Ronde River Basin | last = Rheinheimer | first = David | year = 2007 | url = https://www.geology.ucdavis.edu/shlemoncourse/trips/GrandeRonde/backgroundpapers/Rheinheimer.pdf | accessdate = 22 March 2012 | format = PDF | publisher = University of California, Davis }}{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref> 大部分的降水都是屬於[[地形降水|地形降水]]。在山頂附近每年的降水量可以超過2500毫米，而在山谷中，則可以低到每年只有250毫米。<ref name=TaylorHale>{{cite web | title = Climate of Wallowa County | last1 = Taylor | first1 = George | last2 = Hale | first2 = Candee | last3 = Joos | first3 = Sarah | url = http://www.ocs.oregonstate.edu/county_climate/Wallowa_files/Wallowa.html | work = Oregon Climate Service | publisher = Oregon State University | accessdate = 22 March 2012 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20100611213019/http://www.ocs.oregonstate.edu/county_climate/Wallowa_files/Wallowa.html | archivedate = 2010年6月11日 }}</ref>
