'''瓦莱丽·让·索拉纳斯'''（{{lang-en|'''Valerie Jean Solanas'''}}，{{bd|1936年|4月9日|1988年|4月25日|catIdx=S}}）是一位美国[[激進女性主義|激進女性主義]]作家，曾经试图暗杀艺术家[[安迪·沃霍尔|安迪·沃霍尔]]。

==早期生活==
1936年索拉纳斯出生于新泽西州<ref>State of California. California Death Index, 1940–1997. Sacramento, CA, USA: State of California Department of Health Services, Center for Health Statistics.</ref>，十几岁时父母离婚。她的母亲和继父结婚后<ref>{{Cite book|last=Solanas|first=Valerie|title=SCUM Manifesto|year=1996|publisher=AK Press|location=San Francisco|page=48|isbn=1-873176-44-9}}</ref>，她被送到祖父母那里。她的祖父酗酒还虐待她，她逃离祖父家开始无家可归<ref>{{Cite book|last=Buchanan|first=Paul D.|title=Radical Feminists: A Guide to an American Subculture|publisher=Greenwood|location=Santa Barbara, Calif.|isbn=1-59884-356-7|page=132}}</ref>。在20世纪50年代她[[出柜|出柜]]承认自己是一个女同性恋<ref name=Heller2001>{{Cite journal|last=Heller|first=Dana|title=Shooting Solanas: Radical Feminist History and the Technology of Failure|journal=Feminist Studies|date=Spring 2001|volume=27|issue=1|pages=167–189|url=http://www.jstor.org/stable/3178456|doi=10.2307/3178456}}</ref>。她毕业于[[馬里蘭大學學院市分校|馬里蘭大學學院市分校]]，获得[[心理学|心理学]]学士学位<ref name="HesfordDiedrich2010">{{Cite book|author1=Victoria Hesford|author2=Lisa Diedrich|title=Feminist Time Against Nation Time: Gender, Politics, and the Nation-State in an Age of Permanent War|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=Y4KHFSSEZ2gC&pg=PA154|accessdate=2011-11-25|date=2010-02-28|publisher=Rowman & Littlefield|isbn=978-0-7391-4428-2|page=154}}</ref><ref>Regarding the honor society: Jansen, Sharon L., ''Reading Women's Worlds from Christine de Pizan to Doris Lessing'', ''op. cit.'' (2011), p. 152.</ref>。后来搬到[[伯克利_(加利福尼亞州)|伯克利]]。在那里，她开始创作她最著名的作品《SCUM Manifesto》，作品中呼吁女性“推翻政府，消除金钱系统，机构完全自动化，消灭男性”。<ref>Solanas, Valerie, ''SCUM Manifesto'' (Valerie Solanas, 1967), p. [1] (self-published) (copy from Northwestern Univ.).</ref><ref>DeMonte, Alexandra (2010). "Feminism: Second-Wave". In Chapman, Roger (ed). ''Culture Wars: An Encyclopedia of Issues, Viewpoints, and Voices''. Armonk, N.Y.: M.E. Sharpe, p. 178, ISBN 978-1-84972-713-6.</ref>

==刺杀安迪·沃霍尔==
在60年代中期索拉纳斯以作家身份搬到纽约市<ref name=Heller2001/><ref name="Hamilton2002">{{Cite book|author=Neil A. Hamilton|title=Rebels and renegades: a chronology of social and political dissent in the United States|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=jZymqT1HmqAC&pg=PA264|accessdate=2011-11-25|year=2002|publisher=Taylor & Francis|isbn=978-0-415-93639-2|pages=264–}}</ref>。在那里她遇到了安迪·沃霍尔，并要求沃霍尔改编她的戏<ref name="Nickels2005C" />。她把自己的剧本给沃霍尔，后来她指控他丢失/偷窃了剧本，其次是沃霍尔额外漠视她的活动。后来索拉纳斯要求对脚本损失进行经济赔偿，沃霍尔雇用她在他的电影中演出，并付给她25美元。<ref name="Nickels2005C">{{Cite book|author=Thom Nickels|title=Out in History: Collected Essays|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=7NIjDI9uiEEC&pg=PA15|accessdate=2011-11-27|date=2005-11-01|publisher=STARbooks Press|isbn=978-1-891855-58-0|pages=15–16}}</ref>

1967年，索拉纳斯开始自己出版《SCUM Manifesto》。奥林匹亚出版社老板Maurice Girodias同意出版索拉纳斯以后的作品，她理解的合同意味着Girodias将拥有自己的作品<ref name="BaerAbt-p51">Baer, Freddie, compiler, ''About Valerie Solanas'', ''op. cit.'' (© 1996), p. 51.</ref>。她深信Girodias和沃霍尔密谋窃取她的作品，索拉纳斯在1968年的春天购买了枪<ref>Published in 1996: Baer, Freddie, compiler, ''About Valerie Solanas'', ''op. cit.'', pp. 51–52.</ref><ref>Published in 2004: Baer, Freddie, ''Solanas, Valerie, ''SCUM Manifesto ''(subchap. of ''Fists in the Air'')'', in ''The Outlaw Bible of American Literature'', ''op. cit.''</ref>。6月3日，她试图找到Girodias，但他因为周末已经离开。然后她去了工厂，在那里她发现沃霍尔。她朝沃霍尔枪击三次，前两次没有击中，最后打中沃霍尔<ref name="KaufmanRosset2004C-p203">Kaufman, Alan, ''et al.'', ''The Outlaw Bible of American Literature'', ''op. cit.'', p. 203.</ref>。她还枪击了艺术评论家Mario Amaya，并试图打中沃霍尔的经理Fred Hughes，但枪卡住了<ref name="Harding2010C">{{Cite book|author=James Martin Harding|title=Cutting performances: collage events, feminist artists, and the American avant-garde|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=IX8kznQvH54C&pg=PA151|accessdate=2011-11-27|date=2010-02-25|publisher=University of Michigan Press|isbn=978-0-472-11718-5|pages=151–173}}</ref>。沃霍尔受了重伤，随后因手术成功而获救。索拉纳斯后来自己向警方报案自首<ref>Baer, Freddie, compiler, ''About Valerie Solanas'', ''op. cit.''(© 1996), p. 53.</ref>。她被控企图谋杀，殴打并非法藏有一支枪。她被诊断为[[偏狂型精神分裂症|偏狂型精神分裂症]]<ref name=Watson35>{{Cite book|last=Watson|first=Steven|title=Factory made: Warhol and the sixties|year=2003|publisher=Pantheon Books|location=New York|isbn=0-679-42372-9|edition=1st|pages=35–36}}</ref>，并承认“鲁莽伤人意图伤害”<ref name=Jansen153>{{Cite book|last=Jansen|first=Sharon L.|title=Reading women's worlds from Christine de Pizan to Doris Lessing|publisher=Palgrave Macmillan|location=New York|isbn=0-230-11066-5|edition=1st|page=153}}</ref><ref name=AKPress55>{{Cite book|last=Solanas|first=Valerie|title=SCUM manifesto|year=1996|publisher=AK Press|location=San Francisco, CA|isbn=1-873176-44-9|page=55}}</ref>，被判三年徒刑，其中包括在精神病院的一年时间<ref name=Jansen153/><ref name=AKPress55/>。她出狱后，继续推动《SCUM Manifesto》。

==去世==
她于1988年在旧金山死于[[肺炎|肺炎]]。<ref>{{Cite book|last=Watson|first=Steven|title=Factory Made: Warhol and the Sixties|publisher=Pantheon Books|year=2003|pages=425|isbn=0-679-42372-9}}</ref>
