'''柬埔寨人名'''由[[姓|姓]]和[[名|名]]组成，一般顺序为先姓后名（在一些西方史料中有颠倒顺序的习惯）。<ref name=huffman>[http://homepages.newnet.co.uk/dance/webpjd/intro/worldnamexa-g.htm "Naming systems of the world"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080423002014/http://homepages.newnet.co.uk/dance/webpjd/intro/worldnamexa-g.htm |date=2008-04-23 }} (self-published). Citing Huffman, Franklin Eugene. [http://books.google.com/books?id=NqqbGwAACAAJ ''Cambodian names and titles'']. Institute of Far Eastern Languages, [[Yale_University|Yale University]] (1968). [[OCLC|OCLC]] 20035170.</ref><ref name=short/><ref>Kershaw, Roger. ''Monarchy in South-East Asia: The Faces of Tradition in Transition''. Routledge (2001), [http://books.google.com/books?id=7GHpx6pTPlQC&pg=PR14&sig=rVqCKzRFHi6LWwd4Y_3MtoDq2I0 p xiv]. ISBN 0-415-18531-9.</ref>柬埔寨人名主要使用[[高棉文|高棉文]]，且每个名字都有相应的含义。柬埔寨人的姓氏多为父姓（也有以父名为姓的现象）<ref name=huffman/>，姓氏多为单音节词<ref name=fong>Mary Fong & Rueyling Chuang. ''Communicating Ethnic and Cultural Identity''. Rowman & Littlefield (2003), [http://books.google.com/books?id=lyy15odOFl0C&pg=PA40&sig=8_WAalpR0Va_WA-JrU18sawJILQ p40]. ISBN 0-7425-1739-X.</ref>，其中有许多[[汉姓|汉姓]]。柬埔寨高棉人常见的汉姓有：[[李|李]]、[[林|林]]。[[黄|黄]]、[[兴|兴]]、[[韩|韩]]、[[金|金]]、[[周|周]]、[[洪|洪]]、[[杨|杨]]等等。<ref name=fong/>柬埔寨女名常含美丽之意，而男名则多关于美德，亦有许多男女皆宜的名字。<ref name=struggling>Valerie Ooka Pang & Li-Rong Lilly Cheng. ''Struggling to Be Heard: The Unmet Needs of Asian Pacific American Children''. SUNY Press (1998), [http://books.google.com/books?id=wZyIYK1M1ikC&pg=PA51&sig=RUydq7WW5ZIfNUvTBz9VrOSgQxA p51]. [[OCLC|OCLC]] 10431338.</ref><ref>[http://www.kwintessential.co.uk/articles/cambodia/cambodian-names/1672 Cambodian names] The significance and beauty of Cambodian names</ref>柬埔寨人互相称呼对方时，多称呼对方的名而不是姓，正式场合上还会在名前添加前缀（多表尊敬之意）。<ref name=short>Short, Philip. ''Pol Pot: Anatomy of a Nightmare.'' Macmillan (2006), [http://books.google.com/books?id=XW24koscGMkC&pg=PR15&sig=d6viYwqeWrqU3O6VXdVbEvBxeXc p xv]. ISBN 0-8050-8006-6.</ref><ref name=struggling/>柬埔寨少数族群的命名习惯与[[高棉人|高棉人]]有所不同。佛教徒的名字常与佛教相关，[[穆斯林|穆斯林]]则常采用[[阿拉伯人名|阿拉伯姓氏]]。<ref name=huffman/>发音方面常重读名字<ref>[http://www.khmerinstitute.org/kinamelist.html Khmer Institute]</ref>。

== 常见姓氏列表 ==
由于高棉人也有以父名为姓的习惯，一些姓也会作为名使用，相反，一些名也会作为姓使用。而对于[[柬埔寨华人|柬埔寨华人]]和[[越南人|越南人]]则并非如此，他们有着子随父姓的习俗。因为柬埔寨华人移居柬埔寨已久，并与柬埔寨人互相通婚，故也有许多柬埔寨人用汉姓汉名，目前柬埔寨社会中多出现与流行取名方式：以父名为姓、汉姓柬名、柬姓汉名。<ref>[https://files.pbworks.com/download/UvRGDr1wV3/florin/13015381/what.language.does.he.speak.pdf What Language Does He Speak?] Asian Last Name Guide</ref>
