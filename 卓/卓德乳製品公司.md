'''卓德乳製品公司'''（{{lang-en|Zott}}）是一家於1926年在德国梅尔廷根成立的乳製品公司，生產種類包括{{link-en|加工乾酪|Processed cheese}}、[[甜品|甜點]]、[[鮮奶油|鮮奶油]]和[[酸奶|酸奶]]<ref>FoodBev: [http://www.foodbev.com/news/new-molding-line-for-zott-dairy#.UxbfK5IwfIU Company], March 5th 2014</ref><ref>Bloomberg: [http://investing.businessweek.com/research/stocks/private/snapshot.asp?privcapId=5504061 Company profile], March 5th 2014</ref><ref>Google Books: [https://books.google.com/books?id=iY8TAQAAMAAJ&q=zott+dairy&dq=zott+dairy&hl=en&sa=X&ei=mtkeU-b4D8qAywPnxYDIAw&ved=0CGUQ6AEwBw The European Directory of Consumer Brands and Their Owners], March 11th 2014</ref><ref>Google Books: [https://books.google.com/books?id=YS-NmbtN9TcC&pg=PR9&dq=zott+dairy&hl=en&sa=X&ei=mtkeU-b4D8qAywPnxYDIAw&ved=0CFgQ6AEwBQ#v=onepage&q=zott%20dairy&f=false Applied Evolutionary Economics and the Knowledge-based Economy], March 11th 2014</ref>。該公司主要工廠位於[[巴伐利亚|巴伐利亚]]，其他還有位於德國的[[金茨堡|金茨堡]]和[[波兰|波兰]]。
