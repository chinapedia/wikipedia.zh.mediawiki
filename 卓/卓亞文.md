'''[[拿督|拿督]]卓亞文''' ,SPMJ.，DPMJ.，（Dato' '''Toh Ah Boon''' 1860年- 1932年4月1日），為[[馬來西亞|馬來西亞]][[柔佛州|柔佛州]]著名企业家。

==簡介==
卓亞文，又名卓天文、卓文榮，原籍[[福建|福建]]南安，他經營食品、建築材料生意，為當時[[新山|新山]]擁有產業最多的一位華人，除了擁有房屋百餘間，尚有橡膠園千餘畝<ref>{{cite book|last=宋蘊璞|title=《南洋英屬海峽殖民地誌略》|year=1930}}</ref>。位於新山市中心直律街街頭至結尾的建築，至今絕大部分仍屬卓亞文家族所擁有<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chinapress.com.my/20161123/%E3%80%90%E4%BB%8A%E6%97%A5%E6%9F%94%E4%BD%9B%E9%A0%AD%E6%A2%9D%E3%80%91%E7%81%AB%E7%87%924%E9%96%93%E7%99%BE%E5%B9%B4%E8%80%81%E5%BA%97%E5%B1%8B%E2%80%A7%E7%B6%93%E5%B8%B8%E5%87%BA%E6%B2%92%E8%B6%95/|title=火燒4間百年老店屋‧經常出沒趕不走疑癮君子追龍引火患. 中國報. 2016年11月23日}}</ref>。柔佛蘇丹依布拉欣（Sultan Ibrahim）十分器重卓亞文，讓他承包了政府及民間建築工程<ref>{{cite book|last=吳華|title=《新山華族人物誌》|year=2006}}</ref>。他也獲委為宮庭委員會成員，柔佛蘇丹還封賜他為高級拿督（S.P.M.J）<ref>{{cite web|url=http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/newspapers/Digitised/Article.aspx?articleid=singfreepressb19320412-1.2.14|title=DATOH TOH AH BOON. The Singapore Free Press. 1932年4月12日}}</ref>。

==早期人生==
卓亞文，出生於新加坡汤申路棚戶區的小屋<ref>{{cite web|url=http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/newspapers/Digitised/Article.aspx?articleid=straitstimes19300226-1.2.36&sessionid=a3103c4cd4414e7ba97d0063abe1d866&keyword=%22Toh+Ah+Boon%22&search=advanced&fromdate=&todate=&articles=1&advertisements=1&illustrations=1&letters=1&obituaries=1&miscellaneous=1&newspaperTitles=&fuzzysearch=Off&fcontentType=ARTICLE&token=boon%2cah%2ctoh|title=DATO TOH AH BOON S.P.M.J.. The Straits Times. 1930年2月26日}}</ref>，為卓金水之子<ref>{{cite book|last=林博愛|title=《南洋名人集傳第二冊（下）》（页222）}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/newspapers/Digitised/Article.aspx?articleid=straitstimes19320402-1.2.69&sessionid=cf8db9ccbe9e46d8b7ee3129983a9859&keyword=%22Toh+Ah+Boon%22&search=advanced&fromdate=&todate=&articles=1&advertisements=1&illustrations=1&letters=1&obituaries=1&miscellaneous=1&newspaperTitles=&fuzzysearch=Off&ffromdate=19320101&ftodate=19321231&token=boon%2cah%2ctoh|title=DEATH OF DATO TOH AH BOON. The Straits Times. 1932年4月2日}}</ref>。他少时曾就读英校<ref>{{cite book|last=宋蘊璞|title=《南洋英屬海峽殖民地誌略》：《第一編．新加坡．第四章名人》（頁112）|year=1930}}</ref>，由于年少喪父，他便與擁有一間位於[[新加坡|新加坡]]阿拉伯街雜貨店的叔叔一起生活。 15歲他便陪叔叔到柔佛新山。叔叔開始了承包商的生意，但是過後便退休回[[中國|中國]]，而卓亞文便繼承了叔叔的承包商生意<ref>{{cite web|url=http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/newspapers/Digitised/Article.aspx?articleid=straitstimes19320402-1.2.69&sessionid=cf8db9ccbe9e46d8b7ee3129983a9859&keyword=%22Toh+Ah+Boon%22&search=advanced&fromdate=&todate=&articles=1&advertisements=1&illustrations=1&letters=1&obituaries=1&miscellaneous=1&newspaperTitles=&fuzzysearch=Off&ffromdate=19320101&ftodate=19321231&token=boon%2cah%2ctoh|title=DEATH OF DATO TOH AH BOON. The Straits Times. 1932年4月2日}}</ref>。

他使​​用他生意的利潤投資了有地地產，他也收購了一個位於那那島（Pulau Nanas諧音）的花崗岩礦場來提供花崗岩給柔佛政府和新加坡和柔佛承包商。卓亞文也涉足在新山浦萊山和士姑來種植木薯和橡膠<ref>{{cite web|url=http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/newspapers/Digitised/Article.aspx?articleid=straitstimes19320402-1.2.69&sessionid=cf8db9ccbe9e46d8b7ee3129983a9859&keyword=%22Toh+Ah+Boon%22&search=advanced&fromdate=&todate=&articles=1&advertisements=1&illustrations=1&letters=1&obituaries=1&miscellaneous=1&newspaperTitles=&fuzzysearch=Off&ffromdate=19320101&ftodate=19321231&token=boon%2cah%2ctoh|title=DEATH OF DATO TOH AH BOON. The Straits Times. 1932年4月2日}}</ref>。

1915年，年屆55歲的卓亞文退休，生意則有兒子卓錦成和卓紅毛接管<ref>{{cite book|last=林博愛|title=《南洋名人集傳第二冊（下）》（页223）}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/newspapers/Digitised/Article.aspx?articleid=straitstimes19320402-1.2.69&sessionid=cf8db9ccbe9e46d8b7ee3129983a9859&keyword=%22Toh+Ah+Boon%22&search=advanced&fromdate=&todate=&articles=1&advertisements=1&illustrations=1&letters=1&obituaries=1&miscellaneous=1&newspaperTitles=&fuzzysearch=Off&ffromdate=19320101&ftodate=19321231&token=boon%2cah%2ctoh|title=DEATH OF DATO TOH AH BOON. The Straits Times. 1932年4月2日}}</ref>。

==公共服務==
卓亞文非常活躍於柔佛州事務，尤其是教育事務。1926年9月份，他大方捐出2萬元成立卓亞文獎學金供出生於柔佛的馬來族或華族學生能去醫學學院或萊佛士學院升造<ref>{{cite web|url=http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/newspapers/Digitised/Article.aspx?articleid=singfreepressb19260902-1.2.55&sessionid=7deab9d3260f414c8fac74ae709f71c4&keyword=%22Toh+Ah+Boon%22&search=advanced&fromdate=&todate=&articles=1&advertisements=1&illustrations=1&letters=1&obituaries=1&miscellaneous=1&newspaperTitles=&fuzzysearch=Off&fcontentType=ARTICLE&token=boon%2cah%2ctoh|title=Untitled. The Singapore Free Press. 1926年9月2日}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/newspapers/Digitised/Article.aspx?articleid=straitstimes19320402-1.2.69&sessionid=cf8db9ccbe9e46d8b7ee3129983a9859&keyword=%22Toh+Ah+Boon%22&search=advanced&fromdate=&todate=&articles=1&advertisements=1&illustrations=1&letters=1&obituaries=1&miscellaneous=1&newspaperTitles=&fuzzysearch=Off&ffromdate=19320101&ftodate=19321231&token=boon%2cah%2ctoh|title=DEATH OF DATO TOH AH BOON. The Straits Times. 1932年4月2日}}</ref>。

当[[柔佛御林军|柔佛御林军]]建议扩建其射擊場，拥有与建议地点连接土地的卓亚文不仅大方捐出该土地予柔佛御林軍之外，还额外捐出2万元建造射擊台<ref>{{cite web|url=http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/newspapers/Digitised/Article.aspx?articleid=straitstimes19320402-1.2.69&sessionid=cf8db9ccbe9e46d8b7ee3129983a9859&keyword=%22Toh+Ah+Boon%22&search=advanced&fromdate=&todate=&articles=1&advertisements=1&illustrations=1&letters=1&obituaries=1&miscellaneous=1&newspaperTitles=&fuzzysearch=Off&ffromdate=19320101&ftodate=19321231&token=boon%2cah%2ctoh|title=DEATH OF DATO TOH AH BOON. The Straits Times. 1932年4月2日}}</ref>。

他也於1919年對於萊佛士學院的成立而捐出1萬元<ref>{{cite web|url=http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/newspapers/Digitised/Article.aspx?articleid=straitstimes19191202-1.2.68&sessionid=5f74c961db11486dafcd403dd87b2cb8&keyword=%22Toh+Ah+Boon%22&search=advanced&fromdate=&todate=&articles=1&advertisements=1&illustrations=1&letters=1&obituaries=1&miscellaneous=1&newspaperTitles=&fuzzysearch=Off&fcontentType=ARTICLE&token=boon%2cah%2ctoh|title=RAFFLES COLLEGE. The Straits Times. 1919年12月2日}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/newspapers/Digitised/Article.aspx?articleid=straitstimes19320402-1.2.69&sessionid=cf8db9ccbe9e46d8b7ee3129983a9859&keyword=%22Toh+Ah+Boon%22&search=advanced&fromdate=&todate=&articles=1&advertisements=1&illustrations=1&letters=1&obituaries=1&miscellaneous=1&newspaperTitles=&fuzzysearch=Off&ffromdate=19320101&ftodate=19321231&token=boon%2cah%2ctoh|title=DEATH OF DATO TOH AH BOON. The Straits Times. 1932年4月2日}}</ref>。

卓亞文於1925年一月份分別捐給[[英華中學_(自主)|新加坡英華學校]]與新加坡女子學校各200元作為學校新校舍建築費用<ref>{{cite web|url=http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/newspapers/Digitised/Article.aspx?articleid=straitstimes19250102-1.2.53&sessionid=f3f7b455f5f5459e8679b37ff3a7eb5a&keyword=%22Toh+Ah+Boon%22&search=advanced&fromdate=&todate=&articles=1&advertisements=1&illustrations=1&letters=1&obituaries=1&miscellaneous=1&newspaperTitles=&fuzzysearch=Off&ffromdate=19250101&ftodate=19251231&token=boon%2cah%2ctoh|title=ANGLO-CHINESE SCHOOL. The Straits Times. 1925年1月2日}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/newspapers/Digitised/Article.aspx?articleid=straitstimes19250121-1.2.87&sessionid=f3f7b455f5f5459e8679b37ff3a7eb5a&keyword=%22Toh+Ah+Boon%22&search=advanced&fromdate=&todate=&articles=1&advertisements=1&illustrations=1&letters=1&obituaries=1&miscellaneous=1&newspaperTitles=&fuzzysearch=Off&ffromdate=19250101&ftodate=19251231&token=boon%2cah%2ctoh|title=CHINESE GIRLS' SCHOOL. The Straits Times. 1925年1月21日}}</ref>。

卓亞文自1929年4月起擔任柔佛議政局議員<ref>{{cite web|url=http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/newspapers/Digitised/Article.aspx?articleid=straitstimes19290418-1.2.57&sessionid=c3e721ddf76f4827a8fd7c7a365eec55&keyword=%22Toh+Ah+Boon%22&search=advanced&fromdate=&todate=&articles=1&advertisements=1&illustrations=1&letters=1&obituaries=1&miscellaneous=1&newspaperTitles=&fuzzysearch=Off&ffromdate=19290101&ftodate=19291231&token=boon%2cah%2ctoh|title=JOHORE STATE COUNCIL. The Straits Times. 1929年4月18日}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/newspapers/Digitised/Article.aspx?articleid=singfreepressb19310407-1.2.34&sessionid=a3103c4cd4414e7ba97d0063abe1d866&keyword=%22Toh+Ah+Boon%22&search=advanced&fromdate=&todate=&articles=1&advertisements=1&illustrations=1&letters=1&obituaries=1&miscellaneous=1&newspaperTitles=&fuzzysearch=Off&fcontentType=ARTICLE&token=boon%2cah%2ctoh|title=Untitled. The Singapore Free Press. 1931年4月7日}}</ref>以及自1927年12月份起擔任的柔佛預防瘧疾局委員<ref>{{cite web|url=http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/newspapers/Digitised/Article.aspx?articleid=singfreepressb19271230-1.2.57&sessionid=7deab9d3260f414c8fac74ae709f71c4&keyword=%22Toh+Ah+Boon%22&search=advanced&fromdate=&todate=&articles=1&advertisements=1&illustrations=1&letters=1&obituaries=1&miscellaneous=1&newspaperTitles=&fuzzysearch=Off&fcontentType=ARTICLE&token=boon%2cah%2ctoh|title=ANTI-MALARIA WORK IN JOHORE. The Singapore Free Press.  1927年12月30日}}</ref>，亦是一屆柔佛新山華僑公所主席。他也曾於市議會擔任要職<ref>{{cite web|url=http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/newspapers/Digitised/Article.aspx?articleid=straitstimes19320402-1.2.69&sessionid=cf8db9ccbe9e46d8b7ee3129983a9859&keyword=%22Toh+Ah+Boon%22&search=advanced&fromdate=&todate=&articles=1&advertisements=1&illustrations=1&letters=1&obituaries=1&miscellaneous=1&newspaperTitles=&fuzzysearch=Off&ffromdate=19320101&ftodate=19321231&token=boon%2cah%2ctoh|title=DEATH OF DATO TOH AH BOON. The Straits Times. 1932年4月2日}}</ref>。

[[新山|新山]]市[[淡杯|淡杯]]的拿督卓亞文路是以他的名字來命名，以紀念他對柔佛州巨大的貢獻。

==逝世==
年屆75歲的卓亞文於1932年4月1日逝世於其住家亦是其店舖的“文榮號”，並於1932年4月9日出殯，葬於位於義興路尾的新山中華墓園<ref>{{cite web|url=http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/newspapers/Digitised/Article.aspx?articleid=singfreepressb19320412-1.2.14|title=DATOH TOH AH BOON. The Singapore Free Press. 1932年4月12日}}</ref>。留有7子3女<ref>{{cite web|url=http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/newspapers/Digitised/Article.aspx?articleid=singfreepressb19320404-1.2.40.2&sessionid=cf8db9ccbe9e46d8b7ee3129983a9859&keyword=%22Toh+Ah+Boon%22&search=advanced&fromdate=&todate=&articles=1&advertisements=1&illustrations=1&letters=1&obituaries=1&miscellaneous=1&newspaperTitles=&fuzzysearch=Off&ffromdate=19320101&ftodate=19321231&token=boon%2cah%2ctoh|title=DEATH HON. TOH AH BOON. The Singapore Free Press. 1932年4月4日}}</ref>。其三子卓錦漢（G. H. Toh）畢業於英國[[牛津大學布雷齊諾斯學院|牛津大學布雷齊諾斯學院]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/newspapers/Digitised/Article.aspx?articleid=straitstimes19281011-1.2.34&sessionid=7deab9d3260f414c8fac74ae709f71c4&keyword=%22Toh+Ah+Boon%22&search=advanced&fromdate=&todate=&articles=1&advertisements=1&illustrations=1&letters=1&obituaries=1&miscellaneous=1&newspaperTitles=&fuzzysearch=Off&fcontentType=ARTICLE&token=boon%2cah%2ctoh|title=Untitled. The Straits Times. 1928年10月11日}}</ref>，是英國倫敦四所律師學院之一的[[中殿律師學院|中殿律師學院]]的[[大律師|訟務律師]]。其四子卓錦丕和五子卓錦靜在英國留學<ref>{{cite book|last=林博愛|title=《南洋名人集傳第二冊（下）》（页223）}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/newspapers/Digitised/Article.aspx?articleid=straitstimes19320402-1.2.69&sessionid=cf8db9ccbe9e46d8b7ee3129983a9859&keyword=%22Toh+Ah+Boon%22&search=advanced&fromdate=&todate=&articles=1&advertisements=1&illustrations=1&letters=1&obituaries=1&miscellaneous=1&newspaperTitles=&fuzzysearch=Off&ffromdate=19320101&ftodate=19321231&token=boon%2cah%2ctoh|title=DEATH OF DATO TOH AH BOON.
