'''萊罕姆·罕'''（{{lang-ur|ریحام خان}}，{{lang-en|Reham Khan}}，{{bd|1973年|4月3日|catIdx=Khan, Reham}}）<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.pakistantribune.com.pk/29003/imran-khan-marries-reham-khan-despite-opposition-family.html |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2015-01-01 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141231194733/http://www.pakistantribune.com.pk/29003/imran-khan-marries-reham-khan-despite-opposition-family.html |archivedate=2014-12-31 }}. "Imran Khan Marries Reham Khan Despite Opposition by Family". Pakistan Tribune. 31 December 2015. Retrieved 1 January 2015.</ref>是[[英國|英國]]及[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]]女記者。萊罕姆是巴基斯坦知名媒體[[黎明新聞|黎明新聞]]的新聞訪談節目的主持人，之前她曾是英國[[BBC|BBC]]的報導員。<ref>[http://news.bbc.co.uk/weather/hi/about/newsid_8647000/8647336.stm]. Reham Khan - BBC South Today. "Reham Khan Profile On BBC South". Retrieved 28 June 2010.</ref>她亦是巴基斯坦总理及前板球明星[[伊姆蘭·罕|伊姆蘭·罕]]的第二任妻子。

== 個人生活 ==
萊罕姆的父親是一名醫生並是60年代時搬到[[利比亞|利比亞]]居住，萊罕姆亦在1973年在利比亞出生。萊罕姆是[[普什圖人|普什圖人]]。<ref>[http://tribune.com.pk/story/818700/reham-khan-from-hazara-to-bani-gala/]. "Reham Khan: From Hazara to Bani Gala". Express Tribune. 8 January 2015. Retrieved 8 January 2015.</ref>她在19歲時嫁給表哥、英國巴基斯坦裔醫生Ijaz-ur-Rehman並為他誕下3名孩子。兩人在2005年離婚，萊罕姆得到孩子撫養權。

2015年1月，萊罕姆和巴基斯坦板球明星及政治家[[伊姆蘭·罕|伊姆蘭·罕]]在[[伊斯蘭堡|伊斯蘭堡]]舉行了簡單的婚禮。<ref>[http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2891756/SEBASTIAN-SHAKESPEARE-Imran-Khan-secretly-married-BBC-weather-girl.html]. "Has Imran Khan secretly married BBC weather girl? Rumours rife in Pakistan where his family are 'against' the 'wedding' to divorced mother of three". Daily Mail. 31 December 2014. Retrieved 31 December 2014.</ref>結婚僅10月後，兩人在同年10月30日宣布因性格不合而離婚。萊罕姆在11月7日簽署離婚協議。<ref>[http://www.dawn.com/news/1218155/reham-signs-divorce-papers-refutes-allegations-against-her]. "Reham signs divorce papers, refutes allegations against her". Dawn News. 8 November 2015.</ref>
