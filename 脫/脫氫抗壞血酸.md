'''脫氫抗壞血酸'''（{{lang|en|Dehydroascorbic acid}}, DHA）是氧化形式的[[抗壞血酸|抗壞血酸]]（[[維生素C|維生素C]]）。

===運輸至線粒體===
維生素C以DHA的形式通過葡萄糖轉運體（GLUT1）進入在有大量[[自由基|自由基]]產生的[[線粒體|線粒體]]中並聚集。

===運輸到大腦===

==應用==
脫氫抗壞血酸已被用來作為維生素C膳食補充劑。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://lpi.oregonstate.edu/ss01/bioavailability.html | title = The Bioavailability of Different Forms of Vitamin C | accessdate = 2010-11-10 | last = Higdon | first = Jane | date = May 2001 | publisher = The Linus Pauling Institute}}</ref>

作為化妝品成分，脫氫抗壞血酸用於增強皮膚的外觀。<ref>{{Citation
 | title = Topical Dehydroascorbic Acid (Oxidized Vitamin C) Permeates Stratum Corneum More Rapidly Than Ascorbic Acid
 | url = https://www.researchgate.net/publication/225274699_Topical_Dehydroascorbic_Acid_(Oxidized_Vitamin_C)_Permeates_Stratum_Corneum_More_Rapidly_Than_Ascorbic_Acid
 | year = 2012
 | author = Kitt, D.Q.
 | accessdate = 2012-07-31
}}</ref>它可以用於處理[[燙髮|燙髮]]<ref name="US6506373">
[http://patft.uspto.gov/netacgi/nph-Parser?Sect1=PTO1&Sect2=HITOFF&d=PALL&p=1&u=%2Fnetahtml%2FPTO%2Fsrchnum.htm&r=1&f=G&l=50&s1=6506373.PN.%20&OS=PN/6506373&RS=PN/6506373 US Patent 6,506,373] (issued Jan. 14, 2003)</ref>和進行[[免曬美黑|免曬美黑]]應用的物質。<ref name="LOreal">[http://appft1.uspto.gov/netacgi/nph-Parser?Sect1=PTO1&Sect2=HITOFF&d=PG01&p=1&u=%2Fnetahtml%2FPTO%2Fsrchnum.html&r=1&f=G&l=50&s1=%2220100221203%22.PGNR.%20&OS=DN/20100221203&RS=DN/20100221203 U.S. Patent Application No. 10/685,073] Publication No. 20100221203 (published Sept. 2, 2010)</ref>

在個[[細胞培養|細胞培養]][[培養基|培養基]]，脫氫抗壞血酸已被用來保證細胞攝取的維生素C不含有抗壞血酸轉運類型。<ref name="cellculture">
{{cite journal
|author=Heaney ML, Gardner JR, Karasavvas N, Golde DW, Scheinberg DA, Smith EA, O'Conner OA

作為藥劑，一些研究已經表明，脫氫抗壞血酸的管理可以保護神經元損傷的一種[[中風|中風]]。
