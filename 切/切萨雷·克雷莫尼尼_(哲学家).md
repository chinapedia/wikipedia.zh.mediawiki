'''切萨雷·克雷莫尼尼'''（{{lang-it|'''Cesare Cremonino'''}}，有時候譯為{{lang|it|'''Cesare Cremonini'''}}；{{IPA-it|ˈtʃezare kremoˈnini}}，1550年12月22日<ref>Birth in 1550 is by far the most common date, but sometimes 1552 is found (inferred by some from the assertion that he started teaching at age 21 in 1573, see Pierre Bayle or {{cite web |url=http://lgxserver.uniba.it/lei/filitali.htm |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2007-02-12 |0=2007-04-16 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070416180611/http://lgxserver.uniba.it/lei/filitali.htm |archivedate=2007-04-16 }}). Thus, some sources will say "ca. 1550", or "1550 or 1552".</ref>－1631年7月19日），[[義大利|義大利]]的[[自然哲學|自然哲學]]的教授，積極於[[理性主義|理性主義]]（反對[[启示|启示]]）和[[亞里士多德|亞里士多德]]的[[唯物主義|唯物主義]]（反對[[二元論_(精神哲學)|二元論]]）的[[經院哲學|經院哲學]]。
他的[[拉丁语|拉丁语]]名字是'''Cæsar Cremoninus'''.<ref>[http://www.phil-fak.uni-duesseldorf.de/philo/galerie/neuzeit/cremon.htm "Cæsar Cremoninus", ancient illustration]</ref><ref>[http://jordanus.ign.uni-muenchen.de/cgi-bin/iccmsm-search.pl?sprache=en&datenbank=iccmsm&ausgabe=dkurz&ausdateiformat=%20&listpos=0&listen=keine&listlet=keiner&fn=t52a56.f&fi=Caesar+Cremoninus "Caesar Cremoninus", International Catalogue of Mediaeval Scientific Manuscripts, Munich University] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20071001021934/http://jordanus.ign.uni-muenchen.de/cgi-bin/iccmsm-search.pl?sprache=en&datenbank=iccmsm&ausgabe=dkurz&ausdateiformat=&listpos=0&listen=keine&listlet=keiner&fn=t52a56.f&fi=Caesar+Cremoninus |date=2007-10-01 }}</ref> 或 '''Cæsar Cremonius'''。<ref>[http://www.filosofico.net/cremonino.htm "Cæsar Cremonius", ancient illustration]</ref><ref>[http://www.bl.uk/catalogues/manuscripts/HITS0001.ASP?VPath=c!/inetpub/wwwroot/mss/data/msscat/html/13144.htm&Search=Add.+24215&Highlight=F "Cæsar Cremonius", Manuscripts Catalogue of Italian litterati, British Library] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20040907045701/http://www.bl.uk/catalogues/manuscripts/HITS0001.ASP?VPath=c%21%2Finetpub%2Fwwwroot%2Fmss%2Fdata%2Fmsscat%2Fhtml%2F13144.htm&Search=Add.+24215&Highlight=F |date=2004-09-07 }}</ref>

==著作==

===克雷莫尼尼出版的主要作品===

下面是他的主要作品 (包括獨立的論文), 僅列出它們最常用的標題:

* 1596: ''Explanatio proœmii librorum Aristotelis De physico auditu''
* 1605: ''De formis elementorum''
* 1611: ''De Anima'' (student transcript of a Cremonini lecture)
* 1613: ''Disputatio de cœlo''
* 1616: ''De quinta cœli substantia'' (second series of ''De cœlo'')
* 1626: ''De calido innato'' (於1634重印)
* 1627: ''De origine et principatu membrorum''
* 163?: ''De semine'' (printed or reprinted in 1634)
*: --- ''Posthumous:''
* 1634: ''De calido innato et semine'' (expanding 1626 with 163?)
* 1644: ''De sensibus et facultate appetitiva''
* 1663: ''Dialectica''

(不包括詩歌和其他個人作品。)
