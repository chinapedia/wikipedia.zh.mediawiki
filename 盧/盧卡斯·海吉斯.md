'''盧卡斯·海吉斯'''（{{lang-en|Lucas Hedges}}，{{bd|1996年|12月12日}}），美国演员<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/entertainment/tv-movies/brooklynite-peter-hedges-direct-steve-carell-real-life-article-1.232751|title=Brooklynite Peter Hedges direct Steve Carell's 'Real Life'|publisher=New York Daily News|date=2007-10-26}}</ref>。因演出《[[月昇冒險王國|月昇冒險王國]]》、《[[告密者_(2014年電影)|告密者]]》、《[[海邊的曼徹斯特|海邊的曼徹斯特]]》、《[[三块广告牌|意外]]》和《[[淑女鳥|淑女鳥]]》而广为人知，更凭在《[[海邊的曼徹斯特|海邊的曼徹斯特]]》的表现提名[[奥斯卡金像奖最佳男配角|奥斯卡金像奖最佳男配角]]。

==个人生活和教育经历==
海吉斯出生于[[紐約州|紐約州]][[布魯克林高地|布魯克林高地]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://patch.com/new-york/heights-dumbo/lucas-hedges-born-raised-brooklyn-heights-gets-buzz-role-manchester-sea|title=Lucas Hedges, Born and Raised in Brooklyn Heights, Gets Buzz for Role in 'Manchester by the Sea'|publisher=Brooklyn Heights Patch|date=2016-12-30}}</ref>，母亲是诗人和演员苏珊·布鲁斯（{{lang|en|Susan Bruce}}），父亲是[[奥斯卡金像奖|奥斯卡]]提名编剧兼导演[[彼得·海吉斯|彼得·海吉斯]]（{{lang|en|Peter Hedges}}）<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.news.iastate.edu/news/2010/apr/HedgesLecture|title='Dan in Real Life' director and writer Peter Hedges will speak April 14 at ISU|work=Iowa State University|date=April 6, 2010|accessdate=May 7, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|first=Tom|last=Clavin|url=http://www.27east.com/news/article.cfm/news/224719/For-filmmaker-Peter-Hedges-its-all-in-the-timing|title=For filmmaker Peter Hedges, it's all in the timing|work=27east-com|date=July 28, 2009|accessdate=May 7, 2018}}</ref>。为家中第二子。他有一位哥哥名为赛门<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/regina-weinreich/the-odd-life-of-timothy-green_b_1698279.html |title=Garden Party at Goose Creek: The Odd Life of Timothy Green|publisher=The Huffington Post|date=2012-07-24}}</ref>。他成长于布魯克林高地和科布爾山，并经常参观他父亲的拍摄现场<ref>{{cite web |url=http://wwd.com/eye/people/lucas-hedges-sundance-2016-casey-affleck-matt-damon-10323796/ |title=Sundance 2016: Five Minutes with Lucas Hedges |work=WWD |first=Leigh |last=Nordstrom |date=2016-01-24 |accessdate=2016-12-01}}</ref>。他的外祖父曾經是紐約[[HBO|HBO]]的副總裁<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1993/06/06/style/weddings-susan-b-titman-peter-s-hedges.html|title=WEDDINGS; Susan B. Titman, Peter S. Hedges|work=The New York Times|date=June 6, 1993|accessdate=May 7, 2018}}</ref>。外祖母Narcissa Titman是戲劇導演和講師<ref>{{cite web|first=Peter|last=Stamelman|url=http://www.brooklyneagle.com/articles/2017/11/14/brooklyn-kid-now-star-supporting-roles-lead|title=Brooklyn kid now a star in supporting roles & lead|work=Brooklyn Daily Eagle|date=November 14, 2017|accessdate=May 7, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|first=Lynn|last=Douglass|url= https://www.forbes.com/sites/lynndouglass/2012/11/19/yoko-ono-honored-for-feminist-art-says-not-saying-anything-with-art-a-waste/|title=Yoko Ono Honored For Feminist Art, Says Not Saying Anything With Art "A Waste"|work=Forbes|date=November 19, 2012|accessdate=May 7, 2018}}</ref>。

他毕业于[[圣安娜学校_(纽约州)|圣安娜学校]]（{{lang|en|Saint Ann's School}}）之后，目前在[[北卡羅來納州藝術學院|北卡羅來納州藝術學院]]（{{lang|en|University of North Carolina School of the Arts}}）学习戏剧表演<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/entry/get-to-know-the-breakout-star-of-manchester-by-the-sea-lucas-hedges_us_582cdf58e4b030997bbd632e |title=Get To Know The Breakout Star Of ‘Manchester By The Sea,’ Lucas Hedges |work=[[The_Huffington_Post|The Huffington Post]] | first=Leigh |last=Blickley |date=2016-11-21 |accessdate=2016-12-01}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.filmindependent.org/spirit-awards/nominee/lucas-hedges/ |title=Lucas Hedges |publisher=Independent Spirit Awards |accessdate=2016-12-03 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161220031259/http://www.filmindependent.org/spirit-awards/nominee/lucas-hedges/ |archivedate=2016-12-20 }}</ref><ref>Kaufman, Sarah. ==演艺事业==
===2007至2015年：早期角色===
海吉斯还是孩子的时候，首次出演电影是在他父亲2007年执导的电影《[[老爸行不行|老爸行不行]]》（{{lang|en|''Dan in Real Life''}}）作为临时演员。然而他的台词在后期制作时被剪掉<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.latimes.com/entertainment/envelope/la-en-mn-lucas-hedges-20161109-snap-20161108-story.html |title=Lucas Hedges finds the lightness within the dark drama of 'Manchester by the Sea' |work=[[Los_Angeles_Times|Los Angeles Times]] | first=Lisa |last=Rosen |date=2016-11-10 |accessdate=2016-12-04}}</ref>。他在一场中学舞台剧时被作为观众的《[[月昇冒險王國|月昇冒險王國]]》选角导演[[魏斯·安德森|魏斯·安德森]]所盯上，并其后让海吉斯作为电影的一角，他的第一个角色瑞福（{{lang|en|Redford}}）也就此诞生。他之后出现在《[[亞瑟紐曼|亞瑟紐曼]]》（{{lang|en|''Arthur Newman''}}）、[[傑森·瑞特曼|傑森·瑞特曼]]的《[[一日一生|一日一生]]》和[[泰瑞·吉連|泰瑞·吉連]]的电影《[[明日定律|明日定律]]》。2014年，海吉斯客串于[[魏斯·安德森|魏斯·安德森]]的《[[歡迎來到布達佩斯大飯店|歡迎來到布達佩斯大飯店]]》并出现在[[麥可·奎斯塔|麥可·奎斯塔]]（{{lang|en|Michael Cuesta}}）的《[[告密者_(2014年電影)|告密者]]》，他在《告密者》的表现获得了评论家的一致好评<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.indiewire.com/2014/09/review-kill-the-messenger-starring-jeremy-renner-mary-elizabeth-winstead-rosemarie-dewitt-271870/ |title=Review: ‘Kill The Messenger’ Starring Jeremy Renner, Mary Elizabeth Winstead & Rosemarie DeWitt |publisher=IndieWire | first=Rodrigo |last=Perez |date=2014-09-26 |accessdate=2016-12-04}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/review/kill-messenger-film-review-735836 |title='Kill the Messenger': Film Review |first=Todd |last=McCarthy |date=2014-09-26 |accessdate=2016-12-04}}</ref>。

===2016至现在：取得突破===
2015年4月，海吉斯在[[肯尼斯·洛勒根|肯尼斯·洛勒根]]第三部执导电影《[[海邊的曼徹斯特|海邊的曼徹斯特]]》参与演出。在戏中，他扮演一位16岁的青少年派翠克·錢德勒（{{lang|en|Patrick Chandler}}），父亲死后而在他不愿意的情况下交给他那神经质的叔叔来代为照顾。这部电影在2016年1月13日于[[日舞影展|日舞影展]]中首映<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.sundance.org/projects/manchester-by-the-sea |title=Manchester by The Sea |website=Sundance Film Festival | accessdate=2016-09-03}}</ref>，海吉斯获得了评论家的极力赞扬<ref>{{cite web |url=http://variety.com/2016/film/festivals/manchester-by-the-sea-review-sundance-casey-affleck-1201687256/ |title=Sundance Film Review: 'Manchester by the Sea' |work=[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]] | first=Justin |last=Chang |date=2016-01-23 |accessdate=2016-12-04}}</ref>。而他的表现获得了[[独立精神奖|獨立精神獎最佳男配角]]、[[美國演員工會獎最佳男配角|美國演員工會獎最佳男配角]]和[[奥斯卡金像奖最佳男配角|奥斯卡金像奖最佳男配角]]提名，让他成为有史以来获得这多类提名的八位最年轻男演员的其中一位<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tvguide.com/news/oscar-snubs-and-surprises-amy-adams-mel-gibson/|title=Oscar Snubs and Surprises: Amy Adams Out, but Mel Gibson Is Welcomed Back In|work=''[[TV_Guide|TV Guide]]|first=Joyce|last=Eng|date=2017-01-24|accessdate=2017-01-26}}</ref>。

2017年，他在[[葛莉塔·潔薇|葛莉塔·潔薇]]（{{lang|en|Greta Gerwig}}）的电影处女作《[[淑女鳥|淑女鳥]]》（{{lang|en|''Lady Bird''}}）和[[馬丁·麥克唐納|馬丁·麥克唐納]]（{{lang|en|Martin McDonagh}}）的电影《[[三块广告牌|三块广告牌]]》（{{lang|en|''Three Billboards Outside Ebbing, Missouri''}}）担任主角<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OuTTnXEWZfc |title=Lucas Hedges ('Manchester by the Sea') on his character's 'deep emotional well' |publisher=[[YouTube|YouTube]] | accessdate=2016-12-04}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last1=Sneider |first1=Jeff |url=http://www.thewrap.com/peter-dinklage-john-hawkes-lucas-hedges-join-martin-mcdonaghs-three-billboards/ |title=Peter Dinklage, John Hawkes, Lucas Hedges Join Martin McDonagh’s New Movie (Exclusive) |date=2016-04-07 |work=The Wrap |accessdate=2016-12-04}}</ref>。
