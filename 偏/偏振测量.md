'''偏振测量'''是一种测量并呈现[[横波|横波]][[偏振|偏振]]的方法，尤其是用于[[电磁波|电磁波]]。典型的偏振测量是通过分析目标材料所透过、[[反射|反射]]、[[折射|折射]]乃至[[衍射|衍射]]的电磁波来描述该物体的种种特性。<ref>{{cite book |title=Polarimetric Detection, Characterization and Remote Sensing, Proceedings of the NATO Advanced Study Institute on Special Detection Technique (Polarimetry) and Remote Sensing Yalta, Ukraine 20 September - 1 October 2010, Series: NATO Science for Peace and Security Series C: Environmental Security |editors=Mishchenko, M.I.; Yatskiv, Y.S.; Rosenbush, V.K.; Videen, G. (Eds.) |edition=1st Edition. |year=2011 |url=http://www.springer.com/earth+sciences+and+geography/remote+sensing/book/978-94-007-1635-3 }}</ref><ref>{{cite book |title=Astronomical Polarimetry |author= Jaap Tinbergen  Jaap Tinbergen |publisher= Cambridge University Press |isbn= 0-521-01858-7 |year=2007 }}</ref>

==应用==

[[椭圆偏振技术|椭圆偏振技术]]，常用于薄膜或平面的偏振测量。

[[遥感|遥感]]技术，用于[[行星科学|行星科学]]和[[气象雷达|气象雷达]]。
