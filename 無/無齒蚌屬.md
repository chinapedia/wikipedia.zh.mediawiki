'''无齿蚌屬'''（[[学名|学名]]：{{lang|la|''Anodonta''}}）是[[蚌目|蚌目]][[蚌科|蚌科]]的一個屬。

==物種==
根據ITIS，本屬包括下列各物種<ref name=ITIS_79930>{{ITIS |id=79930 |title=''Anodonta''}}</ref>：
* ''[[Anodonta_anatina|Anodonta anatina]]'' <small>[[Linné|Linné]], 1758</small> – duck mussel
* ''[[Anodonta_beringiana|Anodonta beringiana]]'' <small>Middendorff, 1851</small> – Yukon floater
* ''[[Anodonta_californiensis|Anodonta californiensis]]'' <small>I. Lea, 1852</small> – California floater
* ''[[Anodonta_cataracta|Anodonta cataracta]]'' <small>[[Thomas_Say|Say]], 1817</small> – eastern floater
* ''[[Anodonta_couperiana|Anodonta couperiana]]'' <small>I. Lea, 1840</small> – barrel floater
* ''[[Anodonta_cygnea|Anodonta cygnea]]'' <small>[[Linné|Linné]], 1758</small> – swan mussel
* ''[[Anodonta_dejecta|Anodonta dejecta]]'' <small>Lewis, 1875</small> – woebegone floater
* ''[[Anodonta_gibbosa|Anodonta gibbosa]]'' <small>[[Thomas_Say|Say]], 1824</small>
* ''[[Anodonta_hartfieldorum|Anodonta hartfieldorum]]'' 
* ''[[Anodonta_heardi|Anodonta heardi]]'' <small>M. E. Gordon and Hoeh, 1995</small> – Apalachicola floater
* ''[[Anodonta_imbecillis|Anodonta imbecillis]]'' <small>[[Thomas_Say|Say]], 1829</small>
* ''[[Anodonta_implicata|Anodonta implicata]]'' <small>[[Thomas_Say|Say]], 1829</small> – alewife floater
* ''[[Anodonta_kennerlyi|Anodonta kennerlyi]]'' <small>I. Lea, 1860</small> – western floater
* ''[[Anodonta_nuttalliana|Anodonta nuttalliana]]'' <small>Lea, 1838</small> – winged floater
* ''[[Anodonta_oregonensis|Anodonta oregonensis]]'' <small>I. Lea, 1838</small> – Oregon floater
* ''[[Anodonta_peggyae|Anodonta peggyae]]'' <small>Johnson, 1965</small>
* ''[[Anodonta_pseudodopsis|Anodonta pseudodopsis]]'' <small>Locard, 1883</small>
* ''[[Anodonta_suborbiculata|Anodonta suborbiculata]]'' <small>[[Thomas_Say|Say]], 1831</small> – flat floater

而在[[中国动物物种数据库|中国动物物种数据库]]紀錄有下列九個物種<ref name="bioinfo" />，但這些物種大多數都已被重新歸類到[[船蚌屬|船蚌屬]]去：
* [[背角无齿蚌|背角无齿蚌]] ''[[Anodonta_woodiana_woodiana|Anodonta woodiana woodiana]]'' (Lea)
** [[圆背角无齿蚌|圆背角无齿蚌]] ''[[Anodonta_woodiana_pacifica|Anodonta woodiana pacifica]]'' (Heude)
** [[椭圆背角无齿蚌|椭圆背角无齿蚌]] ''[[Anodonta_woodiana_elliptica|Anodonta woodiana elliptica]]'' (Heude)
** [[鱼形背角无齿蚌|鱼形背角无齿蚌]] ''[[Anodonta_woodiana_piscatorum|Anodonta woodiana piscatorum]]'' (Heude)
* [[舟形无齿蚌|舟形无齿蚌]] ''[[Anodonta_euscaphys|Anodonta euscaphys]]'' (Heude)
* [[球形无齿蚌|球形无齿蚌]] ''[[Anodonta_globosula|Anodonta globosula]]'' (Heude)
* [[河无齿蚌|河无齿蚌]] ''[[Anodonta_fluminea|Anodonta fluminea]]'' (Heude)
* [[蚶形无齿蚌|蚶形无齿蚌]] ''[[Anodonta_arcaeformis|Anodonta arcaeformis]]'' <small>(Heude)</small><ref name='id11295'>{{cite web| url =http://www.bioinfo.cn/db05/BjdwSpecies.php?action=view&id=11295| title =蚶形无齿蚌| work =《中国动物物种编目数据库》| publisher =中国科学院微生物研究所| author =中国科学院动物研究所| accessdate =2009-04-28| deadurl =yes| archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20160305003023/http://www.bioinfo.cn/db05/BjdwSpecies.php?action=view&id=11295| archivedate =2016-03-05}}</ref>
** [[黄色蚶形无齿蚌|黄色蚶形无齿蚌]] ''[[Anodonta_arcaeformis_flavotincta|Anodonta arcaeformis flavotincta]]'' (Martens)
