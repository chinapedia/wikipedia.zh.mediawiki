'''無防獸科'''（[[學名|學名]]''Anoplotheriidae''）是一類已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的[[偶蹄目|偶蹄目]]。牠們生存於[[始新世|始新世]]及[[漸新世|漸新世]]的[[歐洲|歐洲]]。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://paleodb.org/cgi-bin/bridge.pl?action=checkTaxonInfo&taxon_no=57550&is_real_user=1|title=Fossilworks: Anoplotheriidae|accessdate=2018-11-20|work=paleodb.org}}</ref>牠們有很大可能所有都是陸地生物。

==分類==
無防獸科是由[[約翰·愛德華·格雷|約翰·愛德華·格雷]]於1821年所命名，並被分類在Tesserachenae中。<ref name=Gray1851>{{Cite journal en|author=J. E. Gray|year=1821|title=On the natural arrangement of vertebrose animals|journal=The London Medical Repository Monthly Journal and Review|volume=15|pages=296-310}}</ref>後來經過多次的重置後被分類到[[無防獸總科|無防獸總科]]中。<ref>{{Cite book en|author=C.-L. Bonaparte|year=1850|title=Conspectus Systematis Mastozoologiae|edition=Editio Altera Reformata}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal en|author=E. D. Cope|year=1889|title=Synopsis of the families of Vertebrata|journal=The American Naturalist|volume=23|pages=1-29}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal en|author=J. J. Hooker|year=1986|title=Mammals from the Bartonian (middle/late Eocene) of the Hampshire Basin, southern England|journal=Bulletin of the British Museum (Natural History)|volume=39|issue=4|pages=191-478}}</ref>
