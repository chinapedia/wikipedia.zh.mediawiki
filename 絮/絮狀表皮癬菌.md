'''絮狀表皮癬菌'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Epidermophyton floccosum''）是一種[[致病真菌|致病真菌]]，能感染皮膚與指甲<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.mycology.adelaide.edu.au/Fungal_Descriptions/Dermatophytes/Epidermophyton/ | title = Epidermophyton floccosum | accessdate = 2010-05-22 | work = Mycology Online | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20100525073140/http://www.mycology.adelaide.edu.au/Fungal_Descriptions/Dermatophytes/Epidermophyton/ | archivedate = 2010-05-25 }}</ref>，造成[[足癬|足癬]]、[[股癬|股癬]]、[[甲癬|甲癬]]與{{link-en|體癬|tinea corporis}}等[[真菌病|真菌病]]<ref name = Rippon1988>{{Cite book|title=Medical mycology : the pathogenic fungi and the pathogenic actinomycetes|last=Rippon|first=John Willard|date=1988|publisher=W.B. Saunders Co|isbn=978-0721624440|edition= 3rd|location=Philadelphia|oclc=16712232 }}</ref><ref name= Diamond2000>{{Cite book|title=Fungal infections|date=2000|publisher=Current Medicine|others=Diamond, Richard D., 1942-|isbn=978-1573401364|location=Philadelphia|oclc=42862084 }}</ref>。本種菌感染的診斷方式包括身體檢查、組織培養與分子序列分析等<ref name = Liu2011>{{Cite book|title=Molecular detection of human fungal pathogens|last=|first=|publisher=CRC Press|others=Liu, Dongyou.|year=2011|isbn=9781439812419|location=Boca Raton, FL|pages=421–422|oclc=783848203}}</ref>，通常可以[[特比奈芬|特比奈芬]]、[[伊曲康唑|伊曲康唑]]、[[酮康唑|酮康唑]]與{{link-en|伏立康唑|Voriconazole}}等外用性[[抗真菌劑|抗真菌劑]]治療<ref name= Pfaller1998>{{Cite book|title=Reference method for broth dilution antifungal susceptibility testing of conidium-forming filamentous fungi : proposed standard|last=Standards.|first=National Committee for Clinical Laboratory|date=1998|publisher=NCCLS|others=Pfaller, Michael A.|isbn=9781562383558|location=Wayne, Pa.|oclc=244296519}}</ref>。

絮狀表皮癬菌是[[表皮癬菌屬|表皮癬菌屬]]的兩種物種之一（另一種為{{link-sv|斯托克表皮癬菌|Epidermophyton stockdaleae}}）<ref name = Murray2003>{{Cite book|title=Manual of clinical microbiology|date=2003|publisher=ASM Press|others=Murray, Patrick R., Baron, Ellen Jo., American Society for Microbiology.|isbn=978-1555812553|edition= 8th|location=Washington, D.C.|oclc=50035668 }}</ref><ref name = Kushwaha2000>{{Cite book|title=Biology of dermatophytes and other keratinophilic fungi|last=|first=|date=2000|publisher=Revista Iberoamericana de Micología|others=Kushwaha, Rajendra K. S., Guarro Artigas, Josep.|isbn=978-8460707110|location=Bilbao|pages=17–21, 30–43|oclc=807784068}}</ref>。一項統計顯示絮狀表皮癬菌是二十世紀北美洲造成[[皮癬菌病|皮癬菌病]]的病原菌中第四常見者<ref name = Kane1997>{{Cite book|title=Laboratory handbook of dermatophytes : a clinical guide and laboratory handbook of dermatophytes and other filamentous fungi from skin, hair, and nails|date=1997|publisher=Star Pub|others=Kane, Julius, 1924-|isbn=978-0898631579|location=Belmont, CA|oclc=37116438 }}</ref>。本種真菌雖分布於全球，但較常在熱帶與亞熱帶的病人身上發現<ref name="Rippon1988" />。

==分類==
絮狀表皮癬菌最初於1870年由德國真菌學家{{link-en|卡爾·奧托·哈爾茨|Carl Otto Harz}}描述，並將其命名為''Acrothecium floccosum''<ref>{{cite journal |author=Harz OC. |year=1870 |title=Einige neue Hyphomyceten Berlin's and Wien's nebst Beiträgen zur Systematik derselben |journal= Bulletin de la Société Impériale des Naturalistes de Moscou |volume=44 |pages=87–147 |language=de }}</ref>，1907年被重新命名為''Blastotrichum floccosum''，最後在1923年由Ota與Langeron定為現名''Epidermophyton floccosum''<ref>{{cite journal |author=Ota M, Langeron M. |year=1923 |title=Nouvelle classification des Dermatophytes |journal=Annals of Parasitology |volume=1 |pages=305–306 |language=fr}}</ref>。

==描述==
絮狀表皮癬菌的生長速度中等，約在10天後發育成熟，組織由最初的平坦粒狀逐漸發展為帶溝紋且光滑的放射狀，顏色為黃、黃棕或橄欖色。與其他皮膚真菌不同，絮狀表皮癬菌會分泌一種稱為1(3),2-diacylglyceryl-3(1)-O-4′-(N,N,N-trimethyl)homoserine的[[脂類|脂類]]，功能不明<ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1016/0005-2760(79)90239-X | title = An unusual lipid in the human pathogenic fungus Epidermophyton floccosum | journal = Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Lipids and Lipid Metabolism | first1 = T | date = 1979-09-28 | last = Tomiyasu Yamada and Yoshinori Nozawa | volume = 574 | issue = 3 | pages = 433–439| id = | url = http://www.sciencedirect.com/science?_ob=ArticleURL&_udi=B6T1X-47G2PG4-60&_user=10&_coverDate=09%2F28%2F1979&_rdoc=1&_fmt=high&_orig=search&_sort=d&_docanchor=&view=c&_searchStrId=1344606088&_rerunOrigin=google&_acct=C000050221&_version=1&_urlVersion=0&_userid=10&md5=81a59da924fe1788a4843a5058f71f71 | accessdate = 2010-05-22}}</ref> 。絮狀表皮癬菌和其他[[表皮癬菌|表皮癬菌]]一樣缺乏小[[分生孢子|分生孢子]]，其大分生孢子表面平滑，由3-5個細胞組成，呈棒狀，兩端則為圓弧狀，大小約為10-40乘6-12[[微米|微米]]<ref name=Nail />。

==感染==
絮狀表皮癬菌是[[表皮癬菌屬|表皮癬菌屬]]的兩種真菌中唯一會對人類造成感染的，宿主可為人類、野生動物或寵物，但偏好人類<ref name=MSD>{{cite web | url = http://www.msdsonline.com/CustomerSupport/Disease-MSDS/MSDSEpidermophytonFloccosum.aspx | title = Infectious Diseases: ''Epidermophyton floccosum'', ''Microsporum'' spp., ''Trichophyton'' spp. | accessdate = 2010-05-22 | work = MSD Online | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20090718031936/http://www.msdsonline.com/CustomerSupport/Disease-MSDS/MSDSEpidermophytonFloccosum.aspx | archivedate = 2009-07-18 }}</ref> 。可對皮膚與指甲造成感染，造成[[足癬|足癬]]、{{link-en|體癬|tinea corporis}}、[[股癬|股癬]]或[[甲癬|甲癬]]，可透過接觸性感染，特別是在體育館或澡堂中。絮狀表皮癬菌的感染可透過浸泡肥皂水或適當的[[抗真菌劑|抗真菌劑]]（[[髮癬退|髮癬退]]或[[咪唑|咪唑]]）治療<ref name=MSD />。絮狀表皮癬菌可在人類與[[松鼠|松鼠]]間交互傳染<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Hosseininejad | first1 = M. | last2 = Ebrahimi | first2 = A. | last3 = Hosseini | first3 = F. | title = Isolation of ''Epidermophyton floccosum'' from a Persian squirrel (''Sciurus anomalus'') | journal = Comparative Clinical Pathology | volume = 19 | issue = 2 | pages = 215–216 | year = 2009| doi = 10.1007/s00580-009-0829-4 | url = http://www.springerlink.com/content/8552251320242u22/ | accessdate = 2010-05-22}}</ref>。

絮狀表皮癬菌通常只能感染[[角質|角質]]組織，而沒有感染活組織的能力<ref name=Nail>{{cite web |url=http://qurnail.com/Epidermophyton.html |title=Epidermophyton |accessdate=2010-05-22 |work=QurNail |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110715140728/http://qurnail.com/Epidermophyton.html |archivedate=2011-07-15 }}</ref>，但有研究指出絮狀表皮癬菌感染了一名免疫失調的[[貝賽特氏症|貝賽特氏症]]患者，且造成了侵入性感染<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Seddon | first1 = ME | last2 = Thomas | first2 = MG | title = Invasive disease due to Epidermophyton floccosum in an immunocompromised patient with Behçet's syndrome. | journal = Clinical Infectious Diseases | volume = 25 | issue = 1 | pages = 153–4 | year = 1997 | pmid = 9243051 }}</ref>。
