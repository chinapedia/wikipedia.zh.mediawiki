'''多洛雷斯·理查德·斯派克斯''' （Dolores Margaret Richard Spikes，{{bd|1936年|8月24日|2015年|6月1日}}），是一名美国数学家，曾在1997年至2001年，担任[[马里兰大学东海岸分校|马里兰大学东海岸分校]]的校长<ref>{{cite web|url = http://www.umes.edu/125/Content.aspx?id=36248|title = Dolores Spikes|publisher = University of Maryland Eastern Shore|author = Bill Robinson|accessdate = April 7, 2014}}</ref>。

斯派克斯入学[[南方大学_(美国)|南方大学]]学习数学，并于1957年毕业，随后在[[伊利诺伊大学厄巴纳-香槟分校|伊利诺伊大学厄巴纳-香槟分校]]攻读硕士学位。<ref>{{cite news|title = Dolores Spikes, university president, dies at 78|url = http://www.washingtonpost.com/local/obituaries/dolores-spikes-university-president-dies-at-78/2015/06/01/ce487cc6-089d-11e5-95fd-d580f1c5d44e_story.html|accessdate = June 9, 2015|work = Washington Post|date = June 1, 2015}}</ref> 1971年，她毕业于[[路易斯安那州立大學|路易斯安娜州立大学]]，并获得数学博士学位，她也是第一名获得该学位的非裔美国人<ref name="buffalo">{{cite web|title = Dolores Richard Spikes|url = http://www.math.buffalo.edu/mad/PEEPS/spikes_dolores.html|work = Black Women in Mathematics|publisher = State University of New York - Buffalo|accessdate = April 5, 2014}}</ref>。斯派克斯是美国第一名女性大学校监，并随后成为美国大学系统的第一位女性校长。<ref name="historymakers">{{cite web|title = Dolores R. Spikes|url = http://www.thehistorymakers.com/biography/dolores-r-spikes-41|work = Education Makers|publisher = The History Makers|accessdate = April 5, 2014}}</ref>

2015年6月1日，斯派克斯去世，享年78岁<ref name="noa">{{cite web|url = http://www.theneworleansadvocate.com/news/12529256-123/dr-dolores-spikes-dies-at|title = Dolores Spikes, former Southern University president and education trailblazer, dies at age 78|work = New Orleans Advocate|accessdate = 1 June 2015}}</ref><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);">。她嫁给</span>赫蒙·斯派克斯，后者于2008年去世。他们的孩子朗达去世于2010年<ref name="nyt">{{cite news|last1 = Grimes|first1 = William|title = Dolores Spikes, Trailblazer as President of Southern University, Dies at 78|url = http://www.nytimes.com/2015/06/09/us/dolores-spikes-trailblazer-as-president-of-southern-university-dies-at-78.html?hpw&rref=us&action=click&pgtype=Homepage&module=well-region&region=bottom-well&WT.nav=bottom-well&_r=0|accessdate = June 9, 2015|work = New York Times|date = June 8, 2015}}</ref>。
