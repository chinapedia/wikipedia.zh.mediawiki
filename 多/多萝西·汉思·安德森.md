'''多萝西·汉思·安德森'''（{{lang-en|'''Dorothy Hansine Andersen'''}}，{{bd|1901年|5月15日|1963年|3月3日|catIdx=A}}）是一位[[美国|美国]][[医生|医生]]，她第一个确认了[[囊腫性纖維化|囊腫性纖維化]]并描述病情。<ref>[http://www.mtholyoke.edu/lits/library/changingthefaceofmedicine/connection.shtml Alumnae Featured in the Exhibit] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20060901204109/http://www.mtholyoke.edu/lits/library/changingthefaceofmedicine/connection.shtml |date=2006-09-01 }}</ref><ref>[http://www.greatwomen.org/women.php?action=viewone&id=178 National Women's Hall of Fame]</ref>

安德森出生于[[北卡罗来纳州|北卡罗来纳州]][[阿什维尔|阿什维尔]]，1922年她获得[[曼荷莲学院|曼荷莲学院]]学士学位，1926年获得[[约翰·霍普金斯大学|约翰·霍普金斯大学]]博士学位<ref name="J Pediatr">Damrosch, Douglas S.:  Dorothy Hansine Andersen, The Journal of Pediatrics, vol. 65, nr. 4, 1964, s. 477-479.</ref>。她曾在[[羅徹斯特大學|羅徹斯特大學]]和[[哥倫比亞大學醫學院|哥倫比亞大學醫學院]]任教。后来在[[纽约|纽约]]的{{le|哥伦比亚大学医学中心|Columbia-Presbyterian Medical Center}}婴儿医院工作。20世纪40年代她开展了[[囊腫性纖維化|囊腫性纖維化]]的诊断测试<ref name=Andersen1938>{{cite journal | author = Andersen, D.H. | authorlink =| year = 1938 | title = Cyst leiesic fibrosis of the pancreas and its relation to celiac disease | journal = Am J Dis Child | volume = 56 | pages = 344–399 | doi = 10.1001/archpedi.1938.01980140114013 }}</ref>，还促进了[[心脏|心脏]][[外科学|外科]]的培训和对[[营养学|营养学]]的研究。<ref>[http://library.cpmc.columbia.edu/hsl/archives/findingaids/andersen.html Columbia biography]</ref><ref>[http://www.mtholyoke.edu/offices/comm/csj/021502/quidnunc.shtml Mount Holyoke biography] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20051126102904/http://www.mtholyoke.edu/offices/comm/csj/021502/quidnunc.shtml |date=2005-11-26 }}</ref>

1963年3月3日安德森因[[肺癌|肺癌]]在纽约去世。<ref>{{cite book|last=Machol|first=Libby|title=Notable American Women: The Modern Period : a Biographical Dictionary, Volume 4|year=1993|publisher=Belknap Press of Harvard Univ. Press|location=Cambridge, Mass [u.a.]|isbn=9780674627338|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=CfGHM9KU7aEC&pg=PA18&lpg=PA18&dq=Dorothy+Hansine+Andersen+March+3,++1963&source=bl&ots=Or0Gscm4et&sig=zPDpnzEeAFkycyaxkUH4m86wq3k&hl=en&sa=X&ei=-pMKUPrCFcjs2QWy6sEb&ved=0CEQQ6AEwAQ#v=onepage&q=Dorothy%20Hansine%20Andersen%20March%203%2C%20%201963&f=false|pages=18–19}}</ref>

2002年安德森因为自己的科研工作而入选全国女性名人堂。
