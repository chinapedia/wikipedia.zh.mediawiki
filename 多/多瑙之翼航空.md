==历史==
'''多瑙之翼航空'''创建于2000年5月23日，是斯洛伐克的第一家私营航空公司。

==事故==
*On 2 January 2010, [[2010_Bratislava_hidden_explosives_incident_and_Dublin_bomb_alert|Danube Wings Flight 8230]], a [[Boeing_737_Classic|Boeing 737-400]], was involved in an incident when Slovak Police hid 8 explosives in passengers' luggage to test the airport's security systems.  In error, one of the explosives was not removed afterwards, and was later flown to [[Dublin|Dublin]], causing an international incident.
