'''漫射紅外線背景輻射實驗 (Diffuse Infrared Background Experiment，DIRBE)'''<ref name=giapage137/>是[[NASA|NASA]]在[[宇宙背景探測者|宇宙背景探測者]]任務中測量天空漫射[[紅外線|紅外線]]的一項實驗<ref name=giapage137/>。DIRBE的儀器是絕對輻射計和一架口徑19公分的折疊離軸[[格里望遠鏡|格里望遠鏡]]<ref name=giapage137>[http://books.google.com/books?id=7bNzSbqpeYMC&pg=PA137&dq=Diffuse+Infrared+Background+Experiment&hl=en&ei=zL24TODfPIL48AaQ9dzTDg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CCkQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q=Diffuse%20Infrared%20Background%20Experiment&f=false Riccardo Giacconi, Daniela Calzetti, Mario Livio, Piero Madau, Space Telescope Science Institute (U.S.) - '''Extragalactic background radiation: a meeting in honor of Riccardo Giacconi : proceedings of the Extragalactic Background Radiation Meeting, Baltimore, 1993 May 18-20, Volume 1993''' - Page 137 (Google Books accessed October 2010)]</ref>。目標是在從近紅外線到遠紅外線 (1.25至240微米) 的10個頻率波段上觀察宇宙的亮度地圖<ref name=giapage137/>。同時，也在1.25、 2.2 和 3.5 微米測量[[線性偏極化|線性偏極化]]<ref name=giapage137/>。在任務期間，這套儀器每天可以取得半個[[天球|天球]]的樣本<ref name=giapage137/>。
