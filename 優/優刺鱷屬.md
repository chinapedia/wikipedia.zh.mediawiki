'''優刺鱷屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Euscolosuchus''）是[[鑲嵌踝類主龍|鑲嵌踝類主龍]]的一屬，是[[鱷形超目|鱷形超目]]的近親，化石發現於[[美國|美國]][[維吉尼亞州|維吉尼亞州]]，屬於Turkey Branch組的Tomahawk Creek段。根據[[孢粉|孢粉]]的年代測定，優刺鱷的生存年代為[[三疊紀|三疊紀]]晚期的[[卡尼階|卡尼階]]早期。Turkey Branch組屬於一個更大的[[地層單位|地層單位]]：[[紐華克超群|紐華克超群]]（Newark Supergroup）。在紐華克超群中，Turkey Branch組相當獨特，已發現大量的[[四足類|四足類]][[脊椎動物|脊椎動物]]化石，而且化石類似[[南半球|南半球]]的[[動物群|動物群]]，例如[[犬齒獸類|犬齒獸類]]的[[橫齒獸科|橫齒獸科]]（Traversodontidae）。這個地層還發現[[前稜蜥類|前稜蜥類]]<ref>{{Cite journal | doi = 10.1080/02724634.1993.10011510 | last1 = Sues | first1 = H.-D. | last2 = Olsen | first2 = P. E. | year = 1993 | title = A new procolophonid and a new tetrapod of uncertain, possibly procolophonian affinities from the Upper Triassic of Virginia | url = | journal = Journal of Vertebrate Paleontology | volume = 13 | issue = 3| pages = 282–286 }}</ref>、[[奇尼瓜齒獸科|奇尼瓜齒獸科]]、[[喙頭蜥目|喙頭蜥目]]的化石<ref>{{Cite journal | doi = 10.1126/science.249.4972.1020 | last1 = Sues | first1 = H.-D. | last2 = Olsen | first2 = P. E. | year = 1990 | title = Triassic vertebrates of Gondwanan aspect from the Richmond Basin of Virginia | url = | journal = [[Science_(journal)|Science]] | volume = 249 | issue = 4972| pages = 1020–1023 | pmid=17789610}}</ref>。

優刺鱷的屬名意為「有良好尖刺的鱷魚」，意指從頸部、背部往兩側延伸的明顯尖刺。優刺鱷的頸部、背部有多排骨質鱗甲，以重疊方式排列，加上肩膀處的長刺，使牠們的外形類似[[堅蜥目|堅蜥目]]的[[鏈鱷亞科|鏈鱷亞科]]。優刺鱷的[[神經棘|神經棘]]寬廣，協助支撐背部的骨質鱗甲。優刺鱷的化石，目前只有發現[[脊椎|脊椎]]、骨質鱗甲<ref>{{Cite journal | doi = 10.1080/02724634.1992.10011444 | last1 = Sues | first1 = H.-D. | year = 1992 | title = A Remarkable New Armored Archosaur from the Upper Triassic of Virginia | url = | journal = Journal of Vertebrate Paleontology | volume = 12 | issue = 2| pages = 142–149 }}</ref>。

優刺鱷似乎是[[鱷形超目|鱷形超目]]的近親之一，鱷形超目包含現存鱷魚與其史前近親。優刺鱷的近親還包含：[[股薄鱷|股薄鱷]]、''Erpetosuchus''，後者被歸類為鱷形超目的姊妹[[分類單元|分類單元]]。近年有研究質疑這個分類法<ref>{{Cite journal | doi = 10.1671/0272-4634(2000)020[0633:FROERA]2.0.CO;2 | last1 = Olsen | first1 = P. E. | last2 = Sues | first2 = H.-D. | last3 = Norell | first3 = M. A. | year = 2000 | title = First record of Erpetosuchus (Reptilia: Archosauria) from the Late Triassic of North America | url = | journal = Journal of Vertebrate Paleontology | volume = 20 | issue = 4| pages = 633–636 }}</ref><ref name=NB12>Nesbitt, S. J.; Butler, R. J. (2012). "Redescription of the archosaur Parringtonia gracilis from the Middle Triassic Manda beds of Tanzania, and the antiquity of Erpetosuchidae". Geological Magazine: 1. {{doi|10.1017/S0016756812000362}}</ref>。
