《'''希布'''》 ({{lang-ar|'''ذيب'''}}) 是一部2014年[[纳吉·阿布·诺瓦|纳吉·阿布·诺瓦]]编剧并执导的[[约旦|约旦]]剧情片，由非专业演员担任主演<ref name="Jordan Times"/>。2014年9月4日在[[第71届威尼斯影展|第71届威尼斯影展]]地平线单元上首映并赢得最佳导演奖<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.labiennale.org/en/cinema/archive/71st-festival/line-up/off-sel/orizzonti/theeb.html|title=Theeb|accessdate=11 August 2015 |work=La Biennale di Venezia}}</ref><ref name="al-arabiya">{{cite web |url=http://english.alarabiya.net/en/life-style/entertainment/2014/09/07/Jordanian-filmmaker-wins-award-for-best-director-at-Venice-film-festival.html|work=Al Arabiya News|title=Jordanian filmmaker wins award for best director at Venice film festival|accessdate=11 August 2015}}</ref>。获得[[第88届奥斯卡金像奖|第88届奥斯卡金像奖]]最佳外语片提名<ref name="88nom">{{cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/oscar-nominations-2016-full-list-855670 |title=Oscar Nominations: The Complete List |work=[[The_Hollywood_Reporter|The Hollywood Reporter]] |last=Ford |first=Rebecca |date=14 January 2016 |accessdate=14 January 2016}}</ref>，成为首部获得[[奥斯卡最佳外语片|奥斯卡最佳外语片]]提名的约旦电影<ref name="Jordan Times">{{cite web|url=http://www.jordantimes.com/news/local/theeb%E2%80%99-becomes-first-jordanian-film-receive-oscar-nod|accessdate=2016-01-12|date=2016-01-12|work=The Jordan News|publisher=The Jordan Times|title=‘Theeb’ becomes first Jordanian film to receive Oscar nod}}</ref>，以及得到第69届[[英国电影学院奖|英国电影学院奖]]最佳外语片、最佳处女作2项提名<ref name="theguardian">{{cite web|title=Baftas 2016: full list of nominations|url=http://www.theguardian.com/film/2016/jan/08/baftas-2016-full-list-of-nominations|publisher=The Guardian|accessdate=10 January 2016|date=8 January 2016}}</ref>。

==剧情==
故事背景为1910年代的[[一战|一战]]和[[阿拉伯起义|阿拉伯起义]]时期，讲述一名约旦[[贝都因人|贝都因人]]少年在[[瓦地伦|瓦地伦]]艰苦生存的故事<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.theguardian.com/film/2015/aug/13/theeb-review-bedouin-boy-in-the-fog-of-war |title=Theeb review – Bedouin boy in the fog of war |author=Mike McCahill |work=the Guardian |accessdate=15 January 2016}}</ref>。
