'''希臘－辛巴威關係'''指的是[[希臘|希臘]]和[[辛巴威|辛巴威]]的雙邊關係。目前希臘在[[哈拉雷|哈拉雷]]設有大使館，而因為歐盟的制裁，辛巴威在希臘既沒有大使館也沒有名譽領事館<ref name=zim>{{cite web |url=http://www.mfa.gr/www.mfa.gr/en-US/Policy/Geographic+Regions/Sub-Saharan+Africa/Bilateral+Relation/Zimbabwe/ |title=Zimbabwe |accessdate=2009-04-14 |quote=Greece has an Embassy in Harare, whereas Zimbabwe does not have an Embassy and is not able to afford one. Zimbabwe does not have an Honorary Consulate in Greece either.   |publisher= }}</ref>。

== 歷史 ==
居住在辛巴威的希臘人數量在1972年來到最高點，約有13,000-15,000人。因為辛巴威的局勢日趨不穩，導致居住境內的希臘人數量降至約1,100人<ref name=zim/>。

[[哈拉雷|哈拉雷]]共有3所希臘僑民學校，於1954年開辦<ref name=zim/>，分別是幼稚園、小學和中學<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.dailyfrappe.com/Home/tabid/36/articleType/ArticleView/articleId/3518/Default.aspx |title=Zimbabwe Greek Community inaugurates new Hellenic Academy |accessdate=2009-04-14 |date=April 14, 2009 |quote=This is the third Greek language institution to open in the city. The Greek community already operates a Greek Elementary School (with 450 registered children) and a Greek Kindergarten School (with 45 reigistered children.) |publisher=[[Daily_Frappe|Daily Frappe]] }}{{dead link|date=2017年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>。

1982年，希臘同意向津巴布韋提供經濟、旅遊及貿易援助<ref>{{cite news |first= |last= |authorlink= |coauthors= |title=Greece and Zimbabwe Reach Accord on Aid |url=http://www.nytimes.com/1982/06/02/world/greece-and-zimbabwe-reach-accord-on-aid.html |quote=|work=[[Reuters|Reuters]] in [[New_York_Times|New York Times]] |date=June 2, 1982 |accessdate=2009-04-14 }}</ref>。

希臘和津巴布韋之間的關係在2002年時因為[[歐洲聯盟|歐洲聯盟]]的制裁而產生了變化。其制裁措施包括中止給予政府官員簽證、凍結他們在歐盟的資產和禁止出售武器給辛巴威。兩國最近一次的部長級會議於1998年進行<ref name=zim/>。

2008年，希臘駐津巴布韋大使Mihail Koukakis表示，將提供兩項援助給辛巴威人民，分別是價值70,000美元的糧食救助計畫，及給哈拉雷的Chivhu醫院共75,000美元的設備更新經費<ref>{{cite news |first= |last= |authorlink= |coauthors= |title=Greeks 'strongly behind' Zim |url=http://www.news24.com/News24/Africa/Zimbabwe/0,,2-11-1662_2291539,00.html |quote=Greek nationals in Zimbabwe are strongly behind the country and will continue to support the government in its efforts to develop communities, say reports. ... |work=[[News24.com|News24.com]] |date=March 20, 2008 |accessdate=2009-04-14 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080821213603/http://www.news24.com/News24/Africa/Zimbabwe/0%2C%2C2-11-1662_2291539%2C00.html |archivedate=2008年8月21日 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |first= |last= |authorlink= |coauthors= |title=Zimbabwe president meets Pope Theodore II |url=http://english.people.com.cn/200702/13/eng20070213_349735.html |quote=When meeting the visiting Pope and Patriarch of Alexandria and All Africa, Theodore II, at Zimbabwe House on Monday, Mugabe said, "We have a common purpose as Christians although, as we work in government, we are taking care of the body and mind."  |work=[[People's_Daily|People's Daily]] |date=February 13, 2007 |accessdate=2009-04-14 }}</ref>
。
