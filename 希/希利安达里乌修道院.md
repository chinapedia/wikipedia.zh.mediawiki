'''希利安达里乌修道院''' ({{lang-sr-cyr|Манастир Хиландар}}, {{IPA-sh|xilǎndaːr|pron}}, {{lang-el|Μονή Χιλανδαρίου}}；英语：Hilandar Monastery)是[[希腊|希腊]][[阿索斯山|阿索斯山]]的一个[[塞尔维亚正教会|塞尔维亚正教会]][[修道院|修道院]]，由[[塞尔维亚正教会|塞尔维亚正教会]]首任大主教圣萨瓦和他的父亲、放弃王位出家修道的尼曼雅王朝首任大茹潘[[斯特凡·尼曼雅一世|斯特凡·尼曼雅一世]]（西緬修士）创建于1198年。该修道院代表[[塞尔维亚|塞尔维亚]]宗教和世俗文化的焦点<ref>{{cite book|author=Ken Parry|title=The Blackwell Companion to Eastern Christianity|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=fWp9JA3aBvcC&pg=PA233|date= 2010-05-10|publisher=John Wiley & Sons|isbn=978-1-4443-3361-9|pages=233–}}</ref>，以及“塞尔维亚第一所大学”。<ref>{{cite book|author=Om Datt Upadhya|title=The Art of Ajanta and Sopoćani: A Comparative Study : an Enquiry in Prāṇa Aesthetics|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=LCPx0R1TqOMC&pg=PA65|date=1 January 1994|publisher=Motilal Banarsidass Publ.|isbn=978-81-208-0990-1|pages=65–}}</ref> 它在[[阿索斯山|阿索斯山]]的20座修道院中排名第四位。<ref>{{cite web|title=The administration of Mount Athos |url=http://www.mountathos.gr/active.aspx?mode=en%7B403b9a55-a81c-400d-a576-ef00e13fc586%7DView |accessdate=2016-04-06 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160311090150/http://www.mountathos.gr/active.aspx?mode=en%7B403b9a55-a81c-400d-a576-ef00e13fc586%7DView |archivedate=2016-03-11 |df= }}</ref>
