'''希腊正教会圣母领报堂'''（Greek Orthodox Church of the Annunciation）又名'''圣加百列堂'''，是[[以色列|以色列]][[拿撒勒|拿撒勒]]的一座[[东正教|东正教]][[教堂|教堂]]。 

该堂位于一个地下泉水的上方，东正教会认为这里是天使来报喜时，圣母玛利亚正在打水的地点<ref name=Emmett81>{{cite book|title=Beyond the Basilica:Christians and Muslims in Nazareth|author=Chad Fife Emmett|year=1995|publisher=University of Chicago Press|page=81|isbn=0-226-20711-0}}</ref><ref name=Matera>{{cite book|title=Strategies for Preaching Paul|author=Frank J. Matera|year=2001|page=194|publisher=Liturgical Press|isbn=0-8146-1966-5|url=http://books.google.ca/books?id=hSU7opAFlOsC&pg=RA1-PA194&dq=%22greek+orthodox+church%22+nazareth&sig=gNDOWX4kSBkG2mKXxvR0qxMfa2A#PRA1-PA194,M1}}</ref>。[[圣母领报|圣母领报]]的故事见于[[圣经|圣经]][[新约|新约]][[路加福音|路加福音]] (1:27-35)的记载。在拿撒勒有18座圣母领报堂<ref name=Wallace/>。[[天主教|天主教]][[圣母领报堂_(拿撒勒)|圣母领报圣殿]]位于玛利亚住所的上面<ref name=Kesichp32/>，而希腊正教会圣母领报堂位于玛利亚泉水的上方，听见天使加百列报喜的地点<ref name=Wallace/>。这是拿撒勒全村唯一的水源。<ref name=Berettp110>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.ca/books?id=GzWS9al8ZUEC&pg=PA110&dq=%22greek+orthodox+church+of+the+annunciation%22&cd=2#v=onepage&q=%22greek%20orthodox%20church%20of%20the%20annunciation%22&f=false|title=Discovering the World of the Bible|author=LaMar C. Berrett|edition=3rd|publisher=Cedar Fort|year=1996|ISBN=0-910523-52-5}}</ref>，是已有三千年历史的水源地<ref name=Wallace>{{cite book|title=Pilgrim's Progress: A Spiritual Guide for the Holy Land Traveler|author=Robert A. Wallace, Gwynneth Wallace|page=16|year=2000|publisher=Westminster John Knox Press|isbn=0-664-50127-3|url=http://books.google.ca/books?id=VoJMEKSr7mkC&pg=PA16&dq=gabriel+nazareth+%22orthodox+church%22&lr=&as_brr=3&sig=qMX9YuV5It8vpwOeb2gyRgIyAS4#PPA16,M1}}</ref><ref name=Bartonp73>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.ca/books?id=na0XrVa3KgsC&pg=PA74&dq=nazareth+spring+church+mary&cd=1#v=onepage&q=nazareth%20spring%20church%20mary&f=false|title=Jesus of Nazareth; a Biography|author=George A Barton|publisher=BiblioBazaar, LLC|year=2009|ISBN=1-115-16985-8}}</ref>

该堂始建于拜占庭时期，[[君士坦丁一世|君士坦丁一世]]在位时期。在十字军时代重建，18世纪在[[加利利|加利利]]的[[阿拉伯|阿拉伯]]总督Zahir al-Umar统治下又再次重建<ref name=Kesichp32>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.ca/books?id=usROogUUJrYC&pg=PA33&dq=james+nazareth+spring+mary&cd=8#v=onepage&q=james%20nazareth%20spring%20mary&f=false|title=Treasures of the Holy Land: a visit to the places of Christian origins|author=Veselin Kesich and Lydia W. Kesich|edition=Illustrated|publisher=St Vladimir's Seminary Press|year=1985|ISBN=0-88141-045-4|pages=32–33.}}</ref>，当地[[希腊正教会|希腊正教会]]取代[[方济各会|方济各会]]和希腊天主教会，开始管理这座教堂<ref name=Fifep90>Emmett, 1995, p. 90.</ref>。

该堂距离[[圣母领报堂_(拿撒勒)|圣母领报圣殿]]650米。
