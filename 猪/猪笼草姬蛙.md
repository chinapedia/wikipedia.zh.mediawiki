'''猪笼草姬蛙'''（学名：'''''Microhyla nepenthicola'''''，也叫做'''猪笼草小雨蛙'''）存在于[[婆罗洲|婆罗洲]][[砂拉越|砂拉越]]<ref name=das/>，是[[歐亞非大陸|歐亞非大陸]]已知体型最小的[[青蛙|青蛙]]之一<ref>{{cite web| url = http://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2010/08/100825094915.htm| title = Tiny, New, Pea-Sized Frog Is Old World's Smallest| work = Science Daily| date=2010-08-25}}</ref><ref>Gururaja, K.V. 2010. {{PDFlink|[http://www.ias.ac.in/currsci/25oct2010/1000.pdf Old World’s smallest frog discovered residing in killer plants.]}} ''Current Science'' '''99'''(8): 1000.</ref>。成年雄蛙体长仅有10.6至12.8毫米。<ref name=das>{{Cite journal | author = Das, I. & A. Haas. | year = 2010 | title = New species of ''Microhyla'' from Sarawak: Old World’s smallest frogs crawl out of miniature pitcher plants on Borneo (Amphibia: Anura: Microhylidae) | url = http://www.mapress.com/zootaxa/2010/f/z02571p052f.pdf | journal = Zootaxa | volume = 2571 | issue = | pages = 37–52 }}</ref>蝌蚪仅长3毫米。<ref name=post/>

== 原生地 ==
猪笼草姬蛙被发现于[[婆罗洲|婆罗洲]][[砂拉越|砂拉越]][[库巴国家公园|库巴国家公园]][[默拉皮火山|默拉皮火山]]附近。因其生命周期大部分都生活于[[苹果猪笼草|苹果猪笼草]]的捕虫笼中而得名。<ref name=ci>{{cite web|title=''Microhyla nepenthicola'' sp. nov.|url=http://www.conservation.org/explore/discoveries/surveys/amphibians/Pages/Microhyla_nepenthicola.aspx|publisher=[[保護國際|Conservation International]]|accessdate=2010-08-27}}</ref>

== 发现 ==
猪笼草姬蛙至少在100年前就为学界所知，但直到2010年其才被正式描述。之前，学者们都一直认为猪笼草姬蛙是另一种蛙类的幼体。Indraneil Das和亚历山大·哈斯{{small|（Alexander Haas）}}在库巴国家公园内听到了这种青蛙的叫声。因只有成年的蛙类才会发声，所以他们认识到其实际上是成体。<ref name=post>{{cite news|title=World's smallest frog is size of a pea|url=http://www.nypost.com/p/news/international/world_smallest_frog_is_size_of_pea_TcyMF7UJ11NICeyJm2YGaK|newspaper=[[New_York_Post|New York Post]]|accessdate=2010-08-26|date=2006-08-26}}</ref><ref name=aol>{{cite web|title=Scientists Discover Pea-Sized Frog in Borneo|author=Muessig, Ben|url=http://www.aolnews.com/weird-news/article/scientists-discover-pea-sized-frog-in-borneo/19609590?icid=main%7Cmain%7Cdl1%7Csec1_lnk3%7C166349|publisher=[[美国在线|AOL]]|accessdate=2010-08-26}}</ref>在黄昏时分，雄性的猪笼草姬蛙常于猪笼草内鸣叫。<ref name=ci/>
