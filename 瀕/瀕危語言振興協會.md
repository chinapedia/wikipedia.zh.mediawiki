'''瀕危語言振興協會'''({{lang|en|Living Tongues Institute for Endangered Languages}}、{{lang|en|LTIEL}})是一總部位於美國[[塞勒姆_(俄勒冈州)|俄勒岡州塞勒姆]]、以科學方法記錄瀕危語言之組織，並協助社區維護和振興其民族語言的知識。該研究所是由兩位語言學家"[[雷戈里·DS·安德森|雷戈里·DS·安德森]]"(Gregory Anderson (linguist))博士、及"[[K·大偉·哈里森|K·大偉·哈里森]]"博士所主持。<ref name=wbur>{{cite web |url= http://www.onpointradio.org/shows/2008/01/the-linguists-saving-the-worlds-languages/ |title= 'The Linguists': Saving the World's Languages |date= January 25, 2008 |accessdate= February 22, 2009 |last= Brooks |first= Anthony |publisher= [[:en:WBUR-FM|WBUR]]}}</ref><ref name=NYT>{{cite web |date= January 13, 2008 |last= Hughes |first= Jennifer V. |accessdate= February 22, 2009 |url= http://www.nytimes.com/2008/01/13/nyregion/nyregionspecial2/13artswe.html?_r=1 |work= [[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] |title= Racing to Capture Vanishing Languages}}</ref><ref name=NPR>{{cite web |title= Saving Dying Languages in 'The Linguists' |publisher= [[National_Public_Radio|National Public Radio]] |work=[[:en:Weekend_Edition|Weekend Edition]] |date= February 21, 2009 |accessdate= February 22, 2009 |url= http://www.npr.org/templates/story/story.php?storyId=100874724}}</ref>  

一項研究計劃涉及培訓原住民青年，從他們不是社區傳統語母語使用者的角度、來錄音及記錄他們的長輩的語言語音，以提升這些語言語音文件的質量及能在母語使用者之間"打造出驕傲"(build pride)。<ref name=WP>{{cite web |date=March 16, 2009 |accessdate=March 16, 2009 |work= [[Washington_Post|Washington Post]] |title= Preserving Languages Is About More Than Words |url= http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/content/article/2009/03/15/AR2009031501857_2.html?referrer=emailarticle |last= Lyderson |first= Kari}}</ref>
