'''花耳纲'''（[[学名|学名]]：Dacrymycetes）是[[担子菌门|担子菌门]][[伞菌亚门|伞菌亚门]]下的一纲。该纲下仅含一科，即'''花耳科'''（学名：Dacrymycetaceae）。该科真菌为[[胶质菌|胶质菌]]，[[桶孔覆垫|桶孔覆垫]]闭锁，[[担子|担子]]通常呈叉状。<ref>{{cite journal | author=Hibbett, D.S. | title=A higher level phylogenetic classification of the ''Fungi''| journal=Mycol. Res. | volume=111 | issue=5 | pages=509–547 | doi=10.1016/j.mycres.2007.03.004 | pmid=17572334 | display-authors=1 | last2=Binder | first2=Manfred | last3=Bischoff | first3=Joseph F. | last4=Blackwell | first4=Meredith | last5=Cannon | first5=Paul F. | last6=Eriksson | first6=Ove E. | last7=Huhndorf | first7=Sabine | last8=James | first8=Timothy | last9=Kirk | first9=Paul M.|date=March 2007}}</ref>花耳科中共包含9个属、101个种。<ref name=Kirk2008>{{cite book |author=Kirk PM, Cannon PF, Minter DW, Stalpers JA. |title=Dictionary of the Fungi. 10th ed |publisher=CABI |location=Wallingford |year=2008|page=192|isbn=0-85199-826-7}}</ref>
