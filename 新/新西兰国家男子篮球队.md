'''新西兰籃球國家隊'''（'''New Zealand national basketball team'''），是代表[[新西兰|新西兰]]參加國際籃球賽事的籃球國家隊。世錦賽最好成績是2002年第四名。并且在大洋洲籃球錦標賽中经常获得冠軍。篮球队的昵称Tall Blacks来源于[[紐西蘭國家橄欖球隊|紐西蘭國家橄欖球隊]]的暱稱全黑隊（All Blacks）。
== 历史 ==
The Tall Blacks competed at the [[2000年夏季奥林匹克运动会|2000 Sydney Olympics]] and finished with a 1–5 record, their only win coming against [[安哥拉|Angola]] in the playoff for eleventh.

In 2001, they defeated Australia in a three-game series to qualify for the [[2002年世界篮球锦标赛|2002 FIBA World Championship]] in [[印第安納波利斯|Indianapolis]], United States.  At the tournament they finished fourth, after beating [[Puerto_Rico_national_basketball_team|Puerto Rico]] in the quarter-finals before losses to [[塞爾維亞國家男子籃球隊|Serbia and Montenegro]] and [[德國國家男子籃球隊|Germany]]. Tall Blacks captain [[Pero_Cameron|Pero Cameron]] was the only non-[[NBA|NBA]] player named to the all-tournament team in [[印第安納波利斯|Indianapolis]].

The Tall Blacks qualified for the [[2004年夏季奥林匹克运动会|2004 Athens Olympics]] but again finished with a 1–5 record and lost to Australia in the playoff for ninth place. Their most noted moment was on the 7th day of the games, when they defeated reigning World Champion Serbia and Montenegro 90–87.

=== 2006年世锦赛 ===
At the [[2006年世界籃球錦標賽|2006 FIBA World Championship]], the Tall Blacks were not to repeat their fourth-place finish from 2002. After an 0–3 start, the Tall Blacks rallied into the Second round with two straight wins to close out the group stage. However, they would fall in the round of 16 to defending Olympic gold medalists [[阿根廷国家男子篮球队|Argentina]], 79–62. After that disappointment, [[Tab_Baldwin|Tab Baldwin]] resigned as the coach of the Tall Blacks and was replaced by [[Nenad_Vučinić|Nenad Vučinić]], his longtime assistant coach.

=== 2011年[[斯坦科维奇杯|斯坦科维奇杯]] ===
The squad competed at the 2011 Boris Stanković Cup in China. They played 3 games against difficult opposition, China, Russia and Angola.

After going down to Russia in the first game by just 3 points, the Tall Blacks quickly put that behind them to smash China in their second game which led to a good win against a strong Angolan team.

The Tall Blacks came up against a strong Russian team in the final, but the Russians were no match for Tall Blacks star shooting guard [[柯克·佩尼|Kirk Penney]] as he dropped 30 points to give the Tall Blacks the win and the gold medal for 2011.

=== Haka ===
The Tall Blacks perform a traditional [[哈卡舞|Haka]] (Māori challenge) before every game.

== 著名球员 ==
Probably the most well-known former New Zealand Tall Black player in the [[NBA|National Basketball Association]] (NBA) is former [[圣安东尼奥马刺|San Antonio Spurs]] forward [[西恩·马克斯|Sean Marks]]. Another New Zealand player, former [[Wisconsin_Badgers_men's_basketball|University of Wisconsin]] star [[柯克·佩尼|Kirk Penney]], briefly played in the NBA, and later played with top European teams [[Maccabi_Tel_Aviv_B.C.|Maccabi Tel Aviv]] and [[BC_Žalgiris|Žalgiris]], but now plays with [[Baloncesto_Fuenlabrada|Baloncesto Fuenlabrada]] in [[西班牙|Spain]] after being released by the [[New_Zealand_Breakers|New Zealand Breakers]]. In past generations, players such as [[Stan_Hill|Stan Hill]] and [[Glen_Denham|Glen Denham]] were well revered and respected players who were the face of New Zealand basketball.
