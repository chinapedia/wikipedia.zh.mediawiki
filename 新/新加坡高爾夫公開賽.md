'''新加坡高爾夫公開賽'''（{{lang-en|Singapore Open}}），是[[亞洲巡迴賽|亞洲巡迴賽]]的[[新加坡|新加坡]]站，由1961年創立至2001年是每年舉辦的賽事，及後因為贊助商的問題導致缺乏經費而停辦了幾年，至2005年才復辦<ref>[http://www.asiantour.com/story.htm?storyID=ff8080810b56720a010b5fe2b6d20014 Barclays Take up Title Sponsorship of the Singapore Open] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20060602135221/http://www.asiantour.com/story.htm?storyID=ff8080810b56720a010b5fe2b6d20014 |date=2006-06-02 }}, ''asiantour.com'', 23 May 2006.</ref>，2013年又再度停辦<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.straitstimes.com/breaking-news/sports/story/golf-no-singapore-open-2013-tournament-will-be-held-early-next-year-20130 |title=Golf: No Singapore Open in 2013, but tournament will be held early next year |newspaper=The Straits Times |date=19 July 2013 |first=Sanjay |last=Nair}}</ref>，預計2016年重賽<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.asiantour.com/2015/01/28/singapore-open-to-return-in-2016/ |title=Singapore Open to return in 2016 |publisher=Asian Tour |date=28 January 2015 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150131145654/http://www.asiantour.com/2015/01/28/singapore-open-to-return-in-2016/ |archivedate=2015年1月31日 }}</ref>。
