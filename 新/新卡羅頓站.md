'''新卡羅頓車站'''（{{lang-en|New Carrollton station}}）是[[美國|美國]][[馬里蘭州|馬里蘭州]][[乔治王子县_(马里兰州)|喬治王子郡]]{{link-en|新卡羅頓 (馬里蘭州)|New Carrollton, Maryland|新卡羅頓}}的鐵路車站，有[[華盛頓地鐵|華盛頓地鐵]]、[[馬里蘭區域通勤鐵路|馬里蘭區域通勤鐵路]]、[[美鐵|美鐵]]的列車停靠。本站是華盛頓地鐵橘線以及計劃中紫線的東側終點站，位置靠近{{link-en|495號州際公路|Interstate 495 (Capital Beltway)|首都環線公路}}。 

在華盛頓地鐵的月台下方，有一個候車室供搭乘美鐵東北區域號以及維蒙特人號，以及馬里蘭區域通勤鐵路賓州線列車的旅客使用。附近並有一個鐵路機廠。

長途公路客運公司[[灰狗巴士|灰狗巴士]]開往[[維吉尼亞州|維吉尼亞州]]的[[里奇蒙|里奇蒙市]]、[[华盛顿哥伦比亚特区|華盛頓]]、[[紐約市|紐約市]]、[[賓夕法尼亞州|賓夕法尼亞州]]的[[費城|費城]]、[[匹茲堡|匹茲堡]]的班車也停靠本站。<ref name="New Carrollton, MD, station gets Greyhound bus service">{{cite web|url=http://www.autobusesamericanos.us/en/newsroom/viewrelease.aspx?id=439&year=2011|title=Greyhound Brings Premium Greyhound Express Service to the Southeast and Announces Expansion with 24 New Routes and Six New Markets|last=Staff|date=August 25, 2011|publisher=Greyhound Lines, Inc.|accessdate=8 February 2013|archive-url=https://archive.is/20130421192804/http://www.autobusesamericanos.us/en/newsroom/viewrelease.aspx?id=439&year=2011|archive-date=2013-04-21|dead-url=yes}}</ref>

==History==
The New Carrollton station is the third station in the area to serve intercity rail traffic.  

The first station, '''Lanham''', {{convert|0.75|mi|km}} north of the current station, consisted of a small shelter and an asphalt platform served by a few [[Penn_Central_Transportation_Company|Penn Central]] trains.<ref name="New Carrollton Station history">{{cite web |url=http://www.actfortransit.org/archives/publications/TransitTimes-V23-2-Apr2009.pdf |title=30th Anniversary of New Carrollton Station |author=Tom Fuchs |page=5 |accessdate=25 February 2012}}</ref>

[[File:PASSENGERS_WAITING_FOR_AN_AMTRAK_METROLINER_TRAIN_THAT_WILL_TAKE_THEM_FROM_A_WASHINGTON,_DISTRICT_OF_COLUMBIA,_SUBURB..._-_NARA_-_556673.jpg|thumb]]
The second station, '''Capital Beltway Station''', sat just inside the [[Interstate_495_(Capital_Beltway)|Capital Beltway]]. Opened on January 16, 1969, it was served by Penn Central (later Amtrak) [[Budd_Metroliner|Metroliners]].<ref name="New Carrollton Station history" /><ref name="trainorders.com thread">{{cite web |url=http://www.trainorders.com/discussion/read.php?2,991141 |title=Amtrak Capital Beltway Station Location |accessdate=25 February 2012}}</ref>

On November 20, 1978, the Washington Metro opened its New Carrollton station<ref name="November 12, 1978">{{Citation |last =Feaver |first =Douglas B. |title =Orange Line brings Metro to Beltway; Orange Line will bring Metro to P.G. |newspaper =The Washington Post |page =C1 |date =November 12, 1978}}</ref><ref name="November 18, 1978">{{Citation |last =Eisen |first =Jack |coauthor =John Feinstein |title =City-County fanfare opens Orange Line; Ceremonies open new Orange Line |newspaper =The Washington Post |page = D1 |date =November 18, 1978}}</ref> — along with the [[Cheverly_(Washington_Metro)|Cheverly]], [[Deanwood_(Washington_Metro)|Deanwood]], [[Landover_(Washington_Metro)|Landover]], and [[Minnesota_Avenue_(Washington_Metro)|Minnesota Avenue]] stations<ref name="November 12, 1978"/><ref name="November 18, 1978"/> — marking the completion of {{convert|7.4|mi|km}} of Metro track northeast from the [[Stadium–Armory_(Washington_Metro)|Stadium–Armory]] station.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.wmata.com/about_metro/docs/metrofacts.pdf |title=Sequence of Metrorail openings |author=Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority |date=July 2009 |accessdate=August 2, 2010}}</ref>

In the early 1980s, the state of Maryland took over commuter rail from [[Conrail|Conrail]] (the successor to the Penn Central). The newly renamed [[MARC_Train|MARC]] service was moved to Capital Beltway Station and Lanham station was abandoned.  In 1983, Amtrak and MARC shifted service to the New Carrollton station, using a new island platform next to the existing Washington Metro platform.<ref>[http://history.amtrak.com/archives/i-new-i-new-carrollton-station-brochure-1983 Amtrak History & Archives]. Accessed March 28, 2013.</ref>  

The platforms of Capital Beltway were eventually demolished, although as of 2010 the concrete cap over the stairs that led to one of the platforms was still visible.  The former station building off Cobb Road is now used by the Maryland DOT and the station's parking lot is used for road maintenance vehicles.<ref name="New Carrollton Station history" /><ref name="trainorders.com thread" /><ref name="Capital Beltway Station, Google Maps">{{Google maps |url=http://maps.google.com/?ll=38.954929,-76.865019&spn=0.002336,0.00331&t=h&z=18 |title=Google Maps image of the location of the former Capital Beltway Station |accessdate=25 February 2012}}</ref>

==車站配置==
[[File:New_Carrollton_MARC_station_platform.jpg|thumb]]/[[马里兰区域通勤铁路|马里兰区域通勤铁路]]的月台]]
鎂鐵東北走廊在本站有三條軌道，最西邊的兩條（軌道2與3）之間有一座島式月台，而軌道1則無月台配置。在美鐵的月台東邊是華盛頓地鐵的月台，供其橘線使用。鐵路的兩側則是公車行駛的道路以及停車場。
