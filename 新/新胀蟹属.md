'''新胀蟹属'''（[[学名|学名]]：{{lang|la|''Neosarmatium''}}）为[[方蟹總科|方蟹總科]]的物種<ref name="Ng">{{cite journal|journal=[[Raffles_Bulletin_of_Zoology|Raffles Bulletin of Zoology]] |year=2008 |volume=17 |pages=1–286 |title=Systema Brachyurorum: Part I. An annotated checklist of extant Brachyuran crabs of the world |author=Peter K. L. Ng, Danièle Guinot & Peter J. F. Davie |url=http://rmbr.nus.edu.sg/rbz/biblio/s17/s17rbz.pdf |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110606061453/http://rmbr.nus.edu.sg/rbz/biblio/s17/s17rbz.pdf |archivedate=2011-06-06 }}</ref>。舊屬[[方蟹科|方蟹科]]<ref name='id12021'>{{cite web| url =http://www.bioinfo.cn/db05/BjdwSpecies.php?action=view&id=12021| title =迈纳新胀蟹| work =《中国动物物种编目数据库》| publisher =中国科学院微生物研究所| author =中国科学院动物研究所| accessdate =2009-04-28| language =zh-hans| deadurl =yes| archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20160305090759/http://www.bioinfo.cn/db05/BjdwSpecies.php?action=view&id=12021| archivedate =2016-03-05}}</ref>，今屬[[相手蟹科|相手蟹科]]<ref name=WoRMS_205090>{{cite WoRMS |last=Davie |first=Peter |year=2009 |title=''Neosarmatium'' <small>Serène & Soh, 1970</small>|id=205090 }}</ref>。

==物種==
本屬包括下列各物種<ref name=WoRMS_205090 />：
*''Neosarmatium bidentatum'' <small>Rahayu & Davie, 2006</small>
*{{link-nl|Neosarmatium daviei|Neosarmatium daviei}}'' <small>Schubart & Ng, 2003</small>
*''Neosarmatium fourmanoiri'' <small>Serène, 1973</small>
*''Neosarmatium indicum'' <small>(A. Milne-Edwards, 1868)</small>
*''Neosarmatium inerme'' <small>(de Man, 1887)</small>
*''Neosarmatium integrum'' <small>(A. Milne-Edwards, 1873)</small>
*''Neosarmatium laeve'' <small>(A. Milne-Edwards, 1869)</small>
*''Neosarmatium malabaricum'' <small>(Henderson, 1893)</small>
*[[迈纳新胀蟹|迈纳新胀蟹]] {{lang|la|''Neosarmatium meinerti''}} <small>(de Man, 1887)</small>
*''Neosarmatium papuense'' <small>Rahayu & Davie, 2006</small>
*[[斑點新脹蟹|斑點新脹蟹]] ''Neosarmatium punctatum'' <small>(A. Milne-Edwards, 1873)</small>
*''Neosarmatium rotundifrons'' <small>(A. Milne-Edwards, 1869)</small>
*[[粗壯新脹蟹|粗壯新脹蟹]] ''{{link-nl|Neosarmatium smithi|Neosarmatium smithi}}''<small>(H. Milne Edwards, 1853)</small>
*''Neosarmatium spinicarpus'' <small>Davie, 1994</small>
*''[[Neosarmatium_tangi|Neosarmatium tangi]]'' <small> (Rathbun, 1931)</small>
* ''Neosarmatium trispinosum'' <small>Davie, 1994</small>

根據WoRMS，''Neosarmatium aequifrons'' <small>(Rathbun, 1914) </small>及''Neosarmatium ambonensis'' <small>Serène & Moosa, 1971</small>均被認為只是''Neosarmatium laeve'' <small>(A. Milne-Edwards, 1869)</small>的異名物種<ref name=WoRMS_205090 />。
