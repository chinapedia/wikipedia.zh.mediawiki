三星Galaxy A3（2016）是[[三星电子|三星电子]]制造的Android[[智慧型手机|智慧型手机]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.samsung.com/my/smartphones/galaxy-a3-2016-a310/|title=Samsung Galaxy A3 (2016) price, specs, review, camera & features|language=en|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170627193830/http://www.samsung.com/my/smartphones/galaxy-a3-2016-a310/|archivedate=2017-06-27}}</ref>，于2015年12月发布，运行Android 5.1.1 (Lollipop),现已可升级至 6.0.1 (Marshmallow)。这台智慧型手机搭载了[[64位|64位]][[高通|高通]]MSM8916 [[Snapdragon|Snapdragon]] 410或[[Exynos|Exynos]] 7578 [[SoC|SoC]]，由 4个[[ARM|ARM]] Cortex-A53 核心、[[Adreno|Adreno]] 306或Mali-T720MP2 [[GPU|GPU]]、1.5 GB的记忆体和16 GB储存空间组成，最大可以扩充到256 GB的[[MicroSD|MicroSD]]记忆卡,电池为不可拆卸式2300mAh，不支援快充。三星Galaxy A只有132克。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.gsmarena.com/samsung_galaxy_a3_(2016)-7791.php|title= Samsung Galaxy A3 (2016) - Full phone specifications|work=www.gsmarena.com|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://gadgets.ndtv.com/samsung-galaxy-a3-2016-3151|title= Samsung Galaxy A3 (2016) price, specifications, features, comparison|language=en-US}}</ref>
