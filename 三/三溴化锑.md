'''三溴化锑'''（[[锑|Sb]][[溴|Br<sub>3</sub>]]）是锑的一种[[卤化物|卤化物]]，其中锑的[[氧化态|氧化态]]为+3。它可以由金属[[锑|锑]]与[[溴|溴]]单质反应或[[三氧化二锑|三氧化二锑]]溶于[[氢溴酸|氢溴酸]]制得。它可以加入到[[聚合物|聚合物]]（例如[[聚乙烯|聚乙烯]]）中作[[阻燃剂|阻燃剂]]。<ref>{{cite journal | last = Yang | first = Y. P. | coauthors = D. G. Brewer, and J. E. S. Venart | year = 1991 | title = A study of the synergistic action of antimony oxide in fire-retardant polyethylene | journal = Fire and Materials | volume = 15 | pages = 37 | doi = 10.1002/fam.810150107 }}</ref> 它也用于制备其他锑化合物、化学分析以及染色。<ref>"Antimony tribromide" http://cameochemicals.noaa.gov/chemical/2501</ref>

三溴化锑有两种[[晶型|晶型]]，都属于[[正交晶系|正交晶系]]。快速冷却三溴化锑的[[二硫化碳|二硫化碳]]溶液可得针状晶体α-SbBr<sub>3</sub>，它不稳定，会缓慢转化成稳定的β晶型。<ref>{{cite journal|doi=10.1246/bcsj.43.2398|title=Structural Studies of Antimony Tribromide and Its Molecular Complex with Benzene by Means of the 81Br Nuclear Quadrupole Resonance|year=1970|author=Okuda, Tsutomu|journal=Bulletin of the Chemical Society of Japan|volume=43|pages=2398|last2=Terao|first2=Hiromitsu|last3=Ege|first3=Osamu|last4=Negita|first4=Hisao|issue=8}}</ref>

三溴化锑水解产生三氧化二锑和溴化氢：

: 2 SbBr<sub>3</sub> + 3 H<sub>2</sub>O → Sb<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub> + 6 HBr
