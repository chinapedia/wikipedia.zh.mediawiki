==职能==

'''斐济储备银行'''依据《1983年斐济储备银行法案》（{{lang-en|Reserve Bank of Fiji Act, 1983}}）之规定，其职能、权力及责任为<ref name="zhineng">{{Cite web |url = http://www.rbf.gov.fj/About-Bank/Menu-2.aspx|format= |title =STATUTORY RESPONSIBILITIES of Reserve Bank of Fiji|author =|date = |publisher = 斐济储备银行 |language =en |accessdate = 2014-11-23 |quote =The functions, powers, and responsibilities of the Bank are specified in the Reserve Bank of Fiji Act, 1983."The principal purposes of the Bank as stated in Part ll, Section 4 of the Reserve Bank Act are as follows:To regulate the issue of currency, and the supply, availability and international exchange of money; To promote monetary stability; To promote a sound financial structure; and To foster credit and exchange conditions conducive to the orderly and balanced economic development of the country".}}</ref>：
*规范[[货币|货币]]之发行、供给、有效性及国际兑换；
*维系国家货币之稳定性；
*促进金融结构健全发展；
*促进信贷与贸易之有序发展，以及国家经济之均衡发展；
*监管及引导[[保险|保险业]]发展；
*监管[[资本市场|资本市场]]和[[证券业|证券业]]；
