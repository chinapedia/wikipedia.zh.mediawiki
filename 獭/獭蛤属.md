'''獭蛤属'''（学名：{{lang|la|'''''Lutraria'''''}}）是[[帘蛤目|帘蛤目]][[马珂蛤科|马珂蛤科]]的一属<ref name="WoRMS_138157" /><ref name="Huber 2010" />，都是中型的貝類。

==物種==
本屬包括下列物種<ref name="WoRMS_138157" />：
* [[小獺蛤|小獺蛤]] ''[[Lutraria_angustior|Lutraria angustior]]'' <small>Philippi, 1844</small>
* ''[[Lutraria_bruuni|Lutraria bruuni]]'' <small>Powell, 1967</small>
* ''[[Lutraria_budkeri|Lutraria budkeri]]'' <small>Nicklès, 1955</small>
* ''[[Lutraria_capensis|Lutraria capensis]]'' <small>Reeve, 1854</small>
* ''[[Lutraria_complanata|Lutraria complanata]]'' <small>(Gmelin, 1791)</small>
* ''[[Lutraria_curta|Lutraria curta]]'' <small>Reeve, 1854</small>
* ''[[Lutraria_grandis|Lutraria grandis]]'' <small>(Hutton, 1873)</small>{{†}}
* [[奇獭蛤|奇獭蛤]] ''[[Lutraria_impar|Lutraria impar]]'' <small>Reeve, 1854</small>
* ''[[Lutraria_inhacaensis|Lutraria inhacaensis]]'' <small>Boshoff, 1965</small>
* ''[[Lutraria_lutraria|Lutraria lutraria]]'' <small>(Linnaeus, 1758)</small>
* [[大獭蛤|大獭蛤]] ''[[Lutraria_maxima|Lutraria maxima]]'' <small>Jonas, 1844</small>
* ''[[Lutraria_oblonga|Lutraria oblonga]]'' <small>(Gmelin, 1791)</small>
* [[弓獺蛤|弓獺蛤]] ''[[Lutraria_rhynchaena|Lutraria rhynchaena]]'' <small>Jonas, 1844</small>
* [[塞內加爾獺蛤|塞內加爾獺蛤]] ''[[Lutraria_senegalensis|Lutraria senegalensis]]'' <small>Gray, 1837</small>
* [[施獺蛤|施獺蛤]] ''[[Lutraria_sieboldii|Lutraria sieboldii]]'' <small>Reeve, 1854</small>
* ''[[Lutraria_steynlussii|Lutraria steynlussii]]'' <small>M. Huber, 2010</small>
* ''[[Lutraria_trapezoidalis|Lutraria trapezoidalis]]'' <small>Powell & Bartrum, 1929</small>{{†}}
* ''[[Lutraria_turneri|Lutraria turneri]]'' <small>Jousseaume, 1891</small>
* ''[[Lutraria_vellai|Lutraria vellai]]'' <small>(Beu, 1966)</small>{{†}}
