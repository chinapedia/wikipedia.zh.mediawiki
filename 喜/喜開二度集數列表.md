《[[喜開二度|喜開二度]]》（{{lang-en|''The Comeback''}}）是一部由《[[慾望城市|慾望城市]]》執行製作[[麥可·派翠克·金|麥可·派翠克·金]]和[[麗莎·庫卓|麗莎·庫卓]]開創，並由麗莎·庫卓主演的美國[[HBO|HBO]]原創電視喜劇。本劇於2005年6月5日在HBO開播，並在播出第一季後遭到取消。9年後，HBO復活本劇並預訂第2季8集。<ref name="S2">{{cite news|last1=Kondolojy|first1=Amanda|title=HBO confirms November 9 Premieres for 'The Newsroom', 'The Comeback' & 'Getting On'|url=http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2014/09/09/hbo-confirms-november-9-premieres-for-the-newsroom-the-comeback-getting-on/301246/|accessdate=2014-09-10|publisher=[[Zap2it|Zap2it]]|date=2014-09-09|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140910195930/http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2014/09/09/hbo-confirms-november-9-premieres-for-the-newsroom-the-comeback-getting-on/301246/|archivedate=2014-09-10}}</ref>

麗莎·庫卓表示自己和麥可·派翠克·金以及HBO都有意製作第三季。<ref name="blogs.indiewire.com">{{cite news|url=http://blogs.indiewire.com/bent/lisa-kudrow-the-comeback-is-coming-back-20150427|title=Lisa Kudrow: 'The Comeback' Is Coming Back|publisher={{link-en|Indiewire|Indiewire}}|last=Borden|first=J.D.|date=2015-04-27|accessdate=2016-02-27|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150502003153/http://blogs.indiewire.com/bent/lisa-kudrow-the-comeback-is-coming-back-20150427|archive-date=2015-05-02|dead-url=yes}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.indiewire.com/article/lisa-kudrow-the-comeback-interview-how-it-began-how-season-2-ended-and-a-potential-season-3-20150622|title=Lisa Kudrow on How 'The Comeback' Began, How Season 2 Ended and a Potential Season 3|publisher={{link-en|Indiewire|Indiewire}}|last=Miller|first=Liz Shannon|date=2015-06-23|accessdate=2016-02-27}}</ref>
