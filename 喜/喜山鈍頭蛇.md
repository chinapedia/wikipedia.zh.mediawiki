E. 1839 Spicilegium serpentium indicorum [parts 1 and 2]. Proc. Zool. Soc. London, 7: 31-34, 49-55.</ref>。该物种的模式产地在印度[[阿萨姆|阿萨姆]]。<ref name='id4680'>{{cite web| url =http://www.bioinfo.cn/db05/BjdwSpecies.php?action=view&id=4680| title =喜山钝头蛇| work =《中国动物物种编目数据库》| publisher =中国科学院微生物研究所| author =中国科学院动物研究所| accessdate =2009-04-11| deadurl =yes| archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20160305105128/http://www.bioinfo.cn/db05/BjdwSpecies.php?action=view&id=4680| archivedate =2016-03-05}}</ref>
