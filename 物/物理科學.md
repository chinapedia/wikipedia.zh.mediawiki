'''物理科學'''（{{lang-en|Physical science}}）是[[自然科學|自然科學]]的分支，研究沒有生命的系統，與[[生命科學|生命科學]]相對。但是「物理」這個名詞對兩者所造成的差別，是無意的而且有點隨意，因為物理科學之下又有很多分支，當中好些分支也會研究生物的現象（例如有機化學）。

{{TOC limit|limit=2}}

== 物理科學是甚麼？ ==
物理科學的形容如下：
* [[科學|科學]]的一個分支：透過對[[宇宙|宇宙]]作出可試驗的解釋和預測，有系統地不斷嘗試建立和組織[[知識|知識]]<ref name=wilson>{{cite book |last1 = Wilson |first1 = Edward O. |title = Consilience: The Unity of Knowledge |edition=1st |publisher = Vintage Books |location = New York, NY |year = 1998 |isbn = 0-679-45077-7 |pages=49–71}}</ref><ref name=Heilbron>"... modern science is a discovery as well as an invention. It was a discovery that nature generally acts regularly enough to be described by laws and even by [[mathematics|mathematics]]; and required invention to devise the techniques, abstractions, apparatus, and organization for exhibiting the regularities and securing their law-like descriptions." —p.vii, [[J._L._Heilbron|J. L. Heilbron]], (2003, editor-in-chief). ''The Oxford Companion to the History of Modern Science''. New York: Oxford University Press. ISBN 0-19-511229-6.</ref><ref>{{cite dictionary |encyclopedia=Merriam-Webster Online Dictionary |title=science |url=http://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/science |accessdate=2011-10-16 |publisher=[[Merriam-Webster|Merriam-Webster]], Inc |quote='''3 a:''' knowledge or a system of knowledge covering general truths or the operation of general laws especially as obtained and tested through scientific method '''b:''' such knowledge or such a system of knowledge concerned with the physical world and its phenomena }}<!--{{sfn|Popper|2002|p=3}}--></ref>。
** [[自然科學|自然科學]]的一個分支：自然科學是[[科學|科學]]的一個重大分支，憑着{{link-en|經驗證據|empirical evidence}}，試圖解釋和預測大自然的現象。在自然科學，[[假說|假說]]一定要先科學地確證，才可當作科學理論。有效性、準確性、和確保品質的社會機制，例如同行評審和結果的可重複性，是達到目的所用的其中一些準則和方法。自然科學可以分成兩大分支：[[生命科學|生命科學]]和物理科學。這兩個分支、及其下的所有分支，都被稱為自然科學。
