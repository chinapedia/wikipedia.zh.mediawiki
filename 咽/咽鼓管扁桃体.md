'''咽鼓管扁桃體'''({{lang|en|tubal tonsil}})為組成[[瓦爾代爾氏扁桃體環|瓦爾代爾氏扁桃體環]](Waldeyer's tonsillar ring)的四大[[扁桃腺|扁桃腺]]群之一，其中包括有[[腭扁桃體|腭扁桃體]](Palatine tonsil)，[[舌扁桃體|舌扁桃體]](lingual tonsils)，[[咽扁桃體|咽扁桃體]](Adenoid/pharyngeal tonsil)及"咽鼓管扁桃體"。 

咽鼓管扁桃體非常接近[[咽鼓管圓枕|咽鼓管圓枕]](Torus tubarius)，<ref>Michael Tam,"The Pharynx"[http://download.videohelp.com/vitualis/med/pharynx.htm],Medical Student's Retreat-Anatomy Notes,Last updated 30 March 2006.</ref> 這就是為什麼這種扁桃體有時也被稱為"咽鼓管圓枕扁桃體"(tonsil of torus tubarius)。<ref>English Arabic Dictionary of Medical terms,"tonsil of torus tubarius = tubal tonsil"[http://www.almaany.com/home.php?language=english&lang_name=English&category=Medical&word=tonsil+of+torus+tubarius++%3D++tubal+tonsil],Almaany.com,2010-2014.</ref> 咽鼓管扁桃體與扁桃體有等同的"圓環"(torus)，但是可能被視為不正確或不準確的稱呼。 

咽鼓管扁桃體位於[[耳咽管|耳咽管]]的開口進入[[鼻咽|鼻咽]](nasopharynx)的後方。<ref name="isbn0805355111">{{cite book |author=Patricia Brady Wilhelm; Marieb, Elaine Nicpon; Mallatt, Jon |title=Human anatomy |publisher=Pearson/Benjamin Cummings |location=San Francisco |year=2005 |isbn=0-8053-5511-1 |oclc= |doi= |page=572}}</ref>
