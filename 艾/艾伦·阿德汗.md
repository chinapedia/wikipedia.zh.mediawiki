'''艾伦·阿德汗'''（{{lang-en|'''Allen Adham'''}}），是[[埃及裔|埃及裔]]的美国商人。1991年，他与[[麥可·莫懷米|麥可·莫懷米]]、[[弗兰克·皮尔斯|弗兰克·皮尔斯]]成立游戏公司Silicon & Synapse（后公司改名为[[暴雪娱乐|暴雪娱乐]]）。阿德汗曾参与创作[[失落的维京人|失落的维京人]]，RPM赛车，摇滚赛车，[[魔兽争霸：人类与兽人|魔兽争霸：人类与兽人]]，[[星际争霸|星际争霸]]，他也是魔兽世界的首席设计师。

1990年，艾伦·阿德汗从加州大学洛杉矶分校获得了学士学位。<ref name="UCLAHonorDinner2006_11_06">{{cite web | url=http://www.engineer.ucla.edu/newsroom/featured-news/archive/2006/november/ucla-engineering-celebrates-accomplishments-at-annual-awards-dinner | title=UCLA Engineering Celebrates Accomplishments at Annual Awards Dinner | publisher=[[UCLA|UCLA]] Henry Samueli School of Engineering and Applied Science | date=2006-11-06 | accessdate=2013-06-05 | author=M. Abraham | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110716201249/http://www.engineer.ucla.edu/newsroom/featured-news/archive/2006/november/ucla-engineering-celebrates-accomplishments-at-annual-awards-dinner | archivedate=2011-07-16 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.blizzard.com/register/blizzard/ |title=Blizzard Entertainment 10th Anniversary Celebration |accessdate=2013-06-05 |publisher=Blizzard Entertainment |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071012162917/http://blizzard.com/register/blizzard/ |archivedate=2007-10-12 |deadurl=no }}</ref>

2004年，艾伦·阿德汗离开暴雪成立Tenfold Capital Partners，但他仍然是暴雪娱乐的一名顾问。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.gamespot.com/news/6086629.html |title=Gamespot - Blizzard confirms Allen Adham departure |accessdate=2011-02-11}}</ref>

2016年，再度加入暴雪。
