'''艾玛·罗伊纳·盖特伍德'''（{{lang-en|Emma Rowena Gatewood}}，{{bd|1887年|10月25日|1973年|6月4日|catIdx=G}}<ref name="Gallia County Genealogical Socity">[http://www.galliagenealogy.org/Obituaries/obits_g.htm Gallia County Genealogical Socity] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080412212932/http://www.galliagenealogy.org/Obituaries/obits_g.htm |date=2008-04-12 }}</ref>，常被称为'''盖特伍德奶奶'''，{{lang-en|Grandma Gatewood}}），超轻徒步先驱，她是首位独自徒步走完[[阿帕拉契小径|阿帕拉契小径]]的女性，仅耗时三个月。<ref name=Sierra>{{cite news |first= |last= |authorlink= |coauthors= |title=When Grandma Gatewood hiked the Appalachian Trail |url=http://www.accessmylibrary.com/coms2/summary_0286-26641964_ITM |quote=When Grandma Gatewood hiked the Appalachian Trail in 1955, she tramped all 2170 miles in tennis shoes, her only shelter an army blanket, a raincoat, ... |work=[[Sierra_Club|Sierra Club]] |date=November/December 2002 |accessdate=2009-05-08 }}</ref>

== 家庭生活 ==
[[File:Emma_Gatewood_458x604.jpg|thumb]]
盖特伍德生于[[俄亥俄州|俄亥俄州]][[高卢县_(俄亥俄州)|高卢县]][[:en:Guyan_Township,_Gallia_County,_Ohio|古堰乡]]，她是一位农夫的妻子，育有11个子女，截至其去世前，她有24个孙辈，30个重孙，1个玄孙。<ref name="Gallia County Genealogical Socity"/>结婚后，她长期受到家庭暴力的影响。<ref>{{cite book |title=Grandma Gatewood's Walk: The Inspiring Story of the Woman Who Saved the Appalachian Trail |publisher=Chicago Review Press |author=Ben Montgomery |year=2014 |isbn=978-1613747186}}
</ref>

== 徒步 ==
1955年，67岁的盖特伍德前往徒步[[阿帕拉契小径|阿帕拉契小径]]，她穿着[[:en:Keds|Keds]]运动鞋，她把一块军用毛毯、一件雨衣、一块塑料浴帘装在自家缝制的背包里，然后单肩背包徒步，由此她成了[[:en:Ultralight_backpacking|超轻背包]]的先驱。当她到达[[康涅狄格州|康涅狄格州]]后，本地报纸刊登了她的故事，[[运动画刊|运动画刊]]跟进报道<ref>[http://vault.sportsillustrated.cnn.com/vault/article/magazine/MAG1130083/index.htm SI Vault]</ref> ，偶后她还在[[今天_(NBC)|今日秀]]中露脸。

后来她在[[国家地理|国家地理]]中说，"I thought it would be a nice lark," she said, adding "It wasn't." Another time she complained "For some fool reason, they always lead you right up over the biggest rock to the top of the biggest mountain they can find."<ref name=Sierra/>

1960年、1963年（时年75岁），她又两次徒步了[[阿帕拉契小径|阿帕拉契小径]]，最后一次她分段完成了徒步。<ref name=Sierra/>2007年以前，她一直是徒步阿帕拉契小径的最年长女性。<ref>[http://www.appalachiantrail.org/site/c.jkLXJ8MQKtH/b.851151/k.4C05/Noteworthy_2000Milers.htm ''Appalachian Trail Conservancy'' Noteworthy 2000-Milers] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080609082349/http://www.appalachiantrail.org/site/c.jkLXJ8MQKtH/b.851151/k.4C05/Noteworthy_2000Milers.htm |date=2008-06-09 }}</ref> 

另外，她还徒步了2000英里（3200公里）的[[奧勒岡小徑|俄勒冈小径]]，从[[密苏里州|密苏里州]][[独立城_(密苏里州)|独立城]]到[[俄勒冈州|俄勒冈州]][[波特兰_(俄勒冈州)|波特兰]]，平均每天徒步22英里（35公里）。这样她徒步了美国大陆地区的所有州。

1970年，时年83，她在[[弗吉尼亚州|弗吉尼亚州]]奥克顿访问阿帕拉契旅行用品商店时，有人询问她对于当时轻量背包装备的看法，她建议：“制作一个雨衣，一个单肩背包，再买双结实的Keds网球鞋。在当地杂货店买好维也纳香肠……其他吃的你可以在路边找到……and by the way those wild onions are not called "Ramps"... they are "Rampians" ... a ramp is an inclined plane.”

盖特伍德是National Campers 、Hikers Association和the Roanoke Appalachian Trail Club的终生会员，同时她也是Director Emeritus和the Buckeye Trail Association的终生会员。<ref name="Gallia County Genealogical Socity"/>

== 纪念 ==
在俄亥俄州[[:en:Hocking_Hills_State_Park|Hocking Hills州立公园]]，[[:en:North_Country_Trail|North Country Trail]]、[[:en:Buckeye_Trail|uckeye Trail]]和the [[:en:American_Discovery_Trail|American Discovery Trail]]重合的6英里部分，被指定为盖特伍德奶奶小径（Grandma Gatewood Trail），这条小径连接了Old Man's Cave和Cedar Falls to Ash Cave。<ref>[http://www.hcta.org/parks/parks.htm Hocking County Tourism Association]</ref><ref>[http://www.trails.com/tcatalog_trail.aspx?trailid=HGM072-036 Grandma Gatewood Trail - Old Man's Cave to Ash Cave | Logan Ohio Hikes | Trails.com]</ref>
