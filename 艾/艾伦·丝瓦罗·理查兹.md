'''艾伦·亨丽埃塔·丝瓦罗·理查兹'''（{{lang-en|'''Ellen Henrietta Swallow Richards'''}}，{{bd|1842年|12月3日|1911年|3月30日|catIdx=R}}）是一位19世纪美国的[[化学工业|工业]]和[[环境化学|环境化学家]]。她在卫生工程方面的开创性工作和在国内的科学实验研究，为新学科[[家政学|家政学]]奠定了基础。<ref name=obit>{{cite news |author= |coauthors= |title=Mrs. Ellen H. Richards Dead. Head of Social Economics in Massachusetts Institute of Technology  |url=http://query.nytimes.com/mem/archive-free/pdf?res=F40810FA3F5D16738DDDA80B94DB405B818DF1D3 |quote= |newspaper=[[New_York_Times|New York Times]] |date=1911-03-31 |accessdate=2014-03-08 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://tps.cr.nps.gov/nhl/detail.cfm?ResourceId=2135&ResourceType=Building |title=Richards, Ellen Swallow, Residence |date=1991-04-07 |accessdate=2013-09-04 |quote= |publisher=National Historic Landmarks Program |location= |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121011063112/http://tps.cr.nps.gov/nhl/detail.cfm?ResourceId=2135&ResourceType=Building |archivedate=2012年10月11日 }}</ref>她是[[家政学|家政学]]运动的创始人，其特点是将科学应用到家庭，还首先使用化学来进行营养研究。<ref name=mozans>{{cite book|last1=Mozans|first1=H. J.|title=Woman in science|date=1913 |publisher=University of Notre Dame Press|location=London|isbn=0-268-01946-0|page=217}}</ref>

1862年理查兹从{{le|韦斯特福德学院|Westford Academy}}（位于马萨诸塞州历史第二悠久的中学）毕业。后来她考上[[麻省理工学院|麻省理工学院]]，成为考上该校的第一位女性。1873年毕业后她成为了第一位女教授。<ref name=obit/><ref>{{cite news |author= |coauthors= |title=Campus Life: M.I.T.; Salute to Women At a School Once 99.6% Male |url=http://www.nytimes.com/1991/04/07/nyregion/campus-life-mit-salute-to-women-at-a-school-once-99.6-male.html |quote=When Ellen Swallow Richards came to the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in January 1871, she was the first woman to attend the institute, then based in Boston. |newspaper=[[New_York_Times|New York Times]] |date= 1991-04-07|accessdate=2014-03-08 }}</ref>理查兹夫人是美国第一位被科学技术类学校录取的女性，也是第一位获得[[化学|化学]]学位的女性，1870年她从[[瓦萨学院|瓦萨学院]]获得化学学位。<ref>{{cite encyclopedia |encyclopedia=Encyclopedia of Women and American Politics  |coauthors= |title=Ellen Swallow Richards |year= |publisher= |page= |quote= | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=cVtFJ5tvINsC&pg=PA387&lpg=PA387&dq=%22first+woman+to+earn+a+degree+in+chemistry%22&source=bl&ots=Y_P1-rvlze&sig=pfbf2WxVAO6HAxE5ff9nMr-gc_o&hl=en&sa=X&ei=arQXU5uGMIXq0AGY-oGYDw&ved=0CCQQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q=%22first%20woman%20to%20earn%20a%20degree%20in%20chemistry%22&f=false |isbn= }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.chemheritage.org/discover/online-resources/chemistry-in-history/themes/public-and-environmental-health/public-health-and-safety/richards-e.aspx |title=Ellen H. Swallow Richards |date= |accessdate=2012-09-12 |quote= |publisher=Chemical Heritage Foundation |location= }}</ref>

理查兹是一位务实的[[女权主义者|女权主义者]]。她创立了[[生態女性主義|生態女性主義]]，认为妇女在家庭中的工作是经济的一个重要方面。<ref>{{cite journal | author = Richardson, Barbara | title = Ellen Swallow Richards: 'Humanistic Oekologist,' 'Applied Sociologist,' and the Founding of Sociology | journal = American Sociologist | year = 2002 | volume = 33 | pages = 21–58 | doi = 10.1007/s12108-002-1010-6 | issue = 3 }}</ref>
