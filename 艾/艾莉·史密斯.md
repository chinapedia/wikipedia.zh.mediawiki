'''艾莉·史密斯'''（Ali Smith，{{bd|1962年|8月24日}}），[[蘇格蘭|蘇格蘭]][[作家|作家]]。

==生平==
艾莉·史密斯雙親是工人階級<ref name="Jeanette_Winters">{{cite web|url=http://www.jeanettewinterson.com/pages/journalism_01/journalism_01_item.asp?journalism_01ID=90|title=Ali Smith|last=Winterson|first=Jeanette|date=25 April 2003|publisher=The Times|accessdate=2009-02-28|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110611160537/http://www.jeanettewinterson.com/pages/journalism_01/journalism_01_item.asp?journalism_01ID=90|archivedate=2011年6月11日}}</ref>，定居於劍橋<ref name="Contemporary_Writers" /><ref name="Inverness_Courier">{{cite news|url=http://www.inverness-courier.co.uk/news/fullstory.php/aid/2474/Novel_approach_struck_a_chord_with_Inverness_writer.html|title=Novel approach struck a chord with Inverness writer|last=Matthews|first=Elizabeth|date=30 March 2007|publisher=The Inverness Courier|accessdate=2009-02-27|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070927040803/http://www.inverness-courier.co.uk/news/fullstory.php/aid/2474/Novel_approach_struck_a_chord_with_Inverness_writer.html|archivedate=2007年9月27日}}</ref> 。
