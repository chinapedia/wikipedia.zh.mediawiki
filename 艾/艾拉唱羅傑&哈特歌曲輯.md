'''''艾拉唱羅傑&哈特歌曲輯'''''（Ella Fitzgerald Sings the Rodgers and Hart Songbook）是1956年美國爵士女歌手[[艾拉·費茲傑拉|艾拉·費茲傑拉]]與由巴迪·布雷格曼（Buddy Bregman）指揮的室內管弦樂團合作錄製的專輯，演唱由[[理查·勞傑斯|理查·勞傑斯]]作曲，[[勞倫茲·哈特|勞倫茲·哈特]]作詞的作品。

This album was inducted into the [[Grammy_Hall_of_Fame_Award|Grammy Hall of Fame]] in 1999, which is a special Grammy award established in 1973 to honor recordings that are at least twenty-five years old, and that have "qualitative or historical significance."<ref>[http://www.grammy.org/recording-academy/awards/hall-of-fame Grammy Hall of Fame Database]</ref>
