'''艾索倫太陽能館'''位於[[美國|美國]][[伊利诺伊州|伊利诺伊州]][[庫克縣_(伊利諾伊州)|庫克縣]][[芝加哥|芝加哥]]市[[卢普区|卢普区]]的[[千禧公園|千禧公園]]內，是利用太陽能產生電力的小型建築物，共有東南西北角落四座。展館每年產生 19840千瓦小時kWh（67,697MBTU）的電力，價值約每年$2350美元。<ref name=cost>{{cite web|url=http://www.eia.doe.gov/electricity/epm/table5_6_a.html | title = Average Retail Price of Electricity to Ultimate Customers by End-Use Sector, by State | publisher = [[Energy_Information_Administration|Energy Information Administration]], [[United_States_Department_of_Energy|United States Department of Energy]] | date = 2010-11-15 | accessdate = 2010-12-14}}</ref>

name=A&AEPMPWCaGE>{{cite web|url=http://www.millenniumpark.org/artandarchitecture/exelon_pavilions.html|title=Art & Architecture: Exelon Pavilions: Millennium Park Welcome Center and Garage
