'''密拉特县'''（'''{{lang|en|Meerut District}}'''）为[[印度|印度]][[北方邦|北方邦]][[密拉特专区|密拉特专区]]辖[[县|县]]，行政中心驻[[密拉特|密拉特市]]，领域面积共2,522平方千米，于1818年[[英属印度|英属印度]]时期设立<ref name=imp>[http://dsal.uchicago.edu/reference/gazetteer/pager.html?objectid=DS405.1.I34_V17_262.gif Meerut District – History] ''The Imperial Gazetteer of India'', 1909, v. 17, p. 256-257, 261.</ref><ref name="kinship">{{cite book|title=Kinship and Urbanization: White Collar Migrants in North India|author=Sylvia Vatuk|publisher=University of California Press|year=1972|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=cQf7hqm-iXcC|isbn=9780520020641|page=2}}</ref>。根据2011年的统计数据，该县的人口为3,443,689，城镇化率是51.07%<ref>{{cite web |title=District Census 2011 |url=http://www.census2011.co.in/district.php |website=Census 2011 |accessdate=2019-02-27}}</ref>。

该县土壤肥沃，适合种植[[蔬菜|蔬菜]]和[[粮食|粮食]]作物<ref name=ch3>{{cite web|title=Chapter 3 – Findings: Metro Cities of India|url=http://www.cpcb.nic.in/EnvironmetalPlanning/ground/Chapter3.pdf|publisher=Central Pollution Control Board|accessdate=1 April 2011|pages=63|format=pdf|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150923210838/http://www.cpcb.nic.in/EnvironmetalPlanning/ground/Chapter3.pdf|archive-date=2015-09-23|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。
