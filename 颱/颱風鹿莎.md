'''台风鹿莎'''（-{zh-cn:{{lang|en|Typhoon Rusa}}; zh-tw:{{lang|en|Typhoon Rusa}}; zh-hk:{{lang|en|Typhoon Rusa}}; zh-mo:{{lang|pt|Tufão Rusa}}}-）是[[2002年太平洋台风季|2002年太平洋台风季]]期间[[联合台风警报中心|联合台风警报中心]]发现的第21个[[热带气旋|热带低气压]]，也是该季第15场获得命名的风暴和第10场台风，是43年来袭击[[大韩民国|大韩民国]]的最强[[台风|台风]]。系统于8月22日由西北[[太平洋|太平洋]]的[[季风槽|季风槽]]发展形成，具体位置离[[日本|日本]]东南部还有较远距离。气旋连续数日向西北方向移动，逐渐增强成强劲台风。8月26日，风暴行经日本[[奄美群岛|奄美群岛]]，导致当地2人死亡，约2万人失去供电。台风令日本普降暴雨，降雨量最高的[[德岛县|德岛县]]高达902毫米。

接下来鹿莎有小幅减弱，然后以[[最大持续风速|10分钟持续风速]]140公里强度[[登陆_(气象学)|登陆]]韩国[[高兴郡|高兴郡]]{{#tag:ref|[[气象厅_(日本)|日本气象厅]]采用10分钟持续风速衡量[[热带气旋|热带气旋]]强度，而[[联合台风警报中心|联合台风警报中心]]则是采用1分钟持续风速，两者间的换算系数为1.14<ref name="windchange">{{cite web | publisher = United States Navy | deadurl = no | year = 2009 | url = http://www.nrlmry.navy.mil/~chu/chap6/se200.htm | title = Section 2 Intensity Observation and Forecast Errors | accessdate = 2013-12-19 | archivedate = 2013-05-10 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20130510175941/http://www.nrlmry.navy.mil/~chu/chap6/se200.htm}}</ref>。|group="注"}}。由于气温较高，并且附近还有[[冷锋|冷锋]]破坏大气环境稳定性，台风的强度保持了一段时间。鹿莎在行经韩国期间减弱，给当地带去瓢泼大雨，降雨量最高的[[江陵市|江陵市]]达到897.5毫米。该市的24小时降雨量为880毫米，创下韩国日降雨量的新纪录，不过最大的降雨量只出现在局部地区。风暴在韩国至少夺走了233人的生命，创下至少43年来的最高纪录，经济损失高达42亿美元（2002年[[美元|美元]]，相当于{{#time:Y}}年的{{Formatprice|{{Inflation|US|4200000000|2002|r=2}}}}美元），受损房屋超过1万7000套，还有大面积农田被淹。邻近的[[朝鲜|朝鲜]]也降下暴雨，致使3人丧生，2万6000人无家可归。鹿莎来袭时，该国正陷入饥荒，台风又摧毁了大面积的庄稼，令灾情雪上加霜。9月1日，气旋在[[俄罗斯|俄罗斯]]东部上空转变成[[温带气旋|温带气旋]]，最终在3天后消散。

== 气象历史 ==
{{storm path|Rusa 2002 track.png}}
8月22日，[[比基尼环礁|比基尼环礁]]以北、[[威克岛|威克岛]]西南方向的[[季风槽|季风槽]]催生出一股[[热带气旋|热带低气压]]<ref name="jma" /><ref name="atcr">{{cite report | author = Naval Pacific Meteorology and Oceanography Center | author2 = Joint Typhoon Warning Center | publisher = United States Navy, United States Airforce | accessdate = 2015-03-18 | title = Typhoon (TY) 21W (Rusa) | type = 2002 Annual Tropical Cyclone Report | page = 119 | archivedate = 2013-02-21 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20130221071850/http://www.usno.navy.mil/NOOC/nmfc-ph/RSS/jtwc/atcr/2002atcr.pdf | url = http://www.usno.navy.mil/NOOC/nmfc-ph/RSS/jtwc/atcr/2002atcr.pdf | format = PDF}}</ref>。系统向[[罗盘方位|西北偏西]]移动，并且其存在的大部分时间里都会保持这一前进方向。8月23日清晨，气旋在[[关岛|关岛]]以东约1800公里海域<ref name="hydrol">{{cite journal | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20130927075816/http://www.hydrol-earth-syst-sci-discuss.net/3/3147/2006/hessd-3-3147-2006.pdf | journal = Hydrology and Earth System Sciences Discussions | author = N. W. Kim | author2 = Y. S. Won | author3 = I. M. Chung | date = 2006-10-09 | title = The scale of typhoon Rusa | volume = 3 | issue = 5 | pages = 3148–3157 | accessdate = 2015-03-18 | url = http://www.hydrol-earth-syst-sci-discuss.net/3/3147/2006/hessd-3-3147-2006.pdf | format = PDF | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2013-09-27}}</ref>增强成热带风暴并获名“鹿莎”（{{lang|en|Rusa}}）<ref name="jma">{{cite report | author = RSMC Tokyo – Typhoon Center | publisher = Japan Meteorological Agency | page = 22/44 | type = Annual Report on Activities of the RSMC Tokyo – Typhoon Center 2002 | title = Tropical Cyclones 2002: TY Rusa (0215) | accessdate = 2015-03-18 | url = http://www.jma.go.jp/jma/jma-eng/jma-center/rsmc-hp-pub-eg/AnnualReport/2002/Text/Text2002.pdf | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131014032942/http://www.jma.go.jp/jma/jma-eng/jma-center/rsmc-hp-pub-eg/AnnualReport/2002/Text/Text2002.pdf | archivedate = 2013-10-14 | deadurl = no | format = PDF}}</ref>。[[协调世界时|协调世界时]]8月25日下午18点，[[气象厅_(日本)|日本气象厅]]{{#tag:ref|日本气象厅是西太平洋的[[区域专责气象中心|区域专责气象中心]]<ref name="jma" />。|group="注"}}将正位于[[北马里亚纳群岛|北马里亚纳群岛]]东北方向洋面的系统升级成[[台风|台风]]。次日，该机构估计鹿莎达到[[最大持续风速|10分钟持续风速]]每小时150公里的最高强度。<ref name="jma" />与此同时，[[联合台风警报中心|联合台风警报中心]]{{#tag:ref|[[联合台风警报中心|联合台风警报中心]]是[[美国海军|美国海军]]和[[美国空军|空军]]共同组建的工作组，会向西太平洋及其他海域发布[[热带气旋警告|热带气旋警告]]<ref>{{cite web | publisher = United States Navy, United States Airforce | author = Joint Typhoon Warning Center | title = Joint Typhoon Warning Center Mission Statement | year = 2011 | accessdate = 2012-07-25 | url = https://metocph.nmci.navy.mil/jtwc/menu/JTWC_mission.html | archivedate = 2009-08-12 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20090812132014/http://metocph.nmci.navy.mil/jtwc/menu/JTWC_mission.html | deadurl = yes}}</ref>。|group="注"}}估计台风达到的最高强度为1分钟持续风速每小时215公里<ref name="atcr" />。

鹿莎以最高强度吹袭日本[[奄美大岛|奄美大岛]]<ref name="guy">{{cite report | author = Guy Carpenter | date = 2003-01-30 | title = Tropical Cyclone Review 2002 | publisher = Marsh & McLennan Companies | accessdate = 2012-07-25 | url = http://gcportal.guycarp.com/portal/extranet/popup/pdf/GCPub/tropcyc_02.pdf | format = PDF | page = 21 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120206042923/http://gcportal.guycarp.com/portal/extranet/popup/pdf/GCPub/tropcyc_02.pdf | deadurl = yes | archivedate = 2012-02-06}}</ref>。在此强度保持约12小时后，台风略有减弱并继续向西北偏西方向前进，但到了8月28日，日本气象厅再度报告鹿莎的10分钟持续风速达到每小时150公里<ref name="jma" />。由于[[风切变|风切变]]很少，[[海面温度|海面温度]]达到29[[摄氏度|°C]]，风暴并没有像气象部门预计的那样减弱，其强度在穿越日本南部期间基本保持稳定<ref name="hydrol" />。8月29日，气旋从[[奄美群岛|奄美群岛]]和日本之间洋面经过，并在这一过程中再次出现小幅弱化。鹿莎随后转向北上逼近[[朝鲜半岛|朝鲜半岛]]<ref name="jma" />。半岛上空的暖湿空气朝风暴方向吹去，还有逐渐逼近的[[冷锋|冷锋]]使大气环境变得不稳定，这些因素都使台风的强度不会大幅降低<ref name="hydrol" />。{{lang|en|[[协调世界时|UTC]]}}8月31日上午8点左右，鹿莎以10分钟持续风力时速140公里强度从[[大韩民国|大韩民国]][[高兴郡|高兴郡]][[登陆_(气象学)|登陆]]<ref name="jma" /><ref name="hydrol" />。根据联合台风警报中心的数据，鹿莎是1959年以来袭击韩国的最强台风<ref name="atcr" />。风暴在经过韩国期间迅速减弱，于9月1日清晨降级成热带低气压<ref name="jma" />。联合台风警报中心在几乎同一时间发布针对该天气系统的最后一份公告<ref name="atcr" />。低气压转向东北，经过[[日本海|日本海]]后于9月1日晚在[[俄属远东|俄属远东]][[滨海边疆区|滨海边疆区]]上空转变成[[温带气旋|温带气旋]]。系统残留继续向东北方向移动，于9月4日在[[堪察加半岛|堪察加半岛]]上空消散。<ref name="jma" />

== 防灾措施和影响 ==
日本是第一个受台风鹿莎冲击的国家<ref name="guy" />，其威胁促使[[冲绳县|冲绳县]]政府取消岛上的灾害演习<ref>{{cite news | agency = Kyodo News Service | date = 2002-08-28 | title = Okinawa disaster drill with U.S. forces canceled}}{{subscription required | via = Lexis Nexis}}</ref>。岛上有两名[[美国海军陆战队|美国海军陆战队员]]因遭遇大浪而失踪<ref>{{cite news | agency = Kyodo News Service | date = 2002-08-30 | title = 2 U.S. Marines missing in stormy seas in Okinawa}}{{subscription required | via = Lexis Nexis}}</ref>，之后的新闻报道中推定两人已经遇难<ref>{{cite news | agency = Associated Press | newspaper = Times Colonialist | date = 2002-09-01 | title = Rusa batters South Korea}}{{subscription required | via = Lexis Nexis}}</ref>。阿南群岛上有6套房屋被毁，38人不得不撤离。风暴导致2万人失去供电，许多航班被迫取消<ref>{{cite news | agency = Associated Press | date = 2002-08-29 | title = Typhoon Rusa flattens homes in southwestern Japan}}{{subscription required | via = Lexis Nexis}}</ref>。[[长崎县|长崎县]][[野母崎町|野母崎町]]的风速达到每小时104公里，日本的降水持续了7天之久，其中降雨量最高的[[德岛县|德岛县]]达902毫米。[[奈良县|奈良县]]的一处气象站测得的一小时降雨量高达84毫米。该国有至少275套房屋被淹，137套受损。鹿莎沿途导致12人受伤，其中4人重伤<ref name="dt">{{cite report | publisher = Digital Typhoon | title = Typhoon 200215 (RUSA) – Disaster Information | accessdate = 2015-03-18 | url = http://agora.ex.nii.ac.jp/cgi-bin/dt/dsummary.pl?id=200215&basin=wnp&lang=en}}</ref>。台风还给[[臺灣本島|臺灣本島]]海岸沿线挾带细雨与长浪<ref>{{cite news | author = Deborah Kuo | agency = Taiwan Central News Agency | date = 2002-08-30 | title = Typhoon Rusa Brings Rains to Western, Northeastern Taiwan}}{{subscription required | via = Lexis Nexis}}</ref>。

邻近的[[朝鲜|朝鲜]]也受到风暴影响，[[江原道_(朝鲜)|江原道]]出现时速72公里的大风，当地山区的降雨量达到700毫米；[[高城郡_(朝鲜)|高城郡]]的总计降雨量为530毫米。<ref name="afp92" />降水触发山洪，[[地表径流|地表径流]]也相应增多。该国一个月前也经受过类似的暴雨摧残，鹿莎的到来更是使得局势进一步恶化。<ref name="ifrc04">{{cite report | date = 2004-01-08 | title = DPR Korea: Flash Floods/Typhoon Appeal No. 22/02 Final Report | archivedate = 2014-08-08 | deadurl = no | publisher = United Nations Office for the Coordination of Humanitarian Affairs | accessdate = 2015-03-18 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140808054015/http://reliefweb.int/report/democratic-peoples-republic-korea/dpr-korea-flash-floodstyphoon-appeal-no-2202-final-report | agency = International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies | url = http://reliefweb.int/report/democratic-peoples-republic-korea/dpr-korea-flash-floodstyphoon-appeal-no-2202-final-report}}</ref>降雨令数以千计的房屋和许多公共建筑受损<ref name="kcna">{{cite news | agency = Korean Central News Agency | publisher = United Nations Office for the Coordination of Humanitarian Affairs | date = 2002-09-02 | title = Damage caused by typhoon in DPRK | accessdate = 2015-03-18 | archivedate = 2015-03-18 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150318145300/http://reliefweb.int/report/democratic-peoples-republic-korea/damage-caused-typhoon-dprk | url = http://reliefweb.int/report/democratic-peoples-republic-korea/damage-caused-typhoon-dprk}}</ref>，还摧毁了8万6000吨农作物<ref name="afp94" />。朝鲜这时正遭受[[朝鲜饥荒|严重的饥荒]]，因此这场灾害无疑是雪上加霜<ref name="afp92">{{cite news | agency = Agence France-Presse | publisher = United Nations Office for the Coordination of Humanitarian Affairs | title = Storm leaves close to 200 dead or missing in South Korea, scores more killed in North | author = C.W. Lim | accessdate = 2015-03-18 | archivedate = 2015-03-18 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150318145744/http://reliefweb.int/report/democratic-peoples-republic-korea/storm-leaves-close-200-dead-or-missing-south-korea-scores | url = http://reliefweb.int/report/democratic-peoples-republic-korea/storm-leaves-close-200-dead-or-missing-south-korea-scores}}</ref>。[[江原道_(朝鲜)|江原道]]受灾最为严重，全国共有[[朝鲜民主主义人民共和国行政区划|4道和1个特别市]]受到影响。虽然该国提前发布了警报以便居民疏散，但还是有超过2万6000人流离失所。<ref name="ifrc04" />鹿莎摧毁了朝鲜的25公里道路和24座桥梁，但大多是发生在局部地区<ref name="afp94">{{cite news | agency = Agence France-Presse | date = 2002-09-04 | title = Thousands homeless in North Korea from typhoon: aid agencies | publisher = United Nations Office for the Coordination of Humanitarian Affairs | accessdate = 2015-03-18 | archivedate = 2014-08-08 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140808052410/http://reliefweb.int/report/democratic-peoples-republic-korea/thousands-homeless-north-korea-typhoon-aid-agencies | url = http://reliefweb.int/report/democratic-peoples-republic-korea/thousands-homeless-north-korea-typhoon-aid-agencies}}</ref>。该国共有3人遇难<ref name="ifrc04" />。

[[俄属远东|俄属远东]]也受到这场台风的影响<ref name="guy" />。鹿莎的残留给[[库页岛|库页岛]]带去暴雨，降雨量相当于当地平均两个月的总和，产生的洪水令350套房屋被淹，但没有造成人员丧生<ref>{{cite news | agency = RIA Novosti | date = 2002-09-04 | title = Typhoon Rusa Hits Sakhalin}}{{subscription required | via = Lexis Nexis}}</ref>。

== 余波 ==
风暴过去后，韩国的许多受损建筑物导致河流受到化学品和重金属的污染<ref name="guy" />。该国派遣3万名军人协助清理工作并修复风暴造成的破坏。[[韩国总统|总统]][[金大中|金大中]]授权动用应急资金提供救灾援助。江陵市大部分地区停水断电，为此政府将救灾物资送到灾民手中<ref name="afp92" />。受鹿莎袭击10天后，供电已基本修复，交通也恢复正常。韩国[[国际红十字与红新月运动|红十字会]]在呼吁国民慷慨解囊后收到了580亿韩圆（相当于2002年的4900万美元和{{#time:Y}}年的{{Formatprice|{{Inflation|US|49000000|2002|r=2}}}}美元）捐款，其中大部分来自该国西北部地区居民。红十字会向1万6919户家庭提供了5万零680顿膳食，还有提供衣物和烹饪用具。<ref name="ifrc99" />韩国居民筹集了约721亿韩圆（相当于2002年的6000万美元和{{#time:Y}}年的{{Formatprice|{{Inflation|US|60000000|2002|r=2}}}}美元）的救灾援助款，创下该国的新纪录<ref>{{cite news | title = Nation Raises W72 Bil. for Flood Victims | date = 2002-09-12 | newspaper = The Korea Times}}{{subscription required | via = Lexis Nexis}}</ref>。[[中国红十字会|中国红十字会]]在风暴过去数周后向韩国红十字会提供了2万美元<ref>{{cite news | newspaper = The Korea Herald | title = China sends aid to flood victims | date = 2002-09-11}}{{subscription required | via = Lexis Nexis}}</ref>。9月13日，[[韩国政府|韩国政府]]宣布全国203个市县沦为灾区<ref>{{cite news | newspaper = The Korea Herald | date = 2002-09-14 | title = Government names 203 areas as disaster zones}}{{subscription required | via = Lexis Nexis}}</ref>，有8714户受灾家庭因此有资格取得政府贷款<ref>{{cite news | date = 2002-10-15 | title = S.Korea to provide low-interest loans to typhoon victims | agency = Xinhua}}{{subscription required | via = Lexis Nexis}}</ref>。风暴造成的破坏及之前的洪灾导致[[韩国经济|韩国经济]]在2002年第三季度出现萎缩<ref>{{cite news | agency = Agence France-Presse | date = 2002-11-22 | title = South Korean growth slows in third quarter}}{{subscription required | via = Lexis Nexis}}</ref>。该国非寿险类保险公司2003[[财政年度|财政年度]]的报告显示有3亿美元的[[赤字|赤字]]，其中大部分都是因这场台风引起<ref>{{cite news | date = 2004-09-15 | title = Loss in Reinsurance Business | newspaper = The Korea Times}}{{subscription required | via = Lexis Nexis}}</ref>。鹿莎造成的农作物损失导致米价上扬，达到1980年以来的最高价<ref>{{cite news | date = 2003-02-13 | newspaper = The Korea Herald | title = Rice output cost hits highest level}}{{subscription required | via = Lexis Nexis}}</ref>。风暴过后的一年里，韩国政府致力于重建受损道路，还每月向失去家园的家庭提供救济金<ref>{{cite news | author = Andrew Petty | title = Still weathering the storm | date = 2003-08-01 | newspaper = The Korea Herald}}{{subscription required | via = Lexis Nexis}}</ref>。但还是有许多居民无家可归，只能住在临时避难所。该国的[[国际仁人家园|国际仁人家园]]于2003年为风暴灾民建造了69套房屋，但这只能够满足很少的一部分灾民需要<ref>{{cite news | date = 2003-08-01 | title = What a difference a week makes | newspaper = The Korea Herald}}{{subscription required | via = Lexis Nexis}}</ref>。鹿莎来袭约1年后，[[台风梅米|台风梅米]]又以较高强度吹袭韩国，导致117人死亡和价格37亿4000万美元的破坏。虽然梅米对该国造成的损失数额不及鹿莎，但对工业区的破坏程度更为严重。<ref>{{cite report | author = Guy Carpenter | publisher = Marsh & McLennan Companies | date = 红十字会向朝鲜灾民提供补给，该机构的国际救灾基金提供了7万5000[[瑞士法郎|瑞士法郎]]（相当于2002年的5万美元和{{#time:Y}}年的{{Formatprice|{{Inflation|US|50000|2002|r=2}}}}美元）<ref>{{cite web | publisher = Oanda Corporation | year = 2012 | title = 历史汇率 | accessdate = 2015-03-19 | url = http://www.oanda.com/lang/cns/currency/historical-rates/}}</ref>。政府出动军人协助[[搜索及拯救|搜救]]任务，修复受损的基础设施。朝鲜红十字发放了超过210万净水药片和超过1万1000个储水容器，还向灾民提供了3万2753张毛毯和4931套厨具<ref name="ifrc04" />。风暴过去后，流离失所的居民前往邻居或避难所中暂居<ref>{{cite report | date = 2003-02-05 | title = DPR Korea: Appeal No. 01.38/2002 Programme Update No. 02 | publisher = United Nations Office for the Coordination of Humanitarian Affairs | agency = International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies | accessdate = 2015-03-19 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150319004042/http://reliefweb.int/report/democratic-peoples-republic-korea/dpr-korea-appeal-no-01382002-programme-update-no-02 | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2015-03-19 | url = http://reliefweb.int/report/democratic-peoples-republic-korea/dpr-korea-appeal-no-01382002-programme-update-no-02}}</ref>。另外，一家韩国乳业公司向朝鲜捐赠了4万2000罐婴儿配方奶粉<ref>{{cite news | agency = Yonhap News Agency | publisher = British Broadcasting Corporation | date = 2002-09-12 | title = Koreas: South firm to provide free infant foods to North}}{{subscription required | via = Lexis Nexis}}</ref>。

由于这场台风造成大量人员伤亡和财产损失，其名称“鹿莎”予以退役，于2004年由“鸚鵡”（{{lang|en|Nuri}}）取代<ref>{{cite report | page = 31 | publisher = World Meteorological Organization | title = Appendix 1C: List of Names for Tropical Cyclones Adopted by the Typhoon Committee for the Western North Pacific Ocean and the South China Sea | type = Typhoon Committee Operational Manual 2012 Edition | accessdate = 2015-03-19 | archivedate = 2013-08-01 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20130801020116/http://www.wmo.int/pages/prog/www/tcp/documents/TCP-23EDITION2012.pdf | url = http://www.wmo.int/pages/prog/www/tcp/documents/TCP-23EDITION2012.pdf | format = PDF | author = The Typhoon Committee | deadurl = no}}</ref>。
