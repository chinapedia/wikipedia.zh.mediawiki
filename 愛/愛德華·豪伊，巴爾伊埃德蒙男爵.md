'''愛德華·恩達·豪伊，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.exacteditions.com/exact/browse/455/1210/7772/3/113 |title=debretts |publisher=Exacteditions.com |accessdate=18 October 2011}}</ref>巴爾伊埃德蒙男爵'''，[[Order_of_the_British_Empire|OBE]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.theyworkforyou.com/peer/lord_ballyedmond |title=Lord Ballyedmond |publisher=TheyWorkForYou |accessdate=18 October 2011}}</ref>（{{lang-en|'''Edward Enda Haughey, Baron Ballyedmond}}；{{bd|1944年|1月5日|2014年|3月13日}}）是北愛爾蘭的企業家及政治人物。其個人財產估計達7.8億歐元<ref>{{cite web|author=Dan Keenan |url=http://www.irishtimes.com/news/ireland/irish-news/ni-s-richest-man-eddie-haughey-dies-in-helicopter-crash-1.1724601 |title=NI's richest man Eddie Haughey dies in helicopter crash - The Irish Times - Fri, Mar 14, 2014 |publisher=The Irish Times |date= |accessdate=2014-03-14}}</ref>，是[[北愛爾蘭|北愛爾蘭]]最富有的人<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.irishleftreview.org/2009/10/14/poverty-class-northern-ireland/ |title=Irish Left Review · Poverty and Class in Northern Ireland |publisher=Webcache.googleusercontent.com |accessdate=18 October 2011 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150929084358/http://www.irishleftreview.org/2009/10/14/poverty-class-northern-ireland/ |archivedate=2015年9月29日 }}</ref>，也是愛爾蘭250名最富有人物名單中的第9名及英國富豪榜排行第132<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.newsandstar.co.uk/news/lord-ballyedmond-sees-fortune-grow-by-60-million-in-year-1.700201?referrerPath=home |title=News & Star | News | Lord Ballyedmond sees fortune grow by £60 million in year |publisher=Newsandstar.co.uk |date=26 April 2010 |accessdate=18 October 2011 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120322012426/http://www.newsandstar.co.uk/news/lord-ballyedmond-sees-fortune-grow-by-60-million-in-year-1.700201?referrerPath=home |archivedate=2012年3月22日 }}</ref>。

==背景==
愛德華·豪伊<ref>{{cite web|author=norbrookpricing |url=http://www.flickr.com/photos/niexecutive/5509216360/ |title=L-R - Lord Ballyedmond, Junior Minister Gerry Kelly, Mr. Thomas Muller, Chilean Ambassador to the UK and Junior Minister Robin Newton | Flickr - Photo Sharing! |publisher=Flickr |date=2011-03-31 |accessdate=2014-03-14}}</ref>1944年出生於愛爾蘭[[勞斯郡|勞斯郡]][[鄧多克|鄧多克]]北部的基爾庫里，並在鄧多克的{{link-en|基督教兄弟會|Congregation of Christian Brothers}}接受教育<ref>[http://www.stratagem-ni.com/nipeers.php Northern Ireland Peers] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070928094515/http://www.stratagem-ni.com/nipeers.php |date=2007-09-28 }}</ref><ref>[http://www.gcd.ie/index.jsp?1nID=93&2nID=102&nID=214&aID=180 Distinguished Fellowship Award 2004 – Edward Haughey] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20071119213235/http://www.gcd.ie/index.jsp?1nID=93&2nID=102&nID=214&aID=180 |date=2007-11-19 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.zoominfo.com/people/Haughey_Edward_77687663.aspx |title=Edward Haughey: ZoomInfo Business People Information |publisher=Zoominfo.com |accessdate=18 October 2011}}</ref>。1972年他和瑪麗·古登·楊（{{lang-en|Mary Gordon Young}}）結婚，育有2子1女。

豪伊在1960年代時用4年移民到英國，但1968年就回家並創辦了一家獸醫藥品製造業務的公司。

2014年3月13日，愛德華·豪伊被發現在[[諾福克郡|諾福克郡]]的{{link-en|豪伊航空阿古斯特維斯特蘭AW139墜機事件|Haughey Air AgustaWestland AW139 crash}}與其他三名機員一同罹難<ref>{{cite web|url=http://newrytimes.com/2014/03/13/norbrook-boss-lord-haughey-reportedly-killed-in-helicopter-crash/ |title=Norbrook boss Lord Haughey killed in helicopter crash | Latest Newry News, Newry Sport and Newry Business for Newry City |publisher=Newrytimes.com |date=2013-01-24 |accessdate=2014-03-14}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=Dan Keenan |url=http://www.irishtimes.com/news/ireland/irish-news/eddie-haughey-killed-in-helicopter-crash-1.1724601 |title=NI's richest man Eddie Haughey dies in helicopter crash - The Irish Times - Fri, Mar 14, 2014 |publisher=The Irish Times |date= |accessdate=2014-03-14}}</ref>。
