{{作品名稱|name=''Loving''|hk=相愛很難|tw=愛侶|cn=爱恋}}是一部2016年[[英國|英國]]和[[美國|美國]]合拍的[[時代劇|時代]][[傳記片|傳記]][[劇情片|劇情片]]，電影的靈感源自2011年由Nancy Buirski執導的紀錄片《漫長回家路：一個愛情故事》（{{lang|en|''The Loving Story''}}），電影根據發生於1967年美國最高法院對《{{le|洛文夫婦控訴維吉尼亞州案|Loving v. Virginia}}》的裁決，當中涉及維吉尼亞州禁止種族間婚姻的法律<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/cannes-a-fest-few-lows-896614|title=Cannes: A Fest of Few Lows, But Only One Real High|last=McCarthy|first=Todd|work=[[The_Hollywood_Reporter|The Hollywood Reporter]]|date=2016-05-23|accessdate=2017-01-02}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://ew.com/article/2016/09/13/ruth-negga-joel-edgerton-loving-tiff/|title=TIFF 2016: Loving stars Ruth Negga, Joel Edgerton on film's reluctant heroes|last=Coggan|first=Devan|work=[[Entertainment_Weekly|Entertainment Weekly]]|date=2016-09-13|accessdate=2017-01-02}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2016/03/midnight-special-jeff-nichols-aquaman-warner-bros-the-screening-room-1201723429/|title=‘Midnight Special’ Director Jeff Nichols On Final Cut, ‘Aquaman’ & Why HDTV Evolution Is More Important Than The Screening Room|last=D'Alessandro|first=Anthony|work=[[Deadline.com|Deadline.com]]|date=2016-03-20|accessdate=2017-01-02}}</ref>，並隨著《洛文夫婦控訴維吉尼亞州案》的發展而拍攝<ref>{{cite web|url=http://ew.com/article/2015/05/08/loving-supreme-court-case-jeff-nichols/|title=Landmark Loving Supreme Court Case to be made into a film|last=McGovern|first=Joe|work=[[Entertainment_Weekly|Entertainment Weekly]]|date=2016-05-08|accessdate=2016-12-30}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://collider.com/loving-joel-edgerton-and-ruth-negga-lead-jeff-nichols-next-film/|title=Joel Edgerton and Ruth Negga to Lead MUD Director Jeff Nichols’ LOVING|last=Chitwood|first=Adam|work=[[Collider.com|Collider.com]]|date=2016-05-08|accessdate=2016-12-30}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.charlotteobserver.com/entertainment/ent-columns-blogs/lawrence-toppman/article116355443.html|title=Loving was their name, loving was their crime|last=Toppman|first=Lawrence|work=[[The_Charlotte_Observer|The Charlotte Observer]]|date=2016-11-21|accessdate=2016-12-30}}</ref>。

電影由[[傑夫·尼可拉斯|傑夫·尼可拉斯]]執導及編劇<ref>{{cite web|url=http://ew.com/article/2015/05/08/loving-supreme-court-case-jeff-nichols/|title=Landmark Loving Supreme Court Case to be made into a film|last=McGovern|first=Joe|work=[[Entertainment_Weekly|Entertainment Weekly]]|date=2015-05-08|accessdate=2017-01-02}}</ref>，由[[喬爾·埃哲頓|喬爾·埃哲頓]]及[[露絲·奈嘉|露絲·奈嘉]]主演<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.indiewire.com/2015/05/ruth-negga-and-joel-edgerton-will-star-in-adaptation-of-the-loving-story-landmark-civil-rights-anti-miscegenation-case-154417/|title=Ruth Negga and Joel Edgerton Will Star in Adaptation of ‘The Loving Story’ – Landmark Civil Rights Anti-Miscegenation Case|last=A. 電影於2016年5月16日在[[第69屆坎城影展|第69屆坎城影展]]上首映並入選競爭[[金棕櫚獎|金棕櫚獎]]<ref name="IW">{{cite web |url=http://www.indiewire.com/article/cannes-live-stream-2016-film-festival-lineup-list |title=2016 Cannes Film Festival Announces Lineup |accessdate=2016-04-14 |work=IndieWire}}</ref><ref name="VarCannes">{{cite web |url=http://variety.com/2016/film/festivals/cannes-film-festival-official-selection-lineup-2016-1201753269/ |title=Cannes 2016: Film Festival Unveils Official Selection Lineup |accessdate=2016-04-14 |work=Variety}}</ref>，並由[[焦點影業|焦點影業]]定於2016年11月4日在美國作[[有限上映|有限上映]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.gq.com/story/midnight-special-jeff-nichols|title=The Shot Caller Q+A: Midnight Special Director Jeff Nichols|last=Baron|first=Zac|work=[[GQ|GQ]]|date=2016-03-25|accessdate=2016-12-31}}</ref>，並於2016年11月11日廣泛上映<ref name="RELEASE"/><ref>{{cite web|url=http://ew.com/article/2016/03/15/loving-joel-edgerton-ruth-negga-release-date/|title=

==劇情==
故事敘述一名白人男性和黑人女性因相愛而結婚，兩人為此違反了[[反異族通婚法|反異族通婚法]]而遭到了拘捕，並於1958年在[[維吉尼亞州|維吉尼亞州]]被判获罪，缓刑驱逐出州并流放至[[华盛顿哥伦比亚特区|華盛頓]]。兩人提起了一連串的訴訟，以申訴法律禁止[[異族通婚|異族通婚]]是一種違憲的不公行為，并受到[[美国公民自由协会|美国公民自由协会]]（ACLU）的帮助和支持。

==反響==
影片獲得了正面好評。[[爛番茄|爛番茄]]新鮮度高達93%，平均得分7.3／10<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/771439525/|title=Loving (2016)|website=[[Rotten_Tomatoes|Rotten Tomatoes]]|accessdate=2016-05-17}}</ref>。電影在坎城影展上映後，獲得了全場起立鼓掌認同<ref>{{cite news|title=Cannes: Interracial love story 'Loving' is first awards season contender|url=http://www.usatoday.com/story/life/movies/2016/05/16/interracial-love-story-loving-first-awards-season-contender/84435138/|accessdate=2016-05-17|work=USA TODAY|date=2016-05-16}}</ref>。《[[好萊塢報導|好萊塢報導]]》<ref name="The Hollywood Reporter 16 May 2016">{{cite news|last1=Kilday|first1=Gregg|title=Cannes Drama 'Loving' Shows Oscar Potential|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/cannes-drama-loving-shows-oscar-894327|accessdate=2016-05-17|work=The Hollywood Reporter|date=2016-05-16}}</ref>、《[[人物_(雜誌)|人物]]》<ref>{{cite news|title=The Real Story of Richard and Mildred Loving – the Interracial Couple Who Fought for Their Right to Wed and Inspired the Cannes Film Already Earning Oscar Buzz|url=http://www.people.com/article/richard-mildred-loving-real-story-film-supreme-court-fight|accessdate=2016-05-17|work=[[People_(magazine)|People]]}}</ref>和《[[Essence_(雜誌)|Essence]]》<ref>{{cite news|last1=Akindele|first1=Toni|title=Cannes Interracial Marriage Drama ‘Loving’ is an Oscar Contender|url=http://www.essence.com/2016/05/17/cannes-interracial-marriage-drama-loving-oscar-contender|accessdate=2016-05-17|work=[[Essence_(magazine)|Essence]]|date=2016-05-17}}</ref>等多家媒體都給予了肯定的評價，並認定電影有資格競爭[[奧斯卡金像獎|奧斯卡金像獎]]。多數影評人稱讚其敘事和不灑狗血的作風，以及喬爾·埃哲頓與露絲·奈嘉的精采演出。
