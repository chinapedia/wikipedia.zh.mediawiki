'''愛德華·朱利安·埃格頓·李'''（{{lang-en|[[爵士|Sir]] '''Edward Julian Egerton Leigh'''}}，{{bd|1950年|7月20日|||Leigh, Sir Edward}}），[[英國|英國]][[保守黨_(英國)|保守黨]]政治家。自1983年開始，他擔任[[蓋恩斯伯勒_(英國國會選區)|蓋恩斯伯勒]]選區選出的英國下議院議員。他一直反對墮胎和人類胚胎研究<ref name='ePolitix 2004-11-09'>{{cite web|url=http://www.epolitix.com/latestnews/article-detail/newsarticle/conservatives-split-on-civil-partnerships/?no_cache=1|title=ePolitix.com|accessdate=|last=Davie|first=Edward|date=9 November 2004|publisher=ePolitix.com|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/5mk8nWPeC?url=http://www.epolitix.com/latestnews/article-detail/newsarticle/conservatives-split-on-civil-partnerships/?no_cache=1|archivedate=2010-01-13|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name='Changing Attitude'>{{cite web|url=http://www.changingattitude.org.uk/news/newsitem.asp?id=72|title=changingattitude.org.uk|last=Coward|first=Colin|date=9 November 2004|publisher=Changing Attitude|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/5mk2Y1M3W?url=http://www.changingattitude.org.uk/news/newsitem.asp?id=72|archivedate=2010-01-13|access-date=2016-03-08|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。
