'''亞硝胺'''、'''亞硝酸胺'''（Nitrosamine）是一類通式為R<sup>1</sup>N(–R<sup>2</sup>)–N=O的[[胺|胺]][[化合物|化合物]]，其大部分成員都屬'''強'''[[致癌物|致癌物]]。

==用途==
亞硝胺廣泛應用於[[化妝品|化妝品]]、[[殺蟲劑|殺蟲劑]]與[[樹脂|樹脂]]的生產。

其中'''N,N'-二亞硝基五亞甲基四胺'''（''N,N'-Dinitrosopentamethylenetetramine；發泡劑H''）是一種重要的[[膠乳|膠乳發泡劑]]；

'''环三亚甲基三亚硝胺'''（''Trimethylenetrinitrosamine；R鹽''）為[[RDX|RDX]]的中間體之一，[[二戰|二戰]]期間曾用作[[炸藥|軍用炸藥]]。

==分佈==
亞硝胺在橡膠製品、食品和其他消費品中均有分佈<ref>{{cite journal | pmid = 15672455 | year = 2005 | last1 = Altkofer | first1 = W | last2 = Braune | first2 = S | last3 = Ellendt | first3 = K | last4 = Kettl-Grömminger | first4 = M | last5 = Steiner | first5 = G | title = Migration of nitrosamines from rubber products--are balloons and condoms harmful to the human health? | volume = 49 | issue = 3 | pages = 235–8 | doi = 10.1002/mnfr.200400050 | journal = Molecular nutrition & food research}}</ref>。有研究預計[[避孕套|避孕套]]中的亞硝胺沒有[[毒理學|毒理學]]意義。<ref>{{cite journal | pmid = 11759152 | year = 2001 | last1 = Proksch | first1 = E | title = Toxicological evaluation of nitrosamines in condoms | volume = 204 | issue = 2–3 | pages = 103–10 | journal = International journal of hygiene and environmental health | doi=10.1078/1438-4639-00087}}</ref>

在酸性環境（如[[消化液|消化液]]）中，食物中的[[亞硝酸鹽|亞硝酸鹽]]發生[[質子化|質子化]]而產生[[亞硝酰|亞硝酰]][[陽離子|陽離子]]N≡O<sup>+</sup>與[[水|水]]：
:{{chem|H|2|NO|2|+}} → H<sub>2</sub>O + NO<sup>+</sup>.
N≡O<sup>+</sup>隨即與[[胺|胺]]（包括[[蛋白質|蛋白質]]及其降解產物）反應而生成亞硝胺<ref>{{cite book|last=Vogel |first=A. I. |title=Practical Organic Chemistry |edition=3rd |publisher=Impression |date=1962 |page=1074}}</ref>
。高溫對此有促進作用。

{{link-en|Liebermann反應|Liebermann reagent}}能夠提示亞硝胺的存在<ref>{{cite book|last=Vogel |first=A. I. |title=Practical Organic Chemistry |edition=3rd |publisher=Impression |date=1962 |page=649}}</ref>。若檢測結果呈陰性，則試劑顯紅色或蘭色（苯酚存在時）。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chemistry.ccsu.edu/glagovich/teaching/316/qualanal/tests/liebermann.html |title=The Libermann Nitroso Reaction |publisher=[[Connecticut_State_University|Connecticut State University]] |accessdate=28 December 2015 |author=Glagovich, Neil |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130305171538/http://www.chemistry.ccsu.edu/glagovich/teaching/316/qualanal/tests/liebermann.html |archivedate=March 5, 2013 }}</ref>

因亞硝化/亞硝解反應的存在，[[肉類|肉類]]、[[泡菜|泡菜]]、[[奶酪|奶酪]]等富含亞硝酸鹽的食品中亞硝胺濃度水平極高。[[美國政府|美國政府]]對肉製品亞硝酸鹽含量實行嚴格規制，旨在降低公民罹患[[癌症|癌症]]的風險<ref>{{cite journal|last=Honikel|first=Karl-Otto|title=The use and control of nitrate and nitrite for the processing of meat products|journal=Meat Science|date=January–February 2008|volume=78|pages=68–76|doi=10.1016/j.meatsci.2007.05.030|accessdate=8 March 2014}}</ref>。為抑製亞硝胺的生成，[[抗壞血酸|抗壞血酸]]或其衍生物被要求添加到肉製品中<ref>{{cite web | url=http://margotbworldnews.com/News/Apr/Apr22/nitrosamines.html | title=Nitrosamines and Cancer | publisher=The Linus Pauling Institute | work=Nitrosamines and Cancer | date=22 April 2000 | accessdate=28 December 2015 | author=Scanlan, Dr. Richard A.}}</ref>。

包括鼻煙油、口含烟及嗅煙（儘管含量較前二者低）的[[煙草|煙草]]製品均含有亞硝胺<ref>{{Cite journal|author=Gregory N. Connolly, and Howard Saxner |title=Informational Update Research on Tobacco Specific Nitrosamines (TSNAs) in Oral Snuff and a Request to Tobacco Manufacturers to Voluntarily Set Tolerance Limits For TSNAs in Oral Snuff |date=August 21, 2001 | url=}}</ref>。[[電子煙|電子煙]]生產中[[丙二醇|丙二醇]]的使用亦可引入痕量亞硝胺<ref>{{cite web|title=E-cigarettes: harmless inhaled or exhaled, No second hand smoke|url=http://www.healthnz.co.nz/ECigsExhaledSmoke.htm|website=Health New Zealand|accessdate=27 June 2014}}</ref>。

==致突變性==
[[動物實驗|動物實驗]]已證實某些亞硝胺具有明顯的[[致癌性|致癌性]]，並提示其對[[人|人]]可能也有致癌作用。基於[[對照實驗|對照實驗]]的[[流行病学|流行病学]]调查表明，攝入亞硝胺與[[胃癌|胃癌]]、[[食道癌|食道癌]]的發生有著正相關性，但尚無證據直接證實亞硝胺的致癌性質。<ref>{{cite journal|pmid=16865769|year=2006|last1=Jakszyn|first1=P|last2=Gonzalez|first2=CA|title=Nitrosamine and related food intake and gastric and oesophageal cancer risk: A systematic review of the epidemiological evidence|volume=12|issue=27|pages=4296–303|journal=World journal of gastroenterology : WJG|pmc=4087738}}</ref>
