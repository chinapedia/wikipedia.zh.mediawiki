'''Amazon Appstore'''是[[亞馬遜公司|亞馬遜公司]]的一個基於[[安卓|安卓]]系統的[[流動應用程式|應用程式]]，2011年3月22日啟用。該程式在將近200個國家都可以使用，<ref>{{cite web|author=Amazon.com |url=https://developer.amazon.com/post/Tx3IJP9TKDQOXS5/Developers-Can-Now-Distribute-Apps-in-Nearly-200-Countries-Worldwide-on-Amazon.html |title=Developers Can Now Distribute Apps in Nearly 200 Countries Worldwide on Amazon - Amazon Mobile App Distribution Blog |publisher=Developer.amazon.com |date=2013-05-23 |accessdate=2013-11-27}}</ref> 最初仅含有約3,800個免費/付費應用，后来已达过数十万。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://sigitarinto.com/2011/03/22/amazon-app-store/ |title=Amazon Appstore | Android |publisher=SigitArinto.com |date=2011-03-22 |accessdate=2011-04-05 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110827070545/http://sigitarinto.com/2011/03/22/amazon-app-store/ |archivedate=2011-08-27 }}</ref>

==特徵==

Amazon Appstore有“每天一款免費程式”的計劃，<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.andromint.com/google-play-store-vs-amazon-appstore/ |title=Amazon Appstore Compared with Google Play |work=Andromint |date=28 October 2013 |accessdate=2013-10-28 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131031052750/http://www.andromint.com/google-play-store-vs-amazon-appstore/ |archivedate=2013年10月31日 }}</ref> 即該商店會每天提供一款限時免費的程式，而該程式一般是遊戲。而Amazon Appstore首發當天的程式是[[憤怒的小鳥里約版|憤怒的小鳥里約版]]。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://articles.baltimoresun.com/2011-03-22/advertise/bal-new-amazon-android-app-store-1-free-app--day-20110322_1_app-store-free-app-amazon |title=New Amazon Android App Store - 1 Free App / Day - Baltimore Sun |publisher=Articles.baltimoresun.com |date=2011-03-22 |accessdate=2011-04-05 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110412175431/http://articles.baltimoresun.com/2011-03-22/advertise/bal-new-amazon-android-app-store-1-free-app--day-20110322_1_app-store-free-app-amazon |archivedate=2011年4月12日 |deadurl=no }}</ref> 雖然一般程式開發商會佔據70%的利益，但在這種情況下當天收益為0。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://gigaom.com/2011/07/05/amazon-appstore-not-so-amazing/|title=Amazon Appstore, Not so Amazing|publisher=GigaOM|date=2011-07-05|accessdate=2011-07-06|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110709060730/http://gigaom.com/2011/07/05/amazon-appstore-not-so-amazing/|archivedate=2011年7月9日|deadurl=no}}</ref>

此外Amazon Appstore還有試用的功能，用戶可在瀏覽器上通過[[Amazon_EC2|Amazon EC2]][[雲計算|雲]]試用一款程式半個小時。<ref>{{cite web |author=Jason Kincaid |url=http://techcrunch.com/2011/03/22/amazon-android-app-store-3/ |title=Amazon’s Android App Store Launches: Test Drive Apps Directly From Your Browser |publisher=Techcrunch.com |date=2011-03-22 |accessdate=2011-04-05 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110328065244/http://techcrunch.com/2011/03/22/amazon-android-app-store-3/ |archivedate=2011年3月28日 |deadurl=no }}</ref>

== 商標侵權訴訟 ==

[[蘋果公司|蘋果公司]]認為亞馬遜的這項程式冒用了[[App_Store_(iOS)|App Store]]的商標，<ref>{{cite news | url=http://www.cnn.com/2011/TECH/web/03/22/apple.amazon.app.fast/index.html | title=Apple sues Amazon over 'app store' name | publisher=CNN Tech | date=March 22, 2011 | accessdate=September 15, 2012 | author=Eaton, Kit}}</ref> 但亞馬遜方面宣稱這只是一個普通的表達方法。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://arstechnica.com/apple/2011/04/amazon-responds-to-apple-app-store-is-generic-toss-the-suit/ | title=Amazon responds to Apple: "app store" is generic, toss the suit | publisher=Ars Technica | date=April 26, 2011 | accessdate=September 15, 2012 | author=Cheng, Jacqui}}</ref> 一位美國聯邦法官否決了蘋果請求發佈預先禁令的要求。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.reuters.com/article/2011/07/07/us-apple-amazon-ruling-idUSTRE76604S20110707 | title=Judge rejects Apple bid for injunction against Amazon | publisher=Reuters | date=July 6, 2011 | accessdate=September 15, 2012 | author=Levine, Dan}}</ref> 2013年7月，蘋果放棄訴訟。<ref>{{cite web|last=Bostic |first=Kevin |url=http://appleinsider.com/articles/13/07/09/apple-drops-app-store-lawsuit-against-amazon-says-no-need-to-pursue-case |title=Apple drops 'App Store' lawsuit against Amazon, says no need to pursue case |publisher=Appleinsider.com |date=2013-07-09 |accessdate=2014-01-02}}</ref>
