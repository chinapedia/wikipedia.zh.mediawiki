'''康力斯娛樂'''（{{lang-en|Corus Entertainment}}）是[[加拿大|加拿大]]一間媒體及廣播公司，總部位於[[安大略省|安大略省]][[多倫多|多倫多]]，1999年從[[蕭氏通訊|蕭氏通訊]]分拆而成，主要股東亦為蕭氏家族。康力斯涉足加拿大國内的電台和電視廣播業及出版業，旗下資產包括[[環球電視_(加拿大)|環球電視]]、39間地區電台以及45條收費電視頻道。兒童電視頻道構成康力斯收費電視業務的主要一環：此公司除了營運[[YTV|YTV]]和Teletoon兩條創始於加拿大的頻道外，亦獲授權營運[[卡通頻道|卡通頻道]]、[[迪士尼頻道|迪士尼頻道]]、[[迪士尼XD|迪士尼XD]]和[[尼克羅頓國際兒童頻道|尼克羅頓]]等美國品牌的加拿大版頻道。 

==歷史==
===1990至2000年代===
20世紀末期，[[加拿大視訊委員會|加拿大視訊委員會]]（CRTC）並不鼓勵國内[[有線電視|有線電視]]供應商同時持有廣播媒體。為了滿足當局的政策，[[蕭氏通訊|蕭氏通訊]]於1998年9月宣佈將旗下的地區電台及收費電視頻道分拆為一家新公司<ref name="gandm-corus">{{cite news|title=Kids, Music and More|url=http://www.theglobeandmail.com/report-on-business/kids-music-and-more/article22400356/|accessdate=12 March 2016|work=The Globe and Mail}}</ref>。康力斯娛樂遂告成立，並於1999年9月在[[多倫多證券交易所|多倫多證券交易所]]掛牌，但大部分股權仍由蕭氏家族操控<ref name="variety-wicradio">{{cite web|title=Canuck players plan splitting up of WIC|url=http://variety.com/1999/biz/news/canuck-players-plan-splitting-up-of-wic-1117756709/|website=Variety|accessdate=12 March 2016}}</ref><ref name="variety-coruslinesup">{{cite web|title=Corus lines up behind Canuck Shaw’s assets|url=http://variety.com/1999/biz/news/corus-lines-up-behind-canuck-shaw-s-assets-1117503023/|website=Variety|accessdate=12 March 2016}}</ref>。康力斯於同年9月從[[加拿大鮑爾集團|加拿大鮑爾集團]]購入4間地區電視台和16間地區電台<ref>{{cite web|title=Shaw Communications Aquire Power Broadcasting|url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/shaw-communications-aquire-power-broadcasting-1.193245|website=CBC News|accessdate=12 March 2016}}</ref>，再於同年10月購入原屬西方國際通訊（{{lang|en|Western International Communications}}）的12間地區電台和包括家庭頻道（{{lang|en|Family Channel}}）在内的多條收費電視頻道<ref name="variety-wicradio"/>，又於2000年9月宣佈斥資5億4千萬加元收購總部位於多倫多的尼爾凡納動畫工作室（{{lang|en|Nelvana}}）<ref name="gandm-nelvanabuy">{{cite news|title=Corus to buy Nelvana for $540-million|url=http://www.theglobeandmail.com/report-on-business/corus-to-buy-nelvana-for-540-million/article1190841/|accessdate=12 March 2016|work=The Globe and Mail}}</ref>。

在CRTC質疑康力斯近乎壟斷加拿大兒童電視頻道市場的局面下，該公司於2001年3月將其持有的家庭頻道股權以1億2690萬元轉售予星芒傳媒（{{lang|en|Astral Media}}）<ref name="variety-wtnbuy"/>。康力斯又以2260萬元將旗下位於[[加拿大西部|加拿大西部]]的按次付費服務「{{lang|en|Viewers Choice}}」售予蕭氏通訊，再以1億3260萬元從蕭氏購入婦女電視網絡（{{lang|en|Women's Television Network}}）<ref name="variety-wtnbuy">{{cite web|title=Corus sells Family, buys femme web|url=http://variety.com/2001/biz/news/corus-sells-family-buys-femme-web-1117795038/|website=Variety|accessdate=12 March 2016}}</ref>。2004年3月，康力斯和星芒傳媒宣佈在[[魁北克省|魁北克省]]交換數間地區電台，而康力斯又將旗下位於[[亞伯達省|亞伯達省]][[紅鹿市|紅鹿市]]的兩間地區電台售予紐卡普廣播集團（{{lang|en|Newcap Radio}}）<ref name="cbc-astralswap">{{cite web|title=Corus, Astral get regulatory approval of Quebec radio station swap|url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/business/corus-astral-get-regulatory-approval-of-quebec-radio-station-swap-1.532694|website=CBC News|accessdate=12 March 2016}}</ref><ref name="gandm-astralswap">{{cite news|title=Astral, Corus swap Quebec radio stations|url=http://www.theglobeandmail.com/report-on-business/astral-corus-swap-quebec-radio-stations/article18262526/|accessdate=12 March 2016|work=The Globe and Mail}}</ref><ref name="bc-corusswap">{{cite web|title=Astral and Corus swap Quebec radio stations|url=http://www.broadcastermagazine.com/news/astral-and-corus-swap-quebec-radio-stations/1000016359/?&er=NA|website=Broadcaster Magazine|accessdate=12 March 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160312132209/http://www.broadcastermagazine.com/news/astral-and-corus-swap-quebec-radio-stations/1000016359/?&er=NA|archive-date=2016-03-12|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。

康力斯於2007年8月宣佈與[[赫斯特國際集團|赫斯特國際集團]]合作創辦加拿大版[[柯夢波丹|柯夢波丹]]頻道<ref>{{cite web|title=Cosmo comes to Canada via Corus|url=http://variety.com/2007/tv/news/cosmo-comes-to-canada-via-corus-1117970365/|website=Variety|accessdate=12 March 2016}}</ref>，又於2008年3月以7300萬元從CTVglobemedia購入加拿大學習電視（{{lang|en|Canadian Learning Television}}）<ref>{{cite web|title=Corus buys CLT from CTVglobemedia|url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/business/corus-buys-clt-from-ctvglobemedia-1.704949|website=CBC News|accessdate=12 March 2016}}</ref>，再於2009年11月創辦加拿大版[[尼克羅頓國際兒童頻道|尼克羅頓]]頻道<ref>{{Cite news|url = http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1G1-209268431.html|title = Corus Entertainment Brings Nickelodeon North|last = Staff|date = October 17, 2009|work = Pediatrics Week|accessdate = 2015-07-04|via = HighBeam Research|publisher = NewsRx|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20150924151722/http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1G1-209268431.html|archive-date = 2015-09-24|dead-url = yes}}</ref>。

===2010年代===
2010年4月，康力斯宣佈因魁北克省内地區電台的利潤欠佳而將其放售，當中大部分以8千萬元售予科捷科（{{lang|en|Cogeco}}）<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.radio-info.com/news/canadas-corus-entertainment-sells-all-its-stations-in-quebec-including-mont |title=Canada's Corus Entertainment sells all its stations in Quebec, including Montreal |date=30 April 2010 |work=Radio-Info.com |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110605111318/http://www.radio-info.com/news/canadas-corus-entertainment-sells-all-its-stations-in-quebec-including-mont |archivedate=2011年6月5日 }}</ref><ref>St. Petersburg Times, "Canada Report" column, May 9, 2010.</ref>，僅餘位於[[薩格奈|薩格奈]]的CKRS則於同年6月售予當地一班商人<ref>[http://www.thewirereport.ca/reports/content/10981-corus_sells_saguenay_station_to_local_buyers Corus sells Saguenay station to local buyers] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110706205721/http://www.thewirereport.ca/reports/content/10981-corus_sells_saguenay_station_to_local_buyers |date=2011-07-06 }}, The Wire Report, 25 June 2010</ref>；交易於同年12月獲CRTC有條件批准<ref>[http://www.crtc.gc.ca/eng/archive/2010/2010-942.htm Broadcasting Decision CRTC 2010-942: "Transfer of effective control of various commercial radio programming undertakings from Corus Entertainment Inc. to Cogeco inc.", issued December 17, 2010.]</ref>。

康力斯於2010年11月與孩之寶工作室（{{lang|en|Hasbro Studios}}）簽訂協議，在旗下頻道播放該公司製作的節目<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.cartoonbrew.com/biz/hasbro-studios-and-corus-entertainments-kids-networks-reach-broad-animation-and-live-action-programming-agreement.html |title=Hasbro Studios and Corus Entertainment's Kids Networks Reach Broad Animation and Live-Action Programming Agreement |author=The Brewsters |date=November 16, 2010 |work=Cartoon Brew |accessdate=June 5, 2011 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110704235028/http://www.cartoonbrew.com/biz/hasbro-studios-and-corus-entertainments-kids-networks-reach-broad-animation-and-live-action-programming-agreement.html |archivedate=2011年7月4日 }}</ref>。2012年3月，康力斯與蕭氏聯手創辦美國ABC家庭頻道（{{lang|en|ABC Family}}）的加拿大版頻道「ABC火花」（{{lang|en|ABC Spark}}）；兩家公司的持份比例為51比49<ref>[http://www.broadcastermagazine.com/news/abc-spark-to-launch-march-26th/1000805287/ Corus' ABC Spark to Launch March 26th] {{Wayback|url=http://www.broadcastermagazine.com/news/abc-spark-to-launch-march-26th/1000805287/ |date=20160312132306 }}, ''Broadcaster Magazine'', 2012-01-05</ref><ref name=gandm-corusdeals>{{cite web|title=Corus eyes aggressive TV expansion in Quebec|url=http://www.theglobeandmail.com/globe-investor/corus-eyes-aggressive-tv-expansion-in-quebec/article9296228/|publisher=The Globe and Mail|accessdate=27 March 2013}}</ref>。

[[貝爾傳媒|貝爾傳媒]]於2013年3月收購星芒傳媒期間宣佈將該公司持有的Historia、Séries+和Teletoon頻道股權以及數間地區電台以4億60萬元轉售予康力斯；交易於同月18日獲加拿大競爭局批准<ref name=gandm-closer>{{cite web|title=Bell moves closer to Astral with sale of TV assets|url=http://www.theglobeandmail.com/globe-investor/bce-to-sell-assets-to-corus-as-part-of-astral-deal/article9272784/?cmpid=rss1|publisher=The Globe and Mail|accessdate=5 March 2013}}</ref><ref name=gandm-cbcorus>{{cite web|title=Competition Bureau clears Corus acquisition of Astral assets|url=http://www.theglobeandmail.com/report-on-business/competition-bureau-clears-corus-acquisition-of-astral-assets/article9886090/?cmpid=rss1|publisher=Canadian Press|accessdate=20 March 2013}}</ref>。另一方面，康力斯宣佈以現金及其持有的加拿大食物網絡（{{lang|en|Food Network Canada}}）股權換取[[蕭氏傳媒|蕭氏傳媒]]所持有的ABC火花、Historia和Séries+股權<ref name=gandm-corusdeals/>。康力斯再於2013年9月將旗下資產分組成六個部門，分別為康力斯電台（{{lang|en|Corus Radio}}）、康力斯兒童（{{lang|en|Corus Kids}}）、康力斯婦女及家庭（{{lang|en|Corus Women and Family}}）、康力斯内容分佈及收費電視（{{lang|en|Corus Content Distribution and Pay TV}}）、康力斯廣播時段銷售（{{lang|en|Corus Airtime Sales}}）和康力斯法語媒體（{{lang|fr|Corus Média}}）<ref>{{cite news|last1=Ng-See-Quan|first1=Danielle|title=Changes to Corus leadership, including Corus Kids|url=http://kidscreen.com/2013/07/16/changes-to-corus-leadership-including-corus-kids/#ixzz3j0V3PkM2|accessdate=August 16, 2015|agency=Playback Online |work=KidScreen |publisher=Brunico Communications Ltd.|date=July 16, 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite news| last1=Dickson |first1=Jeremy |title=Corus unveils Teletoon integration plan |url=http://kidscreen.com/2014/02/10/corus-unveils-teletoon-integration-plan/|accessdate=August 16, 2015|work=KidScreen|publisher=Brunico Communications Ltd.|date=February 10, 2014}}</ref>。

2015年4月，康力斯宣佈與[[迪士尼ABC電視集團|迪士尼ABC電視集團]]達成協議，從後者購入[[迪士尼頻道|迪士尼頻道]]節目在加拿大的多平台播放權，並於同年9月開播迪士尼頻道的加拿大版英語和法語頻道<ref name=gandm-corusdisney>{{cite news|title=Corus gains Canadian rights to Disney Channel content|url=http://www.theglobeandmail.com/report-on-business/corus-gains-canadian-rights-to-disney-channel-content/article23983642/|author=David Friend|agency=Canadian Press|newspaper=The Globe and Mail|publisher=The Globe and Mail Inc.|date=April 16, 2015|accessdate=April 16, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Padovano|first1=Joanna|title=Disney Channel Sets Premiere Date for Canada|url=http://worldscreen.com/articles/display/2015-08-12-kid-disneychannel-english-french-canada|accessdate=August 16, 2015|work=World Screen|publisher=WSN INC.|date=August 12, 2015|location=Toronto}}</ref>。康力斯於同年11月宣佈將其位於加拿大西部的特級收費電視業務營運權以2億1100萬元轉售予貝爾傳媒，電影中心（{{lang|en|Movie Central}}）和[[安可道|安可道]]兩條頻道遂於2016年停播並由貝爾旗下的電影網絡（{{lang|en|The Movie Network}}）取代，而貝爾亦籍此成為加拿大國内唯一持有[[HBO|HBO]]節目播映權的公司<ref name=cbc-bellhbomc>{{cite news|title=Bell buys HBO rights across Canada as Corus backs out of pay TV|url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/business/bell-buys-hbo-rights-across-canada-as-corus-backs-out-of-pay-tv-1.3328375|accessdate=22 November 2015|agency=Canadian Press}}</ref>。

康力斯於2016年1月13日宣佈以26億5千萬加元向蕭氏通訊收購包括[[環球電視_(加拿大)|環球電視]]在内的蕭氏傳媒資產<ref name=gandm-shawcorus>{{cite news|title=Corus Entertainment acquires Shaw Media for $2.65-billion|url=http://www.theglobeandmail.com/report-on-business/corus-acquires-shaw-media-for-265-billion/article28140989/|accessdate=13 January 2016|work=The Globe and Mail}}</ref>。由於康力斯和蕭氏通訊的最大股東皆為蕭氏家族，CRTC視這宗交易為企業重組，並於同年3月23日批准<ref name="crtc-2016110">{{cite web|title=Broadcasting Decision CRTC 2016-110|url=http://www.crtc.gc.ca/eng/archive/2016/2016-110.htm|publisher=CRTC|accessdate=23 March 2016}}</ref>；康力斯於同年4月1日正式接管蕭氏傳媒<ref name="corus-shawcomplete">{{cite web|title=Corus Entertainment Completes Acquisition of Shaw Media|url=http://www.corusent.com/news/corus-entertainment-completes-acquisition-shaw-media/|publisher=Corus Entertainment|accessdate=1 April 2016}}</ref>。
