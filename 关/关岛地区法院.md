'''关岛地区法院''' <ref name="中文名参考">{{cite web | url=http://www.court.gov.cn/xwzx/rdzt/sfgkxcyhdzt/ywgc/201112/t20111231_168878.html | title=中文名参考 | accessdate=2013-08-08 }}{{dead link|date=2017年11月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>（引用时缩写为'''D. Guam'''）是美国3个[[美国联邦领地法院|领地法院]]之一。该法院审理后的案件需上诉至[[美国联邦第九巡回上诉法院|第九巡回法院]]，专利及《[[塔克法案|塔克法案]]》相关的案件需上诉至[[联邦巡回区法院|联邦巡回区法院]]。该法院是在1950年根据{{usc|48|1424}}的《关岛地区组织法》设立的，但该法院并不是真正的根据[[美国宪法|美国宪法]]第三条设立的联邦法院，其法官也没有终身任期。<ref  name=48USC1424>{{cite web | title=The Organic Act of Guam | url=http://www.guamcourts.org/CompilerofLaws/GCA/OrganicAct/Organic%20Act.PDF |publisher =  District Court of Guam | accessdate=April 8, 2010 | quote=The judicial authority of Guam shall be vested in a court established by Congress designated as the "District Court of Guam" ...The District Court of Guam shall have the jurisdiction of a district court of the United States, including, but not limited to, the diversity jurisdiction provided for in 1332 of title 28, United States Code, and that of a bankruptcy court of the United States.}}</ref>
