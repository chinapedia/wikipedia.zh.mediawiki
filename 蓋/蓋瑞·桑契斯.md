'''蓋瑞·桑契斯'''（'''Gary Sánchez'''，{{bd|1992年|12月2日|catIdx=T}}）出生於[[多明尼加共和國|多明尼加共和國]][[聖多明哥|聖多明哥]]的[[捕手|捕手]]，效力於[[美國職棒大聯盟|美國職棒大聯盟]][[紐約洋基|紐約洋基]]，在2015年隨洋基隊初登場。

==早期生涯==
桑契斯在聖多明哥的{{link-en|La Victoria|La Victoria, Dominican Republic}}長大，年輕時父母離異，由母親和祖母扶養他和四個兄弟姊妹。他的其中一個兄弟Miguel Sánchez在[[西雅圖水手|西雅圖水手]]傘下打球。<ref name=baby>{{cite web |url=http://www.espn.com/mlb/story/_/id/17476422/new-york-yankees-gary-sanchez-owes-august-ages-daughter-sarah|title=Oh, baby! Gary Sanchez owes August for the ages to hard work, maturity — and his daughter Sarah|first=Andrew|last=Marchand|work=[[ESPN|ESPN]]|date=September 6, 2016|accessdate=September 6, 2016}}</ref>他和妻子Sahaira育有一女。<ref name=baby/> 

==職業生涯==

===小聯盟時期===
2009年7月桑契斯以國際[[自由球員|自由球員]]的身分和洋基隊簽約，得到3百萬美元的[[簽約獎金|簽約獎金]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.silive.com/siyankees/index.ssf/2010/08/staten_island_yankees_sanchez.html |title=Staten Island Yankees' Sanchez already thinking big|first=Kevin|last=Flood|work=Staten Island Advance|date=August 25, 2010|accessdate=August 23, 2016}}</ref>在2010年賽季前，運動雜誌''[[Baseball_America|Baseball America]]''將他評為洋基隊的第七大新秀。<ref>{{cite web|last=Manuel |first=John |url=http://www.baseballamerica.com/today/prospects/rankings/organization-top-10-prospects/2010/269277.html |title=Baseball America New York Yankees top 2010 prospects |publisher=Baseballamerica.com |date=December 16, 2009 |accessdate=October 12, 2013}}</ref> 他在[[美國職棒小聯盟#灣岸聯盟（Gulf_Coast_League，GCL）|灣岸洋基]]開啟他的職業生涯。2010年8月18日，他被升入[[美國職棒小聯盟|短期1A]][[美國職棒小聯盟#短期1A（Class_A_Short_Season，SS）|紐約─賓夕弗尼亞聯盟]]的[[美國職棒小聯盟#短期1A（Class_A_Short_Season，SS）|史坦頓島洋基]]，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://riveraveblues.com/2010/08/warren-steals-the-show-in-action-packed-night-34014/ |title=Warren steals the show in action-packed night |publisher=Riveraveblues.com |date=August 19, 2010 |accessdate=October 12, 2013}}</ref>並以.329/.393/.543外加8支[[全壘打|全壘打]]和43分[[打點|打點]]的成績結束此季47場比賽。

[[File:Gary_Sanchez_2012.jpg|220px]]
在2011年賽季前桑契斯被評為最好的新秀之一。<ref>{{cite web|author=MLB.com|url=http://mlb.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20110125&content_id=16498518 |title=MLB.com's 2011 Top 50 Prospects list |publisher=Mlb.mlb.com |date= |accessdate=October 12, 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.aolnews.com/2011/02/09/baseballs-top-100-prospects-of-2011/|title=News & latest headlines from AOL|work=AOL.com|accessdate=October 3, 2015|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140315211027/http://www.aolnews.com/2011/02/09/baseballs-top-100-prospects-of-2011/|archivedate=三月 15, 2014}}</ref><ref name="Top 100 prospects">{{cite web|url=http://www.baseballamerica.com/today/prospects/rankings/top-100-prospects/2011/2611317.html |title=2011 Top 100 Prospects: 21-40 |publisher=Baseballamerica.com |date=February 23, 2011 |accessdate=October 12, 2013}}</ref>他被Baseball America評為洋基傘下的第二大新秀，大聯盟第30名。<ref name="Top 100 prospects"/><ref>{{cite web|last=Manuel |first=John |url=http://www.baseballamerica.com/today/prospects/rankings/organization-top-10-prospects/2011/2610907.html |title=Baseball America New York Yankees top 2011 prospects |publisher=Baseballamerica.com |date=November 10, 2010 |accessdate=October 12, 2013}}</ref>此季在[[美國職棒小聯盟#南大西洋聯盟（South_Atlantic_League）|1A/南大西洋聯盟]]的[[美國職棒小聯盟#南大西洋聯盟（South_Atlantic_League）|查爾斯頓河狗]]打球，在結束賽季的指傷前打了82場，<ref>{{cite web|author=Craig Williams |url=http://www.rantsports.com/new-york-yankees/2011/08/15/new-york-yankees-gary-sanchez-out-for-season-with-broken-finger/ |title=New York Yankees’ Gary Sanchez Out for Season with Broken Finger |publisher=Rantsports.com |date=August 15, 2011 |accessdate=October 12, 2013}}</ref>留下.256/.335/.485外加17支全壘打和52分打點的成績。

2012年季前桑契斯被Baseball America評為洋基的第四大新秀。<ref>{{cite web|last=Manuel |first=John |url=http://www.baseballamerica.com/today/prospects/rankings/organization-top-10-prospects/2012/2612767.html |title=Baseball America New York Yankees top 2012 prospects |publisher=Baseballamerica.com |date=January 4, 2012 |accessdate=October 12, 2013}}</ref> 他在季中被升到[[美國職棒小聯盟#佛羅里達聯盟（Florida_State_League）|高階1A/佛羅里達聯盟]]的[[美國職棒小聯盟#佛羅里達聯盟（Florida_State_League）|坦帕洋基]]。

[[File:Gary_Sánchez_on_July_2,_2014.jpg|upright]]
在坦帕洋基，桑契斯開始了他的2013年賽季。當他累積.254外加13支全壘打和61分打點後，洋基於8月3日將他升至[[東方聯盟|特倫頓雷霆]](隸屬[[東方聯盟|2A/東方聯盟]])，<ref>{{cite web|last=Marchand |first=Andrew |url=http://espn.go.com/blog/new-york/yankees/post/_/id/61463/top-prospect-promoted-to-double-a-trenton |title=Top prospect promoted to Double-A - Yankees Blog - ESPN New York |publisher=Espn.go.com |date= |accessdate=October 12, 2013}}</ref>並在季後被放入[[40人名單|40人名單]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://newyork.yankees.mlb.com/news/article/nyy/new-york-yankees-add-top-prospect-gary-sanchez-to-40-man-roster?ymd=20131120&content_id=64084834&vkey=news_nyy|title=New York Yankees add top prospect Gary Sanchez to 40-man roster|work=New York Yankees|accessdate=October 3, 2015}}</ref>特倫頓雷霆時期，桑契斯獲選代表洋基參加2015年{{link-en|未來之星明星賽|All-Star Futures Game}}。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nj.com/yankees/index.ssf/2015/06/prospects_aaron_judge_and_gary_sanchez_to_represen.html|title=Prospects Aaron Judge and Gary Sanchez to represent Yankees in Futures Game|work=NJ.com|accessdate=June 26, 2015}}</ref>明星賽後，他被升至[[國際聯盟_(棒球)|3A/國際聯盟]]的[[國際聯盟_(棒球)#現存球隊|史克蘭頓/威爾克斯─巴里鐵路騎士]]，第一個打席便擊出一支全壘打。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.milb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20150718&content_id=137282692&fext=.jsp&vkey=news_milb&sid=milb|title=Sanchez homers in first Triple-A at-bat: Yankees No. 7 prospect drives in three runs in RailRiders debut|first=Kelsie|last=Heneghan|work=MiLB.com|date=July 18, 2015|accessdate=July 19, 2015}}</ref>

2015年季末，桑契斯初登大聯盟，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nj.com/yankees/index.ssf/2015/09/yankees_call_up_hyped_gary_sanchez_what_you_need_t.html|title=Yankees call up hyped Gary Sanchez: What you need to know|work=NJ.com|accessdate=October 3, 2015}}</ref>10月3日以[[代打|代打]]身分第一次在大聯盟打擊，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nj.com/yankees/index.ssf/2015/10/yankees_vs_baltimore_orioles_rapid_reaction.html|title=Yankees stomped, 9-2, by Baltimore Orioles - Rapid reaction|work=NJ.com|accessdate=October 3, 2015}}</ref>而例行賽兩[[打席|打席]]均無功而返。<ref name=firststart>http://nypost.com/2016/05/13/gary-sanchezs-first-start-no-results-but-girardi-approval/</ref>洋基為了備戰2015年[[美國職棒大聯盟外卡驟死賽|美國職棒大聯盟外卡驟死賽]]將他移入[[25人名單|25人名單]]，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cbssports.com/mlb/eye-on-baseball/25329224/yankees-astros-2015-al-wild-card-rosters-set|title=Yankees, Astros set 2015 AL wild-card game rosters|work=CBSSports.com|accessdate=February 11, 2016}}</ref>季後讓他到[[亞利桑那秋季聯盟|亞利桑那秋季聯盟]](AFL)打球。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://m.mlb.com/news/article/155191488/yankees-gary-sanchez-heating-up-in-afl|title=Yankees' Gary Sanchez heating up in AFL|work=Major League Baseball|accessdate=February 11, 2016}}</ref>他的全壘打數領先聯盟，<ref name=firststart/>且成為聯盟明星賽的[[MVP|MVP]]，還被Baseball America評為AFL第二大新秀。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nj.com/yankees/index.ssf/2015/11/where_yankees_gary_sanchez_ranked_among_arizona_fa.html|title=Where Yankees' Gary Sanchez ranked among Arizona Fall League prospects|work=NJ.com|accessdate=February 11, 2016}}</ref>

===大聯盟時期===
====2016年====
2016季前桑契斯和[[Austin_Romine|Austin Romine]]競爭開季名單中[[布萊恩·麥肯|布萊恩·麥肯]]的備用捕手位置，但賽季仍在3A出發。5月份重回大聯盟出賽一場，四打數無安打。<ref name=firststart/>8月3日再度升上大聯盟，<ref>http://www.nj.com/yankees/index.ssf/2016/08/gary_sanchez_gets_call_to_yankees_and_hes_ready_tr.html</ref>從[[Hansel_Robles|Hansel Robles]]手中擊出大聯盟第一支安打。<ref>{{cite web |title=New York Mets vs. New York Yankees - Play-by-Play - August 03, 2016 |url=http://www.espn.com/mlb/playbyplay?gameId=360803110 |publisher=ESPN |date=August 3, 2016 |accessdate=August 4, 2016}}</ref>8月10日，桑契斯五打數四安打，包括三支一壘安打和大聯盟第一支全壘打。16日對上多倫多藍鳥，他達成生涯第一次單場兩支全壘打。8月22日，桑契斯以.523打擊率和四支全壘打獲選美聯單周最佳球員。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/sports/baseball/yankees/yankees-gary-sanchez-named-al-player-week-article-1.2761490|title=Yankees' Gary Sánchez named AL Player of the Week|work=New York Daily News|first=Mark|last=Feinsand|date=August 22, 2016|access-date=August 23, 2016}}</ref>他成為大聯盟史上第一人在生涯前23場出賽中打出至少11支全壘打和31支安打，並在29日再度拿下美聯單周最佳球員。隔月3日大聯盟頒予他美聯單月最佳球員及單月最佳新人獎。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.nj.com/yankees/index.ssf/2016/09/yankees_rookie_gary_sanchez_gets_2_more_awards_for.html|title=Yankees rookie Gary Sanchez gets 2 more awards for sensational start|first=Randy|last=Miller|date=September 3, 2016|accessdate=September 6, 2016|work=[[NJ.com|NJ.com]]}}</ref>9月21日，桑契斯在他的第45場出賽擊出第18和19支全壘打，寫下大聯盟現代棒球最快的紀錄。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.espn.com/mlb/story/_/id/17607089/gary-sanchez-new-york-yankees-becomes-fastest-player-mlb-history-reach-19-homers|title=Gary Sanchez becomes fastest player in MLB history to hit 19 HRs|first=Andrew|last=Marchand|publisher=ESPN|date=September 22, 2016|access-date=October 9, 2016}}</ref>桑契斯在2016年[[美國職棒大聯盟年度最佳新人獎|年度最佳新人獎]]投票中排第2，僅次於{{link-en|麥可·富爾馬|Michael Fulmer}}。
