'''肌球蛋白轻链激酶'''（{{lang-en|Myosin light-chain kinase}}，'''MYLK''' 或 '''MLCK'''）是一类[[丝氨酸/苏氨酸特异性蛋白激酶|丝氨酸/苏氨酸特异性蛋白激酶]]，通过[[磷酸化|磷酸化]]来调节II型[[肌球蛋白|肌球蛋白]]的[[肌球蛋白轻链|轻链]]<ref name="pmid11758800">{{cite journal | author = Gao Y, Ye LH, Kishi H, Okagaki T, Samizo K, Nakamura A, Kohama K | title = Myosin light chain kinase as a multifunctional regulatory protein of smooth muscle contraction | journal = IUBMB Life | volume = 51 | issue = 6 | pages = 337–44 | pmid = 11758800 | doi = 10.1080/152165401753366087| url = | issn = |date=June 2001}}</ref>。

== 亚型 ==
现存四种MLCK亚型<ref name="pmid12471243">{{cite journal | author = Manning G, Whyte DB, Martinez R, Hunter T, Sudarsanam S | title = The protein kinase complement of the human genome | journal = Science | volume = 298 | issue = 5600 | pages = 1912–34 | pmid = 12471243 | doi = 10.1126/science.1075762 | url = | issn = |date=December 2002}}</ref>主要分布于不同肌细胞：

* [[MYLK|MYLK]] – 平滑肌
* [[MYLK2|MYLK2]] – 骨骼肌
* [[MYLK3|MYLK3]] – 心肌
* [[MYLK4|MYLK4]] – 未知
