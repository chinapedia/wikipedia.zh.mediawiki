'''瓊記洋行'''（{{lang-en|'''Augustine Heard & Co.'''}}），[[清朝|清朝]]時期活躍於中國的美資公司，由[[旗昌洋行|旗昌洋行]]前合伙人、[[美國|美]]籍商人{{link-en|奧古斯丁·赫德|Augustine Heard}}於1840年成立。公司從事出入口業務，貨物包括[[茶葉|茶葉]]及[[鴉片|鴉片]]。<ref name="swire">{{cite web|title=Swire Pacific Company History |url=http://www.swirepacific.com/eng/about/history.htm |accessdate=2007-08-07 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070524000538/http://www.swirepacific.com/eng/about/history.htm |archivedate=2007-05-24 |deadurl=yes }}</ref><ref name="ipswich">{{cite web|title=Ipswich Historical Society and Museums: Personalities |url=http://www.ipswichmuseum.org/personalities.php |accessdate=2007-08-07 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071010060859/http://www.ipswichmuseum.org/personalities.php |archivedate=2007-10-10 |deadurl=yes }}</ref><ref name="supreme">{{cite web | title = U.S. Supreme Court WILLIAMS v.
