'''奥勒·埃纳尔·比约恩达伦'''（{{lang-no|Ole Einar Bjørndalen}}，{{bd|1974年|1月27日||}}），挪威滑雪运动员，曾被誉为“雪上天王”。他擅长[[冬季两项|冬季两项]]和[[越野滑雪|越野滑雪]]，在奥运会、世锦赛和世界杯中都有过多项斩获，是[[获得多个奥运会金牌的运动员列表|冬季奧運會中最成功的運動員]]。<ref>[http://sports.sina.com.cn/z/slc2002/Bjoerndalen/ 奥勒-埃纳尔-比约恩达伦简介]</ref>2002年曾获挪威最佳运动员称号。<ref>[http://sports.sina.com.cn/o/2003-01-03/0817374167.shtml 挪威名将奥·比约恩达伦当选挪威最佳运动员]</ref>

==個人生活==
Bjørndalen resides in the village of [[上蒂利拉赫|Obertilliach]], Austria. He also used to live in [[多比亚科|Toblach]], Italy, with Italian-Belgian biathlete [[Nathalie_Santer|Nathalie Santer]]. They started dating in 1998 and married on 27 May 2006. On 4 October 2012, they filed for divorce by mutual consent.

In April 2016, along with announcing that he will continue his career until the [[平昌郡|平昌]][[2018年冬季奥林匹克运动会|2018年冬季奥林匹克运动会]] in , Bjørndalen confirmed that he was in a relationship with Belarusian biathlete [[達荷亞·多姆拉切娃|達荷亞·多姆拉切娃]], and that she was pregnant with the couple's first child.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www5.biathlonworld.com/en/press_releases.html/do/detail?presse=2843 |title=No Retirement for Bjorndalen; Aiming for 2018 OWG |author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.--> |date=5 April 2016 |website=[[國際冬季兩項聯盟|國際冬季兩項聯盟]] |access-date=12 April 2016 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160424113539/http://www5.biathlonworld.com/en/press_releases.html/do/detail?presse=2843 |archivedate=2016年4月24日 }}</ref> On 7 July 2016, they married in [[Sjusjøen|Sjusjøen]], Norway.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.gd.no/skiskyting/sjusjoen/bryllup/giftet-seg-pa-sjusjoen/s/5-18-347934 | title = Giftet seg på Sjusjøen}}</ref> Their daughter Xenia was born on October 1, 2016.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.vg.no/sport/skiskyting/ole-einar-bjoerndalen/bjoerndalen-og-domratsjeva-har-faatt-en-datter-vi-er-to-stolte-foreldre/a/23809003/#xtor=CS6-6-[23809003%20bjoerndalen_og_domratsjeva_har_faatt_en_datter] | title = Bjørndalen og Domratsjeva har fått en datter: – Vi er to stolte foreldre}}</ref>
