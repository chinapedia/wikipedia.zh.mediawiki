'''奥利弗·羅伯特·"奧利"·李'''（{{lang|en|'''Oliver Robert  "Olly" Lee'''}}，{{bd|1991年|7月11日|catIdx=F}}），[[英格蘭|英格蘭]][[足球|足球]][[運動員|運動員]]，司職[[中場_(足球)|中場]]，現效力于[[英格蘭足球乙級聯賽|英格蘭足球乙級聯賽]][[卢顿足球俱乐部|卢顿足球俱乐部]]。

==球員生涯==
===西汉姆联===
奧利·李在[[西汉姆联足球俱乐部|西汉姆联]]展開足球員生涯，加入成為青年軍並於2009年簽下職業合約<ref name=DaggersLoan1/>。他是青年隊隊長，亦曾在預備組上場及在。在2011年3月被外借至[[英甲|英甲]]球隊[[达根汉姆和雷德布里奇足球俱乐部|达根汉]]<ref name=DaggersLoan1>{{cite web |url=http://www.daggers.co.uk/page/NewsDetail/0,,10692~2323653,00.html |title=Daggers take Lee on loan |publisher=Dagenham & Redbridge F.C |date=25 March 2011 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120314005920/http://www.daggers.co.uk/page/NewsDetail/0,,10692~2323653,00.html |archivedate=14 March 2012}}</ref>，4月2日對[[埃克塞特城足球俱乐部|埃克塞特]]的聯賽中首次出場，以2-1落敗<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.whufc.com/articles/20110402/lee-makes-league-bow_2236884_2329606 |title=Lee makes league bow |publisher=West Ham United F.C |date=2 April 2011 |accessdate=7 August 2013 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131111155904/http://www.whufc.com/articles/20110402/lee-makes-league-bow_2236884_2329606 |archivedate=2013年11月11日 }}</ref>。外借期間共上場5次，5次均落敗，球隊宣佈保級失敗並降級至[[英格蘭足球乙級聯賽|英乙]]<ref name=ExtendLoan2/>。

2011年8月，奧利·李重返[[达根汉姆和雷德布里奇足球俱乐部|达根汉]]一個月以恢復傷患<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www1.skysports.com/football/news/11095/7082942/ |title=Lee makes Daggers return |publisher=Sky Sports |date=5 August 2011 |accessdate=7 August 2013}}</ref>，後來外借期延長兩個月<ref name=ExtendLoan2>{{cite news |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/14426850 |title=Dagenham & Redbridge extend Oliver Lee's loan deal |publisher=BBC Sport |date=3 October 2011 |accessdate=7 August 2013}}</ref>，期間他共在聯賽上場16次及1次[[英格蘭聯賽錦標|英格蘭聯賽錦標]]，在聯賽打進3球<ref name=sb1112>{{cite web |url=http://www.soccerbase.com/players/player.sd?player_id=52121&season_id=141 |title=Games played by Oliver Lee in 2011/2012 |work=Soccerbase |publisher=Centurycomm |accessdate=7 August 2013}}</ref>。他的首個進球是在9月17日在主場對[[摩尔甘比足球俱乐部|摩尔甘比]]，主射25碼自由球直射入網，但仍無法拯救球隊以2-1落敗<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.romfordrecorder.co.uk/football/daggers/dagenham_redbridge_beaten_after_scaring_leaders_morecambe_1_1025916 |title=Dagenham & Redbridge beaten after scaring leaders Morecambe |author=Power, Lee |newspaper=Romford Recorder |date=18 September 2011 |accessdate=7 August 2013 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140121050042/http://www.romfordrecorder.co.uk/football/daggers/dagenham_redbridge_beaten_after_scaring_leaders_morecambe_1_1025916 |archivedate=2014年1月21日 }}</ref>。

2012年2月，經過兩星期在[[吉林汉姆足球俱乐部|吉林汉姆]]的試訓後，他正式被外借至該球會一個月<ref name=gills>{{cite news |title=West Ham midfielder Lee joins Gills on loan |url=http://www.kentnews.co.uk/sport/west_ham_midfielder_lee_joins_gills_on_loan_1_1213018 |website=kentnews.co.uk |date=17 February 2012 |accessdate=7 August 2013 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140202132310/http://www.kentnews.co.uk/sport/west_ham_midfielder_lee_joins_gills_on_loan_1_1213018 |archivedate=2014年2月2日 }}</ref>。2月21日主場對[[罗瑟勒姆足球俱乐部|罗瑟勒姆]]的比賽中首次上場，以0-0悶和收場<ref name=GillsDebut>{{cite news |title=Gillingham 0–0 Rotherham |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/17023375 |publisher=BBC Sport |date=21 February 2012 |accessdate=7 August 2013}}</ref>。後來外借期延長一個月，期間他參加了8場比賽<ref name=eightgills>{{cite news |title=Lee makes Hammers return |url=http://www1.skysports.com/football/news/11750/7682726/ |publisher=Sky Sports News |date=18 April 2012 |accessdate=7 August 2013}}</ref>。2011/12球季季末，奧利·李被西汉姆联釋放<ref>{{cite news |title=Six players depart West Ham |url=http://www1.skysports.com/football/news/12691/7773496/Six-players-depart-West-Ham |accessdate=23 May 2012 |publisher=Sky Sports |date=23 May 2012}}</ref>。

===巴尼特===
2012年7月3日，[[英乙|英乙]]球會[[巴尼特足球俱乐部|巴尼特]]主教練[[马克·罗布森_(足球員)|马克·罗布森]]簽下奧利·李<ref>{{cite news |title=Barnet sign midfielder Oliver Lee on a free transfer |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football-18694295 |publisher=BBC Sport |date=3 July 2012 |accessdate=7 August 2013}}</ref>，期間獲得穩定的出場機會。但當[[埃德加·戴维斯|埃德加·戴维斯]]加入為聯合主教練後，上場機會大減；戴维斯成為唯一主教練後，他再沒有獲得機會<ref name=sb1213>{{cite web |url=http://www.soccerbase.com/players/player.sd?player_id=52121&season_id=142 |title=Games played by Oliver Lee in 2012/2013 |work=Soccerbase |publisher=Centurycomm |accessdate=7 August 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/19920753 |title=Edgar Davids joins Barnet as joint-head coach |publisher=BBC Sport |date=12 October 2012 |accessdate=7 August 2013}}<br />{{cite news |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/20860955 |title=Edgar Davids in charge at Barnet after Mark Robson departs |publisher=BBC Sport |date=28 December 2012 |accessdate=7 August 2013}}</ref>。2013年2月，奥利·李到伯明翰城試訓<ref>{{cite news |author=Tattum, Colin |url=http://www.birminghammail.co.uk/sport/football/football-news/teenage-forward-koby-arthur-called-1329537 |title=Teenager called up to Blues squad to face Owls |newspaper=Birmingham Mail |date=19 February 2013 |accessdate=7 August 2013}}</ref>，並在3月21日外借至季末<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.barnetfc.com/news/article/olly-lee-joins-birmingham-city-on-loan-727776.aspx |title=Lee joins Birmingham on loan |publisher=Barnet F.C |date=21 March 2013 |accessdate=7 August 2013}}</ref>。

===伯明翰城===
外借至伯明翰城期間，他並沒有在一線隊上場，只在球季最後一輪比賽以替補球員身份觀看比賽，但他參加了不少U21發展隊的比賽並成為隊長<ref name=Raring>{{cite news |url=http://www.birminghammail.co.uk/sport/football/football-news/birmingham-city-olly-lee-raring-4880156 |title=Olly Lee targets big break with Blues |author=Tattum, Colin |newspaper=Birmingham Mail |date=8 July 2013 |accessdate=7 August 2013}}</ref>。2013年5月，伯明翰免費簽下奥利·李，合約期為一年，球會有一年延長權<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www1.skysports.com/football/news/11095/8698890/ |title=Birmingham secure deal to bring in Barnet youngster Olly Lee |publisher=Sky Sports |date=8 May 2013 |accessdate=7 August 2013}}</ref>。

奥利·李跟隨一線隊參加了季前熱身賽，在對[[阿尔费登镇足球俱乐部|阿尔费登镇]]打進一球<ref name=Raring/>。在8月6日的[[英格蘭聯賽盃|英格蘭聯賽盃]]以替補身份首次代表球隊上場對，不久之後[[普利茅斯足球俱乐部|普利茅斯]]進球追成平局，並進入加時比賽<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.birminghammail.co.uk/sport/football/match-reports/birmingham-city-3-plymouth-2-5671539 |title=Blues 3 Plymouth 2 |author=Tattum, Colin |newspaper=Birmingham Mail |date=7 August 2013 |accessdate=7 August 2013}}</ref>。10月20日對[[列斯聯足球會|列斯聯]]的比賽中，由於[[汤姆·阿德耶米|汤姆·阿德耶米]]和[[卡勒姆·莱利|卡勒姆·莱利]]因病缺席，使他能首次在英冠聯賽上場，球隊以4-0大敗<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.birminghammail.co.uk/sport/football/football-news/boss-lee-clark-gives-blues-6214389 |title=Lee Clark: Performance was not acceptable |author=Tattum, Colin |newspaper=Birmingham Mail |date=21 October 2013 |accessdate=28 October 2013}}</ref>。在聯賽盃第四輪對[[斯托克城足球俱乐部|斯托克城]]的比賽中，他和[[彼得·洛文克蘭斯|彼得·洛文克蘭斯]]在79分鐘上場，後者攻進2球使比賽進入加時，奥利·李在加時再打進一球追成4-4，最後進入[[點球|PK大戰]]，伯明翰在大戰中輸了<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/sport/football/competitions/league-cup/10412671/Birmingham-City-4-Stoke-City-4-Stoke-win-4-2-on-pens-match-report.html |title=Birmingham City 4 Stoke City 4 |author=Culley, Jon |newspaper=Daily Telegraph |date=29 October 2013 |accessdate=31 October 2013}}<br>{{cite news |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/24619463 |title=Birmingham City 4–4 Stoke City |publisher=BBC Sport |date=29 October 2013 |accessdate=31 October 2013}}</ref>。

==個人生活==
