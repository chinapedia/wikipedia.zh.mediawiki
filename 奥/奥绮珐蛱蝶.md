'''奥绮珐蛱蝶'''（[[学名|学名]]：{{lang|la|''Phalanta alcippe''}}）是在亚洲发现的一种[[蛱蝶科|蛱蝶科]]蝴蝶。

== 亚种 ==
*''Phalanta alcippe alcippe'' (Ambon, Serang, Saparua)
*''Phalanta alcippe alcippoides'' <small>(Moore, [1900])</small> (India to southern Burma, Thailand, Sumatra, Borneo)
*''Phalanta alcippe drepana'' <small>Fruhstorfer</small> (Java)
*''Phalanta alcippe pallidior'' <small>Staudinger</small> (Palawan)
*''Phalanta alcippe violetta'' <small>Fruhstorfer</small> (Philippines (Basilan))
*''Phalanta alcippe semperi'' <small>Moore</small> (Philippines (Luzon to Cebu))
*''Phalanta alcippe alcesta'' <small>Corbet, 1941</small> (Peninsular Malaya)
*''Phalanta alcippe aurica'' <small>Eliot, 1978</small> (Pulau Aur, Pulau Permanggil)
*''Phalanta alcippe tiomana'' <small>Corbet, 1937</small> (Pulau Tioman)
*''Phalanta alcippe ceylonica'' <small>Mander</small> (Ceylon)
*''Phalanta alcippe andamana'' <small>Fruhstorfer</small> (Andaman Islands)
*''Phalanta alcippe fraterna'' <small>Moore</small> (Nicobar Islands)
*''Phalanta alcippe enganica'' <small>Fruhstorfer</small> (Enggano)
*''Phalanta alcippe floresiana'' <small>Fruhstorfer</small> (Flores, the Lesser Sunda Islands)
*''Phalanta alcippe celebensis'' <small>Wallace</small> (Sulawesi)
*''Phalanta alcippe omarion'' <small>Fruhstorfer</small> (Sula Islands)
*''Phalanta alcippe asinia'' <small>Fruhstorfer</small> (Wetar, Timor)
*''Phalanta alcippe quinta'' <small>Fruhstorfer</small> (Bachan, Halmahera)
*''Phalanta alcippe alcippina'' <small>Fruhstorfer</small> (Obi)
*''Phalanta alcippe cervina'' <small>(Butler, 1876)</small> (north-western Irian)
*''Phalanta alcippe arruanae'' <small>(Felder, 1860)</small> (Aru)
*''Phalanta alcippe cervinides'' <small>(Fruhstorfer, 1904)</small> (Waigeu)
*''Phalanta alcippe kinitis'' <small>Fruhstorfer</small> (eastern New Guinea)
*''Phalanta alcippe denosa'' <small>Fruhstorfer</small> (Bismarck Archipelago)
*''Phalanta alcippe ephyra'' <small>Godman & Salvin</small> (Bougainville, Shortlands)
*''Phalanta alcippe rennellensis'' <small>Howarth</small> (Rennel Island)
*''Phalanta alcippe bellona'' <small>Howarth</small>(Bellona Island)
