'''[[冈比亚共和国|冈比亚共和国]]'''属于[[单一制|单一制]]国家，全国总面积为11,295平方公里（[[国家面积列表|世界第164名]]）。其第一级行政区划共划分为5区1市<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cia.gov/library/publications/the-world-factbook/geos/ga.html |title=GAMBIA, The world factbook |accessdate=2014-07-08 |author= |last= |first= |authorlink= |coauthors= |date= |year= |month= |format= |work= |publisher=[[美国中央情报局|美国中央情报局]] |pages= |language=en |doi= |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140702024610/https://www.cia.gov/library/publications/the-world-factbook/geos/ga.html |archivedate=2014-07-02 |quote=Administrative divisions:5 divisions and 1 city*; Banjul*, Central River, Lower River, North Bank, Upper River, Western . |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。而区下辖县，第二级行政区划则共有37个县<ref name="About21">{{Cite web|url=http://www.statoids.com/ygm.html |title=Districts of Gambia |accessdate=2014-07-12 |author=Gwillim Law |last= |first= |authorlink= |coauthors= |date=2004-01-09 |year= |month= |format= |work=(www.statoids.com) |publisher= |pages= |language=en |doi= |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140626060617/http://statoids.com/ygm.html |archivedate=2014-06-26 |quote= |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。
<br>[[冈比亚|冈比亚]]的[[首都|首都]]叫做[[班珠尔市|班珠尔市]]，是一座坐落于[[西非|西非]]之角的海港城市。
