'''戴恩·威瑟斯普'''（{{lang|en|Dane Witherspoon}}，{{bd|1957年|12月27日|2014年|3月29日}}），是一名美国演员。他出演过电视剧《{{link-en|圣巴巴拉 (电视剧)|Santa Barbara (soap opera)|圣巴巴拉}}》、《{{link-en|国会 (电视剧)|Capitol (TV series)|国会}}》<ref name="ref1">(18 July 1985). [http://news.google.com/newspapers?id=UA1PAAAAIBAJ&sjid=0gIEAAAAIBAJ&pg=3219,2509062&dq=dane+witherspoon&hl=en "A Look At Dane Witherspoon"], ''Toledo Blade''</ref><ref name="Ref2">(November 1, 1985). [http://news.google.com/newspapers?id=-FpaAAAAIBAJ&sjid=oEwNAAAAIBAJ&pg=5307,23228&dq=dane+witherspoon&hl=en "Great Dane Finds New Home on Capitol"], ''Waycross Herald-Journal''; accessed April 1, 2014.</ref><ref name="ref3">(September 1, 1984). [http://news.google.com/newspapers?id=IQQtAAAAIBAJ&sjid=dQEGAAAAIBAJ&pg=4715,640799&dq=dane+witherspoon&hl=en "Success of 'Santa Barbara' is up to Dane Witherspoon"], ''Miami News''; accessed April 1, 2014.</ref>。

他出生于[[德薩斯州|德薩斯州]]的[[登頓_(德克薩斯州)|登頓]]，后在加州旧金山长大。2014年，死于科罗拉多州，享年56岁<ref>{{cite web|last=Newcomb|first=Roger|url=http://www.welovesoaps.net/2014/03/dane-witherspoon-dead-at-56.html|title=Dane Witherspoon Dead at 56|publisher=We Love Soaps|accessdate=2014-03-31}}</ref>。
