'''周晓文'''（{{bd|1954年||}}）是一位中国电影导演。其作品《[[二嫫|二嫫]]》非常成功，並在1994年的[[羅加諾國際電影節|羅加諾國際電影節]]上贏得了多項獎項<ref name=Encyclopedia>Zhang, Yingjin & Xiao, Zhiwei (1998). "Fifth Generation" in [http://books.google.com/books?id=MIkc8os0WPwC&pg=PA164 ''Encyclopedia of Chinese Film'']. {{link-en|Taylor & Francis|Taylor & Francis}}, p. 164. ISBN 0-415-15168-6.</ref>。因此，他的聲名便傳到中国国外。周晓文的電影風格主要為[[惊悚_(类型)|惊悚]]，並且現在仍然是一位事业有成的电影导演。<ref>Xiao, Zhiwei and Yingjin Zhang. ''Encyclopedia of Chinese Film''. {{link-en|Taylor & Francis|Taylor & Francis}}, June 1, 2002. ISBN 0203195558, 9780203195550. p. [http://books.google.com/books?id=MIkc8os0WPwC&pg=PA95&dq=%22Zhou+Xiaowen%22&hl=en&sa=X&ei=8eRfUurNMYPQ9ATW9oCABA&ved=0CDQQ6AEwAQ#v=onepage&q=%22Zhou%20Xiaowen%22&f=false 95]. "while his thrillers have achieved remarkable market success", "Few people outside China had heard of Zhou Xiaowen prior to the release of his Ermo (1994)"</ref>
