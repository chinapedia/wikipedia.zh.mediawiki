'''古蜴甲龍'''（[[學名|學名]]''Palaeoscincus''）又名'''古伊犁龍'''，意為「古代的[[石龍子|石龍子]]」，是[[甲龍下目|甲龍下目]][[恐龍|恐龍]]的一屬，狀態為[[疑名|疑名]]。[[化石|化石]]只有[[牙齒|牙齒]]，發現於[[蒙大拿州|蒙大拿州]]的[[朱迪斯河組|朱迪斯河組]]，地質年代為[[上白堊紀|上白堊紀]][[坎帕階|坎帕階]]中期<ref name ="JL56">{{cite journal | last1 = Leidy | first1 = J. | year = 1856 | title = Notice of remains of extinct reptiles and fishes, discovered by Dr. F. V. Hayden in the Bad Lands of the Judith River, Nebraska Territories | url = | journal = Proceedings of the Academy of Natural Science Philadelphia | volume = 8 | issue = | pages = 72–73 }}</ref>。就像其他由[[約瑟夫·萊迪|約瑟夫·萊迪]]（Joseph Leidy）所命名的屬，例如[[恐齒龍|恐齒龍]]、[[強龍|強龍]]及[[糙齒龍|糙齒龍]]，古蜴甲龍過去層包含許多種，但目前多被[[古生物學家|古生物學家]]廢棄不用。在20世紀早期，古蜴甲龍曾被廣泛地使用，而成為知名的恐龍之一。古蜴甲龍通常會被描繪成有類似[[埃德蒙頓甲龍|埃德蒙頓甲龍]]的裝甲，以及[[甲龍科|甲龍科]]的尾槌。

==物種==
許多年來，古蜴甲龍曾經有6個[[物種|物種]]，包括：

*''P. costatus''：[[模式種|模式種]]，是一顆[[牙齒|牙齒]]。
*非洲古蜴甲龍（''P. africanus''）<ref name="RB12">{{cite journal | doi = 10.1080/00359191009519357 | last1 = Broom | first1 = R. | year = 1912 | title = Observations on some specimens of South African fossil reptiles preserved in the British Museum | url = | journal = Transactions of the Royal Society of South Africa | volume = 2 | issue = | pages = 19–25 }}</ref>：部份下頜，發現於[[南非|南非]]的Kirkwood組，地質年代為晚[[侏羅紀|侏羅紀]][[提通階|提通階]]至[[下白堊紀|下白堊紀]][[凡藍今階|凡藍今階]]。目前被認為屬於[[劍龍下目|劍龍下目]]的[[似花君龍|似花君龍]]<ref name="WC78">{{cite journal | last1 = Coombs | first1 = Jr. | year = 1978 | title = The families of the ornithischian dinosaur order Ankylosauria | url =http://palaeontology.palass-pubs.org/pdf/Vol%2021/Pages%20143-170.pdf | journal = Palaeontology | volume = 21 | issue = 1| pages = 143–170 }}</ref>。
*''P. asper''：化石只有牙齒，化石發現於[[加拿大|加拿大]][[艾伯塔省|艾伯塔省]]的[[恐龍公園組|恐龍公園組]]，地質年代為上白堊紀[[坎帕階|坎帕階]]末期<ref name="LL02">{{cite journal | last1 = Lambe | first1 = L.M. | year = 1902 | title = On Vertebrata of the mid-Cretaceous of the Northwest Territory. 2. New genera and species from the Belly River Series (mid-Cretaceous) | url = | journal = Contributions to Canadian Paleontology | volume = 3 | issue = | pages = 25–81 }}</ref>。目前的狀態為疑名，可能屬於[[包頭龍|包頭龍]]<ref name="WC90">Coombs, Jr., W.P. (1990). Teeth and taxonomy in ankylosaurs. In: Carpenter, K., and Currie, P.J. (eds.). ''Dinosaur Systematics: Approaches and Perspectives''. Cambridge University Press:Cambridge, 269-279.  ISBN 0-521-36672-0</ref>。
*寬牙古蜴甲龍（''P. latus''）：發現於[[懷俄明州|懷俄明州]]的[[蘭斯組|蘭斯組]]，年代為上白堊紀[[麥斯特裡希特階|麥斯特裡希特階]]末期<ref name="OCM92">{{cite journal | last1 = Marsh | first1 = O.C. | year = 1892 | title = Notes on Mesozoic vertebrate fossils | url = | journal = American Journal of Science | volume = 44 | issue = | pages = 171–176 }}</ref>，也是只有一顆牙齒，目前被認為屬於[[厚頭龍下目|厚頭龍下目]]<ref name="WC90"/>。
*''P. magoder''：[[無資格名稱|無資格名稱]]，只曾在一個[[動物群|動物群]]列表中出現<ref name="H14">{{cite journal | doi = 10.1007/BF01496485 | last1 = Henning | first1 = C.L. | year = 1914 | title = Ueber neuer Saurierfunde aus Kanada und deren geologische Position | url = | journal = Naturwissenschaften | volume = 2 | issue = 31| pages = 769–776 }}</ref><ref name="WC90"/>。
*粗齒古蜴甲龍（''P. rugosidens''）：最廣為人知，包含一個[[頭顱骨|頭顱骨]]及部份骨骼，發現於[[蒙大拿州|蒙大拿州]]的[[雙麥迪遜組|雙麥迪遜組]]，年代為坎帕階末期<ref name="CWG30">{{cite journal | last1 = Gilmore | first1 = C.W. | year = 1930 | title = On dinosaurian reptiles from the Two Medicine Formation of Montana | url = | journal = United States National Museum, Proceedings | volume = 77 | issue = | pages = 1–39 }}</ref>。目前被重新命名為[[埃德蒙頓甲龍|粗齒埃德蒙頓甲龍]]（''Edmontonia rugosidens''）<ref name="WC90"/>。古蜴甲龍的重建，多是根據此種而描繪的。

==分類==
古伊犁龍目前被認為是[[甲龍下目|甲龍下目]]的分類未定屬<ref>Carpenter, K. (2001). Phylogenetic analysis of the Ankylosauria. In: Carpenter, K. (ed.). ''The Armored Dinosaurs''. Indiana University Press:Bloomington, 455-483. ISBN 0-253-33964-2</ref><ref name="VMW04">Vickaryous, M.K., Maryańska, T., and Weishampel, D.B., (2004). Ankylosauria. In: Weishampel, D.B., Dodson, P., and Osmólska, H. (eds.). ''The Dinosauria (second edition)''. University of California Press:Berkeley, 363-392.  ISBN 0-520-24209-2</ref>，或可能是[[結節龍科|結節龍科]]的分類未定屬<ref name="WC78"/><ref name="WC90"/><ref name="TF00">Ford, T.L. (2000). A review of ankylosaur osteoderms from New Mexico and a preliminary review of ankylosaur armor. In: Lucas, S.G., and Heckert, A.B. (eds.). ''Dinosaurs of New Mexico''. New Mexico Museum of Natural History and Science Bulletin 17:157-176.</ref>。
