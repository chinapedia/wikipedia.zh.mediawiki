'''赖森县'''，[[印度|印度]][[中央邦|中央邦]]的一个[[县|县]]，隶属[[博帕尔专区|博帕尔专区]]。赖森县北临[[维迪沙县|维迪沙县]]，东临[[萨格尔县|萨格尔县]]。总面积8395 km²，总人口1,331,699人(2011)。行政中心为[[赖森|赖森]]镇。该县县境在北纬22 47'到23 33' ，东经77 21'至78 49' 之间。<ref name=admin>{{cite web|url =http://raisen.nic.in/ | title = Raisen | work = |publisher =  Raisen district administration |accessdate = 2010-08-14 }}</ref>

赖森县距离[[博帕尔|博帕尔]]45公里，通过NH-86连接到博帕尔。NH-12也通过该县。

==历史==
赖森县成立于1950年5月5日。<ref name=admin/>

[[世界遗产|世界遗产]]“[[桑吉|桑吉的佛教古迹]]”位于赖森县。<ref>{{cite web|url = http://projectsouthasia.sdstate.edu/docs/archaeology/primarydocs/Sanchi/HistArt.htm|title = An Historical and Artistic Description of Sanchi (1918)|work = |publisher = |accessdate = 2010-08-14|deadurl = yes|archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20090210033559/http://projectsouthasia.sdstate.edu/docs/archaeology/primarydocs/Sanchi/HistArt.htm|archivedate = 2009-02-10}}</ref>另一个世界遗产[[比莫贝特卡岩荫群|比莫贝特卡岩荫群]]也位于该县。<ref>{{cite web|url = http://whc.unesco.org/pg.cfm?cid=31&id_site=925 | title = Rock Shelters of bhimbhetka| work = |publisher = UNESCO  |accessdate = 2010-08-14 }}</ref>
