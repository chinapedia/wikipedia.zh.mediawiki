'''延斯．福格特（Jens Voigt，'''<small contenteditable="false">German: </small><small contenteditable="false"></small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)" contenteditable="false">[jɛns foːkt]</span>）（1971年9月17日出生）是前自行車職業選手，先後曾加入德國不同的車隊，退役前為 UCI ProTeam [[崔克车队|Trek–Segafredo]]的成員<ref><cite class="citation web" contenteditable="false">[http://www.uciworldtour.com/Modules/SUCI/TEAMS/TeamDetails.asp?id=NDA3Mg&RefDate=10.01.2013&MenuId=MTYzMDE&LangId=1&BackLink=%2Ftemplates%2FUCI%2FUCI2%2Flayout%2Easp%3FMenuId%3DMTYzMDE%26LangId%3D1 "RadioShack-Leopard (RLT) – LUX"]. </cite></ref>。 福格特曾成功兩次穿上[[環法自行車賽|環法自行車賽]]（Tour de France）的[[环法自行车赛总成绩领先者|黃衫]] ，然而由於不擅山路，他從來不是總冠軍的熱門人選。 他生涯中創紀錄五次奪下[[國際準則自行車賽|國際準則自行車賽]]（Critérium International）的冠軍，也贏得不少巡迴賽事及環法自行車賽的分段賽事。2014年9月，他創下[[場地賽事|場地賽事]]紀錄<ref name="Yo"><cite class="citation news">[http://www.sbs.com.au/cyclingcentral/news/56702/Voigt-cruises-to-new-hour-mark "Voigt cruises to new hour mark"]. </cite></ref><ref name="hour"><cite class="citation news" contenteditable="false">[http://www.dw.de/jens-voigt-breaks-uci-hour-record-in-grenchen/a-17933730 "Jens Voigt breaks UCI hour record in Grenchen"]. </cite></ref>。後來同年10月則由 Matthias Brändle 超越<ref name="clown"><cite class="citation news" contenteditable="false">Spencer Powlison (31 October 2014). </cite></ref><ref group="upper-alpha">Interestingly, Brändle was 19 years younger than the 43-year-old Voigt when he eclipsed the record.</ref>

歸功於福格特積極進取的騎車風格，加上面對大眾及媒體，他既親切直率，又善於表達，福格特是位相當受自行車粉絲歡迎的車手，<ref><cite class="citation web" contenteditable="false">[http://www.usaprocyclingchallenge.com/teams/member/jens-voigt "USA Pro Cycling Challenge profile: Jens Voigt"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141113145514/http://www.usaprocyclingchallenge.com/teams/member/jens-voigt |date=2014-11-13 }}. </cite></ref><ref name="READY TO DIE!"><cite class="citation news" contenteditable="false">Jered Gruber (4 February 2012). </cite></ref> 除了母語德文外，他的法文及英文也說得流利。<ref><cite class="citation news" contenteditable="false">[http://www.last-video.com/le-geste-de-jens-voigt-dans-lalpe-dhuez-pour-un-enfant "Le geste de Jens Voigt dans l’Alpe d’Huez pour un enfant"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130721110351/http://www.last-video.com/le-geste-de-jens-voigt-dans-lalpe-dhuez-pour-un-enfant |date=2013-07-21 }}. </cite></ref>
