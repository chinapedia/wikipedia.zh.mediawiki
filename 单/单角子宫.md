'''单角子宫'''（{{lang-en|Unicornuate uterus}}），是一类妇科发育障碍，系仅一侧[[副中肾管|副中肾管]]发育完全而形成。同侧可有发育正常的[[输卵管|输卵管]]、[[卵巢|卵巢]]。另一侧副中肾管未发育，输卵管、卵巢、[[肾脏|肾脏]]亦常同时缺如<ref name=woelfer>{{cite journal |pages=1099–103 |doi=10.1016/S0029-7844(01)01599-X |title=Reproductive outcomes in women with congenital uterine anomalies detected by three-dimensional ultrasound screening |year=2001 |last1=Woelfer |first1=B |journal=Obstetrics & Gynecology |volume=98 |issue=6 |pmid=11755560}}</ref>。

患者[[月经|月经]]及[[性生活|性生活]]可表现正常。可导致[[不孕|不孕]]、[[妊娠|妊娠]]后可造成胎儿宫内发育迟缓、胎儿存活率低<ref>{{cite journal |pages=1886–94 |doi=10.1016/j.fertnstert.2008.02.163 |title=Pregnancy outcomes in unicornuate uteri: a review |year=2009 |last1=Reichman |first1=David |last2=Laufer |first2=Marc R. |last3=Robinson |first3=Barrett K. |journal=Fertility and Sterility |volume=91 |issue=5 |pmid=18439594}}</ref>。妊娠产前检查时，胎儿向一侧突出，子宫呈囊状包裹胎体。子宫可偏向一侧。
