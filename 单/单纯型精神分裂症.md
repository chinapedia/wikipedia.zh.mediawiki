{{medical}}
'''单纯型精神分裂症'''在[[ICD-10|ICD-10]]分类为F20.6,<ref>[[World_Health_Organisation|World Health Organisation]] - [http://www.who.int/classifications/icd/en/bluebook.pdf International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems - Classification of Mental and Behavioural Disorders] RETRIEVED 2012-01-21</ref>在ICD-9的代码是295.00 - 295.05。<ref>[http://www.cms.gov/medicare-coverage-database/staticpages/icd9-code-range.aspx?DocType=LCD&DocID=30491&ver=8&Group=1&RangeStart=295.00&RangeEnd=295.95&LCDId=30491&ContrTypeId=1&ContrNum=00951&Date=%20&SearchType=Advanced&bc=KAAAABAAQAAA&  U.S. Department of Health and Human Services] RETRIEVED 2012-01-21</ref>

==诊断==
与所有精神分裂症患者相比，单纯型精神分裂症在年轻者中最多见，并被认为从大约从童年开始。
即使单纯型精神分裂症患者沒有妄想、幻觉和合作困难，他們還是會缺乏意志、思维贫乏和情感冷漠。
缺乏动机增多和[[社會化|社會化]]减少的普遍功能恶化。<ref>[[Brown_University|Brown University]] - [http://www.brown.edu/Courses/BI_278/Other/Clerkship/Didactics/Readings/Schizophrenia.pdf Schizophrenia DSM-IV TR#295.1-295.30, 295.90）]RETRIEVED 2012-01-22</ref><ref>Baiceng Lou, Thomas Dey - Soothing the troubled mind: acupuncture and moxibustion in the treatment of schizophrenia [http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=xxqMQ7QVsLYC&pg=PA21&dq=%22Simple-type+schizophrenia%22&hl=en&sa=X&ei=ytAbT9rKMcSZ8gOdkJmfCw&redir_esc=y#v=onepage&q=%22Simple-type%20schizophrenia%22&f=false Paradigm Publications, 1999] RETRIEVED 2012-01-22</ref>此症被认为是罕见、没有思维失调症状的一种精神分裂症（Mueser & Jeste  2008）。<ref>[http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=KFMFzuL3husC&dq=schizophrenia+Simple-type&source=gbs_navlinks_s  Kim Tornvall Mueser & Dilip V. Jeste] 2008 - Clinical Handbook of Schizophrenia   -  650 pages [http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=KFMFzuL3husC&pg=PA101&lpg=PA101&dq=schizophrenia+Simple-type&source=bl&ots=-zLd-qUDIU&sig=tx6GsIqgN2HdU8Ouv_kGe8IgiFU&hl=en&sa=X&ei=susbT_fdKpLm8QPR0KS_Cw&ved=0CCwQ6AEwADge#v=onepage&q=schizophrenia%20Simple-type&f=false Guilford Press, 27 Mar 2008] RETRIEVED 2012-01-22  ISBN 1593856520</ref>

==反对==
此类型精神分裂症的定义是不一致的，或有争议的，也可以说成是观点有分歧。此分类已经在U.S. DSM系统中停止，尽管被建议重新加入（Black & Boffeli 1989），<ref>Black DW, Boffeli TJ. -  Simple schizophrenia: past, present, and future. Am J Psychiatry. 1989 Oct;146（10）:1267-73. [http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/sites/entrez/2675642?dopt=Abstract&holding=f1000,f1000m,isrctn PMID: 2675642] RETRIEVED 2012-01-21</ref>之后被证实有根据不同描述的不确定的诊断标准 （Black & Boffeli 1990）。<ref>Donald W. Black, Todd J. Boffeli - Simple schizophrenia: Revisited pubhlished in ''Comprehensive Psychiatry July–August 1990'' [http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/0010440X9090041P Copyright © 1990, Elsevier] RETRIEVED 2012-01-22</ref>

[http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/sites/entrez/16243494?dopt=Abstract&holding=f1000,f1000m,isrctn ncbi.nlm] [http://www.mendeley.com/research/prefrontal-abnormalities-patients-simple-schizophrenia-structural-functional-brainimaging-studies-five-cases/ mendeley] RETRIEVED 2012-01-22</ref> Whitwell ''et al'' 2005年发现，发现了由于此分类“维度性”考虑，在实践基础上保留此分类的正当性，与使用其他分类的判断方向之判断所产生的意见截然相反。<ref>Susannah Whitwell, Jessica Bramham and John Moriarty 2005 - ''Simple schizophrenia or disorganisation syndrome?
