'''文蘭'''是約公元一千年时[[古挪威人|古挪威人]]給[[北美|北美]]一個地區的名字。

[[古挪威人|古挪威人]][[萨迦_(文学)|傳奇史詩]]記載中出現的文蘭，證明了[[維京人|維京人]]早在[[哥倫布|哥倫布]]航行五個世紀之前就已經到達北美洲，此一傳統理論亦為考古學所支持。<ref>Jones, Gwyn (1986). ''The Norse Atlantic Saga: Being the Norse Voyages of Discovery and Settlement to Iceland, Greenland, and North America''. Oxford University Press. ISBN 0-19-285160-8.</ref>1960年代，[[考古學|考古學]]家發現了[[格陵蘭島|格陵蘭島]]之外北美惟一一個[[維京人殖民北美|古挪威人定居點]]，地點是[[紐芬蘭|紐芬蘭]]島北端，今天[[加拿大|加拿大]][[紐芬蘭-拉布拉多|紐芬蘭-拉布拉多]][[省|省]]的[[蘭塞奧茲牧草地|蘭塞奧茲牧草地]]。<ref>Ingstad, Helge; Ingstad, Anne Stine (2001). ''The Viking Discovery of America: The Excavation of a Norse Settlement in L'Anse Aux Meadows, Newfoundland''. Checkmark Books. ISBN 0-8160-4716-2.</ref>這個發現決定性地證明了維京人在哥倫布之前就抵達了北美。最近的考古研究認為此遺址並不全然是古挪威人記錄中的文蘭，而是一個入口，通往一個更大的叫文蘭的地方。<ref name="Vinland">{{cite web|title=Is L'Anse aux Meadows Vinland? |work=L'Anse aux Meadows National Historic Site of Canada |publisher=Parks Canada |year=2003 |url=http://www.pc.gc.ca/lhn-nhs/nl/meadows/natcul/vinland_e.asp |accessdate=2010-04-20 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070522105216/http://www.pc.gc.ca/lhn-nhs/nl/meadows/natcul/vinland_e.asp |archivedate=2007-05-22 }}</ref><ref name="damals">{{cite journal| title=Vinland-Rätsel gelöst (Vinland Riddle Solved)| first=Birgitta |last=Wallace |coauthors=Gerhard E.
