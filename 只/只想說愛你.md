'''只想說愛你'''（'''''In Case You Didn't Know'''''）是英國歌手[[奧利·莫爾斯|奧利·莫爾斯]]的第二張錄音室專輯，發行於2011年11月25日<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ollymurs.com/music/release/olly_murs_-_untitled_2nd_album/ |title=Right Place Right Time Special Edition CD/DVD | The Official Olly Murs Site |publisher=Ollymurs.com |date= |accessdate=2014-06-28 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120102215706/http://www.ollymurs.com/music/release/olly_murs_-_untitled_2nd_album |archivedate=2012-01-02 }}</ref>。專輯的首個單曲Heart Skips a Beat在2011年8月19日公開，另一支單曲Dance with Me Tonight在2011年11月20日發行<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.digitalspy.co.uk/music/news/a344386/olly-murs-unveils-album-in-case-you-didnt-know-artwork-tracklist.html |title=Olly Murs unveils album 'In Case You Didn't Know' artwork, tracklist – Music News |publisher=Digital Spy |date=2011-10-07 |accessdate=2014-06-28}}</ref>。只想說愛你曾排名[[英國專輯排行榜|英國專輯排行榜]]第二名，首周銷量達108,000張<ref name=Jones>{{cite web|last=Jones|first=Alan|title=Official Album Chart analysis: Olly Murs becomes 10th X-Factor No.1|url=http://www.musicweek.com/story.asp?sectioncode=1&storycode=1047728&c=1|work=[[Music_Week|Music Week]]|accessdate=4 December 2011|date=4 December 2011}}</ref>。
