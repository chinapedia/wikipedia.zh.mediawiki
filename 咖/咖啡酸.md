'''咖啡酸'''（{{lang-en|'''Caffeic acid'''}}）是一种归属于羟基肉桂酸的有机化合物，同时具有[[苯酚|酚羟基]]和[[丙烯酸|丙烯酸]]两种官能团结构。因为咖啡酸是生物合成植物生物质及其残留物的主要组分之一[[木质素|木质素]]的关键中间体，所以它广泛存在于各种植物中<ref name=Boerjan>{{cite journal|url=http://www.annualreviews.org/doi/10.1146/annurev.arplant.54.031902.134938|doi=10.1146/annurev.arplant.54.031902.134938|title=Ligninbiosynthesis|year=2003|last1=Boerjan|first1=Wout|last2=Ralph|first2=John|last3=Baucher|first3=Marie|journal=Annual Review of Plant Biology|volume=54|pages=519–546|pmid=14503002}}</ref>。它可以在热水和[[乙醇|乙醇]]中溶解，冷水中微溶。常温下为黄白色[[结晶|结晶]]体。

===食物中的含量===
咖啡酸在[[咖啡|咖啡]]中的含量非常低，仅为0.03毫克/ 100毫升。它是[[摩洛哥坚果油|摩洛哥坚果油]]所含的主要天然酚类之一<ref>{{Cite journal|doi=10.3923/ajft.2007.679.683|
url=http://www.scialert.net/abstract/?doi=ajft.2007.679.683|title=Phenols and Polyphenols ''from Argania'' spinosa|year=2007|last1=.|first1=Z. Charrouf|
last2=.|first2=D. Guillaume|journal=American Journal of Food Technology|volume=2|issue=7|pages=679}}</ref>，在一些草药，特别是百里香、鼠尾草和留兰香中咖啡
酸的含量较高（约20mg/100g）。在[[锡兰肉桂|锡兰肉桂]]和[[八角茴香|八角茴香]]中的含量约22mg/100g。 向日葵种子中的咖啡酸含量较高（8mg/100g），在红葡萄酒存在一定量的咖啡
酸（1.88mg/100ml），苹果酱，杏和李子中也含有咖啡酸（1mg/100g）。 南美州的草药[[巴拉圭冬青|巴拉圭冬青]]中也有相当高含量的咖啡酸（基于薄层色谱密度测定<ref>{{cite journal|url =https://www.hindawi.com/journals/jamc/2013/658596/|doi=10.1155/2013/658596|title=Determination of Flavonoids, Phenolic Acids, and Xanthines in Mate Tea (Ilex paraguariensis St.-Hil.)}}</ref>和[[高效液相色谱法|高效液相色谱法]]<ref>{{cite journal|title =Chemical Composition and Antioxidant Activity of Yerba-Mate (Ilex paraguariensis A.St.-Hil., Aquifoliaceae) Extract as Obtained by Spray Drying|journal = Journal of Analytical Methods in Chemistry|url = http://pubs.acs.org/doi/abs/10.1021/jf2008343|doi = 10.1021/jf2008343}}</ref>测得的含量为150mg /100g）。同时也存在于大麦谷物中<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Quinde-Axtell | first1 = Zory | last2 = Baik | first2 = Byung-Kee | year = 2006 | title = Phenolic Compounds of Barley Grain and Their Implication in Food Product Discoloration | url = http://pubs.acs.org/doi/abs/10.1021/jf060974w| journal = J. Agric. Food Chem. | volume = 54 | issue = 26 | pages = 9978–9984 | doi =10.1021/jf060974w | pmid=17177530}}</ref>。

==代谢途径==
===生源合成===
咖啡酸的生源合成是从[[绿原酸|绿原酸]]的香豆酸酯（酯化的侧链醇）经[[羟基化|羟基化]]产生的。 这种羟基化过程产生莽草酸的咖啡酸酯，进而转化为绿原酸。 它是阿魏酸，松柏醇和芥子醇的前体，它们是形成木质素的重要结构单元<ref name=Boerjan/>。

许多种植物能产生咖啡酸及其衍生物咖啡酸苯乙醇酯<ref name="pmid11208940"/>。

===生物转化===
咖啡因O-甲基转移酶是负责将咖啡酸转化为[[阿魏酸|阿魏酸]]的酶。咖啡酸和相关的邻二酚被组织提取物中的邻二酚氧化酶快速氧化<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Pierpoint | first1 = W. S. | year = 1969 | title = ''o''-Quinones formed in plant extracts. Their reactions with amino acids and peptides | url = http://www.biochemj.org/bj/112/bj1120609.htm | journal = Biochem. J. | volume = 112 | issue = | pages = 609–616 | doi=10.1042/bj1120609}}</ref>。

==药理作用==
在体外研究和动物模型中发现咖啡酸具有多种潜在的药理作用，通过氧化机制咖啡酸对人HT-1080纤维肉瘤细胞系中的对癌细胞增殖的抑制作用。<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Rajendra Prasad | first1 = N. | last2 = Karthikeyan | first2 = A. | last3 = Karthikeyan | first3 = S. | last4 = Reddy | first4 = B. V. | date = Mar 2011 | title = Inhibitory effect of caffeic acid on cancer cell proliferation by oxidative mechanism in human HT-1080 fibrosarcoma cell line | url = | journal = Mol Cell Biochem | volume = 349 | issue = 1–2| pages = 11–19 | doi = 10.1007/s11010-010-0655-7 }}</ref>
咖啡酸在体外和活体内都是一种抗氧化剂<ref name="pmid11208940">{{cite journal |last1=Olthof |first1=M. R. |last2=Hollman |first2=P. C. |last3=Katan |first3=M. B. |title=Chlorogenic acid and caffeic acid are absorbed in humans |journal=J. Nutr. |volume=131 |issue=1 |pages=66–71 |date=January 2001 |pmid=11208940 |doi= |url=http://jn.nutrition.org/cgi/pmidlookup?view=long&pmid=11208940 }}</ref>。咖啡酸还显示出免疫调节和抗炎活性，咖啡酸能使黄曲霉毒素产量降低超过95％，优于其他抗氧化剂。研究表明否则触发[[黄曲霉|黄曲霉]]产生[[黃麴毒素|黃麴毒素]]的应激氧化可被咖啡酸阻碍。
对咖啡酸致癌性的研究具有混合的结果<ref name= Hirose>{{cite journal|journal=Carcinogenesis|volume=19|pages=207–212|year=1998|title=Carcinogenicity of antioxidants BHA, caffeic acid, sesamol, 4-methoxyphenol and catechol at low doses, either alone or in combination, and modulation of their effects in a rat medium-term multi-organ carcinogenesis model|first1=M. |last1=Hirose |first2=Y. |last2=Takesada |first3=H. |last3=Tanaka |first4=S. |last4=Tamano |first5=T. |last5=Kato |first6=T. |last6=Shirai |url=http://carcin.oxfordjournals.org/cgi/reprint/19/1/207.pdf|doi=10.1093/carcin/19.1.207|pmid=9472713|issue=1}}</ref>。 一部分研究结果表明它抑制致癌作用，另一部分结果显示咖啡酸有致癌作用<ref name= Hirose/>。对大鼠进行高剂量咖啡酸的口服给药会引起胃乳头状瘤<ref name= Hirose/>。在相同的研究中，高剂量包含咖啡酸的组合抗氧化剂则能显著减少相同种类大鼠[[结肠|结肠]][[肿瘤|肿瘤]]的生长<ref name= Hirose/>。在一些危险物数据表中咖啡酸被列为潜在致癌物，它已被[[国际癌症研究机构|国际癌症研究机构]]列为[[2B类致癌物|2B类致癌物]]（“可能对人类致癌”）<ref>[http://monographs.iarc.fr/ENG/Classification/ClassificationsGroupOrder.pdf Agents Classified by the IARC Monographs], [[International_Agency_for_Research_on_Cancer|International Agency for Research on Cancer]]</ref>。一些研究数据显示，大鼠肠道中的细菌可能改变咖啡酸[[代谢产物|代谢产物]]的形成<ref>{{cite journal|journal=Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences|volume=69|pages=1413–1415|year=1972|title=Caffeic acid metabolism by gnotobiotic rats and their intestinal bacteria|first1=M. A. |last1=Peppercorn |first2=P. |last2=Goldman |url=http://www.pnas.org/cgi/content/abstract/69/6/1413|pmid=4504351|doi=10.1073/pnas.69.6.1413|issue=6|pmc=426714}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|journal=Journal of Nutrition|volume=133|pages=1853–1859|date=1 June 2003|title=Chlorogenic acid bioavailability largely depends on its metabolism by the gut microflora in rats|first1=M.-P. |last1=Gonthier |first2=M.-A. |last2=Verny |first3=C. |last3=Besson |first4=C. |last4=Rémésy |first5=A. |last5=Scalbert |url=http://jn.nutrition.org/cgi/content/full/133/6/1853|pmid=12771329|issue=6}}</ref>。

==化学性质==
咖啡酸易于自动氧化。[[谷胱甘肽|谷胱甘肽]]以及[[硫醇|硫醇]]类化合物（例如[[半胱氨酸|半胱氨酸]]，[[巯基乙酸|巯基乙酸]]或甲琉酚）或抗坏血酸对咖啡酸的褐变和消失具有抑制作用<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Cilliers | first1 = Johannes J. L. | last2 = Singleton | first2 = Vernon L. | year = 1990 | title = Caffeic acid autoxidation and the effects of thiols | url = http://pubs.acs.org/doi/abs/10.1021/jf00099a002| journal = J. Agric. Food Chem. | volume = 38 | issue = 9| pages = 1789–1796 | doi = 10.1021/jf00099a002 }}</ref>，咖啡酸的褐变是由邻二酚被氧化为邻二醌造成的。在酸性条件下用[[高碘酸钠|高碘酸钠]]对咖啡酸进行化学氧化会生成具有呋喃结构的二聚体（2,5-（3'，4'-二羟基苯基）四氢呋喃3,4-二羧酸的异构体）<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Fulcrand | first1 = Hélène | last2 = Cheminat | first2 = Annie | last3 = Brouillard | first3 = Raymond | last4 = Cheynier | first4 = Véronique | year = 1994 | title = Characterization of compounds obtained by chemical oxidation of caffeic acid in acidic conditions | url = http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0031942200947903?via%3Dihub| journal = Phytochemistry | volume = 35 | issue = 2| pages = 499–505 | doi = 10.1016/S0031-9422(00)94790-3 }}</ref>。使用[[辣根过氧化物酶|辣根过氧化物酶]]/[[过氧化氢|过氧化氢]]的氧化系统能使咖啡酸聚合<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Xu | first1 = Peng | last2 = Uyama | first2 = Hiroshi | last3 = Whitten | first3 = James E. | last4 = Kobayashi | first4 = Shiro | last5 = Kaplan | first5 = David L. | year = 2005 | title = Peroxidase-Catalyzed in Situ Polymerization of Surface Orientated Caffeic Acid | url = http://pubs.acs.org/doi/abs/10.1021/ja051637r| journal = J. Am. Chem. Soc. | volume = 127 | issue = 33| pages = 11745–11753 | doi = 10.1021/ja051637r }}</ref>。
