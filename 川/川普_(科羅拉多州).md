'''川普'''（{{lang|en|Trump}}）為[[美國|美國]][[科羅拉多州|科羅拉多州]][[帕克縣_(科羅拉多州)|帕克縣]]的一座[[鬼鎮|鬼鎮]]<ref name=GNIS/>。

==歷史==
川普一開始為農業及畜牧業的聚居地，而1920年代至1930年代期間曾繁榮一時<ref name="PCLHA">{{cite book | last = Park County Local History Archives | title = Park County | publisher = Arcadia | year = 2015 | url = https://books.google.ca/books?id=WpmvCQAAQBAJ&lpg=PA1&pg=PA1#v=onepage&q&f=false | pages = 69, 120}}</ref><ref name="MS">{{cite book | last = McConnell Simmons | first = Virginia | title = Bayou Salado | publisher = University Press of Colorado | year = 2011 | url = https://books.google.ca/books?id=HL-9AwAAQBAJ&lpg=PP1&pg=PP1#v=onepage&q&f=false}}</ref>。

川普境內設有一間商店以及一座鄰近牧場的郵局。川普還曾舉辦過當地[[牛仔|牛仔]]之間的競技賽<ref name="PCLHA"/><ref name="MS"/>。

川普在1936年人口僅剩3人<ref name="MS"/>。

1937年，J·H·約翰遜（J. H. Johnson）在川普附近鑒定出一種獨特的[[砂岩|砂岩]]，並將此砂岩命名為「川普礫岩」（Trump conglomerate）<ref>{{cite book | last = Keroher | first = Grace C. | title = Lexicon of Geologic Names of the United States for 1936-1960, Part 3 | publisher = U.S. Government Printing Office | year = 1966 | url = https://books.google.ca/books?id=b9YSAQAAIAAJ&pg=PA2885#v=onepage&q&f=false | pages = 3966}}</ref><ref>{{cite book | last1 = Barkmann | first1 = Peter E. | last2 = Sterne | first2 = Edward J. | last3 = Dechesne | first3 = Marieke | last4 = Houck | first4 = Karen J. | year = 2016 | editor1-last = Keller | editor1-first = Stephen M. | editor2-last = Morgan | editor2-first = Matthew L. | title = South Park, Colorado: The Interplay Of Tectonics And Sedimentation Creates One Of Colorado's Crown Jewels | work = Unfolding The Geology Of The West | url = https://books.google.ca/books?id=VVH7DAAAQBAJ&lpg=PP1&pg=PP1#v=onepage&q&f=false | publisher = Geological Society of America | page = 162}}</ref>。
