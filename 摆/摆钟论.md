《'''摆钟论'''》是[[惠更斯|惠更斯]]于1673年出版的书，这是他在[[摆|摆]]和[[钟表学|钟表学]]方面的主要著作。<ref name="Herivel">{{cite web | url=http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/277775/Christiaan-Huygens?anchor=ref136385 | title=Christian Huygens | work=[[Encyclopedia_Britannica|Encyclopedia Britannica]] | accessdate=2013-11-14 | author=Herivel, John}}</ref>该书被认为是17世纪力学三大著作之一，另两本是[[伽利略|伽利略]]的[[两种新科学|两种新科学]](1638)和[[牛顿|牛顿]]的[[自然哲学的数学原理|自然哲学的数学原理]](1687)。<ref name="bell">{{cite web | url=http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/277775/Christiaan-Huygens?anchor=ref136385 | title=The Horologium Oscillatorium of Christian Huygens | date =  30 Aug 1941 | accessdate=2013-11-14 | author=Bell, A. E. | doi= 10.1038/148245a0}}</ref>

这本书包括钟的设计描述，摆动的分析，以及曲线理论。惠更斯采用更明晰的逻辑架构重新推导了伽利略对物体下落的原始研究；<ref>{{cite web |last =Ducheyne|first=Steffen|date =2008|url =http://scielo.isciii.es/scielo.php?script=sci_arttext&pid=S0211-95362008000100011&lng=es&nrm=iso&tlng=es|title =Galileo and Huygens on free fall: Mathematical and methodological differences|journal =Dynamis : Acta Hispanica ad Medicinae Scientiarumque. Historiam Illustrandam| volume = 28 | pages = 243-274 | issn = 0211-9536 |accessdate = 2013-12-27}}</ref>求出了[[等时降落问题|等时降落问题]]的解——[[旋轮线|旋轮线]]，而不是伽利略认为的圆；<ref>{{cite web|last=Mahoney |first=Michael S. |date=March 19, 2007 |url=http://www.princeton.edu/~mike/articles/huygens/timelong/timelong.html |title=Christian Huygens: The Measurement of Time and of Longitude at Sea |publisher=Princeton University |accessdate=2013-12-27 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071204152637/http://www.princeton.edu/~mike/articles/huygens/timelong/timelong.html |archivedate=2007-12-04 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>并且概述了[[渐屈线|渐屈线]]和[[弧长|弧长]]的理论。惠更斯还求出了几个动力学问题的解，比如[[单摆|单摆]]和[[复摆|复摆]]的振动周期、[[振动中心|振动中心]]及其与轴心点的可互换性；<ref>{{cite conference | first = Fabio | last = Bevilaqua |author2=Lidia Falomo |author3=Lucio Fregonese |author4=Enrico Gianetto |author5=Franco Giudise |author6=Paolo Mascheretti  | title = The pendulum: From constrained fall to the concept of potential | booktitle = The Pendulum: Scientific, Historical, Philosophical, and Educational Perspectives | pages = 195–200  | publisher = Springer | year = 2005 | url = http://books.google.com/books?id=3GV2NgDwtjMC&pg=PA195 | isbn = 1-4020-3525-X | accessdate = 2008-02-26}} 详细描述了惠更斯的方法</ref>提出了匀速圆周运动的[[离心率|离心率]]定理，以及[[转动惯量|转动惯量]]的概念。<ref name="huygen">{{cite web | url=http://www.17centurymaths.com/contents/huygenscontents.html | title=Horologium Oscillatorium (An English translation by Ian Bruce)| date =  Aug 2013 | accessdate=14 November 2013 | author=Huygens, Christian}}</ref>

{{harvtxt|Levy|Wallach-Levy|2001}}写道，除了科学之外，这本书里对[[路易十四|路易十四]]措辞奇怪的献词也引起了人们的注意。<ref name="lwl">{{citation|title=Cosmic Discoveries: The Wonders of Astronomy|first1=David H.|last1=Levy|first2=Wendee|last2=Wallach-Levy|publisher=Prometheus Books|year=2001|isbn=9781615925667}}.</ref>[[时钟座|时钟座]]星座就是为纪念该书命名的。
