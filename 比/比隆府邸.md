'''比隆府邸'''（{{lang-fr|Hôtel Biron}}）是位於[[法國|法國]]首都[[巴黎|巴黎]]第7區的一座建築，修建於1727年至1732年<ref>Ayers 2004, p. 147. This ''hôtel'' has long been attributed to [[Jacques_Gabriel|Jacques Gabriel]] as well as Aubert (e.g., Blondel 1752, [http://gallica.bnf.fr/ark:/12148/bpt6k108033j/f229.image.r=.langEN vol. 1, p. 205]; Kimball 1943, p, 148), but recent work has shown that Aubert was the designer. If Gabriel played any role, it was as a consultant.</ref>。自1919年開始，比隆府邸是[[羅丹美術館|羅丹美術館]]的所在地。
