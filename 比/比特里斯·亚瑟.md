'''比特里斯·"碧"·亚瑟'''（{{lang-en|'''Beatrice''' "'''Bea'''" '''Arthur'''}}，{{bd|1922年|5月13日|2009年|4月25日|catIdx=A}}）是一位美国女演员和歌手，职业生涯长达七十年。亚瑟在上世纪70年代的情景喜剧《[[一家子|一家子]]》（1971–72）和80年代的情景喜剧《[[黃金女郎|黃金女郎]]》（1985–92）中出演角色。并因这些角色获得[[黃金時段艾美獎|黃金時段艾美獎]]。也因为戏剧表演而获得[[托尼獎|托尼獎]]音乐剧类最佳女配角。

==早年生活==
1922年5月13日比特里斯·亚瑟在纽约市出生，名字是'''Bernice Frankel'''。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.findagrave.com/cgi-bin/fg.cgi?page=gr&GRid=36356930|title =Rebecca Frankel at Find a Grave|accessdate=2012-01-20}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Service |first=Haaretz|url=http://www.haaretz.com/hasen/spages/1081169.html|title='Golden Girls' star Bea Arthur dies aged 86 – Haaretz Daily Newspaper|publisher=Haaretz.com|date=2009-04-26|accessdate=2013-12-04}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|author=Bureau of Vital Records|title=Certificate and Record of Birth #21106|format=.JPG|publisher=City of New York, Department of Health|url=http://www.kevinbuckstiegel.com/bea/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/bea_birthcert.jpg|date=1922-05-13|accessdate=2008-07-12|journal=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140529052815/http://www.kevinbuckstiegel.com/bea/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/bea_birthcert.jpg|archive-date=2014-05-29|dead-url=yes}}</ref>亚瑟是[[美国犹太人|犹太人]]。1933年，她随家人搬到[[劍橋_(馬里蘭州)|剑桥]]，在那里她的父母经营一家女装服饰专门店。她曾就读于一所全女子寄宿学校，在那里她就活跃于戏剧制作。二战期间，她曾在[[美国海军陆战队|美国海军陆战队]]服役。<ref name="marines">Her Marine Corps records are available for perusal at the National Archives and Records Administration [http://www.archives.gov/st-louis/military-personnel/public/persons-of-prominence.html#R website]</ref><ref>Beatrice Arthur interview (March 15, 2001) with Karen Herman, part 1 of 5. Archive of American Television website; accessed June 11, 2014.</ref>

==个人生活==
亚瑟结过两次婚。她的第一次婚姻是在她从军期间，她嫁给了编剧，电视和电影制片人和导演{{link-en|罗伯特·艾伦·亚瑟|Robert Alan Aurthur}}<ref name=marines/>，他们于1950年离婚。不久后，她嫁给了导演{{link-en|吉恩·塞克斯|Gene Saks}}，与他收养了两个儿子：Matthew（1961年出生），演员；Daniel（1964年出生），设计师。他们的婚姻维持到1980年。<ref>{{cite news|last=Hall|first=Jane|title=Sex and the Senior Girls: NBC's Golden Girls Are the Toast of TV with Their Mid-Life Miami Spice|url=http://www.people.com/people/archive/article/0,,20092686,00.html|accessdate=2013-11-15|newspaper=[[People_(magazine){{!}}People|People (magazine){{!}}People]]|date=1986-01-06}}</ref>

亚瑟长期为妇女，老人，以及犹太人和LGBT社区民权进行倡导，她的慈善工作和个人的坦率直言使她成为[[同志偶像|同志偶像]]。<ref>{{cite news|last=Portwood |first=Jerry |title=My Last Chat With Bea Arthur: Sometimes she felt like Judy Garland |url=http://www.nypress.com/blogs/blog-3973-my-last-chat-with-bea-arthur-sometimes-she-felt-like-judy-garland.html |accessdate=2013-11-15 |newspaper=[[New_York_Press|New York Press]] |date=2009-04-27 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090627032801/http://www.nypress.com/blogs/blog-3973-my-last-chat-with-bea-arthur-sometimes-she-felt-like-judy-garland.html |archivedate=六月 27, 2009 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>

==去世==
2009年4月25日凌晨亚瑟在家中去世。她的家人承认死因是癌症，但拒绝透露具体是什么类型。她遗下两个儿子和两个孙女。<ref>{{cite news|author=Lynn Elber|title=Golden Girls, Maude star Bea Arthur dies at 86|url=http://www.chron.com/disp/story.mpl/chronicle/6392273.html|work=The Houston Chronicle|date=2009-04-25|accessdate=2010-12-28}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.cnn.com/2009/SHOWBIZ/TV/04/25/bea.arthur.obit/index.html |work=CNN |title=Two-time Emmy Award winner Bea Arthur dead at 86 |accessdate=2010-04-26 |date=2009-04-27 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100529033037/http://www.cnn.com/2009/SHOWBIZ/TV/04/25/bea.arthur.obit/index.html |archivedate=五月 29, 2010 |deadurl=no }}</ref>她的遗体被[[火葬|火化]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.findagrave.com/cgi-bin/fg.cgi?page=gr&GRid=36348603|title=Beatrice "Bea" Arthur (1922–2009)|publisher=Findagrave.com|accessdate=2013-12-04}}</ref>

亚瑟遗赠30万美元给纽约市的阿里福尼中心，该组织为无家可归的[[LGBT|LGBT]]青少年提供住房。<ref>{{cite news|last=Garcia|first=Michelle|title=Bea Leaves $300K to Homeless Youths|url=http://www.advocate.com/news/daily-news/2009/10/27/bea-leaves-300k-homeless-youths|accessdate=2013-11-15|newspaper=[[The_Advocate|The Advocate]]|date=2009-10-27}}</ref><ref>[http://www.aliforneycenter.org/Bea/ The Ali Forney Center – The Bea Arthur Residence for LGBT Youth] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20091026043256/http://www.aliforneycenter.org/Bea/ |date=2009-10-26 }}</ref>该中心在2012年10月被[[飓风桑迪|飓风桑迪]]摧毁。<ref>{{cite news|title=Ali Forney Center For LGBT Youth Drop-In Center Destroyed By Hurricane Sandy|url=http://www.pridesource.com/article.html?article=56683|accessdate=2013-11-15|newspaper=[[PrideSource|PrideSource]]|date=2012-11-08}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=Pearce|first=Matt|title=Twitter in the time of Sandy: A few lies, and then redemption|url=http://articles.latimes.com/2012/nov/05/nation/la-na-nn-twitter-social-media-sandy-20121105|accessdate=2013-11-15|newspaper=[[Los_Angeles_Times|Los Angeles Times]]|date=2012-11-05}}</ref>
