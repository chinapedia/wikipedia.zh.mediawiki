'''蜘蛛螺屬'''（學名：{{lang|la|'''''Lambis'''''}}）是[[鳳凰螺科|鳳凰螺科]]的一個[[腹足綱|腹足綱]][[軟體動物|軟體動物]]的[[屬_(生物)|屬]]<ref name="WoRMS_205790" />，舊屬[[中腹足目|中腹足目]]，今屬[[新生腹足類|新生腹足類]][[高腹足類|高腹足類]][[玉黍螺類|玉黍螺類]]支序<ref name="WoRMS_205790" />。

==簡述==
本屬物種都屬於大型的物種<ref name="Vaught 1989" />，殼口有7根突起的長管，因像蜘蛛形狀，故名。高約17公分，淡褐色。棲於[[台灣|台灣]]、[[琉球|琉球]]等暖海沙泥中。其虹管（[[siphonal_canal|siphonal canal]]）長而彎，and the [[apertural_lip|apertural lip]] is adorned with [[circumapertural|circumapertural]] projections, which are fingerlike [[Process_(anatomy)|process]]es emanating from the edge of the [[Aperture_(mollusc)|shell aperture]].<ref name="Stone 2004" />

==物種==
以下為本屬之下的物種<ref name="WoRMS_205790" />：
* ''[[Lambis_arachnoides|Lambis arachnoides]]'' Shikama, 1971
* ''[[Lambis_cristinae|Lambis cristinae]]'' Bozzetti, 1999
* [[金黃蜘蛛螺|金黃蜘蛛螺]]（''[[Lambis_crocata|Lambis crocata]]'' <small>(Link, 1807)</small>）
* ''[[Lambis_indomaris|Lambis indomaris]]'' Abbott, 1961
* ''[[Lambis_lambis|Lambis lambis]]'' (Linnaeus, 1758)
* [[千足鳳凰螺|千足鳳凰螺]]（''[[Lambis_millepeda|Lambis millepeda]]'' <small>(Linnaeus, 1758)</small>）
* ''[[Lambis_pilsbryi|Lambis pilsbryi]]'' Abbott, 1961
* ''[[Lambis_robusta|Lambis robusta]]'' (Swainson, 1821)
* [[蠍螺|蠍螺]]（''[[Lambis_scorpius|Lambis scorpius]]'' <small>(Linnaeus, 1758)</small>）
* ''[[Lambis_sebae|Lambis sebae]]'' (Kiener, 1843)
* ''[[Lambis_sowerbyi|Lambis sowerbyi]]'' (Mörch, 1872)
* [[駱駝螺|駱駝螺]]（''[[Lambis_truncata|Lambis truncata]]'' <small>(Humphrey, 1786)</small>）

;以下物種被認為是其他物種的[[同種異名|同種異名]]
* ''Lambis adamii'' (Bozzetti & Cossignani, 2003) : synonym of ''Lambis lambis'' (Linnaeus, 1758)
* ''Lambis aurantia (''Lamarck, 1822) : synonym of  ''Lambis crocata'' (Link, 1807)
* ''Lambis arthritica'' Röding, 1798 : synonym of ''[[Harpago_arthritica|Harpago arthritica]]'' (Röding, 1798)
* ''Lambis chiragra'' <small>(Linnaeus, 1758)</small>：是[[水字螺|水字螺]]（''[[Harpago_chiragra|Harpago chiragra]]'' <small>(Linnaeus, 1758)</small>）的同種異名。
* ''Lambis digitata'' (Perry, G., 1811): synonym of ''[[Ophioglossolambis_digitata|Ophioglossolambis digitata]]''(Perry, 1811) 
* ''Lambis violacea'' (Swainson, 1821): synonym of ''[[Ophioglossolambis_violacea|Ophioglossolambis violacea]]'' (Swainson, 1821)
