'''擬螟蜆蝶屬'''（學名：'''''Synargis'''''）是[[蜆蝶亞科|蜆蝶亞科]][[蛺蜆蝶族|蛺蜆蝶族]]林蜆蝶亞族裡的一個[[屬|屬]]。共有25個物種，分佈於[[新熱帶界|新熱帶界]]<ref>Brower, Andrew V. Z. 2008. Synargis Hübner 1819. Synargis Boisduval 1836 currently viewed as a subjective junior synonym, Acystipoda Röber 1892 currently viewed as a subjective junior synonym, Ematurgina Röber 1903 currently viewed as a subjective junior synonym, Thysanota Stichel 1910 currently viewed as a subjective junior synonym. Version 04 June 2008 (under construction). http://tolweb.org/Synargis/105815/2008.06.04 in The Tree of Life Web Project, http://tolweb.org/ {{en}}</ref>。
