'''擬纓鰓蟲屬'''（學名：{{lang|la|'''''Parasabella''''' <small>Bush, 1905</small>}}）是[[纓緦蟲科|纓緦蟲科]]之下的一個屬<ref name = "COL">{{cite web |url= http://www.catalogueoflife.org/annual-checklist/2011/search/all/key/parasabella/match/1 |title=Species 2000 & ITIS Catalogue of Life: 2011 Annual Checklist.|accessdate=2014-09-24 |editors=Bisby F.A., Roskov Y.R., Orrell T.M., Nicolson D., Paglinawan L.E., Bailly N., Kirk P.M., Bourgoin T., Baillargeon G., Ouvrard D. |year=2011 |publisher=Species 2000 |location=Reading, UK |language=en }}</ref><ref name="dyn_1007631">{{Dyntaxa |id=1007631|title=Parasabella }}</ref>，包括以下各個物種：
*[[短胸擬纓鰓蟲|短胸擬纓鰓蟲]] ''Parasabella brevithoracicus'' <small>(Pillai, 1961)</small>
*[[擬纓鰓蟲|擬纓鰓蟲]] ''Parasabella krusensterni'' <small>(Kinberg, 1867)</small>
*[[白亮擬纓鰓蟲|白亮擬纓鰓蟲]] ''Parasabella leucaspis'' <small>(Kinberg, 1867)</small>
*[[中擬纓鰓蟲|中擬纓鰓蟲]] ''Parasabella medius'' <small>Bush, 1905</small>
*[[小眼擬纓鰓蟲|小眼擬纓鰓蟲]] ''Parasabella microphthalmus'' <small>(Verrill, 1873)</small>
*[[蒼白擬纓鰓蟲|蒼白擬纓鰓蟲]] ''Parasabella pallidus'' <small>(Moore, 1923)</small>
