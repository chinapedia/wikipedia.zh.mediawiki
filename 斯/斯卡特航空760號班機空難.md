'''斯卡特航空760號班機空難'''是指2013年1月29日，一架從[[哈薩克|哈薩克]][[科克舍套|科克舍套]]飛往[[阿拉木圖|阿拉木圖]]的國內客運航班在濃霧氣侯環境下墜毀於{{Link-en|克孜勒圖 (哈薩克)|Kyzyltu, Kazakhstan|克孜勒圖}}，造成飛機上16名乘客和5名機組人員全數喪生<ref name="Reuters">{{cite news|last=Gordeyeva|first=Mariya|title=Passenger plane crash kills 21 in Kazakhstan|url=http://www.reuters.com/article/2013/01/29/us-kazakhstan-aircrash-idUSBRE90S0AM20130129|accessdate=2013-01-29|publisher=[[Reuters|Reuters]]|date=2013-01-29}}</ref><ref name="The Aviation Herald">{{cite web|last=Hradecky|first=Simon|title=Crash: Scat CRJ2 at Almaty on Jan 29th 2013, impacted ground near airport|url=http://avherald.com/h?article=45ce98f4|publisher=The Aviation Herald|date=2013-01-29|accessdate=2013-06-23}}</ref><ref name=AirSafe.com>{{cite web|title=Recent Fatal Plane Crashes and  Other Significant Airline Safety Events|url=http://www.airsafe.com/events/last_15.htm|publisher=AirSafe.com|date=2013-06-28|accessdate=2013-06-28}}</ref>。
