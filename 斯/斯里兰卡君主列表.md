* [[Dona_Catarina|Dona Catarina]], daughter of Karalliyadde Bandara, defeated and captured by Konnapu Bandara, October 9, 1594, [[Battle_of_Danturë|Battle of Danturë]], and forcibly made his 'queen'; she remained Catholic till her death
