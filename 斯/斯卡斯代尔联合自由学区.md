'''斯卡斯代尔联合自由学区'''<ref>"[http://web.scarsdaleschools.k12.ny.us/esl/elemchinesehandbook.htm 斯卡斯代尔（Scarsdale）小学 家长须知]" ([http://archive.is/1jCu4 Archive]) 斯卡斯代尔联合自由学区.</ref>（英語：Scarsdale Union Free School District'''/'''Scarsdale Public Schools）是一个[[美国|美国]][[纽约州|纽约州]]的[[学区|学区]]，总部在{{link-en|斯卡斯代尔 (纽约州)|Scarsdale, New York|斯卡斯代尔}}。

2012年7月斯卡斯代尔联合自由学区是最有钱的美国学区。<ref>{{cite web|last=Liebeskind|first=Ken|title=Weston Is Second Wealthiest U.S. School District|url=http://weston.dailyvoice.com/schools/weston-second-wealthiest-us-school-district|publisher=The Weston Daily Voice|accessdate=26 July 2012|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20140118032344/http://weston.dailyvoice.com/schools/weston-second-wealthiest-us-school-district|archivedate=2014年1月18日}} ()</ref>

斯卡斯代尔联合自由学区营运5间小学，1间初中和1间高中。

小学名字是斯卡斯代尔斯卡斯代尔街坊的名字。<ref name=Brenner>Brenner, Elsa. "[http://www.nytimes.com/2008/05/18/realestate/18livi.html?pagewanted=all Living in Scarsdale, N.Y. Houses Even Bigger, Scores Way Above Average]." ''[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]''. May 18, 2008. Retrieved on January 17, 2014.</ref>
