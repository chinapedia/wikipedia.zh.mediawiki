'''斯洛維尼亞之地'''（{{lang-sl|Slovenske dežele}}）是指在歷史上中歐及南歐地區[[斯洛維尼亞人|斯洛維尼亞人]]居住的土地<ref>{{cite journal |title=Peace Conference on Yugoslavia |first=Andrej |last=Lenarčič |journal=LiVeS Journal |url=http://www.livesjournal.eu/library/lives2/andle2/peace2.htm |volume=1 |issue=2 |date=July 2010 |issn=1855-8267 |publisher=Revija SRP}}</ref>。斯洛維尼亞之地是[[伊利里亞省|伊利里亞省]]和[[奧匈帝國|奧匈帝國]]的[[內萊塔尼亞|內萊塔尼亞]]的一部分<ref>{{cite journal |url=http://journals.lib.washington.edu/index.php/ssj/article/download/3797/3208 |title=Note: The Terms Wende - Winde, Wendisch - Windisch in the Historiographic Tradition of the Slovene Lands |first=Rado |last=Lenček |author-link=Rado Lenček |year=1990 |journal=Slovene Studies |volume=12 |issue=1 |page=94}}</ref>，其範圍除了斯洛維尼亞之外，還包括現在的奧地利、匈牙利、義大利、克羅埃西亞的部分地區<ref>{{cite book |title=A Short History of Yugoslavia: from Early Times to 1966 |year=1966 |first=Stephen |last=Clissold |first2=Henry |last2=Clifford |page=20 |url=http://books.google.si/books?id=_G43AAAAIAAJ}}</ref>。現在這一地區仍有斯洛維尼亞人生活<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.drustvo-dugs.si/tabor-posocje/Slovenci_v_zamejstvu.pdf |title=Slovenci v zamejstvu |first=Anton |last=Polšak |location=Livške Ravne |date=October 2010 |work=Seminar ZRSŠ: Drugačna geografija [ZRSŠ Seminary: A Different Geography] |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304054352/http://www.drustvo-dugs.si/tabor-posocje/Slovenci_v_zamejstvu.pdf |archivedate=2016-03-04 }}</ref><ref>{{cite book |url=http://books.google.si/books?id=DMW2iyFMZRgC&pg=PA57 |title="Transition" and the Politics of History Education in Southeast Europe |page=57 |chapter=Politics of History Education in Slovenia and Slovene History Textbooks since 1990 |first=Peter |last=Vodopivec |editor-first=Augusta |editor-last=Dimou |year=2009 |publisher=V&R unipress GmbH |isbn=978-3-89971-531-6}}</ref>。
