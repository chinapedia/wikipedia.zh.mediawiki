'''斯圖爾特·唐納森'''（Stuart Donaldson<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.thegazette.co.uk/notice/2334493 |title=List of Members returned to Parliament at the General Election 2015 Scotland |work=[[The_Edinburgh_Gazette|The Edinburgh Gazette]] |date=15 May 2015 |accessdate=3 June 2015}}</ref>，{{bd|1991年|9月5日}}<ref>Birth certificate of Stuart Blair Donaldson, 5 September 1991, Aberdeen District 571/02 3802 – National Records of Scotland</ref>）是一位[[蘇格蘭|蘇格蘭]]政治人物，他的黨籍是[[蘇格蘭民族黨|蘇格蘭民族黨]]。他在畢業之後擔任蘇格蘭議會議員[[克里斯蒂安·阿拉德|克里斯蒂安·阿拉德]]的助理<ref>{{cite web|title=Stuart Donaldson MP|url=http://www.mcneillandstone.co.uk/stuart-donaldson-mp.html|website=McNeill & Stone|accessdate=6 July 2015|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150706100035/http://www.mcneillandstone.co.uk/stuart-donaldson-mp.html|archivedate=2015年7月6日|df=}}</ref><ref name=BBCMay15 />。2015年，他當選[[西阿伯丁郡和金卡丁_(英國國會選區)|西阿伯丁郡和金卡丁]]選區的國會議員<ref name=BBCMay15>{{cite news|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/politics/constituencies/S14000058|title=General Election 2015 Results: Aberdeenshire West & Kincardine|date=8 May 2015|work=BBC News Online|accessdate=8 May 2015}}</ref>，成為蘇格蘭民族黨8位30歲以下的國會議員之一<ref name="bbc-who-are-the-56" /><ref>{{cite news |title=Take 7 facts about the SNP's new MPs|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-scotland-scotland-politics-32802373 |first=Hayley |last=Jarvis |work=BBC News |date=20 May 2015}}</ref>。
