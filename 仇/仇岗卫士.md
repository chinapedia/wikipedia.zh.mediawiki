《'''仇岗卫士'''》（''The Warriors of Qiugang''）是2010年一部[[美国|美国]]纪录短片。由美籍华人[[杨紫烨|杨紫烨]]执导。本片讲述了[[中国|中国]][[安徽省|安徽省]][[蚌埠市|蚌埠市]][[仇岗村|仇岗村]]<ref>[http://maps.google.com/maps?f=q&source=embed&hl=en&geocode=%20&q=%E8%9A%8C%E5%9F%A0%E5%B8%82+%E4%BB%87%E5%B2%97%E6%9D%91&sll=33.379136,117.978401&sspn=0.095755,0.168056&ie=UTF8&hq=%20&hnear=China+Anhui+Bangbu%E4%BB%87%E5%B2%97%E6%9D%91&ll=32.9767,117.423935&spn=0.086403,0.171318&z=12 Qiugang, Bengbu, Anhui], Google Maps</ref>，一個有人口1900<ref>[http://e360.yale.edu/the_warriors_of_qiugang_a_chinese_village_fights_back/ The Warriors of Qiugang: A Chinese Village Fights Back] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110203012202/http://e360.yale.edu/the_warriors_of_qiugang_a_chinese_village_fights_back/ |date=2011-02-03 }}, Yale Environment 360.</ref>的村子裡，一位叫张功利的[[农民|农民]]利用各种渠道和资源，迫使当地的违规污染企业[[九采罗化工厂|九采罗化工厂]]<ref>[http://pacificenvironment.org/article.php?id=2901 "Detoxing" China's Huai River] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110116231519/http://pacificenvironment.org/article.php?id=2901 |date=2011-01-16 }}, ''Pacific Environment'', 2008</ref>撤离村庄的故事。本片获得了[[第83届奥斯卡金像奖|第83届奥斯卡金像奖]][[奥斯卡最佳纪录短片将|最佳纪录短片]]提名<ref>[http://www.oscars.org/press/pressreleases/2010/20101013.html AMPAS Press Release], October 13, 2010</ref><ref name="Strangers">{{cite web
