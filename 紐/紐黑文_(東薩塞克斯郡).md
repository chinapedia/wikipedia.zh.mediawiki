'''紐黑文'''（'''Newhaven'''）是[[英國|英國]][[東薩塞克斯郡|東薩塞克斯郡]]的一個鎮<ref name=EX123>OS Explorer map Eastbourne and Beachy Head Scale: 1:25 000. Publisher:Ordnance Survey – Southampton B2 edition. Publishing Date:2009. ISBN 978 0319240823</ref>。位於[[烏茲河_(薩賽克斯)|烏茲河]]河口、[[英吉利海峽|英吉利海峽]]沿岸。有來往法國的渡輪出行。

==歷史==
在[[不列顛青銅時代|不列顛青銅時代]]曾有一座堡壘，原址在今城堡丘（Castle Hill）<ref name="fort-newhaven">[http://www.newhavenfort.org.uk/history.htm Newhaven Fort - History] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100304131811/http://www.newhavenfort.org.uk/history.htm |date=2010-03-04 }}</ref>，在公元480年，[[盎格魯-撒克遜人|盎格魯-撒克遜人]]在紐黑文所在地建起了一座村莊<ref name="meeching-history">[http://www.meeching.com/history.html History of Meeching]</ref>，並稱之為“Meeching”（亦作“Myching”或“Mitching”）<ref name="salzman-newhaven">[http://www.british-history.ac.uk/report.aspx?compid=56917& Newhaven, A History of the County of Sussex: Volume 7, [[L._F._Salzman|L. F. Salzman]] (editor), retrieved 28 October 2009]</ref>。

16世紀中期因為河口淤塞引發洪水，因而人們開闢了新的河道<ref name="salzman-newhaven"/><ref name="brsh">Brandon, P. and Short, B., 1990: The South-East from AD 1000. Longman.</ref>。但是隨著泥沙堆積，烏茲河河口向東偏移。根據1790年烏茲河法案，新建了防浪堤來阻擋沿岸漂沙<ref name="woodcock">Woodcock, A., 2003: The archaeological implications of coastal change in Sussex. pp1-16 in Rudling, D., (ed.), The Archaeology of Sussex to AD2000. Heritage, University of Sussex.</ref>。隨著河流的穩固，現代紐黑文的出行開始形成。後來防浪堤又在1890年另建，并沿用至今<ref name="meeching-history"/>。
