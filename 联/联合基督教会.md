'''联合基督教会'''<ref>美国驻华大使馆将 United Church of Christ 翻译为'''联合基督教会'''，见此文：{{cite web |url=http://chinese.usembassy-china.org.cn/jl0100_immigration.html |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2010-05-30 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090106193548/http://chinese.usembassy-china.org.cn/jl0100_immigration.html |archivedate=2009-01-06 }}</ref>（'''United Church of Christ'''，縮寫為 '''UCC'''）是美国的[[主流新教|主流新教]]（Oldline Protestant）教會之一，主要遵循[[改革宗|改革宗]]的傳統，但在历史上也深受[[信義宗|信義宗]]的影响，[[教會體制|教會體制]]主要是[[會眾制|會眾制]]又帶有些許[[教政體制#.E9.95.B7.E8.80.81.E5.88.B6|長老制]]的特點（Covenantal Polity）<ref>United Church of Christ Constitution and Bylaws：「Within the United Church of Christ, the various expressions of the church relate to each other in a covenantal manner. Each expression of the church has responsibilities and rights in relation to the others, to the end that the whole church will seek God's will and be faithful to God's mission. Decisions are made in consultation and collaboration among the various parts of the structure. As members of the Body of Christ, each expression of the church is called to honor and respect the work and ministry of each other part. Each expression of the church listens, hears, and carefully considers the advise, counsel and requests of others. In this covenant, the various expressions of the United Church of Christ seek to walk together in all God's ways」</ref>，其總部在[[克里夫蘭_(俄亥俄州)|克里夫蘭]]。

联合基督教会是一間神学思想上比较[[开放|开放]]的教會，支持[[民權|民權]]、[[女權|女權]]、[[墮胎|墮胎]]權和[[各地LGBT權利|LGBT權利]]。在2009年，联合基督教会是少数公開支持[[同性婚姻|同性婚姻]]的教會，并且为同性伴侶举行宗教祝福儀式的教会之一<ref name="UCC-Episcopal bishops">{{Citation |last = GOODSTEIN |first = LAURIE |author-link = |last2 = |first2 = |author2-link = |title = Episcopal Bishops Give Ground on Gay Marriage |newspaper = NY Times |pages = |date = 2009-07-15 |url = http://www.nytimes.com/2009/07/16/us/16episcopal.html?scp=18&sq=%22united%20church%20of%20christ%22&st=cse |accessdate = 2010-01-22}}</ref>。不過UCC採取[[會眾制|會眾制]]，各會堂在教義和會務上保有高度的獨立自主權，不必然採取和總會決議一致的立場<ref>{{cite web|title=Who are you calling liberal?|url=http://www.ucc.org/who-are-you-calling-liberal|quote=To be sure, many UCC members relish the denomination"s left-wing identity. A quick internet search reveals that a number of UCC churches use the words "liberal" or "progressive" to describe either their individual congregations or the denomination as a whole. But for the UCC"s more-conservative members and congregations, the L-word is akin to fingernails on a chalkboard. The 2001 International Congregational Life Survey, which included 21,000 UCC respondents from more than 800 congregations, found that UCC members were slightly more likely to self-identify as "conservative" rather than "liberal"—both theologically and politically.}}</ref>。

UCC參與[[普世教會協會|普世教會協會]]以及{{tsl|en|World Communion of Reformed Churches|世界改革宗聯會}}（World Communion of Reformed Churches），並和[[基督會|基督會]]（Disciples of Christ）、[[美国归正会|美國歸正教會]]、[[美國長老教會|美國長老教會]]、[[美國福音路德教會|美國福音路德教會]]、{{tsl|en|Union of Evangelical Churches|Union of Evangelical Churches}}（德國）、[[加拿大聯合教會|加拿大聯合教會]]達成[[共融|共融]]<ref>{{cite web|title=Ecumenical partnerships and relationships of full communion|url=http://www.ucc.org/ecumenical_ecumenical-partnerships-and}}</ref>。UCC重視和不同信仰傳統的對話<ref>{{cite web|title=Interfaith relations|url=http://www.ucc.org/ecumenical_interfaith-relations}}</ref>，以及參與[[普世教會合一運動|普世教會合一運動]]。

在2017年，{{tsl|en|World Communion of Reformed Churches|世界改革宗聯會}}投票決定採納《[[關於稱義教義的聯合聲明|關於稱義教義的聯合聲明]]》，該聯合聲明由{{tsl|en|Lutheran World Federation|世界信義宗聯會}}和[[羅馬天主教會|羅馬天主教會]]在1999年發表，宣稱雙方教會對「通過對基督的信念，憑藉[[基督教的神|神]]的恩典稱義」這一觀點的一致<ref>{{cite web|url =http://www.anglicannews.org/news/2017/10/lutherans,-catholics-methodists-reformed-and-anglicans-drawn-into-deeper-communion.aspx| title =Lutherans, Catholics, Methodists, Reformed and Anglicans “drawn into deeper communion”|publisher = anglicannews}}</ref>。

== 历史 ==
联合基督教会是由{{tsl|en|Evangelical and Reformed Church|Evangelical and Reformed Church}}(E&R)和{{tsl|en|Congregational Christian Churches|Congregational Christian Churches}}(CC)在1957年联合而成的，这两个教都是由之前的一些小教會联合而成。UCC自我描述為「基督的、改革的、[[公理会|會眾]]的、福音的」（Christian, Reformed, Congregational and Evangelical）<ref>{{cite web|title=What is the United Church of Christ?|url=http://www.ucc.org/about-us_what-is-the-united-church-of}}</ref>，其[[福音主義|福音主義]]源自[[德國|德國]]的[[敬虔主義|敬虔主義]]運動<ref>{{cite web|title=The German Evangelical Movement|url=http://www.ucc.org/about-us_short-course_the-german-evangelical}}</ref>。[[社會福音運動|社會福音運動]]和[[進步主義|進步主義]]的早期代表人物之一[[華盛頓·格拉登|華盛頓·格拉登]]，是UCC的代表人物。

联合基督教会共有5320个大小规模不同的教堂，并且宣称有一百一十万信众，这些信众主要集中在美国<ref name="UCC-report">{{Cite web| title = 2008 Annual Report| url = http://www.ucc.org/about-us/2008-annual-report.html| accessdate = 2009-01-23}}</ref>。美國前總統[[巴拉克·歐巴馬|巴拉克·歐巴馬]]過去曾經是這個教會的成員。

== 信仰文件 ==
UCC將歷史上的信條、信仰聲明等文件，視為「信仰告白」（testimonies of faith），而非要求教義標準答案的「信仰測驗卷」（tests of faith）<ref>{{cite web|title=Constitution and Bylaws|url=http://www.ucc.org/ucc_constitution_and_bylaws#LOCAL|website=United Church of Christ}}</ref>。以下為該教會認為可表達其信仰的典籍與文書：
* 《[[使徒信經|使徒信經]]》
* 《[[尼西亞信經|尼西亞信經]]》
* 《[[海德堡探題|海德堡探題]]》 (inherited from both the German Reformed and German Evangelical heritages),
* {{tsl|en|Luther's Small Catechism|路德小探題}} (inherited from the German Evangelical heritage),
* {{tsl|en|Kansas City Statement of Faith|堪薩斯信仰宣言}}(a 1913 statement in the Congregationalist tradition),
* The Evangelical Catechism (a 1927 catechism in the German Evangelical tradition), and
* {{tsl|en|Statement of Faith of the United Church of Christ|聯合基督教會信仰宣言}} (written at the founding of the denomination).
