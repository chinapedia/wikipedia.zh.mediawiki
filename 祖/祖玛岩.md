'''祖玛岩'''（Zuma Rock)，是位于[[尼日利亚|尼日利亚]]的[[尼日尔州|尼日尔州]][[阿布贾|阿布贾]]北部的[[磐石|磐石]]，為一巨大的{{link-en|侵入岩|Intrusive rock}}。它正好位于阿布贾通往[[卡杜纳|卡杜纳]]的主干道边，所以有时候大家也会称之为“阿布贾门户”。它高出地面{{convert|725|m|ft}}<ref>{{cite news |first=Akin |last= Alofetekun |title= All Eyes on Zuma Rock |url=http://www.sunnewsonline.com/webpages/features/citysun/2008/may/28/citysun-28-05-2008-002.htm |work=Daily Sun |publisher= |date=2008-05-28 |accessdate=2009-01-07 }}</ref>，并且印制在了尼日利亚货币100[[奈拉|奈拉]]上。
<ref>According to numerous on-line sites describing Nigeria as a tourist destination, such as [http://www.nyscbida.5u.com/tourist.htm National Youth Services Corps: "The Power State: Niger"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140416181629/http://www.nyscbida.5u.com/tourist.htm |date=2014-04-16 }}.</ref>
