《'''豪斯医生'''》（{{lang-en|''House''}}或''House, M.D.''）是一部[[美国电视|美国]]医务[[电视连续剧|电视连续剧]]，于2004年11月16日至2012年5月12日在[[福克斯广播公司|福克斯]]电视剧首播，前后分为8季。节目主角是[[休·劳瑞|休·劳瑞]]饰演的[[格瑞利·豪斯|格瑞利·豪斯医生]]，为人特立独行且有些[[厌世|厌世]]和反社会倾-{向}-，医术高明的同时也严重依赖止痛药。豪斯带领一个[[诊断|诊断]]小组，在[[新泽西州|新泽西州]]一家名为普林斯顿一普兰斯堡（{{lang|en|Princeton–Plainsboro}}）的虚构[[教学医院|教学医院]]工作。这部电视剧的初始理念源于保罗·阿坦纳西奥，节目主创人大卫·肖则是创造豪斯一角的主要功臣。剧集的[[执行制片人|执行制片人]]包括肖、阿坦纳西奥和商业合作伙伴凯蒂·雅各布斯以及[[电影导演|电影导演]][[布莱恩·辛格|布莱恩·辛格]]，节目大部分镜头都是在[[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]]的{{link-en|世纪城|Century City, Los Angeles}}拍摄完成。

豪斯经常与包括自己诊断小组在内的其他医生发生冲突，因为他对病人病症提出的许多假设都是以微弱或存在争议的见解为基础。他经常无视医院的规章制度，导致与顶头上司，医院管理者兼医学院院长{{link-en|丽莎·卡蒂|Lisa Cuddy}}（{{lang|en|Lisa Cuddy}}，[[莉萨·埃德尔斯坦|莉萨·埃德尔斯坦]]饰）发生冲突。[[罗伯特·肖恩·莱纳德|罗伯特·肖恩·莱纳德]]扮演的[[肿瘤学|肿瘤科]]主任[[詹姆士·威尔森_(豪斯医生)|詹姆斯·威尔森]]是豪斯唯一真正的朋友。节目前三季中豪斯的诊断小组成员包括{{link-en|罗伯特·蔡斯|Robert Chase}}（{{lang|en|Robert Chase}}，[[杰西·斯宾塞|杰西·斯宾塞]]饰）、{{link-en|阿莉森·卡梅伦|Allison Cameron}}（{{lang|en|Allison Cameron}}，[[詹妮弗·莫里森|詹妮弗·莫里森]]饰）和{{link-en|埃里克·福尔曼|Eric Foreman}}（{{lang|en|Eric Foreman}}，[[奧馬爾·愛普斯|奧馬爾·愛普斯]]饰），但这个小组在[[豪斯医生_(第三季)|第三季]]结束时解散。第四季福尔曼重新加入了诊断小组，豪斯也逐步选择了三位新成员：[[蕾蜜·海德利|蕾蜜·“13”·海德利]]（[[奥利维亚·王尔德|奥利维亚·王尔德]]饰）、{{link-en|克里斯·陶柏|Chris Taub}}（{{lang|en|Chris Taub}}，[[彼得·雅各布森|彼得·雅各布森]]饰）和{{link-en|劳伦斯·库特纳|Lawrence Kutner (House)}}（{{lang|en|Lawrence Kutner}}，[[卡尔·潘|卡尔·潘]]饰）。库特纳在节目第五季还有过一次露面，之后再于第八季最后一集《[[人皆有死|人皆有死]]》再次出现。蔡斯和卡梅伦之后还有在节目中出现，不过到了第六季初，卡梅伦选择了离开医院，而蔡斯则回到了诊断小组。13在[[豪斯医生_(第七季)|第七季]]的大部分剧集中都没有出场，她的位置也由医科学生{{link-en|玛莎·麦斯特|Martha Masters (House)}}（{{lang|en|Martha M. Masters}}，爱波·塔布琳饰）取代。到了[[豪斯医生_(第八季)|第八季]]，卡蒂和麦斯特都离开了节目，福尔曼成为医学院新院长，{{link-en|杰西卡·亚当斯||Jessica Adams}}（{{lang|en|Jessica Adams}}，[[奥黛塔·安纳布尔|奥黛塔·安纳布尔]]饰）和{{link-en|朴琪|Chi Park}}（{{lang|en|Chi Park}}，查琳·易饰）加入了豪斯的诊断小组。

《豪斯医生》的[[豪斯医生_(第二季)|第二]]到第四季都是美国收视排列前十的节目，先后在66个国家和地区播映，是2008年世界上观众人数最多的电视节目<ref name="globalrating">{{cite web | work = The Huffington Post | accessdate = 2014-08-24 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140512232109/http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2009/06/12/house-becomes-worlds-most_n_214704.html | url = http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2009/06/12/house-becomes-worlds-most_n_214704.html | title = 'House' Becomes World's Most Popular TV Show | first = Agence France Presse | last = Eurodata TV Worldwide | archivedate = 2014-05-12 | deadurl = no | date = 2009-07-13}}</ref>。节目获得了多个奖项肯定，包括5座[[黄金时段艾美奖|黄金时段艾美奖]]、两座[[金球奖|金球奖]]、1座[[皮博迪奖|皮博迪奖]]和9座[[人民选择奖|人民选择奖]]。2012年2月8日，福克斯宣布当时正在播映的第八季将为节目划上句号<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2012/02/08/current-season-to-be-the-last-for-house/119252/ |title=Current Season to Be The Last for 'House' |last=Seidman |first=Robert |date=2012-02-08 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131117201451/http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2012/02/08/current-season-to-be-the-last-for-house/119252/ |accessdate=2014-08-24 |work=TV by the Numbers |archivedate=2013-11-17 }}</ref>。节目的最后一集于2012年5月21日播出。

获福克斯认可后，节目获得的[[暂定名称|暂定名称]]是《追逐斑马，环绕枯竭》（{{lang|en|''Chasing Zebras, Circling the Drain''}}）<ref name="House-Holmes">{{cite news | title = House... and Holmes | date = 2006-01 | work = Radio Times | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20110721055444/http://www.radiotimes.com/content/show-features/house/house-and-holmes-parallels/ | url = http://www.radiotimes.com/content/show-features/house/house-and-holmes-parallels/ | publisher = BBC Magazines Ltd. | page = 57 | archivedate = 2011-07-21 | deadurl = yes}}</ref>，这其中斑马（{{lang|en|Zebra}}）一词属医疗俚语，指的是不同寻常或是感觉情况模糊的诊断，而“环绕枯竭”则是指末期病例，病人的情况将无可逆转地恶化<ref name="fullhouse" />。节目起初的构想是由一组医生进行合作，试图“诊断那些无法确诊”的病例<ref name="The Concept">{{cite video | people = Jacobs, Katie; Laurie, Hugh; Shore, David; Singer, Bryan | title = House Season One, The Concept | medium = DVD | publisher = Universal Studios | year = 2005}}</ref>。肖认为，让剧中拥有一个比较有趣的核心人物是非常重要的事，这个人能够洞察病人的个人特点，并且通过发现他们的秘密和谎言来做出诊断<ref name="The Concept" />。随着肖和其他几位主创人员继续发掘人物可能的发展方-{向}-，节目的构想开始逐渐从-{程}-序剧改朝以主角为中心转移<ref name="Variety110eps">{{cite news | title = Fox's medical marvel stays on top | author = Werts, Diane | deadurl = no | url = http://variety.com/2009/scene/news/fox-s-medical-marvel-stays-on-top-1117999278/ | date = 2009-01-29 | work = Variety | accessdate = 2014-08-24 | archivedate = 2014-03-20 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140320120536/http://variety.com/2009/scene/news/fox-s-medical-marvel-stays-on-top-1117999278/}}</ref>。这个人物之后命名为“豪斯”，这也成为节目的名称<ref name="House-Holmes" />。肖对剧中角色作了进一步发展，并亲自编写了《[[试播集_(豪斯医生)|试播集]]》的剧本<ref name="macleans" />。[[布莱恩·辛格|布莱恩·辛格]]是《试播集》的导演，还在主角演员的挑选中起到了重要作用，他曾表示：“试播集的片名是《人皆说谎》，这也是整个剧集的前提”<ref name="Variety110eps" />。肖还表示，1944至1994年间服务于《[[纽约客|纽约客]]》的作家[[伯顿·罗切|伯顿·罗切]]（{{lang|en|Berton Roueché}}）笔下许多耐人寻味的医疗案例对节目早期的多集节目剧情提供了灵感<ref name="Housebuilt" />。

肖在一家教学医院看病的经历成为他创作本剧的一个重要原因<ref name="EWinfo">{{cite news|url=http://www.ew.com/ew/article/0,,1043940,00.html |title=Dr. Feelbad |work=Entertainment Weekly |date=2005-04-08 |author=Jensen, Jeff |accessdate=2014-08-25 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140708103908/http://www.ew.com/ew/article/0%2C%2C1043940%2C00.html |archivedate=2014-07-08 |deadurl=no }}</ref>。他在回首往事时表示：“我知道一出房门，他们就会毫不留情地嘲笑我（的无知），而我觉得，要是让一个（医生）角色在病人还没离开房间时就这样做，效果应该会很有意思。”<ref name="hulu-developing the concept">{{cite web | author = Shore, David | year = 2006 | url = http://www.hulu.com/watch/21606/house-house---developing-the-concept#s-p2-st-i1 | title = Developing The Concept | work = Hulu.com | publisher = The Paley Center for Media | accessdate = 2009-03-24 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20090324225402/http://www.hulu.com/watch/21606/house-house---developing-the-concept | archivedate = 2009-03-24 | deadurl = yes}}</ref>。节目的一个核心前提就是主角在行动上有所不便<ref name="int-disability" />，剧组起初的构思是豪斯需要坐轮椅，但福克斯拒绝了这一思路。雅各布斯之后表示，自己很感激电视台坚持要求他们重新构想这个角色，他们一度给主角的脸加上疤痕，最后再决定为让豪斯的腿因误诊而受到严重伤害，不得不使用拐杖，并且还严重依赖[[麻醉药品|麻醉药品]]<ref name="Variety110eps" /><ref name="int-disability">{{cite web | author = Shore, David; Jacobs, Katie | year = 2006 | archivedate = 2013-06-17 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20130617033642/http://www.hulu.com/watch/21680#s-p2-st-i1#s-p2-st-i1 | deadurl = yes | url = http://www.hulu.com/watch/21680#s-p2-st-i1 | title = House's Disability | work = Hulu.com | publisher = The Paley Center for Media | accessdate = 2014-03-20 }}</ref>。

== 剧集概述 ==
{{See also|豪斯医生剧情列表}}
{{Quote box|width=25em|quote=“不管你什麼时候想要用一个词来总结节目，你听起来都像是在放屁。差不多就这样。”|align=right|source=大卫·肖<ref>{{cite news | title = House Boss David Shore: Everybody Lies, Everybody Dies, Everybody... | url = http://www.eonline.com/news/13489/house-boss-david-shore-everybody-lies-everybody-dies-everybody | author = Godwin, Jennifer | work = E! [[格瑞利·豪斯|格瑞利·豪斯]][[医学博士|博士]]是新泽西州普林斯顿一普兰斯堡教学医院的医生，医术高明，但为人却很[[厌世|厌世]]并且有反社会倾-{向}-，他带领着医院的一个[[诊断|诊断]]小组<ref name="pilot" />。大部分剧集都是围绕一个主要病人的病情诊断展开，最开头时是医院外的一组镜头，显示病人发病后再切入开场片段<ref name="Season 1 DVD review" />。接下来较为典型的剧情就是豪斯的诊断小组试图对病人作出诊断和治疗<ref name="PopMatters" /><ref name="Challen" />{{rp|42}}，并且之前几次通常会失败，病人转入病危阶段<ref name="PopMatters" />。他们治疗的通常是其他医院无法确诊的疑难病例<ref name="Holtz" />，豪斯也经常会拒绝接受自己认为没意思的病例<ref name="Season 1 DVD review" />。剧情往往把重点放在男主角标新立异的医学理论和实践，以及其他医生对此的反映上，并不关注治疗过-{程}-的细节<ref name="macleans" />。

诊断小组采用的是[[鉴别诊断|鉴别诊断]]法<ref name="Challen" />{{rp|42}}，在[[白板|白板]]上列出可能的[[原因论|病因]]，然后根据某位成员的见解（多数情况下就是豪斯）将其中大部分排除<ref name="Holtz" />{{rp|3}}。通常情况下病人至少会被[[医疗错误|误诊]]一次，然后得到相应治疗，只是这些治疗不但没有好处<ref name="Challen" />{{rp|42}}，还经常会导致[[并发症|并发症]]。另一方面，这些并发诊往往会提供有价值的新线索，最终帮助小组对病人做出准确诊断<ref name="Season 1 DVD review" />。豪斯通常会因某个角色所说的话受到启发，然后做出准确诊断<ref name="Challen" />{{rp|42}}。诊断结果从常见病到[[罕见病|罕见病]]都有<ref>{{cite news | title = How "House" Finds all Those Strange Diseases | work = New York Post | author = Albiniak, Paige | date = 2006-05-07}}</ref>。

由于病人隐瞒症状、个人经历和其它方面的实际情况，小组在诊断上面临着诸多困难，因此豪斯经常在小组讨论时强调“病人在说谎”，或是“人皆说谎”，这样的假设也是豪斯做出决定和论断的指导方-{向}-<ref name="fullhouse" />，由于不相信病人的话，他还会让小组成员潜入病人家中寻找线索。由于他的许多假设都是基于顿悟和存在争议的见解，因此很难从上级那里获得许可，进行自己认为必要的医疗-{程}-序，从第一到第七季，这位上级都是医院管理者兼医学院院长丽莎·卡蒂<ref>{{cite news | title = House calls: TV doctor's bedside manner is atrocious, but if you're sick, he's the one you want | work = Detroit Free Press | author = Duffy, Mike | date = 2004-11-15}}</ref>。在相应-{程}-序存在较高风险，或是[[医学伦理学|伦理]]方面存在争议的情况下，豪斯的建议得到上级同意的可能性就更小。豪斯还经常与小组的其他医生出现分歧<ref>{{cite web | title = Doing the Right Thing: The Ethics of Dr. Gregory House, Part I | url = http://blogcritics.org/archives/2008/08/01/103300.php | last = Barnett | first = Barbara | date = 2008-08-01 | publisher = Blogcritics | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20090216150908/http://blogcritics.org/archives/2008/08/01/103300.php | accessdate = 2009-02-16 | archivedate = 2009-02-16 | deadurl = yes}}</ref>，其中又以阿莉森·卡梅伦最为普遍，因为她在医学伦理上的立场比另外几位医生更为保守<ref name="PopMatters" />。

与医院的其他医生一样，豪斯需要在医院的步入式门诊治疗病人<ref name="pilot" /><ref name="Wilson-2007">{{cite book | author = Wilson, Leah | title = House Unauthorized: Vasculitis, Clinic Duty, and Bad Bedside Manner | publisher = Benbella Books | year = 2007 | isbn = 1-933771-23-2 | accessdate = 2014-08-25 | url = http://books.google.com.hk/books?id=ds6sPYerO8AC&lpg=PP1&dq=House+Unauthorized:+Vasculitis,+Clinic+Duty,+and+Bad+Bedside+Manner&pg=PP1&redir_esc=y#v=onepage&q&f=true}}</ref>{{rp|78}}。他很不容易履行这份职责，还会想尽办法避免，这些内容经常作为次要情节出现，起到幽默性质的调节作用<ref name="PopMatters" /><ref name="Challen" />{{rp|103, 114}}<ref name="Wilson-2007" />{{rp|78, 214-215}}。在步入式门诊工作时，豪斯对患者毫不友善，也不尊重他们的隐私，采用的通常是非传统的治疗方法<ref name="pilot" />。面对患者的投诉，他经常报之以迅速而准确的诊断，让病人刮目相看<ref name="FactCheckEd" />。有些时候，豪斯还会在处理简单病例时受到启发，帮助解决小组面对的复杂病例<ref name="Season 1 DVD review" /><ref name="Challen" />{{rp|103}}。

=== 主要角色 ===
[[File:HouseCastSeason1.jpg|thumb]]
《豪斯医生》播出期间，片头中一共出现了6名主要演员的姓名。他们扮演的医生都在节目中虚构的新泽西州普林斯顿一普兰斯堡教学医院工作<ref name="pilot" />。格瑞利·豪斯医生是医学诊断科主任，也是片名所指人物<ref>{{cite news | author = Jauhar, Sandeep | title = Magical Medicine on TV | publisher = The New York Times Company | url = http://www.nytimes.com/2005/07/19/health/19comm.html | work = The New York Times | date = 2005-07-19 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140825154631/http://www.nytimes.com/2005/07/19/health/19comm.html | deadurl = no | accessdate = 2014-08-25 | archivedate = 2014-08-25}}</ref>。豪斯称自己拥有[[美国医学专业委员会|美国医学专业委员会]]认可的[[感染|感染]]和[[肾脏科|肾脏科]]双料诊断专长<ref name="Occam's Razor">{{cite episode | title = Occam's Razor | credits = Writer: Shore, David. Director: Singer, Bryan |series = House | network = Fox | airdate = 2004-11-30 | season = 1| number = 3}}</ref>。詹姆斯·威尔森是豪斯唯一的真正朋友，也是医院肿瘤科主任<ref name="Fidelity">{{cite episode | title = Fidelity | credits = Writer: Moran, Thomas L. Director: Spicer, Bryan | series = House | network = Fox | airdate = 2004-12-28 | season = 1 | number = 7}}</ref>。丽莎·卡蒂是[[内分泌学|内分泌科医生]]<ref name="insensitive">{{cite episode | title = Insensitive | credits = Writer: Lewis, Matthew V. Director: Sarafian, Deran | series = House | network = Fox | airdate = 2007-02-13 | season = 3 | number = 14}}</ref>，也是医学院院长和医院管理者，她是豪斯的顶头上司<ref name="Info">{{cite web | url = http://www.fox.com/house/showInfo/ | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20090505213712/http://www.fox.com/house/showInfo/ | archivedate = 2009-05-05 | title = ''House''—Show Information | accessdate = 2009-05-05 | publisher = Fox.com}}</ref>。豪斯与卡蒂的关系很复杂，两人间经常含沙射影，并且伴随有很高-{程}-度的性紧张<ref>{{cite web | url = http://blogcritics.org/house-in-love-part-2-cuddy/ | title = House in Love, Part 2: Cuddy – The Thin Line Between Love and Hate | accessdate = 2014-08-26 | last = Barnett | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140825161356/http://blogcritics.org/house-in-love-part-2-cuddy/ | first = Barbara | date = 2008-12-15 | publisher = Blogcritics | archivedate = 2014-08-25 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。两人在第五季的第6集《[[快乐_(豪斯医生)|快乐]]》（{{lang|en|''Joy''}}）中首次接吻<ref>{{cite web | url = http://insidetv.ew.com/2008/09/21/lisa-edelstein/ | title = Lisa Edelstein on House-Cuddy Kiss: 'It Was a Big Moment' | accessdate = 2014-08-26 | last = Ausiello | first = Michael | date = 2008-09-21 | work = Entertainment Weekly | archivedate = 2014-08-25 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140825161611/http://insidetv.ew.com/2008/09/21/lisa-edelstein/}}</ref>，但第五季接一来的节目中没有出现任何进一步的肉体关系，豪斯在季终时以为自己和卡蒂发生了性关系，但这只是他因维柯丁成瘾而产生的幻觉<ref name="BothSides" />。到了第六季的最后，卡蒂-{向}-豪斯表白，两人亲吻后同意试着在一起<ref>{{cite web | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140825161943/http://blogcritics.org/huddy-house-and-help-me-an/ | deadurl = no | url = http://blogcritics.org/huddy-house-and-help-me-an/ | title = Huddy, ''House'', and 'Help Me': An Interview With the Season Finale's Writers | accessdate = 2014-08-26 | last = Barnett | first = Barbara | date = 2010-05-19 | publisher = Blogcritics | archivedate = 2014-08-25}}</ref>。整个第七季里穿插了许多有关两人关系的剧情。

豪斯最初的诊断小组包括神经科医生埃里克·福尔曼、重症监护科医生罗伯特·蔡斯和[[免疫学|免疫系统]]医生阿莉森·卡梅伦<ref name="Info" />。到了第三季第21集《家庭》时，福尔曼宣布自己决定辞职，称自己不希望变成豪斯那样的人{{#tag:ref|福尔曼还进一步-{向}-豪斯表示：“你救的人会比我多，但我宁愿自己害死的人也少些。这是我提前两星期的通知。”<ref name="Family">{{cite episode | title = Family | credits = Writer: Friedman, Liz. Director: Straiton, David | series = House | network = Fox | airdate = 2007-05-01 | season = 3 | number = 21}}</ref>|group="注"}}。豪斯在这季最后一集中告诉蔡斯，他要么能够学会自己所有的本事，要么就什么都学不到，然后把他开除出了小组。卡梅伦这时已经与蔡斯发展出恋情，她很快也辞职了<ref name="error" />。这导致豪斯的论断小组在第四季开场时只剩他一个光杆司令<ref name="Alone">{{cite episode | title = Alone | credits = Writers: Blake, Peter; Shore, David. Director: Sarafian, Deran | network = Fox | series = House | airdate = 2007-09-25 | season = 4 | number = 1}}</ref>。

卡蒂要求豪斯招入新的小组成员，豪斯为此一共考虑了40名医生<ref name="Rightstuff" />。第四季的前几集就围绕这一挑选过-{程}-展开，属[[真人实境秀|电视真人秀]]风格的淘汰竞赛<ref name="Rightstuff" />，雅各布森形容这就像“《[[幸存者|幸存者]]》的版本” <ref>{{cite news | title = Note to 'House' fans: 'Things will never be the same' on the Fox medical drama | work = USA Today | date = 2007-07-24}}</ref>。豪斯给每位候选人分配了1到40中的一个编号，把其中大部分人淘汰到只剩7人<ref name="97sec">{{cite episode | title = 97 Seconds | credits = Writers: Friend, Russel; Lerner, Garrett. Director: Platt, David | series = House | airdate = 2007-10-09 | season = 4 | number = 3 | network = Fox}}</ref>。福尔曼因在另一个医院行医时表现出类似豪斯的行为而遭解雇，于是回到了之前的老岗位上，他协助豪斯根据候选人在诊断病例时的表现作出评估<ref name="97sec" /><ref>{{cite episode | title = Guardian Angels | credits = Writer: Hoselton, David. Director: Sarafian, Deran | series = House | network = Fox | airdate = 2007-10-23 | season = 4 | number = 4}}</ref><ref name="MM">{{cite episode | title = Mirror Mirror | credits = Writer: Foster, David. Director: Platt, David | series = House | airdate = 2007-10-30 | season = 4 | number = 5 | network = Fox}}</ref>。虽然福尔曼的回归意味着小组中只剩两个空缺，但豪斯还是用计诱使卡蒂允许他聘请三位新人<ref name="Games">{{cite episode | title = Games | credits = Writer: Attie, Eli. Director: Sarafian, Deran | series = House | airdate = 2007-11-27 | season = 4 | number = 9 | network = Fox}}</ref>。他最终选择的分别是前[[整形|整形医生]]克里斯·陶柏、运动医学专家劳伦斯·库特纳和[[内科|内科医生]]蕾蜜·“13”·海德利（“13”这个绰号源于她在40位候选人中获得的编码）<ref name="Games" /><ref name="Catlin">{{cite web|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20130102134540/http://blogs.courant.com/roger_catlin_tv_eye/2007/11/house-finalists.html |url=http://blogs.courant.com/roger_catlin_tv_eye/2007/11/house-finalists.html |title='House' Finalists |date=2007-11-21 |author=Catlin, Roger |accessdate=2014-08-26 |work=Hartford Courant |archivedate=2013-01-02 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。到第四季结束时，13发现自己一直担心的事情变成了现实，她患上了无药可救的[[亨丁顿舞蹈症|亨丁顿舞蹈症]]<ref name="WH" />。

福尔曼和13在第五季第11集《给全世界的快乐》中有一个深情的吻<ref name="joytoworld" />。13起初不大情愿和福尔曼交往，但两人之后还是开始约会，并且到第五季结束时仍然在一起<ref name="BothSides" />。两人在第六季开始后不久分手。库特纳在第五季第20集《[[简单解释|简单解释]]》（{{lang|en|''Simple Explanation''}}）里死在自己的公寓，头上有枪伤。他没有留下只言片语，豪斯为此怀疑其中另有隐情，但其他角色都认为这是[[自杀|自杀]]<ref>{{cite episode | title = Simple Explanation | series = House | airdate = 2009-04-06 | season = 5 | number = 20 | network = Fox | credits = Writer: Dick, Leonard. Director: Yaitanes, Greg}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | url = http://insidetv.ew.com/2009/04/07/house-exclusive/ | title = 'House' exclusive: The shocking story behind last night's big death | work = Entertainment Weekly | date = 2009-04-07 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140305200152/http://insidetv.ew.com/2009/04/07/house-exclusive/ | author = Ausiello, Michael | accessdate = 2014-08-26 | archivedate = 2014-03-05 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。

卡梅伦和蔡斯在第二季第7集《寻找元凶》中发生了[[一夜情|一夜情]]<ref name="Hunting">{{cite episode | title = Hunting | credits = Writer: Friedman, Liz. Director: Muzio, Gloria | series = House | network = Fox | airdate = 2005-11-22 | season = 2 | number = 7}}</ref>。两人在第三季中期开始经常发生性关系，但卡梅伦坚持这只是逢场作戏<ref name="insensitive" />，当蔡斯表示自己“想要更进一步”时，卡梅伦结束了两人的关系<ref name="Airborne">{{cite episode | title = Airborne | credits = Writer: Hoselton, David. Director: Keene, Elodie | series = House | network = Fox | airdate = 2007-04-10 | season = 3 | number = 18}}</ref>，但到了第三季的结尾，卡梅伦意识到自己对蔡斯有了感情，于是两人开始了正式交往<ref name="error" />。双双离开诊断小组后，两人在医院的不同部门工作，卡梅伦成为[[急症室|急症室]]的资深主治医师{{#tag:ref|根据《豪斯医生》在福克斯官方网站上页面的说法，“卡梅伦主管急诊医学部门”<ref name="Info" />。|group="注"}}，蔡斯则是外科医生<ref name="Rightstuff" />。两人在第五季第21集（库特纳自杀后的一集）《[[救世主_(豪斯医生)|救世主]]》（{{lang|en|''Saviors''}}）中[[订婚|订婚]]<ref name="Saviors" />并在季终成婚<ref>{{cite news | archivedate = 2014-03-04 | url = http://www.tvguide.com/tvshows/house/episode-24-season-5/100213 | work = TV Guide | author = Mittovich, Matt | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140304074556/http://www.tvguide.com/tvshows/house/episode-24-season-5/100213 | date = 2009-05-11 | deadurl = no | title = House Episode Recap: "Both Sides Now" | accessdate = 2014-08-26}}</ref>。蔡斯在第六季第8集《[[团队合作_(豪斯医生)|团队合作]]》中（{{lang|en|''Teamwork''}}）重新加入豪斯的诊断小组，卡梅伦为此离开了丈夫和这所医院<ref>{{cite episode | title = Teamwork | credits = Writer: Attie, Eli. Director: Straiton, David |series = House | network = Fox | airdate = 2009-11-16 | season = 6 | number = 8}}</ref>。她之后在这季的第17集《[[锁定_(豪斯医生)|锁定]]》（{{lang|en|''Lockdown''}}）中[[友情客串|客串出镜]]<ref>{{cite episode | title = Lockdown | credits = Writers: Attie, Eli; Blake, Peter; Friend, Russel; Lerner, Garret. Director: Laurie, Hugh | series = House | network = Fox | airdate = 2010-04-12 | season = 6 | number = 17}}</ref>。

13在第7季开始后不久离开了医院，并且很长时间没有回来，也没有任何解释。卡蒂要求豪斯另请一位女医生填补13的空缺<ref>{{cite news | url = http://www.tvguide.com/News/Amber-Tamblyn-House-1025203.aspx | title = Amber Tamblyn Is in the ''House'' | publisher = TV Guide | last = Keck | first = William | date = 2010-11-08 | accessdate = 2014-08-26 | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2014-08-13 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140813113015/http://www.tvguide.com/News/Amber-Tamblyn-House-1025203.aspx}}</ref>，但之后还是亲自挑选了医科学生玛莎·麦斯特，后者在这季的第6集首度出场<ref>{{cite episode | title = Office Politics | credits = Writer: Hoffman, Seth. Director: Bookstaver, Sanford |series = House | network = Fox | airdate = 2010-11-08 | season = 7 | number = 6}}</ref>。13在这季第18集《挖掘》（{{lang|en|''The Dig''}}）回归，原来她是因给自己进入亨廷顿舞蹈症晚期的兄弟实行[[安乐死|安乐死]]而入狱服刑6个月<ref>{{cite episode | title = The Dig | credits = Writers: Hess, Sara; Hoselton, David.
