Urol. |volume=153 |issue=2 |pages=424–6 |date=February 1995 |pmid=7815607 |doi=10.1097/00005392-199502000-00043 |url=http://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0022-5347(01)67779-6}}</ref> 是一種發生在男性[[龜頭|龜頭]]上的炎症，是有[[漿細胞|漿細胞]]浸潤特徵的良性皮膚損傷。<ref name="Andrews">{{cite book |author=James, William D.; Berger, Timothy G.; et al. |title=Andrews' Diseases of the Skin: clinical Dermatology |publisher=Saunders Elsevier |location= |year=2006 |pages= |isbn=0-7216-2921-0 |oclc= |doi= |accessdate=}}</ref>{{rp|657}}<ref name="Bolognia">{{cite book |author=Rapini, Ronald P.; Bolognia, Jean L.; Jorizzo, Joseph L. |title=Dermatology: 2-Volume Set |publisher=Mosby |location=St. Louis |year=2007 |pages=Chapter 72 |isbn=1-4160-2999-0 |oclc= |doi= |accessdate=}}</ref>
