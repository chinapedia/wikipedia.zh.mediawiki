'''核苷三磷酸還原酶'''（'''Ribonucleoside-triphosphate reductase'''）（{{EC number|1.17.4.2}}''[[ribonucleotide_reductase|ribonucleotide reductase]]'', ''2'-deoxyribonucleoside-triphosphate:oxidized-thioredoxin 2'-oxidoreductase''），系統名「2-2'-脫氧核糖核苷三磷酸：硫氧還蛋白二硫鍵-2'-氧化還原酶」（''2'-deoxyribonucleoside-triphosphate:thioredoxin-disulfide 2'-oxidoreductase''）<ref>{{cite journal | title = Cobamides and ribonucleotide reduction. I. Cobamide stimulation of ribonucleotide reduction in extracts of <em>Lactobacillus leichmannii</em> |author = Blakley, R.L. |journal = J. Biol. Chem. |year = 1965 |volume = 240 |pages = 2173–2180 |pmid = 14299643}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | title = Purification and properties of cobamide-dependent ribonucleotide reductase from <em>Lactobacillus leichmannii</em> |author = Goulian, M. and Beck, W.S. |journal = J. Biol. Chem. |year = 1966 |volume = 241 |pages = 4233–4242 |pmid = 5924645}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | title = The ribonucleotide reductases - a unique group of metalloenzymes essential for cell-proliferation |author = Lammers, M. and Follmann, H. |journal = Struct. Bonding |year = 1983 |volume = 54 |pages = 27–91 |pmid = |doi=10.1007/bfb0111318}}</ref>，係一種能[[催化|催化]]以下化學反應的[[酶|酶]]：

2'-脫氧核糖核苷三磷酸+硫氧化還原蛋白二硫鍵+H<sub>2</sub>O <math>\rightleftharpoons</math>核糖核苷三磷酸+硫氧化還原蛋白

核苷三磷酸還原酶的作用需要cobamide輔酶，同時還要消耗[[ATP|ATP]]。
