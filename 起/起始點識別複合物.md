在[[酵母|酵母]]細胞中，起始點識別複合物亦參與{{link-en|酵母的交配|Mating of yeast|交配型}}相關的基因位點HML和HMR的沉默<ref name="pmid1579162"/><ref name="pmid7585959"/><ref name="pmid16716188"/>。起始點識別複合物參與HML、HMR位點的染色質組裝，讓Sirl沉默蛋白裝入染色質中，使得這兩個位點沉默<ref name="pmid16716188"/><ref name="pmid8622770">{{cite journal | author = Triolo T, Sternglanz R | title = Role of interactions between the origin recognition complex and SIR1 in transcriptional silencing | journal = Nature | volume = 381 | issue = 6579 | pages = 251–3 |date=May 1996 | pmid = 8622770 | doi = 10.1038/381251a0 | url = }}</ref><ref name="pmid9171055">{{cite journal | author = Fox CA, Ehrenhofer-Murray AE, Loo S, Rine J | title = The origin recognition complex, SIR1, and the S phase requirement for silencing | journal = Science | volume = 276 | issue = 5318 | pages = 1547–51 |date=June 1997 | pmid = 9171055 | doi = 10.1126/science.276.5318.1547| url = }}</ref>。
