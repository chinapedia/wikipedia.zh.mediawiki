'''日落黃'''（{{lang|en|Sunset Yellow FCF}}），是一種人工合成的橘橙色[[偶氮化物|偶氮類]][[酸性|酸性]][[染料|染料]]，是從[[石油|石油]]中製造出來的芳香族烴類。當在食品及飲料中作[[食用色素|食用色素]]使用時，根據法例需在其成份標示：在美國售賣時，會標明'''[[联邦食品、药品和化妆品法案|FD&C]] Yellow 6'''；在歐洲售賣時，會標明其[[E編碼|E編碼]]'''E110'''<ref name="isbn1-85573-722-1">{{cite book |author=Wood, Roger M. |title=Analytical methods for food additives |publisher={{tsl|en|CRC Press|}} |location=Boca Raton |date=2004 |isbn=1-85573-722-1 }}</ref>；在台灣售賣時，會標明'''黃色五號'''。

==應用==
日落黃主要用於食品、飲料、日用化妝品及藥物作[[食用色素|食用色素]]<ref>Codex Alimentarius (Codex GFSA) Online. Updated up to the 37th Session of the Codex Alimentarius Commission (2014) [http://www.codexalimentarius.net/gsfaonline/additives/details.html?id=124 Sunset yellow FCF (110)]</ref><ref>FDA December 2009 [http://www.fda.gov/ForIndustry/ColorAdditives/ColorAdditiveInventories/ucm106626.htm Color Additive Status List]</ref><ref>EU Food Additive Database [https://webgate.ec.europa.eu/sanco_foods/main/?event=substance.view&identifier=9 Sunset Yellow FCF/Orange Yellow S] {{Wayback|url=https://webgate.ec.europa.eu/sanco_foods/main/?event=substance.view&identifier=9 |date=20160304084038 }}.  Database accessed 6 December 2014]</ref><ref>European Medicines Agency  19 June 2007 [Guideline on Excipients in the Dossier for Application for Marketing Authorisation of a Medicinal Product]</ref>{{rp|4}}。舉例說：可用於糖果、甜點、小吃、醬料及涼果等<ref name=EncycFoodSafety>Abbey J, et at.  Colorants.  pp 459-465 in Encyclopedia of Food Safety, Vol 2: Hazards and Diseases. Eds, Motarjemi Y et al.  Academic Press, 2013. {{ISBN|9780123786135}}</ref>{{rp|463–465}}。日落黃常與另一種叫作E123的{{link-en|莧菜紅|Amaranth (dye)}}著色劑一起使用，以產生巧克力或焦糖的那種棕色效果<ref name="choc">{{cite book |url={{Google books |id=KF2A8Cz7B-cC |dq=%22sunset%20yellow%22%20%20chocolate%20-citation |page=248 |plainurl=yes}} |title=Food: The Chemistry of Its Components |access-date=2014-12-06 |publisher=[[英国皇家化学学会|英国皇家化学学会]]}}</ref>。

==安全性==
無論是[[歐洲聯盟|歐盟]]或[[世界衛生組織|世衛]]還是[[联合国粮食及农业组织|聯合國糧農組織]]的指引，日落黃的[[一日可接受攝取量|一日可接受攝取量]]（ADI）都是0-4 mg/kg<ref name=EncycFoodSafety/>{{rp|465}}<ref name=EFSA2014>EFSA Panel on Food Additives and Nutrient Sources added to food (ANS) https://www.efsa.europa.eu/en/efsajournal/pub/3765 Reconsideration of the temporary ADI and refined exposure assessment for Sunset Yellow FCF (E 110)] EFSA Journal 2014;12(7):3765 . doi:10.2903/j.efsa.2014.3765</ref>
。日落黃在現時使用量方面沒有[[致癌物質|致癌性]]、[[基因毒性|基因毒性]]或{{link-en|發育毒性|Developmental toxicity}}。<ref name=EncycFoodSafety/>{{rp|465}}<ref name=EFSA2014/>

自1970年代後期以來，在Benjamin Feingold的廣泛宣傳下，有指日落黃等食用色素可能會導致兒童的[[食物不耐症|食物不耐症]]和[[注意力不足過動症|注意力不足過動症]]（ADHD）行為。
但這些説法無確實科學根據。<ref>Tomaska LD and Brooke-Taylor, S. ''Food Additives - General'' pp 449-454  in Encyclopedia of Food Safety, Vol 2: Hazards and Diseases. Eds, Motarjemi Y et al.  Academic Press, 2013. {{ISBN|9780123786135}}</ref>{{rp|452}}  某些食用色素可能會成為{{link-en|遺傳預先傾向性|Genetic predisposition}}的觸發因素，但證據不足。<ref name=Peds2012>{{cite journal |author=Millichap JG |author2= Yee MM |title=The diet factor in attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder|journal=Pediatrics |volume=129 |issue=2 | pages=330–337 |date=February 2012 |pmid=22232312|doi=10.1542/peds.2011-2199 }}</ref><ref name=FDAdyecomm/>

==作食品添加物使用的監管==
===歐洲===
目前日落黃在[[挪威|挪威]]、[[芬兰|芬兰]]及[[瑞典|瑞典]]都被禁止作食品添加物使用<ref name="norway">{{cite news  |url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/health/healthnews/3453522/The-additives-which-could-be-banned.html |title=The additives which could be banned |access-date=2014-12-06 |publisher=[[每日电讯报|The Telegraph]] |date=2009}}</ref><ref>{{cite news| url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/background/foodsafety/additives.html | work={{tsl|en|CBC News|}} | title=Food additives | date=2008-09-29 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130909224854/http://www.cbc.ca/news/background/foodsafety/additives.html | archive-date=2013-09-09 |language=en }}</ref>。

===美國===
日落黃在美國被稱為[[聯邦食品、藥品和化妝品法案|FD&C]]黃色6號（FD&C Yellow 6），被批准用於食用色素、藥物和化妝品中，其規定的[[一日可接受攝取量|一日可接受攝取量]]（ADI）為3.75 mg/kg。<ref name=FDAdyecomm>FDA. [http://www.fda.gov/downloads/AdvisoryCommittees/CommitteesMeetingMaterials/FoodAdvisoryCommittee/UCM248549.pdf Background Document for the Food Advisory Committee: Certified Color Additives in Food and Possible Association with Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder in Children: March 30-31, 2011]</ref>{{rp|2,7}}
