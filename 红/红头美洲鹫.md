'''紅頭美洲鷲'''（[[學名|學名]]：''{{lang|la|Cathartes aura}}''），又名'''火鸡禿鷹'''，是分佈在[[美洲|美洲]]的一種[[新大陸禿鷲|新大陸禿鷲]]。<ref name="Concise">{{cite web|year=2007|title=Britannica Concise Encyclopedia|publisher=Encyclopædia Britannica|url=http://concise.britannica.com/ebc/article-9075791/vultures#citation|accessdate=2007-10-14}}{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>牠們分佈在[[加拿大|加拿大]]南部至[[南美洲|南美洲]]南端，棲息在遼闊至半開放的地區，包括[[亞熱帶|亞熱帶]][[森林|森林]]、[[叢林|叢林]]、草坪及[[沙漠|沙漠]]。<ref name="BirdLife"/>

紅頭美洲鷲是一種大型的[[鳥類|鳥類]]，翼展1.7至1.83米，體長64至81厘米，重0.85至2.26公斤。<ref name="Hilty">{{cite book en|last=Hilty|first=Stephen L.|title=A Guide to the Birds of Colombia|publisher=Princeton University Press|year=1977|pages=87|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=1k5fV_hQqE8C&pg=PA88&dq=|id=ISBN 0-691-08372-X}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|author=Cornell Lab of Ornithology|title=Turkey Vulture|work=All About Birds|url=http://www.allaboutbirds.org/guide/Turkey_Vulture/lifehistory|accessdate=2009-10-19}}</ref><ref name="ADW"/><ref>{{Cite web|author=The Peregrine Fund|title=Turkey Vulture (''Cathartes aura'')|url=http://www.peregrinefund.org/explore_raptors/vultures/turkevul.html|accessdate=2009-10-19|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090917063645/http://www.peregrinefund.org/explore_raptors/vultures/turkevul.html|archivedate=2009-09-17}}</ref>牠們呈深褐色至黑色。[[頭部|頭部]]及[[頸部|頸部]]沒有[[羽毛|羽毛]]及呈紫紅色。喙短而鉤，呈象牙色。牠們生活在野外的壽命為16歲，飼養的可以超過20歲。<ref>{{Cite web|author=Raptor Rehabilitation of Ky.|title=TURKEY VULTURE (''Cathartes aura'')|url=http://www.raptorrehab.org/raptors/tv.htm|accessdate=2009-10-19|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080131235433/http://www.raptorrehab.org/raptors/tv.htm|archivedate=2008-01-31}}</ref>

紅頭美洲鷲是[[食腐動物|食腐動物]]，差不多只吃[[屍體|屍體]]。<ref name="ADW"/>牠們會以[[視覺|視覺]]及[[嗅覺|嗅覺]]來尋找食物，以低飛來嗅出腐屍的味道。<ref name="ADW"/>牠們的飛行是依賴[[空氣|空氣]]的熱流，很少會拍動雙翼。牠們是以一大群聚居。牠們沒有[[鳴管|鳴管]]，只可以發出低嘶聲。<ref name="Miskimen">{{cite journal en|last=Miskimen|first=Mildred|title=Absence of Syrinx in the Turkey Vulture (''Cathartes Aura'')|journal=The Auk|volume=74|issue=1|pages=104–5|url=http://elibrary.unm.edu/sora/Auk/v074n01/p0104-p0105.pdf|format=PDF|accessdate=2006-10-24|date=January 1957}}</ref>牠們會在洞、空心樹或灌木叢裡築巢。牠們每年可以哺育兩隻雛鷲，都是吃雙親的反芻液長大。<ref name="Fergus"/>牠們的天敵非常少。<ref name="snyder"/>牠們在[[美國|美國]]受到了保護。<ref name="FWS">{{cite web|title=Birds Protected by the Migratory Bird Treaty Act|publisher=US Fish & Wildlife Service|url=http://www.fws.gov/migratorybirds/intrnltr/mbta/mbtandx.html|accessdate=2007-10-14}}</ref>

== 分類 ==
[[File:Urubu_a_tete_rouge_-_Turkey_Vulture.jpg|left]]
紅頭美洲鷲又名[[火雞|火雞]]禿鷹，是因牠們的禿頭及深色主羽像雄性的[[野生火雞|野生火雞]]。<ref name="Holloway"/>其[[屬名|屬名]]的意思是「清潔者」<ref>{{cite book en|last=Liddell|first=Henry George|coauthor=Robert Scott|year=1980|title=Greek-English Lexicon, Abridged Edition |publisher=Oxford University Press|location= Oxford|isbn= 0-19-910207-4}}</ref>，[[種小名|種小名]]是源自其[[墨西哥語|墨西哥語]]的稱呼。<ref name="Holloway">{{cite book en|last =Holloway|first=Joel Ellis|title=Dictionary of Birds of the United States: Scientific and Common Names|publisher=Timber Press|year=2003|pages=59|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=41knpiVqnKYC&pg=PA59&dq=Cathartes+aura+subject:%22Nature+/+Birds+%26+Birdwatching%22&as_brr=3&sig=YS_oepqlw59T9RxDny5KFtPliSQ|id=ISBN 0-88192-600-0}}</ref>牠們最初是由[[卡爾·林奈|卡爾·林奈]]（Carolus Linnaeus）於1758年描述。<ref>{{la icon}}{{cite book en|last=Linnaeus|first=Carolus|title=Systema naturae per regna tria naturae, secundum classes, ordines, genera, species, cum characteribus, differentiis, synonymis, locis. Tomus I. Editio decima, reformata|publisher=Holmiae. (Laurentii Salvii)|year=1758|pages=86}}</ref>牠們現時與[[大黃頭美洲鷲|大黃頭美洲鷲]]及[[小黃頭美洲鷲|小黃頭美洲鷲]]一同分類在[[美洲鷲屬|美洲鷲屬]]中。

紅頭美洲鷲共有五個[[亞種|亞種]]：
* ''C. a. aura''：指名亞種。牠們分佈在由[[墨西哥|墨西哥]]，南至[[南美洲|南美洲]]及[[大安地列斯群島|大安地列斯群島]]。這個亞種的分佈地有時會與其他亞種的重疊。牠們是所有亞種中最細小的，而顏色與''C. a. meridionalis''的很難分辨。<ref name="Amadon"/>
* ''C. a. jota''：分佈在[[智利|智利]]，較指名亞種大及褐色，但卻較''C. a. ruficollis''淡色。次羽及廓羽邊緣灰色。<ref name="Blake">{{cite book en|last=Blake, Emmet Reid|title=Birds of Mexico: A Guide for Field Identification|publisher=University of Chicago Press|year=1953|pages=267|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=YP0AX3LW8jYC&dq=|id=ISBN 0-226-05641-4}}</ref>
* ''C. a. meridionalis''：分佈在[[曼尼托巴|曼尼托巴]]南部、[[英屬哥倫比亞|英屬哥倫比亞]]南部、[[艾伯塔省|艾伯塔省]]中部及[[薩克其萬|薩克其萬]]、[[亞利桑那州|亞利桑那州]]中南部及[[德克薩斯州|德克薩斯州]]中南部。<ref>{{cite book en|last=Peters J. L.; Mayr E.& Cottrell,W.|title=Check-list of Birds of the World|publisher=Museum of Comparative Zoology|year=1979|pages=276|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=RNA9AAAAIAAJ&q=}}</ref>牠們會遷徙遠至南美洲，與''C. a. aura''的分佈地重疊。廓羽呈深褐色及較窄。<ref name="Amadon"/>
* ''C. a. ruficollis''：分佈在[[巴拿馬|巴拿馬]]南部，南至[[烏拉圭|烏拉圭]]及[[阿根廷|阿根廷]]。牠們也遠及[[千里達|千里達]]。<ref name="Brown"/>牠們比''C. a. aura''更為深色，翼邊沒有或較少褐色。<ref name="Brown">{{cite book en|last=Brown, Leslie & Amadon, Dean|title=Eagles, Hawks, and Falcons of the World|publisher=McGraw-Hill|year=1968|pages=175|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=fcM9AAAAIAAJ&q=}}</ref>[[頭部|頭部]]及[[頸部|頸部]]呈深紅色，有黃白色或綠白色斑紋。成鳥的冠上有淡黃色。<ref name="Blake"/>
* ''C. a. septentrionalis''：分佈在[[加拿大|加拿大]]東南部，南至[[美國|美國]]東部。牠們遷徙的路線較短，也很少會到美國南部。<ref name="Amadon">{{Cite journal en|last=Amadon|first=Dean|title=Notes on the Taxonomy of Vultures|journal=Condor|volume=79|issue=4|pages=413–6|year=1977|url=http://elibrary.unm.edu/sora/Condor/files/issues/v079n04/p0413-p0416.pdf|format=PDF|doi=10.2307/1367720}}</ref>

== 分佈及棲息地 ==
[[File:Flying_Turkey_Vulture_with_Wings_Held_Upward_crop.jpg|thumb]]
紅頭美洲鷲的分佈地很大，估計佔地達2800萬平方公里。牠們是[[美洲|美洲]]中數量最豐富的[[禿鷹|禿鷹]]<ref name="Concise"/>，全球約有450萬隻。<ref name="BirdLife">{{IUCN2009|assessors=BirdLife International|year=2008|title=Cathartes aura|id=144768|downloaded=2009-10-19}}</ref>整個[[美洲|美洲]]遼闊及半開放的地區都是牠們的棲息地。牠們是[[美國|美國]]南部的永久居民，不過北部的甚至會遷徙至[[南美洲|南美洲]]。<ref name="ADW">{{cite web|last=Attwood|first=E|title=''Cathartes aura''|work=Animal Diversity Web|publisher =University of Michigan Museum of Zoology|url=http://animaldiversity.ummz.umich.edu/site/accounts/information/Cathartes_aura.html|accessdate=2007-09-30}}</ref>牠們廣泛分佈在遼闊的郊野、[[亞熱帶|亞熱帶]][[森林|森林]]、[[叢林|叢林]]、[[沙漠|沙漠]]及山腳。<ref name="Kaufman"/>牠們也會在草坪、草原及[[濕地|濕地]]出沒。<ref name="BirdLife"/>

== 生態及行為 ==
[[File:Turkey_vulture_Bluff.jpg|thumb]]
紅頭美洲鷲是以大群群居的，日間會單獨出來覓食。一群紅頭美洲鷲可以多達幾百隻，甚至包括[[黑美洲鷲|黑美洲鷲]]在內。牠們會在枯萎及沒有[[葉子|葉子]]的樹上築巢。也有在洞穴築巢的，但一般都只會在[[繁殖|繁殖]]季節才會到洞內。<ref name="Fergus"/>牠們夜間的體溫約為6至34 ℃，稍為[[低體溫症|低體溫症]]。<ref name="Feduccia">{{cite book en|last=Feduccia|first=J. Alan|title=The Origin and Evolution of Birds|publisher=Yale University Press|year=1999|pages=116|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=8QRKV7eSqmIC&pg=PA300&dq=Cathartes+melambrotus+%2B+black&as_brr=0&ie=ISO-8859-1&sig=BjxPgGtWEdfcd3N3O9WBACwOiDk#PPA300,M1|id=ISBN 0-226-05641-4}}</ref>

紅頭美洲鷲站立時總會張開雙翼，尤其在潮濕或下雨天的時候。這種姿勢可能有多種功能：烘乾雙翼、暖身及消毒。其他[[新大陸禿鷲|新大陸禿鷲]]、[[舊世界禿鷲|舊世界禿鷲]]及[[鸛科|鸛科]]都會有這種姿勢。<ref name="snyder"/>牠們會像鸛科般在自己的腳上排糞，藉[[蒸發|蒸發]]來幫助降溫。<ref>{{cite web|last=Ridenhou|first=Larry|title=NCA - Turkey Vulture|work=Snake River Birds of Prey National Conservation Area|publisher=Bureau of Land Management|url=http://www.birdsofprey.blm.gov/nat-res/tv.htm|accessdate=2006-12-17|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070502095446/http://www.birdsofprey.blm.gov/nat-res/tv.htm|archivedate=2007-05-02}}</ref>這可以冷卻腳及腳掌上的[[血管|血管]]，而在腳上會有白色[[尿酸|尿酸]]的斑紋。<ref>{{cite book en|last=Gordon|first=Malcolm S.|title=Animal Physiology: Principles and Adaptations|publisher=Macmillan|year=1977|pages=357|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=G7o0AAAAMAAJ&dq=&pgis=1}}</ref>

紅頭美洲鷲只有很少天敵。成年、未成熟及換羽的都會是[[金雕|金雕]]、[[白頭海鵰|白頭海鵰]]及[[大雕鴞|大雕鴞]]的獵物；蛋及雛鷲就會被[[哺乳動物|哺乳動物]]所獵食，如[[浣熊|浣熊]]、[[北美負鼠|北美負鼠]]及[[狐狸|狐狸]]。<ref name="ADW"/><ref name="snyder"/>牠們會反芻出半[[消化|消化]]的肉，造成難聞的氣味來趕走[[掠食者|掠食者]]。<ref name="Fergus">{{cite book en|last=Fergus, Charles|title=Wildlife of Virginia and Maryland Washington D.C.|publisher=Stackpole Books|page=171|year=2003|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=W7UxSPd2XMAC&dq|id=ISBN 0-8117-2821-8}}</ref>若掠食者非常接近，牠們甚至會直接嘔吐到其面或[[眼睛|眼睛]]上。某些情況下，牠們會嘔吐得很劇烈，並飛走逃避掠食者。<ref name="Terres">{{cite book en|last=Terres|first=J. K.|title=The Audubon Society Encyclopedia of North American Birds|publisher=Knopf|year=1980|location=New York, NY|pages=959|id=ISBN 0-394-46651-9}}</ref>

紅頭美洲鷲在地上時很不靈活，要廢很大的功夫才能起飛。<ref name="Terres"/>起飛後，牠們會保持V型，很少會拍動雙翼，只會順著氣流上升。<ref name="USGS">{{cite web|title=Turkey vulture, ''Cathartes aura''|publisher =U.S. Geological Survey|url=http://www.mbr-pwrc.usgs.gov/id/framlst/i3250id.html|accessdate=2007-09-30}}</ref>

=== 食性 ===
[[File:Turkey_Vulture_feeding.jpg|thumb]]的紅頭美洲鷲。]]
紅頭美洲鷲主要吃[[屍體|屍體]]，由細小的[[哺乳動物|哺乳動物]]至大型的牧食動物不等，當中特別喜歡剛死去未腐化的屍體。牠們也會吃[[植物|植物]]、[[南瓜|南瓜]]及其他農作物、[[昆蟲|昆蟲]]及其他[[無脊椎動物|無脊椎動物]]。<ref name="Kaufman"/>在[[南美洲|南美洲]]，牠們甚至會吃[[油棕屬|油棕屬]]的[[果實|果實]]。<ref>{{Cite journal en|author=Pinto, O. M. O.|title=Dos frutos da palmeira Elaeis guineensis na dieta de ''Cathartes aura ruficollis''|journal=Hornero|volume=8|pages=276-7|year=1965}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal en|author=Mauro Galetti & Paulo R. Guimarães Jr.|title=Seed dispersal of Attalea phalerata (Palmae) by Crested caracaras (''Caracara plancus'') in the Pantanal and a review of frugivory by raptors|journal=Ararajuba|volume=12|issue=2|pages=133-5|url=http://www.ararajuba.org.br/sbo/ararajuba/artigos/Volume122/ara122not1.pdf|date=December 2004|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6HJqvn2SL?url=http://www.ararajuba.org.br/sbo/ararajuba/artigos/Volume122/ara122not1.pdf|archivedate=2013-06-12}}</ref>牠們很少會自己動手殺死獵物。<ref name="Kritcher">{{cite book en|last=Kritcher|first=John C.|title=A Neotropical Companion|publisher=Princeton University Press|year=1999|pages=286|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=Z3pgdvrSmG8C&dq=Cathartes+aura+%2B+kill|id=ISBN 0-691-00974-0}}</ref>牠們有時會在路邊吃[[路殺|路殺]]，或是在水邊吃沖上來的[[魚類|魚類]]。<ref name="ADW"/>牠們也會吃淺水中的魚或昆蟲。<ref name="Fergus"/>牠們在[[生態系統|生態系統]]有著重要的角色，可以幫助清理屍體，免於[[細菌|細菌]]滋生。<ref name="cotton"/>

紅頭美洲鷲憑[[嗅覺|嗅覺]]來覓食，是[[鳥類|鳥類]]中較為罕有的。牠們一般會低飛來偵測屍體散發的[[乙硫醇|乙硫醇]]。<ref name="snyder"/>[[腦部|腦部]][[嗅葉|嗅葉]]的體積特別大。<ref name="snyder">{{cite book en|last=Snyder, Noel F. R. and Helen Snyder|title=Raptors of North America: Natural History and Conservation|publisher=Voyageur Press|page=40|year=2006|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=g6aOgkIbEwEC&pg=PA40&dq=Cathartes+aura&as_brr=3&sig=Zc-vwBBgjWSMDx56CatOgteVOtI#PPA40,M1|id=ISBN 0-7603-2582-0}}</ref>[[王鷲|王鷲]]及[[黑美洲鷲|黑美洲鷲]]都不能嗅出屍體所在，只會跟隨紅頭美洲鷲來尋找屍體。紅頭美洲鷲，或與[[大黃頭美洲鷲|大黃頭美洲鷲]]及[[小黃頭美洲鷲|小黃頭美洲鷲]]會先找到屍體<ref name="snyder"/>，紅頭美洲鷲會趕走黃頭美洲鷲<ref name="cotton">{{cite journal en|last=Gomez, LG; Houston, DC; Cotton, P; Tye, A|title=The role of greater yellow-headed vultures Cathartes melambrotus as scavengers in neotropical forest|journal=Ibis|volume=136|issue=2|pages=193–6|year=1994|url=http://md1.csa.com/partners/viewrecord.php?requester=gs&collection=ENV&recid=3646491&q=Cathartes+melambrotus&uid=791396595&setcookie=yes|accessdate=2007-10-03|doi=10.1111/j.1474-919X.1994.tb01084.x|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090216095012/http://md1.csa.com/partners/viewrecord.php?requester=gs&collection=ENV&recid=3646491&q=Cathartes+melambrotus&uid=791396595&setcookie=yes|archivedate=2009-02-16}}</ref>，而王鷲則會待紅頭美洲鷲撕開屍體後才趕走牠們，因王鷲的喙不夠強壯撕開屍體。這是一個[[物種|物種]]間[[共生|共生]]的例子。<ref name="Dietland">{{cite book en|last=Muller-Schwarze|first=Dietland|title=Chemical Ecology of Vertebrates|publisher=Cambridge University Press|page=350|year=2006|url= http://books.google.com/books?id=HaaFlUw4goIC&pg=PA1&dq=%22Chemical+Ecology+of+Vertebrates+%22&sig=onv4c39uHCdcbBzm6a5iwyuSers|id=ISBN 0-521-36377-2}}</ref>

=== 繁殖 ===
[[File:Turkey_vulture_chick.jpg|thumb]]
紅頭美洲鷲的[[繁殖|繁殖]]期是於3月開始，最高峰為4-5月，一直至到6月。<ref name="Georgia">{{cite web|title=Species Description: Turkey Vulture (''Cathartes aura'')|publisher=Georgia Museum of Natural History|url=http://dromus.nhm.uga.edu/~GMNH/gawildlife/index.php?page=speciespages/species_page&key=caura|accessdate=2007-10-14|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090619063859/http://dromus.nhm.uga.edu/~GMNH/gawildlife/index.php?page=speciespages%2Fspecies_page&key=caura|archivedate=2009-06-19}}</ref>牠們示愛時會有幾隻鷲聚集成圈，張開雙翼跳動。在空中，一隻鷲會緊隨另一隻鷲拍動雙翼或俯衝。<ref name="Kaufman">{{cite book en|last=Kaufman|first=Kenn|title=Lives of North American Birds|publisher=Houghton Mifflin Field Guides|year=1996|pages=112|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=JhJwsTkYkoIC&pg=PA112&dq=Cathartes+aura+%2B+nest&sig=HfdEcjgEeyQraZhcAxAyL7ogaEU|id=ISBN 0-618-15988-6}}</ref>

紅頭美洲鷲一般會在受保護的地方，如洞穴、懸崖、石縫、空心樹或灌木叢築巢生蛋。牠們的巢並不怎麼完善，差不多只是在地上生蛋。雌鷲一般會生1-3顆蛋，蛋呈奶白色，有褐色或[[薰衣草|薰衣草]]色的斑點。<ref name="Kaufman"/>雙親也會負責孵化，雛鷲在30-40日就會出生。雛鷲出生時是需要照顧的。雙親會用反芻液來餵哺雛鷲，照顧牠們10-11星期。當受到威脅時，成鷲會逃走、向入侵者嘔吐或裝死。<ref name="Fergus"/>若雛鷲受到威脅，成鷲會保護牠們。<ref name="Kaufman"/>雛鷲約於9-10星期大就會換羽。一家會一起待至冬天的來臨。<ref name="Kaufman"/>

== 與人類關係 ==
紅頭美洲鷲被指是傳播[[炭疽病|炭疽病]]及[[豬瘟|豬瘟]]的兇手<ref name="mossman">{{Cite book en|author=Kirk, D. A., and M. J. Mossman|year=1998|chapter=Turkey Vulture (''Cathartes aura'')|title=The Birds of North America, No. 339|editor=A. Poole and F. Gill, eds.|publisher=The Birds of North America, Inc.|location=Philadelphia, PA}}</ref>，不過，引發豬瘟的[[病毒|病毒]]在經過紅頭美洲鷲的消化道後就已被消滅。<ref name="Terres"/>牠們也被誤會是會攻擊[[歐洲牛|歐洲牛]]，因牠們與[[黑美洲鷲|黑美洲鷲]]混在一起，隨其後吃屍體。牠們的[[糞便|糞便]]對樹木或其他植被是有害的。<ref>{{cite web|last=Paulik|first=Laurie|title=Vultures|publisher=AgNIC Wildlife Damage Management Web|date=2007-08-06|url=http://lib.colostate.edu/research/agnic/birds/vultures/index.html|accessdate=2007-10-15|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070804003554/http://lib.colostate.edu/research/agnic/birds/vultures/index.html|archivedate=2007-08-04}}</ref>牠們可以被飼養，但卻被法例所禁止，除非牠們是受傷或不能回到野外。<ref>{{cite web|title=Migratory Bird Treaty Act|work=US Code Collection|publisher=Cornell Law School|url=http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/html/uscode16/usc_sup_01_16_10_7_20_II.html|accessdate=2007-10-14}}</ref>飼養下的紅頭美洲鷲可以吃鮮肉。<ref name="Terres"/>

紅頭美洲鷲被[[世界自然保護聯盟|世界自然保護聯盟]]列為[[無危|無危]]，群落數量穩定。<ref name="BirdLife"/>
