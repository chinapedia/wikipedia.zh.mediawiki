常見於[[蝴蝶|蝴蝶]]，其他動物也會有此行為，其中主要是[[昆蟲|昆蟲]]。他們會尋找湿润的物質，如腐爛的植物、[[泥土|泥土]]和[[腐肉|腐肉]]<ref name="Sculley">{{aut|Sculley, C.E. & Boggs, C.L.}} (1996): Mating systems and sexual division of foraging effort affect puddling behaviour by butterflies. ''Ecological Entomology'' '''21'''(2): 193-197. [http://www.stanford.edu/group/CCB/Pubs/Boggs_pdfs/1996%20Sculley%20&%20Boggs%20puddling.pdf PDF fulltext]</ref>，去吸取其中的液體。所以昆蟲（如蝴蝶）常常會在潮濕的土壤、糞便或腐肉上聚集。從物質中獲得的營養物質，如[[鹽|鹽]]和[[氨基酸|氨基酸]]，在他們的生理上、行為上和生態上扮演重要角色<ref>{{aut|Boggs, CL}} & {{aut|LA Jackson}} (1991) Mud puddling by butterflies is not a simple matter  Ecological Entomology 16(1):123-127 {{doi|10.1111/j.1365-2311.1991.tb00199.x}} [http://www.stanford.edu/group/CCB/Pubs/Boggs_pdfs/1991%20Boggs%20&%20Jackson%20puddling.pdf PDF fulltext]</ref><ref name="Beck1999">{{aut|Beck, J.; Mühlenberg, E. & Fiedler, K.}} (1999): Mud-puddling behavior in tropical butterflies: In search of proteins or minerals? ''[[Oecologia|Oecologia]]'' '''119'''(1): 140–148. {{doi|10.1007/s004420050770}} (HTML abstract) [http://www.biogeography.unibas.ch/Mitarbeiter/Jan_Beck/JBeck_NLU/abstr_publ/Beck%20etal%20(1999)%20Mud-puddling%20Oecologia.pdf PDF fulltext] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110707004200/http://www.biogeography.unibas.ch/Mitarbeiter/Jan_Beck/JBeck_NLU/abstr_publ/Beck%20etal%20%281999%29%20Mud-puddling%20Oecologia.pdf |date=2011-07-07 }}</ref>。一些其他昆蟲也會有此行為，如{{le|葉蟬|Leafhopper}}與[[馬鈴薯小綠葉蟬|馬鈴薯小綠葉蟬]]<ref>{{aut|Adler, P.H.}} (1982): Nocturnal occurrences of leafhoppers (Homoptera: Cicadellidae) at soil. ''Journal of the Kansas Entomological Society'' '''55'''(1): 73–74. [http://md1.csa.com/partners/viewrecord.php?requester=gs&collection=ENV&recid=238722&q=%20&uid=791271398&setcookie=yes HTML abstract] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110520045033/http://md1.csa.com/partners/viewrecord.php?requester=gs&collection=ENV&recid=238722&q=&uid=791271398&setcookie=yes |date=2011-05-20 }}</ref>
。

[[鱗翅目|鱗翅目]]（像是蝴蝶和[[飛蛾|飛蛾]]）是常常有多種策略來收集液體和營養物。通常，趨泥行為會發生在有潮濕泥土的地方，甚至血、淚與汗也會對蝴蝶有吸引力。並不只有鱗翅目有類似的行為，例如，[[蜜蜂|蜜蜂]]也會被汗和眼淚吸引。有些物種只有雄性有趨泥行為。
