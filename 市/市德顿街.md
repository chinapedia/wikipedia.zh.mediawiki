'''市德顿街'''（Stockton Street）是美国[[旧金山|旧金山]]的一条南北向街道<ref>[http://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g60713-d209630-Reviews-Stockton_Street-San_Francisco_California.html Stockton Street reviews from TripAdvisor]</ref>，开始于[[市场街_(旧金山)|市场街]]，经过该市主要购物区[[联合广场_(旧金山)|联合广场]]<ref>[http://www.sfgate.com/cgi-bin/article.cgi?f=/c/a/2007/11/22/NSCRT9ISC.DTL Stockton Street, Gucci and Cole Haan are located on Stockton St. San Francisco]</ref>，然后通过地下约两个半街区的市德顿街隧道，经过[[唐人街_(旧金山)|唐人街]]和[[北滩_(旧金山)|北滩]]（小意大利），结束于海滩街靠近[[39号码头|39号码头]]购物中心和旅游景点<ref>[https://maps.google.com/maps?q=Stockton+St,+San+Francisco,+CA,+USA&sa=X&oi=map&ct=title The location of Stockton Street in San Francisco], [[Google_Maps|Google Maps]].</ref>。

==唐人街==
市德顿街是唐人街（特别是在华盛顿街和百老汇之间的三个街区）<ref name=nolte>{{cite news|last1=Nolte|first1=Carl|title=Shopping for a new year|url=http://www.sfgate.com/bayarea/nativeson/article/Shopping-for-a-new-year-3273594.php|work=[[San_Francisco_Chronicle|San Francisco Chronicle]]|date=February 7, 2010}}</ref>的主要购物和商业街，是当地人去买最新鲜和最便宜的货物和肉类的地方。商店还出售生鲜海鲜（主要在街道西侧）<ref name=nolte/>。位于市德顿街的商店包括合兴人参公司，九龙市场，连星鱼市场和水果城。<ref>[http://query.nytimes.com/gst/fullpage.html?res=9C05E4DF1F3DF93AA35751C1A9679C8B63]</ref> 在一年一度的市德顿街集市， 自2012年以来允许商人在[[中国新年|中国新年]]前的两周摆放户外展台。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Wildermuth|first1=John|last2=Tucker|first2=Jill|title=Mayor puts his shine on new supervisor in Chinatown|url=http://www.sfgate.com/bayarea/article/Mayor-puts-his-shine-on-new-supervisor-in-6065093.php|work=San Francisco Chronicle|date=February 5, 2015}}</ref>
[[File:Zhonghua_Huiguan,_San_Francisco_(6540).JPG|thumb]]]]
旧金山规划和城市研究协会描述唐人街的市德顿街是“一个真正城市化的例子，充满了强大的交通网络，多代家庭生活在紧密的空间，数以百计的夫妻店，以及繁华的街道生活"，“草根组织保护唐人街的负担能力，文化和城市形态的成功。”<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url = http://www.spur.org/blog/2012-04-09/creating-community-vision-stockton-street|title = Creating a Community Vision for Stockton Street|date = April 9, 2012|accessdate = |website = |publisher = SPUR|last = Noah Christman, Deland Chan, Vivian Chang and Cindy Wu|first = }}</ref> 在这一领域，市德顿街主要是三到四层楼高的混合用途建筑，底楼是商店，楼上是公寓住宅。<ref name=":0" /> 这里还有一些在华人移民社区的历史中发挥重要的政治作用的组织，特别是[[中华公所|中华会馆]]、同源会<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|title = Strolling Stockton Street, the heart of Chinatown|url = http://www.sfchronicle.com/bayarea/nativeson/article/Strolling-Stockton-Street-the-heart-of-Chinatown-6829208.php|website = San Francisco Chronicle|access-date = 2016-02-17|date = 2016-02-13|last = Nolte|first = Carl|last2 = |quote = ... 旧金山最有趣的街道，是市德顿街介于沙加緬度街和百老汇之间的五个街区， [...] 市德顿街的大部分商店都相当的新，1960年代中期移民法放松后，新移民涌入，使唐人街和老市德顿街换了新貌。}}</ref>以及[[国民党|国民党]]美国总部。

在1960年代中期移民法放宽之后，许多商店受较低的租金和方便的电车线路吸引，从附近的都板街搬到市德顿街<ref>{{Cite web|title = A Brief History of the F-Market & Wharves Line {{!}} Market Street Railway|url = http://www.streetcar.org/brief-history/|website = Market Street Railway|access-date = 2016-02-22|language = en-US}}</ref>).<ref name="nolte" /><ref name=":1" />。旧金山纪事报把萨克拉门托和百老汇之间的一段市德顿街称为"旧金山最有趣的街道...令人着迷（但不美丽）。它拥挤，不洁，肮脏，通常还嘈杂。"<ref name=":1" />

预计[[旧金山中央地铁|旧金山中央地铁]]的完工将使这条街的面貌带来重大变化。<ref name=":0" />

==联合广场==
在联合广场区，市德顿街是众多高档商场的所在地，包括梅西西部百货公司旗舰店和尼曼百货公司。自2012年以来由于中央地铁建设工程，梅西百货旁的街区禁止车辆通行，有计划将其永久转换为步行区<ref>{{cite news|last1=Bialick|first1=Aaron|title=Stockton Street "Winter Walk" Plaza to Return Next Holiday Season|url=http://sf.streetsblog.org/2015/01/28/stockton-street-winter-walk-plaza-to-return-next-holiday-season/|work=[[Streetsblog|Streetsblog]] SF|date=January 28, 2015}}</ref>，但有影响力的唐人街社区组织者白兰强烈反对这个项目，威胁要组织数千辆车封锁市政厅<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.sfchronicle.com/bayarea/matier-ross/article/Rose-Pak-flashes-red-light-on-Stockton-Street-9126204.php|title=Rose Pak flashes red light on Stockton Street pedestrian mall|access-date=2016-09-19}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://sanfrancisco.cbslocal.com/2016/08/08/rose-pak-threatens-city-hall-blockade-over-stockton-street-proposal/|title=Rose Pak Threatens City Hall Blockade Over Stockton Street Proposal|last=Matier|first=Phil|date=2016-08-08|website=|publisher=|access-date=2016-09-19}}</ref>。
