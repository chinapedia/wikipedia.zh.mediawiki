'''伍德罗·威尔逊总统图书馆'''（Woodrow Wilson Presidential Library）位于美国[[弗吉尼亚州|弗吉尼亚州]][[斯汤顿_(弗吉尼亚州)|斯汤顿]]福音山历史街区北Coalter街18-24号的[[伍德罗·威尔逊|伍德罗·威尔逊]]出生地<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.woodrowwilson.org/learn/ |title=The Woodrow Wilson Presidential Library: Learn at the Library |website=Woodrowwilson.org |date= |accessdate=2010-01-25 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100123203741/http://www.woodrowwilson.org/learn/ |archivedate= 2010-01-23}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.woodrowwilson.org/visit |title=The Woodrow Wilson Presidential Library: Visit the Museum |website=woodrowwilson.org |date= |accessdate=2015-04-04}}</ref>。

伍德罗·威尔逊出生地是斯汤顿第一[[长老会|长老会]]的牧师住宅，建于1846年。威尔逊一家在1855年搬进该住宅<ref name="woodrowwilson1">{{cite web|url=http://www.woodrowwilson.org/visit_sub/visit_sub_show.htm?doc_id=319960 |title=The Woodrow Wilson Presidential Library: Visit the Museum – Additional Information: The Manse |website=Woodrowwilson.org |date= |accessdate=2010-01-25 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080503043309/http://www.woodrowwilson.org/visit_sub/visit_sub_show.htm?doc_id=319960 |archivedate=May 3, 2008 }}</ref>。<ref name="woodrowwilson1"/>伍德罗·威尔逊于1856年12月28日出生于此，1858年其父调往乔治亚州奥古斯塔后全家离开<ref name=VAnom>{{cite web|url=http://www.dhr.virginia.gov/registers/Cities/Staunton/132-0004_Wilson,Woodrow,Birthplace_1966_Final_Nomination_NHL.pdf|title=National Register of Historic Places Inventory/Nomination: Woodrow Wilson Birthplace|author=Frank S. Melvin|date=June 1972|publisher=Virginia Department of Historic Resources|access-date=2017-01-07|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170125215638/http://www.dhr.virginia.gov/registers/Cities/Staunton/132-0004_Wilson,Woodrow,Birthplace_1966_Final_Nomination_NHL.pdf|archive-date=2017-01-25|dead-url=yes}} and [http://www.dhr.virginia.gov/registers/Cities/Staunton/WoodrowWilson_photo.htm ''Accompanying photo''] {{Wayback|url=http://www.dhr.virginia.gov/registers/Cities/Staunton/WoodrowWilson_photo.htm |date=20170215101843 }}</ref>。

伍德罗·威尔逊出生地于1966年列入[[国家史迹名录|国家史迹名录]]。
