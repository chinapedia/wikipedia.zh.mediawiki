'''龍潭亭'''是一个[[天道教|天道教]]圣地，位于[[慶尙北道|慶尙北道]][[慶州市|慶州市]][[見谷面|見谷面]]。<ref>[http://www.koreatimes.co.kr/www/news/include/print.asp?newsIdx=43618 Korea Times] 150th Anniversary of 'Tonghak'</ref>

==宗教圣地==
天道教是[[朝鲜半岛|朝鲜半岛]]的本土宗教，从朝鲜[[东学|东学]]演变而来。东学的创始人[[崔濟愚|崔濟愚]]在附近出生和被埋葬。<ref>[http://www.gyeongju.go.kr/eng/02/07_04.asp Yongdamjeong vicinity]{{dead link|date=十一月 2017 |bot=InternetArchiveBot }} Gyeongju City</ref><ref>[http://www.gyeongju.go.kr/eng/frame.asp?top=/eng/02/top.asp&left=/eng/02/left.asp&main=/eng/02/07_01.asp North Culture Vicinity] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110613163432/http://www.gyeongju.go.kr/eng/frame.asp?top=%2Feng%2F02%2Ftop.asp&left=%2Feng%2F02%2Fleft.asp&main=%2Feng%2F02%2F07_01.asp |date=2011-06-13 }} Gyeongju City</ref><ref>(in Korean) [http://100.nate.com/dicsearch/pentry.html?s=K&i=287947&v=44 용담성지 龍潭聖地] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110610062754/http://100.nate.com/dicsearch/pentry.html?s=K&i=287947&v=44 |date=2011-06-10 }} [[Nate|Nate]] / [[韓國民族文化大百科|韓國民族文化大百科]]</ref>凉亭附近有一座纪念他的雕像。
