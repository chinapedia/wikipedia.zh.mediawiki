'''龍蝨科'''（{{lang|la|Dytiscidae}}），亦作'''龍虱科'''，為{{tsl|en|water beetle|水甲蟲}}的一種，隸屬於[[鞘翅目|鞘翅目]]之下的[[肉食亞目|肉食亞目]]。生活在田野、水溝、小溪等水體中，是一種[[掠食性動物|掠食性]][[水生昆蟲|水生昆蟲]]。其學名源於希臘文''{{lang|grc|δυτικός}}''（''dytikos''），意思就是「肉食性的潛水甲蟲」。本科物種幾乎見於全世界所有淡水棲境，但亦有部分於落葉堆中棲息{{r|Foster2014}}。成蟲體長一般在{{convert|1|and(-)|2.5|cm|in|abbr=on|1}}之間，儘管物種間的差異會比較大。當中見於歐洲的''{{tsl|en|Dytiscus latissimus|}}''及巴西的''{{tsl|en|Megadytes ducalis|}}''有最大的體型，可分別長至{{convert|4.5|cm|in|abbr=on|1}}到{{convert|4.75|cm|in|abbr=on|1}}{{r|Foster2014|hurdan}}。與之相反，擁有最小體型的物種應當為棲息於[[澳大利亞|澳大利亞]]地下水中的''{{tsl|en|Limbodessus|}} atypicali''，只有約{{convert|0.9|mm|in|abbr=on}}長<ref name=Foster2014/>。
大多數物種的顏色都在深棕色、黑色或深[[橄榄绿|橄榄绿]]，某些[[亚科|亚科]]的物種會在其邊沿帶有金黃色的條狀斑紋<ref name=guokr />。
They have short, but sharp {{tsl|en|mandible (insect)|}}s. Immediately upon biting, they deliver digestive enzymes. The <!-- [[larva|larva]] -->[[幼体|幼体]]e are commonly known as '''water tigers'''.<ref>{{cite book| author=G.C. McGavin | year=2010 | title=Insects | pages=86–87 | isbn=978-1-4053-4997-0 }}</ref> The family includes more than 4,000 described <!-- [[species|species]] -->[[物种|物种]] in numerous <!-- [[genera|genera]] -->[[属|属]].<ref>{{cite web | author=Nilsson, A.N. | year=2013 | url=http://www2.emg.umu.se/projects/biginst/andersn/WCD_20130101.pdf |title=A World Catalogue of the Family Dytiscidae, or the Diving Beetles (Coleoptera, Adephaga) | publisher=University of Umeå |access-date=10 April 2018 }}</ref>

==幼蟲及其發育==
[[Image:Dytiscidae_larva.jpg|thumb]]
When still in larval form, the beetles vary in size from about 1 to 5 cm (0.5 to 2.0 in). The larval bodies are shaped like crescents, with the tail long and covered with thin hairs. Six legs protrude from along the <!-- [[thorax|thorax]] -->[[胸|胸]], which also sports the same thin hairs. The head is flat and square, with a pair of long, large pincers. When hunting, they cling to grasses or pieces of wood along the bottom, and hold perfectly still until prey passes by, then they lunge, trapping their prey between their front legs and biting down with their pincers. Their usual prey includes <!-- [[tadpole|tadpole]] -->[[蝌蚪|蝌蚪]]s and {{tsl|en|glassworm|}}s, among other smaller water-dwelling creatures.

As the larvae mature, they crawl from the water on the sturdy legs, and bury themselves in the mud for <!-- [[pupation|pupation]] -->[[蛹|蛹]]. After about a week, or longer in some species, they emerge from the mud as adults.

==食用==
本科物種的成蟲，特别是''[[Cybister|Cybister]]''屬的物種，均為[[食用昆蟲|可食用昆蟲]]。在美國[[内华达州|内华达州]]{{tsl|en|Humboldt Sink|洪堡乾湖}}的一個山洞内，還在當地史前人類遺下的[[粪化石|粪化石]]内發現有''{{tsl|en|Cybister explanatus|}}''被食用的殘骸<ref name=Miller>{{cite book|last1=Miller|first1=Kelly|last2=Bergsten|first2=Johannes|title=Diving Beetles of the World: Systematics and Biology of the Dytiscidae|date=3 October 2016|publisher=Johns Hopkins University Press|location=Baltimore|page=20}}</ref>。在[[墨西哥|墨西哥]]，他們在吃''C. explanatus''時會以[[墨西哥夹饼|墨西哥夹饼]]夾着來吃。在日本多個地區（例如：[[長野縣|長野縣]]）有以[[日本龍蝨|日本龍蝨]]（''{{tsl|en|Cybister japonicus|}}''）為食的習慣；在[[中华人民共和国|中华人民共和国]][[广东省|广东省]]，除了日本龍蝨以外，亦會食用''[[Cybister_bengalensis|C. bengalensis]]''、''[[Cybister_guerini|C. guerini]]''、''[[Cybister_limbatus|C. limbatus]]''、''[[Cybister_sugillatus|C. sugillatus]]''、''[[Cybister_tripunctatus|C. tripunctatus]]''等物種。{{tsl|en|Great diving beetle||''D. marginalis''}}很可能也包括在內，但這個物種的肉很少。也有報告指在[[台灣|台灣]]、[[泰国|泰国]]及[[新幾內亞|新幾內亞]]亦有食用本科物種的習慣<ref>De Foliart (2002), Jäch (2003), CSIRO (2004)</ref>。
[[File:Dytiscidae.png|thumb]]
Large but slow on land and not particularly fierce as adults, they are also eaten with relish by many midsized <!-- [[bird|bird]] -->[[鸟|鸟]]s, <!-- [[mammal|mammal]] -->[[哺乳动物|哺乳动物]]s, and other larger <!-- [[predator|predator]] -->[[捕食|捕食]]s. The larvae are usually safer, due to their <!-- [[crypsis|camouflage]] -->[[保護色|保護色]] and ability to escape by water jet; they can be quite hard to catch and may become <!-- [[apex_predator|apex predator]] -->[[顶级掠食者|顶级掠食者]]s in small <!-- [[pond|pond]] -->[[池塘|池塘]]s.

==Cultural significance==
The diving beetle plays a role in a <!-- [[Cherokee|Cherokee]] -->[[切羅基人|切羅基人]] creation story. According to the narrative, upon finding nowhere to rest in the "liquid chaos" the beetle brought up soft mud from the bottom. This mud then spread out to form all of the land on Earth.<ref name=Miller/>

==Ethnobiology==
Adult Dytiscidae, as well as <!-- [[Gyrinidae|Gyrinidae]] -->[[豉甲科|豉甲科]], are collected by young girls in <!-- [[East_Africa|East Africa]] -->[[东非|东非]]. It is believed that inducing the beetles to bite the nipples will stimulate breast growth.<ref name=Miller/>
