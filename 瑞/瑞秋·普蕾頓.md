==早年生活==
普蕾頓生於1981年5月20日，帕梅拉（Pamela）與保羅·普蕾頓（Paul Platten）之女。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Platten|first1=Rachel|title=Happy Birthday to my absolutely incredible mom!|url=https://www.facebook.com/rachelplattenmusic/photos/a.405296569785.181405.6346584785/10151220563194786/?_rdr=p&source=46&type=1|website=facebook|accessdate=2015-06-22|date=2013-01-10}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=SUMNER M. PLATTEN Obituary|url=http://www.legacy.com/obituaries/bostonglobe/obituary.aspx?pid=160499318|website=The Boston Globe|accessdate=2015-06-22|date=2012-10-18}}</ref>她在[[美國|美國]][[牛頓_(麻薩諸塞州)|牛頓]]{{le|牛頓中心 (麻薩諸塞州)|Newton Centre, Massachusetts|牛頓中心}}長大，從5歲開始學習古典鋼琴，之後在高中時彈奏吉他。她在高中時進入{{le|白金漢布朗和尼古拉斯學校|Buckingham Browne & Nichols}}就讀，並加入了該校的歌唱團體。<ref>{{cite news |last=McFeeters |first=Stephanie ==職業生涯==

===2003－14年：《Trust in Me》與《Be Here》===
普蕾頓在2003年發行了她的首張錄音室專輯《Trust in Me》。<ref>{{cite web|title=Trust In Me|url=http://www.mtv.com/music/artist/platten__rachel/albums.jhtml?albumId=929890|publisher=MTV|accessdate=2012-07-30}}</ref>他的第二張專輯《Be Here》由{{le|Rock Ridge Music|Rock Ridge Music}}唱片公司在2011年推出，<ref>{{cite web|url= http://allmusic.com/album/be-here-r2154168/review|title= All Music Guide Review of Be Here by William Ruhlmann|work=[[AllMusic|AllMusic]]|accessdate=2015-07-10}}</ref>她曾稱這是一張樣本歌曲的合輯。<ref name="USA TODAY"/>而她在2007年推出的歌曲〈Seven Weeks〉曾出現在電影《{{le|尋找完美情人|The Good Guy (film)}}》當中。<ref name="examiner">{{cite news |url=http://www.examiner.com/article/rachel-platten-and-caitlin-rose-fresh-female-singer-songwriters-on-the-rise |title=Rachel Platten and Caitlin Rose - fresh female singer-songwriters on the rise |work=Examiner.com |publisher=AXS Digital Group |date=2012-03-08 |accessdate=2015-07-10 }}</ref>她也為[[ABC家庭|ABC家庭]]的電視劇《{{le|珍，愛時尚|Jane by Design}}》創作[[主題曲|主題曲]]〈Work of Art〉。<ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1743904/|title= Jane by Design|work=imdb.com|publisher=[[IMDb|IMDb]]|accessdate=2015-07-10}}</ref><ref name="examiner"/>她的歌曲〈Begin Again〉也在ABC家族的電視劇《[[美少女的謊言|美少女的謊言]]》第100集〈Miss Me x100〉中被使用。<ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.imdb.com/title/tt3691228/soundtrack|title= "Pretty Little Liars" Miss Me x100 (TV Episode 2014) - Soundtracks - IMDb|work=imdb.com|publisher=[[IMDb|IMDb]]|accessdate=2015-07-10}}</ref>另外，還有在[[VH1|VH1]]的《{{le|籃球明星的妻子：洛杉磯版|Basketball Wives LA}}》中的〈Don't Care What Time It Is〉一曲，<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.vh1.com/shows/music/basketball-wives-la-2/season-2-episode-3-songs/ |title=Basketball Wives LA 2 Season 2 Episode 3 Songs |work=vh1.com |publisher=[[VH1|VH1]] |date=2012-09-24 |accessdate=2015-07-10 }}</ref>以及[[MTV|MTV]]的《{{le|尋找卡特|Finding Carter}}》中的〈You're Safe〉皆是普蕾頓的創作。<ref>{{cite news |last=Greenaway |first=Johnathan |url=http://soundtrack.mtv.com/post/music-from-finding-carter-season-1-ep-8/ |title=Music from Finding Carter: Season 1, Ep. 8 |work=soundtrack.mtv.com |publisher=[[MTV|MTV]] |date=2014-08-19 |accessdate=2015-07-10 }}</ref>2014年6月27日，普蕾頓首度在{{le|We Heart It|We Heart It}}上發布〈戰鬥之歌〉。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Platten|first1=Rachel|title= I am so excited to debut #FightSong with @weheartit !!!|url=https://twitter.com/RachelPlatten/status/482541614234345472|website=Twitter|publisher=Rachel Platten|date=2014-06-27|accessdate=2015-07-10}}</ref>

===2015年至今===
普蕾頓於2015年4月加盟[[哥倫比亞唱片|哥倫比亞唱片]]，並且發行了〈[[戰鬥之歌_(瑞秋·普蕾頓歌曲)|戰鬥之歌]]〉單曲。<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.columbiarecords.com/news/825 |title=BREAKOUT SINGER/SONGWRITER RACHEL PLATTEN JOINS COLBIE CAILLAT AND CHRISTINA PERRI ON TOUR THIS SUMMER |work=[[Columbia_Records|Columbia Records]] |publisher=[[Sony_Music_Entertainment|Sony Music Entertainment]] |date=2015-04-08 |accessdate=2015-07-10 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150711085223/http://www.columbiarecords.com/news/825 |archive-date=2015-07-11 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>之後在5月發行的《戰鬥之歌》迷你專輯，<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.columbiarecords.com/news/834 |title=RACHEL PLATTEN RELEASES FIGHT SONG EP TODAY |work=[[Columbia_Records|Columbia Records]] |publisher=[[Sony_Music_Entertainment|Sony Music Entertainment]] |date=2015-04-08 |accessdate=2015-07-10 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150711085225/http://www.columbiarecords.com/news/834 |archive-date=2015-07-11 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>更登上[[公告牌二百强专辑榜|《公告牌》二百强专辑榜]]第26名。<ref name="Billboard"/>這首歌也曾被用在《[[美少女的謊言|美少女的謊言]]》第五季的聖誕節特集，<ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.imdb.com/title/tt3981672/soundtrack|title= "Pretty Little Liars" How the 'A' Stole Christmas (TV Episode 2014) - Soundtracks - IMDb|work=imdb.com|publisher=[[IMDb|IMDb]]|accessdate=2015-07-10}}</ref>以及[[澳洲|澳洲]][[七號電視網|七號電視網]]電視劇《[[復仇_(電視劇)|復仇]]》的預告當中。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QF4Hbt-hh6k|title= Revenge 4x06 Australian Promo - Seven Network 2015|website=[[YouTube|YouTube]]|publisher=AUtvPROMOS|accessdate=2015-07-10}}</ref>同樣也出現在[[CBS_(電視網)|CBS]]於2015年5月上映的電視劇《[[女超人_(美國電視劇)|女超人]]》預告，<ref name="dailymail"/>以及[[福特汽車|福特汽車]]的電視廣告當中。<ref>{{cite web|title=Paced by Rachel Platten Hit 'Fight Song,' New Ford Edge Ad Campaign Seeks to Connect with Unstoppable People|url=https://media.ford.com/content/fordmedia/fna/us/en/news/2015/05/29/rachel-platten-ford-edge-ad-campaign.html|website=Ford.com|accessdate=2015-06-22|date=2015-05-29}}</ref>

普蕾頓在2015年{{le|艾力克斯與席耶拉|Alex & Sierra}}和{{le|安迪·格拉默|Andy Grammer}}聯合巡迴演唱會《The Good Guys & A Girl Tour》中擔任嘉賓。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Boedeker|first1=Hal|title=Alex & Sierra joining Andy Grammer on tour|url=http://www.orlandosentinel.com/entertainment/tv/tv-guy/os-alex-sierra-andy-grammer-tour-20150221-post.html|website=Orlando Sentinel|accessdate=2015-02-21|date=2015-06-23}}</ref>她也在6月13日於[[泰勒絲|泰勒絲]]的《[[1989巡迴演唱會|1989巡迴演唱會]]》中演唱〈戰鬥之歌〉，之後將在[[蔻比·凱蕾|蔻比·凱蕾]]與[[克瑞斯蒂娜·佩瑞|克瑞斯蒂娜·佩瑞]]的《The Girls Night Out, Boys Can Come Too Tour》擔任開場嘉賓。<ref>{{cite news |last=Lipshutz |first=Jason |url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop-shop/6598088/taylor-swift-bring-out-rachel-platten-for-fight-song-in-philly |title=Watch Taylor Swift Bring Out Rachel Platten for 'Fight Song' in Philadelphia |work=billboard.com |publisher=[[告示牌_(雜誌)|Billboard]] |date=2015-06-14 |accessdate=2015-07-10 }}</ref>
