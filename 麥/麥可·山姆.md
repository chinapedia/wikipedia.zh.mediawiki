'''麥可·山姆'''（{{bd|1990年|1月7日}}）<ref>{{cite web|url=https://familysearch.org/pal:/MM9.1.1/VD75-25X |title=Person Details for Michael Alan Ii Sam,, "Texas, Birth Index, 1903–1997" — |publisher=Familysearch.org |date= |accessdate=February 10, 2014}}</ref>是一位[[美式足球|美式足球]][[防守邊鋒|防守邊鋒]]。他就讀于[[密蘇里大學|密蘇里大學]]，在學期間也效力於學校的美式足球隊密蘇里之虎。

在完成他的大學美式足球生涯後，山姆出櫃了。在2014年NFL选秀上，山姆以第7轮第256顺位的成绩被[[圣路易斯公羊隊|圣路易斯公羊隊]]选中，成为首位公开同性恋身份的NFL新秀球员。<ref>{{cite news|last=Belson|first=Ken|title=In Historic Pick, Rams Take Michael Sam in Final Round of Draft|date=May 10, 2014|newspaper=The New York Times|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2014/05/11/sports/football/michael-sam-picked-by-st-louis-rams-in-nfl-draft.html?_r=0|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6PUBcQr0j?url=http://www.nytimes.com/2014/05/11/sports/football/michael-sam-picked-by-st-louis-rams-in-nfl-draft.html?_r=1|archivedate=2014-05-11|deadurl=no|access-date=2014-05-11}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cnn.com/2014/05/10/us/football-michael-sam/|title=Michael Sam makes history: First openly gay player drafted in the NFL|first=Chelsea J. |last=Carter|work=CNN|date=May 10, 2014|accessdate=May 10, 2014}}</ref>8月30日，为满足联盟常规赛开始时陣容必须縮減到53人的要求，圣路易斯公羊隊在最后一轮筛选中将山姆裁掉。<ref name=wagoner>{{cite news|url=http://espn.go.com/nfl/story/_/id/11431047/michael-sam-cut-st-louis-rams|title=Michael Sam cut by Rams|date=August 30, 2014|first=Nick |last=Wagoner | work=ESPN.com|accessdate=August 30, 2014}}</ref>在无人问津數日之後，[[达拉斯牛仔隊|达拉斯牛仔隊]]于9月3日将其加入训练候补阵容。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.cbsnews.com/news/michael-sam-signed-to-dallas-cowboys-practice-squad/|title=Michael Sam signed to Dallas Cowboys practice squad|publisher=CBS/AP|date=September 3, 2014}}</ref>
