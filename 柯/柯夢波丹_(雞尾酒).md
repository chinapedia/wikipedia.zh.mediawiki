'''柯夢波丹'''（{{lang-en|cosmopolitan}}）又稱'''大都會雞尾酒'''，是一種以[[伏特加|伏特加]]、[[白橙皮酒|白橙皮酒]]、[[蔓越莓汁|蔓越莓汁]]，加入新鮮現擠[[萊姆|萊姆]]汁或甜檸檬汁調成的[[雞尾酒|雞尾酒]]。

== 歷史 ==
柯夢波丹以檸檬味[[伏特加|伏特加]]作為基酒<ref name="IBA">
{{cite web |url=http://www.iba-world.com/english/cocktails/cosmopolitan.php |title=Official Cocktail recipe: Cosmopolitan |publisher=[http://www.iba-world.com/english/ International Bartenders Association] |accessdate=2007-05-02 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070520072008/http://www.iba-world.com/english/cocktails/cosmopolitan.php |archivedate=2007-05-20 }}</ref>，是蔓越莓調酒系列中的雞尾酒之一<ref name="Grimes">
{{Cite document |last=Grimes |first=William |date=November 2001 |title=Straight Up Or On the Rocks: The Story of the American Cocktail |publisher=North Point Press |page=119 |url= |accessdate=2008-05-19 |postscript=<!--None-->
}}. Online source viewable at [http://www.barrypopik.com/index.php/new_york_city/entry/cosmopolitan_cocktail/ The Big Apple] blog by Barry Popik.</ref>。它在某種程度上和[[神風特攻隊雞尾酒|神風特攻隊雞尾酒]]很類似。

柯夢波丹的起源尚存在爭議。它很可能是1970年代起，由數名調酒師分別獨立發展出來的<ref name="Harrington">{{Cite document |last=Harrington |first=Paul |last2=Moorhead |first2=Laura |year=1998 |title=Cocktail: The Drinks for the 21st Century |publication-place=New York |publisher=Viking (Penguin Putnam Inc.) |page=76 |url= |accessdate=2008-05-19 |postscript=<!--None--> }}. Online source viewable at [http://www.barrypopik.com/index.php/new_york_city/entry/cosmopolitan_cocktail/ The Big Apple] blog by Barry Popik.</ref>。一般公認，約翰·凱恩在1987年將柯夢波丹從[[明尼蘇達州|明尼蘇達州]]引進舊金山<ref name="Kilduff">{{cite web |url=http://eastbaymonthly.com/kilduff-07-06.html |title=Belly Up to the Bar: John Caine brought the cosmo to Frisco |accessdate=2008-05-20 |last=Kilduff |first=Paul |work=The Kilduff Archive |publisher=The Monthly: The East Bay's Premier Magazine of Culture and Commerce}}</ref><ref name="Kilduff" /><ref name="CityPages">{{cite web|url=http://www.citypages.com/bestof2006/sexdrugsrocknroll/bestof2828.asp |title=Best Locally Created Cocktail |work=Best of the Twin Cities 2006 |publisher=CityPages.com |accessdate=2006-12-31 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070109064707/http://citypages.com/bestof2006/sexdrugsrocknroll/bestof2828.asp |archivedate=2007-01-09 }}</ref>。同年，托比·凱奇尼在[[曼哈頓|曼哈頓]]改良雪兒·庫克的調酒，發明了國際公認版本的柯夢波丹雞尾酒<ref name="Cook">
