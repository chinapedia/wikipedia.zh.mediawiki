'''柯洛4b'''(最初也被称为'''柯洛-系外行星-4b'''<ref>{{cite mailing list|url=http://listes.obs.ujf-grenoble.fr/wws/arc/exoplanets/2009-03/msg00003.html|title=Change in CoRoT planets names|author=Schneider, J.|date=2009-03-10|accessdate=2009-03-19|mailinglist=Exoplanets|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20100118162658/http://listes.obs.ujf-grenoble.fr/wws/arc/exoplanets/2009-03/msg00003.html|archivedate=2010-01-18}}</ref>)是一颗[[系外行星|系外行星]]，其母星为[[柯洛4|柯洛4]]。该行星可能运行于[[同步轨道|同步轨道]]上，即其公转周期等于中央恒星的自转周期。<ref name="aigrain08">{{cite journal|journal=[[Astronomy_and_Astrophysics|Astronomy and Astrophysics]]|volume=488|pages=L43–L46|issue=2|year=2008|doi=10.1051/0004-6361:200810246|title=Transiting exoplanets from the CoRoT space mission IV.
