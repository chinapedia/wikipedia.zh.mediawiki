'''柯立夫''' ({{Lang-en|'''Francis Woodman Cleaves'''}}，{{Bd|1911年|3=1995年|4=12月31日}})，是一位任教於[[哈佛大學|哈佛大學]]的[[汉学家|漢學家]]、[[作家|作家]]以及[[史家|歷史學者]]。他在該校建立了[[美國|美國]]的蒙古學研究基礎<ref name="Yuan Studies">Bettine Birge, "Yuan studies in North America: historical overview, contributions, and current trends," in Haihui Zhang. ''A Scholarly Review of Chinese Studies in North America'' (Ann Arbor, MI.: Association for Asian Studies,  2013).   ISBN 9780924304729. [http://www.asian-studies.org/publications/A_Scholarly_Review_ePDF.pdf pp. 55-56] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131003154738/http://www.asian-studies.org/publications/A_Scholarly_Review_ePDF.pdf |date=2013-10-03 }}.</ref>，並以翻譯《[[蒙古秘史|蒙古秘史]]》聞名。

== 軼事 ==
柯立夫熱衷於蒐集書籍，並在求學於北京時在[[琉璃厂|琉璃廠]]一帶流連；他所搜得的大量關於中國、蒙古、[[滿州|滿州]]的書籍構成了哈佛大學滿州藏品的主要部分。<ref>Mark Elliott, "Highlights of the Manchu-Mongol Collection," in Patrick Hanan.  ed., ''Treasures of the Yenching: Seventy-Fifth Anniversary of the Harvard-Yenching Library.'' (Cambridge, MA; Hong Kong: Harvard-Yenching Library, 2003), [https://books.google.com/books?id=OksPvlsQOZEC&pg=PA79 pp. 79-81].</ref>
