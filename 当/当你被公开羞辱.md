'''《So You've Been Publicly Shamed》'''（意：當你被當眾羞辱）是一本2015年出版，由英國記者{{link-en|強·朗森|Jon Ronson}}著作，講述[[網絡羞辱|網絡羞辱]]及其相關歷史背景的一本書。<ref name="Picador">{{cite web | url=http://www.picador.com/books/so-youve-been-publicly-shamed | title=So You've Been Publicly Shamed | publisher=Picador Publishing | date=2015 | accessdate=6 April 2015}}</ref>這本書探討了[[當眾羞辱|當眾羞辱]]於[[互聯網|互聯網]]，尤其是在[[Twitter|Twitter]]上重新崛起的現象。作為一個國家核准的處分，公共羞辱於{{link-en|美國殖民史|Colonial history of the United States|美國殖民地時期}}備受歡迎。在1837年至1839年期間這種處罰方式逐步於英國和美國被淘汰，原因並非普遍認為的社會人口越來越多<ref name="Reason">{{cite web | url=http://reason.com/archives/2013/06/22/the-shame-of-public-shaming | title=The Shame of Public Shaming | publisher=Reason.com | date=22 June 2013 | accessdate=7 April 2015 | author=Beato, Greg}}</ref><ref name="Criminology">{{cite journal | url=http://www.jstor.org/stable/1133114 | title=Punitive Pain and Humiliation | author=Eaton, Marqus | journal=Journal of the American Institute of Criminal Law and Criminology |date=March 1916  | volume=6 | issue=6}}</ref>，而是由於大眾同情心有所提升。<ref name="ziel">{{cite journal | url=http://www.law2.byu.edu/jpl/papers/v19n2_Paul_Ziel.pdf | title=Eighteenth Century Public Humiliation Penalties in Twenty-First Century America: The "Shameful" Return of "Scarlet Letter" Punishments in U.S. v. Gementera | author=Ziel, Paul | journal=BYU JOURNAL OF PUBLIC LAW | volume=19 | issue=499 | page=501 | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140513195408/http://www.law2.byu.edu/jpl/papers/v19n2_Paul_Ziel.pdf | archivedate=2014年5月13日 | df= }}</ref>

在為他的書蒐集資料時，朗森採訪了幾個被大眾集中針對性網上羞辱的對像，包括[[喬納·雷爾|喬納·雷爾]]、海寧·薩科和林西·斯通。<ref name="AVClub">{{cite web | url=http://www.avclub.com/review/jon-ronson-dives-internets-deep-end-so-youve-been--217104 | script-title=en:Jon Ronson dives into the Internet's deep end in So You’ve Been Publicly Shamed | publisher=A.V. Club | date=30 March 2015 | accessdate=7 April 2015 | author=Rife, Katie}}</ref>他還採訪了備受爭議的，從事21世紀當眾羞辱的參與者，包括前[[得克薩斯|得克薩斯]]縣法官和在任{{link-en|德州第二國會選區|Texas's 2nd congressional district|國會議員}}[[特德·坡|特德·坡]]<ref name="Verdict">{{cite web | url=https://verdict.justia.com/2015/03/20/is-shame-necessary-how-about-public-shaming | title=Is Shame Necessary? How About Public Shaming? | publisher=Justia (Verdict Blog) | date=20 March 2015 | accessdate=7 April 2015 | author=Dean, John}}</ref>和一些公開羞辱的煽動者。

==內容==
在引言中，朗森講述一個自動化的戲仿[[Twitter|Twitter]]帳號，號稱@jon_ronson（朗森的實際Twitter用戶名是@jonronson）的故事。該賬號帖出關於食物和派對相關的雜碎推文，其中沒有任何半點關於強·朗森真實生活的內容。<ref name="Guardian1">{{cite web | url=http://www.theguardian.com/technology/2015/feb/21/internet-shaming-lindsey-stone-jon-ronson | title='Overnight, everything I loved was gone': the internet shaming of Lindsey Stone | publisher=The Guardian | date=21 February 2015 | accessdate=6 April 2015 | author=Ronson, Jon}}</ref>這導致朗森要求機器人的創造者將其移除，因為他認為這是一個{{link-en|垃圾訊息機器人|Spambot}}。該帳戶的創建者則稱其為「{{link-en|電子意識|infomorph}}」，並拒絕朗森的要求，但最終還是同意親自與作者見面。<ref name="GuardianReview">{{cite web | url=http://www.theguardian.com/books/2015/mar/05/so-youve-been-publicly-shamed-jon-ronson-review | title=So You've Been Publicly Shamed review – Jon Ronson on rants and tweets | publisher=The Guardian | date=5 March 2015 | accessdate=7 April 2015 | author=Poole, Steven}}</ref>

朗森其後將會面拍下並上載至YouTube<ref name="RonsonYoutube">{{cite web | url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mPUjvP-4Xaw | title=009  JON VS JON Part 2 Escape and Control | publisher=Escape and Control | date=22 March 2012 | accessdate=6 April 2015 | author=Ronson, Jon}}</ref>，出奇地其影片的回響絕大多數是站在他同一立場並有利於他。該垃圾訊息機器人的創造者，被朗森的影片所招來的怒眾群起侮辱後，終於同意停止假冒的Twitter帳戶。<ref name="WSJReview">{{cite web | url=http://www.wsj.com/articles/book-review-so-youve-been-publicly-shamed-by-jon-ronson-1427483570 | title=Smartphone Vigilantes | publisher=Wall Street Journal | date=27 March 2015 | accessdate=7 April 2015 | author=Hutson, Matthew | quote="[the book] raises interesting questions about righteousness, reputation and conformity [but Ronson's] thoughts remain disconnected musings rather than cohering as a calculus of public shaming's costs and benefits."}}</ref>

是次經驗導致朗森重新評估他參加其他的公共恥辱活動，並對這些羞辱事件對雙方，包括羞辱者和被羞者的影響。他開始訪問一些著名的，在互聯網上被公開羞辱的受害者和這些羞辱事件的煽動者。

[[File:Jonah_Lehrer_Knight_Foundation_speech_and_reaction.jpg|thumb]]

第一個主人翁是顏面盡失的[[流行科學|流行科學]]《[[紐約客|紐約客]]》記者和《{{link-en|試想一下：創意如何實現|Imagine: How Creativity Works}}》一書的作者[[喬納·雷爾|喬納·雷爾]]。朗森同時採訪了揭發雷爾抄襲和誤用名句的記者{{link-en|邁克爾·莫伊尼漢|Michael C. Moynihan}}。在雷爾於{{link-en|騎士基金會|John S. and James L. Knight Foundation}}<ref name="KnightFoundation" />研討會的直播道歉前幾天，朗森在{{link-en|魯尼恩峽谷公園|Runyon Canyon Park|魯尼恩峽谷}}邊遠足邊訪問了雷爾。雷爾的道歉講話附帶的當眾羞辱被他講台背後掛著的一個大型投影屏幕以及他看到的一個小電視惡化，當時兩個屏幕都實時播放着任何Twitter用戶使用「infoneeds」[[主題標籤|主題標籤]]的最新推文。<ref name="SlateJonahLehrer">{{cite web | url=http://www.slate.com/articles/arts/culturebox/2015/03/jon_ronson_on_jonah_lehrer_a_new_book_says_we_were_too_hard_on_the_disgraced.html | title=Were We Too Hard on Jonah Lehrer? | publisher=Slate | date=31 March 2015 | accessdate=7 April 2015 | author=Engber, Daniel}}</ref><ref name="KnightJonah">{{cite web | url=http://www.knightfoundation.org/blogs/knightblog/2013/2/12/first-public-comments-since-plagiarism-scandal-jonah-lehrer-blames-arrogance-need-for-attention/ | title=In first public comments since plagiarism scandal, Jonah Lehrer blames "arrogance, need for attention" for lies | publisher=The Knight Foundation | date=12 February 2013 | accessdate=7 April 2015 | author=Hu, Elise}}</ref>雷爾的演講內容釀成一個小爭議，部份人形容他傲慢和缺乏誠意。而採訪了雷爾的朗森，在研討會前曾被對方問及關於演講草稿的意見，他讀後感到不能服眾，但承認自己沒有告訴雷爾。當大眾發現基金會為雷爾的道歉講話支付了$20,000美金的演說費時，再次激發起社交媒體上的一陣騷動。<ref name="NewRepublicLehrer">{{cite web | url=http://www.newrepublic.com/article/112416/jonah-lehrers-20000-apology-wasnt-enough | title=The Real Trouble with Jonah Lehrer | publisher=The New Republic | date=14 February 2013 | accessdate=7 April 2015 | author=Chotiner, Isaac}}</ref>他也訪問了在聽到兩名科技開發者於研討會[[PyCon|PyCon]]談論術語「[[电子狗|dongle]]」和俚語「[[wikt:en:dong#Etymology_2|dong]]」相關的笑話後，公佈他們樣貌的亞德里亞·理查茲。<ref name="SlateReview">{{cite web | url=http://www.slate.com/articles/arts/books/2015/04/jon_ronson_s_so_you_ve_been_publicly_shamed_reviewed.html | title=After Outrage | publisher=Slate | date=6 April 2015 | accessdate=7 April 2015 | author=Silverman, Jacob}}</ref>

==評價==
《So You've Been Publicly Shamed》的評價大多是正面的。直至2016年4月29日，此書在[[Goodreads|Goodreads]]中共有16,830個評分及2,128個評論，平均得分為3.87/5。<ref name="Goodreads">{{cite web | url=https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/22571552-so-you-ve-been-publicly-shamed | title=So You've Been Publicly Shamed | publisher=Goodreads | accessdate=2016-04-29}}</ref>

《[[波士頓環球報|波士頓環球報]]》的詹妮弗·拉森表示：「朗森能同時保持學術性和娛樂性。」<ref name="BostonGlobeReview">{{cite web | url=http://www.bostonglobe.com/arts/books/2015/03/30/review-you-been-publicly-shamed-jon-ronson/x52WEAxjkhdzZNSCejcUJJ/story.html | title='So You've Been Publicly Shamed' by Jon Ronson | publisher=The Boston Globe | date=31 March 2015 | accessdate=7 April 2015 | author=Latson, Jennifer | quote="Ronson manages to be at once academic and entertaining."}}</ref>《[[華爾街日報|華爾街日報]]》的馬修·赫特森則表示此書「引起了人們對正義、信譽和遵從的有趣疑問」，但慨嘆朗森的「思想仍是斷斷續續的沉思，而不是理順公眾羞辱代價和效益的結石」。<ref name="WSJReview"/>
