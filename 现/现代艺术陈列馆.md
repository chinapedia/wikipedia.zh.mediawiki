'''现代艺术陈列馆'''（'''Pinakothek der Moderne'''）是德国慕尼黑的一个现代艺术博物馆，位于该市市中心，与[[老绘画陈列馆|老绘画陈列馆]]（Alte Pinakothek）、[[新绘画陈列馆|新绘画陈列馆]]（Neue Pinakothek）一起组成该市的[[艺术区|艺术区]]（Kunstareal）。

=== 现代艺术收藏 ===
<!-- 与其它城市相比，慕尼黑受到纳粹政权查禁现代艺术的影响较小 "[[degenerate_art|degenerate art]]," since only a few modern paintings were already collected by the "[[Hugo_von_Tschudi|Tschudi Contribution]]" in 1905/1914. Since 1945, however, the collection, previously exhibited in the [[Haus_der_Kunst|Haus der Kunst]], has grown quickly by purchase, as well as donations by individuals and several foundations. Various [[art_movement|art movement]]s of the 20th century are represented in the collection, including [[Expressionism|Expressionism]], [[Fauvism|Fauvism]], [[Cubism|Cubism]], [[New_Objectivity|New Objectivity]], [[Bauhaus|Bauhaus]], [[Surrealism|Surrealism]], [[Abstract_Expressionism|Abstract Expressionism]], [[Pop_Art|Pop Art]] and [[Minimal_Art|Minimal Art]].

=== 图案设计收藏 ===
