'''托波因特'''（Torpoint，{{lang-kw|Penntorr}}<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.magakernow.org.uk/pdf/placename_masterlist.pdf |title=List of Place-names agreed by the MAGA Signage Panel |accessdate=2015-01-11 |publisher=Cornish Language Partnership |date=May 2014 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140729194902/http://www.magakernow.org.uk/pdf/placename_masterlist.pdf |archivedate=2014-07-29 }}</ref>）是英格蘭的一個城鎮和民政教區，位於英格蘭西南部康沃爾<ref>Ordnance Survey: Landranger map sheet 201 ''Plymouth & Launceston'' ISBN 978-0-319-23146-3</ref>。2001年人口普查時，托波因特有人口8,457人，2011年減少至8,364人<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.genuki.org.uk/big/eng/Cornwall/Torpoint/index.html#Population|title=civil parish population 2011|accessdate=15 February 2015|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150210000359/http://www.genuki.org.uk/big/eng/Cornwall/Torpoint/index.html#Population|archivedate=2015年2月10日|df=}}</ref>。
