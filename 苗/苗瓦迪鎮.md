'''苗瓦迪鎮'''（{{lang-my|မြဝတီမြို့နယ်}}；{{lang-en|Myawaddy Township}}）為[[緬甸|緬甸]][[克倫邦|克倫邦]][[苗瓦迪縣|苗瓦迪縣]]的鎮區<ref name="MIMU001">[http://www.burmalibrary.org/docs6/MIMU001_A3_SD%20&%20Township%20Overview.pdf "Myanmar States/Divisions & Townships Overview Map"] Myanmar Information Management Unit (MIMU)</ref>，位於克倫邦東部。該鎮為苗瓦迪縣唯一的鎮區。西南接[[高加力縣|高加力縣]][[賈因色基鎮|賈因色基鎮]]；西鄰高加力縣[[高加力鎮|高加力鎮]]；北臨[[帕安縣|帕安縣]][[蘭貝鎮|蘭貝鎮]]；東鄰[[泰國|泰國]]<ref>[http://www.themimu.info/MapsInfo/Others%20Planning%20Maps/MIMU546v01_100923_States-Divisions%20and%20Townships%20Overview%20Map_A4.pdf "Myanmar States/Divisions and Townships &#151; Overview Map"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101117084320/http://www.themimu.info/MapsInfo/Others%20Planning%20Maps/MIMU546v01_100923_States-Divisions%20and%20Townships%20Overview%20Map_A4.pdf |date=2010-11-17 }} 23 September 2010., Myanmar Information Management Unit (MIMU)</ref>。2014年人口210,540人，區域面積3,140.5平方公里<ref name=citypopulation/>。該鎮轄下43個村莊。[[苗瓦迪|苗瓦迪]]為該鎮之首府。
