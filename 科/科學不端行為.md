'''科學不端行為'''指的是在進行[[科學|科學]]研究過程中違反基本[[學術|學術]][[倫理|倫理]]的行為。[[醫學|醫學]]期刊《[[刺胳針|刺胳針]]》的一篇文章《''對[[斯堪地納維亞|斯堪地納維亞]]國家中學術不端行為之處置''》對此詞彙定義如下：<ref>{{cite journal|title=Handling of scientific dishonesty in the Nordic countries. National Committees on Scientific Dishonesty in the Nordic Countries|pages=57–61|issn=0140-6736|journal=Lancet (London, England)|volume=354|issue=9172|date=1999-07-03|url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/10406378|accessdate=2019-02-13|author=M. Nylenna, D. Andersen, G. Dahlquist, M. Sarvas, A. Aakvaag|pmid=10406378}}</ref><ref><cite id="COPE1999PDF">{{cite journal|title=Coping with fraud |work=The COPE Report 1999 |pages=11–18 |url=http://www.publicationethics.org.uk/reports/1999/1999pdf3.pdf |format=PDF |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070928151119/http://www.publicationethics.org.uk/reports/1999/1999pdf3.pdf |quote=It is 10 years, to the month, since Stephen Lock ... Reproduced with kind permission of the Editor, The Lancet. |archivedate=2007-09-28 |accessdate=2006-09-02 |deadurl=yes }}</cite></ref>

*[[丹麥|丹麥]]定義："蓄意、惡意地產生假的科學資訊、博取虛謊的名聲和注意。"

*[[瑞典|瑞典]]定義："蓄意扭曲他人手稿、發表中的實驗數據、文字、假設或方法；或者其他一切扭曲實驗程序的手段。"

科學不端行為可能使得行為人<ref>{{cite web|last=Xie |first=Yun |url=http://arstechnica.com/science/2008/08/what-are-the-consequences-for-scientific-misconduct/ |title=What are the consequences of scientific misconduct? |doi=10.1126/science.1158052 |publisher=Ars Technica |date=2008-08-12 |accessdate=2013-03-01}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Scientific Misconduct: Do the Punishments Fit the Crime? |author=Redman and Berz |publisher=Sciencemag |volume=321 |url=http://astro.berkeley.edu/~kalas/ethics/documents/redan08.pdf |format=PDF}}</ref>與其他接觸到其行為結果之人蒙受損傷。<ref>{{cite web |publisher=Research Triangle Institute |title=Consequences of  Whistleblowing for the Whistleblower in Misconduct in Science Cases |year= 1995 |url=https://wiki.umn.edu/pub/IBS8099F10/WhistleBlowing/RTI_-_Consequences_of_Whistleblowing_report.pdf |format=PDF}}</ref>[[醫學|醫學]]上的科學不端行為將使大眾健康暴露在極大的風險中。
