'''科技園站'''（{{lang-rus|Технопарк|Tekhnopark}}）是[[莫斯科地鐵|莫斯科地鐵]][[莫斯科河畔線|莫斯科河畔線]]的一個車站。科技園站開通於2015年12月28日，是在既有的地鐵區間中間新設的車站。車站自2013年開始建設<ref name="BakuToday 12-2010">[http://www.bakutoday.net/in-january-2011-will-begin-construction-of-the-metro-station-technopark-moscow.html ''In January 2011, will begin construction of the Metro station “Technopark” (Moscow)''] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120407121429/http://www.bakutoday.net/in-january-2011-will-begin-construction-of-the-metro-station-technopark-moscow.html |date=2012-04-07 }}, BakuToday, December 20, 2010. Retrieved 2012-12-13.</ref>，但實際上在2012年就開始建設<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.themoscowtimes.com/realestate/analysis/article/office-real-estate-heads-west-northwest-south/437563.html | title=Office Real Estate Heads West, Northwest, South | publisher=[http://www.themoscowtimes.com ''The Moscow Times''] | date=2011-06-01 | accessdate=2011-12-14 | author=Luhn, Alec}}</ref>。
