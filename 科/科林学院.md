'''科林学院'''('''{{lang-en|Collin College}}''')，又名'''柯林郡社区学院'''('''Collin County Community College''')，<ref>"欢迎到柯林郡社区学院." ([https://archive.is/20070724185637/http://www.ccccd.edu/frameset.asp?link=http://www.ccccd.edu/chinese/simplified_chinese.html 简体字]，; [https://archive.is/20070816155943/http://www.ccccd.edu/frameset.asp?link=http://www.ccccd.edu/chinese/traditional_chinese.html 繁体字], ) 科林学院.</ref>是位于[[美国|美国]][[得克萨斯州|德州]][[科林县_(得克萨斯州)|科林县]]的一所[[社区学院|社区学院]]，其总部位于[[麦金尼_(德克萨斯州)|麦金尼]]的科林高等教育中心(CHEC)。<ref>"[http://www.collin.edu/chec/maps.html Maps/Visit Us]." ([http://archive.is/rSehx Archive]) 科林学院. Retrieved on July 17, 2013. "The Collin Higher Education Center (CHEC) opened for classes January 2010. Located in the northeast corner of the intersection of State Highway 121 and 75 Central Expressway, the CHEC is home to Collin College district offices and five university partners offering courses toward their baccalaureate and graduate degrees."</ref> 科林学院拥有多个校区，如位于麦金尼的中央公园校区、科林高等教育中心校区、[[普莱诺_(得克萨斯州)|普莱诺]]的Courtyard中心校区、{{link-en|佛里斯科 (得克萨斯州)|Frisco, Texas|佛里斯科}}的普雷斯顿岭校区、普莱诺的春溪校区、艾倫中心校区以及{{link-en|罗克沃尔 (得克萨斯州)|Rockwall, Texas|罗克沃尔}}中心校区。<ref>"[http://www.collin.edu/campuses/index.html Collin College Campuses]." 科林学院. Retrieved 2013-07-17. "Central Park Campus 2200 W. University Drive McKinney, TX 75071"</ref>
