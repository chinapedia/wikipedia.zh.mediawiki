'''科威特中央银行'''（{{lang-en|'''C'''entral '''B'''ank of '''K'''uwait}}，简称'''CBK'''），是[[科威特国|科威特国]]之[[中央银行|中央银行]]，其设立於1969年<ref name=jianlishijian/>，总部位于[[首都|首都]][[科威特市|科威特市]]<ref name=zongbudzhi/>。其不仅负责[[科威特第纳尔|科威特第纳尔]]的专权发行，还负责与{{link-en|科威特证券交易所|Kuwait Stock Exchange}}（'''KSE'''）、{{link-en|科威特工商部|Ministry of Commerce and Industry (Kuwait)}}、{{link-en|科威特财政部|Ministry of Finance (Kuwait)}}一齐负责监管[[证券市场|证券市场]]<ref>{{cite journal|coauthors=David Chappell|title=Modelling the day-of-the-week effect in the Kuwait Stock Exchange: a nonlinear GARCH representation|journal=Applied Financial Economics|year=2001|volume=11|issue=4|url=http://www.tandfonline.com/doi/pdf/10.1080/096031001300313910|accessdate=2013-12-30|author=Nabeel Al Loughani|doi=10.1080/096031001300313910}}</ref>。

== 历史 ==

{{参见|科威特历史}}

在1961年[[科威特|科威特]]宣布独立的前一年，科威特已经从[[英国|英国]]手里先后接管了[[司法权|司法权]]与货币管理权。同年[[科威特埃米尔|科威特埃米尔]]发布《1960年第41号埃米尔令》（Amiri Decree No. 41 of 1960），设立'''科威特货币委员会'''行使[[货币|货币]]发行职能。但是科威特货币委员会的职能仅限于发行[[纸币|纸币]]和[[硬币|硬币]]，不像真正的央行一样拥有广泛的职权。因此在[[科威特|科威特]]独立后，也伴随着国际经济环境的日益变化，到了1968年[[科威特埃米尔|科威特埃米尔]]又发布了《1968年第32号法案》<ref name="sanshierfaan">{{Cite web|url=http://new.cbk.gov.kw/en/images/CBK-Law-32-68-Ar-10-112899-3.pdf |title=《قانون رقم 23 لسنة 8691 يف شأن النقد وبنك الكويت املركزي وتنظيم املهنة املصرفية وتعديالته》 |accessdate=2014-11-28 |author=[[科威特埃米尔|科威特埃米尔]]发布生效 |last= |first= |authorlink= |coauthors= |date= |year=1968 |month= |trans_title=《1968年第32号法案》 |format=pdf |work= |publisher= |pages= |language=ar |doi= |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141128112417/http://new.cbk.gov.kw/en/images/CBK-Law-32-68-Ar-10-112899-3.pdf |archivedate=2014-11-28 |quote= |deadurl=yes }}</ref>，设立'''科威特中央银行'''取代原货币委员会，正式成为一个行使[[央行|央行]]职能的机构<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://new.cbk.gov.kw/en/about-cbk/mission.jsp |title=Mission & Objectives of CBK |accessdate=2014-11-28 |author= |last= |first= |authorlink= |coauthors= |date= |year= |month= |format= |work= |publisher=科威特中央银行官网 |pages= |language=en |doi= |archiveurl= |archivedate= |quote=The Central Bank of Kuwait (CBK) was established by virtue of the Law No. 32 of 1968 concerning Currency, the Central Bank of Kuwait and the Organization of Banking Business. CBK replaced the Kuwaiti Currency Board established by virtue of the Amiri Decree No. 41 of 1960. The role of the Kuwaiti Currency Board was limited to the issuance of banknotes and coins, unlike central banks' broad responsibilities ranging from setting and implementing monetary policies to the regulation and supervision of banking activities. CBK's establishment, hence, was in response of the need to keeping pace with domestic and international developments, particularly in view of the increasingly important role of the monetary and financial policy contributing to the advancement of social and economic development in the country. }}</ref>，同年4月1日，科威特中央银行开始运作<ref name=zhenzhengdeshijian/>。
<br>在2003年[[科威特|科威特]]为加强打击[[恐怖主义|恐怖主义]][[融资|融资]]的力度，在央行辖下设立了'''金融情报组'''（{{lang-en|Financial Intelligence Unit}},简称'''FIU'''）<ref name="jianlifiu">{{Cite web |url =https://cablegatesearch.wikileaks.org/cable.php?id=05KUWAIT806|format= |title = Synopsis Of Kuwait's Actions To Combat Terrorist Finance|author =  |date = 2005-02-23|publisher = [[维基解密|维基解密]]|language =en |accessdate = 2014-11-28|quote =In the wake of January's violent confrontations between extremists and Kuwaiti security forces, the Government of Kuwait has begun to view terrorist finance as an issue of real concern. ==职能==

'''科威特中央银行'''根据[[科威特国|科威特国]]《1968年第32号法案》（Law No. 32 of 1968）之规定<ref name=sanshierfaan/>，其职能包括<ref name="yanghangzhineng1">{{Cite web |url =http://cbk.gov.kw/WWW/index.html|format= |title =CBK's objectives|author =  |date = |publisher = 科威特中央银行官网 |language =en |accessdate = 2014-11-28|quote =CBK's objectives include: Issue the Kuwaiti Dinar on behalf of the State of Kuwait. Direct credit policy to assist social and economic progress. Assist the growth of the national income. Control the banking system in the country.CBK also acts as a banker and financial advisor to the government, while representatives from both the public and private sectors make up the Board of Directors.}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://new.cbk.gov.kw/en/about-cbk/mission.jsp |title=Mission & Objectives of CBK |accessdate=2014-11-28 |author= |last= |first= |authorlink= |coauthors= |date= |year= |month= |format= |work= |publisher=科威特中央银行官网 |pages= |language=en |doi= |archiveurl= |archivedate= |quote=CBK commenced operation on the 1st of April 1969, in fulfillment of the following core objectives stated in Article 15 of its Law No. 32 of 1968: issue the national currency on behalf of the State. maintain the relative stability of the Kuwaiti Dinar, and secure its free convertibility into foreign currencies. to direct the credit policy with the aim to contribute to the social and economic progress and enhance the national income. to supervise the State's banking system. to serve as a banker to the government. to serve as a financial adviser to the government. }}</ref>：
*发行国家[[货币|货币]][[科威特第纳尔|科威特第纳尔]]，以及代表[[科威特国|科威特国]]参与国际金融事务；
*维持科威特第纳尔之价值稳定，并确保其可以自由兑换；
*实施合宜的[[信贷政策|信贷政策]]，以促进社会经济之增长；
*促进[[国民收入|国民收入]]之增长；
*管控国家的银行体系；
*担任政府的经济事务顾问；
