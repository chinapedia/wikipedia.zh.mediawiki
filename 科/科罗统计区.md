'''科罗统计区'''([[斯洛文尼亚语|斯洛文尼亚语]]: Koroška statistična regija)<ref>Jeznik, Janja. 2015. ''Geoinfornacijska podpora umeščanju prometnic na izbranem primeru trase severnega odseka 3. razvojne osi'' (bachelor's thesis). Ljubljana: University of Ljubljana, Department of Geography, p. 47.</ref><ref>OECD. 2012. ''OECD Environmental Performance Reviews: Slovenia 2012''. OECD Publishing, p. 324.</ref><ref>Lapuh, Lucija. 2016. Measuring the Impact of the Recession on Slovenian Statistical Regions and their Ability to Recover. ''Acta geographica Slovenica'' 56(2): 247–256, pp. 252ff.</ref><ref>Šprah, Lilijana, Tatjana Novak, & Jerneja Fridl. 2016. The Wellbeing of Slovenia's Population by Region: Comparison of Indicators with an Emphasis on Health. ''Acta geographica Slovenica'' 54(1): 67–87, p. 68.</ref><ref>Boršič, Darja, & Alenka Kavkler. 2009. Modeling Unemployment Duration in Slovenia Using Cox Regression Models. ''Transition Studies Review'' 54(1): 145–156, p. 148.</ref>，是斯洛文尼亚的一个[[斯洛文尼亚统计区列表|统计区]]。该统计区位於斯洛維尼亞北部，與[[奧地利|奧地利]]接壤，最大的城市为[[斯洛文尼亞格拉代茨|斯洛文尼亞格拉代茨]]。2015年科罗统计区面積1,041平方公里，人口71,218人。
