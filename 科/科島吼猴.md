'''科島吼猴'''（''Alouatta coibensis''）是[[巴拿馬|巴拿馬]]特有的一種[[吼猴|吼猴]]。科島吼猴一般被認為是獨立的[[物種|物種]]，原因是牠們的手及腳上的皮紋與長毛吼猴的不同<ref name=pic>{{cite book en|title=The Pictorial Guide to the Living Primates|author=Rowe, N.|page=109|year=1996|isbn=0-9648825-0-7|publisher=Pogonias Press|location=East Hampton, N.Y.}}</ref>，但[[線粒體DNA|線粒體DNA]]測試卻不能確實牠們是否[[長毛吼猴|長毛吼猴]]的[[亞種|亞種]]。<ref name=perspectives>{{cite book en | author = Rylands, Groves, Mittermeier, Cortes-Ortiz & Hines | editor = Estrada, A.; Garber, P.A.; Pavelka, M.S.M.; Luecke, L. | chapter = Taxonomy and Distributions of Mesoamerican Primates | title = New Perspectives in the Study of Mesoamerican Primates | isbn=978-0-387-25854-6 | year = 2006| page = 53 |publisher = Springer}}</ref>

==亞種==
科島吼猴其下有兩個[[亞種|亞種]]：<ref name=msw3/>
* ''A. c. coibensis''
* ''A. c. trabeata''
