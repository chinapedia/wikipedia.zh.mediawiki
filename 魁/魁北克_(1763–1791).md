'''魁北克 (1763–1791)'''是加拿大历史上存在于1763年到1791年的[[英属北美|英属北美]]殖民地的名称。
==历史==
[[魁北克|魁北克]]（Québec）這個名稱源自於[[阿爾岡昆語族|阿爾岡昆語]]詞彙「'''kébec'''」，意指河川收窄之處，傳統上是指[[魁北克市|魁北克市]]的周圍地區，亦即[[聖羅倫斯河|聖羅倫斯河]]收窄至兩岸皆面臨懸崖的地域。早期該詞也有其他的拼法例如：''Québecq''（Levasseur, 1601）和''Kébec''（Lescarbot 1609）<ref>Afable, Patricia O. and Madison S. Beeler (1996). "Place Names". In "Languages", ed. Ives Goddard. Vol. 17 of ''Handbook of North American Indians'', ed. William C. Sturtevant. Washington, D.C.: Smithsonian Institution, p. 191.</ref>。法國探險家[[山姆·德·尚普蘭|尚普蘭]]在1608年選擇了''Québec''為法屬殖民地[[新法蘭西|新法蘭西]]行政中心的稱呼<ref>{{cite news| url = http://history.cbc.ca/history/?MIval=EpContent.html&series_id=1&episode_id=2&chapter_id=4&page_id=4&lang=E | title = Canada: A People's History – The birth of Quebec | publisher = Canadian Broadcast Corporation|year=2001 | accessdate =August 26, 2006}}</ref>。該省通常也有「美麗之省」("La belle province")的意涵。

[[七年戰爭|七年戰爭]]結束後，英法兩國於1763簽訂《[[巴黎和約_(1763年)|巴黎和約]]》，[[法蘭西殖民帝國|法國]]將[[法屬加拿大|法屬加拿大]]割讓予英國，英國並於同年公佈《[[1763年公告|1763年皇家宣言]]》，正式成立[[魁北克_(1763–1791)|魁北克省]]<ref>"his Most Christian Majesty cedes and guaranties to his said Britannick Majesty, in full right, Canada, with all its dependencies, as well as the island of Cape Breton, and all the other islands and coasts in the gulph and river of St. Lawrence, and in general, every thing that depends on the said countries, lands, islands, and coasts, with the sovereignty, property, possession, and all rights acquired by treaty, or otherwise, which the Most Christian King and the Crown of France have had till now over the said countries, lands, islands, places, coasts, and their inhabitants" – [http://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Treaty_of_Paris_%281763%29 ''Treaty of Paris''], 1763</ref>；這份諭令規定全省限縮於聖羅倫斯河的河畔。英國再於1774年通過《魁北克法案》，將魁北克省的範圍延伸至包括[[五大湖|五大湖]]、[[俄亥俄河|俄亥俄河]]河谷和魯珀特地區（Rupert's Land）的南部，大致恢復該省在法國管治時期的邊界<ref name="Geographers1968">{{cite book|author=Canadian Association of Geographers|title=Canada: a Geographical Interpretation|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=voYOAAAAQAAJ&pg=PA33|year=1968|publisher=Taylor & Francis|page=33|id=GGKEY:E1DDKEKZ35S}}</ref>。[[美國獨立戰爭|美國獨立戰爭]]結束後，英美在1783年簽訂的《[[巴黎條約_(1783年)|巴黎條約]]》約定把五大湖區以南的領地劃為美國所轄<ref name="PeirceCo1877">{{cite book|author1=Henry B. Peirce|author2=L.H. Everts & Co|title=History of Calhoun County, Michigan ... With illustrations descriptive of its scenery, palatial residences, public buildings ...|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=JeMbAQAAMAAJ&pg=PA10|year=1877|publisher=L. H. Everts co.|page=10}}</ref>。在1791年的《憲法法案》頒布後，魁北克省一分為二，分別為[[下加拿大|下加拿大]]（今日的[[魁北克|魁北克]]）和[[上加拿大|上加拿大]]（今日的[[安大略省|安大略省]]），這兩個領地都可以選舉出自己的議會<ref name="KelleyTrebilcock2010">{{cite book|author1=Ninette Kelley|author2=Michael J. Trebilcock|title=The Making of the Mosaic: A History of Canadian Immigration Policy|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=3IHyRvsCiKMC&pg=PA40|date=September 30, 2010|publisher=University of Toronto Press|isbn=978-0-8020-9536-7|page=40}}</ref>。1840年英國國會通過聯合法案，上加拿大改稱[[西加拿大|西加拿大]]，下加拿大改稱[[東加拿大|東加拿大]]，兩者並統編成[[加拿大省|加拿大聯合省]]<ref name="Johnston1881">{{cite book|author=Keith Johnston|title=A physical, historical, political, & descriptive geography|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=nE0pAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA98|year=1881|publisher=E. Stanford|page=98}}</ref>。
