'''杰瑞米·欧文'''(''Jeremy Irvine''，1990年7月1日—)，出生时名叫 ''Jeremy Smith''<ref name="bms">{{cite web|url=http://www.bedmod.co.uk/news_details.html?id=968|title=Former BMS student's role in Disney show|date=10 July 2009|accessdate=5 February 2010|publisher=[[Bedford_Modern_School|Bedford Modern School]]|work=News}}{{dead link|date=2018年3月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref name="bms-obm">{{cite web|url=http://www.bedmod.co.uk/news_details_archive.html?id=1195|title=OBM chosen for Steven Spielberg's new film|date=24 June 2010|accessdate=26 July 2010|publisher=Bedford Modern School|work=News|quote=Jeremy Irvine (known as Jeremy Smith at BMS) .. attended Bedford Modern School between 2000 and 2008}}{{dead link|date=2018年3月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>，是一名英国男演员。

==简介==
他六岁时患上了[[1型糖尿病|1型糖尿病]]<ref name="drn-nhs">{{cite web|url=http://www.diabetesresearchnetworking.org/showvideo.php?tab=&section=4&page=32&video=2|format=Video interview|title=Jeremy Smith|work=Patient interviews|publisher=Diabetes Research Networking; [[National_Health_Service|National Health Service]]|accessdate=5 February 2010|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110725232912/http://www.diabetesresearchnetworking.org/showvideo.php?tab=&section=4&page=32&video=2|archivedate=2011年7月25日}}</ref>。

他先后在[[:en:Bedford_Modern_School|贝德福德现代学校]]<ref name="bms"/><ref name="bms-obm"/><ref name="biggleswade2">{{cite news|url=http://www.biggleswadetoday.co.uk/545/Disney-calls-for-Jeremy.5446297.jp|title=Disney calls for Jeremy|work=Biggleswade Chronicle|date=9 July 2009|accessdate=5 February 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.bedfordtoday.co.uk/bed-news/Hollywood-beckons-for-former-Bedford.6374950.jp|title=Hollywood beckons for former Bedford schoolboy|work=Bedford Today|date=21 June 2010|accessdate=26 July 2010}}</ref>、[[:en:National_Youth_Theatre|国家青年剧院]]和[[:en:London_Academy_of_Music_and_Dramatic_Art|伦敦音乐与表演艺术学院]]<ref name="biggleswade1">{{cite news|url=http://www.bedmod.co.uk/news_details_archive.html?id=968|title=Jeremy lands TV job with Disney|work=Biggleswade Chronicle|date=3 June 2009|accessdate=5 February 2010|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110725201750/http://www.bedmod.co.uk/news_details_archive.html?id=968|archivedate=2011年7月25日}}</ref><ref name="dsm">{{cite news|url=http://www.thedramastudent.co.uk/2010/01/the-first-word-jeremy-irvines-going-it-alone/|title=The First Word: Jeremy Irvine’s Going It Alone|work=The Drama Student Magazine|issue=5|date=23 January 2010|accessdate=5 February 2010|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100128111105/http://www.thedramastudent.co.uk/2010/01/the-first-word-jeremy-irvines-going-it-alone/|archivedate=2010年1月28日}}</ref>就读学习。

2010年6月，他主演[[史蒂芬·斯皮尔伯格|史蒂芬·斯皮尔伯格]]执导的[[一战|一战]]电影《[[战马_(电影)|战马]]》<ref name="mcnary">{{cite news|url=http://www.variety.com/article/VR1118020757.html|title=Jeremy Irvine is Spielberg's 'War Horse'|work=[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]]|first=Dave|last=McNary|date=17 June 2010|accessdate=17 June 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/film/2010/aug/09/steven-spielberg-war-horse|title=Steven Spielberg skips Clinton wedding to meet Dartmoor pigs|work=The Guardian|first=Xan|last=Brooks|date=9 August 2010|accessdate=13 August 2010}}</ref>。2013年，他主演[[二戰|二戰]]電影: The Railway Man。
