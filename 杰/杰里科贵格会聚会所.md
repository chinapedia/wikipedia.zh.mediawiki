'''杰里科贵格会聚会所'''（Jericho Friends Meeting House Complex）是一个历史性的[[贵格会|贵格会]]聚会所，位于美国纽约州[[拿骚县_(纽约州)|拿骚县]]杰里科的旧杰里科收费公路6号。建筑群包括聚会所（1788年），前贵格会学校（1793年）、贵格会墓地等。聚会所为两层木建筑<ref name="NysNrhpNom">{{cite web | url = https://cris.parks.ny.gov/ | title = Cultural Resource Information System (CRIS)| publisher = New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation| format = Searchable database| accessdate = 2016-04-01}} ''Note:'' This includes {{cite web | url = https://cris.parks.ny.gov/Uploads/ViewDoc.aspx?mode=A&id=21888&q=false | title = National Register of Historic Places Registration Form: Jericho Friends Meeting House Complex | accessdate = 2016-04-01 | author = James Warren| format = PDF| date=January 2002}} and [https://cris.parks.ny.gov/Uploads/ViewDoc.aspx?mode=A&id=21889&q=false ''Accompanying nine photographs'']</ref>。

杰里科贵格会聚会所在2002年被列入[[国家史迹名录|国家史迹名录]]。<ref name="nris"/>
