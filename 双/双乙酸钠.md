'''双乙酸钠'''是一种化学式为{{chem|NaH({{chem|C|2|H|3|O|2}})|2}}的化合物，通常用做调味剂以及[[抗微生物剂|抗微生物剂]]。

==製備及結構==
双乙酸钠可以將乙酸钠進行半中和，再將溶液蒸發而得。可以視為是一種在濃縮乙酸溶液中增強酸性的{{link-en|同型缔合|homoassociation}}的結果：
: 2 CH<sub>3</sub>CO<sub>2</sub>H  +  NaOH   →   Na<sup>+</sup>[(CH<sub>3</sub>CO<sub>2</sub>)<sub>2</sub>H]<sup>-</sup>  +  H<sub>2</sub>O

双乙酸钠可以描述成是有[[氫鍵|氫鍵]]的陰離子(CH<sub>3</sub>CO<sub>2</sub>)2H<sup>-</sup>的鈉鹽。O---O的距離約2.47 Å.<ref>Barrow, Michael J.; Currie, Murdoch; Muir, Kenneth W.; Speakman, J. Clare; White, David N, J. "Crystal structures of some acid salts of monobasic acids.  XVII.  Structure of sodium hydrogen diacetate, redetermined by neutron diffraction" Journal of the Chemical Society, Perkin Transactions 2:  Physical Organic Chemistry 1975, pp. 15-18. {{DOI|10.1039/P29750000015}}</ref><!--The species has no significant existence in solution but forms stable crystals.-->

==應用==
双乙酸钠也是一種食品添加劑<ref>Peter J. Taormina "Implications of Salt and Sodium Reduction on Microbial Food Safety" in Critical
Reviews in Food Science and Nutrition, 2010, vol. 50, 209-227.  {{DOI|10.1080/10408391003626207}}</ref>，其[[E编码|E编码]]為E262，可以提供鹹味及醋的口味。
