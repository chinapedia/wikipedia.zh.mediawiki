'''硫酸铀酰钾'''是一种无机化合物，化学式为K<sub>2</sub>UO<sub>2</sub>(SO<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub>。极易溶于水。<ref name=crc />

K<sub>2</sub>UO<sub>2</sub>(SO<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub>·2H<sub>2</sub>O在自然界中以Geschieberite矿（[[捷克|捷克]]境内）的形式存在。<ref>J. Plášil, J. Hloušek, A.V. Kasatkin. Geschieberite, K<sub>2</sub>(UO<sub>2</sub>)(SO<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub>(H<sub>2</sub>O)<sub>2</sub>, a new uranyl sulfate mineral from Jáchymov. Mineralogical Magazine, 2015, 79(1):205-216</ref>

==制备==
该化合物可从[[硫酸钾|硫酸钾]]和[[硫酸铀酰|硫酸铀酰]]的混合溶液中，在室温结晶得到。<ref>L+ Niinistö, J Toivonen, J Valkonen. [http://actachemscand.org/pdf/acta_vol_33a_p0621-0624.pdf ChemInform Abstract: URANYL(VI) COMPOUNDS. II. THE CRYSTAL STRUCTURE OF POTASSIUM URANYL SULFATE DIHYDRATE, K<sub>2</sub>UO<sub>2</sub>(SO<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub>·2H<sub>2</sub>O]. Chemischer Informationsdienst, 1979, 33(10):621-624</ref>
