'''荒唐好萊塢'''(Episodes)為英國/美國合作的電視[[情境喜劇|情境喜劇]]由[[David_Crane_(writer/producer)|David Crane]]和[[Jeffrey_Klarik|Jeffrey Klarik]]編劇並由[[Hat_Trick_Productions|Hat Trick Productions]]製作。<ref name="bbcprdec">{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/pressoffice/pressreleases/stories/2010/12_december/17/episodes.shtml |title=Press Packs: Episodes |publisher=BBC |date=December 17, 2010 |accessdate=December 19, 2010}}</ref> 在2011年1月9日晚上9:30於[[Showtime_(TV_network)|Showtime]]電視台首播。<ref>[http://thefutoncritic.com/news/2010/09/22/biggest-sunday-ever-on-showtime-shameless-episodes-and-californication-to-premiere-january-9th-37549/20100922showtime02/ "Biggest Sunday Ever on Showtime! Shameless, Episodes & Californication to Premiere January 9th"], Showtime [[press_release|press release]], September 22, 2010, via FutonCritic.com. Retrieved November 3, 2010.</ref> 並在2011年1月10日22時在[[BBC2台|BBC2台]]首播。<ref name="bbcprdec">{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/pressoffice/pressreleases/stories/2010/12_december/17/episodes.shtml |title=Press Packs: Episodes |publisher=BBC |date=December 17, 2010 |accessdate=December 19, 2010}}</ref> 本劇描述英國的一對情境喜劇編劇夫妻搬到[[好萊塢|好萊塢]]重新創作他們在英國受歡迎的喜劇，結果卻引起災難性的後果。Showtime電視台和英國BBC電視台已經續約第二季。<ref>{{cite web|title=BBC Two orders more Episodes|url=http://www.atvtoday.co.uk/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=838:bbc-two-orders-more-episodes&catid=1:tv-media&Itemid=3|publisher=www.atvtoday.co.uk|accessdate=27 February 2011}}</ref><ref>[http://www.comedy.co.uk/news/story/00000478/episodes_tamsin_greig_stephen_mangan_series_2/ Second series of 9 episodes]</ref>本劇已經在全世界186個國家播送。<ref name="international">{{cite web|url=http://www.digitalspy.co.uk/broadcasting/news/a304146/episodes-sold-to-186-countries.html?rss |title='Episodes' sold to 186 countries |publisher=Digital Spy |date=February 16, 2011 |accessdate=March 2, 2011}}</ref>2015年6月,Showtime宣布續訂第五季,將於16年於倫敦拍攝,全季共九集,據IMDb公佈第五季全劇會在2016年播畢。

== 劇情概要 ==
{{main|荒唐好萊塢各集列表}}
當這一對編劇夫妻尚恩(Sean)和貝弗莉(Beverly)因為他們成功的英國喜劇''Lyman's Boys''又拿下另一座[[British_Academy_Television_Awards|英國電影和電視藝術學院]]電視喜劇獎，他們受邀到好萊塢去將這部戲翻拍成美國版。他們被迫遇到男主角[[麥特·勒布郎|麥特·勒布郎]]，麥特不但摧毀他們的聲譽還有節目，甚至連婚姻都受到影響。<ref name="bbcprdec">{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/pressoffice/pressreleases/stories/2010/12_december/17/episodes.shtml |title=Press Packs: Episodes |publisher=BBC |date=December 17, 2010 |accessdate=December 19, 2010}}</ref>

== 製作過程 ==
2010年5月,[[BBC|BBC]]電視台宣布訂購七集並且開始拍攝中，由[[James_Griffiths_(director)|James Griffiths]]導演。<ref name="BBCMay13">{{cite press release|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/pressoffice/pressreleases/stories/2010/05_may/13/episodes.shtml |title=Matt LeBlanc, Stephen Mangan and Tamsin Greig star in David Crane and Jeffrey Klarik's Episodes |publisher=[[BBC|BBC]]|date= May 13, 2010}}</ref> 將由[[Stephen_Mangan|Stephen Mangan]]和[[Tamsin_Greig|Tamsin Greig]]分別扮演尚恩和贝弗莉的角色。這兩位之前曾經在英國影集[[Green_Wing|Green Wing]](2004–2007)合作過。貝弗莉這個角色原先預定由演員[[Claire_Forlani|Claire Forlani]]擔任，但是最後由於本劇重新製作的緣故，最終在2010年4月換角。<ref name="Wightman">{{cite web|author=Wightman, Catriona |url=http://www.digitalspy.co.uk/ustv/news/a217351/church-forlani-quit-matt-leblanc-show.html |title=Church, Forlani quit Matt LeBlanc show|publisher=Digitalspy.co.uk |date=April 29, 2010 |accessdate=2011-01-10}}</ref> 勒布郎是飾演一個更自命不凡的他自己，同時命名為麥特·勒布郎。<ref name="BBCMay13"/> [[Thomas_Haden_Church|Thomas Haden Church]] 原先接演本部戲的電視製作人馬克的角色，最終因檔期時間問題並未參與。<ref name="Wightman"/> 最後由[[John_Pankow|John Pankow]]接演。雖然本部戲設定在洛杉磯，但是大部分的場景都在英國拍攝，包括林肯夫妻住的700萬美金的豪宅，偶爾再穿插一些洛杉磯的影像。<ref>"[http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/entertainment-arts-12127298 LeBlanc's 'brave' move on to British TV]", ''BBC News'', 10 January 2011</ref>
