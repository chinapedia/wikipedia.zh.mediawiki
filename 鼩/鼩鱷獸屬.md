'''鼩鱷獸屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Galesuchus''）是[[獸孔目|獸孔目]][[麗齒獸亞目|麗齒獸亞目]]的一屬，化石發現於[[非洲|非洲]]<ref>''The Mammal-like Reptiles of South Africa and the Origin of Mammals'' pages 105 and 110. By Robert Broom; published Published 1932, H. F. & G. Witherby. Retrieved on June 22nd, 2008.</ref>的[[二疊紀|二疊紀]]中期地層<ref>''The New Encyclopaedia Britannica'', Page 386. By Encyclopaedia Britannica, Inc; published 1983. Retrieved on June 22nd, 2008.</ref>。鼩鱷獸具有小型頭顱骨、短口鼻部<ref>''Palaeontologia Africana'', Page 113. By the Bernard Price Institute for Palaeontological Research; published 1953. Retrieved on June 22nd, 2008.</ref>。
