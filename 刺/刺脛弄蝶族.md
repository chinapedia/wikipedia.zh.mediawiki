== 種系發生 ==
刺脛弄蝶族起初被分類為弄蝶科中的一個亞科<ref>Doherty W. A list of butterflies taken in Kumaon. J Asiatic Soc Beng. 1886; 55: 103–140.</ref><ref>Bell TR. The common butterflies of the plains of India (including those met with in the hill stations of the Bombay presidency). J Bomb Nat Hist Soc. 1920–1927</ref>，其後又分類作'''吉弄蝶類'''（Gegenes group）<ref>Evans WH, A Catalogue of the Hesperiidae from Europe, Asia, and Australia in the British Museum (Natural History). London: The British Museum; 1949.</ref>、'''穀弄蝶類'''（Pelopidas group）<ref>Eliot JN. [descriptions and revisions] In: Corbet AS, Pendlebury HM. The Butterflies of the Malay Peninsula, 3rdedn. Kuala Lumpur: Malayan Nature Society; 1978.</ref><ref>Eloit JN. [descriptions and revisions] In: Corbet A.S., Pendlebury H.M. The Butterflies of the Malay Peninsula, 4rdedn. Kuala Lumpur: Malayan Nature Society; 1992.</ref>和'''吉弄蝶族'''（Gegenini）<ref>Chou I. (Ed.) Monographia Rhopalocerorum Sinensium. Henan, China: Henan Scientific and Technological Publishing House; 1994.</ref><ref>Chou I. Classification and Identification of Chinese Butterflies. Henan, China: Henan Scientific and Technological Publishing House; 1998.</ref>，然而[[國際動物命名法委員會|國際動物命名法委員會]]認為刺脛弄蝶族是更合適的命名<ref>International Commission on Zoological Nomenclature (ICZN). International Code of Zoological Nomenclature, 4th edn. London: The International Trust for Zoological Nomenclature; 1999.</ref>，因而分類出此族。

== 型態 ==
翅膀黑褐色，前翅通常有白色透明白斑。後翅腹面黃色，有白色小斑。前翅頂角及後翅臀角突出，後翅後緣稍有瓣。腹部與後翅後緣長略相等。前翅中室較長，上端角尖，M<sub>2</sub>脈從近翅基部分出，2A脈稍彎曲。後翅中室下角在Cu<sub>2</sub>脈處向下彎曲，Rs脈比Cu<sub>2</sub>脈先分出。雄蝶有第二性徵<ref name=z2015/>。
