'''卡门·萨帕塔'''（{{lang-en|Carmen Margarita Zapata}}，{{bd|1927年|7月15日|2014年|1月5日}}），是一名美国电影演员。

萨帕塔出生于纽约市，父亲为墨西哥人，母亲为阿根廷人<ref>''The Sacramento Bee'', "Playing Against Type Long Cast In Stereotypical Mexican Roles, Carmen Zapata Has Found A Part That Fills Her Soul In Stc'S "Driving Miss Daisy"(September 11, 1990,  Page E1); "She had been christened so in New York City daughter of a Mexican father and an Argentine mother"</ref>。她曾经出演上百部电影和电视剧，包括有《[[蝙蝠侠_(动画影集)|蝙蝠侠 (动画影集)]]》、《[[拖家带口|拖家带口]]》、《[[修女也疯狂|修女也疯狂]]》和《{{link-en|圣巴巴拉 (电视剧)|Santa Barbara (TV series)}}》。她演艺周期最长的节目是《{{link-en|阿雷格尔 (电视剧)|Villa Alegre (TV series)}}》，她在九年中担任其中的主要角色。<ref name="watertown">Lowe Kilmer. "Celebrities" (column), ''Watertown Daily Times'' (New York), October 7, 2003, Lifestyles & Leisure section, page B2.</ref><ref name="biodict">
Matt S. Meier, Conchita Franco Serri, and Richard A. Garcia. ''Notable Latino Americans: A Biographical Dictionary'', Westport, Conn. Greenwood Press, 1997, page 410: ''"Most important, in her role of Doña Luz she was, for nine years, the heart and soul of the Public Broadcasting System's bilingual program, 'Villa Alegre.'"''</ref>。

1972年，卡门·萨帕塔和演员[[里卡多·蒙特尔班|里卡多·蒙特尔班]]、{{link-en|伊迪丝·迪亚兹|Edith Diaz}}、{{link-en|亨利·达罗|Henry Darrow}}一同创建{{link-en|美国演员工会|Screen Actors Guild}}少数民族委员会<ref name=thr>{{cite news|first=|last=|title=Actress Edith Diaz dies at 70; Credits include 'Sister Act' films and CBS' 'Popi' sitcom |url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/hr/content_display/news/e3ib96053a9e47796d757c159ec4834e12a |work=[[Hollywood_Reporter|Hollywood Reporter]]|publisher=|date=2010-02-08 |accessdate=2010-03-18}}</ref>。2014年1月5日，卡门·萨帕塔因心脏病去世。
