'''卡罗维发利'''（{{lang-cs|'''Karlovy Vary'''}}；{{lang-de|'''Karlsbad'''}}，曾经以'''卡尔斯巴德'''著称）是[[捷克|捷克]]西部[[波希米亚|波希米亚]]地区[[卡羅維發利州|卡羅維發利州]]的一座[[温泉|温泉]]城市，位于奥赫热河（德语为Eger）和[[Teplá_(river)|Teplá]]2条河流的汇合处。该市名称Karlsbad得名于[[神圣罗马帝国|神圣罗马帝国]]皇帝、[[波西米亚|波西米亚]]国王[[卡尔四世|卡尔四世]]，他在1370年建立城市。该市在历史上以[[温泉|温泉]]著称（13个主要温泉，大约300个较小温泉，以及 热水的Teplá 河；Karlsbad中-bad字根就是德语里温泉的意思。

在19世纪，卡罗维发利成为一个受欢迎的旅游胜地，尤其是国际名人来此进行温泉治疗。该市还以[[卡罗维发利国际电影节|卡罗维发利国际电影节]]和流行的捷克[[liqueur|liqueur]] ''[[Becherovka|Karlovarská Becherovka]]''著称.玻璃制造厂商莫瑟玻璃，位于卡罗维发利。

== 历史 ==

<!-- On 14 August [[1370|1370]], 神圣罗马帝国皇帝[[查理四世|查理四世]] gave city privileges to the place that subsequently was named after him, according to legend after he had acclaimed the healing power of the hot springs. However, earlier settlements could be found in the outskirts of today's city. 

Due to publications by doctors like David Becher and Josef von Löschner, the city developed into a famous spa resort and was visited by many members of European aristocracy. It became popular after the railway lines to [[Cheb|Eger]] (Cheb) and [[Prague|Prague]] were completed in [[1870|1870]]. 

The number of visitors rose from 134 families in the 1756 season to 26,000 guests annually at the end of the 19th century. By 1911 that figure had reached already 71,000 but the [[World_War_I|Great War]] put an end to tourism and also led to the collapse of the [[Austro_Hungarian_Empire|Austro Hungarian Empire]] by late 1918. 

The German-speaking majority protested against being made a part of [[捷克斯洛伐克|捷克斯洛伐克]] by the [[Treaty_of_Saint_Germain|Treaty of Saint Germain]]. A demonstration on 4 March 1919 passed peacefully, but later that month six demonstrators were killed by Czech troops. 

In 1938, the [[Sudetenland|Sudetenland]] – including Carlsbad – became part of [[Germany|Germany]]. Until their [[expulsion_of_Germans_after_World_War_II|expulsion]] in 1945 the majority of the population of the city spoke [[德语|德语]].

Before that, the [[Carlsbad_Decrees|Carlsbad Decrees]] of 1819 had associated the city with anti-liberal censorship within [[German_Confederation|German Confederation]]. -->
