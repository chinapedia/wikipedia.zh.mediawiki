'''卡热疆峰'''是一座位于[[西藏|西藏]]的山峰，毗鄰[[中国|中国]]與[[不丹|不丹]]接壤的邊境，距離[[庫拉岡日峰|庫拉岡日峰]]约4千米，屬於[[喜馬拉雅山脈|喜馬拉雅山脈]]的一部分。卡热疆群峰的最高峰为卡热疆I峰或卡热疆南峰，海拔高度7,221米，<ref name="Ramesh2008"/>至今还未有人登顶。<ref name="BMC_2010"/>其余主要山峰包括卡热疆北峰（7096米），卡热疆II峰（中峰）（7045米），卡热疆III峰（或塔普托岗日峰）（6820米）和东北山肩的顶部（6400米）。

== 攀登尝试 ==
1986年，一支日本探险队成功登上了卡热疆II峰。<ref name="The Himalayan Club Newsletter">{{cite book | title=The Himalayan Club Newsletter | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=2mETAQAAIAAJ | accessdate=26 January 2013 | year=1985 | publisher=Himalayan Club | page = 27 }}</ref><ref name="AAJ2002">{{cite journal | journal = [[American_Alpine_Journal|American Alpine Journal]] | editor=John Harlin | title = Climbs and Expeditions: Tibet - Karjiang attempt, Taptol Kangri and Kangmi Kangri first ascents | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=E50bIVG3Q5QC&pg=PA433 | accessdate=2012-01-26 | date=2002-09-01 | publisher=[[American_Alpine_Club|American Alpine Club]] | isbn=978-0-930410-91-9 | pages=433–434}}</ref>

卡热疆I峰仍为未登峰。一支荷兰登山队于2001年9月至10月间尝试攀登此峰，但未成功。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.pionieren.org/index.php?id=171 | title = Nederlandse Karjiang expeditie 2001 | accessdate = 2012-01-16 | publisher = Pionieren.org }}</ref> 该登山队随后改变目标，登上了卡热疆III峰。根据该队队员的说法，卡热疆I峰整体极为陡峭，攀登困难，而当时恶劣的天气使得攀登行动更加危险。<ref name="AAJ2002"/>

2010年， 美国登山者Joe Puryear和David Gottlieb获得攀登卡热疆I峰的希普顿.提尔曼奖，但是未能取得攀登许可。两人随后尝试攀登了位于该峰以西420千米处的[[拉布吉康峰|拉布吉康峰]]，未能成功，且Joe Puryear因滑坠遇难。<ref name="BMC_2010">{{cite web | url = http://www.thebmc.co.uk/noted-american-killed-in-tibet | title = Noted American killed in Tibet | date = 2010-10-28 | author = Lindsay Griffin | publisher = British Mountaineering Council (BMC) | accessdate = 2012-01-26 }}</ref>
