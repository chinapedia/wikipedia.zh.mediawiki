'''卡林頓'''（{{lang-en|Callington}}，{{lang-kw|Kelliwik}}<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.magakernow.org.uk/pdf/placename_masterlist.pdf |title=List of Place-names agreed by the MAGA Signage Panel |accessdate=2015-01-11 |publisher=Cornish Language Partnership |date=May 2014 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140729194902/http://www.magakernow.org.uk/pdf/placename_masterlist.pdf |archivedate=2014-07-29 }}</ref>）是[[英國|英國]][[英格蘭|英格蘭]][[康沃爾郡|康沃爾郡]]的一個城鎮和民政教區，位於康沃爾東南部<ref>Ordnance Survey: Landranger map sheet 201 ''Plymouth & Launceston'' ISBN 978-0-319-23146-3</ref>。2001年，卡林頓有人口4,783人。2011年略微減少4,698人<ref> 2011 Census for Callington ward[http://www.ukcensusdata.com/callington/e05008207]</ref>。
