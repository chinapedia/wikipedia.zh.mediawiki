'''卡伦·阿斯里安'''（{{lang-hy|Կարեն Ասրյան}}，{{bd|1980年|4月24日|2008年|6月9日}}），[[亚美尼亚|亚美尼亚]][[国际象棋|国际象棋]]棋手，[[国际象棋特级大师|国际象棋特级大师]]。

阿斯里安曾于1999年<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chesscenter.com/twic/twic260.html#4|title=The Week in Chess 260: 59th Armenian Chess Championship|last=Crowther|first=Mark|date=1 November 1999|publisher=London Chess Center|accessdate=1 August 2009}}</ref>、2007年<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chesscenter.com/twic/twic638.html#4|title=The Week in Chess 638: 67th Armenian Chess Championship|last=Crowther|first=Mark|date=29 January 2007|publisher=London Chess Center|accessdate=1 August 2009}}</ref>、2008年<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chesscenter.com/twic/twic690.html#4|title=The Week in Chess 690: 68th Armenian Chess Championship|last=Crowther|first=Mark|date=28 January 2008|publisher=London Chess Center|accessdate=1 August 2009}}</ref>三度获得亚美尼亚国际象棋冠军。2008年6月9日，他在驾车途中因心脏病发而去世，年仅28岁。<ref>{{cite web|author=|title=Grandmaster, Armenian chess champion Asrian dies at 28|publisher=[[MSNBC|MSNBC]]|url=http://www.msnbc.msn.com/id/25061254/|date=9 June 2008|accessdate=9 June 2008 |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20080613032440/http://www.msnbc.msn.com/id/25061254/ <!-- Bot retrieved archive --> |archivedate = 13 June 2008}}</ref>
