'''卡爾森·伊莉莎白·楊'''（{{lang-en|Carlson Elizabeth Young}}，{{bd|1990年|10月29日|||catIdx=L}}）是一名誕生於[[德克薩斯州|德克薩斯州]][[沃斯堡|沃斯堡]]的[[美國|美國]]女演員。她最知名的作品是電影《[[天天初體驗|天天初體驗]]》以及[[MTV|MTV]]所出品的《[[驚聲尖叫_(電視劇)|驚聲尖叫]]》，在劇中飾演主要角色布魯克·梅道斯。<ref>{{cite news |first=Lesley|last=Goldberg |url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/mtvs-scream-adaptation-sets-cast-723337 |title=MTV's 'Scream' Adaptation Sets Cast, Director |newspaper=[[The_Hollywood_Reporter|The Hollywood Reporter]] |date=August 5, 2014 |accessdate=2015-07-24}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |first=Whitney|last=Friedlander |url=http://variety.com/2014/tv/news/mtv-orders-scream-tv-show-1201341402/ |title=MTV Orders ‘Scream’ TV Show to Series |magazine=[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]] |date=October 28, 2014 |accessdate=2015-07-24}}</ref>

==個人生活== 
楊目前定居於[[洛杉磯|洛杉磯]]，並半工半讀在[[南加州大學|南加州大學]]攻讀創意寫作。

楊於2015年與[[擁抱人群樂團|擁抱人群樂團]]成員伊索·因尼斯訂婚。<ref name=PeopleMag>{{cite web |url=http://www.people.com/article/carlson-young-planning-wedding-isom-innis |title=Scream's Carlson Young Dishes on Planning Her Wedding with Foster the People's Isom Innis |first=Patrick|last=Gomez |date=August 16, 2016 |work=[[People_(magazine)|People]] |accessdate=September 28, 2016}}</ref>
因尼斯和楊在[[冰島|冰島]]的一座山上被救援後向楊求婚，兩人預計於2017年4月舉行婚禮<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.flavourmag.co.uk/5-minutes-tv-screams-carlson-young/|title=Interview: 5 Minutes with TV Scream’s Carlson Young|last=Phelan|first=Zehra|date=July 1, 2016|website=FlavourMag|publisher=|access-date=2016-11-08}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://lover.ly/planning/celebrity-buzz/carlson-young-scream-engagement/|title=SCREAM’s Carlson Young Dishes on Her Engagement, Her DIY Nightmares and Which of Her Cast Mates Is Total MOH Material|last=Halibey|first=Danielle|date=|work=Loverly|publisher=|access-date=2016-11-08}}</ref>

楊與因尼斯於2017年4月29日在[[德州|德州]][[沃斯堡|沃斯堡]]結婚。<ref name="wedding">{{Cite web|url=http://people.com/celebrity/arlson-young-foster-the-people-isom-innis-married/|title=Scream's Carlson Young and Foster the People's Isom Innis Are Married!|last=Dugan|first=Christina|date=April 29, 2017|work=[[People_(magazine)|People]]|access-date=2017-04-29}}</ref>
