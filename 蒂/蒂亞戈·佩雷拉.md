'''蒂亞戈·佩雷拉'''（{{lang-en|Thiago Pereira}}；{{bd|1986年|1月26日}}）是一位[[巴西|巴西]]游泳運動員。他是巴西歷史上最偉大游泳運動員之一。在2012年倫敦奧運會男子400米混合泳的比賽中，他獲得了銀牌的成績<ref>{{cite web | title = Thiago is silver in the 400-meter individual medley, Lochte wins, and Phelps is fourth | work = Globoesporte | date = July 28, 2012 | url = http://globoesporte.globo.com/olimpiadas/noticia/2012/07/thiago-e-prata-nos-400m-medley-lochte-vence-e-phelps-e-quarto.html|accessdate =May 19, 2013|language=pt}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | title = Thiago Pereira beats Phelps, wins his 1st Olympic medal and is silver in the 400-meter individual medley | work = UOL | date = 2012-07-28 | url = http://olimpiadas.uol.com.br/noticias/redacao/2012/07/28/thiago-pereira-conquista-1-medalha-olimpica-e-e-prata-nos-400-m-medley.htm|accessdate =2013-05-19|language=pt}}</ref>。
