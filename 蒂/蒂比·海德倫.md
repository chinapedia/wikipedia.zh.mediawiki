'''蒂比·海德倫'''（{{lang-en|Tippi Hedren}}，{{bd|1930年|1月29日||}}），美國演員。

==生平==
蒂比·海德倫出生在[[美國|美國]][[明尼蘇達州|明尼蘇達州]][[新鳥姆|新鳥姆]]（New Ulm），可能是在1935年<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.thebiographychannel.co.uk/biographies/tippi-hedren.html |title=Tippi Hedren - Biography on Bio |publisher=Thebiographychannel.co.uk |accessdate=2012-10-20 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131007104953/http://www.thebiographychannel.co.uk/biographies/tippi-hedren.html |archivedate=2013-10-07 }}</ref><ref>[http://movies.msn.com/celebrities/celebrity-biography/tippi-hedren/ Tippi Hedren - Biography] ''[[MSN|MSN]]'' Retrieved October 20, 2012</ref><ref name="filmreference">[http://www.filmreference.com/film/14/Tippi-Hedren.html Tippi Hedren Biography (1935-)] ''filmreference.com'' Retrieved October 20, 2012</ref><ref>[http://www.nytimes.com/movies/person/31492/Tippi-Hedren Tippi Hedren - About This Person] ''New York Times'' Retrieved January 22, 2014</ref><ref>[http://www.blockbuster.com/browse/catalog/personDetails/27787 Tippi Hedren Movies] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120103011601/http://www.blockbuster.com/browse/catalog/personDetails/27787 |date=2012-01-03 }} ''[[blockbuster.com|blockbuster.com]]'' Retrieved January 22, 2014</ref>。2004年，蒂比·海德倫證實她出生於1930年。

1963年，蒂比·海德倫演出[[希區考克|希區考克]]執導電影《[[鳥_(電影)|鳥]]》（{{lang|en|The Birds}}），也是處女作。

2018年，已高齡88歲的蒂比．海德倫擔綱Gucci珠寶腕錶最新形象廣告短片主角，扮演神祕的占卜師。
