在[[电脑|电脑]]中，'''头像'''（{{lang-en|Avatar}}）是标识用户身份的一张图片。在游戏或者虚拟世界中，头像可以以[[三维计算机图形|三维]]形式表示<ref>[[Lawrence_Lessig|Lessig, Lawrence]]. ''Code and Other Laws of Cyberspace''. Basic Books, 2000. ISBN 0-465-03913-8</ref>；或在网络论坛等在线社区中使用二维形式作为[[图标|图标]]<ref>Fink, Jeri. ''Cyberseduction: Reality in the Age of Psychotechnology''. Prometheus Books, 1999. ISBN 1-57392-743-0</ref><ref>Blackwood, Kevin. ''Casino Gambling For Dummies''. For Dummies, 2006. p.284. ISBN 0-471-75286-X</ref>。它在[[MUD|MUD]]早期系统中还可指文本排列结构<ref>In{{cite book|last1=Mulligan|first1=Jessica|last2=Patrovsky|first2=Bridgette|title=Developing online games: an insider's guide|year=2003|publisher=New Riders}}, Randy Farmer is quoted (p.454): "It is important to realize that the term 'avatar' was used in another game later [??] in that period (Ultima IV) and that the concept of an 'avatar' was in several works of fiction prior to the development of Habitat including [[Vernor_Vinge|Vernor Vinge]]'s ''[[True_Names|True Names]]'' and [[John_Brunner_(novelist)|John Brunner]]'s ''[[The_Shockwave_Rider|Shockwave Rider]]''."</ref>。这是一个代表用户的对象。术语“头像”还可以与[[账户名|账户名]]、别名联系，指代一个互联网用户<ref>Jordan, Tim. ''Cyberpower: The Culture and Politics of Cyberspace and the Internet''. Routledge, 1999. ISBN 0-415-17078-8</ref>。
