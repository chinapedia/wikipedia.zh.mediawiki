《'''生活本身'''》（''Life Itself''）是一部2014年{{tsl|en|Steve James (producer)|史蒂夫·詹姆斯}}执导的美国传记题材[[纪录片|纪录片]]，讲述美国著名影评人[[罗杰·伊伯特|罗杰·伊伯特]]的生平。Zak Piper、Steve James 和 Garrett Basch 监制。改编自罗杰·伊伯特于2011年发表的同名[[回忆录|回忆录]]<ref name="Hollywood">{{cite web |last=Kit|first=Borys|title=Roger Ebert Documentary Filmmakers Vow to Finish Movie | url= http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/roger-ebert-dead-documentary-filmmakers-433065 |work=[[The_Hollywood_Reporter|The Hollywood Reporter]] |accessdate=April 26, 2013 |date=April 4, 2013}}</ref>。

2014年[[圣丹斯影展|圣丹斯影展]]上首映，也是[[第67届坎城影展|第67届坎城影展]]的竞赛片<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.festival-cannes.com/en/theDailyArticle/60980.html|title=CANNES CLASSICS - Life Itself: a critic's life and times|date=2014-05-20|author=Charlotte Pavard|language=en|accessdate=2014-12-15|publisher=Festival de Cannes}}</ref>。第41届[[特莱瑞德影展|特莱瑞德影展]]于8月28日举办了一场特别展映<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/race/telluride-a-film-fest-knows-729301|title=Telluride: A Film Fest That Knows How to Party|date=2014-08-13|author=Scott Feinberg|language=en|accessdate=2014-12-15|publisher=The Hollywood Reporter}}</ref>。[[Magnolia_Pictures|Magnolia Pictures]]于2014年7月4日负责北美的戏院发行和[[视频点播|视频点播]]。<ref>[http://lifeitself.vhx.tv/ "Life Itself."] ''Life Itself''. 20 July 2014.</ref><ref>Tallerico, Brian. [http://www.rogerebert.com/life-itself/where-you-can-see-life-itself "Where You Can See 'Life Itself.'"] ''rogerebert.com''. 30 June 2014. 20 July 2014.</ref>

影片入选[[第87届奥斯卡金像奖|第87届奥斯卡金像奖]]最佳纪录片15部初选名单。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2014/12/oscar-documentary-feature-shortlist-announced-full-list-1201306252/|publisher=Deadline Hollwyood|title=Oscars: Documentary Feature Shortlist At 15|date=December 2, 2014 |language=en|accessdate=2014-12-15}}</ref>

== 评价 ==
影片获得了各方盛赞，[[烂番茄|烂番茄]]上依据164个评论有98%的新鲜度，平均分8.4/10，该网站共识：“细节丰富且温暖感人，这是对传奇影评人快乐而深刻的致敬。”<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/life_itself/ | title=Life Itself | work=[[Rotten_Tomatoes|Rotten Tomatoes]] | publisher=[[Flixster|Flixster]] | accessdate=September 11, 2014}}</ref>[[Metacritic|Metacritic]]上33家媒体给出87分的肯定。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.metacritic.com/movie/life-itself | title=Life Itself Reviews | work=[[Metacritic|Metacritic]] | publisher=[[CBS_Interactive|CBS Interactive]] | accessdate=July 19, 2014}}</ref>
