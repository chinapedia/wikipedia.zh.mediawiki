'''妲薇.蓋文森''' ('''Tavi Gevinson'''){{BD|1997年|}}是[[美國|美國]]的一名[[時尚|時尚]][[部落客|部落客]]、作家及演員<ref>Eva Wiseman, "[http://www.guardian.co.uk/lifeandstyle/2009/sep/20/tavi-gevinson-new-york-fashion Tavi Gevinson: The 13-year-old blogger with the fashion world at her feet]", ''The Observer,'' 20 September 2009. Accessed 23 September 2009.</ref>。她的"Style Rookie"部落閣在2008年[[三月三十一日|三月三十一日]]開始運作，當時她年僅十一歲。 而後她在世界各地陸續擁有了超過四百萬名讀者。<ref>[http://www.dailymail.co.uk/femail/article-1215048/Meet-Tavi-Gevinson-13-tiny-blogger-fashion-industry-feet.html Move over Geldof girls: Meet Tavi, 13, the 'tiny' blogger with the fashion industry at her feet] The Daily Mail on 23rd September 2009</ref>

==簡介==
許多人把蓋文森當成是靈感的來源，她對自己的說法是 "整天穿著彆扭外套、戴著花俏帽子待在屋裡的十三歲小呆瓜"。 <ref>[http://tavi-thenewgirlintown.blogspot.com Style Rookie]</ref>

她家住在[[芝加哥|芝加哥]]歐克公園附近，雖然在家裡寫部落閣的文章，但妲薇的父親一直到她向他要求許可去[[紐約時報|紐約時報]]活動的邀請時，才真的了解她在做什麼。妲薇的狀況一如引發了青少年部落客在交際活動與父母管教之間的牽扯。<ref>[http://www.usatoday.com/tech/webguide/internetlife/2008-08-12-girl-fashion-blogs_N.htm Young fashion bloggers are worrisome trend to parents] USA Today</ref>在2009年年中，妲薇和'''London-based Borders&Frontiers''' 合作設計並出售自己的[[襯衫|襯衫]]。<ref>[http://www.catwalkqueen.tv/2009/06/bloggers_turn_designer.html Bloggers turn designers... who's next?] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090925063632/http://www.catwalkqueen.tv/2009/06/bloggers_turn_designer.html |date=2009-09-25 }} Catwalk Queen</ref>在同年八月，她出現在流行雜誌的封面上，如妲薇一般的[[部落客|部落客]]被稱為是[[時尚|時尚]]界的前鋒，她也成為各時尚秀的常駐佳賓。<ref>[http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2009/09/17/tavi-gevinson-13-year-old_n_287269.html Tavi Gevinson: 13-Year-Old Fashion Blogger Skips School, Attends Fashion Week (PHOTOS)] The Huffington Post on 09-17-09 10:35 AM</ref>
