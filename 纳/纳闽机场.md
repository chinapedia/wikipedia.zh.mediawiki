'''纳闽机场'''（{{lang-ms|Lapangan Terbang Labuan}}，{{airport codes|LBU|WBKL}}），是一座位于[[马来西亚|马来西亚]][[纳闽|纳闽]]的机场，距离市中心约{{convert|2.5|km}}，通过道路则需{{convert|5|km}}。纳闽机场跑道长2800米、宽45米。纳闽机场前身是日军于[[二战|二战]]遗留下来的跑道<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thestar.com.my/metro/focus/2017/05/08/discovering-labuan-and-loving-it-dutyfree-island-in-borneo-charms-tourists-with-rich-history-and-la/|title=Discovering Labuan and loving it  - Focus - The Star Online|work=www.thestar.com.my}}</ref>，于1999年正式投入使用<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.malaysiaairports.com.my/?m=business&c=business_about&id=3|title=Domestic Airports|work=www.malaysiaairports.com.my}}</ref>，也设有[[马来西亚皇家空军|马来西亚皇家空军]]基地。作为一个免税机场，纳闽设有检验设施以供乘客检查入境至拥有移民自主权的[[沙巴|沙巴]]和[[砂拉越|砂拉越]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2017/04/27/full-immigration-check-at-labuan-airport-from-may-2/|title=Full immigration check at Labuan Airport from May 2 - Nation - The Star Online|publisher=}}</ref>
