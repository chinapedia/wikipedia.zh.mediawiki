'''无机纳米笼'''（Inorganic Nanocages）通常指空心的带孔洞的[[金|金]][[纳米颗粒|纳米颗粒]]，大小为10到150纳米。金纳米笼可由[[氯金酸|氯金酸]]（[[氢|H]][[金|Au]][[氯|Cl]]<sub>4</sub>）在沸水中取代[[銀|銀]]纳米颗粒合成<ref>{{cite journal | author=Jingyi Chen, Fusayo Saeki, Benjamin J. Wiley, Hu Cang, Michael J. Cobb, Zhi-Yuan Li, Leslie Au, Hui Zhang, Michael B. Kimmey, Xingde Li, and Younan Xia | title= Gold Nanocages: Bioconjugation and Their Potential Use as Optical Imaging Contrast Agents | journal=Nano Lett. | year=2005 | volume=5 | issue=3 | pages= 473–7 | url= | doi= 10.1021/nl047950t | pmid=15755097}}</ref>。通常金纳米颗粒的吸收峰在[[可见光|可见光]]波段（约550nm），而金纳米笼的吸收峰则位于[[红外线|近红外]]波段。近红外光的[[组织_(生物学)|生物组织]]穿透性较好，且金纳米笼也有较好的生物相容性，使其能作为[[光学相干断层扫描|光学相干断层扫描]]的[[造影劑|顯影劑]]，分辨率可达微米级。金纳米笼也可以通过吸收近红外光来加热，纳米笼的最初发现者[[圣路易斯华盛顿大学|华盛顿大学]]的夏幼南等将肿瘤特异性的[[抗体|抗体]]（如抗[[表皮生长因子受体|EGFR]]的抗体）加在金纳米笼上，使其可特异性富集在[[癌细胞|癌细胞]]表面，再远程施加近红外光，以[[光热效应|光热效应]]加热杀死癌细胞<ref>{{cite journal | author=Jingyi Chen, Danling Wang, Jiefeng Xi , Leslie Au, Andy Siekkinen, Addie Warsen, Zhi-Yuan Li, Hui Zhang, Younan Xia, and Xingde Li | title= Gold Nanocages: Bioconjugation and Their Potential Use as Optical Imaging Contrast Agents | journal=Nano Lett. | year=2007 | volume=7 | issue=5 | pages= 1318–22 | url= | doi= 10.1021/nl070345g | pmid=17430005}}</ref><ref> {{cite journal | author=Jingyi Chen, Charles Glaus, Richard Laforest, Qiang Zhang, Miaoxian Yang, Michael Gidding, Michael J. Welch, and Younan Xia | title= Gold Nanocages as Photothermal Transducers for Cancer Treatment | journal=Small. | year=2010 | volume=6 | issue=7| pages= 811–7 | url= | doi= 10.1002/smll.200902216 | pmid=20225187}}</ref>。
