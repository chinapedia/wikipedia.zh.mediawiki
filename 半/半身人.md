'''半身人'''（{{lang-en|Halfling}}）為[[角色扮演遊戲|角色扮演遊戲]]《[[龍與地下城|龍與地下城]]》（Dungeons & Dragons）中的一種虛構生物。其體型只有一般人類的二分之一。其原型來自[[英國|英國]]作家[[托爾金|托爾金]]作品中的虛構[[中土大陸的種族|種族]]：[[哈比人|哈比人]]<ref>{{cite web | last = Gygax | first = Gary | title = Gary Gygax (Interview) | work = TheOneRing.net | url = http://archives.theonering.net/features/interviews/gary_gygax.html#maincontent | accessdate = 2018-09-02 }}</ref><ref name=weinstock2014>{{citation| title=The Ashgate Encyclopedia of Literary and Cinematic Monsters | editor1-first=Jeffrey | editor1-last=Weinstock | publisher=Ashgate Publishing, Ltd. | year=2014 | isbn=1409425622 | page=193 | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=NI1zBAAAQBAJ&pg=PA193 | postscript=.}}</ref>。但由於哈比人為托爾金特有的商標，故《龍與地下城》及其他[[奇幻|奇幻]]作品將哈比人及類哈比人的種族稱為半身人<ref name=langford2005>{{citation| title=The Sex Column and Other Misprints | first1=David | last1=Langford | page=188 | publisher=Wildside Press LLC | year=2005 | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=n78kYbvUd_8C&pg=PA188 | isbn=1930997787 | postscript=.}}</ref>。
