'''半乳糖激酶'''（{{lang-en|Galactokinase}}）是一种催化[[半乳糖|α-D-半乳糖]][[磷酸化|磷酸化]]为[[半乳糖-1-磷酸|半乳糖-1-磷酸]]的磷酸转移酶，在此过程中消耗一[[分子|分子]][[三磷酸腺苷|ATP]]<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.theodora.com/medical_dictionary/gamma_galactosis.html#galactokinase |title=galactokinase |work=Medical Dictionary |accessdate=2013-01-26}}</ref>。半乳糖激酶催化[[Leloir途径|Leloir途径]]（有机体中将α-D-半乳糖转化为[[葡萄糖-1-磷酸|葡萄糖-1-磷酸]]的[[代謝途徑|代謝途徑]]）的第二步反应，最早在[[哺乳动物|哺乳动物]]的[[肝臟|肝臟]]中发现, 后来在[[酵母|酵母]]、[[古菌|古菌]]<ref>{{cite journal|last=Hartley A, Glynn SE, Barynin V, Baker PJ, et al|title=Substrate specificity and mechanism from the structure of Pyrococcus furiosus galactokinase|journal=J Mol Biol|date=Mar 2004|volume=337|issue=2|pages=387–98|pmid = 15003454| doi = 10.1016/j.jmb.2004.01.043|first1=Andrew|last2=Glynn|first2=Steven E.|last3=Barynin|first3=Vladimir|last4=Baker|first4=Patrick J.|last5=Sedelnikova|first5=Svetlana E.|last6=Verhees|first6=Corné|last7=De Geus|first7=Daniel|last8=Van Der Oost|first8=John|last9=Timson|first9=David J.|last10=Reece|first10=Richard J.|last11=Rice|first11=David W.|display-authors=8}}</ref>、[[植物|植物]]<ref>{{cite journal|last=Foglietti MJ, Percheron F|title=Purification and mechanism of action of a plant galactokinase|journal=Biochimie|year=1976|volume=58|issue=5|pages=499–504|pmid = 182286|first1=MJ|last2=Percheron|first2=F|doi=10.1016/s0300-9084(76)80218-0}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last=Dey PM|title=Galactokinase of Vicia faba seeds|journal=Eur J Biochem|date=Oct 1983|volume=136|issue=1|pages=155–9|pmid = 6617655|doi=10.1111/j.1432-1033.1983.tb07720.x|first1=PM}}</ref>和[[人类|人类]]中都有发现<ref name="holden04">{{cite journal|last=Holden HM, Thoden JB, Timson DJ, Reece RJ|title=Galactokinase: structure, function and role in type II galactosemia|journal=Cell Mol Life Sci|date=Oct 2004|volume=61|issue=19–20|pages=2471–84|pmid = 15526155| doi = 10.1007/s00018-004-4160-6|first1=H. M.|last2=Thoden|first2=J. B.|last3=Timson|first3=D. J.|last4=Reece|first4=R. J.}}</ref>。
