'''半乳糖醇'''（{{lang-en|Galactitol}} 或 dulcitol）是一种[[糖醇|糖醇]]，是[[半乳糖|半乳糖]]的[[氧化还原反应|还原产物]]<ref name=pubchem>{{cite web|url=http://pubchem.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/summary/summary.cgi?cid=11850|title=Galactitol - Compound Summary |publisher=[[National_Center_for_Biotechnology_Information|National Center for Biotechnology Information]]|accessdate=2008-08-06}}</ref>。在{{le|半乳糖激酶缺乏症|galactokinase deficiency}}（一种[[半乳糖血症|半乳糖血症]]）患者中，多余的半乳糖醇会在[[晶状体|晶状体]]积累而导致[[白内障|白内障]]<ref name=eMedicine>{{cite web |url = http://www.emedicine.com/ped/TOPIC815.HTM |title = Galactokinase Deficiency |accessdate = 2008-08-08 |author = Roth, KS |date = September 10, 2007 |work = [[eMedicine|eMedicine]] |publisher = [[WebMD|WebMD]]}}</ref>。

半乳糖醇由[[半乳糖|半乳糖]]在[[醛糖还原酶|醛糖还原酶]]催化反应中产生。半乳糖本身来自二糖[[乳糖|乳糖]]的代谢成[[葡萄糖|葡萄糖]]和半乳糖。
