'''莎拉·梅鐸'''（{{bd|1972年|5月31日}}，{{lang-en|'''Sarah Murdoch'''}}，本姓：奧黑爾（{{lang|en|O'Hare}}）），[[英國|英國]][[倫敦|倫敦]]南部城鎮[[克羅伊登|克羅伊登]]出生，澳洲籍超級模特兒、演員及電視節目主持人，傳媒大亨[[魯柏·梅鐸|魯柏·梅鐸]]的[[媳婦|媳婦]]。

==職業生涯==
===模特兒===
莎拉中學時在麥當勞書院學習[[芭蕾舞|芭蕾舞]]，當時的老師指她身材太高，無法成為職業芭蕾舞演員<ref name="life">{{cite web |url=http://www.lifeetc.com.au/__data/page/6179/cover_story3.pdf |title=Sarah Murdoch: How I stay real |publisher=Life etc |accessdate=29 November 2006}}</ref>。由於身高「問題」和擁有標緻的臉孔，促使她成為各大時裝及護膚化妝品牌爭相聘用的模特兒。她曾經為[[萊雅|萊雅]]、[[露華濃|露華濃]]、[[勞夫·羅倫|勞夫·羅倫]]、[[伊夫聖洛朗|伊夫聖洛朗]]、[[雅詩蘭黛公司|雅詩蘭黛公司]]等拍下不少廣告，在《[[運動畫刊|運動畫刊]]》中擔當泳裝模特兒<ref>{{cite news|url=http://sportsillustrated.cnn.com/swimsuit/collection/issues/1999/99_sohare_08_bycategory.html|title=SI Swimsuit Collection: Sarah O'Hare|accessdate=18 February 2008|publisher=[[Time_Inc.|Time Inc.]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://sportsillustrated.cnn.com/swimsuit/collection/issues/1999/99_sohare_10_bycategory.html|title=SI Swimsuit Collection: Sarah O'Hare|accessdate=18 February 2008|publisher=[[Time_Inc.|Time Inc.]]}}</ref>，亦曾在各地的時裝雜誌當封面女郎。

===電視節目主持===
2006年接替放產假的{{link-en|傑西卡·羅|Jessica Rowe}}在[[第九頻道電視台|第九頻道電視台]]的早晨節目當了4個月的主持<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.smh.com.au/news/tv--radio/tvs-beauty-complex/2006/11/28/1164476204612.html |title=Welcome to fickle world of a career in television |publisher=[[The_Sydney_Morning_Herald|The Sydney Morning Herald]] |date=29 November 2006 |accessdate=29 November 2006}}</ref>。

2009-11年，在《[[澳洲超級模特兒新秀大賽|澳洲超級模特兒新秀大賽]]》（[[全美超級模特兒新秀大賽|全美超級模特兒新秀大賽]]的澳洲版）中擔任主持（類似[[泰雅·賓絲|泰雅·賓絲]]的角色），但是因為錯誤讀出優勝者的名字，備受大眾批評<ref>{{cite news | newspaper = [[The_Australian|The Australian]] | title = Wrong model announced as contest winner| first = Amanda | last = Meade | section = Local | page = 3 | date = 29 September 2010 | accessdate = 29 September 2010 | url = http://www.theaustralian.com.au/business/media/wrong-model-announced-as-top-model-contest-winner/story-e6frg996-1225931339053}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/tvshowbiz/article-1315970/Australias-Next-Top-Model-host-Sarah-Murdoch-announces-WRONG-winner.html?ito=feeds-newsxml|title=Australia's Next Top Model host announces the WRONG winner|date=29 September 2010|work=Daily Mail|accessdate=29 September 2010 | location=London | first=Richard | last=Shears}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.smh.com.au/entertainment/tv-and-radio/murdoch-leaves-model-20111212-1or4n.html|title=Murdoch leaves Model |publisher=''[[Sydney_Morning_Herald|Sydney Morning Herald]]''|last=Hardie|first=Giles|date=2011-12-12 |accessdate=2011-12-12}}</ref>。

===電視劇及電影===
在2001年愛情喜劇《{{link-en|神魂顛倒|Head over Heels (2001 film)}}》及知名處境喜劇《[[老友記|老友記]]》（第七季）中都有演出機會。
