'''假人挑戰'''（{{lang-en|Mannequin Challenge}}）為一部[[網絡爆紅短片|網絡爆紅短片]]，被拍攝者需做出一個定格動作，且要表現與[[人体模型|人体模型]]一樣靜止不動，並由攝影師透過移動鏡頭的技巧進行拍攝，通常影片會使用{{tsl|en|Rae Sremmurd}}的《{{le|黑色披頭四|Black Beatles}}》作為背景音樂<ref name=":4" /><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2016/11/08/style/mannequin-challenge-is-the-new-viral-video-sensation-you-probably-cant-avoid.html|title=Mannequin Challenge Is the New Viral Video Sensation You Probably Can’t Avoid|last=Victor|first=Daniel|date=2016-11-07|newspaper=The New York Times|issn=0362-4331|access-date=2016-11-08}}</ref>。知名[[社会化媒体|社交媒體]]像是[[推特|推特]]以及[[Instagram|Instagram]]等使用「#MannequinChallenge」作為[[主題標籤|主題標籤]]。這種現象源自於2016年10月12日，位於[[美國|美國]][[佛羅里達州|佛羅里達州]][[杰克逊维尔_(佛罗里达州)|杰克逊维尔]]的學生所發想<ref name=":4">{{Cite web|url=http://mashable.com/2016/11/03/mannequin-challenge/|title=The Mannequin Challenge will actually make you miss planking|last=Tesema|first=Martha|website=Mashable|access-date=2016-11-07}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://knowyourmeme.com/memes/mannequin-challenge|title=Mannequin Challenge|newspaper=Know Your Meme|access-date=2016-11-07}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Suber-Jenkins|first1=Aric|title=Mannequin Challenge Origin: The true story of the coolest viral video trend of the year|url=https://mic.com/articles/158806/mannequin-challenge-origin-the-true-story-of-the-coolest-viral-video-trend-of-the-year#.lgOvtV1VE|website=.Mic|accessdate=2016-11-15|date=2016-11-07}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Curtis|first1=Charles|title='The Mannequin Challenge' is the next big thing taking over college football|url=http://ftw.usatoday.com/2016/11/mannequin-challenge-sports-michigan-football-meme-video|website=FORTHEWIN|publisher=USA Today Sports|accessdate=2016-11-04}}</ref>。

但在2013時，已有一位街頭藝術家Silviu Caraba 於街頭表演假人挑戰，且經常與路人一起擺像動作電影一樣的姿勢以拍照。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fGaJjfc3M8U|title=Coolest Street Performer in London! Silviu Caraba turns you into an action hero!|last=Robbie|first=Backpacking|website=Youtube|access-date=2013-6-21}}</ref>
