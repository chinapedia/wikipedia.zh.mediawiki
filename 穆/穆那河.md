'''穆那河'''（{{lang-en|Mula River}}）是印度[[浦那|浦那]]的一條河流，於西高止山脈附近被{{le|穆爾希水壩|Mulshi Dam}}攔截，而形成穆爾希湖<ref name="getaway">{{cite news|title=Quick getaway: Mulshi dam, Chail |url=http://articles.economictimes.indiatimes.com/2012-04-12/news/31331134_1_chail-mulshi-kms |date=12 April 2012 |agency=The Economic Times (India)|accessdate=7 July 2014}}</ref>。在浦那市的下游地帶，穆那河與左側的{{le|帕瓦那河|Pawana River}}及右側的{{le|穆薩河|Mutha River}}合流，形成{{le|穆那-穆薩河|Mula-Mutha}}，之後匯入{{le|比馬河|Bhima River}}<ref name="riversystems">{{cite book|title=GAZETTEERS OF BOMBAY STATE – POONA|url=https://cultural.maharashtra.gov.in/english/gazetteer/Poona/PART%20I/Chap(1)/River%20Systems.htm |accessdate=7 Jul 2014|publisher=Ministry of Culture and Tourism, [[Government_of_Maharashtra|Government of Maharashtra]]|chapter=RIVER SYSTEMS }}</ref>。

本河流沿著{{le|舊浦那-孟買高速公路|Old Pune–Mumbai Highway}}，為{{le|賓布里金傑沃德市政公司|Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation}}與{{le|浦那市政公司|Pune Municipal Corporation}}的界線<ref name="newbridge">{{cite news|title=Another bridge across Mula river in Dapodi planned |url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/pune/Another-bridge-across-Mula-river-in-Dapodi-planned/articleshow/21471699.cms |date=30 July 2013 |agency=[[Times_of_India|Times of India]]|location=Pune|accessdate=7 July 2014}}</ref>。

== 橋樑 ==

== 污染與洪水 ==
2010年，由於高污染物質與垃圾傾倒於穆那河中，造成其周邊地區遭遇大洪水<ref name="wasted">{{cite news|title=Mula river ‘wasted’, get set for flash floods this monsoon |url=http://www.dnaindia.com/pune/report-mula-river-wasted-get-set-for-flash-floods-this-monsoon-1677459 |first=Partha Sarthi |last=Biswas |date=18 April 2012 |location=Pune |publisher=[[Daily_News_and_Analysis|Daily News and Analysis]]|accessdate=7 July 2014}}</ref>。

{{le|浦那工程學院|College of Engineering, Pune}}每年都會在其校址附近的穆那河舉辦划舟比賽。2012年，他們發現該河流無法從基爾凱埃航行到學院，因為馬哈拉施特拉天然氣有限公司（{{lang|en|Maharashtra Natural Gas Limited}}）建造了泥土小路用以興建管線，造成河流阻塞<ref name="mngl" />。

2003年，有報導指稱{{le|瓦卡德|Wakad}}附近的穆那河發現死魚。{{le|賓布里金傑沃德市政公司|Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation}}無法判定這起河流污染的原因<ref name="deadfish">{{cite news|title=Dead fish in Mula river raise concern |url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/pune/Dead-fish-in-Mula-river-raise-concern/articleshow/19119782.cms |date=22 March 2013 |agency=[[Times_of_India|Times of India]]|location=Pune |accessdate=9 July 2014}}</ref>。

由於因每日1.25億的未處理廢水從{{le|浦那市政公司|Pune Municipal Corporation}}排放到穆那河中所造成的高度污染，{{le|馬哈拉施特拉污染控制局|Maharashtra Pollution Control Board}}將其水質等級列為「{{lang|en|Class-IV}}<ref name="mpcbchap5">{{Cite web|title=MAJOR SOURCES OF POLLUTION in PUNE|url=http://mpcb.gov.in/relatedtopics/CHAPTER5.pdf |publisher=[[Maharashtra_Pollution_Control_Board|Maharashtra Pollution Control Board]]|accessdate=9 July 2014}}</ref>」。

2014年，{{le|浦那警察局|Pune Police}}發現{{le|卡拉迪|Kharadi, Pune}}的穆那河沿岸生長出[[大麻屬|大麻]]<ref name="cannabis">{{cite news|title=More cannabis growth found on Mula-Mutha river bed |url=http://indianexpress.com/article/cities/pune/more-cannabis-growth-found-on-mula-mutha-river-bed/ |date=2 March 2014 |agency=[[Indian_Express|Indian Express]]|location=Pune|accessdate=9 July 2014}}</ref>。
