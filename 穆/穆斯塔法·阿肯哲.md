'''穆斯塔法·阿肯哲'''（{{lang-tr|Mustafa Akıncı}}，{{bd|1947年|12月28日|||}}），2015年当选第四任[[北塞浦路斯总统|北塞浦路斯总统]]。他28岁时就成为[[北尼科西亚|尼科西亚土耳其直辖市]]市长，也是北塞浦路斯国会议员、副总理和旅游部长。<ref>[http://www.hurriyetdailynews.com/presidential-polls-in-north-cyprus.aspx?pageID=449&nID=72372&NewsCatID=425 Presidential polls in north Cyprus] Hurriyet Daily News, 1 October 2014</ref>

1975年，他被选为土耳其裔塞浦路斯制宪会议成员。<ref name="auto">{{Cite web|title = Mustafa Akıncı Timeline - Mustafa Akıncı Resmi Web Sitesi|url = http://www.mustafaakinci.com/mustafa-akinci-timeline/?lang=en|accessdate = 2015-04-04}}</ref>这次选举后，他成为了尼科西亚土耳其直辖市第一位民选市长，从1976年到1990年连续14年担任市长。1993年至2009年间，他当选北塞浦路斯民选议员。<ref>{{Cite web|title = Akinci: practical steps towards a solution  {{!}}   Cyprus Mail|url = http://cyprus-mail.com/2015/04/20/akinci-practical-steps-towards-a-solution/|website = cyprus-mail.com|accessdate = 2015-04-23}}</ref>

2015年3月13日，穆斯塔法·阿肯哲申请竞选总统。<ref>{{Cite web|title = Akıncı adaylık başvurusunu saat 09.00’da yapacak|url = http://www.gundemkibris.com/akinci-adaylik-basvurusunu-saat-09-00da-yapacak-108713h.htm|accessdate = 2015-04-04}}</ref>2015年4月19日，北塞浦路斯开始举行总统选举，阿肯哲获得了26.9%的选票，仅次于[[德尔维什·埃尔奥卢|德尔维什·埃尔奥卢]]总统（Derviş Eroğlu）获得的28.2%的选票。2015年4月26日进行第二轮投票<ref>{{Cite web|title = EUROPE - Turkish Cypriot leader ahead after first round of vote|url = http://www.hurriyetdailynews.com/turkish-cypriot-leader-ahead-after-first-round-of-vote.aspx?pageID=238&nID=81291&NewsCatID=351|accessdate = 2015-04-23}}</ref>，阿肯哲最终赢得选举，当选总统<ref name="yahoo.com">{{cite web|url=http://news.yahoo.com/turkish-cypriots-vote-poll-could-speed-peace-talks-064917247.html|title=Turkish Cypriots elect new leader for breakaway enclave|date=26 April 2015|work=Yahoo News|accessdate=26 April 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://uk.reuters.com/article/2015/04/26/uk-cyprus-north-election-idUKKBN0NH09O20150426|title=Leftist Akinci wins north Cyprus election, seeking peace deal|work=Reuters UK|accessdate=26 April 2015}}</ref>。

== 著作 ==
2010年，阿肯哲出版他的第一本书《Belediye Başkanlığı'nda 14 yıl》。这本书有479页，旨在总结他担任尼科西亚土耳其直辖市市长的经验。<ref>{{Cite web|title = Belediye Başkanlığı'nda 14 yıl|url = http://www.goodreads.com/book/show/12529506-belediye-ba-kanl-nda-14-y-l|website = Goodreads|accessdate = 2015-04-23}}</ref>
