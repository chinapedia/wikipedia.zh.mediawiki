'''穆罕默德·叶夫洛耶夫'''（{{lang-ru|'''Магомед Евлоев'''}}，{{bd|1971年||2008年|8月31日|catIdx=Yevloyev, Magomed}}），[[俄羅斯|俄羅斯]][[印古什|印古什]]著名[[記者|記者]]，[[異見人士|異見人士]]。叶夫洛耶夫是「印古什亚」网站（ingushetiaya.ru）的创始人，經常對[[印古什|印古什]]當局和印古什共和國總統[[穆拉特·贾济科夫|穆拉特·贾济科夫]]提出嚴厲批評。<ref>[http://ingushetiya.wordpress.com/2008/08/31/owner-of-site-ingushetiyaru-killed/ Owner of the news site "Ingushetiya.Ru", Magomed Yevloev], 2008-08-31</ref>其網站也揭露了俄羅斯安全部隊在印古什的惡行。<ref name=BBC0901>[http://news.bbc.co.uk/chinese/simp/hi/newsid_7590000/newsid_7591100/7591138.stm 抨击莫斯科网站负责人被俄警方打死] BBC 2008-09-01</ref>

2008年8月31日，叶夫洛耶夫在纳兹兰机场时被俄羅斯警方逮捕并在拘留過程中被警方開槍擊斃。<ref>[http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/world/europe/7590719.stm Ingushetia website owner killed], [[BBC_News|BBC News]], 2008-08-31</ref><ref>[http://www.theaustralian.news.com.au/story/0,25197,24272873-12377,00.html Kremlin critic found dead] {{Wayback|url=http://www.theaustralian.news.com.au/story/0,25197,24272873-12377,00.html |date=20080916144238 }}, [[The_Australian|The Australian]], 2008-08-31</ref><ref name=AP>[http://www.sfgate.com/cgi-bin/article.cgi?f=/n/a/2008/08/31/international/i072332D67.DTL&tsp=1 Deputy says Russian police kill Web site owner], [[Associated_Press|Associated Press]], 2008-08-31</ref>俄警方聲稱是因為在押送過程中叶夫洛耶夫突然襲擊警方并試圖搶槍，警方開槍擊中其頭部。<ref name=BBC0901/>有傳言說叶夫洛耶夫遇害之前已經知道自己的危險，打算到[[歐盟|歐盟]]國家尋求[[政治庇護|政治庇護]]。<ref>[http://www.themoscowtimes.com/article/1010/42/369633.htm Editor of News Portal Flees Country], [[The_Moscow_Times|The Moscow Times]]</ref>
