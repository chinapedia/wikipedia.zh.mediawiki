'''碘化铋'''是一种绿黑色晶体，化学式 [[铋|Bi]][[碘|I]]<sub>3</sub>。
==晶体结构==
碘化铋的晶体结构特殊,[[晶胞|晶胞]]中碘按六方最密堆积，铋隔一层填充于三分之二的八面体空隙,形成一种层状结构。

==合成==
碘化铋可直接从单质合成，加热碘和铋的混合粉末：<ref name="Erdmann">{{Citation | last = Erdmann | first = Hugo | author-link = Hugo Erdmann | last2 = Dunlap | first2 = Frederick Leavy | author2-link = | publication-date = | date = | year = 1900 | title = Handbook of Basic Tables for Chemical Analysis | edition = | volume = | series = | publication-place = New York | place =  | publisher = John Wiley & Sons | id = | isbn =  | doi = | oclc = | page = 76 | url = http://books.google.com/?id=IxlIAAAAIAAJ&pg=PA76&dq=%22Bismuth+iodide%22 | accessdate = 2008-06-19 }}</ref>

:2Bi + 3I<sub>2</sub> → 2BiI<sub>3</sub>

也可用[[氢碘酸|氢碘酸]]溶解氧化铋得到碘化铋：
:Bi<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub>(s) + 6HI(aq) → 2BiI<sub>3</sub>(s) + 3H<sub>2</sub>O(l)

==反应==
