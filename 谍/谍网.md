《'''谍网'''》（{{lang-en|'''''Quantico'''''}}）是一部[[美国|美国]]电视剧，于2015年9月27日在[[美国广播公司|ABC]]首播。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2015/05/the-catch-jenna-bans-family-kings-prophets-boom-la-crime-wicked-city-quantico-series-abc-1201422660/|title=ABC Picks Up Dramas 'The Catch', Jenna Bans, 'Kings & Prophets', 'Boom', 'L.A. Crime' & 'Quantico' To Series|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|work=[[Deadline.com|Deadline.com]]|date=May 7, 2015|accessdate=May 8, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.deadline.com/2015/06/of-kings-prophets-pulled-quantico-move-sunday-abc-1201436369/|title='Of Kings and Prophets' Pulled From ABC Fall Schedule, 'Quantico' Goes To Sunday|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|work=[[Deadline.com|Deadline.com]]|date=June 2, 2015|accessdate=June 2, 2015}}</ref><ref name=fallspd>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2015/06/abc-fall-premiere-dates-wicked-city-october-launch-1201440727/|title=ABC Fall Premiere Dates: ‘Wicked City’ Gets October Launch On Tuesday|author=Nellie Andreeva|work=Deadline|accessdate=11 June 2015}}</ref>劇中主角彭雅麗（[[琵豔卡·喬普拉|琵豔卡·喬普拉]]飾）被懷疑發動恐怖襲擊。回憶片段講述她和她的同期新學員在[[匡蒂科|匡蒂科]](Quantico){{link-en|FBI學院|FBI Academy}}受訓的故事。ABC預訂了本劇，並在10月和11月追加至全季集數22集。2016年3月3日，ABC宣布續訂第2季。<ref name="ABC Renewals">{{cite web|url=http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2016/03/03/once-upon-a-time-renewed-for-season-6-at-abc/|title='Once Upon a Time,' 'The Goldbergs', 'Fresh Off the Boat' and 'Quantico' renewed at ABC|last=Porter|first=Rick|work=TV by the Numbers|date=March 3, 2016|accessdate=March 3, 2016}}</ref>

== 劇情 ==
在[[維珍尼亞州|維珍尼亞州]][[匡提科|匡提科]]，{{link-en|FBI學院|FBI Academy}}裏一群新學員排除萬難通過訓練，然而他們卻不知道，他們當中有人正在策劃一場自[[九一一襲擊|九一一襲擊]]以來最大的恐怖襲擊。
