'''赫蘇斯·蒙特羅'''（{{lang-en| Jesús Alejandro Montero }}，{{bd|1989年|11月28日}}）出生於[[委內瑞拉|委內瑞拉]]的瓜卡拉（Guacara），現為[[美國職棒大聯盟|美國職棒大聯盟]][[西雅圖水手|西雅圖水手]]的[[捕手|捕手]]。

蒙特羅在和洋基簽下合約以後，球隊計畫[[賀黑·波薩塔|賀黑·波薩塔]]退休以後，由他擔任接班人。無論如何，有些人對蒙特羅的身高和體重是否能夠每天都任捕手的工作抱持著懷疑的態度<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/blogs/yankees/2009/02/montero-is-posadas-heir-appare.html|title=Montero is Posada's heir apparent|first=Mark|last=Feinsand|work=[[New_York_Daily_News|New York Daily News]]|date=2009-02-11|accessdate=2009-02-11|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090213222516/http://www.nydailynews.com/blogs/yankees/2009/02/montero-is-posadas-heir-appare.html|archivedate=2009-02-13}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2011/09/06/sports/baseball/montero-leads-yankees-display-of-power-against-orioles.html|title=Yankees’ Top Prospect Fits In Quickly|first=Mark|last=Viera|work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|date=2011-09-06|accessdate=2011-09-06}}</ref>。

==早期生活==
2006年7月2日，蒙特羅和[[紐約洋基|紐約洋基]]簽下160萬美金的合約<ref>{{cite news|first=Enrique|last=Rojas|title=Top Latino prospects get major-league deals |url=http://sports.espn.go.com/mlb/news/story?id=2508432|publisher=ESPN.com|date=2006-07-03|accessdate=2008-01-25}}</ref>。蒙特羅被外界評價為具有長打能力的好球員<ref>{{cite news|first=John|last=Manuel|title=Baseball America: Prospects: Top 10 Prospects: New York Yankees|url=http://www.baseballamerica.com/today/prospects/features/262804.html|publisher=Baseball America|date=2006-11-08|accessdate=2008-01-25}}</ref>。2007年，17歲的蒙特羅在新人聯盟灣岸洋基（Gulf Coast Yankees）開始他的小聯盟職業生涯。2008年他獲得邀請參加春訓，在這裡他打出了第1支全壘打，也是他來到小聯盟以後的第1支全壘打<ref>{{cite news|first=Bryan|last=Hoch|title=Yankees invite 26 to Spring Training|url=http://mlb.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20080115&content_id=2346268&vkey=news_mlb&fext=.jsp&c_id=mlb|publisher=MLB.com|date=2008-01-15|accessdate=2008-01-25}}</ref>。在春訓之後，蒙特羅被送到1A查爾斯頓河狗（Charleston RiverDogs）開始他今年的球季，這一年總計他繳出0.326打擊率、17發全壘打和87分打點的成績<ref>{{cite news|title=MiLB.com Player Stats Page|url=http://web.minorleaguebaseball.com/milb/stats/stats.jsp?n=Jesus%20Montero&pos=C&sid=milb&t=p_pbp&pid=524968|publisher=MiLB.com|date=2008-09-02|accessdate=2008-11-20}}</ref>。

2009年球季，蒙特羅在佛羅里達聯盟高階1A的坦帕洋基（Tampa Yankees）開始，6月3日，球隊將他升級到送到2A的特倫頓轟雷（Trenton Thunder）<ref>{{cite news|first=Mike|last=Ashmore|title=Montero's coming|url=http://thunderbaseball.wordpress.com/2009/06/03/monteros-coming/|publisher=Mike Ashmore's Thunder Thoughts|date=2009-06-03}}</ref>。在這一年，他入選參加[[東方聯盟|東方聯盟]]的明星賽<ref>{{cite press release|url=http://www.trentonthunder.com/release/zRelease.asp?pYYYYMMDD=20090708|title=Thunder C Montero Added To EL All Star Roster|publisher=Trentonthunder.com|date=2009-07-08|accessdate=2010-09-01}}</ref>，也參加了全明星未來之星賽的比賽<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.altoonamirror.com/page/content.detail/id/521240.html|title=Yankees prospect Montero taken to hospital with hand injury|author=Cory Giger|work=[[Altoona_Mirror|Altoona Mirror]]|date=2009-08-01|accessdate=2009-08-02}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://yankees.lhblogs.com/2009/08/02/game-105-yankees-at-white-sox/|title=Game 105: Yankees at White Sox|work=LoHud Yankees Blog|publisher=The Journal News|first=Peter|last=Abraham|date=2009-08-02|accessdate=2009-08-02}}</ref>。另外今年還被評為洋基農場裡面第二名的選手<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.baseballamerica.com/today/prospects/rankings/organization-top-10-prospects/2009/267148.html|title=New York Yankees Top 10 Prospects, 2009|work=[[Baseball_America|Baseball America]]|date=2008-11-10|accessdate=2009-12-23}}</ref>，和被美國棒球雜誌評為第三名的選手<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.baseballamerica.com/blog/prospects/?p=5456|title=Baseball America Prospects Blog | Midseason Top 25 Prospects|publisher=Baseball America|date=2009-07-09|accessdate=2010-09-01}}</ref>。

2010|work=[[Baseball_America|Baseball America]]|date=2009-12-16|accessdate=2009-12-23}}</ref>，還被評為所有小聯盟球員裡面第五名的球員<ref>{{cite news|url=http://sportsillustrated.cnn.com/2009/baseball/mlb/12/23/top.prospects/index.html|work=CNN|date=2009-12-23|accessdate=2010-05-06|title=Nationals phenom  flashed top form in Fall League play|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100306075213/http://sportsillustrated.cnn.com/2009/baseball/mlb/12/23/top.prospects/index.html|archivedate=2010-03-06}}</ref>。在今年他或邀參加春訓<ref>{{cite News |publisher=Mlb.mlb.com|date=2010-02-23|accessdate=2010-09-01}}</ref>，在這裡他還受到洋基的打擊教練[[凱文·隆恩|凱文·隆恩]]指導他在大聯盟的打擊技巧<ref>{{cite Rights|first=Ben|last=Shpigel|date=2010-03-13|accessdate=2010-03-13}}</ref>。因為打擊上的改進受到球團的注意，而在季中他被送到3A的史克蘭頓/威爾克斯─巴里洋基（Scranton/Wilkes-Barre Yankees）<ref>{{cite news|last=Raskin|first=Alex|url=http://www.minorleaguebaseball.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20100713&content_id=12241592&vkey=news_milb&fext=.jsp|title=IL, PCL set to renew All-Star rivalry | MiLB.com news|url=http://www.timesleader.com/sports/Yankees_champs_again_08-29-2010.html|title=Yankees champs again|work=The Times Leader|publisher=Timesleader.com|date=2010-08-29|accessdate=2010-09-01|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100901114347/http://www.timesleader.com/sports/Yankees_champs_again_08-29-2010.html|archivedate=2010-09-01}}</ref>。

在2010年球季明星賽之後，洋基和[[西雅圖水手|西雅圖水手]]曾達成協議，要以蒙特羅和其他兩名潛力新秀交易[[克里夫·李|克里夫·李]]，但在最後一刻水手卻轉頭和[[德州遊騎兵|德州遊騎兵]]完成交易，遊騎兵以Justin Smoak和其他球員水手交易李<ref>{{cite news|url=http://seattletimes.nwsource.com/html/thehotstoneleague/2016295602_brian_cashman_jesus_montero_wo.html|title=Brian Cashman: Jesus Montero would have been best player "by far" traded for Cliff Lee|publisher=[[Seattle_Times|Seattle Times]]|first=Larry|last=Stone|date=2011-09-23|accessdate=2011-09-28}}</ref>，蒙特羅得以繼續留在洋基。

2011年，蒙特羅獲邀參加春訓，並且要競爭25人名單<ref>{{cite news|url=http://sports.espn.go.com/new-york/mlb/columns/story?columnist=marchand_andrew&id=5726945|first=Andrew|last=Marchand|title=Jesus Montero next in line for Yankees|work=ESPN New York|publisher=ESPN.com|date=2010-10-25|accessdate=2010-10-26}}</ref>，今年洋基計畫讓[[賀黑·波薩塔|賀黑·波薩塔]]擔任指定打擊，所以蒙特羅有機會競爭擔任先發捕手<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/sports/baseball/yankees/2010/11/05/2010-11-05_jesus_montero_will_get_chance_to_win_starting_catching_job_jorge_posada_will_sli.html|title=Jesus Montero will get chance to win starting catching job, Jorge Posada will slide in as Yankees DH|first=Mark|last=Feinsand|work=New York Daily News|date=2010-11-05|accessdate=2010-11-07}}</ref>。之後洋基讓[[羅素·馬丁|羅素·馬丁]]擔任先發捕手，蒙特羅則送至小聯盟<ref>{{cite news|url=http://yankees.lhblogs.com/2010/12/16/russell-martin-his-knee-his-hip-and-his-role/ |title=Russell Martin: His knee, his hip and his role |work=The Lohud Yankees Blog |publisher=Yankees.lhblogs.com |date=2010-12-16 |accessdate=2011-03-14}}</ref>。在春訓中受傷的[[弗朗西斯科·瑟維里|弗朗西斯科·瑟維里]]，則擔任第二號捕手<ref>{{cite news|last=Barbarisi |first=Daniel |url=http://online.wsj.com/article/SB10001424052748704570904576180972909728098.html |title=Yankees' Montero Gets His Chance |work=Wall Street Journal |publisher=WSJ.com |date=2011-03-05 |accessdate=2011-03-14}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=Shpigel |first=Ben |url=http://bats.blogs.nytimes.com/2011/03/04/cervelli-to-miss-6-8-weeks-with-broken-foot/ |title=Cervelli To Miss 6-8 Weeks With Broken Foot |publisher=New York Times |work=The New York Times Baseball Blog |date=2011-03-04 |accessdate=2011-03-14}}</ref>。

2012年，洋基與[[西雅圖水手|西雅圖水手]]達成協議，用蒙特羅與Héctor Noesí交換[[麥可·皮內達|皮內達]]及José Campos。

2016年3月27日，水手隊DFA蒙特羅，隔日多倫多藍鳥將其撿回，但在四月就將其劃出40人名單之外。蒙特羅至今仍在三A打拼。

===大聯盟時期===
2011年9月1日，蒙特羅的大聯盟初登板是在[[芬威球場|芬威球場]]面對[[波士頓紅襪|波士頓紅襪]]，這場比賽擔任指定打擊，4個打數並沒有擊出安打<ref name=nyt0901>{{cite web |last=Waldstein |first=David |url=http://bats.blogs.nytimes.com/2011/09/01/montero-is-set-for-his-major-league-debut/ |title=Yankees Welcome Jesus Montero to the Majors |work=NYTimes.com |publisher=Bats.blogs.nytimes.com |date=2011-09-01 |accessdate=2011-09-06 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=http://archive.wikiwix.com/cache/20110916150534/http://bats.blogs.nytimes.com/2011/09/01/montero-is-set-for-his-major-league-debut/ |archivedate=2011-09-16 }}</ref>。9月3日在主場[[洋基體育場|洋基體育場]]面對[[多倫多藍鳥|多倫多藍鳥]]時，於第6局2出局時擊出大聯盟生涯的第1支安打<ref>{{cite news|last=Boorstein|first=Thomas |url=http://newyork.yankees.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20110903&content_id=24148578&notebook_id=24148586&vkey=notebook_nyy&c_id=nyy |title=Montero on the board with first big league hit |publisher=MLB.com |work=Yankees.com Notebook |date=2011-09-03 |accessdate=2011-09-03}}</ref>。9月5日對[[巴爾的摩金鶯|巴爾的摩金鶯]]的比賽時，擊出大聯盟生涯的第1支全壘打，而且是單場2發全壘打，球隊最後以11：10獲勝<ref>{{cite news|last=Boorstein|first=Thomas |url=http://scores.espn.go.com/mlb/recap?gameId=310905110 |title= Jesus Montero blasts first 2 career HRs as Yanks edge O's |publisher=[[ESPN|ESPN]] |work=[[ESPN.com|ESPN.com]] Notebook |date=2011-09-05 |accessdate=2011-09-06}}</ref>，成為1993年[[曼尼·拉米瑞茲|曼尼·拉米瑞茲]]之後第1位21歲年紀的球員擊出單場2發全壘打的球員<ref>{{cite web|url=http://hardballtalk.nbcsports.com/2011/09/05/what-took-so-long-jesus-montero-homers-twice-for-yankees-in-fourth-career-game/ |title=What took so long?
