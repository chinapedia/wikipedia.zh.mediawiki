'''赫尔曼·凯恩'''（{{lang-en|'''Herman Cain'''}}，{{bd|1945年|12月13日|catIdx=Cain}}），[[美国|美国]][[佐治亚州|佐治亚州]]的作家、商业主管、电台主持、联合专栏作家及[[茶党运动|茶党运动]]支持者。<ref name=Memmot>{{cite web |title=GOP Presidential Contender Takes Lead in Second Poll |author=Mark Memmot |publisher=NPR |url=http://www.npr.org/blogs/thetwo-way/2011/10/13/141306111/gop-presidential-contender-herman-cain-takes-lead-in-second-poll}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Analyzing Herman Cain's 9-9-9 Tax Plan |author= Diana Furchtgott-Roth |publisher= Real Clear Markets |url=http://www.realclearmarkets.com/articles/2011/10/13/analyzing_herman_cains_9-9-9_tax_plan_99308.html}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Herman Cain launches run for the White House |author=Michael A. Memoli |publisher=L.A. Times |url=http://articles.latimes.com/2011/may/21/news/la-pn-herman-cain-announcement-20110521 |date=May 21, 2011}}</ref>他曾参与[[2012年美国总统选舉|2012年美国总统选舉]]共和党内初选。<ref name=Memmot/>

== 簡介 ==
凯恩成长于[[佐治亚州|佐治亚州]]，于1967年从[[莫尔豪斯学院|莫尔豪斯学院]]拿到[[数学|数学]]专业的理学学士学位。<ref name=Green>Green, Joshua [http://www.theatlantic.com/magazine/archive/1969/12/herman-cain-the-gop-wild-card/8367/ Herman Cain, the GOP Wild Card], ''[[The_Atlantic|The Atlantic]]''</ref>之后，他又于1971年从[[普渡大学|普渡大学]]拿到[[计算机科学|计算机科学]]专业的理学硕士学位，<ref name=purdue>{{cite web |url=https://www.science.purdue.edu/for-alumni-and-friends/alumni-recognition-a-awards/honorary-doctorates/83-herman-cain |title=Purdue University – College of Science – Herman Cain |publisher=Purdue University |quote=2004 Honorary Doctoral Recipient Computer Science, M.S. 1971 |accessdate=May 20, 2011 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110720041618/https://www.science.purdue.edu/for-alumni-and-friends/alumni-recognition-a-awards/honorary-doctorates/83-herman-cain |archivedate=2011年7月20日 }}</ref>并且还在[[美国海军部|美国海军部]]有一份全职工作。<ref name=civil>{{cite web|url=http://civilliberty.about.com/od/profiles/p/Herman-Cain-Civil-Liberties.htm |title=Herman Cain on Civil Liberties and Civil Rights - Civil Liberties Views of Herman Cain |publisher=Civilliberty.about.com |date= |accessdate=2012-01-15}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.theatlantic.com/politics/archive/2011/05/picture-of-the-day-herman-cain-in-high-school-and-college/239225/ |title= Picture of the Day: Herman Cain in High School and College |publisher=The Atlantic |date=May 20, 2011 |accessdate=August 15, 2011}}</ref>1977年，他进入[[皮尔斯百利公司|皮尔斯百利公司]]，在该公司最高做到副总裁。<ref name=ericberg>{{cite news |url=http://www.nytimes.com/1988/09/20/business/godfather-s-pizza-sold-by-pillsbury.html|title= Godfather's Pizza Sold By Pillsbury|first=Eric|last=Berg|date=September 20, 1988|work=[[纽约时报|The New York Times]]|accessdate=December 19, 2011}}</ref>

20世纪80年代，他在[[汉堡王|汉堡王]]的成功运作使得皮尔斯百利公司任命他为{{link-en|教父披萨|Godfather's Pizza}}的主席和首席执行官，他在该职位上从1986年做到1996年。<ref name="Henninger">{{cite news |last=Henninger |first=Daniel |title=Taking Cain Seriously |newspaper=The Wall Street Journal |date=September 29, 2011}}</ref> 

1989年到1991年，凯恩任[[堪萨斯城联邦储备银行|堪萨斯城联邦储备银行]]奥马哈支行主席。<ref name=kcfed>{{cite web |url=http://kansascityfed.org/publicat/newsroom/2011pdf/press.release.05.26.11.pdf |format=PDF |title=Herman Cain's Service as a Director of the Federal Reserve Bank of Kansas City |publisher=kansascityfed.org |date=May 26, 2011 |accessdate=October 28, 2011 |author=Thomas M. Hoenig, president and chief executive officer |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110617100029/http://www.kansascityfed.org/publicat/newsroom/2011pdf/press.release.05.26.11.pdf |archivedate=2011年6月17日 }}</ref>他于1992-1994年成为堪萨斯城联邦储备银行副主席，1995-1996年晋升为主席。<ref name=kcfed/>1995年，受[[美國眾議院議長|美國眾議院議長]][[紐特·金里奇|紐特·金里奇]]任命，凯恩进入[[肯普委员会|肯普委员会]]，<ref>{{cite news|url=http://news.google.com/newspapers?id=3x5EAAAAIBAJ&sjid=9rAMAAAAIBAJ&pg=2918,3644773&dq=economic-growth-and-tax-reform-commission+cain&hl=en|title= Cain should impress voters|first=|last=|date=September 1, 2003|work=[[The_Albany_Herald|The Albany Herald]]|accessdate=December 3, 2011}}</ref>并担任[[鲍勃·多尔|鲍勃·多尔]]总统竞选时的高级经济顾问。<ref name=rlc>{{cite web|author=Posted by: admin |url=http://www.rlc2011.com/speakers_list/herman-cain/ |title=Herman Cain |publisher=Rlc2011.com |date= |accessdate=August 6, 2011|page=m}}</ref>1996－1999年，凯恩成为全国餐饮业协会的首席执行官。<ref name="NYTimesLobbyist">{{cite news |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2011/10/23/us/politics/herman-cain-running-as-outsider-came-to-washington-as-lobbyist.html |title=Cain, Now Running as Outsider, Came to Washington as Lobbyist |publisher=nytimes.com |newspaper=New York Times |date=October 22, 2011 |first=Sheryl Gay |last=Stoberg}}</ref><ref name="NYTimesLobbyist" /><ref name="politico-accused">{{cite web |work = Politico |title = Herman Cain accused by two women of inappropriate behavior |url = http://www.politico.com/news/stories/1011/67194.html |date = October 31, 2011 |accessdate = October 31, 2011 |first = Jonathan |last = Martin |coauthors = Maggie Halberman; Anna Palmer; Kenneth P. Vogel}}</ref>

2011年5月，凯恩宣布自己参加总统竞选，成為共和黨唯一的[[非裔美國人|非裔美國人]]參選人。他提出的“9-9-9税收计划”以及在辩论中的表现，使他在2011年秋季共和党候选人中成为领跑者，在民意调查中领先于[[巴拉克·奥巴马|奥巴马]]总统。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.rasmussenreports.com/public_content/politics/obama_administration/daily_presidential_tracking_poll|title= Daily Presidential Tracking Poll|first=|last=|date=February 5, 2012|work=[[拉斯穆森报导|Rasmussen Reports]]|accessdate=February 5, 2012}}</ref>11月，凯恩受到性丑闻指控，这最终导致他在12月3日宣布中止竞选活动。<ref>Robin Abcarian, [http://www.latimes.com/news/nationworld/nation/la-na-cain-decision-20111203,0,65635.story Herman Cain prepares announcement on campaign's future] ''[[Los_Angeles_Times|Los Angeles Times]]'' December 2, 2011</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.foxnews.com/politics/2011/12/03/cain-prepares-to-discuss-campaigns-future-after-damage-assessment/|title= Cain Suspends Presidential Campaign, Cites 'Hurt' Caused by 'False' Allegations|publisher=Fox News|date=December 3, 2011|accessdate=December 3, 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.washingtontimes.com/news/2011/dec/2/end-line-cain-announcement-saturday/|title= Cain suspends campaign shifting GOP race|first=Shannon|last=McCaffrey|date=December 2, 2011|work=[[华盛顿时报|The Washington Times]]|accessdate=December 3, 2011}}</ref>2012年1月28日，凯恩公开宣布，他支持[[紐特·金里奇|紐特·金里奇]]作为共和党的总统候选人。<ref name=ap2012>{{cite news|url=http://thecaucus.blogs.nytimes.com/2012/01/28/cain-endorses-gingrich-for-president/|title=Cain Endorses Gingrich for President |publisher=The New York Times|newspaper=[[纽约时报|The New York Times]]|date=January 28, 2012|first= Katharine|last=Seeyle}}</ref>他其后支持[[米特·羅姆尼|米特·羅姆尼]]參選[[美國總統|美國總統]]。
