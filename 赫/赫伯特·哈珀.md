'''赫伯特·哈珀''' (1920年7月2日—2012年1月21日<ref>[http://www.lajazz.org/Obits/HerbieHarperobit.html Herbie Harper 1920-2012] {{webarchive|url=https://archive.is/20130630084747/http://www.lajazz.org/Obits/HerbieHarperobit.html |date=2013-06-30 }} Los Angeles Jazz Society. </ref>)是一个 [[美国|美国]][[爵士乐|爵士乐]][[长号|长号手]]。

出生于薩利納，他在1940年代和1950年代第一次开始与与[[本尼·古德曼|本尼·古德曼]]和[[查理·斯皮瓦克|查理·斯皮瓦克]]玩[[搖擺樂|搖擺音樂]]。<ref>{{Cite web|url={{Allmusic|class=artist|id=p6671|pure_url=yes}}|title=Herbie Harper Biography|accessdate=2007-06-24|last=Yanow|first=Scott|authorlink=Scott Yanow|publisher=[[Allmusic|Allmusic]]}}</ref><ref>''All Music Guide to Jazz, The experts' guide to the best jazz recordings,'' second edition, edited by [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michael_Erlewine Michael Erlewine], San Francisco: [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Miller_Freeman,_Inc. Miller Freeman] Books, 1996</ref><ref>''All Music Guide to Jazz, The definitive guide to jazz music,'' fourth edition, edited by [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vladimir_Bogdanov Vladimir Bogdanov], Chris Woodstra, and [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stephen_Thomas_Erlewine Stephen Thomas Erlewine], San Francisco: [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Backbeat_Books Backbeat Books], 2002</ref><ref>''The New Grove Dictionary of Jazz, First edition,'' two volumes, edited by [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barry_Kernfeld Barry Kernfeld],  London: [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Macmillan_Press Macmillan Press], 1988</ref><ref>''The New Grove Dictionary of Jazz,'' edited by [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barry_Kernfeld Barry Kernfeld], New York: [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/St._Martin's_Press St. Martin's Press], 1994</ref><ref>''The New Grove Dictionary of Jazz, Second edition,'' Three volumes, edited by [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barry_Kernfeld Barry Kernfeld], London: [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Macmillan_Publishers Macmillan Publishers], 2002</ref>
