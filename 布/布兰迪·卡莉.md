'''布兰迪·M·卡莉'''（{{lang-en|'''Brandi M. Carlile'''}}，{{bd|1981年|6月1日|catIdx=C}}）是一位美国{{link-en|另类乡村|Alternative country}}和[[民谣摇滚|民谣摇滚]][[创作歌手|创作歌手]]。<ref>{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.medleyville.us/2006/04/qa_brandi_carlile.html |title=Medleyville: Q&A: BRANDI CARLILE |publisher=Medleyville.us |date=2006-04-04 |accessdate=2011-09-10}}</ref>她已发行多张唱片，包括《The Story》，《Give Up the Ghost》和《Live at Benaroya Hall with the Seattle Symphony》。专辑曾经达到“Top Rock Albums”排行榜第14名。<ref name="Graff">{{cite news|last=Graff|first=Gary|title=Brandi Carlile Finishes Fourth Album Before Ray LaMontagne Tour|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/471645/brandi-carlile-finishes-fourth-album-before-ray-lamontagne-tour|accessdate=2011-09-18|newspaper=Billboard|date=2011-05-10}}</ref>
