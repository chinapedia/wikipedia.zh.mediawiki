'''布莉·歐森'''（{{lang-en|Bree Olson}}，{{bd|1986年|10月7日||}}）是名[[美國|美國]][[色情演員|色情演員]]及[[閣樓_(雜誌)|閣樓]]寵物。<ref name=PenthouseMagazine>{{cite web | url=http://www.penthousemagazine.com | title=Penthouse Magazine }}</ref>

歐森在2006年11月進入[[成人電影|成人電影]]界<ref name=Signs/>，曾與[[數位遊樂場|數位遊樂場]]（Digital Playground）、[[優雅天使|優雅天使]]（Elegant Angel）和[[紅燈區影帶公司|紅燈區影帶公司]]（Red Light District Video）等製作公司合作。<ref name=aboutme/> 她在2007年與[[亞當與伊芙|亞當與伊芙]]（Adam & Eve）簽下三年合約。<ref name=Signs/><ref>{{cite web|url=http://business.avn.com/articles/Bree-Olson-Splits-With-Adam-Eve-409845.html|title=Bree Olson Splits With Adam & Eve|last=Peter Warren|date=2010-08-27|work=AVN|accessdate=28 August 2010}}</ref> 歐森從小就希望成為一位色情演員，但一直到20歲與交往四年的男友分手後才進入成人電影界。<ref name=Signs>{{cite web |url=http://www.avn.com/performer/articles/28932.html|title=Adam & Eve Signs Bree Olson|author=David Sullivan|date=2007-07-23|work=[[成人影帶新聞|成人影帶新聞]]|accessdate=2008-12-04}}</ref> 她已演出超過170部影片<ref name=iafd/>，並被選為2008年3月的閣樓寵物。<ref name=PenthouseMagazine /><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.avn.com/porn-stars/64743.html |title=Bree Olson |publisher=AVN |date=2008-02-06 |accessdate=2010-02-21 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090905230737/http://www.avn.com/porn-stars/64743.html |archivedate=2009-09-05 }}</ref>

歐森曾演出實境秀《Keeping Up with the Kardashians》<ref>{{cite web|author=Published 12/18/07 by |url=http://www.dotspotter.com/news/536303_Did_the_Kardashians_Hire_a_Porn_Star_Nanny |title=Did the Kardashians Hire a Porn Star Nanny? |publisher=Dotspotter.com |date= |accessdate=2010-02-21 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081205022806/http://www.dotspotter.com/news/536303_Did_the_Kardashians_Hire_a_Porn_Star_Nanny |archivedate=2008-12-05 }}</ref><ref>[http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1122731/ Keeping Up with the Kardashians, Managing Mom]</ref>，並登上《Opie and Anthony》<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.foundrymusic.com/opieanthony/displayheadline.cfm/id/12953/page/show_headline_number_12953.html |title=Bree Olson and Kayden Kross return |date=2008-09-11 |publisher=foundrymusic.com |accessdate=2008-12-04}}</ref>、《The Howard Stern Show]》<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.howardstern.com/rundown.hs?d=1191988800|title=Does Age Matter? |date=2007-10-27|publisher=howardstern.com|accessdate=2008-12-04}}</ref>《Insomniac with Dave Attell》<ref name=ReSigns/>、《Spike Guys' Choice Awards》<ref name=ReSigns>{{cite web |url=http://business.avn.com/articles/36155.html|title=Adam & Eve Re-Signs Bree Olson|date=2009-08-24|work=[[成人影帶新聞|成人影帶新聞]]|accessdate=2009-10-28}}</ref>、《James Gunn's PG Porn》等節目和饒舌歌手[[Flo_Rida|佛羅里達]]的《Zoosk Girl》音樂錄影帶。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://business.avn.com/articles/37922.html|title=Bree Olson Stars in Flo Rida-T Pain Music Video |date=2010-05-03 |publisher=AVN.com|accessdate=2010-05-04}}</ref>
