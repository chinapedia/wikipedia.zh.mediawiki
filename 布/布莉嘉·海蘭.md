'''布莉嘉·海蘭'''（{{lang-en|Briga Heelan}}，{{bd|1987年|4月2日}}<ref name=FamilySearch-USPR-Andover>{{cite web|title=Briga D Heelan, "United States Public Records"|url=https://familysearch.org/pal:/MM9.1.1/QJGP-4RJ3|website=United States Public Records|publisher=FamilySearch|accessdate=7 June 2014}}</ref>）為美國喜劇女演員，知名的作品有[[熟女鎮|熟女鎮]]、[[愛情零距離|愛情零距離]]以及[[不懂約會|不懂約會]]。

== 早期生活 ==
布莉嘉出生於[[马萨诸塞州|麻薩諸塞州]]的[[安多佛|安多佛]]<ref name=ProspectTheater-2014>{{cite web|title=Meet the Cast – Part 3. Name: Briga Heelan|url=http://prospecttheater.wordpress.com/|website=Prospect Theater Company's Weblog|accessdate=7 June 2014|date=October 14, 2010}}</ref> ，母親 Kimball Heelan 是國小老師，父親 Kevin Heelan 則是劇作家。<ref name=Improper-GroundUp-2014>{{cite web|last1=Soroff|first1=Jonathan|title=Briga Heelan - From the Ground Up|url=http://www.improper.com/features/briga-heelan-from-the-ground-up/|website=Improper Bostonian|accessdate=7 June 2014|date=May 2014}}</ref><ref name=Phillips-Andover-KevinHeelan>{{cite web|title=Kevin Heelan: Instructor of Playwriting, Acting and Directing |url=http://www.andover.edu/Academics/TheatreDance/Faculty/Pages/Kevin-Heelan.aspx|website=[[Phillips_Academy_Andover|Phillips Academy Andover]]|accessdate=7 June 2014}}</ref> 她有一個兄弟 Conor。<ref name=Phillips-Andover-KevinHeelan />

布莉嘉的父親，從1983年於[[菲利普斯学院|菲利普斯学院]]任教。<ref name=AndoverBulletin-KevinWheelan-91-93>{{cite web|last1=Britton|first1=Sharon|title=Kevin Wheelan: Bring On the Ghosts|url=https://archive.org/stream/andoverbulletin9193phil|website=Andover Bulletin|publisher=Phillips Academy|accessdate=7 June 2014|pages=14–15|date=Fall 1997}}</ref> 布莉嘉的父母是在她父親於[[史密斯學院|史密斯學院]]攻讀碩士時認識。<ref name=Phillips-Andover-KevinHeelan />

布莉嘉曾就讀於[[南加州大学|南加州大学]]<ref name=Improper-GroundUp-2014 />。

== 職業生涯 ==
布莉嘉一開始自認要從事音樂劇演出「從頭到尾都這樣認為，一直到我大學第一年之後。就像是，我覺得有另外一條路。就這樣碰上喜劇。然後我非常開心最終是這樣。」<ref name=TVLine-8Things-2013 />

布莉嘉的電視劇突破是參與[[熟女鎮|熟女鎮]]當中的演出。<ref name=HitFix-Sepinwall-CougarTown-2012>{{cite web|last1=Sepinwall|first1=Alan|title=Review: 'Cougar Town' - 'Something Big': Who's Your Daddy|url=http://www.hitfix.com/blogs/whats-alan-watching/posts/cougar-town-something-big-whos-your-daddy|website=[[HitFix|HitFix]]|accessdate=7 June 2014|date=20 March 2012}}</ref>

布莉嘉表示她第一次見到[[愛情零距離|愛情零距離]]的男主角[[史蓋勒·奧斯丁|史蓋勒·奧斯丁]]，是在一場音樂劇的場合。<ref name=TVLine-8Things-2013>{{cite web|last1=Mitovich|first1=Matt Webb|title=Ground Floor's Briga Heelan: 8 Things to Know About the TBS Comedy's Pretty 'Revelation' |url=http://tvline.com/2013/11/14/ground-floor-preview-tbs-briga-heelan/|website=[[TVLine|TVLine]]|accessdate=7 June 2014|date=14 November 2013}}</ref>

布莉嘉同時參與製作人比尔·劳伦斯底下的兩部喜劇[[愛情零距離|愛情零距離]]以及[[不懂約會|不懂約會]]，<ref name=HitFix-FienPrint-BLawrenceInterview-2014>{{cite web|last1=Fienberg|first1=Daniel|title=Interview: Bill Lawrence and Adam Sztykiel on 'Undateable' and The State of TV Comedy|url=http://www.hitfix.com/the-fien-print/interview-bill-lawrence-and-adam-sztykiel-on-undateable-and-the-state-of-tv-comedy/single-page|website=[[HitFix|HitFix]]|accessdate=7 June 2014|date=29 May 2014|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140714125702/http://www.hitfix.com/the-fien-print/interview-bill-lawrence-and-adam-sztykiel-on-undateable-and-the-state-of-tv-comedy/single-page|archivedate=2014年7月14日}}</ref> 兩部都是多鏡頭的喜劇類戲劇，並比單鏡頭戲劇所需製作時間較少。雖然在[[不懂約會|不懂約會]]裡的鏡頭較多，然而在該部劇中依然被認為是客串演出，並且增加三集。<ref name=Deadline-Recurring-2013>{{cite web|last1=Andreeva|first1=Nellie|title=Briga Heelan Back On NBC Comedy ‘Undateable’ As Recurring|url=http://www.deadline.com/2013/09/briga-heelan-back-on-nbc-comedy-undateable-as-recurring/|website=[[Deadline.com|Deadline]]|publisher=PMC|accessdate=7 June 2014|date=5 September 2013}}</ref>

布莉嘉在「愛情零距離」的演出中：電視評論者阿伦·赛平沃尔表示布莉嘉「非常適合珍妮這個角色：溫暖且古靈精怪，同時讓人覺得堅強與獨立的角色。」  自「六人行」後創出的現象－－一個主要角色使多鏡頭喜劇的邊緣化－－讓有吸引力的演員或女演員能夠有效地帶出笑點與喜劇效果，而布莉嘉是少數能做出這種效果的演員。<ref name=HitFix-Sepinwall-GroundFloor-2013>{{cite web|last1=Sepinwall|first1=Alan|title=Opposites Attract Comedy from Lawrence & Malins a Reminder that Multi-Cam Can Be Fun|url=http://www.hitfix.com/whats-alan-watching/review-tbs-ground-floor-a-retro-comedy-treat|website=[[HitFix|HitFix]]|accessdate=7 June 2014|date=13 November 2013}}</ref><ref name=HitFix-Sepinwall-PickedUp-2014>{{cite web|last1=Sepinwall|first1=Alan|title=Brody Has to Choose Between Mansfield and Jenny; Will the Show Come Back?|url=http://www.hitfix.com/whats-alan-watching/season-finale-review-ground-floor-the-decision-come-fly-with-me|website=[[HitFix|HitFix]]|accessdate=7 June 2014|date=17 January 2014}}</ref> [[洛杉磯時報|洛杉磯時報]]評論布莉嘉使「愛情零距離」更可看，並進一步指出布莉嘉出色地演出；她使她的角色更活靈活現。我可以輕易地穿件轉台去看她的演出--而且特地去看。<ref name=LATimes-GFloor-Watchable-2013>{{cite news|last1=Lloyd|first1=Robert|title=TV review: Briga Heelan makes 'Ground Floor' watchable|url=http://www.latimes.com/entertainment/tv/showtracker/la-et-st-ground-floor-review-story.html|accessdate=7 June 2014|work=[[L.A._Times|L.A. Times]]|date=14 November 2013}}</ref>

== 個人生活 ==
布莉嘉目前住在洛杉磯。<ref name=Improper-GroundUp-2014/>

關於她的特殊名字，她曾表示「那是義大利名字，事實上，我的姓過去是O’Heelahan。而我的名字布莉嘉，我曾經在別的地方也看到過，是凱特爾的女神名，聽起來還不錯，所以我真的很喜歡我的名字。但事實上我的名字是來自我祖母的好朋友，她住在都柏林。」<ref name=Improper-GroundUp-2014/>

2014年10月17日，布莉嘉於個人的推特上宣布與愛情零距離合作演員Rene Gube訂婚。<ref name=布莉嘉推特>{{cite web|title=Briga Heelan tweet|url=https://twitter.com/BrigaHeelan/status/522848350702690304|date=17 October 2014}}</ref>
