'''布里格豪斯'''（Brighouse）是[[英格蘭|英格蘭]][[西約克郡|西約克郡]]的一個城市<ref name=EB1911>{{cite EB1911|wstitle=Brighouse}}</ref>，位於哈利法克斯以東{{convert|4|mi|km}}。據2011年英國人口普查，布里格豪斯 / 拉斯翠克有人口32,360人<ref>http://www.ons.gov.uk/ons/rel/census/census-2001-key-statistics/urban-areas-in-england-and-wales/urban-areas-in-england-and-wales-ks01-usual-resident-population.xls</ref>。布里格豪斯本身有人口11,195人<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.neighbourhood.statistics.gov.uk/dissemination/LeadKeyFigures.do?a=7&b=13689876&c=Brighouse&d=14&e=62&g=6370442&i=1001x1003x1032x1004&m=0&r=1&s=1456224800688&enc=1|title=Calderdale Ward population 2011|accessdate=23 February 2016|publisher=Office for National Statistics|work=Neighbourhood Statistics}}</ref>。
