'''布拉'''（Bula）是[[印度尼西亚|印度尼西亚]][[马鲁古省|马鲁古省]]的一个城镇，位于[[斯兰岛|斯兰岛]]的东北海岸<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.indonesiatravelingguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/Seram.jpg|title=Seram Island (map)|publisher=Indonesiatravelingguide.com|accessdate=8 June 2015}}</ref>，是[[东斯兰县|东斯兰县]]的实际行政中心。2010年人口普查，'''布拉区'''（[[印尼语|印尼语]]：Kecamatan Bula）共有人口24,037。城镇附近是Bula Fields，建于1919年，现有石油储备<ref name="Association1994">{{cite book|last=Association|first=Indonesian Petroleum|title=Proceedings of the Annual Convention - Indonesian Petroleum Association|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Z4ZQAQAAIAAJ|year=1994|publisher=Indonesian Petroleum Association.}}</ref><ref name="Areas1994">{{cite book|title=Proceedings of the ... Session: Technical reports|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=X4MaAQAAIAAJ|year=1994|publisher=Committee for Co-ordination of Joint Prospecting for Mineral Resources in Asian Offshore Areas Project Office|isbn=978-974-89158-0-7|page=29}}</ref>。布拉的部分居民使用{{le|马西望语|Masiwang language|马西望语}}<ref name="Frawley2003">{{cite book|last=Frawley|first=William J.|title=International Encyclopedia of Linguistics|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=sl_dDVctycgC&pg=PA299|year=2003|publisher=Oxford University Press|isbn=978-0-19-513977-8|page=299}}</ref>。城镇东南有一座小机场。
