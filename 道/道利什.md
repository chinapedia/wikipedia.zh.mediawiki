'''道利什'''（Dawlish）是[[英格蘭|英格蘭]][[德文郡|德文郡]]的一個城鎮的民政教區，位於德文郡南海岸，距埃克塞特有{{convert|12|mi|km}}。道利什2001年有人口12,819人<ref>[http://www.neighbourhood.statistics.gov.uk/dissemination/viewFullDataset.do?instanceSelection=03070&productId=779&$ph=60_61&datasetInstanceId=3070&startColumn=1&numberOfColumns=8&containerAreaId=790364 Office for National Statistics : ''Census 2001 : Parish Headcounts : Teignbridge''] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130524064816/http://www.neighbourhood.statistics.gov.uk/dissemination/viewFullDataset.do?instanceSelection=03070&productId=779&$ph=60_61&datasetInstanceId=3070&startColumn=1&numberOfColumns=8&containerAreaId=790364 |date=2013-05-24 }} Retrieved 27 January 2010</ref>，比2011年的11,312人減少<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.citypopulation.de/php/uk-england-southwestengland.php?cityid=E34001067|title= Town population 2011|accessdate= 18 February 2015}}</ref>。
