<!-- 定義及症狀 -->
'''不寧腿綜合症'''（英語：'''Restless legs syndrome''', '''RLS'''），又稱'''睡眠肢動症'''、'''睡眠腿動症'''、'''不安腿综合征'''、'''不寧腿症候群'''、'''Willis-Ekbom病'''或'''Wittmaack-Ekbom症候群'''是一種強烈想要讓腿部移動的障礙<ref name=NIH2010What>{{cite web|title=What Is Restless Legs Syndrome?|url=http://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/health-topics/topics/rls/|website=NHLBI|accessdate=19 August 2016|date=November 1, 2010|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160821190844/http://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/health-topics/topics/rls/|archivedate=21 August 2016}}</ref>。平常患者腿部有一種令人不快的感覺，這種感覺會隨著腿部移動而有所改善<ref name=NIH2010What/>，不適感可能是疼痛、刺痛或是蟲爬感<ref name=NIH2010What/>，雙臂偶爾也可能受到影響<ref name=NIH2010What/>。 不寧腿綜合徵通常在休息時發生，因此可能使患者難以[[睡眠|入睡]]<ref name=NIH2010What/>。 由於睡眠不良，患者可能會在白天嗜睡、精力不集中、煩躁不安和[[抑郁_(情绪)|情緒低落]]<ref name=NIH2010What/>。此外，許多人在睡眠時都有{{le|定期肢體運動障礙|Periodic limb movement disorder|肢體抽動}}的狀況<ref name=NIH2010Sym>{{cite web|title=What Are the Signs and Symptoms of Restless Legs Syndrome?|url=http://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/health-topics/topics/rls/signs|website=NHLBI|accessdate=19 August 2016|date=November 1, 2010|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160827200314/http://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/health-topics/topics/rls/signs|archivedate=27 August 2016}}</ref>。

<!--病因及診斷-->
导致不寧腿綜合症的危险因素包括[[缺铁|缺铁]]、[[腎功能衰竭|腎功能衰竭]]、[[帕金森氏症|帕金森氏症]]、[[糖尿病|糖尿病]]、[[類風濕性關節炎|類風濕性關節炎]]和妊娠。<ref name=NIH2010What/><ref name=AFP2013/> 一些药物治疗也会触发症状。包括[[抗抑郁药|抗抑郁药]]、[[抗精神病药|抗精神病药]]、 [[抗組織胺藥|抗組織胺藥]]和[[钙离子通道阻滞剂|钙离子通道阻滞剂]]<ref name=NIH2010Ca>{{cite web|title=What Causes Restless Legs Syndrome?|url=http://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/health-topics/topics/rls/causes|website=NHLBI|accessdate=19 August 2016|date=November 1, 2010}}</ref>。不寧腿綜合症主要有以下两个类型<ref name=NIH2010What/>。一种是早期的发病，在45岁之前。發生在家族中，随着时间推移而恶化。另一种是后期的发病，在45岁之后，突然发生并且不会恶化<ref name=NIH2010What/>。诊断通常是在排除了其他潜在因素之后，根据病人的症状而定。<ref name=NIH2010Diag>{{cite web|title=How Is Restless Legs Syndrome Diagnosed?|url=http://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/health-topics/topics/rls/diagnosis|website=NHLBI|accessdate=19 August 2016|date=November 1, 2010}}</ref>。

<!-- 治療及流行病學 -->
如果能處理好潛在問題，就可以解決不寧腿症候群的狀況<ref name=NIH2010Tx>{{cite web|title=How Is Restless Legs Syndrome Treated?|url=http://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/health-topics/topics/rls/treatment|website=NHLBI|accessdate=19 August 2016|date=November 1, 2010|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160827200302/http://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/health-topics/topics/rls/treatment|archivedate=27 August 2016}}</ref>；其他治療方式包括改變生活方式和藥物治療<ref name=NIH2010What/>有助於消除不寧腿症候群的生活方式改變包括戒酒、戒菸、以及{{le|睡眠衛教|sleep hygiene}}<ref name=NIH2010Tx/>；藥物使用包括[[左旋多巴|左旋多巴]]或{{le|多巴胺激動劑|Dopamine agonist}}，如{{le|普拉克索|Pramipexole}}<ref name=AFP2013/>。不寧腿症候群大約影響美國人口中的2.5-15%<ref name=AFP2013>{{cite journal|last=Ramar|first=K|author2=Olson, EJ |title=Management of common sleep disorders.|journal=American Family Physician|date=Aug 15, 2013|volume=88|issue=4|pages=231–8|pmid=23944726}}</ref>；性別方面，女性比男性更易受影響；隨著年齡漸增也會更常見。<ref name=NIH2010Age>{{cite web|title=Who Is at Risk for Restless Legs Syndrome?|url=http://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/health-topics/topics/rls/atrisk|website=NHLBI|accessdate=19 August 2016|date=November 1, 2010|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160826015603/http://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/health-topics/topics/rls/atrisk|archivedate=26 August 2016}}</ref>。

== 症状 ==
不寧腿綜合症的感觉包括疼痛或肌肉疼痛，“瘙痒却不能刮擦”、一种“嗡嗡的感觉”、一种令人不快的“挠痒痒”、“爬行”的感觉，或者在醒着的时候肢体抽搐。这种感觉通常在安静的清醒状态下开始或加强，比如放松、阅读、学习或者尝试着睡觉。<ref name=pmid14592341>{{cite journal |pages=101–19 |doi=10.1016/S1389-9457(03)00010-8 |title=Restless legs syndrome: diagnostic criteria, special considerations, and epidemiology A report from the restless legs syndrome diagnosis and epidemiology workshop at the National Institutes of Health |year=2003 |last1=Allen |first1=R |journal=Sleep Medicine |volume=4 |issue=2 |pmid=14592341 |last2=Picchietti |first2=D |last3=Hening |first3=WA |last4=Trenkwalder |first4=C |last5=Walters |first5=AS |last6=Montplaisi |first6=J |author7=Restless Legs Syndrome Diagnosis Epidemiology workshop at the National Institutes of Health |author8=International Restless Legs Syndrome Study Group}}</ref>

这是一种“{{le|谱性障碍|Spectrum disorder}}”疾病。有些人只经历了一点小烦恼，而另一些人则在生活质量上有严重的睡眠和缺陷。<ref>{{cite journal |pages=807–15 |doi=10.1016/j.sleep.2010.07.007 |title=Restless legs syndrome: Understanding its consequences and the need for better treatment |year=2010 |last1=Earley |first1=Christopher J. |last2=Silber |first2=Michael H. |journal=Sleep Medicine |volume=11 |issue=9 |pmid=20817595}}</ref>

==病因==
不寧腿綜合症通常是由于[[缺铁|缺铁]]导致的，这一情况占病因20%。<ref name="pmid10905782">{{cite journal |pmid=10905782 |year=2000 |title=Restless legs syndrome: detection and management in primary care. National Heart, Lung, and Blood Institute Working Group on Restless Legs Syndrome |volume=62 |issue=1 |pages=108–14 |journal=American family physician}}</ref> 一项在2007年发表的研究显示34%的不寧腿綜合症是由于缺铁导致的。<ref name="pmid17368978">{{cite journal |pages=247–51 |doi=10.1016/j.sleep.2006.10.004 |title=Restless legs syndrome in Indian patients having iron deficiency anemia in a tertiary care hospital |year=2007 |last1=Rangarajan |first1=Sunad |last2=d'Souza |first2=George Albert |journal=Sleep Medicine |volume=8 |issue=3 |pmid=17368978}}</ref>

==历史==
已知最早对于不寧腿綜合徵的描述是于1672年由托马斯·威里司所作。<ref name=pmid15165536
>{{cite journal |pages=279–83 |doi=10.1016/j.sleep.2004.01.002 |title=Restless legs syndrome: an historical note |year=2004 |last1=Coccagna |first1=G |journal=Sleep Medicine |volume=5 |issue=3 |pmid=15165536 |last2=Vetrugno |first2=R |last3=Lombardi |first3=C |last4=Provini |first4=F}}</ref> 威里司强调具有不寧腿綜合徵的人群会睡觉中断并且具有肢体动作。这个最初用拉丁文发表（《The London Practice of Physick》，1685年）

==争议==
一些医生表示不寧腿綜合徵的症状过去曾被一些制药公司夸大。<ref>{{cite journal |doi=10.1371/journal.pmed.0030170 |title=Giving Legs to Restless Legs: A Case Study of How the Media Helps Make People Sick |year=2006 |last1=Woloshin |first1=Steven |last2=Schwartz |first2=Lisa M. |journal=PLoS Medicine |volume=3 |issue=4 |pages=e170 |pmid=16597175 |pmc=1434499}}</ref> 其他人认为这是一种被低估和未得到治疗的疾病<ref name="ChestReview2006">{{cite journal |pages=1596–604 |doi=10.1378/chest.130.5.1596 |title=Restless Legs Syndrome: A Clinical Update |year=2006 |last1=Gamaldo |first1=C. E. |last2=Earley |first2=C. J. |journal=Chest |volume=130 |issue=5 |pmid=17099042}}</ref>。此外，[[葛蘭素史克|葛蘭素史克]]还制作了一些广告。虽然没有推广使用他们的药物来治疗不寧腿綜合徵，但是这个广告可以链接到一个支持Ekbom小组的网站。该网站建议使用罗匹尼罗来治疗不寧腿綜合徵。英国制药工业协会对葛兰素史克这种方式表示反对。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.timesonline.co.uk/tol/news/uk/article601394.ece|title=Glaxo's cure for 'restless legs' was an unlicensed drug|last=Templeton|first=Sarah-Kate |date=August 6, 2006|work=Times Online|publisher=Times Newspapers Ltd.|accessdate=2009-07-24}}</ref>
